## Feature Gap Analysis

### Most Common Features (Table Stakes)
1. Daily reminders/notifications
2. Audio content
3. Progress tracking
4. Offline mode
5. Social sharing

### Differentiating Features (Competitive Advantages)
1. Celebrity narrations (Hallow)
2. Sleep content depth (Hallow, Abide)
3. Community prayer wall (Pray.com)
4. Journaling + mood (Glorify)
5. Church integration (YouVersion)
6. Multi-platform content (Pray.com podcasts)

### Underserved Features (Opportunity)
1. AI-powered personalization
2. Video devotionals
3. Interactive Bible studies
4. Men-focused content
5. Short-form content (TikTok-style)
6. Gamification beyond streaks
7. Integration with smart speakers
8. Family sharing with kid profiles
9. Accountability partners
10. Prayer answered analytics
