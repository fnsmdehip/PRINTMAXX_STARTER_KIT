# Sales Page Teardowns - What's Working in Info Products

## High-Converting Sales Page Elements

### Structure That Converts

**Above the Fold:**
1. Clear headline (problem or outcome)
2. Subheadline (who it's for + what they get)
3. Visual (product mockup or video)
4. Primary CTA button
5. Social proof snippet

**Body:**
1. Problem agitation
2. Solution introduction
3. Benefit bullets (not features)
4. Social proof (testimonials)
5. Product breakdown
6. Bonus stack
7. Pricing/offer
8. FAQ
9. Guarantee
10. Final CTA

## Teardown #1: She Reads Truth (Faith)

### What Works

**Headline Approach:**
- "Study the Word of God Every Day"
- Clear, outcome-focused
- Not clever, just clear

**Visual Design:**
- Clean, minimal aesthetic
- High-quality photography
- Consistent brand colors (muted)
- Mobile-first layout

**Product Presentation:**
- Multiple format options visible
- Price clear upfront
- "Add to Cart" prominent
- Related products shown

**Social Proof:**
- Review count visible
- Star ratings
- Instagram photos from users
- "1M+ community" claim

**What to Copy:**
- Consistent visual brand
- Clear pricing (no hiding)

**What to Avoid:**
- No urgency mechanisms
- Limited product descriptions
- No video content

## Common Conversion Elements

### Headlines That Work

**Problem-Focused:**
- "Struggling to [problem]? Here's the solution..."
- "Stop wasting [time/money] on [pain point]"

**Outcome-Focused:**
- "Get [specific result] in [timeframe]"
- "The complete system for [outcome]"

**Curiosity:**
- "The [number] [things] that [transformed/changed]..."
- "What [expert type] don't tell you about [topic]"

### Social Proof Types

**Most Effective:**
1. Specific numbers ("10,000+ customers")
2. Named testimonials with photos
3. Before/after results
4. Brand logos (if B2B)
5. Media mentions

**Less Effective:**
1. Anonymous testimonials
2. Vague praise
3. Self-reported stats
4. Celebrity without relevance

### Pricing Display

**High Conversion:**
- Price visible (not hidden)
- Comparison to alternatives
- Payment plans available
- Value stacking
- Crossed-out original price

**Low Conversion:**
- Price hidden behind click
- No context for value
- Only one option
- No payment flexibility

### Guarantee Formats

**Strong:**
- "100% money-back, no questions"
- Specific timeframe (30, 60, 90 days)
- Named guarantee ("The [Brand] Promise")
- Risk explicitly addressed

**Weak:**
- Generic refund policy
- Hidden in T&C
- Conditions attached
- Short window

## Sales Page Frameworks

### Framework 1: PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)

1. **Problem:** Identify pain
2. **Agitate:** Make it feel worse
3. **Solve:** Present product as answer

Best for: Pain-driven purchases

### Framework 2: AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

1. **Attention:** Hook with headline
2. **Interest:** Build with benefits
3. **Desire:** Create with proof
4. **Action:** Direct with CTA

Best for: General products

### Framework 3: Before-After-Bridge

1. **Before:** Current painful state
2. **After:** Desired future state
3. **Bridge:** Product gets them there

Best for: Transformation products

### Framework 4: Feature-Advantage-Benefit

1. **Feature:** What it is
2. **Advantage:** What it does
3. **Benefit:** What that means for them

Best for: Technical products
