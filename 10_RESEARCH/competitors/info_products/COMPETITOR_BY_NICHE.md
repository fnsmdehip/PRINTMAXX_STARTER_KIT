## Cross-Niche Observations

### What's Working in 2024-2025

1. **Bundles over singles** - 3-5 products together
2. **Lifetime access** - One-time fee, forever access
3. **Community add-on** - Product + Discord/Circle
4. **AI-enhanced products** - Templates with AI components
5. **Video walkthroughs** - Not just PDF, show how to use

### Pricing Trends

1. **Race to bottom for basics** - Simple templates $5-15
2. **Premium for systems** - Complete systems $97-297
3. **High-ticket for transformation** - Courses $500-2000
4. **Membership middle ground** - $19-49/month

### Distribution Trends

1. **Platform diversification** - Not just one channel
2. **Email list is king** - Direct access to customers
3. **Social proof visible** - Numbers, testimonials everywhere
4. **Affiliate networks** - Creator cross-promotion
