## Feature Gap Summary

### Most Common (Table Stakes)
1. Streak tracking
2. Basic statistics
3. Reminders/notifications
4. Daily/weekly goals
5. Widget support

### Differentiating Features
1. Strict/locked mode blocking (Opal, Freedom)
2. Real-world impact (Forest trees)
3. RPG gamification (Habitica)
4. Financial stakes (Beeminder)
5. Friction approach (One Sec)
6. Science-based journeys (Fabulous)
7. ADHD-specific features (Focus Bear)

### Underserved Features
1. AI-powered recommendations
2. Couples/family accountability
3. Workplace integration
4. Cross-platform sync (most lack)
5. Mental health correlation
6. Voice assistant integration
7. Financial accountability (mainstream version)
8. Group challenges with stakes
9. Professional coaching integration
10. Hardware integration (dumb phone pairing)
