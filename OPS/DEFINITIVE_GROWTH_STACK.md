## Engagement Rules (Stay Clean)

1. **Human-like timing.** Random delays between actions (30s-3min). Never action faster than a human could.
2. **No mass follow/unfollow.** Gradual growth only. 10-20 follows/day per account during warmup.
3. **No fake engagement.** Real comments, real replies, real value. Quality > quantity.
4. **Rate limits per platform:**
   - Twitter: 50 tweets/day, 400 follows/day (new accounts: 10/day)
   - Instagram: 60 likes/hour, 20 follows/hour
   - TikTok: 50 likes/hour, 20 follows/hour
5. **Warmup period.** New accounts: 2 weeks of manual activity before any automation.
6. **Content quality.** Every post must pass the copy-style.md voice check. No slop.
