## Part 1: Platform Algorithm Changes

### X/Twitter - MAJOR SHIFT (ALPHA791)

**What Changed:**
- Deliberate algorithm shift to **favor smaller accounts** over established ones
- Premium accounts get **4x visibility boost** in-network + **2x out-of-network**
- Q1 2026 testing shows Premium accounts get **30-40% higher reply impressions**
- **Two-tier system**: Only Premium user engagement counts toward ad revenue

**Impact:**
- Small accounts now have algorithmic advantage (first time)
- Premium subscription ($8/mo) = gateway to monetization + reach
- Non-Premium engagement doesn't count for payouts
- X Money wallet (Visa-backed) rolling out 2026

**Strategy:**
- Premium subscription = non-negotiable for monetization
- Small accounts = take advantage of boost window
- Engagement from Premium users only = target Premium audience

**ROI:** HIGHEST
**Priority:** NOW
**Sources:** [Tweet Archivist](https://www.tweetarchivist.com/how-twitter-algorithm-works-2025)

### TikTok - Algorithm + Monetization Overhaul (ALPHA792, ALPHA793)

**What Changed:**
- Jan 2026 algorithm update: **watch time weighted 30% heavier**
- Content watched **30+ seconds** gets preference
- **60-90 second videos** favored over quick-cut
- **Engagement rate > views** (1K views 50 comments = 5% beats 10K views 100 comments = 1%)
- **Follower-first distribution**: new videos shown to existing followers first few days
- **Creator Fund DEAD** → Creator Rewards Program

**New Monetization Requirements:**
- 100K views + **300K watch-hours in 30 days** for full-rate payment (vs. old view-count-only)
- Educational content **+18% in recommendations**
- Niche communities **1K-50K members** getting algorithm boost
- **Precision > popularity**

**Impact:**
- Longer-form content (60-90s) required vs. quick cuts
- Watch time = king metric (30+ second retention critical)
- Existing follower base = critical for initial push
- Creator Fund gone = higher bar for monetization

**Strategy:**
- 60-90s content with 30+ second retention
- Build niche communities (1K-50K sweet spot)
- Educational content rising
- Engagement rate focus over raw views

**ROI:** HIGHEST (algorithm), HIGH (monetization)
**Priority:** NOW
**Sources:** [TikTok Creator Hub 2026](https://medium.com/@marketolog4limes/tiktok-creator-hub-2026-new-monetization-opportunities-for-micro-creators-bb90d9635573), [OpusClip Blog](https://www.opus.pro/blog/tiktoks-new-algorithm-2026)

### Instagram Reels - Transparency + Interest-Based (ALPHA794)

**What Changed:**
- **'Your Algorithm'** launched Dec 10 2025 (US only, expanding 2026)
- Users can **view + customize** topics Instagram thinks they're interested in
- Major shift: **follower-based 'social graph' → interest-based** recommendation for Reels
- **53% of all IG ads** ran on Reels in Q4 2025 (up from 35% in 2024)
- Zuckerberg: Reels hit **$50B annual run rate** but "monetization efficiency much less than Feed"

**Impact:**
- Algorithm transparency = users can game it
- Interest-based > follower-based = reach beyond followers easier
- 53% of ads on Reels BUT lower profit margin than Feed
- Volume strategy needed (Reels less profitable per view)

**Strategy:**
- Optimize for interest categories users select
- Volume approach (lower RPM requires more views)
- Reels = discovery, Feed = conversion

**ROI:** HIGH
**Priority:** MEDIUM

### YouTube Shorts - Low RPM Reality (ALPHA795)

**What Changed:**
- Standard tier: 1K subs + 4K watch-hours (or 10M Shorts views/90d)
- Basic tier: 500 subs + 3K hours (or 3M Shorts) for Memberships/Super Chat (**NO ad revenue**)
- Shorts RPM: **$0.01-$0.07/1K views** ($0.03 avg = 3.3M views for $100)
- **50-60s Shorts** perform best (76% watch-through rate)
- Pooled revenue model (not individual ad placements)

**Impact:**
- Shorts pay **$50-500 per million views** (very low)
- 50-60s optimal length
- Use Shorts for discovery → funnel to long-form for real revenue
- Basic tier unlocks memberships but NOT ads

**Strategy:**
- Shorts = audience growth tool, not primary revenue
- 50-60s length for completion rate
- Funnel to long-form for monetization

**ROI:** MEDIUM
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Sources:** [Loop Ex Digital](https://www.loopexdigital.com/blog/youtube-shorts-statistics)

### LinkedIn - Organic Reach CRASHED (ALPHA797)

**Action Required:**
