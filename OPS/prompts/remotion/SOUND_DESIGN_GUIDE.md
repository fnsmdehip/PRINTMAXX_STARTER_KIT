## Sound Design Best Practices

### When to Use Music vs SFX

| Scenario | Music | SFX | Both |
|----------|-------|-----|------|
| App demo screen recording | Background only | UI clicks, transitions | - |
| Transformation reveal | Build + drop | Whoosh on reveal | Yes |
| Feature showcase | Subtle background | Highlight key moments | Yes |
| Testimonial | Low ambient | None | - |
| Hook (first 3 sec) | Strong beat | Impact sound | Yes |
| CTA/Outro | Fade out | Click on button | Yes |

### Transition Sound Timing

**The 0.5 Second Rule:**
- Place transition SFX 0.5 seconds BEFORE the visual cut
- Human brain processes audio faster than video
- Creates anticipation and cleaner feel

**Beat Sync Guidelines:**
```
For 120 BPM music:
- 1 beat = 0.5 seconds
- Cut on every 2nd or 4th beat for clean transitions
- Visual hits should land ON the beat, not after

For 140 BPM phonk:
- 1 beat = 0.43 seconds
- Faster cuts feel natural
- Match phone screen changes to beat drops
```

### Volume Mixing Guidelines

| Element | Volume Level | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| Background music | -12 to -15 dB | Never compete with voiceover |
| Music during hook | -6 to -9 dB | Can be louder, no voice |
| Voiceover | 0 dB (reference) | Always clearest element |
| SFX transitions | -6 to -9 dB | Punchy but not jarring |
| SFX UI clicks | -12 to -18 dB | Subtle enhancement |
| Music outro | Fade to -20 dB | Clean exit |

**The Ducking Rule:**
When voiceover starts, music should duck (reduce) by 6-9 dB automatically. Most video editors have this built in.

### Scene-Based Music Switching

**15-30 Second Video Structure:**
```
0-3s:   HOOK - Trending sound at full energy
3-15s:  PROBLEM/DEMO - Same track, possibly lower
15-25s: SOLUTION - Build or maintain energy
25-30s: CTA - Music fades, clean ending
```

**30-60 Second Video Structure:**
```
0-5s:   HOOK - Attention-grabbing sound
5-15s:  PROBLEM - Tension music (darker)
15-35s: SOLUTION - Uplifting build
35-50s: PROOF - Maintain energy
50-60s: CTA - Clean fade
```

**Music Change Points:**
- Only change music at natural scene breaks
- Use transition SFX to bridge changes
- Maximum 2 music changes per 60-second video
- Consider using same song, different energy sections

## TikTok Algorithm Tips

### How Sounds Affect Reach

**The Algorithm's Sound Prioritization:**
1. Videos with background music get 98% more views on average
2. Trending sounds get pushed to FYP more aggressively
3. Audio clips work like hashtags - popular audio = more visibility
4. Early adoption of rising sounds = highest boost potential

### Rising vs Peaked Sounds

**How to Identify Sound Stage:**

| Stage | Characteristics | Action |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| **Rising** | <100K videos, increasing daily | USE NOW - highest potential |
| **Trending** | 100K-1M videos, still growing | Good opportunity, more competition |
| **Peaked** | 1M+ videos, growth slowing | Still useful, lower boost |
| **Declining** | Usage dropping day over day | Avoid unless perfect fit |

**Where to Check:**
- TikTok Creative Center (official)
- Tokchart (tracks trajectory over time)
- Spotify Viral charts

### Best Practices for Sound Selection

**DO:**
- Jump on sounds within first 2-3 days of rise
- Adapt trending sounds to your niche
- Use sound as container for unique content
- Check sound's current meaning/context
- Test with and without trending sounds

**DON'T:**
- Copy trend exactly (adaption > imitation)
- Use sounds that don't match brand values
- Rely solely on sound for reach
- Use dated/peaked sounds expecting viral
- Ignore that sounds can shift meaning quickly

### Sound Selection Decision Tree

```
Is there a trending sound that fits your content?
├── YES → Is it still rising/trending (not peaked)?
│   ├── YES → Use it! Adapt to your niche
│   └── NO → Consider anyway if perfect fit
└── NO → Find royalty-free that matches vibe
    └── Focus on content quality over sound virality
```

### Timing Your Posts with Trending Sounds

**The 48-Hour Window:**
When a sound starts trending, you have roughly 48-72 hours of maximum algorithm boost. After that, competition increases and boost decreases.

**Research Cadence:**
- Check TikTok Creative Center daily
- Look for sounds with 10K-50K uses growing fast
- Create content same day you identify opportunity
- Have pre-made templates ready for quick turnaround
