# Influencer Outreach Templates

Proven templates for collaborations, sponsorships, ambassador programs, and affiliate partnerships.

---

## 1. Initial Outreach DMs

### Template A: Fan-First Approach (Highest Response Rate)

**Best for:** Micro-influencers (1k-50k followers)

```
Hey [Name]!

Been following your content on [topic] for a while. Your post about [specific post] was exactly what I needed to hear.

I'm building [product/service] and think you'd genuinely find it useful for [specific use case].

Would love to send you free access. No strings attached. If you like it, cool. If not, no worries.

Interested?
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows genuine familiarity with their content
- No immediate ask
- Removes pressure with "no strings attached"
- Short and easy to respond to

### Template B: Collab Opportunity

**Best for:** Creators in similar niche (10k-100k followers)

```
Hey [Name]!

Your [content type] on [topic] is really resonating. I especially liked [specific detail].

I run [account/brand] and we're doing a [specific campaign/collab idea]. Think your audience would love it.

Quick idea: [1-2 sentence pitch of what you're proposing]

Open to chatting about it?
```

### Template C: Direct Business Pitch

**Best for:** Larger creators with clear sponsorship rates

```
Hi [Name],

[Brand] here. We're launching [product] and looking for authentic voices in the [niche] space.

Loved your [specific content]. Your approach to [specific thing they do] aligns with our brand.

Would you be open to a paid collaboration? Happy to share campaign details and budget.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## 5. Response Templates

### When They Say Yes

```
Amazing! So excited to work together.

Here's what happens next:
1. I'll send over the [brief/agreement/product]
2. You review and let me know if anything needs adjusting
3. We align on timeline
4. You create, we amplify, everyone wins

Any questions before we kick things off?
```

### When They Ask for Rates

```
Thanks for asking! Here's our standard structure:

[Platform] content:
- [Deliverable type]: $[X] - $[Y]
- [Deliverable type]: $[X] - $[Y]

This includes:
- [What's included]
- [Usage rights]
- [Timeline]

We can also discuss:
- Long-term partnerships (better rates)
- Performance bonuses
- Product-only deals

What fits your goals best?
```

### When They Ghost

**Follow-up 1 (3-5 days later):**
```
Hey [Name]! Just bumping this up. Still interested in chatting about [project]?
```

**Follow-up 2 (7-10 days later):**
```
Hi [Name], totally understand if you're swamped. Just wanted to check in one more time about [project]. If the timing isn't right, no worries. Let me know either way?
```

**Final follow-up (14+ days):**
```
Last check-in on this! If you're not interested, totally fine. Would love to know so I can close the loop on my end. Thanks either way!
```

### When They Say No

```
Totally understand. Thanks for letting me know!

If anything changes or you want to explore something different in the future, my inbox is always open.

Keep creating great stuff.

[Your name]
```

## 6. Negotiation Templates

### When Their Rate Is Too High

```
Thanks for sharing your rates!

That's a bit above our budget for this campaign. Here's what we can do:

Option A: Reduced scope
- [Fewer deliverables] for $[X]

Option B: Performance bonus
- Base: $[X]
- Bonus: +$[Y] if [metric] hits [target]

Option C: Long-term deal
- [X] posts over [Y] months at $[Z] total

Any of these work for you?
```

### When You Want Exclusivity

```
Quick question about exclusivity.

For this campaign, we'd need you to avoid promoting [competitor category] for [X days/weeks] around the launch.

In exchange, we can:
- Increase the base rate by [X]%
- Add a bonus payment of $[Y]
- Extend partnership for additional content

Does that work?
```

## 7. Contract Essentials

### Key Terms to Include

**Deliverables:**
- Exact number and type of content pieces
- Platforms where content will be posted
- Content format specifications (length, format)
- Approval process and revision rounds

**Timeline:**
- Draft due date
- Review period
- Publish date(s)
- Content live duration (if removing allowed)

**Compensation:**
- Total payment amount
- Payment schedule (upfront, on publish, net 30)
- Payment method
- Performance bonuses (if any)

**Usage Rights:**
- Organic posting only vs paid amplification
- Duration of usage rights
- Exclusivity period
- Whitelisting/dark posting rights

**Disclosure:**
- FTC compliance requirement
- Specific disclosure language
- Hashtag requirements (#ad, #sponsored, #partner)

## 9. Finding Influencers

### Manual Discovery

**Platform search:**
- Hashtag research in your niche
- "For you" page content in your category
- Competitor tagged posts
- Comments on viral posts in niche

**What to look for:**
- Engagement rate >3% (likes + comments / followers)
- Authentic comment section (real conversations)
- Content quality matches your brand
- Posting consistency (3+ posts/week)
- Audience demographics alignment

### Tools

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| Modash | Discovery + analytics | $99+/mo |
| Upfluence | Enterprise campaigns | Custom |
| Heepsy | Budget-friendly search | $49+/mo |
| Collabstr | Marketplace model | Free to browse |
| AspireIQ | Full campaign management | Custom |

### Free Methods

1. **Instagram search:**
   - Search niche hashtags
   - Check "Similar accounts" on competitor profiles
   - Browse tagged posts on competitor accounts

2. **TikTok Creator Marketplace:**
   - Free access for businesses
   - Filter by category, followers, engagement
   - Direct outreach through platform

3. **YouTube:**
   - Search niche keywords
   - Filter by upload date and view count
   - Check channels with 10k-100k subscribers

```

[Awareness / Traffic / Sales / App installs]

- Bonus: [If applicable]

TRACKING:
- Use link: [Tracking URL]
- Use code: [Discount code]

CONTACT:
[Name] - [Email] - [Phone]
```

## Quick Reference: Response Rates by Approach

| Approach | Avg Response Rate | Best For |
|----------|-------------------|----------|
| Fan-first DM | 25-35% | Micro-influencers |
| Direct business DM | 10-15% | Mid-tier creators |
| Cold email | 5-10% | All sizes |
| Warm intro | 40-50% | All sizes |
| Platform marketplace | 15-25% | TikTok, YouTube |

**Tips to improve rates:**
- Personalize first line (reference specific content)
- Keep initial message under 100 words
- Follow up 2-3 times minimum
- Send during their active hours
- Offer clear value upfront
