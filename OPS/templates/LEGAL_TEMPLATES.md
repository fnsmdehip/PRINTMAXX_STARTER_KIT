### 3.2 FTC Compliance Checklist

**Use this checklist for ALL promotional content:**

```markdown
# FTC Compliance Checklist

## Before Publishing Content with Material Connections

### Disclosure Requirements

- [ ] Is there a material connection? (payment, free products, affiliate relationship)
- [ ] Is the disclosure clear and conspicuous?
- [ ] Is the disclosure near the relevant claim/link (not buried at bottom)?
- [ ] Would average consumer understand the disclosure?
- [ ] Is disclosure in same format as content (video disclosure for video, etc.)?

### Disclosure Language

- [ ] Use clear language: "ad," "sponsored," "paid partnership"
- [ ] Avoid vague terms: "sp," "spon," "partner" alone
- [ ] State relationship specifically when relevant
- [ ] Disclose BEFORE clicks happen (not just on landing page)

### Platform-Specific Requirements

**Instagram:**
- [ ] Use "Paid Partnership" tag when available
- [ ] Disclose in first 3 lines of caption (before "more")
- [ ] Disclose in Stories (not hidden in small text)

**YouTube:**
- [ ] Use "Includes Paid Promotion" checkbox
- [ ] Verbal disclosure in video
- [ ] Written disclosure in video description

**TikTok:**
- [ ] Use "Paid Partnership" label
- [ ] Include disclosure in video/caption

**Blogs/Websites:**
- [ ] Disclosure at TOP of post
- [ ] Disclose before affiliate links
- [ ] Site-wide disclosure page

### Testimonials and Reviews

- [ ] Only claim benefits you actually experienced
- [ ] If results atypical, disclose typical results
- [ ] Don't make claims you can't substantiate
- [ ] Disclose if you received product free
- [ ] Disclose any ongoing relationship with brand

### Red Flags to Avoid

- [ ] NOT using disclosure when required
- [ ] Burying disclosure where consumers won't see it
- [ ] Using unclear abbreviations
- [ ] Making false or unsubstantiated claims
- [ ] Fake testimonials or reviews
- [ ] Misleading "before and after" images
```

**Use for:** Hiring freelancers, contractors, developers, designers

1. **Parties and Engagement** - Client/contractor info, services, dates
2. **Scope of Work** - Deliverables, specs, milestones, revision limits
3. **Compensation** - Payment amount, schedule, method, expenses
4. **Intellectual Property** - Work made for hire, IP assignment
5. **Confidentiality** - NDA terms, duration, return of materials
6. **Independent Contractor Status** - Not employee, own taxes/equipment
7. **Warranties** - Original work, no infringement, authority
8. **Termination** - For convenience, for cause, payment upon termination
9. **Liability and Indemnification** - Limits, insurance requirements
10. **General Provisions** - Governing law, disputes, amendments

---

### 4.2 NDA Key Sections

**Use for:** Protecting confidential information with partners, contractors

1. **Parties** - Disclosing party, receiving party
2. **Definition of Confidential Information** - What IS and is NOT covered
3. **Obligations** - Maintain confidentiality, use only for permitted purpose
4. **Permitted Purpose** - Why information is being shared
5. **Term** - Duration (typical: 2-5 years, trade secrets: indefinite)
6. **Return of Information** - Obligation to return or destroy
7. **No License** - No IP rights transferred
8. **Remedies** - Injunctive relief, damages
9. **General Provisions** - Governing law, jurisdiction, severability

- Verbal disclosure in video if possible

**Gifted Products**
- "[Brand] sent me this product" or "#gifted"

---

### 5.2 YouTube Disclosure Requirements

**Required:**
- Check "Video contains paid promotion" in YouTube Studio
- Verbal disclosure within first 30 seconds
- Written disclosure in video description

**What to Say:**
- Sponsored: "This video is sponsored by [Brand]."
- Gifted: "[Brand] sent me this [product] to try."
- Affiliate: "Links in the description are affiliate links."

---

### 5.3 TikTok Disclosure Requirements

**Required:**
- Enable "Branded content" toggle when posting
- Select "Promotional content" or "Paid partnership"
- Add visible disclosure in video: "Ad" or "#ad"
- Caption: #ad or "Sponsored by [Brand]"

**Affiliate Links:**
- Add "#ad" or "Affiliate link" to caption
- Mention in video: "Link in bio is an affiliate link"

---

### 5.4 Email Marketing Compliance (CAN-SPAM)

**Requirements:**
1. No false headers (accurate "From" name and email)
2. Subject line must accurately reflect content
3. Physical address required in every email
4. Working unsubscribe link (process within 10 business days)
5. Identify as advertisement if promotional

**GDPR Additional:**
- Explicit opt-in required (no pre-checked boxes)
- Document when and how consent was given
- Easy to withdraw consent

**Penalties:** Up to $50,120 per violating email
