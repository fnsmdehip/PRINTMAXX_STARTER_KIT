# Ad Script Templates

Templates for Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram ads. 15-60 seconds.

---

## Template 1: Problem-Solution (30 sec)

**Best for:** App downloads, SaaS, products

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] PROBLEM - Pain point they feel
[5-15s] AGITATE - Make it worse
[15-25s] SOLUTION - Your product
[25-28s] PROOF - Quick evidence
[28-30s] CTA - Clear action
```

**Example (Focus App):**

```
[Problem]
"Can't focus because you keep checking your phone?"

[Agitate]
"Every notification pulls you away.
You waste hours scrolling.
Your work suffers. Your goals slip."

[Solution]
"PrayerLock fixes this.
Set a timer. Lock your phone.
Can't open anything until time's up."

[Proof]
"50,000 people use it to focus 2+ hours daily."

[CTA]
"Download free. Link below."
```

## Template 2: Before/After (30 sec)

**Best for:** Transformations, fitness, productivity

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] HOOK - Relatable before state
[5-15s] BEFORE - What life was like
[15-25s] AFTER - What changed
[25-28s] HOW - The product/solution
[28-30s] CTA
```

**Example (Walking App):**

```
[Hook]
"I used to sit at my desk for 8 hours straight."

[Before]
"Gained 20 pounds. Back pain. Zero energy.
Knew I needed to walk more.
But I never did."

[After]
"Now I walk 8,000 steps daily.
Lost the weight. Feel amazing.
And it only took one app."

[How]
"WalkToUnlock locks my phone until I hit my step goal.
Simple as that."

[CTA]
"Download free. Start walking."
```

## Template 3: Social Proof (30 sec)

**Best for:** Apps, SaaS, courses

**Structure:**
```
[0-3s] HOOK - Big number or claim
[3-15s] TESTIMONIALS - 2-3 quick quotes
[15-22s] PRODUCT DEMO - What it does
[22-28s] OFFER - Why act now
[28-30s] CTA
```

**Example (Study App):**

```
[Hook]
"100,000 students use this to study smarter."

"My GPA went from 2.8 to 3.7" - Emma, UCLA
"I actually finish my homework now" - Jake, High School
"Game changer for exam prep" - Sarah, Law School

[Demo]
"StudyLock locks your phone during study sessions.
No TikTok. No Instagram. Just focus."

[Offer]
"Free to download. Premium is 50% off this week."

[CTA]
"Link in bio. Start studying."
```

## Template 4: UGC Style (45 sec)

**Best for:** Direct response, authenticity

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] SELFIE HOOK - Personal, direct
[5-20s] STORY - Personal experience
[20-35s] PRODUCT - What it is, how to use
[35-42s] RESULTS - What happened
[42-45s] CTA
```

**Example (POD Course):**

```
[Selfie Hook]
"Okay so I have to tell you about this thing I found..."

[Story]
"I was looking for a side hustle that doesn't require
me to buy inventory or deal with shipping.
Tried dropshipping - disaster.
Then I found print on demand."

[Product]
"Basically you design t-shirts, mugs, whatever.
When someone buys, the company prints and ships it.
You keep the profit. Never touch a thing."

[Results]
"Started 3 months ago. Made $2,400 last month.
Still work my full-time job.
This is just... extra money."

[CTA]
"I made a free guide. Link in my bio."
```

## Template 7: Objection Handler (45 sec)

**Best for:** Higher-ticket items, overcoming resistance

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] OBJECTION - What they're thinking
[5-15s] VALIDATE - Acknowledge it
[15-30s] REFRAME - New perspective
[30-40s] PROOF - Evidence it works
[40-45s] CTA
```

**Example (Course):**

```
[Objection]
"'I don't have time to start a side business.'"

[Validate]
"I get it. You're busy. Job, family, life.
Where does a side project fit?"

[Reframe]
"Here's the thing: print on demand takes 5 hours/week.
That's one hour on weeknights. That's it.
Once you set up designs, they sell while you sleep."

[Proof]
"I make $3k/month on 5 hours/week.
That's $150/hour. Worth it?"

[CTA]
"Free training. Link below."
```

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

- Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5) for feed
- First 3 seconds MUST hook
- Captions essential (sound off)
- Clear CTA button
- Avoid text >20% of image

### TikTok Ads

- Vertical (9:16) only
- UGC style outperforms polished
- Hook in 0.5 seconds
- Native look and feel
- Trending sounds help
- Spark Ads use real posts

### YouTube Ads

- Skippable after 5 seconds (pre-roll)
- Non-skip: 15-20 seconds
- Brand/CTA before 5-second mark
- Longer formats (30-60s) work for discovery
- Remarketing: can be longer

## Hook Library for Ads

### Question Hooks
- "Tired of [problem]?"
- "What if you could [benefit]?"
- "Still struggling with [thing]?"
- "Want to [desirable outcome]?"

### Statement Hooks
- "This app changed everything."
- "I found the secret to [outcome]."
- "Stop doing [common mistake]."
- "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]."

### Social Proof Hooks
- "10,000 people already use this."
- "This is how I made $[X]."
- "My [metric] went from [A] to [B]."
- "Join 50,000+ who [benefit]."

### Curiosity Hooks
- "I can't believe this is free."
- "This shouldn't work, but it does."
- "The weird trick that [outcome]."
- "Nobody's talking about this."

### Direct Hooks
- "If you [target audience], watch this."
- "For [specific person] only."
- "This is for you if [situation]."
- "3 seconds. That's all I need."

## CTA Options

### Soft CTAs
- "Check it out. Link below."
- "Worth a try. It's free."
- "See for yourself."

### Direct CTAs
- "Download now."
- "Get started today."
- "Sign up free."
- "Click the link."

### Urgency CTAs
- "Limited spots. Link below."
- "50% off ends tonight."
- "Don't miss this."

### Benefit CTAs
- "Start saving time today."
- "Get your free [thing]."
- "Join 10,000 [people]."

## Quick Checklist

### Before Recording
- [ ] Hook written and strong
- [ ] Script timed (speak it out loud)
- [ ] CTA is clear and single
- [ ] Matches platform specs

### During Recording
- [ ] Good lighting (face visible)
- [ ] Clear audio (no background noise)
- [ ] Energy matches brand
- [ ] Product/demo visible

### Post-Production
- [ ] Captions added
- [ ] Cut dead air
- [ ] First 3 seconds engaging
- [ ] Thumbnail set
