# Short Form Video Script Templates

Templates for TikTok, Reels, Shorts. 15-60 seconds.

---

## Template 1: Hook + Value + CTA (30 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-3s] HOOK - Stop scroll, create curiosity
[3-20s] VALUE - Main content, 3-5 points
[20-28s] PROOF - Example or result
[28-30s] CTA - What to do next
```

**Example (POD Business):**
```
HOOK: "I make $3k/month from t-shirts I never touch."

VALUE:
"Here's the 3-step system:
1. Find a niche with passion but no good merch
2. Design 10 simple products using Canva
3. List on Printful + Etsy, let them handle everything"

PROOF: "Last month: 127 orders, $3,247 revenue, 4 hours of work."

CTA: "Follow for more POD tips."
```

## Template 2: Problem + Solution (45 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] PROBLEM - State the pain
[5-15s] AGITATE - Make it worse
[15-35s] SOLUTION - Your method
[35-43s] RESULT - What happens
[43-45s] CTA - Follow/link
```

**Example (Focus App):**
```
PROBLEM: "You sit down to work and immediately check your phone."

AGITATE: "30 minutes later you've watched 15 TikToks and done nothing.
Every day. For months. Your goals stay dreams."

SOLUTION: "PrayerLock solves this. Set a timer. Phone locks.
You can't open anything until time's up.
No willpower needed. Just physics."

RESULT: "My users average 2 extra focused hours daily."

CTA: "Link in bio. Stop the scroll."
```

## Template 3: Tutorial/How-To (60 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-3s] HOOK - What they'll learn
[3-10s] CONTEXT - Why this matters
[10-50s] STEPS - 3-5 clear steps
[50-58s] RESULT - What they get
[58-60s] CTA - Next step
```

**Example (Content Creation):**
```
HOOK: "How to get 100k views on your first TikTok."

CONTEXT: "Most people post randomly and wonder why nobody watches."

STEPS:
"Step 1: Study your niche. Watch 50 top videos, note the hooks.
Step 2: Copy the format, not the content. Same structure, your topic.
Step 3: First 3 seconds. That's it. Spend 80% of your time on the hook.
Step 4: Post when your audience is awake. 7pm-10pm weekdays.
Step 5: Post 3x per day for a week. Algorithm needs data."

RESULT: "My third video hit 200k using exactly this."

CTA: "Save this. Follow for more."
```

## Template 4: Myth Buster (30 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-3s] MYTH - Common belief
[3-8s] CONTRADICTION - Why it's wrong
[8-25s] TRUTH - What actually works
[25-28s] PROOF - Your evidence
[28-30s] CTA
```

**Example (Solopreneur):**
```
MYTH: "You need to work 80 hours a week to build a successful business."

CONTRADICTION: "That's just hustle culture nonsense."

TRUTH: "The most successful solopreneurs I know work 4-6 hours daily.
They're ruthless about what matters.
Build one thing. Master one channel. Ignore everything else."

PROOF: "I built a $10k/month app working 20 hours per week."

CTA: "Disagree? Tell me in comments."
```

## Template 5: List/Ranking (45 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-3s] HOOK - What list, why care
[3-40s] LIST - 3-5 items with brief explanation
[40-43s] SUMMARY - Key takeaway
[43-45s] CTA
```

**Example (Tools):**
```
HOOK: "5 free tools that replaced my $200/month stack."

LIST:
"1. Notion - $0. Replaced Asana, Evernote, and Coda. Does everything.

2. Canva Free - $0. 90% of Pro features. Make anything.

3. Mailchimp Free - $0. Up to 500 subscribers. Perfect for starting.

4. Zapier Free - $0. 100 tasks/month. Automate the basics.

5. Buffer Free - $0. 3 social accounts. Schedule a week ahead."

SUMMARY: "Total savings: $2,400/year. Same results."

CTA: "Which do you use? Comment below."
```

## Template 6: Story (60 sec)

**Structure:**
```
[0-5s] HOOK - Intriguing start
[5-25s] SETUP - Context and challenge
[25-45s] TURNING POINT - What changed
[45-55s] LESSON - Key takeaway
[55-60s] CTA
```

**Example (Personal):**
```
HOOK: "I got fired on a Friday. Best thing that ever happened."

SETUP: "I was making $85k as a product manager. Good salary, soul-crushing work.
Every Sunday night, dread. Every Monday morning, forcing myself in.
I kept saying 'next year I'll start something.'"

TURNING POINT: "Then they laid off my whole team. No warning.
That night, instead of panicking, I felt free.
I gave myself 6 months to build something of my own."

LESSON: "4 months later, I replaced my salary.
Sometimes you need the push.
Don't wait for it. Jump."

CTA: "What would you build if you got laid off tomorrow?"
```

## Hook Library (50 Hooks)

1. "This is what nobody tells you about [topic]"
2. "I found something weird about [topic]"
3. "Stop doing [common thing]. Here's why."
4. "The real reason [thing] doesn't work"
5. "I tested [thing] for 30 days. Results shocked me."

6. "I make $X/month doing [thing]"
7. "How I went from [bad state] to [good state]"
8. "$X in [timeframe] with this strategy"
9. "This one change doubled my [metric]"
10. "Zero to [result] in [timeframe]"

### Problem Hooks

12. "Why you're not getting [result]"
13. "The mistake killing your [area]"

### Question Hooks

## CTA Templates

**Follow CTAs:**
- "Follow for more [niche] tips"
- "Follow if you want to [benefit]"
- "More like this on my page"

**Engagement CTAs:**
- "Save this for later"
- "Share with someone who needs this"
- "Drop a [emoji] if you agree"
- "Comment [X] for the full guide"
- "Which one resonates? Comment below"

**Link CTAs:**
- "Full tutorial in bio"
- "Link in bio for the [free thing]"
- "Grab the [thing] - link in bio"

**Soft CTAs:**
- "If this helped, let me know"
- "What do you think?"
- "Agree or disagree?"

## Quick Reference

### Timing

| Duration | Hook | Value | CTA |
|----------|------|-------|-----|
| 15 sec | 2 sec | 11 sec | 2 sec |
| 30 sec | 3 sec | 23 sec | 4 sec |
| 45 sec | 4 sec | 36 sec | 5 sec |
| 60 sec | 5 sec | 48 sec | 7 sec |

### Speaking Pace

- Short form: 150-180 words/minute
- Slower = more authority
- Faster = more energy
- Match to content type

### On-Screen Text

- Reinforce key points
- Not word-for-word script
- Large, readable font
- 2-5 words per card
