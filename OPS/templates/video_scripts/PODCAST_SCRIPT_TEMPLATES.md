# Podcast Script Templates

Templates for solo episodes, interviews, and clips. Various lengths.

---

## Template 1: Solo Episode (20-30 min)

**Structure:**
```
[0:00-2:00] INTRO - Hook and context
[2:00-5:00] SETUP - Why this matters
[5:00-20:00] MAIN CONTENT - 3-5 key points
[20:00-25:00] SUMMARY - Recap and takeaways
[25:00-30:00] CTA - Next steps and close
```

**Script Framework:**

```
INTRO:
"Welcome back to [Podcast Name]. I'm [Name].

Today we're talking about [topic].
If you've ever [relatable situation], this one's for you.
By the end of this episode, you'll know exactly how to [outcome].

Let's get into it."

SETUP:
"Here's why I wanted to cover this.
[Personal story or context]
[Why it matters now]
[What most people get wrong]

So let me break down what actually works."

MAIN CONTENT:
"Point number one: [First key insight]

[Detailed explanation]
[Example or story]
[How to apply it]

Point number two: [Second key insight]

[Detailed explanation]
[Example or story]
[How to apply it]

[Continue for each point]"

SUMMARY:
"Let me recap what we covered:
1. [Point one summary]
2. [Point two summary]
3. [Point three summary]

The biggest takeaway: [Core message]"

CTA:
"If you found this valuable, here's what to do:
- Subscribe so you don't miss the next one
- Leave a review if you have 30 seconds
- Share this with someone who needs to hear it

Next week, we're covering [teaser].
See you then."
```

## Template 2: Interview Episode (45-60 min)

**Structure:**
```
[0:00-3:00] HOST INTRO - Set the stage
[3:00-8:00] GUEST INTRO - Background story
[8:00-45:00] MAIN CONVERSATION - Key topics
[45:00-55:00] RAPID FIRE - Quick questions
[55:00-60:00] CLOSE - Guest promo and wrap
```

**Script Framework:**

```
HOST INTRO:
"Welcome to [Podcast Name]. I'm [Host Name].

Today I'm talking with [Guest Name], who [impressive credential].
[Why they're worth listening to]
[What we're going to cover]

[Guest], welcome to the show."

GUEST INTRO:
"Before we dive in, give us the quick version.
How did you get to where you are today?"

[Let guest tell their story - 3-5 minutes]

"That's incredible. Let's unpack some of that."

MAIN CONVERSATION:
"I want to start with [first topic].
[First question - open-ended]"

[Guest answers]

"That's interesting. [Follow-up or transition]
[Next question]"

[Continue conversation naturally]

TOPIC TRANSITIONS:
- "Let's shift gears..."
- "I want to dig into [new topic]..."
- "You mentioned [thing]. Can you expand on that?"
- "Let's get tactical. How would someone actually..."

RAPID FIRE:
"Alright, we're going to do some quick hits.
First answer that comes to mind.

- Best book you've read recently?
- One tool you can't live without?
- Advice to your 20-year-old self?
- What's next for you?"

CLOSE:
"This was incredible. Where can people find you?
What are you working on that you want people to check out?"

[Guest shares links/promo]

"We'll put all those links in the show notes.
[Guest Name], thanks for being here."

"That's it for today. Subscribe, review, share.
See you next time."
```

## Template 3: Podcast Clip (1-3 min)

**Structure:**
```
[0:00-0:10] HOOK - Attention grabber
[0:10-2:30] CONTENT - The valuable insight
[2:30-2:50] CONTEXT - Where to find more
[2:50-3:00] CTA - Subscribe/follow
```

**Best Moments to Clip:**

| Clip Type | What to Look For |
|-----------|------------------|
| Hot Take | Strong opinion, surprising stance |
| Story | Emotional moment, turning point |
| Tactic | Specific, actionable advice |
| Failure | Mistake or lesson learned |
| Number | Revenue, results, specific data |

**Clip Script Format:**

```
[Hook - pulled from mid-clip]
"The thing nobody tells you about [topic]..."

[Content - the full valuable segment]
[Let the content play naturally]

[Context]
"That was [Guest Name] on [Podcast Name].
Full episode wherever you listen."

[CTA]
"Subscribe for more."
```

## Template 4: News/Update Episode (10-15 min)

**Structure:**
```
[0:00-1:00] INTRO - What we're covering
[1:00-10:00] NEWS ITEMS - 3-5 stories
[10:00-13:00] ANALYSIS - What this means
[13:00-15:00] CLOSE - CTA and sign-off
```

**Script Framework:**

```
INTRO:
"Welcome back to [Podcast Name].
This is your [daily/weekly] update on [niche].

Today we're covering:
- [Story 1 headline]
- [Story 2 headline]
- [Story 3 headline]

Let's get into it."

NEWS ITEMS:
"First up: [Story 1]

[What happened]
[Why it matters]
[Your quick take]

Moving on: [Story 2]

[What happened]
[Why it matters]
[Your quick take]

[Continue for each story]"

ANALYSIS:
"So what does all this mean?

[Connect the dots between stories]
[Bigger picture implications]
[What to watch for]"

CLOSE:
"That's your update for [day/week].
Subscribe if you want these delivered regularly.
See you next time."
```

### The Question Open

"97% of [people] fail at [thing]. Here's why."

### The Controversy Open
"I'm going to say something that might upset people..."

"By the end of this episode, you'll know exactly how to [outcome]."

### The Recap Open
"Last episode, we covered [topic]. Today, we're going deeper."

### The News Open
"Big news in [industry] this week..."

## Interview Questions by Category

### Background
- "Walk us through your journey to where you are now."
- "What was the turning point that changed everything?"
- "What did you believe early on that turned out to be wrong?"

### Process
- "What does your typical day/week look like?"
- "What's your process for [key activity]?"
- "Walk us through how you [specific task]."

### Tactics
- "What's the one thing that's working best right now?"
- "If you were starting over today, what would you do first?"
- "What's something tactical that most people overlook?"

### Lessons
- "What's the biggest mistake you've made?"
- "What would you tell your younger self?"
- "What do you wish you knew earlier?"

### Opinions
- "What's a controversial opinion you hold?"
- "What do most people in [industry] get wrong?"
- "What's overhyped? What's underrated?"

### Personal
- "What are you working on right now?"
- "What gets you excited to work every day?"
- "Outside of work, what are you into?"

## Transitions and Filler Phrases

### Topic Transitions
- "Let's shift gears..."
- "I want to pivot to..."
- "Speaking of which..."
- "That brings up something else..."
- "Let's zoom out for a second..."
- "Okay, let's get tactical..."

### Buying Time
- "That's a great question..."
- "Let me think about that..."
- "You know, I've actually never been asked that..."

### Clarifying
- "To put it another way..."
- "Let me give you an example..."
- "What I mean by that is..."

### Affirming (Use Sparingly)
- "Exactly."
- "That's so true."
- "I love that."
- "That's a great point."

## Recording Checklist

### Pre-Recording
- [ ] Outline prepared
- [ ] Guest research done (for interviews)
- [ ] Questions ready
- [ ] Recording software tested
- [ ] Quiet environment
- [ ] Water nearby

### During Recording
- [ ] Energy level up
- [ ] Speaking clearly
- [ ] Listening actively (interviews)
- [ ] Making notes for follow-ups
- [ ] Tracking time

### Post-Recording
- [ ] Backup raw file
- [ ] Note timestamps for clips
- [ ] Schedule editing
- [ ] Prepare show notes
- [ ] Create promotional content
