## 1. Cold Email Templates

### 1A. Agency Services Pitch (Results-Focused)

**Subject:** I got [SIMILAR_CLIENT] 47 leads last month

**Body:**
```
[FIRST_NAME],

Saw your [SPECIFIC_THING_ABOUT_THEIR_BUSINESS]. You're doing [WHAT_THEY_DO_WELL].

I help [NICHE] companies get more [OUTCOME] through [METHOD]. Last month I did this for [CLIENT_EXAMPLE]: [SPECIFIC_RESULT].

Here's what I'd do for [COMPANY_NAME]:
- [SPECIFIC_TACTIC_1]
- [SPECIFIC_TACTIC_2]

Takes 15 minutes to walk through. Worth a call?

[YOUR_NAME]
[CALENDAR_LINK]
```

**Follow-up timing:** 3 days, then 7 days, then 14 days

## 2. DM Templates

### 2A. Instagram Collaboration

**Opening DM:**
```
Hey [NAME], love your content on [SPECIFIC_TOPIC]. Your [SPECIFIC_POST] was [ONE_GENUINE_COMPLIMENT].
```

**Follow-up (if they respond positively):**
```
Appreciate that! Quick idea: I'm working on [PROJECT]. Thinking we could [SPECIFIC_COLLAB_IDEA].

Would take [TIME_ESTIMATE]. You'd get [THEIR_BENEFIT].

Want to try it?
```


### 2B. Twitter/X Networking

**Opening DM:**
```
[NAME], your thread on [TOPIC] was solid. [SPECIFIC_POINT] is something most people miss.

Quick Q: [GENUINE_QUESTION_RELATED_TO_THEIR_EXPERTISE]
```

**Follow-up (after conversation):**
```
Thanks for the insight. Building something in [YOUR_SPACE]. Would love to pick your brain for 15 min sometime. No pitch, just questions.
```


### 2C. LinkedIn Connection

**Connection request note (300 chars max):**
```
[NAME], saw your post on [TOPIC]. Building in the same space. Would like to connect.
```

**Follow-up message (after accepted):**
```
Thanks for connecting. Read your [SPECIFIC_CONTENT]. [ONE_SENTENCE_INSIGHT_OR_QUESTION].

Working on [YOUR_PROJECT]. Happy to share what's working on my end if helpful.
```


### 2D. TikTok Duet Request

**Comment first (on their video):**
```
This is exactly what [AUDIENCE] needs to hear. The part about [SPECIFIC_POINT] is underrated.
```

**DM (after they respond or like):**
```
Hey! Your video on [TOPIC] matches something I've been working on. Mind if I duet it with [YOUR_ANGLE]? I'll tag you and link back.
```

## Usage Notes

### Personalization Rules
1. Never send templates without customizing [BRACKETS]
2. Research for 5 minutes before sending
3. Reference something specific they created
4. One clear CTA per message

### Timing
- Best send times: Tue-Thu, 8-10am recipient's timezone
- Avoid: Monday morning, Friday afternoon
- DMs: Send when they're active (check recent post times)

### Tracking
- Log all outreach in LEDGER/OUTREACH_TRACKER.csv
- Track: sent_date, response_date, outcome, follow_ups_sent
- Review monthly: what templates get responses, what gets ignored

### A/B Testing
- Test one variable at a time (subject line, hook, CTA)
- Need 50+ sends per variant for signal
- Winner becomes new control, test next variable

---

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any outreach:

- [ ] Personalization is genuine (not generic)
- [ ] Under 100 words for cold emails
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] No AI vocabulary (leverage, utilize, delve, etc.)
- [ ] No "I hope this finds you well"
- [ ] Clear but not pushy CTA
- [ ] Subject line is specific to them
- [ ] Would you respond to this message?
- [ ] Spell-checked their name and company
- [ ] Checked their recent content for relevance
