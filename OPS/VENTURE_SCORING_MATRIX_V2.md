## SCORING METHODOLOGY

Each venture scored 1-10 on six dimensions. Higher = better.

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| market_size | Total addressable market. 1 = tiny niche, 10 = billion-dollar category |
| competition_inv | Inverted competition score. 10 = no competition, 1 = saturated |
| time_to_revenue_inv | Inverted time to first dollar. 10 = revenue this week, 1 = 6+ months |
| automation_potential | How much can run without you. 10 = fully automated, 1 = pure manual labor |
| defensibility | Moat strength. 10 = hard to replicate, 1 = anyone copies in a day |
| recurring_potential | Revenue repeatability. 10 = 100% MRR, 1 = pure one-shot |

**Composite Score** = weighted average: market_size(15%) + competition_inv(20%) + time_to_revenue_inv(20%) + automation_potential(15%) + defensibility(15%) + recurring_potential(15%)

Weights favor low competition and speed to revenue (capital-constrained solopreneur optimization).
