## PHASE 1: Account Setup (10 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Gumroad Account

1. Open browser. Go to **gumroad.com**
2. Click **Start selling** (top right)
3. Sign up with: **printmaxxer@protonmail.com**
4. Set password. Save in password manager.
5. Check email for verification link. Click it.
6. You're in the dashboard.

### Step 2: Set Up Profile

1. Click your **avatar** (top right) then **Settings**
2. Click **Profile** tab
3. **Display name:** PRINTMAXX
4. **Bio:** AI-powered playbooks for solopreneurs. specific tactics. real numbers. zero fluff.
5. **Profile URL:** gumroad.com/printmaxx (or printmaxxer if taken)
6. Upload a profile picture (use the PRINTMAXX logo or a simple dark icon)
7. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Connect Stripe for Payouts

1. In Settings, click **Payments** tab
2. Click **Connect with Stripe**
3. If you don't have Stripe yet:
   - New tab: go to **stripe.com**
   - Click **Start now**
   - Sign up with **printmaxxer@protonmail.com**
   - Enter real legal name, address, SSN (required for payouts)
   - Add bank account for deposits
   - Go back to Gumroad tab
   - Click **Connect with Stripe** again
4. Authorize the connection
5. You should see a green checkmark next to Stripe

### Step 4: Gumroad Settings

1. Still in Settings, click **General** tab
2. **Enable Gumroad Discover:** Toggle ON (this puts your products in their marketplace)
3. **Enable email marketing:** Toggle ON
4. Click **Save**

## PRICING OPTIMIZATION NOTES

### Tier Strategy

**Impulse buys ($7-17):** Funnel Teardown, Sleep YouTube Kit, Tech Stack Guide
- These exist to get people into the ecosystem
- Low price = low friction = high volume
- Upsell to mid-tier on thank-you page

**Mid-tier ($27-47):** Cold Email, Twitter Growth, AI Automation, Vibe Coding, Content Farm
- Core revenue products
- $27-47 is the sweet spot for digital products: expensive enough to feel valuable, cheap enough for impulse
- PWYW minimum at 50-60% lets price-sensitive buyers in

**Premium ($97):** Local Biz Client Machine
- Highest perceived value (includes Python scripts, 19K+ words)
- Premium price signals quality
- Buyers at this level are serious, lower refund rate

**Bundles ($47-197):** Volume discounts that increase AOV
- Starter Bundle at $47 = cheaper than buying the 3 products individually
- Automation Stack at $97 = 39% off individual pricing
- Everything Bundle at $197 = 44% off, plus 90-day new product guarantee

### Why PWYW Minimums

Pay-what-you-want with minimums does two things:
1. People who pay more feel ownership and are less likely to refund
2. People who pay the minimum still convert (you get the sale + the email)
3. Average PWYW payment is typically 1.2-1.5x the minimum

### Bundle Psychology

The Everything Bundle at $197 is the anchor that makes the $47 and $97 bundles feel like steals. Most purchases will cluster at $47 (Starter) and individual $7-27 products. The $197 bundle is for the 5-10% who want everything. Those buyers are your best customers.

### Price Testing (After 50+ Sales)

- Raise the $7 product to $9 (still impulse, higher AOV)
- Raise one $47 product to $57 (test one at a time)
- Add a $297 "VIP" tier with a 30-minute call included
- Offer a limited-time 50% discount code for Twitter followers
