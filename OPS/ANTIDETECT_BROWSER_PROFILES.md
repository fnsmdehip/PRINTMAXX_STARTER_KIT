## Warmup Schedule (CRITICAL — Don't Skip)

Posting immediately on new accounts = shadowban risk. Follow this schedule.

### Days 1-3: Lurk Mode
- Follow 10-20 niche accounts per platform
- Like 20-30 posts per day
- NO posting
- NO DMs
- Browse for 5-10 min per session (looks natural)
- Save/bookmark posts (signals engagement to algorithm)

### Days 4-7: Light Engagement
- Continue following + liking
- Start commenting (genuine, 5+ words, relevant)
- 1 post per day per platform
- NO links in posts yet
- NO hashtag spam (max 3-5 per post)
- Retweet/share 2-3 relevant posts

### Days 8-14: Ramp Up
- 2-3 posts per day
- Start using links (sparingly)
- Engage with larger accounts (quote tweets, thoughtful replies)
- Can start threads
- Begin hashtag strategy (5-10 per post on IG/TikTok)

### Day 15+: Full Schedule
- Full posting cadence (see content calendar)
- Links allowed
- DMs for outreach
- Cross-promotion between accounts (subtle, not spam)

### Platform-Specific Notes

**X/Twitter:**
- New accounts are throttled. First week engagement will be low. Normal.
- Reply to larger accounts in your niche. This is the #1 growth hack for new accounts.
- Don't follow/unfollow rapidly. Max 50 follows per day.

**TikTok:**
- First 3 videos determine your FYP category. Make them count.
- Use trending sounds from day 1.
- 100% completion rate > virality for new accounts.
- Post 1-3x per day during warmup. Algorithm rewards consistency.

**Instagram:**
- Reels get 3-5x more reach than static posts for new accounts.
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags (not spammy ones).
- Stories increase account trust score.
- Respond to every comment in first 30 min (algorithm signal).

**YouTube:**
- Shorts are boosted for new channels.
- First 10 Shorts determine channel categorization. Pick your niche.
- Titles matter more than thumbnails for Shorts.
- Post 1 Short per day during warmup.

**LinkedIn:**
- New accounts get boosted for first 2 weeks (LinkedIn wants engagement).
- Text-only posts outperform posts with links.
- Comment on industry posts before posting your own.
- Post during business hours (7-9am, 12-1pm, 5-7pm).
