## KEY INSIGHTS

### What Makes High-Synergy Stacks Work:

1. **Multi-Layer Monetization**
   - Best stacks monetize same audience 3-5 different ways
   - Example: Fitness persona → app subs + TikTok Shop + affiliates + sponsorships
   - Diversifies revenue, protects against single point of failure

2. **Asset Reuse Multiplier**
   - Content created once, used everywhere
   - Example: Workout video → social content + app demo + TikTok Shop promo + SEO embed
   - Reduces creation cost, increases ROI per piece

3. **Natural Progression Funnel**
   - Each method feeds the next in logical sequence
   - Example: Content → email capture → app download → course sale → consulting
   - Customers naturally ascend value ladder

4. **Niche Authority Compounding**
   - Focused niches (fitness, faith, ADHD, finance) score higher than broad
   - All methods reinforce expertise in one area
   - Example: Faith expert + prayer app + devotional course = niche domination

5. **AI Persona Leverage**
   - AI influencers appear in 60% of top stacks
   - Provides consistent brand voice/appearance across all methods
   - No creator burnout, infinitely scalable
   - Example: Single AI fitness coach → all content, app promo, product demos
