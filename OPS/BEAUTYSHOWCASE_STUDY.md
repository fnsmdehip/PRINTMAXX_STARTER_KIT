## 2. CAPTION STRATEGY (THE PLAYBOOK)

the #1 thing @beautyshowcase does differently from generic pages: **ultra-minimal captions.** average caption length = 8.8 characters. most posts are 0-15 characters.

### 6 Caption Formats (ranked by frequency + performance)

#### Format 1: NO CAPTION (just the image)
- **Frequency:** 4/16 posts (25%)
- **Avg likes:** 5,069
- **Avg ER:** 6.5%
- **Why it works:** zero friction. the image IS the content. followers don't need to read, they just engage. also signals confidence in the content quality.
- **@velvetframes action:** make 25% of posts captionless. only use your strongest images for this.

#### Format 2: NATIONALITY TAG
- **Examples:** "French", "Greek", "American excellence"
- **Frequency:** 3/16 posts (19%)
- **Avg likes:** 5,041
- **Top performer:** "French" = 9,240 likes, 246K views
- **Why it works:** triggers identity engagement. French people RT it ("that's us"), Americans engage with "American excellence" out of patriotism. it's algorithmic engagement bait disguised as a simple label.
- **@velvetframes action:** label photos by nationality. keep it to ONE WORD when possible. "Italian", "Brazilian", "Swedish", "Japanese". the single word is the hook.

#### Format 3: "PICK A NUMBER" POLL
- **Examples:** "1, 2 or 3 ?", "1, 2, 3, 4 or 5 ?"
- **Frequency:** 2/16 posts (13%)
- **Avg likes:** 4,757
- **Avg REPLIES:** 869 (10-15x normal)
- **Top views:** "1, 2 or 3 ?" = 521,333 views (highest views of ALL posts)
- **Why it works:** THE reply farming hack. every reply boosts the algo. 980 replies on a single post. X's 2026 algo weights replies at 13.5x likes. this is the #1 growth format.
- **@velvetframes action:** post 1 pick-a-number per day. 3-5 options. carousel of images. the replies will train the algo to show you to more people.

#### Format 4: "GOOD MORNING" RECURRING
- **Examples:** "Good morning" (same text, different photo)
- **Frequency:** 2/16 posts (13%)
- **Avg likes:** 3,372
- **Avg ER:** 5.8%
- **Why it works:** creates a daily habit loop. followers expect it. consistent series = consistent engagement. also positions the account as a "daily ritual" in followers' feeds.
- **@velvetframes action:** start every day with "Good morning" + a golden hour beauty shot. same text every day. the photo does the work.

#### Format 5: SHORT DESCRIPTOR
- **Examples:** "Tanlines >>", "Elite build", "Rack of the day"
- **Frequency:** 3/16 posts (19%)
- **Avg likes:** 1,371
- **Why it works:** adds context/opinion without over-explaining. ">>" is a power symbol that signals "this is good." "of the day" creates recurring series.
- **@velvetframes action:** use 2-3 word descriptors. "golden hour >>", "natural beauty >>", "face of the day", "grace >>". keep the >> pattern for strongest images.

#### Format 6: PROVOCATIVE OPINION
- **Examples:** "Women are getting bigger"
- **Frequency:** 1/16 posts (6%)
- **Replies:** 188 (3x normal)
- **Why it works:** opinion bait. people agree and disagree in replies. debate = engagement = algo boost. use sparingly.
- **@velvetframes action:** 1-2x per week. mild opinion that sparks discussion. "beauty standards are changing", "freckles >>", "no makeup > heavy makeup". never inflammatory, just enough to get replies.

### NAME CREDITS (bonus format)
- **Example:** "Sam Dubner" (just the model's name as caption)
- **Why:** shows legitimacy. model's fans engage. model might retweet. professional feel.
- **@velvetframes action:** when you know the model/photographer, credit them in the caption. keep it minimal: just the name.

## 5. REPOST / RETWEET STRATEGY

from the 16 posts analyzed, ALL 16 were original posts (no retweets or quote tweets in the timeline). this means @beautyshowcase:

- **does NOT retweet other accounts** in their main feed
- **does NOT quote tweet** in their main feed
- keeps the timeline 100% curated photos/videos
- any cross-promotion or engagement happens in replies, not the main timeline

**@velvetframes action:** keep your main timeline CLEAN. only your curated posts. no retweets cluttering the feed. engage with other pages in replies and quote tweets from your personal, not from the curation page.

**growth via replies instead:**
- reply to larger pages with your best images
- reply to viral tweets in adjacent niches (fashion, fitness, lifestyle) with a relevant photo
- reply to model/influencer posts with compliments (gets you in front of their audience)
- quote tweet OTHER beauty pages' best content with a simple ">>>" or one-word reaction (this CAN go on timeline if it's very strong content)

## 9. WHAT NOT TO DO (anti-patterns)

- long captions. @beautyshowcase avg = 8.8 chars. don't write paragraphs.
- hashtags. zero hashtags in any of the 16 posts scraped.
- retweet spam. keep main timeline clean.
- inconsistent posting. 8/day, every day, like clockwork.
- generic engagement bait ("thoughts?", "what do you think?"). the "1, 2 or 3?" format is specific and proven.
- over-branding. no watermarks, no "follow for more", no "link in bio" text on images.
- text overlays on images. the content is the photo, not text on top of it.
