## Voice Calibration Checklist (Before Every Quote Tweet)

- [ ] Consequence-first (lead with result, not explanation)
- [ ] Specific number included
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Lowercase energy

## POSTING RHYTHM

**Daily Target:** 3-5 quote tweets across categories

**Mix:**
- 1 Contrarian (morning, high energy time)
- 1-2 Adding Value (throughout day)

**Self-Reply Rule:** Add funnel self-reply to any quote that hits 50+ likes within 2 hours.
