## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Reply Targets

| Account | Reply Target | Focus |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| AI | 20 replies/day | Trending tech posts, competitor accounts |
| Faith | 15 replies/day | Faith leaders, community posts |
| Fitness | 20 replies/day | Fitness influencers, transformation posts |

### Reply Strategy

**First 15 Minutes After Posting:**
- Reply to 3-5 related posts from high-engagement accounts
- Quote tweet a trending post in your niche
- Engage with people who commented on your last post

**Reply Types (Ranked by Value):**
1. **Insight Addition** - Add value to their post
2. **Personal Experience** - Share relevant story
3. **Question** - Ask follow-up that invites response
4. **Agreement + Extension** - "this. and also [NEW POINT]"
5. **Thoughtful Disagreement** - Contrarian but respectful

**Avoid:**
- Generic "great post!" replies
- Spammy link drops
- Arguing in comments
- Replying to political content

### Quote Tweet Strategy

**Daily Target:** 2-3 quote tweets per account

**Quote Tweet Patterns:**

1. **Add Context:**
   ```
   [QUOTE TWEET]

   this worked for me too.

   my results: [SPECIFIC NUMBERS]
   ```

2. **Contrarian Take:**
   ```
   [QUOTE TWEET]

   respectfully disagree.

   here's why: [REASONING]
   ```

3. **Expansion:**
   ```
   [QUOTE TWEET]

   adding to this:
   - [POINT 1]
   - [POINT 2]
   - [POINT 3]
   ```

4. **Personal Spin:**
   ```
   [QUOTE TWEET]

   tried this in [MY NICHE].

   results: [OUTCOME]
   ```
