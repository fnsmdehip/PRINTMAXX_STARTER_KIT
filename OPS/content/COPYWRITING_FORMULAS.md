# Copywriting Formulas Library

Proven copywriting frameworks for headlines, emails, social posts, CTAs, and sales pages. Use these templates across all PRINTMAXX content.

---

## 1. Headline Formulas

### Number + Adjective + Noun + Promise

**Formula:** [Number] [Adjective] [Noun] That [Promise/Benefit]

**Why it works:** Specific numbers create credibility. Adjective adds interest. Promise delivers value.

**Examples:**
- 7 Simple Apps That Make $5K/Month While You Sleep
- 12 Underrated Tools That Will 10x Your Productivity
- 5 Proven Scripts That Close High-Ticket Clients

---

### How to [X] Without [Pain]

**Formula:** How to [Achieve Desired Outcome] Without [Common Obstacle]

**Why it works:** Removes objection before it arises. Shows path exists.

**Examples:**
- How to Build a 6-Figure Business Without Quitting Your Job
- How to Grow to 10K Followers Without Posting Daily
- How to Launch an App Without Writing Code

---

### The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic]

**Formula:** The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic for Audience]

**Why it works:** "The" implies authority. Adjective sets expectation.

**Examples:**
- The Complete Guide to Cold Email in 2025
- The No-BS Guide to App Store Optimization
- The Lazy Person's Guide to Content Repurposing

---

### [Number] Ways to [Benefit]

**Formula:** [Number] Ways to [Achieve Benefit] [Qualifier]

**Why it works:** Listicles are scannable. Multiple options = something for everyone.

**Examples:**
- 15 Ways to Monetize Your Newsletter This Week
- 9 Ways to Get Your First 100 Customers
- 21 Ways to Increase Your App's Retention

---

### What [Group] Know About [Topic]

**Formula:** What [Successful Group] Know About [Topic] That You Don't

**Why it works:** Insider knowledge appeal. Creates curiosity gap.

**Examples:**
- What Top 1% Founders Know About Growth That You Don't
- What Senior Developers Know About Shipping Fast
- What 7-Figure Creators Know About Monetization

---

### The [Timeframe] [Topic] Blueprint

**Formula:** The [Timeframe] [Topic] Blueprint for [Audience/Result]

**Why it works:** Specific timeframe adds urgency. "Blueprint" implies actionable system.

**Examples:**
- The 30-Day App Launch Blueprint
- The 90-Day Content Empire Blueprint
- The Weekend MVP Blueprint for Solo Founders

---

### [Verb] Your [X] in [Timeframe]

**Formula:** [Action Verb] Your [Thing They Want] in [Believable Timeframe]

**Why it works:** Action-oriented. Specific timeframe creates accountability.

**Examples:**
- Double Your Email Open Rates in 30 Days
- Build Your MVP in One Weekend
- Grow Your List to 1,000 Subscribers in 60 Days

---

### Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong

**Formula:** Why [Commonly Accepted Advice] Is Wrong (And What to Do Instead)

**Why it works:** Contrarian takes stand out. Challenges assumptions.

**Examples:**
- Why "Just Ship It" Is Terrible Advice
- Why Building in Public Is Hurting Your Launch
- Why SEO Is Dead for New Creators

---

### I [Did X] and Here's What Happened

**Formula:** I [Did Bold/Interesting Action] and Here's What Happened

**Why it works:** First-person stories are relatable. Creates curiosity.

**Examples:**
- I Cold Emailed 1,000 CEOs and Here's What Happened
- I Quit Social Media for 30 Days and Here's What Happened
- I Launched 5 Apps in 5 Weeks and Here's What Happened

---

### Stop [Bad Thing], Start [Good Thing]

**Formula:** Stop [Common Mistake]. Start [Better Alternative].

**Why it works:** Direct command. Clear contrast between wrong and right.

**Examples:**
- Stop Chasing Followers. Start Building an Audience.
- Stop Building Features. Start Solving Problems.
- Stop Creating Content. Start Building Systems.

---

### The [X] That [Result]

**Formula:** The [Single Thing] That [Achieved Impressive Result]

**Why it works:** Simplicity is appealing. One thing is actionable.

**Examples:**
- The One Email That Closed My Biggest Deal
- The Single Change That 3x'd My Conversion Rate
- The Script That Gets 40% Reply Rates

---

### Everything You Need to Know About [Topic]

**Formula:** Everything You Need to Know About [Topic] in [Timeframe/Format]

**Why it works:** Comprehensive promise. Saves research time.

**Examples:**
- Everything You Need to Know About ASO in 15 Minutes
- Everything You Need to Know About RevenueCat Integration
- Everything You Need to Know About Cold Email Deliverability

---

### The [Audience] Playbook for [Result]

**Formula:** The [Specific Audience] Playbook for [Specific Result]

**Why it works:** Highly targeted. "Playbook" implies actionable steps.

**Examples:**
- The Solopreneur Playbook for $10K/Month
- The Developer Playbook for Building in Public
- The Creator Playbook for Monetizing Twitter

---

### [Result] Without [Sacrifice]

**Formula:** [Desirable Result] Without [Expected Sacrifice]

**Why it works:** Removes the perceived trade-off.

**Examples:**
- Scale to 6 Figures Without Hiring
- Build Muscle Without Living in the Gym
- Grow Your Audience Without Posting Daily

---

### From [Bad State] to [Good State]

**Formula:** From [Starting Point] to [End Point] in [Timeframe]

**Why it works:** Clear transformation arc. Timeframe adds believability.

**Examples:**
- From 0 to 1,000 Subscribers in 90 Days
- From Side Project to $5K MRR in 6 Months
- From Overwhelmed to Automated in One Weekend

## 2. Email Subject Lines

### Curiosity Gap

**Formula:** Create information gap that can only be closed by opening

**Examples:**
- I was wrong about [topic]
- The one thing I'd change
- This cost me $10K
- I almost didn't send this
- What nobody told me about [X]

**When to use:** Newsletters, relationship-building emails, story-based content.

---

### Benefit-Driven

**Formula:** Lead with what they get

**Examples:**
- Your [outcome] starts here
- [Number] ways to [benefit] this week
- The shortcut to [desired result]
- How to get [result] in [timeframe]
- [Result] without [pain point]

**When to use:** Sales emails, course announcements, value-focused sends.

---

### Urgency

**Formula:** Create time pressure (only when genuine)

**Examples:**
- [X] hours left
- Last chance: [offer]
- Closing tonight
- Price goes up [date]
- [Number] spots remaining

**When to use:** Launch sequences, limited offers. Never fake urgency.

---

### Question

**Formula:** Ask a question they want answered

**Examples:**
- Are you making this mistake?
- What would you do with [benefit]?
- Ready to [achieve X]?
- Why is [surprising thing] happening?
- Did you see this?

**When to use:** Engagement emails, surveys, re-engagement campaigns.

---

### Story Teaser

**Formula:** Hint at a story worth reading

**Examples:**
- The email that changed everything
- What happened when I [did X]
- A lesson from [unexpected source]
- The $50K mistake
- I shouldn't share this, but...

**When to use:** Newsletters, personal brand emails, narrative content.

---

### Pattern Interrupt

**Formula:** Say something unexpected

**Examples:**
- (no subject)
- .
- re: re: re:
- [First name only]
- Warning: [counterintuitive statement]

**When to use:** Cold email, re-engagement. Use sparingly.

---

### Social Proof

**Formula:** Lead with results or endorsement

**Examples:**
- How [name] got [result]
- [Number] people are doing this
- [Name] just joined
- What [successful person] said about [X]
- [Metric] and counting

**When to use:** Testimonial-driven emails, launch sequences.

## 3. Social Media Hooks

### Pattern Interrupt

**Formula:** Break the scroll with unexpected opening

**Examples:**
- Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]
- Hot take: [bold statement]
- Confession: [honest admission]
- Plot twist: [unexpected turn]
- Warning: [something they should know]

---

### Story Hook

**Formula:** Open with the most interesting part

**Examples:**
- Last month I almost quit. Here's why I didn't.
- A stranger DM'd me yesterday. Changed my entire strategy.
- I was 2 days from bankruptcy. Then this happened.
- My first customer asked for a refund. Best thing that happened.
- 3 years ago I had $47 in my bank account.

---

### Controversial Hook

**Formula:** Challenge accepted wisdom

**Examples:**
- [Common advice] is terrible advice. Here's why:
- Everyone says [X]. They're wrong.
- [Popular thing] is overrated. [Alternative] works better.
- Stop doing [common practice]. Do this instead.
- The [industry] doesn't want you to know this.

---

### List Hook

**Formula:** Promise organized, scannable value

**Examples:**
- [Number] things I learned [doing X]:
- [Number] tools that changed how I [outcome]:
- [Number] mistakes I made so you don't have to:
- [Number] lessons from [source]:
- [Number] underrated [things]:

---

### Credibility Hook

**Formula:** Lead with proof, then teach

**Examples:**
- I've [impressive metric]. Here's exactly how:
- [Result] in [timeframe]. The playbook:
- After [experience], here's what actually works:
- I've helped [number] people [result]. The pattern:
- $[revenue/result] later, here's what I know:

---

### "Most People" Hook

**Formula:** Create in-group/out-group dynamic

**Examples:**
- Most people [wrong approach]. Top performers [right approach].
- 99% of [people] don't know this.
- Most advice about [X] is wrong. Here's the truth:
- Everyone focuses on [X]. The money is in [Y].
- Most [people] will ignore this. That's why they stay stuck.

## 4. CTA Formulas

### Action + Benefit

**Formula:** [Verb] + [What They Get]

**Examples:**
- Get the free template
- Download the playbook
- Start your free trial
- Grab the cheatsheet
- Join 5,000+ subscribers

---

### Urgency + Action

**Formula:** [Time Pressure] + [Verb]

**Examples:**
- Join before price increases
- Get instant access now
- Claim your spot today
- Start free for 14 days
- Lock in founding member pricing

---

### Risk Reversal

**Formula:** Remove the risk

**Examples:**
- Try free for 14 days
- No credit card required
- Cancel anytime
- Full refund if it doesn't work
- Risk-free trial

---

### Social Proof CTA

**Formula:** Show others taking action

**Examples:**
- Join [number]+ [people]
- See why [companies/people] chose us
- Trusted by [impressive names]
- [Number] people signed up this week

---

**Formula:** Lead with outcome, action second

**Examples:**
- Save 10 hours this week. Get the system.
- Double your replies. Learn how.
- Build faster. Start your trial.
- Write better copy. Grab the templates.
- Grow your list. Join free.

## 5. Sales Page Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)

**Structure:**
1. **Attention:** Hook with headline and opening
2. **Interest:** Present the problem and your unique approach
3. **Desire:** Show benefits, proof, and transformation
4. **Action:** Clear CTA with urgency or incentive

**Outline:**
```
ATTENTION:
- Headline: How to [Result] Without [Pain]
- Subhead: The step-by-step system used by [social proof]

INTEREST:
- The problem: You want X but can't get past Y
- Why other approaches fail
- What's different about this approach

DESIRE:
- Benefits: What they get
- Proof: Case studies, testimonials
- What's included: Modules, templates, etc.
- Bonuses: Extra value

ACTION:
- CTA: Join/Buy/Start
- Risk reversal: Guarantee
- Urgency: Deadline or scarcity
```

---

### PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)

**Structure:**
1. **Problem:** State the pain they're experiencing
2. **Agitate:** Make them feel the pain more deeply
3. **Solution:** Present your offer as the fix

**Example:**
```
PROBLEM:
You're creating content every day but your audience isn't growing.
You've tried posting more, different formats, new platforms.
Nothing moves the needle.

AGITATE:
Meanwhile, creators who started after you are getting brand deals.
They have engaged audiences. They're monetizing.
You're still at 500 followers wondering what you're doing wrong.
Every hour you spend creating feels wasted.

SOLUTION:
The Content Flywheel System shows you exactly how to create
content that compounds. No more guessing. Just a proven system
that 200+ creators have used to grow from 0 to 10K+ followers.
```

---

### Before-After-Bridge (BAB)

**Structure:**
1. **Before:** Their current painful state
2. **After:** The desired future state
3. **Bridge:** Your solution that gets them there

**Example:**
```
BEFORE:
Right now you're drowning in content creation.
5 hours to make one post. Burnout incoming.
No time left to actually run your business.

AFTER:
Imagine creating a week's content in 2 hours.
Batch recording, scheduled posting, everything systematized.
You show up online consistently without sacrificing your life.

BRIDGE:
The Content OS Template is how you get there.
Pre-built workflows. Copy-paste scripts.
Set it up once, run it forever.
```

## 6. Story Frameworks

### Before/After Transformation

**Structure:**
1. **Before State:** Specific pain points
2. **Turning Point:** What changed
3. **After State:** Specific improvements
4. **How:** Brief explanation of method

**Example:**
```
BEFORE:
- Working 60+ hours/week
- $3K MRR after 2 years
- Constantly firefighting bugs
- Zero time for marketing

TURNING POINT:
I hired a VA for $500/month and systematized everything.

AFTER:
- Working 30 hours/week
- $12K MRR (grew 4x in 6 months)
- Automated 80% of support
- 10 hours/week on growth

HOW:
The Leverage Framework: Document, Delegate, Delete.
```

---

### Origin Story Template

**Structure:**
1. **The Moment:** When everything changed
2. **The Backstory:** Quick context
3. **The Struggle:** What made it hard
4. **The Breakthrough:** What clicked
5. **The Mission:** Why you do what you do

**Example:**
```
THE MOMENT:
The day I got laid off, I had $2,400 in savings
and a 6-month-old daughter.

THE BACKSTORY:
I'd been a content marketer for 8 years.
Always working on other people's brands.

THE STRUGGLE:
For 4 months I applied to jobs and got rejected.
Started freelancing out of desperation.

THE BREAKTHROUGH:
A client asked me to build their content system.
I realized I could productize it.
Launched a template. Made $4K in the first week.

THE MISSION:
Now I help other marketers escape the agency grind
and build their own income streams.
```

---

### Customer Success Story

**Structure:**
1. **Who:** Brief intro of the person
2. **Problem:** What they struggled with
3. **Solution:** How they found you
4. **Process:** What they did
5. **Result:** Specific outcomes
6. **Quote:** In their words

**Example:**
```
WHO:
Sarah runs a freelance design business. Solo. Remote. Overwhelmed.

PROBLEM:
She was getting clients but couldn't keep up with the work.
No systems. Everything manual. Working 70-hour weeks.

SOLUTION:
She joined the Productized Service Accelerator in January.

PROCESS:
Built a service package. Created templates.
Documented her process. Raised prices 40%.

RESULT:
- Revenue: $6K/month to $14K/month
- Hours: 70/week to 35/week
- Clients: Fewer but better

QUOTE:
"I finally feel like I own a business instead of having
a job that owns me." - Sarah K.
```

## Quick Reference

| Situation | Best Formula |
|-----------|--------------|
| Blog post headline | Number + Adjective + Noun |
| Cold email subject | Personalization + Question |
| Twitter hook | Pattern interrupt or Story |
| Course sales page | PASTOR or PAS |
| Product launch email | Urgency + Benefit |
| About page | Origin Story Template |
| Case study | Before/After Transformation |
| Newsletter CTA | Action + Benefit |
| High-ticket offer | AIDA with heavy proof |
| Quick landing page | BAB or Star-Chain-Hook |

---

## Testing Notes

- Headlines: Test 3-5 variations minimum
- Subject lines: A/B test with 10%+ sample
- CTAs: Test button text, color, and placement
- Hooks: First 3 words matter most
- Stories: Specific details beat vague claims

Always measure. What works for one audience may not work for another.
