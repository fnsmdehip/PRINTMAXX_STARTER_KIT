## Platform Monetization Hierarchy (Jan 2026)

### 1. Facebook Reels - HIGHEST CPM
- Best short-form monetization
- Outperforms YouTube Shorts significantly
- 30 posts/day volume strategy working
- Repurpose 1 content into 4 formats (text, image, reel, story)

### 2. YouTube Shorts - Reliable Baseline
- $0.40 RPM baseline (some creators seeing higher)
- Real data: @thedankoe shared $4,495.60 payout for Jan 3-17 2026 (~$9K/month potential)
- More reliable than TikTok Creator Fund
- Priority: YouTube > everything else for monetization

### 3. YouTube Long-form - Best Overall LTV
- 10min+ videos for mid-roll ads
- Premium CPM in right niches
- Evergreen content compounds over time

### 4. Instagram Reels - Medium CPM
- Brand building and authority
- Algorithm update: Original audio > trending sounds (Jan 2026)
- Use AI voice tools (ElevenLabs, Descript) for faceless original audio

### 5. TikTok - UNRELIABLE (Use for Testing Only)
- Ad revenue share declining/zero for many creators in 2026
- Algorithm prioritizing engagement over monetization
- Creators reporting reach drops after enabling monetization
- **Use only as top-of-funnel traffic source**

## Current Algorithm Priorities (All Platforms)

### First 3 Seconds = Everything
- Universal pattern across TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook
- Hook must grab attention immediately
- Winning hook patterns:
  - Bold text statement
  - Shocking statistic
  - Direct question
  - Controversial claim
- ZERO intro fluff
- Value in first second

### Instagram Reels Specific (Jan 2026)
- Original audio heavily prioritized
- Trending sounds less effective than 2025
- Original voiceover or music = better reach
- Tools: CapCut, Descript, ElevenLabs for AI voice

## Trending Content Formats

### Multi-Platform Distribution Strategy
- Post identical content to 4+ platforms simultaneously
- Track CPM/RPM per platform per niche
- Some niches pay more on FB, others on YT (test YOUR niche)
- Use Buffer/Hootsuite for scheduling

### Content Multiplication System

- 10 platform variants (YT Shorts, FB Reels, IG Reels, TikTok, YT long)
- 10 text posts (X, LinkedIn threads)
- 10 images (quote cards, IG carousel)
- Template-based repurposing
- Track which formats perform best per platform

## Triple Monetization Model

### Best Practice 2026: Never Rely on Ads Alone

**Primary:** YouTube ad revenue (baseline)
**Secondary:** Affiliate links in description
- Meditation apps (Calm, Headspace)
- Sleep products (weighted blankets, sleep masks)
- Supplements (melatonin, magnesium)
- 2-3 relevant links per video

**Tertiary:** Simple digital product
- Sleep sounds pack $7-27
- Guided meditation audio
- Sleep training guide PDF
- One product per niche

**Implementation:**
- Link all monetization in description + pinned comment
- Track which revenue stream performs best
- Optimize based on data

## Pre-Save Algorithm Hack

**Observation:** Music industry TikTok/Reels tactics
- Artists use pre-save CTAs extensively
- Challenge format: "Drop X after Y pre-saves reached"
- Gives more algorithmic reach
- More playlist adds
- **Application:** Can adapt to non-music niches
  - "Comment GUIDE for the template"
  - "Save this post to see more"
  - "Share this and tag 3 friends"
