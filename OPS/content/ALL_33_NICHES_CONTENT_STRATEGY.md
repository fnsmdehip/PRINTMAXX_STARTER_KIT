# PLATFORM-SPECIFIC PLAYBOOKS

## Twitter/X Playbook

**Posting Schedule:**
- 3-5 tweets per day
- Threads 2-3x per week
- Best times: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-9 PM (audience time zone)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Value (educational, tips)
- 25% Engagement (questions, polls)
- 20% Social proof (results, testimonials)
- 15% Personality (opinions, stories)

**Growth Tactics:**
- Reply to larger accounts in your niche
- Quote tweet with added value
- Join relevant conversations
- Consistent posting schedule

## TikTok Playbook

**Posting Schedule:**
- 1-3 videos per day
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-9 PM

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Educational (how-tos, tips)
- 30% Entertainment (relatable, trending)
- 20% Promotional (lead magnets, products)

**Video Structure:**
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Get to the point fast
- Use captions (most watch muted)
- End with CTA or question

## Instagram Playbook

**Posting Schedule:**
- 1 feed post per day
- 5-10 stories per day
- 1-2 Reels per day

**Content Mix:**
- Feed: Carousels (highest engagement), single images, Reels
- Stories: Behind-scenes, polls, Q&A
- Reels: Same as TikTok

**Growth Tactics:**
- Carousels get most saves/shares
- Use all 30 hashtags (mix of sizes)
- Engage with followers in stories
- Collaborate with similar accounts

## YouTube Shorts Playbook

**Posting Schedule:**
- 1-3 Shorts per day
- Best times: Morning, lunch, evening

**Content Strategy:**
- Repurpose TikTok content
- Add YouTube-specific hooks
- Use end screens when available
- Link to long-form content
