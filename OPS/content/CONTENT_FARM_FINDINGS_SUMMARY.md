### Algorithm & Format Tactics

**7. First 3 Seconds Hook (Universal)**
- All platforms prioritize first 3 seconds
- Winning patterns: Bold text, shocking stat, question, controversy
- ZERO intro fluff
- Value in first second

**8. Instagram Original Audio Priority**
- Original audio > trending sounds (Jan 2026 update)
- Use AI voice tools: ElevenLabs, Descript, CapCut
- Test original vs trending, track reach

**9. Pre-Save Algorithm Hack**
- Music industry tactic applicable to all niches
- Challenge format: "Drop X after Y saves"
- Boosts algorithmic reach + distribution
- Adapt: "Comment GUIDE" or "Save this post"
