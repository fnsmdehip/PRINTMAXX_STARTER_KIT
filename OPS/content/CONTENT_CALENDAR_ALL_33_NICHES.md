## Universal Posting Framework

### Daily Content Rhythm (Per Account)

Every niche follows this 7-day rhythm. Specific themes adjust per niche.

| Day | Content Type | Purpose | Format Priority |
|-----|--------------|---------|-----------------|
| **Monday** | Educational | Start week with value | Thread (7 tweets) / Carousel |
| **Tuesday** | Engagement | Build community | Poll / Q&A / Story |
| **Wednesday** | Value Bomb | Mid-week resource drop | Quick tip / Reel / Tool share |
| **Thursday** | Story/Proof | Build trust | Case study / Testimonial |
| **Friday** | Entertainment | Ride weekend energy | Meme / Trend / Relatable |
| **Saturday** | Recap/Best Of | Catch weekend scrollers | Compilation / Highlights |
| **Sunday** | Personal/BTS | Authentic connection | Behind scenes / Day in life |

### Platform-Specific Timing

**Twitter/X:**
- Post 4x/day at peak hours
- Morning (6-9 AM), Lunch (11 AM-1 PM), Evening (5-7 PM), Night (8-10 PM)

**TikTok:**
- Post 2-3x/day
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-9 PM (all time zones matter)

**Instagram:**
- Post 1-2x/day (feed) + 3-5 stories
- Peak: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM

**YouTube Shorts:**
- Post 1-2x/day
- Peak: 2-4 PM, 7-9 PM

## Content Batching System

### Monthly Batch Day

**Time Investment:** 1 full day per niche per month

**Output:** 30 days of content per platform

### Batch Process

1. **Morning (3 hrs):** Write all Twitter posts
   - 4 posts/day x 30 days = 120 tweets
   - Batch by content type (all tutorials together, all memes together)

2. **Afternoon (3 hrs):** Create visual content
   - 10 TikToks (batch filming)
   - 30 Instagram carousels (template-based)
   - 8 Reels (repurposed TikToks)

3. **Evening (2 hrs):** Schedule everything
   - Buffer for Twitter
   - TikTok scheduled posts
   - Later for Instagram

### Repurposing Matrix

| Original Content | Twitter | TikTok | Instagram | YouTube Shorts |
|-----------------|---------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| 60s Tutorial Video | Thread summary | Full video | Reel | Short |
| Thread (7 tweets) | Original | Talking head reading | Carousel | Script for Short |
| Carousel | Tweet each slide | Slideshow video | Original | Slideshow Short |
| Photo | Tweet + caption | Photo slideshow | Feed post | - |
