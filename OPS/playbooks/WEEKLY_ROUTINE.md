# PRINTMAXX Weekly Routine

**Total weekly commitment:** 25-35 hours
**Goal:** Structured variety that covers all business functions

---

## Weekly Overview

| Day | Theme | Primary Focus | Hours |
|-----|-------|---------------|-------|
| Monday | Planning + Batch | Content creation | 6h |
| Tuesday | Outreach | Sales + relationships | 5h |
| Wednesday | Product | Building + shipping | 6h |
| Thursday | Marketing | Promotion + distribution | 5h |
| Friday | Analysis | Review + optimize | 4h |
| Saturday | Rest | Optional bonus work | 0-2h |
| Sunday | Prep | Week planning | 1-2h |

---

## Monday: Planning + Content Batch

### Morning (2h)

**Weekly planning session (60 min):**
- Review previous week's metrics
- Set 3 weekly goals (outcomes, not tasks)
- Block calendar for deep work
- Identify any blockers to address

**Content planning (60 min):**
- Batch 5-7 social posts for the week
- Outline 1 long-form piece (blog/newsletter)
- Schedule all social content

### Afternoon (3h)

**Content creation deep work:**
- Write newsletter or long-form content
- Create any supporting assets
- Edit and polish Friday's content
- Prepare email sequence content

### Evening (1h)

**Standard evening block:**
- Schedule Tuesday's content
- Review plan for tomorrow
- Clear inbox

**Monday deliverables:**
- [ ] Week planned with 3 clear goals
- [ ] 5-7 social posts scheduled
- [ ] 1 long-form piece drafted
- [ ] Newsletter outlined or drafted

---

## Tuesday: Outreach + Sales

### Morning (2h)

**Cold outreach (60 min):**
- Research 10-15 prospects
- Personalize outreach messages
- Send cold emails/DMs
- Update outreach tracker

**Warm relationship building (60 min):**
- Follow up on outstanding conversations
- Share helpful content with connections
- Comment on target accounts' posts
- Nurture existing relationships

### Afternoon (2.5h)

**Sales calls (if scheduled):**
- Discovery calls
- Demo calls
- Closing conversations
- Follow-up tasks immediately after

**Partnership outreach (remaining time):**
- Identify potential collaborators
- Reach out for podcast/guest post opportunities
- Follow up on pending collaborations

### Evening (30 min)

**Pipeline review:**
- Update LEDGER/outreach_tracker.csv
- Identify who needs follow-up
- Plan Wednesday's quick outreach tasks

**Tuesday deliverables:**
- [ ] 10+ outreach messages sent
- [ ] All open conversations followed up
- [ ] Pipeline updated
- [ ] 5+ valuable engagements on social

---

## Wednesday: Product Work

### Morning (2h)

**User research (60 min):**
- Review customer feedback/support tickets
- Check analytics for user behavior
- Identify friction points in funnel
- Talk to 1 customer if possible

**Planning product work (60 min):**
- Prioritize improvements for today
- Break into 2-hour chunks
- Set clear "done" definition

### Afternoon (3.5h)

**Deep product work:**
- Build new feature OR
- Fix critical bugs OR
- Improve existing functionality OR
- Create new lead magnet content

**Testing:**
- Test everything you build
- Get feedback if possible
- Document changes

### Evening (30 min)

**Ship or stage:**
- Push changes to staging
- Document what was built
- Plan any needed follow-up

**Wednesday deliverables:**
- [ ] 1 meaningful product improvement shipped
- [ ] User feedback reviewed and documented
- [ ] No new bugs introduced
- [ ] LEDGER updated with product changes

---

## Thursday: Marketing + Promotion

### Morning (2h)

**Promotion execution (90 min):**
- Cross-post content to secondary platforms
- Share in relevant communities/subreddits
- Update product listings (ProductHunt, directories)
- Submit guest posts if queued

**Paid ads management (30 min, if applicable):**
- Review ad performance
- Adjust targeting/creative
- Pause underperformers
- Test new variations

### Afternoon (2.5h)

**Content distribution:**
- Repurpose best-performing content
- Create threads from blog posts
- Turn customer wins into case studies
- Update SEO for existing content

**Community engagement:**
- Participate in relevant communities
- Answer questions in niche
- Share expertise without pitching

### Evening (30 min)

**Track marketing metrics:**
- Update LEDGER/FUNNEL_METRICS.csv
- Note what's working/not working
- Identify tomorrow's quick wins

**Thursday deliverables:**
- [ ] Content repurposed 2+ ways
- [ ] 3+ community engagements
- [ ] Promotion tasks completed
- [ ] Marketing metrics updated

---

## Friday: Analysis + Optimization

### Morning (2h)

**Weekly metrics review (90 min):**

Check and document in LEDGER/FUNNEL_METRICS.csv:
- Traffic (by source)
- Leads captured
- Email metrics (open, click, reply)
- Social growth and engagement
- Revenue and conversion rates

**Identify patterns (30 min):**
- What content performed best?
- What outreach got responses?
- What's not working?
- What should we do more/less of?

### Afternoon (1.5h)

**Optimization work (60 min):**
- A/B test one element (headline, CTA, email subject)
- Fix one conversion bottleneck
- Improve one underperforming asset

**Documentation (30 min):**
- Update SOPs with lessons learned
- Document what worked
- Archive failed experiments

### Evening (30 min)

**Week closure:**
- Complete weekly review template
- Celebrate wins
- Note lessons learned
- Light prep for next week

**Friday deliverables:**
- [ ] All metrics documented
- [ ] 1 optimization implemented
- [ ] Weekly review completed
- [ ] Next week's priorities identified

---

## Saturday: Rest (Optional Work)

**Primary goal:** Recover and recharge

**Optional bonus tasks (1-2h max):**
- Consume industry content (podcasts, newsletters)
- Light creative work if inspired
- Networking (coffee chats, DMs)
- Learn new skill

**Rules:**
- No urgent work
- No sales activities
- No metrics checking
- Phone stays away

---

## Sunday: Week Prep

**Planning session (1-2h):**

### Quick inbox clear (15 min)
- Archive non-essential emails
- Flag anything needing response Monday
- Clear Slack/DM backlog

### Review upcoming week (30 min)
- Check calendar for commitments
- Identify any conflicts
- Block deep work time

### Content prep (30 min)
- Gather content ideas from weekend consumption
- Prep Monday's batch session
- Stage any assets needed

### Goal setting (15 min)
- Set 3 outcomes for the week
- Identify potential obstacles
- Mental rehearsal of key activities

---

## Weekly Rhythm Adjustments

### Early Stage (Pre-Revenue)
- More time on content (build audience)
- More time on product (build offering)
- Less time on optimization (not enough data)

| Activity | Hours |
|----------|-------|
| Content | 12 |
| Product | 10 |
| Outreach | 5 |
| Analysis | 3 |

### Growth Stage ($1k-10k/month)
- More time on sales (convert audience)
- Balanced content (maintain momentum)
- More optimization (improve conversions)

| Activity | Hours |
|----------|-------|
| Content | 8 |
| Product | 8 |
| Outreach | 8 |
| Analysis | 6 |

### Scaling Stage ($10k+/month)
- More time on systems (prepare to delegate)
- Less daily content (more evergreen)
- More partnerships (leverage others' audiences)

| Activity | Hours |
|----------|-------|
| Content | 5 |
| Product | 6 |
| Sales | 6 |
| Systems | 8 |
| Analysis | 5 |

## Weekly Non-Negotiables

These happen every week, no matter what:

1. **Plan the week** (Sunday or Monday morning)
2. **Review metrics** (Friday)
3. **Batch content** (Monday)
4. **Deep product work** (Wednesday)
5. **At least 1 optimization** (Friday)
6. **At least 1 day of rest** (Saturday or Sunday)
