# Content Creation Checklist

**Use for:** Any content piece (social post, newsletter, blog, etc.)
**Time estimate:** Varies by type (see below)

---

## Pre-Creation (5-10 min)

### Define Purpose
- [ ] What's the goal? (traffic, leads, authority, engagement)
- [ ] Who's the audience? (specific person/segment)
- [ ] What action should they take after consuming?
- [ ] How does this fit into content strategy?

### Research
- [ ] Check what's worked before (top posts, emails)
- [ ] Look at competitor angles on topic
- [ ] Gather supporting data/examples
- [ ] Identify unique angle or insight

---

## Creation (Time varies)

### Social Post (15-30 min)

- [ ] Hook in first line (no filler)
- [ ] One clear idea per post
- [ ] Specific numbers over vague claims
- [ ] End with value, not desperate CTA
- [ ] Check for banned AI words (see copy-style.md)
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Would a real person post this?

### Newsletter/Email (45-90 min)

- [ ] Subject line = specific benefit (<50 chars)
- [ ] First line = hook (why keep reading)
- [ ] Clear structure (problem → solution → action)
- [ ] One main CTA
- [ ] PS line only if valuable secondary offer
- [ ] No "I hope this finds you well"
- [ ] Preview text set

### Long-form Blog (2-4 hours)

- [ ] Answer the question in first paragraph
- [ ] H2 subheadings for scanning
- [ ] Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- [ ] Specific examples and data
- [ ] Internal links to relevant content
- [ ] External links to sources
- [ ] Clear conclusion with next steps

### Lead Magnet Content (4-8 hours)

- [ ] Delivers on promise immediately
- [ ] Actionable, not just informational
- [ ] Professional formatting
- [ ] Clear branding
- [ ] CTA to next step in funnel

---

## Quality Check (10-15 min)

### Voice Check
- [ ] Zero banned AI vocabulary
- [ ] No em dashes (-- or -)
- [ ] No "It's not just X, it's Y" constructions
- [ ] Direct statements (single hedge max)
- [ ] Would PRINTMAXXER actually say this?
- [ ] First sentence delivers value

### Technical Check
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Links work
- [ ] Images load and look good
- [ ] Formatting displays correctly
- [ ] Mobile-friendly

### SEO Check (for blog/web content)
- [ ] Target keyword in title
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Meta description set (<155 chars)
- [ ] URL slug is clean and descriptive
- [ ] Alt text on images
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 other pages

### Compliance Check
- [ ] No unsubstantiated claims
- [ ] Affiliate disclosures present (if applicable)
- [ ] No fake testimonials
- [ ] Income claims have disclaimers (if applicable)

---

## Publishing (5 min)

### Pre-publish
- [ ] Preview in actual platform
- [ ] Scheduled for optimal time
- [ ] Tracking/UTMs added where relevant
- [ ] Cross-post or repurpose planned

### Post-publish
- [ ] Confirm it went live
- [ ] Engage with early comments
- [ ] Share to secondary channels if relevant
- [ ] Add to content calendar/tracker

---

## Content Types Time Guide

| Type | Creation | Editing | Total |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| X/Twitter post | 15 min | 10 min | 25 min |
| Thread (5-7 posts) | 45 min | 20 min | 65 min |
| Newsletter | 60 min | 30 min | 90 min |
| Blog post (1500 words) | 120 min | 45 min | 165 min |
| Lead magnet | 4-6 hours | 2 hours | 6-8 hours |
| Video script | 60 min | 30 min | 90 min |

---

## Quick Reference: PRINTMAXXER Voice

**Good content sounds like:**
- Texting a smart friend
- @levelsio sharing what worked
- Direct, specific, practical

**Good content does NOT sound like:**
- Marketing brochure
- AI assistant output
- Desperate salesperson

**Example transformation:**

BAD: "In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, leveraging cutting-edge automation tools has become essential for solopreneurs seeking to unlock unprecedented levels of productivity."

GOOD: "I automated my content posting. Took 3 hours to set up. Saves 5 hours per week."
