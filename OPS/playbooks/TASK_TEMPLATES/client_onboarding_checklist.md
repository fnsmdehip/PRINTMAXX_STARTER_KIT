# Client Onboarding Checklist

**Use for:** New customers, course enrollees, consulting clients
**Goal:** Fast time-to-value, reduce support burden, increase retention

---

## Phase 1: Immediate (First 24 hours)

### Automated Actions (happens instantly)

- [ ] Purchase confirmation email sent
- [ ] Access credentials delivered
- [ ] Welcome message triggered
- [ ] Added to customer segment in email tool
- [ ] Added to LEDGER/customers.csv
- [ ] Removed from sales sequences

### Personal Touch (within 24 hours)

- [ ] Personal welcome email or DM (if high-ticket)
- [ ] Quick-win resource sent
- [ ] Set expectations for what's next
- [ ] Provide primary support channel

### Access Verification

- [ ] Customer can log in / access product
- [ ] All files/modules accessible
- [ ] Download links work
- [ ] No technical barriers to getting started

---

## Phase 2: First Week (Days 1-7)

### Day 1-2: Quick Win

- [ ] Guide to first action (specific, achievable)
- [ ] Checklist or roadmap provided
- [ ] Common questions answered proactively
- [ ] Clear next step communicated

### Day 3-4: Check-in

- [ ] "How's it going?" email
- [ ] Offer support if stuck
- [ ] Share additional resource
- [ ] Ask for early feedback

### Day 5-7: Momentum

- [ ] Milestone reminder
- [ ] Success story or case study shared
- [ ] Community invitation (if applicable)
- [ ] Preview of what's coming

---

## Phase 3: First Month (Days 8-30)

### Week 2

- [ ] Check progress on main objective
- [ ] Address common sticking points
- [ ] Share advanced tip or resource
- [ ] Invite to office hours / Q&A (if offered)

### Week 3

- [ ] Mid-point check-in
- [ ] Gather feedback survey
- [ ] Identify any at-risk customers
- [ ] Offer additional support if needed

### Week 4

- [ ] Final "30-day" check-in
- [ ] Request testimonial (if satisfied)
- [ ] Introduce next step / upsell (if appropriate)
- [ ] Ensure they have long-term support path

---

## Onboarding Email Sequence

### Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)
**Subject:** You're in! Here's what's next
- Confirm purchase
- Access instructions
- First quick action
- Support contact

### Email 2: Quick Win (Day 1)
**Subject:** Start here for your first result
- Specific first step
- Why this first
- Time estimate
- Success story

### Email 3: Check-in (Day 3)
**Subject:** How's it going so far?
- Genuine question
- Common FAQ
- Offer help
- Reply encouraged

### Email 4: Tip (Day 5)
**Subject:** Quick tip that helped [result]
- Advanced tip
- Why it matters
- How to implement

### Email 5: Milestone (Day 7)
**Subject:** You're 1 week in - here's the path forward
- Celebrate progress
- What's next
- Resources
- Community mention

### Email 6: Feedback (Day 14)
**Subject:** Quick question about your experience
- Request feedback
- 1-2 specific questions
- Offer to improve
- Link to survey (optional)

### Email 7: Testimonial (Day 21-30)
**Subject:** Would you share your experience?
- Only if feedback was positive
- Make it easy (template or questions)
- Incentive if appropriate
- Thank them regardless

## Customer Segments

### Happy Path (majority)
- Complete onboarding smoothly
- Light touch needed
- Standard sequence

### At-Risk
- No engagement after 48 hours
- Reported issue
- Negative feedback

**Intervention:**
- Personal outreach
- Offer call or extra support
- Fast-track to help

### VIP / High-Touch
- High-ticket customers
- Referral sources
- Influencers

**Extra attention:**
- Personal welcome call
- Direct access to you
- Extra resources

## Customer Communication Templates

### Welcome Message

```
Welcome to [Product]!

You now have access to [what they got].

**Your first step:**
[Specific action] - takes about [time].

If you run into any issues, reply to this email or contact [support].

Talk soon,
[Name]
```

### Check-in Message

```
Hey [Name],

Checking in - how's [Product] going for you?

If you're stuck anywhere or have questions, just reply to this email.

[Optional: Share a tip or resource]

[Name]
```

### Testimonial Request

```
Hey [Name],

Quick favor - would you be open to sharing your experience with [Product]?

If yes, just answer these 2 questions:
1. What result did you get?
2. What surprised you most?

No pressure either way. Thanks for being a customer.

[Name]
```

---

## Red Flags to Watch

**Customer at risk if:**
- No login within 72 hours
- No progress after 1 week
- Negative feedback received
- Multiple support tickets
- Radio silence after asking question

**Action:** Personal outreach immediately. Offer help or refund.
