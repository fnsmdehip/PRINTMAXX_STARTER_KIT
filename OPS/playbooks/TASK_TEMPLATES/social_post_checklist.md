# Social Post Checklist

**Use for:** X/Twitter posts, threads, LinkedIn, Threads
**Time estimate:** 15-30 minutes per post

---

## Pre-Creation (5 min)

### Define Intent
- [ ] Goal: Engagement / Traffic / Leads / Authority / Entertainment
- [ ] Target audience for this specific post
- [ ] What should they do after reading?
- [ ] How does this fit content calendar?

### Source Material
- [ ] Original insight or experience
- [ ] Data point or statistic
- [ ] Customer story or testimonial
- [ ] Observation from work
- [ ] Response to trending topic

---

## Writing (10-15 min)

### Single Post

**Hook (first line):**
- [ ] Grabs attention immediately
- [ ] No filler words
- [ ] Sets up the rest
- [ ] Stops the scroll

**Body:**
- [ ] One clear idea
- [ ] Specific numbers or examples
- [ ] Short sentences
- [ ] Natural line breaks

**Ending:**
- [ ] Value, not desperate CTA
- [ ] Soft CTA if any (profile link, reply prompt)
- [ ] No "Like if you agree"

### Thread (5-10 posts)

- [ ] Hook in post 1 (standalone value)
- [ ] Each post works independently
- [ ] Clear progression
- [ ] Numbers or emoji markers (1/, 2/ or similar)
- [ ] Last post = summary + CTA
- [ ] Under 10 posts ideally

---

## Quality Check (5 min)

### Voice Check
- [ ] Zero banned AI words
- [ ] No em dashes (use comma or period)
- [ ] No "It's not just X, it's Y"
- [ ] Sounds like real person
- [ ] Would @levelsio post this?

### Content Check
- [ ] Specific > vague
- [ ] Numbers where possible
- [ ] No promotional adjectives
- [ ] Value before ask
- [ ] Not just rehashing common advice

### Platform Check

**X/Twitter:**
- [ ] Under 280 characters (or properly formatted thread)
- [ ] No external links in first reply (kills reach)
- [ ] Images properly sized if included
- [ ] Alt text on images

**LinkedIn:**
- [ ] Line breaks for readability
- [ ] More professional tone
- [ ] Longer form acceptable
- [ ] Links okay in post

**Threads:**
- [ ] Casual tone
- [ ] Shorter format
- [ ] Less hashtag dependent

---

## Post Types

### Insight Post
Share something you learned or observed.
- What I learned from [X]
- The mistake I see [audience] making
- [Number] lessons from [experience]

### Story Post
Tell a brief narrative.
- Background (1-2 sentences)
- Challenge (what happened)
- Resolution (what you did)
- Lesson (takeaway)

### How-To Post
Teach something specific.
- Clear outcome
- Numbered steps
- Each step actionable
- Result at end

### List Post
Curated items with context.
- [Number] [things] for [audience]
- Brief explanation per item
- Specific, not generic

### Engagement Post
Ask for interaction.
- Genuine question
- Easy to answer
- Relevant to audience
- Not desperate

### Contrarian Post
Challenge common belief.
- State popular opinion
- Your different take
- Evidence/reasoning
- Invitation to discuss

---

## Scheduling

### Best Times (X/Twitter)
- **Weekdays:** 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm (audience timezone)
- **Weekends:** Lower engagement, skip or casual content
- **Test:** Track your own best times over 30 days

### Frequency
- **Minimum:** 1 post/day
- **Optimal:** 2-3 posts/day
- **Max:** 5-7 posts/day (risk overexposure)

### Scheduling Tools
- Buffer
- Typefully
- Tweet Hunter
- Native scheduling

---

## Post-Publish (5 min)

### First Hour
- [ ] Confirm post went live
- [ ] Reply to early comments
- [ ] Engage on 5-10 other posts (reciprocity)
- [ ] Don't check obsessively

### 24 Hours Later
- [ ] Check engagement metrics
- [ ] Note what worked/didn't
- [ ] Save high performers for repurpose

---

## Metrics to Track

| Post Type | Good Engagement | Great Engagement |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Single post | >2% engagement | >5% engagement |
| Thread | >1% engagement | >3% engagement |
| Image post | >3% engagement | >6% engagement |

**Engagement rate = (likes + replies + retweets) / impressions**

## Quick Copy Fixes

| Instead of | Write |
|------------|-------|
| I think that maybe | [Just state it] |
| It's important to note | [Delete and continue] |
| In my experience | I [verb] |
| Basically | [Delete] |
| Actually | [Delete] |
| Really | [Delete] |
| Very | [Delete or replace with specific] |
| A lot | [Specific number] |
| Things | [Specific nouns] |
