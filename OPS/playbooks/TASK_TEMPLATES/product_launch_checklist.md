# Product Launch Checklist

**Use for:** Digital products, courses, tools, templates
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks before launch

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)

### Product Readiness

- [ ] Product complete and tested
- [ ] All content/modules finalized
- [ ] Delivery mechanism tested (download, access, etc.)
- [ ] Payment flow tested (Stripe checkout works)
- [ ] Confirmation emails set up and tested
- [ ] Support documentation ready
- [ ] FAQ prepared
- [ ] Refund policy clear

### Landing Page

- [ ] Headline = main promise (<10 words)
- [ ] Subhead = who it's for + what they get
- [ ] Benefits section with specifics
- [ ] Product preview/screenshots
- [ ] Pricing clearly displayed
- [ ] Social proof (if available)
- [ ] FAQ section
- [ ] CTA buttons prominent
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] Page speed optimized

### Pricing Strategy

- [ ] Launch price decided
- [ ] Regular price decided (if different)
- [ ] Launch discount percentage
- [ ] Bundle options (if any)
- [ ] Guarantee/refund terms
- [ ] Payment plans (if offering)

---

## Phase 2: Launch Prep (1 week before)

### Email Sequence

- [ ] Announcement email drafted
- [ ] Launch email (day of) drafted
- [ ] Reminder email (day 2-3) drafted
- [ ] Last chance email drafted
- [ ] All emails scheduled
- [ ] Subject lines A/B tested (if possible)
- [ ] Links all work
- [ ] Unsubscribe works

### Social Content

- [ ] Launch day post(s) drafted
- [ ] 3-5 supporting posts drafted
- [ ] Any graphics/images created
- [ ] Hashtags researched (if applicable)
- [ ] Thread or carousel version prepped
- [ ] All content scheduled

### Logistics

- [ ] Launch date/time confirmed
- [ ] Timezone considerations handled
- [ ] Clear calendar on launch day
- [ ] Support system ready (email, DM monitoring)
- [ ] Backup plan if tech fails

---

## Phase 3: Launch Day

### Morning (before launch)

- [ ] Final test of purchase flow
- [ ] Check all pages load correctly
- [ ] Verify email sequences active
- [ ] Clear schedule for monitoring

### Launch

- [ ] Publish launch post
- [ ] Send launch email
- [ ] Update website CTA
- [ ] Monitor for issues

### Throughout Day

- [ ] Respond to questions within 1 hour
- [ ] Post updates/reminders
- [ ] Thank early buyers publicly
- [ ] Monitor for technical issues
- [ ] Track sales in real-time

### End of Day

- [ ] Tally results
- [ ] Thank audience
- [ ] Identify any issues to fix
- [ ] Plan next day's content

---

## Phase 4: Launch Week

### Days 2-3

- [ ] Send reminder email
- [ ] Share customer wins/testimonials
- [ ] Answer common questions publicly
- [ ] Continue engagement
- [ ] Monitor conversion rate

### Days 4-6

- [ ] Mid-launch content push
- [ ] Address objections in content
- [ ] Personal outreach to warm leads
- [ ] Case studies if possible
- [ ] Deadline reminder

### Day 7 (Final Day)

- [ ] "Last chance" email
- [ ] Deadline reminder posts
- [ ] Final hours countdown
- [ ] Close cart (if limited launch)
- [ ] Thank everyone

---

## Phase 5: Post-Launch (Week 2)

### Analysis

- [ ] Calculate total revenue
- [ ] Count total customers
- [ ] Calculate conversion rate
- [ ] Identify top traffic sources
- [ ] Review customer feedback
- [ ] Document what worked
- [ ] Document what didn't

### Follow-up

- [ ] Welcome sequence for new customers
- [ ] Check-in email after 7 days
- [ ] Request testimonials
- [ ] Address any support issues
- [ ] Update product if needed

### Documentation

- [ ] Write launch post-mortem
- [ ] Update LEDGER with results
- [ ] Archive launch assets
- [ ] Update SOPs with lessons

## Launch Timeline Template

### 4 Weeks Before
- Product complete
- Landing page built
- Pricing set

- Email sequence drafted
- Social content planned
- Early access list building

### 2 Weeks Before
- All content finalized
- Tech fully tested
- Preview given to select people

### 1 Week Before
- Teaser content live
- All assets scheduled
- Final walkthrough

### Launch Day
- Execute launch
- Monitor and engage
- Celebrate wins

### Week After
- Complete launch sequence
- Gather feedback
- Plan improvements

---

## Common Launch Mistakes

1. **Launching to cold audience** - Build anticipation first
2. **Too many options** - One product, one price
3. **Weak deadline** - Real scarcity or none
4. **No social proof** - Get beta users first
5. **Ignoring time zones** - Launch for your main audience
6. **No follow-up sequence** - Sales happen in days 2-7
7. **Going dark after launch** - Keep engaging
8. **No post-mortem** - Miss lessons for next time
