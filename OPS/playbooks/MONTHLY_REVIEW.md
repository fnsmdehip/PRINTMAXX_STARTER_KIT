## Part 2: Questions to Answer (30 min)

### Growth Questions

- What drove the most leads this month?
- What content performed best and why?
- What's the biggest bottleneck in my funnel?
- Where are people dropping off?
- What did customers say they wanted?

### Operations Questions

- Did I stick to my daily/weekly routine?
- What took longer than expected?
- What can I eliminate or automate?
- Where am I spending time but not getting results?
- What should I delegate?

### Product Questions

- What feature requests came in?
- What complaints or support issues arose?
- Is the product-market fit getting stronger or weaker?
- What competitor moves happened?
- What opportunities did I miss?

### Financial Questions

- Am I on track for quarterly revenue goal?
- What's my runway at current burn rate?
- Which products are most profitable (time-adjusted)?
- Where can I cut costs without hurting growth?
- Should I raise prices?

### Personal Questions

- Am I energized or burned out?
- What work did I enjoy most?
- What work did I dread?
- Am I building something I still believe in?
- What would I tell myself from 30 days ago?
