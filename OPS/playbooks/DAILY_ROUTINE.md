# PRINTMAXX Daily Routine

**Total time commitment:** 4-6 hours/day
**Goal:** Consistent execution that compounds over time

---

## Morning Block (1-2 hours)

### 6:00-6:30 AM - Metrics Check (30 min)

**Dashboard review:**
- [ ] Check overnight lead captures (LEDGER/leads.csv)
- [ ] Review traffic numbers (Google Analytics)
- [ ] Check email stats (open rates, clicks, replies)
- [ ] Scan social notifications for important mentions

**Action items:**
- Respond to any urgent DMs (customers, collaboration requests)
- Note any anomalies for deeper investigation later
- Update LEDGER/FUNNEL_METRICS.csv if needed

### 6:30-7:30 AM - Content Creation (60 min)

**Priority order:**
1. Daily social post (X/Twitter) - 15 min
2. Newsletter section or email draft - 30 min
3. Content idea capture in CONTENT/ideas.md - 15 min

**Content creation rules:**
- Write in PRINTMAXXER voice (direct, specific, practical)
- No editing during creation time
- Batch similar content together
- Use templates from TASK_TEMPLATES/

### 7:30-8:00 AM - Engagement (30 min)

**Platform-specific tasks:**

**X/Twitter:**
- Reply to 10 relevant posts in niche
- Engage with 5 larger accounts you want to connect with
- Answer any questions on your posts
- Quote tweet 1 valuable post with your take

**LinkedIn (if applicable):**
- Comment on 5 industry posts
- Reply to comments on your content
- Accept connection requests with note

**Rule:** Give value first. No pitch in engagement comments.

---

## Afternoon Block (2-3 hours)

### 1:00-3:00 PM - Deep Work (120 min)

**Rotate daily focus:**

| Day | Deep Work Focus | Output |
|-----|-----------------|--------|
| Monday | Content batch | 5-7 social posts drafted |
| Tuesday | Product development | 1 feature shipped or tested |
| Wednesday | Longtail pages | 3-5 pages published |
| Thursday | Lead magnet work | Section completed or tested |
| Friday | Optimization | 1 funnel improved |

**Deep work rules:**
- Phone on airplane mode
- Close email and social tabs
- Single task only
- Use 45/15 pomodoro splits

### 3:00-4:00 PM - Outreach/Sales (60 min)

**Cold outreach:**
- Send 5-10 personalized outreach messages
- Follow up on outstanding conversations
- Track in LEDGER/outreach_tracker.csv

**Warm outreach:**
- Reply to inbound inquiries within 24 hours
- Send value to existing contacts (share relevant content)
- Nurture relationships without asking for anything

**Sales calls (if scheduled):**
- 15 min discovery
- 20 min demo/pitch
- 10 min next steps
- Update pipeline immediately after

---

## Evening Block (1 hour)

### 8:00-8:30 PM - Schedule Tomorrow's Content (30 min)

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Schedule 1 social post for tomorrow morning
- [ ] Schedule email if part of sequence
- [ ] Prep any images/assets needed
- [ ] Double-check links and tracking

**Scheduling tools:**
- Buffer/Typefully for social
- ConvertKit/Beehiiv for email
- Notion/Sheets for content calendar

### 8:30-9:00 PM - Review and Plan (30 min)

**Daily review:**
- [ ] What worked today?
- [ ] What didn't work?
- [ ] What will I do differently tomorrow?

**Next day planning:**
- [ ] Identify top 3 priorities for tomorrow
- [ ] Block time for deep work
- [ ] Check calendar for calls/meetings
- [ ] Update LEDGER/MASTER_TASKS.md

**Shutdown ritual:**
- Close all work tabs
- Write tomorrow's top priority on paper
- Physical separation from workspace

- Early stage (pre-revenue): More content, less sales
- Growth stage: More sales, less content

## Daily Non-Negotiables

These happen every single day, no exceptions:

1. **Post 1 piece of content** - Even if it's a reply thread
2. **Engage for 30 minutes** - Build relationships
3. **Check leads.csv** - Know who's coming in
4. **Plan tomorrow** - Never start a day without a plan
5. **One deep work block** - Minimum 45 minutes uninterrupted

---

## Energy Management

**High energy tasks (morning):**
- Content creation
- Important decisions
- Complex problem solving

**Medium energy tasks (afternoon):**
- Meetings and calls
- Outreach
- Product work

**Low energy tasks (evening):**
- Scheduling
- Planning
- Admin

**Never:**
- Create content when tired
- Make pricing decisions end of day
- Send outreach after 6 PM

---

## Interruption Protocol

**If something urgent comes up:**

1. Is it truly urgent? (Revenue impact > $500 or reputation risk)
2. If yes: Handle it, document impact on deep work
3. If no: Add to tomorrow's list, return to current task

**Acceptable interruptions:**
- Customer payment issues
- Site/service down
- Collaboration with deadline today

**Not acceptable interruptions:**
- Social media notifications
- Non-urgent emails
- "Quick question" from anyone
