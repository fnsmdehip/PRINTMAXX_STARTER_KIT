## 1. FACEBOOK REELS: The $4.40 Myth

### What we claimed
FB Reels pays $4.40/1K views, representing a 4-440x arbitrage over TikTok/YouTube Shorts.

### What research found
The $4.40 figure comes from a specific creator with optimized US-only audience. Real-world data:

**RPM by creator type:**
- Bottom 50% of creators: $0.02-$0.08/1K views
- Average creator: $0.15-$0.60/1K views
- Optimized US-audience niche creator: $1-$4/1K views
- Advertiser CPM for US viewers: $20.48 (but creator payout is fraction of this)

**Key structural changes (2025-2026):**
- Content Monetization Program (CMP) replaced all previous programs Aug 31, 2025
- ALL new video uploads are Reels format (no separation)
- Unified earnings dashboard for Reels, Stories, photos, text, long-form
- Auto-translation feature opens international monetization (2026)

**Comparison to other platforms:**
| Platform | Creator RPM | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| FB Reels (CMP) | $0.02-$4/1K | Highly variable, US audience premium |
| TikTok Rewards | $0.40-$6/1K | 1+ minute videos only, niche dependent |
| YouTube Shorts | $0.01-$0.06/1K | Lowest paying short-form platform |
| X Revenue Share | $0.07/1K | Based on Levelsio data ($8.3K on 120M views) |

**Verdict:** The arbitrage is REAL but smaller than claimed. FB Reels pays 2-10x YouTube Shorts (not 440x). TikTok Rewards actually pays comparably or better in some niches. The strategy should be CROSS-POST EVERYWHERE (zero cost) rather than "FB Reels is the profit center."
