# Swarm Promotion Playbook

**Purpose:** Coordinate multiple accounts across platforms to create organic-appearing viral momentum funneling to single monetization point (Roblox game, app, info product).

---

## The Concept

10-20 accounts working together:
- AI influencer creates persona content
- Clip accounts share and react
- Meme accounts spread format
- Gaming personas engage community
- All funnel to monetization point

one piece of content becomes 20+ touchpoints. creates appearance of organic virality.

---

## Account Types in the Swarm

| Account Type | Platform | Role | Content |
|--------------|----------|------|---------|
| AI Influencer | TikTok, IG | Main persona | Original content, hooks |
| Clip Account | YouTube Shorts, TikTok | Amplifier | Clips + reactions |
| Meme Account | X, Instagram | Spreader | Format remixes |
| Gaming Persona | Roblox, Discord | Community | Game-specific content |
| Commentary | X, TikTok | Authority | Analysis, explanation |

## Implementation Steps

### Phase 1: Account Infrastructure (Week 1-2)

**Per platform, create:**
- 3-5 accounts with distinct personas
- Different posting styles
- Slightly different niches (adjacent, not identical)
- Warm up properly (see ACCOUNT_WARMING_SOP.md)

**Account Identity Checklist:**
- [ ] Unique profile photo (AI generated, different styles)
- [ ] Different bio formats
- [ ] Varied posting times
- [ ] Different device fingerprints
- [ ] Separate proxies (mobile for IG/TikTok)

### Phase 2: Content Cascade (Week 3-4)

**Day 1: Original Drop**
1. AI influencer posts original video/content
2. Wait 2-4 hours for initial engagement

**Day 1-2: First Amplification**
1. Clip account posts reaction/clip
2. Links/mentions original
3. Meme account creates format remix
4. Gaming persona posts related content

**Day 2-3: Second Wave**
1. Commentary account explains why it's interesting
2. More meme remixes
3. Cross-platform shares

**Day 3-5: Sustained**
1. Continue reactions and remixes
2. Community accounts engage
3. All CTAs point to monetization

### Phase 3: Funnel Optimization (Ongoing)

Track which accounts drive most conversions.
Double down on high performers.
Kill or pivot low performers.

## Anti-Detection

### Account Separation
- Different devices per account (or VM/emulator)
- Different IPs (mobile proxies for IG/TikTok)
- No direct follows between swarm accounts
- No same-device logins

### Behavior Variation
- Different posting times
- Different engagement patterns
- Not all accounts active same days
- Natural growth curves (don't all spike together)

### Content Variation
- No copy-paste captions
- Different hashtag sets
- Varied video lengths
- Slightly different hooks

### Red Flags to Avoid
- All accounts posting at exact same time
- Identical captions/hashtags
- Obvious coordination in comments
- Same location/device data
- Accounts only engaging with each other

## Example: Roblox Game Launch

**Goal:** Drive 10,000 players to new Roblox game in first week

**Swarm:**
- 2 AI influencer accounts (TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
- 3 clip accounts (TikTok, YouTube)
- 4 meme accounts (X, Instagram)
- 2 gaming personas (TikTok, YouTube)
- 1 commentary account (X)

**Timeline:**

**Day -7 to -1: Warmup**
- Tease game concept across accounts
- Build anticipation
- "Something big coming"

**Day 0: Launch**
- AI influencer posts main reveal video
- All accounts begin cascade
- Discord/Roblox community activated

**Day 1-3: Amplification**
- Clips, memes, reactions
- Respond to organic engagement
- Double down on what's hitting

**Day 4-7: Sustained**
- User-generated content if any
- Second wave of original content
- Community events in game

**Expected Results:**
- 500k-2M total views across swarm
- 50k-100k clicks to Roblox
- 10k-20k game visits
- 2k-5k return players

## Legal/Compliance Notes

**Disclosure:**
- If accounts are paid promoters, disclose
- "Sponsored" or "Ad" where required
- FTC guidelines apply

**Platform TOS:**
- Multi-accounting violates most TOS
- Risk of ban exists
- Mitigation: proper separation, unique content

**Risk Tolerance:**
- High risk strategy
- Accounts can be banned
- Have backup accounts ready
- Don't put all eggs in one basket
