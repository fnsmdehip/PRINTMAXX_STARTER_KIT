## TOP 10 ACTION ITEMS (Priority Order)

### 1. CRITICAL: Apple Age Ratings (PAST DUE - Jan 31, 2026)
- **What:** Complete updated age rating questionnaire in App Store Connect for ALL apps
- **Why:** Cannot submit ANY app updates until completed
- **How:** Log into App Store Connect > each app > App Information > Age Rating > answer new questions about AI features, chatbots, medical content, violence
- **Time:** 15-30 min per app

### 2. CRITICAL: Google Play External Links (PAST DUE - Jan 28, 2026)
- **What:** Enroll in External Content Links program if apps link to external content
- **Why:** Apps with external links that are not enrolled may be removed
- **How:** Google Play Console > Policy > ECL Program enrollment. Must provide refund process, customer support for external transactions, clear destination disclosure
- **Time:** 1-2 hours

### 3. HIGH: Email Authentication Setup
- **What:** Configure SPF + DKIM + DMARC on ALL sending domains
- **Why:** Gmail (Nov 2025), Yahoo, and Microsoft (May 2025) permanently REJECT unauthenticated email. No retry. No junk folder. Bounced.
- **How:** DNS records for each domain. SPF TXT record listing authorized senders. DKIM key pair. DMARC record with at least p=none. Use Google Postmaster Tools + Microsoft SNDS for monitoring
- **Time:** 2-4 hours for all domains

### 4. HIGH: FTC Affiliate Disclosure Audit
- **What:** Review ALL content (social posts, emails, landing pages, app content) for undisclosed affiliate/sponsor relationships
- **Why:** $51,744 per violation. 150+ FTC enforcement actions in 2025. Disclosure must be unavoidable (not behind a click)
- **How:** Add "#ad" or "affiliate link" disclosure to every piece of content with a material connection. Must be visible without clicking. Must appear in same language as content
- **Time:** 2-3 hours for full audit

### 5. HIGH: AI Persona Disclosure Labels
- **What:** Add "AI-generated" labels to ALL AI persona profiles and content
- **Why:** FTC treats AI endorsers same as human endorsers ($53,088/violation). NY law effective June 9 ($1K-$5K/violation). TikTok MANDATORY AI labeling
- **How:** Profile bios: "AI-generated persona." Posts: "#AIgenerated" or equivalent. Ads in NY: conspicuous synthetic performer disclosure. TikTok: use built-in AI content label
- **Time:** 1-2 hours

### 6. HIGH: Gmail Spam Rate Monitoring
- **What:** Set up real-time spam complaint monitoring, target under 0.1%
- **Why:** 0.3% = permanent delivery failure. Since Nov 2025, Gmail issues permanent 5xx rejections (no retry) for non-compliant senders
- **How:** Google Postmaster Tools. Monitor daily. If approaching 0.2%, immediately pause campaigns and clean lists
- **Time:** 30 min setup, 5 min daily check

### 7. HIGH: TikTok Shop Logistics Migration (Due Feb 25 - Mar 31)
- **What:** Move all fulfillment to TikTok Shop Logistics Services
- **Why:** Seller Shipping being phased out. Cannot fulfill orders with own shipping after transition
- **How:** TikTok Seller Center > Shipping > Enroll in TikTok Shop Logistics
- **Time:** 1-2 hours

### 8. MEDIUM: NY Synthetic Performer Law Prep (Due Jun 9, 2026)
- **What:** Build disclosure system for AI-generated human images in advertisements distributed in New York
- **Why:** $1,000 first violation, $5,000 subsequent. First state law of its kind
- **How:** Any ad with a synthetic performer (AI-generated person image) needs conspicuous disclosure. Does NOT need specific language, just "prominent, unavoidable, noticeable." Exempt: audio-only ads, AI translation, expressive works
- **Who it affects:** AI_INFLUENCER personas in ads, AI UGC in paid campaigns, any Meta/TikTok/Google ads with AI-generated people shown to NY users
- **Time:** 2-4 hours to build disclosure templates and implement

### 9. MEDIUM: EU AI Act Transparency Prep (Due Aug 2, 2026)
- **What:** Build labeling/disclosure system for AI content visible to EU users
- **Why:** Penalties up to EUR 35M or 7% of global annual turnover
- **How:** (1) Label all AI-generated content (2) Disclose AI interactions (3) Mark synthetic/deepfake content (4) Transparency documentation. SMEs get reduced fines
- **Time:** Ongoing, start now, 6 months to full compliance

### 10. MEDIUM: France B2C Email Consent (Due Aug 2026)
- **What:** Segment French B2C contacts out of cold email campaigns OR obtain explicit consent
- **Why:** GDPR penalties: EUR 20M or 4% global turnover
- **How:** Geo-filter email lists. Remove French B2C addresses from cold lists. B2B legitimate interest may still apply
- **Time:** 1-2 hours

## EMAIL DELIVERABILITY DEEP DIVE

### The Current State (Feb 2026)

All four major mailbox providers (Gmail, Yahoo, Microsoft Outlook, Apple Mail = ~90% of B2C inboxes) now enforce:

| Provider | SPF | DKIM | DMARC | One-Click Unsub | Spam Rate | Enforcement Level |
|----------|-----|------|-------|-----------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Gmail | Required | Required | Required (p=none min) | Required (marketing) | <0.3% hard, <0.1% target | **Permanent 5xx rejection** (Nov 2025) |
| Yahoo | Required | Required | Required (p=none min) | Required (marketing) | <0.3% | Junk folder then rejection |
| Microsoft | Required | Required | Required (p=none min) | Recommended | Not specified | **Permanent 5xx rejection** (May 2025) |
| Apple | Recommended | Recommended | Recommended | Recommended | Not specified | Filtering |

### Cold Email Specific Risks

1. **Volume triggers:** Sending 5,000+/day to any single provider = you are a "bulk sender" with stricter rules
2. **Warmup is mandatory:** New domains need 2-4 weeks of gradually increasing volume
3. **Domain reputation is domain-level:** One bad campaign can poison all future sends from that domain
4. **Provider penalties are faster than legal penalties:** Gmail will block you in days. FTC takes months. But both hurt
5. **Pre-warmed inboxes (DeliverOn/EmailBison) skip warmup** but still require proper authentication

### Cold Email Legal Compliance Checklist

- [ ] SPF record configured for all sending domains
- [ ] DKIM signing enabled for all outbound email
- [ ] DMARC record published (minimum p=none)
- [ ] Physical mailing address in every email
- [ ] Accurate From/Reply-To fields
- [ ] Honest subject lines (no deception)
- [ ] Working one-click unsubscribe
- [ ] Opt-outs honored within 10 business days (CAN-SPAM) / 24-48 hours (GDPR)
- [ ] No EU B2C cold email without explicit consent
- [ ] No French B2C cold email without consent (from Aug 2026)
- [ ] Spam complaint rate monitored daily (target <0.1%)
- [ ] List hygiene: remove bounces, complaints, unsubscribes immediately
- [ ] Document legitimate interest basis for all EU B2B outreach
