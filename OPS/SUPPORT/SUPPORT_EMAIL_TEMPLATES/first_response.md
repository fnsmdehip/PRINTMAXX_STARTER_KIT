# First response email templates

Templates for initial replies to support tickets. Personalize bracketed items.

---

## Simple question - Immediate answer

**Use when:** You can answer completely in first response.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

[Direct answer to their question]

[One additional tip or resource if helpful]

- [AGENT]

---

## Investigation needed

**Use when:** You need to look into their account or reproduce an issue.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Looking into this now.

Quick question: [Specific question that helps you troubleshoot faster]

I'll have an update within [TIMEFRAME].

- [AGENT]

---

## Known issue acknowledgment

**Use when:** Issue is already known and being worked on.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

This is a known issue. Engineering is on it.

Workaround for now: [Steps if any]

I'll email you when the fix is live. Probably [TIMEFRAME].

- [AGENT]

## More info needed

**Use when:** Can't proceed without additional details.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

1. [Specific info needed]
2. [Another piece of info]
3. [Screenshot/video if helpful]

## Frustrated customer - First response

**Use when:** Customer is clearly upset. Prioritize empathy and speed.

---

Hi [NAME],

That's frustrating. Sorry you're dealing with this.

I'm looking into it right now and will have an answer within [SHORT TIMEFRAME - be aggressive].

## Feature request received

**Use when:** Customer suggests a feature.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Good idea. Added to our feature backlog.

We prioritize by demand, so no timeline yet. If this becomes urgent for your workflow, reply with more context about your use case. That helps us prioritize.

Anything else I can help with?

- [AGENT]

## Bug report received

**Use when:** Customer reports a bug you haven't seen before.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Thanks for the detailed report. I'm trying to reproduce this on my end.

While I investigate:
- Can you confirm your device/browser version?
- Does it happen every time or intermittently?
- Any error messages, even briefly?

Screenshots or a quick screen recording help a lot if you have time.

- [AGENT]
