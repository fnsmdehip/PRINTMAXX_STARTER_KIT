# Resolution follow-up email templates

Templates for closing tickets and following up on resolved issues.

---

## Issue resolved - Simple

**Use when:** Straightforward issue, resolved quickly.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Fixed. [One sentence explaining what was done]

You should be good now. Let me know if anything else comes up.

- [AGENT]

---

## Issue resolved - Required multiple steps

**Use when:** Complex issue that took back-and-forth to resolve.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Good news - this should be working now.

Here's what we did:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

If you see the problem again, the fix is [quick reference for self-service].

Thanks for your patience while we sorted this out.

- [AGENT]

---

## Bug fix deployed

**Use when:** Engineering fixed a bug customer reported.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

The fix is live. Update to version [X.X.X] and you should be good.

## Workaround provided - Permanent fix coming

**Use when:** Gave workaround, real fix is in progress.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Wanted to update you - the permanent fix is still in progress. ETA is [TIMEFRAME].

Your workaround should keep working until then. I'll email you when the fix ships.

- [AGENT]

**Use when:** Confirming refund was issued.

Hi [NAME],

Refund processed. $[AMOUNT] back to your [PAYMENT METHOD] ending in [LAST 4].

Your subscription is cancelled. You keep access until [END DATE].

## Account issue resolved

**Use when:** Fixed account-level problem (access, permissions, etc.).

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Your account is fixed. Try logging in now.

What happened: [Brief non-technical explanation]

You shouldn't see this again, but if you do, [preventive tip or contact info].

- [AGENT]

## Closing ticket - Customer went silent

**Use when:** Awaiting customer response for 3+ days, no reply.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Following up on this. Did the issue get resolved, or do you still need help?

If I don't hear back, I'll close this ticket in a couple days. You can always reply to reopen it.

- [AGENT]

---

## Final close - No response

**Use when:** Closing after follow-up with no customer reply.

---

Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [NAME],

Closing this ticket since I haven't heard back.

If you still need help, just reply to this email and we'll pick up where we left off.

- [AGENT]

---
