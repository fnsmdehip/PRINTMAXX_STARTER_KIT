# Feedback request email templates

Templates for proactively requesting product feedback and reviews.

---

## General product feedback

**Use when:** Requesting feedback from active users (not tied to support).

---

Subject: Quick question

Hi [NAME],

You've been using [PRODUCT] for [X WEEKS/MONTHS]. I'm curious:

What's one thing that annoys you about it?

Hit reply with whatever comes to mind. No wrong answers.

- [FOUNDER/PM NAME]

---

## Feature-specific feedback

**Use when:** Want feedback on a specific feature after they've used it.

---

Subject: How's [FEATURE] working for you?

Hi [NAME],

I noticed you've been using [FEATURE] since we launched it.

Two questions:
1. What's working well?
2. What's frustrating or missing?

Your feedback shapes what we build next.

- [NAME]

---

## New user check-in

**Use when:** User signed up recently, checking if they're getting value.

---

Subject: How's it going?

Hi [NAME],

You signed up [X] days ago. Just checking in.

Are you finding what you need? Stuck on anything?

Reply with any questions. I read every response.

- [NAME]

---

## Churned user feedback

**Use when:** User cancelled. Want to understand why.

---

Subject: Quick question before you go

Hi [NAME],

I saw you cancelled. Totally respect that.

If you have 30 seconds: What made you leave?

Not trying to win you back. Just want to understand what we could've done better.

- [NAME]

## Beta feedback request

**Use when:** User is testing beta feature, want structured feedback.

---

Subject: Beta feedback: [FEATURE NAME]

Hi [NAME],

Thanks for testing [FEATURE]. Here's what I'd love to know:

1. Does it do what you expected?
2. What's confusing or unclear?
3. Would you use this regularly? Why or why not?

Reply with whatever format works for you - bullet points, stream of consciousness, whatever.

Bugs go here: [BUG REPORT LINK]

- [NAME]

## Implementation notes

**Timing guidelines:**
- New user check-in: Day 7 after signup
- Feature feedback: 2 weeks after feature launch
- Review requests: After positive support interaction or milestone
- Churned feedback: Within 24 hours of cancellation

**Best practices:**
- Keep emails short (under 100 words ideal)
- Ask one or two questions max
- Make it easy to reply (no forms, just email)
- Personal sender name, not "noreply@"
- Follow up once if no response, then stop

**Don't send feedback requests to:**
- Users with open support tickets
- Users who recently complained
- Users who opted out of marketing
- Same user more than once per month
