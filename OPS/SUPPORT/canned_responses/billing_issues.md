# Billing issues - Canned responses

---

## 1. Charge not recognized

**Use when:** Customer doesn't recognize a charge on their statement.

---

Hi [NAME],

That charge is from your [PRODUCT] subscription. It shows as "[DESCRIPTOR]" on bank statements.

Your account email is [EMAIL] and you signed up on [DATE].

If this wasn't you, reply and I'll refund it immediately.

- [AGENT]

---

## 2. Subscription not showing after payment

**Use when:** Payment went through but account still shows free/inactive.

---

Hi [NAME],

Fixed. Your subscription is now active. Sometimes there's a sync delay between Stripe and our system.

If you still see "Free" after logging out and back in, send me a screenshot and I'll dig deeper.

- [AGENT]

---

## 3. Refund request - Approved

**Use when:** Refund meets automatic approval criteria.

---

Hi [NAME],

Done. Refunded $[AMOUNT] to your [CARD TYPE] ending in [LAST 4]. Takes 5-10 business days to show up.

Your subscription is now cancelled. You'll have access until [END DATE].

- [AGENT]

---

## 4. Refund request - Denied

**Use when:** Refund doesn't meet criteria and no exception warranted.

---

Hi [NAME],

Can't do a refund here. Your purchase was [X] days ago and outside our 7-day window.

Two options:
1. I can give you [X] months credit toward future use
2. I can downgrade you to our free plan and pause billing

Which works better for you?

- [AGENT]

---

## 5. Failed payment - First notice

**Use when:** Payment failed, first attempt.

---

Hi [NAME],

Heads up: your payment didn't go through. This happens sometimes with card updates or bank holds.

You have 7 days to update your payment method before your access pauses. Update here: [BILLING LINK]

If you meant to cancel, just reply and I'll handle it.

- [AGENT]

---
