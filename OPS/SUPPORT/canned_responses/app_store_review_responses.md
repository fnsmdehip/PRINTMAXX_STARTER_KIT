# App Store review responses - Canned responses

Public responses to App Store and Google Play reviews.

**Rules:**
- Keep under 350 characters (App Store limit)
- Never argue or get defensive
- Offer direct support channel
- Thank negative reviewers for feedback

---

## 1. Generic positive review - 5 stars

**Use when:** Happy customer, generic praise.

---

Thanks for the kind words! Glad it's working well for you.

---

## 2. Detailed positive review - 5 stars

**Use when:** Customer wrote a detailed review.

---

Thanks for the detailed review. Feedback like this helps others find us and shows us what's working. Appreciate you taking the time.

---

## 3. Positive with suggestion - 4-5 stars

**Use when:** Happy customer but mentioned a feature request.

---

Thanks! Your suggestion about [FEATURE] is noted. We're always improving based on feedback like yours.

---

## 4. Bug report in review - 3-4 stars

**Use when:** Customer reported a bug in their review.

---

Sorry about that bug. We're looking into it. Email support@[DOMAIN].com with details and we'll get it fixed faster. Thanks for reporting.

---

## 5. Negative - App crashing

**Use when:** Complaints about crashes or stability.

---

Sorry for the crashes. We pushed a fix in the latest update. Please update to [VERSION] and let us know if it's still happening at support@[DOMAIN].com.

---

## 6. Negative - Feature complaint

**Use when:** Customer upset about missing feature.

---

Thanks for the feedback on [FEATURE]. We're working on improvements. Email support@[DOMAIN].com if you want to share more details. We read every message.

---

## 7. Negative - Price complaint

**Use when:** Customer thinks it's too expensive.

---

Thanks for the feedback. We try to price fairly for the value provided. Have you tried our free tier? It covers the basics. Email us if you need help finding the right plan.

---

## 8. Negative - Subscription complaint

**Use when:** Angry about subscription model or auto-renewal.

---

All subscriptions can be cancelled anytime in your device settings. For refunds, email support@[DOMAIN].com and we'll help within 24 hours.

---

## 9. Negative - Unclear/vague

**Use when:** 1-2 star review with no specific complaint.

---

Sorry it didn't work for you. Email support@[DOMAIN].com and tell us what went wrong. We want to make it right.

---

## 10. Spam/irrelevant review

**Use when:** Review is clearly spam or about wrong app.

---

This review seems to be about a different app. If you need help with [OUR APP], please email support@[DOMAIN].com.
