# Cancellation and retention - Canned responses

---

## 1. Cancel request - No questions asked

**Use when:** Customer clearly wants to cancel, don't want to push.

---

Hi [NAME],

Done. Subscription cancelled.

You have access until [END DATE], then it converts to our free plan. Your data stays safe.

If you ever want to resubscribe, your account and settings will be waiting.

- [AGENT]

---

## 2. Cancel request - Offer pause

**Use when:** Customer seems on the fence or mentions temporary reasons.

---

Hi [NAME],

Before you cancel, want to pause instead?

Pausing keeps your account and data intact for up to 3 months. No charge. You unpause whenever you're ready.

If you definitely want to cancel, reply "cancel" and I'll process it.

- [AGENT]

---

## 3. Cancel request - Offer downgrade

**Use when:** Customer mentions price as the reason.

---

Hi [NAME],

Would a downgrade work?

Our [LOWER PLAN] is $[AMOUNT]/month and includes [KEY FEATURES]. You lose [FEATURES REMOVED], but keep [WHAT MATTERS].

If that works: I'll switch you now and prorate the difference as credit.

If not: Reply "cancel" and I'll process it. No hard feelings.

- [AGENT]

---

## 4. Cancel request - Understand why

**Use when:** Customer gave no reason, worth asking.

---

Hi [NAME],

Cancelled.

Quick question if you have 30 seconds: What made you cancel?

Not trying to change your mind. Just helps us improve.

- [AGENT]

---

## 5. Win-back offer

**Use when:** Past customer, reaching out to re-engage.

---

Hi [NAME],

Been a while. We've added a lot since you left:
- [NEW FEATURE 1]
- [NEW FEATURE 2]
- [NEW FEATURE 3]

Want to try it again? Here's 30% off your first 3 months: [COUPON CODE]

No pressure. Just thought you'd want to know.

- [AGENT]
