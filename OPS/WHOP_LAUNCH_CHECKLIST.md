## PHASE 1: Account Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Whop Account

1. Open browser. Go to **whop.com**
2. Click **Start selling** or **Create a whop** (top right)
3. Sign up with: **printmaxxer@protonmail.com**
4. Set password. Save in password manager.
5. Verify email.

### Step 2: Create Your Whop (Storefront)

1. After signup, you'll be prompted to **Create a whop**
2. **Whop name:** PRINTMAXX
3. **Whop URL:** whop.com/printmaxx (or printmaxxer if taken)
4. **Category:** Business & Finance (or Education if that fits better)
5. Upload profile image (same as Gumroad)
6. **Description:** AI-powered playbooks for solopreneurs. specific numbers. real tactics. zero fluff.
7. Click **Create**

### Step 3: Connect Payments

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon or sidebar)
2. Click **Payments** or **Billing**
3. Connect **Stripe** (same Stripe account you used for Gumroad)
4. Authorize the connection
5. Verify green checkmark / connected status

---

## PHASE 2: Upload Products (20 minutes)

**Whop works differently from Gumroad.** Each product is an "access pass" that can include files, courses, community access, etc. For digital products, you create a pass and attach downloadable files to it.

**For each product below:**
1. Go to dashboard > **Products** or **Passes**
2. Click **Create new product** (or **Add pass**)
3. Paste name, tagline, price, description from below
4. Set as **one-time** payment
5. Set visibility to **Public**
6. Upload cover image + product PDF
7. Click **Publish** or **Go live**
