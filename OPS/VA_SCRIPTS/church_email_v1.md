# Church Email Template v1 - Standard Approach

Use this template 50% of the time. Focus: Partnership opportunity overview.

---

## Subject Line Options (Rotate)

- "Free prayer app for [Church Name] congregation"
- "Partnership idea for [Church Name]"
- "[Church Name] + PrayerLock partnership"

---

## Email Body

```
Hi [Pastor/Admin Name],

I came across [Church Name] on [Facebook/Instagram] and noticed [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION - be genuine and specific].

We built PrayerLock, an app that helps Christians build consistent prayer habits. It blocks distracting apps until users complete their daily prayer time. Simple concept, real results.

I'd like to offer it free to your congregation. Additionally, [Church Name] would receive 20% of any premium subscriptions from your members.

Here's how it works:
- Your congregation gets a unique link to download the app
- Members set their own prayer goals (time, frequency)
- You get a dashboard showing engagement (no individual data)
- Any members who go premium = revenue for the church

Would you have 15 minutes this week to discuss whether this could serve your congregation?

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
```

---

## Personalization Examples

Replace [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION] with one of these patterns:

**Sermon reference:**
"your recent sermon series on spiritual disciplines - building daily prayer habits aligns perfectly with that message"

**Social media activity:**
"your Easter livestream had great engagement - your congregation clearly values digital connection"

**Church milestone:**
"you're celebrating 25 years this spring - congratulations on that milestone"

**Ministry focus:**
"your youth ministry posts about phone-free retreats - we tackle the same phone addiction issue from a habit-building angle"

**Community involvement:**
"your community outreach during the holidays - churches that serve like that tend to have engaged members who'd benefit from tools like this"

---

## What Makes This Template Work

1. Opens with specific, genuine observation (not generic)
2. Explains what the app does in one sentence
3. Leads with FREE offer
4. Revenue share is secondary benefit
5. Simple bullet points for how it works
6. Low-commitment ask (15 minutes)

---

## When to Use

- Default template for most outreach
- Works for all denomination types
- Good for pastors and admin contacts
- Use when you don't have a strong angle for v2 or v3
