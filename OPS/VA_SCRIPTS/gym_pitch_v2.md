# Gym Pitch Template v2 - Revenue Focus

Use this template 30% of the time. Focus: New revenue stream for the gym.

---

## Subject Line Options (Rotate)

- "New revenue stream for [Gym Name]"
- "Partnership opportunity - [Gym Name]"
- "$X/month potential for [Gym Name]"

---

## Email Body

```
Hi [Owner/Manager Name],

What if [Gym Name] could earn an extra $200-500/month without adding classes, equipment, or staff?

We partner with gyms to offer a branded fitness app to members. You get 15% of every premium subscription - passive, recurring revenue.

Here's how it works:

1. We create a [Gym Name]-branded version of WalkToUnlock
2. Your members download via your unique link
3. The app blocks distracting apps until they hit activity goals
4. Members who go premium = $7.50/month to you, per member

Example math for a 500-member gym:
- 10% download = 50 users
- 30% premium = 15 paying members
- 15 x $7.50 = $112.50/month
- $1,350/year recurring

No cost to you. We handle tech, support, updates. You share the link.

[SPECIFIC OBSERVATION showing you researched their gym]

Interested in a pilot? We can set it up in a week.

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
```

---

## Personalization Examples

Connect [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION] to their business:

**Multiple locations:**
"With 3 locations, the math scales nicely - even modest adoption means real numbers."

**Growing gym:**
"Your growth over the last year is impressive. Adding revenue streams early compounds."

**Boutique pricing:**
"Your premium positioning means members expect value-adds. This delivers that."

**Busy schedule:**
"I know gym owners wear every hat. This is truly passive - no extra work from your team."

---

## Math Variations

Adjust based on gym size:

**Small gym (200 members):**
```
- 10% download = 20 users
- 30% premium = 6 paying members
- 6 x $7.50 = $45/month
- $540/year
```

**Medium gym (500 members):**
```
- 10% download = 50 users
- 30% premium = 15 paying members
- 15 x $7.50 = $112.50/month
- $1,350/year
```

**Large gym (1000+ members):**
```
- 10% download = 100 users
- 30% premium = 30 paying members
- 30 x $7.50 = $225/month
- $2,700/year
```

---

## What Makes This Template Work

1. Leads with money (gets attention)
2. Shows exactly how they earn
3. Specific numbers make it real
4. Emphasizes passive/low effort
5. Clear example calculation
6. Quick pilot offer reduces risk

---

## When to Use

- Gyms that seem business-focused (multiple locations, growing)
- Manager contacts (more operational mindset)
- Gyms posting about growth or expansion
- When you sense they'd respond to numbers

---

## Cautions

- Some gym owners are allergic to "sales" emails
- If they seem passion-driven (about fitness, community), use v1 instead
- Don't oversell the numbers - these are realistic estimates
