# Gym Pitch Template v1 - Problem/Solution Angle

Use this template 40% of the time. Focus: Solve the phone distraction problem.

---

## Subject Line Options (Rotate)

- "Member engagement tool for [Gym Name]"
- "Quick question about [Gym Name] members"
- "Idea for [Gym Name] - 2 min read"

---

## Email Body

```
Hi [Owner/Manager Name],

Quick question: Do your members scroll their phones between sets?

Most gyms deal with this. Members show up, but they're half-present - more focused on Instagram than their workout. It kills momentum and results.

We built WalkToUnlock to fix this. The app blocks social media and distracting apps until users hit their step/activity goals. For gyms, it keeps members engaged and moving.

What we're offering [Gym Name]:

- Branded version of the app for your members
- 15% of any premium subscriptions ($7.50 per signup, recurring)
- Member engagement dashboard (see who's active, run challenges)
- Free 30-day pilot to test it

[SPECIFIC OBSERVATION about their gym - shows you did research]

Worth a 10-minute call to see if it fits?

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
```

---

## Personalization Examples

Replace [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION] with:

**Challenges they run:**
"Saw you just finished a 6-week transformation challenge - our challenge features would level up the next one."

**Member testimonials:**
"Your member success stories on Instagram are great. This gives them another tool to stay consistent."

**Gym culture:**
"Your 'no phone zone' policy shows you get the distraction problem - we're building the same mindset outside the gym."

**New location:**
"Congrats on the second location - engagement tools help maintain culture as you scale."

**Equipment focus:**
"Your investment in equipment shows you care about results. We help members actually use what you've built."

---

## What Makes This Template Work

1. Opens with question they can relate to
2. Names the real problem (phone distraction)
3. Shows understanding of gym business
4. Clear bullet points of offer
5. Revenue share makes it a business win
6. Low-commitment ask

---

## When to Use

- Default template for most gym outreach
- Works for independent gyms and franchisees
- Good for owner-operators who are on the floor
- Use when gym posts about member results or engagement
