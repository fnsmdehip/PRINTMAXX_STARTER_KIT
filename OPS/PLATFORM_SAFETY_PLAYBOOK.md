# Platform Safety Playbook — Algo Optimization + Ban Avoidance

Updated: 2026-02-19
Covers: Gumroad, Fiverr, Upwork, Etsy, Redbubble, Amazon KDP, Twitter/X, Buffer, Reddit, TikTok

---

## UNIVERSAL RULES (All Platforms)

1. **Every marketplace gives new listings an algorithmic boost.** First impressions are permanent. Listings MUST be fully optimized BEFORE publishing. You cannot recover from a bad launch window.
2. **Automated enforcement is the default.** Fiverr permanently bans within hours with zero human review. Etsy's fraud detection triggers on new sellers. KDP auto-rejects metadata mismatches.
3. **Universal ban triggers:** Copyrighted/trademarked content, off-platform payments, multiple accounts (same IP/device), keyword stuffing, fake reviews.

## GUMROAD

### Algo Optimization
- **Discover marketplace** is Gumroad's search engine. Enable on ALL products.
- New products get a temporary visibility boost in Discover. Optimize BEFORE enabling.
- **Tags matter more than title** for Discover rankings. Use all 10 tag slots.
- Products with $0+ (PWYW) get more traffic because free downloads count as "sales."
- **Star ratings** heavily influence ranking. First 5 reviews are critical.
- Gumroad emails your buyers when you launch new products (built-in audience).

### Safety Rules
- No copyrighted content in product files or listings
- No resale rights products unless you explicitly have rights
- Income claims need disclaimers ("results not typical")
- No promoting illegal activity
- Gumroad is lenient compared to other platforms. Low ban risk if content is original.

### DO
- Enable Discover on every product
- Use all 10 tags with high-search keywords
- Set $0+ PWYW on lead magnet to maximize email captures
- Add upsells on thank-you pages (Gumroad supports this natively)
- Launch products one at a time (each gets its own boost)

### DON'T
- Don't launch all 13 products in one hour (spread over 2-3 days for individual boosts)
- Don't use copyrighted images in covers
- Don't promise specific income amounts without disclaimers

## FIVERR

### Algo Optimization
- **New gig boost** lasts 14-30 days. Your gig gets extra visibility in search results.
- **Response time** is the #1 ranking factor. Respond to ALL messages within 5 minutes.
- **Online status** affects ranking. Log in daily. The algorithm rewards active sellers.
- **Order completion rate** must stay above 90%. Never cancel orders.
- **Delivery time**: Deliver early. Under-promise delivery time, over-deliver on speed.
- Gigs with **video descriptions** rank higher than text-only.
- **Buyer requests**: Respond within minutes. First responders get 3x more orders.

### Safety Rules — CRITICAL
- Fiverr bans permanently for: multiple accounts, off-platform payments, fake reviews, spam messaging.
- **No mention of other platforms** in messages (no "find me on Upwork" or "email me directly").
- **No contact info** in gig descriptions, images, or messages until an order is placed.
- Account age + first review = trust milestone. Do NOT risk the account before first review.

### DO
- Log in to Fiverr daily (even for 1 minute)
- Respond to every message within 5 minutes
- Deliver 24-48 hours before deadline
- Add a gig video (even a simple screen recording)
- Price aggressively for first 5 orders (30-40% below market to get reviews fast)
- After first review: raise prices 20%
- Send custom offers to buyer requests immediately
- Use the cross-sell triggers from FIVERR_METADATA.md after each delivery

### DON'T
- Don't create a second Fiverr account (permanent ban, no appeal)
- Don't mention email, phone, or other platforms in any message
- Don't cancel orders (destroys ranking)
- Don't miss the 24-hour response window on buyer requests
- Don't copy gig descriptions from other sellers (Fiverr detects this)

## UPWORK

### Algo Optimization
- **Proposal quality** > proposal quantity. Upwork's algorithm ranks proposals by relevance.
- **Job Success Score (JSS)** is everything. Maintain 90%+.
- **Rising Talent badge** (new freelancers) gives 30-day visibility boost. Use it.
- Filter for: jobs posted < 2 hours ago, budget $100-500, client with payment verified.
- **Specialized profiles** rank higher than general ones. Create niche-specific profiles.
- First 3 sentences of your proposal are visible in preview. Make them count.

### Safety Rules
- No off-platform payments (Upwork monitors messages for phone numbers, emails, external links)
- No multiple accounts
- No fake portfolio pieces
- Upwork reads your messages. They flag words like "PayPal," "Venmo," phone numbers.

### DO
- Send 5+ proposals per day
- Apply to jobs posted less than 2 hours ago
- Customize first 3 lines for each job (reference something specific from the posting)
- Price 30-40% below market for first 5 jobs to get reviews
- After first review, raise prices 20%
- Use Connects strategically (apply to high-budget jobs)

### DON'T
- Don't send generic proposals (Upwork penalizes low-quality proposals)
- Don't share contact info before a contract is in place
- Don't ghost a client (tanks your JSS permanently)
- Don't take jobs you can't deliver (cancellations destroy your score)

## ETSY

### Algo Optimization
- **New listing boost**: Etsy gives every new listing 30 days of elevated visibility. Optimize fully before publishing.
- **Title is critical**: Etsy search is keyword-based. Use all 140 characters. Front-load best keywords.
- **13 tags**: Use all 13. Each tag can be a multi-word phrase (up to 20 chars). Don't repeat exact title words in tags.
- **Listing quality score** improves with: views, favorites, purchases, review ratings.
- **Renewing listings** ($0.20) resets the new listing boost. Renew every 30 days for underperforming items.
- **Photos**: First photo is everything. Mockups with product visible on dark backgrounds convert best.
- **Etsy Ads**: Optional, but even $1/day budget shows Etsy you're serious (algorithm signal).

### Safety Rules — New Seller Fraud Detection
- Etsy's fraud detection is AGGRESSIVE on new sellers. Red flags:
  - Uploading 20+ listings in first hour (looks automated)
  - Using stock photos that appear on other Etsy shops
  - Pricing drastically below market
  - Multiple shops from same IP/payment method
- **Trademark**: Etsy auto-scans for trademarked terms. Don't use brand names.

### DO
- Upload 3-5 listings per day (not all at once)
- Fill in EVERY attribute Etsy asks for (more data = more visibility)
- Use mockup images (not flat design screenshots)
- Add a shop announcement and policies
- Respond to any messages within 24 hours
- Enable Star Seller tracking from day 1

### DON'T
- Don't upload all 20 listings at once (pace over 4-5 days)
- Don't use trademarked brand names in titles/tags
- Don't use photos from other Etsy sellers
- Don't create multiple Etsy shops (same person = ban risk)
- Don't keyword stuff the description (tags and title are what matter)

## REDBUBBLE

### Algo Optimization
- Redbubble search is TAG-DRIVEN. Use all 15 tags on every design.
- **Title + tags** = discoverability. Title should contain primary keywords.
- More product types enabled = more visibility. Enable ALL products for every design.
- **Volume matters**: 50+ designs gets you more algorithmic visibility than 10.
- Redbubble indexes with Google. Your listings can appear in Google Shopping results.
- **Trending topics**: Designs related to current events/holidays sell 5-10x normal.

### Safety Rules
- **No copyrighted/trademarked content**: Redbubble will remove and warn. 3 strikes = ban.
- No fan art of copyrighted characters (Disney, Marvel, anime characters)
- No brand logos or slogans (Nike, Adidas, etc.)
- Bible verses and generic religious text: SAFE
- Arabic calligraphy of Quran verses: SAFE (religious text, not copyrighted)
- Generic motivational text: SAFE

### DO
- Use all 15 tags per design
- Enable every product type
- Upload in batches of 5-10 per day
- Create designs for upcoming holidays/events 30 days in advance
- Target niches (faith, fitness, coding) rather than generic motivation

### DON'T
- Don't use trademarked phrases ("Just Do It", "May the Force", etc.)
- Don't copy other Redbubble sellers' designs
- Don't use AI-generated art that's clearly copyrighted characters
- Don't keyword stuff irrelevant tags (Redbubble penalizes this)

## AMAZON KDP

### Algo Optimization
- **Keywords** (7 slots): These are the primary ranking factor. Research competitors' keywords.
- **Categories** (2 slots): Pick the most specific subcategory possible (less competition).
- **Title + subtitle**: Keyword-rich. Amazon search uses title heavily.
- **A+ Content** (enhanced brand content): Not available for new KDP publishers, but descriptions matter.
- **Price sweet spot**: $7.99-$12.99 for journals. Under $10 gets impulse buys.
- KDP review takes 24-72 hours. Submit early for time-sensitive products (Ramadan journal).

### Safety Rules
- **Metadata must match content**: KDP auto-rejects if title/description doesn't match interior.
- No content that violates Amazon's content guidelines (hate speech, illegal content, public domain misrepresentation).
- **Low-content book scrutiny**: Amazon has tightened rules on low-content/no-content books. Every journal must have actual prompts/content, not just blank pages.
- No trademarked terms in titles or keywords.
- Don't publish the same interior under multiple titles (Amazon flags this as spam).

### DO
- Use all 7 keyword slots
- Make interior pages genuinely useful (prompts, trackers, not just lines)
- Use both category slots with specific subcategories
- Price at $7.99-$12.99 range
- Submit Ramadan journal ASAP (Feb 28 deadline)
- Use KDP Cover Calculator for exact cover dimensions

### DON'T
- Don't upload blank lined journals (KDP rejects these now)
- Don't use the same interior for multiple titles
- Don't use trademarked terms
- Don't price below $4.99 (royalty drops to 40% below this)
- Don't rush covers (ugly cover = zero sales regardless of content)

## TWITTER/X (12 Accounts)

### New Account Warmup Schedule — STRICT

**Week 1 (Days 1-7): Observation Mode**
- Follow 5-10 relevant accounts per day (no more)
- Like 10-15 posts per day
- NO original tweets for first 48 hours
- After 48 hours: 1-2 tweets per day maximum
- NO links in tweets for first week
- NO hashtags for first week
- Set profile photo, bio, header image BEFORE first activity

**Week 2 (Days 8-14): Light Engagement**
- 2-3 tweets per day
- 5-10 quality replies per day (not "great post!" but actual thoughts)
- Can start using 1-2 hashtags per tweet
- Still no link-heavy tweets
- Follow 5-10 more accounts per day

**Week 3 (Days 15-21): Ramp Up**
- 3-5 tweets per day
- 10+ replies per day
- Can post links occasionally (1 per 5 tweets)
- Start using Buffer/Typefully for scheduling
- Bookmark content actively (algorithm signal)

**Week 4+ (Days 22-30): Normal Operations**
- 3-5 tweets per day (content from `CONTENT/social/{handle}/`)
- Use scheduling tools (Buffer "Add to Queue", not "Share Now")
- Can post threads
- DM only people who engaged with you first

### Safety Rules — CRITICAL
- **New unverified accounts are capped at ~500 views/day.** Don't waste premium content on a new account.
- **Shadowban triggers**: Aggressive following (20+/day on new account), identical content across accounts, link spam, rapid-fire posting.
- **Multi-account detection**: X detects coordinated accounts. Use separate email per account. Use different posting times. Vary content between accounts (don't copy-paste identical tweets).
- **IP clustering**: If running from same network, X flags multiple new accounts. Use mobile data or different IPs for initial creation.
- **API vs browser posting**: Buffer/Typefully post via official API (SAFE). Browser automation posting (RISKY). Always prefer API-based schedulers.

### DO
- Use separate ProtonMail for each account
- Create accounts in incognito/private windows
- Space account creation (2-3 per day max)
- Let each account age 24-48 hours before first post
- Use Buffer/Typefully for scheduling (official API, safe)
- Post 4-5 variations of content across accounts (never identical)
- Reply to large accounts for free distribution

### DON'T
- Don't create all 12 accounts in one day (stagger over 5-7 days)
- Don't post identical content across accounts
- Don't follow 50+ accounts per day on a new account
- Don't DM people who didn't engage with you
- Don't use automation for liking/following (only for scheduled posting)
- Don't link to the same URL from multiple accounts simultaneously

## BUFFER (Scheduling Tool)

### Safe Usage
- Use "Add to Queue" (not "Share Now") for each post
- 4-5 post variations across accounts (Buffer doesn't flag this, but Twitter does)
- Stagger posting times by 30-60 minutes between accounts
- Don't exceed platform-specific daily limits

### Limits
- Free plan: 3 social channels
- Paid ($6/mo): Unlimited channels
- Buffer doesn't get accounts banned (it uses official APIs). The risk is in WHAT you post, not HOW.

## REDDIT

### Algo Optimization
- **Karma thresholds**: Most subreddits require 10-50 karma before posting. Build karma first.
- **New account penalty**: Reddit shadowbans new accounts that post links immediately. Comment first for 2-3 days.
- **Self-promotion rule**: Reddit's site-wide 10% rule. No more than 10% of your posts should be self-promotional.
- **Best posting times**: 8-10 AM EST (maximum visibility for US audience).
- Posts with specific numbers and proof get 5-10x more engagement than vague advice.

### Safety Rules
- **No spamming the same link** across subreddits (Reddit auto-flags this).
- **No vote manipulation** (asking for upvotes, using alt accounts to upvote).
- **Each subreddit has different rules.** Read sidebar before posting.
- r/forhire: Must include [FOR HIRE] tag and rate range.
- r/slavelabour: Budget under $50 typically.

### DO
- Build karma by commenting helpfully for 2-3 days before posting
- Post [FOR HIRE] on r/forhire with portfolio links
- Respond to [HIRING] posts with specific, customized pitches
- Provide genuine value in comments (builds credibility for self-promotion)
- Use the free lead magnet Gumroad link as a soft CTA (free = Reddit-friendly)

### DON'T
- Don't post links in your first week
- Don't cross-post the same pitch to 10 subreddits
- Don't use multiple Reddit accounts for voting
- Don't hard-sell (Reddit culture hates overt marketing)

## TIKTOK

### New Account Warmup
- **Days 1-3**: Watch content in your niche (10-15 min/day). Like 10-20 videos. Follow 5-10 creators. Don't post.
- **Days 4-7**: Post 1 video per day. Use 3-5 hashtags. Respond to comments.
- **Week 2**: Post 1-2 videos per day. Use trending sounds.
- **Week 3+**: 2-3 videos per day. Experiment with formats.

### Safety Rules
- TikTok shadowbans new accounts that post links in bio immediately. Wait 7 days.
- Don't use copyrighted music (use TikTok's commercial sound library).
- Don't post content that looks AI-generated without disclosure (TikTok's policy requires it).
- Multiple accounts from same device/IP can be flagged.

## WHOP / LEMON SQUEEZY / CONTRA

- No special ban concerns. Follow standard content guidelines.

### Lemon Squeezy
- Alternative to Gumroad. Lower fees (5% + $0.50).

- No marketplace discovery (all traffic must be driven by you).

- List same services as Fiverr but at full price (no commission cut).
