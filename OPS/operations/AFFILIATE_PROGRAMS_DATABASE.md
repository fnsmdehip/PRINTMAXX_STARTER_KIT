## Quick Reference

### Commission Types
- **Percentage:** X% of sale price
- **Flat:** Fixed $ per conversion
- **Recurring:** Commission on subscription renewals
- **Tiered:** Higher rates at higher volumes

### Cookie Duration
- **Short:** 7-30 days (must convert quickly)
- **Standard:** 30-60 days
- **Long:** 90+ days (better for content creators)
- **Lifetime:** Attributed forever (rare, valuable)

## 10. Application Tips

### Getting Accepted

**Before applying:**
- Have established content (30+ posts, 1k+ followers)
- Show niche relevance
- Clean, professional profiles
- Clear content about the product category

**Application best practices:**
- Be specific about promotion methods
- Share traffic numbers honestly
- Explain audience fit
- Mention any existing relationship with brand

### Maximizing Earnings

**Content that converts:**
- Honest reviews with pros/cons
- Tutorial content showing product use
- Comparison content (X vs Y)
- "Best of" roundups with top pick
- Case studies with results

**Placement optimization:**
- In-content links (highest CTR)
- Resource pages (good for SEO)
- Email newsletters
- YouTube descriptions
- Social media bio links

### Compliance Requirements

**FTC disclosure required:**
- "This post contains affiliate links"
- "I earn a commission if you purchase"
- Must be clear and conspicuous
- Required on all platforms

**Platform-specific:**
- Instagram: #ad or #affiliate in caption
- YouTube: Verbal + written disclosure
- TikTok: Branded Content toggle
- Blog: Disclosure at top of post
