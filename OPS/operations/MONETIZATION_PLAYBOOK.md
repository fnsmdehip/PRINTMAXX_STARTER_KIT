## 1. App Monetization

### Subscription Models

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Price Point | Psychology |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Weekly | $4.99-$9.99/wk | Low commitment, high annualized |
| Monthly | $9.99-$14.99/mo | Standard subscription |
| Annual | $49.99-$99.99/yr | Best value positioning |
| Lifetime | 3-5x annual | Captures "whale" segment |

**Optimal Pricing Strategy:**
- Default to annual (2.6x retention vs monthly)
- Show annual first in paywall
- Weekly only for specific use cases (impulse apps)
- Lifetime as premium option (captures whales)

**Trial Strategy:**
- 7-day trial on annual only (higher LTV)
- 3-day trial for weekly (impulse conversion)
- No trial on monthly (encourage annual)

### Freemium Conversion

**Freemium Benchmarks:**
| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Great |
|--------|------|---------|------|-------|
| Free to Paid | <2% | 2-5% | 5-8% | 8%+ |
| Trial to Paid | <10% | 10-20% | 20-30% | 30%+ |
| Day 1 Retention | <20% | 20-30% | 30-40% | 40%+ |
| Day 7 Retention | <5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | 20%+ |

**Free vs Premium Features:**

| Free | Premium |
|------|---------|
| Core functionality | Advanced features |
| Basic themes | All themes/customization |
| Limited usage (3 per day) | Unlimited usage |
| Ads | Ad-free |
| Basic stats | Detailed analytics |
| Single device | Multi-device sync |

**Conversion Triggers:**
- Feature gate (try to use locked feature)
- Usage limit reached
- After positive action (completed task)
- Time-based (after 7 days of use)

### Paywall Optimization

**Paywall Timing:**

| Trigger | Conversion Rate |
|---------|-----------------|
| Day 0 (onboarding) | 50% of total trials |
| Feature gate | 10-15% |
| After value moment | 20-35% |
| After success | 10-20% |
| Loss aversion | 15-25% |

**High-Converting Paywall Elements:**

1. **HEADLINE:** Clear value proposition
   - BAD: "Go Premium"
   - GOOD: "Keep your 14-day streak going"

2. **SOCIAL PROOF:** Real numbers
   - "Join 500,000+ focused users"

3. **FEATURE COMPARISON:** Visual table
   - Free vs Premium side by side

4. **PRICE ANCHORING:** Show savings
   - "$9.99/mo or $49.99/yr (save 58%)"

5. **RISK REVERSAL:** Remove friction
   - "7-day free trial. Cancel anytime."

6. **CTA BUTTON:** Action-oriented
   - BAD: "Subscribe"
   - GOOD: "Start My Free Week"

**A/B Test Priority:**
1. Paywall trigger timing (when to show)
2. Headline copy (what to say)
3. Price point ($7.99 vs $9.99)
4. Trial length (3 vs 7 days)
5. Annual vs monthly emphasis

## 2. Content Monetization

### Creator Fund Optimization

**Platform Payouts (2026 Rates):**

| Platform | Requirement | Payout | Optimization |
|----------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| TikTok Creator Fund | 10k followers, 100k views/30d | $0.02-0.04/1k views | Maximize watch time |
| TikTok Creativity Program | 10k followers | $0.50-1/1k views | Long-form (1-3 min) |
| YouTube Shorts | 1k subs, 10M views/90d | $0.03-0.06/1k views | Consistent posting |
| YouTube Long-form | 1k subs, 4k watch hours | $2-10/1k views | 8-15 min videos |
| Instagram Reels | Invite-only | $0.01-0.02/1k views | Engagement rate |
| Snap Spotlight | Open | Variable bonuses | Viral potential |

**Maximizing Creator Fund Revenue:**

Formula: Views x Watch Time x Engagement = Revenue

Tactics:
1. Hook in first 0.5 seconds (stops scrolling)
2. Text on screen (keeps attention)
3. Loop point at end (rewatches count)
4. Call-to-action (drives engagement)
5. Post 3-5x daily (algorithm rewards consistency)

**Long-Form vs Short-Form Math:**

Short-form (TikTok):
- 1M views = $200-400 (Creator Fund)
- Time to create: 30 minutes

Long-form (YouTube):
- 100k views = $200-1000 (AdSense)
- Time to create: 4-8 hours

Optimization: Short-form to build audience, long-form for revenue

**Rate Benchmarks by Platform:**

| Followers | TikTok | Instagram | YouTube | Twitter |
|-----------|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| 10k-50k | $100-500 | $100-500 | $200-1000 | $50-200 |

| 1M+ | $25k+ | $25k+ | $50k+ | $5k+ |

**Pricing Formula:**

Base rate = (Followers / 1000) x $10-50 (depending on niche)

Multipliers:
- High engagement (>5%): 1.5x
- Niche relevance: 1.3x
- Exclusivity: 2x
- Usage rights: 1.5x
- Revisions: 1.2x per round

Example: 50k followers, finance niche, high engagement
= (50k / 1000) x $30 x 1.5 x 1.3 = $2,925 per post

## 3. Service Monetization

### Pricing Strategy

**Pricing Models:**

| Model | Use Case | Pros | Cons |
|-------|----------|------|------|
| Hourly | Consulting, unclear scope | Simple, flexible | Caps earning, scope creep |
| Project | Clear deliverables | Predictable, scalable | Scope creep risk |
| Retainer | Ongoing relationship | Predictable revenue | Client dependency |
| Value-based | High-impact work | Highest margin | Harder to sell |
| Productized | Standardized service | Scalable, efficient | Less customization |

**Price Calculation:**

Hourly Rate Calculation:
- Target annual income: $150,000
- Billable hours (50%): 1,000
- Hourly rate: $150/hour
- With 20% buffer: $180/hour

Project Pricing:
- Estimated hours x Hourly rate x 1.3 (buffer)
- Example: 20 hours x $180 x 1.3 = $4,680

Value-Based Pricing:
- Client's expected ROI x 10-20%
- Example: $50k expected revenue = $5-10k fee

**Price Anchoring:**

Always present 3 options:
1. Basic: $X (limited scope)
2. Standard: $2X (full scope) <- position as "most popular"
3. Premium: $3X (full scope + extras)

Result: 60% choose Standard, you've anchored their expectation

### Package Structuring

**Service Package Template:**

| Tier | Name | Includes | Price | Margin |
|------|------|----------|-------|--------|
| Basic | Starter | Core deliverable only | $X | 60% |
| Standard | Growth | Core + support + revisions | $2X | 70% |
| Premium | Scale | Everything + priority + extras | $3X | 75% |

**Example: UGC Service Packages:**

STARTER - $500
- 3 UGC videos (15-30 sec)
- 1 revision round
- 7-day delivery
- Standard usage rights

GROWTH - $1,200 (most popular)
- 8 UGC videos (15-60 sec)
- 2 revision rounds
- 5-day delivery

- Performance consultation call

SCALE - $2,500
- 20 UGC videos (15-90 sec)
- Unlimited revisions
- 3-day priority delivery
- Full usage rights
- Monthly strategy calls
- A/B variants included

## 4. Info Product Monetization

### Pricing Tiers

**Info Product Price Points:**

| Type | Price | Buyer Psychology | Volume |
|------|-------|------------------|--------|
| Lead magnet | Free | "What's the catch?" | Highest |
| Mini-product | $9-29 | "Low risk to try" | High |
| Standard course | $97-297 | "Investing in myself" | Medium |
| Premium course | $497-997 | "Serious about results" | Low-Medium |
| High-ticket | $1997-4997 | "Want transformation" | Low |
| Cohort/Coaching | $2997-9997 | "Want accountability" | Lowest |

**Pricing Psychology by Audience:**

| Audience | Sweet Spot | Why |
|----------|------------|-----|
| Beginners | $47-97 | Low barrier, impulse |
| Intermediates | $197-497 | Proven interest, seeking edge |
| Professionals | $497-1997 | ROI-focused, expense-able |
| Business buyers | $1997+ | Budget available, outcome-driven |

### Bundle Strategies

**Bundle Types:**

1. STACKING BUNDLE
   - Course A ($97) + Course B ($97) + Course C ($97)
   - Bundle: $197 (save $94)
   - Works for: Multiple related products

2. TIER BUNDLE
   - Course ($197) + Templates ($47) + Community ($19/mo)
   - Bundle: $247 + templates free + 3 months community
   - Works for: Increasing average order value

3. ASCENSION BUNDLE
   - Mini-course ($47) -> Full course ($297) -> Coaching ($997)
   - Early buyer discount: Buy all three for $997
   - Works for: Committed buyers

4. SEASONAL BUNDLE
   - Black Friday: Everything for 50% off
   - Works for: List clearing, cash injection

**Bundle Pricing Formula:**

Sum of individual prices x 0.4-0.6 = Bundle price

Example:
- Course 1: $197
- Course 2: $147
- Templates: $47
- Total: $391
- Bundle: $197 (50% of total)

Perception: "I'm getting Course 1 and everything else free"

### Launch Pricing

**Launch Price Ladder:**

| Phase | Price | Duration | Goal |
|-------|-------|----------|------|
| Founding rate | 40% off | First 50 buyers | Early testimonials |

| Standard launch | 20% off | Launch week | Main revenue |
| Full price | Retail | Ongoing | Baseline |

**Launch Sequence:**

Week -2: Teaser content, waitlist building
Week -1: Value content, pre-launch access
Day 1-3: Cart open, early bird
Day 4-5: Testimonials, case studies
Day 6: Final call, objection handling
Day 7: Cart close, urgency

**Scarcity Tactics (Use Honestly):**

| Tactic | Example | Works Because |
|--------|---------|---------------|

| Limited quantity | "Only 50 spots this cohort" | Exclusivity |
| Bonuses expire | "Bonus removed after Wednesday" | Loss aversion |

**Email Monetization Strategies:**

## 6. Revenue Stacking

### How to Layer Multiple Revenue Streams

**The Revenue Pyramid:**

```
                    HIGH-TICKET ($2-10k)
                   Coaching, consulting, done-for-you
                              |
                              v
                  PREMIUM ($200-1000)
                 Courses, cohorts, services
                              |
                              v
              CORE ($50-200)
            Products, memberships, mid-tier
                              |
                              v
          ENTRY ($10-50)
        Mini-products, templates, trials
                              |
                              v
      FREE (Lead gen)
    Content, lead magnets, free tier
```

**Stack Example: Fitness Niche**

Layer 1: FREE
- Fitness TikTok content (builds audience)
- Free workout PDF (captures emails)

Layer 2: ENTRY ($29)
- 30-day challenge template
- Meal plan templates

Layer 3: CORE ($97/mo)
- Fitness app subscription
- Community membership

Layer 4: PREMIUM ($297)
- 12-week transformation course

Layer 5: HIGH-TICKET ($997)
- 1-on-1 coaching package

### LTV Calculations

**LTV Formulas:**

Simple LTV:
Average Revenue Per User / Churn Rate = LTV

Example:
$10/month subscription / 5% monthly churn = $200 LTV

Expanded LTV (with upsells):
(Subscription revenue + Upsell revenue) / Churn Rate

Example:
($10/mo + $5/mo avg upsell) / 5% = $300 LTV

**LTV:CAC Ratio Targets:**

| Ratio | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Less than 1:1 | Losing money | Stop spending, fix funnel |
| 1:1 - 2:1 | Barely profitable | Optimize before scaling |
| 3:1 | Healthy | Scale carefully |
| 4:1+ | Very healthy | Scale aggressively |

| Churn Rate | Lost customers / Total customers | <5%/month |
| Activation Rate | Users completing key action / New users | >40% |
