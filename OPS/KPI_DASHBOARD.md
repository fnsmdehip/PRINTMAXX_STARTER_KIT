## SECTION 7: PORTFOLIO VIEW (The Numbers Game)

### Why 96 Ventures Matters

The core thesis: **individual venture outcomes are unpredictable, but portfolio outcomes are not.**

If you launch 20 ventures and each has a 30% chance of producing $500+/mo:
- P(at least 1 works) = 1 - (0.7^20) = **99.2%**
- Expected number that work: **6 ventures**
- Expected revenue: 6 x $500 = **$3,000/mo** (conservative per-venture estimate)

If each working venture averages $1,500/mo (medium estimate):
- Expected revenue: 6 x $1,500 = **$9,000/mo**

If each working venture averages $3,000/mo (one breakout included):
- Expected revenue: 6 x $3,000 = **$18,000/mo**

- Claude Code does in minutes what takes humans hours (freelance delivery, app builds, content generation)

- Cross-pollination between ventures (content feeds products feeds services)
- Each new venture has near-zero marginal cost (shared infra)

**Conservative estimate of automation multiplier: 3-5x output per hour of human time vs traditional solopreneur.**

**The system is designed to be worth more at month 6 than the sum of its parts at month 1.**
