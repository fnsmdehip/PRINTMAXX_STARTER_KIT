# Pricing Strategy Guide

## Pricing Psychology Fundamentals

### Anchoring Effect
Show a higher "value" price before the actual price.

```
Value: $997
Today: $297
```

This makes $297 feel like a deal, even if you never intended to charge $997.

### Charm Pricing
Prices ending in 7 or 9 convert better than round numbers.

| Round | Charm | Why Charm Wins |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| $50 | $47 | Feels significantly cheaper |
| $100 | $97 | Left-digit effect |
| $30 | $29 | Classic retail psychology |

### Decoy Pricing
Add a middle option that makes the top option look valuable.

```
Basic: $9/mo - 1 feature
Pro: $29/mo - 5 features (decoy)
Premium: $39/mo - All features + support
```

Most people choose Premium because Pro looks like bad value.

### Price-Quality Heuristic
Higher price = perceived higher quality. Underpricing can hurt conversions.

## App Pricing Strategy

### Subscription Tiers (Typical)

| Tier | Price Range | What to Include |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | Core feature, limited use |
| Basic | $4.99/mo | Unlimited core feature |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | All features |
| Premium | $14.99/mo | All + priority support |

### One-Time Purchase (Alternative)

| Type | Price Range | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Lite | $2.99-4.99 | Simple utilities |
| Standard | $9.99-19.99 | Feature-complete apps |
| Pro | $29.99-49.99 | Professional tools |

### Freemium Conversion Benchmarks

| Category | Free-to-Paid Rate |
|----------|-------------------|
| Productivity | 2-5% |
| Health/Fitness | 3-7% |
| Games | 1-3% |
| Education | 2-4% |
| Utilities | 1-2% |

### Free Trial Best Practices

**Duration:**
- 3 days: Creates urgency, lower conversion
- 7 days: Sweet spot for most apps
- 14 days: Complex tools needing more time
- 30 days: Enterprise/high-ticket

**Trial Type:**
- Full access: Higher conversion, some abuse
- Limited features: Lower conversion, less abuse
- Usage-limited: Good balance

### Paywall Placement

| Timing | Conversion | Best For |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Immediate (soft) | Low | Brand awareness |
| After onboarding | Medium | Most apps |
| After value delivered | High | Apps with quick wins |
| Usage limit hit | Highest | Feature-rich apps |

## Info Product Pricing

### Digital Products

| Product Type | Price Range | Notes |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Ebook/PDF Guide | $19-47 | Quick to create, lower perceived value |
| Template Pack | $27-97 | High perceived value, done-for-you |
| Mini Course (1-2 hrs) | $47-97 | Video adds value |
| Full Course (5+ hrs) | $197-497 | Comprehensive, transformation |
| Premium Course | $497-997 | Community, support, certification |
| High-Ticket | $997-2500+ | Coaching, done-with-you |

### Pricing Factors

**Price Up:**
- Specific niche (B2B, professional)
- Proven results/testimonials
- Community/support included
- Certification/credential
- Done-for-you elements

**Price Down:**
- Broad audience (B2C)
- New creator (no track record)
- Self-paced only
- Commodity topic
- Heavy competition

### Launch Pricing Strategy

```
Early Bird: 40% off (first 48 hours)
Launch Week: 25% off
Regular Price: Full price
```

### Bundle Pricing

| Configuration | Discount |
|---------------|----------|
| 2 products | 15-20% off |
| 3 products | 25-30% off |
| Everything bundle | 40-50% off |

## Service Pricing

### Hourly vs. Value-Based

| Model | When to Use | Pros/Cons |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Hourly | Early stage, variable scope | Safe but caps earnings |
| Project | Defined deliverables | Better margins, scope creep risk |
| Value-based | Clear ROI | Highest margins, harder to sell |
| Retainer | Ongoing relationships | Predictable revenue |

### Service Pricing Benchmarks

| Service Type | Beginner | Experienced | Expert |
|--------------|----------|-------------|--------|
| Copywriting | $50-100/hr | $150-300/hr | $500+/hr |
| Design | $50-100/hr | $100-200/hr | $300+/hr |
| Development | $75-150/hr | $150-300/hr | $400+/hr |
| Consulting | $100-200/hr | $250-500/hr | $1000+/hr |

### Productized Service Pricing

Fixed scope, fixed price. Scales better than hourly.

| Service | Price Range | Delivery Time |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Logo design | $500-2000 | 1-2 weeks |
| Landing page | $1500-5000 | 1-2 weeks |
| Website | $5000-15000 | 4-8 weeks |
| Brand identity | $3000-10000 | 2-4 weeks |

### Retainer Pricing

```
Monthly commitment = (Hourly rate × Hours) × 0.85

Example:
$150/hr × 20 hours = $3000
$3000 × 0.85 = $2550/month retainer
```

Discount incentivizes commitment, guaranteed income for you.

## SaaS Pricing

### Pricing Models

| Model | Best For | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Flat-rate | Simple tools | $29/mo for all features |
| Per-user | B2B collaboration | $10/user/month |
| Usage-based | APIs, infrastructure | $0.01 per request |
| Tiered | Most SaaS | Free, Pro, Enterprise |

### Typical SaaS Tiers

```
Free: $0
- Limited features
- Branding/watermark
- Community support

Pro: $29-99/mo
- All features
- Remove limits
- Email support

Enterprise: Custom
- SLA
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
```

### SaaS Pricing Benchmarks

| Category | Avg Price | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Email tools | $20-50/mo | Per subscriber tiers |
| Design tools | $10-30/mo | Per user |
| Dev tools | $20-50/mo | Per seat |
| Analytics | $50-200/mo | By usage |
| Marketing | $50-500/mo | By features |

### Annual vs. Monthly

- Annual: 15-20% discount standard
- Incentivizes commitment
- Better cash flow
- Lower churn

```
Monthly: $29/mo ($348/year)
Annual: $24/mo ($288/year) - "Save $60"
```

## Pricing Tests to Run

### A/B Test Price Points
Test 2-3 prices simultaneously:
- Option A: $47
- Option B: $67
- Option C: $97

Track: Conversion rate × Price = Revenue per visitor

### Test Pricing Display

| Test | Variants |
|------|----------|
| Monthly vs. Annual | Default to annual? |
| Price anchoring | With/without crossed-out price |
| Decimal vs. round | $47 vs. $47.00 |
| Currency symbol | $47 vs. 47 USD |

### Test Bundles
- Individual products
- Curated bundles
- "Everything" package

## Pricing Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Underpricing
- Signals low quality
- Attracts wrong customers
- Leaves money on table
- Hard to raise later

### 2. No Anchoring
- Always show "value" vs. "price"
- Reference competitor prices
- Use crossed-out original price

### 3. Too Many Options
- 3-4 tiers maximum
- Paradox of choice kills conversions
- Make one option obvious "best value"

### 4. Hiding Price
- Builds distrust
- Wastes everyone's time
- Exception: Enterprise sales

### 5. Never Testing
- Run price tests quarterly
- Small changes compound
- Data beats intuition

## Pricing Formulas

### Cost-Plus Pricing
```
Price = Cost × (1 + Markup%)
Example: $10 cost × 3 = $30 price
```

### Value-Based Pricing
```
Price = Customer Value × 10-20%
Example: Saves customer $10k/year → charge $1-2k
```

### Competitive Pricing
```
Price = Competitor Average × Position Multiplier
Premium: 1.2-1.5x
Parity: 1.0x
Budget: 0.7-0.9x
```
