## TikTok Algorithm Changes 2026

### Major Updates
1. **Follower-first testing** - Videos shown to followers first before wider distribution
2. **70% completion rate** required for viral (was 50% in 2024)
3. **Shares/saves weighted above likes**
4. **60-180 second videos** outperforming 15-second clips
5. **Niche specificity** rewarded

### Small Creator Impact
- Content relevance still beats follower count
- Interest-based algorithm = level playing field remains
- Specific content = better audience matching
- TikTok becoming search engine (tutorials thrive)

### Strategy Adjustments
- Build engaged follower base (quality > quantity)
- Focus on 60-180s content length
- Optimize for completion rate
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags + keywords in caption
- Reply to every comment in first hour
