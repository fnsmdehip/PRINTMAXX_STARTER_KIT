## Platform Deep Dives

### 1. Facebook Reels

**Source:** [Post-Bridge](https://www.post-bridge.com/blog/how-much-does-facebook-reels-pay-per-1000-views), [Meta Engineering](https://engineering.fb.com/2026/01/14/ml-applications/adapting-the-facebook-reels-recsys-ai-model-based-on-user-feedback/)

#### Current Payout Rates (2026)
- **RPM Range:** $0.02-$0.60 per 1,000 views
- **Most creators:** $0.04-$0.08 per 1,000 views
- **US audience premium:** CPM up to $20.48
- **1M views earnings:** $40-$600 depending on audience geography

#### Major Changes (August 2025)
- Unified Content Monetization program replaced fragmented system
- Old programs (Reels Play Bonus, In-Stream Ads, Performance Bonus) all merged
- Invite-only bonus program can pay up to $35,000/month for top creators

#### Algorithm Updates (2026)
- **Same-day content prioritized:** 50% more distribution for content uploaded same day
- **Original content rewarded:** Watermarked/reposted content suppressed
- **Optimal length:** 15-30 seconds (45% higher completion than longer videos)
- **First 3 seconds critical:** Hook required within first 3 seconds
- **Rewatches count more than views:** Completion rate is key signal
- **Friend engagement amplified:** "Friend bubbles" show which Reels friends liked

#### Best Practices
- Vertical 9:16 format required
- Under 30 seconds optimal
- Use trending audio
- Post when audience most active
- Encourage tagging and sharing
- No watermarks from other platforms

#### Arbitrage Opportunity
**HIGHEST priority for cross-posting.** Facebook Reels pays 4-10x YouTube Shorts for same content in many cases. US-focused audience = maximum RPM.

### 2. TikTok Creator Rewards Program

**Source:** [ContentStudio](https://contentstudio.io/blog/tiktok-creativity-program), [DemandSage](https://www.demandsage.com/how-much-do-you-get-paid-on-tiktok/), [Opus.pro](https://www.opus.pro/blog/tiktoks-new-algorithm-2026)

#### Current Payout Rates (2026)
- **Standard RPM:** $0.40-$1.00 per 1,000 views
- **High-retention niche content:** Up to $6.00 RPM
- **1M views earnings:** $400-$6,000 (depending on niche and retention)
- **Upgrade from old Creator Fund:** 10-25x higher than old $0.02-$0.04 rates

#### RPM by Niche
| Niche | RPM Range |
|-------|-----------|
| Entertainment, Dance, Vlogs | $0.30-$0.60 |
| Educational, How-To | $0.60-$1.00 |
| Finance, Tech, Business | $1.00+ |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- 10,000 followers
- 100,000 monthly views
- Videos must be 1+ minute long
- Only "Qualified Views" count

#### Major Algorithm Changes (January 2026)
- **Oracle deal impact:** 45% US operations divested, algorithm retraining in progress
- **Follower-first distribution:** New videos shown to followers first before wider push
- **70% completion rate needed for viral:** Up from 50% in 2024
- **Saves and shares weighted above likes**
- **Comment quality matters more than quantity**
- **First 60 minutes critical:** Strong early engagement gets aggressive push
- **TikTok as search engine:** Keywords in captions, text, and audio are scanned
- **60-180 second videos outperforming 15-second clips**
- **Original content heavily favored:** Reposted content from other platforms suppressed
- **3-5 relevant hashtags:** Hashtag stuffing is dead

#### Best Practices
- Create 1+ minute videos for Creator Rewards eligibility
- Focus on high-retention niches (finance, tech, education)
- Hook in first second
- Use keywords for TikTok SEO
- Post when followers most active
- One quality video > seven mediocre daily posts

#### Arbitrage Opportunity
**Highest RPM potential** but requires 1min+ videos. Create finance/tech/business content for maximum RPM. Same content can be trimmed for other platforms.

### 3. YouTube Shorts

**Source:** [Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketinghub.com/youtube-shorts-rpm/), [Mediacube](https://mediacube.io/en-US/blog/youtube-shorts-rpm), [Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/blog/youtube-shorts-monetization)

#### Current Payout Rates (2026)
- **RPM Range:** $0.01-$0.50 per 1,000 views
- **Most creators:** $0.03-$0.07 per 1,000 views
- **Finance niche:** $0.05-$0.30 (10x comedy/lifestyle)
- **1M views earnings:** $10-$100 typical, up to $500 for premium niches

#### Revenue Model
- **Ad pool system:** Revenue pooled regionally, not tied to specific video
- **Creator share:** 45% of allocated Shorts revenue (YouTube keeps 55%)
- **Shorts = 22% of YouTube's total ad revenue** but low creator payouts

#### Eligibility Requirements
- **Basic:** 500 subscribers + 3 public uploads in 90 days + 3M valid Shorts views OR 3,000 watch hours
- **Full YPP:** 1,000 subscribers + 10M Shorts views OR 4,000 watch hours

#### Best Practices
- Shorts best for **audience growth**, not revenue
- Finance Shorts earn 10x comedy Shorts
- Use Shorts to drive traffic to long-form content
- Long-form pays 50-100x more than Shorts

#### Arbitrage Opportunity
**Lowest priority for revenue** but essential for growth. Use YouTube Shorts to build audience, then monetize through long-form content or drive traffic to higher-paying platforms.

### 4. X/Twitter Creator Program

**Source:** [PPC.land](https://ppc.land/x-doubles-creator-revenue-pool-and-offers-1m-for-top-article-but-why/), [The Street](https://www.thestreet.com/entertainment/elon-musk-quietly-tests-bold-new-payout-plan-for-x-creators), [Social Media Today](https://www.socialmediatoday.com/news/x-formerly-twitter-updates-creator-revenue-share-program/734944/)

#### Major Changes (January 2026)
- **Revenue pool DOUBLED** (announced January 16, 2026)
- **Timeline impressions now weighted**
- **Articles heavily prioritized**
- **$1M prize for top Article** (US only, 1,000+ words required)
- **X Money wallet coming 2026** (Visa-backed, handles creator payouts)

#### Updated Monetization Model
- **Engagement-based payouts** (shifted from ad revenue late 2024)
- **25% of Premium subscription revenue** allocated to creator pool
- **X Premium growth = creator earnings growth**

#### Payout Changes
- Creators reporting **2-3x previous payouts** even with flat impressions
- "First time X feels like a real income stream" per creator testimonials
- Musk directive: "crank creator payouts way way way way up"
- Goal: Pay select creators more than YouTube

#### Best Practices
- Focus on Articles (1,000+ words) for $1M prize eligibility
- Timeline impressions matter most
- Build X Premium subscriber engagement
- Quality over quantity

#### Arbitrage Opportunity
**HIGH priority given doubled pool.** X is actively trying to outpay YouTube. Articles offer outsized rewards. Worth testing content on X alongside other platforms.

### 6. Kick vs Twitch (Live Streaming)

**Source:** [Gumlet](https://www.gumlet.com/learn/kick-vs-twitch/), [Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketinghub.com/kick-vs-twitch-vs-youtube/), [OneStream](https://onestream.live/blog/kick-vs-twitch/)

#### Revenue Split Comparison

| Platform | Standard Split | Best Split | $5 Sub Revenue |
|----------|---------------|------------|----------------|
| **Kick** | 95/5 | 95/5 | $4.75 |
| **Twitch** | 50/50 | 70/30 (Partner Plus) | $2.50-$3.50 |

#### Kick Advantages
- **95/5 split for everyone** (Twitch requires 350 recurring subs for 70/30)
- **Hourly pay incentive:** ~$16/hour for eligible streamers
- **Creator Incentive Program:** Guaranteed wages for qualifying streamers
- **100% of donations kept** (same as Twitch)
- **Major streamer migrations:** xQc ($100M contract), Adin Ross

#### Twitch Advantages
- **140M monthly users** (much larger audience)
- **Established ecosystem**
- **Discovery features mature**

#### Arbitrage Opportunity
**1.9x more revenue per subscriber on Kick.** For smaller streamers, Kick's 95/5 split is transformational. Consider dual-streaming or Kick-first strategy for maximum earnings per sub.
