## 3. Account Verification Checklist

**Run EVERY check before buying. no exceptions.**

### Before Purchase (Mandatory)

```
[ ] ENGAGEMENT RATIO CHECK
    - Instagram: 1-3% engagement rate = healthy. <0.5% = bot followers.
    - Twitter: 0.5-2% engagement rate = healthy. check reply quality.
    - TikTok: 3-8% engagement rate = healthy. check comment quality.
    - YouTube: 2-5% avg view-to-sub ratio = healthy.

[ ] COMMENT QUALITY AUDIT
    - Read 20+ comments on recent posts.
    - Real comments have questions, opinions, specific references to content.
    - Bot comments: "Great post!", single emojis, generic praise.
    - If 80%+ comments are generic = bot engagement = worthless account.

[ ] FOLLOWER GROWTH PATTERN
    - Ask seller for analytics screenshots showing follower growth over time.
    - Organic growth = gradual upward curve.
    - Bot growth = sudden spikes followed by plateaus or drops.
    - Use Social Blade (socialblade.com) to check publicly available data.

[ ] ACCOUNT AGE
    - Older = better. accounts 2+ years old have more trust with platforms.
    - Check account creation date (visible on Twitter, check "About" on YouTube).
    - Accounts under 6 months old = higher ban risk after transfer.

[ ] NICHE MATCH
    - Does the account's content history match your intended niche?
    - Completely pivoting a meme page into a business page = follower dropout.
    - Best case: buy an account already in your niche or a close adjacent.

[ ] BAN/STRIKE HISTORY
    - Ask seller directly: "Has this account ever been suspended, restricted, or warned?"
    - For YouTube: check Community Guidelines strikes (3 strikes = termination).
    - For Twitter: check if account has ever been in "read-only" mode.
    - For TikTok: check if account has shadowban history.

[ ] ORIGINAL EMAIL ACCESS
    - Can you change the email to yours?
    - Can you change the phone number?
    - Can you enable YOUR 2FA and remove theirs?
    - If seller says "no" to any of these = DO NOT BUY.

[ ] MONETIZATION STATUS (if applicable)
    - YouTube: Is it currently monetized? check Analytics for revenue proof.
    - TikTok: Is TikTok Shop enabled? (2-3x price premium, worth it).
    - Instagram: Is it a Creator/Business account?
    - Twitter: Is it subscribed to X Premium? (blue check).
```

### During Escrow (Mandatory)

```
[ ] Log in with provided credentials from a CLEAN browser/anti-detect profile.
[ ] Verify follower count matches listing.
[ ] Verify engagement rate matches listing.
[ ] Check DMs for any warnings or reports from platform.
[ ] Change password IMMEDIATELY.
[ ] Change email to YOUR email.
[ ] Add YOUR phone number.
[ ] Enable YOUR 2FA (authenticator app, not SMS).
[ ] Remove seller's recovery options.
```

## 7. Account Warming Protocol

**After buying an account, DO NOT immediately start posting your content. warm it first.**

### Week 1: Passive Engagement (Days 1-7)

```
- Log in 1-2x per day for 10-15 minutes.
- Scroll feed naturally.
- Like 5-10 posts in your niche.
- Read 3-5 full articles/threads.
- Follow 2-3 relevant accounts.
- DO NOT post anything.
- DO NOT change bio, profile pic, or display name.
- DO NOT follow/unfollow more than 10 accounts per day.
```

### Week 2: Light Activity (Days 8-14)

```
- Log in 2-3x per day for 15-20 minutes.
- Like 10-20 posts.
- Reply to 3-5 posts with genuine comments (2+ sentences, not generic).
- Retweet/share 1-2 posts.
- Follow 5-10 relevant accounts.
- Update bio slightly (don't overhaul it).
- Still DO NOT post original content.
```

### Week 3: Content Transition (Days 15-21)

```
- Post 1 piece of content every other day.
- Continue engaging with others (replies, likes, retweets).
- Gradually change profile pic and bio to your brand.
- Start following your target audience.
- Monitor for any platform warnings or restrictions.
```

### Week 4+: Full Operation

```
- Post 1-3x per day (platform dependent).
- Engage 20+ minutes per day.
- Use scheduling tools (Buffer, Publer) for consistency.
- Monitor analytics for any drops that might signal issues.
```

### Warming Shortcuts (If Budget Allows)

If you need to move faster (Ramadan launch deadline, for example):

- Compress the 3-week warmup into 10 days by doing more passive engagement per session.
- Buy accounts that are ALREADY active and posting regularly. pay the premium. an account that posted yesterday is worth 3x an account that last posted 6 months ago.
- Buy accounts from the same niche you want to operate in. less drastic pivot = less algorithmic suspicion.

## 9. Risk Assessment

### Ban Risks by Platform

| Platform | Transfer Detection | Ban Risk | Recovery Difficulty |
|----------|-------------------|----------|---------------------|
| **Twitter/X** | MODERATE. checks IP, device, behavior patterns. | MODERATE. accounts get suspended but can often be appealed. | EASY-MODERATE. appeals usually work if account has real history. |
| **Instagram** | HIGH. aggressive fingerprinting, login location tracking. | HIGH. Instagram bans are harder to appeal. action blocks are common. | HARD. Instagram support is notoriously unresponsive. |
| **TikTok** | MODERATE. checks device ID, IP, behavior. | MODERATE-HIGH. shadowbans are common and silent. | MODERATE. can often recover by changing content approach. |
| **YouTube** | LOW. Google is less aggressive on login detection. | LOW. YouTube rarely bans for ownership changes. | EASY. YouTube support is best of all platforms. |

### Risk Mitigation Checklist

```
[ ] NEVER log into a purchased account from your main browser.
[ ] NEVER log into two purchased accounts from the same IP.
[ ] NEVER change everything at once (bio, pic, name, content style).
[ ] ALWAYS use anti-detect browser with unique fingerprint per account.
[ ] ALWAYS use dedicated proxy per account (mobile or residential).
[ ] ALWAYS warm up for at least 7 days before heavy posting.
[ ] ALWAYS keep engagement looking natural (no 0-to-100 posting volume).
[ ] NEVER use automation tools in the first 2 weeks after transfer.
[ ] ALWAYS verify you have FULL control before confirming escrow.
[ ] KEEP old content on the account for at least 30 days before deleting.
```

### Legal Status

- Buying/selling social media accounts is LEGAL in most jurisdictions.
- It violates the Terms of Service of every major platform.
- TOS violation = account risk, not legal risk. platforms can ban you but can't sue you.
- You will NOT face legal consequences for buying accounts.
- Exception: buying accounts to commit fraud, impersonation, or scams IS illegal.

### Worst Case Scenarios

| Scenario | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|----------|-----------|------------|

| Seller recovers account after escrow | 5-10% | Use escrow platforms only. change ALL credentials immediately. |
