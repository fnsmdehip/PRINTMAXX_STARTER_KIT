## SYNERGY SCORING METHODOLOGY

### How Scores Are Calculated

**Components (100 points total):**
- Revenue Multiplier (40 points): How much more revenue vs single method
- Implementation Ease (20 points): Time and effort to deploy
- Time to First Dollar (15 points): How fast it generates revenue
- Cross-Niche Applicability (10 points): How many niches can use it
- Validation Strength (10 points): How much proof exists
- Platform Risk (5 points): Inverse - lower risk = higher score
