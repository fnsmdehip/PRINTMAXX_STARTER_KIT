## REDBUBBLE UPLOAD STEPS (Per Design)

1. Go to https://www.redbubble.com/portfolio/manage_works
2. Click "Add New Work"
3. Upload the 4500x5400 PNG
4. **Title**: Use design name + descriptive keywords
   - Example: "Trust the Process Proverbs 3:5 Christian Faith T-Shirt Design"
5. **Tags (up to 15)**: Paste relevant tags from design spec
6. **Description**: 2-3 sentences about the design
7. **Enable ALL products** (t-shirt, hoodie, mug, poster, sticker, phone case, tote, etc.)
8. **Set markup**: 30-40% on each product type
9. **Position the design** on each product mockup (center, adjust size)
10. Publish
