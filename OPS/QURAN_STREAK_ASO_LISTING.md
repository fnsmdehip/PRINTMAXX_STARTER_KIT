## DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max — paste directly)

Build a lifelong relationship with the Quran. One ayah. One day. One streak at a time.

Quran Streak helps Muslims develop consistent daily Quranic reading habits through streak tracking, beautiful verse displays, and smart reminders. Whether you are a student of the Quran, a parent building family habits, or simply returning after a break — Quran Streak meets you where you are.

DAILY AYAH
Receive a new verse every day with Arabic text, transliteration, and English translation. See the full context. Learn the meaning, not just the sounds.

STREAK TRACKING
Track your unbroken reading streak. Consistency builds mastery. Miss a day and your streak resets — the pressure is the point.

RAMADAN MODE
During the blessed month, Ramadan Mode adds Tarawih tracking, Juz Al-Quran progress, and iftar reminders alongside your daily reading. Make the most of every night.

PRAYER TIME INTEGRATION
Display your local prayer times. Stay synced to the Islamic day. Never miss Fajr because you forgot to check.

PROGRESS DASHBOARD
See your longest streak, total days read, ayahs completed, and consistency percentage over time. Data-driven spiritual growth.

COMMUNITY WALL
Share your milestone streaks anonymously. See how the global ummah is doing. You are not reading alone.

CUSTOM REMINDERS
Set notifications for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, or Isha. Or any time that fits your routine. No excuses.

OFFLINE FIRST
Everything works without internet. Airport, mountain, basement, blackout — your streak never breaks because of a bad connection.
