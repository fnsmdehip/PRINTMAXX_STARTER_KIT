## What to Check Before Buying (Red Flags)

### Must-Check Checklist

1. **Account age** - Older accounts (2+ years) perform better and face lower suspension risk. Avoid accounts created in last 6 months
2. **Follower authenticity** - Review follower list manually. Look for real profiles with bios, posts, profile photos. Bot followers = worthless
3. **Engagement rate** - Calculate: (avg likes + replies per post) / followers. Below 1% on a small account = likely bot followers
4. **Engagement ratio** - 10K likes but 3 comments = suspicious. Authentic engagement has a natural like-to-comment ratio (roughly 20:1 to 50:1)
5. **Posting history** - Check for spam, automation patterns, or sudden topic shifts. Consistent niche posting = good sign
6. **Account restrictions** - Ask seller if account has ever been suspended, locked, or restricted. Check for shadowban
7. **Monetization eligibility** - If the account has X Premium/ad revenue sharing, verify it is in good standing
8. **Transfer completeness** - Seller must provide: email access, phone number change, 2FA transfer, all recovery methods revoked

### Red Flags (DO NOT BUY)

- Sudden follower spikes (bought followers, will drop)
- High followers but zero engagement (bot farm)
- Account age does not match follower count (1 month old with 50K followers)
- Seller refuses to use escrow
- Seller cannot provide analytics/insights screenshots
- Generic comments only ("Great post!", fire emojis, single words)
- Account has active strikes or policy violations
- Seller wants payment outside the platform
- Price too good to be true (5K followers for $10 = bot account)

## Post-Purchase Warmup/Transition Protocol

This is the most critical part. Rushing this gets accounts suspended.

### Day 0: Secure the Account (Immediately)

- [ ] Change password to a strong unique one
- [ ] Change registered email to your own
- [ ] Change phone number to your own
- [ ] Enable 2FA with your authenticator app
- [ ] Revoke all connected apps/sessions
- [ ] Verify previous owner cannot regain access via recovery
- [ ] Do NOT change username, display name, bio, or profile pic yet

### Days 1-3: Observation Period

- [ ] Do NOT post anything yet
- [ ] Log in once per day from your primary device only
- [ ] Browse timeline, like 2-3 posts per day (in niche)
- [ ] Follow 0 new accounts
- [ ] Do NOT change any profile details

### Days 4-7: Light Activity

- [ ] Like 5-10 posts per day (all in niche)
- [ ] Reply to 1-2 posts per day (thoughtful, not generic)
- [ ] Follow 3-5 accounts per day (in niche, real accounts only)
- [ ] Still no posting yet
- [ ] Introduce DMs or links: DO NOT. Too early

### Days 8-14: Begin Posting

- [ ] Post 1x per day (niche-relevant content)
- [ ] Continue liking and replying naturally
- [ ] Update bio to new brand (subtle change, not a complete overhaul in one shot)
- [ ] Change profile picture
- [ ] Follow 5-10 accounts per day
- [ ] No links in posts yet

### Days 15-21: Ramp Up

- [ ] Post 2-3x per day
- [ ] Begin including links sparingly (1 link per 5 posts max)
- [ ] Change username if needed (do this once, not multiple times)
- [ ] Update display name
- [ ] Engage in threads and spaces
- [ ] Follow 10-15 accounts per day

### Days 22-30: Full Operation

- [ ] Post on normal schedule (3-5x per day)
- [ ] Include links as needed
- [ ] Run the account as if it is fully your own
- [ ] Monitor for any restrictions or shadowban
- [ ] Compare metrics against organic account

### Critical Rules During Warmup

1. **One device, one IP** - Do not log in from multiple devices or locations during warmup
2. **No automation** - No scheduling tools, no bots, no bulk follows during first 30 days
3. **No sudden behavior changes** - Gradual transition only. X flags sudden spikes in any activity
4. **No links in DMs** - Fastest way to get restricted. Wait until day 30+
5. **Use anti-detect browser if managing multiple accounts** - Each account needs its own browser profile with unique fingerprint
6. **Match the timezone** - If the account was active in EST, keep logging in during EST hours initially

## Risk Assessment

### Risks of Buying Pre-Warmed Accounts

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Account suspension post-transfer | Medium (15-25%) | High - lose account + money | Use escrow, follow warmup protocol |
| Seller reclaims account | Low (5-10% with escrow) | High - lose account + money | Change ALL credentials immediately |
| Fake/bot followers that drop | Medium (20-30%) | Medium - inflated metrics | Verify follower quality before buying |
| Shadowban during transition | Medium (10-20%) | Medium - reduced reach | Follow warmup protocol strictly |
| Platform detects ownership change | Low-Medium (10-15%) | High - suspension | Anti-detect browser, gradual changes |
| Money lost on bad account | Low with escrow (5%) | Medium - budget hit | Use Fameswap/Swapd escrow only |
