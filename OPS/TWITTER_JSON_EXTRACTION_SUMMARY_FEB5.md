## Key Patterns Identified

### 1. Multi-Account TikTok App Promotion
Multiple entries confirm: successful apps run 5-15 TikTok accounts simultaneously, all promoting the same app with different hooks. AI-generated avatars for the ads. 3 hook templates repeated across all accounts. POV Camera ($150K MRR) uses 11 accounts.

### 2. Emotion-Driven UGC
Data from 10K+ video analysis: negative emotions (confused, frustrated, struggling, shocked, tired) generate 10x more views than positive. 5-7 second clips filmed on phone. No script needed. $60 for 100 reaction clips = best ROI in content.

### 3. Hard Paywall + Long Onboarding
Walking app at $750K/mo uses hard paywall with 28-step onboarding quiz. Long onboarding creates psychological investment: users who complete 28 steps are more likely to pay. Counter-intuitive but proven.

### 4. Content Sites Need Fewer Articles Than Expected
@Hightrafficsite proves: 44 articles at DR7 can generate $6-8K/mo. Quality keyword research > article volume. Captions/quotes niche hit 1M traffic at DR13 in 9 months.

### 5. AI App Factory Model Validated
Astrology ($70K MRR/90 days), Bible ($100K/mo), tattoo ($100K/mo from 40K downloads), walking ($750K/mo) -- all AI-powered apps with TikTok-first marketing. Build fast, promote via short-form video, iterate.

### 6. Geographic Info Product Arbitrage
Take proven US info products, translate/rebrand for non-English markets. Same frameworks, different language. Latin American B2B = wide open. First-mover advantage in non-English markets.
