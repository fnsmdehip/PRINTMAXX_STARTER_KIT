# Reply Guy Target Lists — Per-Account Reply Targets

**Created:** 2026-02-14
**Purpose:** Curated target lists of 10-20 accounts per niche for the reply guy strategy. Reply within 15 min of their posts for 30x visibility vs original posts. Author-engaged reply = +75 algorithm weight (150x a like).
**Updated:** 2026-02-14
**Reference:** `OPS/GREY_HAT_EDGE_GROWTH_MASTER.md` Section 2D

---

## How to Use This Document

1. Set up notifications for each target account (turn on post notifications in X)
2. When they post, reply within 15 minutes with genuine value-add
3. Reference specific content (show you actually read their post)
4. Daily targets by your follower count: 0-1K = 20-30 replies/day, 1K-10K = 30-50, 10K+ = 50+
5. Use SuperX ($29/mo) to identify pre-viral posts from these targets
6. Use Apex Agents for AI-assisted reply drafting (maintain your voice)
7. Rotate through the list daily. Don't reply to the same person 5x in a row.
8. Mix large accounts (visibility play) with smaller accounts (reciprocity play)

- Replies sent per day (target: 20-50 depending on follower count)
