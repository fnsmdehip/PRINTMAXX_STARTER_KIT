## SECTION 3: BETTER OPTIONS FOUND

Cases where existing docs recommend Tool X but new research found Tool Y with a better free tier.

### 1. Anti-Detect Browser: Dolphin{anty} > GoLogin (for free tier)

| Factor | GoLogin (Current Pick) | Dolphin{anty} (Better Free Tier) |
|--------|----------------------|----------------------------------|
| Free profiles | 3 | **10** |
| Duration | Forever | Forever |
| Paid from | $49/mo | $89/mo |
| Existing docs status | Default Tier 0 pick | Listed in COMPREHENSIVE_STACK_COMPARISON but NOT recommended |

**Verdict:** Dolphin{anty} gives 3.3x more free profiles than GoLogin. For bootstrapping with multiple social accounts, 10 profiles covers more ground than 3. GoLogin has better UX and documentation, but Dolphin{anty} wins on free tier value. **Incogniton also offers 10 free profiles** and was already listed in COMPREHENSIVE_STACK_COMPARISON.

**Recommendation:** Switch Tier 0 default to Dolphin{anty} (10 free) OR Incogniton (10 free). Keep GoLogin as backup.

### 2. Email Warmup: TrulyInbox > Built-in warmup tools

| Factor | Built-in (Instantly/Smartlead) | TrulyInbox (New Find) |
|--------|-------------------------------|----------------------|
| Cost | $37-97/mo (bundled with cold email tool) | $0 forever |
| Standalone warmup | N/A (requires the cold email tool) | Yes, works with any inbox |
| Capacity | Depends on plan | Unlimited |

**Verdict:** TrulyInbox provides free standalone email warmup that works independently of your cold email tool choice. This is significant because BOOTSTRAP_STACK_ANALYSIS says "Email warmup cannot DIY reliably - BUY." TrulyInbox challenges that assumption.

**Recommendation:** Add TrulyInbox as Tier 0 email warmup. Still use built-in warmup from Instantly/Smartlead when you subscribe, but TrulyInbox covers the gap before you can afford those tools.

### 3. Proxies: Webshare/Bright Data free tiers > Soax paid ($50/mo minimum)

| Factor | Soax (Current Tier 0-1) | Webshare Free | Bright Data Free |
|--------|------------------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Cost | $50/mo minimum | $0 | $0 |
| What you get | Mobile rotating proxies | 10 static datacenter IPs | 15 IPs + 2GB/mo |
| Quality | High (mobile) | Medium (datacenter) | High (enterprise) |
| Good for | Social media automation | Scraping, email verification | Testing, light scraping |

**Verdict:** Webshare 10 free proxies + Bright Data 2GB/mo free covers light proxy needs at $0. Not a replacement for Soax mobile proxies for IG/TikTok (mobile fingerprint required), but eliminates the need for paid proxies for non-social tasks.

**Recommendation:** Add Webshare free + Bright Data free to Tier 0 for scraping/verification. Keep Soax for social media accounts (Tier 1+).

### 4. Newsletter/Email: Kit (ConvertKit) > Beehiiv for subscriber count

| Factor | Beehiiv (Current Default) | Kit/ConvertKit (Better Free Tier) |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Free subscribers | 2,500 | **10,000** (4x more) |
| Landing pages | Limited | Unlimited free |
| Custom domain | No (free tier) | No (free tier) |
| Growth tools | Yes | Basic |
| Paid subs feature | No (free tier) | No (free tier) |

**Verdict:** Kit/ConvertKit now offers 10,000 free subscribers (up from 1,000 documented in existing files). That's 4x Beehiiv's free tier. However, Beehiiv has better growth tools (referral program, recommendations network).

**Recommendation:** Use Kit for pure subscriber volume, Beehiiv for growth features. Split test: Kit for the largest list, Beehiiv for the highest-engagement list.

### 5. Landing Pages: Systeme.io > Vercel free + separate tools

| Factor | Vercel + Beehiiv + Notion (Current Stack) | Systeme.io (New Find) |
|--------|------------------------------------------|----------------------|
| Cost | $0 (but requires dev knowledge) | $0 (no-code) |
| Funnels | Manual build | Built-in funnel builder |
| Email | Separate tool (Beehiiv) | Built-in (2K contacts) |
| Courses | Separate tool (Skool $99/mo) | Built-in free |
| Custom domain | Yes (via Cloudflare) | Yes (free) |
| Blog | Manual build | Built-in |
| Affiliate program | Manual | Built-in |

**Verdict:** Systeme.io is an all-in-one platform that replaces 3-4 separate tools for non-technical users. It's NOT a replacement for Vercel if you're building a custom Next.js site, but for quick landing pages, funnels, and course hosting, it's superior at $0.

**Recommendation:** Add Systeme.io as the no-code alternative for funnels/courses. Keep Vercel for custom dev work.

### 6. Social Scheduling: Tweetlio free > Hypefury paid

| Factor | Hypefury (Current X Pick) | Tweetlio (Better Value) |
|--------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| Free tier | 7-day trial only | Free plan available |
| Paid from | $19/mo | $12/mo |
| Existing docs | Listed at $19-49/mo | Listed at $12/mo but free plan not documented |

**Verdict:** Existing docs list Tweetlio at $12/mo paid. New research shows a free plan exists. For X/Twitter-specific scheduling, Tweetlio free beats Hypefury's trial-only model.

**Recommendation:** Update Tweetlio entry to note free plan. Use as Tier 0 X/Twitter scheduler alongside Buffer.

1. **The $0 tier just got much more powerful.** TrulyInbox (free warmup), Systeme.io (free funnels/courses/email), Dolphin{anty}/Incogniton (10 free anti-detect profiles), Webshare (10 free proxies), and Kit (10K free subs) dramatically expand what's possible at zero cost.

6. **Anti-detect browser default should change.** GoLogin (3 free) should be replaced by Dolphin{anty} or Incogniton (10 free) as the Tier 0 default. GoLogin remains a good paid option.

7. **Most "enterprise-only" proxy providers now have free tiers.** Bright Data (15 IPs + 2GB) and Oxylabs (5 IPs + 5GB) both offer free entry points. These were listed as $15/GB enterprise-only in existing docs.
