## SECTION 5: KEY INTELLIGENCE SIGNALS

### Market Gaps Identified

1. **Done-for-you AI websites for local biz:** B12 charges $1,999 setup + $49-$399/mo. We can offer $500-$997 one-time + $29/mo and still be profitable using AI generation tools.

2. **AI Visibility tracking (a la Squarespace):** Standalone tool that tracks how local businesses appear in ChatGPT/Perplexity answers. No standalone product exists. Potential SaaS play.

3. **Cold outreach + website builder bundle:** Nobody bundles "we'll build your site AND send cold emails to get you customers." This is the local biz dream package.

4. **Agency white-label gap:** 10Web charges $24-$250/mo for agency plans. Framer charges $100/mo for extra editors. Building a white-label AI site builder for agencies at $19/mo could capture market share.

5. **Wix/Squarespace overwhelming for local biz:** 282M Wix users but only 6.2M paid = 2.2% conversion. Most local biz owners sign up, get overwhelmed, and abandon. "We'll do it for you in 48 hours" positioning.

### Revenue Benchmarks

- Durable: $990K revenue / 3M users = $0.33 per user
- Framer: $50M ARR / 50K paying = $1,000 ARPU
- Wix: $1.76B / 6.2M paid = $284 ARPU
- Instantly: $20M ARR / 17K customers = $1,176 ARPU
- Smartlead: $14M / 31K customers = $452 ARPU
