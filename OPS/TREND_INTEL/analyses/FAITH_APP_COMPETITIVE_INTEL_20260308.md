The faith app market is in a simultaneous Ramadan + Lent cycle for the first time in years, creating unprecedented seasonal demand. Hallow hit #1 on the App Store on Ash Wednesday (Feb 18), but is now dealing with Tucker Carlson partnership backlash. Muslim Pro is printing $300K/month during Ramadan. The spiritual wellness apps market is projected at $2.89B in 2026, growing 14.66% CAGR.

Our window: denomination-specific streak apps fill a gap NONE of these players are addressing. PrayerLock (Islamic) and Scripture Streak variants (Christian denominations) target the micro-niche that the big players ignore.
