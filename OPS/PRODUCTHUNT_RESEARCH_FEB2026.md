## AI Tools - Detailed Analysis

### AI Dictation & Notetakers

**Wispr Flow**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/wisprflow
- What: Mac dictation that works EVERYWHERE (not just meetings)
- Differentiator: Learns your writing style, auto-edits, command mode
- Pricing: ~$10/mo
- User quote: "completely changed how I interact with my devices, I rarely type anymore"
- **Clone Opportunity (HIGH):** Faith-specific dictation for sermon notes, prayer journaling. Fitness dictation for workout logging.

**Granola**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/granola
- What: AI notepad that blends YOUR notes with transcription
- Differentiator: NO meeting bot joins calls (privacy-first), transcribes system audio
- Pricing: Freemium
- User quote: "champion for personal and individual notes"
- **Clone Opportunity (MEDIUM):** Bible study note-taker that enriches notes with scripture references.

### AI Flashcard & Learning Apps

**Figy.ai**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/figy-ai

- Differentiator: "Flashcards that grow" - unlimited cards, keeps making more as you progress
- Pricing: Free
- **Clone Opportunity (HIGHEST):**

  - **FitCards** - Upload workout protocol → AI flashcards for form cues, rep schemes
  - **StudyLock integration** - Lock phone until flashcard review complete

**KardsAI**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/kardsai-mobile-app
- What: Paste text, upload PDF, or prompt any topic → instant flashcards
- Differentiator: Mobile-first, works from prompts (not just documents)
- **Clone Opportunity (HIGH):** Sermon-to-flashcards app for faith niche

**PDF2Anki**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/pdf2anki
- What: Convert PDFs directly to Anki flashcard format
- Differentiator: Medical students love it - automates tedious Anki card creation
- **Clone Opportunity (HIGH):** Bible PDF to Anki deck converter

**Turbo AI (TurboLearn)**
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com/products/turbo-ai-turbolearn-ai-2
- What: Notes, flashcards, quizzes, podcasts from audio/PDFs/YouTube
- Users: 5M+ users, trusted by Harvard/MIT
