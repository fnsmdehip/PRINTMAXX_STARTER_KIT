## STEP 9: First 5 Reviews Strategy (CRITICAL - Start Day 1)

Fiverr algorithm heavily weights early reviews. No reviews = invisible. 5 reviews with 5.0 rating = page 1 for your keywords.

### Strategy A: r/slavelabour Quick Reviews (fastest)

- [ ] Go to https://www.reddit.com/r/slavelabour
- [ ] Create a post offering your cheapest gig at a steep discount

**Post template (copy-paste):**
```
[OFFER] I will write a 1,000-word SEO blog post for $5

I'm a new Fiverr seller building my portfolio. I'll write a 1,000-word SEO-optimized article on any topic for $5 (normally $15).

The only ask: leave an honest review on Fiverr after delivery.

I use AI for the first draft and human-edit every piece for accuracy and flow. Turnaround: 24 hours.

DM me your topic. First 5 people only.
```

- [ ] Repeat with variations for other gigs:
  - "I will clip 1 viral moment from your YouTube video for $5"
  - "I will audit your website and tell you what's broken for $10"
  - "I will create a 7-day content calendar for $5"

### Strategy B: Direct Outreach to Prospects (higher quality)

- [ ] Find 10 podcasters/YouTubers with 1K-50K subscribers (small enough to respond)
- [ ] DM them on Twitter or email:

**Outreach template:**
```
Hey [name],

I watched your latest [video/episode] and found 3 moments that would crush as short-form clips on TikTok/Reels.

Want me to make one for free? If you like it, I'd love for you to order through my Fiverr page (I'm a new seller building reviews).

No obligation. Just want to show what I can do.

[Fiverr gig link]
```

- [ ] Deliver free sample within 24 hours
- [ ] Follow up: "If that was useful, here's my Fiverr link for more clips"

### Strategy C: Fiverr Buyer Requests (passive)

- [ ] Check "Buyer Requests" section daily (Fiverr dashboard -> Buyer Requests)
- [ ] Apply to every relevant request with a personalized pitch
- [ ] Respond within 1 hour of any buyer request appearing (speed matters)

## STEP 10: Fiverr SEO Optimization (do after publishing all 5)

- [ ] Check that all gig titles include high-search keywords
- [ ] Verify all 5 tags per gig are relevant buyer-intent keywords
- [ ] Turn on "Buyer Requests" notifications (mobile + email)
- [ ] Set your online status to "Available" (shows green dot to buyers)
- [ ] Set response time expectation: reply to ALL messages within 1 hour
- [ ] Enable push notifications on Fiverr mobile app

### Fiverr Algorithm Ranking Factors (what to optimize for)

1. **Response time** - reply to every message within 1 hour. This is the #1 ranking factor for new sellers.
2. **Order completion rate** - deliver every order on time, no cancellations
3. **Review score** - 5.0 stars in first 10 reviews
4. **Gig clicks / impressions** - your thumbnails determine this (make them clean)
5. **Order-to-impression ratio** - high conversion = Fiverr shows you more
6. **Repeat buyers** - returning clients boost your ranking
