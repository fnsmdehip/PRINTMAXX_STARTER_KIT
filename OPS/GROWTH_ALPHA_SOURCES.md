### Shadowban Detection

| Tool | URL | Method |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Yuzurisa** (gold standard) | https://shadowban.yuzurisa.com/ | No account connection needed, checks search/reply visibility |
| **HiSubway** | https://hisubway.online/shadowban/ | Free, quick check |
| **Bulkoid** | https://bulkoid.com/twitter-shadowban-checker | Checks search, replies, followers feed |
| **Circleboom** | https://circleboom.com/twitter-management-tool/twitter-search-tool/twitter-shadowban-test | Most features |
| **Manual test** | Incognito browser | Log out, search your tweets/username — if invisible, you're banned |

### Shadowban Recovery

- Stop ALL automation immediately
- Reduce activity to Week 1 levels for 48-72 hours
- Remove any suspicious links from bio
- Delete any tweets that got flagged
- Most temporary bans reset within 15 min to 24 hours
- Repeated violations = permanent suspension

### Critical Rules

1. **NEVER automate likes, follows, retweets** — this is the #1 suspension trigger
2. Vary action timing — uniform patterns trigger AI detection even from manual actions
3. Don't post links in first 5 tweets of a new account
4. Complete profile (bio, avatar, banner) before ANY activity
5. Verify email + phone number immediately
6. Don't follow/unfollow the same accounts repeatedly

### Hard Platform Limits

| Action | Daily Max | Hourly Max | Per Session | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|------------|-------|
| **Follows** | 200 | 10 | 15 | 10-15 min break between sessions |
| **Likes** | 500 | ~50 | ~30 | Exceeding = 24hr block |
| **Comments** | 200 | 20-30 | ~15 | Must be genuine, varied text |
| **Video uploads** | 12 (established) | N/A | N/A | New accounts: max 3/day first 3 days |
| **DMs** | ~50 | ~10 | N/A | New accounts: severely restricted |

### TikTok Account Warmup Schedule (7-Day Plan)

```
DAY 1: Browse only
  - Download app, complete profile (bio, avatar)
  - Browse FYP for 15-20 minutes
  - Watch videos FULLY (don't skip)
  - Like 5-10 videos in your niche
  - Follow 5-8 accounts in your niche
  - DO NOT POST

DAY 2: Light engagement
  - Browse 20-30 minutes
  - Like 10-15 videos
  - Follow 10-15 accounts
  - Leave 3-5 genuine comments (10+ words each)
  - DO NOT POST

DAY 3: First posts
  - Continue engagement (likes, follows, comments)
  - Post 1 video (original content, NOT repost)
  - Respond to any comments on your video

DAY 4-5: Ramp up
  - Post 2-3 videos/day
  - Like 15-20 videos
  - Follow 10-15 accounts
  - Leave 5-10 comments
  - Engage with duets/stitches

DAY 6-7: Normal activity
  - Post 3 videos/day
  - Full engagement (likes, follows, comments at safe levels)
  - Start using trending sounds/hashtags

AFTER DAY 7:
  - Can increase to 6-12 videos/day
  - Maintain consistent daily posting
  - If 15 videos in and still 0 views = shadowbanned, take 2-3 day break
```

### TikTok Proxy Requirements

- **Mobile proxies (4G/5G) are MANDATORY** for multi-account
- Most real TikTok users are on mobile data = highest trust score
- Residential proxies work but are second-tier
- **NEVER use datacenter proxies** — instant detection
- **NEVER use VPNs** — TikTok detects and penalizes VPN usage
- One proxy per account (don't share IPs between accounts)
- Use same proxy consistently (IP switching triggers flags)

### TikTok Shadowban Signs

1. Zero views after 15+ videos
2. Views stuck at exactly 0 or capped at ~200
3. Videos not appearing in hashtag feeds
4. FYP reach drops to zero suddenly
5. "Under review" messages on uploads

### TikTok Shadowban Recovery

- Stop posting for 2-3 days
- Continue browsing/engaging passively
- Delete any flagged content
- Don't change account details during ban
- Resume with 1 video/day, ramp back up slowly
