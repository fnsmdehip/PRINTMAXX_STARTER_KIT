### Inbox Placement Rates

- **Outlook:** 75.6% (lowest)

- SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication
- DMARC p=reject becoming industry standard (no longer p=none)
- Bounce rate under 2%
- Spam complaints under 0.3%

- Intent signals (hiring/funding/role changes/launches) outperform generic outreach
- Too polished = AI flag. Casual beats perfect.
