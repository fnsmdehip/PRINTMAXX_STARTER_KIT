## THE UPSELL STACK

Every PDF product has a natural service extension. The product is the proof. The service is the revenue.

```
$7-$47 PDF purchase (proves buying intent)
    -> Last page: "Want me to do this FOR you?"
    -> Calendly link to free 15-min strategy call
    -> 15-min call qualifies, pitches service
    -> Service closes at $300-$2,000
```

**Conversion math:**
- 100 PDF sales -> 10 view high-ticket page (10%) -> 3 book call (30%) -> 1 close (33%) = 1 client
- Average service = $750
- 100 PDF sales = $750 in services + ~$2,000 in PDF revenue = $2,750 total
- Per-PDF-buyer value: $27.50
