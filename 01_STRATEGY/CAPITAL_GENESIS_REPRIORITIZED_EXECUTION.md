## 3. A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

Every method gets tested. No assumptions survive contact with real users.

### Universal A/B Testing Rules

1. **Minimum 2 variants per method.** Always test, never guess.
2. **14-day test cycles for Tier 1-2.** 30-day for Tier 3-4.
3. **Define success metric BEFORE launch.** Not after.
4. **Kill fast.** If a variant shows zero traction at Day 14, it's dead.

## 5. META-STRATEGY SUMMARY

The system operates on 7 principles. Violate any of them and the machine breaks.

### Principle 1: Launch Everything Bootstrapable on Day 1

If it costs $0 and you can list it today, list it today. Notion templates on Gumroad. Content accounts posting. Digital products live. No waiting for perfection. Revenue compounds from the moment you start, not from the moment you plan to start.

### Principle 2: A/B Test from Launch

Never launch a single variant of anything. Always 2-3 variants. The market decides what works, not your intuition. Your intuition is wrong 60% of the time. Data is wrong 10% of the time. Use data.

### Principle 3: Kill Losers Fast (2-Week Checkpoints)

At Day 14, every variant faces judgment. Zero traction? Dead. Pivot the format, angle, or niche. Don't throw good time after bad time. The sunk cost fallacy is the #1 killer of solopreneur momentum.

### Principle 4: Scale Winners Aggressively (3x Effort)

When something works, triple down. 3x the content volume. 3x the ad spend. 3x the product variants. Winners are rare. When you find one, extract every dollar from it before the window closes.

### Principle 5: Continuously Add Methods from Alpha Research

The mega ralph loop runs every night scanning 81+ sources. New methods get discovered weekly. The portfolio grows organically. You're not limited to the 7 lanes you start with. By Month 6 you should have 12-15 active lanes. By Month 12, 20+.

### Principle 6: Never Let Any Single Method/Platform/Niche Dominate

Concentration risk kills solopreneurs. The diversification matrix enforces discipline. 40% max per method. 50% max per platform. 40% max per niche. When something grows too big relative to the portfolio, invest in the weak spots.

### Principle 7: Reinvest Revenue into Higher-Ceiling Methods

Tier 1-2 methods generate seed capital. That capital funds Tier 3-4 methods with 10-100x higher ceilings. Templates fund apps. Apps fund SaaS. SaaS funds acquisitions. The escalation ladder:

```
$0/mo:      Tier 1 (templates, content) -> generates $200-500/mo
$500/mo:    Tier 2 (AI personas, UGC, newsletters) -> generates $1k-3k/mo
$3k/mo:     Tier 3 (apps, cold outbound) -> generates $5k-15k/mo
$15k/mo:    Tier 4 (paid ads, agency, SaaS) -> generates $25k-50k/mo
$50k/mo:    Acquisitions + investments -> generates $100k+/mo
```

### The Machine Grows Itself

The mega ralph loop (6-phase rotating cycle) handles research, content generation, execution, and intelligence gathering autonomously. Human input required only for:
- Approving alpha (weekly, 30 min)
- Checking checkpoint items (daily, 10 min)
- A/B test decisions at 14-day marks (weekly, 20 min)
- Monthly portfolio audit (monthly, 1 hour)

Everything else runs on autopilot. Ralph loops discover. A/B tests validate. Kill criteria prune. Scale criteria amplify. The machine compounds.
