# 5 exercises you're doing wrong (and fixes)

Tweet 1:
I filmed my workouts for 2 weeks.

Turns out I was doing 5 major exercises with trash form.

No wonder my shoulders hurt and my bench stalled.

Here's what I fixed.

Tweet 2:
1. Bench press

Wrong: Elbows flared out at 90 degrees. Shoulders doing all the work.

Fix: Tuck elbows to 45 degrees. Press in a slight arc toward your face.

Shoulder pain gone in 1 week. Added 20 lbs in 6 weeks.

Tweet 3:
2. Squat

Wrong: Knees caving inward at the bottom. Lower back rounding.

Fix: Push knees out hard. Think "spread the floor apart."

Felt it in my quads immediately. Depth improved. Back pain disappeared.

Tweet 4:
3. Deadlift

Wrong: Pulling with my back first. Hips shooting up.

Fix: Push the floor away with legs. Hips and shoulders rise together.

This one took 3 weeks to feel natural. Worth it. Back is safe now.

Tweet 5:
4. Overhead press

Wrong: Leaning back too far. Using momentum.

Fix: Squeeze glutes hard. Press straight up. Bar path close to face.

Strict form cut my weight by 20 lbs. Strength caught up in 8 weeks.

Tweet 6:
5. Barbell row

Wrong: Using my lower back and momentum. Barely hitting lats.

Fix: Hinge at hips. Pull to belly button, not chest. Squeeze at the top.

Lats started growing. Lower back stopped hurting.
