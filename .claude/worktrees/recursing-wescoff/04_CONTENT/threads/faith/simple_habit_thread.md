# The simple habit that changed my perspective

**Tweet 1:**
One habit changed my entire perspective on faith.

It's embarrassingly simple.

**Tweet 2:**
I started saying "thank you" out loud.

For everything.

Coffee. Sunrise. Paycheck. Traffic light turning green.

**Tweet 3:**
Week 1: Felt ridiculous.

Saying thank you to an empty room.

Did it anyway.

**Tweet 4:**
Week 2: Started noticing things I usually ignore.

Hot water. Clean clothes. Working car.

Things I took for granted.

**Tweet 5:**
Week 3: My complaining dropped by half.

Hard to complain and give thanks at the same time.

Your brain picks one.

**Tweet 6:**
Week 4: Started thanking God for things before I got them.

For answers I hadn't seen yet.

Changed how I waited.

**Tweet 7:**
Now it's automatic.

I wake up and start listing things.

My whole day looks different.
