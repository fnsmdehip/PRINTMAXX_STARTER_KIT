# Cold outbound playbook: email, LinkedIn, cold calls 2026

## Quick answer

Multi-channel outbound (email + LinkedIn + calls) converts 3-5x better than single-channel. The system: 7-email sequence over 25 days, LinkedIn connection + 3 messages in parallel, VA cold calling for high-value prospects. Average reply rate: 28-47% across all channels combined. Cost: $80-200/month for tools + $200-400/month for VA.

## The multi-channel sequence

### Week 1-2: Email + LinkedIn parallel

Day 0: Send email 1 + LinkedIn connection request
Day 3: Send email 2 (case study)
Day 5: LinkedIn message 1 (if connected)
Day 7: Send email 3 (insight)
Day 10: LinkedIn message 2
Day 11: Send email 4 (social proof)

### Week 3-4: Escalation

Day 15: Send email 5 (resource with link)
Day 17: LinkedIn voice note (60 seconds)
Day 20: Send email 6 (direct ask)
Day 22: VA cold call (if no response to anything)
Day 25: Send email 7 (breakup)

### Results by channel

| Channel | Reply rate | Meeting rate | Cost per lead |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Cold email alone | 5-15% | 1-3% | $2-5 |
| LinkedIn alone | 10-20% | 2-5% | $5-10 |
| Cold calling alone | 15-25% | 3-7% | $10-20 |
| Multi-channel combined | 28-47% | 5-12% | $8-15 |

## Email infrastructure

### DNS setup (30 minutes)

SPF, DKIM, DMARC on a separate domain. Never use your main domain for cold email. Target: 9+/10 on mail-tester.com.

### Domain warmup (14 days)

Day 1-3: 5 emails/day to your own accounts. Day 4-7: 10/day to contacts. Day 8-14: 20/day mix. Day 15+: scale to 50-100/day.

## LinkedIn outreach

### Connection request templates

Keep under 300 characters. Personalized to their profile.

Template: "Hey [name], saw your post about [topic]. Working on something similar for [their industry]. Would love to connect."

### Message sequence

Message 1 (day after connection): share a relevant resource, ask about their current approach to [problem].
Message 2 (3 days later): case study from their industry.
Message 3 (5 days later): direct ask for a call.

### LinkedIn automation warning

LinkedIn aggressively bans automation. Manual outreach only. Or use Taplio ($49/mo) for scheduling (not automation of DMs/connections).

Safe limits: 20-30 connection requests/day, 50 messages/day.

## Cold calling (VA-powered)

### Why cold calls work in 2026

Everyone assumes cold calling is dead. Which means fewer people do it. Which means less competition for attention. A phone call stands out when every inbox is flooded with emails.

### VA setup

Hire on Fiverr or OnlineJobs.ph. Cost: $3-8/hour. Train with a script and objection handling sheet. 100 calls/day per VA.

### Cold call script structure

Opening (10 seconds): "Hi [name], this is [VA name] calling on behalf of [your company]. I noticed [specific observation about their business]. We helped [similar company] [specific result]. Do you have 30 seconds?"

If yes: deliver value statement + ask for a meeting with the founder (you).
If no: "When would be better?" or "Can I send you a quick email?"

## FAQ

### How many prospects should I contact per month?

Start with 200/month (across all channels). Scale to 500-1,000/month as you optimize. Quality of targeting matters more than volume.

### What industries respond best to cold outreach?

Professional services (agencies, consultants), SaaS companies, e-commerce brands, and local businesses. Avoid: enterprise companies (long sales cycles), consumers (wrong channel).

### How do I avoid being marked as spam?

Separate domain, proper DNS, warmup period, plain text emails, no links in first email, personal subject lines, and never send more than 100/day per domain.

### When should I hire a VA?

When you consistently book 5+ meetings/month from outbound and want to scale. VA handles the calling, you handle the meetings.
