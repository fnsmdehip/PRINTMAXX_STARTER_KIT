## Quick Answer

You can launch a print business for $200-500 using print on demand (no inventory). Full budget: $50-100 for design tools, $0-200 for Shopify/store, $100-300 for initial marketing, $50-100 for samples. Skip equipment until you hit $5k/month revenue.

## FAQ Section

### Do I need an LLC to start?
No. Start as a sole proprietor. Form an LLC when you hit consistent revenue ($1000+/month) or if you want liability protection sooner. Don't let paperwork delay launching.

### Can I start with zero dollars?
Technically yes with free tools and Etsy. But $50-100 for samples dramatically improves your product photos and conversion rate. Worth the investment.

### What's the minimum to make a real profit?
You need about $200-300 to run a real test. This covers samples, basic marketing, and platform fees. Under $200, you're unlikely to gather enough data.

### Should I trademark my brand name first?
No. Trademark after you validate the business works. Trademarks cost $250-350 per class. That money is better spent on marketing when starting.

### Free vs paid Shopify themes?
Free themes work fine. Dawn (Shopify's free theme) converts well and looks professional. Don't pay $300+ for a theme until you're making $2000+/month.
