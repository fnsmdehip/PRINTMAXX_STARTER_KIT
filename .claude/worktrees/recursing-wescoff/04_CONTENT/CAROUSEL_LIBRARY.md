# Carousel Content Library

15 ready-to-use Instagram/LinkedIn carousels for solopreneur topics.

---

## Format Guide

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Hook/Title (bold, curiosity-inducing)
- Slides 2-8: Core content (one point per slide)
- Slide 9: Summary/Recap
- Slide 10: CTA (Follow, Save, Share)

**Design Specs:**
- Size: 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- Font: Large, readable (minimum 24pt for body)
- Colors: Consistent brand palette
- Text: Minimal per slide (max 20 words)
