Results compound slowly, then all at once.

Months 1-3: Nothing visible. Felt the same.
Months 4-6: Clothes fit different. Scale moved.
Months 7-9: People asked what I was doing.
Months 10-12: Looked in the mirror and didn't recognize myself.

The early months feel pointless. They're not. They're building the foundation.

Trust the process when you can't see the results yet.
