## What makes this sequence work

**Day 1-2: Credibility**
- Personal story shows you've been there
- No guru positioning
- Real struggles, real results

**Day 3-4: Education**
- Clear framework they can use immediately
- Practical, not theoretical
- Shows you know what you're talking about

**Day 5-6: Social proof + pitch**
- Real results from real people
- Soft pitch (not pushy)
- Clear value proposition

**Day 7: Final value**
- Give even more for free
- Set expectations for future emails
- Invite conversation

---

## Tracking metrics

Monitor:
- Open rates per email (should stay above 35%)
- Click rates on resources (should be 8-15%)
- Course conversion rate (target 2-5%)
- Reply rate (shows engagement)

If open rates drop below 30% after email 3, your content isn't resonating. Test different stories or problems.

If clicks are low but opens are high, your offers aren't clear or compelling enough.

---

## Common mistakes to avoid

1. **Pitching too early:** Let them get value first
2. **Being too salesy:** Faith audience hates this
3. **Generic content:** They can get Bible verses anywhere
4. **No personality:** Your story is your differentiator
5. **Too long:** Most emails should be under 300 words

---

## Customization tips

**Adjust for your offer:**
- Digital product: Focus on transformation stories
- Coaching: Emphasize personal connection
- Community: Highlight belonging and support
- Physical product: Show practical results

**Segment by action:**
- Downloaded resource but didn't buy: Send case study email
- Opened all emails but didn't click: Send personal outreach
- Bought in first 3 days: Move to customer onboarding sequence

---

## After the sequence

Move them to:
- Weekly newsletter (tips + stories)
- Launch sequence (when releasing new product)
- Nurture sequence (keep providing value)

Don't just stop emailing after day 7. The relationship is just starting.
