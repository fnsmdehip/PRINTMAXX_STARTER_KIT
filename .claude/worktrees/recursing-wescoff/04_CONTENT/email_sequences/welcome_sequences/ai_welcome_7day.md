### Body

Yesterday I mentioned the workflow. Here it is.

Every Monday I do this:

1. Write one short post about something I learned (10 min)
2. Paste it into Claude with this prompt: "Turn this into 3 Twitter posts, 2 LinkedIn posts, 1 newsletter intro, 2 thread hooks, and 2 quote graphics"
3. Review and edit the outputs (10 min)
4. Schedule everything using Buffer

Total time: 20-25 minutes
Output: 10+ pieces of content

The trick is the prompt structure. Most people ask Claude for "content ideas." That's too vague. You get generic responses.

Instead, start with YOUR idea. Then ask for specific formats with specific lengths.

Here's the exact prompt I use:

---
Take this post: [paste your post]

Create:
- 3 standalone tweets (under 280 chars each)
- 2 LinkedIn posts (150-200 words)
- 1 newsletter paragraph (100 words)
- 2 thread opening hooks

Keep my voice. No em dashes. No "in today's world" phrases.
---

Try it today with something you posted recently.
