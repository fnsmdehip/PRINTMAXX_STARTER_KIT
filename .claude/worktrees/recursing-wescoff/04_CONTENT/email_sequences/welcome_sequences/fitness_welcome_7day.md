Simple rules that actually work

### Body

Calorie counting works. I'm not saying it doesn't.

But I don't do it. Too tedious for my lifestyle.

Here's what I do instead:

**The hand method:**
- Protein: Palm-sized portion each meal
- Veggies: Fist-sized portion (or more)
- Carbs: Cupped handful
- Fats: Thumb-sized portion

**Simple rules:**
- Protein at every meal
- Eat vegetables with lunch and dinner
- Drink water before eating
- Stop when 80% full

**Weekly check:**
- Weigh yourself same time, same day, weekly
- Trending up? Slightly smaller portions
- Trending down? Slightly larger portions

That's it. No apps. No measuring. No obsessing.

I've maintained within 5 pounds of my target weight for 2 years using this approach.
