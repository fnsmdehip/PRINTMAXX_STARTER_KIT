# App launch sequence (5 emails)

Use this for: Launching SaaS products, mobile apps, or web applications. Focuses on problem-solving and free trial conversion.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Announcement + free trial |
| 2 | Day 2 | Problem and solution demo |
| 3 | Day 4 | Use cases and social proof |
| 4 | Day 6 | Feature deep dive |
| 5 | Day 7 | Final push (launch special) |

Expected open rate: 45-65%
Expected trial signup rate: 8-15%

---

## What makes this work

**Email 1: Clear problem + solution**
- States exactly what app does
- Free trial removes friction
- No credit card = trust signal

**Email 2: Show, don't tell**
- Video demo is worth 1000 words
- Time savings quantified
- Simple workflow shown

**Email 3: Real use cases**
- Shows versatility
- Helps people see themselves using it
- Social proof from real users

**Email 4: Feature transparency**
- Removes mystery about what's included
- Shows depth of product
- Invites feedback

**Email 5: Urgency + discount**
- Clear deadline
- Money-back guarantee
- Final push for fence-sitters

## Performance benchmarks

**Expected metrics:**
- Email 1: 60-75% open, 50% of trial signups
- Email 2: 45-60% open, 25% of trial signups
- Email 3: 40-55% open, 15% of trial signups
- Email 4: 35-50% open, 5% of trial signups
- Email 5: 50-65% open, 5% of trial signups

**Trial to paid conversion:**
- Target: 15-25%
- SaaS average: 12-18%
- Good onboarding improves this

---

## Common mistakes

1. **Too technical:** Explain benefits, not just features
2. **No demo:** People need to see it working
3. **Complicated pricing:** Keep it simple
4. **Weak onboarding:** First 5 minutes make or break trial conversion
5. **Ignoring trial users:** Email them during trial period

---

## Pricing strategy for apps

**Freemium:**
- Free tier with limits
- Paid tier removes limits
- Easiest to get users

**Free trial:**
- 7-14 days full access
- Credit card required or not (test both)
- Higher conversion if onboarding is good

**Free forever:**
- Premium features behind paywall
- Hard to convert but large user base
- Works for viral apps

**Launch pricing:**
- 50% off first year/month
- Lifetime deal for early adopters
- Creates urgency

## Trial onboarding sequence

During the 14-day trial, send:

**Day 1:**
- Welcome email
- Quick start guide
- Key features to try first

**Day 3:**
- Check-in: "How's it going?"
- Help with common issues
- Link to tutorials

**Day 7:**
- Midpoint reminder
- Share advanced tips
- Invite to customer community

**Day 11:**
- Trial ending in 3 days
- Show what they'll lose
- Discount code if needed

**Day 14:**
- Last day notification
- One-click upgrade
- Final discount offer

## App-specific tactics

**Mobile apps:**
- Show app store screenshots in emails
- Include rating if above 4.5 stars
- Mention downloads count if impressive
- Link to both iOS and Android

**SaaS products:**
- Offer live demo calls
- Create video walkthrough library
- Show integrations with popular tools
- Emphasize data security/privacy

**Web apps:**
- Browser extension first, then full app
- Show before/after workflow
- Emphasize no installation needed
- Mobile responsive screenshots

## Social proof elements

Include in emails:

**Quantitative:**
- "142 signups in first week"
- "Average user saves 5 hours/week"
- "4.8/5 rating from beta users"

**Qualitative:**
- Specific user testimonials
- Case study summaries
- Expert endorsements

**Visual:**
- Screenshots of positive tweets
- Video testimonials
- Before/after comparisons

---

## Handling objections

**"Too expensive"**
- Show ROI calculation (time saved × hourly rate)
- Offer annual pricing (cheaper per month)
- Compare to alternatives

**"Too complicated"**
- Show 2-minute setup video
- Offer onboarding call
- Highlight ease of use testimonials

**"Don't need all features"**
- Show basic use case
- Offer starter plan
- Explain they can grow into it

**"Not sure it works for my situation"**
- Show multiple use cases
- Offer personalized demo
- Free trial removes risk

## Post-launch priorities

**Week 1:**
- Monitor for bugs
- Respond to all feedback
- Track signup and activation rates
- Fix critical issues immediately

**Week 2-4:**
- Improve onboarding based on user behavior
- Add most-requested features
- Create more tutorial content
- Start trial-to-paid optimization

**Month 2-3:**
- Focus on retention
- Build user community
- Expand marketing channels
- Plan next major feature

## Follow-up sequences

**For trial users who convert:**
- Thank you + welcome to paid
- Advanced tips and tricks
- Invite to customer-only community
- Request testimonial after 30 days

**For trial users who don't convert:**
- Exit survey: "Why didn't you upgrade?"
- Limited-time discount offer
- Offer to jump on call
- Move to nurture sequence

**For people who never tried:**
- Highlight easiest use case
- Share success stories
- Offer 1-on-1 demo
- Survey about barriers

---

## Repurposing launch content

Turn launch emails into:

- YouTube demo video
- Blog post series
- Podcast interview talking points

Launch week content has high energy. Capture it across all channels.
