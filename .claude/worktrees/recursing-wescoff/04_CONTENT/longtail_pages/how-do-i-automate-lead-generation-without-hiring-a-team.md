## How to automate lead generation without hiring a team

You're running a solo business. You can't afford a full marketing team. But you need leads.

The good news: you don't need a team. You need a system.

Here's how to build one in a weekend.

## The automation framework

Lead generation has three steps. Automate each one.

**Step 1: Source leads.** Find people who might care about your product. (List of prospects.)

**Step 2: Contact them.** Send them a message. (Email, DM, or ad.)

**Step 3: Nurture them.** Follow up. Qualify. Move them into your sales process. (Email sequences.)

Most solopreneurs try to do all three manually. It's why they fail. You can't do 1000 manual outreach attempts per week.

## Step 1: Automate sourcing

You need a list of prospects. Manually finding them takes forever.

**Option 1: Use a list broker.**
Services like Hunter, Seamless, or Clearbit have databases of 500M+ email addresses. You search for your ideal customer (by job title, company size, industry). They export a CSV.

Cost: $100-300 for 5000 emails.
Time: 30 minutes to get a list.
Quality: 70-80% valid email addresses.

**Option 2: Scrape manually with Playwright.**
You find a directory (LinkedIn, ProductHunt, GitHub, industry list). You write a scraper to extract emails/contact info.

Cost: $0 (you write the code).
Time: 2-3 hours to build the scraper.
Quality: 90%+ (depends on the directory).

**Option 3: Use a SaaS like Clay or RocketReach.**
These combine data from multiple sources. Search for your ideal customer. They fill in contact info automatically.

Cost: $50-200/month.
Time: 5 minutes to set up a search.
Quality: 85%+.

For a solo founder, start with Option 1 (list broker). Fastest to results.

## Step 2: Automate contact

Now you have 5000 leads. You can't email them manually. You need automation.

**Email automation:**

Use a tool like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or a cold email platform (Lemlist, Outreach).

Setup:
1. Import your lead list.
2. Write 3-5 email templates (opening email, follow-up if no reply, follow-up if opened).
3. Set a schedule (send 20 emails per day, space them 2 minutes apart).
4. Let it run.

Cost: $20-50/month.
Time: 1 hour to write emails + set up.
Leads: 5000 emails in 250 days at 20/day.

**LinkedIn automation:**

Use a tool like Dripify or Lemlist with LinkedIn integration.

Setup:
1. Target prospects by job title, company, industry.
2. Send connection requests + follow-up messages.
3. Tool sends 30-50 per day automatically.

Cost: $50-100/month.
Time: 30 minutes to target + write messages.
Leads: 1500 connections in 50 days.

**Social media automation:**

Post your content on Twitter/LinkedIn/Facebook automatically. Attract inbound leads.

Setup:
1. Write 20 tweets/posts about your space.
2. Use Buffer or Later to schedule them.
3. Tool posts 3-5 per day.

Cost: $15/month.
Time: 1 hour to write content.
Leads: Varies (1-5 per week typically).

For fastest results: cold email + LinkedIn automation together. About 2000 real leads in 30 days.

## Step 3: Automate nurturing

People get your first email. Most won't reply. You need follow-ups.

**Email sequences:**

Use Zapier or Make to automate follow-ups.

Setup:
1. Person receives your first email → Zapier logs to a spreadsheet or CRM.
2. 3 days later → Zapier sends follow-up email.
3. If they reply → Move them to "qualified" list.
4. If they don't reply after 5 emails → Move to "not interested."

Cost: $10-20/month.
Time: 1 hour to set up.

**SMS follow-ups:**

If you have phone numbers, follow up via SMS.

Setup:
1. Email gets no reply after 2 days → SMS sent automatically (via Twilio + Zapier).
2. SMS says "Hey, saw my email?" + link to book a call.

Cost: $20-50/month (Twilio + Zapier).
Time: 30 minutes.

**AI chatbot qualifying:**

Use a tool like Chatsonic or ChatGPT API to auto-qualify leads.

Setup:
1. Lead signs up → chatbot asks qualifying questions.
2. Answers go to a spreadsheet.
3. Hot leads get flagged. You follow up personally.

Cost: $50-100/month.
Time: 2 hours to build.

**Week 2:** Set up LinkedIn automation. Write email sequences for follow-ups.

**Week 3:** Launch LinkedIn outreach. Start tracking which emails get replies.

**Week 4:** Analyze data. Optimize. Which subject lines work? Which follow-ups convert? Double down.

By the end of month 1, you'll have your first batch of qualified leads coming in automatically.

## Common mistakes

Sending the same email to everyone. Different industries, company sizes, and job titles need different messages. Test 2-3 versions and use the one that gets the best reply rate.

Not following up. The first email has a 2-3% reply rate. The 5th follow-up has 8-10%. You need persistence.

Using a personal email address. Lemlist, Dripify, etc., all flag you as bulk sender if you use Gmail from a personal account. Set up a custom domain and a proper email account first.

Automating the qualification. Some people think "chatbot does the sales job" but the best leads need a human touch. Use automation to filter, then you close.
