# Gumroad vs Whop vs Lemonsqueezy for digital products 2026

## Quick answer

Whop has the lowest total fees at 5.7%. Gumroad charges 10% + processing (13-14% total). Lemonsqueezy charges 5% + processing (~8% total) and handles EU VAT automatically. For solopreneurs selling $1,000+/month, switching from Gumroad to Whop saves $430-740/year per $10K in revenue.

## When to use each

### Gumroad: best for beginners

Pros: easiest setup (under 10 minutes), built-in audience discovery, clean checkout.
Cons: most expensive fees, limited customization, basic analytics.
Best for: your first 1-3 products when you just want to sell and not think about tech.

### Whop: best for scale

Pros: lowest total fees, growing marketplace, community features, course hosting.
Cons: newer platform, less brand recognition, marketplace still growing.
Best for: anyone selling $1,000+/month who wants to maximize revenue. Migrate from Gumroad here.

### Lemonsqueezy: best for international sellers

Pros: automatic EU VAT/tax handling, clean UI, good API.
Cons: 5% platform fee adds up, smaller marketplace.
Best for: sellers with significant EU customer base who do not want to deal with VAT compliance.

### Stripe direct: best for developers

Pros: lowest fees (2.9% + $0.30), full control.
Cons: need to build your own checkout, handle taxes, no marketplace.
Best for: developers who can build a custom checkout page in 2-4 hours.

## Migration guide: Gumroad to Whop

Step 1: Create Whop account and set up products (30 min).
Step 2: Import product listings (copy descriptions, upload files).
Step 3: Update all links on social media, website, bio.
Step 4: Offer existing customers a migration discount.
Step 5: Keep Gumroad active for 30 days during transition.

## FAQ

### Is Whop trustworthy?

Whop processes millions in transactions monthly. Backed by major investors. Growing fast as Gumroad alternative.

### Can I sell on multiple platforms simultaneously?

Yes. List on Gumroad AND Whop. Use Gumroad for marketplace discovery, Whop for direct sales. Shift traffic to Whop over time.

### What about Payhip?

Payhip has a free plan with 5% fee (no additional processing fee). Good for testing but limited features compared to Whop.

### Does Whop handle taxes?

Whop provides 1099 forms for US sellers. For international tax compliance, Lemonsqueezy or Paddle are better options.
