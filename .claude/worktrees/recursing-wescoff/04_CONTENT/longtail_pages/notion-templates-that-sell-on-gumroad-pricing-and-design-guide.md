# Notion templates that sell on Gumroad: pricing and design guide

## Quick answer

The best-selling Notion templates on Gumroad are priced $7-27. They solve one specific problem (habit tracking, content calendar, CRM). Design matters less than the cover image and description. List 5 templates in week 1. Revenue possible the same day. Top sellers make $2,000-10,000/month from Notion templates alone.

### What makes a template sell

- Solves one specific problem clearly
- Looks good in the cover image (dark mode, clean layout)
- Has a video walkthrough (30-60 seconds)
- Includes setup instructions
- Mobile-friendly (many Notion users are on phone)

## Design principles

Keep it minimal. Use 2-3 colors max. Dark backgrounds photograph better for Gumroad thumbnails. Add icons from Notion's built-in icon set. Use database views (gallery, board, calendar) for visual appeal.

Cover image tips:
- Show the template on a mockup device (Figma or Canva)
- Dark gradient background
- Template name in large, clean font
- One screenshot of the actual template
- Size: 1280x720px or 1600x900px

## Gumroad listing optimization

Title format: "[Specific use case] Notion Template" not "My Cool Template"

Description structure:
1. Problem statement (what struggle does this solve)
2. Screenshot or GIF of the template
3. Feature list (5-7 bullets)
4. Video walkthrough embed
5. "What's included" section
6. FAQ (2-3 questions)

## Marketing without ads

1. Post template previews on X/Twitter (screenshot + "free duplicate link in replies")
2. Share in Notion-related subreddits (r/Notion, r/NotionTemplates)
3. Create TikTok/Reels showing the template in use (30-second screen recording)
4. Cross-post to Pinterest (templates get saved and discovered for months)
5. Answer Notion questions on Reddit and link your template as the solution

## FAQ

### How long to make a Notion template?

Simple template: 1-2 hours. Complex dashboard: 4-8 hours. Bundle of 3: 6-12 hours total.

### Do I need to be a Notion expert?

No. Watch 2-3 YouTube tutorials on databases and formulas. That covers 90% of what you need.

### Can I sell templates someone else made?

No. Notion templates must be original. However, you can study competitor templates for inspiration and build your own version with your own design.

### What is the refund rate?

Digital products on Gumroad have 2-5% refund rates. Acceptable. Do not stress about it.
