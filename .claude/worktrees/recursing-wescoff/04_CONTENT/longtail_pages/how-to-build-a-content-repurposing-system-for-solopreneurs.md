# How to build a content repurposing system for solopreneurs

A content repurposing system turns 1 piece of original content into 6+ platform-specific posts in under 15 minutes. The system uses Claude for adaptation, Buffer for scheduling, and a simple hub-and-spoke model. Expected output: 180+ posts per month from 30 original pieces.

## Quick answer

Write one original piece per day (thread, blog post, or newsletter issue). Repurpose it across 5-6 platforms using format-specific templates. Total daily time: 15-20 minutes for repurposing once you have the system. Tools needed: Claude ($20/mo), Buffer ($15/mo), and Typefully ($12/mo). Total: $47/month.

## The hub-and-spoke model

| Step | Platform | Format | Time | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|------|-------|
| 1 (Hub) | X/Twitter | Thread (5-7 tweets) | 20 min (original) | This is your original creation |
| 2 | LinkedIn | Single-line break post | 2 min | Copy thread, reformat, add question |
| 3 | Instagram | Carousel (5-7 slides) | 5 min | One point per slide, screenshot or Canva |
| 4 | TikTok/Reels | Talking head video | 3 min | Read the thread, 60 seconds, text overlays |
| 5 | Newsletter | Email issue section | 2 min | Thread becomes main content block |
| 6 | Substack Notes | Short-form note | 1 min | Hook + 2 points + link to full newsletter |

## Automation with AI

Use Claude to adapt your original thread for each platform:

Prompt template: "Take this X/Twitter thread and convert it to a [LinkedIn post / Instagram carousel outline / TikTok script]. Keep the same information but adapt the format for [platform] best practices. Keep it under [word count]."

This reduces repurposing time from 15 minutes to 5-7 minutes per day.

## Weekly workflow

Monday-Friday: Write 1 original thread. Repurpose to all platforms.
Saturday: Review weekly analytics. Identify top 3 performers.
Sunday: Recycle top performers with new hooks (repost in 30 days).

Monthly output: 30 original pieces become 180+ total posts across 6 platforms.

## FAQ

### Won't people notice the same content on multiple platforms?
No. Less than 5% of your audience follows you on more than one platform. And even those who do expect platform-native formats. A thread on X looks completely different from a carousel on Instagram.

### Which platform should I create for first?
X/Twitter. Threads are the easiest to repurpose because they're already broken into discrete points. Each tweet becomes a carousel slide, a LinkedIn paragraph, or a video talking point.

### How do I track what's working?
Use Typefully for X analytics and Buffer for cross-platform metrics. Track: impressions, engagement rate, and clicks per post. Double down on topics and formats that outperform.

### Should I post the same content the same day across platforms?
Stagger by 24-48 hours. Post original on X first. LinkedIn next day. Instagram and TikTok the day after. Newsletter weekly. This prevents algorithmic overlap for the small audience that follows you everywhere.
