# Best content production templates for solo founders

Writing from scratch takes 2-4 hours per piece. Most solo founders quit after 2 pieces. Templates cut this to 30 minutes.

Here are the templates that work.

## Template 1: The case study

**Structure:**
- Problem (what was broken)
- What we tried (failed approach)
- What worked (solution)
- Numbers (results)
- How to replicate it

**Time to write:** 40 minutes

**Conversion rate:** 2.5-4%

**Example:**
"I tested 12 cold email tools. Most charged $300+ monthly. Turned out the cheapest one had the best conversion rate. Here's why."

## Template 2: The comparison

**Structure:**
- Define the category
- Compare 3-5 options (pros/cons table)
- Recommendation based on use case
- Pricing breakdown
- FAQ

**Time to write:** 50 minutes

**Conversion rate:** 2-3%

**Example:**
"Comparing Zapier vs n8n vs Make for cold outreach automation. One costs $0, one costs $50, one costs $200."

## Template 3: The how-to

**Structure:**
- Problem statement
- Tools needed (3-5 items)
- Step-by-step (5-7 steps max)
- Common mistakes (3 things)
- Timeline
- Next action

**Time to write:** 60 minutes

**Conversion rate:** 1.5-2.5%

**Example:**
"How to automate cold email follow-ups without tools. Three scripts, no subscriptions."

## Template 4: The debate

**Structure:**
- Question (X vs Y)
- Case for X (real pros)
- Case for Y (real pros)
- When to pick each
- Recommendation with caveats
- Real-world test results

**Time to write:** 45 minutes

**Conversion rate:** 2.5-3.5%

**Example:**
"Playwright vs Selenium for lead scraping. We tested both. Here's what we found."

## Template 5: The list

**Structure:**
- Headline (Best X for Y)
- Intro (why this list matters)
- 5-7 items (description + when to use)
- Comparison table (optional)
- FAQ
- CTA

**Time to write:** 50 minutes

**Conversion rate:** 1.5-2%

**Example:**
"Best free tools for lead generation. Tested by 50 solopreneurs. Updated monthly."

## Batching workflow

**Monday (2 hours):**
- Pick 5 topics
- Assign template to each
- Outline each piece

**Tuesday (2.5 hours):**
- Write 3 pieces (40 min each)
- Quick edit

**Wednesday (1.5 hours):**
- Write 2 pieces
- Add images or tables
- Schedule publication

**Total:** 6 hours for 5 pieces. 1.2 hours per piece.

## Template toolkit

Keep these saved:

1. Google Doc with template structures
2. AI prompt that follows each structure
3. Image size guide (1200x630 for previews)
4. CTA variations (10 options)
5. Headline formulas (20 tested options)

## Common mistakes

**Mistake 1: Writing without template.** You ramble. Reader loses interest. Output: 4 hours, weak piece.

**Mistake 2: Template too rigid.** You force content into structure that doesn't fit. Output: feels robotic.

**Mistake 3: No editing.** Templates are fast. Editing is faster than rewriting. Skip it? Pieces read sloppy.

## Customization per platform

Same content, different templates:

**Blog (1500 words):** Comparison or case study
**Email (200 words):** List or how-to
**LinkedIn (150 words):** Debate or single insight
**Twitter (280 characters):** List item or stat

Same research, 4 formats. 2 hours of production for all 4 channels.

## When templates don't work

Some topics need custom structure:
- Announcements
- Opinion pieces
- News/trends
- Personal stories

Use templates 80% of the time. Keep space for original formats.

## Scaling to 20 pieces per month

Using templates:
- Week 1: 5 pieces
- Week 2: 5 pieces
- Week 3: 5 pieces
- Week 4: 5 pieces + repurposing

Total: 24 hours. Without templates: 80+ hours.

Same output. 70% time savings.
