# Budget stack under $500 for social scheduling automation

Most agencies waste $200/month on tools they don't use. Here's the lean stack: $30/month, all you need.

## The stack ($30/month)

**1. Buffer ($5/month)** - Schedule posts across platforms
**2. Airtable free ($0)** - Content calendar
**3. Claude API ($20/month budget)** - Content generation
**4. GitHub ($0)** - Version control for prompts

Total: $25/month. Everything else is free.

## Why each tool

**Buffer:** Better than Hootsuite for solopreneurs. Simple interface. Works.

**Airtable:** Free tier handles 1200 rows. That's 40 posts/month for 30 months.

**Claude API:** Generates 5-10 posts daily at $0.003 per 1K tokens. About $5-10/month for active use.

**GitHub:** You're not paying. Backup your prompts and workflows.

## The workflow

**Monday:**
1. Open Airtable calendar
2. Plan 10 posts for the week
3. Prompt Claude for content ideas
4. Save drafts to Airtable

**Tuesday-Friday (daily):**
1. Open Buffer
2. Schedule 2 posts for next week
3. Reply to comments
4. Done in 10 minutes

**Weekend:**
1. Check analytics
2. Update what worked
3. Plan next week

## One-time costs ($100 max)

- Microphone ($50) - record video content
- Ring light ($30) - better lighting for videos
- Phone tripod ($10) - hands-free recording

Everything else: apps on your phone or computer (free).

## Alternative stack (if you hate Buffer)

**1. Later ($15/month)** - Instagram-focused, cheaper
**2. Zapier ($25/month)** - Automation instead of Buffer
**3. Notion free ($0)** - Content calendar
**4. Claude API ($10/month)** - Content

Total: $50/month. Same output.

Pick either Buffer or Later. Don't use both.

## The content production process

**Method 1 (AI-assisted):**
1. Prompt Claude: "5 posts about cold email"
2. Edit outputs in Buffer
3. Schedule
4. Takes 20 minutes for 5 posts

**Method 2 (Manual but faster):**
1. Batch write 5 posts Monday
2. Drop into Buffer Tuesday
3. Schedule
4. Takes 30 minutes once

Both work. Method 1 scales. Method 2 feels more authentic.

For $600/year, you cover 365 days of social.

## Common budget mistakes

**Mistake 1: Subscribing to everything.** Hootsuite ($60) + Later ($15) + Sprout ($200) + Buffer ($5) = $280/month for the same job.

**Mistake 2: Hiring an intern.** "It's cheaper." 10 hours/week at $15/hour = $150/week = $600/month. 4x more expensive than automation.

**Mistake 3: Free tools only.** Struggling with clunky UX, missing features. $30/month tools save 5 hours/week. ROI is clear.

Still under $100/month.

Budget scales with revenue.
