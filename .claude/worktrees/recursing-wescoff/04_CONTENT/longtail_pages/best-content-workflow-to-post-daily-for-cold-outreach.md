# Best content workflow to post daily for cold outreach

## Quick Answer

Spend 1 hour daily on content. Write 5-7 posts in batches. Schedule them. Reply for 30 minutes. That's it. 20-30 posts per week, 1-2 hours per day total.

## The Daily Workflow

### 8:00 AM - Batch Writing (30 min)

Write 5-7 posts at once. Pick one topic (your niche pain point or what you learned yesterday).

Format each post:
- Hook (first line grabs attention)
- 3 main points
- CTA (question or link)

Example cold outreach hook:
"Just realized most solopreneurs waste $500/mo on tools they don't use."

Write 5 variations on the same idea. Copy/paste base format, change the hook.

### 8:30 AM - Quality Check (15 min)

Review for:
- Typos
- Clarity
- Hook strength
- No AI cringe (remove "leverage," "empower," "streamline")

Kill weak posts. Keep top 5.

### 8:45 AM - Schedule (10 min)

Use Buffer or Later to schedule across the week:
- Monday: Post 1
- Tuesday: Post 2
- Wednesday: Post 3
- Thursday: Post 4
- Friday: Post 5

Spread 2+ hours apart so your account looks active.

### 6:00 PM - Replies (30 min)

Spend 30 minutes replying to top comments on your last 3 posts.

Each reply:
- Answer their question directly
- Add 1 useful insight
- Don't pitch

This kills the cold outreach vibe and builds genuine engagement.

## Weekly Schedule

**Monday:**
- Batch write 10 posts (60 min)
- Schedule for the week
- Reply to comments (30 min)

**Tues-Fri:**
- Reply to comments (30 min/day)
- Refresh queue with new posts (30 min)

**Saturday-Sunday:**
- Off
- Or: Review best-performing posts, plan next week

## Content Ideas for Cold Outreach

Write about:
- Problems you solve
- Tools you use
- Mistakes you made
- Learnings from experiments
- Behind-the-scenes of your process
- Contrarian takes on your niche

Avoid:
- Motivational fluff
- "Here's my latest offer"
- Fake success stories
- Engagement bait

## Real Example: 5-Post Batch

**Post 1 (Monday):**
"I automated my cold outreach. Saved 3 hours per week. But I made 1 mistake that almost got me rate-limited. Here's what I learned."

**Post 2 (Tuesday):**
"Most cold outreach fails because people follow up once. I follow up 5 times over 2 weeks. 35% open rate by follow-up #3."

**Post 3 (Wednesday):**
"Cold email vs cold LinkedIn: Which converts better? I tested both. Numbers inside."

**Post 4 (Thursday):**
"Your cold email template is wrong. Here's why. [specific reason based on your niche]"

**Post 5 (Friday):**
"5 cold outreach templates that actually work for [your niche]. I use these weekly."

Each post gets 10-30 replies. You spend 30 min replying to all.

## Avoiding Platform Ban

- Don't use bots to auto-reply
- Don't spam the same message
- Don't follow/unfollow strategies
- DO reply manually to genuine comments
- DO keep human in the loop

## Measurement

Track daily:
- Impressions
- Clicks to your link
- Email opt-ins from cold outreach
- Sales

Weekly:
- Which post types get highest engagement
- Which hooks work best
- Cost per lead

Adjust based on data. Kill posts that don't convert. Double down on winners.

## How to Scale This

Week 1-4: 1 post/day, 30 min replies
Week 5-8: 2 posts/day, add DM follow-ups
Week 9+: Add email sequences to new followers

But don't scale until you have a process that works.
