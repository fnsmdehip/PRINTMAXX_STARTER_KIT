# What Tools Do Indie Hackers Use for GEO AI SEO?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) requires different tools than traditional SEO. Here's what indie hackers actually use.

## Quick Answer

**Core stack ($0-50/mo):**
- Content: Claude/ChatGPT for drafts
- Research: Perplexity for citation analysis
- Monitoring: Google Search Console (free)
- Automation: n8n for tracking
- Hosting: Vercel/Netlify (free tier)

## The Indie Hacker GEO Stack

### Tier 1: Free Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Why It Works |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Claude Free | Draft content | Good for structured content |
| Perplexity | See what gets cited | Shows citation patterns |
| Google Search Console | Track impressions | Free, reliable |
| ChatGPT Free | Test queries | See what AI recommends |

### Tier 2: Budget Tools ($20-50/mo)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Claude Pro | Better drafts | $20/mo |
| Ahrefs Lite | Keyword research | $29/mo |
| n8n Cloud | Automation | $20/mo |

### Tier 3: Growth Tools ($100+/mo)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Ahrefs Standard | Full SEO suite | $99/mo |
| Clearscope | Content optimization | $170/mo |
| Custom monitoring | Citation tracking | Varies |

## How Indie Hackers Use These Tools

### Research Phase

1. **Query Perplexity** with target keywords
2. **Note which sites** get cited
3. **Analyze their content structure**
4. **Identify gaps** in existing answers

### Content Creation

1. **Draft with Claude** using structured prompts
2. **Add real data** and examples
3. **Include comparison tables**
4. **Answer follow-up questions** in FAQ

### Monitoring

1. **Track Search Console** weekly
2. **Query AI assistants** monthly to check citations
3. **Update content** based on what's getting cited

## What NOT to Buy

- Enterprise SEO tools (overkill)
- AI content detectors (irrelevant for GEO)
- Link building services (traditional SEO)
- Expensive keyword tools (Perplexity is free)

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need paid tools to start?**
A: No. Free tier tools are enough for first 6 months.

**Q: Which AI should I optimize for first?**
A: ChatGPT and Perplexity have the most users.

**Q: How do I know if I'm getting cited?**
A: Query AI assistants with your target keywords. Check if your content appears.
