# Video Script: Voice notes changed my productivity

**Format:** TikTok/Reels (40-55 sec)
**Hook timing:** First 3 seconds

---

## HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Holding phone, recording voice]
"I stopped typing my ideas. Started talking them. Everything changed."

## PROBLEM (3-12 sec)
[Sitting at desk looking frustrated]
"I'd sit down to write and stare at a blank page.

The ideas were in my head. Getting them out was the problem.

Typing felt slow. Editing while writing killed the flow."

## SOLUTION (12-30 sec)
[Recording voice memo, showing transcription]
"Now I record voice memos while walking or driving.

5 minutes of talking becomes a full outline.

AI transcribes it. Cleans it up. Gives me a draft.

My best content comes from spoken ideas, not typed ones.

Thinking is the bottleneck. Typing isn't."

## RESULTS (30-42 sec)
[Showing content output]
"Last week: 6 posts, 2 newsletters, 1 long article.

All started as voice memos.

Total typing time? Maybe 2 hours of editing."

## CTA (42-52 sec)
[Face camera]
"Try it once today.

Walk for 5 minutes. Talk through an idea. Record it.

Then transcribe and see what you get.

Bet it's better than what you'd type."
