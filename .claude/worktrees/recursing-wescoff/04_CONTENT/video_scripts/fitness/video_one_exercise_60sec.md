# One Exercise You Need (Romanian Deadlift)

**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Hook:** First 3 seconds

---

## Script

**[0-3 sec - HOOK]**
"This one exercise fixed my lower back pain and built my hamstrings."

[Show before/after leg comparison]

**[3-15 sec - EXERCISE INTRO]**
"Romanian deadlift. It's not a regular deadlift. The bar never touches the ground."

[Demo the movement, slow motion]

**[15-30 sec - SETUP]**
"Start standing with the bar at your hips. Push hips back. Slight knee bend. Feel the stretch in your hamstrings."

[Show setup position, hip hinge motion]

**[30-45 sec - EXECUTION]**
"Lower until you feel a deep stretch. Don't round your back. Drive hips forward to stand up. Squeeze glutes at the top."

[Demo 3 reps with form cues on screen]

**[45-55 sec - RESULTS]**
"I do 3 sets of 8 reps every Wednesday. Added 60 lbs to my regular deadlift in 3 months."

[Show current working weight]

**[55-60 sec - CTA]**
"Try it next leg day. Start light. Focus on the stretch."

[Text overlay: "Save for leg day"]
