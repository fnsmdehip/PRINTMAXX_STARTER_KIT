# Myth: You Need Protein Immediately After Working Out

**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Hook:** First 3 seconds

---

## Script

**[0-3 sec - HOOK]**
"The post-workout protein window is fake. Here's what actually matters."

[Show someone chugging a shake in the gym parking lot, dramatic music]

**[3-15 sec - THE MYTH]**
"Everyone says you need protein within 30 minutes of training or you lose your gains."

[Show bro-science clips, protein shake ads]

**[15-30 sec - THE TRUTH]**
"Studies show the window is actually 4 to 6 hours. Your body doesn't work on a 30-minute timer."

[Show research paper titles on screen]

**[30-45 sec - WHAT MATTERS]**
"Total daily protein matters. 0.8 to 1g per pound of bodyweight. Spread across 3 to 4 meals."

[Show meal timing graphic, daily total]

**[45-55 sec - PRACTICAL ADVICE]**
"I train at 6am. Eat breakfast at 8am. Zero difference in results compared to drinking a shake at 6:15am."

[Show personal timeline]

**[55-60 sec - CTA]**
"Stop rushing to chug shakes. Hit your daily protein. That's it."

[Text overlay: "Save this"]
