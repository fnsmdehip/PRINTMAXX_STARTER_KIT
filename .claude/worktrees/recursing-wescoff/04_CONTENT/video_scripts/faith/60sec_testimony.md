# 60-Second Story/Testimony Format

**Duration:** 60 seconds

**Visual:** Direct to camera, authentic setting

---

**Script:**

I used to think I needed perfect faith to pray.

Like I had to have my life together before I could talk to God.

So I waited.

For years.

Then my mom got sick.

I didn't have time to wait for perfect faith.

I prayed messy prayers.

Angry prayers.

Confused prayers.

And something changed.

Not all at once.

But I noticed I could breathe better.

My mom's still dealing with health stuff.

I still don't have all the answers.

But I stopped waiting for perfect faith.

I just show up.

With whatever I have that day.

Turns out that's enough.

If you're waiting to have it all figured out, stop waiting.

Show up with what you have.

It's enough.

---

**CTA:** None. Let the message sit.
