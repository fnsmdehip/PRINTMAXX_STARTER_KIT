# Video Script: Prayer before phone

**Format:** TikTok/Reels (30-45 sec)
**Hook timing:** First 3 seconds

---

## HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Face camera, holding phone]
"I used to check my phone before talking to God. Here's what changed."

## PROBLEM (3-12 sec)
[Walking, showing phone screen time]
"My screen time was 4 hours a day. My prayer time? Maybe 5 minutes.

I was giving my phone my best attention and God my leftovers."

## SOLUTION (12-25 sec)
[Sitting, Bible and coffee visible]
"New rule: Phone stays in another room until after I pray and read.

Just 15 minutes. Coffee, Bible, prayer. Then the phone comes out.

30 days in and my mornings feel completely different."

## CTA (25-35 sec)
[Face camera]
"Try it for one week. 15 minutes with God before the scroll.

Comment if you're in. I'll check back with you."

---

**Text overlays:**
- 0:03 - "The phone rule that changed everything"
- 0:12 - "4 hrs screen time vs 5 min prayer"
- 0:20 - "New morning order"

**Audio:** Calm background, trending sound optional
