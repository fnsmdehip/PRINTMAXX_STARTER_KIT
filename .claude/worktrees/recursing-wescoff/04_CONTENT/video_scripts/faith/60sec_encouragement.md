# 60-Second "One Thing" Encouragement

**Duration:** 60 seconds

**Visual:** Sitting, casual setting, natural light

---

**Script:**

You don't need to fix everything today.

Pick one thing.

One conversation you've been avoiding.

One apology you need to make.

One prayer you need to pray.

I spent years trying to overhaul my entire spiritual life at once.

Read more. Pray more. Serve more. Give more.

Burned out every time.

Then I started doing one thing per week.

Week 1: Texted one person encouragement every Monday.

Week 2: Added 10 minutes of silence each morning.

Week 3: Started praying for 3 people by name.

Each thing stuck because I didn't pile on.

Progress isn't perfection.

It's showing up with one thing.

What's your one thing this week?
