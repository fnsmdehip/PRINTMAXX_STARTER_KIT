Interested? I can send details in 2 minutes.

## Content Brief Template (Send After Agreement)

```
CONTENT BRIEF - [VERTICAL] COMPARISON GUIDE

INFLUENCER: [Name / @handle]
UNIQUE LINK: [tracking link]
DEADLINE: [Date]
DELIVERABLE: [1 Instagram Reel / 1 TikTok / etc.]

----- WHAT WE'RE PROMOTING -----

A comparison guide for online [GLP-1 weight loss / TRT / peptide therapy]
programs. Real pricing. Real reviews. Side-by-side breakdowns. Updated monthly.

Link: [tracking link]

----- SUGGESTED TALKING POINTS (pick 1-3) -----

For GLP-1:
- "I found this guide that compares 11 online GLP-1 programs side by side"
- "Prices range from $149 to $1,899/month for basically the same medication"
- "If you're researching semaglutide or tirzepatide, this saves hours of Googling"
- "They break down compounded vs brand, side effects, and what to look for in a clinic"

For TRT:
- "This comparison covers 9 online TRT programs with real pricing"
- "They show you what blood work to get before starting, which most clinics skip"
- "If you've been thinking about getting your testosterone checked, start here"
- "Online TRT vs going through your doctor. The cost difference is significant."

For Peptides:
- "This guide compares 8 peptide therapy programs. BPC-157, Sermorelin, NAD+"
- "They break down 503A vs 503B pharmacies and why your source matters"
- "If you're curious about peptides but overwhelmed by the options, this is your cheat sheet"
- "Real pricing, real reviews, no wellness guru energy"

----- MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS -----

[x] Include #ad or #sponsored in caption (or platform partnership disclosure tool)
[x] Include the tracking link (link in bio, comment, or description)
[x] Do NOT make health claims ("this will cure X" or "guaranteed results")
[x] Do NOT create false urgency ("only 5 spots left!")
[x] Be authentic. Talk about it in YOUR voice. Don't script it.

----- WHAT NOT TO SAY -----

- "FDA approved" (the comparison guide is not FDA approved; individual programs have their own regulatory status)
- "Guaranteed results" or "guaranteed weight loss"
- "Doctor recommended" (unless you are a doctor and you are recommending it)
- "No side effects" (every treatment has potential side effects)
- Any specific medical claims about curing or treating conditions

----- AFTER POSTING -----

1. Send us a screenshot or link to the live post
2. At 7 days, share a screenshot of your analytics for that post (reach, clicks, saves)
3. We'll send your flat fee within 48 hours of confirming the post is live
4. Affiliate commissions are tracked automatically and paid monthly

----- QUESTIONS? -----

Reply to this email or DM [handle]. We respond within 24 hours.
```

## Quick Reference: What to Send When
