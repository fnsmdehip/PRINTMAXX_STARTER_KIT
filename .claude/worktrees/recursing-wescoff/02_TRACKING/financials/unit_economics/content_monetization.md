### Funnel Math

```
Followers → Email List → Product Buyers

Twitter (10K followers):
- Email opt-in rate: 2-5% = 200-500 subscribers
- Product conversion: 3% = 6-15 buyers
- At $47 product: $282-705 revenue

Newsletter (10K subscribers):
- Product conversion: 3% = 300 buyers
- At $47 product: $14,100 revenue

10x revenue difference with same audience size
```

## Affiliate Click-Through Rates

### By Content Format

| Format | CTR to Affiliate | Why |
|--------|------------------|-----|
| Dedicated review | 5-15% | High intent |
| Comparison content | 4-10% | Decision stage |
| Tutorial with tool | 2-5% | Tool in context |
| Newsletter recommendation | 2-8% | Trust built |
| Social post | 0.5-3% | Casual browsing |
| Podcast mention | 1-4% | Audio harder to act on |

### By Placement

| Placement | CTR | Notes |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Above the fold | 3-8% | Best visibility |
| In-content contextual | 2-5% | Natural flow |
| Comparison table | 4-10% | Decision format |
| End of post | 1-3% | Lower attention |
| Sidebar | 0.5-2% | Banner blindness |
| Pop-up | 1-4% | Can annoy users |

### Optimizing Affiliate Revenue

| Tactic | CTR Increase | Implementation |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| Comparison tables | +50-100% | Easy |
| In-text links | +20-40% | Easy |
| Dedicated review boxes | +30-60% | Medium |
| Exclusive discounts | +40-80% | Medium (negotiate) |
| Video reviews | +60-120% | High effort |

## Revenue Diversification Model

### Healthy Revenue Mix

| Source | Target % | Why |
|--------|----------|-----|
| Own products | 40-60% | Highest margin |
| Affiliate | 15-25% | Passive, compounding |
| Sponsorships | 10-20% | Lumpy but high value |
| Services/consulting | 10-25% | Trade time, but validates |
| Platform revenue | 5-15% | Nice to have, not rely on |

### Risk Assessment

| Concentration | Risk | Action |
|---------------|------|--------|
| >70% one source | High | Diversify immediately |
| >50% one platform | High | Build alternatives |
| >50% one product | Medium | Create product ladder |
| Even split | Low | Maintain and optimize |

## Action Items

1. **Build newsletter first** - Highest RPM, you own the audience
2. **Add 1 affiliate per month** - Compound over time
3. **Launch product by month 6** - Don't wait for "enough" followers
4. **Pursue sponsorships at 5K** - Don't undervalue early
5. **Track revenue per platform weekly** - Double down on what works
6. **Repurpose everything** - One piece of content → 5 platforms
