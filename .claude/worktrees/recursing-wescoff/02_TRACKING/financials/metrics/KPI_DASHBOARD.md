# KPI Dashboard

## North Star Metrics

These 5 metrics determine business health. Track weekly.

| Metric | Formula | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Business stability |
| **Email List Size** | Total active subscribers | Owned audience |
| **Revenue per Subscriber** | MRR / Subscribers | Monetization efficiency |
| **Gross Margin** | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Profitability |
| **Runway** | Cash / Monthly Burn | Survival buffer |

## Quarterly Dashboard

### Business Health Score

Rate each 1-5, calculate weighted average:

| Area | Weight | Score (1-5) | Weighted |
|------|--------|-------------|----------|
| Revenue growth | 25% | /5 | |
| Profit margin | 20% | /5 | |
| Audience growth | 20% | /5 | |
| Product performance | 15% | /5 | |
| Cash position | 10% | /5 | |
| Team/systems | 10% | /5 | |
| **Total** | **100%** | | **/5** |

**Score interpretation:**
- 4.5-5.0: Excellent, scale aggressively
- 3.5-4.4: Good, continue current path
- 2.5-3.4: Okay, identify and fix weak areas
- 1.5-2.4: Struggling, major intervention needed
- <1.5: Critical, survival mode

## By Business Model

### Info Products KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Sales page conversion | Purchases / Visitors | 2% | 4%+ |
| Email to sale rate | Sales / List size | 1% | 3%+ |
| Refund rate | Refunds / Sales | <5% | <2% |
| Upsell conversion | Upsells / Purchases | 15% | 30%+ |
| Customer LTV | Total rev / Customers | 1.5x AOV | 2x+ AOV |
| Revenue per visitor | Total rev / Total visitors | $0.50+ | $1.00+ |

### SaaS KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| MRR | Monthly recurring revenue | Growth | Growth |
| MRR growth | (This MRR - Last MRR) / Last MRR | 10%/mo | 20%/mo |
| Churn rate | Lost customers / Start customers | <7% | <3% |
| Net revenue retention | (MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Start MRR | >100% | >120% |
| CAC payback | CAC / (ARPU x Gross Margin) | <6 mo | <3 mo |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC | >3:1 | >5:1 |

### Services KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Utilization rate | Billable hours / Available hours | 65% | 75%+ |
| Effective hourly rate | Revenue / Hours worked | $100+ | $150+ |
| Client retention | Retained clients / Total clients | 70% | 85%+ |
| Project margin | (Revenue - Costs) / Revenue | 50% | 70%+ |
| Pipeline value | Sum of potential projects | 3x monthly | 6x monthly |

### Affiliate KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Earnings per click (EPC) | Revenue / Total clicks | $0.50+ | $1.00+ |
| Click-through rate | Clicks / Visitors | 2% | 5%+ |
| Conversion rate | Sales / Clicks | 1% | 3%+ |
| Revenue per 1K visitors | (CTR x Conv x Commission) x 1000 | $20+ | $50+ |
| Content ROI | Revenue / Time to create | $10/hr | $25+/hr |

### Content/Newsletter KPIs

| Metric | Formula | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Subscriber growth rate | New subs / Start subs | 5%/mo | 15%+/mo |
| Open rate | Opens / Delivered | 35% | 45%+ |
| Click rate | Clicks / Opens | 3% | 7%+ |
| Revenue per subscriber | Monthly rev / Subscribers | $1/mo | $3+/mo |
| Sponsorship CPM | Sponsor rate / (Subs / 1000) | $20 | $50+ |

## Review Cadence

### Daily (5 minutes)

- Revenue (new sales, total)
- Email signups
- Urgent issues

### Weekly (30 minutes)

- Full revenue breakdown
- Audience growth
- Traffic sources
- Content performance
- Upcoming actions

### Monthly (2 hours)

- Financial statements
- Customer metrics
- Product performance
- Channel analysis
- Goal progress
- Next month planning

### Quarterly (Half day)

- Business health score
- Trend analysis
- Strategy review
- Goal setting
- Budget adjustment
- Team/systems review
