## TikTok

### Algorithm Changes
- **Oracle now controls the US algorithm** after September 2025 deal. US TikTok may diverge from international TikTok as separate algorithms optimize for their audiences.
- **Follower-first distribution model**: New videos shown primarily to existing followers during first few days. TikTok analyzes performance among followers before wider distribution.
- **Watch time over views**: Content quality and completion rates now matter more than posting frequency or trending audio alone.
- **Predictive AI**: TikTok now surfaces videos that anticipate what users will like before they've even searched for it.

### Favored Content Formats
- Longer-form content (60-180 seconds) optimized for first-hour engagement
- High-retention clips from substantial source videos
- Problem-solution format (hook in first 3 seconds with clear pain point)
- AI avatar influencers for 24/7 product promotion
- Live shopping (especially beauty, home, tech)

### Monetization Updates
- TikTok Shop requires 1,000+ followers for livestreams
- Account holder must be 16+ for live features
- Affiliate-first models: hundreds of smaller creators posting authentic content outperform single large partnerships
- Volume of micro-creators beats celebrity partnerships

### Growth Tactics Working NOW
- 1 high-quality, well-optimized video per day beats 5 low-quality posts
- Community interaction: respond to comments, turn them into content
- Consequence-first hooks (what happened, not explanation)
- Products built around daily habits have stronger staying power than gimmick-driven viral products

### What Stopped Working
- Posting frequency alone no longer drives reach
- Trending audio alone insufficient
- Historical benchmarks no longer predict future success
- Engagement patterns and viral signals recalibrated under Oracle

## Instagram

### Algorithm Changes
- **4 distinct algorithms** working simultaneously: Feed, Reels, Stories, Explore. Each has unique ranking signals.
- **Originality and creativity prioritized**: Algorithm rewards content that feels fresh, original, and made for Instagram (not recycled from elsewhere).
- **"Your Algorithm" feature launched**: Users can now customize Reels recommendations by choosing topics to see more/less of.
- **AI-powered content recognition**: Analyzes visuals, text in images, and video clips beyond hashtags.
- **Hashtags declining**: Instagram notifying users they won't be able to follow hashtags soon.

### Favored Content Formats
- **Reels** remain top distribution format
- **Carousels** generate 12% more interactions than Reels, 2.1x more than single photos
- **Mixed-format carousels** (images + videos) achieve highest engagement at 2.33%
- **Trial Reels**: Test content with non-followers before sharing to audience
- 3-second rule: First 3 seconds decide whether someone keeps watching

### Monetization Updates
- Meta influencer marketing spending projected at $4.93B in 2026
- Content Monetization Program: qualifying creators get notified when content is stolen
- Saves, shares, and watch time signal deeper relevance than likes

### Growth Tactics Working NOW
- Post 3-5 Reels per week with trending audio and clear hooks
- Use Stories daily with polls, quizzes, "Ask me anything" stickers
- 1-2 relevant hashtags outperform posts with 20 hashtags
- First 24-48 hours of engagement matter most
- Cross-platform repurposing (TikTok to Reel with minor adjustments)
- Creators posting 5+ times weekly get 7.3% more engagement

### What Stopped Working
- Bot farms, follow/unfollow automation systematically nullified by Meta AI
- Engagement pods detected and deprioritized (must be small, 5-7 members, same niche)
- Clickbait, vague storytelling, overly promotional posts
- Engagement bait phrases ("What do you think?") flagged and deprioritized
- Purely AI-generated content without human insight being deprioritized

## YouTube

### Algorithm Changes
- **Less aggressive pushing, more preference matching**: Videos evaluated on whether viewers return, continue watching, maintain trust
- **Shorts heavily weigh early swipe-through behavior**: Videos with clear topic and visual context in first seconds perform best
- **Completion rate and replays** signal stronger satisfaction than likes or comments
- **"Inauthentic content" policy** (renamed from "repetitive content", effective July 2025): Mass-produced, template-like videos ineligible for ads

### Favored Content Formats
- Shorts as discovery tool, funneling to long-form content
- Shorts perform best when value is obvious immediately
- 2-3 Shorts per week signals algorithm you're serious (1x weekly = 2x slower to monetize)
- Long-form still drives deeper engagement and session time
- AI tools available: text to video, auto-editing, Google DeepMind powered

### Monetization Updates
- **Shorts RPM**: $0.01-$0.07 per 1,000 views (pooled model, not per-ad)
- **Full monetization**: 1,000 subs + 10M Shorts views in 90 days OR 4,000 watch hours
- **Basic tier**: 500 subs + 3 public videos + 3,000 hours or 3M Shorts views (memberships, Super Chat, no ads)
- **Shorts generate 200B+ views per day, 2B monthly users**
- Only 8% of Shorts creators rely on ads as primary income
- Audience-funded revenue (memberships, Super Thanks, Super Chats) becoming critical for mid-size creators

### Growth Tactics Working NOW
- Use Shorts to introduce new viewers, tease longer content, funnel to memberships
- AI for ideas and efficiency, then infuse with unique voice and value
- Multi-language scaling through voice cloning and dubbing
- Cross-platform repurposing of Shorts to TikTok/Reels
- Multiple revenue streams: ads, memberships, Super Chat, merch, sponsorships, affiliates, licensing

### What Stopped Working
- Mass-produced AI content without human insight flagged as "inauthentic"
- Template-based content farms penalized
- Mindless AI abuse hurts monetization
- Copy-pasted content without new insight flagged
- Single income source dependency

## X/Twitter

### Algorithm Changes
- **Small account boost**: Algorithm actively promotes content from emerging voices over established accounts
- **Premium accounts get 4x visibility boost** for in-network content, 2x for out-of-network
- **Engagement quality over audience size**: Follower count no longer key credibility signal
- **Premium effectively mandatory**: 30-40% higher reply impressions for Premium vs identical non-Premium accounts
- **Only Premium user engagement counts** toward ad revenue payouts

### Favored Content Formats
- Links in first comment, not in post (algorithm penalizes external links)
- Authentic engaging content that adds value to conversations
- Creator videos (sponsored video format coming)
- Threads and longer-form text posts

### Monetization Updates
- **Creator payouts 2-3x previous checks** in early 2026 (Musk directive to "crank creator payouts way up")
- **X Premium**: $8/month for blue checkmark + monetization access
- **5M views in 3 months** required for ad revenue sharing
- **X Money**: Visa-backed wallet coming 2026 (creator payouts, P2P, tipping, live shopping)
- **Shop Spotlight**: Product showcase directly on profile
- Brand deals still highest-paying path on X

### Growth Tactics Working NOW
- Drop links in first comment, not post
- Post authentic content that drives Premium user engagement
- Build for brand deals (native monetization is supplementary)
- Use Tips, Ad Revenue Sharing, Creator Subscriptions
- Premium subscription is ROI-positive for serious creators at $8/mo

### What Stopped Working
- External links in main post body
- Free accounts maintaining reach without Premium
- Relying solely on follower count for distribution
- Algorithm changes frequently, requiring constant adaptation

## LinkedIn

### Algorithm Changes
- **Views down 50%, engagement down 25%, follower growth down 59%** (Richard van der Blom Algorithm Insights report)
- **Expertise, conversation quality, contextual relevance** prioritized over recency or superficial engagement
- **Comments 15x more impactful than likes, 5x more than shares**
- **AI content detection**: Systems to identify and deprioritize purely AI-generated content
- **Niche consistency rewarded**: Algorithm directs content to right feeds based on topical focus

### Favored Content Formats
- **Document/carousel posts**: 2-3x more dwell time than single-image or text posts
- **Text posts**: Most reliable format for reach (1,000-1,300 characters optimal)
- **Videos**: Second-best performing format
- **Newsletters**: Own distribution mechanism through subscriber notifications
- Comment threads where different users reply to each other (not just isolated comments)

### Monetization Updates
- Newsletter subscriptions for consistent reach
- Dwell time is key metric (longer time on content = more distribution)
- Comment length matters (longer comments increase visibility)

### Growth Tactics Working NOW
- Golden Hour: First 60-90 minutes of engagement influences reach expansion
- Post during work hours (8-9 AM and 2-3 PM local time)
- Consistent niche expertise beats broad posting
- Longer comments significantly increase visibility vs emoji reactions
- Treat it as professional growth platform, not casual social

### What Stopped Working
- Engagement pods detected and penalized
- Engagement bait phrases deprioritized
- Broad "broadcast" posting style
- Low-effort AI-generated content
- Clickbait and overly promotional posts
