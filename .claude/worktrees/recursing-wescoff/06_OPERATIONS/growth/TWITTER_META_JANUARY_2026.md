## ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### Reply Guy Strategy (PROVEN ROI)

**@FranVests documented 3 months of reply guy strategy:**

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Verified Followers | 6.3K |
| Total Impressions | 3.1M |
| Engagement Rate | 11.6% |
| Engagements | 367.9K |
| Profile Visits | 49.4K |
| Replies Written | 91.7K |
| Likes Received | 129.8K |
| Reposts | 9.9K |
| Bookmarks | 2.7K |

**Key Insight:** Reply strategy generates profile visits at scale. 49.4K profile visits from 3 months = funnel opportunity.

**How to Execute:**
1. Identify 10-20 large accounts in your niche
2. Turn on notifications for their posts
3. Reply within first 5 minutes with value-add comment
4. Be early, be specific, be helpful
5. Link in bio captures the profile visitors

### Quote Tweet Strategy

**Pattern:** Quote viral content with your unique angle

**Example:** Quote a viral AI demo with "I built something similar for [niche]. Here's what I learned about [specific insight]."

**Why it works:** Borrows virality, positions you as practitioner not just observer

### A/B Contrarian Posts (From NICHE_POSTING_STRATEGY.md)

Post same topic with opposite takes:
```
Post A: "start using claude code"
Post B: "stop using claude code"
```
Track which performs. Contrarian "stop" often gets 2x engagement.

### Self-Reply Funnel

**Pattern observed in viral posts:**
1. Hook tweet gets attention
2. Self-reply contains CTA/link
3. Self-reply rises to top due to engagement

**Example:**
```
Tweet: "I made $50K last month from cold email"

Self-reply: "Here's the exact framework I use (saved in my highlights)"
```
