## 1. Algorithm Mechanics (Post-Musk Era)

### How the For You Feed Works

The X algorithm is a recommendation system that ranks content based on predicted engagement.

**Content Sourcing:**
1. **In-Network (50%):** Posts from accounts you follow
2. **Out-of-Network (50%):** Posts from accounts you don't follow, surfaced by:
   - Engagement from people you follow
   - Topic/interest matching
   - Social graph proximity (followers of followers)
   - Trending velocity

**Ranking Signals (Weighted by Importance):**

| Signal | Weight | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Replies | Highest | Conversation starters win |
| Retweets/Reposts | High | Amplification signal |
| Time spent reading | High | Dwell time matters |
| Quote tweets | High | Context matters |
| Likes | Medium | Engagement signal |
| Profile clicks | Medium | Interest signal |
| Link clicks | Low | External links deprioritized |
| Follows from post | Bonus | Viral potential indicator |

### X Premium Boost

| Feature | Free Account | X Premium | X Premium+ |
|---------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Reply ranking | Normal | Boosted | Most boosted |
| For You visibility | Standard | 2-3x impressions | 3-5x impressions |

## 2. Content That Performs

### Thread vs Single Tweets

**Single tweets beat threads when:**
- Making a quick observation
- Hot takes
- Engagement bait (questions, polls)

**Threads beat singles when:**
- Teaching a framework (5-7 tweets max)
- Telling a story
- Step-by-step tutorials

**Thread best practices:**
1. Number your tweets (1/ 2/ 3/)
2. Make each tweet standalone-valuable
3. Hook in tweet 1, CTA in last tweet
4. 5-7 tweets optimal
5. Don't use "Thread:" opener

### Format Performance

| Format | vs Text-Only |

## 3. Growth Tactics

### Posting Frequency

| Posts/Day | Growth Speed |
|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | Slow (must be excellent) |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 5 | Fast |
| 10+ | Very fast (volume play) |

### Reply Strategy (0-5k followers)

**Reply-guy playbook:**
1. Follow 100-200 big accounts in your niche
2. Turn on notifications for top 20
3. Reply within 5 minutes of their posts
4. Add value, don't just agree

**High-value reply formats:**
- "This works because [insight]"
- "I tested this. Here's what happened: [result]"
- "Counterpoint: [thoughtful disagreement]"

**Avoid:** "Great post!", "100% agree", anything under 10 words

**Growth math:**
- 10 quality replies/day = 200+ followers/month minimum

### Quote Tweet Strategy

When to quote tweet:
1. Adding contrarian take
2. Providing additional context
3. Applying concept to different niche
4. Sharing your results with their framework

### List Building

**Create these lists:**
1. **ICP:** 100-200 target customers
2. **Competitors:** Monitor content angles
3. **Amplifiers:** 50-100 accounts who repost good content
4. **Inspiration:** Great accounts outside your niche

## 5. Account Health

### Shadowban Detection
1. Log out, search exact username
2. Check if replies appear under others' posts
3. Monitor for sudden 80%+ impression drop

### Suspension Triggers (High Risk)
- Buying followers/engagement
- Auto-follows, auto-likes
- Mass unsolicited DMs
- Rapid follow/unfollow

### Recovery
- Stop automation immediately
- Don't post for 24-48 hours
- Return with high-quality content only
- Recovery typically 1-7 days

### Rate Limits

| Action | Free | Premium |
|--------|------|---------|
| Tweets/day | ~2,400 | Higher |
| DMs/day | ~1,000 | Higher |
| Follows/day | ~400 | Similar |
| Likes/day | ~1,000 | Higher |

## 7. High-Signal Tactics

### Self-Reply Funnel

1. Post hook tweet (problem or curiosity gap)
2. Self-reply immediately with CTA/link
3. Self-reply appears as "top reply"
4. Separates value from promotion

**Example:**
```
Tweet: "Here's how I made $30k in one month with email."

Self-reply: "The full breakdown is in my newsletter. Link in bio."
```

### Comments > Engagement

Ask for comments instead of likes/reposts:
- Comments boost algorithmic ranking
- "Comment 'X' for Y" drives action
- People comment, you DM them, builds list

### X Launch Strategy

1. Build to 5-8k followers in niche
2. Create compelling demo/video
3. Post with strong hook
4. Ask for comments for early access
5. Reply to every comment within 1 hour
