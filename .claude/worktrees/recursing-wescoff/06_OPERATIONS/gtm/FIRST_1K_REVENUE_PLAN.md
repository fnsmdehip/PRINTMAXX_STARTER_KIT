## THE $1,000 BREAKPOINT MATH

**To hit $1,000 in 7 days, you need ONE of these:**

| Scenario | What Needs to Happen |
|----------|---------------------|
| Volume play | 37 sales at $27 AOV (5.3 sales/day) |
| Viral play | 1 thread with 500K impressions, 2% CTR, 3% conversion = 300 sales |
| Service play | 2 consulting calls at $500 each |
| Bundle play | 143 sales at $7 AOV (20/day -- harder) |
| Mixed play | 15 PDF sales ($350) + 10 templates ($80) + 1 consult ($500) + Medium ($70) |

**Most likely path to $1K:** Mixed play. You don't need a viral moment. 15 PDF sales over 7 days is 2/day, which is achievable with consistent posting + Gumroad marketplace + Medium traffic.

## FAILURE MODES AND MITIGATIONS

| Failure Mode | Probability | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Zero sales in 7 days | 15% | Lower prices, more content volume, Reddit distribution |
| Low traffic (under 100 visitors) | 25% | Post more aggressively, use reply-bait, engage in communities |
| Products feel too thin | 10% | All products are 30-55 pages. This is above market average for the price point |
| Refund requests | 5% | 30-day guarantee builds trust. At these prices, refunds are negligible |
| Gumroad account issues | 5% | Whop as backup platform |
| Content falls flat on X | 30% | 295+ posts to test. Some will hit. A/B test hooks. |

**The only actual risk is not executing.** The assets exist. The products are specced. The content is written. The platforms are free. The only variable is whether you spend the 21 hours building and posting.
