# TREND029: Skool Community $0 to $10K/mo Case Study

**Handle:** Skool platform case study (GroupMasteryTools)
**Niche:** Community Platform / Creator Economy
**Platform:** Skool.com
**Est. MRR:** $10K case study example
**Replication Score:** 9/10
**Date Analyzed:** 2026-02-01

## What They're Doing
Creator went from $0 to $10K/month with a Skool community using strategy, clarity, and consistency. Key insight: one weekly call builds habits and retention. Skool's pricing in 2026: $99/mo (Pro plan) with 2.9% transaction fee, or $9/mo (Hobby) with 10% fee.

## Funnel Map
Free content (YouTube/Twitter) → Lead magnet → Free Skool community tier OR direct paid signup → Weekly live calls → Retention via gamification + courses → Recurring revenue

## Monetization Stack
1. Skool community membership ($49-199/mo typical pricing range for members)
2. Weekly accountability calls (retention driver)
3. Course library (included in membership)
4. Gamification (leaderboards, levels) - drives engagement = retention

## Unique Mechanism
**Retention-First Design:** Skool's gamification features yield industry-leading retention rates. Day 30 retention of 30%+ is good, 50%+ is excellent. Monthly active users (MAU) of 20%+ good, 40%+ excellent.

**Platform simplicity:** Minimalist by design. Combines discussions, courses, live sessions in one space. No bloat = higher engagement.

**Key metric:** Retention pays the bills. 50% monthly churn = need 50 new members just to stay flat. 10% churn = only need 10. Focus retention before growth.

## Replicable Frameworks
1. **One weekly call minimum** - If you show up, they show up. Consistency = habit formation
2. **Gamification for engagement** - Levels, leaderboards, points drive participation
3. **Hobby vs Pro pricing tiers** - Start at $9/mo to test, scale to $99/mo when proven
4. **30-day retention focus** - Get 50%+ D30 retention before scaling acquisition

## Gap/Weakness
Platform fee ($99/mo) + transaction fee (2.9%) means need 100+ members at $49/mo to justify Pro plan. Start with Hobby plan ($9/mo, 10% fee) until validated.

**Math:**
- 100 members × $49/mo = $4,900/mo gross
- Platform fee: $99/mo
- Transaction fees: $4,900 × 2.9% = $142
- Net: $4,900 - $99 - $142 = $4,659/mo
- **Margin: 95%**

**Replication score 9:** Extremely replicable. Platform handles tech. Just need content + consistency. Case study proves $10K/mo achievable.
