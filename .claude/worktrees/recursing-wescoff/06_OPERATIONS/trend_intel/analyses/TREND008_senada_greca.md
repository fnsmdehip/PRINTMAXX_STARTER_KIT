## What They're Doing

Senada Greca built a 2.6M TikTok following by posting free workout content demonstrating strength training techniques. Every video promotes her proprietary WeRise app, which offers exclusive workout programs behind a subscription paywall. The funnel is simple: give away workout teasers on TikTok to drive massive organic reach, then convert followers to paid app subscribers via link in bio. Subscription model creates recurring revenue at scale.

## Funnel Map

TikTok Workout Demo Videos (free) → Massive organic reach → Link in bio → WeRise App Landing Page → Subscription signup (pricing undisclosed but locked in for life) → Retention via exclusive programs and new content drops

## Monetization Stack

1. **WeRise App subscriptions** - $100K+/mo (estimated based on 2.6M TikTok following and subscription model)
2. **TikTok Creator Rewards Program** - $10-30K/mo (estimated from 2.6M followers, consistent posting)
3. **Brand partnerships** - $5-20K/mo (supplement brands, activewear companies)

**Total estimated:** $115K-150K/mo MRR

## Unique Mechanism

**WeRise** - Branded fitness app positioning Senada's workouts as exclusive and superior to generic fitness content. The app promises "strength training loved by millions worldwide" and locks users into a subscription "for life" at their signup price, creating urgency and FOMO.

## Replicable Frameworks

1. **Teaser Content Strategy:** Post full workout demos on TikTok to prove value → drive app signups for "exclusive" expanded programs
2. **Subscription App Funnel:** Build branded app (React Native) → subscription paywall → retention via new program drops
3. **Locked-in Pricing Psychology:** "Sale price locked in for life" messaging creates urgency without traditional scarcity tactics
4. **Platform-Specific Content:** TikTok = short workout clips optimized for vertical video, Instagram = longer form tutorials + community engagement
5. **No Ads, Pure Organic:** 2.6M followers built entirely on organic content + consistent posting cadence

## Gap/Weakness

**Weaknesses we can exploit:**
1. **No free tier:** Senada's app is subscription-only. We could offer freemium model (free basic workouts → upsell premium) to capture more of the funnel.
2. **Single niche:** Only targets strength training. We can launch multiple AI personas across fitness sub-niches (yoga, HIIT, calisthenics, running) and cross-promote.
3. **No community:** App appears to be workout library only. Adding Skool community or Discord would increase retention and LTV.
4. **No coaching upsell:** Senada could easily add 1:1 coaching tier at $200-500/session. We should build this into our model from day 1.
5. **Platform risk:** 100% reliant on TikTok/IG for distribution. We should diversify to YouTube Shorts, Pinterest, email list from day 1.

**Execution advantage:**
- AI persona can post 3x/day vs. Senada's human posting limits
- Multiple niche personas can cross-promote vs. single creator
- Content farm model scales to 10+ accounts vs. single account
- Lower CAC via bought engagement vs. pure organic grind
