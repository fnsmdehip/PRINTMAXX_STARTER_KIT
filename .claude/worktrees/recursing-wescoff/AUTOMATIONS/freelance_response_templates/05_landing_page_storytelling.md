## DM Follow-Up

```
hey, thanks for the reply.

here's what i'm thinking for your landing page:

the narrative arc i'd use for an accountability product:

section 1 - THE MOMENT (hero)
"you set the goal. again. you downloaded the app. again. you told yourself this time would be different. again."
-> this is the hook. everyone who needs accountability has lived this exact loop.

section 2 - THE REAL PROBLEM
not "you lack discipline." instead: "the problem isn't motivation. it's that nobody's watching."
-> reframe from personal failure to structural gap

section 3 - THE BRIDGE
introduce your product as the structural solution, not another willpower tool

section 4 - HOW IT WORKS
3-step visual walkthrough. keep it concrete.

section 5 - PROOF
early users, testimonials, or even just "built by someone who struggled with the same thing"

section 6 - CTA
low-commitment entry: free trial, waitlist, or "start in 30 seconds"

want me to flesh this out into a full wireframe? i can have it ready in 24 hours.
```
