Best Newsletter Platforms for Solo Founders (2026)

Beehiiv, Kit, Moosend, Substack, MailerLite. which one gets a solopreneur from 0 subscribers to $1K/mo in email revenue? the answer depends on where you are today.

tl;dr — the verdict up front

Beehiiv wins for most solopreneurs. built-in referral program, ad network, and subscriber analytics that other platforms charge extra for or don't offer. free up to 2,500 subscribers.

Kit (ConvertKit) wins if your business runs on complex automation sequences. multiple conditional forks, product triggers, tag-based logic. the best email automation engine for solo creators.

Moosend wins on price. $9/mo for automation, landing pages, and list management. best pick if you want paid features without the Beehiiv or Kit price tag.

Substack is fine if you want the simplest possible setup and don't mind giving up 10% of paid subscription revenue.

bottom line: start on Beehiiv free. upgrade when you hit 2,500 subscribers or need paid subscriptions. switch to Kit only if automation is a real bottleneck.

frequently asked questions

the questions that get googled most often about these platforms.

Is Beehiiv really better than ConvertKit for solopreneurs?

For most solopreneurs, yes. Beehiiv's free plan includes a referral program, ad network access, and subscriber growth analytics that Kit locks behind paid plans. if you need complex conditional automation sequences, Kit is the stronger choice. if you want built-in growth tools from day one without paying $59/mo, Beehiiv wins.

What is the best free newsletter platform in 2026?

Beehiiv's free plan (up to 2,500 subscribers) is the best free newsletter option for solopreneurs who care about growth. it includes the referral program and ad network access. Kit's free plan (up to 10,000 subscribers) wins on subscriber cap but has no growth tools or monetization. Substack is free but takes 10% of paid revenue forever.

How much does Beehiiv cost compared to Kit?

Beehiiv free: up to 2,500 subscribers, no charge. Beehiiv Scale: $42/mo. Kit free: up to 10,000 subscribers, no automations. Kit Creator: $59/mo with automations. for a solopreneur at 3,000 subscribers who wants automation and growth tools, Beehiiv Scale ($42/mo) is cheaper than Kit Creator ($59/mo) and includes the referral program and ad network that Kit does not.

Is Substack good for a newsletter with paid subscriptions?

Substack is good if simplicity matters more than margin. the 10% cut feels small at $100/mo revenue but adds up fast. at $2,500/mo gross revenue from paid subs, Substack takes $250. Beehiiv Scale takes $42. the $208/mo difference is $2,496/year. if you have 200+ paying subscribers, the math says move to Beehiiv.

What is the cheapest paid newsletter platform with automation?

Moosend at $9/mo. you get visual automation sequences, landing pages, and unlimited email sends. MailerLite starts at $10/mo and is also strong at this price point. both lack growth tools, but for basic list-building and nurture sequences, either works for under $15/mo.

Can I migrate from Substack to Beehiiv?

Yes. Beehiiv has a Substack import tool that migrates subscriber lists, posts, and paid subscriber data. most creators report the migration takes under an hour. Beehiiv actively courts Substack creators and offers migration support. the main consideration: notify your paid subscribers before the switch so they know where payments go.
