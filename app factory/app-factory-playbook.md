- **Appfigures** - Cheaper alternative (~$50-200/month), shows downloads, revenue, rankings

- **AppGoblin** - Open source Android analytics

**2. Validate Demand:**
- **Google Trends** - Search volume over time, compare niches
- **TikTok Search** - Type keywords, filter by "Most Liked" all time
- **Reddit** - Search pain points in niche subreddits

**3. Spy on What's Going Viral:**
- **Viral Ad Library** (viraladlibrary.com) - See ads from top apps
- **TikTok Creative Center** - Official TikTok ad library
- **Facebook Ad Library** - All active ads on Meta platforms
- **Paywall Screens** (paywallscreens.com) - Swipe paywalls from $25M+ apps
- **Screens Design** (screensdesign.com) - Spy on onboarding flows

### The Research Process

```
1. Pick a psychological trigger from the framework above
2. Search TikTok for related content, filter by Most Liked
3. Note which videos have 1M+ views (validated demand)
4. Search Sensor Tower/Appfigures for apps in that space
5. Find apps doing $10K-100K/month (achievable targets)
6. Analyze their paywalls, onboarding, and ad creatives
7. Build a better version or find an underserved sub-niche
```

## PHASE 3: MONETIZATION

### The Hard Paywall Strategy

Steven Cravotta and Yi both used this:

```
1. App is free to download
2. User goes through onboarding (builds investment)
3. Hard paywall before any features
4. Free trial requiring payment method
5. Weekly subscription ($4.99-8.99/week most common)
```

**Why Weekly Works:**
- 50% of all subscription purchases are weekly (2025 data)
- Lower perceived commitment than monthly
- Higher LTV than monthly when calculated annually
- Easier to A/B test price points

### Pricing Framework

| App Type | Weekly | Monthly | Annual |
|----------|--------|---------|--------|
| Utility (simple) | $2.99-4.99 | $7.99-9.99 | $29.99-39.99 |
| Health/Fitness | $4.99-7.99 | $12.99-19.99 | $49.99-79.99 |
| AI-powered | $6.99-9.99 | $19.99-29.99 | $79.99-99.99 |
| Premium/Niche | $9.99-14.99 | $29.99-49.99 | $99.99-149.99 |

**Key Insight:** Higher-priced apps convert BETTER (2.7% vs 1.5% median)

### Tools for Monetization

- **RevenueCat** - Subscription management, free up to $2.5K MTR
- **Superwall** - A/B test paywalls remotely without app updates
- **Adapty** - Alternative to RevenueCat, free up to $10K MTR

### Affiliate Revenue (Secondary)

Apple killed the App Store affiliate program, but you can:
- Link to supplements/products via Amazon Associates
- Recommend info products you create
- Partner with brands for preferred rates
- Track what converts, white-label winners

### THE GUILT-DRIVEN CONTENT STRATEGY (Follow Jesus App: $138K in 5 days)

This is the most effective content strategy for niches with emotional pain points:

**The Formula:**
```
1. Identify your niche's GUILT POINT (what do they feel bad about?)
2. Create content that TRIGGERS that guilt (fear, shame, inadequacy)
3. Immediately offer YOUR APP as the SOLUTION/REDEMPTION
4. Make SHARING part of using the product (built-in viral loop)
```

**Video Format (works on TikTok/Reels):**
```
[0-3 sec] Hook that triggers guilt/fear
"Warning: You're not doing enough to [niche goal]"
"God showed me something about people who don't [desired behavior]"
"The reason you're failing at [goal] is..."

[3-15 sec] Amplify the pain
Make them feel the shame/inadequacy
Show consequences of not taking action

[15-25 sec] Introduce your solution
"That's why I built this app..."
"This app helps you [solve guilt] every day"

[25-30 sec] Hard CTA
"Download now" - no soft pitch
"Link in bio"
```

**Why It Works:**
- Emotionally manipulative content FORCES engagement
- People feel obligated to like/share/comment to prove they're "real" [identity]
- The app solves a guilt problem - users feel PRODUCTIVE
- Sharing is part of the product = users become promoters

**Guilt Points by Niche:**
| Niche | Guilt Point | Content Hook |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Christian | Not praying/evangelizing enough | "Warning from God about..." |
| Fitness | Not being disciplined | "You're lying to yourself about..." |
| Parents | Not being present enough | "Your kids will remember this..." |
| Students | Procrastinating | "You're wasting your potential..." |
| Health | Not taking care of body | "Your future self is watching..." |
| Relationships | Not being a good partner | "This is why they'll leave..." |

**Timing Matters:**
- Christian apps → Christmas, Easter, Lent
- Fitness apps → January, pre-summer
- Productivity → September (back to school/work)
- Relationship → Valentine's, engagement season

## PHASE 5: THE PORTFOLIO APPROACH

### The Math

- Only ~5% of indie apps make $100K+/year
- Solution: Build 10-30 apps, let winners emerge
- Kill losers fast, double down on winners

### Portfolio Framework (Max Artemov: $22K MRR from 30 apps)

**Before Building:**
1. ASO research: keyword popularity >20, difficulty <60
2. Confirm search volume in Google Trends
3. Check if similar apps are making money (Sensor Tower)

**After Launching:**
- Week 1-2: Initial App Store boost, measure downloads
- Week 3-4: If <100 downloads, likely won't grow
- Month 2: Kill if no organic traction, double down if growing

### Kill vs Double-Down Criteria

**KILL when:**
- Consistently negative acquisition ROI
- DAU declining with no recovery
- High support burden vs revenue
- Market shrinking

**DOUBLE DOWN when:**
- LTV > CAC (positive unit economics)
- Organic traffic growing
- Day 30 retention >5.8% (benchmark)
- Trends aligning with your positioning

### Scaling Winners

**Revenue Milestones:**
| MRR | Action |
|-----|--------|
| $1K | Proof of concept, keep iterating |
| $2.5K | Switch to RevenueCat paid tier |
| $5K | Invest in paid ads (TikTok first) |
| $10K | Consider hiring support help |
| $30K | Optimize seriously, maybe build team |

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Pick niche by psychology, not technology** - What deep need does it fulfill?

2. **Research before building** - 80% of success is picking the right app to build

3. **Speed > Perfection** - Ship in 2-3 weeks, iterate based on data

4. **Build in public** - Your journey IS the marketing

5. **Weekly subscriptions win** - Higher LTV, easier to test

6. **Portfolio beats single app** - Numbers game, let winners emerge

7. **Distribution > Product** - An audience will make any decent app work

8. **Kill fast, double down faster** - Don't get emotionally attached
