## the 5 biggest takeaways

### 1. clipping army > your own following

clavvicular has 700K on tiktok and 237K on instagram. decent numbers. but that's not where his reach comes from.

he runs 750 people who repost his content to THEIR accounts. some of those pages have 70K+ followers each.

750 accounts posting daily = 750 chances at virality per video. compare that to posting once on your own page.

cost? $1,700/month to the clipping community. what does $1,700/month in tiktok ads get you? maybe 500K impressions.

750 organic accounts posting? easily 5M+ impressions.

**takeaway:** stop thinking about growing your account. build an army that grows for you.

### 2. new mechanism naming is the highest-leverage copywriting move

he doesn't sell "looksmaxxing." he sells "Ascension."

why? if someone tried looksmaxxing and it didn't work, they won't buy "better looksmaxxing." they'll think "i already tried that."

new name = new buying decision. same outcome, completely different framing.

**takeaway:** create a proprietary name for whatever you teach. "Email Protocol" not "email marketing." "Divine Protocol" not "prayer routine." "Metabolic Override" not "workout plan."

### 3. the community IS lead gen for high-ticket

$49/month times 1,400 members = $68K. nice.

but the real money is $3-5K coaching upsells to the most engaged members.

community pays for itself while filtering for serious buyers. then you ascend them.

**takeaway:** price your community to break even or profit slightly. the real margin is in coaching/done-for-you upsells to people who already trust you.

### 4. gate your best content at day 14

his content unlocks:
- day 0: basics (quick win)
- day 7: module 2 (1 week invested)
- day 10: module 3 (past cancel window)
- day 14: premium stuff (inside billing cycle)

by the time they access the best content, they've renewed or decided to stay.

**takeaway:** don't dump everything on day 1. timed unlocks create anticipation and reduce churn.

### 5. infrastructure cost is almost nothing

- skool: $99/mo
- framer: $20/mo
- wistia: $19/mo
- typeform: $29/mo

total: $167/mo for a $70K/mo business.

99.7% margin on infrastructure.

**takeaway:** stop looking for the perfect tech stack. $200/mo of tools can run a six-figure operation.
