## FAQ Section

### Can I use multiple POD platforms?
Yes. Many successful sellers use 2-3 platforms. Route orders by product type, customer location, or margin requirements. Apps like ShipStation help manage multi-platform fulfillment.

### Which has the best Etsy integration?
Printful and Printify both have strong Etsy integrations. Printful's is slightly more polished. Both auto-sync orders and update tracking. Printify offers more product options on Etsy.

### How do I choose a Printify print provider?
Look at: Star rating (4.5+), review count (500+), production time, location (closer = faster shipping), product quality photos. Order samples from top 3 candidates before committing.

### Do any offer same-day shipping?
No POD platform guarantees same-day. Printful averages fastest production (2-3 days). Some Printify providers offer rush processing. Set customer expectations for 5-7 day delivery.

### Which is best for international sellers?
Gooten has the broadest international network. Printful has EU and Mexico facilities. Printify depends on provider locations. For consistent global service, Gooten edges ahead.

### Can I white-label shipping labels?
Printful offers custom pack-ins, inside labels, and neutral shipping labels. Printify depends on provider. Gooten offers basic white-labeling. Printful has best branding options.
