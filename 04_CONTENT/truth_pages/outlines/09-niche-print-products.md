## FAQ Section

### What's the highest margin print product?
Custom pet portraits and phone cases both hit 60-70% margins. Canvas prints at large sizes reach 70-75%. Specialty items like guitar picks can exceed 80% but have smaller markets.

### Which products have the lowest return rates?
Home decor (canvas, metal prints): under 2% returns. Phone cases: 3-5%. Apparel: 8-15%. Avoid fitted apparel to minimize returns. Clear size guides help.

### Should I add products one at a time?
Yes. Add 1-2 products, test for 30-60 days, then evaluate. Adding too many dilutes focus and complicates inventory/listing management. Kill non-performers.

### What about seasonal products?
Seasonal products (holiday ornaments, summer items) can be profitable but require timing. Launch 6-8 weeks before season. Clear inventory after. Don't hold year-round.

### How do I choose products for my niche?
Ask your audience. Look at what competitors sell successfully. Consider gifting occasions. Match product to content (gaming audience = desk mats, not kitchen items).

### Do I need different designs for different products?
Often yes. A t-shirt design may not work on a phone case. Optimize per product. Some designs are universal, most need adaptation.
