## Implementation Notes

### File Formats by Type
- **Calculators:** Google Sheets (shareable, no download friction)
- **Checklists:** PDF (professional, easy to consume)
- **Templates:** Notion or Google Docs (editable, collaborative)
- **Visual guides:** PDF with diagrams (clear, printable)
- **Swipe files:** PDF + raw Google Doc (readable + copy-pasteable)

### Delivery Method
1. Gated with email capture on landing page
2. Instant download via email autoresponder
3. Follow-up sequence (3-5 emails) leading to upsell

### Tracking
- Add UTM parameters to all download links
- Track in LEDGER/FUNNEL_METRICS.csv
- A/B test headlines and CTAs monthly
