# Thread: How I Automated Content Posting (3 Hour Setup)

## Tweet 1
I spent 6 hours per week posting content to 5 platforms.

Now it takes 45 minutes per week.

Setup took 3 hours. Here's the exact system:

## Tweet 2
Step 1: Content creation stays manual

I write 1 long-form post per week (2000 words).

AI can't match my voice yet. This is the only part I don't automate.

Takes 3 hours on Sunday morning.

## Tweet 3
Step 2: Repurposing with Repurpose.io ($12.50/month)

Paste my blog post URL into Repurpose.

It creates: 10 tweets, 3 LinkedIn posts, 1 newsletter, 5 Instagram captions.

Takes 5 minutes. I edit for 15 minutes. Much better than starting from scratch.

## Tweet 4
Step 3: Scheduling with Buffer ($6/month for Essentials)

Load all content into Buffer on Sunday.

Schedule tweets for M/W/F 9am and 3pm.
LinkedIn posts for T/Th 11am.
Instagram for daily at 7pm.

Takes 10 minutes to schedule a full week.

## Tweet 5
Step 4: Newsletter auto-send with ConvertKit ($29/month)

My blog post publishes Monday 6am.

ConvertKit automation sends it to 2,847 subscribers at 8am automatically.

I write the email intro on Sunday (15 minutes), schedule it, forget about it.

## Tweet 6
Step 5: Cross-posting with Zapier ($20/month)

When blog post goes live, Zapier auto-posts to:
- Dev.to
- Medium
- Hashnode

Adds canonical URL so no SEO penalty. Takes 0 minutes of my time.

## Tweet 7
The math:

Old way: 6 hours per week = 24 hours per month
New way: 45 minutes per week = 3 hours per month

Saved: 21 hours per month
Cost: $67.50/month for tools

Worth $10/hour? Absolutely. More time for revenue-generating work.
