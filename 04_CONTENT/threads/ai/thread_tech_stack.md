# Thread: My $0 Tech Stack That Makes $4.2k/Month

## Tweet 1
My $0 tech stack makes $4,200/month.

No fancy tools. No subscriptions until revenue hit $2k.

Here's everything I used to build a profitable side project:

## Tweet 2
Writing: Google Docs

Yes, really. I wrote 47 blog posts in Google Docs before buying any writing tools.

Free. Works offline. Has version history. That's all you need to start.

## Tweet 3
Design: Canva Free

Made my landing page, social graphics, and lead magnets.

The free version has 250k templates. I used maybe 12 of them.

Upgraded to Pro ($12.99/mo) after my first $500 month.

## Tweet 4
Website: GitHub Pages + Jekyll

Static site. Free hosting. Custom domain was my only cost ($12/year).

No Webflow. No WordPress hosting. No monthly fees.

Loaded in 0.8 seconds. Google loved it.

## Tweet 5
Email: Mailchimp Free

2,000 contacts free. I didn't hit that limit until month 7.

Basic automation worked fine. Welcome emails, lead magnet delivery.

Now I use ConvertKit but only because I hit 2,500 subscribers.

## Tweet 6
Analytics: Google Analytics + Plausible Free

GA for deep insights. Plausible for quick daily checks.

Plausible free tier gives 10k pageviews/month. I was at 3k for the first 6 months.

## Tweet 7
The pattern: Use free tools until they hurt.

Notion free plan stopped working at 500 pages. Then I paid.

GitHub Actions free tier covered me until 5k builds/month. Then I paid.

Pay when the free version blocks growth, not before.

Total first-year cost: $12 domain + $0 tools = $12
