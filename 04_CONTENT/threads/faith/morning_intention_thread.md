# 5 ways to start your morning with intention

**Tweet 1:**
5 ways to start your morning with intention.

Tested these for 30 days. All of them work.

**Tweet 2:**
1. Don't check your phone for the first 30 minutes.

Put it in another room when you go to bed.

Your brain needs time to wake up without notifications.

**Tweet 3:**
2. Write 3 things you're grateful for.

Use your notes app or paper.

Takes 90 seconds. Shifts your whole perspective.

**Tweet 4:**
3. Read something true before anything else.

One chapter. One Psalm. Two verses.

Truth before noise changes your filter for the day.

**Tweet 5:**
4. Pray for 5 minutes before planning your day.

Talk like you're talking to someone who cares.

Your tasks look different when you start here.

**Tweet 6:**
5. Move your body for 10 minutes.

Walk. Stretch. Push-ups.

Physical movement wakes up your mind.

**Tweet 7:**
Pick one of these tomorrow.

Just one.

Build from there.
