# Thread: The morning routine that changed my spiritual life

## Tweet 1 (Hook)
I changed one thing about my mornings 6 months ago.

My prayer life went from sporadic to daily.

Here's the exact routine:

## Tweet 2
First: Phone stays in another room until after my routine.

This alone removed 90% of my morning distractions.

No notifications. No "quick check" that turns into 20 minutes.

## Tweet 3
5:45 AM: Wake up, splash water on face, make coffee.

No checking anything. No thinking about work.

Just the physical routine. Muscle memory kicks in.

## Tweet 4
6:00 AM: Open Bible to whatever book I'm reading through.

Not random verses. Sequential reading.

Currently in Isaiah. Last month was Proverbs.

Read for 15-20 minutes.

## Tweet 5
6:20 AM: Journal for 10 minutes.

I write:
- What stood out from reading
- What I'm grateful for (3 things)
- One request I'm bringing to God

Simple. Repeatable.

## Tweet 6
6:30 AM: Prayer time.

I use the ACTS model:
- Adoration (who God is)
- Confession (where I failed)
- Thanksgiving (what I'm grateful for)
- Supplication (what I need)

10-15 minutes.

## Tweet 7
That's it. 45 minutes total.

Done before 7 AM. Before the chaos starts.

The key: Make it so simple you can do it tired, stressed, or distracted.

Your morning sets your whole day.
