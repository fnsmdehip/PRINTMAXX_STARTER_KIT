# How to find peace when everything feels chaotic

**Tweet 1:**
Everything felt chaotic last month.

Work. Family. Money. All of it.

Here's what helped me find peace.

**Tweet 2:**
1. I stopped trying to fix everything at once.

Wrote down 3 things I could control.

Let go of the rest (for now).

**Tweet 3:**
2. I prayed shorter prayers.

Stopped trying to cover everything in one prayer.

5 sentences. One topic. Done.

**Tweet 4:**
3. I deleted news apps for 2 weeks.

Checked news once a day on my laptop.

Cut my anxiety in half.

**Tweet 5:**
4. I told 2 people I was struggling.

Texted them Saturday morning.

Both responded within an hour.

**Tweet 6:**
5. I went outside every day for 15 minutes.

No phone. No agenda.

Fresh air resets your nervous system.

**Tweet 7:**
Peace isn't the absence of chaos.

It's knowing what to do when chaos comes.

You build it in small decisions.
