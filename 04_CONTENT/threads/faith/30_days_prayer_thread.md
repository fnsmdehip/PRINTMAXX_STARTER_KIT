# What I learned from 30 days of daily prayer

**Tweet 1:**
I prayed every day for 30 days.

Missed zero days.

Here's what changed.

**Tweet 2:**
Day 1-7: Felt awkward.

Didn't know what to say. Used a lot of "um" and "uh."

Kept going anyway.

**Tweet 3:**
Day 8-14: Started talking instead of performing.

Stopped trying to sound spiritual.

Just talked.

**Tweet 4:**
Day 15-21: Noticed I was less reactive.

Someone cut me off in traffic. Didn't spiral.

Small shift, big impact.

**Tweet 5:**
Day 22-28: Started praying for other people by name.

3 people per day.

Felt less focused on my own problems.

**Tweet 6:**
Day 29-30: Realized I wasn't doing this out of obligation anymore.

I actually wanted to.

That was new.

**Tweet 7:**
What I learned:

Consistency beats intensity.

5 minutes every day beats 2 hours once a week.
