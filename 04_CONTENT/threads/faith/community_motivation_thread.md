# Why community matters more than motivation

**Tweet 1:**
Motivation fades.

Community stays.

Here's why that matters.

**Tweet 2:**
I spent 2 years trying to "stay motivated" about faith.

Read books. Listened to podcasts. Watched videos.

Worked for 3 days, then faded.

**Tweet 3:**
Then I joined a small group.

6 people. Met every Thursday.

Didn't feel motivated most Thursdays. Went anyway.

**Tweet 4:**
Month 1: Showed up out of commitment.

Month 2: Showed up because I'd miss them.

Month 3: Showed up because they knew my story.

**Tweet 5:**
Motivation is a feeling.

Community is a structure.

Structures hold you when feelings don't.

**Tweet 6:**
You don't need 50 friends.

You need 3-5 people who check in.

Who notice when you're quiet.

**Tweet 7:**
Find one group.

Show up for 6 weeks straight.

You'll see the difference.
