# The 3-hour physique: my actual routine

Tweet 1:
I spend 3 hours per week in the gym.

That's it. No 6-day splits. No 2-hour sessions.

Lost 22 lbs. Added visible muscle. Strength up 40% on all major lifts.

Here's the exact routine I've run for 11 months.

Tweet 2:
Monday, Wednesday, Friday. 60 minutes each.

Upper body Monday.
Lower body Wednesday.
Full body Friday.

3 compound movements per session.
3-4 sets each.
Rest as long as needed.

Total: 9 exercises per week.

Tweet 3:
Monday (Upper):
- Bench press 4x6
- Barbell row 4x8
- Overhead press 3x10

That's the session. 45 minutes with warm-up.

No cable flies. No tricep kickbacks. Just push, pull, press heavy.

Tweet 4:
Wednesday (Lower):
- Squat 4x6
- Romanian deadlift 3x8
- Bulgarian split squat 3x10

Legs smoked in 40 minutes.

I used to do 8 leg exercises. Made zero progress. Cut to 3, went heavier.

Tweet 5:
Friday (Full Body):
- Deadlift 3x5
- Pull-ups 3x8
- Dumbbell bench 3x10

Mix of everything. Lighter weight. Practice the patterns.

This day feels easy but keeps me consistent.

Tweet 6:
Progressive overload is simple.

Add 5 lbs when I hit all reps.

Bench went from 135x6 to 205x6 in 11 months.

Same 3 exercises. Just heavier each month.

Tweet 7:
Why this works:

You can't skip. It's only 3 days.
You recover fully. Strength goes up.
No decision fatigue. Same pattern every week.

I ran complicated programs for 2 years. Got weaker.

Simple beats perfect every time.
