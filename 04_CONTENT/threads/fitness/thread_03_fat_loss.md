# Thread: Lost 20 lbs in 4 months without feeling hungry

## Tweet 1 (Hook)
January: 195 lbs, 26% body fat.
May: 175 lbs, 18% body fat.

No meal prep company. No keto. No suffering.

Here's exactly what I did:

## Tweet 2
Step 1: Found my maintenance calories.

Used an online calculator: got 2,400.
Ate 2,400 for 2 weeks. Weight stayed flat.

That's my actual maintenance. Now I had a baseline.

## Tweet 3
Step 2: Cut 500 calories per day.

2,400 - 500 = 1,900 calories.

That's 1 lb per week. Sustainable. Not dramatic.

I could still eat real food. Just less of it.

## Tweet 4
Step 3: Prioritized protein.

Target: 1g per lb of body weight = ~175g/day.

Why: Keeps you full. Preserves muscle. Makes the deficit feel easier.

Chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, protein shakes got me there.

## Tweet 5
Step 4: Walked more.

Added 30-minute walks after lunch. 5 days per week.

No extra gym time. Just walking.

Burns ~150 calories. Adds up to 3,000+ per month.

## Tweet 6
Step 5: Kept lifting heavy.

Didn't switch to "fat loss workouts" with light weights.

Same program. Same intensity.

Muscle is expensive to maintain. Your body will burn fat instead.

## Tweet 7
The results timeline:

Month 1: 5 lbs lost. Felt easy.
Month 2: 5 lbs lost. Got hungry around week 6.
Month 3: 5 lbs lost. Added a diet break (ate at maintenance for 1 week).
Month 4: 5 lbs lost. Hit 175.

Slow works. Fast doesn't last.
