# Video Script: You're not eating enough protein

**Format:** TikTok/Reels (30-45 sec)
**Hook timing:** First 3 seconds

---

## HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Holding food container]
"You think you eat enough protein. You probably don't."

## PROBLEM (3-15 sec)
[Showing tracking app]
"I thought I was getting 120 grams a day.

Started tracking. Reality: 67 grams.

Almost half of what I thought. That's why I wasn't seeing results."

## SOLUTION (15-30 sec)
[Quick cuts of protein sources]
"Now I track and hit 150g minimum.

Breakfast: 4 eggs, Greek yogurt. That's 40g before 8 AM.

Lunch: Chicken or fish. Another 40g.

Dinner and snacks handle the rest.

Same meals. Just measured."

## CTA (30-40 sec)
[Face camera]
"Track your protein for 3 days. Just 3.

Bet you're eating less than you think.

Comment your number when you find out."

---

**Text overlays:**
- 0:02 - "The protein reality check"
- 0:10 - "Thought: 120g / Actual: 67g"
- 0:20 - "150g target"
- 0:35 - "Track for 3 days"

**Audio:** Upbeat, energetic background
