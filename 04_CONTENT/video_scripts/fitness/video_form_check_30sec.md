# 30-Second Form Check (Squat)

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Hook:** First 3 seconds

---

## Script

**[0-3 sec - HOOK]**
"Your squat is breaking your knees. Here's the fix."

[Show clip of knees caving in during squat]

**[3-12 sec - PROBLEM]**
"Most people let their knees collapse inward at the bottom. That's how you get knee pain."

[Demo the wrong way, knees caving]

**[12-25 sec - SOLUTION]**
"Push your knees OUT as you descend. Think about spreading the floor apart with your feet."

[Demo the correct way, knees tracking over toes]

**[25-30 sec - CTA]**
"Film yourself from the side. Check your knee position. Fix it before you add weight."

[Text overlay: "Save this for leg day"]

---

## B-Roll Needed
- Close-up of knees caving (wrong)
- Close-up of knees tracking properly (right)
- Side angle of full squat with good form

## Text Overlays
- "Knees out, not in"
- "Spread the floor"
