# 30-Day Consistency Challenge

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Hook:** First 3 seconds

---

## Script

**[0-3 sec - HOOK]**
"Can you do this for 30 days straight? Most people can't."

[Show intense face, challenge accepted energy]

**[3-10 sec - THE CHALLENGE]**
"Train 3 days per week for 30 days. That's 12 workouts. No skipping."

[Text overlay appears with the rules]

**[10-18 sec - THE RULES]**
"Each workout: 3 exercises, 3 sets each. 45 minutes max. That's it."

[Show example workout on screen]

**[18-25 sec - THE PROOF]**
"Take a photo today. Take another photo on day 30. Post both."

[Show before photo example, calendar with X marks]

**[25-30 sec - CTA]**
"Comment 'I'm in' if you're starting today. I'll check on you in 30 days."

[Text overlay: "30-Day Challenge. You in?"]

- Example workout list on phone
