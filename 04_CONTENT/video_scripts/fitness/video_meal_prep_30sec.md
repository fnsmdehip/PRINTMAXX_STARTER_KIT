# Meal Prep in 30 Minutes

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Hook:** First 3 seconds

---

## Script

**[0-3 sec - HOOK]**
"I meal prep 5 days of lunch in 30 minutes. Here's how."

[Show 5 containers lined up, ready to go]

**[3-10 sec - INGREDIENTS]**
"2 lbs chicken thighs. 3 cups rice. 1 bag frozen broccoli. Salt, pepper, garlic powder."

[Quick shots of each ingredient]

**[10-22 sec - PROCESS]**
"Rice cooker: 20 minutes. Oven: chicken at 400 degrees for 25 minutes. Microwave: broccoli for 8 minutes."

[Time-lapse of cooking, all three happening at once]

**[22-28 sec - PORTIONING]**
"Divide into 5 containers. 6oz chicken, 1 cup rice, broccoli. Done."

[Show portioning process, stacking in fridge]

**[28-30 sec - CTA]**
"No thinking. No decisions. Just eat."

[Text overlay: "Save this"]
