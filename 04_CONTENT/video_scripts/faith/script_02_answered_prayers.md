# Video Script: Why I track my prayers

**Format:** TikTok/Reels (45-60 sec)
**Hook timing:** First 3 seconds

---

## HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Holding notebook, opening it]
"I've written down 247 prayer requests. Here's why."

## STORY (3-20 sec)
[Flipping through pages]
"Last year I felt like God wasn't answering my prayers.

Then I looked back at this notebook.

23 answered prayers in one month. I just forgot them.

Our memory is terrible at tracking God's faithfulness."

## METHOD (20-40 sec)
[Showing the page layout]
"Here's my simple system.

Date I prayed. What I asked for. Space for the answer.

When God answers, I write the date and how.

Some things took 18 months. Some took 3 days.

But seeing them written down? That builds faith."

## CTA (40-55 sec)
[Face camera, holding notebook]
"You don't need a fancy journal. A note on your phone works.

Start today. Write one prayer request.

Check back in a month.

I promise you'll see God working."

---

**Text overlays:**
- 0:02 - "247 prayers tracked"
- 0:15 - "23 answered in one month"
- 0:30 - "Simple system"
- 0:45 - "Start with one prayer"
