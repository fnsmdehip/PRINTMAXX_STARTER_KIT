# Video Script: 60-Second Workflow Breakdown

**Length:** 60 seconds
**Topic:** Weekly content creation workflow
**Hook:** "How I create 20 pieces of content per week in 4 hours."

---

## Script

**[0-5s] Hook**
I publish 20 pieces of content per week.
Takes 4 hours total. Here's the system.

**[6-15s] Step 1: Core Creation**
[Screen: Google Doc]
Sunday morning, write 1 long blog post.
2000 words. Takes 3 hours.
This is the only manual writing I do.

**[16-28s] Step 2: AI Repurposing**
[Screen: Repurpose.io interface]
Paste blog URL into Repurpose.io.
It generates:
- 10 tweets
- 3 LinkedIn posts
- 5 Instagram captions
- 1 newsletter
Takes 5 minutes. I edit for 15 minutes.

**[29-42s] Step 3: Scheduling**
[Screen: Buffer dashboard]
Load everything into Buffer.
Schedule for the whole week.
Monday through Sunday, all platforms.
Takes 10 minutes.

**[43-52s] Step 4: Auto-Distribution**
[Screen: Zapier automation]
Blog publishes Monday 6am.
Zapier auto-posts to Medium, Dev.to, Hashnode.
Newsletter auto-sends at 8am.

**[53-60s] Results**
Old way: 15 hours per week
New way: 4 hours per week
11 hours saved. Tools cost $67/month.

- Fast screen recordings of each tool
