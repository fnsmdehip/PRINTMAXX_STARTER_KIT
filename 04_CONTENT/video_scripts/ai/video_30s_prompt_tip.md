# Video Script: 30-Second Prompt Tip

**Length:** 30 seconds
**Topic:** Negative examples in prompts
**Hook:** "Your AI prompts are bad because you're only showing what to do."

---

## Script

**[0-4s] Hook**
Your AI prompts suck. Here's why.

**[5-12s] Problem**
You write: "Use a casual tone."
AI doesn't know what you mean by casual.
[Screen: Show generic AI output]

**[13-24s] Solution**
Try this instead:
"Don't use these words: leverage, synergy, utilize."
[Screen: Show improved prompt]
[Screen: Show much better output]
AI learns faster from "avoid this" than "be like this."

**[25-30s] Results & CTA**
My outputs got 3x better using this. Try it.

---

## Visual Notes
- Text on screen for banned words
- Side-by-side comparison of outputs
- Fast-paced editing
- Bold text for key phrases
- No music, just clear voice
