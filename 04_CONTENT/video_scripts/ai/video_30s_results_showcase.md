# Video Script: 30-Second Results Showcase

**Length:** 30 seconds
**Topic:** AI automation ROI
**Hook:** "Here's what happened after I automated my content for 90 days."

---

## Script

**[0-4s] Hook**
I automated my content 90 days ago.
Here are the numbers.

**[5-12s] Metric 1: Time**
[Screen: Graph showing time saved]
Before: 15 hours per week on content
After: 4 hours per week
Saved: 11 hours weekly = 143 hours in 90 days

**[13-20s] Metric 2: Output**
[Screen: Content count visualization]
Before: 3 blog posts per week
After: 5 blog posts + 15 social posts per week
Output increased 4x

**[21-26s] Metric 3: Traffic**
[Screen: Analytics dashboard]
Website traffic: Up 340%
Email list: Grew from 487 to 2,847 subscribers

**[27-30s] Cost & CTA**
Total tool cost: $67/month. ROI is obvious. Tutorial in bio.
