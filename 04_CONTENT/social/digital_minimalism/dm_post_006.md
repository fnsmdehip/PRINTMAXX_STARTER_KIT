why is the digital minimalism movement mostly secular?

86% of Gen Z wants less screen time. the tools they have: generic timers, screen time limits they can bypass in 2 taps.

what they don't have: purpose-driven screen blocking. something that replaces the scroll with something meaningful.

prayer before phone. scripture before social media. devotion before doomscrolling.

the faith community has the answer to phone addiction. we just haven't built it yet.
