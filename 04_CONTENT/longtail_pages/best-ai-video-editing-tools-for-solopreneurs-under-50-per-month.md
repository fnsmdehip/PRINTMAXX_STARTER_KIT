# Best AI video editing tools for solopreneurs under $50 per month

## Tool Deep Dives

### Descript - $12/mo (The Core Tool)

**What makes it special:**

Edits video by editing text. Record video, Descript transcribes it, you cut the transcript, video gets cut automatically.

**Key features:**

1. **Filler word removal**
   - Automatically detects "um," "uh," "like," "you know"
   - Removes them with one click
   - Saves 15-20 minutes per video

2. **Overdub (voice cloning)**
   - Record your voice once
   - Type corrections
   - AI speaks them in your voice
   - Fix mistakes without re-recording

3. **Studio Sound**
   - Makes cheap mic sound like expensive mic
   - Removes background noise
   - One-click improvement

4. **Caption generation**
   - Auto-generates captions
   - Multiple styles (TikTok style, minimal, bold)
   - Export SRT file for other platforms

5. **Multi-track editing**
   - Edit multiple video/audio tracks
   - Add b-roll overlays
   - Picture-in-picture support

**Best for:**
- Podcasts
- Talking head videos
- Screen recordings with voiceover
- Interview content

**Limits on $12/mo plan:**
- 10 hours of transcription per month
- 720p export (1080p costs $24/mo)
- Watermark on videos

**When to upgrade to $24/mo:**
- Need 1080p export
- Making more than 10 videos/month
- Want to remove watermark

**Real workflow example:**

1. Record 15-minute video (iPhone or webcam)
2. Upload to Descript (processes in 3 minutes)
3. Read transcript, delete rambling sections (5 minutes)
4. Click "Remove filler words" (automatic)
5. Add captions (2 minutes to customize)
6. Export vertical version for TikTok (2 minutes)

Total editing time: 12 minutes.

## Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Over-Editing

AI makes editing too easy. Don't spend 2 hours perfecting a 30-second TikTok.

Rule: Editing time should be < video length. 30-second video = max 30 minutes editing.

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Audio

Good audio > good video for most content.

Use Descript's Studio Sound on every video. Takes 0 effort, improves quality by 50%.

### Mistake 3: Wrong Aspect Ratio

TikTok needs 9:16. YouTube needs 16:9. Instagram Reels needs 9:16. LinkedIn video works best at 1:1.

Export different versions for each platform. CapCut and Descript both support this.

### Mistake 4: Overusing Effects

AI tools offer 100+ effects. Use 2-3 max per video.

More effects = looks amateur.

### Mistake 5: Not Reviewing AI Suggestions

OpusClip's auto-clips need manual review. Sometimes picks boring moments.

Always watch before posting.
