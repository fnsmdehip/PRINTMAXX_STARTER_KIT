# Best AI tools for content production (solopreneurs)

## Quick Answer

Use Claude ($20/mo) for depth and reasoning. ChatGPT ($20/mo) for outlines and quick drafts. Perplexity ($20/mo) for research. Total: $25/mo if you combine subscriptions. Or start free with ChatGPT free tier.

## The Tool Stack

### 1. Claude Pro ($20/mo)
**Best for:** Long-form content, detailed reasoning

- 200k token context window (entire codebase fits)
- Can reason through complex arguments
- Better at nuance than ChatGPT
- Slower (5-10 sec vs 1-2 sec)

Use for:
- Blog posts (800+ words)
- Email sequences
- Long tutorials
- Analysis and deep dives

Not great for:
- Quick outlines (too slow)
- Social posts (overkill)

### 2. ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo)
**Best for:** Speed and volume

- Fast outputs (1-2 seconds)
- Good for lists and outlines
- Best for social media content
- Can use image uploads for visual ideas

Use for:
- Social post ideas (5 at a time)
- Blog post outlines
- Email subject lines
- Quick summaries

Not great for:
- Nuanced analysis (Claude better)
- Technical depth

### 3. Perplexity AI ($20/mo)
**Best for:** Research

- Searches the internet in real time
- Cites sources directly
- Better than Google + ChatGPT for recent data
- Fast enough for quick research

Use for:
- Finding statistics
- Checking recent trends
- Verifying claims
- Building research databases

Not great for:
- Creative writing
- Opinion pieces

## Real Workflow: 1000-Word Blog Post

**Step 1: Research (Perplexity, 15 min)**
- Query: "What's new in [topic] in 2026"
- Get 5 sources, recent data points
- Copy key facts into doc

**Step 2: Outline (ChatGPT, 10 min)**
- Paste research into ChatGPT
- Prompt: "Create 5-point outline for [topic]"
- Copy outline to doc

**Step 3: First Draft (Claude, 30 min)**
- Paste outline + research into Claude
- Prompt: "Write 1000-word post based on outline. Use conversational tone, include specific examples"
- Claude writes while you grab coffee

**Step 4: Edit (Your brain, 20 min)**
- Read Claude's draft
- Fix any AI cringe (remove "leverage," "empower")
- Add your personal story
- Verify facts

**Total time: 75 minutes for a publishable 1000-word post**

Without AI: 2-3 hours. AI saves 50% time.

## Cost Per Article

- Claude Pro: $20/100 articles = $0.20
- ChatGPT Plus: $20/100 articles = $0.20
- Perplexity: $20/50 articles = $0.40
- Total: ~$0.80 per article

If you write 4 articles per month, that's $3.20/month in AI costs. $20 subscription covers 6+ writers.

## When to Skip AI

Don't use AI for:
- Personal stories (you write this)
- Takes on controversial topics (needs your voice)
- Technical tutorials (you test the steps)
- Product reviews (you review it yourself)

AI handles 60% of the work (research, structure, first draft). You handle 40% (voice, stories, verification, editing).

## Free Tier Alternative

If bootstrapping:
- ChatGPT free: 30 messages/day (enough for 1 post)
- Google Gemini free: 50 messages/day
- Perplexity free: 5 searches/day

Using free tiers takes 2x longer but costs $0.

## Red Flags

- Claude output is robotic (your editing skills matter)
- ChatGPT repeats itself (cut unnecessary paragraphs)
- Perplexity has outdated source (verify dates)
- Using AI without editing (obvious to readers)

## Pro Tips

**Prompt better, get better output:**

Bad: "Write a blog post about content marketing"

Good: "Write a 1000-word blog post about content marketing for solopreneurs. Include 3 specific examples. Use a conversational tone like you're texting a smart friend. End with a clear CTA."

The more specific, the less editing you do.
