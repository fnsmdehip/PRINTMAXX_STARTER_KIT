# Platform arbitrage: where to post for maximum revenue 2026

## Quick answer

Platform arbitrage means posting the same content where it pays the most. Facebook Reels: $4.40/1K views. YouTube Shorts: $0.01-$0.06/1K. Same video, 70-440x more revenue on FB. Cross-post everything to FB Reels first.

|----------|-----|----------|
| Facebook Reels | $4.40 | Short video, all niches |

| X/Twitter | $1-5 (verified) | Text, threads |

| Pinterest | $0 direct | Affiliate traffic |

## The arbitrage strategy

Tier 1 (post first): Facebook Reels ($4.40/1K), YouTube Long-form ($3-15/1K).
Tier 2 (cross-post within 24h): X/Twitter, TikTok.
Tier 3 (repurpose format): Medium, Substack, Instagram, Pinterest.
Tier 4 (bonus): Kick (95/5 split), LinkedIn (B2B audience).

## FAQ

### Should I be on every platform?

No. Start with 2-3 where your niche has engagement. Add one at a time.

### How do I avoid duplicate content penalties?

Upload natively. Never share with competitor watermarks.

### Is platform arbitrage sustainable?

RPMs change. The strategy is sustainable because you follow the money. When rates shift, you shift.
