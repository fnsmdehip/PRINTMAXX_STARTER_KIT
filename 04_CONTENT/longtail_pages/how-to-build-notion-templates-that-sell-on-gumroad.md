# How to build Notion templates that sell on Gumroad

The most profitable Notion templates solve one specific problem for one specific audience, take 4-8 hours to build, and sell for $7-27. Top creators earn $500-5,000 per month from 3-5 templates with zero ad spend.

## Quick answer

Build a Notion template that solves a problem you've already solved for yourself. Price it at $7-27. List it on Gumroad. Post about the problem (not the product) 3x per week on social media. Expected timeline to first sale: 7-14 days.

## Step-by-step process

1. Pick a problem you've solved with a system. Prayer tracking, workout logging, AI tool management, content calendars, job searching, habit tracking. If you built a personal system for it, someone will buy a polished version.

2. Research competition on Gumroad. Search your topic. If templates exist and sell 100+ copies, the market is validated. If none exist, the market might not exist either. Validated competition is good.

3. Build the template with these components: 2-3 databases with useful properties, 4-5 page templates with pre-filled content, 2-3 views per database (table, board, calendar, gallery), and a clean design with consistent colors and icons.

4. Write a listing that focuses on the problem, not the features. Lead with pain. Show what's inside with bullet points. Include "who this is for" and "how it works" sections. Keep it under 200 words.

5. Price using this framework: simple single-purpose template ($7-12), multi-database system ($12-19), comprehensive operating system ($19-27). Set pay-what-you-want minimum at 60% of list price.

## Pricing comparison

| Template Type | Price Range | Build Time | Monthly Revenue Potential |
|---------------|-------------|------------|--------------------------|
| Simple tracker | $7-12 | 2-4 hours | $200-500/mo |
| Multi-database system | $12-19 | 4-8 hours | $500-1,500/mo |
| Comprehensive dashboard | $19-27 | 8-16 hours | $1,000-3,000/mo |
| Bundle (3+ templates) | $29-49 | Combined | $1,500-5,000/mo |

## Distribution without ads

Post about the problem your template solves 3x per week on X/Twitter, LinkedIn, and one other platform. Never hard-sell the template. Instead: describe the problem, share how you solved it, mention the template exists in your bio or as a casual PS.

This approach generates 5-15 sales per week for a well-targeted template with a 1,000+ follower audience.

## FAQ

### How many templates should I build?
Start with one. If it sells 20+ copies in the first month, build a second template for the same audience. Then bundle them. 3-5 templates is the sweet spot before diminishing returns.

### Do I need design skills?
No. Notion's built-in formatting (colors, icons, callout blocks, dividers) creates a professional look. Consistent color palette and clean spacing matter more than graphic design.

### What niches sell best on Gumroad?
Productivity, fitness/health tracking, content creation, finance/budgeting, job search, and niche-specific systems (faith, ADHD management, freelancing) consistently perform well.

### How long before I see sales?
With active social posting: 7-14 days to first sale. Without social presence: unpredictable. Gumroad's internal discovery drives some traffic but social media is the primary growth engine.
