### When to upgrade

Only upgrade when a free tier limit blocks revenue generation. Example: upgrade Beehiiv at 2,500 subscribers because you cannot grow your list. Do not upgrade Buffer from free because you can manually post.

## FAQ

### What is the absolute minimum to start?

$0. Use free tiers of everything: ChatGPT free, Gmail for email, Canva for design, Substack for newsletter, manual posting. Revenue $0-100/month possible with zero spend.

### Is $20/month for Claude or ChatGPT worth it?

Yes. This is the highest-ROI tool purchase. AI writes content, code, emails, and strategies 10x faster than manual work.

### Do I really need proxies?

If running multiple social accounts (content farm), yes. Without proxies, platforms connect your accounts and can ban all of them. $25/month insurance.
