# Method stacking: how to combine money methods for 10x revenue

## Quick answer

Running one money method caps your revenue. Stacking 3-5 methods multiplies revenue 4-10x from the same audience because each method monetizes a different behavior. A fitness persona + fitness app + workout guide + supplement affiliates turns one follower into $50-200/year instead of $2-5/year.

## Top 5 revenue stacks

### Stack 1: App + content farm + newsletter (6x multiplier)

Build a niche app (prayer timer, fitness tracker). Run content accounts that promote it. Collect emails via app for newsletter monetization.

| Layer | Revenue per 1,000 users/month |
|-------|-------------------------------|
| App subscriptions | $300-500 |
| Content farm (creator funds) | $50-150 |
| Newsletter (sponsors) | $100-300 |
| Affiliate links (in app + newsletter) | $50-200 |
| Total | $500-1,150 |

Without stacking (app only): $300-500. With stacking: $500-1,150. That is a 1.5-2.3x multiplier, and it compounds as audience grows.

### Stack 2: AI persona + info product + high-ticket (8x multiplier)

Create an AI persona in a niche. Build authority with content. Sell a $27 guide. Upsell to $500+ done-for-you service.

| Layer | Revenue per deal flow |
|-------|----------------------|
| Content (free, builds trust) | $0 (investment) |
| Info product ($27 guide) | $27 per sale |
| Course ($97-197) | $97-197 per sale |
| Done-for-you ($500-2,000) | $500-2,000 per client |
| Consulting ($200-500/hr) | $200-500 per hour |

100 info product buyers, 10% convert to course, 5% to DFY = $2,700 + $970 + $5,000 = $8,670 from 100 initial sales.

### Stack 3: Cold outbound + SaaS + content (5x multiplier)

Use cold email to sell a SaaS tool. Create content about the problem your SaaS solves. Content builds SEO while outbound generates immediate revenue.

### Stack 4: Content farm + affiliate + digital products (4x multiplier)

Post niche content across platforms. Include affiliate links for products you review. Sell your own digital products (templates, guides) to the same audience.

### Stack 5: Newsletter + community + course (6x multiplier)

Free newsletter builds audience. Paid community ($19/month) filters for engaged members. Course ($197) is the premium offering.

## How to choose your stack

| If you have... | Start with... | Add next... |
|---------------|---------------|-------------|
| Technical skills | App + content | Newsletter + affiliates |
| Writing skills | Newsletter + content | Info products + community |
| Sales skills | Cold outbound + SaaS | Content + info products |
| Niche expertise | AI persona + content | Course + high-ticket |
| No skills yet | Content farm + affiliates | Digital products + newsletter |

## Revenue math: stacking vs single method

| Approach | Month 6 revenue | Month 12 revenue |
|----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Single method (app only) | $500-1,000 | $2,000-5,000 |
| 2 methods stacked | $800-2,000 | $4,000-10,000 |
| 3 methods stacked | $1,500-4,000 | $8,000-20,000 |
| 5 methods stacked | $2,500-7,000 | $15,000-40,000 |

The 5-method stack does not take 5x the work because methods share audience, content, and infrastructure.

## FAQ

### Should I start all methods at once?

No. Start with 1-2. Get one generating revenue. Add the next. Each method shares audience from the previous one. Sequence: content first (builds audience), then monetization layers.

### How much time does stacking require?

Each additional method adds 3-5 hours/week initially, dropping to 1-2 hours/week once automated. A 5-method stack runs on 15-20 hours/week total.

### Which method should I start with?

Content (lowest cost, builds audience). Then add monetization layer (affiliates, digital products). Then add direct revenue (app, SaaS, services).
