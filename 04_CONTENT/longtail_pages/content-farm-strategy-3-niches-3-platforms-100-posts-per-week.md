# Content farm strategy: 3 niches, 3 platforms, 100 posts/week

## Quick answer

Pick 3 niches (faith, fitness, tech). Post on 3 platforms each (X, TikTok, Facebook Reels). That is 9 accounts posting 11+ times per week each. 100+ pieces of content total. Cost: $50-200/month. Revenue: $500-$3,000/month from creator funds + affiliates + digital products.

9 accounts. 11-15 posts/week each. 100-135 total.

## Weekly workflow

Monday: Batch create 35 pieces (3 hours). 15 text posts (5/niche for X), 10 short videos (for TikTok), 10 reels (repurposed).

Tuesday-Friday: Schedule + engage (30 min/day). 10 min schedule, 20 min reply to comments.

Saturday-Sunday: Off or light engagement.

## Content types by niche

Faith: prayer prompts, Bible verse graphics, faith + productivity, testimonials, controversial takes.
Fitness: workout tips, supplement reviews, progress stats, contrarian takes, app reviews.
Tech: tool reviews, how-tos, revenue reports, hot takes, behind-the-scenes.

## FAQ

### Is running multiple accounts against TOS?

Technically yes on most platforms. Millions of businesses do it. Risk: account suspension. Mitigate with unique profiles, unique proxies, unique content per account.

### How much time per week?

5 hours total. 3 hours Monday batch + 2 hours engagement through week.

### Can AI create all the content?

80% of text and scripts. You edit for voice and accuracy. Never post without reading.

### What if a niche flops?

8 weeks to decide. Under 50% of average engagement across niches after 8 weeks = drop and replace.
