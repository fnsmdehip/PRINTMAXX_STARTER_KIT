# DM funnel playbook: turn replies into sales on X/Twitter

## Quick answer

Reply bait posts generate 10x more algorithmic reach than regular posts. Each reply is a warm lead. The DM funnel: post reply bait, collect keyword replies, send automated or manual DM with resource, follow up with offer. Conversion rate: 5-15% from DM to sale. On a post with 200 replies, that is 10-30 sales.

## How the DM funnel works

```
Reply bait post (hook + partial value + "reply KEYWORD")
    ↓
200 people reply "KEYWORD"
    ↓
DM each with free resource (PDF, link, template)
    ↓
Follow up in 24 hours with paid offer
    ↓
10-30 sales at $27-97 each
    ↓
$270-$2,910 from one post
```

## Writing reply bait posts

### The formula

Hook (surprising result) + 3-4 bullet insights (enough value to be useful, not enough to fully replicate) + CTA ("reply KEYWORD for the full breakdown")

### Examples

Example 1: "I automated my entire content pipeline. 6 platforms, 10 minutes per day. reply SYSTEM and I'll send you the full workflow."

Example 2: "cold email framework that gets 40% open rates: 1. personal subject line 2. one question per email 3. plain text only. reply EMAIL for all 7 templates."

Example 3: "built a $3K/month app in one weekend. expo + revenuecat + 20 hours of work. reply APP for the step-by-step."

### What makes reply bait work

1. Algorithm boost: replies signal engagement more than likes or retweets. X pushes the post to more timelines.
2. Lead qualification: people who reply want the solution. They are self-selecting as interested.
3. Notification reach: when someone replies, their followers see the notification. Each reply = mini distribution event.

## The DM sequence

### DM 1: Deliver the resource (immediately after reply)

"Hey [name], here's the [resource] I mentioned: [link]

Let me know if you have any questions."

Keep it short. Deliver exactly what you promised.

### DM 2: Follow-up with offer (24 hours later)

"How was the [resource]? Found it useful?

I have a more complete version with [additional value] here: [paid product link]

It's $27 and covers [specific benefit]."

### DM 3: Soft close (3 days later, only if no response)

"No pressure at all. Just wanted to make sure you saw the full version.

If [topic] isn't a priority right now, totally get it."

## Scaling the DM funnel

### Manual (0-50 replies)

Reply to each DM manually. Takes 10-15 minutes per batch of 50. Personal touch converts better.

### Semi-automated (50-200 replies)

Use templates in X DMs. Copy/paste with name customization. 20-30 minutes per batch.

### Automated (200+ replies)

Use tools like Typefully or custom scripts to auto-DM keyword repliers. Careful: X rate limits DMs. Stay under 50 DMs per hour to avoid restrictions.

## Common mistakes

- Sending the DM too late (reply within 1 hour)
- Not delivering the promised resource (breaks trust instantly)
- Hard selling in the first DM (deliver value first)
- Generic DMs without personalization
- Spamming (stay under platform limits)
- Promising something amazing and delivering a mediocre PDF

## FAQ

### Is this against X/Twitter TOS?

Sending manual DMs is fine. Automated mass DMs may violate TOS. Use semi-automated (templates with manual send) to stay safe. X allows automated DMs for business accounts within rate limits.

### How do I get more replies?

Post when your audience is active (8-10 AM EST, 12-2 PM EST). Use strong hooks with specific numbers. Make the reply keyword simple (one word). Post consistently so the algorithm learns your pattern.

### What should I give away for free?

Something valuable enough that people feel good about responding. A PDF checklist, a template, a short video tutorial. Not your full course. Just enough to build trust.

### Can I sell high-ticket through DMs?

Yes. The $27 product is the entry. Follow up buyers with a $97 course or $500+ service. The DM funnel is the top of a larger funnel.
