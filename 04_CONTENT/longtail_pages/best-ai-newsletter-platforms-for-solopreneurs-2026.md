# Best AI newsletter platforms for solopreneurs in 2026

Beehiiv is the best newsletter platform for solopreneurs in 2026. Free tier supports up to 2,500 subscribers with built-in referral programs, monetization tools, and analytics. ConvertKit and Substack are strong alternatives depending on your monetization strategy.

## Quick answer

Choose Beehiiv for growth features and ad monetization. Choose Substack for built-in discovery and paid subscriptions. Choose ConvertKit for advanced automation and product sales. All three have free tiers sufficient for the first 1,000-2,500 subscribers.

## Growth strategy for first 1,000 subscribers

Post daily on one social platform about your newsletter topic. Bio link goes to your signup page. Offer a lead magnet (free PDF, template, or tool) for new subscribers. Use the referral program (Beehiiv) to incentivize sharing: free resource at 3 referrals, template at 5, premium content at 10.

Expected timeline: 1,000 subscribers in 60-90 days with consistent daily posting.

## FAQ

### Which platform is easiest to start with?
Substack. Zero setup required. Write and publish in minutes. The trade-off: fewer growth and monetization features than Beehiiv.

### Can I switch platforms later?
Yes. All platforms support subscriber list export. Migration takes 1-2 hours. Most subscribers won't notice the change.

### How often should I publish?
3x per week minimum for growth. Daily if your niche supports it (faith devotionals, news, market updates). Weekly works for long-form analysis but growth is slower.

### Do I need a website to run a newsletter?
No. Beehiiv and Substack provide hosted landing pages. A custom domain ($10/year) adds professionalism but isn't required to start.
