# How to start a faceless YouTube channel that makes money 2026

## Quick answer

Faceless YouTube channels use stock footage, screen recordings, or AI-generated visuals instead of a camera. Top niches: finance explainers ($15-25 RPM), tech tutorials ($8-15 RPM), motivation ($3-8 RPM). One video per day. Monetization at 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours. Most channels reach this in 3-6 months. Revenue: $1,000-5,000/month at 100K monthly views.

## Step-by-step setup

### 1. Pick one niche (day 1)

Pick from the table above. Consider: can you make 100 videos in this niche without running out of topics? If yes, proceed.

### 2. Create channel and brand (day 1)

Channel name: descriptive, not clever. "Finance Explained" beats "MoneyVibes2026". Generate a logo with Gemini or Leonardo. Dark background, gradient, clean text.

### 3. Script your first 10 videos (day 2-3)

Use AI for first drafts. Edit for voice. Each script: hook (15 sec), problem (30 sec), solution (2-5 min), CTA (15 sec). Target 5-8 minutes per video. Longer videos get more ad placements.

### 4. Create visuals (day 3-5)

Options:
- Stock footage (Pexels, Pixabay, free)
- Screen recordings (tutorials, walkthroughs)
- AI-generated images (Gemini, Leonardo)
- Text-on-screen with motion graphics (CapCut)

### 5. Edit and publish (ongoing)

CapCut (free) or DaVinci Resolve (free) for editing. Add: background music (YouTube Audio Library), captions (auto-generated), transitions between clips. Publish 1 video/day for first 30 days. Then 3-5/week.

### 6. Optimize for search

Title: keyword-first, under 60 characters. Thumbnail: high contrast, 3 words max, face or reaction image (even if not yours). Description: first 2 lines = keyword-rich summary. Tags: 10-15 relevant keywords.

## FAQ

### Can YouTube detect AI voiceovers?

YouTube does not penalize AI voiceovers. They require disclosure of AI-generated content that appears realistic. Text-to-speech narration does not trigger this requirement.

### How many videos before monetization?

Typically 50-100 videos to reach 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours. Varies by niche. Finance/business channels often monetize faster due to higher watch time.

### What is the best AI voice tool?

ElevenLabs at $5/month for basic or $22/month for better quality. Alternatively, use your own voice with a basic microphone. Real voices build more trust.

### Can I use the same content on TikTok and Shorts?

Yes. Cut your long-form videos into 30-60 second clips for TikTok and YouTube Shorts. This drives traffic back to your main channel. Cross-post to Facebook Reels for $4.40/1K views.
