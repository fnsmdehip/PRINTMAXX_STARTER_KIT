# How to start a Beehiiv newsletter from zero subscribers

Start a Beehiiv newsletter by picking one specific topic, creating a lead magnet, setting up a free Beehiiv account, and posting about your topic 3x per week on social media. Expected timeline: 100 subscribers in 2-3 weeks, 1,000 in 60-90 days.

## Quick answer

The fastest path from zero to 1,000 subscribers: choose a niche (AI tools, fitness, faith, finance), create a free lead magnet (PDF checklist or template), post about your niche topic daily on X/Twitter, and link to your Beehiiv signup page in your bio. The referral program handles viral growth once you hit 200+ subscribers.

## Week-by-week launch plan

| Week | Action | Expected Subscribers |
|------|--------|---------------------|
| Week 1 | Set up Beehiiv, write first 3 issues, create lead magnet | 0-20 (friends/network) |
| Week 2 | Launch on social, post daily about your topic | 20-80 |
| Week 3 | Activate referral program, continue posting | 80-150 |
| Week 4 | Guest on 1-2 podcasts or cross-promote with another newsletter | 150-300 |
| Month 2 | Consistent posting + referral momentum | 300-600 |
| Month 3 | Add paid growth (optional) or double content frequency | 600-1,000 |

## Setup checklist

1. Create free Beehiiv account (takes 5 minutes)
2. Choose your sending frequency (3x/week recommended)
3. Write your first 3 issues before launching (never start from zero buffer)
4. Create a lead magnet PDF or template (1-2 hours)
5. Set up referral milestones: 3 referrals = free resource, 5 = template, 10 = premium content
6. Connect your custom domain ($10/year, not required but professional)
7. Announce launch on all social platforms

## Growth strategies that work in 2026

Post about your newsletter topic on X/Twitter every day. Not promotional posts about the newsletter. Value posts about the topic itself. "Here's what I learned about X today." Bio link goes to signup page.

Use the Beehiiv Boost network. Other newsletters recommend yours to their subscribers. You earn when they recommend you, and you can recommend others to earn additional revenue.

Cross-promote with 2-3 newsletters in adjacent niches. Trade recommendations in your issues. Both lists grow.

## FAQ

### Is Beehiiv really free?
Yes. The free tier supports up to 2,500 subscribers with full features including referral programs, analytics, and custom landing pages. Paid tiers start at $35/month for advanced features.

### How long should each newsletter issue be?
300-600 words for daily newsletters. 600-1,000 words for 2-3x per week. Consistency matters more than length.

### When should I add paid subscriptions?
At 2,500-5,000 subscribers. You need a large enough free audience to convert 2-5% to paid ($5-10/month). That's 50-250 paying subscribers at $5-10 each.

### What's the best niche for newsletters?
Niches with high email open rates: faith/devotionals (45%+ open rates), finance (35-40%), health/fitness (35%+), AI/tech (30-35%). Pick what you can write about consistently.
