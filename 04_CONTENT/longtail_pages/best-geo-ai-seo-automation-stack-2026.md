## Content that ranks

AI overviews cite:
- Step-by-step guides (with code examples)
- Comparisons (with data)
- Data-backed findings (with numbers)
- Templates (ready to use)

AI doesn't cite:
- Opinion pieces
- Thin pages
- Outdated content
- Unsubstantiated claims

Write only the first 4 types.
