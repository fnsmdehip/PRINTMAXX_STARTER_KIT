# App store optimization (ASO) checklist for indie developers 2026

## Quick answer

ASO is 80% of your organic app downloads. The title drives 65% of search visibility. Screenshots drive 70% of install decisions. Keyword research takes 2 hours. Full ASO setup takes 1 day. Most indie devs skip this and wonder why nobody downloads their app.

## The full ASO checklist

### Title (30 characters iOS, 50 characters Android)

- Primary keyword first, brand name last
- Example: "PrayerLock - Focus Timer" not "PrayerLock App"
- Include the action word users search for

| Good titles | Bad titles |
|-------------|-----------|
| PrayerLock - Focus Timer | PrayerLock |
| WalkToUnlock - Step Counter | My Walking App |
| BioMaxx - Supplement Tracker | BioMaxx App v2.1 |

### Subtitle (30 characters, iOS only)

Secondary keyword here. Benefit statement, not feature list. Example: "Faith-based productivity" not "With timer and alerts"

### Keyword field (100 characters, iOS only)

Comma-separated, no spaces after commas. Never duplicate words from title or subtitle. Use singular forms. Example: `prayer,focus,christian,bible,faith,lock,screen,productivity,timer,daily`

### Description

First 3 lines visible before "Read More" tap. Line 1: Problem. Line 2: Solution. Line 3: Differentiator + proof. Lines 4+: Feature bullets.

### Screenshots

- First 2 screenshots get 80% of attention
- Show app in use, not splash screens
- Text overlay: benefit in 5-7 words
- Required sizes: 6.7" iPhone, 6.5" iPhone, 12.9" iPad

Sequence: Hero shot, core feature, results screen, social proof, secondary feature, pricing comparison.

### App preview video

15-30 seconds max. Core feature in first 3 seconds. No audio needed. Portrait orientation.

### Icon

Must have depth (3D, gradients). Recognizable at 29x29px. No text. Color that stands out from competitors.

## Keyword research process

### Step 1: Seed keywords (15 min)

List 20-30 words: direct searches ("prayer timer"), problem searches ("how to pray more"), competitor searches ("apps like X").

### Step 2: Expand with tools (30 min)

Use AppTweak, Sensor Tower, or App Store search suggest. Note auto-complete suggestions.

### Step 3: Score and select (30 min)

For each keyword estimate volume, competition, relevance. Pick 10-15. Highest-volume goes in title.

### Step 4: Track weekly

Check rankings every Monday. Adjust quarterly.

## Common ASO mistakes

- Using the app name as the entire title
- Generic screenshots
- Ignoring subtitle field
- Not responding to reviews
- Updating keywords too frequently (wait 4-6 weeks)

## FAQ

### How long before ASO changes take effect?

Apple indexes new metadata within 24-48 hours. Ranking changes take 1-2 weeks. Wait 4 weeks minimum before judging results.

### Do ratings affect ASO ranking?

Yes. Apps with 4.5+ stars rank higher. Prompt for ratings after positive in-app moments.

### Should I pay for ASO tools?

Start free. Pay for AppTweak ($70/mo) only after 1,000+ downloads when you need granular keyword tracking.

### How often should I A/B test screenshots?

Every 2-4 weeks. Test the first screenshot since it has the most impact on conversion.
