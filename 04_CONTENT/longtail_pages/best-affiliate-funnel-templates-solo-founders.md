# Best affiliate funnel templates for solo founders

## Quick Answer

Use 4-email launch sequence → product review page → email nurture sequence. This funnel converts 2-5% of cold traffic to affiliate clicks. Average: $500-2000/month from 1000 emails per month.

All templates included below. Copy and adapt.

## The Funnel Stages

1. **Awareness:** Email or content about a problem
2. **Interest:** Product review page showing solution
3. **Desire:** Email sequence hyping the product
4. **Action:** Affiliate link in CTA
5. **Loyalty:** Ongoing product recommendations

## Email Sequence (Pre-Launch)

**Email 1: Hook (Problem)**
```
Subject: You're probably doing [problem] wrong

Hi [Name],

I see this all the time. People spend $50+/month on tools
that don't solve their main problem.

Sound familiar?

Tomorrow, I'm sharing the tool I wish I found years ago.
(Saves me 10+ hours per week)

See you in your inbox,
[Your name]
```

Goal: Create curiosity. Make them open email 2.

**Email 2: Education (Introduce Product)**
```
Subject: Here's the tool that changed my workflow

Hi [Name],

Quick backstory: I wasted 6 months on [wrong solution].

Then I found [Product]. It took me 20 minutes to set up.
Immediately saved me 5 hours per week.

Here's why it's the best:
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

Most people pay $X for this. There's a deal (link) if you want to check it out.

Not for everyone, but worth looking at.

[Affiliate Link]

[Your name]
```

Goal: Present product as solution.

**Email 3: Social Proof**
```
Subject: Why 5000+ people switched to [Product]

Hi [Name],

Remember that tool I mentioned?

Here's what changed for people who switched:
- [Customer A]: "Saved $200/month" - real customer
- [Customer B]: "Setup takes 10 minutes" - real customer
- [Customer C]: "Finally found what I needed" - real customer

If you're still doing [old way], this is worth 15 minutes of your time.

[Affiliate Link]

[Your name]
```

Goal: Use social proof. Reduce buying anxiety.

**Email 4: Urgency**
```
Subject: Last day of the deal

Hi [Name],

If you've been thinking about [Product], today's the last day
of the limited-time offer (if applicable).

Honestly, it's worth full price anyway. But might as well grab the deal.

[Affiliate Link]

(No pressure if it's not for you!)

[Your name]
```

Goal: Create urgency. Close the sale.

Repeat every month with new angles.

## Building the Funnel

### Step 1: Create Email Sequence (1 hour)

Copy templates above. Personalize for your product.

Add to Convertkit/Beehiiv.

### Step 2: Create Review Page (1 hour)

Copy template above. Add to your blog or Carrd.

Include honest pros/cons. People trust authenticity.

### Step 3: Drive Traffic (Ongoing)

- Twitter: Share review page
- Cold email: Direct to review page
- Content: Link to review page from blog
- Referrals: Ask customers to share

### Step 4: Optimize (Weekly)

Track:
- Email open rates (target: 30%+)
- Click rates (target: 5%+)
- Affiliate conversions

If click rate is low: Change email subject or copy.
If conversion rate is low: Your product recommendation sucks.

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Affiliate link disclosed ("Full disclosure: I earn commission")
- [ ] Your honest opinion (not just sales pitch)
- [ ] No fake testimonials
- [ ] No income claims without backing
- [ ] You've actually used the product
