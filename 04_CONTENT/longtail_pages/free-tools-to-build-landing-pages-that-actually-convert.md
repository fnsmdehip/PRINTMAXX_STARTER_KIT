# Free tools to build landing pages that actually convert

## Quick Answer

Use Carrd if you want simple and fast (free, 3 sites). Use Framer if you want design flexibility (free, unlimited). Use Webflow if you need custom interactions (free, 2 sites). All convert well if your copy is good.

## The Free Tools

### 1. Carrd (Simplest)

**Free tier:** 3 sites, custom domain with upgrade

**Best for:**
- Total beginners
- Simple one-page sites
- Fast launches (build in 30 min)

**Pros:**
- Dead simple
- Loads fast
- Mobile-responsive by default
- Clean templates

**Cons:**
- Limited customization
- Basic forms only
- No A/B testing

**Conversion features:**
- Email capture forms
- Button CTAs
- Image backgrounds
- Custom code injection (for analytics)

**Example sites that convert:**
- Waitlist pages
- Lead magnet landing pages
- Simple product launches

**Average conversion rate:** 3-5% for email capture

### 2. Framer (Most Flexible)

**Free tier:** Unlimited sites, 1000 visitors/month

**Best for:**
- Designers
- Polished, animated sites
- Portfolio + product pages

**Pros:**
- Beautiful out of box
- Animation library
- CMS built in
- No visitor limit on paid tier ($5/mo)

**Cons:**
- Learning curve (2-3 hours)
- Overkill for simple pages
- Can get slow if over-animated

**Conversion features:**
- Form builder
- CMS for testimonials
- Scroll animations (keep attention)
- Custom components

**Example sites that convert:**
- SaaS landing pages
- Agency portfolios
- App pre-launch pages

**Average conversion rate:** 4-7% (better design = more trust)

### 3. Webflow (Most Powerful)

**Free tier:** 2 sites, webflow.io domain

**Best for:**
- Advanced users
- Complex multi-page sites
- E-commerce (paid tier)

**Pros:**
- Full design control
- CMS included
- Interactions/animations
- SEO-friendly

**Cons:**
- Steep learning curve (4-6 hours)
- Can be slow to build
- Free tier has branding

**Conversion features:**
- Form builder with logic
- CMS for dynamic content
- Advanced interactions
- Custom code

**Example sites that convert:**
- SaaS homepages
- Multi-step funnels
- Content-heavy sites

**Average conversion rate:** 5-8% (if well-designed)

## What Actually Converts

**Tool matters less than copy.** A Carrd page with great copy beats a Framer page with weak copy.

### High-Converting Structure

**1. Above the fold (what visitors see first):**
- Clear headline (8 words max)
- Subhead (who it's for)
- One CTA button
- Hero image/video

**2. Social proof:**
- "Join 247 founders" or
- Testimonials (real names + photos) or
- Logos (if you have them)

**3. Features (3-5 bullets):**
- What they get
- Specific benefits
- No fluff

**4. Final CTA:**
- Repeat the button
- Remove friction ("No credit card required")

### Example: High-Converting Page

> **Headline:** Get your first 10 customers in 30 days
>
> **Subhead:** Cold outreach playbook for B2B SaaS founders
>
> **CTA:** Download free checklist
>
> **Social proof:** Join 342 founders who got their first customers
>
> **Features:**
> - Daily outreach plan (15 min/day)
> - Email templates that convert
> - Follow-up sequences
>
> **Final CTA:** Get the checklist (it's free)

Simple. Clear. Converts.

- Your product is straightforward

### Use Framer if:
- You care about design
- You want animations

### Use Webflow if:

## Advanced: What to Test

Once your page is live, test these elements:

**High-impact tests:**
- Headline variations
- CTA button text
- Form length (fewer fields = higher conversion)

**Medium-impact tests:**
- Hero image vs video
- Social proof placement
- Color scheme

**Low-impact tests:**
- Font choices
- Spacing
- Footer content

Test one element at a time. Run for 1000+ visitors before deciding.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1:** Too much text.

**Fix:** Cut 50% of your copy. Say less.

**Mistake 2:** Multiple CTAs.

**Fix:** One action per page. Don't confuse visitors.

**Mistake 3:** No social proof.

**Fix:** Add "Join X users" or testimonials. Reduces risk.

**Mistake 4:** Slow page speed.

**Fix:** Compress images. Remove unnecessary animations.

## Templates That Convert

**For lead magnets:**
```
Headline: [Specific outcome in 8 words]
Subhead: For [specific audience]
3 bullets: What they get
Form: Email only
CTA: "Download free [thing]"
```

**For SaaS waitlist:**
```
Headline: [Promise in 8 words]
Subhead: [Problem you solve]
Demo video: 30-60 seconds
Social proof: "Join X on waitlist"
Form: Email + optional feedback
CTA: "Get early access"
```

**For pre-orders:**
```
Headline: [Product name + benefit]
Subhead: [Who it's for]
3-5 feature bullets
Pricing: Clear upfront
CTA: "Pre-order now" (with guarantee)
```

## Page Speed Matters

Target: < 2 seconds load time

**How to stay fast:**
- Compress images (use TinyPNG)
- Limit animations
- Remove unnecessary scripts
- Use system fonts

Carrd is fastest by default. Framer and Webflow can get slow if you add too much.

## Mobile First

70%+ of traffic is mobile.

**Check on mobile:**
- Text readable (16px+ font)
- Buttons big enough (44px+ tap target)
- Forms work smoothly
- No horizontal scroll

All three tools are mobile-responsive by default, but test anyway.

## When to Upgrade

**Stay on free tier until:**
- You need custom domain (Carrd: $19/year, Framer: $5/mo, Webflow: $14/mo)
- You hit visitor limits
- You need A/B testing
- You want to remove branding

Don't pay until you're converting on free tier.
