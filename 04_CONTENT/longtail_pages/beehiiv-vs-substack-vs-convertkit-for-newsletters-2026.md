# Beehiiv vs Substack vs ConvertKit for newsletters 2026

## Quick answer

Beehiiv is best for monetization (ad network, referral program, analytics). Substack is best for discovery (Notes feature, recommendation network). ConvertKit is best for automation (sequences, segments, commerce). For solopreneurs starting from zero, use Substack for discovery then move to Beehiiv when you hit 2,500 subscribers.

## Feature comparison

| Feature | Beehiiv (free-$99/mo) | Substack (free + 10% of paid) | ConvertKit ($29-$79/mo) |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------------------|------------------------|
| Free tier limit | 2,500 subs | Unlimited | 1,000 subs |
| Discovery network | No | Yes (Notes, recommendations) | No |
| Ad network | Yes (built-in) | No | No |
| Referral program | Yes (milestone rewards) | No | No |
| Paid subscriptions | Yes | Yes (10% fee) | Yes |
| Automation | Good | Basic | Best |
| Analytics | Best | Basic | Good |
| Custom domain | Yes (all plans) | Yes (paid plan) | Yes |
| A/B testing | Yes | No | Yes |
| API access | Yes | Limited | Yes |

## When to use each

### Substack: start here if you have 0 subscribers

Substack Notes is a social network built into the newsletter platform. Every post on Notes is discoverable by other Substack readers. This is free distribution you cannot get anywhere else.

Strategy: post on Notes 3-5x per day. Cross-post your newsletter to Substack. Use recommendations to swap growth with other writers. Free subscribers are cheap on Substack.

Downside: when you want to charge, Substack takes 10% + Stripe fees (13-14% total). No ad network.

### Beehiiv: move here at 2,500+ subscribers

Beehiiv free tier covers up to 2,500 subscribers. After that, Scale plan at $99/month gives you: ad network (earn from every send), referral program, A/B testing, and the best analytics in the space.

Strategy: migrate from Substack once you have a base. Use Beehiiv's ad network for immediate revenue. Run referral program where subscribers share for rewards.

### ConvertKit: use if you sell products

ConvertKit has the best email automation. Visual automation builder, advanced segments, and built-in commerce (sell digital products from your emails).

Strategy: if your newsletter is primarily a sales channel for courses, templates, or services, ConvertKit's automation handles the funnel better than Beehiiv or Substack.

## Migration path

Month 1-3: Start on Substack (free, discovery network).
Month 3-6: Cross-post on Substack, build on Beehiiv in parallel.
Month 6+: Primary on Beehiiv, keep Substack for discovery.

## FAQ

### Can I use both Substack and Beehiiv?

Yes. Cross-post to Substack for discovery. Primary list on Beehiiv for monetization. Many successful newsletter operators do this.

### Is Beehiiv free tier enough?

Up to 2,500 subscribers, yes. You get custom domain, analytics, and basic features. Upgrade when you need A/B testing or the ad network.

### How fast can I grow on Substack?

With consistent Notes posting (3-5x/day) and cross-recommendations: 100-500 subscribers/month is typical for active writers. Some hit 1,000+ in month 1 with aggressive Notes engagement.

### What about Ghost or Buttondown?

Ghost is good for developers who want self-hosted. Buttondown is minimal and clean. Neither has the discovery network of Substack or the monetization tools of Beehiiv.
