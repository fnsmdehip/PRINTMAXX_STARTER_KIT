# Programmatic SEO plan for cold outreach solopreneurs

Most pages take 4 hours to write. At that rate, 100 pages is 400 hours. You can't do it alone.

Programmatic SEO fixes this. You generate pages from templates. 100 pages in 2 days.

Here's the plan.

## The template approach

Pick one structure that converts. Example: "Best [tool] for [use case]"

Example pages:
- Best tools for lead generation
- Best tools for cold outreach
- Best tools for sales follow-ups

Same structure. Different keywords. One template. 50+ pages.

## Step 1: Build one page perfectly

Write "Best tools for lead generation" with full SEO optimization. Include:
- Hook (15 words max)
- 3-5 tools with honest reviews
- Pricing comparison
- Real use cases
- FAQ section
- CTA

Time: 4 hours. This is your template.

## Step 2: Identify 50 keyword variations

Your niche is lead generation. Variations:
- Best lead generation software
- Best lead generation tools for SMBs
- Cheapest lead generation tools
- Best free lead generation tools
- Best lead generation tools 2026

Use SEMrush or Google Trends. Extract keywords. 30 minutes.

## Step 3: Generate pages programmatically

Use Claude to fill in your template:

```
Template: "Best [CATEGORY] for [USE_CASE]"
Category: lead generation
Use case: cold outreach

Generate a 1200-word page following this structure:
[YOUR_TEMPLATE]
```

Takes 2 minutes per page. 50 pages = 100 minutes.

## Step 4: Batch review (quality gate)

Read every 10th page. Fix tone, update numbers, remove spam-sounding claims. 1-2 hours total.

## Step 5: Publish + link them

All 50 pages link back to your main lead gen page. This clusters them in Google's eyes. More authority to your core page.

## Real example

You have 1 strong page: "Lead generation stack 2026"

You generate 50 longtail pages around it:
- Best lead generation tools
- Lead generation software comparison
- Cheapest lead generation platform
- Free lead generation tools

All link to the core page. Traffic to lontails amplifies traffic to your main page. Your main page ranks higher.

Result: Higher authority. More organic traffic overall.

## Cost

- Claude API: $5-10 (50 pages)
- Your review time: 3 hours
- Hosting: included

Total: $10, 3 hours.

## Common mistakes

**Mistake 1: Thin pages.** Auto-generated without review reads like spam. It ranks worse. Don't cut corners on quality review.

**Mistake 2: Duplicate content.** If pages are 95% identical, Google penalizes. Keep them 70% similar structure, 30% unique research.

**Mistake 3: No internal linking.** Pages exist but don't link to each other. You lose the clustering benefit.

## Timeline

- Day 1: Perfect one template (4 hours)
- Day 2: Generate 50 pages (2 hours)
- Day 3: Batch review (2 hours)
- Day 4: Publish + internal link (1 hour)

Total: 9 hours for 50 pages. That's 11 minutes per page including review.

## When to use this

This works for:
- Tool comparisons
- Feature breakdowns
- Industry guides
- How-to variations

This doesn't work for:
- News or trends (dates matter)
- Opinion pieces (need originality)
- Highly technical guides (need expertise)

## Scaling to 100+

After 50 work well, repeat with a new template:

"How to [ACTION] for [USE_CASE]"

Examples:
- How to automate lead generation
- How to scale cold outreach
- How to track conversions

50 more pages. Same 9-hour sprint.

Programmatic SEO works. It just requires a solid template first.
