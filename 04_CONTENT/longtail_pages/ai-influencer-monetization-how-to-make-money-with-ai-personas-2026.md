## Legal requirements (non-negotiable)

FTC Fake Reviews Rule (August 2024): $51,744 per violation for undisclosed synthetic testimonials.
NY S.8420-A (June 2026): first state synthetic performer disclosure law.
TikTok enforcement up 340% in H2 2025: 51,618 removals, 8,600 bans for undisclosed AI content.

What you must do:
- Disclose AI-generated content in bio and post descriptions
- Never present AI testimonials as real customer reviews
- Never create deepfakes of real people without consent
- Label AI voiceovers and synthetic media clearly

What is fine:
- AI personas with original character design
- AI-generated UGC ads with disclosure
- Synthetic voices for narration with label
- AI avatars for educational/entertainment content

## Step-by-step setup

### 1. Choose your persona niche

Best AI persona niches by monetization:
- Finance/investing education ($15-25 RPM, high affiliate)
- Fitness coaching ($8-15 RPM, supplement affiliates)
- Tech reviews ($10-20 RPM, SaaS affiliates)
- Self-improvement ($5-12 RPM, course upsell)
- Faith/spirituality ($3-8 RPM, app + product affiliates)

### 2. Generate visual identity

Tools: Leonardo.ai ($12/month) for images, HeyGen ($24/month) for talking head videos.

Create consistent character: same face, same style across all content. Generate 50-100 images in different poses, expressions, and settings.

### 3. Create voice

ElevenLabs ($5-22/month) for consistent AI voice. Clone a unique voice or use a stock voice. Same voice across all content builds brand recognition.

### 4. Post content daily

Mix of: text posts (5/week), short videos with AI avatar (3/week), stories/behind-the-scenes (daily).

Content topics: niche expertise, opinion posts, tutorials, Q&A responses, collaborations with real creators.

### 5. Monetize at 1,000+ followers

Start with: affiliate links in bio, digital product (template, guide, checklist). Add as you grow: brand deals, community, subscription content.

## FAQ

### Is this legal?

Yes with disclosure. The law requires transparency, not prohibition. Lil Miquela, Imma, and thousands of AI personas operate legally because they disclose.

### Can AI personas get verified on platforms?

Not typically. Verification requires identity proof. AI personas operate as business/brand accounts.

### How do I handle comments and DMs?

Respond as the persona. Use AI (ChatGPT/Claude) to draft responses in character. Be transparent if asked: "I'm an AI persona created by [your name/brand]."

### What if a platform bans AI content?

Diversify across platforms. No major platform bans AI content outright. They ban undisclosed AI content. Disclosure protects you.
