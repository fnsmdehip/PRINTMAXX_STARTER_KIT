# How to build email list from zero with no budget

## Quick Answer

Create a free lead magnet (PDF checklist or template). Use Mailchimp free tier (500 subscribers). Post about your magnet daily on X/LinkedIn. Get 100 emails in 30 days.

## The Free Stack

| Component | Tool | Cost | Limit |
|-----------|------|------|-------|
| Email service | Mailchimp | Free | 500 subscribers |
| Lead magnet | Google Docs → PDF | Free | Unlimited |
| Landing page | Carrd | Free | 3 sites |
| Form | Tally | Free | Unlimited |
| Analytics | Google Analytics | Free | Unlimited |

Total cost: $0

## Step-by-Step

### Week 1: Create Lead Magnet

Pick a specific problem your audience has.

**Good lead magnets:**
- Checklist ("10-point SaaS launch checklist")
- Template ("Cold email template that converts")
- Cheat sheet ("Pricing calculator for freelancers")
- Mini-guide ("How to get your first 10 customers")

**Bad lead magnets:**
- Vague guides ("Complete guide to success")
- Long ebooks (no one reads)
- Generic templates

Write in Google Docs. Export as PDF. Takes 2-4 hours.

**Format:**
1. Title page
2. Problem statement (1 paragraph)
3. Solution (5-10 bullet points)
4. Action steps (checklist format)
5. CTA (link to your site/product)

### Week 2: Build Landing Page

Use Carrd (free).

**Landing page structure:**
1. Headline (promise in 8 words)
2. Subhead (who it's for)
3. 3 bullet points (what they get)
4. Email form (Tally embed)
5. Social proof (optional if you have it)

**Example:**

> Get your first 10 customers in 30 days
>
> Free checklist for B2B SaaS founders
>
> - Daily outreach plan
> - Email templates that convert
> - Follow-up sequence
>
> [Email input field]
>
> Join 247 founders building their first $1k MRR

Takes 1 hour to build.

### Week 3-4: Drive Traffic

Post about your lead magnet daily.

**Platform strategy:**

**X/Twitter (best ROI for solopreneurs):**
- Post about the problem your magnet solves
- Drop link in replies
- Engage with 10 posts before promoting yours
- Post 5-7 times per week

**LinkedIn:**
- Post 2-3 times per week
- Focus on lessons learned
- Add link in first comment
- Engage with connections

**Reddit (if niche applies):**
- Find relevant subreddits
- Answer questions genuinely
- Drop link where helpful
- Don't spam

**Example posts:**

> I spent 6 months getting my first customer. Then I created a checklist. Got the next 10 in 30 days. Free template: [link]

> Most SaaS founders quit before customer #10. Here's the exact checklist I used to get there: [link]

Post variations daily. Test what works.

## Email Setup (Mailchimp Free)

**Create welcome sequence:**

**Email 1 (immediate):**
- Subject: "Here's your [lead magnet name]"
- Body: Link to download + one tip

**Email 2 (day 3):**
- Subject: "Quick question"
- Body: Ask for feedback on magnet

**Email 3 (day 7):**
- Subject: "How's it going?"
- Body: Share related content or offer help

Send weekly after that.

## Realistic Growth Numbers

**Month 1 (zero to start):**
- Week 1: 0 signups (building)
- Week 2: 5-10 signups (testing)
- Week 3: 20-30 signups (momentum)
- Week 4: 30-50 signups (consistent posting)
- Total: 55-90 emails

**Month 2:**
- 100-150 new signups (if you post daily)

**Month 3:**
- 150-250 new signups (word of mouth kicks in)

This assumes daily posting and decent content.

## What Actually Works

**Post frequency:**
- 5-7 posts per week minimum
- Consistency beats volume

**Content that converts:**
- Specific problems ("Why your cold emails get ignored")
- Numbers ("I got 50 emails in 14 days")
- Behind-the-scenes ("My lead magnet got 100 downloads")

**What doesn't work:**
- Asking for follows ("Follow for more")
- Vague promises ("Learn to grow your business")
- Boring posts ("Check out my new guide")

## When to Upgrade Tools

**Stay free until:**
- You hit 500 subscribers (Mailchimp limit)
- You need automation beyond welcome sequence
- You want A/B testing

**Then upgrade to:**
- Mailchimp paid ($13/mo for 500-1000 subscribers)
- ConvertKit ($29/mo, better for creators)
- Beehiiv ($0 for 2500 subscribers, $49/mo after)

Don't pay until you hit free limits.

## Advanced Tactics (Free)

**1. Cross-promote with other creators:**
Find someone with similar audience. Swap newsletter mentions.

**2. Guest post:**
Write for sites your audience reads. Add CTA for lead magnet.

**3. Answer questions:**
Quora, Reddit, X. Genuinely help. Add magnet link when relevant.

**4. Repurpose content:**
Turn lead magnet into thread. Into carousel. Into video. Post everywhere.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1:** Waiting for perfect lead magnet.

**Fix:** Ship v1 in 4 hours. Improve based on feedback.

**Mistake 2:** Not posting enough.

**Fix:** Commit to 5 posts per week minimum.

**Mistake 3:** Promoting only once.

**Fix:** Talk about your magnet 20+ times. Most people won't see it the first time.

**Mistake 4:** No follow-up emails.

**Fix:** Send weekly. Give value. Build relationship.
