# Content production SOP template for solopreneurs

## Quick Answer

Batch content production: 2 hours ideating on Monday, 2 hours writing on Tuesday-Wednesday, 2 hours scheduling Thursday. Output: 5 social posts, 1 email, 1 blog draft per week. Use Google Docs for drafting, Zapier to schedule posts.

## The System

Most solopreneurs fail at content because they write daily. Daily writing kills creativity and burns out fast. Instead, batch produce.

This SOP keeps production predictable and forces quality over quantity.

## Phase 1: Ideation (Monday, 90 minutes)

List 3 topic pillars for the week:
1. What problem did you solve this week?
2. What question came up from customers?
3. What's a contrarian opinion you have?

From each pillar, generate 5 posts:
- Hook post (why this matters)
- How-to post (specific steps)
- Story post (your experience)
- Data post (numbers that matter)
- Mistake post (what NOT to do)

Total: 15 post ideas. Pick 5 that feel strongest.

## Phase 2: Writing (Tuesday-Wednesday, 2 hours each)

### Social Posts (30 min)
Write all 5 posts in one block. Short-form forces clarity. If a post takes 10 minutes, kill it and pick the next idea.

Template per post:
- Hook (why should I read?)
- Proof (1 number or story)
- Action (what do I do next?)

### Email Draft (45 min)
One email per week to your list. Topic: one of your social posts expanded.

Structure:
- Subject: Specific benefit
- Body: Story + lesson + CTA
- CTA: What's next (link to offer, or reply)

### Blog Outline (45 min)
Pick your strongest post idea. Expand to 800 words.

Outline only (don't write full yet):
- H1 (main topic)
- H2s (3-4 subheadings)
- Bullet points under each
- CTA

## Phase 3: Scheduling (Thursday, 1 hour)

### Posts (20 min)
- Copy 5 posts into Buffer, Later, or Hootsuite
- Schedule 1 per day Mon-Fri next week
- Alternate formats: 2 text, 2 image/video, 1 engagement post

### Email (10 min)
- Paste email into your email tool (ConvertKit, Beehiiv)
- Schedule for Wednesday or Thursday next week

### Blog (30 min)
- Flesh out outline into full post
- Add 1 internal link, 1 CTA
- Publish or schedule for the following week

## Red Flags

- Posts taking 20+ minutes each (you're overthinking)
- Skipping weeks (add calendar block: 2-4pm Mon-Thu)
- Writing at different times (time-shifting kills momentum)
- No metrics tracking (you don't know what works)

## Tools Needed

- Google Docs (free)
- Buffer/Later ($10-20/mo)
- ConvertKit/Beehiiv ($20/mo)
- Optional: Zapier for auto-scheduling ($20/mo)

Total: $50-60/mo. Skip email tool if bootstrapping (use free tier).

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] 3 pillars chosen (Monday)
- [ ] 15 ideas generated
- [ ] 5 posts selected
- [ ] All posts written (Tuesday-Wed)
- [ ] Email drafted
- [ ] Blog outline done
- [ ] Posts scheduled (Thursday)
- [ ] Email scheduled
- [ ] Blog published/drafted

Check off each week. Track how many readers/subscribers grow.
