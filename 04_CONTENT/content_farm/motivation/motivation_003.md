SLIDE 4 (seconds 26-38):
TEXT: "design your environment. stop relying on motivation."
VOICEOVER: "put the gym bag by the door. delete the apps. lock your phone until you walk 2,000 steps. make the healthy meal the only thing in the fridge. the person who needs zero willpower to do the right thing will always beat the person who needs a lot of it."

SLIDE 5 (seconds 38-52):
TEXT: "friction is the only cheat code."
VOICEOVER: "the $200 billion attention economy is designed to make the wrong choice frictionless. one tap, infinite scroll, instant dopamine. the countermove isn't more discipline. it's adding friction to the bad choices and removing friction from the good ones. design beats discipline every single time."
