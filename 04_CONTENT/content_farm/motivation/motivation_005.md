## Hook (first 3 seconds)
TEXT ON SCREEN: "why do 92% of people never reach their goals?"

VOICEOVER: "92% of people who set new year's goals fail. every year. here's why."

## Body (seconds 3-50)

SLIDE 2 (seconds 3-14):
TEXT: "it's not effort. it's identity."
VOICEOVER: "it's not because they don't try hard enough. a University of Scranton study tracked goal completion over 10 years. the people who failed worked just as hard in January as the people who succeeded. the difference wasn't effort. it was identity."

SLIDE 3 (seconds 14-26):
TEXT: "you don't rise to the level of your goals. you fall to the level of your systems."
VOICEOVER: "the 8% who succeed didn't say 'I want to run a marathon.' they said 'I'm a runner.' the shift is tiny but the outcome is completely different. because when you identify AS the thing, every decision filters through that identity. a runner doesn't skip Tuesday's run. that's just what runners do."

SLIDE 4 (seconds 26-38):
TEXT: "identity + systems > goals + willpower"
VOICEOVER: "a person who identifies as a builder ships code every day. a person who identifies as someone of faith prays every morning. a person who identifies as fit doesn't negotiate with the gym. the goal becomes irrelevant. the identity does the work."

SLIDE 5 (seconds 38-50):
TEXT: "change the sentence. change the outcome."
VOICEOVER: "stop saying 'I want to' and start saying 'I am.' I want to lose weight becomes I'm someone who moves every day. I want to start a business becomes I'm a builder. one sentence. that's the entire system. the behavior follows the belief."

## CTA (seconds 50-60)
TEXT: "finish this sentence: 'I am ___'"
VOICEOVER: "finish this sentence in the comments. I am blank. commit to the identity. not the goal."
