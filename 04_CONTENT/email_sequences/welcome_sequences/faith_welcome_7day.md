## Email 4: The system

**Send timing:** Day 3, 5am local time
**Segment:** All new faith niche subscribers (did not purchase)

### Subject line
The 4-part anchor system

**A/B variants:**
- A: What I do every morning at 5:47am
- B: Simple enough to actually do

### Preview text
No complicated frameworks

### Body

After 3 years of trial and error, here's the morning anchor system that stuck:

**STOP** (1 min)
Before doing anything, pause. Take 3 deep breaths. Acknowledge God's presence.

**READ** (3 min)
One short passage. Don't try to cover chapters. One verse is enough.

**REFLECT** (3 min)
Write one sentence about what stood out. Ask God what He's showing you.

**REQUEST** (3 min)
Bring your day to Him. Specific concerns. Specific gratitude.

Total: 10 minutes.

I do this at 5:47am (before anyone else is awake). The specific time matters less than the consistency.

Some practical tips:
- Put your phone in another room
- Keep your Bible and journal next to where you sit
- Don't judge yourself on "quality" of prayer
- Missing a day means starting fresh the next day
