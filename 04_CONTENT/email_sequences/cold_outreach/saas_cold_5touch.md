## Finding prospects

**Sources:**
- Shopify store directories (BuiltWith, Storeleads)
- Product Hunt launches
- Shopify Facebook groups (observe, don't spam)
- LinkedIn: "Shopify store owner" or "e-commerce founder"
- Apps: filter by stores using Zendesk, Gorgias (ready for support tools)

**Qualification criteria:**
- Shopify or WooCommerce store
- Appears to have real traffic (reviews, social following)
- Product type that generates support questions
- Not already using enterprise-level support tools

## Automation notes

- Verify emails before sending (high bounce rate kills deliverability)
- Warm domain 2+ weeks before starting outreach
- Max 30-50 emails/day from new domain
- Stop sequence immediately on any reply
- Positive replies: move to demo scheduling flow
- Negative replies: tag "not interested" and remove
- Track which touch converts for optimization

---

## After demo

**If they're interested:**
1. Start free trial immediately
2. Send onboarding email with setup steps
3. Check-in on Day 3 to ensure it's working
4. Payment prompt on Day 25 (5 days before trial ends)

**If they're not ready:**
1. Add to monthly newsletter
2. Re-engage in 90 days with new feature or case study

## Compliance notes

- CAN-SPAM compliant: physical address, unsubscribe option
- GDPR: only reach out to business emails, not personal
- Respect "remove" requests immediately
- Don't send to info@, support@, or generic addresses
- Keep outreach records for 2 years
