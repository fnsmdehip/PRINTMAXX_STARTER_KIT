## Personalization hooks (examples)

- "I noticed you're hiring for a marketing coordinator" (suggests growth, need for efficiency)
- "I saw the announcement about your new product line" (suggests complexity increase)
- "I noticed you're using [TOOL] for [PURPOSE]" (suggests automation opportunity)
- "Congrats on the recent funding round" (suggests resources for investment)

## Automation notes

- Use email verification before sending (ZeroBounce, NeverBounce)
- Warm sending domain for 2+ weeks before campaign
- Max 50 emails/day from new domain
- Personalize Touch 1 heavily; touches 2-5 can be more templated
- Stop sequence immediately if prospect replies (any response)
- Tag "not interested" responses for exclusion from future campaigns
- Track which touch converts for optimization

---

## Follow-up after meeting

If meeting is booked, send:
- Calendar confirmation with agenda
- 24-hour reminder
- Post-meeting summary with next steps and quote

---

## Compliance notes

- CAN-SPAM compliant: physical address in footer, unsubscribe option
- Don't purchase email lists; use LinkedIn Sales Navigator + manual verification
- Respect "not interested" responses immediately
- Keep records of consent and outreach for compliance
