# Service launch sequence (5 emails)

Use this for: Launching coaching, consulting, agency services, or done-for-you offerings. Emphasizes transformation and limited availability.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Announcement + what's included |
| 2 | Day 2 | Problem and transformation story |
| 3 | Day 4 | Process breakdown + social proof |
| 4 | Day 6 | Limited spots urgency |
| 5 | Day 7 | Final call with bonus |

Expected open rate: 50-70%
Expected booking rate: 5-12% of engaged subscribers

---

## What makes this work

**Email 1: Clear positioning**
- Specific about deliverables
- Transparent pricing
- Limited availability creates urgency

**Email 2: Proof of concept**
- Real client story
- Specific results
- Shows your process works

**Email 3: Process transparency**
- Week-by-week breakdown
- Removes mystery
- Sets clear expectations

**Email 4: Real scarcity**
- Countdown to deadline
- Spots filling up
- Application process explained

**Email 5: Final push**
- Deadline reminder
- Bonus sweetener
- Clear next steps

---

## Performance benchmarks

**Expected metrics:**
- Email 1: 60-75% open, 40% of applications
- Email 2: 50-65% open, 25% of applications
- Email 3: 45-60% open, 15% of applications
- Email 4: 50-65% open, 10% of applications
- Email 5: 55-70% open, 10% of applications

**Application to client conversion:**
- Target: 30-50% (selective acceptance)
- Discovery call to close: 60-80%

---

## Common mistakes

1. **No scarcity:** Unlimited spots = no urgency
2. **Vague deliverables:** Be specific about what they get
3. **Too cheap:** Low price signals low value for services
4. **No qualification:** Accept everyone = bad clients
5. **Weak case studies:** Generic results don't sell

---

## Pricing strategy for services

**Project-based:**
- One-time fee for specific deliverable
- Clear scope and timeline
- Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on completion

**Retainer:**
- Monthly recurring
- Ongoing support and work
- Payment: Monthly or quarterly

**Package tiers:**
- Bronze/Silver/Gold structure
- Different levels of support
- Lets clients self-select

**Value-based:**
- Price tied to results
- Performance bonus structure
- Requires clear metrics

## Application form essentials

Ask these questions:

**Qualifying:**
- What's your current situation?
- What's your biggest challenge?
- What have you tried already?
- What's your timeline?

**Budget:**
- What's your budget for solving this?
- Is this a priority investment?

**Fit:**
- Why now?
- Why me specifically?
- What happens if you don't solve this?

**Logistics:**
- How did you hear about me?
- Best way to contact you?
- Preferred start date?

---

## Discovery call framework

**30-minute structure:**

**Minutes 0-5: Rapport**
- Small talk
- Set agenda
- Get them talking

**Minutes 5-15: Diagnosis**
- Current situation deep dive
- What they've tried
- Why it hasn't worked

**Minutes 15-25: Solution**
- How your service solves it
- Specific process for them
- Expected outcomes and timeline

**Minutes 25-30: Close**
- Investment and terms
- Answer objections
- Next steps if yes

**Don't pitch the call:**
- It's a fit assessment, not a sales call
- You're qualifying them as much as they're qualifying you

---

## Client qualification checklist

Accept if they have:
- [ ] Budget to invest
- [ ] Clear problem you can solve
- [ ] Realistic timeline expectations
- [ ] Willingness to implement advice
- [ ] Good communication style

Red flags:
- [ ] Shopping for cheapest option
- [ ] Unrealistic expectations
- [ ] History of not following through
- [ ] Poor communication
- [ ] Budget doesn't match needs

## Service-specific tactics

**Coaching:**
- Emphasize transformation
- Show before/after stories
- Group vs 1-on-1 positioning
- Include accountability component

**Consulting:**
- Lead with expertise and credentials
- Show ROI calculation
- Position as strategic advisor
- Emphasize custom solutions

**Agency/Done-for-you:**
- Highlight time savings
- Show portfolio or case studies
- Emphasize quality of work
- Include revision rounds

---

## Objection handling

**"Too expensive"**
- Break down ROI: "This pays for itself if you [specific outcome]"
- Offer payment plan
- Compare to cost of not solving problem

**"Need to think about it"**
- "What specifically do you need to think about?"
- Address real concern
- Set follow-up date

**"Not sure it will work for me"**
- Show similar client success story
- Offer guarantee or trial period
- Address specific doubts

**"Bad timing"**
- "When would be better?"
- Highlight cost of waiting
- Offer to reserve spot

---

## Guarantee structures

**Results guarantee:**
- "Hit [metric] or work continues free until you do"
- Only if you control the outcome

**Satisfaction guarantee:**
- "30-day money-back if not satisfied"
- Filters tire-kickers

**Deliverable guarantee:**
- "Get [specific deliverable] or full refund"
- Works for project-based

**No guarantee:**
- Fine if you're selective about clients
- Position as high-demand service

---

## Payment structures

**Upfront:**
- Full payment before starting
- Best cash flow
- Requires strong trust

**50/50 split:**
- Half upfront, half at completion
- Balances risk
- Standard for projects

**Milestone-based:**
- Payment at completion of phases
- Works for long engagements
- Keeps both parties accountable

**Monthly retainer:**
- Recurring payment
- Predictable revenue
- Requires delivering ongoing value

---

## Post-launch actions

**For accepted clients:**
- Welcome email with onboarding details
- Schedule first call
- Send pre-work or questionnaire
- Add to client portal/system

**For rejected applications:**
- Kind rejection email
- Explain why (if appropriate)
- Offer alternative resource
- Invite to reapply later

**For people who didn't apply:**
- Survey: "What stopped you?"
- Move to nurture sequence
- Share more case studies
- Announce next opening date

---

## Scaling considerations

**At 5-10 clients:**
- Systemize onboarding
- Create templates for common deliverables
- Document your process

**At 10-20 clients:**
- Consider hiring support
- Create group components
- Raise prices

**At 20+ clients:**
- Build a team
- Move to group model
- Create productized offerings
