# Legal services cold email sequence

## Strategy notes

Legal services = highest cold email reply rate at 10% (ALPHA468). AI personalization pushes that to 20-35%. The key: lawyers respond to specificity and results. They're trained to evaluate evidence.

**Personalization variables (AI-generated per prospect):**
- {{case_type}}: their practice area (personal injury, family, immigration, estate, business)
- {{recent_win}}: specific case result, settlement, or verdict from news/court records
- {{local_competitor}}: a competing firm in their market
- {{review_gap}}: specific weakness in their online reviews (Google, Avvo, Justia)
- {{content_gap}}: what content they're not producing that competitors are

**Multichannel sequence:**
1. Day -2: LinkedIn connection request (personalized note)
2. Day 0: Email 1 (45-word opener)
3. Day 3: Email 2 (case study)
4. Day 5: LinkedIn DM referencing email
5. Day 7: Email 3 (different angle)
6. Day 14: Email 4 (proof stack)
7. Day 21: Email 5 (breakup)

## Personalization research checklist (AI does this per prospect)

Before sending to ANY legal prospect, AI agent must research and fill:

1. **{{recent_win}}**: Check court records, press releases, firm website for recent case outcomes
2. **{{review_gap}}**: Google "{{firm_name}} reviews" and count reviews. Compare to top 3 local competitors
3. **{{content_gap}}**: Check firm blog. If no blog or blog hasn't posted in 60+ days, note it
4. **{{case_type}}**: From firm website "Practice Areas" page
5. **{{local_competitor}}**: Search "[practice area] attorney [city]" and pick #1 organic result that isn't them
6. **{{competitor_review_count}}**: Google review count of that competitor

**Time per prospect:** 2-3 minutes with AI research agent. Without AI: 15-20 minutes. This is the 7x efficiency gap that makes the economics work.

## ICP scoring criteria

| Signal | Score | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 5-15 attorneys | +3 | Sweet spot. Big enough to pay, small enough to need help |
| Solo practitioner | +2 | Decision maker is the contact |
| Under 50 Google reviews | +3 | Clear review gap = pain point |
| No blog or inactive blog | +3 | Content gap = easy win |
| Practice area: PI, family, immigration, estate | +2 | High-volume, review-dependent |
| Practice area: corporate, IP, securities | -2 | Low volume, referral-dependent, cold email resistant |
| Has paid ads running | +2 | Already spending on acquisition, understands CAC |
| Located in city 100K-500K population | +2 | Enough market, not oversaturated |

**Target score:** 8+ out of possible 15

## A/B test plan

**Week 1-2:**
- Subject line A vs B vs C (Email 1)
- With LinkedIn pre-touch vs without
- 45-word Email 1 vs 80-word Email 1

**Week 3-4:**
- Review angle (Email 3) vs content angle as Email 1
- Case study with numbers vs case study with testimonial quote
- "show me" CTA vs "15-min call" CTA

**Kill criteria:** Any variant under 5% reply rate at 50+ sends gets killed.

---

## Send cadence limits (per ALPHA461 + EDGE_GROWTH_TACTICS.md)

- Max 35-50 emails per inbox per day
- Warm up inbox 2 weeks, domain 4 weeks before sending
- Small batches of 50 recipients max (5.8% avg vs 2.1% for large blasts)
- Best send time: Wednesday 7-11am recipient local time
- Track reply rate only. Open rates are vanity.

## Compliance notes

- No income claims without substantiation
- Case study results must be real or clearly marked as hypothetical examples
- If using AI-generated personalization, the emails must still be truthful about your services
