# Healthcare/dental cold email sequence

## Strategy notes

Healthcare/dental = second-highest cold email reply vertical at 7-8% (ALPHA468). Dentists are data-driven but time-poor. They respond to specific numbers about THEIR practice (not generic "grow your practice" copy). Google reviews and local SEO are their currency.

**Why dental works for cold outreach:**
- Patient lifetime value $12,000-$15,000 means even 1 new patient/month pays for any service
- 97% of patients search online before choosing a dentist
- 90% call the practice with the most 5-star Google reviews first
- Most practices have no automated follow-up system (25-30% of calls go unanswered)
- Dentists understand ROI. $1 in email marketing returns $36-44 in dental

**Personalization variables (AI-generated per prospect):**
- {{practice_name}}: their practice name
- {{dentist_name}}: lead dentist name
- {{review_count}}: their Google review count
- {{review_avg}}: their Google star average (4.2, 4.7, etc.)
- {{competitor_name}}: top-ranked competitor in their area
- {{competitor_reviews}}: competitor's review count
- {{review_gap}}: difference between their reviews and competitor
- {{city}}: their market/city
- {{specialty}}: general, cosmetic, implants, ortho, pediatric
- {{website_issue}}: specific website/SEO weakness (no blog, no FAQ, missing schema)

**ICP scoring system (target 8+ out of 15):**

| Signal | Points | How to Check |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| 2-5 dentists in practice | 3 | Google Business Profile |
| Under 100 Google reviews | 3 | Google Maps |
| Competitor has 2x+ reviews | 2 | Google Maps comparison |
| No blog or content on website | 2 | Quick site check |
| Running Google Ads | 2 | SpyFu or search "[city] dentist" |
| Reviews are 3-6 months stale | 1 | Google date check |
| No online scheduling | 1 | Website check |
| Practice established 3+ years | 1 | Google info |

**Multichannel sequence:**
1. Day -2: LinkedIn connection request (personalized note)
2. Day 0: Email 1 (45-word opener with review gap data)

4. Day 5: LinkedIn DM referencing email
5. Day 7: Email 3 (different angle - missed calls revenue leak)

7. Day 21: Email 5 (breakup)

## A/B testing plan

**Week 1-2: Subject line test**
- Variant A: practice name reference ({{practice_name}} Google reviews)
- Variant B: question format (quick question about {{practice_name}})
- Variant C: competitor reference ({{competitor_name}} has {{competitor_reviews}} reviews)
- Min 200 sends per variant. Track open rate + reply rate separately.

**Week 3-4: First email length test**
- Variant A: 45-word version (above)
- Variant B: 80-word version (add one more proof point)
- Keep winning subject from week 1-2.

**Week 5-6: Multichannel test**
- Variant A: LinkedIn pre-touch + email sequence (full multichannel)
- Variant B: Email-only sequence
- Measure: reply rate, meeting rate, cost per meeting

**Week 7-8: Angle test**
- Variant A: Review gap opener (current email 1)
- Variant B: Missed calls opener (current email 3 content as email 1)
- Variant C: Patient reactivation opener (new: "68% of patients leave due to lack of communication")

**Where to find dental practice leads:**

| Source | Data Available | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Google Maps scrape | Name, address, phone, reviews, hours, website | Free (Apify actor) |
| Apollo.io | Email, LinkedIn, company size, tech stack | Free tier: 50 leads/mo |

| Yelp | Reviews, response patterns, photos | Free (scrape) |
| Healthgrades | Provider profiles, patient reviews, specialties | Free (scrape) |

**Scraping workflow:**
1. Google Maps: search "dentist [city]" for target markets
2. Extract: practice name, address, phone, review count, star rating, website
3. Enrich: Apollo for decision maker email + LinkedIn
4. Score: ICP scoring system (target 8+)
5. Personalize: AI generates opening line per prospect using review data + website analysis
6. Load: into Instantly/Smartlead with sequence

**Target markets (highest ROI):**
- Suburban areas (less competition, higher lifetime value)
- Growing cities (new residents need dentists)
- Areas with dental school nearby (new practitioners starting practices)

## RETVec avoidance checklist

- [x] Plain text only (no HTML, no images, no buttons)
- [x] Every email body is unique (personalization variables ensure this)
- [x] Under 100 words per email
- [x] No urgency language ("act now", "limited time", "don't miss")
- [x] No spammy formatting (no ALL CAPS, no excessive punctuation)
- [x] Conversational tone (reads like 1:1 message, not template)
- [x] No links in first email (CTA is reply-based)
- [x] No tracking pixels in first email
- [x] Subject lines under 50 chars, no clickbait

## Compliance notes

- CAN-SPAM: include unsubscribe option (footer, not in body)
- HIPAA: never reference specific patient data or health information in cold outreach
- FTC: no false claims about results (use "one practice" not "guaranteed")
- State laws: check state-specific dental advertising regulations (varies by state)
- Testimonials: all case study numbers must be from real clients (change identifying details)
