Common questions

Before you buy

What if I don't know much about his family history?
You only need three things: his family surname, a rough idea of his heritage (Irish, Italian, English, or German), and optionally his grandparents' first names. We fill the rest in from historical records. The less you know, the more surprising the result.
Does he need to do anything to receive it?
Nothing. You enter the information. You receive the link. You share it with him whenever you're ready — there's no account, no login, and nothing for him to install.
What heritages are covered?
Currently: Irish, Italian, English, and German ancestry. Each heritage has its own deep historical narrative. If his family is mixed heritage, we cover all branches included in the form.
How long does it take?
Most sites are delivered within 24 hours. Premium tier orders are prioritized. There's no deadline — you can purchase now and receive the link in plenty of time for Father's Day.
Is this the same as Ancestry.com?
No. Ancestry.com shows a family tree of names and dates and charges $99/year. Before You creates a narrative — his family's actual story, written beautifully, in prose. It's not a database. It's a piece of writing.
Can he share the site with other family members?
Yes — that's the point. He gets a permanent URL he can send to siblings, children, and grandchildren. The site lives at that address indefinitely.
