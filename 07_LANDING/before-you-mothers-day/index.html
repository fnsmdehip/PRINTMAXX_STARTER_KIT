Common questions

Before you buy

What if I don't know much about her family history?
You only need three things: her family surname, a rough idea of her heritage (Irish, Italian, English, or German), and optionally her grandparents' first names. We fill the rest in from historical records. The less you know, the more surprising the result.
Does she need to do anything to receive it?
Nothing. You enter the information. You receive the link. You share it with her whenever you're ready — there's no account, no login, and nothing for her to install.
What heritages are covered?
Currently: Irish, Italian, English, and German ancestry. Each heritage has its own deep historical narrative. If her family is mixed heritage, we cover all branches included in the form.
How long does it take?
Most sites are delivered within 24 hours. Premium tier orders are prioritized. There's no deadline — you can purchase now and receive the link in plenty of time for Mother's Day.
Is this the same as Ancestry.com?
No. Ancestry.com shows a family tree of names and dates and charges $99/year. Before You creates a narrative — her family's actual story, written beautifully, in prose. It's not a database. It's a piece of writing.
Can she share the site with other family members?
Yes — that's the point. She gets a permanent URL she can send to siblings, cousins, and children. The site lives at that address indefinitely.
