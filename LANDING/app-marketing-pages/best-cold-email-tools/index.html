rank #4
Lemlist
Best for Personalization
$59/mo
Email Outreach plan • affiliate link
Send limit: 400/day/account Warmup pool: Lemwarm (14,000+ inboxes) Deliverability (our test): 88% Plans: $59-$99/mo

Lemlist invented image personalization for cold email. you can inject a prospect's company logo, LinkedIn headshot, or custom text into an image in the email body. open rates improve 15-40% vs plain-text in their published data - we saw 18% lift in our test. Lemlist also does LinkedIn sequences: connect request, message, and follow-up all in one flow. Lemwarm has 14,000+ inboxes in the warmup pool, the largest of any tool here. the downsides are price ($59/mo is the steepest in this list) and a cluttered interface. the lead database is smaller than Apollo's or Instantly's. if your ICP is senior buyers at larger companies who respond to personalized outreach at lower volume, Lemlist earns its cost.

pros

  • Lemwarm: 14,000+ inbox warmup pool
  • video prospecting supported

cons

  • 88% deliverability - behind Instantly/Smartlead
  • LinkedIn steps require Chrome extension
bottom line: worth it if you do low-volume, high-personalization outreach and LinkedIn is part of your sequence. overkill for anyone sending plain-text at volume.
