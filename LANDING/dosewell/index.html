What to expect

We believe in honest information

GLP-1 medications are effective, but they are not without side effects. Here is what the clinical data shows.

Common Side Effects (may occur, especially in early weeks)

  • Nausea (most common, usually decreases over time)
  • Diarrhea or constipation
  • Stomach pain or discomfort
  • Reduced appetite (this is partly how the medication works)
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Injection site reactions
  • Vomiting (usually in first 4-8 weeks)

Serious but rare: pancreatitis, gallbladder problems, thyroid tumors (seen in animal studies). GLP-1 medications carry an FDA boxed warning about thyroid C-cell tumors. Your doctor will discuss all risks during your consultation. These medications are not appropriate for everyone.
