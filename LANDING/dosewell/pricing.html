Insurance Information

A note about insurance

Dosewell does not currently accept insurance. Our pricing is designed to be affordable without insurance coverage.

For context: Many insurance plans do not cover GLP-1 medications for weight loss (they may cover them for diabetes). Even with insurance, copays for brand-name Ozempic or Wegovy often range from $25-500/month depending on your plan, and many patients face prior authorization requirements that can delay treatment by weeks.

We may accept insurance in the future. If you would like to be notified when this changes, join our mailing list below.

You can request a superbill (itemized receipt) to submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Reimbursement is not guaranteed and depends on your individual plan.
