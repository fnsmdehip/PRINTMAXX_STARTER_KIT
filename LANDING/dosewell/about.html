About

Weight loss medicine, done honestly

Dosewell exists because getting prescription weight loss medication should not require a $300 office visit, insurance battles, or weeks of waiting.

Our Mission

Make GLP-1 treatment accessible and honest

GLP-1 medications are among the most studied weight loss treatments in history. Clinical trials with thousands of participants have shown real results. But for most people, getting these medications is expensive, time-consuming, and confusing.

Dosewell makes it simpler. We connect you with licensed physicians who evaluate whether GLP-1 medication is right for you. If it is, we handle the prescription, pharmacy coordination, shipping, and ongoing monitoring. If it is not right for you, we tell you that too.

We do not promise miracle results. We do not use fake doctor photos or fabricated testimonials. We give you real information, connect you with real doctors, and let the medicine speak for itself.

What We Believe

How we operate

🔍 Transparency over marketing

We publish our pricing with no hidden fees. We list the real side effects, including the serious ones. We explain exactly what compounded medication is and how it differs from brand-name drugs. We do not use scare tactics or countdown timers to pressure you into buying. If something sounds too good to be true in this industry, it probably is.

⚕ Physicians make medical decisions

Dosewell does not influence prescribing decisions. Our physicians are independent practitioners who evaluate each patient individually. They can and do deny prescriptions when the medication is not appropriate. An approval rate below 100% is a feature, not a bug. It means the doctors are doing their jobs.

💬 Honest about limitations

Telehealth cannot replace a physical examination. GLP-1 medications are not appropriate for everyone. Weight regain after stopping is common and well-documented. Side effects are real and can be unpleasant. We tell you these things upfront because informed patients make better decisions.

💰 Fair pricing, not lowest pricing

Our pricing covers licensed physicians, FDA-registered pharmacy compounding, quality medication, cold-chain shipping, and ongoing medical supervision. We are significantly cheaper than brand-name retail, but we are not a $50/month operation because the clinical infrastructure costs real money to maintain properly.

🔒 Privacy as a right

Your health information is protected under HIPAA. We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers. Medical weight loss is personal, and your privacy is non-negotiable. Packaging is discreet. Your employer, neighbors, and mail carrier will not know what is in the box.

📈 Results measured, not promised

We do not guarantee you will lose a specific amount of weight. Anyone who does is lying to you. We track your progress with real data, adjust treatment based on how you respond, and give you the tools and medical support to achieve the best outcome your body allows.

The Evidence

What the clinical research shows

GLP-1 medications are among the most studied weight loss treatments. Here is a summary of the key clinical trials.

Trial Medication Participants Average Weight Loss Duration
STEP 1 Semaglutide 2.4mg 1,961 ~14.9% body weight 68 weeks
STEP 2 Semaglutide 2.4mg 1,210 ~9.6% body weight 68 weeks
SURMOUNT-1 Tirzepatide 15mg 2,539 ~22.5% body weight 72 weeks
SELECT Semaglutide 2.4mg 17,604 ~9.4% body weight ~40 months

Data from published, peer-reviewed clinical trials. These are study averages, not Dosewell patient results. Individual outcomes vary. SELECT trial focused on cardiovascular outcomes in patients with established CVD, not weight loss as primary endpoint. All trials included diet and exercise interventions alongside medication.
