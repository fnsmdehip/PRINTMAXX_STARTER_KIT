The Science

How we actually detect deception

No gimmicks. No random number generators. Real peer-reviewed biometric methods adapted for mobile hardware.

Photoplethysmography (PPG)

Your phone's camera LED illuminates blood vessels beneath the skin. As your heart beats, blood volume changes cause subtle color shifts captured at 30+ fps. We extract heart rate, HRV, and pulse transit time -- the same signals clinical polygraphs use, adapted for a phone camera sensor.

Vocal Micro-Tremor Analysis

Under cognitive load, the muscles controlling your vocal cords exhibit involuntary micro-tremors. TruthScope's audio pipeline isolates these sub-audible patterns by analyzing fundamental frequency jitter, shimmer, and harmonic-to-noise ratio in real time using your phone's microphone.

AU-Coded Facial Tracking

Using the Facial Action Coding System (FACS), we map 68 facial landmarks and track Action Units associated with deception -- asymmetric expressions, delayed smiles, blink-rate changes, and gaze aversion. On-device ML inference keeps all processing local and private.
