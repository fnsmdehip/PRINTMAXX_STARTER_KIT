About Androx

Testosterone therapy
without the runaround

Androx exists because getting tested and treated for low testosterone shouldn't require weeks of scheduling, multiple office visits, and insurance bureaucracy.

The problem we're fixing

Getting diagnosed with low testosterone through the traditional medical system looks like this: wait 2-4 weeks for a primary care appointment, get a referral to an endocrinologist, wait another 3-6 weeks for that appointment, get sent to a lab for a blood draw, wait for results, schedule a follow-up to discuss results, get a prescription, drive to a pharmacy, start treatment, and hope your insurance covers enough of it to make it affordable.

That's 6-12 weeks from "I think something's off" to "I'm actually being treated." And that's if everything goes smoothly. Many men get told their levels are "in range" (at 310 ng/dL) and sent home with no treatment.

We built Androx to collapse that timeline to under 10 days while maintaining the medical rigor that matters. At-home blood testing, video consultations with physicians who specialize in this, medication shipped to your door, and ongoing monitoring that actually tracks whether the treatment is working.

Our Principles

What we stand for

1

Bloodwork first, always

We don't prescribe based on a symptom questionnaire. Lab confirmation is required before treatment begins and continues throughout therapy. If your levels are normal, we tell you that directly.

2

Real physicians, not rubber stamps

Your doctor is a licensed MD or DO with training in hormone therapy. Consultations are real conversations about your health, not 5-minute prescription factories. About 30% of consultations don't result in a TRT prescription.

3

Honest about limitations

TRT has real side effects. It's not for everyone. It impacts fertility. It's a long-term commitment. We say all of this upfront because informed patients make better decisions and have better outcomes.

4

Monitoring is the whole point

Anyone can write a prescription. The hard part is managing it correctly. Regular labs, dose adjustments, side effect management, and health tracking over time. That's where the value is.

5

Transparent pricing

One price covers everything. No surprise lab fees, no separate consultation charges, no pharmacy markup. You know what you're paying from day one.

6

No overselling

We don't push peptide stacks, growth hormone, or supplement bundles. If testosterone therapy is appropriate for you, we provide it. If lifestyle changes are the better path, your doctor will tell you that instead.

How we operate

Androx is a telehealth platform. We connect patients with licensed physician groups who specialize in hormone therapy. Labs are processed through CLIA-certified laboratories. Medications are dispensed by licensed U.S. pharmacies.

We handle the technology, scheduling, patient communication, and coordination between all parties. Your physician handles the medicine. The pharmacy handles the dispensing. Each entity operates within its licensed scope.

All patient data is encrypted and stored in HIPAA-compliant systems. We don't sell your data, don't share it with advertisers, and don't use it for anything beyond your medical care.

What we're not

✕

Not a pill mill

We don't prescribe to everyone who asks. Clinical criteria must be met. If TRT isn't right for you, we'll say so.

✕

Not an anti-aging clinic

We don't sell "youth optimization packages" or push unnecessary supplements. We treat diagnosed low testosterone.

✕

Not a pharmacy

We don't dispense medication ourselves. Prescriptions are filled by independent licensed pharmacies and shipped directly to you.

✕

Not a performance enhancement service

We don't prescribe supraphysiological doses. If you're looking for bodybuilding-level testosterone, this isn't the right service.
