🧠

💔

💤

Straight Talk

What we won't tell you

Other clinics promise miracles. We don't. Here's the honest version.

!

TRT is not for everyone

If your bloodwork comes back normal, we won't prescribe. We treat clinical hypogonadism, not vanity. Roughly 30% of consultations don't result in a prescription.

!

Side effects exist

Potential side effects include: raised hematocrit, acne, hair thinning, testicular atrophy, mood changes, and sleep apnea worsening. Your doctor monitors for these.

!

Fertility impact

Exogenous testosterone suppresses natural production and can reduce sperm count. If you're planning children, discuss HCG co-therapy or alternative protocols first.

!

It's a commitment

Most men on TRT stay on it long-term. Stopping abruptly can cause withdrawal symptoms and temporarily lower natural production below baseline. Plan accordingly.
