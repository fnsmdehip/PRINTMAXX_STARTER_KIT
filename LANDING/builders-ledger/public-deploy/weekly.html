SIGCRAFT 73 files · 17,430 lines · Built from scratch this week

Entire prediction engine built in 6 days, from nothing to paper-trading signals across crypto, prediction markets, equities, and macro. Scrapes 22+ sources, runs them through signal processors and LLM forecasting via claude -p, then executes simulated positions with Kelly sizing and risk gates. Every trade passes a 5%/position, 40%-total exposure hard cap before it touches even paper money.

Trading Infrastructure (Built March 21)

paper.py 13KB Paper Trading Simulator

  • Processes signals in real-time, applies strategy + risk rules per trade
  • PaperTrade dataclass: entry/exit price, size, P&L, slippage model
  • Supports crypto, prediction markets, equities, macro asset classes

strategy.py 16KB Strategy Framework

  • Abstract Strategy base: should_enter() / should_exit()
  • EdgeStrategy: entries on signal confidence > threshold, exits on trailing stop or target
  • default_strategies() factory per asset class

risk.py 8KB Risk Management

  • Max 5% per position, 40% total exposure cap
  • Drawdown gates, correlation-adjusted sizing
  • Every trade passes through RiskManager.check() before execution

scaler.py 7KB Strategy Auto-Scaler

  • Rolling Sharpe ratio per strategy. Scale up at Sharpe > 1.0, down at < 0.0
  • Min 20 trades before scaling. Drawdown cut at 15%. 95% confidence gating
  • Bounds: 0.2x min, 5x max allocation multiplier

engine.py + metrics.py 26KB Signal-Replay Backtesting

  • Replays SIGCRAFT's own signals against historical data (not traditional OHLCV)
  • Flow: Historical Signals → Strategy.evaluate() → RiskManager.check() → Virtual Trades → Metrics
  • BacktestResult: total return, Sharpe, max drawdown, win rate, profit factor
