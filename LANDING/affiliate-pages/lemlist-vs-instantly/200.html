frequently asked questions

the things people actually search for.

Is Lemlist or Instantly better for cold email in 2026?

Instantly is better for high-volume pure-email outreach at lower cost ($30/mo, unlimited email accounts). Lemlist is better for multichannel outreach combining email, LinkedIn, and phone calls, plus personalized images ($39/mo). Choose based on whether you need multichannel or just email volume.

How much does Lemlist cost vs Instantly?

Lemlist starts at $39/mo (Email Starter, 1 email account). Instantly starts at $30/mo (Growth, unlimited email accounts, 1,000 leads/mo). Instantly is $9/mo cheaper at entry. More importantly, Instantly's unlimited accounts model means costs stay flat as you scale inboxes. Lemlist charges $69/mo for 3 accounts, $99/mo for 5, and $159/mo for 15 accounts.

Does Lemlist have LinkedIn outreach?

Yes. Lemlist's Multichannel Expert ($99/mo) and Outreach Scale ($159/mo) plans include LinkedIn automation: connection requests, direct messages, and profile visits, all sequenced alongside email steps. Instantly is email-only and does not offer LinkedIn or phone outreach at any price tier.

Which has better email deliverability, Lemlist or Instantly?

Both include email warmup at no extra cost. Instantly's unlimited account model lets you rotate sending across more addresses, which helps deliverability at scale. Lemlist's lemwarm is solid but capped by how many accounts your plan includes. At high volume (25K+ emails/month), Instantly's unlimited inbox rotation gives it a practical edge.

Can Lemlist send personalized images and videos?

Yes. Lemlist is known for dynamic image personalization: you can insert the recipient's name, company logo, or LinkedIn profile picture directly into a custom image inside the email body. Video thumbnail personalization works the same way. Instantly does not have this feature. Text personalization via variables works in both tools, but visual personalization is exclusive to Lemlist.

Does Instantly have a lead database?

Yes. Instantly includes a B2B lead finder with 160M+ verified contacts. The Growth plan ($30/mo) includes 1,000 leads/mo. Hypergrowth ($77.6/mo) includes 25,000. Light Speed ($286.3/mo) includes 500,000. Lemlist has no built-in lead database and requires a separate tool like Apollo, Clay, or Hunter to source contacts.

Which is better for agencies, Lemlist or Instantly?

Both serve agencies differently. Instantly is better for agencies running high-volume pure-email campaigns at scale, with predictable flat pricing. Lemlist is better for agencies offering multichannel outreach (email + LinkedIn) to clients, especially when CRM integrations are required. Lemlist's Outreach Scale ($159/mo) supports up to 15 email accounts for multi-client operations.
