Sermorelin vs. HGH: What's the Difference

Synthetic HGH (somatropin) directly replaces growth hormone. Sermorelin stimulates your own pituitary to produce more. The practical differences:

  • Cost: HGH runs $800-3,000/month. Sermorelin $200-450/month.
  • Feedback: Sermorelin maintains natural feedback loops. HGH bypasses them, which can suppress natural production.
  • Side effects: HGH has higher risk of fluid retention, joint pain, and carpal tunnel at supraphysiological doses. Sermorelin rarely produces these because your pituitary self-regulates.
  • Potency: HGH is more potent. If you need significant GH elevation for medical reasons, sermorelin may not be sufficient.

Realistic Expectations

Sermorelin typically increases GH output 20-40%, not the 200%+ that exogenous HGH provides. The effects are subtler: improved sleep quality (often the first noticed benefit), gradual body composition improvement, better skin quality over 3-6 months, and improved recovery from exercise. It won't produce dramatic muscle gains or de-aging. If someone promises that, they're selling, not prescribing.
