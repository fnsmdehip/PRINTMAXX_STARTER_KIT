Where to Start: A Basic Supplement Stack for Men Over 50

If you're healthy and want a foundation for healthy aging, clinical evidence points to a small core stack rather than 10+ supplements:

  1. Vitamin D3 + K2 — 70%+ of men over 50 are deficient. D3 with K2 for calcium metabolism. ~$15/month.
  2. Magnesium Glycinate — Supports sleep quality, muscle function, blood pressure. Glycinate form best tolerated. ~$15/month.
  3. Omega-3 (EPA/DHA) — 1-2g daily. Heart, brain, joint anti-inflammation. Choose triglyceride form for absorption. ~$20/month.
  4. Zinc — Testosterone cofactor. Many older men are deficient. 25-30mg/day. ~$8/month.
  5. Creatine Monohydrate — 3-5g/day. The most evidence-backed supplement for muscle mass preservation in aging men. ~$12/month.

Add category-specific supplements (prostate, joints, etc.) on top of this foundation based on your specific concerns.
