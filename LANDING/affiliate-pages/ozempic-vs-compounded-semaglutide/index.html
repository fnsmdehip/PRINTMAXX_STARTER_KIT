The "Same Molecule" Argument

Semaglutide is semaglutide. The molecule doesn't know whether Novo Nordisk or a compounding pharmacy made it. At the molecular level, the products are identical. This is chemistry, not marketing.

The nuance: excipients, concentration precision, sterility assurance, and storage stability differ between manufacturers. These don't change the drug but affect consistency and reliability.

When Brand Matters

  • Insurance covers it (effectively free)
  • Complex medical history requiring most documented form
  • Wegovy indication needed for insurance/employer programs
  • You prefer full FDA approval peace of mind

When Compounded Makes Sense
