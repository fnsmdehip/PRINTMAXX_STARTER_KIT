Honest Pricing Breakdown (No Hidden Costs)

Most AI video tools advertise a headline price but charge credits that run out faster than you expect. Here is the reality for a creator making 20 videos per month (each 2-3 minutes):

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HeyGen or Synthesia better?

HeyGen wins on avatar realism and lip sync for creator/marketing content. Synthesia wins on enterprise localization (130+ languages) and compliance certifications for corporate deployment. For YouTube and social content: HeyGen. For corporate training in 10 languages: Synthesia.

Can AI video tools replace human presenters?

For internal training, product demos, and process documentation — yes, in many cases. For external marketing content and brand storytelling where authenticity is the product, human presenters still convert better. The business case is strongest for high-volume, update-heavy, or multilingual content where traditional production is cost-prohibitive.

Are AI video tools legal to use for marketing?

Yes, with proper disclosure where required. The FTC's guidelines on AI-generated content are still developing. Standard practice: disclose AI-generated content in descriptions or footers. Using your own likeness cloned via HeyGen or Synthesia is legal. Creating video of someone else's likeness without consent is not. All platforms in this comparison require agreement to terms prohibiting non-consensual deepfakes.

What is the best free AI video tool?

D-ID offers 20 free credits per month — the most generous free tier. HeyGen's free tier gives 3 videos per month (1-minute limit each). Synthesia's free tier gives 3 videos. Runway's free plan is extremely limited. Start with D-ID or HeyGen free tiers to test the technology before committing.

Our Methodology

We evaluated AI video platforms across five dimensions: (1) output quality (avatar realism, lip sync accuracy, video coherence), (2) workflow speed (from script to finished video), (3) pricing value at practical production volumes, (4) unique features not replicated elsewhere, (5) reliability and support quality.

We generated test videos across all platforms using identical scripts and measured quality in blind comparisons. Rankings reflect performance for the most common use cases among creators and small business teams — not maximum enterprise features or minimum startup cost.
