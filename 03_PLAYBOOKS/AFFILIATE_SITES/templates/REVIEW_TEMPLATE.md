# Product Review Article Template

Use this template for individual product reviews (e.g., "Bowflex SelectTech 552 Review", "Hallow App Review").

---

## Meta Information

```
Title Tag: [Product Name] Review: [Hook/Question] ([Time Period] Test)
Meta Description: [Promise of honest review] + [key finding] + [who it's for]
Target Keyword: [product name] review
Secondary Keywords: [product] pros cons, is [product] worth it, [product] honest review
```

**Example:**
- Title: Bowflex SelectTech 552 Review: Worth It After 2 Years?
- Meta: After 2 years of daily use, here's my honest Bowflex SelectTech 552 review. The good, the bad, and who should buy them.

---

## Article Structure

### 1. Hook (100-150 words)

```markdown
[Personal credibility statement - how long you've used it]

[The core question readers have]

[Promise of what this review covers]
```

**Example:**
> I've used the Bowflex SelectTech 552 dumbbells nearly every day for two years. Before buying, I read dozens of reviews but still wasn't sure if they were worth $350. Now I know. Here's my honest assessment of what they do well, where they fall short, and whether you should buy them.

---

### 2. Quick Verdict Box

```markdown
**Quick Verdict**

Rating: [X/10]
Price: [$XXX]
Best for: [specific user type]
Not for: [who should skip]

**Bottom line:** [1-2 sentence verdict]
```

**Example:**
> **Quick Verdict**
>
> Rating: 8.5/10
> Price: $349-429
> Best for: Home gym users wanting 5-52.5 lb range
> Not for: Serious lifters needing 70+ lbs
>
> **Bottom line:** Best adjustable dumbbells for most people. Not perfect, but nothing else balances convenience, price, and quality this well.

---

### 3. What Is [Product]? (150-200 words)

```markdown
- Brief product description
- Company background (if relevant)
- Key specs/features at a glance
- Price and where to buy
- What it competes with
```

---

### 4. What [Product] Does Well (500-800 words)

Cover 4-6 specific positives with:
- **Subheading for each positive**
- Specific details, not vague praise
- Personal experience/example
- Photo evidence where applicable

**Example structure:**
```markdown
### 1. [Specific Positive]

[Detailed explanation with example]

[Screenshot or photo]

### 2. [Specific Positive]

[Detailed explanation with example]
```

---

### 5. Where [Product] Falls Short (300-500 words)

Cover 2-4 specific negatives with:
- **Subheading for each negative**
- Honest assessment
- Context (is it a dealbreaker?)
- Workarounds if any

**Note:** Being honest about cons builds trust and helps conversions.

---

### 6. [Product] vs Alternatives (300-400 words)

```markdown
| Feature | [Product] | [Alternative 1] | [Alternative 2] |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Price | $XXX | $XXX | $XXX |
| [Key Feature 1] | X | X | X |
| [Key Feature 2] | X | X | X |
| Best for | [user] | [user] | [user] |
```

Brief analysis of when to choose each option.

---

### 7. Who Should Buy [Product]? (200-300 words)

```markdown
**Buy [Product] if:**
- [Specific user trait/need]
- [Specific user trait/need]
- [Specific user trait/need]

**Skip [Product] if:**
- [Specific user trait/need]
- [Specific user trait/need]
```

---

### 8. FAQ (200-400 words)

Answer 4-6 common questions:
- Is [product] worth the price?
- [Product-specific common question]
- How does [product] compare to [competitor]?
- [Durability/longevity question]
- [Use case specific question]

Use FAQ schema markup.

---

### 9. Final Verdict (150-200 words)

```markdown
**Rating: [X/10]**

[Summary of key points]

[Clear recommendation]

[Primary CTA button]
```

---

## Affiliate Link Placement

1. **Quick verdict box** - First CTA
2. **After "What it does well"** - Reader is convinced
3. **Comparison table** - All options linked
4. **Final verdict** - Strong closing CTA

**CTA Examples:**
- "Check Current Price on Amazon"
- "Try [Product] Free"
- "See [Product] at [Store]"
