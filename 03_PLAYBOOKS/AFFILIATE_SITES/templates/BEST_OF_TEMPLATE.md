# "Best Of" Roundup Article Template

Use this template for "best X" roundup articles (e.g., "Best AI Writing Tools", "Best Adjustable Dumbbells", "Best Prayer Apps").

---

## Meta Information

```
Title Tag: Best [Product Category] in [Year]: [Number] [Products] Tested
Meta Description: We tested [number] [products]. Here are the [number] best [products] for [primary use case], [secondary use case], and every budget.
Target Keyword: best [product category]
Secondary Keywords: top [product category], [product category] reviews, best [product] for [use case]
```

**Example:**
- Title: Best AI Writing Tools in 2024: 8 Tools Tested Head-to-Head
- Meta: We tested 15 AI writing tools for 60 days. Here are the 8 best for content, copy, and business writing. Real tests, real results.

---

## Article Structure

### 1. Hook (100-150 words)

```markdown
[The problem - too many options, hard to choose]

[Your credibility - testing methodology]

[Promise - what this article delivers]
```

**Example:**
> There are 50+ AI writing tools claiming to be the best. Most are mediocre. I spent 60 days testing 15 of them on real projects to find which actually work. Here are the 8 worth your money, and the rest you should skip.

---

### 2. Quick Picks Box

```markdown
**Quick Picks**
```

### 3. How We Tested (200-300 words)

```markdown
### Our Testing Process

**What we evaluated:**
- [Criterion 1]
- [Criterion 2]
- [Criterion 3]
- [Criterion 4]
```

Establishes authority and methodology.

```markdown
[2-3 sentence summary of what makes it stand out]

**What we liked:**
- [Specific positive with detail]

- [Specific negative]
```

**Product order options:**

## Content Refresh Schedule

**Monthly:**
- Check prices
- Verify availability
- Update out-of-stock items

**Quarterly:**
- Add new products to market
- Remove discontinued items
- Update comparison data

**Annually:**
- Full content refresh
- Re-test top products
- Update title year
- Re-evaluate rankings

---

## Ranking Integrity Rules

1. **Test everything personally** - No secondhand reviews
2. **Update when products change** - Features evolve
3. **Disclose affiliate relationships** - Transparency
4. **Don't let commission rates affect rankings** - Quality first
5. **Include products without affiliates** - If they're best
