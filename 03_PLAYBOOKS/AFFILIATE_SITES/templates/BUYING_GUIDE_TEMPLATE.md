# Buying Guide Article Template

Use this template for comprehensive buying guides (e.g., "How to Choose a Treadmill", "Home Gym Setup Guide", "Complete Guide to AI Writing Tools").

---

## Meta Information

```
Title Tag: [Product Category] Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right [Product] ([Year])
Meta Description: Everything you need to know before buying a [product]. [Key consideration 1], [key consideration 2], and what to avoid. Complete guide.
Target Keyword: [product category] buying guide OR how to choose [product]
Secondary Keywords: [product] guide, what to look for in [product], [product] for beginners
```

**Example:**
- Title: Treadmill Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right One (2024)
- Meta: Everything you need to know before buying a treadmill. Motor size, belt dimensions, features that matter, and what to avoid. Complete guide.

---

## Article Structure

### 1. Hook (150-200 words)

```markdown
[Why this guide exists - the problem with buying this product]

[What most people get wrong]

[What this guide covers]

[Who this guide is for]
```

**Example:**
> Buying a treadmill is confusing. There are dozens of options from $300 to $4,000, and the specs don't mean anything to most people. I've tested 20+ treadmills and helped hundreds of people choose. This guide covers everything you need to know to pick the right one without overspending.

## Content Strategy Notes

**Buying guides serve as:**
1. Authority builders (comprehensive, helpful)
2. Top-of-funnel traffic (informational queries)
3. Internal link hubs (connect to reviews)
4. Trust builders (help before selling)

**Update frequency:** Annually or when major category changes occur

**Link structure:**
- Buying Guide → "Best Of" article → Individual reviews
- Creates content hub and passes link equity
