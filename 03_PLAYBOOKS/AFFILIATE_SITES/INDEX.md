## REVENUE MODEL COMPARISON

### Affiliate Programs by Commission Type

**High Commission Recurring (Best for Revenue):**
- Jasper AI: 30% recurring ($49/mo)
- Copy.ai: 25% recurring
- Zapier: 30% first year
- **Expected: $200-500/mo per 1000 visitors**

**Volume Play (Best for Beginners):**
- Amazon Associates: 1-4% (but massive volume)
- Chewy: 8% (pet supplies)
- iHerb: 8% (supplements)
- **Expected: $300-800/mo per 3000 visitors**

**High-Ticket B2B (Best for few conversions):**
- AI Voice platforms: $1000-5000 per deal
- Clinic software: $500-2000 per deal
- B2B SaaS: $200-500 per deal
- **Expected: $2000-5000/mo with 5-10 conversions/mo**

**Hybrid Model (Most Reliable):**
- Mix of recurring (Jasper) + volume (Amazon) + high-ticket (B2B)
- Diversifies revenue across programs
- Reduces dependency on single affiliate
- **Expected: $800-2000/mo per site by month 6**
