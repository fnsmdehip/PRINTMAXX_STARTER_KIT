## 1. Affiliate Revenue (Primary)

### Target: $35,000/year combined

**Amazon Associates: $20,000**
- 40k monthly visitors
- 6% average CTR to Amazon
- 2,400 clicks/month
- 8% conversion (Amazon is high)
- 192 orders/month
- $350 average order value
- 3% commission
- = $2,016/month

**Direct Brand Programs: $15,000**
- Bowflex, Rogue, Theragun, supplements
- Higher commissions (5-15%)
- Lower volume but higher per-sale
- = $1,250/month

### Content Strategy for Affiliate Revenue

**Highest converting content:**
1. **"Best X" articles** - Buyer intent, comparison shopping
2. **Product reviews** - Branded search, ready to buy
3. **"X vs Y" comparisons** - Decision stage
4. **Budget guides** - Price-conscious but purchasing

**Article types by revenue:**
- Best adjustable dumbbells → $300-500/month
- Best treadmills → $400-600/month
- Individual reviews → $50-150/month each
- Comparison articles → $100-200/month each

## 2. Display Advertising

### Target: $8,000/year

**Timeline:**
- Month 1-4: No ads (build traffic)
- Month 5-8: Ezoic ($12-18 RPM)
- Month 9-12: Mediavine ($20-30 RPM)

**RPM Expectations (Fitness Niche):**
- Ezoic: $12-18 (good CPCs for fitness)
- Mediavine: $20-30
- AdThrive: $25-40

**Fitness advantage:** Advertisers pay premium for fitness demographic

### Ad Placement
- In-content ads every 3-4 paragraphs
- Sidebar ads on desktop
- Avoid intrusive formats
- Balance with affiliate clicks (don't cannibalize)

## Optimization Tactics

### Increase Affiliate Conversions
1. **Add product tables** - Easy comparison
2. **Use real photos** - Build trust
3. **Update prices regularly** - Accuracy matters
4. **Clear CTAs** - "Check Price on Amazon"
5. **Comparison content** - Decision-ready visitors

### Increase Average Order Value
1. **Bundle recommendations** - Dumbbells + mat + bands
2. **Good/better/best** - Price anchoring
3. **Accessories section** - Add-ons after main product

### Maximize Ad Revenue
1. **Increase pageviews** - Internal linking
2. **Longer content** - More ad slots
3. **Video content** - Higher RPM
4. **Quiz/interactive** - Time on site
