# Home Fitness Gear - Affiliate Programs

## Primary Affiliate Programs

### Tier 1: Amazon Associates (Foundation)

**Details:**
- Commission: 1-4% (Fitness equipment typically 3%)
- Cookie: 24 hours
- Payout: $10 minimum
- Apply: affiliate-program.amazon.com

**Why it's essential:**
- Huge product catalog
- High trust/conversion
- Easy to use
- Most equipment available
- Prime shipping benefit

**Optimization:**
- Focus on higher-priced items ($200+)
- $500 dumbbell set x 3% = $15 commission
- Bundle recommendations increase cart value

## Commission Optimization

### High-Value Product Focus

**Best commission per click:**
1. **Treadmills** - $800 x 3% = $24/sale (Amazon)
2. **Power racks** - $500 x 5% = $25/sale (Rogue)
3. **Adjustable dumbbells** - $400 x 3-8% = $12-32/sale
4. **Massage guns** - $400 x 8% = $32/sale (Theragun direct)

**Strategy:** Prioritize content for high-AOV products

### Supplement Volume Play

Supplements have:
- Higher commission rates (8-15%)
- Repeat purchases (consumable)
- Lower price point but volume

**Strategy:** Create protein powder guides, link to Legion/Transparent Labs (15%)

## Disclosure Requirements

### FTC Compliance

**Every affiliate page needs:**

**Short disclosure (top):**
> This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you.

**Full disclosure (footer):**
> Affiliate Disclosure: HomeFitnessGearGuide.com is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission. We only recommend products we've personally tested and believe provide value. See our full disclosure policy.

### Amazon-Specific

Amazon requires specific disclosure language:
> As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases.

Include on every page with Amazon links.

## Program-Specific Notes

### Amazon Associates
- 24-hour cookie is short - optimize for immediate purchase
- Bundle products to increase cart value
- Holiday seasons can boost commission rates
- Keep associate links updated (products go out of stock)

### Bowflex (Icon Health)
- Popular SelectTech dumbbells
- Good brand recognition
- Seasonal promotions to leverage
- Max and other machines available

### Rogue Fitness
- Premium/enthusiast market
- Higher price points
- Selective about who they approve
- Great for serious home gym content

### Therabody (Theragun)
- High-ticket massage guns
- Good conversion rates
- Premium brand positioning
- 8% is solid for price point

### Supplement Brands
- Legion Athletics (15%) - Best commission
- Transparent Labs (15%) - Clean label appeal
- MyProtein (8%) - Volume brand
- Focus on 15% brands when quality is comparable
