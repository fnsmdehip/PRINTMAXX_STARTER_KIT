## Content Production Guidelines

### Article Requirements

**Every article must include:**
1. **Actual testing** - Real photos, usage experience
2. **Specs table** - Dimensions, weight, capacity
3. **Price tracking** - Current prices with date
4. **Pros/cons box** - Scannable summary
5. **Space requirements** - Footprint, ceiling height
6. **Assembly notes** - How hard to set up
7. **Warranty info** - Coverage details
8. **Affiliate disclosure** - FTC compliant

### SEO Requirements

- Target keyword in H1, first 100 words, URL
- Product schema markup
- FAQ schema where applicable
- Image alt text with keywords
- Internal links to related content
- Comparison tables with structured data

### Photo Requirements

- Real product photos (not stock)
- Scale reference (person using)
- Detail shots (construction, features)
- Assembly process if relevant
- Before/after setup
