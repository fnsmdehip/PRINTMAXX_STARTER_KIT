   - Adjustment: Dial system

3. **PowerBlock Elite** - $299-549
   - Weight: 5-50 lbs (expandable to 90)
   - Adjustment: Pin selector
   - Pros: Most compact, expandable, durable
   - Cons: Awkward feel for some, no round shape
   - Best for: Small spaces

4. **NordicTrack Select-A-Weight** - $299-399
   - Weight: 10-55 lbs

5. **Ironmaster Quick-Lock** - $599+
   - Weight: 5-75 lbs (expandable to 165)

6. **NUOBELL** - $449-549
   - Weight: 5-80 lbs

7. **Yes4All Adjustable** - $99-199
