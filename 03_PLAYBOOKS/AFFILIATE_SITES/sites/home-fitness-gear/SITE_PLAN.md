# Home Fitness Gear - Site Architecture

## Site Overview

**Domain idea:** homefitnessgearguide.com or garagegymreviews.com
**Niche:** Home gym equipment, fitness apps, supplements
**Monetization:** Amazon Associates + direct affiliate + display ads
**Traffic goal:** 40k monthly visitors within 12 months

---

## Market Opportunity

**Why this niche:**
- Massive market: Home fitness grew 50%+ post-COVID
- High purchase intent: People searching are ready to buy
- Strong affiliate programs: Amazon + direct brand programs
- Evergreen + seasonal: Core demand year-round, spikes in January
- Product variety: Equipment, apps, supplements, accessories

**Market size:**
- Home fitness equipment: $14B+ market
- Fitness apps: $4B+ market
- Sports nutrition: $50B+ market

## Target Audience

**Primary:**
- Home gym builders (budget to premium)
- Busy professionals wanting convenience
- Parents who can't get to gym

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-55
- Income: Middle to upper-middle
- Location: US primarily (UK, Canada secondary)
- Intent: High (searching for specific products)

**Pain points:**
- Limited space
- Budget constraints
- Quality concerns (lots of junk products)
- Analysis paralysis (too many options)
