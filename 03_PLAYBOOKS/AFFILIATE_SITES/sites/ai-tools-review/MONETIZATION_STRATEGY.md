## 1. Affiliate Revenue (Primary)

### Target: $30,000/year ($2,500/month)

**Strategy:**
- 100 articles driving 50,000 monthly visitors
- 2% click-through to affiliate links
- 3% conversion rate on clicks
- Average $50 commission

**Math:** 50,000 visitors x 2% CTR = 1,000 clicks x 3% = 30 sales x $50 = $1,500/month base
Recurring from previous months adds $1,000+

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Comparison articles convert best** - "A vs B" gets decision-ready buyers
2. **Review articles second** - People searching "[tool] review" are close to buying
3. **Best-of articles for volume** - Lower conversion but high traffic
4. **Link early and often** - First mention of tool = affiliate link
5. **Use comparison tables** - Easy scanning, multiple link opportunities

## 2. Sponsored Content

### Target: $6,000/year ($500/month average)

**Types:**
- Sponsored reviews: $300-500 each
- Tool inclusion in best-of lists: $100-200
- Newsletter sponsorships: $200-400

**How to Get Sponsors:**
1. Build media kit after 10,000 monthly visitors
2. Reach out to tools not in major affiliate networks
3. List "Advertise" page on site
4. Respond to incoming requests (they'll find you)

**Rules:**
- Clearly label sponsored content
- Maintain honest reviews (bad reviews build trust)
- Don't accept sponsors that don't fit audience

## 3. Display Advertising

### Target: $3,000/year

**Timeline:**
- Month 1-3: No ads (focus on content)
- Month 4-6: Join Ezoic (10k pageviews minimum)
- Month 7-12: Upgrade to Mediavine (50k sessions minimum)

**RPM Expectations:**
- Ezoic: $8-15 RPM
- Mediavine: $15-30 RPM
- AdThrive: $20-40 RPM

**Placement Strategy:**
- Above-fold ad hurts UX - skip it
- In-content ads every 3-4 paragraphs
- Sidebar ads (desktop only)
- No video ads that autoplay

## Pricing Psychology

### Affiliate Link Placement
1. **First mention** - Link the tool name
2. **In comparison tables** - "Try [Tool]" button
3. **After testimonial/proof** - Social proof then link
4. **In verdict box** - Clear CTA
5. **End of article** - Final push

### Product Pricing
- $19-29: Impulse buy territory
- $47-97: Requires consideration
- $147+: Needs sales page
- Odd numbers convert better ($47 vs $50)
