**Tier 1: Direct Competitors**

1. **Claude** (Anthropic) - $20/mo Pro, Free tier
   - Better at: Long-form writing, nuance, document analysis
   - Worse at: Plugins, web access
   - Best for: Writers, researchers

2. **Google Gemini** - Free, $20/mo Advanced
   - Better at: Google integration, current info
   - Worse at: Some complex reasoning
   - Best for: Google ecosystem users

3. **Microsoft Copilot** - Free, Pro $20/mo
   - Better at: Microsoft 365 integration
   - Worse at: Standalone use
   - Best for: Business users on Microsoft

**Tier 2: Specialized Tools**

4. **Perplexity AI** - Free, $20/mo Pro
   - Better at: Research with sources
   - Worse at: Creative writing
   - Best for: Researchers, fact-checking

5. **GitHub Copilot** - $10/mo
   - Better at: Code completion in IDE
   - Worse at: General conversation
   - Best for: Developers

6. **Jasper** - $49/mo
   - Better at: Marketing copy
   - Worse at: General tasks
   - Best for: Marketers, content creators

7. **Copy.ai** - Free tier, $36/mo Pro
   - Better at: Templates, workflows
   - Worse at: Long-form
   - Best for: Quick copy needs

**Tier 3: Budget Options**

8. **Poe** - Free, $20/mo
   - Better at: Access to multiple models
   - Worse at: Individual model limits
   - Best for: Model exploration

9. **You.com** - Free
   - Better at: Search integration
   - Worse at: Complex tasks
   - Best for: Quick questions

10. **Hugging Face Chat** - Free
    - Better at: Open-source model access
