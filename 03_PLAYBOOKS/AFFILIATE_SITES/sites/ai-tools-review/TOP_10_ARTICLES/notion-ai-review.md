### Section 1: What is Notion AI? (150 words)
- Add-on to Notion workspace
- AI writing, summarization, Q&A within your docs
- Launched early 2023, continuously improved

### Section 2: Pricing Breakdown (200 words)

| Notion Plan | Price | + Notion AI |
|-------------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | +$10/mo |
| Plus | $8/mo | +$10/mo |
| Business | $15/mo | +$10/mo |

**Total cost:** $18-25/month for Notion + AI
**Comparison:** ChatGPT Plus is $20/month for general AI

### Section 3: What Notion AI Does Well (500 words)

**1. Summarization**
- Summarize meeting notes in one click
- Works great for long documents
- Screenshot example

**2. Q&A on Your Docs**
- "What were the action items from last week?"
- Searches across your workspace
- Actually useful for large knowledge bases

**3. In-Context Writing**
- Write within your existing workflow
- No copy-paste between tools
- Understands your document context

**4. Translation**
- Quick translation in docs
- Useful for multilingual teams

**5. Simple Prompts**
- Fix grammar/spelling
- Make shorter/longer
- Explain like I'm 5

### Section 4: Where Notion AI Falls Short (400 words)

**1. Not as Smart as ChatGPT/Claude**
- Complex prompts get worse results
- Less creative
- Shorter context window

**2. Pricing Adds Up**
- $10/month on top of Notion
- Per-member pricing for teams (expensive)
- ChatGPT often better value

**3. Limited Creativity**
- Not great for marketing copy
- Jasper/Copy.ai better for content
- Works best for simple tasks

**4. No Chat Interface**
- Prompt → response → done
- No back-and-forth refinement
- Have to re-prompt for changes

### Section 5: Real Use Cases (300 words)

- After: 2 minutes

### Section 7: Who Should Buy? (200 words)

**Buy Notion AI if:**
- You already use Notion daily
- You have large documentation needs
- Your team works in Notion
- You want in-context AI (no switching apps)

**Skip Notion AI if:**
- You just want AI writing
- You don't already use Notion
- Budget is tight (ChatGPT covers more)
- You need creative/marketing copy
