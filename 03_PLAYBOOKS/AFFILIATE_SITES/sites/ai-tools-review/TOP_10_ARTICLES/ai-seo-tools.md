### Section 3: My Recommended Workflow (400 words)

**The workflow that works:**

```
Step 1: Research (Ahrefs/Semrush)
- Find keyword opportunities
- Analyze competitors
- Identify content gaps

Step 2: Brief (Frase or Surfer)
- Generate content brief
- Get NLP terms to include
- Outline structure

Step 3: Write (Jasper or ChatGPT)
- Draft content quickly
- Use SEO-optimized prompts
- Maintain voice/style

Step 4: Optimize (Surfer)
- Run through content editor
- Hit target score (70+)
- Add missing terms

Step 5: Publish & Monitor
- Track rankings
- Update based on performance
```
