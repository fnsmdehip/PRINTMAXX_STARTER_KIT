## Disclosure Requirements

### FTC Compliance (Required)

Every page with affiliate links must include:

**Short version (top of article):**
> This article contains affiliate links. If you purchase through our links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

**Full disclosure (footer):**
> Affiliate Disclosure: [Site Name] is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission. We only recommend products we've tested and believe provide value. See our full disclosure policy.

### Placement
- Disclosure at TOP of article (before any links)
- Disclosure in footer
- "Affiliate" label on obviously promotional content
