## Article Requirements

### Every Article Must Have:

1. **Personal testing** - Screenshots of actual usage
2. **Pricing table** - Current prices with update date
3. **Pros/cons box** - Clear summary at top
4. **Verdict box** - Who should/shouldn't buy
5. **Comparison table** - For "best of" articles
6. **Affiliate disclosure** - FTC compliant
7. **Last updated date** - Trust signal
8. **Author byline** - Real person, not brand

### SEO Requirements

- Target keyword in H1, first 100 words, URL
- 5+ related keywords naturally included
- 3-5 internal links to other site content
- 1-2 external links to authoritative sources
- Alt text on all images
- Meta description with keyword
- FAQ schema for comparison articles
