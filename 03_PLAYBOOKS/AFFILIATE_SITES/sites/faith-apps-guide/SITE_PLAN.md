# Faith Apps Guide - Site Architecture

## Site Overview

**Domain idea:** faithappsguide.com or christianappreviews.com
**Niche:** Christian/faith-based apps, tools, and digital resources
**Monetization:** Affiliate commissions + display ads
**Traffic goal:** 30k monthly visitors within 12 months

---

## Market Opportunity

**Why this niche:**
- Large audience: 200M+ Christians in US alone
- Underserved: Few quality review sites
- Growing market: Digital faith tools expanding
- High intent: People actively seeking tools
- Low competition: SEO easier than tech/finance niches

## Target Audience

**Primary:**
- Active Christians seeking digital faith tools
- Parents wanting faith-based apps for children
- Church leaders evaluating software

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-65 (broad)
- Location: US, UK, Australia primarily
- Device: 60% mobile, 40% desktop
- Intent: High (searching for specific solutions)

**Pain points:**
- Too many faith apps, hard to choose
- Quality varies widely
- Want trustworthy recommendations
- Privacy concerns with prayer/journal apps

## Content Strategy

### Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Foundation
- 5 "best of" cornerstone articles
- 5 individual app reviews
- Homepage with app directory

### Phase 2 (Month 3-4): Expansion
- 10 more app reviews
- 5 comparison articles
- Audience-specific landing pages

### Phase 3 (Month 5-6): Authority
- Guest posts on Christian blogs
- Church leader outreach
- Email list building

## Unique Positioning

1. **Genuine faith perspective** - Reviews from active Christians
2. **Privacy focus** - Highlight data practices for prayer apps
3. **Family-friendly filter** - Clear content ratings
4. **Denominational awareness** - Note Protestant/Catholic/Orthodox relevance
5. **Free vs paid transparency** - Clear about costs
