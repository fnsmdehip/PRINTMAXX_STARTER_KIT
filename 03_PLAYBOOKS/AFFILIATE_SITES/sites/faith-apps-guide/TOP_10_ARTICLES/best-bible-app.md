   - Serious study tools

   - Best for: Pastors, seminary students, deep studiers

3. **Bible Gateway** - Best Web Experience (Free/Premium)
   - Clean reading
   - Side-by-side versions
   - Audio options
   - Best for: Web-first users

4. **Dwell** - Best Audio Experience ($40/year)
   - Professional narration
   - Beautiful design
   - Playlists and timing
   - Best for: Audio listeners, commuters

5. **Olive Tree Bible** - Best Offline ($0-99+)
   - Strong offline support

   - Best for: Travelers, offline users

6. **Bible App for Kids** - Best for Children (Free)
   - Interactive stories

   - No ads
