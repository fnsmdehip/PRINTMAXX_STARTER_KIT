### Section 4: Where Hallow Falls Short (400 words)

**1. Catholic Focus May Alienate Some**
- Rosary, saints content
- Protestant users may find some content unfamiliar
- Though core meditations are broadly Christian

**2. Subscription Required for Most Content**
- Free tier is very limited
- $60/year adds up
- Competitors have more free content

**3. No Bible Reading Feature**
- Meditation focused, not Bible study
- Need separate Bible app
- Would be nice to have integrated

**4. Some Content Quality Varies**
- Celebrity content sometimes gimmicky
- Core content is excellent
- Skip what doesn't resonate

### Section 5: Who Should Use Hallow? (200 words)

**Buy Hallow if:**
- You want guided prayer/meditation
- Catholic or open to Catholic spirituality
- You struggle with sleep/anxiety
- You prefer audio to reading
- You can commit to daily practice

**Skip Hallow if:**
- You only want Bible reading
- Catholic content is uncomfortable
- You prefer silence to guided prayer
- $60/year is a stretch
