## Content Production Guidelines

### Article Requirements

**Every article must include:**
1. Genuine testing - Actually use the apps
2. Pricing current - Verify subscription costs
3. Platform availability - iOS, Android, Web
4. Pros/cons box - Clear summary
5. Faith perspective - Genuine, not preachy
6. Affiliate disclosure - FTC compliant
7. Privacy notes - For apps handling sensitive data

### Voice/Tone Guidelines

- **Authentic faith** - Write as a fellow believer
- **Not preachy** - Inform, don't lecture
- **Respectful of traditions** - Acknowledge Catholic, Orthodox, Protestant
- **Practical first** - Focus on usefulness
- **Honest limitations** - Apps can't replace real community

### SEO Requirements

- Target keyword in H1, first 100 words, URL
- Related keywords naturally included
- Internal links to other content
- FAQ schema where applicable
- Table schema for comparisons
