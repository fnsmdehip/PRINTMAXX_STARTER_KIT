# Faith Apps Guide - Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams Overview

| Stream | Year 1 Target | Effort | Notes |
|--------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Affiliate commissions | $15,000 | Medium | Primary focus |
| Display ads | $6,000 | Low | After 10k sessions |
| Sponsored content | $3,000 | Low | Church software sponsors |

| **Total Year 1** | **$30,000** | - | - |

---

## 1. Affiliate Revenue (Primary)

### Target: $15,000/year ($1,250/month)

**Strategy:**
- 50 articles driving 30,000 monthly visitors
- 3% click-through to affiliate links
- 2% conversion rate
- Average $15 commission

**Top Converting Content:**
1. "Best Bible App" - highest volume
2. App vs App comparisons - decision-ready visitors
3. Individual app reviews - branded search
4. "Best apps for [audience]" - targeted intent

### Optimization Tactics

1. **Comparison tables convert** - Visual, easy to scan
2. **Direct recommendations** - "If you want X, get Y"
3. **Pricing transparency** - Build trust, reduce friction
4. **Regular updates** - Fresh content ranks, converts better

---

## 2. Display Advertising

### Target: $6,000/year ($500/month at scale)

**Timeline:**
- Month 1-3: No ads (build content/traffic)
- Month 4-6: Apply to Ezoic (10k pageviews)
- Month 7-12: Upgrade to Mediavine (50k sessions)

**RPM Expectations (Faith Niche):**
- Ezoic: $10-18 RPM
- Mediavine: $18-30 RPM
- AdThrive: $25-40 RPM

**Faith niche advantage:** Family-friendly content = premium advertisers

### Ad Placement
- In-content ads (non-intrusive)
- Sidebar (desktop)
- No pop-ups (audience expects respectful experience)
- No video autoplay

---

## 3. Sponsored Content

### Target: $3,000/year

**Sponsors to Target:**
- Church management software companies
- Christian publishers
- Faith-based app developers
- Christian conferences/events

**Sponsored Content Types:**
- App reviews (disclosed)
- Feature articles
- Newsletter mentions
- Resource guides

**Rates:**
- Sponsored review: $200-500
- Newsletter mention: $100-200
- Featured placement: $300-600

**Rules:**
- Always disclose sponsorship
- Maintain honest assessment
- Only accept relevant sponsors
- Don't compromise trust for money

## Monetization by Content Type

### "Best Of" Articles
- **Primary:** Affiliate links to all apps
- **Secondary:** Email capture for guide/newsletter
- **Tertiary:** Display ads

### Individual App Reviews
- **Primary:** Affiliate link to reviewed app
- **Secondary:** Comparison link to alternatives
- **Tertiary:** Related product (Bible, journal)

### Comparison Articles
- **Primary:** Affiliate links to both apps
- **Secondary:** "Best for [use case]" recommendations
- **Tertiary:** Email capture

### Guide/How-To Content
- **Primary:** Lead magnet for email list
- **Secondary:** Related app recommendations
- **Tertiary:** Digital product upsell

## Niche-Specific Considerations

### Trust is Everything
- Faith audience values authenticity
- Don't oversell or be salesy
- Recommendations should genuinely help
- Personal faith perspective adds credibility

### Seasonal Opportunities
- **Christmas:** Gift guides, devotionals
- **Easter:** Lent apps, reflection tools
- **New Year:** Bible reading plans
- **Back to school:** Family/kids content

### Denominational Sensitivity
- Note Catholic vs Protestant apps
- Respect different traditions
- Don't alienate audience segments
- Inclusive language where possible
