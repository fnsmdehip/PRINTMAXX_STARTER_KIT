## Disclosure Requirements

### FTC Compliance

Every affiliate page needs:

**Short disclosure (top):**
> This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you.

**Full disclosure (footer):**
> Affiliate Disclosure: FaithAppsGuide.com is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission. We only recommend apps and resources we genuinely believe will help your faith journey. See our full disclosure.

### Faith-Specific Note

Be transparent about:
- Financial relationships with app companies
- Whether you've actually used the apps
- Personal faith perspective (if relevant)

Trust is paramount in faith niche.
