## Application Tips

### For High-Value Programs

1. **Have a real website** - Even a simple one
2. **Show traffic proof** - Analytics screenshots if you have them
3. **Match niche** - Apply to relevant programs
4. **Professional application** - No typos, explain your strategy
5. **Follow up** - If rejected, ask why and reapply

### Getting Approved Faster

- Start with easy-approval programs (ShareASale, FlexOffers)
- Build a track record of sales
- Apply to harder programs with proof of performance
- Some programs have instant approval
- Enterprise programs may require a call
