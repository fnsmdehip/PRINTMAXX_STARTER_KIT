## Niche Selection Framework

### Criteria for Selection

| Factor | Weight | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Commission rates | 30% | % per sale or fixed amount |
| Cookie duration | 15% | Days credit persists |
| Competition level | 25% | SERP difficulty, backlink requirements |
| Search volume | 20% | Monthly searches for core terms |
| Buyer intent | 10% | How close to purchase when searching |

### The Winning Formula

**Best niches combine:**
1. High commissions ($50+ per sale OR 20%+ recurring)
2. Long cookies (30+ days)
3. Specific problems (not generic broad topics)
4. Buyer intent keywords available
5. Evergreen demand (not seasonal only)

## Competition Analysis Framework

### How to Assess Niche Competition

**Step 1: SERP Analysis**
- Search main keywords
- Count how many DR 50+ sites rank
- Check if any sites are pure affiliate

**Step 2: Backlink Requirements**
- Use Ahrefs free tool or similar
- Check backlinks on top 3 results
- Estimate effort to compete

**Step 3: Content Gap Analysis**
- What questions aren't answered well?
- What long-tail terms have weak results?
- What formats are missing (video, tools)?

### Competition Scoring

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1-3 | Low competition | Go aggressive |
| 4-6 | Medium competition | Niche down further |
| 7-8 | High competition | Need unique angle |
| 9-10 | Very high | Avoid or very specific long-tail |

## Niche Validation Checklist

Before committing to a niche:

- [ ] 3+ affiliate programs with 20%+ commission
- [ ] 30+ day cookie on primary program
- [ ] 10+ keywords with 1000+ monthly searches
- [ ] Competition score under 7
- [ ] Not heavily seasonal (or plan for seasonality)
- [ ] Clear buyer intent keywords available
- [ ] Content you can actually create (expertise or can learn)
- [ ] FTC compliance achievable (no risky claims needed)
