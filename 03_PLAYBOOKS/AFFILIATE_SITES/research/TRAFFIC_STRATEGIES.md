## SEO Traffic (Primary Strategy)

### Keyword Research Process

**Step 1: Seed Keywords**
- List 10-20 main topics in your niche
- Use free tools: Google autocomplete, Related searches, People also ask
- Check competitor sites for keyword ideas

**Step 2: Expand with Tools**

| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| Ubersuggest | Free tier | Basic research |
| Ahrefs Free | Free | Keyword difficulty |
| Keywords Everywhere | $10/mo | Browser extension |
| Semrush | Free trial | Competitor analysis |
| Google Keyword Planner | Free | Volume data |

**Step 3: Filter by Intent**

| Intent | Keyword Pattern | Conversion | Competition |
|--------|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| Transactional | "buy X", "best X for Y" | Highest | Highest |
| Commercial | "X vs Y", "X review" | High | Medium-High |
| Informational | "how to X", "what is X" | Low-Medium | Low-Medium |
| Navigational | "brand name" | Low | Low |

**Prioritize:** Commercial intent keywords with medium competition

**Step 4: Create Content Map**

```
Category: [Main Topic]
├── Hub: Best [Topic] Guide (main keyword)
├── Spoke: [Product A] vs [Product B] (comparison)
├── Spoke: [Product] Review (review)
├── Spoke: How to Use [Product] (tutorial)
├── Spoke: [Topic] for Beginners (informational)
└── Spoke: [Specific Use Case] Guide (long-tail)
```

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**Title Tag:**
- [ ] Primary keyword at start
- [ ] Under 60 characters
- [ ] Compelling (include number or power word)

**Meta Description:**
- [ ] Include primary keyword
- [ ] Under 155 characters
- [ ] Clear benefit/CTA

**H1 Tag:**
- [ ] One H1 per page
- [ ] Contains primary keyword
- [ ] Matches search intent

**Content:**
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Related keywords throughout
- [ ] At least 1500 words for pillar content
- [ ] Internal links to related posts
- [ ] External links to authority sources

**Images:**
- [ ] Descriptive file names (keyword-topic.webp)
- [ ] Alt text with keywords
- [ ] Compressed file size

**URL:**
- [ ] Short and descriptive
- [ ] Contains primary keyword
- [ ] No unnecessary words

### Link Building Strategies

**Tier 1: Easy Wins (Do First)**

| Strategy | Difficulty | Time | Links/Month |
|----------|------------|------|-------------|
| Guest posting | Medium | 4-8 hrs/post | 2-4 |
| HARO responses | Easy | 1 hr/day | 1-3 |
| Podcast guesting | Medium | 2-3 hrs/episode | 1-2 |
| Resource page links | Easy | 2-4 hrs | 2-5 |
| Broken link building | Medium | 3-5 hrs | 1-3 |

**Tier 2: Advanced (Once Established)**

| Strategy | Difficulty | Time | Links/Month |
|----------|------------|------|-------------|
| Original research | Hard | 20+ hrs | 5-20 |
| Free tools | Hard | 40+ hrs | 10-50 |
| Infographics | Medium | 10-15 hrs | 3-10 |
| Expert roundups | Medium | 5-10 hrs | 5-15 |

**Guest Posting Process:**

1. Find sites in your niche accepting guest posts
2. Search: "[niche] + write for us" or "[niche] + guest post"
3. Analyze their content style
4. Pitch unique angle (not already covered)
5. Write quality content (not thin)
6. Include 1-2 contextual links to your site
7. Promote the published post

**HARO (Help a Reporter Out) Process:**

1. Sign up at helpareporter.com
2. Receive daily journalist queries
3. Respond to relevant queries quickly
4. Provide expert value
5. Get featured (with link back)

### Content Update Strategy

**Monthly:**
- Check rankings for target keywords
- Update dates (if using "2026" in titles)
- Fix broken internal/external links
- Add new internal links from new content

**Quarterly:**
- Refresh top 10 traffic posts
- Add new sections/information
- Update screenshots/images
- Consolidate underperforming posts

**Annually:**
- Full content audit
- Prune or redirect dead content
- Rewrite posts that dropped in rankings

## Pinterest Traffic

### Why Pinterest for Affiliates

- Visual search engine (not social media)
- Long content lifespan (pins work for months/years)
- High purchase intent audience
- Direct linking to affiliate content allowed
- Less competitive than Google

### Pinterest Setup

**Step 1: Business Account**
- Convert to business account (free)
- Claim your website
- Enable rich pins

**Step 2: Board Strategy**
```
Account: [Your Brand]
├── Board: [Main Category 1] (10+ pins)
├── Board: [Main Category 2] (10+ pins)
├── Board: [Product Reviews] (10+ pins)
├── Board: [How-To Guides] (10+ pins)
└── Board: [Inspiration/Lifestyle] (10+ pins)
```

**Step 3: Pin Design**
- Use Canva (free)
- 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio)
- Bold text overlay
- Bright colors
- Clear value proposition

### Pinterest Posting Strategy

**Volume:**
- 5-15 pins per day
- Mix of fresh pins + repins
- 80% your content, 20% others

**Timing:**
- Schedule with Tailwind ($15/mo) or free schedulers
- Best times: evenings and weekends
- Consistency matters more than perfect timing

**Pin Optimization:**
- Keyword-rich titles
- Detailed descriptions (100-200 words)
- Relevant hashtags (2-5)
- Link to specific blog posts (not homepage)

### Best Niches for Pinterest

| Niche | Pinterest Fit | Notes |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Home decor | Excellent | Visual + high purchase intent |
| Fashion | Excellent | Outfit inspiration |
| Food/Recipes | Excellent | Recipe discovery |
| DIY/Crafts | Excellent | Tutorial content |
| Finance | Good | Infographic style |
| Health/Fitness | Good | Workout + meal pins |
| Travel | Good | Destination content |
| Tech/Software | Poor | Not visual enough |

## YouTube Traffic

### YouTube SEO Fundamentals

**Keyword Research:**
- YouTube autocomplete
- vidIQ or TubeBuddy (free tiers)
- Check competitor videos

**Title Optimization:**
- Keyword at start
- Under 60 characters
- Clear benefit

**Description:**
- First 200 characters most important
- Include affiliate links with disclosure
- Add timestamps
- Include related keywords

**Tags:**
- Primary keyword first
- Related keywords
- Brand names
- Common misspellings

### Video Types for Affiliates

| Type | Length | Purpose | Conversion |
|------|--------|---------|------------|
| Product review | 8-15 min | Single product deep dive | High |
| Comparison | 10-20 min | vs videos | Highest |
| Tutorial | 5-15 min | How to use product | Medium |
| Listicle | 10-15 min | Top 10 videos | Medium |
| Unboxing | 5-10 min | First impressions | Low-Medium |

### Video to Website Strategy

1. Create video covering topic
2. Write matching blog post
3. Link to blog post in description
4. Embed video in blog post
5. Traffic flows both directions

### YouTube Shorts for Discovery

- 60 seconds or less
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Text overlays help
- Link to full video in comments
- Builds subscriber base

## Paid Traffic

### When to Use Paid Ads

**Use paid if:**
- You have validated converting content
- Know your EPC (earnings per click)
- Can afford to test ($500+ budget)
- Need fast scale

**Don't use paid if:**
- No proven conversions yet
- Affiliate commissions are low
- Can't track conversions properly
- Bootstrap budget only

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | Min Budget | Difficulty |
|----------|----------|------------|------------|
| Google Ads | High intent buyers | $500/mo | Medium |
| Facebook/IG | Interest targeting | $300/mo | Medium |
| TikTok | Young demographics | $300/mo | Low |
| Pinterest | Visual products | $200/mo | Low |
| Taboola/Outbrain | Native/content | $500/mo | Medium |

### Google Ads for Affiliates

**Campaign Types:**
- Search ads (best for buyer intent keywords)
- Display ads (retargeting only)
- YouTube ads (brand awareness)

**Important:**
- Some affiliate programs prohibit direct PPC
- Use landing page (bridge page) between ad and affiliate
- Track conversions with proper attribution

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Best for:**
- Visual products
- Impulse purchases
- Building remarketing audiences

**Structure:**
1. Top of funnel: Blog content (informational)
2. Middle of funnel: Comparison content (commercial)
3. Bottom of funnel: Retargeting to visited posts

**Caution:**
- Facebook ad costs rising
- iOS tracking changes hurt attribution
- Test with small budget first

## Social Media Traffic

### Platform Selection

| Platform | Best Content | Affiliate Fit | Effort |
|----------|--------------|---------------|--------|
| X (Twitter) | Tips, threads | Medium | Low |
| Instagram | Visual, stories | Medium | Medium |
| TikTok | Short video | High | Medium |
| LinkedIn | B2B content | Low | Low |
| Facebook Groups | Community | Medium | Medium |

### X (Twitter) Strategy

**Content mix:**
- 40% Value (tips, insights)
- 30% Engagement (questions, opinions)
- 20% Content promotion (blog links)
- 10% Personal/behind-the-scenes

**Posting frequency:**
- 3-5 tweets per day
- 1-2 threads per week
- Reply to relevant conversations

**Link Strategy:**
- Don't link every post (kills engagement)
- Use threads with link at end
- Pin tweet with best content link

### TikTok Strategy

**Content types:**
- Quick tips (60 seconds)
- Product reactions
- Before/after results
- Myth busting
- Behind the scenes

**Posting:**
- 1-3 posts per day
- Test different times
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Strong hook in first second

**Conversion:**
- Link in bio (Linktree or similar)
- Direct to specific blog post
- Build email list from TikTok

## Email Traffic

### Why Build an Email List

- You own the audience
- Direct access (no algorithm)
- High conversion rates
- Repeat traffic on demand
- Can sell directly

### Email Capture Methods

| Method | Conversion Rate | Best Placement |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| Exit intent popup | 2-5% | Sitewide |
| Content upgrade | 5-15% | In relevant posts |
| Homepage signup | 1-3% | Above fold |
| Sidebar widget | 0.5-1% | All pages |
| Floating bar | 0.5-2% | Top/bottom of page |

### Lead Magnet Ideas

**High-converting:**
- Checklist (quick value)
- Template (immediate use)
- Cheat sheet (reference)
- Comparison guide (buying help)
- Email course (5-day)

**Create once, use everywhere:**
- Gate best content
- Offer in content upgrades
- Promote on social
- Use in email sequences

### Email Sequence for Affiliates

**Welcome Sequence (5-7 emails):**
```
Email 1: Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself
Email 2: Share your story/credibility
Email 3: Provide value (tip or tutorial)
Email 4: Soft product mention
Email 5: Case study or results
Email 6: Product recommendation + affiliate link
Email 7: FAQ or objection handling + final CTA
```

**Ongoing Newsletter:**
- Weekly or bi-weekly
- 80% value, 20% promotion
- Link to new blog posts
- Curate helpful content
- Occasional direct affiliate promotions

## Traffic Tracking & Optimization

### Essential Metrics

| Metric | Tool | Target |
|--------|------|--------|
| Sessions | Google Analytics | Growing MoM |
| Bounce rate | Google Analytics | <60% |
| Avg. session duration | Google Analytics | >2 minutes |
| Pages per session | Google Analytics | >1.5 |
| Organic keywords | Search Console | Growing |
| Click-through rate | Search Console | >3% |
| Affiliate clicks | Affiliate dashboard | Correlated with traffic |
| Conversion rate | Affiliate dashboard | >1% |

### Monthly Traffic Review

**Questions to ask:**
1. Which posts drive most traffic?
2. Which posts convert best?
3. What keywords are growing?
4. Where did traffic come from?
5. What content should we create more of?

### A/B Testing Traffic

**Test elements:**
- Title tags (impact CTR)
- Meta descriptions (impact CTR)
- Headlines (impact engagement)
- CTA placement (impact conversions)
- Lead magnets (impact email capture)

**Testing process:**
1. Identify metric to improve
2. Create variation
3. Run for 2+ weeks
4. Measure results
5. Implement winner
