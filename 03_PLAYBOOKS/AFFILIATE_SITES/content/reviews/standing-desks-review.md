## What actually matters in a standing desk

After testing these daily, here's what separates good desks from great ones:

**Stability at standing height.** Cheap desks wobble when you type. Annoying and distracting. Uplift and Jarvis are rock solid. Autonomous wobbles.

**Motor quality.** Dual motors are faster and quieter. All these desks have them except basic Flexispot models.

**Warranty length.** Motors fail. Frames warp. A 15-year warranty means you're covered. Vari's 5 years is too short.

**Height range.** If you're over 6'2", check max height. Autonomous goes highest at 52".

## FAQ

**Is a standing desk worth it?**
Yes, if you alternate between sitting and standing. Standing all day is as bad as sitting all day. The benefit is movement throughout the day.

**How long should you stand?**
Start with 15-30 minutes per hour. Build up over weeks. I do about 4 hours standing, 4 sitting during a workday.

**Do standing desks help with back pain?**
They can, if you alternate positions. Standing desks alone don't fix posture. Movement does.

**Uplift vs Flexispot?**
Uplift is more stable and has better customization. Flexispot is $100-200 cheaper with 90% of the quality. Both are good choices.
