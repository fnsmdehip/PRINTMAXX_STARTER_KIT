**Strategy:**

2. Focus on profession + hobby combinations
3. Use all 5 bullet points for keywords

### Etsy POD Strategy

**Why Etsy + POD Works:**
- 96M+ active buyers seeking unique/custom items
- Personalization = higher perceived value
- Search intent (people searching to buy)
- Less price-sensitive than Amazon

**Winning Etsy POD Formula:**

1. **Niche Selection:** Faith + Fitness + Professions dominate
2. **Personalization:** Name, date, location customization
3. **Bundle Offers:** Sets of 2-4 items increase AOV
4. **SEO Titles:** 140 chars max, front-load keywords
5. **Tags:** All 13 tags, mix broad and specific

**Etsy SEO Structure:**
```
Title: [Primary Keyword] [Personalization] [Product] | [Secondary Keyword] | [Gift Occasion]

```

```
   ├── Check Etsy bestsellers for niche

2. GENERATION (Batch of 20)

3. POST-PROCESSING (Per Design)

5. LISTING OPTIMIZATION

   └── Set competitive pricing
```

## Marketing Playbook

### Organic Traffic Strategy

**1. Etsy SEO (Primary)**
- Title: Front-load main keywords
- Tags: Use all 13, mix broad + specific
- Attributes: Fill out every option
- Photos: 10 images minimum, mockups + lifestyle
- Reviews: Critical for ranking (target 5+ quickly)

**2. Pinterest (Secondary)**
- Create pins for every product
- Rich pins enabled
- Pin to niche-specific boards
- Consistent pinning schedule (5-10/day)
- Link directly to Etsy listings

**3. TikTok (Viral Potential)**
- Design process videos
- "POD Shop Check" format
- Trending sound + product showcase
- Link to TikTok Shop or bio

**4. Instagram (Brand Building)**
- Product flat-lays
- Customer photos (UGC)
- Behind-the-scenes design
- Reels for discovery

### Paid Traffic Strategy

**Etsy Ads (Recommended Start)**
- Budget: $5-10/day per niche
- Let Etsy optimize initially
- Disable non-converting listings after 14 days
- Target 20% ROAS minimum

**TikTok Ads (Scale Method)**
- Creative: UGC-style product videos
- Targeting: Interest + behavior
- Budget: $50-100/day test phase
- Scale winning ads 20% daily

**Google Shopping (Advanced)**
- Requires Shopify store
- Google Merchant Center setup
- Budget: $10-20/day
- Focus on long-tail product keywords

### Launch Strategy (New Design)

**Week 1: Soft Launch**
1. List on Etsy with full SEO optimization
2. Create 3 Pinterest pins
3. Share in relevant Facebook groups
4. Run $5/day Etsy ads

**Week 2: Content Push**
1. TikTok video of design/product
2. Instagram reel with product
3. Pinterest video pin
4. Analyze Etsy stats

**Week 3: Scale or Kill**
- Sales > 3: Increase ad spend, add variations
- Sales 1-2: Adjust listing, test new mockups
- Sales 0: Archive, move to next design
