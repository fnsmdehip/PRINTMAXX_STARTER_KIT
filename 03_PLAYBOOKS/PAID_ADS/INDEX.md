## When to Use Paid Ads

### Use Native Ads When:
- You have proven organic conversion
- You need to scale without account risk
- Your audience is 35+ (native skews older)
- You have content-based funnel (advertorials)
- Budget: $500+/day to scale

### Use Meta Ads When:
- Testing new offers quickly
- Visual product (fashion, beauty, fitness)
- Younger audience (18-34)
- UGC content available
- Budget: Any size, but risky at scale

### Don't Use Paid Ads When:
- No product-market fit yet
- Conversion rate <1%
- LTV < $20 (margins too thin)
- No retargeting in place
