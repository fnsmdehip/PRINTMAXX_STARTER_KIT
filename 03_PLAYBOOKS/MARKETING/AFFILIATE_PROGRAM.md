## Why Build an Affiliate Army

**Affiliate math:**
- 100 affiliates x 5 referrals each x $30/yr = $15,000 ARR
- You pay 30% = $4,500 in commissions
- Net: $10,500 with zero ad spend

**Best for:**
- Subscription products (recurring commissions)
- Products with clear target audience
- Niches with active bloggers/creators

## Commission Structure

### Standard Tiers

| Tier | Commission | Requirement |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Standard | 20% recurring | Sign up |
| Silver | 25% recurring | 10+ referrals |
| Gold | 30% recurring | 50+ referrals |
| Elite | 40% recurring | 200+ referrals or special partners |

### Payment Terms

- **Payout threshold:** $50 minimum
- **Payment schedule:** Monthly, Net 30
- **Cookie duration:** 60-90 days
- **Attribution:** Last click

### What to Offer

**For subscriptions:**
```
Annual plan ($29.99):
- 30% commission = $9/sale
- Recurring for subscription life

Monthly plan ($3.99):
- 30% commission = $1.20/month
- Recurring as long as user stays
```

## Affiliate Types to Recruit

### Tier 1: Niche Bloggers (Highest ROI)

**Why:** High intent traffic, trusted recommendations

**Where to find:**
- Search "[niche] blog" + "[niche] tips"
- Check backlinks of competitors
- Browse niche subreddits for linked blogs

**Outreach:**
```
Subject: Partnership opportunity for [Blog Name]

Hey [Name],

I've been reading [Blog Name] for a while - your post on [specific article] was super helpful.

I built [Product] that helps [audience] with [problem]. Thought it might be a good fit for your readers.

We offer 30% recurring commission on all referrals.

Interested in learning more?

[Name]
```

### Tier 2: YouTube Reviewers

**Why:** Demonstration builds trust, long-tail views

**Where to find:**
- Search "[category] app review"
- Check who reviewed competitors
- Look for channels 10K-100K subscribers

**Outreach:**
```
Subject: Interested in reviewing [Product]?

Hey [Name],

Love your app reviews - especially the [specific video].

I built [Product] that [value prop]. Would love to send you a free premium account and see if it's something you'd want to review.

If you're interested, we also have an affiliate program (30% recurring).

Let me know!

[Name]
```

### Tier 3: Niche Influencers

**Why:** Direct access to target audience

**Where to find:**
- Search hashtags on Instagram/TikTok
- Check who competitors are working with
- Browse niche podcasts

**Outreach:**
```
Hey [Name]!

Saw your content about [topic] - really resonated.

Built [Product] that helps with exactly that. Would love to partner:
- Free premium access
- 30% of every sale you refer

Interested?
```

### Tier 4: Course Creators

**Why:** Built-in audience, high trust

**Where to find:**
- Udemy, Skillshare instructors
- Gumroad sellers in niche
- Cohort course creators

**Offer:**
- Special discount for their students
- Higher commission (35-40%)
- Co-marketing opportunities

## Affiliate Assets

### What to Provide

1. **Unique affiliate link**
   - `yourapp.com/?ref=AFFILIATE123`
   - Tracked automatically

2. **Coupon codes**
   - `AFFILIATE20` for 20% off
   - Tracks back to affiliate

3. **Banner ads**
   - 300x250, 728x90, 160x600
   - Multiple variations

4. **Email swipe copy**
   - 3-5 email templates
   - Easy to customize

5. **Social media posts**
   - Twitter/X threads
   - Instagram captions
   - TikTok scripts

6. **Landing page**
   - Co-branded option
   - Higher conversion

### Asset Examples

**Email Swipe:**

```
Subject: The app that fixed my [problem]

Hey,

I've been using [Product] for [time] and it's been game-changing.

[One sentence about what it does]

[One sentence about your result]

If you want to try it, here's my link for [discount]%: [affiliate link]

[Name]
```

**Tweet Template:**

```
I've been using [Product] for [time period].

Results:
• [Benefit 1]
• [Benefit 2]
• [Benefit 3]

If you struggle with [problem], check it out: [affiliate link]
```

## Legal & Compliance

### Affiliate Agreement (Key Terms)

1. **FTC Disclosure Required**
   - Affiliates must disclose relationship
   - "#ad" or "affiliate link" in posts
   - Clear and conspicuous

2. **Prohibited Activities**
   - No false claims
   - No spam
   - No trademark bidding (PPC)
   - No coupon sites without approval

3. **Commission Terms**
   - 60-day cookie window
   - 30-day refund window
   - Commissions void on refunds
   - Monthly payouts, Net 30

4. **Termination**
   - Either party, 30 days notice
   - Unpaid commissions honored
   - Fraud = immediate termination

### Template Clause

```
AFFILIATE DISCLOSURE:
As an affiliate, you MUST disclose your relationship with [Product]
in all promotional content. This includes but is not limited to:
- Adding "#ad" or "affiliate link" to social posts
- Including "I may earn a commission" in blog posts
- Verbal disclosure in videos

Failure to disclose may result in termination and FTC penalties.
```

## App-Specific: RevenueCat + Branch

### In-App Referrals

**Setup:**
1. Integrate Branch SDK
2. Create referral deeplinks
3. Track attribution in RevenueCat
4. Credit referrer with free month

**User Flow:**
```
User A → Shares referral link → User B clicks →
User B downloads → User B subscribes →
User A gets 1 month free
```

### Commission for Power Users

For users who refer 10+ people:
- Upgrade to formal affiliate
- Offer 20% cash commission
- Provide affiliate dashboard access
