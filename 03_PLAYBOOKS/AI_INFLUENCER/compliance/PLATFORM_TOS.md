# Platform Terms of Service for AI Influencers

Platform-specific rules and best practices for AI-generated content.

---

## General principles across platforms

### What most platforms require:

1. **Transparency** - Don't mislead users about content origin
2. **Authenticity** - Don't impersonate real people
3. **Disclosure** - Label AI/synthetic content appropriately
4. **Compliance** - Follow advertising and promotion rules
5. **Safety** - Don't create harmful or deceptive content

### Universal red lines:

- No impersonation of real people
- No deepfakes intended to deceive
- No synthetic media for fraud/scams
- No manipulation of political content
- No violations of intellectual property

## TikTok

### AI content policy:

TikTok requires disclosure of AI-generated content, particularly:
- Synthetic media (AI avatars, voices)
- AI-generated or AI-edited content that could mislead
- Content that could be mistaken for real events/people

### Disclosure requirements:

- Use TikTok's AI content labels when available
- Include disclosure in bio
- Verbal disclosure in videos recommended

### Branded content:

- Must use branded content toggle for sponsorships
- Must comply with branded content policies
- Disclosure required in addition to toggle

### What's allowed:

- AI avatars (disclosed)
- AI voices (disclosed)
- AI-generated creative content (disclosed)
- AI influencer accounts (disclosed)

### What's not allowed:

- Impersonating real people
- Undisclosed synthetic media
- Misleading content about real events
- Violating community guidelines with AI

### Best practices:

```
[ ] Bio includes "AI" explicitly
[ ] Use AI content label when posting
[ ] Branded content toggle for sponsorships
[ ] Don't mimic real people
[ ] Regular disclosure in content
```

## LinkedIn

### AI content considerations:

LinkedIn has stricter professional standards:
- Personal profiles require real human identity
- Company pages work for AI projects
- Professional community standards apply

### Recommended approach:

**Option 1: Company page**
- Create company page for AI persona
- Clear disclosure in company description
- Post as company, not personal profile

**Option 2: Human operator with AI attribution**
- Human shares AI content
- Clear attribution: "Content created with AI"
- Maintains professional standards

### What's allowed:

- AI-assisted content (disclosed)
- AI personas on company pages
- Transparent AI projects

### What's not allowed:

- Fake human profiles
- Undisclosed AI impersonation
- Misleading professional credentials

### Best practices:

```
[ ] Use company page for AI persona
[ ] Clear disclosure in page description
[ ] Don't create fake human profiles
[ ] Maintain professional standards
[ ] Attribute AI in content
```

## Platform policy monitoring

### How to stay updated:

1. **Follow official channels**
   - Platform creator blogs
   - Policy update announcements
   - Creator newsletters

2. **Set up alerts**
   - Google alerts for "[platform] AI policy"
   - Follow platform help accounts
   - Join creator communities

3. **Regular review**
   - Quarterly policy check
   - Before major campaigns
   - When launching on new platforms

### Policy change response:

1. Read update carefully
2. Assess impact on your account
3. Make required changes immediately
4. Document compliance efforts
5. Monitor for enforcement patterns

## Emergency procedures

### If account flagged/restricted:

1. Don't panic or delete content
2. Review the specific violation
3. Gather compliance documentation
4. Submit appeal with evidence
5. Consult lawyer if needed

### Appeal template:

```
Subject: Appeal for [Account] - AI Content Creator

Account: [Handle]
```
