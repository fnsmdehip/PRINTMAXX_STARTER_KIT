# Disclosure Checklist for AI Influencers

Per-post and periodic compliance verification.

---

## Quick reference: Disclosure decision tree

```
Is content promotional? ──YES──> Disclose sponsorship/affiliate
         │
         NO
         │
         v
Is this a new viewer's first exposure? ──YES──> Ensure AI nature clear
         │
         NO
         │
         v
Has it been 1+ week since AI disclosure? ──YES──> Include AI reminder
         │
         NO
         │
         v
Standard content - proceed with bio disclosure
```

---

## Per-post checklist

### For ALL content:

- [ ] AI disclosure accessible (bio or content)
- [ ] No unsubstantiated claims
- [ ] No fake testimonials
- [ ] Content matches character voice
- [ ] Platform guidelines followed

### For SPONSORED content:

- [ ] Sponsorship disclosed in first 5-10 seconds (video)
- [ ] Sponsorship disclosed before "see more" (caption)
- [ ] Clear language used (ad/sponsored/paid partnership)
- [ ] AI nature disclosed
- [ ] Platform partnership tools used if available
- [ ] Contract requirements met
- [ ] Claims are substantiated
- [ ] Product experience framed appropriately for AI

### For AFFILIATE content:

- [ ] Affiliate relationship disclosed
- [ ] Commission disclosure present
- [ ] Links clearly identified as affiliate
- [ ] AI nature disclosed
- [ ] Product claims substantiated
- [ ] Honest assessment (pros AND cons)
- [ ] No misleading income claims

### For PRODUCT REVIEWS:

- [ ] Source of product disclosed (bought/gifted/sent)
- [ ] Affiliate status disclosed if applicable
- [ ] AI nature disclosed
- [ ] Honest assessment provided
- [ ] Claims verifiable
- [ ] Not misleading about personal use (AI doesn't "use" products)

## Disclosure language library

### Sponsorship disclosures:

**Video (verbal):**
- "This video is sponsored by [Brand]."
- "Thanks to [Brand] for sponsoring this video."
- "[Brand] paid me to create this content."

**Caption:**
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" at beginning
- "Paid partnership with [Brand]"
- "Sponsored by [Brand]"

### Affiliate disclosures:

**Video (verbal):**
- "Links in my bio are affiliate links. I earn commission if you purchase."
- "Quick disclosure: I make a small commission if you buy through my links."

**Caption:**
- "Affiliate link - I earn commission on purchases"
- "Contains affiliate links"
- "#affiliate"

### AI disclosures:

**Bio:**
- "AI-generated content creator"
- "[Name] is an AI"
- "AI-powered [role]"

**In content:**
- "I'm [Name], an AI..."
- "As an AI, I..."
- "Quick reminder: I'm an AI - avatar, voice, all AI-generated."

### Combined disclosures:

**Sponsored + AI:**
"This is sponsored by [Brand]. Also, I'm [Name], an AI - just want to be transparent about both!"

**Affiliate + AI:**
"Affiliate links below - I earn commission. And as an AI, my recommendations are based on research, not personal use."

## Weekly compliance audit

### Every week, verify:

**Bio compliance:**
- [ ] AI disclosure present in all platform bios
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure present if applicable
- [ ] Contact information for partnerships
- [ ] Links working

**Content compliance (review last 7 days):**
- [ ] All sponsored content properly disclosed
- [ ] All affiliate content properly disclosed
- [ ] No unsubstantiated claims made
- [ ] AI nature reminder included at least once

**Platform features:**
- [ ] Partnership labels used where applicable
- [ ] AI content labels used where applicable
- [ ] Content meets platform guidelines

## Common compliance failures

### Red flags to catch:

**Disclosure issues:**
- [ ] Disclosure at end of content (too late)
- [ ] Disclosure in small/unreadable text
- [ ] Disclosure using unclear language
- [ ] Missing disclosure entirely
- [ ] AI nature hidden or obscured

**Claim issues:**
- [ ] Unverifiable product claims
- [ ] Implied personal experience (AI can't have)
- [ ] Health claims without disclaimers
- [ ] Income claims without disclaimers
- [ ] Fake testimonials or reviews

**Relationship issues:**
- [ ] Undisclosed material connections
- [ ] Unclear sponsorship status
- [ ] Hidden affiliate relationships
- [ ] Gifted products not disclosed

## Emergency compliance fixes

### If you missed a disclosure:

1. Add disclosure immediately (edit if possible)
2. If can't edit, add comment/reply with disclosure
3. Document the error and correction
4. Review process to prevent repeat

### If claim was unsubstantiated:

1. Remove or correct claim
2. Document the error
3. If can't remove, add disclaimer
4. Review claim verification process

### If FTC contacts you:

1. Don't panic
2. Don't delete content
3. Contact lawyer immediately
4. Gather documentation
5. Respond promptly and honestly
