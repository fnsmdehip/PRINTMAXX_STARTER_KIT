## Copyright & Legal Landscape

### Key Legal Reality
- Copyright depends on **human authorship** (US, Canada, UK, EU)
- Suno's commercial use license =/= copyright ownership
- Fully AI-generated works (zero human creative input) may not be copyrightable
- This means: You can earn streaming royalties but may not be able to register with PROs or collect publishing royalties
- Adding human creative elements (lyrics, arrangement, mixing) strengthens copyright claims

### Risk Areas
- Voice cloning of specific artists = high legal risk
- Using copyrighted material as AI training prompts = grey area
- "Style" mimicry (genre, not specific artist) = generally safer
- Platform policies evolving faster than law
