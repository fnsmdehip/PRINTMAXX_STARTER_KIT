### All Roads Lead to Owned Assets

```
Social followers → Newsletter subscribers → Product buyers → App users
     (rented)          (owned)              (revenue)       (recurring revenue)

GOAL: Convert rented audience (social) into owned audience (email)
      into recurring revenue (apps + subscriptions)
```

**The flywheel:**
1. Content farm accounts cast the widest net (high volume, low effort)
2. AI personas convert casual followers into engaged community
3. Newsletters convert engaged followers into email subscribers (owned)
4. Products convert subscribers into customers
5. Revenue reinvests into better tools, more content, wider reach
6. Cycle repeats and compounds
