### Content Boundaries

**Allowed:**
- General exercise education and form guidance
- Evidence-based nutrition information with citations
- Supplement analysis based on published research
- Motivational content
- Community Q&A with proper disclaimers

**Prohibited:**
- Specific medical advice ("do this exercise for your knee injury")
- Promising specific results ("lose 20 lbs in 30 days")
- Promoting dangerous diets (extremely low calorie, elimination without medical supervision)
- Steroid/PED discussion or promotion
- Body shaming or negative comparison content
- Before/after photos with implied guarantees
- Claiming to be a real person or certified professional
