# AI Influencer System

Complete system for running disclosed AI influencer accounts across platforms.

---

## Quick start

### 1. Design your character
Start with `/setup/CHARACTER_DESIGN.md` to define:
- Niche and audience
- Character archetype
- Voice and personality
- Visual identity

### 2. Create your avatar
Use `/setup/AVATAR_CREATION.md` for:
- Static images (Midjourney, Leonardo.ai)
- Video avatars (HeyGen, D-ID)
- Voice (ElevenLabs, PlayHT)

### 3. Set up platforms
Follow `/setup/PLATFORM_SETUP.md` for:
- Account creation with proper disclosure
- Bio templates
- Platform-specific requirements

### 4. Plan content
Use `/content/CONTENT_STRATEGY.md` and `/content/SCRIPT_TEMPLATES/` for:
- Content pillars
- Posting schedule
- Script frameworks

### 5. Launch and monetize
Explore `/monetization/` for:
- Affiliate integration
- Subscriptions
- Digital products
- Brand deals

## Core principles

### 1. Full transparency
AI nature is disclosed clearly, consistently, and without apology.

### 2. Compliance first
FTC requirements and platform TOS are non-negotiable foundations.

### 3. Value-driven content
90% value, 10% promotion. Audience trust comes from helping, not selling.

### 4. Lean into AI identity
Being AI is a feature, not a bug. Use it as a strength.

### 5. Human-in-the-loop
Human oversight for quality, safety, and compliance. AI execution for consistency and scale.

## Cluely case study analysis

### What successful disclosed AI influencers do right:

1. **Lead with transparency** - AI nature front and center
2. **Find the AI advantage** - Consistency, research synthesis, availability
3. **Clear value proposition** - Specific value, not human pretense
4. **Quality production** - AI doesn't mean low quality
5. **Engaged community** - Real community through consistent value

### Key lesson:
Don't apologize for being AI. Own it as a feature.

## Common mistakes to avoid

1. **Hiding AI nature** - Backfires when discovered
2. **Over-promising** - Be honest about limits
3. **Inconsistent voice** - Use voice guidelines
4. **Neglecting compliance** - FTC rules are foundational
5. **Copying human influencers** - Find AI's unique strengths
6. **Rushing monetization** - Build trust first
7. **Ignoring engagement** - Community still matters
