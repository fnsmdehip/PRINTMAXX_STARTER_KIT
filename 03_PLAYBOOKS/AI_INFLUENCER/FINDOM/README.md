## Compliance Requirements

### Legal
- All participants must be adults (verify)
- No coercion (consensual dynamic)
- Clear ToS for transactions
- Tax reporting on income

### Platform ToS
- Read each platform's rules carefully
- Some explicitly allow, others gray area
- Have backup accounts

### AI Disclosure
- Varies by jurisdiction
- Some platforms require disclosure
- Consider implications
