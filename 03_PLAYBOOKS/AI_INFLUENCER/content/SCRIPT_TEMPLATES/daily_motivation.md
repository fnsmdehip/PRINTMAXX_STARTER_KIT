# Daily Motivation Script Template

Short-form motivational content that drives engagement.

---

## Format specs

- Length: 15-60 seconds
- Hook: First 3 seconds
- Message: Single focus
- CTA: Soft engagement prompt

---

## Template 1: The reframe

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"You didn't fail. Here's what actually happened."

BODY (15-30 sec):
"[Reframe the common negative thought]

When [relatable struggle happens], you think [common negative interpretation].

But what if [positive reframe]?

[Specific evidence or logic supporting reframe]

[One actionable shift]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Save this for the days you need it."
```

**Example:**
```
"You didn't fail. Here's what actually happened.

You showed up. You tried. It didn't work this time.

That's not failure. That's data.

Now you know one approach that doesn't fit your situation. You're closer, not further.

Today, try again with what you learned."

"Save this for the days you need it."
```

## Template 2: The reminder

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Your reminder for today:"

BODY (10-20 sec):
"[Single powerful statement]

[Brief expansion - why this matters]

[Make it personal to the viewer]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Comment [word] if you needed to hear this."
```

**Example:**
```
"Your reminder for today:

Progress isn't always visible.

The work you're doing right now? It's compounding. Seeds planted underground don't look like anything until they break through.

Keep going. Even when you can't see it working."

"Comment 'growing' if you needed to hear this."
```

## Template 3: The permission

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Permission to [thing they feel guilty about]."

BODY (15-25 sec):
"You're allowed to [specific permission].

[Why they might feel guilty or hesitant]

But [why it's actually okay/beneficial].

[The reframe]

[Brief encouragement]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Share this with someone who needs permission too."
```

**Example:**
```
"Permission to rest today.

You might feel like you haven't earned it. Like there's more to do.

But rest isn't a reward. It's a requirement.

Pushing through exhaustion doesn't make you stronger. It makes you slower.

Take the rest. Come back better."

"Share this with someone who needs permission too."
```

## Template 4: The truth bomb

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Nobody tells you this about [topic]:"

BODY (20-40 sec):
"[Uncomfortable truth]

[Why people avoid saying it]

[Why it matters anyway]

[How to actually apply this truth]

[Quick encouragement]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Follow for more real talk."
```

**Example:**
```
"Nobody tells you this about motivation:

It's not coming.

Waiting to feel motivated is why things stay undone. Motivation follows action, not the other way around.

Start before you're ready. Feel motivated after you begin.

Just start. The feeling will catch up."

"Follow for more real talk."
```

---

## Template 5: The challenge

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Do this for the next [timeframe]:"

BODY (20-35 sec):
"[Specific, simple action]

Not [common overcomplicated version].

Just [the simple version].

[Why this works]

[What to expect]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Comment 'in' if you're starting today."
```

**Example:**
```
"Do this for the next 7 days:

Write down one thing you did well. Every night. Just one.

Not a journal. Not a gratitude list. One thing you did well.

It rewires how you see yourself. Progress you would have forgotten becomes undeniable.

By day 7, you'll have proof that you're further than you think."

"Comment 'in' if you're starting today."
```

## Template 6: The affirmation

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Say this until you believe it:"

BODY (15-25 sec):
"[Affirmation statement]

[Say it again, slightly different]

[Why this matters]

[Brief reinforcement]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Save and repeat daily."
```

**Example:**
```
"Say this until you believe it:

'I am building something that takes time.'

My timeline is mine. Comparison is distraction.

I am exactly where I'm supposed to be on the path I'm walking.

Today, I focus on the next step. That's enough."

"Save and repeat daily."
```

## Production notes

### Visual recommendations:
- Face-to-camera delivery
- Calm, steady eye contact
- Minimal background distraction
- Consistent lighting

### Audio recommendations:
- Clear, measured pace
- Slight emphasis on key words
- Pause after hook
- Lower energy than typical content (sincerity over hype)

### Posting recommendations:
- Best times: Morning (6-9 AM), evening (8-10 PM)
- Pair with relatable caption
- Use trending audio sparingly (message should stand alone)

## Customization guide

### For different niches:

**Fitness:**
- Reframe: "You didn't skip the gym. You rested."
- Permission: "Permission to do half the workout."
- Truth: "Nobody tells you soreness isn't progress."

**Faith:**
- Reframe: "You didn't lose faith. You're being refined."
- Reminder: "You are never too far."
- Permission: "Permission to bring your doubts to prayer."

**Productivity:**
- Reframe: "An unproductive day isn't a waste."
- Truth: "Nobody tells you busy isn't productive."
- Challenge: "Do one thing first. Before email."

**Business:**
- Reframe: "That failure was market research."
- Permission: "Permission to start ugly."
- Truth: "Nobody tells you most success is boring."
