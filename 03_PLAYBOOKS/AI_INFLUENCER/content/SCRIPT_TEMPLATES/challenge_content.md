# Challenge Content Script Template

Creating and promoting challenges that build community and drive engagement.

---

## Format specs

- Length: 30-60 seconds for launch, updates shorter
- Structure: Challenge > Rules > Benefits > CTA
- Duration: 7, 14, 21, or 30 days work best
- Follow-up: Daily/weekly check-in content

---

## Template 1: Challenge launch

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Join me for the [X]-day [Name] challenge:"

WHAT (15-20 sec):
"For the next [timeframe], we're going to [specific action].

Here's exactly what you'll do:
[Clear, simple daily requirement]"

WHY (15-20 sec):
"Why this works:
[Benefit 1]
[Benefit 2]
[Benefit 3]"

HOW (10-15 sec):
"How to join:
1. [Step - usually follow + engage]
2. [Step - daily action]
3. [Step - share progress]"

CTA (5-10 sec):
"Comment '[keyword]' to commit publicly.
Starting [date]. Let's go."
```

**Example:**
```
"Join me for the 7-day Focus challenge:

For the next 7 days, we're going to do one focused work session before checking any notifications.

Here's exactly what you'll do:
Wake up. Work on one important task for 30 minutes. Then check your phone.

Why this works:
Your morning attention is peak quality.
Starting with your priority sets the day's tone.
7 days is long enough to feel the difference.

How to join:
1. Follow for daily reminders
2. Complete the session each morning
3. Comment your progress daily

Comment 'FOCUS' to commit publicly.
Starting Monday. Let's go."
```

## Template 2: Challenge rules and expectations

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"[Challenge name] rules you need to know:"

RULES (30-50 sec):
"Rule 1: [Requirement]
[Why this rule exists]

Rule 2: [Requirement]
[Why this rule exists]

Rule 3: [Flexibility/grace rule]
[How to handle imperfect days]"

EXPECTATIONS (15-20 sec):
"What to expect:
Day 1-3: [Common experience]
Day 4-7: [Common experience]
By end: [Expected outcome]"

SUPPORT (10 sec):
"How to get help:
[Where to ask questions/find community]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Tag someone who needs this challenge."
```

## Template 3: Daily check-in

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Day [X] of the [Challenge name]:"

PROMPT (15-25 sec):
"Today's focus:
[Specific element or variation]

Quick tip:
[Relevant advice for this stage]"

ENGAGEMENT (10-15 sec):
"Check-in question:
[Question to prompt comments]"

ENCOURAGEMENT (10 sec):
"Remember: [Motivational note relevant to current stage]"

CTA (5 sec):
"Comment your update. See you tomorrow."
```

**Example:**
```
"Day 4 of the Focus challenge:

Today's focus:
You might notice resistance building. That's normal. The habit isn't automatic yet.

Quick tip:
Make it easier. Set your phone in another room tonight. Remove the option.

Check-in question:
What's your biggest distraction trying to pull you away?

Remember: You're halfway through the hardest part. Days 1-4 are where most people quit. You haven't."

"Comment your update. See you tomorrow."
```

```
"What I'm seeing:
[Observation from community]

Common breakthrough at this stage:
[Expected progress]

What separates finishers:
[Key behavior]"

"The back half:
[What to focus on for remaining days]"

"Comment where you're at."
```

## Template 5: Challenge completion celebration

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"You finished [Challenge]. Here's what that means:"

CELEBRATION (15-20 sec):
"[X] days of [action].
That's not nothing. That's a pattern.

[Specific acknowledgment of effort]"

RESULTS (20-30 sec):
"What you've built:
[Outcome 1]
[Outcome 2]
[Outcome 3]

What participants are reporting:
[Common results or testimonials]"

NEXT STEPS (15-20 sec):
"Where to go from here:
Option 1: [Continue the habit]
Option 2: [Level up the challenge]
Option 3: [Apply to new area]"

CTA (10 sec):
"Comment your biggest win from these [X] days.
Next challenge announced [date]."
```

## Template 6: Challenge failure recovery

```
HOOK (3 sec):
"Fell off the challenge? Read this:"

NORMALIZE (15-20 sec):
"[X]% of people don't complete challenges perfectly.
Missing a day doesn't erase the days you showed up.

[Specific encouragement]"

OPTIONS (20-30 sec):
"You have choices:

Option 1: Pick up where you left off
[How to restart]

Option 2: Modify the challenge
[Easier version]

Option 3: Wait for next round
[When it starts again]"

REFRAME (10-15 sec):
"The goal isn't perfection.
The goal is [actual benefit].
Even partial participation gets you [result]."

CTA (5 sec):
"Coming back? Comment 'back' and start tomorrow."
```

## Challenge design principles

### Good challenges:
- Simple daily action (under 15 minutes)
- Clear success criteria
- Community element
- Achievable by beginners
- Meaningful outcome

### Duration guidelines:
- 7 days: Habit introduction
- 14 days: Habit building
- 21 days: Habit solidification
- 30 days: Transformation

### Difficulty curve:
- Days 1-3: Easy wins, build momentum
- Days 4-7: Core challenge, most quit here
- Days 8-14: Adaptation phase
- Days 15+: Maintenance and deepening

## Community building during challenges

### Engagement tactics:
- Daily check-in posts
- Progress sharing prompts
- Buddy system (pair participants)
- Live Q&A sessions
- Participant spotlights

### Recognition:
- Acknowledge consistent participants
- Celebrate completions publicly
- Create "finisher" badge/graphic
- Share success stories (with permission)

### Support:
- FAQ content for common struggles
- Modification options
- Grace for missed days
- Recovery paths for dropouts

---

## Tracking challenge success

### Metrics to monitor:
- Signup rate (comments with keyword)
- Daily participation rate
- Completion rate
- Community engagement
- Follower growth during challenge

### Post-challenge:
- Survey participants
- Collect testimonials
- Document what worked
- Plan improvements for next round
