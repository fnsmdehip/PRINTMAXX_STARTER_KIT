# Product Recommendation Script Template

Compliant affiliate and sponsored content scripts.

---

## Compliance requirements

Every product recommendation MUST include:
1. Clear affiliate/sponsorship disclosure
2. AI persona disclosure (if not prominently in bio)
3. Honest assessment (pros AND cons)
4. No unsubstantiated claims

---

## Format specs

- Length: 30-90 seconds
- Disclosure: First 5-10 seconds
- Structure: Problem > Solution > How > Disclosure > CTA
- Tone: Helpful, not salesy

---

## Template 1: The problem-solution review

```
DISCLOSURE (5 sec):
"Quick note: This contains an affiliate link in my bio. I earn commission if you purchase."

HOOK (3 sec):
"If you struggle with [problem], this might help."

BODY (40-60 sec):
"[Product] does [primary function].

What I like:
- [Pro 1]
- [Pro 2]
- [Pro 3]

What could be better:
- [Con 1]
- [Con 2]

Who it's for:
[Ideal user description]

Who should skip it:
[Not right for description]"

CTA (10 sec):
"Link in bio if you want to check it out. No pressure - just sharing what works."
```

**Example:**
```
"Quick note: This is an affiliate link. I earn commission if you purchase.

If you struggle with morning focus, this might help.

A sunrise alarm clock wakes you with gradual light instead of jarring sound.

What I like:
- You wake up more naturally
- No startling alarm anxiety
- Most have sunset mode for sleep

What could be better:
- Takes a few days to adjust
- Needs to be positioned right
- Not effective if room has blackout curtains

Who it's for:
People who feel groggy despite sleeping enough.

Who should skip it:
If you wake fine with regular alarms, you don't need this."

"Link in bio if you want to check it out. No pressure - just sharing what works."
```

## Disclosure placement rules

### Video content:
- Verbal disclosure in first 10 seconds
- Text overlay during disclosure
- Repeat in description

### Caption only:
- First line: #ad or #sponsored or #affiliate
- Clear language in first paragraph
- Don't bury after "see more"

### Stories:
- "Paid partnership" label if available
- Text disclosure visible in frame
- Verbal mention if audio

---

## Language to use

**Good disclosure phrases:**
- "This contains affiliate links"
- "I earn commission if you purchase"
- "This is sponsored by [Brand]"
- "Paid partnership with [Brand]"
- "[Brand] sent this for review"

**Don't use:**
- Ambiguous: "Thanks to [Brand]" (sounds like gratitude, not disclosure)
- Buried: Disclosure only in last second
- Symbolic only: Just #ad with no context

---

## Honesty guidelines

### Always include:
- At least one genuine limitation
- Who the product ISN'T for
- Your actual assessment, not marketing copy

### Never claim:
- Results you can't substantiate
- Medical/health benefits without evidence
- Guaranteed outcomes
- That you use something you don't

### If you haven't used it:
- Say so clearly: "I haven't tested this myself, but based on reviews..."
- Or don't recommend it

---

## FTC compliance checklist per post

- [ ] Disclosure is clear and conspicuous
- [ ] Disclosure is near the beginning
- [ ] Relationship clearly stated (affiliate/sponsored/gifted)
- [ ] Claims are substantiated
- [ ] Not misleading about experience with product
- [ ] AI nature disclosed (bio or in content)
- [ ] Pros AND cons included
- [ ] Not targeting vulnerable audiences with pressure tactics
