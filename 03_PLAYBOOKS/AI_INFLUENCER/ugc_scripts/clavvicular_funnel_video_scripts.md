## Production Notes

### For TikTok/Reels slideshow:
- Use CapCut or Remotion for text animations
- Keep transitions fast (0.3-0.5 sec)
- Text should be readable on mobile (large, high contrast)
- Audio should match energy of content

### For YouTube Shorts talking head:
- HeyGen or D-ID for AI avatar
- Keep background simple (blur or solid color)
- Direct eye contact essential
- Speaking pace: slightly fast, urgent
- Add captions (80% of viewers watch muted)

### For LinkedIn carousel:
- Canva or Figma for slides
- Consistent color scheme across all slides
- Text size minimum 24pt for mobile
- No more than 6 bullet points per slide
- First slide must hook (visible in feed)
- Last slide MUST have clear CTA

### Cross-posting notes:
- TikTok slideshow can repost to Reels and Shorts
- YouTube Shorts can repost to TikTok (adjust aspect ratio)
- LinkedIn carousel can adapt to Instagram carousel
- All content links back to same Gumroad PDF
