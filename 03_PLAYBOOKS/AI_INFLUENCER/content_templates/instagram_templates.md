# Instagram content templates

Carousel templates, Reel scripts, and Story templates for all niches.

---

## Carousel templates

### Structure: The 10-slide formula

```
Slide 1: Hook (stops the scroll)
Slide 2-8: Value delivery (one point per slide)
Slide 9: Summary/takeaway
Slide 10: CTA + disclosure
```

### Design principles

- Consistent visual style across slides
- Large, readable text (40+ point minimum)
- One key point per slide
- Brand colors maintained
- Character avatar on first and last slide

- Rotate to avoid shadowban

## Disclosure placement

### Reels

- Verbal disclosure in first 10 seconds for sponsored
- Caption disclosure always
- "AI-generated content" in caption

### Carousels

- Last slide includes disclosure
- Caption includes disclosure line

### Stories

- For sponsored: "Paid partnership" label
- Periodic story: "Reminder: I'm [Name], an AI"

### Caption format

```
---
AI [niche] content | [Additional context]
[Hashtags]
```

Or for sponsored:

```
---

AI [niche] content | Not [medical/financial/spiritual] advice
```
