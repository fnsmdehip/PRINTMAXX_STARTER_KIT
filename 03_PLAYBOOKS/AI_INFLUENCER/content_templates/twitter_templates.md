# Twitter/X content templates

20 tweet templates per niche, hook formulas, and thread structures.

---

## Hook formulas (all niches)

### Pattern interrupts

- "[Unpopular opinion about common belief]"
- "Stop [common action everyone does]"
- "Most [audience] get [thing] wrong"
- "[Number] years of [activity] taught me [lesson]"

### Curiosity gaps

- "The [thing] nobody talks about:"
- "What I wish I knew about [topic] sooner:"
- "The real reason [thing] isn't working:"
- "Here's why [common approach] fails:"

### Direct value

- "[Number] [things] that actually work:"
- "How to [desired outcome] in [timeframe]:"
- "The only [thing] you need to [outcome]:"
- "[Thing] explained in [number] seconds:"

### Contrarian takes

- "[Thing] is overrated. Here's what works better:"
- "You don't need [common advice]. You need [alternative]:"
- "[Popular method] is dead. Try this instead:"

### Engagement bait (ethical)

- "What's your [relevant thing]?"
- "Reply with your [thing], I'll [value offer]"
- "Am I wrong for thinking [take]?"
- "Which do you prefer: [A] or [B]?"

## Thread structures

### The "lessons learned" thread

```
Tweet 1: "I [studied/researched/analyzed] [topic] for [time].

Here's what actually works:

(thread)"

Tweet 2-7: One lesson per tweet. Short. Specific.

Tweet 8: Summary + CTA to follow/save

Final tweet: Disclosure if needed
```

### The "myth vs reality" thread

```
Tweet 1: "[Number] [topic] myths that need to die:

(thread)"

Tweet 2-7: "Myth: [common belief]
Reality: [what's actually true]"

Tweet 8: "Save this. Share with someone who needs it."
```

### The "step by step" thread

```
Tweet 1: "How to [desired outcome] in [number] steps:

(thread)"

Tweet 2-7: One step per tweet with brief explanation

Tweet 8: "That's it. Simple beats complex. Start today."
```

### The "honest review" thread

```
Tweet 1: "I [tested/researched/analyzed] [products] for [time].

Honest ranking:

(thread)"

Tweet 2-6: One product per tweet with pros/cons

Tweet 7: "Summary: [winner] for [use case]"

Tweet 8: "Links in bio. (Affiliate - I earn commission on some. I'm also an AI.)"
```

## Posting schedule

### Optimal frequency

- 5-10 tweets daily
- 1-2 threads per week
- Engagement replies throughout day

### Time slots (test and adjust)

- Early morning: 6-8am
- Midday: 11am-1pm
- Evening: 5-7pm
- Late night: 9-11pm (depending on audience)

### Content mix daily

- 3-4 value tweets
- 1-2 engagement tweets
- 1-2 replies/quote tweets
- 0-1 promotional (max 10% of content)
