## Executive Summary

This guide consolidates ALL platforms, tools, and monetization channels for AI influencer operations. Entry cost is $40/mo. Revenue timeline is 3-6 months to meaningful income. The space has matured with clear playbooks for each platform type.

**Core Insight:** The AI influencer ecosystem splits into three categories:
1. **Character Creation Platforms** - Where AI personas live and interact (Character.ai, Replika)
2. **Content Generation Tools** - How AI visuals/voice/video are created (HeyGen, ElevenLabs)
3. **Monetization Channels** - Where revenue comes from (Patreon, brand deals, affiliate)

Most profitable path: Use content generation tools to create AI persona, distribute on social platforms, monetize through subscriptions + affiliate + brand deals.

## PART 2: AI CONTENT GENERATION TOOLS

The core stack for creating AI influencer content.

### IMAGE GENERATION

#### Leonardo.ai (RECOMMENDED FOR CONSISTENCY)

**What It Is:** AI image generation with character consistency features
**Cost:** $12/mo (Apprentice) to $48/mo (Artisan)
**Best For:** Maintaining consistent AI persona across all images

**Why It's Best:**
- Train custom models on 10-20 images
- Generate consistent character across poses/settings
- Better for photorealistic than Midjourney
- API access for automation

**Workflow:**
1. Generate initial character design
2. Create 10-20 reference images
3. Train character model
4. Use model for all future generations
5. Build expression and pose library

**Tips:**
- Lock face seed for consistency
- Build outfit library
- Create setting templates
- Batch generate for efficiency

---

## PART 6: COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

### Double Disclosure Requirement

AI influencers need TWO disclosures on monetized content:

1. **AI Nature Disclosure:**
   - Bio: "I'm an AI [role]"
   - Videos: Verbal mention + visual indicator
   - Captions: "#AIcreator" or "AI-generated content"

2. **Sponsorship/Affiliate Disclosure:**
   - "#ad" or "#sponsored" (sponsored posts)
   - "#affiliate" or "Affiliate link" (affiliate content)
   - Verbal disclosure in videos
   - Before the affiliate link, not after

### Combined Disclosure Examples

**Video Intro:**
"This video is sponsored by [Brand]. Quick reminder: I'm [Name], an AI, so my avatar and voice are AI-generated. Now let me tell you about [product]..."

**Caption Format:**
```
#ad #sponsored #AIcreator

[Content]

---
AI-generated content. Paid partnership with [Brand].
```

**Bio Format:**
```
I'm an AI [role] | Links may be affiliate | Partnering with [Brand]
```

### NY Law (June 2026)

- AI disclosure required for ads
- Must be conspicuous
- Video disclosure must be spoken (not just caption)
- Applies to content targeting NY audiences

### FTC Requirements

- Disclosure before commercial content
- Same medium (video = verbal)
- Clear and conspicuous
- Understandable to audience
- No buried disclosures
