# VA cold calling script: AI content services for local businesses

For VAs calling local businesses (restaurants, gyms, salons, dentists, etc.) to sell AI content creation and social media management.

---

## Pre-call prep

Before calling:
1. Check their Google Business profile (reviews, photos, posts)
2. Check their social media (what platform, posting frequency)
3. Note 1-2 specific observations to reference
4. Know their industry and typical pain points

---

## The call script

### Opener (10 seconds)

```
Hi, is this {{firstName}}?

Hey {{firstName}}, this is {{vaName}} with [Company Name].

I'll be quick. I'm calling because I noticed {{company}} has a solid Google profile but you're not posting much on social media. Is that something you've been meaning to get to?
```

**If gatekeeper:**
```
Hi, I'm looking for the owner or whoever handles marketing for {{company}}. Is that {{firstName}}?
```

**If asked "What's this about?":**
```
We help local businesses like {{company}} with social media content. I noticed you're not posting consistently and wanted to see if that's something you'd want help with. Takes 2 minutes.
```

---

### Qualifying questions (30 seconds)

Ask 2-3 of these:

```
1. "How are you currently handling social media for {{company}}? Do you do it yourself or does someone on your team handle it?"

2. "How much time per week would you say you spend on content? Posting, photos, responding to comments?"

3. "Where are most of your new customers coming from right now? Google, word of mouth, social?"

4. "Have you tried running ads on Facebook or Instagram?"

5. "What's your biggest frustration with marketing right now?"
```

**Listen for pain signals:**
- "I don't have time for it"
- "I tried but it didn't work"
- "I know I should be posting more"
- "We had someone but they left"
- "It's too expensive to hire someone"

---

### Value pitch (30 seconds)

```
Got it. Here's why I'm calling:

We help businesses like {{company}} post on social media without you lifting a finger.

Here's how it works:
- We create 20-30 posts per month for you using AI
- We post across Instagram, Facebook, and Google Business
- Everything matches your brand and voice
- You approve before anything goes live

Our clients typically see a 40-60% increase in social media engagement and 15-20 new customer inquiries per month.

Cost is $500-$1,000 per month depending on what you need. Way less than hiring someone.
```

---

### Objection handling

**"I'm not interested"**
```
Totally understand. Quick question though: are you getting new customers from social media right now? [If no:] That's exactly why I called. Your competitors are, and we can fix that in 30 days. Can I send you a quick example of what we'd do for {{company}}?
```

**"I don't have the budget"**
```
I hear you. What are you spending on marketing right now? [Listen] Our plans start at $500/month. If we bring in even 5 new customers, that pays for itself. And you save the 5-10 hours per week you'd spend doing it yourself.
```

**"I already have someone"**
```
That's great. How's it going? Are you seeing results? [Listen] We work with a lot of businesses that had an agency or freelancer but weren't seeing ROI. Happy to do a free audit of your current setup to see if there are opportunities you're missing. No obligation.
```

**"Can you email me?"**
```
Absolutely. What's the best email? [Get email] I'll send over a quick overview and some examples from businesses similar to yours. Is there a good time to follow up? I want to make sure it doesn't get buried in your inbox.
```

**"I tried social media and it didn't work"**
```
What did you try? [Listen] Most businesses fail at social media because they post randomly without a strategy. We use data to figure out what content your audience wants, then create and post it consistently. That's the difference between random posting and a system. Can I show you what that looks like?
```

**"How is AI content any good?"**
```
Great question. The AI creates the first draft, but a real person reviews and edits everything before it goes out. Nothing posts without your approval. The AI just makes it faster and cheaper so you get 30 posts a month instead of 3.
```

---

### Close (15 seconds)

**Option A: Meeting close**
```
Here's what I suggest: let me put together a free content mockup for {{company}}. I'll show you exactly what 30 days of posts would look like. No cost, no obligation. Takes me about an hour.

Can we schedule 15 minutes later this week to go over it? What day works better, {{dayOption1}} or {{dayOption2}}?
```

**Option B: Trial close**
```
Tell you what. Let us do a free one-week trial. We'll create and post content for {{company}} for 7 days. If you like it, we'll talk about continuing. If not, no hard feelings.

Sound fair?
```

**Option C: Email close (if they won't commit to a call)**
```
I understand. Let me send you a quick email with:
- 3 sample posts we'd create for {{company}}
- a case study from a similar business
- our pricing

What's your email? And can I follow up next {{day}} to see if you had a chance to look at it?
```

## Call metrics targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Calls per hour | 15-20 |
| Connect rate | 25-35% |
| Conversation rate (past gatekeeper) | 60-70% |
| Interest rate (want more info) | 15-25% |
| Meeting booked rate | 5-10% |
| Voicemails per hour | 10-15 |

## Best calling times

- Local businesses: 10am-12pm and 2-4pm (avoid lunch rush)
- Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (checked out)
- Tuesday-Thursday are best

## VA notes

- Be conversational, not robotic
- Use their name 2-3 times in the conversation
- Match their energy (if they're casual, be casual)
- If they say "call back later" - set exact date/time and call back
- Log every call in CRM with outcome and notes
