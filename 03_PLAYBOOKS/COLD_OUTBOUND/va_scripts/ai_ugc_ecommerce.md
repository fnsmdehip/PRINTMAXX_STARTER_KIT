# VA cold calling script: AI UGC video for e-commerce brands

For VAs calling e-commerce brands running paid ads to sell AI-generated UGC video ad creative. Target: marketing managers, founders, media buyers at DTC brands.

---

## Pre-call prep

Before calling:
1. Check Meta Ad Library for their active ads (search by brand name)
2. Note how many creatives they're running and how old they are
3. Check their website/Shopify store for product info
4. Note their price range and product category

---

## The call script

### Opener (10 seconds)

```
Hi, is this {{firstName}}?

Hey {{firstName}}, this is {{vaName}} with [Company Name].

Quick call. I was looking at {{company}}'s ads in the Meta Ad Library and noticed you're running about {{adCount}} creatives, some of them 30+ days old. I know creative fatigue kills CPAs. Is that something you're dealing with right now?
```

**If gatekeeper:**
```
Hi, I'm looking for whoever handles ad creative or paid media at {{company}}. Would that be {{firstName}} or someone else?
```

**If asked "What's this about?":**
```
We produce UGC-style video ads for e-commerce brands using AI. Way faster and cheaper than hiring creators. I noticed {{company}} could use fresh creative and wanted to see if there's a fit.
```

---

### Qualifying questions (30 seconds)

Ask 2-3:

```
1. "How are you currently sourcing UGC creative? Hiring creators directly, using an agency, doing it in-house?"

2. "How many new creatives are you testing per month?"

3. "What are you paying per UGC video right now?"

4. "Have your CPAs been going up over the last 30-60 days?"

5. "What's your monthly ad spend roughly? Just so I know what level of creative volume we're talking about."
```

**Listen for pain signals:**
- "Creators are expensive"
- "Takes forever to get videos back"
- "CPAs are going up"
- "We can't test fast enough"
- "Quality is inconsistent"
- "We need more volume"

---

### Value pitch (30 seconds)

```
Here's what we do:

We produce UGC-style video ads using AI. They look and feel like real creator content. The difference:

- $50-150 per video instead of $300-500 from creators
- 48-hour turnaround instead of 1-2 weeks
- Unlimited revisions included
- We can produce 20-50 videos per month

Our last client ran 47 AI UGC videos. Their CPA dropped 34% because they were testing fresh creative every 5 days instead of every 30.

If you're spending ${{adSpend}} on ads, even a 10% CPA improvement is ${{savings}} saved per month.
```

---

### Objection handling

**"AI UGC doesn't look real"**
```
Fair concern. That was true a year ago. The quality now is indistinguishable from real UGC. Tell you what: let me produce 3 sample videos for {{company}}'s products. Free. You tell me if you can spot the difference. If you can, no hard feelings.
```

**"We have a creator network"**
```
That's great. Most of our clients still use creators for hero content. They use us to fill the gap between creator shoots: testing hooks, iterating on angles, scaling what works. It's not either/or. It's both.

How many new angles are you testing per month? If it's under 10, you're probably leaving money on the table.
```

**"Too expensive / no budget"**
```
What are you paying per video right now? [Listen] Our videos are $50-150 each. If you're paying $300-500 per creator video, we're saving you 60-70% on creative costs while you can test 3-5x more variations.

The math: if we drop your CPA by even 15%, that's ${{savings}} per month in savings on a ${{adSpend}} spend. The creative pays for itself 5-10x over.
```

**"We tried AI content and it was bad"**
```
When was that? The tech has changed dramatically in the last 6 months. What we're producing now wins A/B tests against real UGC about 40% of the time. And the other 60%, it's close enough that the volume advantage makes up for it.

Let me send you some samples from brands in your space. See if the quality meets your bar.
```

**"Can you email me?"**
```
Absolutely. What's the best email? I'll send over 3 sample videos from brands similar to {{company}} plus our pricing. Is {{dayOption1}} or {{dayOption2}} a good time to follow up?
```

---

### Close

**Option A: Free sample close**
```
Here's my suggestion: let me produce 3 free UGC videos for {{company}}'s top product. 48 hours, no cost, no obligation. You test them against your current creative. If they perform, we talk about a monthly package. If not, you keep the videos.

What product should we start with?
```

**Option B: Meeting close**
```
I'd love to show you some examples from brands in your space and talk through what a creative testing plan would look like for {{company}}.

Can we grab 15 minutes this week? {{dayOption1}} or {{dayOption2}} work?
```

**Option C: Email close**
```
Let me send you our portfolio and 3 samples in your vertical. You can see the quality before we talk further.

Best email? And when should I follow up?
```

## Call metrics targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Calls per hour | 12-15 |

| Conversation rate | 50-60% |
| Interest rate | 20-30% |
| Meeting/sample request rate | 8-15% |

## Best calling times

- E-commerce brands: 10am-12pm and 2-4pm
- Avoid Mondays (planning) and Fridays (wrapping up)
- Wednesday is typically best for DTC brands

## Prospect sources

- Meta Ad Library (search by vertical)
- Shopify store databases (myip.ms, BuiltWith)
- Apollo (filter: e-commerce, 5-100 employees, ad spend signals)
- Brands with active Facebook/Instagram ads but no TikTok presence
