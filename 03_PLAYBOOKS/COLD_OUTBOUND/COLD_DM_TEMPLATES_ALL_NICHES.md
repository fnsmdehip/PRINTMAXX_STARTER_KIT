## Universal Follow-Up Framework

**If no response after 3-5 days:**
1. Don't repeat the pitch
2. Add new value (stat, insight, or resource)
3. Drop urgency but keep it short
4. Redirect to something specific

**If no response after follow-up:**
- Move to cold email sequence if you have their email
- Save for 2-3 month re-engagement window
- Don't spam

**If they reject:**
- Say "no worries" and leave
- No aggressive follow-ups
- Save for future if circumstances change

## Niche-Specific Targeting Notes

**Instagram DM:** Best for creators, lifestyle, consumer apps. Need to find them first (scout their profiles, followers, engagement).

**Twitter DM:** Best for B2B, tech-focused, investors, thought leaders. More acceptable to cold DM. Keep character count reasonable.

**LinkedIn DM:** Best for B2B, services, professionals, executives. Most formal. Mention mutual connections if possible.

**Response Rate Baseline:** 3-8% on Instagram/Twitter, 8-12% on LinkedIn for well-targeted messages.

**Testing Framework:** A/B test openers and value hooks. Track what resonates per niche. Update templates quarterly.
