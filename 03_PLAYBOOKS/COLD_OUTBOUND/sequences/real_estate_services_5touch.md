# Real estate services 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Marketing for agents/brokers, real estate photography, virtual tours, CRM/tech for real estate.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Listing observation |
| 2 | Day 3 | Competition angle |
| 3 | Day 7 | Days on market impact |
| 4 | Day 11 | Success story |
| 5 | Day 16 | Try one listing |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-5%

---

## Email 1: Listing observation

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Your [Address] listing
- B: Quick thought on your listings
- C: [MLS#] observation

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Looked at your listing on [Address/MLS#].

[Specific observation]:
- "Photos could be more impactful"
- "No virtual tour, which buyers expect now"
- "Description doesn't highlight key features"
- "No video walkthrough"

In [Market], listings with [what you provide] sell [X] days faster and for [X%] more.

We help agents like you [your value prop in one sentence].

Worth a quick call to see if we can help your listings stand out?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific listing they recognize
- Observation shows you did homework
- Clear benefit

## Finding real estate leads

### Where to find agents:
- MLS listings (see who has active listings)
- Zillow/Realtor.com agent profiles
- Real estate brokerage websites
- LinkedIn (filter by Real Estate Agent)
- Local real estate associations

### Trigger events:
- New listing just went live (perfect timing)
- Listing sitting on market (needs better marketing)
- Agent just joined new brokerage
- Top producer awards (has budget, wants to stay on top)
- New to area (building their brand)

### Qualification:
- Volume: At least [X] listings/year
- Price point: $[X]+ average (budget for services)
- Current marketing: Weak (opportunity)

## Objection handlers

**"My brokerage provides photography"**
```
A lot of brokerages do. Question is: is it good enough to compete?

Professional photography vs iPhone photos is the difference between
"nice house" and "I have to see this in person."

What does your current provider deliver? Happy to show you the difference.
```

**"Sellers won't pay for this"**
```
Actually, sellers pay for effective marketing all the time.

Position it as: "I invest in marketing your home properly because I
want it to sell fast and for top dollar."

Many agents build it into their listing presentation. Others charge
it separately. Either way, it works.
```

**"Too expensive"**
```
Understand. What's the cost of a listing sitting 30 extra days?

- Carrying costs for seller
- Price reductions
- Your time
- Your reputation

Our service costs $[X] and typically reduces days on market by [Y].
The math works out.
```

**"I'm not tech-savvy"**
```
No problem. We handle everything.

You just tell us the listing address. We schedule, shoot, edit, and
deliver files you can upload anywhere.

Zero tech required on your end.
```
