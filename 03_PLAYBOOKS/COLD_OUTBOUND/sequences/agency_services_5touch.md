# Agency services 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Marketing agencies, dev agencies, design agencies selling retainer services to businesses.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Introduce problem + credibility |
| 2 | Day 3 | Different angle, social proof |
| 3 | Day 7 | Value-add, no pitch |
| 4 | Day 12 | Direct case study |
| 5 | Day 18 | Breakup email |

Expected reply rate: 4-8%
Expected meeting rate: 1-3%

---

## Email 1: The opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}} + [your service]
- B: Quick question about {{company}}
- C: [Their pain point] at {{company}}

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Noticed {{company}} is [specific observation about their business - website, marketing, social, etc.].

We help [type of company] [specific result]. Last month we helped [similar company] [specific metric improvement].

Worth a 15-min call to see if we can do the same for you?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Personalized observation shows research
- Specific result, not vague claims
- Low-commitment ask

---

## Email 2: Social proof angle

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} (reply thread)
- B: [Similar company] did this
- C: Following up

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Quick follow-up.

[Similar company in their space] was in a similar position before working with us. In [timeframe], they [specific result].

Here's what we did differently for them: [1-2 sentence explanation].

Would that kind of result move the needle for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Builds on first email
- Shows relevant experience
- Question invites response

---

## Email 3: Value-add (no pitch)

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Thought this might help
- B: Resource for {{company}}
- C: [Specific topic] tip

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Not trying to sell you anything here.

Found this [resource/article/guide] about [their pain point] and thought of {{company}}: [link]

The takeaway: [1 sentence summary of actionable insight].

Let me know if you found it useful.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Breaks the sales pattern
- Provides genuine value
- Builds goodwill for next email

---

## Email 4: Direct case study

**Subject line variants:**
- A: How [client] got [result]
- B: {{company}} case study
- C: Relevant for {{company}}

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Want to share a quick case study since it's relevant to {{company}}.

Client: [Similar company, similar size]
Challenge: [The problem they had]
Result: [Specific outcome with numbers]
Timeline: [How long it took]

Full breakdown here if you're interested: [link to case study]

If {{company}} is dealing with similar challenges, happy to walk you through our approach.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Concrete proof
- Relevant to their situation
- Clear next step

---

## Email 5: The breakup

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Should I close your file?
- B: One last thing
- C: Moving on

**Timing:** 6 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Haven't heard back, so I'll assume the timing isn't right.

Totally get it. I'll close out your file for now.

If things change and you want to explore [what you offer], just reply to this email. I'll be here.

Either way, good luck with {{company}}.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Creates urgency without being pushy
- Often gets replies from fence-sitters
- Leaves door open professionally

## Customization notes

### For marketing agencies:
- Observation: Their ad presence, SEO gaps, social activity
- Result: ROAS improvement, lead increase, traffic growth
- Case study: Marketing metrics

### For dev agencies:
- Observation: Site speed, tech stack, mobile experience
- Result: Conversion lift, performance improvement, launch timeline
- Case study: Development outcomes

### For design agencies:
- Observation: Brand consistency, competitor comparison, UI/UX issues
- Result: Conversion improvement, brand recognition, user engagement
- Case study: Design impact

---

## A/B test priorities

1. **Subject lines** - Test all variants for Email 1, use winner for rest
2. **First line** - Test personalization depth (generic vs. specific observation)
3. **CTA** - Test "15-min call" vs "quick chat" vs "worth exploring?"
4. **Send time** - Test morning vs afternoon for your ICP

---

## Performance benchmarks

**Good:**
- Open rate: 50%+
- Reply rate: 5%+
- Meeting rate: 2%+

**Needs work:**
- Open rate under 40%: Fix subject lines
- Opens but no replies: Fix body copy
- Replies but no meetings: Fix CTA or targeting

---

## Follow-up call script

When they reply interested, book the call with:

```
Great to hear from you, {{firstName}}.

Here's my calendar to grab 15 minutes: [Calendly link]

Or if easier, just let me know your availability and I'll send an invite.

Looking forward to it.
```
