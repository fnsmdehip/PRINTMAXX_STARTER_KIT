# Consulting offer 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Independent consultants, fractional executives, advisory services.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Establish expertise + relevance |
| 2 | Day 3 | Share insight/methodology |
| 3 | Day 7 | Offer quick win |
| 4 | Day 12 | Client transformation story |
| 5 | Day 18 | Soft breakup with resource |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-4%

---

## Email 1: Expertise opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}}'s [area you consult on]
- B: Noticed something about {{company}}
- C: [Industry] + [your specialty]

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

I've spent [X years] helping [type of company] fix [specific problem].

Looking at {{company}}, [specific observation about their situation].

The companies I work with typically see [specific outcome] within [timeframe] of implementing [your methodology/approach].

If [problem] is on your radar, worth a conversation?

[Your name]
[Your title/specialty]
```

**Why it works:**
- Leads with credibility
- Shows you did homework
- Specific outcomes, not vague promises

---

## Email 2: Insight share

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} (reply thread)
- B: [Industry] insight
- C: What I'm seeing in [industry]

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Quick thought I wanted to share.

Working with [X] companies in [industry], I've noticed [pattern or trend relevant to them].

The ones who [do X] tend to [positive outcome].
The ones who [do Y] tend to [negative outcome].

From what I can see, {{company}} might be [observation about which camp they're in].

If you want to dig into this, happy to share what I've learned.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Positions you as industry expert
- Provides value without pitching
- Creates curiosity

---

## Email 3: Quick win offer

**Subject line variants:**
- A: 30-min audit for {{company}}
- B: Free [deliverable]
- C: Quick assessment offer

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Want to offer something useful.

I've helped [X] companies identify their biggest [area] gaps in a 30-minute call. No pitch, just diagnosis.

What you'd get:
- Assessment of your current [area]
- Top 3 issues holding you back
- Prioritized roadmap to fix them

Worst case: You get free advice.
Best case: We find something worth working on together.

Open to it?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Low-risk offer
- Clear value proposition
- Demonstrates confidence in your diagnosis

---

## Email 4: Transformation story

**Subject line variants:**
- A: How [client] fixed [problem]
- B: [Industry] turnaround story
- C: From [bad state] to [good state]

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Wanted to share a story since the situation reminds me of {{company}}.

[Client] came to me with [problem - similar to prospect's likely situation].

They had tried: [failed approaches]
What was actually wrong: [root cause]
What we did: [your methodology in 2-3 sentences]

Result: [Specific outcomes with numbers/timeframe]

If {{company}} is dealing with similar challenges, this approach might apply.

Happy to walk through how we'd adapt it for your situation.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Concrete proof of results
- Shows your thinking process
- Relevant to their situation

---

## Email 5: Resource-based breakup

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Resource for {{firstName}}
- B: Before I sign off
- C: One more thing

**Timing:** 6 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

I'll stop filling your inbox after this one.

Before I go, wanted to share a [resource type: guide, framework, checklist] I put together on [topic]:

[Link to resource]

It covers [what it includes] and you can use it without ever talking to me.

If down the road you want help implementing any of it, you know where to find me.

Good luck with {{company}}.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Leaves on positive note
- Provides lasting value
- Plants seed for future outreach

## Consulting-specific personalization

### Research checklist before sending:
- [ ] Their LinkedIn activity (what are they posting about?)
- [ ] Company news (funding, hiring, pivots)
- [ ] Glassdoor reviews (internal problems surfacing)
- [ ] Competitor analysis (where do they stand?)
- [ ] Their content (blog posts, podcasts, interviews)

### Observation examples by consulting type:

**Strategy consulting:**
- "Noticed you're expanding into [market]"
- "Your recent acquisition of [company] suggests..."
- "Your hiring patterns indicate [growth area]"

**Operations consulting:**
- "Your team size vs revenue suggests [efficiency issue]"
- "Companies at your stage typically struggle with [bottleneck]"
- "Your tech stack shows [operational complexity]"

**Marketing consulting:**
- "Your SEO visibility is [observation]"
- "Your paid spend vs organic mix looks [observation]"
- "Your content strategy seems focused on [observation]"

**Sales consulting:**
- "Your sales team structure suggests [observation]"
- "Your pricing page indicates [positioning issue]"
- "Your outbound approach looks [observation]"

## The 30-minute audit call structure

When they accept your Email 3 offer:

**Before call:**
- Research their business for 20 minutes
- Prepare 5 diagnostic questions
- Have framework ready to share

**On call (30 min):**
- 5 min: Rapport + confirm goals
- 10 min: Diagnostic questions
- 10 min: Share initial observations
- 5 min: Discuss potential next steps

**After call:**
- Send summary within 24 hours
- Include top 3 findings
- Propose engagement if fit exists

## Pricing the engagement

Consulting structures that work for cold outreach:

**Discovery project:**
- Small scope (2-4 weeks)
- Fixed fee ($3K-$15K)
- Clear deliverable
- Lower risk for buyer

**Example:** "I'll audit your [area], interview key stakeholders, and deliver a prioritized roadmap. $5K, 2 weeks."

**Why this beats pitching retainers:**
- Easier to say yes to
- Proves value before bigger commitment
- Leads to ongoing work naturally

## Performance benchmarks

**Consulting email typically performs better than SaaS/agency:**
- Open rate: 55-65%
- Reply rate: 6-12%
- Meeting rate: 3-5%

**Why:** More senior targets, less competitive inbox, higher perceived value of conversation.

## Follow-up after sequence ends

If no reply after 5 emails:

**Option 1:** Add to quarterly nurture
- Send 1 email per quarter with industry insight
- No pitch, just value
- Stay top of mind

**Option 2:** Different trigger later
- Job change
- Company news
- Industry shift

**Option 3:** Different contact same company
- Try another decision maker
- Reference your previous outreach to [other person]
