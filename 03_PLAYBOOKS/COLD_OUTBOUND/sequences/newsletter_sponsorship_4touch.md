# Newsletter sponsorship outreach to brands

Pitch brands on sponsoring our newsletters (Morning Grace, 5AM Gains, The Stack). Monetize subscriber attention. This is for when newsletters have 1,000+ subscribers.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Introduce newsletter + audience match |
| 2 | Day 3 | Media kit + performance data |
| 3 | Day 7 | Limited availability angle |
| 4 | Day 14 | Breakup |

Expected reply rate: 10-18%
Expected meeting rate: 4-8%

## Newsletter-specific pitches

### Morning Grace (faith audience)
Target sponsors: Bible apps, devotional journals, faith-based products, Christian bookstores, wellness brands, prayer accessories

### 5AM Gains (fitness audience)
Target sponsors: Supplement brands, fitness apps, workout gear, protein bars, fitness trackers, gym memberships

### The Stack (solopreneur/tech audience)
Target sponsors: SaaS tools, hosting providers, dev tools, online course platforms, productivity apps, API services

## Finding sponsor prospects

1. **Who sponsors similar newsletters** - check Swapstack, Paved, Passionfroot
2. **Brands running Meta ads** in your niche - they have ad budget
3. **Affiliate programs** - brands already paying for performance
4. **Apollo** - marketing managers at relevant brands

## A/B test priorities

1. Subject lines: "sponsorship" vs "your brand + subscribers" vs "company x newsletter"
2. Email 1: data-first vs audience-first
3. Email 3: scarcity vs results focus
4. CTA: "send media kit" vs "hold a slot" vs "test one send"
