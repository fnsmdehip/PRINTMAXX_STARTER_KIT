# Coaching programs 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Business coaches, executive coaches, group programs, masterminds.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Identify the gap |
| 2 | Day 4 | Transformation story |
| 3 | Day 8 | Framework share |
| 4 | Day 13 | Results proof |
| 5 | Day 18 | Strategy call offer |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-5%

---

## Email 1: Identify the gap

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Question for {{firstName}}
- B: {{company}}'s next level
- C: Stuck at [stage]?

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Quick question: What's the biggest thing holding {{company}} back right now?

For most founders at your stage, it's one of these:
- [Common bottleneck 1 for their stage]
- [Common bottleneck 2]
- [Common bottleneck 3]

I help [type of founder] break through these plateaus.

Recent client went from $[X] to $[Y] in [timeframe] after we worked together.

Worth a conversation to see if I can help {{company}} do the same?

[Your name]
[Your title/program]
```

**Why it works:**
- Opens with their situation
- Stage-specific pain points
- Proof of transformation

## Email 2: Transformation story

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} (reply thread)
- B: How [client] broke through
- C: [Similar founder]'s story

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Want to share a story since it might resonate.

[Client name] came to me at a similar stage to {{company}}.

**Their situation:**
- [Challenge 1]
- [Challenge 2]
- [How they felt about it]

**What we worked on:**
- [Focus area 1]
- [Focus area 2]
- [Mindset/strategy shift]

**Where they are now:**
- [Result 1]
- [Result 2]
- [How they feel now]

The shift happened in [timeframe]. Not overnight, but faster than they expected.

If {{company}} is stuck in a similar place, let's talk.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Relatable story
- Emotional + practical elements
- Shows your process

[Your name]

- Demonstrates your thinking

## Email 5: Strategy call offer

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Free strategy session
- B: 30 minutes for {{firstName}}
- C: Before I move on

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Last email.

Here's my offer: a free 30-minute strategy call.

On the call, I'll help you:
- Identify the #1 thing holding {{company}} back
- Create a roadmap to break through it
- Give you actionable next steps (whether you work with me or not)

No pitch unless you ask. Just pure value.

If it's helpful, great. If we decide to work together, even better.

Want the call?

[Your name]

P.S. I only do [X] of these per month, so let me know soon if interested.
```

**Why it works:**
- Free value offer
- Clear agenda
- Soft scarcity

## Coaching program positioning

### 1:1 Executive coaching:
- $1-5K/month
- Weekly or bi-weekly calls
- Personalized focus
- Best for: C-suite, funded founders

### Group coaching program:
- $500-2K/month
- Cohort-based
- Community + curriculum
- Best for: Earlier-stage founders, budget-conscious

### Mastermind:
- $1-10K/month
- Small group of peers
- Facilitated sessions
- Best for: Established entrepreneurs wanting peer network

### Intensive/VIP Day:
- $3-10K one-time
- Full day session
- Concentrated problem-solving
- Best for: Specific challenges, quick decisions needed

### Course + community:
- $500-2K one-time
- Self-paced curriculum
- Community access
- Best for: DIY types, lower price point

## Target audience specificity

### Be specific about who you help:
**Bad:** "I help entrepreneurs grow their business"
**Good:** "I help SaaS founders at $500K-$2M ARR scale to $5M"

### Stage-specific messaging:

**Startup stage ($0-$100K):**
Pain: Finding product-market fit, first customers
Promise: "Go from idea to consistent revenue"

**Growth stage ($100K-$1M):**
Pain: Doing everything themselves, no systems
Promise: "Build the team and systems to scale beyond you"

**Scale stage ($1M-$10M):**
Pain: Complexity, leadership challenges
Promise: "Scale efficiently without burning out"

### Industry-specific angles:
- "I help agency owners add $50K/month"
- "I help SaaS founders hit $10K MRR"
- "I help coaches launch group programs"
- "I help ecommerce brands scale past $1M"

## Objection handlers

**"I've tried coaching before"**
```
What worked and what didn't?

Most coaching fails because it's either:
1. Too generic (not specific to your situation)
2. Too passive (just accountability, no strategy)
3. Wrong fit (coach didn't understand your business)

My approach is [how you're different].

What would coaching need to look like for you to try again?
```

**"I don't have time"**
```
That's usually a symptom, not a cause.

Most of my clients come to me working 60+ hours/week. Within months,
they're working less AND making more.

The time investment is [X hours/week]. The return is getting 10+ hours
back plus growing revenue.

If you don't have time to work ON your business, that's exactly why
you need this.
```

**"Can't afford it right now"**
```
Understand. Let me ask: what's the cost of staying stuck for another year?

Most clients find that one breakthrough pays for the entire program.
And we offer [payment plans/money-back guarantee/etc.] to make it accessible.

What would the program need to cost for it to be a no-brainer?
```

**"Let me think about it"**
```
Of course. What specifically do you want to think about?

Usually when people say that, they have a specific concern that's
not addressed. If you share it, I can either answer it or we'll both
know it's not a fit.

Either way, I want to make the decision easy for you.
```

## Performance benchmarks

**Coaching outreach:**
- Open rate: 50-60%
- Reply rate: 6-12%
- Call booking rate: 3-6%

**Why decent:**
- Personal nature (not corporate)
- Emotional appeal
- Transformation promise

**Key success factor:** Specificity of targeting. Generic coaching emails fail. Specific-to-their-situation coaching emails convert.
