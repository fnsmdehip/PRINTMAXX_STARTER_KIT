# SaaS demo 3-day sequence

Use this for: SaaS companies booking product demos. Shorter sequence for faster velocity.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Problem + quick proof |
| 2 | Day 2 | Feature spotlight + visual |
| 3 | Day 4 | Breakup with options |

Expected reply rate: 4-7%
Expected demo rate: 2-4%

---

## Email 1: Problem-led opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}}'s [function they struggle with]
- B: Quick question, {{first_name}}
- C: [Pain point] at {{company}}?

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

[Type of companies] usually spend [X hours/dollars] on [pain point your product solves].

[Product] fixes that. [One sentence of what it does].

[Similar company] cut their [metric] by [%] after switching. Took them [timeframe] to see results.

Worth 15 min to see if it fits {{company}}?

[calendar link]

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

Marketing teams usually spend 8+ hours per week pulling reports from different tools.

DataSync fixes that. One dashboard, all your data, updates automatically.

Verve Marketing cut their reporting time by 70% after switching. Took them 2 weeks to see results.

Worth 15 min to see if it fits {{company}}?

[calendar link]

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Opens with their pain, not your product
- Specific outcome from similar company
- Easy next step

---

## Email 2: Feature + visual

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} (reply thread)
- B: Screenshot: [Product] in action
- C: This feature alone

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Following up.

One feature our users love: [specific feature in plain language].

Before: [How they did it before]
After: [How they do it with your product]

[Embedded screenshot or Loom link]

[Customer name] said it saves them [specific time/money] per [week/month].

15 min to show you how it works for [their use case]: [calendar link]

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

Following up.

One feature our users love: one-click report scheduling.

Before: Manually pull 5 reports every Monday, copy into Slides
After: Reports auto-generate and land in stakeholders' inboxes

[Screenshot of report scheduler]

Beacon Digital said it saves them 3 hours per week.

15 min to show you how it works for {{company}}: [calendar link]

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Visual breaks through inbox noise
- Before/after is concrete
- Low-friction ask

---

## Email 3: Breakup with options

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Last one from me
- B: 1 or 2?
- C: Before I move on

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Reached out a couple times. Haven't heard back.

Totally get it if [Product] isn't a priority right now.

Before I move on, two options:

1. 15-min demo: [calendar link]
2. Self-serve trial: [trial link]

Reply "1" or "2" and I'll set you up.

If neither, no worries. Best of luck with {{company}}.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Acknowledges their silence
- Simple response options (low friction)
- Self-serve option for avoiders

## Subject line best practices

**Do:**
- Keep under 40 characters
- Use their company name
- Reference their problem
- Sound human

**Don't:**
- ALL CAPS
- "Free trial" (spam trigger)
- Numbers at start
- Clickbait

---

## A/B test priorities

1. **Subject lines** - Biggest open rate lever
2. **First line** - Hook or lose them
3. **CTA type** - Calendar link vs "reply interested"
4. **Screenshot vs Loom** - Test visual format

---

## Performance benchmarks

**SaaS cold email averages:**
- Open rate: 45-55%
- Reply rate: 4-7%
- Demo show rate: 65-75%

**Improve opens:** Better subjects, warmer domain
**Improve replies:** Stronger pain point, clearer value
**Improve show rate:** Confirmation email + day-before reminder

---

## Sequence variations by deal size

### Low-touch SaaS (< $500 ACV)
- Keep 3 emails
- Lead with free trial
- Less personalization needed
- Consider PLG motion instead

### Mid-market SaaS ($500-$10K ACV)
- This sequence works well
- Moderate personalization
- Book live demos

### Enterprise SaaS ($10K+ ACV)
- Extend to 5-7 emails
- Heavy personalization (account research)
- Multi-thread (reach multiple people)
- Longer timing gaps

## Demo booking reply

**When they reply interested:**

```
Great, {{first_name}}.

Here's my calendar: [link]

Pick whatever works. You'll get a Zoom link automatically.

Quick question before we talk: what's the main thing you'd want [Product] to solve for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Why ask what to cover:**
- Shows you care about their time
- Lets you prepare relevant demo
- Increases show rate

---

## No-show follow-up

If they miss the demo:

```
{{first_name}},

Looks like we missed each other. No worries, things come up.

Here's my calendar to reschedule: [link]

Or if you'd rather just poke around, here's a trial: [trial link]

[Your name]
```

## Objection handling

**"We already use [competitor]"**

```
Makes sense. A lot of our customers switched from [competitor].

Main reasons they moved:
- [Difference 1]
- [Difference 2]

If you're open to it, I can show you a side-by-side in 15 min. Up to you.

[Your name]
```

**"Not a priority right now"**

```
Totally get it.

Mind if I check back in [X] months? Or I can send over a quick case study to review when timing's better.

[Your name]
```

**"Send me more info"**

```
Sure. Here's a quick overview: [one-pager or short video]

If anything catches your eye, happy to do a live walkthrough: [calendar]

[Your name]
```
