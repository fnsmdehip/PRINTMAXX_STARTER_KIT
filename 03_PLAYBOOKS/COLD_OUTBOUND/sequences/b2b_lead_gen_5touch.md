# B2B lead gen services 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Lead gen agencies, appointment setting services, SDR-as-a-service, outbound agencies selling to B2B companies.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Meeting problem |
| 2 | Day 3 | ROI angle |
| 3 | Day 6 | Process explanation |
| 4 | Day 10 | Client success story |
| 5 | Day 15 | Test campaign offer |

Expected reply rate: 4-8%
Expected meeting rate: 2-4%

---

## Email 1: The meeting problem

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}} pipeline
- B: Filling your calendar
- C: Where do {{company}}'s leads come from?

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Question: Where do most of {{company}}'s new clients come from right now?

If it's mostly referrals or inbound, you're probably leaving money on the table.

We help [type of company] book [X] qualified meetings per month through outbound.

Recent example: [Similar company] went from [X] to [Y] meetings/month within [timeframe].

Worth a quick call to see if we could do similar for {{company}}?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Question makes them think about their situation
- Addresses common dependency on referrals
- Specific metrics

## Email 2: ROI math

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} pipeline (reply thread)
- B: Quick math
- C: {{company}} meeting math

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Let me share some quick math:

Let's say:
- Your average deal size is $[X]
- You close 20% of qualified meetings
- We book you 10 meetings/month

That's 2 new clients/month = $[Y] in new revenue.

Our fee: $[Z]/month

ROI: [X]x return on outbound investment.

The math usually works for companies like {{company}}. Worth a conversation?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Concrete ROI calculation
- Easy to follow logic
- Makes value obvious

## Email 3: Process transparency

**Subject line variants:**
- A: How we do it
- B: Behind the scenes
- C: Our process

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```

In case you're wondering how we actually book meetings:

1. We build your ICP list (verified, targeted contacts)

- Show up to meetings

Want to see exactly how it would work for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Demystifies the service
- Shows it's turnkey
- Addresses "how does this work" objection

## ICP targeting for lead gen services

### Best prospects:
- B2B companies with $5K+ deal sizes
- Sales teams of 1-5 people (need help scaling)
- Currently reliant on referrals/inbound
- Have sales capacity but not enough meetings
- Recently funded (have budget, need growth)

### Red flags:
- Deal size too small (< $1K) = ROI doesn't work
- Already have large outbound team
- No sales process (will waste meetings)
- Early stage with no product-market fit

## Objection handlers

**"We tried outbound, it didn't work"**
```
That's actually common. Usually it's one of three things:
1. Wrong list (targeting didn't match ICP)
2. Wrong message (copy was too salesy)
3. Wrong infrastructure (emails went to spam)

We've diagnosed and fixed all three for other clients. Happy to look at what you tried and see if we can pinpoint the issue.
```

**"We can do this ourselves"**
```
Definitely. Question is: do you want to?

Building the infrastructure, learning deliverability, testing copy, managing responses... it's a full-time job.

Most clients come to us after trying it themselves and realizing they'd rather focus on closing than on generating.
```

**"It's too expensive"**
```
Fair. Let's look at the math again.

If we book [X] meetings and you close [Y%], that's [Z] new clients worth $[total].

Compared to our fee of $[X], that's a [Y]x return.

If those numbers don't work for {{company}}, you're right, it's not a fit. But usually they do.
```

## Metrics to share with prospects

**Industry benchmarks:**
- Average positive reply rate: 8-15%
- Meetings per 1,000 emails: 10-25
- Show rate for booked meetings: 70-85%
- Time to first meeting: 2-4 weeks

**Your results to highlight:**
- Average meetings booked per client per month
- Cost per meeting booked
- Client retention rate
- Biggest success story

## Service positioning options

### Full-service (premium)
- We do everything
- You just show up to meetings
- Higher price, higher value

### Hybrid (mid-market)
- We build lists and write copy
- You send from your inboxes
- We coach on responses

### Consulting only (entry point)
- We set up your infrastructure
- We train your team
- One-time fee

Start with full-service pitch, fall back to hybrid or consulting if needed.
