# UGC creator outreach sequence

Use this for: Recruiting UGC creators to your roster for agency or brand work.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Compliment + opportunity |
| 2 | Day 3 | Volume + earnings clarity |
| 3 | Day 6 | Social proof from creators |
| 4 | Day 10 | Low-commitment test |
| 5 | Day 14 | Breakup |

Expected reply rate: 15-25%
Expected conversion rate: 8-15%

---

## Email 1: The opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Paid UGC work for {{first_name}}
- B: Your UGC content caught my eye
- C: UGC opportunity

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Came across your [specific content] on [platform]. Love the [specific thing you liked].

I run a UGC sourcing operation. We work with [type of brands] and need creators for ongoing projects.

What we offer:
- [X-Y] videos per month
- $[X] per video
- Detailed briefs (you just follow them)
- Fast payment (within 7 days of delivery)

Would you be open to a quick chat about joining our roster?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```


- Specific compliment shows you watched their content

- Low-commitment ask

## Where to find UGC creators

### Platforms
- TikTok (search #ugccreator, #ugcportfolio)
- Instagram (search UGC hashtags)
- Fiverr/Upwork (UGC category)
- Facebook groups (UGC creator communities)
- Twitter/X (creators posting portfolios)

### What to look for
- [ ] Good lighting in their content
- [ ] Natural, non-scripted delivery
- [ ] Clear audio
- [ ] Diverse content styles
- [ ] Active posting (not dormant)
- [ ] Fits your brand niches

### Red flags
- Overproduced, too polished
- Generic portfolios (all the same)
- Slow response times
- Unrealistic rate expectations
- No examples of recent work

## Rate guidance

| Video type | Length | Rate range |
|------------|--------|------------|
| Simple testimonial | 15-30s | $25-50 |
| Product demo | 30-60s | $40-75 |
| Tutorial/how-to | 60-90s | $60-100 |
| Multiple hooks/versions | Varies | +$15-25 per variation |

**Bulk discount:** Offer 10-15% off for 5+ videos at once

## A/B test priorities

1. **Subject line** - "Paid UGC work" vs "Your content caught my eye"
2. **Rate range** - Test slightly higher to attract quality
3. **Platform mention** - Reference where you found them
4. **CTA** - "Quick chat" vs "Send you a test brief"

## Managing your roster

### Tier your creators

**A-tier (prioritize):**
- Fast turnaround
- Minimal revisions needed
- Professional communication
- Consistent quality

**B-tier (regular):**
- Good quality
- Standard turnaround
- Occasional revisions
- Reliable

**C-tier (backup):**
- Slower turnaround
- More revisions
- Use when A/B are at capacity

### Keeping creators engaged

**Monthly:**
- Send payment promptly
- Share any performance data (if brand shares)
- Ask about availability

**Quarterly:**
- Rate review for top performers
- Feedback session
- Referral bonus for new creators

**Annually:**
- Roster cleanup (remove inactive)
- Rate adjustments
- Top performer bonuses

## Sequence variations

### For beauty/skincare niche
- Look for creators with good skin/makeup skills
- Reference specific product type experience
- Mention brand names you work with (if allowed)

### For tech/gadgets niche
- Look for creators who explain clearly
- Reference unboxing or tutorial experience
- Emphasize brief clarity (tech can be complex)

### For fitness/wellness niche
- Look for authentic fitness content
- Reference supplement/activewear experience
- Mention flexible filming requirements

### For food/CPG niche
- Look for appealing food presentation
- Reference taste test or recipe content
- Mention product shipping process
