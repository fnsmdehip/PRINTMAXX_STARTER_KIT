# Agency outbound 5-day sequence

Use this for: Marketing, dev, design, or growth agencies selling retainers or project work to SMBs and mid-market.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Specific observation + credibility |
| 2 | Day 2 | Social proof from similar company |
| 3 | Day 4 | Pure value, no pitch |
| 4 | Day 6 | Direct offer with urgency |
| 5 | Day 9 | Breakup |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-4%

---

## Email 1: The specific opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}}'s [area you help with]
- B: Quick note for {{first_name}}
- C: Noticed something about {{company}}

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

[Specific observation about their business - be concrete]

We help [type of company] fix exactly that. Last month, [similar company] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe].

Worth a 15-min call?

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

Checked out {{company}}'s landing page. The value prop is solid but the page load time is 4.2 seconds. You're probably losing 30%+ of mobile visitors.

We help SaaS companies fix exactly that. Last month, Acme Software went from 4s load time to 1.2s and saw a 23% lift in trial signups.

Worth a 15-min call?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows you did real research
- Specific number builds credibility
- Low-commitment ask

---

## Email 2: Social proof

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}}'s [area] (reply thread)
- B: What [similar company] did
- C: Following up

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Quick follow-up.

[Similar company to them] had the same {{pain_point}}.

What we did:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]
- [Action 3]

Result: [Specific outcome with numbers]

That relevant for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

Quick follow-up.

TechFlow (similar size SaaS) had the same conversion problem.

What we did:
- Rebuilt landing page in Webflow (cut load time 70%)
- Rewrote headline using their customer language
- Added 3 trust signals above the fold

Result: 34% more demo requests in 6 weeks.

That relevant for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Case study from similar company
- Concrete actions, not vague claims
- Question invites response

## Email 3: Pure value

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Resource for {{company}}
- B: Thought this might help
- C: Free [resource type]

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Not selling anything here.

Found this [resource/tool/guide] that might help with {{pain_point}}: [link]

Key takeaway: [One sentence of actionable insight they can use immediately]

Let me know if useful.

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

Not selling anything here.

Found this PageSpeed audit guide that might help: [link]

Key takeaway: Most SaaS sites can cut 2 seconds off load time just by lazy-loading images below the fold. Takes 10 minutes to implement.

Let me know if useful.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Breaks the sales pattern
- Genuine value builds trust
- Sets up next email

## Email 4: Direct offer

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Quick question about {{company}}
- B: 2 spots left this month
- C: Idea for {{company}}

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

I'll be direct.

We have capacity for [X] new clients this month. Given what I've seen about {{company}}'s {{pain_point}}, you'd be a good fit.

What we'd do:
1. [Specific deliverable]
2. [Specific deliverable]
3. [Specific deliverable]

Timeline: [X] weeks
Investment: Starting at $[X]

If timing works, let's talk: [calendar link]

If not, no worries at all.

[Your name]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

I'll be direct.

We have capacity for 2 new clients this month. Given what I've seen about {{company}}'s conversion setup, you'd be a good fit.

What we'd do:
1. Full site audit with prioritized fixes
2. Landing page rebuild (design + dev)
3. 30-day A/B test setup

Timeline: 4-6 weeks
Investment: Starting at $8k

If timing works, let's talk: [calendar link]

If not, no worries at all.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Creates natural urgency (limited spots)
- Specific scope and price
- Clear next step

## Email 5: The breakup

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Should I close your file?
- B: Moving on
- C: Last note from me

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Haven't heard back, so I'm guessing timing isn't right.

Totally get it. I'll close your file for now.

If things change and {{company}} wants to tackle {{pain_point}}, just reply to this thread. Happy to pick up where we left off.

Good luck with everything.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- No guilt trip
- Often triggers fence-sitters
- Leaves door open

## Personalization tokens

| Token | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| {{first_name}} | Contact first name | Sarah |
| {{company}} | Company name | Acme Corp |
| {{pain_point}} | Their specific problem | slow site speed |

**High-effort personalization (for high-value targets):**
- Check their website for obvious issues
- Look at their tech stack (BuiltWith)
- Check their latest blog/content
- See if they're hiring (signals pain points)
- Review their reviews/testimonials

---

## A/B test priorities

1. **Subject lines** - Test Email 1 variants, use winner format for sequence
2. **Specificity level** - Generic vs highly personalized opener
3. **CTA format** - Calendar link vs "reply if interested"
4. **Send time** - Test Tuesday 9am vs Thursday 2pm

---

## Performance benchmarks

**Good:**
- Open rate: 55%+
- Reply rate: 6%+
- Meeting rate: 3%+

**Needs work:**
- Open rate under 45%: Fix subject lines
- Opens but no replies: Strengthen offer or relevance
- Replies but no meetings: CTA too weak or pricing issue

## When they reply

**Interested reply:**

```
Great to hear from you, {{first_name}}.

Here's my calendar for 15 minutes: [Calendly link]

Before we talk, anything specific you want me to cover?

[Your name]
```

**Questions about price:**

```
Good question.

For context, our projects typically run $[X]-$[Y] depending on scope. Most similar to {{company}} land around $[X].

Happy to walk through what that includes on a call: [calendar]

[Your name]
```

**Not now reply:**

```
Appreciate you letting me know, {{first_name}}.

Mind if I check back in [X] months? No pressure either way.

[Your name]
```

## Sequence variations

### For marketing agencies:
- **Observation:** Ad performance, SEO gaps, conversion issues
- **Pain point:** Leads, CAC, brand visibility
- **Proof:** Marketing metrics (ROAS, traffic, leads)

### For dev agencies:
- **Observation:** Site speed, mobile UX, outdated stack
- **Pain point:** Technical debt, slow releases, bugs
- **Proof:** Development outcomes (launch time, performance)

### For design agencies:
- **Observation:** Brand inconsistency, UI issues, dated look
- **Pain point:** Conversion, user confusion, trust
- **Proof:** Design impact (conversion lift, engagement)

### For growth agencies:
- **Observation:** Funnel leaks, channel gaps, retention
- **Pain point:** Growth stall, high churn, low LTV
- **Proof:** Growth metrics (MRR, activation, retention)
