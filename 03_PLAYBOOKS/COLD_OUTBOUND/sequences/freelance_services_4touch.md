# Freelance services 4-touch sequence

Use this for: Freelance designers, developers, writers, consultants pitching direct to companies.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Specific observation + offer |
| 2 | Day 4 | Portfolio proof |
| 3 | Day 8 | Quick win proposal |
| 4 | Day 13 | Availability/breakup |

Expected reply rate: 6-12%
Expected project rate: 2-5%

---

## Email 1: Observation opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: [Specific observation] at {{company}}
- B: Quick idea for {{company}}
- C: [Your skill] for {{firstName}}

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

[Specific observation about their business that relates to what you do]:

Examples:
- "Your landing page doesn't have a clear CTA above the fold"
- "Your blog posts are great but the design doesn't match your brand"
- "Your app's onboarding flow could use some UX work"

I'm a [your role] and I specialize in [what you do].

Recent project: [One relevant example with result]

Want me to put together a quick proposal for fixing [the thing you observed]?

[Your name]
[Portfolio link]
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows you actually looked
- Specific, not generic
- Clear offer

---

## Email 2: Portfolio proof

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} (reply thread)
- B: What I've done
- C: Relevant work

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Following up with some relevant work:

**[Project 1]** - [Client type]
[What I did + result]
[Link or image]

**[Project 2]** - [Client type]
[What I did + result]
[Link or image]

**[Project 3]** - [Similar to their situation]
[What I did + result]
[Link or image]

These give you a sense of my style and what I could do for {{company}}.

Interested in exploring a project together?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Visual proof
- Results attached
- Relevant to their needs

---

## Email 3: Quick win proposal

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Specific proposal for {{company}}
- B: Quick project idea
- C: Start small

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Instead of a big project, here's a quick win idea:

**The project:** [Specific, defined scope]
**Timeline:** [X days/weeks]
**Investment:** $[X]
**Result:** [What they'll get]

This is a low-risk way to see if we work well together.

If it goes great, we can discuss bigger projects.
If it doesn't, you've got [the deliverable] at worst.

Worth trying?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Low commitment
- Defined scope
- Easy yes

---

## Email 4: Availability/breakup

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Availability update
- B: One more thing
- C: Before I move on

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Last email.

I have a few project slots opening up [this month/next month].

If {{company}} needs [what you do] help, this would be a good time.

If not, no worries. I'll close your file and maybe we'll connect another time.

Either way, wishing you well.

[Your name]

P.S. If you know anyone who might need [what you do] help, I'd appreciate a referral.
```

**Why it works:**
- Scarcity (slots)
- Professional close
- Referral ask

## Freelance specialization angles

### Freelance designer:

**Observation examples:**
- "Your website looks like it's using a template"
- "Your brand consistency across touchpoints could be stronger"
- "Your competitor [X] just rebranded and looks more modern"

**Quick wins:**
- Landing page redesign
- Social media templates
- Brand refresh (logo + colors)
- Presentation deck design

### Freelance developer:

**Observation examples:**
- "Your site loads in [X] seconds (should be under 3)"
- "Your checkout flow has [X] unnecessary steps"
- "Your mobile experience needs work"

**Quick wins:**
- Performance optimization
- Bug fixes
- New feature build
- API integration

### Freelance writer:

**Observation examples:**
- "Your blog hasn't been updated in [X] months"
- "Your website copy doesn't clearly explain what you do"
- "Your competitors are publishing more content"

**Quick wins:**
- Homepage copy rewrite
- Blog post package (3-5 posts)
- Email sequence
- Case study

### Freelance marketer:

**Observation examples:**
- "You're not running retargeting ads"
- "Your email signup doesn't have a lead magnet"
- "Your LinkedIn presence could be stronger"

**Quick wins:**
- Ad campaign setup
- Lead magnet creation
- Social media audit + strategy

## Finding freelance clients

### Direct approach targets:
- Growing startups (visible hiring, funding)
- Agencies with overflow work
- Companies with obvious gaps
- Businesses mentioning needs on social

### Where to find them:
- LinkedIn (job posts, company updates)
- Twitter (founders sharing pain points)
- Product Hunt (new launches need help)
- Job boards (hiring posts signal budget)
- Their website (obvious gaps)

### Signals they need help:
- Active job posting for your skill
- Complaining about current vendor
- Scaling quickly (need support)
- Launching new product
- Website hasn't been updated

## Objection handlers

**"We use agencies"**
```
Agencies are great for ongoing work.

For specific projects, freelancers can be faster, more cost-effective,
and you get direct access to the person doing the work.

If you ever have a project that's too small for your agency but needs
attention, that's where I fit.
```

**"Send your rate card"**
```
Happy to. What's the project scope?

I price by project, not hourly. Once I understand what you need,
I'll send a clear proposal with fixed pricing.

What are you trying to accomplish?
```

**"We'll keep you in mind"**
```
Appreciate that.

To make it easier, what kinds of projects would you typically need
[your skill] help with? I can follow up when I have availability
for those specific things.
```

**"Not in the budget right now"**
```
Understand. Timing matters.

Would it be helpful if I checked back in [X weeks/months]?
Or if something smaller comes up, I can usually make it work.
```

## Building long-term relationships

### After completing a project:

**Immediate:**
- Deliver on time (or early)
- Over-communicate during project
- Ask for feedback

**Follow-up:**
- Send testimonial request
- Ask for referrals
- Stay in touch (not salesy)

**Quarterly touch:**
```
{{firstName}},

Hope things are going well at {{company}}.

I recently completed a project you might find interesting:
[Brief description + result]

If anything comes up where you need [your skill] help, give me a shout.

[Your name]
```

## Performance benchmarks

**Freelance cold outreach:**
- Open rate: 50-60%
- Reply rate: 8-15%
- Project conversion: 3-7%

**Why decent rates:**
- Highly personalized
- Specific observations show value
- Lower commitment than agencies

**Key success factor:** Specificity. Generic "I'm a designer, want to work together?" fails. "Your landing page's headline doesn't match your ad copy, want me to fix it?" works.
