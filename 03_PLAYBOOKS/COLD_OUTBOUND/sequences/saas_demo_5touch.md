# SaaS demo request 5-touch sequence

Use this for: SaaS companies trying to book product demos with target accounts.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Problem-aware intro |
| 2 | Day 2 | Feature benefit |
| 3 | Day 5 | Customer story |
| 4 | Day 9 | Quick video/screenshot |
| 5 | Day 14 | Final ask + breakup |

Expected reply rate: 3-6%
Expected demo rate: 1-2%

---

## Email 1: Problem-aware opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}}'s [function they struggle with]
- B: [Pain point] problem?
- C: Quick question for {{firstName}}

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

[Type of companies in their industry] usually waste [X hours/dollars] on [pain point your product solves].

We built [Product] specifically to fix that. [One sentence of what it does].

[Similar company] cut their [metric] by [%] in [timeframe] after switching.

Worth a 15-minute demo to see if it could work for {{company}}?

[Your name]
[Company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Leads with their pain, not your product
- Specific metric shows credibility
- Demo is low commitment

---

## Email 2: Feature benefit

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}}'s [function] (reply thread)
- B: This feature alone
- C: How {{company}} could [benefit]

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Following up on my last note.

One feature our customers love: [specific feature in plain language].

Before: [How they did it before]
After: [How they do it with your product]

[Customer name] said it saved them [specific time/money].

Demo takes 15 min and I can show you exactly how it works for [their use case].

[Calendar link]

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Focuses on one concrete feature
- Before/after shows transformation
- Calendar link makes booking easy

---

## Email 3: Customer story

**Subject line variants:**
- A: How [similar company] uses [Product]
- B: [Industry] company results
- C: Thought you'd relate to this

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Wanted to share how [Similar Company] uses [Product].

Their situation: [1-2 sentences about their challenge - similar to prospect's]

What happened: [Specific results with numbers]

[Quote from that customer if you have one]

{{company}} seems like a similar fit. Happy to show you their exact setup.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Social proof from relevant company
- Specific results, not vague claims
- Shows you understand their situation

---

## Email 4: Visual/video teaser

**Subject line variants:**
- A: 30-second video for {{firstName}}
- B: Screenshot: [Product] in action
- C: Quick look

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Figured a picture is worth 1,000 emails.

[Embedded screenshot or Loom video thumbnail with link]

This is [Product] doing [specific thing they care about].

Takes [X minutes] to set up, and you'd see [benefit] within [timeframe].

Worth 15 min to see if it fits {{company}}'s workflow?

[Your name]

P.S. If video's not your thing, happy to do a quick call instead: [calendar]
```

**Why it works:**
- Visual stands out in inbox
- Shows product without requiring commitment
- Multiple response options

---

## Email 5: Breakup with one more offer

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Should I stop reaching out?
- B: Last one from me
- C: Closing the loop

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back.

Totally understand if [Product] isn't a priority right now.

Before I move on, wanted to offer two options:

1. Quick 15-min demo: [calendar link]
2. Or, I can send you our [resource: ROI calculator, free trial, comparison guide] to review on your own time

Just reply "1" or "2" and I'll take care of it.

If neither, no worries at all. Best of luck with {{company}}.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Acknowledges their silence respectfully
- Gives easy response options
- Often converts fence-sitters

## Subject line best practices for SaaS

**Do:**
- Keep under 40 characters
- Use their company name
- Reference their problem
- Sound like a human, not marketing

**Don't:**
- Use ALL CAPS
- Use "free trial" (spam filter trigger)
- Start with numbers
- Use clickbait

---

## Demo booking tips

When they reply interested:

**Fast response matters.** Reply within 1 hour if possible.

**Good booking message:**

```
Awesome, {{firstName}}. Thanks for getting back to me.

Here's my calendar: [link]

Pick any time that works and you'll get a confirmation with the Zoom link.

Before the call, anything specific you want me to cover?

[Your name]
```

**Why ask what to cover:**
- Shows you care about their time
- Lets you prepare relevant examples
- Increases show rate

## A/B test priorities

1. **Subject lines** - Biggest impact on opens
2. **First line** - Hook or lose them
3. **CTA type** - Calendar link vs "reply to this email"
4. **Timing** - Test Day 2 vs Day 3 gaps

---

## Performance benchmarks

**SaaS averages:**
- Open rate: 40-55%
- Reply rate: 3-6%
- Demo show rate: 60-70%

**Improve opens:** Better subject lines, better sender reputation
**Improve replies:** Stronger pain point, clearer value prop
**Improve show rate:** Confirmation emails, day-before reminder

---

## Sequence variations by deal size

### Low-touch SaaS (< $1K ACV)
- Shorten to 3 emails
- Link to self-serve demo or free trial
- Less personalization needed

### Mid-market SaaS ($1K-$20K ACV)
- Use full 5-touch sequence
- Moderate personalization
- Book live demos

### Enterprise SaaS ($20K+ ACV)
- Extend to 7-touch
- Heavy personalization (account research)
- Multi-thread (reach multiple people)
- Longer timing gaps (enterprise moves slow)
