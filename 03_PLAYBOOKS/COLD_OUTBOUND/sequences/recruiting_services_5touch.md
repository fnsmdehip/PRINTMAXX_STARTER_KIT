# Recruiting services 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Recruiting agencies, headhunters, RPO providers selling to companies with open roles.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Reference their open role |
| 2 | Day 2 | Speed and cost angle |
| 3 | Day 5 | Candidate quality proof |
| 4 | Day 9 | Risk-reduction offer |
| 5 | Day 14 | Last touch |

Expected reply rate: 8-15%
Expected meeting rate: 4-7%

---

## Email 1: Open role trigger

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Your [role title] search
- B: Filling the [role title] position
- C: Candidates for {{company}}

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Saw you're hiring a [role title] at {{company}}.

We specialize in placing [type of talent] and typically fill roles like this in [X] weeks.

Currently have [X] qualified candidates in our network who could be a fit.

Worth a quick call to see if any match what you're looking for?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Timely and relevant (they have active need)
- Specific role mentioned
- Promise of speed

---

## Email 2: Speed and cost

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: Your [role title] search (reply thread)
- B: Cost of unfilled roles
- C: Quick follow-up

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Quick follow-up on the [role title] position.

Every week that role stays unfilled costs you:
- [Lost productivity/revenue estimate]
- Time spent screening unqualified candidates
- Opportunity cost of [specific impact]

We've helped companies like {{company}} cut time-to-hire from [X] weeks to [Y] weeks.

Our fee is [X%] and we offer a [guarantee period] guarantee.

Would it make sense to see our candidate pool?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Quantifies the problem
- Shows you understand urgency
- Transparent about pricing

---

## Email 3: Candidate quality proof

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Recent [role type] placements
- B: Candidate quality
- C: Who we've placed

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Want to share some recent placements to show the caliber of talent we work with:

**[Company 1]:** Placed [role] who [achievement/outcome]
**[Company 2]:** Placed [role] who [achievement/outcome]
**[Company 3]:** Placed [role] who [achievement/outcome]

Average time to placement: [X] weeks
Retention rate after 12 months: [X]%

If you're looking for this level of talent for the [role] position, we should connect.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Social proof from placements
- Quality over quantity
- Relevant to their search

---

## Email 4: Risk reduction offer

**Subject line variants:**
- A: No-risk option
- B: Guarantee offer
- C: Before you decide

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

If you're hesitant to work with a recruiter, here's how we reduce risk:

**Our guarantee:**
- [X] day guarantee on all placements
- If the hire doesn't work out, we replace them free
- You only pay when you hire someone

**Why this works for you:**
- No upfront cost
- No risk if it doesn't work
- We're incentivized to find the right fit, not just fill the seat

Still have the [role title] open? Let's talk about who we have.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Addresses "what if it doesn't work" concern
- Shows confidence in quality
- Low-risk proposition

---

## Email 5: Urgency close

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Still looking?
- B: Closing the loop
- C: One more option

**Timing:** 5 days after Email 4

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Checking in one more time on the [role title] search.

If you've already filled it: congrats, ignore this.

If still looking: The candidates I mentioned are getting snapped up. Two have already accepted offers elsewhere this week.

If there's still interest, let me know and I'll share who's still available.

If the timing isn't right, no worries. I'll keep {{company}} in mind for future roles.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Creates urgency without being pushy
- Candidate scarcity is real
- Professional close

## Finding open roles

### Where to find hiring companies:

**LinkedIn:**
- Company page job postings
- "Hiring" posts from founders/hiring managers
- Sales Navigator filter: Company headcount growth

**Job boards:**
- Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter
- Industry-specific boards
- AngelList for startups

**Triggers:**
- Funding announcements (will hire)
- New office locations (need local staff)
- Executive departures (need backfill)

### Tools:
- **HiringSignal:** Tracks job postings across companies
- **BuiltWith + Hiring:** Tech companies with open tech roles
- **LinkedIn Recruiter:** Premium features for recruiters

## Recruiter-specific personalization

### Role-specific angles:

**Engineering roles:**
```
Saw you're hiring a [role]. In this market, good [specialty] engineers
get 5+ offers. We have relationships with passive candidates who aren't
on job boards.
```

**Sales roles:**
```
Noticed the [AE/SDR/etc.] opening. We specialize in sales talent and
focus on candidates who actually have track records of hitting quota,
not just interview well.
```

**Executive roles:**
```
The [C-level/VP] search can take 6+ months if you go traditional routes.
We have a network of executives who are quietly looking. Faster and
more discreet.
```

**Niche/specialist roles:**
```
[Specialty] talent is scarce. We've built a network specifically of
[specialty] professionals over the last [X] years. Currently have
[X] qualified candidates in our database.
```

## Pricing models to mention

### Contingency (most common):
- You pay [15-25%] of first year salary
- Only pay if you hire someone
- Standard guarantee: 60-90 days

### Retained (premium):
- Upfront fee + success fee
- For executive/specialized searches
- Dedicated attention

### Hourly/RPO:
- For ongoing hiring needs
- Embedded recruiter model
- Monthly retainer

## Objection handlers

**"We use internal recruiters"**
```
Makes sense. Most of our clients do too. We typically help when:
- Internal team is at capacity
- Role is specialized/hard to fill
- Speed is critical

We can work alongside your team without stepping on toes.
```

**"We've had bad experiences with recruiters"**
```
I hear that a lot. Usually it's because the recruiter didn't understand
the role or just sent unqualified resumes.

Our process is different: [explain your vetting process].

Happy to show you our approach and you can decide if it's different enough.
```

**"Too expensive"**
```
I get it. Here's another way to think about it:

Unfilled role for 3 months = [cost calculation]
Bad hire = [cost calculation]
Our fee = [X]

If we save you time and reduce bad hire risk, the math usually works.
```

## Performance benchmarks

**Recruiting outreach to companies with open roles:**
- Open rate: 55-70%
- Reply rate: 10-18%
- Meeting rate: 5-9%

**Why high rates:**
- Timely (they have active pain)
- Specific (you know the role)
- Aligned incentives (they want to hire)
