# Video production 5-touch sequence

Use this for: Video production agencies, YouTube content, social video, corporate video.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Video gap observation |
| 2 | Day 4 | What video does |
| 3 | Day 8 | Show don't tell (samples) |
| 4 | Day 12 | Client ROI story |
| 5 | Day 17 | Simple next step |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-4%

---

## Email 1: Video gap observation

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}} video content
- B: Noticed something about {{company}}
- C: Video opportunity

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Looked at {{company}}'s marketing and noticed you don't have much video content.

In [their industry], companies using video see:
- [X%] more engagement on social
- [X%] longer time on website
- [X%] higher conversion rates

Your competitors [Name] and [Name] are using video consistently.

We help companies like {{company}} create professional video content without the headaches of production.

Worth a quick call to explore what video could do for you?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific observation
- Industry-relevant stats
- Competitive angle

---

## Email 2: What video does

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Re: {{company}} video (reply thread)
- B: Why video works
- C: The video advantage

**Timing:** 4 days after Email 1

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Quick thought on why video matters for {{company}}:

**For your website:**
- Visitors watch rather than read
- Explains complex offerings quickly
- Builds trust faster (they see you)

**For social:**
- Algorithm favors video (more reach)
- More shareable than text/image
- Builds personal connection

**For sales:**
- Send video proposals (stand out)
- Answer objections before they're asked
- Close faster with visual demonstrations

The companies winning in [their industry] are the ones showing up on video.

Want to discuss what types of video would move the needle for {{company}}?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Clear use cases
- Multiple value angles
- Relevant to their business

## Video service packages

### One-time projects:
- **Brand video:** $3-15K (company story, homepage hero)
- **Product video:** $2-8K (demo, explainer)
- **Testimonial package:** $3-10K (3-5 customer stories)
- **Event coverage:** $2-10K (conference, launch)

### Ongoing content:
- **Social content retainer:** $2-8K/month ([X] videos per month)
- **YouTube channel:** $5-15K/month (weekly episodes)
- **Podcast video:** $1-3K/month (video versions of audio)

### DIY support:
- **Equipment setup:** $1-3K (lighting, camera, mic recommendations + training)

## Finding video-hungry prospects

### Triggers that signal need:
- New funding (budget for marketing)
- New product launch
- Rebrand/new website
- Hiring marketing roles
- Competitors using video

### Observable gaps:
- No video on homepage
- No YouTube presence
- Static social content only
- Outdated existing videos
- No testimonial videos

## Objection handlers

**"We can shoot video ourselves"**
```
You definitely can. Many clients start that way.

The question is: is the result professional enough for your brand?
And is your time better spent on other things?

We also offer hybrid options. You shoot, we edit and produce.
Best of both worlds.
```

**"Video is too expensive"**
```
It can be. But it doesn't have to be.

A lot of production companies quote $20K+ for everything.
We have packages starting at $[X].

More importantly, what's the ROI? If a $5K video brings $20K in
new business, it's not an expense. It's an investment.

What budget range would work for {{company}}?
```

**"We don't know what to make"**
```
That's actually where we start.

Part of our process is video strategy. We figure out:
- What content moves the needle for your business
- What's achievable with your budget
- What order to create things in

You don't need to know what to make. That's our job.
```

**"Our industry is boring"**
```
Heard that before. Here's the thing: every industry looks boring
from the inside.

But your customers and prospects aren't bored by what you do.
They need what you sell.

The challenge is making it relatable and engaging. That's what we specialize in.
```

## Performance benchmarks

**Video production outreach:**
- Open rate: 45-55%
- Reply rate: 6-10%
- Meeting rate: 2-5%

**Why moderate:**
- Video not always top priority
- Perceived as expensive
- Longer consideration cycles

**Better results:** Target companies with clear video gaps and recent triggers (funding, launch, rebrand)
