# HR services 5-touch sequence

Use this for: HR consultants, PEOs, HR software, benefits brokers, payroll services.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Growth-triggered HR pain |
| 2 | Day 3 | Compliance risk angle |
| 3 | Day 7 | Cost savings math |
| 4 | Day 11 | Before/after story |
| 5 | Day 16 | Simple next step |

Expected reply rate: 5-10%
Expected meeting rate: 2-5%

---

## Email 1: Growth-triggered pain

**Subject line variants:**
- A: HR at {{company}}'s size
- B: Scaling from [X] to [Y] employees
- C: HR headaches

**Body:**

```
{{firstName}},

Companies around {{company}}'s size usually hit an HR wall.

[Specific pain point based on their stage]:
- "We're still doing payroll in spreadsheets"
- "I'm spending 10+ hours/week on HR instead of growing the business"
- "We're not sure if we're compliant anymore"

Sound familiar?

We help companies like {{company}} [your value prop in one sentence].

Worth a quick call to see if we can help?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Why it works:**
- Stage-specific resonance
- Common pain points hit home
- Direct question

## Service-specific variations

### PEO (Professional Employer Organization):

**Value prop focus:**
- Benefits at big company rates
- Compliance handled
- Payroll/taxes managed
- HR guidance included

**Target:** 10-100 employees, no HR team

### HR Consulting:

**Value prop focus:**
- On-demand HR expertise
- Project-based work (handbooks, policies)
- Audit and compliance review
- Management training

**Target:** Companies with basic HR but gaps

### Benefits Broker:

**Value prop focus:**
- Better benefits at lower cost
- Market shopping annually
- Employee communication
- Compliance (ACA, COBRA)

**Target:** Companies overpaying or with poor benefits

### Payroll Services:

**Value prop focus:**
- Accurate, on-time payroll
- Tax filing handled
- Integration with other systems
- Time tracking included

**Target:** Companies outgrowing manual payroll

### HR Software:

**Value prop focus:**
- All-in-one platform
- Employee self-service
- Automated workflows
- Compliance features

**Target:** Companies with multiple disconnected tools

## Finding HR-triggered leads

### Trigger events:
- **Hiring posts:** Growing = more HR complexity
- **Funding announcements:** Will hire rapidly
- **New state offices:** Multi-state compliance
- **Glassdoor reviews about HR:** Pain points visible
- **Key departures:** Might have compliance gaps

### Signals of HR pain:
- Job posting for first HR hire = founder has been doing it
- Multiple open roles = scaling fast
- Reviews mentioning "benefits" or "culture" issues
- No clear HR presence on LinkedIn

## Objection handlers

**"We use [competitor/existing solution]"**
```
How's it going? Most companies I talk to who are on [competitor] mention
[common pain point with competitor]. Is that your experience?

If you're happy, great. If not, worth comparing.
```

**"We're too small"**
```
That's actually when HR issues are most dangerous. Smaller companies:
- Have less margin for compliance mistakes
- Overpay for benefits (no scale)
- Lose key hires to companies with better benefits

The companies that get HR right early scale faster and smoother.
```

**"The founder handles it fine"**
```
How many hours a week does that take? And what else could they be
doing with that time?

Most founders I work with realize they're leaving growth on the table
by spending time on HR instead of sales, product, or strategy.
```

**"We'll hire an HR person"**
```
Good long-term plan. A few things to consider:
- HR manager salary: $60-90K
- Time to hire: 2-3 months
- Time to ramp: 3-6 months
- One person handles everything?

Our solution: [cost], starts in 2 weeks, scales with you.
When you're ready for dedicated HR, we can help hire them and transition.
```
