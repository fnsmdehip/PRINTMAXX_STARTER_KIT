# Affiliate partnership intro 4-day sequence

Use this for: Recruiting affiliates, influencers, and content creators to promote your product/service.

## Sequence overview

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Mutual value proposition |
| 2 | Day 2 | Earnings proof + specifics |
| 3 | Day 4 | Lower barrier option |
| 4 | Day 7 | Breakup |

Expected reply rate: 12-20%
Expected partnership rate: 5-10%

---

## Email 1: Mutual value opener

**Subject line variants:**
- A: {{company}} + [Your product] partnership
- B: Paid partnership idea for {{first_name}}
- C: Quick collab idea

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

I follow your content on [platform]. Your [specific content piece] was solid.

We run [Your company/product]. Looking for partners to promote it to their audience.

The deal:
- [Commission rate]% on every sale you refer
- Average affiliate earns $[X-Y]/month
- We provide all creative assets

Your audience of [their audience type] is a perfect fit.

Interested in hearing more?

[Your name]
[Your company]
```

**Example filled in:**

```
{{first_name}},

I follow your content on YouTube. Your video on productivity apps was solid.

We run TaskFlow, a project management tool for creators. Looking for partners to promote it to their audience.

The deal:
- 30% recurring commission on every sale
- Average affiliate earns $500-2,000/month
- We provide all creative assets

Your audience of productivity-focused creators is a perfect fit.

Interested in hearing more?

[Your name]
TaskFlow
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows you know their content
- Clear value proposition
- Specific earnings range

- [Simple action 1]

- [Simple action 3]

Want me to set up your affiliate link?

[Your name]

## Email 3: Lower the barrier

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Simpler option
- B: No commitment needed
- C: Just try it

**Timing:** 2 days after Email 2

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Maybe a full partnership feels like too much commitment right now. Get it.

Here's an easier start:

1. I give you free access to [Product] (no strings)
2. You try it out
3. If you like it, share it naturally with your audience
4. I set up your affiliate link and you earn on any signups

No pressure to promote. No content requirements.

If you end up not liking it, no hard feelings.

Want me to set up free access?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Removes commitment barrier
- Product-led approach
- Still sets up affiliate relationship

---

## Email 4: The breakup

**Subject line variants:**
- A: Last one from me
- B: Moving on
- C: Quick close

**Timing:** 3 days after Email 3

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Haven't heard back, so I'll assume the timing's off.

No worries. If you ever want to explore the affiliate program, just reply to this thread.

The offer stands:
- [Commission rate]% recurring
- Free product access
- No minimum commitment

Good luck with your content.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Clean close
- Keeps door open
- Reminds them of offer

## Affiliate program specifics to include

**Commission structure options:**
- Flat rate per sale ($X per signup)
- Percentage per sale (20-40%)
- Recurring commission (% of MRR)
- Tiered rates (increases with volume)

**What to provide affiliates:**
- Unique tracking link
- Creative assets (banners, copy, videos)
- Product access for review
- Performance dashboard
- Dedicated affiliate manager

---

## A/B test priorities

1. **Commission rate** - Test 25% vs 30% vs 35%
2. **Subject line** - Partnership vs Collab vs Paid opportunity
3. **Earnings proof** - Real names vs anonymized
4. **CTA** - "Set up your link" vs "Want to chat?"

---

## Performance benchmarks

**Affiliate outreach typically converts well:**
- Open rate: 50-65%
- Reply rate: 12-22%
- Partnership rate: 5-12%

**Why:** You're offering them money, not asking for it.

## Where to find affiliates

### Content creators
- YouTube creators in your niche
- Newsletter writers with engaged lists
- Podcast hosts
- TikTok/Instagram content creators

### Finding them
- Search "[your category] review" on YouTube
- Look at who writes about competitors
- Check affiliate directories (ShareASale, Impact)
- Reddit/community moderators

### Qualification checklist
- [ ] Audience matches your ICP
- [ ] Content is quality (not spammy)
- [ ] Active posting schedule
- [ ] Engaged comments/followers
- [ ] No conflicts with competitors

## Sequence variations

### For SaaS products
- Emphasize recurring commission
- Offer extended trials for review
- Provide demo accounts

### For info products/courses
- Higher commission rates (40-50%)
- Provide preview access
- Offer affiliate-exclusive bonuses

### For physical products
- Flat fee per sale
- Send free product for review
- Offer discount codes for their audience

### For services
- Per-referral fee
- Offer intro calls for their referrals
- Revenue share on closed deals
