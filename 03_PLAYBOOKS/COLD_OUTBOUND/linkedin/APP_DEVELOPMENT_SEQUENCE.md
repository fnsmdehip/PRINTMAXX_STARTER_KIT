# LinkedIn sequence: App development services

Connection request + 3 follow-up messages for selling custom mobile app development to businesses. Target: founders, CTOs, product managers at companies without a mobile app or with an outdated one.

---

## Connection request (under 300 chars)

**Variant A: Gap identification**
```
{{firstName}}, noticed {{company}} doesn't have a mobile app yet. We build custom apps in 4-6 weeks for a fraction of the usual cost. Worth connecting?
```
*Characters: ~130*

**Variant B: Industry angle**
```
{{firstName}}, we build mobile apps for {{industry}} companies. Just shipped one with 4,200 downloads in month one. Would love to connect.
```
*Characters: ~115*

**Variant C: Trigger-based**
```
{{firstName}}, saw {{company}} is {{trigger}}. Companies at your stage usually benefit from a mobile app. We build them fast and affordable. Connect?
```
*Characters: ~135*

**Variant D: Competitive**
```
{{firstName}}, noticed some of {{company}}'s competitors just launched mobile apps. We help businesses catch up fast. Worth connecting?
```
*Characters: ~120*

## Target titles

- Founder, Co-Founder, CEO
- CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Product
- COO, VP Operations
- Director of Digital, Head of Digital
- Marketing Director (if no technical leadership)

## Target company signals

- No mobile app in App Store/Google Play
- Growing company (hiring, funding, expanding)
- E-commerce or service business with repeat customers
- Competitor recently launched an app
