# LinkedIn InMail templates for cold outreach

InMails cost credits. Use for high-value prospects you can't reach through connection requests. These must convert higher than DMs to justify the cost.

---

## InMail rules

- Keep under 100 words
- Subject line under 50 chars
- One clear ask per InMail
- Save for prospects worth $5k+ in potential deal value
- Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am their timezone
- Personalize the first line (no generic openers)

## InMail credit strategy

**Sales Navigator credits: 50/month on Professional, unlimited on Team+**

Allocate credits by tier:

| Tier | Credits/month | Target | Expected deals |
|------|---------------|--------|----------------|
| Tier 1 (highest value) | 20 | C-suite at 100+ employee companies | 1-2 meetings |
| Tier 2 (high value) | 20 | Directors/VPs at growth-stage | 3-5 meetings |
| Tier 3 (test) | 10 | New verticals/offers | 1-2 meetings |

**Save credits by:**
- Using connection requests first (free)
- Only InMailing when connection request was rejected or no mutual connections
- Batch sending on Tuesday-Thursday (higher reply rates = better credit efficiency)

---

## A/B test priorities

1. Subject lines: company name vs benefit vs curiosity
2. First line: observation vs compliment vs question
3. CTA: "15 min call" vs "want me to send more info" vs "worth a look?"
4. Length: 80 words vs 120 words
