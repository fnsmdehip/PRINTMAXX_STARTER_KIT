## Offer 1: AI content agency (SMBs)

### ICA (Ideal Customer Avatar)
- **Who:** Marketing manager or founder at SMBs doing content poorly or not at all
- **Pain:** Posting inconsistently, only on 1-2 platforms, no content strategy
- **Budget:** $1,500-$3,000/mo for content services

### Apollo/ZoomInfo filters

| Filter | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Company size | 10-200 employees |
| Industry | Retail, Professional Services, Health & Wellness, Food & Beverage, Real Estate, Home Services, Education |
| Revenue | $500K-$50M |
| Job titles | Marketing Manager, Marketing Director, Head of Marketing, CMO, Founder, CEO, Owner |
| Geography | US (start), expand to UK/Canada/Australia |
| Tech stack | Shopify, WordPress, Squarespace (signals they have a web presence but may lack content) |
| Signals | Recently hired marketing role, recently raised funding, recently launched website |

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator filters

```
Title: ("Marketing Manager" OR "Marketing Director" OR "Head of Marketing" OR "CMO" OR "Founder" OR "CEO")
Company size: 11-200
Industry: All industries except Mining, Oil, Utilities, Government
Geography: United States
Keywords: content, social media, marketing
Posted on LinkedIn: Past 30 days (active users more likely to reply)
```

### BuiltWith signals
- Has Shopify/WordPress/Squarespace
- Does NOT have HubSpot, Hootsuite, Sprout Social (means no content tool = bigger pain)
- Has Facebook Pixel (running ads but possibly no organic strategy)

### Estimated list size
- Apollo: 50,000-100,000 contacts matching filters
- Pull: 500-1,000 per week
- After verification: 85-90% valid

### Exclusions
- Companies with 5+ social media posts/week (already handled)
- Companies with "Social Media Manager" on staff
- Marketing agencies (they do this themselves)
- Companies under 10 employees (too small, low budget)

| Industry | Health & Wellness, Fitness, Food & Beverage, Retail, Real Estate, Education, Financial Services, Professional Services |
| Revenue | $1M-$100M |
| Job titles | Founder, CEO, CTO, VP Product, VP Engineering, Head of Digital, COO |

For top 100 prospects each week:
1. Search company name in App Store
2. Search in Google Play Store
3. Only keep companies with NO app listed
4. Note if competitors in their space have apps (stronger pitch)

```
Title: ("Founder" OR "CEO" OR "CTO" OR "VP Product" OR "Head of Digital" OR "COO")
Company size: 11-500
Industry: All except Technology (they build their own)
Keywords: mobile, app, digital transformation
Growth: Company growing (headcount increase past 12 months)
```

### Estimated list size
- Apollo: 80,000-150,000 contacts
- After App Store verification: 60-70% have no app
- Pull: 500-1,000 per week
- After verification: 85-90% valid emails

### Exclusions
- Software/tech companies (build their own)
- Companies with existing apps
- Companies under 10 employees (low budget)
- Startups pre-revenue (can't afford $5k)

## Email verification requirements

Before loading ANY list into sending tools:
- Run through ZeroBounce or NeverBounce
- Remove all "unknown" and "catch-all" results
- Only send to "valid" emails
- Target: under 3% bounce rate
- Cost: ~$0.008 per verification ($8 per 1,000 contacts)
