# Smartlead AI cold email infrastructure spec

Built from SYN035 (AI Cold Email Quality Stack, score 94), ALPHA972901 (Cody Schneider $1M ARR with 2 channels), ALPHA972911 (Instantly 3.43% avg, <50 recipients = 5.8%), ALPHA972920 (engagement quality > volume), ALPHA429 (Gmail RETVec 0.1%), ALPHA461 (45-word emails 58% replies from step 1), ALPHA468 (legal services 10% reply, AI personalization 7x).

**Bottom line:** Smartlead replaces Instantly for our Tier 1 stack. 2.7x reply rate improvement, unlimited inboxes, intent-signal-led campaigns with <50 recipients each, AI-generated unique body copy per recipient. Legal services ICP first (10% baseline). This spec covers full infrastructure from domain purchase to first AI-personalized send.

### Inbox setup

2 Google Workspace inboxes per domain = 10 inboxes minimum.

| Domain | Inbox 1 | Inbox 2 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| localgrowthpartner.com | alex@localgrowthpartner.com | jordan@localgrowthpartner.com |
| reviewgrowthpro.com | alex@reviewgrowthpro.com | sam@reviewgrowthpro.com |
| leadflowagency.com | chris@leadflowagency.com | morgan@leadflowagency.com |
| faithgrowthco.com | alex@faithgrowthco.com | jamie@faithgrowthco.com |
| fitnessgrowthlab.com | taylor@fitnessgrowthlab.com | casey@fitnessgrowthlab.com |

**Naming rules:**
- First-name-only emails convert better than full names
- Use common, gender-neutral names (Alex, Jordan, Sam, Chris, Morgan, Jamie, Taylor, Casey)
- Each inbox gets its own signature with name, title, phone
- Never use info@, support@, hello@ for cold email

**Cost:** 10 inboxes x $7.20/mo Google Workspace Starter = $72/mo

### DNS records per domain

```
SPF:   v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com include:_spf.smartlead.ai ~all
DKIM:  Generated by Google Workspace (2048-bit)
DMARC: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@[domain].com; pct=100
```

**Checklist per domain:**
- [ ] SPF record added (include both Google and Smartlead)
- [ ] DKIM record generated and verified in Google Workspace admin
- [ ] DMARC record added (start with p=none, move to p=quarantine at Day 30)
- [ ] Custom tracking domain in Smartlead (track.[domain].com CNAME)
- [ ] Verified in Google Postmaster Tools
- [ ] MX records pointing to Google

### Website setup per domain

Cold email domains need a basic website. Google checks.

**Minimum viable site (15 minutes per domain):**
- Simple Carrd.co page ($19/yr) or static HTML on Cloudflare Pages (free)
- Company name + 2-sentence description + contact info
- Privacy policy page (use free generator)
- SSL certificate (auto via Cloudflare)

This isn't for conversions. It's so Gmail doesn't flag you as a fly-by-night domain.

## Phase 2: Smartlead configuration (Week 1)

### Account setup

1. Sign up Smartlead Pro ($94/mo)
2. Connect all 10 Google Workspace inboxes
3. Enable warmup on ALL inboxes immediately
4. Set warmup settings:
   - Start: 5/day per inbox
   - Ramp: +3/day increase
   - Target: 40/day per inbox (never exceed 50)
   - Reply rate target: 30%+ on warmup emails
   - Warmup duration: 14 days minimum before cold sends

### Sending configuration

```
Per inbox limits:
  - Max 50 emails/day (hard ceiling per ALPHA468)
  - Sending window: 7:00 AM - 11:00 AM recipient local time
  - Time between emails: 90-180 second random delay
  - Weekend sending: OFF (B2B only)

Per campaign limits:
  - Max 48 recipients per campaign (under 50 threshold per ALPHA972911)
  - 3-5 email sequence per campaign
  - 2-3 day gap between emails in sequence
  - 14-day max campaign duration

Global limits:
  - Max 500 sends/day across all inboxes
  - Complaint rate ceiling: 0.05% (half of Google's 0.1% threshold for safety margin)
  - Bounce rate ceiling: 3%
  - Auto-pause if either threshold hit
```

### Campaign structure (micro-campaigns)

**The <50 recipient rule is the core differentiator.** ALPHA972911 confirmed: campaigns with <50 recipients get 69% higher reply rates (5.8% vs 3.43%).

```
WRONG: 1 campaign → 500 recipients → blast
RIGHT: 10 micro-campaigns → 48 recipients each → intent-matched

Campaign naming convention:
[ICP]_[intent_signal]_[date]_[variant]

Examples:
legal_new_hire_20260215_A
legal_bad_reviews_20260215_B
dental_no_ads_20260220_A
dental_growth_signal_20260220_B
```

Each micro-campaign targets ONE intent signal:
- Recently hired (growth signal)
- Low Google reviews (pain point)
- No paid ads running (budget available)
- Recent funding (cash to spend)
- Technology change (implementation need)
- Competitor just raised prices (switching window)

### Reply categorization (Smartlead AI)

Smartlead auto-categorizes replies:

| Category | Action | SLA |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Interested | Move to CRM, respond within 2 hours | 2h |
| Meeting request | Send Calendly link immediately | 30min |
| More info needed | Send relevant case study | 4h |
| Not interested | Add to nurture (Beehiiv newsletter) | 24h |
| OOO | Re-queue for follow-up on return date | Auto |
| Unsubscribe | Remove immediately + log | Instant |
| Wrong person | Ask for referral to right person | 4h |
| Angry/negative | Apologize + remove + blacklist domain | 1h |

## Phase 3: Clay enrichment pipeline (Week 2)

### Clay setup ($50-100/mo)

Clay replaces manual prospect research. 150+ data providers in one interface.

**Enrichment workflow:**

```
INPUT: Lead list from Apollo (name, email, company, title)
    ↓
STEP 1: Company enrichment
    - BuiltWith (tech stack)
    - Crunchbase (funding, headcount)
    - Google (recent news)
    - LinkedIn Company (employee count, growth rate)
    ↓
STEP 2: Person enrichment
    - LinkedIn profile (recent posts, activity)
    - Twitter/X (if public)
    - Published content (articles, podcasts, interviews)
    - Job change history
    ↓
STEP 3: Intent signal extraction
    - Currently hiring? (growth signal)
    - Recent funding? (cash to spend)
    - Competitor using similar service? (warm lead)
    - Google review count vs competitors? (pain point)
    - Running paid ads? (marketing budget exists)
    ↓
STEP 4: AI personalization
    Clay AI Writer generates:
    - Personalized first line (15-25 words, specific reference)
    - Custom case study match (their industry + size)
    - Unique email body variation (avoids RETVec pattern detection)
    ↓
OUTPUT: Enriched CSV → Smartlead import
```

### Clay AI Writer prompt

```
You write cold email opening lines for B2B outreach. Rules:
1. 15-25 words maximum
2. Reference ONE specific thing about the prospect (achievement, content, company news)
3. No flattery or generic compliments
4. No "I noticed" or "I saw" openers (overused)
5. Start with their situation, not about you
6. Sound like a human texting a colleague, not a salesperson

Prospect data:
Name: {{name}}
Title: {{title}}
Company: {{company}}
Industry: {{industry}}
Company size: {{employee_count}}
Recent news: {{recent_news}}
Their content: {{recent_linkedin_post}}
Tech stack: {{tech_stack}}
Google reviews: {{review_count}} (competitor avg: {{competitor_review_avg}})
Currently hiring: {{hiring_roles}}

Write ONE opening line. No greeting.
```

### Clay → Smartlead integration

Export enriched CSV from Clay with these columns:

```csv
email,first_name,last_name,company,title,industry,ai_first_line,ai_body_variation,case_study_match,intent_signal,campaign_tag
```

Import to Smartlead using CSV upload. Map merge tags:
- `{{ai_first_line}}` → personalized opening
- `{{ai_body_variation}}` → unique body per recipient
- `{{case_study_match}}` → industry-specific proof point
- `{{intent_signal}}` → the reason we're reaching out now

| Recent funding | Crunchbase alerts | "growth investment" angle | MEDIUM |

| Content published | LinkedIn/blog monitoring | "thought leadership" angle | LOW |

## Phase 6: Multichannel integration (Week 3-4)

### LinkedIn parallel sequence (Expandi $99/mo)

Runs alongside email. Conditional branching based on email engagement.

```
Day -2: LinkedIn connection request
  - Note: "{{first_name}}, I work with {{industry}} firms on [specific]. Thought we should connect."
  - Max 20-25 connections/day

Day 1: Profile view (automated warm touch)

Day 3: Like/comment their recent post
  - Genuine comment, not generic
  - 10-15 words minimum

Day 5: LinkedIn DM (if email not replied)
  - "Sent you a note about {{topic}} earlier this week. Wanted to make sure it didn't get buried."
  - Keep under 50 words

Day 10: Voice note (if no response)
  - 30 seconds max
  - "Hey {{first_name}}, this is {{sender_name}}. Noticed {{specific thing}} and thought..."
  - End with "no pressure, just flagging it"
```

### Conditional branching

```
IF email replied (interested) → Skip LinkedIn DM, go to CRM
IF email replied (not interested) → Skip all LinkedIn
IF email bounced → LinkedIn-only sequence
IF email opened but no reply → LinkedIn DM on Day 5
IF zero engagement → LinkedIn voice note on Day 10
IF LinkedIn accepted → Warm email follow-up (different from cold sequence)
```

## Phase 7: Monitoring and optimization (Ongoing)

### Daily monitoring checklist (5 minutes)

- [ ] Check Smartlead dashboard: delivery rate >98%, bounce <3%
- [ ] Check Google Postmaster Tools: domain reputation
- [ ] Review AI-categorized replies: respond to "interested" within 2 hours
- [ ] Check complaint rate: must stay under 0.05%
- [ ] Review today's scheduled sends: nothing weird queued

### Weekly monitoring (30 minutes)

- [ ] Reply rate per campaign (target: >5%, legal should be >10%)
- [ ] Meeting rate per campaign (target: >30% of replies)
- [ ] A/B test results (declare winner at 200+ sends per variant)
- [ ] Warmup health across all inboxes
- [ ] Lead quality audit (are intent signals accurate?)
- [ ] Update LEDGER/FUNNEL_METRICS.csv

### Kill criteria

| Metric | Threshold | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Reply rate <2% after 200 sends | Campaign problem | Rewrite copy, check ICP |
| Reply rate <1% after 500 sends | Channel problem | Audit deliverability, check domains |
| Complaint rate >0.08% | Emergency | Pause ALL sends, investigate |
| Bounce rate >5% | List quality | Switch verification provider |
| Meeting rate <10% of replies | Qualification problem | Tighten ICP scoring (min 8/15) |
| Zero meetings after 30 days | Kill decision | Pivot vertical or review entire stack |

### A/B testing priority

Per AI_PERSONALIZATION_STACK_2026.md testing framework. One test at a time, 2-week cycles, 200+ sends per variant.

| Week | Test | Variant A | Variant B |
|------|------|-----------|-----------|
| 3-4 | Campaign size | <50 recipients | 100 recipients |
| 5-6 | Personalization | AI first line only | AI full body |
| 7-8 | CTA | Question ("does this match?") | Direct ("15 min call?") |
| 9-10 | Multichannel | Email only | Email + LinkedIn |
| 11-12 | Word count | 45 words | 80 words |
