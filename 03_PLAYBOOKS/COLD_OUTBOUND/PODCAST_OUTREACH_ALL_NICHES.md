## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Pitching without listening** - Hosts know immediately. They get dozens of irrelevant pitches daily.
2. **Generic pitch copy** - "I'd love to be on your show" works 0% of the time.
3. **No specific angle** - "Talk about my app" is boring. "Here's why 90% of X fails" is compelling.
4. **Not prepared** - Don't pitch unless you have content ready (pre-recorded segment, talking points).
5. **Giving up after rejection** - 70% of hosts say no to the first pitch. Follow-up 3-4 times.
6. **Not tracking results** - You won't know which podcasts drove actual value. Track everything.
7. **Too salesy** - Podcast guests sell through value, not pitches. Lead with education, not offers.
