Clean list ready for campaigns

|--------|--------|-------|

## Legal Considerations

### GDPR (Europe)

- Need legitimate interest basis
- Document your justification
- Provide opt-out
- Don't scrape excessively

### CAN-SPAM (US)

- Provide unsubscribe
- Include physical address
- Don't use deceptive headers

### CASL (Canada)

- Need consent (implied or express)
- Document consent records
- Stricter than US rules

### Best Practice

- Assume strict compliance
- Document everything
- Easy unsubscribe always
- Respect opt-outs immediately
