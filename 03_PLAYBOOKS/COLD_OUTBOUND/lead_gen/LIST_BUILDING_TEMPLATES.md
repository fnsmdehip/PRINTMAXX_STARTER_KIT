Copy-paste these search queries into Apollo, Sales Navigator, or LinkedIn to find your ICP. Adjust filters for your specific market.

## Exclusion filters

### Always exclude
- Competitors (by name)
- Enterprise companies (1000+ employees) unless that's your target
- Current customers
- Previous bounces/unsubscribes
- Catch-all domains
- Role-based emails (info@, sales@, support@)

### Apollo exclusion syntax
```
NOT "consultant" NOT "freelancer" NOT "intern"
```

### Sales Navigator exclusions
Use the "Exclude" option in filters to remove:
- Specific companies
- Specific industries
- Specific seniority levels

## Quality check filters

### High-quality lead indicators
- Has LinkedIn profile photo
- Profile updated in last 6 months
- Company has website
- Email is verified (not catch-all)
- Employee count matches company size claims

### Red flags to filter out
- No company website
- Generic company names ("Consulting LLC")
- Employee count = 1 but title is "VP"
- Personal email domain (gmail, yahoo, hotmail)

## Sample list sizes

For a 4-week campaign sending 500 emails/week:

**Needed:** 2,000 verified leads

**Search breakdown:**
- Primary ICP search: 1,200 leads
- Trigger event leads: 400 leads
- Second tier ICP: 400 leads

**Budget:**
- Apollo credits: ~2,500 (with some waste)
- Verification: ~$20
- Total: ~$100 in data costs for 1 month of campaigns

## Quick start template

### Step 1: Define ICP
```
Target title: ________________
Target industry: ________________
Company size: ________________
Geography: ________________
Trigger events: ________________
```

### Step 2: Build Apollo search
```
1. Job Titles: [3-5 title variations]
2. Industries: [2-3 related industries]
3. Employee Count: [range]
4. Technologies: [if relevant]
5. Keywords: [if needed for specificity]
```

### Step 3: Export and verify
```
1. Export 500 leads
2. Run through ZeroBounce
3. Remove bounces (target < 3%)
4. Clean duplicates
5. Ready for campaigns
```

### Step 4: Refine
```
After first campaign:
- Which titles responded best?
- Which company sizes converted?
- What made high-quality leads different?
- Update search accordingly
```
