## Invoice numbering system

**Format:** INV-YYYY-XXX

- YYYY = Year
- XXX = Sequential number (reset each year or continuous)

**Examples:**
- INV-2024-001
- INV-2024-002
- INV-2024-003

**Alternative format:** [INITIALS]-YYYY-MM-XXX
- JD-2024-01-001 (John Doe, January 2024, first invoice)

## Tax considerations

**Note:** Consult a tax professional for your situation.

**General guidance:**
- Track all income
- Issue 1099s to US contractors over $600
- Keep records of all invoices
- Separate business and personal accounts
- Set aside 25-30% for taxes

**For international clients:**
- Usually no sales tax/VAT for services
- Still taxable income for you
- May need W-8BEN from international clients
