# Standard Project Timelines

Standard timelines for each service. Adjust based on package tier and scope.

---

## General timeline rules

1. **Kickoff call is Day 0** - Timeline starts after kickoff
2. **Client feedback windows are 48 hours** - Build this into estimates
3. **Buffer 1-2 days** - Things take longer than expected
4. **Warmup periods are fixed** - Email warmup cannot be rushed
5. **Communicate delays immediately** - Don't wait until deadline day

## Timeline communication templates

### Kickoff email with timeline

Subject: Project timeline: [PROJECT NAME]

---

Hey [NAME],

Great kickoff call! Here's the project timeline:

**Week 1:**
- [ACTIVITY 1]
- [ACTIVITY 2]

**Week 2:**
- [ACTIVITY 3]
- Review window (need your feedback by [DATE])

**Week 3:**
- [ACTIVITY 4]
- Training call: [DATE/TIME]

Key dates:
- Your feedback needed by: [DATE]
- Expected completion: [DATE]
- Training call: [DATE]

Let me know if any of these dates don't work.

[YOUR NAME]
