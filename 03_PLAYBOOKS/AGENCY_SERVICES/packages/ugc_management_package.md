## Questions

**How much should I budget for creators?**
Depends on quality level. Beginners: $30-75/video. Mid-tier: $75-150. Established: $150-300+. We help you find the right balance.

**Do you own the content rights?**
No, you do. We handle rights agreements but ownership transfers to you. Standard usage rights for ads and organic.

**Can I use my own creators?**
Yes. We can manage creators you've already found. Just include them in your brief allocation.

**What if a creator delivers bad content?**
We coordinate revisions. If they can't deliver after revisions, we replace them. That's why we over-source.

**How fast can I get content?**
Standard turnaround: 7-14 days from brief to final. Rush available for 50% premium.
