## Questions

**How soon will I see results?**
Keyword ranking changes typically show within 2-4 weeks. Conversion improvements can be immediate after implementing screenshot and copy changes.

**Do you manage the implementation?**
We provide the optimized copy and recommendations. You implement in App Store Connect/Google Play Console. We can do a screenshare to walk you through it.

**What if I have multiple apps?**
We offer a 15% discount for bundling multiple apps. Each app gets its own full analysis.

**Do I need ASO tools?**
Not required. We use professional tools for research. If you want ongoing tracking, we recommend AppTweak or App Radar (starts around $50/month).
