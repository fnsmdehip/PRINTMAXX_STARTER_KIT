## Implementation Framework

### Delivery Template for Any Service

**Step 1: Intake (Days 1-2)**
- Send client questionnaire
- Schedule 30-min discovery call (if needed)
- Document specific requirements

**Step 2: Customization (Days 3-5)**
- Claude generates personalized content/strategy
- Create templates, systems, or documents
- Prepare training materials

**Step 3: Delivery (Days 6-X)**
- Deliver all assets
- Record Loom walkthrough video
- Conduct training/handoff call

**Step 4: Support**
- Email support for 30 days
- Scheduled check-in calls
- Refinement/optimization

### Fulfillment Tools Stack
- **Content Generation:** Claude (customization, writing)
- **Automation:** Zapier/Make (scheduling, reminders, data flow)
- **Video/Training:** Loom (recorded walkthroughs), Canva (visuals)
- **Design:** Notion, Airtable (templates, dashboards)
- **Email:** Mailchimp, Zapier automation
- **Tracking:** Google Sheets, Airtable
- **Integration:** API connectors, Zapier

### Scaling Strategy

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** Launch 3 services per niche to test market fit
- Pick highest-leverage service
- Deliver 5-10 manual clients
- Document exact process

**Phase 2 (Months 4-6):** Productize and systematize
- Create standard templates
- Build Claude prompts for customization
- Set up Zapier automation flows

**Phase 3 (Months 7+):** Scale with VA support
- Train VA on service delivery
- Create operations manual
- Add to sales page

**Pricing Model:**
- Services priced to deliver 70% gross margin
- Higher margins on high-touch services (consulting)
- Lower margins on high-volume services (content)
- Tiered pricing (basic/premium/elite) within each category

## Key Success Factors

1. **Fixed scope = predictable margins** - No scope creep on price
2. **Repeatable process = leverage** - Use same Claude prompts for 100 clients
3. **AI assists fulfillment = profitability** - Not doing manual work for each client
4. **Clear deliverables = easy selling** - Clients know exactly what they get
5. **Automated delivery = scale** - Zapier/email handles most touchpoints
6. **Quarterly pricing = retention** - Ongoing revenue vs. one-time sales
