# Content Engine Setup - Upsells and Retainer Options

## Immediate upsells (offer at proposal stage)

### 1. Scheduling integration - $100

**Pitch:** "Instead of copying outputs manually, I can connect to [Buffer/Hypefury/Publer] so posts queue automatically."

**Includes:**
- Connection to scheduling tool
- Default posting times setup
- Draft vs. auto-publish configuration

**When to offer:** Always with Silver or Gold packages.

---

### 2. Additional output format - $75 each

**Pitch:** "You mentioned wanting [Instagram/TikTok/email]. I can add that format for $75."

**Includes:**
- AI prompt for new format
- Integrated into existing workflow
- Documentation update

**When to offer:** When client mentions platforms not in proposal.

---

### 3. Voice deep-dive - $150

**Pitch:** "For best results, I can do a deeper voice analysis. I'll review 20+ pieces of your content and create a comprehensive voice guide you can use for any content."

**Includes:**
- Analysis of 20+ content pieces
- Detailed voice document (vocabulary, patterns, tone)
- Prompt optimization based on analysis
- Voice guide for future use (even outside this system)

**When to offer:** Clients who care deeply about brand voice consistency.

---

## Post-project upsells

### 4. Prompt optimization - $200

**Pitch (send at week 1):** "Now that you've run content through the system, I can review outputs and refine prompts for better results."

**Includes:**
- Review of 10+ generated outputs
- Prompt adjustments based on patterns
- A/B variations for testing
- 30-minute review call

**When to offer:** 7-10 days after delivery.

---

### 5. New content source - $200

**Pitch:** "Ready to add [podcast/video/new blog] to your engine? I can extend the system."

**Includes:**
- New input connection
- Prompts adapted for that content type
- Integration with existing workflows

**When to offer:** When client starts creating new content types.

---

### 6. Monthly content review - $150/month

**Pitch:** "I can review your outputs monthly and keep prompts optimized as your voice evolves."

**Includes:**
- Monthly review of 20+ outputs
- Prompt adjustments as needed
- New format suggestions
- Priority support

**When to offer:** At project close or 30 days after.

## Conversion benchmarks

Typical rates:
- Scheduling integration: 40-50% of Silver/Gold
- Additional format: 30% at proposal, 50% post-project
- Voice deep-dive: 15-20%
- Prompt optimization: 35-45%
- Monthly review: 20-25%

**Highest ROI:** Prompt optimization (easy delivery) and scheduling integration (natural add-on).
