# Content Engine Setup Service

## Overview

We build content repurposing systems that turn one piece of content into 10+. Write once, publish everywhere, automatically.

## What you get

### Core deliverables
- n8n/Make workflow for content transformation
- AI prompts for each content format
- Templates for all output types
- Publishing integrations (social, email, blog)
- 45-minute training session
- 7-14 days of support

### What the system does
- Take long-form content (blog, video transcript, podcast)
- Transform into multiple formats:
  - Twitter/X threads
  - LinkedIn posts
  - Instagram captions
  - Email newsletter sections
  - Short-form video scripts
  - Quote graphics (text extraction)

## Who this is for

- Content creators posting manually to multiple platforms
- Solopreneurs who write blogs but struggle with social
- Podcasters who want written content from episodes
- Anyone spending 5+ hours/week on content distribution

## Who this is NOT for

- Businesses without existing content to repurpose
- Those who need content strategy (this is execution)
- Anyone expecting AI to create fully original content
