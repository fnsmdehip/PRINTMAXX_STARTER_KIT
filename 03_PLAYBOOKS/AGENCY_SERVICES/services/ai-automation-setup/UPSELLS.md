# AI Automation Setup - Upsells and Retainer Options

## Immediate upsells (offer at proposal stage)

### 1. Monthly maintenance retainer - $200/month

**Pitch:** "Automations need occasional updates when tools change or your process evolves. This keeps everything running smoothly."

**Includes:**
- Up to 4 hours of maintenance work
- Tool integration updates when APIs change
- Minor workflow modifications
- Performance monitoring
- Priority support (4-hour response)

**When to offer:** Always. Best conversion after Gold package.

---

### 2. Additional workflows - $200 each

**Pitch:** "You mentioned [OTHER PAIN POINT] during our call. I can add that for $200."

**Includes:**
- 1 additional workflow
- Documentation
- Included in training session

**When to offer:** When client mentions problems outside original scope.

---

### 3. Team training add-on - $100/person

**Pitch:** "If [OTHER TEAM MEMBER] will also use this, I can include them in a dedicated session."

**Includes:**
- 30-minute 1-on-1 training
- Recording for future reference
- Email support during main support period

**When to offer:** When client mentions team members.

---

## Post-project upsells (offer after delivery)

### 4. Optimization sprint - $500

**Pitch (send at week 2):** "Now that you've used the system for 2 weeks, I can review the data and optimize for better results."

**Includes:**
- Analysis of workflow performance
- Prompt optimization based on real outputs
- Speed/reliability improvements
- 2-3 refinements
- 30-minute review call

**When to offer:** 2 weeks after project completion.

---

### 5. New workflow development - project rate

**Pitch:** "Ready to automate [NEXT AREA]? I'll give you 15% off since we already have your systems documented."

**Includes:**
- Return client discount (15%)
- Faster setup (existing access)
- Integrated with current workflows

**When to offer:** 30 days after project, or when client mentions new pain point.

---

### 6. Full system audit - $300

**Pitch:** "I'll review all your automation and tech stack, then give you a prioritized plan for the next 6 months."

**Includes:**
- 2-hour deep dive into current systems
- Audit document with findings
- Prioritized recommendations
- 45-minute strategy call
- Rough estimates for each recommendation

**When to offer:** 60-90 days after project, or when client seems ready to expand.

---

## Conversion benchmarks

Typical upsell conversion rates:
- Maintenance retainer: 25-35% of project clients
- Additional workflows: 40% (when pitched during proposal)
- Team training: 15-20%
- Optimization sprint: 20-30%
- New project: 15-20% at 30 days, 30-40% at 90 days

**Focus on:** Maintenance retainer (recurring) and optimization sprint (easy delivery, high margin).
