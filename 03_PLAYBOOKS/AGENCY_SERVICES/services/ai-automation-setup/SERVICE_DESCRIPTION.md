## What you get

### Core deliverables
- Custom n8n/Make workflow setup

- Connection to your existing tools (CRM, email, spreadsheets)
- Documentation for every workflow
- 60-minute training session
- 14 days of support after delivery

### Typical automations we build
- Lead qualification and scoring
- Email response drafting
- Content generation pipelines
- Data extraction and organization
- Report generation
- Social media scheduling
- Customer support triage

## Who this is for

- Solopreneurs spending 10+ hours/week on repetitive tasks
- Small teams (2-10 people) with manual data entry

## Who this is NOT for
