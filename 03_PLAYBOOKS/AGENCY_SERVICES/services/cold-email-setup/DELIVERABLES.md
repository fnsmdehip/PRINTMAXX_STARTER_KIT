## Deliverable specifications

### Domain standards
Every domain includes:
- Clean registration (no prior spam history)
- SPF record (authorize sending)
- DKIM signing (email authentication)
- DMARC policy (p=none initially)
- Verified in MXToolbox

### Email account standards
Every account includes:
- Professional naming (firstname@domain)
- Profile photo
- Email signature
- Connected to sending tool
- Daily limits configured

### Warmup standards
- Minimum 2 weeks before sending
- Gradual volume increase
- Reply engagement enabled
- Monitored daily during warmup
- Warmup continues after launch

### Sequence standards
Every sequence includes:
- Subject line A/B variants
- Hook-first opening
- Clear value proposition
- Single CTA per email
- Follow-up logic
- Breakup email final

### Lead list standards
Every list includes:
- 95%+ email validity
- Job title verification
- Company match to ICP
- No catch-all addresses (removed)
- Formatted for sending tool
