# Cold Email Setup Service

## Overview

We build complete cold email infrastructure: warmed domains, authenticated inboxes, proven sequences, and targeted lead lists. You get a system ready to book meetings.

## What you get

### Core deliverables
- Domain purchase and DNS configuration
- Email account setup with proper authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Inbox warmup (2-4 weeks)
- Email sequence copywriting (3-5 emails)
- Lead list with verified emails (100-500 contacts)
- Sending tool configuration
- Campaign launch and monitoring

### Technical setup
- Secondary domains (protect your main domain)
- Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 accounts
- Warmup tool integration (Instantly, Warmbox)
- Sending tool setup (Instantly, Lemlist, or similar)
- Inbox rotation configuration

## Who this is for

- B2B businesses wanting to book meetings via cold email
- Agencies looking to scale outbound
- Consultants and freelancers needing a steady lead pipeline
- Anyone who tried cold email and landed in spam

## Who this is NOT for

- B2C businesses (cold email is for B2B)
- Anyone expecting 100% deliverability (no such thing)
- Those without capacity to handle booked meetings
- Businesses selling to enterprise (need different approach)

## Timeline

- Bronze: 2-3 weeks (warmup takes time)
- Silver: 3-4 weeks
- Gold: 4-6 weeks

Note: Warmup cannot be rushed. 2 weeks minimum for reliable deliverability.

## Process

1. Discovery call (30 min) - understand your ICP and offer
2. Proposal with technical specifications
3. 50% deposit to start
4. Domain purchase and DNS setup (Day 1-3)
5. Warmup period (2-4 weeks)
6. Sequence copywriting and lead sourcing (parallel)
7. Campaign configuration
8. Launch and monitor first week
9. Final 50% payment
10. Handoff with documentation

## Results clients typically see

- 40-60% open rates (vs. 15-20% on cold domains)
- 5-15% reply rates (with good copy and targeting)
- 3-8 meetings per month per inbox (depends on volume)
- No deliverability issues when following best practices
