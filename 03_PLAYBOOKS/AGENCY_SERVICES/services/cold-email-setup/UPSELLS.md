# Cold Email Setup - Upsells and Retainer Options

## Immediate upsells (offer at proposal stage)

### 1. Additional leads - $75 per 100

**Pitch:** "More leads = more at-bats. I can source additional 100 contacts for $75."

**Includes:**
- 100 verified emails
- Same ICP criteria
- Loaded into campaign

**When to offer:** Always. More leads = better testing.

---

### 2. CRM integration - $200

**Pitch:** "I can connect to [HubSpot/Pipedrive/etc.] so replies and interested leads flow directly into your pipeline."

**Includes:**
- CRM connection setup
- Lead routing rules
- Deal/contact creation
- Stage mapping

**When to offer:** When client has CRM. Essential for tracking ROI.

---

### 3. Additional domain + inboxes - $150

**Pitch:** "More inboxes = more volume without hurting deliverability. I can add another domain with 3 accounts."

**Includes:**
- 1 additional domain
- 3 email accounts
- Full DNS setup
- 2-week warmup

**When to offer:** Clients who want higher volume.

---

### 4. Additional sequence - $150

**Pitch:** "Different angles perform differently. I can write a second sequence to A/B test positioning."

**Includes:**
- 3-4 email sequence
- Different hook/angle
- Loaded and ready to test

**When to offer:** Gold clients or those with multiple ICPs.

---

## Post-project upsells

### 5. Monthly campaign management - $400/month

**Pitch (offer at handoff):** "I can run your campaigns ongoing. Monitor deliverability, optimize copy, handle replies, refresh leads."

**Includes:**
- Campaign monitoring (daily)
- A/B test analysis and optimization
- Copy updates based on performance
- 200 new leads/month
- Deliverability management
- Reply categorization
- Monthly performance report
- 4 hours of support

**When to offer:** At project handoff. High-value recurring.

---

### 6. Reply handling - $200/month

**Pitch:** "I'll categorize and route your replies so you only see interested prospects."

**Includes:**
- Daily reply review
- Categorization (interested, not now, not interested, OOO)
- Route to CRM or spreadsheet
- Flag hot leads for immediate attention

**When to offer:** Clients who get volume and are overwhelmed.

---

### 7. Lead refresh - $150/quarter

**Pitch:** "Fresh leads every quarter keeps deliverability high and prevents list fatigue."

**Includes:**
- 150 new verified leads
- Same ICP criteria
- Loaded into active campaigns
- Remove exhausted contacts

**When to offer:** 60-90 days after launch.

---

### 8. Deliverability audit - $150

**Pitch:** "If replies drop, I can audit your setup and identify issues."

**Includes:**
- Full DNS check
- Inbox placement testing
- Warmup health review
- Sending pattern analysis
- Recommendations document

**When to offer:** When client reports deliverability issues.

---

### 9. Scale infrastructure - $500

**Pitch:** "Ready to double volume? I can add 3 more domains and 9 inboxes."

**Includes:**
- 3 new domains
- 9 email accounts
- Full DNS + warmup
- Integration with existing campaigns

**When to offer:** 60-90 days in, when campaigns are working.

---

## Conversion benchmarks

Typical rates:
- Additional leads: 50-60% (easy add-on)
- CRM integration: 30-40%
- Monthly management: 25-35%
- Infrastructure scale: 15-20% at 60 days
- Lead refresh: 40-50% at 90 days

**Focus on:** Monthly management (recurring revenue) and lead add-ons (easy delivery, recurring need).
