# Landing Page Build Service

## Overview

We build high-converting landing pages that turn visitors into leads or customers. Strategy, copy, and design in one package.

## What you get

### Core deliverables
- Landing page strategy and wireframe
- Conversion-focused copywriting
- Responsive design and build
- Lead capture form integration
- Basic analytics setup
- 30-minute walkthrough

### Page types we build
- Lead generation pages (email capture)
- Product/service pages
- Webinar/event registration
- Waitlist pages
- Consultation booking pages
- Simple sales pages

## Who this is for

- Solopreneurs launching a product or service
- Businesses running ads needing a conversion page
- Anyone with traffic but no dedicated landing page
- Cold email campaigns needing a destination

## Who this is NOT for

- Full website builds (this is single page)
- E-commerce with checkout (use Shopify)
- Complex web apps
- Pages needing custom backend functionality

## Timeline

- Bronze: 3-5 business days
- Silver: 5-7 business days
- Gold: 7-10 business days

## Process

1. Discovery call (30 min) - understand your offer and audience
2. Proposal with page structure
3. 50% deposit to start
4. Wireframe and copy draft
5. Your review and feedback
6. Design and build
7. Final review and revisions
8. Launch
9. Final 50% payment

## Results clients typically see

- 20-40% conversion rates on lead gen pages (vs. 2-5% on generic pages)
- Clear visitor-to-action pathway
- Mobile-optimized experience
- Fast load times (<3 seconds)
