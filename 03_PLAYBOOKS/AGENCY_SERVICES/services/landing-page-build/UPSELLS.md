# Landing Page Build - Upsells and Retainer Options

## Immediate upsells (offer at proposal stage)

### 1. Thank you page - $50

**Pitch:** "After someone converts, what's next? A thank you page can deliver their download, set expectations, or offer something else."

**Includes:**
- Custom thank you page
- Next steps instructions
- Optional: secondary offer or upsell

**When to offer:** Always. Every form needs a destination.

---

### 2. A/B test variant - $75

**Pitch:** "Let's test two headlines from day one. Find the winner faster."

**Includes:**
- Second variant of page
- Different headline or CTA
- A/B testing tool setup
- Instructions for reading results

**When to offer:** Clients running paid traffic. Data matters.

---

### 3. Exit intent popup - $50

**Pitch:** "Capture leaving visitors with a popup offer. Usually adds 5-15% more conversions."

**Includes:**
- Exit popup design
- Different offer or messaging
- Integration with email tool

**When to offer:** Lead gen pages. Not for sales pages (can feel pushy).

---

### 4. Urgency elements - $50

**Pitch:** "Countdown timer or limited spots messaging can increase conversions 10-20%."

**Includes:**
- Countdown timer
- Limited availability messaging
- Automatic expiration (optional)

**When to offer:** Launches, webinars, limited offers.

---

## Post-project upsells

**Pitch (send after 2 weeks):** "Now that you have traffic data, I can review and optimize the page for better conversions."

**Includes:**
- Heatmap analysis
- Conversion funnel review
- 3-5 specific recommendations
- Implementation of top changes
- 30-minute review call

**When to offer:** 2-4 weeks after launch, when they have data.

---

**Includes:**

- Faster turnaround (assets exist)

**When to offer:** When client launches new offer or tests new angle.

**Includes:**
- Multi-page website
- Navigation and structure
- Consistent design system
- All pages conversion-optimized

**When to offer:** After successful landing page, when client is ready to expand.

---

### 8. Monthly page management - $100/month

**Pitch:** "I can maintain your page monthly. Updates, tweaks, A/B tests, keeping it optimized."

**Includes:**
- Monthly content updates
- A/B test management
- Performance monitoring

- Priority support

**When to offer:** Clients running ongoing campaigns.

## Conversion benchmarks

Typical rates:
- Thank you page: 50-60% (almost mandatory)
- A/B variant: 30-40% (paid traffic clients)
- Exit popup: 25-35%
- Conversion optimization: 40-50% (after 2+ weeks)
- Additional page: 35-45% (return clients)
- Monthly management: 15-20%

**Focus on:** Thank you page (easy delivery, high conversion) and conversion optimization (demonstrates ongoing value).
