# Case Study Template

Use this template to document client results. Case studies are your most powerful sales tool.

---

## Quick case study (social proof snippet)

**Use for:** Testimonial sections, social posts, quick references in sales calls

---

### Format

"[CLIENT NAME/TYPE] was spending [TIME/MONEY] on [PROBLEM]. After working together, they [SPECIFIC RESULT]."

### Examples

"A SaaS founder was spending 15 hours/week on lead follow-up. After we built their automation system, they're down to 2 hours and haven't missed a lead in 3 months."

"An agency owner was manually posting to 5 platforms daily. Now their content engine does it automatically. They've 3x'd their posting frequency without adding time."

"A consultant was getting 8% open rates on cold email. After we set up proper infrastructure, they hit 52% open rates and booked 12 meetings in the first month."

## How to collect case study information

### Right after project completion

Send this email:

---

Subject: Quick favor (2 min)

Hey [NAME],

Now that we've wrapped up, I'd love to capture your experience for a case study.

Quick questions:
1. What was your biggest challenge before we started?
2. What specific results have you seen?
3. What would you tell someone considering working with me?

Just reply with a few sentences for each. Or, if easier, I can send a Loom and you can record a quick video.

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]

---

### Questions to ask

**Before state:**
- What was the main problem you were trying to solve?
- How much time/money was this costing you?
- What had you tried before?
- What made you decide to finally fix it?

**After state:**
- What results have you seen?
- How much time/money are you saving?
- What's been the biggest unexpected benefit?
- Would you recommend this to others?

**Process:**
- What was the experience like working together?
- Was there anything that surprised you?
- What would you tell someone who's considering this?

### Get permission

Always get explicit permission before publishing.

---

Subject: Case study approval

Hey [NAME],

I put together a case study based on our project. Would love your approval before sharing.

[LINK TO DRAFT]

Let me know if anything needs changing. Happy to adjust names/details for privacy if needed.

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]

## Case study distribution

Once approved, use everywhere:

- Website case studies page
- Proposal attachments
- Sales calls ("I did something similar for...")
- Social media posts
- Email sequences
- Slide decks
- Cold outreach ("Here's what I did for [SIMILAR COMPANY]...")

The more you use it, the more value it provides.
