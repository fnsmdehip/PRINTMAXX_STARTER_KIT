# Cold Outreach Scripts

10 DM and email scripts to find clients for agency services.

---

## Script 1: Direct value offer (DM)

**Best for:** Twitter/X DMs, LinkedIn DMs
**Target:** Solopreneurs posting about manual work

---

Hey [NAME],

Saw your post about [SPECIFIC THING THEY MENTIONED]. I build automation systems that fix exactly that.

Quick example: I helped [SIMILAR PERSON] automate [SPECIFIC TASK] and it saved them 15 hours/week.

Would a 15-min call make sense to see if I can help?

## Script 5: LinkedIn connection + follow-up

**Best for:** LinkedIn
**Target:** Ideal client profile connections

---

**Connection request:**
Hey [NAME], I work with [THEIR ROLE/INDUSTRY] on automation and content systems. Your profile caught my eye - would love to connect.

---

**Follow-up (day 2-3):**
Thanks for connecting, [NAME].

I ask because I specialize in automating exactly those things. Happy to share what's worked for others in [THEIR SPACE].

## Outreach best practices

### Do:
- Lead with specific observation
- Reference something personal
- Keep it under 100 words
- One clear ask
- Easy way to say yes
- Follow up 2-3 times

### Don't:
- Generic "I can help your business" messages
- Long paragraphs about yourself
- Multiple asks in one message
- Attachments in first email
- "Let me know your thoughts"
- Give up after one message

### Follow-up cadence:
- Day 3: Bump (just the original message)
- Day 7: New angle or add value
- Day 14: Final follow-up, create soft close

### Response rate benchmarks:
- Cold email: 5-15% reply rate is good
- LinkedIn DM: 10-20% reply rate
- Twitter DM: 15-25% reply rate
- Loom outreach: 20-40% reply rate

## Where to find prospects

**Twitter/X:**
- Search: "[pain point] is killing me"
- Search: "anyone know a good [solution]"
- Follow hashtags: #buildinpublic, #solopreneur, #indiehacker
- Look at who follows competitors

**LinkedIn:**
- Sales Navigator filters
- Engage with content in your niche
- Check who's commenting on thought leaders
- Job title + company size search

**Communities:**
- Indie Hackers
- Product Hunt discussions
- Reddit (r/entrepreneur, r/smallbusiness)
- Slack communities in your niche

**Trigger events:**
- Just launched (Product Hunt, Twitter)
- Hiring for role you can automate
- Complaining about specific pain
- Growing fast (need systems)
