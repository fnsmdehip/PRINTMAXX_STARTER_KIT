## Pricing Strategies

### Value-Based vs. Hourly

| Model | When to Use | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Value-Based** | Clear ROI measurable | "Save 20 hours/month = $2,000" |
| **Hourly** | Consulting, uncertain scope | "$200/hour strategy" |
| **Retainer** | Ongoing relationships | "$1,500/mo marketing" |
| **Project** | Defined deliverables | "$5,000 app setup" |
| **Commission** | Sales-related services | "15% of brand deals" |
| **Hybrid** | Setup + ongoing | "$2,000 setup + $500/mo" |

### Pricing by Client Type

| Client Type | Budget Range | Pricing Approach |
|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| **Solopreneurs** | $200-2,000 | Lower touch, productized |
| **Small Business** | $1,000-5,000 | Balanced service |
| **Mid-Market** | $5,000-20,000 | White-glove, custom |
| **Enterprise** | $20,000+ | Full service, account management |

### Price Anchoring Strategy

1. **Show expensive option first** (even if you don't expect them to buy it)
2. **Recommended tier in middle** (where most buy)
3. **Budget option for price-sensitive** (but make it obviously limited)

Example:
- **Premium:** $5,000 (full done-for-you)
- **Standard:** $2,000 (setup + training) *MOST POPULAR*
- **Starter:** $500 (templates + 1 hour support)

### Raising Prices

**When to raise:**
- Fully booked for 2+ months
- Getting 80%+ close rate
- Results exceeding expectations
- Adding significant new capabilities

**How to raise:**
- Grandfather existing clients (for a period)
- Raise for new clients immediately
- Add services to justify new price
- Test with next 5 clients before full rollout

### Content to Service Pipeline

1. **Free content** (X posts, YouTube, TikTok) → builds audience
2. **Lead magnet** (template, checklist, mini-course) → captures emails
3. **Nurture sequence** (5-7 emails) → builds trust
4. **Low-ticket offer** ($27-97) → qualifies buyers
5. **High-ticket service** → converts best customers

### Pricing Your Lead Gen Spend

- **Target CAC:** 10-20% of first service sale
- **Example:** $2,000 service → $200-400 max to acquire
- **Break-even on first sale:** Acceptable if high LTV
- **Profitability target:** 3x ROAS minimum
