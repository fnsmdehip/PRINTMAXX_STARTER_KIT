## UNIVERSAL FIVERR GIG STRUCTURE

### 1. Gig Title Formula

**Pattern:** "I will [specific service] for [target audience]"

**Examples:**
- "I will automate your workflow with AI tools"
- "I will design a faith-based habit app for you"
- "I will build a personalized fitness plan"

**Avoid:**
- Generic: "I will help you" (too broad)
- Vague: "I will work with you" (unclear service)
- Buzzwords: "I will revolutionize" (engagement bait)

**Title Length:** 60 characters max. Search algorithm favors keywords within 60 chars.

**Keywords to include:**
1. Primary service (automation, design, plan, audit, etc.)
2. Specific outcome (workflow, app, plan, strategy)
3. Target audience (tech workers, Christians, entrepreneurs, etc.)

---

### 2. Gig Description Template (500-1000 words)

**Structure (ALWAYS follow this order):**

#### Section 1: Hook (50 words)
What problem do you solve? Lead with outcome, not process.

```
BAD: "I provide coaching services for entrepreneurs"
GOOD: "Stop wasting 10 hours/week on manual tasks. I'll audit your workflow,
recommend 3-5 AI tools, and set them up for you. Most clients save 8+ hours/week."
```

#### Section 2: Who This Is For (100 words)
Be SPECIFIC about ideal client. Fiverr algorithm matches specific → generic.

```
This gig is perfect for:
- Tech workers earning $80K+ who are overwhelmed by AI options
- People running same tasks manually 5+ times/week
- Those ready to implement (not just learning)
- People with basic tech comfort (can follow tutorials)

NOT for: Non-technical people, budget-only buyers, those who want free consulting
```

#### Section 3: What You'll Get (150 words)
List EXACT deliverables. No vagueness.

```
✓ 1-hour workflow audit (via video call or screen recording)
✓ Personalized AI tool stack (custom to YOUR use case)
✓ Full setup guides for 3-5 recommended tools
✓ 5 automation templates you can use immediately
✓ 2-week follow-up check-in via Slack/email
✓ Documentation of all workflows (copy-paste ready)

NOT included: Ongoing support, custom coding, 24/7 availability
```

#### Section 4: The Process (150 words)
How does engagement work?

```
Step 1: Order gig (you get Fiverr messaging link)
Step 2: Message me your current tools/workflows
Step 3: I'll send audit request form (5 min to fill)
Step 4: Optional 30-min discovery call (included)
Step 5: I deliver custom recommendations + setup guides
Step 6: You implement (I answer questions on Slack for 2 weeks)
Step 7: Follow-up call to ensure everything works

Timeline: 3-5 days for initial delivery
```

#### Section 5: Why Me (100 words)
Social proof + authority (without being salesy).

```
- 8+ years working with [niche] professionals
- Built automation systems for 200+ clients
- Certified in [relevant tools/skills]
- Average client saves 8-12 hours/week
- 4.9/5 rating across 500+ projects
- I only work with serious implementers (quality > quantity)
```

#### Section 6: CTA (50 words)
Push them to message before ordering (get their email).

```
Before ordering, send me a message with:
1. What tools you currently use
2. Your biggest time bottleneck
3. When you'd like to implement

This ensures I build the perfect stack for YOU, not a generic solution.
```

### 3. Fiverr Pricing Tiers

**Fiverr Algorithm:** Higher-priced gigs → More visibility if conversion rate is 10%+

**Tier Structure (ALWAYS use 3 tiers):**

| Tier | Price | Scope | Positioning |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Basic** | $49-99 | Minimal (1 hour work) | Entry-level, no call |
| **Standard** | $199-299 | Core offering (3-5 hours) | Most popular, includes 30-min call |
| **Premium** | $499-999 | Full-service (8+ hours) | High-touch, includes multiple calls |

**Pricing Logic:**
- Basic = 1 hour of your time worth $49-99 (covers platform fee)
- Standard = 3-5 hours of your time (your sweet spot)
- Premium = Upsell for clients who want custom/ongoing

**Platform Fees:** Fiverr takes 20%, so $100 gig = $80 to you.

**Initial Pricing Strategy:**
- Start Standard gig at $199 (minimum viable price)
- After 5 positive reviews, raise to $249
- At 10+ reviews, introduce Premium at $499+

**Fiverr allows 5-6 FAQ answers. Answer the objections that kill deals.**

```

I handle all the technical setup. You just follow my step-by-step guides
```

```
I'll suggest alternatives and set those up instead. 100% satisfaction or revision request.
```

```
Actual setup on your end: 1-3 hours across 2 weeks
Follow-up support: Included for 2 weeks after delivery
Most people see results within week 1 of implementation.
```

```
A: Yes. All gigs include 2 weeks of follow-up support via Slack/email.
Questions about setup? Reply to my messages and I'll help.
If you need ongoing support, we can discuss a longer-term coaching arrangement.
```

#### Q5: "Why is your price higher than competitors?"
```
A: My gigs deliver implementation, not theory.
Other sellers sell you a list. I deliver:
✓ Customized to YOUR workflow (not generic)
✓ Everything fully set up (not just theory)
✓ 2 weeks of support included
✓ 4.9/5 rating on 500+ successful projects

You pay more upfront, save 8+ hours/week long-term. ROI positive in week 1.
```

#### Q6: "Do you do ongoing support?"

```
If you want ongoing optimization, we can discuss:
- Monthly retainer ($500-1,500/mo)

Message me after delivery if interested.
```

### 6. Getting First Reviews (The Hardest Part)

**The Fiverr Catch-22:** Need reviews to get orders, need orders to get reviews.

**Bypass Strategy (Ethical & Effective):**

#### Phase 1: Friends & Family (First 5 Reviews)
- Message 5-10 people in your network
- Offer first gig at $25-50 (deeply discounted)
- Ask them to message you BEFORE ordering with their problem
- Over-deliver (provide $300 value for $50 sale)
- Ask for 5-star review (most will if you deliver)
- Cost: $125-500, gain: 5 reviews + credibility

#### Phase 2: Niche Community (Reviews 5-15)
- Post in Facebook groups for your niche
- Say: "Testing new Fiverr gig, offering first 10 customers 50% off"
- Pre-qualify in group (ask their problem first)
- Send them link to Fiverr gig (not direct link, through group)
- Track who converts + reviews
- Cost: Time to post + 50% discount on 10 gigs, gain: 10 reviews + real customers

#### Phase 3: Organic Growth (Review 15+)
- Once you have 15+ reviews and 4.8+ rating, Fiverr algorithm kicks in
- Each new order gets featured in search
- Conversion rate compounds (20% of views → orders)
- At this point: Raise prices 20-30% (algorithm doesn't penalize price increases)

**Review Generation Timeline:**
- Week 1-2: 5 friends/family reviews
- Week 3-4: 5-10 community reviews
- Week 5+: Organic orders begin
- Week 8+: 20+ reviews, algorithm boost, $500+/week revenue

## FAQ About Creating Multiple Gigs

**Q: Should I create all 3 gigs at once?**
A: No. Start with 1 gig. Get 10+ reviews, then add second gig. Quality > quantity.

**Q: What if I mess up the first delivery?**
A: Request revision (don't just refund). Use it as learning. Over-deliver on revision.

**Q: How many revisions should I allow?**
A: Unlimited for Standard/Premium. (Unlimited revisions for satisfied customers = 5-star reviews)

**Q: Should I lower prices to get first customers?**
A: Yes, initially. But raise prices after getting reviews. Most people confuse low price with low quality.

**Q: Can I use the same description for multiple gigs?**
A: No. Fiverr penalizes duplicate content. Customize each description.
