## Why Coaching = Highest $/Hour & Least Scale Required

| Model | Per-Client Revenue | Clients Needed | Total Monthly Revenue | Time Investment | $/Hour |
|-------|-------------------|-----------------|----------------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Coaching (1:1)** | $2,000-5,000/mo | 5-10 | $10-50K | 20-40 hrs | $250-500 |
| Coaching (Group) | $500-1,500/mo | 10-20 | $5-30K | 10-20 hrs | $250-300 |
| Info Products | $100-300/mo | 50-200 | $5-60K | 5-10 hrs | $50-100 |
| Courses | $47-297 | 30-100 | $1-30K | 3-6 hrs | $25-50 |

**Key insight:** 1 high-ticket coaching client = 10 course customers in revenue, but 1/10th the customer support.

## UNIVERSAL COACHING FRAMEWORK

Every niche uses this structure:

### Offer Stack (Funnel to Coaching)

```
Free Content (YouTube/Blog)
    ↓
Lead Magnet (Email capture)
    ↓
Low-Ticket Product ($17-67)
    ↓
Mid-Ticket Product ($97-297)
    ↓
Group Coaching ($297-997/mo)
    ↓
1:1 Coaching ($2,000-5,000/mo)
```

### 1:1 Coaching Structure (Template)

**Application Process** (Qualification)
- Short application (5 questions)
- 15-min discovery call
- Close on call or follow-up (48 hours)
- Target close rate: 30-50%

**Deliverables** (Value Delivery)
- X weekly 1:1 coaching calls
- Between-session support (Voxer/Slack)
- Custom strategy/audit
- Implementation support
- Accountability tracking

**Price Anchoring** (Positioning)
- High-ticket price ($2,000-5,000/mo)
- Limited spots (max 10 clients)
- Results guarantee (if applicable)
- Money-back guarantee (60 days)

## UNIVERSAL COACHING CONVERSION GUIDE

### Application Process (Convert Cold → Client)

**Step 1: Qualification**
- 5-question form (Google Form or Typeform)
- Filters out unqualified leads
- Identifies best-fit niches

**Step 2: Discovery Call (15-30 min)**
- Schedule via Calendly link
- Ask about situation, goals, timeline
- Identify specific pain point
- Introduce your coaching approach
- DO NOT pitch yet

**Step 3: Pitch (On Same Call or Email)**
- Reference their specific situation
- Describe your exact deliverables
- State investment amount clearly
- Mention limited spots
- Ask: "Are you interested in moving forward?"
- If yes: "Let me send you our agreement"
- If hesitant: "What would need to be different for this to feel like a yes?"

**Step 4: Close (48 Hours)**
- Send contract + payment link same day
- Payment due before first call
- First call scheduled in calendar
- Welcome package sent

**Step 5: Onboard (First Week)**
- Intake forms collected
- Goals & expectations clarified
- Systems & communication established
- First call completed

### Conversion Rate Targets

| Metric | Target Rate |
|--------|-------------|
| Form submission → Discovery call | 70% (good follow-up) |
| Discovery call → Pitch interest | 60% |
| Pitch interest → Closed deal | 50% |
| **Total conversion (form → client)** | **~20-25%** |
| **Close rate on call** | **30-50%** |

### Pricing Psychology (Why These Numbers?)

**$1,997 Sweet Spot:**
- High enough to feel premium
- Low enough for professionals to justify
- Sounds better than "$2,000"
- Monthly ($997/mo) or full with discount

**$3,997-4,997 High-Ticket:**
- Attracts serious clients
- Creates real commitment
- Allows for intensive support
- Results-driven buyer

**Monthly vs. One-Time:**
- Offer both options
- Monthly: 30% lower close rate but lower barrier
- Full: 50% higher close rate (committed buyers)
- Include discount (-10%) for upfront payment

### Upsell Strategy (After Coaching Closes)

**During Coaching:**
- Offer group program option (lower cost entry)
- Offer extended coaching ($297-597/mo ongoing)
- Offer community access ($97-197/mo)

**After Coaching:**
- Results bonuses (additional products at discount)
- Referral bonuses ($500-1K per referred client)
- VIP packages ($5,000+ retainers)
- Team/group scaling (10x original price)

5. **Strong value prop** - 3-5 specific results per niche
6. **Social proof** - Testimonials/case studies from first 3 clients
7. **Limited spots messaging** - "Only accepting 3 more clients this quarter"
8. **Clear deliverables** - Exactly what coaching includes (no mystery)
9. **Implementation support** - Between-session support critical
10. **Results tracking** - Show progress to make renewals easy

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Offering discovery calls to everyone (waste of time)
- Pitching via email instead of call (7% close vs 30%+)
- Unclear pricing ("let's chat about investment")
- No follow-up after discovery call
- Vague deliverables (people need specifics)
- Underpricing (signals low quality)
- Overcommitting (more than 15 clients unsustainable)
- No community/support (clients feel alone)
