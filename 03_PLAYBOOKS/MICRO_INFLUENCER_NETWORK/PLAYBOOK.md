## THE STRATEGY

Pay 300-500 micro-creators to test hooks simultaneously. Volume testing beats single influencer gambles.

**Key Insight:** Most app launches bet on 1-2 macro influencers ($5k-20k). If hook fails, entire budget wasted. Clipper network lets you test 50+ hooks for same budget and scale only winners.

## BUILDING THE ROSTER

### Where to Find Clippers

1. **TikTok/Instagram Reels:**
   - Search: "[your niche] clips"
   - Filter: 5k-50k followers
   - Look for: consistent posting (3-5x/week)

2. **YouTube Shorts:**
   - Search: "shorts creator" + niche
   - Check: subscriber count 10k-100k
   - Engagement: 3%+ average

3. **Twitter/X:**
   - Search: "content creator" "UGC creator"
   - DM: "Looking for creators for paid campaigns"

4. **Discord/Whop Communities:**
   - Join: Creator economy servers
   - Search: "Looking for paid promo opportunities"

5. **Reddit:**
   - r/SmallYTChannel
   - r/ContentCreators
   - r/Affiliatemarketing

### Roster Criteria

**Green Flags:**
- 5k-50k followers (sweet spot for engagement)
- 3%+ engagement rate
- Consistent posting schedule
- Niche-relevant content
- Responds to DMs quickly

**Red Flags:**
- Fake followers (low engagement despite high count)
- Inconsistent posting (1-2x/month)
- Wrong niche alignment
- Unresponsive to DMs

### Target Roster Size

- **Minimum viable:** 50 creators
- **Optimal:** 100-200 creators

## PAYMENT STRUCTURE

### Option 1: Performance-Based (Recommended)

**$1 per 1,000 views**
- Track views via platform analytics
- Pay weekly or bi-weekly
- Top performers get bonuses

**Pros:**
- Low risk (only pay for results)
- Aligns incentives (creators want views)
- Easy to scale winners

**Cons:**
- Requires tracking infrastructure
- Some creators prefer flat rate

### Option 2: Flat Rate

**$20-50 per post**
- Fixed payment regardless of views
- Good for testing new creators
- Simpler accounting

**Pros:**
- Easier to manage
- Creators know exact payment
- No tracking needed

**Cons:**
- Higher upfront cost
- No performance incentive
- Harder to scale

### Option 3: Hybrid

**$10 base + $0.50/1k views**
- Guaranteed minimum payment
- Performance bonus on top
- Best of both models

**Pros:**
- Attracts creators (guaranteed pay)
- Performance incentive (bonus views)
- Flexible scaling

## HOOK TESTING STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Wide Testing (Week 1)

- Create 10-20 hook variations
- Distribute to 50-100 creators
- Each creator picks 1-2 hooks
- Track performance by hook variant

### Phase 2: Data Analysis (Week 1-2)

- Identify top 3 performing hooks
- Analyze: view-through rate, engagement, conversion
- Look for patterns (problem angle vs benefit angle vs social proof)

### Phase 3: Scale Winners (Week 2-4)

- Double down on top 3 hooks
- Allocate 70% of budget to winners
- Keep 30% for continued testing
- Increase payment for top performers

### Phase 4: Optimization (Ongoing)

- Refresh hooks every 2-3 weeks
- Test new angles
- Rotate creators to avoid audience fatigue
- A/B test CTAs (link in bio vs comment vs DM)

## PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Betting on Macro Influencers Only**
   - High cost, single point of failure
   - Clipper network spreads risk

2. **Not Testing Hooks**
   - Assuming first hook will work
   - Test 10-20 variants always

3. **Slow Payment**
   - Pay within 7 days max
   - Fast payment = loyal roster

4. **Ignoring Data**
   - Track everything
   - Double down on winners only

5. **Wrong Niche Alignment**
   - Gaming clipper for fitness app = waste
   - Match creator niche to product
