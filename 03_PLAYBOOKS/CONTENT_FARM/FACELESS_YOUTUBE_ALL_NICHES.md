## SECTION 1: EDUCATIONAL & UTILITY NICHES

### N001: AI Utilities

**Channel name:** "AI Workflow Genius" / "Automation Unlocked"

**Target audience:** Tech workers, productivity enthusiasts, freelancers seeking efficiency

**Content format:** Tutorial + Explainer hybrid
- Step-by-step AI tool walkthroughs (5 min segments)
- Comparison videos (AI tool A vs B for specific task)
- Workflow automation case studies
- "How I automated X in my business" breakdowns

**Video length sweet spot:** 8-12 minutes
- Hook (0-30s): "This one AI tool saves me 10 hours/week"
- Setup (30s-1.5m): Why automation matters
- Tutorial (1.5m-10m): Step-by-step walkthrough with screen recording
- Payoff (10m-11.5m): Results and ROI
- CTA (11.5m-12m): Subscribe + link to templates

**Thumbnail style:**
- Screenshot of AI output
- Before/after workflow comparison
- Person with shocked expression (stock photo)
- Bold text: "10 Hours Saved" or "Free AI Hack"

**Monetization path:**
1. **Ad revenue:** $6-15 CPM (tech audience = high advertiser spend)
2. **Affiliate links:** Claude Pro, ChatGPT Plus, tool subscriptions (5-10% affiliate)
3. **Digital products:** $47 AI workflow templates, $97 automation course
4. **Sponsorships:** Tool integrations at 10K+ subs ($500-1,500 per video)

**Scaling strategy:**
- Week 1-2: Setup channel, record 10 tutorials
- Week 3+: 2-3 videos per week (batch record)
- Month 2: Add affiliate links to every video description
- Month 3: Launch $47 template bundle
- Month 6: Sponsor integrations

**Automation level:** 70% (AI script generation + templates, 30% human editing)
