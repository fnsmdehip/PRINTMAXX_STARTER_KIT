## Monetization Strategy by Stage

### Stage 1: Building (0-10K)

**Focus:** Growth over monetization

**Viable revenue:**
- Affiliate links in content (low volume)
- Freelance services (trade followers for clients)
- Small sponsorships (reach out proactively)

**Estimated income:** $0-500/mo

### Stage 2: Growing (10K-50K)

**Focus:** Balance of growth and monetization

**Viable revenue:**
- Platform ad programs (if eligible)
- Affiliate marketing (meaningful volume)
- Sponsored content (micro-influencer rates)
- Digital products (small launches)

**Estimated income:** $500-5K/mo

### Stage 3: Established (50K-200K)

**Focus:** Monetization optimization

**Viable revenue:**
- All platform monetization
- Regular sponsorships
- Affiliate partnerships
- Digital products (regular launches)
- Community/subscriptions

**Estimated income:** $5K-25K/mo

### Stage 4: Authority (200K+)

**Focus:** Maximizing and diversifying

**Viable revenue:**
- Premium sponsorships
- Own products/brands
- Speaking/consulting
- Licensing/partnerships
- Equity deals

**Estimated income:** $25K+/mo

## Sponsorship Rate Calculator

### Formula

```
Base rate = Followers / 1000 * Industry multiplier * Engagement rate multiplier

Industry multipliers:
- Finance: 3.0x
- Tech/SaaS: 2.5x
- Health/Fitness: 1.5x
- Lifestyle: 1.0x
- Entertainment: 0.8x

Engagement rate multipliers:
- >10%: 2.0x
- 5-10%: 1.5x
- 3-5%: 1.0x
- 1-3%: 0.7x
- <1%: 0.5x
```

### Example Calculation

```
Account: 50K followers, Tech niche, 6% engagement

Base: 50,000 / 1000 = $50
Industry: $50 × 2.5 = $125
Engagement: $125 × 1.5 = $187.50

Suggested rate: $180-200 per 1K followers
Full post: $9,000-10,000
```

### Platform Adjustments

| Platform | Adjustment |
|----------|------------|
| TikTok | 1.2x (high reach) |
| Instagram Reel | 1.0x (baseline) |
| Instagram Story | 0.3-0.5x |
| X Thread | 0.8x |
| YouTube Long-form | 2-3x |
| YouTube Shorts | 0.5x |

## Maximizing Each Stream

### Platform Revenue

**Tactics:**
- Optimize for watch time (higher RPM)
- Post in high-CPM time slots
- Create longer content when appropriate
- Target high-CPM keywords/topics

### Affiliate

**Tactics:**
- Promote recurring commission products
- Create comparison/review content
- Use trackable links for optimization
- Negotiate custom rates with volume

### Sponsorships

**Tactics:**
- Create media kit with analytics
- Reach out proactively (don't wait)
- Offer package deals (multi-platform)
- Build long-term relationships

### Digital Products

**Tactics:**
- Launch to email list
- Create products from popular content
- Price based on value, not time
- Build product ladder (low → high ticket)
