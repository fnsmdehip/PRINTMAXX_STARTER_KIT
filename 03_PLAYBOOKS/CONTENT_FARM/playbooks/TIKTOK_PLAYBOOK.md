## Account infrastructure

### Device strategy

TikTok's device fingerprinting is aggressive. Options:

| Method | Cost | Risk level | Best for |
|--------|------|------------|----------|
| Multiple phones | $50-100 each (used) | Low | Serious operators |
| Phone emulator (BlueStacks) | Free | High | Testing only |
| GoLogin mobile | $49/month | Medium | Budget scaling |
| Cloud phones (Genymotion) | $30/month | Medium | Remote management |

**Recommended setup:**
- Used iPhone SE or iPhone 8: $80-150
- Separate SIM card per phone: $10/month
- Separate iCloud account per phone
- Factory reset before each new account

### SIM and phone requirements

```
Per account needs:
- Unique phone number (real SIM preferred)
- Unique device ID
- Unique IP address
- Fresh app install
- New email or phone login

SIM options:
- Prepaid carriers (Mint, Visible): $15-30/month
- eSIM services: $5-10/month
- Virtual numbers (higher ban risk): $1-3/account
```

### Account creation checklist

- [ ] Factory reset device (or create new profile)
- [ ] Install TikTok fresh
- [ ] Use new IP (mobile data or residential proxy)
- [ ] Sign up with phone number (not email)
- [ ] Complete profile immediately
- [ ] Add profile photo
- [ ] Write bio (keep simple initially)
- [ ] Don't link other socials yet
- [ ] Browse For You Page for 30 minutes before any action

## 14-day warmup protocol

TikTok flags new accounts that post immediately. This protocol builds trust.

### Days 1-3: Consumer mode

**Actions per day:**
- Browse FYP: 30-45 minutes
- Like videos: 20-30 (in your niche)
- Watch videos fully: 15-20 (signals interest)
- Follow accounts: 5-10 (mix of sizes)
- No posting
- No commenting
- No duets/stitches

**Goal:** Train algorithm + look like real user

### Days 4-7: Light engagement

**Actions per day:**
- Browse FYP: 30 minutes
- Like videos: 30-40
- Follow accounts: 10-15
- Comments: 5-10 (genuine, 5+ words)
- Save videos: 5-10 (to collections)
- First post: Day 6

**First post guidelines:**
- Under 15 seconds
- Trending sound
- No links or CTAs
- Simple, native-looking content
- Post at peak time (7-9pm local)

### Days 8-10: Active posting

**Actions per day:**
- Engagement: Continue previous levels
- Posts: 1-2 per day
- Reply to all comments on your videos
- Duet/stitch: 1 per day (popular videos)

**Content guidelines:**
- 15-30 seconds
- Hook in first 1 second
- Trending sounds or original audio
- Text overlays for accessibility
- Strong CTA at end (follow, comment)

### Days 11-14: Growth mode

**Actions per day:**
- Posts: 2-3 per day
- Full engagement activity
- Test different content formats
- Analyze what's working

**Day 14 milestone:**
- Account considered "established"
- Can post 3-5x daily
- Can include soft CTAs
- Begin monetization prep

### Warmup metrics to track

| Day | Followers | Views (total) | Avg view duration |
|-----|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 0 | 0 | - |
| 7 | 10-50 | 500-2K | 50%+ |
| 14 | 50-200 | 5K-20K | 60%+ |

If views stay under 200 per video by day 10, account may be shadowbanned.

## Algorithm tactics

### The TikTok algorithm explained

TikTok scores videos on:
1. **Watch time %** (most important)
2. **Completion rate**
3. **Rewatches**
4. **Shares**
5. **Comments**
6. **Likes**
7. **Follows from video**

### Video structure for algorithm

```
0:00-0:01 - Hook (pattern interrupt)
0:01-0:03 - Promise (what they'll learn/see)
0:03-0:08 - Value/entertainment
0:08-0:12 - Payoff or twist
0:12-0:15 - CTA (comment prompt)
```

### Hook formulas that work

**Pattern interrupts:**
- Start mid-sentence
- Unexpected visual
- Loud sound or silence
- Direct eye contact + question

**Proven hooks:**
- "Wait for it..."
- "I can't believe this worked"
- "Here's what nobody tells you about..."
- "You've been doing [X] wrong"
- "This is your sign to..."
- Numbers: "I made $X in Y days"

### Optimal posting strategy

**Best times to post (EST):**
- Morning: 7-9am
- Lunch: 12-2pm
- Evening: 7-10pm (peak)

**Best days:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: Highest engagement
- Friday: Good for entertainment
- Weekend: Lower but less competition

**Posting cadence:**
- Minimum: 1x/day
- Optimal: 3x/day
- Maximum: 5-7x/day (diminishing returns after)

### Sound strategy

**Trending sounds:**
- Check "Discover" tab daily
- Use sounds with < 50K videos (sweet spot)
- Original audio for educational content
- Trending sound + unique twist = algorithm boost

**Finding trending sounds:**
- TokBoard.com
- TrendTok app
- FYP scroll (save sounds you see multiple times)

### Hashtag strategy

```
Formula: 3-5 hashtags per video

Mix:
- 1 broad (#fyp, #foryou) - optional, minimal impact
- 2 niche-specific (#[yourniche]tok)
- 1-2 trending/relevant
- 1 branded (optional)

Example for productivity niche:
#productivitytips #workfromhome #getorganized #9to5 #sidehustle
```

### Engagement hacks

**Comment baiting:**
- Ask controversial questions
- Make intentional small "mistakes"
- Say "comment [word] for part 2"
- "Only [type of person] will understand"

**Watch time tricks:**
- Promise payoff at end
- Use visual mystery
- Text that requires rewatching
- Loops that seem continuous

**Share triggers:**
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- Extremely relatable content
- Useful information worth saving
- Inside jokes for communities

## Risk management

### Shadowban detection

**Signs of shadowban:**
- Views stuck at 0-200 for 48+ hours
- Videos not showing in hashtag feeds
- FYP reach drops to zero
- Only followers see content

**Testing:**
1. Post video with unique hashtag
2. Search hashtag immediately
3. If video doesn't appear = shadowbanned

**Recovery:**
- Stop posting for 48-72 hours
- Continue consuming content (browse FYP)
- Delete any flagged videos
- Resume with safe content (no links, no controversy)
- Gradually return to normal posting over 7 days

### Ban prevention

**Content that gets flagged:**
- Nudity/suggestive content
- Violence or dangerous acts
- Copyrighted music (certain tracks)
- Political content
- Medical misinformation
- Get-rich-quick claims
- Spam behavior

**Actions that get flagged:**
- Mass following/unfollowing
- Same caption across accounts
- Posting identical videos
- External link in first 7 days
- Aggressive commenting
- Buying followers/engagement

### Device isolation

**TikTok tracks:**
- Device ID
- Phone number
- IP address
- App behavior patterns
- Linked accounts

**Isolation rules:**
- One account per device
- Don't log into second account ever
- Different Wi-Fi/mobile data per device
- No cross-promotion between accounts
- Different content styles per account

### Account recovery

**If account gets banned:**
1. Don't create new account on same device
2. Appeal through app (one chance usually)
3. If appeal fails, account is gone
4. Wait 30 days before new account on that device
5. Factory reset before new account

## Scaling from 1 to 10 accounts

### Phase 1: First account mastery (Month 1-2)

**Goals:**
- 5,000+ followers
- 100K+ views on best video
- Understand what works in niche
- First $100 in revenue

**Focus:**
- Post 3x/day minimum
- Test 5 different content formats
- Analyze top performers
- Document everything

**Time investment:** 2-3 hours/day

### Phase 2: Add accounts 2-3 (Month 3-4)

**Setup:**
- Different sub-niches or personas
- Different devices required
- Different posting schedules

**Management:**
- Morning: Account 1 content + engagement
- Afternoon: Account 2 content + engagement
- Evening: Account 3 + analytics review

**Content batching:**
- Film 1 day per week (all accounts)
- Edit in batches
- Schedule using Later or Buffer
- Leave room for trending content

### Phase 3: Add accounts 4-6 (Month 5-6)

**Infrastructure needed:**
- Content library system
- Trend tracking process
- Analytics dashboard
- Batch editing workflow

**Team consideration:**

- Content researcher: $200-400/month

### Phase 4: Add accounts 7-10 (Month 7+)

**Full operation:**
- Content calendar per account
- SOP for every task
- Quality control checklist
- Emergency response plan

**Revenue targets at scale:**

|----------|-------------------|---------------|
