# RELAX_CHANNELS

Ambient/relaxation content accounts for passive income.

---

## Overview

Create and post relaxing content: nature sounds, rain, fireplace, lofi beats, meditation visuals. Monetize through ads, affiliate links to sleep apps/products, and creator fund payouts.

---

## Content Types

| Type | Examples | Effort | Performance |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Nature Sounds | Rain, ocean, forest | Low | High |
| Ambient Visuals | Fireplace, aquarium, snow | Low | High |
| Lofi Beats | Chill music compilations | Medium | High |
| Meditation | Guided, breathing exercises | Medium | Medium |
| ASMR-lite | Soft sounds, no talking | Low | Medium |

---

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Format | Monetization |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| YouTube | Long-form (8-10hr) | Ads (CPM $2-8) |
| TikTok | Short clips (60s) | Creator fund |
| Spotify | Audio podcasts | Anchor ads |
| Instagram | Reels + carousel tips | Affiliate |

---

## Monetization

### Primary
- YouTube AdSense: Long videos = more ad breaks = more revenue
- 8-hour sleep videos can run 10+ mid-roll ads

### Secondary
- Sleep app affiliate (Calm, Headspace)
- White noise machine affiliate (Amazon)
- Pillow/mattress affiliate
- Meditation course affiliate

### Tertiary
- Patreon for exclusive content
- Merch (sleep masks, etc.)

---

## Content Creation

### Video Sources (Legal)
- Pexels, Pixabay (free stock)
- Your own footage
- AI-generated visuals (Runway, Pika)

### Audio Sources
- Royalty-free libraries
- Generate with AI (Suno for music)
- Record yourself (rain, nature)
- License from AudioJungle

### Tools
- DaVinci Resolve (free editing)
- Canva (thumbnails)
- Descript (audio editing)

---

## Automation Potential

**HIGH** - Most of this pipeline can be automated:

```
1. Pull royalty-free video assets
2. Pull/generate ambient audio
3. Combine video + audio (FFmpeg)
4. Generate thumbnail (Canva API)
5. Upload to YouTube (API)
6. Cross-post clips to TikTok/Reels
```

---

## Growth Tactics

1. SEO titles: "Rain Sounds for Sleep 10 Hours"
2. Keyword-rich descriptions
3. Playlists for different moods
4. Post consistently (algorithm rewards it)
5. Engage with comments
6. Cross-promote from shorts to long-form

---

## Ralph Tasks

- Generate 10 video variations per week
- Automate thumbnail creation
- Schedule uploads across platforms
- Track performance, iterate on titles

---

## Getting Started

1. Create YouTube channel with niche branding
2. Create 3 long-form videos (8+ hours)
3. Create 10 short clips for TikTok/Reels
4. Set up affiliate links in descriptions
5. Monitor and iterate
