## Universal Quora Answer Structure

**Opening (3-5 sentences):**
- Lead with empathy or surprising statement
- Validate the question (show you understand the struggle)
- Preview your answer (tease the insight)

**Main Content (300-500 words):**
- 3-5 specific, actionable points
- Use numbers/specifics, not vague advice
- Example or case study (if relevant)
- Common mistakes (what NOT to do)

**CTA Section (2-3 sentences):**
- Soft mention of what you offer (if relevant)
- Link in bio statement (not aggressive)
- Call them to take one specific action

**Bonus: Authority Signals:**
- Mention specific tools/apps you built or use
- Reference your niche expertise without name-dropping
- Numbers always (helped X people, 50K+ users, etc.)
- Personal experience (not "I read that")

**Rules:**
- No clickbait titles
- Never say "join my newsletter" (ban-trigger)
- Never link directly to sales pages (ban-trigger)
- Always link to content first (free guide, tool, etc.)
- Answer length: 400-800 words (algorithms reward depth)
