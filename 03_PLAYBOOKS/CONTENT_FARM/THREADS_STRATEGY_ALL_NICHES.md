## 5 Post Formats That Work Across ALL Niches

### Format 1: The Hook + Breakdown Thread

**Structure:** Lead with consequence, then elaborate in replies

```
OPENING POST (Hook):
[Specific claim/surprising fact in 150 chars max]

REPLY 1 (Proof):
[Why this is true + specific numbers]

REPLY 2 (How to use):
[Step-by-step application]

REPLY 3 (Result):
[Outcome or CTA to link in bio]
```

**Why it works:**
- Threads prioritizes thread interactions (replies)
- Each reply gets its own algorithmically boost
- Readers scroll through and stay on your account longer
- Link CTA at end feels natural

**Example (AI Tools niche):**
```
POST: I use Claude to write 3 weeks of content in 2 hours. saves ~10 hours/week.

REPLY 1: The trick: structured prompts. feed it your brand voice once. then every prompt includes: "write in [voice] style. keep it 280 chars."

REPLY 2: Step 1: Save your best 5 posts. Step 2: Copy them as "brand voice reference." Step 3: Paste reference in every prompt. Done.

REPLY 3: Each piece takes 2 min now instead of 20. Get my exact prompts in bio link 👇
```

## Cross-Posting Strategy: Instagram → Threads

### Why This Matters

- **1 upload, 2 platforms:** Post to IG, auto-share to Threads
- **Threads shows IG reposts:** Users see it's from popular IG account = instant credibility
- **IG Reels → Threads video:** Same assets, double the reach

### How to Cross-Post Effectively

**Step 1: Post on Instagram Reels first**
- Optimize for IG algorithm (trending sounds, hooks, call-to-action)
- Post at best IG time (typically 11 AM or 7 PM your audience timezone)
- Wait 2-4 hours for IG to boost it

**Step 2: Share to Threads (Via Meta's integrated system)**
- Go to post → Share → Add to Threads
- Threads shows: Video + Author (your IG profile) + Caption
- Edit caption for Threads (shorter, include CTA)

**Step 3: Optimize for Threads algorithm**
- Edit Threads caption to add: call-to-action + hashtags (Threads uses #s differently)
- Pin best-performing post to profile
- Reply to your own thread with call-to-action

### Cross-Posting Cadence

**Optimal schedule:**
- **Monday-Friday:** Post to IG at 7-9 AM, share to Threads at 10 AM
- **Wednesday/Thursday:** Post to IG at 5-6 PM, share to Threads at 7 PM
- **Do NOT post same time on both:** Let IG algorithm run first

### Platform-Specific Optimization

| Element | Instagram Reels | Threads | Strategy |
|---------|-----------------|---------|----------|
| **Length** | 15-60 sec | Text-only or up to 5 min video | Create 45-sec IG Reels, share full version to Threads |
| **Captions** | Trending sounds critical | Text-first, sounds optional | Reels hook = visual, Threads hook = copy |
| **Hashtags** | 25-30 (#) per post | 5-10 (#) per post | Use different hashtag strategies per platform |
| **Call-to-Action** | Subtle (link in bio) | Direct (link in bio or bio link button) | Threads users more likely to click links |
| **Best Times** | 11 AM, 7 PM | 7-9 AM, 5-7 PM | Stagger posts by 2-4 hours |

## Engagement Tactics (Building Algorithmic Momentum)

### Tactic 1: The Self-Reply Thread (Your Best Tactic)

**What:** Post, then reply to your own post 3-5 times

**Why:** Threads algorithm boosts posts with conversation. Your replies count as engagement.

**How:**

```
1. Post main hook
2. Wait 30 sec
3. Reply with proof/data
4. Reply with how-to
5. Reply with CTA
6. Each reply gets boost
7. Total thread = 5 algorithmic signals
```

**Expected result:** Main post reach 2-5x higher than single post

### Tactic 2: Strategic Replying to Bigger Accounts

**What:** Reply to posts from accounts 10-50K followers in your niche

**Why:** Their audience sees your reply. If good, they follow you.

**How:**

- Find posts with 100-500 likes (not mega-viral, but visible)
- Reply with genuine insight (not spam)
- Include emoji or mention if relevant
- Reply within first hour (gets more visibility)

**Target 2-3 accounts per day**

### Tactic 3: The Hook-and-Question Strategy

**What:** Every post ends with a question

**Why:** Questions get replies. Replies = algorithm boost.

**How:**

```
[Your content]

[Question that's easy to answer]
"What's your biggest struggle with [topic]?"
```

**Expected:** 20-30% reply rate on engaged posts

### Tactic 4: Hashtag Strategy (Different than Twitter/X)

**Threads hashtag rules (different from X):**
- Max 10 hashtags per post
- Hashtags group by topic, not trending
- No hashtag algorithm boost (unlike TikTok)
- Use for content categorization only

**Strategy:**

```
5 niche-specific hashtags:
#[NicheName]
#[Niche]Community
#[Niche]Tips
#[NicheRealTalk]
#[NicheResults]

5 growth hashtags:
#ThreadsGrowth
#ContentCreator
#SmallCreator
#GrowOnThreads
#ThreadsCreators
```

### Tactic 5: Pin Your Best Converting Post

**What:** Set 1 post as pinned profile post

**Why:** New visitors see this first. If it converts, they click bio.

**How:**

- Identify your highest click-through post (check analytics)
- Pin it to your profile
- Update it monthly as new content performs better
- Pinned post should be FORMAT 2 or 4 (drives action)

### Tactic 6: The Daily Ritual (Community Building)

**What:** Reply to 5-10 posts from followers + small creators daily

**Why:** Threads algorithm rewards interaction frequency

**How:**

- Set 15 min timer daily
- Find 5 posts from niche accounts (1K-10K followers)
- Reply with genuine feedback (1-2 sentences)
- Don't spam, don't self-promote
- Just be a good community member

**Expected:** 5-10% of people you engage with follow back
