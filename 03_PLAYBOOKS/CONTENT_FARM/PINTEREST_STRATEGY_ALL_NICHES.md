# Pinterest Marketing Strategy - All 33 Niches

Pinterest = underrated traffic source, especially for visual niches. 465M+ monthly active users, high conversion intent, long pin lifespan (6 months+).

**Why Pinterest Wins:**
- Users actively saving ideas (not mindless scrolling)
- Drives 5-10x more traffic than TikTok per follower
- Search engine (Google ranks Pinterest pins)
- Educational/aspirational content performs best
- High income demographics (women 25-45 = prime audience)
- Shopping intent = affiliate link ready

**Key Stats:**
- 80% female audience
- $71K median household income
- 73% plan purchases on Pinterest
- Average pin gets 400+ impressions
- Optimal post frequency: 3-5 pins/day per board

3. **Include exact numbers** (viewers trust specifics)
4. **Create 3 pin variations per topic** (vertical, square, landscape tests)
5. **Add calls-to-action** ("Save this," "Learn how," "Join us")

### Optimal Link Strategy
- Link to your landing page (build email list first)
- Link to affiliate products (highest monetization)
- Link to your course/digital product
- Use Pinterest-friendly link shorteners (Linktree, Pretty Links)

- Comment on pins in your niche (with value, not spam)
- Repin high-performing content (3-6 months after original)
- Join group boards in your niche

## Why This System Works

**Pinterest's Algorithm Favors:**
1. Relevant keywords in pin + description
2. Consistent pinning (3-5 daily shows algorithm activity)
3. Long pin lifespan (doesn't need constant repins)
4. High engagement (saves > clicks in algo weighting)
5. Click-through to quality content (rewards for driving real traffic)

**Money Niches Win Because:**
- 80% female audience = higher purchasing power per capita
- Users actively seeking ideas, not distracting (unlike TikTok)
- Long content lifespan = compounding traffic
- High intent + educational content = affiliate-ready
- Minimal competition for underserved niches

- Week 1: Design 50 pins per niche (start with top 5 niches)
