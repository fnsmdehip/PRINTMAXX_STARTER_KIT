# Bio Templates

60 proven bio templates across 3 niches. Copy, customize, and deploy.

---

## Bio structure principles

### Anatomy of a high-converting bio

```
Line 1: Who you are / What you do (identity)
Line 2: What value you provide (promise)
Line 3: Proof/credibility (trust)
Line 4: Call to action (conversion)
```

### Character limits by platform

| Platform | Limit | Optimal |
|----------|-------|---------|
| X/Twitter | 160 | 120-150 |
| TikTok | 80 | 60-80 |
| Instagram | 150 | 120-150 |
| YouTube | 1000 | 200-300 |

## Platform-specific adaptations

### X/Twitter (160 chars)

Condense to essentials:
```
Original (200+ chars):
"I help busy professionals build passive income streams
through smart investing and side hustles.
10+ years experience. Get my free guide: link"

Twitter version (155 chars):
"Helping busy pros build passive income
10+ yrs investing experience
Free guide: [link]"
```

### TikTok (80 chars)

Ultra-condensed:
```
Original: "I help busy professionals build passive income"
TikTok: "Passive income for busy people"
```

Focus on hook and CTA:
```
"$XXK/mo from side hustles
Free guide in link"
```

### Instagram (150 chars)

Room for more detail:
```
"Building wealth in my 30s
$XXK invested | X income streams
```

## Bio optimization checklist

Before publishing:
- [ ] Clear who you serve
- [ ] Clear what value you provide
- [ ] Some form of proof/credibility
- [ ] Call to action present
- [ ] Within character limit
- [ ] No typos or errors
- [ ] Link works (test it)
- [ ] Matches content you post

---

## A/B testing your bio

### What to test
- Different value propositions
- Different proof points
- Different CTAs
- With/without emojis
- With/without numbers

### How to measure
- Profile visits → follows ratio
- Link click rate
- DM volume
- Overall growth rate

### Testing schedule
- Run each version 2 weeks
- Track key metrics daily
- Compare and pick winner
- Test new variation vs winner
