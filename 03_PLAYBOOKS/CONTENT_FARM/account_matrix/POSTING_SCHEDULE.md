## Multi-account coordination

### Avoiding pattern detection

**Rule 1: Stagger times**
- No two accounts post within 30 minutes
- Vary posting times by 1-2 hours across accounts

**Rule 2: Vary days**
- Don't post same content type on same day across accounts
- Rotate heavy posting days

**Rule 3: Different patterns**
- Account 1: Morning heavy
- Account 2: Afternoon heavy
- Account 3: Evening heavy
- Account 4: Mixed throughout
- Account 5: Weekend focus

## Time zone considerations

### Targeting US audience

Primary zones to consider:
- EST (New York): Base timezone
- CST (Chicago): -1 hour
- MST (Denver): -2 hours
- PST (Los Angeles): -3 hours

**Optimal coverage posting:**
- 9am EST = 6am PST (early risers)
- 12pm EST = 9am PST (morning all US)
- 3pm EST = 12pm PST (lunch all US)
- 6pm EST = 3pm PST (afternoon all)
- 9pm EST = 6pm PST (evening all)

### Targeting international

**UK/Europe:**
- Add 5-8 hours to EST
- Post early morning EST for European afternoon

**Australia:**
- Add 14-17 hours to EST
- Post evening EST for Australian morning

### Multi-region strategy

If targeting multiple regions, create region-specific accounts or post 24/7 rotation:
- 6am EST (Europe afternoon)
- 12pm EST (US day)
- 6pm EST (US evening)
- 12am EST (Asia/Australia morning)

## Emergency posting rules

### When to skip scheduled posts

- Breaking news in your niche (post about it instead)
- Platform having issues (save content for later)
- Previous post going viral (let it ride)
- Negative event related to your content

### When to post extra

- Trending topic you can comment on
- Viral potential content ready
- Competitor made a mistake you can capitalize on
- Community event or challenge

### Recovery posting

If you miss posting for days:
1. Don't post 10x in one day to catch up
2. Resume normal schedule immediately
3. Add 1-2 extra posts per day for a week
4. Don't mention the gap to audience
