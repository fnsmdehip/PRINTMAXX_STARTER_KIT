# Content Pillars

Strategic content themes for each niche. Build your content calendar around these pillars to maintain consistency and cover all audience needs.

---

## What are content pillars?

Content pillars are 4-6 recurring themes that define your account. They:
- Keep content focused and consistent
- Make content planning easier
- Build authority in specific areas
- Help audience know what to expect
- Prevent random/scattered posting

---

## Pillar structure

Each pillar should include:
1. **Theme** - The topic area
2. **Purpose** - Why this serves your audience
3. **Content types** - Specific formats that work
4. **Posting frequency** - How often to post this pillar
5. **Example topics** - Specific ideas to execute

## Pillar evolution

### When to adjust pillars

Review pillars quarterly. Consider changes when:
- One pillar consistently underperforms
- Audience asks for content you don't cover
- You want to pivot your positioning
- New trends emerge in your niche
- You've exhausted ideas in a pillar

### How to add a new pillar

1. Test content in new area (2-3 posts)
2. Measure engagement vs existing pillars
3. If positive, formally add to rotation
4. Reduce weakest pillar's frequency
5. Update content calendar

### Signs of pillar fatigue

- Ideas feel repetitive
- Engagement dropping on pillar
- Content feels forced
- Audience complaints
- You dread creating that content

**Solution:** Refresh the angle, merge with another pillar, or retire it.
