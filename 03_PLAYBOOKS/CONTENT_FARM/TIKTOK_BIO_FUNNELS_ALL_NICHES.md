## Core Framework: The Bio Link Funnel

### Why TikTok Bio Matters (It's Everything)

- **Only clickable link** - Videos can't have links (no swipe-up before 10K)
- **Drives off-platform traffic** - Your only exit from TikTok
- **Conversion window** - Most people click within first 2-5 days of video posting
- **Monetization chokepoint** - All money flows through this link
- **A/B testable** - Change bio link, track conversion changes
- **Platform arbitrage** - Attention → TikTok → Your funnel → Revenue

### The Bio Link Funnel Structure

```
TikTok Video (Hook)
    ↓ (3-8% click rate)
Bio Link → Landing Page
    ↓ (Email capture, lead magnet, or direct product)
Email List / Customer
    ↓
Monetization (Affiliate, Products, Upsells)
```

## TikTok Bio Link Best Practices

### What Works (High Conversion)
1. **Specific benefit in bio** - "Save $100s on vet bills" not "check us out"
2. **Social proof** - "10K+ people saved 5 hours/week"
3. **Scarcity/urgency** - "Limited spots" or "Free for next 100"
4. **Question in bio** - "What's your biggest productivity struggle?" (curiosity)
5. **Action-oriented copy** - "Discover," "Unlock," "Get access"
6. **Call-to-action** - "Link in bio" with arrow emoji 👇

### A/B Test Bio Copy

**Month 1:** Test 3 versions
- Version A: Benefit-focused
- Version B: Question-focused
- Version C: Social proof-focused

**Which wins?** Rotate winner to all accounts, test new challenger next month.

### Link-in-Bio Tool Comparison

**Best for high conversions:** Leadpages, ConvertKit, Stan Store
- Multiple A/B testing options
- Built-in analytics
- Email integration

**Best for quick setup:** Linktree, Carrd, Beacons
- Simple drag-and-drop
- No coding needed
- Quick to implement

**Best for creators:** Substack (newsletters), OnlyFans (exclusive), Beacons (hybrid)

## Revenue Multiplier

**Formula for estimating revenue:**
```
Monthly Video Views × Click Rate × Landing Page Conversion × Average Order Value = Monthly Revenue

Example:
100K video views × 5% click = 5,000 visitors
5,000 × 20% conversion = 1,000 conversions
1,000 × $5 average (mix of free/paid) = $5,000/month
```

**Quick estimates by niche:**
- **High-ticket niches** (Crypto, Stocks, Coaching): $8-30/1K views
- **Mid-ticket niches** (Courses, Programs): $3-12/1K views
- **Low-ticket niches** (Affiliate, Merch): $1-5/1K views
- **Subscription-based** (Newsletter, Membership): $3-15/1K subscribers
