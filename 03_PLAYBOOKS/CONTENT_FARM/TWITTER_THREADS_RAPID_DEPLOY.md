## Thread Customization Checklist

Before posting EVERY thread:

- [ ] **Hook is consequence-first** (what happened, not explanation)
- [ ] **Each tweet has ONE idea** (no fluff, no combining tweets)
- [ ] **Numbers are SPECIFIC** ($2.5K not "a lot", "3 weeks" not "recently")
- [ ] **No em-dashes** (use commas or periods instead)
- [ ] **No AI vocabulary** (leverage, utilize, delve, comprehensive, robust, innovative, seamless, game-changer, unlock, empower, cutting-edge)
- [ ] **CTA is SOFT** (not pushy)
- [ ] **CTA is at END** (not mid-thread)
- [ ] **Self-reply planned** (2-4 hours after posting)
- [ ] **Voice is conversational** (like texting smart friend, not copywriting)
- [ ] **Pacing allows 30-45 sec per tweet** (not paragraphs)
