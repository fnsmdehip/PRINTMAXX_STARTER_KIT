## EVERGREEN RATE CARD PHILOSOPHY

**Rule 1: Know Your Worth**
- Followers × Engagement Rate × Niche Premium = Base Rate
- Example: 50K followers × 4% engagement × 1.8 (high-income niche) = $3,600 base

**Rule 2: Price Transparency Attracts Sponsors**
- Public rate card = sponsors know what to expect
- Private rates = slow response, negotiations, tire-kicking
- Build confidence through transparency

**Rule 3: Raise Rates Quarterly**
- After every 3 successful sponsorships, increase by 25-50%
- New sponsors don't know your old rates
- As you grow followers, refresh rate card
- Premium niches command higher rates (AI, finance, health)

**Rule 4: Package Deals Close More Deals**
- Single post: $X
- 3-post package: $2.5X (save 15%)
- 6-post package: $4X (save 30%)
- Retainer: Lower per-post rate, guaranteed monthly income

**Rule 5: Specificity Drives Sponsorship**
- "I have 50K followers in finance" → $3,000/post
- "I have high engagement with investors" → $4,000/post
- "My audience makes $150K+ and trusts my investing advice" → $5,000/post
- Niche positioning = higher rates
