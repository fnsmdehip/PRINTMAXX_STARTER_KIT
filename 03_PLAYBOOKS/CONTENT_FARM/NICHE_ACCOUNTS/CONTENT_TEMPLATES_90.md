| [BRAND] | Product brand name |
| [CAREER] | Career or business type |

## Posting Rules

1. Never post more than 1 affiliate post per 5 posts (20% max)
2. Always disclose affiliate links (#ad or disclosure text)
3. Engagement posts go at peak hours (12 PM, 5-7 PM EST)
4. Value posts go at morning hours (5:30-8 AM EST)
5. Never post the same template twice in a week
6. Rotate between all 5 content types evenly
7. Threads only on X (not IG or TikTok)
8. Video scripts adapt for both TikTok and IG Reels

## Quality Checklist

Before posting, verify:
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Lowercase for X/Twitter
- [ ] Specific numbers (not "many" or "several")
- [ ] Honest (includes cons for reviews)
- [ ] Has a clear value (teaches, inspires, or entertains)
- [ ] FTC compliant (affiliate disclosure where needed)
- [ ] Matches account voice (warm for faith, direct for fitness, technical for tech)
