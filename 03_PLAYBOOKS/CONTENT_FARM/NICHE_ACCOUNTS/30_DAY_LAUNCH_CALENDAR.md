### Reply Strategy (Critical for Growth)

**Rules for replies:**
1. Never reply "great post!" or generic praise
2. Always add value: a stat, personal experience, or follow-up question
3. Reply to big accounts first (more visibility)
4. Reply within 15 minutes of their post for maximum visibility
5. Your reply IS your content. Put effort into it.

**Reply templates:**

Faith niche:
- "this verse hit different for me during [specific situation]. [1 sentence on how it applied]."
- "I read this with [book/study Bible] and the context adds so much. [1 specific insight]."

Fitness niche:
- "I had the same issue with [exercise]. what fixed it for me: [specific cue]. game changer."
- "ran this exact protocol for 12 weeks. results: [specific outcome]. can confirm."

AI/Tech niche:
- "I tested this too. one thing I'd add: [specific tip or shortcut]. saves another [X] minutes."
- "tried the free version for 2 weeks. the upgrade was worth it for [specific feature]."
