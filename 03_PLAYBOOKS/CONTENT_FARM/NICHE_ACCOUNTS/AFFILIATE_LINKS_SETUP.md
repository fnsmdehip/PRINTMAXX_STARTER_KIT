## Link Placement Strategy (All Accounts)

### Bio Link Structure (Linktree or Beacons)

Each account gets a link-in-bio page with this hierarchy:

```
1. FREE LEAD MAGNET (email capture)
   - Faith: Free faith journal template
   - Fitness: Free workout tracker template
   - Tech: Free AI dashboard template

2. NEWSLETTER SIGNUP
   - Faith: Morning Grace newsletter
   - Fitness: 5AM Gains weekly
   - Tech: The Stack Daily newsletter

3. TOP AFFILIATE LINK (highest converting)
   - Faith: Amazon Christian books page
   - Fitness: MyProtein or Transparent Labs
   - Tech: Notion or Jasper

4. SECONDARY AFFILIATE LINKS
   - "Tools I use" page with all affiliate links

5. OWN PRODUCTS (when ready)
   - Notion templates
   - Apps
   - Courses
```

### Link Placement Rules

| Placement | Frequency | Style |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Bio link (permanent) | Always there | "free [lead magnet] + tools I use" |
| Pinned post | Updated monthly | Natural mention of top product |
| In-post mention | Max 1 in 5 posts (20%) | Contextual, never forced |
| Thread closer | On relevant threads | "Resources I mentioned: [links in bio]" |
| Story/highlight (IG) | Weekly | "Tools I use" highlight |
| Reply self-thread | On viral posts | Self-reply with relevant link |

### Compliance (FTC Required)

**Every post with affiliate links MUST include:**
- X/Twitter: "contains affiliate links" or "ad" in post, OR "#ad" hashtag
- Instagram: Paid partnership tag OR "#ad #affiliate" in caption
- TikTok: "Paid promotion" toggle OR "#ad" in description
- LinkedIn: "Affiliate links included" disclosure
- Linktree: "Some links are affiliate links" disclaimer at top

**Template disclosure text:**
```
(some links are affiliate - I earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. I only recommend what I actually use.)
```
