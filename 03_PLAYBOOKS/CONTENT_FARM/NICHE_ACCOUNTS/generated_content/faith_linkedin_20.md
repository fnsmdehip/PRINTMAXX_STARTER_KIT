## Post 12
Practical prayer for business decisions:

1. Write the decision on paper
2. List pros and cons
3. Pray: "God, is there anything I'm not seeing?"
4. Wait 24 hours
5. Check your gut after the wait

12 major decisions this year. 10 turned out well.

The waiting period is key. Most bad decisions are made in urgency.
