## Design notes

### Colors
- Primary: Dark gradient (black to purple or black to blue)
- Accent: Electric blue or gold for emphasis
- Text: White on dark backgrounds
- Secondary text: Gray or muted accent color

### Fonts
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold, Montserrat Bold)
- Body: Regular sans-serif (Inter Regular)
- Numbers: Extra bold or different weight for emphasis

### Layout style
- Centered text on most slides
- Large text (minimum 48pt for headlines on mobile)
- Maximum 3-4 lines of text per slide
- Plenty of negative space
- Consistent margins (40px minimum from edges)

### Visual elements
- Subtle gradients preferred over solid colors
- Minimal icons (only where they add meaning)
- No stock photos
- Abstract shapes for visual interest
- Consistent corner radius if using containers
