# Faith Niche - TikTok/Reels Scripts (10 Scripts)

**Account:** @DailyGraceQuotes
**Format:** 3-sec hook, 15-sec value, CTA
**Style:** Calm but direct. Warm lighting. Text overlays with scripture. Talking head or text-on-screen.

---

## Script 1 - Problem-Solution (CS001)

**Hook (0-3s):**
[Text overlay: "stop praying like this"]
"Stop praying the same vague prayer every night."

**Value (3-18s):**
"Instead, try the 5-minute framework. 30 seconds gratitude. 30 seconds confession. 1 minute asking for specific things. 2 minutes of silence just listening. 1 minute committing your day. I did this for 30 days. My anxiety dropped and I actually started hearing answers."

**CTA (18-22s):**
"Comment PRAYER if you want the full template."

**Text overlays:** "5-Min Prayer Framework" / each step numbered on screen
