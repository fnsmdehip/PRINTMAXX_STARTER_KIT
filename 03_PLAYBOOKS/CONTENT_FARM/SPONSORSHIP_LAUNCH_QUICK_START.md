# Sponsorship Launch Quick Start - This Week

**Goal:** Close your first sponsorship deal within 14 days.

---

## DAY 1-2: CHOOSE YOUR NICHE & GATHER METRICS

### Step 1: Pick your strongest niche
- Which niche has your highest followers?
- Which niche has highest engagement rate?
- Which niche uploads content most frequently (sponsors care about velocity)?
- Pick ONE niche to focus on first

### Step 2: Gather these metrics
```
Your niche: _______________
Your followers: _______________
Your engagement rate: _______________
Average views per post: _______________
Your audience age: _______________
Your audience income level (if known): _______________
Top performing content topic: _______________
Posts per week: _______________
```

### Step 3: Calculate your base rate
```
Formula: Followers × Engagement Rate × Niche Multiplier = Base Rate

Example calculation:
- 50,000 followers
- 4% engagement rate
- 1.8 niche multiplier (tech/finance/health = 1.6-2.2)
- 50,000 × 0.04 × 1.8 = $3,600 base rate

Your calculation:
___________ × _____ × _____ = $_____ base rate
```

### Step 4: Set your prices
- TIER 1 (single post): Base rate × 0.5-0.7
- TIER 2 (series of 3-4): Base rate × 1.2-1.5
- TIER 3 (monthly retainer): Base rate × 0.8-1.0 per post

**Example:**
- Base: $3,600
- TIER 1: $1,800-2,200 (single post)
- TIER 2: $4,500-5,500 (3-post package)
- TIER 3: $3,000-3,600/month (monthly retainer)

"No problem! Here's what we could do at different price points:
- $500: [specific deliverable]

### Close first deal at TIER 1

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

❌ No contract → Ask for one
❌ "Exposure" as payment → Decline (exception: very early, micro-accounts)
❌ Demanding all rights to content → Limit to 6-12 months
❌ Unrealistic deadline (24 hours) → Negotiate or decline
❌ Brand has bad reputation → Decline (worth more than quick $)
❌ Sketchy product → Decline (hurts your credibility)
❌ Competitor of established partner → Check exclusivity clause

## SCRIPTS FOR NEGOTIATION

### When they offer $500 and you want $2,000:

"Thanks for the offer! For [specific deliverables], my rate is $2,000.

I could do $1,500 if we can [extend timeline / add exclusivity / include Stories / longer partnership].

Would that work for your budget?"

### When they say "We only have $500":

"I understand budget constraints. Here's what $500 gets you:
- 1 story mention + Instagram story
- 3-day discount code

If you want the full video + multiple posts (higher ROI), that's $2,000.

But let's do $500 as a start, and if it performs well, we can expand!"

### When they want free content:

"I appreciate the opportunity, but I charge for content creation. Here's why:
- Professional production (not phone video)
- Authentic integration (not obvious ad)
- Performance tracking and reporting

I'd rather do paid partnership where both win, or we could do [product trade for fair value].

What would make sense?"
