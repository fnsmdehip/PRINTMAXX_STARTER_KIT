# DM Funnel Scripts

10 proven DM funnel scripts for converting followers to customers. Copy, customize, deploy.

---

## DM funnel fundamentals

### The psychology

**Why DMs convert:**
- Personal and direct
- Higher attention than feed
- Conversation builds trust
- Lower resistance than cold sales
- Permission-based (they messaged first)

**Conversion benchmarks:**
| Stage | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Post viewers → DM | 1-3% |
| DM open rate | 80-95% |
| Reply rate | 30-50% |
| Conversation → click | 20-40% |
| Click → purchase | 5-15% |

### The funnel structure

```
1. Trigger post (CTA: DM me "[keyword]")
      ↓
2. Auto-response (deliver value + qualify)
      ↓
3. Follow-up 1 (build rapport + introduce offer)
      ↓
4. Follow-up 2 (handle objections + urgency)
      ↓
5. Follow-up 3 (final push or nurture)
```

## Script 1: Free resource funnel

**Best for:** Lead magnets, email list building

### Trigger post

```
I spent 40 hours building a [resource name].

It includes:
• [Benefit 1]
• [Benefit 2]
• [Benefit 3]

Comment "RESOURCE" and I'll DM it to you for free.

(Giving it away to first 100 people)
```

### Auto-response (Message 1)

```
Hey [Name]!

Thanks for your interest in the [resource name].

Before I send it over - quick question:

What's your biggest challenge with [topic] right now?

(This helps me make sure the resource is actually useful for you)
```

### Follow-up 1 (After they reply)

```
That makes total sense - [acknowledge their challenge].

Here's the [resource]: [link]

This should help you with [specific benefit related to their answer].

Let me know if you have any questions once you go through it!
```

### Follow-up 2 (24-48 hours later)

```
Hey [Name] - did you get a chance to check out the [resource]?

Curious what you thought about [specific part].

Also, if you found it helpful, I have a [paid offer] that goes deeper on [topic].

Want me to share more about it?
```

### Follow-up 3 (If interested in paid offer)

```
Awesome! Here's what's included:

[Benefit 1]
[Benefit 2]
[Benefit 3]

People who've used it say [social proof].

Here's the link: [link]

Any questions before you check it out?
```

## Script 2: Free consultation funnel

**Best for:** Services, coaching, high-ticket

### Trigger post

```

• Your current [situation]
• What's holding you back
• A custom action plan

If you're serious about [result], DM me "STRATEGY".

```

### Auto-response (Message 1)

```

1. What's your current [metric/situation]?
2. Where do you want to be in 90 days?
3. What have you already tried?

```

### Follow-up 1 (After they answer)

```

Based on what you told me, I think I can definitely help.

The main thing I'd focus on is [personalized insight].

Want to hop on a quick call to map out a plan?

Here's my calendar: [link]

Pick any time that works for you.
```

### Follow-up 2 (If no booking - 24 hours)

```
Hey [Name] - just following up.

I have a few spots left this week for strategy calls.

Based on what you shared, I have some specific ideas that could help you [reach goal].

Is there anything holding you back from booking?

Happy to answer any questions.
```

### Follow-up 3 (After call - pitch paid service)

```
Great chatting with you, [Name]!

As promised, here's a summary of what we discussed:

1. [Key insight 1]
2. [Key insight 2]
3. [Action step]

If you want help implementing this, I'd love to work with you through my [program/service].

Here's more info: [link]

No pressure - let me know if you have any questions!
```

### Setup best practices

1. **Always personalize**
   - Use {{first_name}} variables
   - Reference their content
   - Don't sound like a bot

2. **Delay messages**
   - Don't send instantly (looks automated)
   - 5-10 minute delays minimum
   - Vary timing

3. **Have manual fallback**
   - Some responses need human touch
   - Check DMs daily
   - Jump in for complex questions

## Compliance notes

### What to avoid

- No spam (only message people who opted in)
- No fake urgency (if you say 24 hours, mean it)
- No false claims (be honest about results)
- Clear opt-out option
- Don't scrape or buy DM lists

### Disclosure requirements

- If affiliate: Mention it
- If paid promotion: Disclose
- If collecting emails: Privacy policy needed
