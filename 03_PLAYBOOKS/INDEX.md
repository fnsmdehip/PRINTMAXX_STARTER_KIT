## TIKTOK_SHOP (New Method)

Social commerce via TikTok's integrated shopping platform. $20B+ GMV in 2024.

### Revenue Streams
1. **Affiliate** - Promote others' products (5-30% commission)
2. **Seller** - Sell own inventory (30-60% margins)
3. **Dropship** - US warehouse fulfillment (15-40% margins)
4. **Live selling** - Real-time shopping streams

### Business Model Comparison

| Model | Capital | Inventory | Risk | Best For |
|-------|---------|-----------|------|----------|
| Affiliate | $0 | None | Low | Content creators, beginners |
| Seller | $500-5000 | Yes | Medium | Product owners |
| Dropship | $100-500 | No | Medium | Arbitrage, scaling |
| Live | $0-500 | Optional | Low-Med | Engagement-focused |

### Requirements
- **Affiliate:** 1,000+ followers, 18+, US account
- **Seller:** US business registration, EIN, bank account

### Fee Structure (Seller)
- Referral fee: 2-8%
- Transaction fee: 2.8% + $0.30
- Affiliate commission: You set (typically 10-25%)

### PRINTMAXX Integration
- Content Farm accounts drive traffic to Shop affiliate links
- Faith/Fitness/Women showcases with curated products
- Apps cross-promote related physical products

## STREAMER_CLIPS (New Method)

Clip trending streamers, repurpose on viral pages.

### Revenue Streams
1. **Creator Fund** (TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
2. **Direct payments from streamers** (promotion value)
3. **Sponsorships** (gaming/streaming products)
4. **Affiliate** (streaming gear, games)

### Automation Pipeline

```
1. Find top trending streamers (Twitch, YouTube)
2. Pull YouTube re-uploads of streams
3. Extract transcript via YouTube API
4. AI analyzes for clippable moments (high engagement, funny, dramatic)
5. Auto-clip segments (30-60 sec)
6. Add captions, format for platform
7. Queue for posting across accounts
8. Track performance, iterate on clip selection
```
