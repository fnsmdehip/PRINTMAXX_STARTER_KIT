## 5AG Email 2: Best Content (Send Day 1, 5AM)

**Subject:** The 3-exercise leg day that changed my physique
**Preview text:** I stopped doing leg press. Started doing these instead. Results in 4 weeks.

---

I spent 2 years doing leg press, leg extensions, and hamstring curls.

My legs looked the same.

Then I stripped it back to 3 exercises. Did them heavy. Did them consistently. Saw more progress in 4 weeks than the previous 6 months.

**The 3-Exercise Leg Day:**

**1. Barbell Back Squat -- 5 sets of 5**
- Go heavy. Rest 2-3 minutes between sets.
- Depth matters more than weight. Below parallel or it doesn't count.
- Beginner: Goblet squat, 4 sets of 8

**2. Romanian Deadlift -- 4 sets of 8**
- Slow on the way down. 3-second eccentric.
- Feel the stretch in your hamstrings at the bottom.
- Beginner: Dumbbell RDL, 3 sets of 10

**3. Walking Lunges -- 3 sets of 12 per leg**
- Hold dumbbells at your side.
- Long stride. Knee tracks over toes.
- Beginner: Bodyweight lunges, 3 sets of 10 per leg

**That's the whole workout.** 3 exercises. 12 working sets. 35-40 minutes.

The reason most leg days don't work: too many isolation exercises, not enough compound movements with progressive overload.

Squat heavy. Hinge heavy. Lunge loaded. Repeat every week. Add 5 lbs when you can. Your legs will grow.

**Nutrition tip:** Eat 30-40g of protein within 2 hours of this workout. Doesn't matter if it's a shake, chicken, or Greek yogurt. Just hit the protein.

# BEEHIIV AUTOMATION SETUP NOTES

## How to Load These Into Beehiiv

1. Go to Automations > Create New Automation
2. Trigger: "New subscriber joins"
3. Add 5 email steps with delays:
   - Email 1: Immediate (0 delay)
   - Email 2: 1 day delay
   - Email 3: 3 days delay (2 days after Email 2)
   - Email 4: 5 days delay (2 days after Email 3)
   - Email 5: 7 days delay (2 days after Email 4)
4. Paste subject line, preview text, and body for each email
5. Add UTM parameters to all links for tracking
6. Test with your own email before activating

## Key Principles Behind the Sequence

| Email | Goal | CTA |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Deliverability + set expectations | Whitelist + read best content |
| 2 | Prove value immediately | Reply to email (boosts deliverability) |
| 3 | Build connection + referral program | Share with a friend |
| 4 | First product mention (soft sell) | Buy template (discounted) |
| 5 | Learn what they want + social follow | Reply with number + follow on social |

## Deliverability Best Practices

- Email 2 asks for a reply. This trains Gmail/Outlook that your emails are wanted.
- Keep images minimal in welcome sequence (text-heavy emails have better deliverability for new senders).
- Always include unsubscribe link (Beehiiv handles this automatically).
- Monitor open rates per email. If any email drops below 30%, rewrite the subject line.
