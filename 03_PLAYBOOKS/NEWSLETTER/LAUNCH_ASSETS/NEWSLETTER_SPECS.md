## Newsletter 1: Morning Grace (Faith)

**Platform:** Beehiiv (free tier)
**Frequency:** Daily, 6:00 AM EST send
**Length:** 300-500 words
**Tagline:** "Start every morning anchored in scripture."
**From name:** Morning Grace
**Reply-to:** morninggrace@[domain].com

### Format (Every Issue)

```
1. Scripture of the Day (verse + reference)
2. 2-Minute Devotional (200-300 words, personal reflection)
3. Prayer Prompt (1-3 sentences to guide morning prayer)
4. Faith Resource of the Day (book, app, podcast, or article)
5. Footer: Share link, social links, unsubscribe
```

### Subject Line Formula

`[Scripture Reference] + [Benefit/Hook]`

Examples:
- "Philippians 4:6 -- The anxiety antidote nobody talks about"
- "Psalm 23:4 -- Read this before your hardest day"
- "Proverbs 3:5 -- Why your plan B might be God's plan A"
- "Romans 8:28 -- The verse that changed how I handle failure"
- "Isaiah 41:10 -- 30 seconds that will reset your fear"

### Monetization Path

| Milestone | Revenue Stream | Expected Revenue |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Day 1 | Affiliate links (Bible apps, Christian books) | $50-200/mo |
| 1K subs | Sponsored devotionals (1x/week) | $200-400/mo |
| 2.5K subs | Beehiiv Boost network | $100-300/mo |
| 5K subs | Premium tier ($5/mo, deeper study guides + prayer community) | $1,250-2,500/mo |
| 10K subs | Multiple sponsors per week | $2,000-4,000/mo |

### Cross-Sell Products

1. **Prayer Warrior Notion Template** ($17) -- Daily prayer tracker, scripture journal, gratitude log
2. **PrayerLock App** (when ready) -- Promote in every issue footer
3. **30-Day Faith Challenge** (free lead magnet) -- Drives signups
4. **Sunday Study Deep Dive** (premium content) -- Gated at $5/mo tier

### Growth Funnel

```

    --> Bio link to Beehiiv landing page
    --> Lead magnet: "30-Day Prayer Challenge" PDF
    --> Welcome sequence (5 emails)
    --> Daily newsletter
    --> Paid tier at 5K subs
```

### Audience Profile

- Women 25-55 (primary), men 30-60 (secondary)
- Active church attendees or seeking spiritual growth
- Morning routine people
- Values: community, growth, hope, consistency
- Already reads devotionals (Our Daily Bread, She Reads Truth, etc.)
