# Newsletter Growth Playbook: 0 to 1,000 Subscribers

How to grow Morning Grace, 5AM Gains, and The Stack to 1K subs each.

---

## The Growth Engine (Same for All 3)

```
Social post (value-first)
    --> Bio link to landing page
    --> Lead magnet download (email captured)
    --> Welcome sequence (5 emails, builds trust)
    --> Regular newsletter delivery
    --> Referral program (subscriber brings friends)
    --> Repeat
```

Every growth tactic feeds into this funnel. No exceptions.

---

## Tactic 1: Social Media Funnel

### The System

Post 3-5x/day on primary platform. Every post is a micro-preview of newsletter content. Bio link drives to Beehiiv landing page.

### Per-Newsletter Social Strategy

**Morning Grace (@DailyGraceQuotes)**

Primary platforms: Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok
Post types:
- Scripture quote graphics (Canva, 1080x1080)
- 60-second devotional video (talking head or text overlay)

- "The verse I needed to hear this morning."

- 30-second workout demo (film at gym, phone on tripod)

Bio CTA: "Free workout + meal plan every Mon/Wed/Fri at 5AM. Join 5AM Gains."

Primary platforms: X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok
Post types:

## Tactic 3: Cross-Promotion (Beehiiv Boost + Manual Swaps)

### Beehiiv Boost Network

Beehiiv's built-in cross-promotion system. Subscribers of similar newsletters see your recommendation.

**Setup:**
1. Go to Grow > Boosts
2. Set target CPM ($1-3/sub is typical)
3. Select audience filters (niche, geography, engagement)
4. Set daily budget ($5-20/day to start)
5. Monitor conversion rate, pause if CAC exceeds $3

**Budget allocation:**
- Month 1: $0 (organic only)
- Month 2: $5/day per newsletter ($450/mo total)
- Month 3: Scale winners to $10-20/day, pause losers

### Manual Newsletter Swaps

Find complementary newsletters. Agree to promote each other to your lists.

**Where to find swap partners:**
- Beehiiv community Slack
- Newsletter Crew Slack
- X/Twitter: search "[niche] newsletter" and DM creators
- Sparkloop marketplace
- r/Newsletters on Reddit

**Swap outreach template:**

```
Subject: Newsletter swap? [Your Newsletter] x [Their Newsletter]

Hey [Name],

I run [Newsletter Name] -- [one-line description]. We have [X] subscribers
in the [niche] space.

I've been reading [their newsletter] and think our audiences overlap well.
Would you be open to a newsletter swap? I'd mention your newsletter to my
list, you mention mine to yours.

Happy to go first if you want to see how I position it.

[Your name]
[Newsletter link]
```

**Ideal swap partners per newsletter:**

Morning Grace:
- Other faith/devotional newsletters
- Women's lifestyle newsletters
- Personal development / morning routine newsletters
- Parenting newsletters (faith-adjacent)

5AM Gains:
- Other fitness newsletters (different angle -- running, yoga, etc.)
- Men's health / biohacking newsletters
- Nutrition-focused newsletters
- Morning routine / productivity newsletters

The Stack:
- Other AI/tech newsletters (different angle -- news vs. tools vs. tutorials)
- Solopreneur / indie hacker newsletters
- Productivity newsletters
- Marketing automation newsletters

## Tactic 4: Lead Magnets

### Per-Newsletter Lead Magnets

**Morning Grace: "30-Day Prayer Challenge"**

Format: 12-page PDF
Contents:
- Day-by-day prayer prompts
- 30 scripture references organized by theme
- Prayer journal template pages
- Morning routine suggestions

Landing page headline: "Start every morning in prayer. 30 days. 30 prompts. Free."
CTA button text: "Send me the challenge"

**5AM Gains: "7-Day Meal Prep Starter Kit"**

Format: 8-page PDF
Contents:
- 7 days of meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
- Grocery list for the full week
- Macro breakdown per meal
- 15-minute prep instructions per meal

Landing page headline: "Meal prep Sunday sorted. 7 days of meals. 1 grocery run. Free."
CTA button text: "Get the meal plan"

**The Stack: "50 AI Tools That Replace $5,000/mo in Software"**

Format: 15-page PDF
Contents:
- 50 tools organized by category (writing, design, video, code, research, automation)
- Pricing comparison with what they replace
- Best free alternatives highlighted
- Setup difficulty rating (1-5)

Landing page headline: "50 AI tools. $5,000/mo in savings. One free PDF."
CTA button text: "Send me the list"

### Lead Magnet Distribution

Put the lead magnet link everywhere:
- Social media bio (primary)
- Pinned post on X/Twitter
- Instagram link in bio (Linktree or direct)
- LinkedIn featured section
- Reddit posts (where allowed, add value first)
- YouTube video descriptions (when creating video content)
- Guest post author bio
- Podcast show notes (if guesting)

## Tactic 6: Sponsor Outreach at 1K Subscribers

### When to Start Pitching

- 1,000 subscribers minimum
- 35%+ open rate
- Consistent publishing history (8+ weeks)
- Clean engagement metrics to share

### Recommended First Sponsors

**Morning Grace (1K subs):**
- Hallow App (Catholic prayer app, growing fast)
- Pray.com (meditation + prayer app)
- Christian Book Distributors (CBD)
- Faithful Counseling (BetterHelp faith brand)
- Local church management software (Planning Center, Tithe.ly)

**5AM Gains (1K subs):**
- LMNT (electrolyte brand, creator-friendly program)
- Athletic Greens / AG1
- Momentous supplements
- Whoop fitness tracker
- NOBULL (fitness apparel)

**The Stack (1K subs):**
- Notion (affiliate + sponsor hybrid)
- Jasper AI
- Cursor (AI code editor)
- Zapier
- Lemon Squeezy (payment platform for solopreneurs)

### Sponsor Pitch Email Template

```
Subject: Sponsorship opportunity: [Newsletter Name] ([X] subscribers, [Y]% open rate)

Hey [Sponsor Contact Name],

I run [Newsletter Name], a [frequency] newsletter for [audience description].
We have [subscriber count] subscribers with a [open rate]% open rate and
[click rate]% click rate.

Our audience is [1-sentence demographic + psychographic profile].

I'm reaching out because [specific reason their product fits your audience].

Sponsorship options:
- Primary placement (top of email): $[rate]/send
- Dedicated product review: $[rate]
- 4-send package: $[rate] (15% discount)

Every placement includes:
- Native copy written by me (sounds like editorial, not an ad)
- Direct link to your landing page or product
- Performance report after send (opens, clicks, click rate)

Happy to send a sample issue and full media kit.

Best,
[Your name]
[Newsletter name]
[Link to landing page]
```

### Pricing Guide at 1K Subscribers

| Placement | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Primary sponsor (top of email) | $50-100/send | Highest visibility |
| Mid-email mention | $30-50/send | Natural integration |
| Classified link | $15-25/send | Small text link at bottom |
| Dedicated send (full email about sponsor) | $200-300 | Rare, premium only |
| 4-send package | $150-350 | Bundle discount |

Raise prices 20-30% every time you double subscriber count.

### Media Kit (Create at 500+ Subs)

Include:
- Subscriber count + growth trend
- Open rate + click rate
- Audience demographics (age, gender, interests, location)
- Publishing schedule
- 3 sample issues
- Pricing table
- Testimonials from readers
- Social proof (social followers, notable subscribers)

## Growth Timeline: 0 to 1,000

### Week 1-2: Foundation

- [ ] Create Beehiiv account and publication
- [ ] Write landing page copy (use templates above)
- [ ] Create lead magnet PDF
- [ ] Write first 4 newsletter issues
- [ ] Set up welcome sequence (5 emails)
- [ ] Create social media accounts
- [ ] Post 3-5x/day on primary platform

### Week 3-4: Consistency

- [ ] Publish on schedule without missing
- [ ] Post 3-5x/day on social
- [ ] Reply to every subscriber who responds
- [ ] Enable Beehiiv referral program
- [ ] Reach out to 5 newsletter swap partners
- [ ] Target: 100-200 subscribers

### Month 2: Acceleration

- [ ] Start Beehiiv Boost ($5/day)
- [ ] Execute first newsletter swap
- [ ] Create second lead magnet or upgrade existing one
- [ ] Post in relevant Reddit communities (add value, link in bio only)
- [ ] Cross-promote between your own 3 newsletters
- [ ] Target: 300-500 subscribers

### Month 3: Push to 1K

- [ ] Increase Boost budget for best-performing newsletter
- [ ] Execute 3-5 more newsletter swaps
- [ ] Launch "subscriber challenge" (refer 3 friends this week, win X)
- [ ] Create viral-potential content (listicles, controversies, hot takes)
- [ ] Guest on 1-2 podcasts or write guest posts
- [ ] Target: 500-1,000 subscribers

### Month 4-6: Monetization

- [ ] Pitch first sponsors at 1K subs
- [ ] Optimize affiliate placements based on click data
- [ ] Test paid tier pricing with existing engaged subscribers
- [ ] Double down on highest-converting growth channel
- [ ] Target: 1,000-2,500 subscribers

## Growth Math

To reach 1,000 subscribers in 90 days, you need ~11 new subscribers per day.

Where they come from:

| Channel | Daily Subs | % of Total |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Social media (organic posts) | 4-5 | 40% |
| Lead magnet conversions | 2-3 | 25% |
| Referral program | 1-2 | 15% |
| Newsletter swaps | 1 | 10% |
| Beehiiv Boost (paid) | 1 | 10% |
| **Total** | **~11** | **100%** |

This is conservative. If any single piece of content goes viral on social, you'll blow past this in a week.
