## Issue 9 - Friday Legs

**Subject:** the deload week nobody takes (but should)

**Workout of the Day:**

DELOAD LEGS (35 min - reduced volume)

1. Barbell Squat: 3 x 6 at 60% of working weight (2 min rest)
2. Romanian Deadlift: 2 x 10 at 60% (90 sec rest)
3. Leg Press: 2 x 12 light (60 sec rest)
4. Walking Lunges: 2 x 8 bodyweight (45 sec rest)
5. Light stretching and foam rolling: 10 min

**Why this matters:** Every 4th week should be a deload. Cut volume by 40-50%. Keep exercises the same. This lets your joints, tendons, and nervous system recover. Many people see their best visual changes during deload weeks because the body finally has time to supercompensate.

**Nutrition Tip:** Keep protein the same during deload. Reduce carbs slightly if you want since training demand is lower. Don't increase calories just because you feel recovered. The deload is rest, not reward.

**Supplement Spotlight:** Melatonin. 0.5-1mg before bed on deload week. Not every day. Just during recovery phases. Lower doses work better than 5-10mg (which causes grogginess). $4/month.

**Closer:** Rest is not weakness. It's strategy. Deload now. Come back stronger next week.
