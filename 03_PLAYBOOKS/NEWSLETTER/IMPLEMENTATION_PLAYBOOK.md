## Overview

Build email newsletters on Beehiiv (primary) and Substack (secondary/cross-post). Monetize through Beehiiv ad network, sponsorships, paid subscriptions, affiliate links, and cross-selling own products. Email is the lowest platform-risk channel because you own the list. Run 3 newsletters across faith, fitness, and tech/productivity niches.

Key insight: Annual subscribers retain at 44.1% vs 17.0% for monthly. Push annual pricing aggressively.

Proof: The Hustle sold for $27M (1.5M subs). Morning Brew $75M acquisition (4M subs). Solo newsletters at 10K subs commonly make $2-5K/mo from sponsorships alone. Beehiiv ad network pays $2-10 CPM with zero effort.

## Step-by-step implementation

### Day 1: Newsletter setup (3-4 hours)

**Task 1: Create Beehiiv publication**

1. Sign up at beehiiv.com
2. Create publication:
   - Name: "[Niche] Insider" or "[Topic] Weekly" (clear, benefit-oriented)
   - URL: newsletter.yourdomain.com (or free beehiiv.com/subscribe/...)
   - Description: "One [frequency] email with [specific value]"
3. Choose template (clean, minimal, fast-loading)
4. Set up custom domain (if you have one)

**Task 2: Create welcome sequence**

Build 5-email welcome sequence (automated, sent after signup):

```
Email 1 (Immediate): Welcome + deliverability ask
  Subject: "Welcome! Do this first..."
  Body: Welcome, what to expect, ask to move to Primary inbox,
        link to best 3 past issues

Email 2 (Day 1): Quick win
  Subject: "The [niche] trick I use every day"
  Body: One actionable tip, builds trust

Email 3 (Day 3): Your story
  Subject: "Why I started this newsletter"
  Body: Origin story, credibility, what's coming

Email 4 (Day 5): Best resource
  Subject: "The most popular thing I've ever shared"
  Body: High-value content piece, affiliate link (natural)

Email 5 (Day 7): Soft pitch
  Subject: "Quick question..."
  Body: Ask what they want more of, mention paid tier if exists
```

Reference: `LAUNCH_ASSETS/` for pre-built sequences.

**Task 3: Create landing page**

Beehiiv has built-in landing pages. Optimize:
```
Headline: "[Specific benefit] in your inbox every [frequency]"
Subheadline: "Join [X]+ [target audience] getting [value prop]"
Email input + Subscribe button
Social proof: "As featured in..." or subscriber count
Sample content: Preview of what they'll get
```

Example:
```
Headline: "One prayer prompt + productivity tip every morning"
Subheadline: "Join 500+ Christian professionals starting their day right"
[Email] [Subscribe Free]
"Here's what last Tuesday's email looked like..." [preview]
```

### Day 2-3: Create first 4 issues (4-6 hours)

**Task 4: Write and schedule first 4 issues**

Issue structure (consistent format every week):
```
Subject line: Specific, curiosity-driven (not clickbait)
Preview text: Complement the subject, add intrigue

Issue body:
1. Opening hook (1-2 sentences, consequence-first)
2. Main content (3-5 key points with specifics)
3. Resource/tool of the week
4. Quick tip / actionable takeaway
5. CTA (reply to this email / share / upgrade)

Footer:
- Social links
- Referral program link
- Unsubscribe
```

Write 4 issues before launch so you have a buffer. Schedule weekly (Tues-Thurs mornings perform best).

**Task 5: Enable Beehiiv features**

Set up monetization features from Day 1:
1. Beehiiv Ad Network: Enable in settings (pays $2-10 CPM, zero effort)
2. Boosts: Enable to earn $1-3 per new subscriber from cross-promotions
3. Recommendations: Add 3-5 complementary newsletters (they'll recommend you back)
4. Referral program: Set up milestones (5 referrals = bonus content, 10 = template)

### Day 3-7: Launch and first subscribers

**Task 6: Seed initial subscribers**

First 100 subscribers (hardest to get, most important):
```
1. Post on Twitter/X: "I just launched a newsletter about [topic]. Here's what issue #1 looks like..." (screenshot + link)
2. Post on Reddit: Share valuable insight from issue, CTA to newsletter
3. Email personal contacts: "Hey, I started a newsletter about [X]. Would mean a lot if you subscribed."
4. LinkedIn post: Professional angle on your newsletter topic
5. Cross-promote: Subscribe to similar newsletters, offer to swap recommendations
6. Existing audience: Add subscribe CTA to all bios, websites, content
```

Target: 100 subscribers in first week.

**Task 7: Set up daily promotion engine**

Daily promotion routine (30 min):
```
1. Post newsletter excerpt on Twitter (2-3 posts from this week's issue)
2. Engage with 10-15 people in your niche
3. Reply to newsletter feedback/questions
4. Share one free insight that ties to newsletter content
5. Check Beehiiv analytics: opens, clicks, growth
```

### Week 2-4: Grow to 500+ subscribers

**Task 8: Implement referral program**

Beehiiv has built-in referral system:
```
Milestone 1 (3 referrals):  Free PDF guide
Milestone 2 (5 referrals):  Access to resource library
Milestone 3 (10 referrals): Exclusive template/tool
Milestone 4 (25 referrals): 1-on-1 call or premium content
```

Add referral CTA to every email:
"Share this newsletter with a friend and unlock [reward]"

**Task 9: Enable Beehiiv Boosts (passive income)**

Beehiiv Boosts pays you $1-3 every time a new subscriber opts into a partner newsletter through your publication.

Setup:
1. Go to Beehiiv > Monetize > Boosts
2. Browse available boost partners
3. Select relevant newsletters for your audience
4. They appear in your email footer or dedicated section
5. Get paid per opt-in

At 500 subs with 25% open rate, expect $10-50/mo from Boosts passively.

**Task 10: Cross-post to Substack**

Cross-post every issue to Substack for additional discovery:
1. Create matching Substack publication
2. Copy issue content after sending on Beehiiv
3. Use Substack Notes for short-form (like Twitter, built-in audience)
4. Substack discovery recommends your newsletter to new readers

Double distribution, zero extra content creation.

## Scaling path

### $0-500/mo (Month 1-3)
1 newsletter, 0-1K subs. Beehiiv ads + Boosts only. Focus: content quality, subscriber growth.

### $500-5K/mo (Month 3-6)
1-3 newsletters, 1K-5K subs each. First sponsorships. Launch paid tier on best performer. Focus: referral program, cross-promotion.

### $5K-68K/mo (Month 6-12)
3 newsletters, 5K-50K+ subs each. Regular sponsor deals. Paid tier at 5-10% conversion. Cross-sell products. Focus: automation, ad sales, premium content.

## Risk management

- **Email deliverability:** Use Beehiiv (handles authentication). Monitor open rates. Clean list quarterly (remove inactive >90 days).
- **Subscriber churn:** Push annual subscriptions (44.1% retention vs 17.0% monthly). Maintain consistent quality.
- **Low open rates:** Test subject lines. Send Tues-Thurs 7-9am. Segment by engagement level.
- **Sponsorship dependency:** Diversify: ads + paid subs + affiliate + products. Never depend on one sponsor.
- **Platform risk:** Export email list monthly. Beehiiv allows full export. You own the list.
