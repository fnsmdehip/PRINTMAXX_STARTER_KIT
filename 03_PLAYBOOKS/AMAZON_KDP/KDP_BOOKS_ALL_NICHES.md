# Amazon KDP Book Ideas - All 33 Niches

**Purpose:** Passive income through low-content and ebook publishing on Amazon KDP. Each niche contains 5 book ideas (3 low-content + 2 ebooks) with keywords, design directions, and title formulas.

**Revenue Model:** $1-3 per journal/planner sale (100-500 copies/month potential) + $2-8 per ebook (higher margins, SEO-driven).

**Setup Cost:** $0 upfront (free KDP account) + minimal design time (AI-generated covers via Gemini).

**Time to Revenue:** First book live in 2-3 days. Break-even on first 10 sales per book.
