## 1. Lead Magnet (Free): "5 Clips That Went Viral"

### Purpose
- Capture emails from aspiring clippers
- Demonstrate expertise with real examples
- Pre-qualify buyers (serious clippers vs curious)
- Build anticipation for paid product

### Content Outline

**Page 1: Cover**
```
5 CLIPS THAT MADE $10K+ EACH
(and the exact formula behind each one)

by [Your Name]
```

**Page 2: Intro (1 page)**
- "These 5 clips changed everything for me"
- Brief credibility (your total views, accounts, clients)
- What you'll learn in this PDF
- No fluff promise

**Page 3-7: The 5 Clips (1 page each)**

Each clip breakdown includes:
1. Screenshot with view count
2. The hook used (exact words)
3. Why it worked (platform algorithm insight)
4. The viral trigger (emotion/curiosity/shock)
5. One actionable takeaway

**Clip 1: The Rage Clip**
- Streamer loses it
- Hook: "He just broke his $3,000 monitor"
- Trigger: Schadenfreude + curiosity
- Takeaway: Clip the moment AFTER the peak emotion

**Clip 2: The Skill Clip**
- Insane gameplay moment
- Hook: "This shouldn't be possible"
- Trigger: Awe + "how did they do that"
- Takeaway: Add slow-mo replay for impact

**Clip 3: The Drama Clip**
- Two streamers beef
- Hook: "He really said that on stream"
- Trigger: Conflict + parasocial investment
- Takeaway: Include reaction, not just statement

**Clip 4: The Wholesome Clip**
- Unexpected heartwarming moment
- Hook: "This is why I love this community"
- Trigger: Emotional contrast (unexpected wholesomeness in gaming)
- Takeaway: Wholesome clips get shared, not just watched

**Clip 5: The Meta/Commentary Clip**
- Streamer drops wisdom
- Hook: "No one talks about this"
- Trigger: Insight + "they said what I was thinking"
- Takeaway: Commentary clips build authority, not just views

**Page 8: The Hook Formula**
```
[EMOTION] + [SPECIFICITY] + [CURIOSITY GAP]

Bad:  "Funny gaming moment"
Good: "He lost $50,000 because of ONE missed shot"
```

**Page 9: Next Steps CTA**
- "Want the full 47 hook templates I use?"
- "Ready to build your clipper business?"
- Link to Clipper Cash Course
- Scarcity: "Currently accepting new students"

### Delivery Mechanism

**Primary: Gumroad (Free product)**
- Zero friction signup
- Automatic PDF delivery
- Captures email for sequence

**Secondary: ConvertKit Landing Page**
- Embedded form on your own domain
- Delivers via automation
- Tags subscriber as "clipper_lead"

**Tertiary: Direct on Twitter**
- "Reply CLIPS and I'll send it"
- Manual DM first 100, then automate with TypeForm

## 3. Premium Offer ($247/mo): "Clipper Network Inner Circle"

### What's Included

**1. Full Course Access**
- Everything in Clipper Cash Course
- All future updates

**2. Private Community (Discord or Skool)**
- General chat
- Clip critiques channel
- Deal flow channel (share opportunities)
- Wins channel
- Direct access to you

**3. Weekly Group Calls (1 hour)**
- Hot seats (2-3 per call)
- Q&A
- Live clip reviews
- Market updates

**4. Deal Flow Access**
- You share streamer opportunities you can't fulfill
- Members get first dibs on paid gigs
- Revenue share on referred deals (you take 10%)

**5. Clipper Network Inclusion**
- Members can join your network as clippers
- Guaranteed $1/1k views work
- Build portfolio while earning

**6. Templates & Tools Updates**
- New hook templates monthly
- Platform algorithm updates
- New outreach scripts

### Retention Strategies

**Month 1: Quick Win Focus**
- Day 1: Welcome call or Loom
- Day 3: First clip assignment
- Day 7: Check-in, troubleshoot
- Day 14: First deal referral opportunity
- Day 30: Win celebration + testimonial ask

**Ongoing Retention:**
- Monthly "Clipper of the Month" award
- Leaderboard (views generated)
- Exclusive deal access (creates FOMO if they leave)
- Annual member discount ($197/mo instead of $247)

**Churn Prevention:**
- Cancel flow asks "what would make you stay?"
- Offer pause option (1 month) vs cancel
- Win-back email at 30 days post-cancel

### Member Journey

```
Week 1:  Onboarding, set goals, join calls
Week 2:  First clips live, get feedback
Week 3:  First deal referral (if ready)
Week 4:  Full integration, sharing wins
Month 2: Independent operation, helping newbies
Month 3+: Leadership, may become moderator
```

### Economics

**Conservative (20 members):**
- 20 x $247 = $4,940/mo

**Target (50 members):**
- 50 x $247 = $12,350/mo

**Scaled (100 members):**
- 100 x $247 = $24,700/mo

**Churn assumptions:**
- Target <10% monthly
- 6-month average tenure
- LTV: $1,482 per member

## 4. Done-With-You ($1,997): "Clipper Agency Setup"

- Account architecture (how many, what platforms)
- Revenue goals and timeline

**Week 2: Infrastructure Setup**
- Set up 3 accounts (TikTok, YouTube, IG)

**Week 4: Monetization & Handoff**
- Creator fund applications submitted

- Affiliate links integrated
- Full documentation handoff
- 30-day async support begins

### Who This Is For

**Ideal Client:**
- Streamers who want clip distribution but no time
- Brands entering gaming/streaming space
- Content agencies adding clip services
- Entrepreneurs with capital but no expertise

**Red Flags (Decline These):**
- "I want to make $10k/mo in 30 days"
- No budget for tools ($50-100/mo minimum)
- Expecting done-FOR-you (it's done-WITH-you)
- Already tried and failed without understanding why

### Sales Process

```
1. Apply via form (qualify budget, timeline, goals)
2. 15-min discovery call (or async Loom exchange)
3. Custom proposal (if fit)
4. Payment (50% upfront, 50% at week 2)
5. Onboarding call scheduled
```
