## FAQ section

### Q1: How long until I see results?
Most users see improvement in 7 days. Full warmup takes 14-21 days. Badly damaged domains may take 30+ days.

### Q2: Will this affect my normal email sending?
No. Warmup emails go to a separate folder. Your regular inbox stays clean. Warmup conversations auto-archive after 24 hours.

### Q3: Is this against Gmail/Outlook terms?
We use real engagement between real inboxes. No bots, no fake addresses. This is how email naturally works. Just automated.

### Q4: What if I'm already on a blacklist?
Our blacklist monitor will detect it. We provide steps to request removal. Some blacklists clear in 24 hours, others take longer.

### Q5: Can I warm up multiple domains at once?
Yes. Each inbox is separate. Warm up 10 different domains simultaneously. Each gets its own reputation score.

### Q6: Do you support Google Workspace and Office 365?
Yes. Both fully supported. Personal Gmail accounts work too, but business accounts are recommended for cold outreach.

### Q7: What happens when I stop warming up?
Your reputation slowly declines over 2-4 weeks. We recommend keeping warmup running at low volume (10/day) even during active campaigns.
