**Last updated:** 2026-01-20
**Duration:** 10 minutes (for church license prospects)

## Introduction (1 minute)

### Opening

"Thanks for taking the time. I'll show you how DailyAnchor works for churches in about 10 minutes.

Quick question: How does your congregation currently do daily devotionals? Apps, printed guides, nothing formal?"

[Listen, then tailor demo]

### Common answers and pivots

**"We use YouVersion"** - "Great app. DailyAnchor is simpler and lets you upload your own content. No social features, no distractions."

**"We print devotional guides"** - "That works, but hard to track engagement. With DailyAnchor, you see who's reading, who needs encouragement."

**"We don't have anything"** - "Perfect. I'll show you how to get your whole congregation reading together in a week."

## Part 1: Individual experience (3 minutes)

### Show the daily devotional

"First, let me show you what your members see."

[Open app on phone]

"Each morning, they get this: a short scripture passage, a brief reflection, and a prayer prompt. The whole thing takes 5 minutes."

### Walk through a devotional

[Read through example devotional]

"Scripture here. Reflection here - notice it's short, not a 10-paragraph essay. Prayer prompt at the bottom. Tap to mark complete."

### Show the streak

"This is the streak counter. 45 days in a row for this account. People get attached to their streaks. It's healthy accountability without public shaming."

### Show the calendar

"Calendar view shows which days they completed. Green means done. They can tap any past day to revisit that devotional."

"This is the part churches love most. You can upload your own devotionals."

[Show upload interface]

"Your pastor writes a devotional? Upload it here. Add the scripture reference, the reflection, the prayer prompt. Schedule it for a specific date or add it to a plan."

"Churches use this for:
- Pastor's daily devotional (replaces email newsletters)

- Special seasons (Lent, Advent, mission trips)
- New member onboarding (welcome series)

Your content, your voice, delivered through an app people actually open."

## Common questions during demos

### "Can members see each other's streaks?"
"Only if you enable it. Default is private. You can turn on optional leaderboards for small groups if they want friendly competition."

### "What if we already have content?"
"Even better. Upload your existing devotionals. We format them for the app. Most churches migrate their content in an afternoon."

### "Do members need to pay?"
"No. The church license covers all members. Individual members don't pay anything. They just download the free app and join your church."

### "What about youth/kids?"
"The current content is written for adults. Some churches create separate groups with age-appropriate content they upload themselves. Teen ministry is on our roadmap."

### "How is this different from YouVersion?"
"YouVersion is a Bible app with reading plans. We're a devotional app with church features. Key differences: no social feed, church dashboard, custom content upload, simpler interface."

### "What denomination?"
"Non-denominational. Our default content is biblically grounded without taking sides on theological debates. With custom upload, you control the theological slant."
