## FAQ section

### Q1: What translation do you use?
NIV for most content. Some devotionals reference ESV or NLT when the translation matters for meaning. We always note which version.

### Q2: Is this for a specific denomination?
Non-denominational. We focus on scripture, not theology debates. Christians from Catholic, Protestant, Orthodox, and non-denominational backgrounds use the app.

### Q3: Can I use this with my small group?
Yes. Premium users can share reading plans. Church License gives you a dashboard to see group progress and discussion guides.

### Q4: What if I miss a day?
Your streak resets, but your history stays. The app shows a message like "Welcome back" not "You failed." Grace, not guilt.

### Q5: How long are the reading plans?
7 days (starter), 14 days (standard), 21 days (deep dive), and 40 days (intensive). Most users start with 7-day plans to build the habit.

### Q6: Can I write my own devotionals?
Church License includes custom devotional upload. Write content for your congregation, upload to DailyAnchor, distribute through the app.

### Q7: Is my journal private?
Yes. Encrypted on your device. We can't read it. You can export as text anytime.

### Q8: Do you have audio versions?
Premium includes audio devotionals. Listen during commute, workout, or when you'd rather not look at a screen.

### Q9: What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Free gives you daily devotionals and basic streaks. Premium adds audio, advanced analytics, 30-day offline, prayer journal with reminders, and early access to new content.

### Q10: Can I cancel Premium anytime?
Yes. Monthly subscriptions cancel instantly. No questions, no hoops.
