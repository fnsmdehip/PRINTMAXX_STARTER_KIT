# DailyAnchor App Onboarding Flow

**Last updated:** 2026-01-20

---

## Overview

Goal: Get users to complete their first devotional within 5 minutes of opening the app.

Key metric: First devotional completion rate
Target: > 70% complete first devotional on day 1

---

## Step 1: Welcome (30 seconds)

### Screen: Welcome

**Headline:** Start your day grounded

**Subhead:** 5 minutes of scripture, reflection, and prayer. Every morning.

**Visual:** Calm sunrise illustration, no photos of people (inclusive)

**CTA:** Get started

---

## Step 2: Choose your focus (30 seconds)

### Screen: What would you like to focus on?

**Options (pick one):**
- Building a daily habit (for new believers or returning to faith)
- Deepening my prayer life (for consistent believers wanting more)
- Finding peace in a busy season (for stressed/anxious users)
- Growing with my church community (if church-referred)

**Note:** This selection personalizes their first reading plan recommendation. All content is still accessible.

**CTA:** Continue

### Skip option
"Skip for now" - defaults to general daily devotional

---

## Step 3: Set your time (30 seconds)

### Screen: When do you want to be reminded?

**Headline:** Pick your anchor time

**Time picker:**
- Default: 7:00 AM
- Quick options: 6:00 AM, 7:00 AM, 8:00 AM, Custom

**Note:** This is when we send the daily notification. Users can change anytime.

**CTA:** Set reminder

### Skip option
"Remind me later" - prompts again after first week

---

## Step 4: First devotional (3 minutes)

### Screen: Today's devotional

**Headline:** Here's today's reading

Go straight into the devotional content:
1. Scripture passage (3-5 verses)
2. Brief reflection (150 words max)
3. Prayer prompt

**After reading:**
- Mark complete button
- "How was this?" (simple feedback: good / too long / not relevant)

**CTA:** Mark complete

---

## Step 5: Streak started (30 seconds)

### Screen: Day 1 complete

**Headline:** You're anchored

**Visual:** Green checkmark, streak counter showing "1"

**Message:** "Tomorrow at [selected time], we'll have another reading ready. See you then."

**Optional actions:**
- Explore reading plans
- Set up prayer journal
- Share with a friend

**CTA:** Done (goes to home screen)

---

## Home screen elements

After onboarding, the home screen shows:

1. **Today's card** - Current devotional with "Start" or "Completed" state
2. **Streak counter** - Current streak days
3. **Calendar view** - Month view with completed days highlighted
4. **Quick actions** - Journal, Reading Plans, Settings

## Drop-off recovery

### If user downloads but never opens
- Push notification after 24 hours: "Your first devotional is waiting"
- Email after 48 hours: "5 minutes to start"

### If user opens but doesn't complete onboarding
- Push notification after 4 hours: "Pick up where you left off"
- Show "Continue setup" card on next open

### If user completes Day 1 but not Day 2
- Push notification at their reminder time
- Email in evening: "Still time today"

### If user misses 3+ days
- Email: "We're still here" (no guilt, just invitation)
- Show "Welcome back" screen on next open with fresh devotional
