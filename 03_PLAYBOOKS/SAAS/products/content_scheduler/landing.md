## FAQ section

### Q1: Why is it so cheap?
We built this for solopreneurs. No enterprise features you don't need. No sales team. No fancy office. Savings passed to you.

### Q2: Do you support Instagram Reels and TikTok videos?
Yes. Upload videos up to 60 seconds. Longer videos on Pro and Agency plans. We handle the publishing.

### Q3: Can I customize posts per platform?
Yes. Write once and post everywhere. Or edit for each platform before scheduling. Your choice.

### Q4: What happens if a post fails to publish?
You get an email immediately. The post stays in your queue. Re-authenticate if needed and we retry automatically.

### Q5: Can I import content from a spreadsheet?
Yes. Upload a CSV with your posts. Map columns to our fields. Bulk schedule hundreds of posts at once.

### Q6: Do you offer a free plan?
No free plan, but a 14-day free trial with full features. No credit card required to start.

### Q7: How is this different from Buffer?
Price and features. Buffer charges $15/month for one channel. ContentQueue is $12 for four channels plus thread scheduling and content recycling.
