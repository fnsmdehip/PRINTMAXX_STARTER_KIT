## FAQ section

### Q1: How does this affect app performance?
SDK adds less than 100KB to your binary. Background processing only. No noticeable battery or CPU impact. Users don't notice.

### Q2: Is this privacy compliant?
Yes. No personal data collected by default. Session recordings auto-mask text inputs, keyboards, and sensitive fields. GDPR and CCPA compliant.

### Q3: Can users opt out?
Yes. Call one method to disable tracking for specific users. Or disable session recordings while keeping events.

### Q4: How do session recordings work?
We capture the view hierarchy, not actual screen content. Reconstructed playback, not video. Smaller files, less bandwidth, more privacy.

### Q5: Will Apple reject my app for using this?
No. We follow App Store guidelines. No private APIs. Many top apps use similar analytics. No rejections reported.

### Q6: How long does setup take?
Most developers are tracking sessions in under 10 minutes. Copy SDK, add API key, build, done.

### Q7: Can I export my data?
Yes. Export events and user data via API or CSV. Your data is yours.
