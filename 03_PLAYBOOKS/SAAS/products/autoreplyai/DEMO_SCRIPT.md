### Opening

"Thanks for taking the time. I'll show you how AutoReplyAI works in about 15 minutes. Feel free to ask questions as we go.

Quick background: you mentioned [their specific pain point from discovery call]. I'll focus on how we solve that, but let me know if you want to see anything else."

### Set expectations

"Here's what we'll cover:
1. How the AI learns from your docs
2. Live demo answering real questions
3. Dashboard and conversation management
4. Pricing and next steps

Sound good?"

## Part 1: Knowledge base (3 minutes)

### Show the knowledge base

"This is where you teach the AI. Right now I have an e-commerce FAQ loaded, similar to what you'd use.

You can add content three ways:
- Paste text directly (fastest)
- Upload PDFs or docs
- Enter a URL and we'll crawl it

Let me show you how fast this is."

### Live demo: Add content

"I'll paste a new FAQ question right now."

[Paste: "What's your return window? 30 days from purchase date."]

"Done. That took 3 seconds. The AI can now answer questions about returns. Let me prove it."

---

## Part 2: Live testing (5 minutes)

### Widget demo

"Here's what your customers see."

[Open demo site with widget]

"I'll ask about returns."

[Type: "Can I return this after 2 weeks?"]

[AI responds: "Yes, you can return items within 30 days of purchase..."]

"There it is. The AI found the right info and gave a clear answer. Response time was about 2 seconds.

Now watch what happens when I ask something NOT in the knowledge base."

### Edge case handling

[Type: "Do you sell wholesale?"]

[AI responds: "I don't have information about wholesale orders. Let me connect you with our team..."]

"See that? The AI knows when it doesn't know. Instead of making things up, it routes to you. You set the confidence threshold. Low confidence = human handoff."

### Customization

"You control how confident the AI needs to be. Conservative: route more to humans. Aggressive: handle more automatically. Most customers start at 70% and adjust from there."

---

## Part 3: Dashboard walkthrough (3 minutes)

### Conversation view

"Every conversation shows up here. You see:
- Customer question
- AI response
- Confidence score
- Whether they needed human help

Click any conversation to see the full thread."

### Editing responses

"If the AI gets something wrong, click Edit. Type the correct response. The AI learns from your corrections and won't make that mistake again."

### Analytics

"High-level numbers:
- Total conversations this month
- Automation rate (how many the AI handled solo)
- Average response time
- Customer satisfaction (if you enable ratings)

Most customers see 70-85% automation rates after the first week of training."

## Pricing and close (3 minutes)

### Recap benefits

"Quick recap based on what you told me earlier:

You're spending [X hours/day] on support. At your ticket volume, AutoReplyAI would handle about [Y%] of those automatically. That's roughly [Z hours/week] you get back.

The Pro plan at $29/month covers your volume."

### Handle objections

**"What if the AI says something wrong?"**
"Two safeguards. First, confidence thresholds route uncertain questions to you. Second, you review and correct responses. The AI learns from corrections. After a week of training, most customers see very few errors."

**"How does it compare to Intercom?"**
"Intercom starts at $39/seat/month. For a team of 3, that's $117/month minimum, and their AI features cost extra. We're $29/month flat, no per-seat charges, AI included."

**"We have complex products."**
"More complexity = more value from AI. Upload your product docs, technical specs, whatever you have. The AI handles the repetitive questions. Complex edge cases still go to your experts."

### CTA

"Two options:

1. Start with the free plan. 100 conversations/month, no credit card. See how it works with your real customers.

2. Start with Pro for 14 days free. Get all the features. If it doesn't save you time, cancel before the trial ends.

Which works better for you?"

## Common questions during demos

### "How long does setup take?"
"Most customers are live in under 30 minutes. Upload docs, install widget, test a few questions, go live."

### "What if we have thousands of documents?"
"Upload them all. We index up to 100MB on Pro, 1GB on Business. Larger knowledge bases just mean more accurate answers."

### "Can we train it on our tone of voice?"
"Yes. In settings, you add tone instructions. 'Be friendly and casual' or 'Be professional and concise.' The AI adjusts accordingly."

### "What about languages?"
"English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese. The AI auto-detects and responds in the same language."

### "Is our data secure?"
"Yes. Encrypted at rest and in transit. We don't train our models on your conversations. You can delete customer data anytime. SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress."
