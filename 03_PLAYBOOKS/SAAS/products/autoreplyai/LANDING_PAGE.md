## FAQ section

### Q1: How is this different from Intercom or Zendesk?
Price. Intercom starts at $39/seat/month and gets expensive fast. Zendesk AI requires their most expensive plan. We charge flat rates, not per-seat. A 5-person team using Intercom would pay $195/month minimum. With us, you pay $29.

### Q2: What if the AI answers wrong?
Two safeguards. First, you set confidence thresholds. If the AI isn't confident, it routes to you. Second, you review conversation logs and correct mistakes. The AI learns from corrections.

### Q3: Can I train it on my specific products?
Yes. Upload product pages, help docs, PDFs, or paste text directly. The AI indexes everything in about 5 minutes. You can update the knowledge base anytime.

### Q4: Does it work with my existing help desk?
We integrate with Slack and email. For other tools (Freshdesk, Help Scout, etc.), use our API or Zapier integration. Custom integrations available on Business plan.

### Q5: What happens when I hit my conversation limit?
You get an email at 80% usage. After you hit the limit, conversations route to your fallback (email or Slack). Upgrade anytime to continue automated responses.

### Q6: Is my customer data secure?
Yes. Data encrypted at rest and in transit. We don't train our models on your conversations. You can delete customer data anytime. SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress.

### Q7: Can I customize the chat widget appearance?
Yes. Match your brand colors, add your logo, customize the greeting message. On Business plan, remove "powered by AutoReplyAI" entirely.

### Q8: How long does setup take?
Most customers are live in under 30 minutes. Upload docs, install widget, test a few questions, go live. No developer required for basic setup.

### Q9: What languages do you support?
English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Japanese. More coming. The AI auto-detects customer language and responds accordingly.

### Q10: Can I try before buying?
Yes. Free plan includes 100 conversations/month forever. Paid plans have 14-day free trials. No credit card required for free plan.
