## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Pricing too low** - You're competing on value, not price. Increase prices by 2x.
2. **Unclear product description** - People need to know exactly what they're getting.
3. **No email integration** - Set up Zapier → email list immediately.
4. **Ignoring affiliates** - 30% commission attracts partners. Enable this day 1.
5. **Not A/B testing** - Test pricing, copy, or offer every month.
6. **Over-complicating products** - Start simple. Add complexity after validation.
7. **No post-purchase sequence** - Email customers immediately after purchase with delivery + upsell.
