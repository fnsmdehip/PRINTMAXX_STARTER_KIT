================================================================================
VALIDATION FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

3-Day Validation Sprint (Before Building):

Day 1: Problem Validation
- Ask 20 people in community if they have this problem
- Would they pay $X/month? (test pricing)
- 10 cold emails + 10 Reddit comments
- Document all responses

Day 2: Build Landing Page MVP
- Create mockup (Pico, Carrd)
- Collect emails from 100 interested people
- Test headline + value prop

Day 3: Build & Launch
- Minimum viable version
- Invite 20 beta users
- Start charging (money tests commitment)

Success Criteria: 50%+ of Day 1 respondents say "I have this problem"
