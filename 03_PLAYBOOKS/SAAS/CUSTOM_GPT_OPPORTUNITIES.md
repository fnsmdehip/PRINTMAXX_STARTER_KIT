## Executive Summary

The GPT Store revenue share model is a weak primary income (opaque formula, 25 conversations/week minimum, unpredictable payouts). Smart creators skip the store and sell direct. The real money: build niche GPTs, validate on the store, then sell access directly for $10-$500/mo via Stripe/Gumroad. One creator generates $2,300/mo extra from a consulting GPT. Prompt libraries sell at $37 each with 150-person waitlists.

**Strategy:** Build GPTs for our niches, sell direct on Whop/Gumroad, use GPT Store as free discovery channel.

## GPT Store Revenue Reality Check

### How It Works
- Engagement-based revenue sharing
- Must maintain 25+ conversations per week to qualify
- US-based verified account required
- Formula is opaque (OpenAI decides your cut)
- OpenAI guarantees $1,000 minimum to "select" developers

### The Math Problem
- Every GPT competes for the same revenue pool
- As more creators join, slices get smaller
- You cannot set your own price
- Zero leverage to capture more value from a better GPT

### Verdict
GPT Store = free discovery channel, NOT a business model. Real revenue comes from direct sales.
