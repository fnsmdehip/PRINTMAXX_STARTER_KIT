## CHANNEL-SPECIFIC TACTICS

### X/Twitter

**Posting Strategy:**
- 2-3 posts per day (morning, noon, evening)
- 1 thread per week (minimum)
- Engage 30 min/day (reply to others in niche)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (tips, how-tos)
- 30% Personal (journey, lessons learned)
- 20% Promotional (product mentions)
- 10% Engagement (questions, polls)

**Winning Hooks:**
- "I tested 47 AI tools. Here's the only 5 worth using."
- "Stop paying for AI subscriptions. These free alternatives are better."
- "My AI workflow saves 15 hours/week. Here's the exact setup:"

**CTA Strategy:**
- Threads: "Comment 'STACK' and I'll DM the free framework"
- Posts: Link in bio to landing page
- Never: "Buy my product" in main feed

**Budget:** $0 (organic only initially)
**Expected CAC:** $0 (organic)
**Target Conversion Rate:** 2-3% of profile visitors

### TikTok

**Posting Strategy:**
- 1-2 shorts per day
- Post between 7-9 PM EST
- Use trending sounds when relevant

**Content Types:**
- Screen recordings of AI workflows
- "Day in my life" with AI tools
- Tool comparisons (split screen)
- Reaction to AI news/updates

**Winning Hooks:**
- "POV: You finally figured out which AI tools to use"
- "Nobody talks about this AI trick"
- "I replaced 10 apps with these 3 AI tools"

**CTA Strategy:**
- "Comment 'AI' for the free template"
- Bio link to landing page
- Never: Direct product pitch in video

**Budget:** $100-200 (Spark Ads to boost winners)
**Expected CAC:** $8-15 with Spark Ads
**Target Conversion Rate:** 1-2% of bio link clicks
