# Daily Anchor System - Go-to-Market Strategy

**Product:** Daily Anchor System
**Price:** $27
**Niche:** Faith
**Target Audience:** Christians seeking consistent daily devotional habits; busy believers who struggle with spiritual discipline
**Core Promise:** A practical system to anchor your day in Scripture and prayer in 15 minutes or less

## LAUNCH PHASE (Week 1-2)

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] Finalize product (guided devotional system + templates + audio meditations)
- [ ] Set up Gumroad product page with faith-focused copy (avoid promotional language)
- [ ] Create lead magnet: "7-Day Morning Anchor Challenge" (free PDF)
- [ ] Build landing page on LANDING/printmaxx-site
- [ ] Prepare 10 launch-day posts (Instagram, Facebook, X)
- [ ] Set up UTM tracking for all links
- [ ] Create 2-3 UGC testimonial videos ($10-40 total via Eastern EU creators)
- [ ] Draft launch email sequence (3 emails with devotional tone)
- [ ] Identify 15 faith-based micro-influencers for outreach
- [ ] Research 10 church newsletter opportunities

### Beta User Acquisition Plan

**Target:** 20-30 beta users
**Method:** Community engagement + direct outreach

1. **Facebook Groups (10-15 users)**
   - Join Christian entrepreneur, devotional, and prayer groups
   - Offer value first (share tips, answer questions)
   - Post: "Building a simple devotional system. Looking for 10 busy Christians to test it free. DM me."

2. **Instagram DM Outreach (5-10 users)**
   - Search: #morningdevotional, #faithjourney, #christianentrepreneur
   - DM people who post about struggling with consistency
   - Template: "Saw your post about morning routine struggles. Working on something to help. Free to test?"

3. **Church Networks (5 users)**
   - Reach out to personal church connections
   - Offer to small group leaders for testing

**Beta Feedback Loop:**
- Collect feedback via simple Google Form
- Ask: "What would make this more helpful for your walk?"
- Request testimonials focused on spiritual impact (not just productivity)

### Launch Day Sequence

**Hour 0:** Email blast to beta users + warm list
**Hour 1:** Instagram post + Stories (personal testimony angle)
**Hour 2:** Facebook post in personal profile + relevant groups (check rules)
**Hour 3:** X thread about the system (practical tips)
**Hour 4:** YouTube Short or TikTok (screen recording of system)
**Hour 6:** Engage with all comments and shares
**Hour 12:** Share early testimonials in Stories
**Hour 24:** Launch recap with "Day 1 blessings" angle

## CHURCH PARTNERSHIP PLAYBOOK

This is the highest-ROI channel for faith products. Detailed strategy:

### Phase 1: Identify (Week 1-2)
- Use BuiltWith or Google to find churches with modern websites
- Filter: Congregation >200, no existing devotional app
- Compile list of 50 target churches

### Phase 2: Outreach (Week 3-4)
- Email pastor + ministry leader (dual contact)
- Follow up with phone call if no response
- Offer: Free trial for one small group

### Phase 3: Trial (Week 5-6)
- Provide dedicated support for trial group
- Collect feedback and testimonials
- Track usage and engagement

### Phase 4: Partnership (Week 7+)
- Offer: Free app/system for all members
- Church receives 20% of any premium upgrades
- Co-branded landing page for the church

### Expected Results:
- 1 active partnership per 20 outreach emails
- Average church drives 10-50 sales
- LTV of church partnership: $500-2,500
