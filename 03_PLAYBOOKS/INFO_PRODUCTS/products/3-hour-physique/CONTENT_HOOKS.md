## Usage notes

- These work on fitness-adjacent platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
- Pair with transformation content, workout clips, or talking-head videos
- Follow with value, then soft CTA to lead magnet
- Avoid "fitness bro" tone. Target regular people, not gym enthusiasts.
