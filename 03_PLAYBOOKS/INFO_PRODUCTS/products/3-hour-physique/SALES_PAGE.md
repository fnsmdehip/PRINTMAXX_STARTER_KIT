## FAQ

**What equipment do I need?**

For the home track: Pull-up bar, resistance bands, and something heavy (backpack, water jugs, or dumbbells). Total investment: $30-80.

For the dumbbell track: Adjustable dumbbells (or 3 pairs of fixed weights).

For the gym track: Standard gym equipment.

**I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?**

Yes. The program starts at beginner level and progresses from there. All exercises have easier variations.

**Will I get "bulky"?**

No. Getting bulky requires years of dedicated training and specific nutrition. This program builds functional strength and lean muscle. You'll look fit, not like a bodybuilder.

**Can I do this with bad knees/back/shoulders?**

The program includes modifications for common issues. If you have a serious injury, check with your doctor first. But most people with "bad knees" just need better movement patterns.

**What if I can only work out twice a week?**

There's a 2x/week modification in the guide. Results will be slower, but you'll still progress.
