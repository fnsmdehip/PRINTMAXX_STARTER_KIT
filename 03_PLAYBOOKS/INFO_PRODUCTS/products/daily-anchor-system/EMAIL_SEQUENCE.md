## Email 4: Solution overview

**Subject:** The 10-minute anchor (here's how it works)

**Body:**

The Daily Anchor System has three parts. Same order every day.

**Part 1: Scripture (3 minutes)**

One verse. Read it slowly. Read it again. Sit with it.

Not a chapter. Not a study. One verse, absorbed.

**Part 2: Silence (4 minutes)**

No phone. No podcast. No noise.

This is the hardest part for most people. It's also where the anchor does its work.

You can pray. You can listen. You can just breathe. But you stay in the silence.

**Part 3: Single focus (3 minutes)**

What's the one thing that matters most today? Write it down. Ask God to help you with it.

Not your to-do list. Not your 12 priorities. One thing.

That's it. 10 minutes. Scripture, silence, single focus.

The system includes prompts and guidance for each part. You don't have to figure out what to read or what to pray. It's structured enough to follow, flexible enough to make your own.
