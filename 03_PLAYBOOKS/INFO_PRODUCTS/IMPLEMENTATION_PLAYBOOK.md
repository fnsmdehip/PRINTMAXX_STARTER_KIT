## Overview

Create and sell information products: PDF guides ($7-47), video courses ($49-199), community access ($29-99/mo), and high-ticket coaching ($500-2,000). Build a product ladder from free lead magnet to premium offering. Sell on Gumroad, Whop, Teachable/Skool.

Key insight: The product ladder maximizes customer lifetime value. Free content builds audience, $7 PDF qualifies buyers, $49 course deepens relationship, $99/mo community creates recurring revenue, $500+ coaching monetizes top 1%.

Proof: @eptwts AI Frontrunners community generating $10K+/mo. Notion template creators averaging $500-2K/mo. PRINTMAXX has full course outlines, product ladders, and templates for all 33 niches ready.

## Step-by-step implementation

### Day 1-3: Create first digital product (8-15 hours)

**Task 1: Pick your first product**

Start with a PDF guide ($7-19). Fastest to create and validates demand before building a course.

Best first product by niche:

| Niche | Product | Price | Build Time |
|-------|---------|-------|------------|
| Faith | "30-Day Prayer Challenge Guide" | $9 | 4-6 hrs |
| Fitness | "Home Workout Blueprint: 12-Week Plan" | $12 | 6-8 hrs |
| Productivity | "The Solopreneur Tech Stack Guide" | $15 | 5-7 hrs |
| Content Creation | "The Faceless Content Playbook" | $19 | 8-10 hrs |
| Cold Outreach | "100 Cold Email Templates That Actually Work" | $27 | 6-8 hrs |

Reference: `INFOPRODUCT_LADDER_ALL_33_NICHES.md` for product ideas in every niche.

**Task 2: Write the product**

Structure for a PDF guide (30-50 pages):
```
Page 1:     Cover page (designed in Canva)
Page 2:     Table of contents
Page 3-5:   Introduction (problem + why this guide)
Page 6-8:   Chapter 1: Foundation/basics
Page 9-15:  Chapter 2: Core method (the meat)
Page 16-22: Chapter 3: Advanced tactics
Page 23-28: Chapter 4: Implementation plan (day-by-day)
Page 29-35: Chapter 5: Templates/swipe files
Page 36-40: Resources + tools
Page 41-42: About the author + CTA for next product
```

Quality checklist:
- [ ] Specific numbers and examples throughout
- [ ] Actionable steps (not just theory)
- [ ] Screenshots/visuals every 2-3 pages
- [ ] Templates/swipe files included (adds massive perceived value)
- [ ] CTA for next product on product ladder
- [ ] Professional design (use Canva PDF template)

**Task 3: Design and export**

Use Canva for professional PDF:
1. Search "ebook template" in Canva
2. Pick a clean template matching your brand
3. Replace content with your material
4. Add screenshots, charts, and callout boxes
5. Export as PDF (File > Download > PDF Standard)

### Day 3-4: List and launch (3-4 hours)

**Task 4: Create Gumroad listing**

1. Create product on Gumroad
2. Upload PDF
3. Write listing copy:

```
Title: [Benefit-Focused Title]
Price: $[X] (use $X9 pricing: $9, $19, $27, $47)

Description:
[Pain point in 1 sentence]

This [X]-page guide gives you:

- [Specific deliverable 1] (saves you [X] hours)
- [Specific deliverable 2] ([X] templates included)
- [Specific deliverable 3] (step-by-step, no guessing)
- [Specific deliverable 4] (copy-paste ready)

What's inside:
Chapter 1: [Title] - [1-sentence benefit]
Chapter 2: [Title] - [1-sentence benefit]
Chapter 3: [Title] - [1-sentence benefit]
Chapter 4: [Title] - [1-sentence benefit]
Chapter 5: [Title] - [1-sentence benefit]

[X] people already bought this. Average review: [X]/5.

Get instant access.
```

4. Add 3-5 mockup images (Canva)
5. Set price ($7-19 for first product)
6. Publish

**Task 5: Create free lead magnet**

Give away a mini-version to build email list:
- Shorter version (10-15 pages vs 30-50)
- Or a single chapter extracted
- Or a template/checklist from the guide
- List on Gumroad as "$0+" (pay what you want)

Email sequence after free download (reference `EMAIL_SEQUENCES_ALL_NICHES.md`):
```
Email 1 (Immediate): Deliver free content + quick win
Email 2 (Day 2): More value + "btw, the full guide has X more..."
Email 3 (Day 4): Case study or testimonial
Email 4 (Day 7): Direct pitch for paid product + discount code
Email 5 (Day 14): Last chance / new product preview
```

### Week 2-3: Promote and sell

**Task 6: Twitter/X launch**

Launch sequence:
```
Day 1: Teaser post ("I spent 40 hours writing a guide on [X]. Dropping it tomorrow.")
Day 2: Launch post (screenshot of guide + link + "First 24 hours: 30% off")
Day 3: Social proof post ("12 copies sold in first day. Here's what people are saying...")
Day 4-7: Daily value posts with CTA to product
Week 2: Thread breaking down one chapter (give value, sell at end)
```

**Task 7: Reddit + community promotion**

- Post genuine value in relevant subreddits
- Link to free lead magnet (not paid product directly)
- Answer questions, establish expertise, mention product naturally
- Post in r/SideProject about your launch journey

**Task 8: Build the product ladder**

After first product sells, build the ladder:
```
FREE:        Lead magnet (mini PDF or checklist)
$7-19:       PDF guide (your first product)
$27-47:      Premium guide + templates + video walkthrough
$49-99:      Video course (5-10 modules, 2-5 hours)
$29-99/mo:   Community/membership (Skool or Whop)
$500-2,000:  1-on-1 coaching or done-for-you service
```

Each level should upsell to the next.

### Week 3-4: Create course (optional, if PDF validates)

**Task 9: Build video course**

If PDF sells 20+ copies, validate demand for a course:

Course structure (reference `COURSE_OUTLINES_ALL_NICHES.md`):
```
Module 1: Overview + Mindset (15-20 min)
Module 2: Foundation Setup (30-45 min)
Module 3: Core Method Deep Dive (45-60 min)
Module 4: Advanced Tactics (30-45 min)
Module 5: Implementation + Templates (30-45 min)
Module 6: Scaling + Optimization (20-30 min)
Bonus: Q&A Recording / Community Access
```

Record with Loom (screen share + face optional).
Total: 3-5 hours of content. Sell for $49-199.

## Risk management

- **No sales:** Validate with free content first. If free content gets engagement, paid will sell. If it doesn't, pivot topic.
- **Refund requests:** Keep refund rate under 5%. Deliver genuine value. Offer satisfaction guarantee (builds trust, refunds rarely happen).
- **Copycats:** Speed and distribution win. Build email list and audience loyalty. Your voice and perspective are the moat.
- **Platform risk:** Sell on multiple platforms (Gumroad + Whop + own site). Export customer list regularly.
- **Pricing too low:** Start at $7-19 to validate. Raise prices as testimonials accumulate. $27-47 for guides, $99-199 for courses.
