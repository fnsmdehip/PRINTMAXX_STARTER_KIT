# Notion Templates: Complete Product Line (33 Niches)

**Strategy:** Build 99 template products (3 per niche) across all 33 niches. Each template = recurring passive income with minimal ongoing work after creation.

**Business Model:**
- Templates sell on Gumroad, Send.app, Template.app, Notion template marketplaces
- Price: $9-$49 per template (depends on complexity)
- Recurring: Update 1x quarterly, add to email sequence, mention in content
- Passive: 100+ sales/month at $25 avg = $2,500/mo passive per top template

**Key Insight:** Notion templates have best margins in info products because:
1. Creation time: 2-4 hours per template
2. Support time: Nearly zero (it's just a Notion file)
3. Perceived value: High ($25-49 despite low creation cost)
4. Repeat customers: People buy multiple templates from same creator
