## Monetization Math

**Average conversion rates:**
- $17 products: 2-3% conversion rate
- $27 products: 1-2% conversion rate
- $47+ products: 0.5-1% conversion rate

**Example: 10K email list, 5% click-through rate = 500 visitors**
- 500 visitors × 2% conversion on $27 product = $270 revenue
- 500 visitors × 0.5% conversion on $97 product = $485 revenue

**Parallel stacking (all 3 products per niche):**
- Low tier ($17-27): Drive volume, build trust
- Mid tier ($37-97): Capture serious buyers
- High tier ($147-297): Upsell, consults, coaching

**Monthly revenue opportunity:**
- 1 niche, 10K followers × 3 products = $1K-$2K/mo
- 3 niches, 30K followers × 3 products = $3K-$6K/mo
- Optimize top performer, kill bottom 20%
