# INFO_PRODUCTS

Digital products: ebooks, courses, templates, Notion packs.

## Status: Planning

## Revenue Model
- One-time sales: $19-$199 per product
- Bundles for higher AOV
- Target: 10+ sales/month per product

## Execution Steps

1. Identify pain points in target audience
2. Create outline and validate with audience
3. Build product (ebook, course, template pack)
4. Set up Gumroad/Lemon Squeezy storefront
5. Launch with email list and social proof
6. Iterate based on feedback

## Folder Contents

- products/ - Individual product folders (ebooks, courses, templates)
- marketing/ - Sales pages, email sequences, social posts
- research/ - Customer interviews, competitor analysis
- gtm/ - Launch checklists, promotion strategies
- assets/ - Cover images, mockups, screenshots

## Key Metrics

- Sales per month
- Conversion rate
- Average order value
- Refund rate

## Product Ideas

- Notion template packs for specific workflows
- Ebooks on niche topics
- Video courses (mini-courses, 1-2 hours)
- Prompt libraries
- Checklists and SOPs

## Tools

- Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy for sales
- Notion for template products
- Canva for design
- Loom for video content
