## What is Skool?

Skool = Discord (community) + Teachable (courses) + Patreon (memberships) combined. Single monthly subscription unlocks:
- Private community (Discord-style)
- Courses and curriculum
- Live Q&As
- Leaderboards and gamification
- Member directory

**Why Skool > Separate Tools:**
- One payment integration (Stripe)
- Sticky due to community (30-40% lower churn than courses)
- Engagement drives retention (gamification)
- Built-in accountability = higher completion rates

## CLOSING: THE SKOOL ADVANTAGE

Skool communities win because they combine:

1. **Stickiness:** Community engagement reduces churn by 40-60% vs courses alone
2. **Recurring revenue:** Monthly subscriptions create predictable MRR
3. **Leverage:** One person can manage 2,000+ members with community and curriculum
4. **Compound growth:** Every member potentially refers others (0.5-1.0 referral rate)
5. **Multiple monetization:** Subscriptions + affiliate recommendations + consulting calls
6. **Audience building:** Every community builds your personal brand for next venture

**Critical success factor:** The community + curriculum must deliver real value. Engagement requires daily moderation, authentic presence, and member spotlighting. This is NOT a passive income stream - it requires active participation first 6-12 months.

**Recommended approach:**
1. Launch 3 Phase 1 communities simultaneously (parallel operations)
2. Run each for 6 months before deciding to scale
3. Double down on the 1-2 that show highest engagement + retention
4. Scale those to 5,000+ members
5. Add Phase 2 niches once you've systematized operations
