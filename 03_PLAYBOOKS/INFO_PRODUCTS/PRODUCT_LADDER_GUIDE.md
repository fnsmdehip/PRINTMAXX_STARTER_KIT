## The Universal Product Ladder Framework

Every info product business follows the same core structure. The key is adapting it to your niche with specific offers, pricing psychology, and launch strategies.

```
FREE: Lead Magnet (PDF, mini-course, checklist)
     ↓ (email capture → nurture sequence)
$17-47: Low Ticket (detailed guide, template pack)
     ↓ (order bump, immediate upsell)
$97-297: Core Product (full course, workshop)
     ↓ (community upsell, coaching)
$297-997/mo: Premium Community (mastermind, group coaching)
     ↓ (application, done-with-you)
$997-2000: Done-With-You (setup service, intensive)
     ↓ (retainer, partnership)
$2000+/mo: Done-For-You (agency, retainer)
```

## Pricing Psychology Fundamentals

### Price Anchoring

Always establish high perceived value before revealing price:
- Stack total value ($1,456) then reveal price ($67)
- Compare to alternatives (coaching $200/hr vs $247/mo)
- Show ROI math (one deal pays for 6 months)

### Psychological Price Points

| Price | Psychology | Use For |
|-------|------------|---------|
| $7-17 | Impulse, no-brainer | Templates, checklists, mini-guides |
| $27-47 | Low friction, test commitment | Detailed guides, template packs |
| $67-97 | Sweet spot for core products | Full courses, workshop recordings |
| $147-197 | Premium but accessible | Comprehensive courses with community |
| $247-297 | Serious commitment | Courses with coaching/community |
| $497-997 | High-ticket threshold | Intensive programs, DWY |
| $1,497-1,997 | Investment mindset | Done-with-you, intensive coaching |
| $2,997+ | Premium transformation | Done-for-you, retainer |

### The $X7 Pattern

Prices ending in 7 outperform round numbers:
- $67 > $65 or $70
- $197 > $200
- $997 > $1,000

Exception: Premium offers use round numbers ($2,000) to signal premium positioning.

### Subscription vs One-Time

| Model | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|-------|------|------|----------|
| One-time | Higher conversion, simpler | No recurring | Courses, templates |
| Monthly | Predictable MRR, higher LTV | Churn management | Communities, coaching |
| Annual | Higher upfront, lower churn | Bigger commitment | Established communities |

**Recommended:** Offer both. Annual at 2 months free (10/12 = 17% discount).

## LAUNCH STRATEGIES

### Pre-Launch Phase (4-8 Weeks Before)

**Waitlist Building:**
1. Create simple landing page (Carrd, Framer, or Notion)
2. Offer exclusive bonus for waitlist signups
3. Collect emails with "notify me" form
4. Build anticipation with behind-the-scenes content

**Waitlist Email Sequence:**
```
Day 1: Thanks for joining + what to expect
Day 3: Sneak peek of content/value
Day 7: Behind the scenes of creation
Day 14: Early feedback request (builds investment)
Day 21: Launch date announcement
Day 28: 24-hour warning
```

**Content Strategy:**
- Share value related to course topic (daily/weekly)
- Document your process (build in public)
- Answer common questions (FAQ becomes sales objection handling)
- Share testimonials/results (if beta tested)

### Launch Week Strategy

**Day -1 (24 Hours Before):**
- Email: "Tomorrow at [time]"
- Social: Countdown, last chance to join waitlist

**Day 1 (Launch Day):**
- Morning email: Cart open, founding member pricing
- Social: Announcement with urgency
- DM outreach to warm leads
- Live Q&A (optional)

**Day 2-3:**
- Email: Case study or testimonial
- Social: Student wins, transformation stories
- Address common objections

**Day 4-5:**
- Email: FAQ, handle objections
- Social: Behind the scenes of delivery
- Offer bonus for fence-sitters

**Day 6:**
- Email: 24-hour warning
- Social: Urgency, last chance messaging
- Personal outreach to engaged non-buyers

**Day 7 (Close Day):**
- Email AM: Last chance
- Email PM: Cart closing
- Social: Final call
- Remove founding member pricing

### Evergreen Launch Strategy

After initial launch, transition to evergreen:

**Webinar Funnel:**
```
Ad/Content → Webinar Registration → Watch Webinar → Sales Page → Purchase
```

**Email Sequence Funnel:**
```
Lead Magnet → 5-7 Day Nurture → Sales Sequence (5 emails) → Evergreen Offer
```

**Deadline Funnel (Fake Scarcity Done Right):**
- Use Deadline Funnel or similar tool
- Each person gets unique deadline based on when they enter
- Price increase or bonus removal after deadline
- Ethically disclosed as "limited-time offer for new subscribers"

### Launch Pricing Strategy

**Founding Member Launch:**
- 30-50% off final price
- Limited spots (first 50, 100)
- Genuine scarcity (once filled, price increases)
- Extra access (calls with you, input on content)

**Beta Launch (Before Full Course Ready):**
- 50-70% off final price
- Deliver live, record for future
- Students help shape content
- Testimonials in exchange for discount

**Full Launch Pricing:**
- Establish full price early
- Use anchoring (was $497, now $197)
- Time-limited discounts only

## UPSELL AND CROSS-SELL STRATEGIES

### Order Bump Strategy

At checkout, offer complementary add-on:
- Core product → Templates add-on (+$17-37)
- Course → Community trial (+$47-97 for first month)
- Template pack → Video walkthrough (+$27)

**Conversion tip:** Order bumps convert 15-25% when relevant and low-priced.

### One-Time Offer (OTO)

Immediately after purchase, present upgrade:
- Low ticket → Core course (50% off for buyers only)
- Core course → Premium community (first month discounted)
- Community → 1:1 coaching call ($97-197)

**Structure:**
- Show value stack
- Exclusive pricing (today only)
- Genuine scarcity (this offer not repeated)

### Downsell Strategy

If they don't buy OTO:
- Offer payment plan
- Offer smaller version
- Offer delayed-delivery version (access in 30 days, lower price)

### Cross-Sell Between Products

Connect products in different niches/ladders:
- Faith buyer → Fitness product (holistic wellness angle)
- AI buyer → Faith/mindfulness (balance with technology)
- Fitness buyer → Productivity (energy for performance)

**Email Strategy:**
```
Subject: something different...

you've been crushing [NICHE] lately.

here's what i've noticed: people who [CURRENT SUCCESS] often struggle with [ADJACENT PROBLEM].

i have something that might help: [CROSS-SELL]

- [Name]
```

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Pricing Mistakes

1. **Pricing too low** - Undervalues expertise, attracts wrong customers
2. **No value stack** - Fails to anchor high value before price reveal
3. **Missing tiers** - Leaves money on table from high-intent buyers
4. **No payment plans** - Loses sales from cash-strapped buyers

### Launch Mistakes

1. **No warmup** - Launching to cold audience
2. **No urgency** - Evergreen from day one, no reason to buy now
3. **Overcomplicating** - Too many products/tiers at once
4. **Under-communicating** - People buy on 5th-7th touch, not first

### Product Mistakes

1. **Too much content** - Overwhelming, low completion rates
2. **No quick wins** - Students don't see results, request refunds
3. **No community** - High churn, no retention mechanism
4. **No support** - Students stuck, don't progress, bad reviews

### Mindset Mistakes

1. **Perfectionism** - Never launching, always "improving"
2. **Comparison** - Copying competitors instead of differentiating
3. **Undercharging** - Fear of rejection, pricing for approval
4. **Over-delivering** - Burning out, setting unsustainable expectations

## CROSS-POLLINATION WITH OTHER MONEY METHODS

### APP_FACTORY Connection

Each niche can have a companion app:
- **Faith:** PrayerLock (focus timer for prayer)
- **Fitness:** WalkToUnlock (gamified walking)
- **AI:** Workflow app or tool

**Cross-sell flow:**
```
Info product buyer → App affiliate link → App download → App subscription
```

Revenue share or affiliate commission adds 10-20% to course revenue.

### CONTENT_FARM Connection

Course content repurposes to social content:
- Course clips → TikTok/Reels
- Student wins → Testimonial content
- Module summaries → Carousel posts

Social content drives leads back to funnel:
```
Content post → Bio link → Lead magnet → Funnel
```

### COLD_OUTBOUND Connection

High-ticket offers benefit from outbound:
- Identify ideal clients (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Cold DM with value (free resource)
- Nurture to sales call
- Close high-ticket

**Use for:** $997+ coaching, $2000+ done-for-you

### AI_INFLUENCER Connection

Expert persona builds authority for info products:
- AI persona posts educational content
- Builds following in niche
- Launches products to audience
- Cross-promotes other offers
