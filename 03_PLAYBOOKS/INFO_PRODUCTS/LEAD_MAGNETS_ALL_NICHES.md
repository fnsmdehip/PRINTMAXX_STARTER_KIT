1. **Conversion Optimization:**
   - Benefit-driven headline (not generic)
   - Specific numbers in title
   - No hype, just results
   - Small form (3 fields max: email, first name, condition/niche)

2. **Delivery Sequence:**
   - Immediate autoresponder with magnet (within 5 minutes)
   - Day 3: Mid-tier product offer
   - Day 7: High-tier product offer
   - Day 14+: Testimonials, case studies, upsell reminders

3. **Tracking:**
   - Add all leads to `LEDGER/leads.csv`
   - Track source (niche, magnet type, platform)
   - Monitor conversion rates by magnet
   - A/B test titles and formats

4. **Scaling:**
   - Start with top 3 niches in Phase 1
   - Expand to 10 niches in Phase 2
   - Full rollout to all 33 by end of Phase 3
   - Use ralph loops to automate magnet creation/updates

5. **Content Updates:**
