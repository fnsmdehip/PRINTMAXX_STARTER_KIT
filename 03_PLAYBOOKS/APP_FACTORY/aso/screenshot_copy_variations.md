| 4 | Social proof | Trust and validation |
| 5 | Additional value | Bonus features |
| 6 | CTA/pricing | Final conversion push |

## A/B test plan

### Test schedule

| Week | App | Test | Metric |
|------|-----|------|--------|
| 1-2 | PrayerLock | Slot 1 copy | Conversion rate |
| 3-4 | WalkToUnlock | Slot 1 copy | Conversion rate |

| 7-8 | Winner rollout | Apply winners | Validate |

### Success criteria

- Minimum 5,000 impressions per variant
- 95% statistical confidence
- >10% improvement = winner
- 5-10% improvement = test longer
- <5% improvement = no change

## Copy formulas reference

### Hook formulas

1. **Problem-Solution:** "[Problem]? [Solution with app]"
2. **Benefit-First:** "[Benefit] [How it works]"
3. **Social Proof:** "[Number] people already [benefit]"
4. **Challenge:** "Can you [action]? [App helps]"
5. **Time-Based:** "[Timeframe] to [result]"

### Feature formulas

1. **How It Works:** "1. [Step] 2. [Step] 3. [Result]"
2. **Versus:** "Without [app]: [bad] / With [app]: [good]"
3. **Simple Statement:** "[Feature name] [What it does]"

### Social proof formulas

1. **User Quote:** "[Result quote] - [Name]"
2. **Numbers:** "[X]+ users, [Y] star rating"
3. **Authority:** "Featured in [publication]"

### CTA formulas

1. **Risk-Free:** "Try free for [X] days, then [price]"
2. **Community:** "Join [X]+ people [benefiting]"
3. **Action:** "[Action verb] now, [result]"
