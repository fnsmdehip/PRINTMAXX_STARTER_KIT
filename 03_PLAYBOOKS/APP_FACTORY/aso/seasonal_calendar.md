## Q1: January - March

### January: New Year resolutions

**All niches peak.** Highest download period for faith, fitness, and productivity apps.

**Faith apps (PrayerLock, DailyDevotion):**
- Keywords to add: "new year prayer," "spiritual resolution," "faith goals 2027"
- Screenshot update: "Start 2027 with Prayer"
- Description update: Reference new year fresh start
- Marketing push: 2x normal budget

**Fitness apps (WalkToUnlock, FemFit):**
- Keywords to add: "new year fitness," "resolution," "get fit 2027"
- Screenshot update: "Your 2027 Fitness Goal"
- Description update: New year fitness motivation
- Marketing push: 3x normal budget (highest competition)

**AI/Productivity apps (PromptVault, StudyLock, DailyAnchor):**
- Keywords to add: "productivity 2027," "new year routine," "better habits"
- Screenshot update: "Crush 2027 Goals"
- Description update: New year productivity focus
- Marketing push: 2x normal budget

**Timeline:**
- Dec 15: Submit metadata updates for Jan 1
- Dec 26: Launch marketing campaigns
- Jan 1-31: Maximum spend period
- Feb 1: Return to normal

### February: Lent and Valentine's

**Faith apps:**
- Lent begins (mid-Feb to early-March, date varies)
- Keywords: "lent devotional," "40 day prayer," "lenten discipline"
- Screenshot: "40 Days of Prayer"
- Marketing: Church partnerships for Lent programs

**Fitness apps:**
- Valentine's couples fitness angle
- Keywords: "couples workout," "valentine fitness," "date night exercise"
- Screenshot: Can skip or light update
- Marketing: Couples challenge campaigns

**AI/Productivity:**
- Lower priority month
- Focus on content/SEO
- No major ASO changes needed

### March: Easter prep and spring

**Faith apps:**
- Easter buildup (date varies, typically late March to April)
- Keywords: "easter devotional," "holy week prayer," "resurrection"
- Screenshot: "Holy Week Journey"
- Marketing: Church partnerships, Easter content

**Fitness apps:**
- Spring fitness push
- Keywords: "spring fitness," "outdoor workout," "get outside"
- Screenshot: "Spring Into Fitness"
- Marketing: Outdoor activity angle

**AI/Productivity:**
- Spring cleaning angle
- Keywords: "digital declutter," "organize," "spring productivity"
- Screenshot: Can skip
- Marketing: Content marketing focus

## Holiday-specific playbooks

### New Year (Jan 1)

**Prep timeline:**
- Nov 15: Finalize NY creative concepts
- Dec 1: Create NY screenshots and copy
- Dec 15: Submit metadata updates
- Dec 26: Launch NY campaigns
- Jan 1: All hands on deck
- Jan 15: Peak conversion (New Year motivation fading)
- Feb 1: Return to normal

**Messaging:**
- "New Year, New You" (fitness)
- "Start 2027 with Purpose" (faith)
- "Your Most Productive Year" (AI)

### Lent (40 days before Easter)

**Prep timeline:**
- 8 weeks before: Plan Lent content
- 6 weeks before: Create assets
- 4 weeks before: Submit updates
- Ash Wednesday: Launch campaigns
- Through Easter: Maintain

**Messaging:**
- "40 Days of Faith"
- "Lenten Prayer Journey"
- "Give Up Scrolling, Take Up Prayer"

### Easter

**Prep timeline:**
- 4 weeks before: Create Easter assets
- 2 weeks before: Submit updates
- Holy Week: Peak engagement
- Easter Sunday: Celebration content

**Messaging:**
- "Holy Week Prayer Guide"
- "He Is Risen"
- "Easter Joy"

### Back to School (mid-Aug to mid-Sep)

**Prep timeline:**
- Jul 15: Plan back-to-school campaigns
- Aug 1: Create assets
- Aug 10: Submit updates
- Aug 15: Launch campaigns
- Sep 15: Transition to fall

**Messaging:**
- "Ace This Semester" (StudyLock)
- "Back to School Routine" (DailyAnchor)
- "Campus Fitness" (fitness apps)

## Preparation checklist

### 30 days before major season

- [ ] Plan seasonal messaging
- [ ] Draft new screenshot copy
- [ ] Design seasonal screenshots
- [ ] Update keyword fields (draft)
- [ ] Prepare description updates
- [ ] Plan marketing campaigns
- [ ] Brief any partners/influencers

### 14 days before major season

- [ ] Finalize all creative assets
- [ ] Submit App Store updates
- [ ] Schedule social content
- [ ] Prepare email campaigns
- [ ] Test all tracking

### 7 days before major season

- [ ] Verify App Store updates approved
- [ ] Launch soft marketing
- [ ] Monitor competitor moves
- [ ] Final budget allocation

### Day of season start

- [ ] Launch full campaigns
- [ ] Monitor hourly metrics
- [ ] Respond to reviews promptly
- [ ] Adjust bids as needed
