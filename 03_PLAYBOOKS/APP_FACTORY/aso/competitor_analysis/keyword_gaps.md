## Competitor weaknesses to exploit

### Faith apps

1. **Feature bloat** - Hallow, Pray.com have too much content
   - Our angle: Simple, single-purpose apps

2. **No accountability** - None enforce behavior
   - Our angle: Lock mechanisms create accountability

3. **Passive consumption** - All about reading/listening
   - Our angle: Active engagement required

### Fitness apps

1. **Hardware dependent** - Fitbit, Apple Watch required
   - Our angle: Works with just your phone

2. **Rewards-based** - Sweatcoin gives fake currency
   - Our angle: Real consequence (locked phone)

3. **No discipline** - Easy to skip, no enforcement
   - Our angle: You literally cannot use phone until goal met

### AI apps

1. **Web-only** - PromptBase, FlowGPT, AIPRM
   - Our angle: Native mobile app

2. **Complex** - Too many features, overwhelming
   - Our angle: Simple prompt storage and access

3. **Community-focused** - Social features dominate
   - Our angle: Personal productivity tool
