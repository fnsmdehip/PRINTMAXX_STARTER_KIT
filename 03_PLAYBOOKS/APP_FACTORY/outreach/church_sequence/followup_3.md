# Church outreach followup 3 (final)

Send 14 days after initial email (7 days after followup 2). Goal: breakup email, clear the pipeline.

---

## Followup 3A: The breakup

**Subject:** Should I close your file?

**Body:**

```

If that changes, my info is below. I'd still love to help {{church_name}} members build consistent prayer habits.

Blessings on your ministry.

{{your_name}}
{{your_email}}

```

## Usage notes

**Timing:** 14 days after initial email

**Why breakup emails work:**
- Creates closure
- Removes pressure
- Often triggers "wait, I meant to respond" replies
- Clears your pipeline of dead leads

**Expected response:** 5-8% reply rate (often the highest in the sequence)

**After this sequence:**
- If no response: add to 6-month re-engagement list
- If "not interested": remove from active list
- If "maybe later": add to 3-month follow-up list
- If interested: book the call

---

## Sequence summary

| Email | Timing | Goal | Expected reply |
|-------|--------|------|----------------|
| Initial | Day 0 | Make the offer | 6-10% |
| Followup 1 | Day 3 | Add value/proof | 3-5% |
| Followup 2 | Day 7 | Lower the bar | 4-6% |
| Followup 3 | Day 14 | Breakup/close | 5-8% |
| **Total** | | | **18-29%** |

**Key insight:** Most responses come from followup emails, not the initial send. Persistence matters.
