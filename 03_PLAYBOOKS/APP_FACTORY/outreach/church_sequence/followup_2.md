# Church outreach followup 2

Send 7 days after initial email (4 days after followup 1). Goal: lower the bar.

---

## Followup 2A: The simple ask

**Subject:** Re: Free app for {{church_name}} congregation

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Maybe a full partnership isn't the right fit right now.

Here's a simpler option:

I'll send you a few free promo codes for PrayerLock premium.

Share them with 5-10 members who might benefit. See how they like it.

No commitment. No revenue share setup needed. Just a trial run.

Want me to send the codes?

{{your_name}}
```

## Followup 2C: The question approach

**Subject:** Re: Partnership idea for {{church_name}}

**Body:**

```
{{first_name}},

Quick question: is this something you'd consider, or should I stop reaching out?

Three options:
1. Let's talk (I'll send my calendar)
2. Not now, try again in a few months
3. Not interested (totally fine, I'll stop emailing)

Any response helps.

{{your_name}}
```

---

## Usage notes

**Timing:** 7 days after initial email (4 days after followup 1)

**Strategy by variant:**
- 2A: Offers low-friction trial (best for cold leads)
- 2B: Provides free value (best for building relationship)
- 2C: Gets a definitive response (best for pipeline clarity)

**Expected response:** 4-6% reply rate from followup 2

**Key insight:** This email often converts people who were "interested but busy." Lower commitment = easier yes.
