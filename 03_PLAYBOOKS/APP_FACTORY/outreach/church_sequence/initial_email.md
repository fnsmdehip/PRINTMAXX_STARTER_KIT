## A/B test recommendations

**Test order:**

1. Run A vs B (value angle vs problem angle)
2. Winner vs C (revenue focus)
3. Winner vs D (partnership framing)
4. Final winner vs E (personal story)

**Sample sizes:**

- 50 sends per variant minimum
- Track open rate, reply rate, positive reply rate
- Run each test for 2 weeks

**Expected benchmarks:**

| Metric | Target | Good | Excellent |
|--------|--------|------|-----------|
| Open rate | 50%+ | 55% | 65%+ |
| Reply rate | 8%+ | 12% | 18%+ |
| Positive reply rate | 5%+ | 8% | 12%+ |
