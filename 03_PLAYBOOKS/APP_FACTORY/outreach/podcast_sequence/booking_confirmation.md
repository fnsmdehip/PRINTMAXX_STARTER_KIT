# Podcast booking confirmation templates

Templates for after you get booked. Goal: be professional, prepared, and easy to work with.

---

## Confirmation after booking

**Subject:** Confirmed: {{podcast_name}} recording {{date}}

```
{{host_name}},

Confirmed for {{day}}, {{date}} at {{time}} {{timezone}}.

Quick recap:
- Recording platform: {{platform}}
- Topics we'll cover: {{topics}}
- Episode length: {{length}}

I'll send a few things before we record:
- Short bio (2-3 sentences)
- Headshot
- Relevant links
- Promo code for your listeners (if you want one)

Anything else you need from me before then?

Looking forward to it.

{{your_name}}
```

## Maximizing episode value

### Before recording

- Prepare 3-5 talking points
- Have 2-3 stories ready
- Test audio/video setup
- Clear your throat, drink water

### During recording

- Mention your lead magnet naturally
- Give actionable advice
- Tell stories, not just facts
- Be conversational, not rehearsed

### After episode airs

- Share everywhere (all platforms)
- Tag the host and show
- Clip best moments for social content
- Add to your "As Seen On" page
- Respond to comments

### Long-term

- Stay in touch with host
- Ask if they know other hosts
- Repurpose content from episode
- Update your pitch with this episode as social proof

## Things to have ready

### Bio options

**Short (1 sentence):**
{{your_name}} is the creator of {{app_name}}, a {{one_line_description}}.

**Medium (2-3 sentences):**
{{your_name}} is the creator of {{app_name}}, which helps {{who}} with {{what}}. Built as a solo project, it now has {{users}} users. {{your_name}} writes about {{topic}} at {{website}}.

**Long (for show notes):**
{{your_name}} is a {{title}} and the creator of {{app_name}}, a {{description}}. After {{backstory}}, {{your_name}} built {{app_name}} to solve {{problem}}. The app now has {{users}} users and generates {{revenue}}. {{your_name}} is passionate about {{topic}} and shares lessons learned at {{website}}.

### Links to provide

- Website
- App download links (both platforms)
- Social handles
- Promo code landing page
- Email signup/lead magnet

### Headshot requirements

- High resolution (at least 1000x1000)
- Clear background or professional setting
- Recent photo
- Multiple options (square, rectangular)
