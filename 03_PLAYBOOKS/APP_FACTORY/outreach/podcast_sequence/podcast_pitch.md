# Podcast pitch templates

For getting booked on podcasts to promote apps. Target: faith, fitness, and productivity podcasts.

---

## Generic app founder pitch

### Subject line variants

- A: Guest pitch for {{podcast_name}}
- B: Topic idea: How I built a ${{revenue}} app in {{timeframe}}
- C: Indie app story for {{podcast_name}}

### Body

```
{{host_name}},

Been listening to {{podcast_name}} for a while. Loved your episode with {{recent_guest}} about {{topic}}.

Quick pitch:

**Who I am:**
{{your_name}}, indie app developer. Built {{app_name}}, a {{one_line_description}}.

**The story:**
- Built in {{timeframe}} with {{budget}}
- Now at {{users}} users / {{revenue}} revenue
- No VC funding, no team, just me

**Topics I could cover:**
- How to validate an app idea in a weekend
- Getting your first 100 users with $0 marketing
- The exact playbook I used to grow {{app_name}}
- Mistakes I made and how to avoid them

**Why your audience would care:**
Your listeners want to build things. I can share the exact steps, tools, and tactics that worked.

Previous appearances: {{list_if_any}}

Worth considering?

{{your_name}}
{{website_or_social}}
```

## Target podcast criteria

### Good fit indicators

- Episode length: 30-90 minutes
- Has guests (not solo shows)
- Posted episode in last 30 days
- Under 50K downloads (easier to book)
- Audience matches your app users
- Host responds to emails/DMs

### Red flags

- Hasn't published in 3+ months
- Pay-to-play model
- Wrong audience entirely
- No contact information
- Only features celebrity guests

## Research before pitching

**Listen to at least 1 episode:**
- Note the host's style
- Identify topics they've covered
- Find gaps you could fill
- Note recent guests you could reference

**Find hooks:**
- Something you can add to a recent episode
- A contrarian take on common advice
- Unique data or results
- Personal story that fits their themes

---

## Volume and expectations

**To get 1 booking per week:**
- Pitch 15-20 podcasts per week
- At 10% booking rate = 1-2 bookings
- Allow 2-4 weeks from pitch to recording

**To get 1 booking per month:**
- Pitch 8-10 podcasts per week
- Lower effort, sustainable long-term

**Typical response rates:**
- Open rate: 55-70%
- Reply rate: 15-25%
- Booking rate: 8-15%
