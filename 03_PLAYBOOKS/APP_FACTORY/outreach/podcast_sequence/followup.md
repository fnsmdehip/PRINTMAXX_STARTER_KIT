# Podcast pitch followup sequence

Send after initial podcast pitch. Goal: get booked without being annoying.

---

## Followup 1: Add value (5 days)

**Subject:** Re: Guest pitch for {{podcast_name}}

```
{{host_name}},

Following up on my guest pitch.

Had another topic idea that might work better:

**Topic:** {{alternative_topic}}

This one might resonate because {{reason_it_fits_their_show}}.

I could share:
- {{specific_thing_1}}
- {{specific_thing_2}}
- {{actionable_takeaway}}

Also, if you're looking for future guests, happy to introduce you to people in my network who'd be good fits.

Let me know either way.

{{your_name}}
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows flexibility
- Offers value beyond yourself
- Not desperate

---

## Followup 2: Simple close (10 days)

**Subject:** Quick question

```
{{host_name}},

Last follow-up on this.

If timing isn't right, no worries. Just let me know:

1. Not interested (totally fine, I'll stop emailing)
2. Maybe later (I'll follow up in a few months)
3. Let's talk (I'll send my calendar)

Either way, keep up the great work with {{podcast_name}}.

{{your_name}}
```

**Why it works:**
- Easy to respond to
- Respects their time
- Often converts fence-sitters

---

## Re-engagement (3 months later)

**Subject:** Checking back in - {{podcast_name}}

```
{{host_name}},

Reaching back out about being a guest on {{podcast_name}}.

Since I last reached out:
- {{app_name}} hit {{milestone_1}}
- {{update_2}}
- {{update_3}}

Thought these updates might make for a better story now.

Topics I could cover:
- {{updated_topic_1}}
- {{updated_topic_2}}

Worth revisiting?

{{your_name}}
```

## Response handling

### If they say "Yes, let's book"

```
{{host_name}},

Great. Looking forward to it.

Here's my availability for the next 2 weeks:
{{availability_list}}

Or grab a time here: {{calendar_link}}

Before we record, it would help to know:
- What topics do you want to focus on?
- Any topics I should avoid?
- Will you send questions in advance?
- Recording platform (Zoom, Riverside, etc.)?

Talk soon.

{{your_name}}
```

---

### If they say "Maybe later"

```
{{host_name}},

Totally understand. I'll reach out in {{timeframe}}.

If anything changes before then, I'm here.

Keep up the great work with the show.

{{your_name}}
```

Add to calendar reminder for 3-month follow-up.

---

### If they say "Not a fit"

```
{{host_name}},

Appreciate you letting me know.

If you know other podcasts that might be interested, feel free to pass along my info.

Keep up the good work.

{{your_name}}
```

---

### If they ask questions

**"What makes you different from other guests?"**

```
{{host_name}},

Good question.

Most guests talk theory. I talk specifics:
- Exact numbers (revenue, users, costs)
- Actual tactics (not vague advice)
- Real failures (not just success stories)

I also come prepared with stories and takeaways, not just answers.

Your listeners will walk away with specific things they can do, not just inspiration.

Does that help?

{{your_name}}
```

**"Do you have previous podcast experience?"**

If yes:
```
Yes. Here are a few:
- {{podcast_1}}: {{link}}
- {{podcast_2}}: {{link}}
- {{podcast_3}}: {{link}}

Happy to send audio samples if helpful.

{{your_name}}
```

If no:
```
This would be my first podcast interview.

That said, I've done plenty of video content and presentations. I'm comfortable on camera/mic and come prepared.

Happy to do a quick test call if you want to make sure I'm not a disaster.

{{your_name}}
```

```
{{host_name}},

Here's where I'm at:

- {{platform_1}}: {{followers_1}}
- {{platform_2}}: {{followers_2}}
- Email list: {{size}}

Total reach: ~{{total}}

I'll promote the episode to my audience when it airs.

{{your_name}}
```

| Followup 2 | Day 10 | Simple close |
| Re-engagement | Day 90+ | Second chance with updates |

---

## Best practices

### Timing

- Send pitches Tuesday-Thursday
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
- Morning (9-11am their timezone) works best

### Personalization

Always:
- Reference a specific episode
- Explain why you're a fit for their audience
- Offer unique value

Never:
- Send obvious mass emails
- Pitch shows you haven't listened to
- Be vague about what you'd discuss

### Follow-up rules

- Maximum 2 follow-ups after initial pitch
- Space them 5-7 days apart
- Stop if they say no or don't respond after 2nd follow-up
- Wait 3+ months before re-engaging

### Red flags to avoid

- Begging or being desperate
- Following up too frequently
- Pitching topics they've already covered
- Not doing basic research
