# Affiliate program pitch sequence

For recruiting affiliates and partners to promote your apps for commission.

---

## Individual affiliate outreach

### Subject line variants

- A: Affiliate opportunity - {{app_name}}
- B: 25% commission offer for you
- C: Paid partnership for {{their_name}}

### Email 1: The pitch

```
{{first_name}},

Found you through {{how_you_found_them}}. Your content on {{topic}} is solid.

Quick pitch: I'm looking for affiliates to promote {{app_name}}, a {{one_liner}}.

The offer:
- 25% commission on every sale ({{commission_amount}} per annual subscription)
- Free lifetime premium access
- Custom promo code with your name
- Content ideas if you want them

Current affiliates earn $200-800/month depending on audience size.

Interested?

{{your_name}}
```

---

### Email 2: Value add (Day 4)

**Subject:** Re: Affiliate opportunity

```
{{first_name}},

Following up on the affiliate offer.

Here's what other affiliates are doing with {{app_name}}:

- {{affiliate_1}}: Posted a review, earned {{amount}} last month
- {{affiliate_2}}: Does "day in my life" content, steady $300/month
- {{affiliate_3}}: Created a tutorial, gets organic traffic from SEO

Happy to share content ideas that fit your style.

Also, the free premium access is yours regardless. Even if you decide not to promote it, you can use the app.

Interested?

{{your_name}}
```

---

### Email 3: The close (Day 9)

**Subject:** Should I keep you on my list?

```
{{first_name}},

Last note on the affiliate program.

Quick question: should I keep you on my list for future opportunities, or should I stop emailing?

Either way, your content is good. Keep creating.

{{your_name}}
```

## Affiliate program structure

### Commission tiers

| Tier | Monthly sales | Commission |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Bronze | 1-10 | 20% |
| Silver | 11-50 | 25% |
| Gold | 51+ | 30% |

### Bonuses

- $50 bonus for first 5 sales
- $100 bonus for hitting 25 sales
- $250 bonus for hitting 50 sales
- Monthly leaderboard with cash prizes

### Payment terms

- Minimum payout: $50
- Payment schedule: Monthly (15th of following month)
- Payment methods: PayPal, direct deposit, check
- Cookie duration: 30 days

## Response handling

### If they say "Yes, sign me up"

```
{{first_name}},

Great. Let's get you set up.

Here's your affiliate dashboard: {{dashboard_link}}
Your tracking link: {{tracking_link}}
Your promo code: {{their_name}} (or customize)

I'm upgrading your account to lifetime premium now.

Resources available in the dashboard:
- Pre-written posts
- Banner ads
- Email copy
- Screenshots and assets

First steps I recommend:
1. Try the app yourself (important for authentic promotion)
2. Pick a content format that fits your style
3. Start with one post to test

Questions? Reply here or ping me on {{preferred_channel}}.

Let's make some money.

{{your_name}}
```

---

### If they say "What's the catch?"

```
{{first_name}},

Fair question. Here's how it works:

We make money when people pay for premium ($50/year).
You help us reach potential customers.
We pay you 25% of each sale you generate.

Why we give 25%?
- Cheaper than paid ads
- Your audience trusts you more than our ads
- Win-win for everyone

The "catch" is that we need you to actually promote it. If you sign up but never post, nobody makes money.

No upfront cost. No obligation. You can stop anytime.

Clear enough?

{{your_name}}
```

---

### If they say "I don't do affiliate stuff"

```
{{first_name}},

Totally get it. Affiliate marketing isn't for everyone.

Alternative: If you genuinely like the app, would you share it with your audience anyway? No tracking, no commission, just a recommendation.

I can send you free premium access either way.

Let me know.

{{your_name}}
```

## Affiliate activation tips

### Onboarding sequence

**Day 1:** Welcome email with login and resources
**Day 3:** Check-in email with content ideas
**Day 7:** First strategy call offer
**Day 14:** Reminder to post if they haven't
**Day 30:** Performance review and next steps

### Keeping affiliates active

- Monthly newsletter with tips and updates
- Slack channel for community
- Contests and bonus opportunities
- Feature top performers
- Share success stories

### Reactivating dormant affiliates

```
{{first_name}},

Noticed you haven't posted about {{app_name}} recently.

Quick check-in:
- Are you still interested in promoting?
- Is there something I can help with?
- Do you need fresh content ideas?

If you're done with affiliate marketing, no problem. Just let me know so I can update my records.

If you want to restart, I have some new content angles that are working well.

Let me know either way.

{{your_name}}
```
