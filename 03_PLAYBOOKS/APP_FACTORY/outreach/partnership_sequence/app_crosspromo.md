# App cross-promotion sequence

For partnering with other apps to cross-promote to each other's users.

---

## Initial outreach

### Subject line variants

- A: Cross-promo idea for {{their_app}}
- B: {{your_app}} x {{their_app}} partnership
- C: User swap idea

### Email 1: The pitch

```
{{first_name}},

I'm the founder of {{your_app}}, a {{your_one_liner}}.

Your app {{their_app}} has a similar audience ({{shared_audience_description}}), and I think there's a cross-promo opportunity here.

The idea:
- We promote {{their_app}} to our users
- You promote {{your_app}} to yours
- Both apps get exposure to relevant users
- Zero cost for either of us

We have {{your_user_count}} users. Looks like you have a similar size base.

Worth exploring?

{{your_name}}
```

**Why it works:**
- Clear, specific proposal
- Mutual benefit obvious
- Zero cost positioning
- Quick to respond to

---

### Email 2: The specific offer (Day 4)

**Subject:** Re: Cross-promo idea

```
{{first_name}},

Following up on the cross-promo idea.

Here's specifically what I'm proposing:

**What we'd do for {{their_app}}:**
- In-app banner promoting your app for 2 weeks
- Feature in our newsletter ({{newsletter_size}} subscribers)
- Social post mentioning you

**What we'd ask in return:**
- Same treatment in your app/newsletter/social
- Fair exchange based on audience size

**Timing:**
Start next month. Run for 2-4 weeks. Measure results.

If the numbers work, we could make it ongoing.

Does this make sense for {{their_app}}?

{{your_name}}
```

---

### Email 3: The close (Day 9)

**Subject:** Quick question

```
{{first_name}},

Last note on this.

If cross-promo doesn't make sense right now, no problem.

Just let me know:
1. Let's talk (I'll send calendar)
2. Maybe later (I'll follow up in a few months)
3. Not interested (I'll stop emailing)

Either way, {{their_app}} looks solid. Keep building.

{{your_name}}
```

## Target app criteria

### Good fit indicators

- Similar audience demographics
- Complementary (not competitive) functionality
- Similar stage (avoid huge apps that won't respond)
- Active user base (regular updates)
- Has newsletter or social presence for promotion

### Examples by app type

**For PrayerLock (faith habit app):**
- Bible reading apps
- Devotional apps
- Christian meditation apps
- Church apps
- Faith journaling apps

**For WalkToUnlock (fitness habit app):**
- Step tracking apps
- Workout apps
- Habit trackers
- Health/wellness apps
- Running/walking apps

**For productivity apps:**
- Other focus/distraction blockers
- Note-taking apps
- Task managers
- Time trackers
- Pomodoro apps

## Finding cross-promo partners

### Where to look

- App Store categories (similar apps)
- Product Hunt launches (same category)
- Indie Hackers community
- Twitter/X (app dev accounts)
- Subreddits (r/androiddev, r/iOSProgramming)

### Research checklist

- [ ] Check their user count (if public)
- [ ] Verify they're indie/small team (not big company)
- [ ] Find founder email or contact
- [ ] Check if they've done cross-promos before
- [ ] Confirm apps are complementary, not competitive

---

## Partnership logistics

### What to offer

**In-app promotion:**
- Banner ad for partner app
- "Apps we recommend" section
- Onboarding mention
- Settings page link

**Newsletter:**
- Dedicated email
- Feature in regular newsletter
- Sponsored section

**Social:**
- Twitter/X shoutout
- Instagram story
- LinkedIn post

### What to ask for

Match what you offer. Fair exchange based on relative audience sizes.

**If you're smaller:**
- Offer more promotion time
- Include extra channels
- Be flexible on timing

**If you're larger:**
- Ask for fair value match
- Or do it as a favor to build relationship
- Can lead to bigger partnerships later

### Measurement

- Track downloads from cross-promo period
- Use UTM parameters on links
- Compare to baseline acquisition
- Share results with partner

## Response handling

### If they say "Yes, let's do it"

```

Great. Let's set it up.

Here's what I need from you:
1. App name and tagline for our promotion
2. App store links (iOS and Android)
3. Logo/icon (high res)
4. Preferred messaging/description

- Start: {{date}}
- Duration: 2 weeks
- Channels: In-app + newsletter + social

Work for you?

{{your_name}}
```

## Cross-promo best practices

### Do

- Start small (test before big commitment)
- Track everything (downloads, signups)
- Be fair on exchange value
- Communicate results with partner
- Build relationship for future partnerships

### Don't

- Oversell your audience size
- Promise more than you can deliver
- Partner with competitors
- Spam users with too many promos
- Ghost partners after the promo
