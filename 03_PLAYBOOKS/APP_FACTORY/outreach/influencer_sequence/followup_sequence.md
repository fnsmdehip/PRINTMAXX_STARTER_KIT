# Influencer followup sequence

Use for all influencer outreach (faith, fitness, productivity). Send after initial DM or email.

---

## Followup 1: Gentle nudge (3 days)

### DM version

```
{{name}},

Following up on my message about {{app_name}}.

Happy to answer any questions. Or if you just want free access to try it, I can send that over.

Let me know either way.
```

### Email version

**Subject:** Re: {{original_subject}}

```
{{name}},

Following up on {{app_name}}.

Quick recap:
- Free lifetime premium access
- 25% commission if you share it
- No obligation

Happy to send access so you can try it before deciding anything.

Interested?

{{your_name}}
```

## Followup 3: Direct ask (14 days)

### DM version

```
{{name}},

Last message from me on this.

Quick question: should I keep you on my list for future updates, or would you prefer I stop reaching out?

Either way is fine. Just let me know.
```

### Email version

**Subject:** Should I follow up later?

```
{{name}},

Last email on {{app_name}}.

Three options:

1. **Yes, I'm interested** - I'll send free access
2. **Maybe later** - I'll check back in a few months
3. **Not interested** - I'll stop emailing

Any response helps.

{{your_name}}
```

## Best practices

### Timing

- DMs: Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm their timezone
- Emails: Send Tuesday-Wednesday, 9am-11am their timezone
- Avoid weekends and Mondays

### Personalization

Always mention:
- Specific content you liked
- Why you think they're a fit
- Something unique about their style

Never:
- Use generic templates without personalization
- Send to accounts you haven't actually checked
- Mass DM from the same account in short time

### Engagement before outreach

Recommended:
- Like 3-5 of their recent posts
- Leave 1-2 genuine comments
- Share their content (if relevant)
- Wait 2-3 days, then DM

This warms them up and makes your DM less cold.

### Volume guidelines

| Platform | Daily limit |
|----------|-------------|
| Instagram DMs | 15-20 new DMs |
| TikTok DMs | 10-15 new DMs |
| Twitter DMs | 20-25 new DMs |
| Email | 50-100 (if warmed) |

Going over these limits risks account restrictions.
