## Targeting criteria

### Ideal faith influencers

**Audience size:**
- Sweet spot: 5K-50K followers
- Under 5K: low volume
- Over 50K: harder to reach, more expensive

**Content themes:**
- Daily devotionals
- Faith and lifestyle
- Christian living tips
- Prayer and spiritual growth
- Bible study content

**Engagement signals:**
- Comments on posts (not just likes)
- Responds to DMs
- Active posting (at least 2x/week)
- Authentic voice (not overly polished)

### Red flags

- Only posts sponsored content
- Engagement seems fake (lots of likes, no comments)
- Hasn't posted in 2+ weeks
- Only promotes MLM or get-rich content
- Controversial theological positions

## Where to find faith influencers

**Instagram:**
- Search hashtags: #christiancreator #faithblogger #bibleverse #devotional
- Browse explore page when logged into faith account
- Check who follows similar creators

**TikTok:**
- Search: Christian TikTok, faith content, prayer
- FYP discovery when logged into faith account
- Check duets/stitches on popular faith videos

**YouTube:**
- Search: daily devotional, prayer routine, Christian lifestyle
- Check small channels in suggested videos
- Look at comments for active smaller creators

**Podcasts:**
- Listen Notes search: faith, Christian, prayer
- Apple Podcasts browse: Religion & Spirituality
- Look for podcasters who also have social presence
