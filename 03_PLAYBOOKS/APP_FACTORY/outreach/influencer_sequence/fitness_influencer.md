## Targeting criteria

### Ideal fitness influencers

**Audience size:**
- Sweet spot: 5K-50K followers
- Under 5K: low volume
- Over 50K: harder to reach, more expensive

**Content themes:**
- Workout routines
- Fitness motivation
- Weight loss journeys
- Running/walking content
- Gym tips
- Home workouts
- Active lifestyle

**Engagement signals:**
- Real comments on posts
- Responds to DMs
- Posts consistently (3x/week minimum)
- Authentic voice (not just sponsored content)
- Actually works out (not just poses)

### Red flags

- Only posts thirst traps
- Engagement seems fake
- Promotes sketchy supplements
- No actual fitness content
- Hasn't posted in 2+ weeks

## Where to find fitness influencers

**Instagram:**
- Search hashtags: #fitnessmotivation #workoutroutine #gymlife #activewear #fitfam
- Explore page (logged into fitness account)
- Check tagged posts on fitness brands

**TikTok:**
- Search: workout, gym, fitness routine, step challenge
- FYP discovery (fitness account)
- Check stitches on popular fitness videos

**YouTube:**
- Search: workout routine, fitness vlog, gym motivation
- Small channels in suggested videos
- Comments section for engaged smaller creators

### Home workout creators
