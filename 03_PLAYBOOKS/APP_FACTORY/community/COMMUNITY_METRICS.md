# Community metrics

What to measure. What it means. How to act on it.

---

## Engagement metrics

### Daily Active Users (DAU)

**Definition:** Unique members who take an action (post, react, comment) per day

**Formula:** Count of unique members with activity / day

**Target benchmarks:**
- Poor: < 5% of total members
- Average: 5-15%
- Good: 15-30%
- Excellent: 30%+

**Action if low:**
- More engaging prompts
- Tag specific members to participate
- Reduce friction (simpler channels)
- Check if content matches member interests

### Messages per active user

**Definition:** Average posts/reactions per active member

**Target:** 3-5 messages per active user per day

**Why it matters:** Distinguishes "checked in" from "actively participating"

### Response rate

**Definition:** % of posts that get at least one reply

**Target:** 90%+ of member posts get replies

**Action if low:**
- Train moderators to respond faster
- Set up auto-notifications for new posts
- Create dedicated "support" or "questions" channels
- Assign "greeters" to welcome and engage

### Response time

**Definition:** Average time from post to first reply

**Target:** < 30 minutes for questions, < 4 hours for general posts

**How to measure:**
- Discord: Use statbot.net
- Circle: Built-in analytics
- Skool: Built-in analytics

## Growth metrics

### Member growth rate

**Formula:** (New members - Churned members) / Previous total * 100

**Target:** 10-20% month-over-month net growth

### Acquisition sources

Track where members come from:
- App referral
- Social media
- Word of mouth (ask on join)
- Ambassador referrals
- Organic search

**Action:** Double down on top 2 sources.

### Activation rate

**Definition:** % of new members who take meaningful action in first 7 days

**Meaningful actions:**
- Post introduction
- Comment on someone else's post
- Complete first challenge
- Earn first badge

**Target:** 60%+ activation within 7 days

**Formula:** Members with Day 1-7 action / New members * 100

### Retention cohorts

Track by join week:
- Week 1 retention: % still active after 7 days
- Month 1 retention: % still active after 30 days
- Month 3 retention: % still active after 90 days

**Targets:**
- Week 1: 70%+
- Month 1: 50%+
- Month 3: 30%+

## Health indicators

### Lurker ratio

**Definition:** Members who view but never post/react

**Formula:** (Total members - Active members) / Total members * 100

**Target:** < 70% lurkers

**Action if high:**
- Lower barrier to first post
- Create polls (easier than writing)
- Direct DM outreach to lurkers
- "Lurker appreciation" posts to draw them out

### Power user concentration

**Definition:** % of content from top 10% of posters

**Target:** < 50% from top 10%

**Why it matters:** If 5 people create 80% of content, community dies if they leave.

**Action if high:**
- Actively recruit new voices
- Create structured opportunities for quiet members
- Reduce frequency of power user posts

### Sentiment analysis

**What to track:**
- Positive vs negative post ratio
- Support request trends
- Complaint patterns
- Member satisfaction surveys (quarterly)

**Red flags:**
- Increase in complaints
- Fewer celebration posts
- More "I'm leaving" posts
- Drop in emoji reactions

### Thread depth

**Definition:** Average replies per original post

**Target:** 3-5 replies per thread

**Why it matters:** Shows real conversation happening, not just broadcast.

---

## Conversion metrics

### Community to paid conversion

**Formula:** Members who upgrade to paid app tier / Total community members * 100

**Target:** 5-15% conversion to paid

### Time to conversion

**Definition:** Days from community join to paid conversion

**Track:** Average and median (median more useful)

**Why it matters:** Identifies optimal nurture length

### Referral rate

**Formula:** New members from existing member referrals / Total new members * 100

**Target:** 30%+ of growth from referrals

## Dashboard setup

### Discord dashboard (using Statbot)

1. Add Statbot to server
2. Configure tracking:
   - Messages per day
   - Active users per day
   - New members per day
   - Top channels by activity
   - Member retention

3. Set up weekly report to DM

### Circle dashboard (built-in)

Navigate to: Admin > Analytics

Track:
- Member growth
- Active members
- Top contributors
- Most popular posts
- Event attendance

### Skool dashboard (built-in)

Navigate to: Analytics tab

Track:
- Leaderboard activity
- Course completions
- Member levels
- Post engagement
- Event signups

## Reporting cadence

### Daily (quick check)

- New member count
- Response rate (any posts unanswered?)
- Red flag scan (complaints, conflicts)

### Weekly (team review)

- DAU trend
- Activation rate
- Top posts/members
- Content calendar review
- Moderator feedback

### Monthly (strategic)

- Retention cohorts
- Growth rate
- Conversion metrics
- Sentiment trends
- Ambassador performance
- Platform comparison (if testing)

### Quarterly (deep dive)

- Member satisfaction survey
- Community ROI calculation
- Platform decision review
- Ambassador program review
- Strategy adjustment

## ROI calculation

### Community costs

| Item | Monthly cost |
|------|--------------|
| Platform fee | $0-99 |
| Moderator time (10 hrs/wk @ $20/hr) | $800 |
| Tools (bots, analytics) | $50 |
| Events/prizes | $200 |
| **Total** | ~$1,150 |

### Community revenue

| Source | Monthly value |
|--------|---------------|
| Paid conversions (50 @ $10 LTV uplift) | $500 |
| Reduced churn (100 saves @ $5 LTV) | $500 |
| Referral value (30 @ $10 CAC saved) | $300 |
| UGC value (content creation saved) | $300 |
| Ambassador labor saved | $400 |
| **Total** | ~$2,000 |

**Net ROI:** $850/month (74% return)

## Alert thresholds

Set up alerts for:

| Metric | Red alert | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Response rate < 80% | Same day | Clear backlog, add moderator coverage |
| DAU drops 20%+ | Same day | Diagnose cause, send re-engagement |
| Activation rate < 40% | Weekly | Revamp onboarding |
| Complaints spike 2x | Same day | Direct outreach, address issue |
| Power user leaves | Same day | Thank you message, exit interview |

---

## Key takeaways

1. Engagement > size (100 active beats 10,000 lurkers)
2. Response rate is the #1 early indicator
3. Track cohorts, not just totals
4. Weekly reviews catch problems before they compound
5. Community has real ROI when measured properly
