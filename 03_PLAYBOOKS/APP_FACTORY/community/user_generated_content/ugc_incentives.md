# UGC incentives

How to motivate users to create content without making it feel transactional.

---

## Incentive hierarchy

Not all incentives are equal. Use the right one for the right content.

```
Recognition (free, scales infinitely)
      |
      v
Access (low cost, high perceived value)
      |
      v
Rewards (moderate cost, tangible)
      |
      v
Cash (high cost, use sparingly)
```

**Principle:** Start at the top. Only go down if needed.

---

## Recognition incentives

### Public features

**What it is:** Showcase their content publicly

**Where to use:**
- Feature on social media (tag them)
- Homepage testimonial spot
- "Member of the week" spotlight
- App Store screenshot featuring their win
- Newsletter feature

**Why it works:** People crave recognition. Being featured = social capital.

**Cost:** $0

**Best for:** Most UGC requests

### Badges and roles

**What it is:** Visual status in community

**Examples:**
- "Featured Creator" badge
- Special Discord role
- Verified contributor checkmark
- Leaderboard ranking

**Why it works:** Status is valuable in communities.

**Cost:** $0

**Best for:** Ongoing contributors

### Personal thank-you

**What it is:** Direct message from founder/team

**Example:**
```
Hey Sarah - just wanted to personally thank you for sharing your story.
Reading about your journey literally made my day. Screenshots like
yours are why we built this. Keep crushing it.

- [Founder name]
```

**Why it works:** Personal attention from leadership is rare and valued.

**Cost:** 2 minutes of time

**Best for:** High-quality submissions

## Access incentives

### Early access

**What it is:** First look at new features

**Examples:**
- Beta tester group
- Early access to new challenges
- Preview of upcoming content
- Input on feature development

**Why it works:** Makes them feel like insiders.

**Cost:** $0 (organizational effort only)

**Best for:** Power users, repeat contributors

### Premium unlocks

**What it is:** Free access to paid features

**Examples:**
- 1 month free premium
- Unlock specific feature permanently
- Extended trial period

**Why it works:** Real value, feel exclusive.

**Cost:** Opportunity cost only

**Tiers:**
| UGC type | Premium reward |
|----------|----------------|
| Screenshot share | 1 week free |
| Written testimonial | 1 month free |
| Video testimonial | 3 months free |
| Case study interview | 6 months free |

### Exclusive community

**What it is:** Access to inner circle

**Examples:**
- Private Discord channel
- Monthly founder calls
- VIP events
- Mastermind group

**Why it works:** Belonging to exclusive group is desirable.

**Cost:** Time + organization

**Best for:** Top contributors, ambassadors

---

## Reward incentives

### Merch

**What it is:** Physical branded items

**Examples:**
- T-shirts
- Stickers
- Notebooks
- Water bottles
- Phone cases

**Why it works:** Tangible item = real value + walking advertisement.

**Cost:** $5-30 per item + shipping

**Best for:** Video testimonials, case studies, ambassador milestones

### Gift cards

**What it is:** Store credit or gift cards

**Examples:**
- Amazon gift card
- App Store credit
- Partner brand gift card

**Why it works:** Flexible value, easy to deliver.

**Cost:** $10-50 typically

**Best for:** Specific campaigns, larger asks

### Product bundles

**What it is:** Related products as reward

**Examples:**
- Fitness app: Protein samples, resistance bands
- Productivity app: Notebook, pen set
- Faith app: Journal, devotional book

**Why it works:** Aligned with their interests.

**Cost:** Variable

**Best for:** Niche-relevant rewards feel more thoughtful than cash

## Cash incentives

### When to use cash

- Video content (production effort)
- Large time commitment (interviews, case studies)
- Professional creators (their livelihood)
- Competitive campaigns

### When NOT to use cash

- Simple testimonials (feels transactional)
- Community posts (kills organic feel)
- First-time contributors (sets wrong expectation)

### Cash rates

| Content type | Rate range | Notes |
|--------------|------------|-------|
| Screenshot | $0-5 | Usually recognition is enough |
| Written testimonial | $0-25 | Gift cards preferred |
| 30-second video | $25-50 | Basic smartphone quality |
| Professional video | $100-300 | Edited, high quality |
| Case study interview | $50-150 | 30-60 min of their time |
| Ongoing ambassador | $100-500/mo | Depends on requirements |

### Disclosure requirements

**FTC Rule:** If you compensate for testimonials, must disclose.

**Disclosure examples:**
```
"Sarah received free access to [App] in exchange for her honest review."

"Sponsored testimonial"

"#ad" or "#sponsored"
```

## Gamification incentives

### Point systems

**How it works:**
- Earn points for UGC actions
- Redeem points for rewards

**Point structure:**
| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Post a win | 10 |
| Share screenshot | 25 |
| Write tip | 50 |
| Submit testimonial | 100 |
| Create video | 200 |

**Redemption:**
| Reward | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Sticker pack | 100 |
| 1 month premium | 300 |
| T-shirt | 500 |
| $25 gift card | 750 |

### Leaderboards

**Monthly UGC leaderboard:**
```
TOP CONTRIBUTORS THIS MONTH
1. @user1 - 450 points
2. @user2 - 380 points
3. @user3 - 275 points
...
```

**Prizes:**
- #1: $50 + featured spot
- #2-3: 3 months premium
- #4-10: 1 month premium

### Challenges with prizes

**Structure:**
```
FEBRUARY UGC CHALLENGE

Theme: "My morning routine with [App]"
Format: 30-second video OR screenshot carousel
Deadline: Feb 28

Prizes:
- Grand prize: $100 + 1 year premium
- Runner up (x2): $50 + 6 months premium
- Honorable mentions (x5): 3 months premium

All participants: Exclusive badge
```

## Incentive delivery

### Immediate (recognition)

- Tag them in social post same day
- DM thank you within hours
- Add badge within 24 hours

### Fast (access/digital)

- Premium unlock within 24-48 hours
- Gift card code within 48 hours
- Beta access within 1 week

### Scheduled (physical)

- Merch ships within 2 weeks
- Collect shipping info promptly
- Provide tracking number

### Communication

**When promising incentive:**
```
Thanks for submitting your testimonial! Here's what happens next:

1. We'll review within 48 hours
2. If selected, your 1-month premium unlock arrives via email
3. You'll be notified when we feature your content

Questions? Reply to this message.
```

**When delivering:**
```
Your testimonial is now live on our website!

As promised: [Premium code / Gift card / Tracking number]

Thanks for being part of the community.
```

## Avoiding incentive pitfalls

### Problem: Incentive hunters

**Signs:** Submit low-quality content just for rewards

**Fix:**
- Quality requirements clearly stated
- Review before rewarding
- Cap rewards per person per period

### Problem: Entitled expectations

**Signs:** Users expect rewards for everything

**Fix:**
- Lead with recognition (free)
- Reserve tangible rewards for exceptional content
- Be selective, not automatic

### Problem: Feels fake

**Signs:** Testimonials sound scripted, users mention reward

**Fix:**
- Ask for honest feedback, not positive feedback
- Don't script testimonials
- Proper disclosure of compensation

### Problem: Budget blowout

**Signs:** Rewards cost more than content value

**Fix:**
- Set monthly UGC reward budget
- Track cost per piece
- Prioritize free/low-cost incentives
- Reserve cash for high-impact content

## Budget planning

### Monthly UGC budget example

| Category | Budget | Allocation |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Premium unlocks | $0 (opportunity cost) | 50% of rewards |
| Gift cards | $100 | 25% |
| Merch | $75 | 15% |
| Cash (video) | $75 | 10% |
| **Total** | **$250** | |

### ROI calculation

```
UGC pieces collected: 50
Total cost: $250
Cost per piece: $5

Compare to:
- Stock photo: $20-50/image
- Professional testimonial video: $500-2000
- Influencer post: $100-1000

UGC at $5/piece = massive ROI
```
