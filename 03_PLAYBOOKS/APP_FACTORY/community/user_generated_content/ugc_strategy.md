# UGC strategy

User-generated content is free marketing that converts better than anything you create.

---

## Why UGC matters

### The numbers

- UGC converts 4x higher than branded content
- 79% of people say UGC influences purchase decisions
- UGC posts get 6x more engagement than brand posts
- Cost per UGC piece: $0 (vs $500-5000 for professional content)

### The psychology

People trust people like them. Not brands. Not polished marketing.

When a real user says "This app changed my routine," it hits different than when the brand says "Transform your routine with our app."

## Types of UGC to collect

| Type | Example | Use case |
|------|---------|----------|

| Type | Example | Use case |

## Collection systems

### Passive collection (always running)

**1. In-app prompts**
Trigger after positive moments:
- Completed first challenge: "Share your win?"
- 7-day streak: "Screenshot your progress?"
- Hit milestone: "Tell others about your journey?"

**2. Community hashtags**
Create branded hashtags:
- #[AppName]Wins
- #[AppName]Journey
- #[AppName]Tips

Monitor and reshare.

**3. Email requests**
After 30 days active:
```
Subject: You're crushing it - mind sharing?

Hey [name],

You've hit [X milestone]. That's worth celebrating.

Would you share a quick thought on what's working for you?

[Simple form link]

Takes 2 minutes. Helps others like you find us.

Thanks!
```

### Active collection (campaigns)

**1. Monthly UGC challenge**
```
This month's challenge: Show us your [specific thing]

Post in #ugc-challenge with #[hashtag]
Best submission gets [prize]
Deadline: [date]
```

**2. Testimonial drives**
DM power users directly:
```
Hey [name]!

Your progress has been incredible. Would you be willing to share a quick testimonial?

Could be:
- 30-second video
- Written paragraph
- Screenshot of your stats

We'd feature you + [incentive].

No pressure, but your story could inspire others.
```

**3. Feature user interviews**
Monthly "Member Spotlight":
- 15-min call with selected user
- Record for podcast/video
- Create written case study

## UGC funnels

### The ask ladder

Don't start with "Record a video testimonial." Build up:

```
Level 1: React to something (1 click)
    |
Level 2: Comment on post (30 seconds)
    |
Level 3: Answer simple question (2 minutes)
    |
Level 4: Share screenshot (2 minutes)
    |
Level 5: Write short testimonial (5 minutes)
    |
Level 6: Record video (10 minutes)
```

Move users up the ladder gradually.

### Automation triggers

Set up automatic prompts at these moments:

| Trigger | Ask | Why it works |
|---------|-----|--------------|
| 7-day streak | Screenshot prompt | Pride moment |
| First win | Share in community | Celebration |
| 30 days active | Written testimonial | Enough experience |
| 100 days | Video testimonial | Power user |
| Cancelled trial | Exit survey | Honest feedback |

## Quality vs quantity

### What makes good UGC

**Good UGC has:**
- Specific details ("Lost 15 lbs in 8 weeks")
- Authentic voice (not corporate speak)
- Visual proof (screenshots, photos)
- Emotional element (why it matters)

**Avoid UGC that:**
- Sounds scripted
- Makes unverifiable claims
- Is obviously incentivized
- Has legal issues (minors, medical claims)

### Curating for quality

Not all UGC should be featured. Criteria for featuring:

- [ ] Authentic voice (sounds like a real person)
- [ ] Specific outcome or experience
- [ ] Proper rights obtained
- [ ] No problematic claims
- [ ] High enough quality (readable, audible)

## Incentive structures

### What works

| Incentive | Best for | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Recognition | Most users | Feature them, tag them |
| Badges/roles | Gamified communities | Special status |
| Free premium | Testimonials | 1-3 months free |
| Merch | Active creators | T-shirts, stickers |
| Cash | Video content | $25-100 per video |

### What to avoid

- Paying for written reviews (looks fake)
- Over-incentivizing (attracts wrong people)
- Making it transactional (kills authenticity)

### Reward framework

```
Small UGC (screenshot, comment): Recognition only
Medium UGC (testimonial, story): Free month
Large UGC (video, case study): Premium perk + merch
Ongoing creators: Ambassador program
```
