## Template Usage Guidelines

### How to Adapt This Template

1. **Headline:** Keep it under 10 words. Consequence-first. Specific benefit, not feature.
2. **Subheadline:** Who it's for + what they get. Specific outcome in measurable terms.
3. **Bullets:** Feature → specific benefit. Include numbers. Add proof or the reason it works.
4. **CTA:** Action verb + what they'll get. "Start Building," "Download," "Try Free," "Request Demo."
5. **Social Proof:** Real quote (placeholder format shown). Specific metric or transformation.

### Voice Checklist Before Publishing

- [ ] No em dashes (use commas or periods)
- [ ] No banned AI vocabulary (leverage, utilize, innovative, comprehensive, etc.)
- [ ] Specific numbers in every bullet (not "saves time" but "saves 8 hours")
- [ ] Consequence-first (lead with what happens, not explanation)
- [ ] Zero hedging ("probably," "might," "may")
- [ ] Lowercase casual where appropriate (@pipelineabuser energy)
- [ ] Single hedge maximum per sentence

### When to Write New Copy vs Use Template

- **Use template:** You're launching a new niche app and need fast landing copy
- **Write new copy:** You have specific user research or different positioning
- **Hybrid:** Use template as starting point, customize with real user quotes and data
