## VA instructions for creator outreach

1. **Build list:**
   - Search Twitter/X for "ChatGPT tips" creators
   - Search YouTube for "productivity apps" reviewers
   - Search TikTok for AI content creators
   - Minimum 1,000 followers

2. **Collect info:**
   - Name
   - Platform
   - Follower count
   - Niche
   - Email (from bio, website, or YouTube about)
   - Recent relevant content

3. **Personalize:**
   - Reference specific content they made
   - Don't send generic templates
   - Match their tone/style

4. **Track:**
   - Log in spreadsheet immediately
   - Set follow-up reminder for 4 days
   - Update status after each response

5. **Escalate:**
   - Forward positive responses to [Name]
   - Include all context
   - Don't negotiate terms without approval
