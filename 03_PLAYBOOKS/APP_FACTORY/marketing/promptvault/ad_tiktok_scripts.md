## Production notes for TikTok Ads

**Format:**
- Vertical 9:16
- 15-25 seconds optimal
- First 2 seconds must hook
- Captions required

**Style:**
- UGC-feeling, not polished
- Screen recordings showing real usage
- Show actual prompts and outputs
- Fast cuts, no dead space
- Relatable frustration in "before" states

**Creative testing:**
- Test time-saving angle vs organization angle
- Test professional vs casual creator
- Test with/without numbers
- Run 3-5 creatives, kill losers after 48 hours

**Targeting:**
- Interest: ChatGPT, AI tools, productivity, automation
- Behavior: Tech-savvy users, app installers
- Lookalike: From email list or purchasers
- Age: 20-45 primary

**Budget:**
- Start at $20/day per creative
- Scale winners to $50-100/day
- Target CPI under $2
