**Section headline:**
Common questions.

**Q1: Does this work with all AI tools or just ChatGPT?**
PromptVault works with any AI tool. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot. Save and organize prompts for any of them.

**Q2: How is this different from just saving prompts in a note app?**
Variables, folders, one-tap copy, cloud sync, and search. Note apps work, but they're not built for prompts. PromptVault is.

**Q3: Can I share prompts with my team?**
Yes. PromptVault Pro includes sharing. Send individual prompts or entire folders to colleagues.

**Q4: What are variables?**
Placeholders like [TOPIC] or [AUDIENCE] that you fill in before using a prompt. One template becomes infinite prompts.

**Q5: Is there a free version?**
Yes. Free version includes 20 prompts and 3 folders. Pro includes unlimited prompts, folders, variables, sharing, and cloud sync.

**Q6: How much does Pro cost?**
$4.99/month or $29.99/year. That's less than $2.50/month for unlimited prompt storage.

**Q7: Does it sync across devices?**
Yes. Pro users get cloud sync across iOS, Android, and web. Your library is always accessible.

**Q8: What's in the starter pack?**
25 proven prompts for writing, research, coding, and productivity. Ready to use or customize.

**Q9: Can I import my existing prompts?**
Yes. Import from text files, CSV, or paste directly. Getting started takes minutes.

**Q10: What if I don't use ChatGPT that often?**
If you use AI even a few times per week, saving good prompts compounds over time. Start small. Your library grows naturally.
