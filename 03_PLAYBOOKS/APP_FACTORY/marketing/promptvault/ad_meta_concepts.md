## Targeting recommendations

**Audience 1: AI enthusiasts**
- Interest: ChatGPT, OpenAI, AI tools, machine learning
- Age: 22-45
- Exclude: Recent app installers

**Audience 2: Productivity focused**
- Interest: Productivity apps, Notion, automation, efficiency
- Age: 25-45

**Audience 3: Content creators**
- Interest: Content creation, copywriting, social media marketing
- Age: 22-40

**Audience 4: Freelancers**
- Interest: Freelancing, Upwork, Fiverr, self-employment
- Age: 25-45

**Audience 5: Lookalike**
- Source: App purchasers or email subscribers
- 1-3% lookalike
- US only

**Audience 6: Retargeting**
- Website visitors last 30 days
- Video viewers (50%+)
- Engaged with AI/productivity ads
