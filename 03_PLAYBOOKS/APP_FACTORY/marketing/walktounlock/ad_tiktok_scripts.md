## Production notes for TikTok Ads

**Format:**
- Vertical 9:16
- 15-25 seconds optimal
- First 2 seconds must hook
- Captions required

**Style:**
- UGC-feeling, not polished
- Outdoor shots when possible
- Show real step counters
- Before/after transformation content
- Couples content performs well in fitness

**Creative testing:**
- Test each hook variation
- Test with/without transformation angle
- Test male vs female creators
- Run 3-5 creatives, kill losers after 48 hours

**Targeting:**
- Interest: Fitness, weight loss, walking, health
- Behavior: Fitness app users, health conscious
- Lookalike: From email list or purchasers
- Age: 22-45 primary

**Budget:**
- Start at $20/day per creative
- Scale winners to $50-100/day
- Target CPI under $2.50
