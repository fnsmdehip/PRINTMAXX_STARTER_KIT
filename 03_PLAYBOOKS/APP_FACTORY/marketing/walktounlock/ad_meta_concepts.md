## Targeting recommendations

**Audience 1: Fitness interests**
- Interest: Fitness, weight loss, walking, health, Apple Watch, Fitbit
- Age: 22-50
- Exclude: Recent app installers

**Audience 2: Remote workers**
- Interest: Remote work, work from home, digital nomad
- Job title: Works remotely (if available)
- Age: 25-45

**Audience 3: Screen time concerned**
- Interest: Digital minimalism, screen time management, phone addiction
- Age: 22-40

**Audience 4: Lookalike**
- Source: App purchasers or email subscribers
- 1-3% lookalike
- US only

**Audience 5: Retargeting**
- Website visitors last 30 days
- Video viewers (50%+)
- Engaged with past fitness ads

## Budget allocation

**Testing phase (Week 1-2):**
- $10/day per ad concept
- $50/day total
- Run all 5 concepts

**Scaling phase (Week 3+):**
- Kill bottom 2 performers
- Scale top 3 to $30/day each
- Add new variations of winners

**Target metrics:**
- CPI (cost per install): Under $2.50
- CPA (cost per trial): Under $4
- CTR: Above 1%
