## Email 3: The walking tricks (sent day 3)

**Subject:** Easy ways to add 3,000 steps

**Body:**

Day 3.

By now you've probably noticed: getting steps isn't always convenient.

Here are tricks that help:

**Morning walk (2,000-3,000 steps)**
Wake up, walk for 20-30 minutes. Don't check phone first. The walk IS the way to unlock your phone.

**Walking calls (1,000-2,000 steps)**
Take phone meetings while walking. AirPods + walking = steps without extra time.

**Lunch walk (1,500-2,000 steps)**
Instead of eating at your desk, walk to get food. Or walk after eating.

**TV walking (1,000+ steps)**
Walk in place during a show. Sounds weird. Works well.

**Parking far (500-800 steps)**
Park at the back of every lot. Adds up.

**Take stairs always (varies)**
Elevator is easier. Stairs add steps.

You don't need one big walk. You need steps throughout the day.
