## Copy Style Compliance

All marketing materials follow `.claude/rules/copy-style.md`:

### Avoided AI patterns
- Zero em dashes
- No "It's not just X, it's Y" constructions
- No banned vocabulary (leverage, utilize, delve, comprehensive, robust, innovative, seamless, game-changer, unlock, empower)
- No vague attributions without specifics
- No promotional adjectives (breathtaking, revolutionary, cutting-edge)
- Sentence case headings
- Direct statements without excessive hedging

### PRINTMAXXER voice characteristics
- Direct, get to the point in first sentence
- Specific numbers and results
- Practical, "here's what works" tone
- Casual, like texting a smart friend
- Honest about what the app does and doesn't do

## Marketing Materials Summary

### PrayerLock
**Target:** Christians wanting morning devotion habits
**Hook:** "Your phone is the first thing you reach for. Make prayer the first thing you do."
**Key metric:** Users pray 23 min/day average, up from 8 min before
**Pricing:** $9.99/mo or $49.99/yr

**Taglines:**
1. No prayer, no scrolling
2. Pray first, scroll later
3. Block apps, build faith
4. Your phone, God's schedule
5. Morning prayer enforced

### WalkToUnlock
**Target:** Health-conscious, sedentary workers
**Hook:** "Want to scroll? Take a walk first."
**Key metric:** Users walk 2,847 more steps/day, 38% increase
**Pricing:** $7.99/mo or $39.99/yr

**Taglines:**
1. No steps, no scrolling
2. Walk to unlock your apps
3. Steps first, scroll later
4. Earn your screen time
5. Walk more, scroll less
