## Targeting recommendations

**Audience 1: Christian interests**
- Interest: Christianity, Jesus Christ, Prayer, Devotional, Bible study
- Age: 25-55
- Exclude: Recent app installers

**Audience 2: Parenting**
- Interest: Parenting, Christian parenting, Family devotional
- Age: 30-50
- Gender: Female primary

**Audience 3: Screen time concerned**
- Interest: Digital minimalism, Screen time management, Phone addiction
- Add: Christian/faith interests
- Age: 25-45

**Audience 4: Lookalike**
- Source: App purchasers
- 1-3% lookalike
- US only

**Audience 5: Retargeting**
- Website visitors last 30 days
- Video viewers (50%+)
- Engaged with past ads

## Budget allocation

**Testing phase (Week 1-2):**
- $10/day per ad concept
- $50/day total
- Run all 5 concepts

**Scaling phase (Week 3+):**
- Kill bottom 2 performers
- Scale top 3 to $30/day each
- Add new variations of winners

**Target metrics:**
- CPI (cost per install): Under $2
- CPA (cost per trial): Under $3
- CTR: Above 1%
