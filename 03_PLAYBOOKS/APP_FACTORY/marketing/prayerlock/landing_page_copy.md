## FAQ section

**Section headline:**
Common questions.

**Q1: What if I need my phone for an emergency?**
Phone calls and texts always work. Only the apps you choose to block get locked. Emergency functions are never affected.

**Q2: Can I skip the devotional if I'm running late?**
No. That's the point. If you could skip it, the app wouldn't work. But you can set a shorter devotional time (even 2 minutes) on days you know are rushed.

**Q3: What stops me from just deleting the app?**
Nothing. But if you're the kind of person who would delete it to scroll, you probably wouldn't download it in the first place. The app works for people who want accountability, not people trying to cheat.

**Q4: Does it work on Android?**
Yes. PrayerLock works on both iOS and Android. The experience is nearly identical on both platforms.

**Q5: Can I use my own Bible app?**
Yes. You can connect any Bible or devotional app. The lock releases when you've spent the required time in your chosen app.

**Q6: How much does it cost?**
The app is free to download with basic features (3 app blocks). PrayerLock Pro is $4.99/month or $39.99/year and includes unlimited blocks, family accounts, and advanced features.

**Q7: What if I miss a day?**
Your streak resets, but you can start again immediately. Many users track "current streak" and "longest streak" separately. One missed day doesn't erase your progress.

**Q8: Is there a family plan?**
Yes. The Family Plan ($5.99/month) covers up to 6 family members. Parents can manage settings for kids' accounts.

**Q9: How is this different from Screen Time or app limits?**
Screen Time lets you ignore the limit. PrayerLock doesn't. There's no "ignore for today" button. The block is absolute until you complete your devotional.

**Q10: What denomination is this for?**
All of them. PrayerLock works for Catholics, Protestants, Orthodox, and non-denominational Christians. The devotional content is flexible. You choose what you read or pray.
