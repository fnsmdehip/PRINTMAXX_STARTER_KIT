## Production notes for TikTok Ads

**Format:**
- Vertical 9:16
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- First 3 seconds must hook
- Captions required (use TikTok's auto-captions)

**Style:**
- UGC-feeling, not polished
- Natural lighting preferred
- Real locations (bedroom, kitchen, living room)
- No studio look
- Show the actual app interface

**Creative testing:**
- Test each hook variation
- Test different demographics (age, gender)
- Test with/without music
- Run 3-5 creatives, kill losers after 48 hours

**Targeting:**
- Interest: Christianity, prayer, faith, devotional
- Behavior: App installers, religious content consumers
- Lookalike: From email list or purchasers
- Age: 25-55 primary

**Budget:**
- Start at $20/day per creative
- Scale winners to $50-100/day
- Target CPI under $2
