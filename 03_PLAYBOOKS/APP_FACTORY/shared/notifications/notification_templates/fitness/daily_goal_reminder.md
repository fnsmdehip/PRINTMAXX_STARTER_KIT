# Daily goal reminder notifications

Fitness apps: WalkToUnlock, FemFit

Sent in the morning or at user's preferred activity time.

---

## Primary variants

**Variant A - Direct**
- Title: "Today's goal"
- Body: "[X] steps. You've got this."

**Variant B - Progress**
- Title: "[X] to go"
- Body: "Hit your goal by end of day."

**Variant C - Simple**
- Title: "Time to move"
- Body: "Your daily goal is waiting."

---

## WalkToUnlock specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Phone's locked"
- Body: "[X] steps to unlock. Start walking."

**Variant B**
- Title: "Earn your screen time"
- Body: "[X] steps first."

**Variant C**
- Title: "Walk first"
- Body: "[X] steps to unlock your apps."

---

## FemFit specific

**Variant A - Workout reminder**
- Title: "Today's workout"
- Body: "[Workout name] - [X] minutes."

**Variant B - Streak aware**
- Title: "Day [X]"
- Body: "[Workout name] is ready."

**Variant C - Low barrier**
- Title: "[X] minutes"
- Body: "That's all today's workout needs."

---

## Personalized by time of day

**Morning (6-9 AM)**
- Body: "Start your day strong."

**Midday (11 AM - 1 PM)**
- Body: "Lunch break workout?"

**Evening (5-7 PM)**
- Body: "End your day with movement."

---

## Timing

- Default: 7 AM
- Allow custom time based on user preference
- Detect patterns: if user usually works out at 6 PM, remind at 5:30 PM
- Skip if goal already completed today
- Weekend timing: 8 AM default

---
