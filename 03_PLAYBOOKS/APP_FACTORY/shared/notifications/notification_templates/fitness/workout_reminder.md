# Workout reminder notifications

Fitness apps: WalkToUnlock, FemFit

Scheduled reminders for specific workout times.

---

## Pre-workout reminders

**30 minutes before**
- Title: "Workout in 30"
- Body: "[Workout name] starts soon."

**15 minutes before**
- Title: "Almost time"
- Body: "[Workout name] in 15 minutes."

**At scheduled time**
- Title: "Time to start"
- Body: "[Workout name] is ready."

---

## FemFit specific

**Variant A - Workout type**
- Title: "[Workout type] time"
- Body: "[Duration] minutes of [focus area]."

**Variant B - Instructor**
- Title: "Workout with [Instructor]"
- Body: "[Workout name] is ready."

**Variant C - Equipment**
- Title: "Grab your [equipment]"
- Body: "Today's workout uses [bands/weights/mat]."

---

## Program reminders

**Day X of program**
- Title: "Day [X] of [Program]"
- Body: "[Workout name] - [duration] min."

**Final day of program**
- Title: "Last day"
- Body: "Finish [Program name] today."

---

## Rest day reminders

**Active recovery**
- Title: "Rest day"
- Body: "Light stretching today. 10 minutes."

**Complete rest**
- Title: "Recovery day"
- Body: "No workout today. Rest up for tomorrow."

---

## Weekly schedule overview

**Sunday evening**
- Title: "This week's plan"
- Body: "[X] workouts scheduled. Ready?"

**Monday morning**
- Title: "Week starts"
- Body: "First workout: [Workout name] at [time]."

---

## Smart scheduling

1. Learn user's preferred workout times
2. Suggest optimal times based on completion rates
3. Adjust for time zones and travel
4. Respect calendar integration if available

---

## Skip and reschedule

When user skips:
- Offer reschedule option in notification
- "Can't make it? Tap to reschedule."
- Don't shame, just offer alternatives

---

## Timing rules

- First reminder: 30 min before (dismissible)
- Second reminder: at scheduled time
- No follow-up if dismissed
- If user opens app before reminder, cancel notification
- Never more than 1 workout reminder per 4 hours
