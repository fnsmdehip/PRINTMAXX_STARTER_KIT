# Goal achieved notifications

Fitness apps: WalkToUnlock, FemFit

Sent immediately when user completes their daily goal.

---

## Primary variants

**Variant A - Celebration**
- Title: "Goal crushed"
- Body: "[X] steps done. Nice work."

**Variant B - Simple**
- Title: "Done"
- Body: "Daily goal complete."

**Variant C - Streak**
- Title: "Day [X]"
- Body: "[Steps/workout] done. Streak continues."

---

## WalkToUnlock specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Unlocked"
- Body: "[X] steps walked. Phone's yours."

**Variant B**
- Title: "You earned it"
- Body: "[X] steps done. Enjoy your apps."

---

## FemFit specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Workout complete"
- Body: "[Workout name] done. [X] calories burned."

**Variant B**
- Title: "You showed up"
- Body: "[X] minutes of movement today."

---

## Overachievement variants

**At 150% of goal**
- Title: "Going extra"
- Body: "You passed your goal by [X]%."

**At 200% of goal**
- Title: "Double goal"
- Body: "2x your daily target. Beast mode."

---

## Time-aware variants

**Completed early (before noon)**
- Title: "Already done"
- Body: "Goal hit before lunch. Impressive."

**Completed late (after 8 PM)**
- Title: "Finished"
- Body: "Late but you did it. That counts."

---

## Weekly milestone

**7 days in a row**
- Title: "Perfect week"
- Body: "7 goals in 7 days."

**Monthly milestone**
- Title: "30 for 30"
- Body: "Every day this month. Incredible."

---

## Sound and vibration

- Use celebratory sound
- Haptic feedback on iOS
- Consider confetti animation when user opens app

---

## Follow-up

- Don't send any more notifications that day
- Badge count: 0 (goal complete = clean slate)
- Next notification: tomorrow's reminder
