# Streak celebration notifications

Fitness apps: WalkToUnlock, FemFit

Sent when user hits streak milestones.

---

## Milestone thresholds

- 3 days (first milestone, low bar)
- 7 days (1 week)
- 14 days (2 weeks)
- 21 days (habit formed)
- 30 days (1 month)
- 60 days (2 months)
- 90 days (3 months)
- 180 days (6 months)
- 365 days (1 year)

---

## 3 day milestone

**Variant A**
- Title: "3 days"
- Body: "You're building momentum."

**Variant B**
- Title: "First milestone"
- Body: "3 days in a row. Keep it going."

## Fitness-specific messaging

**WalkToUnlock**
- Include total steps walked in streak period
- "You've walked [X] miles in [Y] days."

**FemFit**
- Include total workout minutes
- "You've worked out [X] hours this month."

---

## Sharing prompts

After 30+ day milestones, offer share option:
- Generate shareable image with stats
- "Share your streak" button in-app
- Don't push sharing via notification

---

## Gamification tie-in

- Unlock achievement badge
- Award bonus points/coins if app has them
- Unlock special workout or feature at milestones
