# Session complete notifications

Productivity apps: StudyLock, PromptVault

Sent when a focus session ends.

---

## Primary variants

**Variant A - Simple**
- Title: "Session done"
- Body: "[X] minutes complete."

**Variant B - Celebration**
- Title: "Nice work"
- Body: "[X] minutes of focused time."

**Variant C - Stats**
- Title: "Done"
- Body: "[X] min today. [Y] min this week."

---

## StudyLock specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Apps unlocked"
- Body: "[X] minutes of study complete."

**Variant B**
- Title: "Break time"
- Body: "[X] minute session done. Take 5."

**Variant C - Streak**
- Title: "Day [X]"
- Body: "[Y] minutes studied. Streak continues."

---

## PromptVault specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Session logged"
- Body: "[X] minutes of prompt work done."

**Variant B**
- Title: "Progress saved"
- Body: "Your work is auto-saved."

---

## Break suggestions

**After 25 min session (Pomodoro)**
- Title: "Take 5"
- Body: "Session done. Short break, then continue?"

**After 50 min session**
- Title: "Break time"
- Body: "10 minute break. Stretch, hydrate."

**After 90 min session**
- Title: "Long session done"
- Body: "Great work. Take 15-20 min off."

---

## Streak and milestone

**Daily goal reached**
- Title: "Goal hit"
- Body: "[X] minutes today. Daily target complete."

**Weekly milestone**
- Title: "Weekly goal"
- Body: "[X] hours this week. On track."

---

## Follow-up prompts

**Option to continue**
- Action button: "Start another"
- Action button: "Done for now"

**Tomorrow's session**
- "Same time tomorrow?"
- Quick-schedule option

---

## Sound and haptics

- Gentle chime (not jarring after focus)
- Soft haptic
- No badge increment (session complete is positive)

---

## Timing

- Send exactly when session timer ends
- If user ended early, adjust message: "Session ended. [X] of [Y] minutes complete."
- No follow-up notifications for 30 min (respect break time)
