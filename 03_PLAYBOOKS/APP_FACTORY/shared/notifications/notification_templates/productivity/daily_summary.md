# Daily summary notifications

Productivity apps: StudyLock, PromptVault

End-of-day summary of productivity stats.

---

## Primary variants

**Variant A - Stats**
- Title: "Today's focus"
- Body: "[X] hours, [Y] sessions."

**Variant B - Comparison**
- Title: "Today: [X] min"
- Body: "[+/-Y] from yesterday."

**Variant C - Simple**
- Title: "Day complete"
- Body: "[X] minutes of focused work."

---

## StudyLock specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Study recap"
- Body: "[X] hours studied. [Y] breaks taken."

**Variant B**
- Title: "You studied [X] hours"
- Body: "That's [Y] more than average."

**Variant C - Subject breakdown**
- Title: "Today's study"
- Body: "[Subject 1]: [X] min. [Subject 2]: [Y] min."

---

## PromptVault specific

**Variant A**
- Title: "Prompts created: [X]"
- Body: "[Y] new prompts today."

**Variant B**
- Title: "Prompt stats"
- Body: "[X] created, [Y] tested, [Z] saved."

---

## Comparative messaging

**Better than yesterday**
- Title: "[X]% more"
- Body: "You focused [Y] more minutes than yesterday."

**Same as yesterday**
- Title: "Consistent"
- Body: "[X] minutes again today."

**Less than yesterday**
- Title: "[X] minutes"
- Body: "Lighter day. That's okay."

---

## Weekly summary (Sunday)

**Variant A**
- Title: "Week in review"
- Body: "[X] hours across [Y] sessions."

**Variant B**
- Title: "This week"
- Body: "Best day: [Day]. Total: [X] hours."

---

## Monthly milestone

**End of month**
- Title: "[Month] complete"
- Body: "[X] hours of focus. [Y] sessions."

---

## Timing

- Send at 8 PM or 9 PM local time
- Skip if user had 0 sessions (don't rub it in)
- Optional: morning summary of previous day instead
- Respect "summary notifications" preference

---

## Progressive detail

- If user ignores summaries, simplify to weekly only
- If user engages with summaries, add more detail
- Track open rate to optimize timing
