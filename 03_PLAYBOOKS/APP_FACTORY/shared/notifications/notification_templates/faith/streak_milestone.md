# Streak milestone notifications

Faith apps: PrayerLock, DailyAnchor, DailyDevotion

Sent immediately after user completes an activity that hits a milestone.

---

## Milestone thresholds

- 7 days (1 week)
- 14 days (2 weeks)
- 21 days (3 weeks, habit forming)
- 30 days (1 month)
- 50 days
- 100 days
- 365 days (1 year)
