# New content notifications

Faith apps: PrayerLock, DailyAnchor, DailyDevotion

Sent when new devotions, prayers, or content series are available.

---

## Daily content (automatic)

**Variant A**
- Title: "Today's verse"
- Body: "Fresh content ready."

**Variant B**
- Title: "New reading"
- Body: "Your daily devotion just dropped."

---

## New series launch

**Variant A**
- Title: "New series: [Title]"
- Body: "[X] days on [topic]. Start today."

**Variant B**
- Title: "[Title] is live"
- Body: "New [X]-day series available now."

**Variant C**
- Title: "Just released"
- Body: "[Title]: a [X]-day series on [topic]."

---

## Seasonal content

**Advent**
- Title: "Advent begins"
- Body: "25 days of reflection. Start now."

**Lent**
- Title: "Lent starts tomorrow"
- Body: "40 days. We'll do it together."

**Easter**
- Title: "Easter week"
- Body: "Special readings for Holy Week."

---

## Author/contributor content

**When featuring specific author**
- Title: "[Author name]"
- Body: "New devotions from [Author]. Available now."

---

## Timing rules

1. Daily content: Don't send separate push, use morning reminder
2. New series: Send once at launch, at 10 AM
3. Seasonal: Send 1 day before season starts
4. Never more than 1 content notification per week
5. Respect user's new content preference setting

---

## Segmentation

- Users who finished a series: notify about similar new series
- Users who started but didn't finish: don't notify about new (focus on completion)
- Inactive users: use new content as re-engagement hook
