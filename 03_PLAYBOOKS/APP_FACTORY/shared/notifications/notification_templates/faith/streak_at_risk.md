# Streak at risk notifications

Faith apps: PrayerLock, DailyAnchor, DailyDevotion

Sent when user hasn't opened the app today and it's past 6 PM.

---

## Primary variants

**Variant A - Direct**
- Title: "[X] days"
- Body: "Don't let it reset. 2 minutes is enough."

**Variant B - Supportive**
- Title: "Still time"
- Body: "Your [X]-day streak is waiting."

**Variant C - Low pressure**
- Title: "Quick check-in?"
- Body: "Even 1 minute counts."

---

## Secondary variants

**Variant D - Challenge**
- Title: "Day [X+1]?"
- Body: "Or back to day 1. Your call."

**Variant E - Encouraging**
- Title: "You've built something"
- Body: "[X] days of consistency. Keep it going."

**Variant F - Simple reminder**
- Title: "Streak check"
- Body: "[X] days. Opens in 30 seconds."

---

## Milestone-aware variants

**At 7 days**
- Title: "One week"
- Body: "7 days straight. Worth protecting."

**At 21 days**
- Title: "3 weeks strong"
- Body: "Habit forming. Don't stop now."

**At 30 days**
- Title: "30 days"
- Body: "A full month. Finish today."

**At 100 days**
- Title: "100 days"
- Body: "Triple digits. This matters."

---

## Timing

- First reminder: 6 PM local time
- Second reminder: 9 PM if not opened
- No third reminder (respect boundaries)
- Never send past 10 PM

---

## Rules

1. Only send if user has active streak > 0
2. Cap at 2 notifications per day
3. If user ignores 3 days in a row, reduce frequency
4. Re-engage differently after streak breaks (see re-engagement template)
