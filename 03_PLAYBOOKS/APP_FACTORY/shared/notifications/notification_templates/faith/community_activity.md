# Community activity notifications

Faith apps: PrayerLock, DailyAnchor, DailyDevotion

For apps with community features (prayer groups, shared reflections).

---

## Prayer request responses

**Someone prayed for user's request**
- Title: "[Name] prayed for you"
- Body: "Tap to see their message."

**Multiple prayers**
- Title: "[X] people prayed"
- Body: "Your prayer request got support."

---

## Group activity

**New member joined group**
- Title: "New member"
- Body: "[Name] joined [Group name]."

**Activity in group**
- Title: "[Group name]"
- Body: "[Name] shared a reflection."

---

## Mentions and replies

**Someone replied to user's reflection**
- Title: "[Name] replied"
- Body: "[First 30 chars of reply]..."

**Someone mentioned user**
- Title: "[Name] mentioned you"
- Body: "In [Group/Context]"

---

## Weekly digests

**Weekly group summary**
- Title: "This week in [Group name]"
- Body: "[X] reflections, [Y] prayers. Catch up."

**Personal impact**
- Title: "Your week"
- Body: "You prayed for [X] people this week."

---

## Timing and frequency

1. Individual notifications: real-time during waking hours
2. Batch if > 3 notifications would fire in 1 hour
3. Digest: Sunday 7 PM
4. Quiet hours: no community notifications 10 PM - 7 AM
5. Max 5 community notifications per day

---

## Privacy settings

Respect these user preferences:
- Notify when someone prays for me: on/off
- Notify about group activity: on/off
- Notify about mentions only: option
- Weekly digest only: option (batch all community)

---

## Anti-spam rules

1. Same person's activity: max 1 notification per day
2. Same group: max 3 notifications per day
3. If user hasn't opened in 7 days: reduce to digest only
4. If user ignores 10 community notifications: suggest turning off
