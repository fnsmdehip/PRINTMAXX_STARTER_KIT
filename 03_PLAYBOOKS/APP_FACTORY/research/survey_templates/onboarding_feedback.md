# Onboarding Feedback Survey Template

Improving the first-time user experience.

**When to trigger:** Day 3-7 after signup
**Target responses:** 30+ per month
**Goal:** Find and fix onboarding friction

---

## Survey structure

### Opening

> Quick feedback on your first few days with [product]? Takes 2 minutes.

---

### Question 1: Initial success

> Have you been able to [core action/value prop] with [product] yet?

- Yes, multiple times
- Yes, once
- I tried but couldn't figure it out
- Not yet, but I plan to
- Not interested in that feature

**Why this matters:** Measures activation rate and blockers.

---

### Question 2: Time to value

> How long did it take you to get value from [product]?

- Within the first session
- Within the first day
- Within the first week
- I haven't gotten value yet
- I'm not sure

---

### Question 3: Ease of getting started

> How easy was it to get started with [product]?

- Very easy
- Somewhat easy
- Neither easy nor difficult
- Somewhat difficult
- Very difficult

**Follow-up if "difficult":**
> What was the hardest part?

_Open text_

---

### Question 4: Clarity of next steps

> After signing up, was it clear what you should do first?

- Very clear
- Somewhat clear
- Neutral
- Somewhat unclear
- Very unclear

---

### Question 5: Feature discovery

> Which features have you tried? (select all that apply)

- [ ] Feature A
- [ ] Feature B
- [ ] Feature C
- [ ] Feature D
- [ ] Feature E
- [ ] None yet

---

### Question 6: Missing information

> Was there anything confusing or missing in your first experience?

_Open text_

---

### Question 7: Intent to continue

> How likely are you to keep using [product]?

- Definitely will
- Probably will
- Not sure
- Probably won't
- Definitely won't

**Follow-up if "won't":**
> What would change your mind?

_Open text_

---

### Question 8: Setup blockers

> Did you complete the initial setup?

- Yes, fully
- Partially
- No

**Follow-up if "Partially" or "No":**
> What stopped you?

- Too complicated
- Took too long
- Didn't understand instructions
- Technical error
- Ran out of time
- Changed my mind about the product
- Other: ___

---

### Question 9: Expectations vs reality

> Compared to your expectations, [product] is:

- Much better than expected
- Somewhat better than expected
- About what I expected
- Somewhat worse than expected
- Much worse than expected

---

### Question 10: Open feedback

> Any other feedback on your onboarding experience?

_Open text_

## Improvement framework

### Priority matrix

|  | Easy fix | Hard fix |
|--|----------|----------|
| **High frequency blocker** | FIX NOW | PLAN FIX |
| **Low frequency blocker** | QUICK WIN | BACKLOG |

### Common onboarding problems and solutions

| Problem | Signal | Potential fix |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Users don't start setup | Low completion Q8 | Simplify first step |
| Users start but don't finish | High partial completion | Reduce steps, add progress |
| Users finish but don't use core feature | Low activation Q1 | Add guided tutorial |
| Users don't understand value | Low expectations Q9 | Better pre-signup messaging |
| Users find it hard | Low ease scores Q3 | UX improvements |
| Users unsure what to do | Low clarity Q4 | Add checklist/guidance |

## Onboarding benchmarks

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Setup completion | <50% | 50-70% | 70-85% | >85% |
| Day 1 activation | <20% | 20-40% | 40-60% | >60% |
| Week 1 retention | <30% | 30-50% | 50-70% | >70% |
| Ease score (avg) | <3.0 | 3.0-3.5 | 3.5-4.0 | >4.0 |
| Intent to continue | <40% | 40-60% | 60-80% | >80% |

## Survey timing experiments

Test different trigger points:

| Timing | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| Day 1 | Fresh memory | Incomplete experience |
| Day 3 | Balance | Might not have tried much |
| Day 7 | Full picture | Memory fades, some churned |
| After first core action | Relevant | Selection bias |

**Recommendation:** Day 3-7 is usually optimal. Test for your product.

## In-app trigger

Show after:
- User has logged in 2+ times
- AND at least 3 days since signup
- AND hasn't completed survey before
- AND hasn't achieved [activation milestone]

Modal content:
> Quick feedback? Your first few days with [product] - 2 minutes.
>
> [Start Survey] [Remind me later] [Don't ask again]
