## Faith journey

### Where he started (8 months ago)
- Didn't know difference between Old and New Testament
- Had never read the Bible
- Thought all Christians were judgmental
- Skeptical of organized religion

### Where he is now
- Reads Bible occasionally (Genesis, Psalms, some Gospels)
- Prays but feels unsure if doing it "right"
- Has Christian friends but still mostly non-Christian circle
- Lots of questions, few answers
- Worried about being "that guy" who won't shut up about faith

---

## Pain points

### Primary frustrations

1. **Knowledge gap**
   - Friends quote verses he's never heard
   - Doesn't know Bible stories everyone references
   - Feels behind, imposter syndrome

2. **Uncertainty**
   - What does he actually believe?
   - How to reconcile faith with progressive friends?
   - Is he doing this whole thing right?

3. **Identity navigation**
   - Old friends don't get his new faith
   - Christian friends feel different from him
   - Dating is confusing now
   - Doesn't want to be preachy or weird

4. **Information overload**
   - YouTube has 100 different opinions
   - Doesn't know who to trust
   - Theological terms are confusing

## What he actually needs

### Must-have
- Clear starting point (where to begin)
- Foundational knowledge (Bible 101)
- Modern presentation (not cheesy)
- Safe space for doubt and questions

### Nice-to-have
- Community of similar new believers
- Answers to hard questions (science, sexuality, politics)
- Something he could share with skeptical friends

## Decision factors

**What would make him try:**
- Modern design (not "churchy" aesthetic)
- Recommended by someone his age
- Progressive-friendly positioning
- Clear promise: "Christianity 101"

**What would make him stay:**
- Actually learned something
- Didn't feel judged for questions
- Cool enough to mention to friends
- Made Bible make sense

**What would make him leave:**
- Felt preachy or judgmental
- Assumed prior knowledge he didn't have
- Conservative political overtones
- Looked like it was made in 2005

---

## Messaging that resonates

**Yes:**
- "Starting from zero is fine"
- "Your questions are welcome"
- "Faith without the weird"
- "Finally understand what Christians are talking about"

**No:**
- "Deep theological study"
- "Biblical manhood" (coded language)
- "Surrender everything"
- "Join the family of believers"

---

## Design implications

1. **Beginner-first** - Assume no prior knowledge
2. **Modern aesthetic** - Design matters, no clip art
3. **Question-friendly** - Doubt is welcome
4. **Structured path** - Clear progression from start
5. **Jargon-free** - Explain terms, skip Christianese
6. **Bite-sized** - Short lessons that build
7. **Social proof** - Show others on same journey
8. **Sharable** - Content he could send to skeptical friend
