# Product Hunt Launch Guide

Complete playbook for a successful Product Hunt launch.

---

## Is Product Hunt right for your app?

### Good fit:
- B2B or productivity apps
- Developer tools
- Indie/solopreneur products
- Unique or interesting approach
- English-speaking audience

### Less ideal fit:
- Pure consumer apps (fitness, lifestyle)
- Apps only useful to niche audiences
- Products without a web presence
- Apps that require long explanations

### Reality check:
- Product Hunt won't make or break your app
- Top 5 gets ~500-2000 visits, decent press coverage
- Outside top 5 gets ~100-500 visits
- It's one channel, not a magic bullet

## Preparation checklist (T-14 to T-7)

### Product Hunt account

- [ ] Create account (if you don't have one)
- [ ] Start engaging on Product Hunt daily (upvote, comment)
- [ ] Build some reputation before launch
- [ ] Follow relevant makers and hunters

### Find a hunter (optional but helpful)

A "hunter" is someone who posts your product. Benefits:
- Their followers get notified
- Adds credibility
- Experienced hunters know the platform

**How to find a hunter:**
- Check top hunters list on Product Hunt
- Reach out on X/Twitter
- Use networks like "Indie Hackers"
- Many hunters have open DMs

**Message template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've been following your hunts on Product Hunt. [Specific compliment about recent hunt].

I'm launching [App Name] on [date]. It's [one sentence description].

Would you be interested in hunting it?

Happy to provide all assets and answer any questions.

[Your name]
```

Note: Many successful launches are self-hunted. Don't stress if no one responds.

### Assets preparation

**Thumbnail (240x240):**
- App icon or logo
- Clear on white background
- GIF can work but keep it simple

**Gallery images (1200x900 or 1270x760):**
- 5-7 images
- First image is most important (appears in feed)
- Show the product in action
- Use text overlays to explain
- Include pricing/features if relevant

**Gallery video (optional):**
- 1-2 minute max
- Show the product working
- Include audio or captions
- First 10 seconds crucial

**Tagline (60 characters max):**
- Clear value proposition
- Not "The best X" or "Revolutionary Y"
- Good: "Track your habits in 30 seconds daily"
- Bad: "The ultimate habit tracking solution"

**Description (260 characters intro):**
```
[App Name] helps [target audience] [achieve result].

Key features:
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

[Call to action or special offer for PH]
```

**Maker comment (first comment on your launch):**
Write this in advance. Include:
- Why you built it
- Personal story
- What makes it different
- Ask for feedback
- Offer to answer questions

## Launch day strategy

### Timing

**Best day:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
**Avoid:** Monday (crowded), Friday-Sunday (low traffic)
**Time:** 12:01 AM PT (Product Hunt resets at midnight PT)

### Hour-by-hour plan

**12:01 AM PT (Launch):**
- Product goes live
- Post your maker comment immediately
- Share to X/Twitter, Discord, Slack communities
- Don't ask for upvotes (against PH rules)

**6-9 AM PT:**
- Peak traffic begins
- Engage with every comment
- Share updates to social media
- Send email to your list (mention PH link)

**12-2 PM PT:**
- Second traffic peak
- More comments and questions
- Continue engaging
- Share milestone if doing well

**5-8 PM PT:**
- Final push
- Last round of social shares
- Thank everyone who engaged
- Rankings start to solidify

**12:00 AM PT (Day ends):**
- Final ranking locked
- Top 5 get "Product of the Day" badge
- Top product gets featured email

## Engagement rules

### Do:

1. **Respond to every comment** - Fast, helpful, genuine
2. **Ask questions back** - "What feature would you want next?"
3. **Share authentically** - "We just launched on Product Hunt" (not "upvote us")
4. **Thank supporters** - Personal thank yous go a long way
5. **Provide value** - Answer questions thoroughly, share insights

### Don't:

1. **Ask for upvotes** - Against PH rules, can get you removed
2. **Use upvote exchange groups** - Detectable, ruins credibility
3. **Spam communities** - Will backfire
4. **Argue with criticism** - Thank them, learn from it
5. **Disappear after launch** - Stay engaged all day

## Outreach on launch day

### Acceptable outreach

**Email to your list:**
```
Subject: We just launched on Product Hunt

Hey [Name],

[App Name] is live on Product Hunt today!

[Link to Product Hunt page]

If you have a PH account, we'd love your support. Check it out and let us know what you think.

Thanks for being part of this journey.

[Your name]
```

**X/Twitter post:**
```
[App Name] is live on Product Hunt today!

[What it does in one line]

Check it out and let me know what you think:
[Link]
```

**DM to supporters (who asked to be notified):**
```
Hey! Remember when I mentioned [App Name]? It's live on Product Hunt today. Would love your thoughts if you get a chance to check it out: [link]
```

### Not acceptable

- Mass messaging people who didn't ask
- "Please upvote" messages
- Joining PH upvote groups
- Buying upvotes
- Creating fake accounts

## Maker comment template

Post this immediately after launch:

```
Hey Product Hunt!

I'm [Name], the maker of [App Name].

**Why I built this:**
[2-3 sentences about the problem you experienced personally]

**What it does:**
[App Name] helps you [main benefit]. Specifically:
- [Feature 1 with benefit]
- [Feature 2 with benefit]
- [Feature 3 with benefit]

**For Product Hunt:**
[Special offer - discount, extended trial, exclusive feature]

**What's next:**
Currently working on [upcoming feature]. Would love to hear what you'd want to see.

Happy to answer any questions!
```

## Community engagement

### Where to share (appropriately)

**High-signal:**
- Your X/Twitter
- Your LinkedIn
- Your email list
- Discord communities you're active in
- Slack workspaces you're part of
- Indie Hackers (if you're a member)

**Medium-signal:**
- Relevant subreddits (follow their rules)
- Facebook groups (if genuinely relevant)
- Hacker News (if it fits)

**Avoid:**
- Random subreddits you've never posted in
- Mass DMs to strangers
- Automated posting
- Any "upvote for upvote" schemes

## Post-launch follow-up

### Same day

- [ ] Thank top commenters on PH
- [ ] Screenshot final ranking
- [ ] Share results on social media
- [ ] Send thank you email to supporters

### Next day

- [ ] Write launch retrospective
- [ ] Follow up with journalists who commented
- [ ] Connect with other makers who supported
- [ ] Analyze traffic and conversion data

### Week after

- [ ] Publish "What I learned launching on Product Hunt" post
- [ ] Reach out to top supporters for testimonials
- [ ] Update product based on PH feedback
- [ ] Continue engaging on Product Hunt (builds credibility for next launch)

## Realistic expectations

### Top 5 Product of the Day

- 1,000-5,000 website visits
- 50-500 signups (depending on product)
- Press coverage opportunities
- Long-term SEO benefit from backlink

### Top 10

- 500-2,000 website visits
- 20-200 signups
- Some press attention
- Good badge for marketing

### Top 20

- 100-500 website visits
- 10-50 signups
- Learning experience
- Still a successful launch

### Factors that help

1. Strong product-market fit
2. Good visuals and copy
3. Active maker engagement
4. Existing audience/reputation
5. Timing (less competitive day)
6. Genuine community engagement

### Factors that don't matter as much

1. Having a famous hunter
2. Launching at exactly 12:01 AM
3. Perfect tagline
4. Expensive video production

## Launch day checklist

### Before midnight PT

- [ ] Product page complete and reviewed
- [ ] All assets uploaded
- [ ] Maker comment drafted
- [ ] Email to list scheduled (for 6 AM PT)
- [ ] Social posts drafted
- [ ] Support channels ready

### At launch (12:01 AM PT)

- [ ] Verify product is live
- [ ] Post maker comment
- [ ] Share to X/Twitter
- [ ] DM close supporters

### Throughout the day

- [ ] Respond to every comment (within 1 hour)
- [ ] Share updates on social media
- [ ] Send email to list
- [ ] Thank supporters
- [ ] Note feedback for product roadmap

### End of day

- [ ] Final thank you post
- [ ] Screenshot ranking
- [ ] Plan follow-up content
- [ ] Celebrate (regardless of ranking)
