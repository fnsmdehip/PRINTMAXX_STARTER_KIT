## Gathering testimonials

### Where to find them

1. App Store reviews (screenshot and quote)
2. Reply emails from users
3. DMs on social media
4. Beta tester feedback
5. Community posts (Reddit, Discord)

### How to ask

Send this to active users:

```
Subject: Quick favor?

Hey [Name],

Thanks for using [App Name] this week.

Quick favor: Could you share what you think of it in 1-2 sentences?

I'd love to share your feedback with others considering the app.

Just reply to this email.

Thanks!
[Your name]
```

### Testimonial format

Good testimonial includes:
- Specific problem it solved
- Specific outcome or feeling
- Their name and context

Bad testimonial:
- "Great app!" (too vague)
- No name attached
- Sounds like marketing copy

---

## Email settings

- **Send time:** 72 hours after launch (same time of day)
- **Segment:** Everyone minus active subscribers/purchasers
- **Track:** Opens, clicks, replies
- **Goal:** Convert fence-sitters with social proof

---

## Pre-send checklist

- [ ] Testimonials are real (from actual users)
- [ ] Permission to use names (or anonymize)
- [ ] Numbers are accurate and current
- [ ] Links work
- [ ] Different angle than previous emails
- [ ] Not too pushy - focus on proof, not hard sell
