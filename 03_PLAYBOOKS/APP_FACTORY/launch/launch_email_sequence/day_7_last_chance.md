# Day 7 Last Chance Email

Send 7 days after launch (T+7d). Final push before launch period ends.

---

## Email details

**From:** [Your name] <hello@yourdomain.com>
**To:** Non-purchasers, non-downloaders, trial users who haven't converted
**Subject options:**
- "Last day for [launch special]"
- "[App Name] launch week ends tonight"
- "Final reminder: [discount/bonus]"
- "Closing the launch special at midnight"

---

## Email body

### Version A (Deadline-focused)

```
Subject: [Launch special] ends tonight

[First name],

Quick heads up: The [App Name] launch special ends at midnight.

After tonight:
- Price goes from $[X] to $[Y]
- Lifetime deal no longer available
- [Other scarcity element]

If you've been on the fence, this is the moment.

Last chance: [link]

[Your name]

P.S. If you already purchased, thank you. Ignore this email.
```

### Version B (Recap + urgency)

```
Subject: Launch week recap (and a deadline)

[First name],

7 days ago [App Name] launched. Quick recap:

- [X] downloads
- [Y] star average rating
- [Z] feature updates shipped (yes, in 7 days)

What's next: Regular price kicks in tomorrow.

Launch special ends tonight at midnight:
- [Special offer details]

Don't miss it: [link]

Thanks for following along this week. More updates coming soon.

[Your name]
```

### Version C (For trial users)

```
Subject: Your [App Name] trial ends soon

[First name],

You started a [App Name] trial [X] days ago.

Just checking in: Did you get a chance to [main value action]?

If you found it useful, here's a deal:

Launch special (ends tonight):
- [Special offer]
- Save $[X] vs regular price

Upgrade here: [link]

If you didn't find it useful, I'd love to know why. Just reply.

Either way, thanks for trying it.

[Your name]
```

### Version D (Honest, no-pressure)

```
Subject: Last email about this, promise

[First name],

This is the last email I'll send about the [App Name] launch.

If you haven't downloaded it yet, no pressure. It'll still be there next month.

But if you were waiting for a reason to try it:
- Launch special ends tonight
- [X] people are using it
- It takes 2 minutes to see if it's useful

Your call: [link]

Back to regular content next week.

[Your name]
```

## Timing and segments

### Send schedule

- **Morning send (9 AM):** First reminder
- **Evening send (6 PM):** Final reminder (optional, to non-openers only)

### Segments

| Segment | Email version |
|---------|---------------|
| Never downloaded | Version A or B |
| Downloaded, no purchase | Version C |
| Opened emails, never clicked | Version D |
| Already purchased | Skip |

---

## Creating real urgency

### Legitimate scarcity

Do:
- Time-limited discount
- Launch-only bonus (extra months, lifetime deal)
- Early adopter pricing
- Limited spots for 1:1 onboarding

Don't:
- Fake countdown timers that reset
- "Only 3 left" when there's unlimited inventory
- Extending deadline repeatedly

### After deadline passes

Actually raise the price or remove the offer. If you extend, you lose credibility.

## Email settings

- **Send time:** 9 AM + optional 6 PM reminder
- **Segment:** Non-purchasers only
- **Track:** Opens, clicks, purchases
- **Goal:** Convert remaining interested users

---

## Pre-send checklist

- [ ] Deadline is real and will be enforced
- [ ] Price change is configured and ready
- [ ] Segment excludes existing customers
- [ ] Links go to correct checkout
- [ ] Subject line creates urgency without being spammy
- [ ] Body is honest about what's ending

---

## Post-launch email strategy

After launch week, shift to:

1. **Value emails** - Tips, how-tos, use cases
2. **Update emails** - New features, improvements
3. **Engagement emails** - Surveys, feedback requests
4. **Re-engagement** - For inactive users

Frequency: 1-2 emails per week max

## Full launch sequence recap

| Day | Email | Goal |
|-----|-------|------|
| 0 | Announcement | Awareness + first downloads |
| 1 | Follow-up | Catch non-openers |
| 3 | Social proof | Convert fence-sitters |
| 7 | Last chance | Final conversions |

Expected performance:
- Announcement: 40-50% open, 10-15% click
- Follow-up: 30-40% open, 5-10% click
- Social proof: 25-35% open, 5-8% click
- Last chance: 35-45% open, 8-12% click
