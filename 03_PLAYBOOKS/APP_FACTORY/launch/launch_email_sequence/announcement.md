# Launch Announcement Email

Send at T-0 (launch moment).

---

## Email details

**From:** [Your name] <hello@yourdomain.com>
**To:** Full email list
**Subject options:**
- "[App Name] is live"
- "It's here: [App Name]"
- "[App Name] just launched"
- "You asked for it. I built it."

---

## Email body

### Version A (Short and direct)

```
Subject: [App Name] is live

[First name],

[App Name] is live on the App Store and Google Play.

[One sentence: what the app does]

Download here:
- iOS: [link]
- Android: [link]

Launch week special: [discount/bonus if any]

Thanks for being on this list from the start.

[Your name]

P.S. First 100 downloads get [bonus]. [X] spots left.
```


---

## Email settings

- **Send time:** Match your launch time (usually 9 AM in primary time zone)
- **Segment:** Full list (unless you have a VIP segment to send to first)
- **Track:** Opens, clicks, unsubscribes
- **Test:** Send test to yourself first

---

## Subject line A/B test

If your email platform supports it:

- Version A: "[App Name] is live"
- Version B: "I finally shipped it"
- Version C: "[App Name]: Download now"

Winner criteria: Open rate after 2 hours

---

## Pre-send checklist

- [ ] Links are correct and tracking-enabled
- [ ] App Store and Google Play links work
- [ ] Name personalization working
- [ ] No typos (read it out loud)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present
- [ ] Mobile preview looks good
- [ ] Test email received successfully
