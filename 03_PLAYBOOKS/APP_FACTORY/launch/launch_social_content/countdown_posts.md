# Countdown Posts

Build anticipation in the week before launch.

## Countdown best practices

### Do:

1. **Be authentic** - Share real feelings, not marketing speak
2. **Show progress** - Behind-the-scenes content builds connection
3. **Create anticipation** - Tease features without revealing everything
4. **Engage replies** - Respond to everyone who comments
5. **Build your list** - Point people to landing page for notifications

### Don't:

1. **Oversell** - Save the hype for launch day
2. **Spam** - 1-2 posts per day max during countdown
3. **Be vague** - Give people a reason to care
4. **Ignore engagement** - Pre-launch followers are your day 1 customers
