## Story format ideas

### Instagram/TikTok Stories

1. **Code timelapse** - Screen recording of coding session (sped up)
2. **Bug hunt** - Show the process of finding and fixing a bug
3. **Design iteration** - Before/after of UI changes
4. **Testing session** - Real device testing footage
5. **Crash and fix** - Show crash, show fix, cathartic
6. **Feature celebration** - Mini celebration when something works

### Quick BTS video ideas

- Walking through your workspace
- Morning routine before coding
- The moment something finally works
- Reaction to first review
- Reading beta feedback
- Launch day prep montage

---

## Authenticity guidelines

### Do:

1. Show real struggles, not just wins
2. Share actual screenshots and footage
3. Admit mistakes and pivots
4. Respond to comments with genuine answers
5. Let your personality show

### Don't:

1. Fake failures for engagement
2. Over-polish everything
3. Pretend you have it figured out
4. Ignore negative feedback
5. Be someone you're not
