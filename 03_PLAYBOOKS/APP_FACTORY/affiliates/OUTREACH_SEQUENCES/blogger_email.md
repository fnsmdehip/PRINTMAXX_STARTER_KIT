# Blogger Email Templates

For blog owners who write about your niche. Focus on those already writing affiliate content or comparison posts.

---

## Template 1: The comparison post pitch

Best for: Bloggers with existing "best X" or comparison articles

```
Subject: Add [App Name] to your [topic] roundup?

Hey [Name],

Found your article "[Article Title]" while researching [topic]. Good breakdown.

I built [App Name] - [one-line description]. Might be worth adding to your list.

Quick comparison to others you covered:
- [Key differentiator 1]
- [Key differentiator 2]
- Price: [price point]

Affiliate program: 25% commission, 60-day cookie.

Happy to send you free premium access to test. Let me know if you want me to provide any assets (screenshots, comparison data, etc).

[Your name]
[App Name] founder
```

**Why it works:**
- References their specific content
- Provides value (more content for their article)
- Makes it easy with assets offer

## Template 2: The dedicated review pitch

Best for: Bloggers who do in-depth product reviews

```
Subject: [App Name] review opportunity

Hey [Name],

I'm [Your name], founder of [App Name].

Your review of [competitor] was thorough. Would you be open to reviewing [App Name]?

What I can offer:
- Free lifetime premium access
- 25% affiliate commission
- Custom promo code for your readers
- Any screenshots or assets you need

The app: [2-3 sentence description of what it does and for whom]

If you're interested, I'll send login details. No obligation to publish if it doesn't meet your standards.

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows you read their work
- No-obligation offer reduces friction
- Clear compensation structure

## Template 3: The guest post opportunity

Best for: Bloggers open to guest content

```
Subject: Guest post idea for [Blog Name]

Hey [Name],

I read your post on [topic]. Got an idea for a follow-up that could work well for your audience.

Title: "[Proposed title related to your app's value prop]"

Outline:
- [Section 1]
- [Section 2]
- [Section 3 - natural place to mention your app]
- [Section 4]

I'll write the full thing. You can edit freely. My only ask: include a link to [App Name] (affiliate link so you earn commission too).

Worth exploring?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Provides ready-to-publish value
- Less work for them
- Win-win with affiliate link

## Template 4: The data pitch

Best for: Bloggers who use data/statistics in content

```
Subject: Data for your [niche] content

Hey [Name],

I run [App Name] with [X] users in [niche].

We've got some interesting data your audience might find useful:
- [Stat 1]
- [Stat 2]
- [Stat 3]

Happy to share more details for a post. Could be an original data angle for your content calendar.

In exchange, a mention of [App Name] with your affiliate link would be great.

Interested in the full dataset?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Original data is valuable for SEO
- Creates unique content opportunity
- Natural reason for mention

## Template 5: The direct affiliate pitch

Best for: Bloggers with clear affiliate strategy

```
Subject: Affiliate partnership - [App Name]

Hey [Name],

Saw you promote [similar products] to your audience. [App Name] could be a good fit.

Quick stats:
- EPC: $[X] (what our top affiliates earn per click)
- Conversion rate: [X]%
- Commission: 25% (lifetime for subscriptions)
- Cookie: 60 days

Your content on [topic] would convert well.

Want me to set up your affiliate account? Takes 2 minutes.

[Your name]
[App Name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Leads with numbers (speaks their language)
- Shows you understand affiliate marketing
- Quick ask

## Follow-up sequence

### Day 4 (first follow-up):

```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hey [Name],

Just floating this back up. Let me know if there's interest in [the offer from original email].

If not, no worries at all.

[Your name]
```

### Day 10 (second follow-up):

```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Last note on this -

If [App Name] ever fits into your content calendar, the affiliate offer stands. 25% commission, 60-day cookie.

Here's the affiliate signup if you want to bookmark it: [link]

Good luck with [Blog Name].

[Your name]
```

### Day 30+ (break-up email):

```
Subject: Closing the loop

Hey [Name],

Wanted to close the loop on my [App Name] outreach.

I'll assume the timing isn't right. If that changes, affiliate program is always open: [link]

Keep up the great content.

[Your name]
```

## Finding blogger emails

1. **Contact page:** Most obvious, check first
2. **About page:** Often has email
3. **Footer:** Sometimes buried there
4. **Hunter.io:** Email finder tool
5. **LinkedIn:** Connect and message
6. **Twitter:** DM if they're active there

---

## Qualification criteria

Before emailing, check:
- [ ] Site has real traffic (SimilarWeb estimate)
- [ ] Content updated within 6 months
- [ ] Already has affiliate links (familiar with monetization)
- [ ] Content quality is decent
- [ ] Topics align with your app

Skip if:
- Site looks abandoned
- Content is thin/spammy
- No organic traffic
- No way to contact

## Volume targets

Per app launch:
- Identify 100 relevant blogs
- Send 50 initial emails
- Over 2 weeks

Expected outcomes:
- Response rate: 10-20%
- Conversion to affiliate: 50-70% of responders
- Target: 15-25 blogger affiliates
