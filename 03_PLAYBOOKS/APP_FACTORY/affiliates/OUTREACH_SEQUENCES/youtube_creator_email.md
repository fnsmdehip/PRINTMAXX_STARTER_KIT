# YouTube Creator Email Templates

For YouTube creators in your niche. Focus on channels under 100k subs that are actively looking for partnerships.

---

## Template 1: The dedicated video pitch

Best for: Creators who do product reviews

```
Subject: Sponsored video opportunity - [App Name]

Hey [Name],

Love your channel. Your video on [specific video] was exactly the kind of thorough breakdown I appreciate.

I'm the founder of [App Name] - [one-line description]. Looking for a creator to do an honest review.

What I'm offering:
- $[X] for a dedicated 5-10 min video
- Free lifetime premium access
- 25% affiliate commission on all sales from your link
- Full creative control (I want your honest take)

Your audience of [niche] users would be a great fit.

Interested in discussing?

[Your name]
Founder, [App Name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Combines upfront payment with affiliate
- "Honest take" shows confidence
- References specific content

## Template 2: The integration pitch

Best for: Creators who do tutorials or how-tos

```
Subject: Integration for your [topic] videos

Hey [Name],

Your [topic] tutorials are solid. I've used [specific tip from their video] myself.

Quick pitch: I built [App Name] that helps with [related problem]. Could fit naturally into your workflow content.

Not asking for a dedicated video - just wondering if you'd be open to trying it and mentioning it if it genuinely helps your process.

Deal: Free premium forever + 25% affiliate commission on anyone who signs up.

Want me to send login details?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Lower commitment than dedicated video
- Fits into their existing content style
- Natural integration vs hard sell

## Template 3: The comparison video pitch

Best for: Creators who've reviewed competitors

```
Subject: [Your App] vs [Competitor] video idea

Hey [Name],

Saw your video comparing [Competitor A] and [Competitor B]. Your take on [specific point] was spot-on.

Might be worth adding [App Name] to the comparison. We do things differently:
- [Key differentiator 1]
- [Key differentiator 2]
- [Price comparison]

I can send you free premium access to test. If you think it's worth covering, I'll set up a 25% affiliate link. If not, keep the free access anyway.

Worth trying?

[Your name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Builds on content they've already made
- Easy content idea (just add another option)
- No-obligation offer

## Template 5: The sponsorship package

Best for: Larger channels (50k+ subs) with established rates

```
Subject: Sponsorship inquiry - [App Name]

Hey [Name],

I'm [Your name], founder of [App Name].

Looking to sponsor a video on your channel. What are your current rates for:
1. Dedicated video (5-10 min)
2. Integrated mention (60-90 sec)
3. Shorts/clips

About us: [App Name] is [one-line description]. We've worked with [other creators if applicable].

Also happy to add affiliate commission on top of your rate.

Let me know your media kit or rate card and we can go from there.

[Your name]
[App Name]
[Website]
```

**Why it works:**
- Professional approach for larger creators
- Asks for their terms (shows respect)
- Flexible on format

## Finding YouTube creator emails

1. **About page:** Most reliable - click "View email address" (requires being logged in)
2. **Video description:** Some include business email
3. **Channel banner:** Social links may lead to contact
4. **Their website:** If linked, often has contact
5. **Twitter/Instagram:** Listed in channel, can DM

## Rate expectations by channel size

| Subscribers | Dedicated video | Integration (60-90s) |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1k-10k | $50-200 | $25-75 |
| 10k-50k | $200-1,000 | $75-300 |
| 50k-100k | $1,000-3,000 | $300-800 |
| 100k+ | $3,000+ | $800+ |

Note: Rates vary wildly. Some creators charge 10x these. Always ask for their rates first.

## Negotiation responses

### "That's below my rate"

```
What's your standard rate for a dedicated video?

Happy to discuss. Could also do hybrid: $[lower amount] upfront + 30% affiliate commission. If the video performs, you earn more than your standard rate.
```

### "I only do dedicated videos, not integrations"

```
Dedicated works for me. What's your rate?

And would you be open to affiliate commission on top of the flat fee? 25% on all signups could add up if your audience converts well.
```

### "I need to try it first"

```
Absolutely. Here's free premium access: [link]

Take a week with it. If you think it's worth covering, let's talk details. If not, keep the free access.
```

### "I have a manager/MCN"

```
Great. What's the best way to connect with them?

Happy to send a brief or rate inquiry through official channels.
```

## Video brief template

When a creator agrees, send this:

```
Subject: [App Name] video brief

Hey [Name],

Excited to work together. Here's everything you need:

PRODUCT INFO:
- App name: [App Name]
- What it does: [2-3 sentences]
- Target user: [who it's for]
- Key features: [3-5 bullet points]
- Pricing: [pricing structure]
- Your affiliate link: [link]
- Your promo code: [CODE] (gives their viewers [discount])

TALKING POINTS (optional, use what feels natural):
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

REQUIRED:
- Include affiliate disclosure (verbal or checkbox)
- Link in description with disclosure
- No false claims about results

ASSETS:
- Logo: [link]
- Screenshots: [link]
- B-roll footage: [link]

TIMELINE:
- Draft due: [date] (optional review)
- Publish by: [date]

PAYMENT:
- $[X] upfront (PayPal: [email])
- 25% affiliate commission (tracked via your link)

Questions? Reply to this email.

Thanks!
[Your name]
```

```

Following up on my [App Name] pitch. Any interest in exploring a partnership?

[Your name]

If you ever want to try [App Name] for your own use, here's free premium access: [link]

Keep making great content.

[Your name]
```
