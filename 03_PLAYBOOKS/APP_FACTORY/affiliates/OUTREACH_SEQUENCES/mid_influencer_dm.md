# Mid Influencer DM Templates (10k-100k followers)

For TikTok and Instagram creators with 10k-100k followers. More selective, expect some negotiation, but worth the effort for reach.

---

## Template 1: The value-first approach

Best for: Creators who get lots of DMs

```
Hey [Name],

Short version: I built [App Name]. Your audience would love it.

Offer: 30% commission + $500 bonus if you hit 50 signups in first month.

I know you probably get a lot of these. Happy to send product demo and swipe files if interested. If not, no stress.
```

**Why it works:**
- Respects that they're busy
- Higher commission shows you're serious
- Bonus creates clear target

---

## Template 2: The social proof open

Best for: Creators who care about credibility

```
[Name] -

[Similar creator name] just did [X signups] in a week promoting [App Name].

Looking for a few more creators in [niche] to partner with. Saw your [specific content] and thought you'd be a good fit.

Deal: 30% commission, free premium forever, plus I'll send you the exact content format that's working.

Worth a chat?
```

**Why it works:**
- Shows others are succeeding
- Specific data builds credibility
- Offers proven playbook

---

## Template 3: The collaboration frame

Best for: Creators who see themselves as partners, not promoters

```
Hey [Name],

I'm the founder of [App Name]. Looking for creator partners, not just affiliates.

What that means:
- 30% revenue share (not one-time commission)
- Input on product features
- Co-created content opportunities

Your [specific content type] style would work well with what we're building.

Open to a quick chat?
```

**Why it works:**
- "Partner" vs "affiliate" framing
- Recurring revenue appeal
- Makes them feel valued

---

## Template 4: The exclusivity angle

Best for: Creators who want to stand out

```
[Name],

Launching [App Name] in [niche]. Only working with 10 creators.

Why limited: I want to give each partner real support - custom assets, dedicated affiliate manager, early access to everything.

Looked at 50+ accounts. Yours stood out because [specific reason].

Interested in one of the 10 spots?
```

**Why it works:**
- Exclusivity appeals to bigger creators
- Shows selectivity
- Specific compliment feels genuine

---

## Template 5: The content opportunity frame

Best for: Creators always looking for content ideas

```
Hey [Name],

I've got a content angle that could work really well for your audience.

The hook: [Specific content idea using your app]

I built the app ([App Name]) and can send you the exact setup. If you want to monetize it too, I'll add a 30% affiliate link.

But honestly, even if you just want to try the content format without promoting, I'm down.
```

**Why it works:**
- Leads with value (content idea)
- Low-pressure monetization offer
- Shows flexibility

## Negotiation responses

### "What's your budget?"

```
For mid-sized creators, I typically do:
- 30% commission on all signups
- $200-500 upfront for dedicated video
- OR 35% commission with no upfront

Depends what works better for you. What's your usual setup?
```

### "I need more than 30%"

```
For the right creator, I can do:
- 35% commission OR
- 30% + $X upfront for dedicated content OR
- 40% if you can commit to [X posts/month]

What structure works best for you?
```

### "I want flat fee only"

```
I get it. Brand deals are simpler.

What's your usual rate for a 60-second TikTok?

Could also do hybrid: $[X] upfront + 15% commission. That way you're covered either way.
```

### "I need to try it first"

```
100%. Let me set you up with free premium.

Link: [link]

Try it for a week. If you like it, we'll talk partnership. If not, keep the free access anyway.
```

## Red flags to watch for

**Skip if:**
- Engagement rate under 2% (fake followers likely)
- Only posts sponsored content (audience is ad-fatigued)
- Hasn't posted in 2+ weeks (inactive)
- Comments are all spam/bots
- Wants 4-figure upfront with no performance component

**Negotiate carefully if:**
- Has management (will add fees/friction)
- Wants exclusivity (usually not worth it)
- Very specific posting requirements (limits creativity)

## Follow-up sequence

### Day 3 (if no response):

```
Hey, just floating this back up. Let me know if the affiliate thing interests you or if you'd rather skip it. Either way works.
```

### Day 7 (if no response):

```
Last ping - I'll leave you alone after this.

If you ever want to check out [App Name], here's free premium access: [link]

Good luck with the content.
```

### Never follow up more than twice

Mid-influencers get tons of DMs. If they're not responding, they're not interested. Move on.

## Volume targets

Per app launch:
- Send 100 mid-influencer DMs
- Across TikTok + Instagram
- Over 3 weeks
- 5-10 DMs per day per platform

Expected outcomes:
- Response rate: 5-15%
- Conversion to affiliate: 30-50% of responders
- Target: 10-20 mid-influencer affiliates
