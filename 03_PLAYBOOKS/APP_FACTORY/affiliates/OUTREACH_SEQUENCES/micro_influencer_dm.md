# Micro Influencer DM Templates (1k-10k followers)

For TikTok and Instagram creators with 1k-10k followers. These creators are responsive, affordable, and hungry for opportunities.

---

## Template 1: The genuine fan

Best for: Creators whose content you actually like

```
Hey [Name]!

Saw your [specific video/post about X]. The part where you [specific detail] was actually useful.

I'm building [App Name], a [one-line description]. Feels like it'd fit your audience.

Want free lifetime access + 20% commission on anyone who signs up through you?

No pressure either way.
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific reference proves you watched their content
- Low-pressure ask
- Clear value (free access + commission)

---

## Template 2: The problem solver

Best for: Creators who post about problems your app solves

```
Hey [Name],

Your post about [problem they mentioned] hit home.

Built [App Name] specifically for that. [One sentence about how it solves the problem].

Down to try it free and share your honest take if it helps? Also pay 20% commission if you want to share your link.

Let me know.
```

**Why it works:**
- Connects to their expressed pain point
- Positions as solution, not sales pitch
- "Honest take" feels authentic

---

## Template 3: The competitor upgrade

Best for: Creators who've posted about similar apps

```
Hey [Name],

Noticed you posted about [competitor app].

We built [App Name] - similar idea but [key differentiator]. Some people are switching.

Want to try it and compare? Free access for you. If you end up liking it more, I'll set you up with a 20% affiliate link.

Either way, curious what you think.
```

**Why it works:**
- Meets them where they already are
- Non-threatening comparison offer
- Shows confidence in product

---

## Template 4: The direct approach

Best for: When you want to maximize response rate

```
Hey [Name], quick question:

Do you do paid partnerships or affiliate deals?

I run [App Name] ([one-line description]). Looking for [niche] creators.

20% commission per signup. Free premium access.

If you're interested, I'll send details. If not, no worries.
```

**Why it works:**
- Gets to the point fast
- Respects their time
- Easy yes/no response

---

## Template 5: The exclusive offer

Best for: Creating urgency

```
[Name] -

Launching [App Name] affiliate program next week. Picking 50 creators for launch team.

Benefits:
- 30% commission (vs 20% later)
- Free lifetime premium
- First access to new features

Your content about [topic] would fit well.

Want a spot? Reply "in" and I'll send the signup link.
```

**Why it works:**
- Scarcity (50 spots)
- Better terms for early adopters
- Simple call to action

## Platform-specific adjustments

### TikTok DMs

- Keep under 100 words (people check on phone)
- Don't use links in first message (may get filtered)
- Follow them first (increases visibility)
- DM during their active hours (check when they post)

### Instagram DMs

- Can be slightly longer
- Story replies get higher open rates
- React to their story first, then DM
- Links work but keep first message link-free

## Response handling

### If they say "Tell me more":

```
Cool! Here's the quick version:

[App Name] is [one sentence description].

As an affiliate:
- You get 20% of every signup through your link
- Free lifetime premium access
- I'll send swipe files (captions, hooks, video ideas)

Takes 2 mins to sign up: [link]

Any questions?
```

### If they ask about the product:

```
[App Name] helps [target user] do [main benefit].

Main features:
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

Most users see [specific result] within [timeframe].

Want to try it? I'll set you up with free premium.
```

### If they say "Maybe later":

```
Totally get it. I'll follow up in a few weeks.

If you want to try the app in the meantime (no commitment), here's free access: [link]

Talk soon.
```

## Volume targets

Per app launch:
- Send 200 micro-influencer DMs
- Across TikTok + Instagram
- Over 2 weeks (avoid spam flags)
- 10-20 DMs per day per platform

Expected outcomes:
- Response rate: 10-20%
- Conversion to affiliate: 40-60% of responders
- Target: 25-40 new micro-influencer affiliates
