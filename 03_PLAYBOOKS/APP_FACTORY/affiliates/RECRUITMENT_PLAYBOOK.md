# Affiliate Recruitment Playbook

System for recruiting 100+ affiliates per app. From finding creators to onboarding and compliance.

---

## Finding affiliates by platform

### TikTok

**Search methods:**

1. **Hashtag mining:**
   - Search `#[niche]tips` (e.g., #fitnesstips, #faithtips, #aitips)
   - Search `#[competitor]review` for people already promoting similar apps
   - Filter by posts with 10k-100k views (sweet spot for responsiveness)

2. **Sound mining:**
   - Find viral sounds in your niche
   - Check who's using them with good engagement
   - These creators understand trends

3. **Comment section scouting:**
   - Look at competitor app accounts
   - Find engaged commenters with creator accounts
   - Check their content quality

**Qualification criteria:**
- 1k-100k followers (responsive, not overpriced)
- Posts at least 3x per week
- Engagement rate above 3%
- Content quality matches your app aesthetic
- No obvious bot activity

**Data to collect:**
```
username, follower_count, avg_views, engagement_rate, email_in_bio, niche_fit_score
```

### Instagram

**Search methods:**

1. **Hashtag explorer:**
   - Search niche hashtags
   - Filter "Recent" posts for active creators
   - Check story consistency

2. **Reels tab:**
   - Search niche keywords
   - Find creators with 10k-50k views per reel
   - Check if they respond to comments

3. **Competitor tagged photos:**
   - Go to competitor accounts
   - Check "Tagged" section
   - Find organic advocates

**Qualification criteria:**
- 5k-50k followers
- Regular story activity (shows engagement)
- Reels getting 2x+ follower count in views
- Bio has link tree or email
- Content aesthetic matches your brand

### YouTube

**Search methods:**

1. **Search queries:**
   - "[niche] for beginners"
   - "best [product category] 2026"
   - "[competitor] review"

2. **Channel analysis:**
   - Find channels with 1k-50k subs
   - Check upload consistency
   - Look for channels hungry for sponsorships

3. **Comment section:**
   - Find active commenters on bigger channels
   - Check if they have their own channels
   - Often hungry for opportunities

**Qualification criteria:**
- 1k-50k subscribers
- Regular uploads (at least 2x/month)
- Videos get 500+ views
- Has working email in "About"
- Content quality is decent

### Blogs

**Search methods:**

1. **Google search:**
   - "best [product category]" + check page 2-3 of results
   - "[niche] blog" + affiliate disclosure
   - "[competitor] vs" comparison posts

2. **Ahrefs/SEMrush (if budget):**
   - Find blogs ranking for affiliate keywords
   - Check domain authority 20-50 (reachable)
   - Look at traffic estimates

3. **Pinterest:**
   - Search niche keywords
   - Find pins linking to blogs
   - Check if blogs have affiliate programs

**Qualification criteria:**
- Some organic traffic (check SimilarWeb)
- Content updated within 6 months
- Has existing affiliate links (familiar with monetization)
- Contact page or email available
- Not spammy/thin content

## Outreach templates

### Key principles

1. **Personal first line:** Reference specific content they made
2. **Clear value:** What's in it for them (commission, free product)
3. **Low commitment ask:** Free trial, not "sign this contract"
4. **Easy reply:** Yes/no question, not essay response

### Volume approach

Per app launch:
- Send 200 DMs to micro-influencers (1k-10k)
- Send 100 DMs to mid-influencers (10k-100k)
- Send 50 emails to bloggers
- Send 50 emails to YouTubers

Expected response rate: 5-15%
Expected conversion to affiliate: 30-50% of responders
Target: 100+ affiliates per app

## Incentive structures

### Commission tiers

**Standard tier (everyone):**
- 20% of first payment
- 10% recurring for 12 months
- Minimum payout: $25
- Payment: Monthly via PayPal

**Power tier (10+ conversions/month):**
- 30% of first payment
- 15% recurring for 12 months
- Minimum payout: $10
- Priority support

**Elite tier (50+ conversions/month):**
- 40% of first payment
- 20% recurring for lifetime
- No minimum payout
- Weekly payments
- Direct line to founder

### Bonuses

**Launch bonus:**
- First 50 affiliates get 30% base commission
- Limited time creates urgency

**Volume bonuses:**
- 25 sales in first month: $50 bonus
- 50 sales in first month: $150 bonus
- 100 sales in first month: $500 bonus

**Content bonuses:**
- Post video review: +5% commission for 30 days
- Post comparison article: +5% commission for 30 days
- Feature in email: +5% commission for 30 days

### Non-cash incentives

- Free lifetime premium access
- Early access to new features
- Featured on "Partners" page
- Exclusive swipe files and assets
- Priority customer support for their referrals

## Onboarding process

### Step 1: Application (Day 0)

Affiliate fills out form:
```
- Name
- Platform(s) they create on
- Account handle(s)
- Follower count
- Niche focus
- Why they want to promote (optional)
- PayPal email for payments
```

### Step 2: Review (Day 0-1)

Check for:
- [ ] Real account (not bot farm)
- [ ] Content quality acceptable
- [ ] Niche alignment
- [ ] No red flags (scammy content, fake followers)

Auto-approve if:
- 1k+ followers
- 3+ posts in last month
- No obvious issues

Manual review if:
- Under 1k followers
- Niche seems off
- Account age under 3 months

### Step 3: Welcome (Day 1)

Send onboarding email:
```
Subject: You're in! Here's your affiliate setup

Hey [Name],

You're approved as a [App Name] affiliate.

Your unique link: [link]
Your promo code: [CODE]

Quick start:
1. Download swipe files: [link]
2. Join affiliate Discord: [link]
3. Track your stats: [dashboard link]

Commission: 20% per sale
Paid: Monthly via PayPal

First content tip: [specific suggestion based on their platform]

Questions? Reply to this email.

[Signature]
```

### Step 4: First week check-in (Day 7)

```
Subject: How's it going?

Hey [Name],

Quick check-in. You've been an affiliate for a week.

Your stats:
- Link clicks: [X]

[If sales = 0]: No sales yet, but that's normal. Here are 3 quick content ideas:
  1. [Idea based on their platform]
  2. [Idea based on trending topics]
  3. [Idea using swipe files]

Reply if you have any questions or need custom assets.

[Signature]
```

### Step 5: Monthly reporting (Ongoing)

## Compliance requirements

### FTC disclosure requirements

**Affiliates must disclose:**
- Affiliate relationship with our app
- That they earn commission from recommendations
- Clear and conspicuous placement

**Minimum acceptable disclosure:**

TikTok/Instagram:
- "#ad" or "#affiliate" at start of caption
- OR verbal disclosure in video
- OR code in bio + mention "link in bio"

YouTube:
- Verbal disclosure in first 30 seconds
- OR "Includes paid promotion" checkbox
- AND affiliate disclosure in description

Blog:
- Affiliate disclosure page
- AND disclaimer near affiliate links

**What's NOT acceptable:**
- Disclosure buried after 10 hashtags
- Tiny text at end of video
- No disclosure at all

### Our compliance documentation

We require affiliates to agree:
```
By joining [App Name] Affiliate Program, I agree to:

1. Disclose my affiliate relationship per FTC guidelines
2. Not make false claims about the product
3. Not use fake testimonials or reviews
4. Not spam or use deceptive practices
5. Comply with platform terms of service

I understand violation may result in removal from the program
and forfeiture of unpaid commissions.

[Checkbox] I agree to these terms
```

### What we provide for compliance

1. **Pre-approved disclosure language:**
   - "I'm an affiliate and earn commission if you sign up"
   - "#ad #affiliate"
   - "Paid partnership with [App Name]"

2. **Template with disclosure built in:**
   - All swipe files include disclosure
   - Easy to copy and customize

3. **Documentation:**
   - Affiliate Agreement on file
   - Policy sent to each affiliate
   - Reminder in onboarding

### Our liability protection

We document:
- Affiliate agreement acceptance (timestamp)
- Disclosure policy sent (timestamp)
- Screenshots of compliant posts
- Warnings sent for violations

If FTC inquiry:
- Show we have clear policy
- Show we communicated requirements
- Individual affiliate responsibility
- Ready to increase enforcement

## Tools and tracking

### Affiliate platform options

**Budget option (free):**
- Rewardful (free tier)
- FirstPromoter (free trial)
- Custom spreadsheet tracking

**Growth option ($50-200/mo):**
- Rewardful paid
- Tapfiliate
- PartnerStack

**Scale option ($200+/mo):**
- Impact
- ShareASale
- CJ Affiliate

### Recommended stack

For apps under $10k MRR:
```
Tracking: Rewardful (Stripe integration)
Links: Custom short links (app.link/affiliate)
Dashboard: Rewardful dashboard
Payouts: PayPal mass pay
Communication: Email + Discord
```

### Key metrics to track

Per affiliate:
- Clicks
- Signups (free)
- Conversions (paid)
- Revenue generated
- Commission owed
- Commission paid
- Content posted (count)
- Last active date

Program-wide:
- Total affiliates
- Active affiliates (posted in last 30 days)
- Total revenue from affiliates
- Average revenue per affiliate
- Cost per acquisition via affiliate
- Top performing content types

## Scaling to 100+ affiliates

### Week 1-2: Foundation

- [ ] Set up affiliate tracking (Rewardful)
- [ ] Create swipe files
- [ ] Write outreach templates
- [ ] Build affiliate landing page
- [ ] Set up affiliate Discord

### Week 3-4: Seed round

- [ ] Send 50 micro-influencer DMs
- [ ] Send 25 blogger emails
- [ ] Target: 10-15 initial affiliates
- [ ] Gather feedback on onboarding
- [ ] Refine swipe files based on what works

### Week 5-8: Scale round

- [ ] Send 200+ DMs across platforms
- [ ] Send 100+ emails to bloggers/YouTubers
- [ ] Run small affiliate recruitment ads
- [ ] Target: 50+ total affiliates
- [ ] Identify top performers

### Week 9-12: Optimize

- [ ] Double down on high-performing channels
- [ ] Create case studies from top affiliates
- [ ] Launch referral program (affiliates recruit affiliates)
- [ ] Target: 100+ total affiliates
- [ ] Establish automated nurture sequence

### Ongoing: Maintain

- [ ] Monthly performance emails
- [ ] Quarterly top performer rewards
- [ ] New swipe files each month
- [ ] Platform trend updates
- [ ] Remove inactive affiliates (no activity in 90 days)
