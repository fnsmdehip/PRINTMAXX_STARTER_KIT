# Video Script Swipe Files

Ready-to-film scripts for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Reels. 30-60 second formats that convert.

---

## How to use these scripts

1. Pick a script that matches your style
2. Customize bracketed sections
3. Film it (natural is better than perfect)
4. Add your affiliate link in bio/description
5. Include disclosure (verbal or caption)

**Disclosure options:**
- Say "I'm an affiliate" in the video
- "#ad" at start of caption
- "Link in bio - I earn commission" in caption

## Filming tips

### What works
- Natural lighting (face a window)
- Eye contact with camera
- Authentic energy (not scripted-feeling)
- Good audio (quiet room, or use lapel mic)
- Quick cuts keep attention

### What to avoid
- Reading from script obviously
- Over-produced polish (feels like ad)
- Weak hook (lose viewers in 1 second)
- Too long (under 60 sec performs best)
- No clear CTA

### Equipment (minimal)
- Phone with decent camera
- Ring light or window
- Tripod or stack of books
- Quiet room
- Optional: lapel mic for better audio

### Editing
- Cut dead air
- Add captions (most watch muted)
- Keep it fast-paced
- Use trending sounds if relevant (TikTok)

## Performance tracking

For each video, track:

- Commission earned

Test different:
- Hooks (first 1-3 seconds)
- Lengths (15s vs 30s vs 60s)
- Formats (talking head vs demo vs story)
- Posting times

Double down on what works.
