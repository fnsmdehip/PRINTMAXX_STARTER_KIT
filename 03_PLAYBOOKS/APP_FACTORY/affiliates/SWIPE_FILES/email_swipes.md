## Email best practices for affiliates

### Subject line tips
- Be specific, not clickbaity
- Include a curiosity hook
- Test different approaches
- Avoid spam trigger words

### Structure
- Hook in first 2 lines
- Problem/solution format works
- Specific details beat vague claims
- Clear CTA (one link, repeated if long)
- Disclosure visible but not intrusive

### Timing
- Tuesday-Thursday mornings best
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload)
- Avoid Fridays (weekend mode)
- Test your specific audience

### Compliance
- Must include unsubscribe link
- Must include affiliate disclosure
- Don't make unsubstantiated claims
- Be honest about your experience
