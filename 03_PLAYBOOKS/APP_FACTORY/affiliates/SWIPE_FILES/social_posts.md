## Customization tips

### For TikTok
- Keep first line punchy (hook in 1 second)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Add trending sound if relevant
- Disclosure can be verbal in video

### For Instagram
- Can be slightly longer
- Hashtags at end (3-5 max)
- Stories: Use link sticker
- Reels: Keep it fast-paced

### For Twitter/X
- Shorter versions work better
- Thread for longer content
- Link in reply or last tweet
- "#ad" required somewhere visible

## Disclosure reminder

Every post needs disclosure. Options:
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" at start of caption
- "Paid partnership with [Brand]"
- Verbal: "I'm an affiliate and earn commission"
- "Ad" or "Sponsored" built into video

FTC requires "clear and conspicuous." Don't bury it.
