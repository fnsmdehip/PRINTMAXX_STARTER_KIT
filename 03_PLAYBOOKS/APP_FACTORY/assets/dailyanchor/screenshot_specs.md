## Production Notes

### File Naming
- `dailyanchor_ss_01_hero_6.5.png`
- `dailyanchor_ss_02_habits_6.5.png`
- `dailyanchor_ss_03_verse_6.5.png`
- `dailyanchor_ss_04_streak_6.5.png`
- `dailyanchor_ss_05_journal_6.5.png`

### Export Settings
- Format: PNG (no transparency)
- Color profile: sRGB
- Compression: Optimized (< 500KB each)

### Design Notes
- Target: Christians 25-45
- Aesthetic: Calm, minimal, slightly warm
- Avoid: Overly colorful or childish
- Feel: Peaceful morning, not church-y
