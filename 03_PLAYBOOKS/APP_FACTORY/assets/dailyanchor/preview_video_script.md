# DailyAnchor - App Preview Video Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 portrait)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Format:** H.264, .mov or .mp4
- **Audio:** Soft, peaceful acoustic/ambient

## Background Music Mood

**Style:** Peaceful acoustic, soft ambient, gentle instrumental
**Tempo:** 60-80 BPM (calm, morning pace)
**Instruments:** Acoustic guitar, soft piano, ambient pads
**Avoid:** Worship music (too on-the-nose), drums, vocals

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (search: "peaceful morning", "calm acoustic")
- Artlist (search: "gentle", "meditative")
- YouTube Audio Library

**Track Characteristics:**
- Should feel like a quiet morning
- Not religious music specifically
- Volume: 25-35% (very subtle)

---

## Text Overlay Styling

**Font:** SF Pro Display
**Size:**
- Headlines: 42pt
- Feature callouts: 28pt
**Color:** #F9FAFB (off-white) primary
**Position:** Top third, centered
**Animation:** Gentle fade in, 0.5s duration

## Production Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Configure app with sample habit data
- [ ] Build up streak history for demo
- [ ] Add sample journal entries
- [ ] Set verse to inspiring selection

### Recording
- [ ] Screen record full habit flow
- [ ] Capture streak celebration
- [ ] Record verse card interaction
- [ ] Get clean progress screen
- [ ] 60fps for smooth playback

### Post-Production
- [ ] Assemble scenes
- [ ] Add text overlays (minimal, calm)
- [ ] Add background music
- [ ] Keep pace slow and peaceful
- [ ] Export 1080x1920, H.264

### Quality Check
- [ ] All text readable
- [ ] Habit checkboxes clearly visible
- [ ] Streak numbers prominent
- [ ] Music not overpowering
- [ ] Under 30 seconds
- [ ] Feels peaceful, not rushed

## Tone Guidelines

**The video should feel like:**
- A calm morning moment
- Simple and achievable
- Encouraging without being preachy
- Minimal without being cold

**Avoid:**
- Church service aesthetic
- High-energy motivational style
- Guilt-based messaging
- Complex feature demonstrations
