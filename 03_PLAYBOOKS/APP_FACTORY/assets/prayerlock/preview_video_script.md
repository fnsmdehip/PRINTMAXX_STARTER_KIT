# PrayerLock - App Preview Video Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 portrait for App Store)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Format:** H.264, .mov or .mp4
- **Audio:** Optional background music, no voiceover required for App Store

"Every morning, you plan to pray. But notifications win. With PrayerLock, your apps stay locked until you've spent time with God. Set your devotional. Complete it. Then unlock everything. Download PrayerLock. Pray first, scroll later."

## Background Music Mood

**Style:** Ambient, peaceful, slightly inspirational
**Tempo:** 70-90 BPM (calm, not energetic)
**Instruments:** Soft piano, acoustic guitar, subtle pads
**Avoid:** Drums, electronic beats, corporate motivational music

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (search: "peaceful morning", "meditation ambient")
- Artlist (search: "calm", "inspirational soft")
- YouTube Audio Library (free)

**Specific Track Recommendations:**
- "Morning Light" style ambient
- "New Day" piano instrumental
- Avoid anything with vocals

---

## Text Overlay Styling

**Font:** SF Pro Display (iOS native)
**Size:**
- Headlines: 48pt
- Subtext: 32pt
**Color:** White with 2px black drop shadow (for readability)
**Position:** Center screen, top third
**Animation:** Fade in 0.3s, hold, fade out 0.3s

## Production Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Install PrayerLock on test device
- [ ] Configure 3 blocked apps (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
- [ ] Set prayer timer to 15 minutes for demo
- [ ] Clear all notifications except desired ones
- [ ] Set phone to Do Not Disturb for clean recording

### Recording
- [ ] Use screen recording (iOS built-in or OBS)
- [ ] Record each scene separately for easier editing
- [ ] Capture at 60fps, export at 30fps for smoother slow-mo
- [ ] Test recordings for visibility of UI elements

### Post-Production
- [ ] Assemble scenes in order
- [ ] Add text overlays with timing
- [ ] Add background music (start at low volume, 30%)
- [ ] Add fade transitions between scenes
- [ ] Export at 1080x1920, H.264
- [ ] Test on iPhone to verify quality

### Quality Check
- [ ] All text readable at phone screen size
- [ ] No personal information visible in recordings
- [ ] Audio levels consistent throughout
- [ ] Total duration under 30 seconds
- [ ] Meets App Store preview guidelines

---

## App Store Preview Guidelines

**Apple Requirements:**
- Device-captured footage only (no external cameras)
- Must show actual app functionality
- No "demo" or "simulation" labels required if real
- No pricing in video (show in text below)
- No calls to download from stores other than App Store

**Avoid:**
- Hands/fingers visible (some reviewers reject)
- External footage (real-world scenes)
- Music with lyrics
- Excessive text that can't be read quickly
