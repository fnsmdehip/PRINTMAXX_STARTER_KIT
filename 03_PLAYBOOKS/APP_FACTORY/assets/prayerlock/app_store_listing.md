## Description (4000 chars)

Your phone unlocks after you pray. That's it.

PrayerLock blocks your distracting apps until you complete your morning devotional. Social media, games, YouTube, whatever pulls your attention away from God, stays locked until you've spent time in prayer and scripture.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Pick the apps you want blocked (TikTok, Instagram, games, etc.)
2. Set your devotional routine (prayer timer, Bible reading, or both)
3. Every morning, those apps stay locked until you're done
4. Complete your devotional, apps unlock automatically

No cheating. No shortcuts. Just you and God, first thing.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

You want to pray more. You plan to wake up and spend time with God. But then you grab your phone, check one notification, and suddenly 45 minutes have disappeared into Instagram. Sound familiar?

PrayerLock breaks that cycle. When your distracting apps are literally locked, you can't scroll instead of pray. You have to do the thing you actually want to do.

FEATURES

Prayer Timer
Set 5-60 minutes. Timer counts down. Apps stay locked until it hits zero. Simple.

Scripture Reading
Read today's Bible passage before unlocking. We track scroll position so you can't just skip to the bottom.

Streak Tracking
See your consecutive days of faithfulness. Current streak, longest streak, calendar view. Watch your consistency grow.

Emergency Unlock
Genuine emergency? Type "I am breaking my commitment" to unlock early. It logs the bypass and resets your streak. High friction by design.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Other screen time apps let you set limits, then let you bypass them with one tap. That defeats the purpose.

PrayerLock has no easy bypass. You either complete your devotional or you don't use your locked apps. Your future self will thank you.
