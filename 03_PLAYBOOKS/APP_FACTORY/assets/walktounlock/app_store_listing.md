## Description (4000 chars)

Your apps unlock when you hit your step goal. Until then, they stay locked.

WalkToUnlock connects to your health data and blocks distracting apps until you've walked enough. No steps, no scrolling. Simple as that.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Set your daily step goal (3,000 to 15,000 steps)
2. Pick the apps you want blocked
3. Walk until your HealthKit or Google Fit data shows goal reached
4. Apps unlock automatically when you hit your target

You know you should walk more. You know you scroll too much. Now one fixes the other.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

You have a step goal. Maybe it's 5,000, maybe it's 10,000. Either way, you don't hit it most days. Meanwhile, you spend hours on social media.

What if you couldn't scroll until you walked?

That's WalkToUnlock. Your phone becomes a reward for moving, not a replacement for it.

FEATURES

Step Goal Setting
Choose from 1,000 to 20,000 steps. Pick a preset (3k, 5k, 8k, 10k) or set a custom goal. We show estimated walking time so you know what you're committing to.

Health Data Integration
Connects to Apple Health (iOS) or Google Fit (Android). We read your step count every 5 minutes. When you hit your goal, we unlock your apps automatically.

Progress Display
Big, clear progress ring on the home screen. See exactly how many steps you have left. Pull to refresh for instant updates after a walk.

App Blocking
Select which apps get blocked. Social media, games, streaming, whatever distracts you. Essential apps (Phone, Messages, Maps) stay available.

Automatic Unlock
Hit your goal while walking? We detect it in the background and send you a notification. Open any locked app and it's ready to use. No manual check-in needed.

Streak Tracking
See your consecutive days of goal completion. Current streak, longest streak, calendar view. Watch your consistency build over time.

Emergency Unlock
Real emergency? Type "I am skipping my walk" to unlock early. It resets your streak and logs the bypass. Intentionally hard so you don't overuse it.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Other fitness apps track your steps. That's it. Tracking doesn't change behavior.

WalkToUnlock blocks access to distractions until you move. That changes behavior. Users report walking 2,000+ more steps per day on average after 2 weeks.

- Want their phone habit to motivate fitness, not replace it
- Need a forcing function to get moving
