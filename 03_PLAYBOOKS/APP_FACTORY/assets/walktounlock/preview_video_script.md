# WalkToUnlock - App Preview Video Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 portrait)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Format:** H.264, .mov or .mp4
- **Audio:** Upbeat background music, no voiceover required

---

## 30-Second Storyboard

### Scene 1: The Problem (0:00-0:05)
**Visual:** Person sitting on couch, phone in hand, scrolling social media. Step counter widget shows "1,247 steps" in corner.

**Text Overlay:** "Your step goal: 5,000"
**Text Overlay 2:** "Your actual steps: 1,247"

**Screen Recording:** Social media feed scrolling

**Transition:** Cut to black

---

### Scene 2: The Solution Intro (0:05-0:08)
**Visual:** WalkToUnlock app icon with energetic animation. Teal and green color burst.

**Text Overlay:** "What if you couldn't scroll..."

**Screen Recording:** App icon bounce animation

**Transition:** Slide up

---

### Scene 3: The Lock (0:08-0:13)
**Visual:** User taps Instagram. Lock screen appears showing "3,753 steps remaining to unlock."

**Text Overlay:** "...until you walked?"

**Screen Recording:**
1. Home screen with social media apps
2. Tap Instagram
3. WalkToUnlock lock overlay appears
4. Step counter showing remaining steps

**Transition:** Smooth zoom

---

### Scene 4: The Walk (0:13-0:18)
**Visual:** Progress ring filling up. Steps counting up. Outdoor walking footage optional (or just screen animation).

**Text Overlay:** "Just 50 minutes of walking"

**Screen Recording:**
1. Progress ring at 40%
2. Animate to 60%
3. Animate to 80%
4. Step count increasing: 3,500 -> 4,200 -> 4,800

**Transition:** Speed ramp (fast forward effect)

---

### Scene 5: The Unlock (0:18-0:23)
**Visual:** Goal reached notification. Celebration confetti. Apps now show unlocked.

**Text Overlay:** "Goal hit. Apps unlocked."

**Screen Recording:**
1. Ring hits 100%
2. "5,000 steps" celebration screen
3. Confetti animation
4. "Apps Unlocked" banner
5. Instagram now opens normally

**Transition:** Satisfying pop animation

---

### Scene 6: The Benefit (0:23-0:27)
**Visual:** Streak calendar showing consistent days. Stats showing "2,347 extra steps per day."

**Text Overlay:** "More steps. Less scrolling. Every day."

**Screen Recording:**
1. Streak display: "18 days"
2. Calendar with green checkmarks
3. Weekly stats summary

**Transition:** Fade

---

### Scene 7: Call to Action (0:27-0:30)
**Visual:** App icon, app name, pricing, download button.

**Text Overlay:**
- "WalkToUnlock"
- "3-day free trial"
- App Store badge

**Screen Recording:** None (static graphic)

**Transition:** Fade out

## Voiceover Script (Optional)

**Voice Style:** Energetic, motivational, friendly. Not aggressive gym bro.

**Script:**
"You have a step goal. You're not hitting it. You're scrolling instead. What if your apps stayed locked until you walked? WalkToUnlock blocks distractions until you hit your steps. Walk first, scroll after. Download WalkToUnlock and start moving."

**Duration:** 22 seconds at natural pace

---

## Background Music Mood

**Style:** Upbeat, energetic, motivational
**Tempo:** 120-140 BPM (walking/jogging pace)
**Instruments:** Electronic beats, light percussion, positive synths
**Avoid:** Heavy bass drops, aggressive vocals, generic corporate music

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (search: "walking", "fitness motivation", "positive energy")
- Artlist (search: "exercise", "uplifting electronic")
- YouTube Audio Library (free)

**Track Characteristics:**
- Builds energy through the video
- Peak at unlock celebration
- Not overpowering (music at 40% volume)

---

## Text Overlay Styling

**Font:** SF Pro Display (iOS native)
**Size:**
- Headlines: 48pt
- Stats/Numbers: 56pt (larger for impact)
**Color:** White with subtle green/teal accent on key numbers
**Position:** Center screen, varies by scene
**Animation:** Pop in with slight bounce, hold, fade out

---

## Production Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Install WalkToUnlock on test device
- [ ] Set step goal to 5,000 for demo
- [ ] Configure 4 blocked apps
- [ ] Ensure HealthKit connected and showing steps
- [ ] Plan recording locations (indoors sufficient)

### Recording
- [ ] Use built-in screen recording
- [ ] Record each scene separately
- [ ] Get multiple takes of progress ring animation
- [ ] Capture celebration at actual goal completion
- [ ] Record at 60fps for smooth slow-motion options

### Post-Production
- [ ] Assemble scenes in order
- [ ] Speed up walking/progress sections
- [ ] Add text overlays with precise timing
- [ ] Add background music
- [ ] Sync celebration music beat with unlock moment
- [ ] Export 1080x1920, H.264

### Quality Check
- [ ] All text readable at phone screen size
- [ ] Step numbers clearly visible
- [ ] Progress ring animation smooth
- [ ] Audio levels consistent
- [ ] Total duration under 30 seconds
- [ ] No personal health data visible (use staged numbers)

---

## App Store Preview Guidelines Compliance

**Apple Requirements:**
- Device-captured footage only
- Must show actual app functionality
- No pricing in video

**Tips for Approval:**
- Keep all recordings on-device
- Don't show external fitness equipment
- Step counts can be staged for demo purposes
- Music must be royalty-free or owned
