## Description (4000 chars)

Your apps unlock after you study. No timer bypass. No cheating.

StudyLock blocks your distracting apps until you complete a Pomodoro study session. TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, they all stay locked until you actually study. Then you get a break. Then you can repeat.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Pick the apps that distract you (we suggest TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Discord)
2. Set your study timer (25 minutes default, adjustable 5-60 min)
3. Start a session. Apps are locked until timer completes.
4. No skip button. No bypass. Just study.
5. Timer done? Take a 5-minute break. Apps unlock.

You wanted to study. Now you have to.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

You plan to study. You open your phone to check one thing. Two hours later, you've scrolled through your entire feed and haven't opened a textbook.

StudyLock removes the choice. When distracting apps are locked, you can't scroll instead of study. You do the thing you actually need to do.

FEATURES

Pomodoro Timer
Classic 25-minute work, 5-minute break format. Or customize: anywhere from 5 to 60 minutes of focus time. Timer counts down full-screen so you can't ignore it.

App Blocking
Select which apps get blocked during study sessions. Social media, games, streaming. We pre-select the usual suspects. Essential apps like Messages and Phone stay available.

No Bypass
Other focus apps let you skip timers with one tap. That defeats the purpose. StudyLock has no skip button. You study, or you don't use your locked apps. Simple.

Study Stats
See today's study time, weekly total, sessions completed. Track your improvement over time. Average students using StudyLock increase study time by 47% in the first month.

Streak Tracking
Consecutive days with at least one completed session. Watch your streak grow. Get a warning at 8pm if you haven't studied yet.

Emergency Unlock
Real emergency? Type "I am quitting my study session" to unlock. It logs the quit and counts against your stats. High friction is the point.
