- Resolution: 1284 x 2778 px
- Devices: iPhone 14 Plus, 13 Pro Max, 12 Pro Max

### iPhone 5.5" (Required)
- Resolution: 1242 x 2208 px

## Screenshot 1: Hero/Value Proposition

### Concept
Study timer at center with locked social media apps surrounding it

### Copy
**Headline:** "Study first. Scroll later."
**Subtext:** "Apps unlock after your Pomodoro"

### Visual Elements
- Phone mockup centered
- Large circular timer showing "25:00"
- Locked app icons (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) with lock symbols
- Book/study elements in background (subtle)
- Clean, focused aesthetic

### Background
- Gradient: #6366F1 (indigo) to #4F46E5 (deep indigo)
- Subtle geometric pattern at 5% opacity

### Text Positioning
- Headline: Center, 40px from top safe area
- Subtext: Below headline, 16px margin
- Phone: Centered, bottom edge 100px from bottom

### Typography
- Headline: SF Pro Display Bold, 44pt, #FFFFFF
- Subtext: SF Pro Text Regular, 20pt, #C7D2FE (light indigo)

## Production Notes

### File Naming
- `studylock_ss_01_hero_6.5.png`
- `studylock_ss_02_timer_6.5.png`
- `studylock_ss_03_blocking_6.5.png`
- `studylock_ss_04_stats_6.5.png`
- `studylock_ss_05_streak_6.5.png`

### Export Settings
- Format: PNG (no transparency)
- Color profile: sRGB
- Compression: Optimized (< 500KB each)

### Design Notes
- Target audience: students (18-25)
- Aesthetic: clean, modern, slightly academic
- Avoid: childish elements, overly playful designs
- Feel: serious about results, but not boring
