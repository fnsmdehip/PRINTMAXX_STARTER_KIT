# StudyLock - App Preview Video Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 portrait)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Format:** H.264, .mov or .mp4
- **Audio:** Lo-fi study beats background music

**Script:**
"You were supposed to study. Three hours ago. Every time you try to focus, your phone wins. StudyLock blocks your apps until you complete a 25-minute study session. No skip button. No cheating. You study, then you scroll. Students using StudyLock study 47% more. Download now. Your GPA will thank you."

**Duration:** 26 seconds

## Background Music Mood

**Style:** Lo-fi hip hop, study beats, chill instrumental
**Tempo:** 80-100 BPM (relaxed but not sleepy)
**Instruments:** Soft drums, piano loops, ambient pads
**Avoid:** Lyrics, energetic drops, aggressive sounds

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (search: "lo-fi study", "chill beats")
- Artlist (search: "lo-fi", "study music")
- YouTube Audio Library (search: "lo-fi")

**Track Selection Notes:**
- Should feel familiar to study music playlists
- Students associate lo-fi with studying (positive conditioning)
- Keep at 30-40% volume so text is readable

---

## Text Overlay Styling

**Font:** SF Pro Display
**Size:**
- Headlines: 44pt
- Stats: 52pt (emphasis on numbers)
**Color:** White primary, #A5B4FC (light indigo) for secondary
**Position:** Top third of screen, centered
**Animation:** Fade in 0.3s, subtle slide up on entry

## Target Audience Notes

**Primary viewer:** College student, 18-22
- Relates to scrolling instead of studying
- Knows lo-fi study beats
- Skeptical of productivity apps (needs proof)
- Responds to concrete numbers (47% improvement)
- Cares about GPA and grades

**Tone:** Understanding, not preachy
- "We know you scroll. That's why we built this."
- Not shaming, just honest
- Results-focused messaging
