# FemFit - App Preview Video Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 portrait)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Format:** H.264, .mov or .mp4
- **Audio:** Upbeat pop/electronic, feminine energy

## Background Music Mood

**Style:** Upbeat pop, electronic, feminine energy
**Tempo:** 120-135 BPM (workout pace but not aggressive)
**Vibe:** Confident, fun, empowering
**Avoid:** Aggressive gym music, heavy bass, masculine energy

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (search: "female empowerment", "confidence", "pop workout")
- Artlist (search: "girl power", "upbeat pop")
- YouTube Audio Library

**Track Characteristics:**
- Feels like a playlist you'd actually work out to
- Positive and energetic
- Not cheesy
- Volume: 40-50% (can be a bit louder than other apps)

---

## Text Overlay Styling

**Font:** Nunito Bold (matches app)
**Size:**
- Headlines: 44pt
- Feature text: 28pt
**Color:** #2D2D2D (soft black) on light backgrounds
**Position:** Top third, centered
**Animation:** Playful bounce on entry

## Luna Animation Timing

**Key Luna moments:**
- 0:02 - Wave hello (introduction)
- 0:14 - Small cheer (workout encouragement)
- 0:17 - Big celebration (PR achievement)
- 0:28 - Wave goodbye (closing)

**Luna should feel:**
- Natural, not forced
- Part of the experience, not the whole video
- Cheerful without being obnoxious

## Production Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Configure app with sample workout data
- [ ] Create fake PR scenario for celebration
- [ ] Build streak history
- [ ] Ensure Luna animations are working

### Recording
- [ ] Screen record all workout flows
- [ ] Capture PR celebration cleanly
- [ ] Get Luna wave animation
- [ ] Record at 60fps
- [ ] Test Luna visibility at small sizes

### Post-Production
- [ ] Assemble scenes with energy
- [ ] Add text overlays with bounce
- [ ] Add upbeat music
- [ ] Time Luna celebrations to music beats
- [ ] Export 1080x1920, H.264

### Quality Check
- [ ] Luna clearly visible and cute
- [ ] Exercise names readable
- [ ] Progress charts show improvement
- [ ] Energy feels feminine, not aggressive
- [ ] Under 30 seconds
- [ ] Appeals to women 18-35

---

## Differentiation Notes

**FemFit video should feel different from:**
- Strong app: More personality, softer colors
- Generic fitness apps: Specifically women-focused
- Cute apps: Still shows real workout tracking

**Balance:**
- Luna adds personality (20% of video)
- Actual app functionality (80% of video)
- Don't let cute mascot overshadow utility
