## Description (4000 chars)

Meet Luna, your workout bestie. Track your gym sessions with a fitness app that actually feels good to use.

FemFit is a workout tracker designed for women. Log your sets, track your progress, and let Luna cheer you on. No drill sergeant energy. Just support, encouragement, and a cute cat.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Browse 50+ exercises organized by muscle group
2. Create workouts or use our templates
3. Log your weight and reps during each set
4. Watch Luna celebrate your progress
5. See your strength gains over time

It's Strong, but with better vibes.

MEET LUNA

Luna is a cartoon cat who lives in your workout app. She:
- Waves hello when you open the app
- Cheers you on during your workout
- Celebrates when you hit a PR
- Naps on rest days (she needs her beauty sleep too)

She's encouraging without being annoying. Turn her off in settings if you want (but she'll be sad).
