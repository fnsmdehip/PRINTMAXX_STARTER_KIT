## Luna Mascot Placement

### Rules for Luna in Screenshots
- Luna should appear in 3-4 of 5 screenshots
- Never more than 20% of screen real estate
- Always cheerful/supportive pose
- Positioned in corners or as overlay
- Should enhance, not distract from UI

### Luna Poses to Use
- Screenshot 1: Waving (main introduction)
- Screenshot 3: Cheering small (during workout)
- Screenshot 4: Celebrating (PR achievement)
- Screenshot 5: Sleeping (rest day indicator)

## Production Notes

### File Naming
- `femfit_ss_01_luna_6.5.png`
- `femfit_ss_02_exercises_6.5.png`
- `femfit_ss_03_logging_6.5.png`
- `femfit_ss_04_progress_6.5.png`
- `femfit_ss_05_streak_6.5.png`

### Export Settings
- Format: PNG (no transparency)
- Color profile: sRGB
- Compression: Optimized (< 500KB each)

### Design Notes
- Target: Women 18-35 who gym regularly
- Aesthetic: Soft, feminine, but not childish
- Show actual workout data (realistic weights)
- Luna should feel like a bonus, not the whole product
