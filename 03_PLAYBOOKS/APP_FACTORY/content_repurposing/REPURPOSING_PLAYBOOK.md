# Content Repurposing Playbook

Turn one piece of content into 10+ pieces. Work smarter, not harder.

---

## The 1-to-10 Framework

One long-form piece becomes:

| Source | Output Count | Platforms |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 Blog post (2000 words) | 1 | Website, Medium |
| 1 X thread | 7-10 tweets | X/Twitter |
| 1 LinkedIn post | 1 | LinkedIn |
| 1 Email newsletter | 1 | Email list |
| 3 Quote cards | 3 | IG, X, LinkedIn |
| 1 Carousel | 1 | IG, LinkedIn |
| 1 Short video script | 1 | TikTok, Reels, Shorts |
| 1 Podcast talking points | 1 | Podcast episode |

**Total: 10+ pieces from 1 source**

## Content Pillar Strategy

### What is a Content Pillar?

A content pillar is a core topic you own. All your content branches from 3-5 pillars.

### Example: Solopreneur Niche

| Pillar | Sub-topics | Content Types |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Automation | No-code tools, Zapier, Make, AI automation | Tutorials, tool reviews, case studies |
| Revenue | Pricing, monetization, MRR tracking | Breakdowns, experiments, results |
| Distribution | SEO, social, email, paid ads | How-tos, platform updates, strategies |
| Mindset | Burnout, focus, solo challenges | Personal stories, lessons, frameworks |
| Building | Code, design, MVP, shipping | Build logs, tech stacks, launches |

### Pillar Rotation

Don't post the same pillar 5 days in a row. Rotate:

```
Mon: Automation
Tue: Revenue
Wed: Distribution
Thu: Building
Fri: Mindset
Sat: Best performer repost
Sun: Engagement/community
```

## The Repurposing Workflow

### Phase 1: Create the Source (2-4 hours)

1. Pick pillar topic
2. Research (competitors, trending angles, questions)
3. Write long-form (blog, newsletter, or video script)
4. This is your "content anchor"

### Phase 2: Extract Atomic Pieces (30 min)

From your anchor, pull:
- 3-5 standalone insights
- 2-3 quotable lines
- 1 main framework or process
- Key stats or numbers
- Before/after comparisons

### Phase 3: Transform (1-2 hours)

| Atomic Piece | Transform Into |
|-------------|----------------|
| Main insight | X thread hook |
| Framework | Carousel slides |
| Quote | Quote card graphic |
| Stats | Data visualization |
| Process | Short video script |
| Full piece | Newsletter |

### Phase 4: Schedule (15 min)

Use scheduling tools:
- Buffer (free tier: 3 channels)
- Hypefury (X-focused, $19/mo)
- Later (IG-focused, free tier)
- Notion calendar (manual but free)

### Phase 5: Track Performance (ongoing)

Log in `templates/repurposing_tracker.csv`:
- Source piece
- Derived pieces
- Platform posted
- Engagement metrics
- Best performer

## Platform-Specific Adaptation

### X/Twitter

**Format:** Thread (7-15 tweets) or single tweet with image

**Adaptation rules:**
- Hook must stop scroll in 0.5 seconds
- One idea per tweet
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with clear value or CTA
- Add relevant image to first tweet

**Example transformation:**

*Original blog intro:*
> "I spent 6 months testing automation tools for my content workflow. Most were overcomplicated garbage. Three actually saved me time..."

*X thread hook:*
> "I tested 47 automation tools over 6 months.
>
> 44 were garbage.
>
> 3 changed everything.
>
> Here's the stack that saves me 12 hours/week:"

### LinkedIn

**Format:** Text post (1300 chars ideal) or carousel (PDF)

**Adaptation rules:**
- Professional but not corporate
- First line = hook (shows in preview)
- Use line breaks every 1-2 sentences
- Personal stories perform 2x better
- End with question for engagement

**Example transformation:**

*Same content, LinkedIn version:*
> "I automated my entire content workflow.
>
> It took 6 months of testing.
> 47 tools evaluated.
> Most failed within a week.
>
> Here's what I learned about picking automation tools that actually stick:
>
> [list of insights]
>
> What's the one tool you can't live without?"

### Instagram

**Format:** Carousel (10 slides max), Reel, or Story

**Adaptation rules:**
- First slide = hook + visual interest
- One point per slide
- Swipe motivation on each slide
- Last slide = CTA or summary
- Save captions for context, not content

### TikTok/Reels/Shorts

**Format:** Vertical video, 30-90 seconds

**Adaptation rules:**
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Talk to camera or use text overlays
- Fast cuts keep attention
- End with loop potential or CTA
- Trending audio helps discovery

### YouTube

**Format:** Long-form (8-15 min) or Shorts (60 sec)

**Adaptation rules:**
- Long-form: Expand blog into full tutorial
- First 30 seconds = preview value
- Chapters for navigation
- Shorts: One tip, fast delivery

### Email Newsletter

**Format:** 500-1000 words

**Adaptation rules:**
- Subject line = specific benefit
- TL;DR at top for skimmers
- One primary CTA
- Personal tone, direct address
- PS line for secondary offer

## Automation Opportunities

### What to Automate

| Task | Automation Level | Tool Type |
|------|-----------------|-----------|
| Transcription | Full auto | Whisper, Descript |
| Caption generation | Full auto | CapCut, Descript |
| Scheduling posts | Full auto | Buffer, Hypefury |
| Thumbnail variants | Semi-auto | Canva bulk, AI tools |
| Cross-posting | Semi-auto | Repurpose.io, Zapier |
| Analytics collection | Full auto | Spreadsheet + API |

### What NOT to Automate

- Hook writing (needs human creativity)
- Platform-specific voice adjustments
- Engagement replies
- Trend surfing
- Quality control final review

### Automation Stack (Budget Tiers)

**Free ($0/mo):**
- CapCut (editing, captions)
- Canva free (graphics)
- Buffer free (3 channels)
- Google Sheets (tracking)

**Starter ($50/mo):**
- Descript ($12) - transcription + editing
- Canva Pro ($13) - brand kit, resize
- Hypefury ($19) - X automation

**Pro ($150/mo):**
- Above stack +
- Repurpose.io ($25) - cross-posting
- Opus Clip ($19) - AI clip extraction
- Notion ($10) - content hub
- Later ($25) - IG scheduling

## Measuring Repurposing ROI

### Metrics to Track

| Metric | Good | Great | Excellent |
|--------|------|-------|-----------|
| Pieces per anchor | 5 | 8 | 12+ |
| Time per piece | 30 min | 15 min | 10 min |
| Cross-platform reach | 2x anchor | 5x | 10x |
| Engagement lift | 20% | 50% | 100% |

### ROI Calculation

```
Hours saved = (Pieces created) x (Avg creation time) - (Repurposing time)

Example:
- 10 pieces at 1 hour each = 10 hours traditional
- 1 anchor (3 hours) + repurposing (2 hours) = 5 hours
- Hours saved = 10 - 5 = 5 hours
- ROI = 50% time savings
```
