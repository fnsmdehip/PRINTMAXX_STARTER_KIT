## Church outreach strategy (PrayerLock)

### The opportunity

- 380,000+ churches in the US
- Most don't have tech partnerships
- Pastors want to help congregation with phone addiction
- Built-in distribution channel (announcements, bulletins)

### The offer

**To churches:**
- Free app for entire congregation
- Church gets 20% of subscription revenue from their members
- Custom church branding option (premium tier)
- Usage dashboard for pastors

**Example math:**
- Church has 500 members
- 10% download = 50 users
- 20% convert = 10 paying users
- 10 x $50/yr = $500/yr
- Church gets $100/yr
- You get $400/yr from ONE church

Scale to 100 churches = $40k/yr recurring

### Execution

**Phase 1: VA outreach**

Hire VAs ($5-10/hr) to:
1. Find churches with active social media
2. Find pastor/admin contact email
3. Send personalized outreach
4. Follow up 2x

**Outreach script:**
```
Subject: Free app for [Church Name] congregation

Hi [Pastor Name],

I noticed [Church Name] is active on social media - love what you're doing with [specific thing].

We built PrayerLock, an app that helps people build consistent prayer habits by blocking distracting apps until they complete their morning devotional.

I'd like to offer it free to your congregation, with your church receiving 20% of any premium subscriptions.

Would you have 10 minutes this week to discuss?

[Name]
```

**VA incentive structure:**
- Base: $5/hr for research + outreach
- Bonus: $10 per church that responds positively
- Bonus: $50 per church partnership signed

**Phase 2: Church directory scraping**

Sources:
- Church Finder databases
- Google Maps scraping
- Denomination directories (Catholic, Baptist, etc.)

**Phase 3: Scale**

Once model proven:
- Hire dedicated church partnership manager
- Create affiliate program for pastors
- Sponsor church tech conferences
- Partner with church management software (Planning Center, etc.)

## Gym partnership strategy (WalkToUnlock)

### The opportunity

- 40,000+ gyms in the US
- Gyms want member engagement tools
- Phone addiction = members scrolling instead of working out
- Differentiation for gyms

### The offer

**To gyms:**
- Branded version of app for their members
- Gym gets 15% of subscription revenue
- Member engagement dashboard
- Challenge/leaderboard features

**Execution:**

**Phase 1: Local gyms**

1. Start with 10 local gyms
2. Offer free pilot program
3. Measure member engagement
4. Create case study
5. Scale regionally

**Outreach:**
```
Subject: Member engagement tool for [Gym Name]

Hi [Owner/Manager],

Quick question: do your members scroll their phones between sets?

We built WalkToUnlock - it requires users to hit step goals before unlocking social media. Perfect for gyms.

I'd like to offer [Gym Name] a free pilot:
- Branded app for your members
- 15% of any premium subscriptions
- Engagement dashboard

Worth a quick call?

[Name]
```

**Phase 2: Gym chains**

Target regional chains:
- Planet Fitness franchisees
- Gold's Gym locations
- Boutique fitness (F45, OrangeTheory)

**Phase 3: Corporate wellness**

- Partner with corporate wellness programs
- Offer bulk licensing to employers
- Track "active minutes" for wellness incentives

## School/university strategy (StudyLock)

### The opportunity

- Students are target demographic for screen blockers
- Schools want academic performance tools
- Parents will pay for study aids
- Built-in viral distribution (classmates)

### The offer

**To schools:**
- Discounted bulk licensing
- Teacher dashboard to set study hours
- Parent reports
- Gamified study challenges

**To parents:**
- "Your kid's grades improved 15% with StudyLock"
- Report card integration (future)
- Parental controls

### Execution

**Phase 1: Influencer seeding**

1. Find student influencers (TikTok, YouTube)
2. Offer affiliate deals (30% commission)
3. Create "study with me" content format
4. Viral challenges (#StudyLockChallenge)

**Phase 2: Parent Facebook groups**

1. Find parenting groups (millions of members)
2. Create valuable content about screen time
3. Soft promotion with affiliate links
4. Mom influencer partnerships

**Phase 3: School pilots**

1. Contact school counselors
2. Offer free pilot for struggling students
3. Measure GPA impact
4. Create case studies
5. Pitch to school boards

### Execution

3. Target HR conferences

## Affiliate army strategy (all apps)

### The model

Recruit 100+ micro-affiliates who:
- Create organic-feeling content
- Have affiliate link in bio
- Earn 20-30% commission

### Recruitment

**Where to find affiliates:**
- TikTok creators in niche (1k-50k followers)
- Instagram micro-influencers
- YouTube small creators
- Faith/fitness bloggers

**Outreach:**
```
Subject: Paid collab opportunity

Hey [Name],

Love your content about [topic]. Your [specific post] was great.

We have an affiliate program for [app] - 25% recurring commission.

Interested? I can send details and some content ideas.

[Name]
```

**Incentive tiers:**

| Tier | Monthly sales | Commission |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Bronze | 1-10 | 20% |
| Silver | 11-50 | 25% |
| Gold | 51+ | 30% |

**Bonus structures:**
- $100 bonus for first 10 sales
- $500 bonus for hitting 100 sales
- Leaderboard with monthly prizes

## Referral program strategy (built into apps)

### In-app referral

Every user gets unique referral code.

**Rewards:**
- Referrer: 1 month free premium
- Referee: 1 week extended trial

**Viral mechanics:**
- Share streak achievements (with referral link)
- "Challenge a friend" feature
- Family plans with referral bonus

### Social proof automation

When user hits milestone:
- Prompt to share on social
- Pre-written post with referral link
- Track shares → reward points

## Content creator seeding

### Strategy

## Podcast advertising (performance-based)

### Strategy

Instead of CPM ads, offer performance deals:
- Pay per download/signup
- Typically $5-20 per conversion
- Track with unique URLs or codes

### Target podcasts

**Faith:**
- The Bible Recap
- Pray As You Go
- Daily Grace Co

**Fitness:**
- Mind Pump
- The Fitness Entrepreneur
- BarBend

**Productivity:**
- Deep Questions (Cal Newport)
- Cortex
- Focused

### Pitch template

```
Subject: Performance partnership for [Podcast]

Hi [Host],

Big fan of [Podcast]. Your episode on [topic] was helpful.

We'd love to sponsor - but with a twist. Instead of flat CPM, we pay per conversion:

- $15 per app download
- Unique promo code [PODCASTNAME]
- Monthly reporting and payment

This way you only win if your audience actually finds value.

Interested?

[Name]
```

## A/B test matrix for GTM

| Strategy | Effort | Time to results | Scalability | Test with |
|----------|--------|-----------------|-------------|-----------|
| Church outreach | Medium | 2-4 weeks | HIGH | 50 churches |
| Gym partnerships | Medium | 2-4 weeks | Medium | 10 gyms |
| Affiliate army | Low | 1-2 weeks | HIGH | 20 affiliates |
| School pilots | High | 1-2 months | Medium | 5 schools |
| Podcast ads | Low | 1-2 weeks | Medium | 3 podcasts |
| Creator seeding | Low | 2-4 weeks | Medium | 50 creators |

**Recommended test order:**
1. Affiliate army (fastest to test)
2. Creator seeding (low cost)
3. Church outreach (highest potential)
4. Podcast ads (if budget allows)

- Upwork (more expensive, vetted)

We need help reaching out to churches to partner on a prayer app.

Requirements:
- Excellent English writing
- Experience with email outreach
- Attention to detail
