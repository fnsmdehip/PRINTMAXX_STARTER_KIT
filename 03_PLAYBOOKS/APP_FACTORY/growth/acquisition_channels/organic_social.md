# Organic social: content strategy for app growth

Organic social builds audience before you need them. It's slow, but compounds over time and costs nothing but effort.

---

## Platform selection

### Where to focus

Pick 1-2 platforms. Master them before expanding.

| Platform | Best For | Content Type | Time to Traction |
|----------|----------|--------------|------------------|
| Twitter/X | Tech, productivity, B2B | Short-form, threads | 3-6 months |
| Instagram | Fitness, lifestyle, visual | Photos, Reels, Stories | 6-12 months |
| TikTok | Gen Z, entertainment, trends | Short video | 1-3 months |
| YouTube | Tutorials, reviews, long-form | Video | 6-18 months |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional | Posts, articles | 3-6 months |
| Reddit | Niche communities | Discussion, value-adds | Immediate if done right |

### Platform-app fit

| App Type | Primary Platform | Secondary |
|----------|------------------|-----------|
| Fitness | Instagram, TikTok | YouTube |
| Meditation | Instagram, YouTube | TikTok |
| Productivity | Twitter, LinkedIn | YouTube |
| Faith | Instagram, Facebook | YouTube |
| Finance | Twitter, YouTube | LinkedIn |

## Content pillars

Every account needs 3-5 content pillars. Rotate between them.

### Example pillars for fitness app

1. **Workout tips** - Exercise demonstrations, form corrections
2. **Motivation** - Transformation stories, mindset
3. **Education** - Nutrition basics, recovery science
4. **Behind-the-scenes** - App development, founder journey
5. **Community** - User spotlights, challenge highlights

### Example pillars for prayer app

1. **Daily devotionals** - Short spiritual content
2. **Prayer prompts** - Guided reflection
3. **Testimonials** - User stories
4. **Faith education** - Biblical context
5. **Community** - Prayer requests, group highlights

## Content formats by platform

### Twitter/X

**What works:**
- Single insight tweets (1-2 sentences)
- Threads (5-15 tweets telling a story)
- Hot takes on industry news
- Data/charts (unique insights)
- Personal stories

**Posting frequency:** 3-5x per day

**Template: Insight tweet**
```
[Counterintuitive observation]
[Why it's true]
[What to do about it]
```

**Template: Thread**
```
Tweet 1: Hook + promise
Tweets 2-9: Deliver value (one point each)
Tweet 10: Summary + CTA
```

### Instagram

**What works:**
- Carousels (educational, swipeable)
- Reels (15-60 second videos)
- Stories (behind-the-scenes, polls)
- Static posts (quotes, single images)

**Posting frequency:** 1x feed daily, 5-10x Stories daily

**Template: Carousel**
```
Slide 1: Hook (bold text, visual)
Slides 2-8: One point per slide
Slide 9: Summary
Slide 10: CTA (save/share/follow)
```

**Template: Reel**
```
0-3 sec: Hook (text on screen + voice)
3-45 sec: Deliver value
45-60 sec: CTA
```

### TikTok

**What works:**
- Trend participation with twist
- Educational quick-tips
- Day-in-the-life
- Transformations
- Duets and stitches

**Posting frequency:** 1-3x per day

**Template: Educational**
```
0-2 sec: "Here's why [common belief] is wrong"
2-30 sec: Explanation
30-45 sec: What to do instead
45-60 sec: Results/proof
```

### YouTube

**What works:**
- Tutorials and how-tos
- Reviews and comparisons
- Deep dives on topics
- Shorts (< 60 sec) for discovery
- Consistency over frequency

**Posting frequency:** 1-2x per week (long), daily Shorts

**Template: Tutorial**
```
0-30 sec: Hook + what you'll learn
30-60 sec: Why this matters
1-10 min: Step-by-step content
Last 30 sec: Recap + CTA
```

## Content creation workflow

### Batch creation

Create 1 week of content in one session.

1. **Monday:** Brainstorm topics (30 min)
2. **Tuesday:** Write scripts/copy (2 hours)
3. **Wednesday:** Record/design (2 hours)
4. **Thursday:** Edit and schedule (1 hour)
5. **Friday-Sunday:** Engage with comments

### Repurposing system

One idea becomes multiple pieces:

```
Blog post/long video
  → Thread (Twitter)
  → Carousel (Instagram)
  → Multiple Reels (Instagram/TikTok)
  → Shorts (YouTube)
  → Newsletter section
  → Podcast topic
```

### Tools

**Scheduling:** Buffer, Later, Hootsuite
**Design:** Canva, Figma
**Video:** CapCut, Descript
**Analytics:** Native + Metricool

## Growing followers

### The engagement-first approach

1. Find accounts your audience follows
2. Engage genuinely on their posts (not spam)
3. Add value in comments
4. Some will check your profile
5. Good content converts visitors to followers

**Time allocation:**
- 30% creating content
- 70% engaging with others (especially early)

### Hashtag strategy

**Instagram:**
- Mix of sizes: 3 large (1M+), 5 medium (100K-1M), 5 small (10K-100K)
- Relevant to content, not just popular
- Research competitor hashtags

**TikTok:**
- 3-5 hashtags max
- Include trending sounds/hashtags when relevant
- Niche-specific tags perform better

**Twitter:**
- 0-2 hashtags (less is more)
- Only use when joining conversation

### Collaboration tactics

1. **Duets/Stitches:** Respond to creators in your niche
2. **Quote tweets:** Add value to others' posts
3. **Guest content:** Appear on podcasts, Lives
4. **Shoutouts:** Feature other creators
5. **Challenges:** Start or join community challenges

## Converting followers to users

### Bio optimization

```
[What you do] for [who you help]
[Key benefit or differentiator]
[Social proof if any]
[CTA + link]
```

**Example:**
```
5-minute meditation app for busy people
Used by 100K+ to reduce stress
Download free: [link]
```

### Link strategy

- **Link in bio:** Main app download link
- **Linktree/Beacons:** Multiple destinations
- **Story links:** Feature-specific deep links
- **Swipe-ups:** Specific CTAs per content

### Content-to-download bridge

Don't just say "download my app." Bridge the gap.

**Weak CTA:**
"Download our meditation app"

**Strong CTA:**
"Want the 5-minute routine I showed? It's free in the app. Link in bio."

### Tracking

Use UTM parameters for every link:
```
yourapp.com/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=jan_2024
```

Track:
- Clicks per platform
- Installs per platform
- Retention by source

## Content that converts

### High-converting content types

1. **Problem-solution:** Show pain point + how app solves it
2. **Demo/walkthrough:** Show app in action
3. **Results/transformation:** Before/after from real users
4. **Comparison:** Your app vs alternatives
5. **Tutorial:** "How to do X" (requires your app)

### Low-converting content

- Generic motivation (nice engagement, no installs)
- Pure entertainment (fun but no connection to app)
- Hard sells (audiences tune out)

### The 80/20 rule

- 80% value content (builds audience)
- 20% promotional content (drives installs)

## Metrics to track

### Vanity vs actionable metrics

| Vanity (Monitor) | Actionable (Optimize) |
|------------------|----------------------|
| Follower count | Follower growth rate |
| Likes | Engagement rate |
| Views | Watch time |
| Impressions | Click-through rate |

### Weekly tracking

| Metric | How to Calculate |
|--------|------------------|
| Engagement rate | (likes + comments + shares) / followers |
| Link clicks | Bio link clicks / impressions |
| Follower growth | (New followers - lost) / total |
| Content performance | Top 3 posts by engagement |

### Attribution to app

Track: Social follower → Link click → Install → Activation

## Platform-specific tactics

### Twitter/X quick wins

1. Reply to big accounts in your niche (first hour after posting)
2. Quote tweet with added value
3. Turn your best tweets into threads
4. Pin your best-performing tweet
5. Use Twitter analytics to find best posting times

### Instagram quick wins

1. Post Reels (algorithm favors them)
2. Use all Story features (polls, questions, sliders)
3. Collaborate with similar-sized accounts
4. Engage in DMs with followers
5. Use Instagram's suggested content trends

### TikTok quick wins

1. Use trending sounds (even quietly)
2. Post when your audience is active (analytics)
3. Hook in first 2 seconds (or they scroll)
4. Reply to comments with video
5. Stitch/duet popular content in your niche
