# Growth Playbook for Mobile Apps

Build products that grow themselves. This playbook covers growth loops, viral mechanics, retention-as-growth, and channel strategy.

---

## Growth loops explained

A growth loop is a system where output becomes input. Users create value that attracts more users.

### The three growth loop types

**1. Content loops**
- User creates content
- Content gets indexed/shared
- New users discover content
- New users join and create content

Example: Strava activity posts appear in Google searches. Runner finds route. Downloads app. Posts their own run.

**2. Viral loops**
- User gets value
- User invites others to multiply value
- Invited users join
- New users invite their network

Example: Splitwise. You add a friend to split a bill. They need the app to see what they owe. They add their roommates. Loop continues.

**3. Paid loops**
- User pays
- Revenue funds ads
- Ads acquire new users
- New users pay

Example: Calm spends $3 to acquire user. User pays $70/year. $67 profit funds more ads.

### Building your growth loop

Questions to answer:
1. What action creates value for the user?
2. How does that action expose the product to non-users?
3. What motivates non-users to convert?
4. How fast does the loop complete?

| App Type | Primary Loop | Secondary Loop |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Social fitness | Content (activities) | Viral (challenges) |
| Prayer app | Viral (prayer circles) | Content (testimonials) |
| Habit tracker | Viral (accountability) | Content (progress posts) |
| Meditation | Paid (subscriptions) | Content (reviews) |

## Viral mechanics

Viral coefficient (K) = invites sent x conversion rate

K > 1 = exponential growth
K = 0.5 = each user brings half a user (still valuable)

### Viral coefficient by feature

| Feature | Typical K | Best-in-class K |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| Share to social | 0.05 | 0.15 |
| In-app referral | 0.10 | 0.40 |
| Multiplayer feature | 0.20 | 0.60 |
| Required invites | 0.50 | 1.20 |

### Making features inherently viral

Bad: "Share this app with friends"
Good: "Challenge a friend to 7 days of meditation"

The share must benefit the sharer, not just the app.

| Feature | Selfish Benefit | Social Benefit |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| Workout share | Show off progress | Inspire others |
| Prayer request | Get more prayers | Support network |
| Challenge invite | Accountability | Shared goal |
| Milestone share | Validation | Motivation |

## Retention as growth

Retention IS growth. A 5% improvement in retention beats a 20% improvement in acquisition.

### Retention math

Day 30 retention of 20%:
- 1000 installs = 200 active users

Day 30 retention of 25%:
- 1000 installs = 250 active users
- 25% more users from same spend

### Retention benchmarks (Day 30)

| Category | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|----------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Social | <5% | 10% | 20% | 30%+ |
| Health/Fitness | <3% | 8% | 15% | 25%+ |
| Productivity | <2% | 5% | 12% | 20%+ |
| Games | <2% | 5% | 10% | 15%+ |

### Retention-first features

1. **Habit hooks** - Daily triggers that bring users back
2. **Progress systems** - Streaks, levels, achievements
3. **Social ties** - Friends, groups, accountability
4. **Variable rewards** - Unpredictable value delivery
5. **Investment** - User data/content increases switching cost

## Channel strategy

### Channel selection framework

Rate each channel 1-5:

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Fit | Does your audience use this channel? |
| Cost | CAC relative to LTV |
| Scale | Can you 10x spend profitably? |
| Speed | Time to first results |
| Competition | How saturated is the channel? |

### Channel performance benchmarks

| Channel | Typical CAC | Best-in-class CAC | Scale Potential |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| ASO | $0.50-2 | $0.20 | Medium |
| Organic social | $1-3 | $0.30 | Medium |
| Influencer | $2-8 | $0.80 | Medium |
| Facebook/Meta ads | $3-15 | $1.50 | High |
| Google UAC | $2-10 | $1.00 | High |
| TikTok ads | $1-8 | $0.50 | High |
| Apple Search Ads | $2-6 | $1.00 | Medium |

### Channel sequencing

**Phase 1: Validation (0-1000 users)**
- Organic social (free, fast feedback)
- Community seeding (Reddit, Discord, Facebook groups)
- Friends and family referrals

**Phase 2: Product-market fit (1000-10000 users)**
- ASO optimization
- Influencer partnerships (micro)
- Content marketing
- Referral program launch

**Phase 3: Scale (10000+ users)**
- Paid social (Meta, TikTok)
- Apple Search Ads
- Influencer partnerships (macro)
- PR and press

## Growth team structure

### Solo founder priorities

Week 1-4: One channel, deep
Week 5-8: Add second channel
Week 9-12: Optimize or replace underperformers

### Metrics to track weekly

1. **Installs** - Top of funnel
2. **Activation rate** - First value moment
3. **Day 1 retention** - Initial hook
4. **Day 7 retention** - Habit forming
5. **Day 30 retention** - Long-term value
6. **Referral rate** - Organic growth
7. **LTV** - Revenue per user

### Growth meeting template

Every week, answer:
1. What did we ship?
2. What did we learn?
3. What's blocking growth?
4. What's the one thing to focus on this week?

## Quick wins checklist

Implement in first week:

- [ ] App Store listing optimized (screenshots, description)
- [ ] Deep links working (share links open app)
- [ ] Push notifications enabled (with good defaults)
- [ ] Share buttons on key screens
- [ ] Referral tracking in place
- [ ] Analytics on all key events
- [ ] Day 1/7/30 retention tracked

---

## Growth principles

1. **Distribution is product** - Build sharing into core features
2. **Retention beats acquisition** - Fix churn before scaling spend
3. **One channel at a time** - Master before moving on
4. **Speed of learning** - Fast experiments beat perfect plans
5. **Compounding** - Small gains multiply over time
