# Milestone sharing: achievement-driven virality

Milestones are the highest-converting share moments. Users want to celebrate. Give them something worth sharing.

---

## Why milestones work

People share milestones because:
1. **Pride** - Accomplishment deserves recognition
2. **Identity** - "I'm the type of person who does X"
3. **Connection** - Shared experience with others
4. **Accountability** - Public commitment reinforces behavior

The share feels natural, not forced.

---

## Milestone types

### Streak milestones

Consecutive days of activity.

| Streak | Significance | Share Priority |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| 3 days | Habit starting | Low |
| 7 days | First week complete | Medium |
| 21 days | Habit forming | High |
| 30 days | Monthly milestone | High |
| 60 days | Two months | Medium |
| 90 days | Quarter year | High |
| 100 days | Triple digits | Very high |
| 365 days | Full year | Maximum |

**Best practice:** Don't prompt every streak. Save it for meaningful ones.

### Cumulative milestones

Total activity over time.

**Examples:**
- 10/50/100/500 workouts
- 1/10/100 hours meditated
- 100/1000/10000 prayers
- 10/50/100 habits completed

**Spacing:** Use exponential intervals (1, 10, 100) not linear (10, 20, 30).

### Progress milestones

Movement toward a goal.

**Examples:**
- 25%/50%/75%/100% of goal
- First [action] completed
- Level up moments
- Badge unlocked

### Social milestones

Community-related achievements.

**Examples:**
- First friend added
- Challenge completed with partner
- Group goal achieved
- Community rank improved

### Personal record milestones

Best-ever achievements.

**Examples:**
- Longest workout
- Fastest mile
- Heaviest lift
- Longest meditation
- Best sleep score

## Milestone content design

### The anatomy of a shareable milestone

```
[Visual celebration]
[User's name/avatar]
[The milestone: "30-day streak"]
[Supporting stat: "Total: 450 minutes"]
[Date achieved]
[Subtle app branding]
```

### Visual hierarchy

1. **The number** - Biggest, boldest
2. **The context** - What it means
3. **The user** - Personalization
4. **The brand** - Small, tasteful

### Platform-specific designs

**Instagram Story:**
- Full screen celebration
- Animated elements
- "Add to your story" button
- Sticker overlay style

**Twitter/X:**
- Horizontal card
- Clean metric + context
- Thread-friendly (can quote tweet)

**iMessage:**
- Rich link preview
- Deep link to app
- Personal message template

## Milestone timing

### When to show

**Immediately after achievement:**
- Streak reached at session end
- Cumulative milestone hit mid-session
- PR broken during activity

**Do not interrupt:** If user is mid-flow, queue for session end.

### How long to show

- Full-screen celebration: 3-5 seconds auto-dismiss
- User can tap to dismiss earlier
- Share option persists in profile/history

### Second-chance prompts

If user dismisses without sharing:
- Add to "achievements" section
- Show in weekly summary
- Resurface at next milestone ("Last week you hit 30 days...")

## Celebration design

### Motion and animation

Celebrations should feel earned.

**Elements:**
- Confetti burst
- Badge/trophy animation
- Number counting up
- Glow/shine effects
- Sound (optional, respect mute)

**Calibrate to milestone:**

| Milestone Size | Celebration Level |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Minor (7-day) | Subtle animation |
| Medium (30-day) | Full animation |
| Major (100-day) | Premium celebration |
| Epic (365-day) | Maximum fanfare |

### Haptic feedback

On iOS:
- Light tap for minor
- Medium tap for medium
- Heavy tap for major

### Sound

If app has sound:
- Subtle chime for minor
- Fanfare for major
- Always respect silent mode

## Milestone gamification

### Achievement systems

Create tiered achievement structure:

**Streak achievements:**
- Bronze: 7 days
- Silver: 30 days
- Gold: 100 days
- Platinum: 365 days

**Volume achievements:**
- Beginner: 10 sessions
- Regular: 50 sessions
- Dedicated: 100 sessions
- Master: 500 sessions

### Badges and collections

Let users collect and display badges.

**Badge categories:**
- Streak badges
- Volume badges
- Challenge badges
- Community badges
- Seasonal badges

### Progress visualization

Show progress toward next milestone:
```
[Progress bar]
Day 23 of 30-day streak
7 days to go!
```

## Measuring milestone impact

### Metrics per milestone

Track for each milestone type:
1. Users reaching milestone
2. Share prompt shown
3. Share initiated
4. Share completed
5. Clicks on shared content
6. Installs from shares
7. Retention of new users

### Benchmark ratios

| Milestone Type | Share Rate | Click Rate | Install Rate |
|----------------|------------|------------|--------------|
| Streak (30-day) | 8-15% | 5-10% | 2-5% |
| PR | 12-20% | 8-15% | 3-6% |
| Completion | 10-18% | 6-12% | 2-5% |

### Optimize based on data

1. Which milestones have highest share rate?
2. Which milestone shares convert best?
3. Where do users drop off before milestones?
4. Which celebration designs perform best?
