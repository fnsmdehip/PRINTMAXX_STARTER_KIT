# StudyLock PRD

**App Name:** StudyLock
**Tagline:** Study First, Scroll Later
**Category:** Screen Time Blocker (Education Niche)
**Target:** Students and learners who procrastinate with phones

---

## Problem

- Students spend 5+ hours daily on social media
- Phone addiction tanks academic performance
- Existing study apps don't block phone access
- Pomodoro apps don't force commitment

## Solution

Lock phone until user completes study session. Quiz integration ensures active engagement.

---

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Study Session Lock
- Set study duration (15/25/45/60 min)
- Phone locks during session
- Must complete full timer to unlock
- Optional: Answer quiz questions to prove focus

### 2. Focus Modes
- **Pomodoro Mode:** 25 min study, 5 min break cycles
- **Deep Work Mode:** 90 min continuous
- **Exam Prep Mode:** Custom duration, no breaks

### 3. Quiz Integration
- Random questions appear during study
- Flashcard-style knowledge checks
- Import from Quizlet/Anki (premium)
- Wrong answers add study time

### 4. Progress Tracking
- Study hours logged
- Subject breakdown
- Streak counter
- Weekly/monthly reports
