# DailyDevotion Marketing Plan

**App:** DailyDevotion - Faith Habit Tracker
**Target:** Christian habit trackers
**Budget:** $200-500 initial
**Goal:** 1000 downloads in first month, $500 MRR by month 3

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Committed Christians (25-45)
- Regular church attendance
- Active in small groups/bible studies
- Use smartphones daily
- Value self-improvement
- Already use productivity apps

### Demographics
- 60% women, 40% men (faith apps skew female)
- 25-45 age range
- Suburban/urban
- Household income $50k+
- College educated

### Psychographics
- Want to grow spiritually but struggle with consistency
- Feel guilty about missed quiet time
- Active on social media (especially Facebook, Instagram)
- Trust recommendations from church friends
- Willing to pay for quality faith tools

---

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Week -2 to 0)

**Social Setup**
- [ ] Create @dailydevotion_app Instagram
- [ ] Create @dailydevotionapp TikTok
- [ ] Create @DailyDevotionHQ Twitter/X
- [ ] Design profile images with dove mascot
- [ ] Write bios with clear value prop

**Content Bank**
- [ ] Create 20 verse graphics (Canva templates)
- [ ] Write 10 habit tip posts
- [ ] Record 5 short-form videos
- [ ] Draft 15 tweets/threads

**Community Research**
- [ ] Join 10 Christian Facebook groups (observe rules)
- [ ] Follow 50 faith influencers
- [ ] Note posting times and formats that work
- [ ] Identify potential partners

### Phase 2: Launch Week (Week 1)

**Day 1-2: Soft Launch**
- Post "just launched" announcement
- Share in 3-5 Facebook groups (where allowed)
- DM 10 faith creators about app
- Email any existing contacts

**Day 3-5: Content Push**
- Post 3x daily on TikTok
- Daily Instagram posts + stories
- Share first user testimonial (even if from beta)
- Reply to every comment/DM

**Day 6-7: Engagement**
- Go live on Instagram (app walkthrough)
- Share launch learnings thread on Twitter
- Thank early adopters publicly

### Phase 3: Growth (Week 2-8)

**Weekly Rhythm**
- Monday: Motivational verse + habit tip
- Tuesday: Feature highlight
- Wednesday: User testimonial (or quote from review)
- Thursday: Behind-the-scenes / building in public
- Friday: Weekend worship habit reminder
- Saturday: User-generated content repost
- Sunday: Sabbath rest reminder

**Content Pillars**
1. **Habit tips** (40%) - Practical advice for spiritual disciplines
2. **Verses** (25%) - Shareable scripture graphics
3. **Testimonials** (20%) - User success stories
4. **Behind scenes** (15%) - Indie dev journey

## Channel Strategy

### TikTok (Primary Growth Channel)

**Why:** Highest organic reach, faith content performs well

**Content Types:**
- "POV: You finally built a consistent prayer habit" (relatable)
- "3 habits every Christian should track" (educational)
- "How I read my Bible every day for 30 days" (transformation)
- "Ranking spiritual disciplines by difficulty" (engagement bait)
- "Reply to @user about habit tracking for faith" (response videos)

**Posting Cadence:** 2-3x daily during launch, 1x daily after

**Hashtags:**
#christiancontent #faithtok #prayerlife #bibleverse #spiritualgrowth #habittok #productivitytok #devotional #quiettime #christiantiktok

### Instagram (Community Building)

**Why:** Strong faith community, good for testimonials

**Content Types:**
- Carousel: "5 morning habits for spiritual growth"
- Reels: Same content as TikTok (repurpose)
- Stories: Daily verse, polls, Q&A
- Posts: Aesthetic verse graphics

**Posting Cadence:** 1 post + 3-5 stories daily

**Hashtags:**
#christianapp #faithjourney #dailydevotional #prayerwarrior #biblereadingplan #spiritualdisciplines #christianwomen #faithbased

### Facebook Groups (Direct Access)

**Strategy:** Provide value first, soft promote second

**Target Groups:**
- Christian Women Entrepreneurs
- Bible Study Fellowship groups
- Church small group communities
- Christian Productivity groups
- Praying Wives / Praying Moms groups

**Approach:**
1. Join and observe 1-2 weeks
2. Provide helpful comments (no promotion)
3. Share genuinely helpful content
4. Soft mention app when relevant
5. Never spam or violate group rules

### Twitter/X (Building in Public)

**Why:** Indie hacker community, SEO value

**Content Types:**
- Weekly MRR updates
- Feature announcements
- User feedback threads
- Faith + productivity intersection

**Hashtags:** #buildinpublic #indiehackers #solopreneur

## Influencer Strategy

### Micro-Influencers (1k-50k followers)

**Why:** Higher engagement, more affordable, authentic

**Target Profiles:**
- Christian lifestyle bloggers
- Bible study teachers on YouTube
- Faith-based productivity creators
- Church worship leaders with audience

**Outreach Template:**
```
Hey [Name],

Love your content on [specific thing].

I just launched DailyDevotion, a habit tracker built specifically for Christians. It has pre-built templates for prayer, scripture reading, and gratitude journaling.

Would you be interested in trying it out? Happy to give you a free premium subscription. If you like it, maybe we could work together on a post.

Either way, keep creating great content!

[Your name]
```

**Compensation:**
- Free premium subscription (always)
- $50-200 for dedicated post (if budget allows)
- Affiliate code (15% commission on referrals)

### Macro-Influencers (50k+)

**Approach:** Build relationship first, pitch later

**Targets:**
- She Reads Truth
- Proverbs 31 Ministries creators
- I Am Second creators
- Bible Project adjacent creators

## Launch Hooks (Content Angles)

1. **Personal story:** "I built an app because I kept forgetting to pray"
2. **Transformation:** "This app helped me read my Bible every day for 30 days"
3. **Problem/solution:** "Why generic habit apps fail Christians (and what to use instead)"
4. **Comparison:** "I quit [popular app] for a faith-based habit tracker"
5. **Educational:** "The science of habit formation + biblical wisdom"
6. **Controversy (light):** "Unpopular opinion: You don't need a Bible app, you need a habit tracker"

## ASO (App Store Optimization)

### Keywords to Target
- Primary: prayer tracker, christian habit tracker
- Secondary: bible reading habit, devotional app, faith habits
- Long-tail: morning prayer habit, scripture memory app

### Title Optimization
Current: "DailyDevotion: Faith Habit Tracker"
Test: "DailyDevotion - Prayer & Bible Habit Tracker"

### Description Optimization
- Keywords in first paragraph
- Feature bullets scannable
- Social proof when available
- Clear CTA

### Screenshot A/B Testing
Test:
- Verse card first vs. habit list first
- With/without text overlays
- Dark mode vs. light mode shots

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low organic reach | Test multiple content formats, double down on what works |
| Negative reviews | Respond quickly, fix issues, request updated reviews |
| Influencer ghosting | Reach out to 10x target, expect 10% response |
| Ad costs too high | Focus on organic until profitable |
| Competitor copies | Move fast, build brand loyalty |
