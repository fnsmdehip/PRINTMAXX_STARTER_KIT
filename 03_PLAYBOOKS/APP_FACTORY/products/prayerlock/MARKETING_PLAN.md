# PrayerLock - Marketing Plan

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Active Christians (25-45)
- Attend church regularly
- Use apps for faith content (Bible apps, devotionals)
- Struggle with phone addiction
- Want to improve prayer life
- Income: $50k+ (can afford subscription)

### Secondary: Parents of Teens
- Want to help kids develop faith habits
- Already concerned about screen time
- Willing to pay for family solution

### Psychographics
- Feel guilty about phone use during prayer time
- Have tried and failed to build prayer habits
- Active in faith communities (church, small groups)
- Consume Christian content on social media

---

## Positioning

**One-liner:**
"Lock your phone until you pray."

**Elevator pitch:**
"PrayerLock blocks your distracting apps until you complete your morning devotional. Set a prayer timer or read scripture, then your apps unlock. No willpower required."

**Unique angle:**
Most productivity apps are secular. PrayerLock speaks directly to Christians who want their faith to come before their feed.

---

## Channel Strategy

### 1. TikTok (Primary - Organic)

**Why:** Christian TikTok is massive and underserved with quality content. Screen blocker content performs well (see Opal, BePresent viral videos).

**Content pillars:**
1. Before/after testimonials (real users, real streaks)
2. "Day 1 vs Day 30" transformations
3. App demo videos (satisfying UI)
4. Relatable Christian humor about phone addiction
5. Duets with viral prayer/faith content

**Posting cadence:**
- 3 posts per day minimum
- Test different times (5am, 12pm, 7pm)
- Respond to every comment

**Example hooks:**
- "I locked my phone until I prayed for 30 days. Here's what happened."
- "My screen time dropped 4 hours after this one change."
- "If you struggle to pray every morning, try this."
- "POV: You can't open Instagram until you read your Bible."

---

### 2. Instagram Reels (Secondary - Organic)

**Why:** Christian community is active. Repurpose TikTok content.

**Strategy:**
- Cross-post TikToks within 24 hours
- Use Instagram-specific hashtags (#christianity #prayerlife #faithjourney)
- Engage in comments on faith accounts

---

### 3. Facebook Groups (Direct - Free)

**Why:** Highest intent. People in faith groups actively discuss habits.

**Target groups:**
- Christian women's groups (highest engagement)
- Young adult ministry groups
- Prayer groups
- Bible study groups
- Church small groups

**Approach:**
- Join 20-30 relevant groups
- Provide value first (comment, help)
- Share personal story with app mention
- Not spammy, be a community member
- Ask permission before promotional posts

**Example post:**
"Hey everyone, I struggled with morning prayer for years. I kept grabbing my phone first thing and getting distracted. I found an app that blocks my social media until I complete my devotional. Been using it for 3 weeks and my streak is the longest ever. If anyone's interested I can share the name."

---

### 4. Christian Podcasts (Outreach)

**Why:** Trust transfer from host. Targeted audience.

**Approach:**
- List 50 mid-size Christian podcasts (1k-50k downloads)
- Offer free annual subscription for review
- Pitch story angle: "How one app helped me pray daily"
- Affiliate opportunity: 20% rev share

**Target shows:**
- Habits and faith focused
- Young adult demographic
- Not mega-podcasts (too expensive/competitive)

---

### 5. Church Partnerships (B2B2C)

**Why:** Built-in trust, group adoption, recurring revenue.

**Offer:**
- Church gets bulk discount (30% off)
- Dashboard to track congregation engagement (optional)
- Custom challenge: "21 Days of Prayer" campaign

**Outreach:**
- Start with small/medium churches (500-2000 members)
- Target youth pastors and small group leaders
- Offer free pilot program

---

### 6. Influencer Partnerships

**Micro-influencers (5k-50k followers):**
- Cost: Free product + affiliate deal
- Higher trust, better conversion
- Target: Christian lifestyle, young adult ministry

**Examples to reach out:**
- Christian TikTokers doing daily devotional content
- Faith-based productivity creators
- Christian mommy bloggers

**Deal structure:**
- Free lifetime access
- 25% affiliate commission
- Unique referral code for tracking

## Pre-Launch Campaign

### Week -4 to -2: Build anticipation
- Start TikTok account, post general prayer content
- Build email list via simple landing page
- "Join 500 Christians waiting for this app"

### Week -2 to -1: Beta access
- TestFlight beta to email list
- Collect testimonials and screenshots
- Fix critical bugs from feedback

### Week 0: Launch
- Submit to app stores
- Email list with download links
- Post launch video on TikTok
- Share in all Facebook groups
- Reach out to 20 micro-influencers

### Week 1-4: Growth push
- 3 TikToks per day
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- A/B test paywall messaging
- Collect and share testimonials
- Target first 100 paying users

## Messaging Framework

### Pain points to address:
1. "I want to pray more but I keep getting distracted"
2. "I grab my phone before I even get out of bed"
3. "I've tried willpower, it doesn't work"
4. "I feel guilty about my screen time"
5. "Nothing holds me accountable"

### Transformation promise:
- "From 0 minutes to 30 minutes of daily prayer"
- "From guilty scrolling to intentional mornings"
- "From inconsistent to 30-day streak"

### Social proof angles:
- Streak screenshots
- Screen time reduction stats
- Testimonial quotes
- Star ratings

## Paid Acquisition (Phase 2)

### When to start paid:
- After organic validates product-market fit
- LTV > 3x CAC
- Monthly revenue > $5k MRR

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- Target: Christian interest categories
- Creative: User testimonials (video)
- Budget: Start $20/day, scale winners

### Apple Search Ads
- Keywords: "prayer app", "christian morning routine", "bible habit"
- Budget: $10/day
- Expect high CPT but good conversion

### TikTok Ads
- Spark Ads (boost organic winners)
- Start after identifying viral organic content
