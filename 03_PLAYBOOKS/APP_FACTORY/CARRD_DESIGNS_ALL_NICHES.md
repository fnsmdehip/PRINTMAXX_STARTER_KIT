# Implementation Quick Reference

## Carrd Setup Steps (Per Niche)

1. **Domain:** Buy `.com` or niche-specific domain ($19 for year 1, usually $9/year renewal)
2. **Template:** Use "Portfolio" or "Landing Page" Carrd template as base
3. **Customize:**
   - Swap colors (use provided scheme)
   - Add hero image (use Gemini or Unsplash)
   - Update copy (use provided templates)
   - Add form (Carrd built-in)
   - Link CTA buttons to form
4. **Form Integration:** Carrd integrates with Zapier → Google Sheets CSV
5. **Launch:** Publish. Share on relevant subreddits, Twitter, Discord communities
6. **Track:** Monitor form submissions in Carrd dashboard + Google Sheets

## Lead Magnet Hosting (Per Niche)

- **Free PDFs:** Host in Google Drive (shareable link)
- **Video:** YouTube unlisted or Loom
- **Courses:** Gumroad or Teachable (if $47+ tier)
- **Tracking:** Zapier → Google Sheets captures email + form data

## Attribution Model (For Testing)

Track by niche:
- Email submitted
- Lead magnet claimed
- Which $X offer clicked (if applicable)
- Days to conversion

Store in: `LEDGER/CARRD_VALIDATION_RESULTS.csv`

# Test Strategy (How to Validate Fastest)

1. **Launch 3-5 niches simultaneously** (lowest CAC niches: ADHD, Gen Z Finance, Students)
2. **Run for 7 days** → minimal paid ads, just organic share
3. **Measure:** Emails captured, lead magnet downloads, CTR to offer
4. **Win metric:** 100+ emails or 10+ conversions to paid offer
5. **Scale winners:** Paid ads to this niche
6. **Iterate:** A/B test hero copy/colors on winners

**Cost:** $19 × niches + time = $57 for 3 niches
