# Post-purchase review request sequence

3-email sequence triggered after purchase confirmation.

---

## Email 1: Quick check-in (Day 3)

**Subject:** Quick question about [App Name]

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

You got [App Name] 3 days ago. Quick question: have you had a chance to try [core feature]?

If something's confusing or broken, reply to this email. I read every message and fix issues fast.

If it's working well, no need to reply. You'll hear from me in a few days with some tips.

[Your Name]
Founder, [App Name]

---

## Email 2: Value delivered + soft ask (Day 7)

**Subject:** You've now [achieved X] with [App Name]

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Quick update: you've now used [App Name] for a week.

Based on your activity, you've [specific metric if available - e.g., "saved approximately 2 hours" or "created 15 entries"].

If the app has helped you, would you mind leaving a quick review? Takes about 30 seconds.

[Direct link to app store review page]

Your review helps other people like you find the app. And honestly, it helps me keep working on it full-time.

If something's not working right, reply here instead. I'd rather fix it than have you leave a frustrated review.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

P.S. - If you haven't used the app much yet, ignore this. I'll check back in later.

---

## Email 3: Direct ask with specific prompt (Day 14)

**Subject:** 30 seconds to help [App Name]?

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Last email about reviews, I promise.

If [App Name] has saved you time or made [task] easier, a review would mean a lot.

Here's what helps most in a review:
- What problem you were trying to solve
- How the app helped (even if it's small)
- How long you've been using it

[Direct link to review page]

No pressure if you're not ready. The app will keep working either way.

Thanks for being an early user.

[Your Name]

---

## Timing notes

- Day 3: Check-in only. No ask. Build rapport.
- Day 7: Soft ask. Offer alternative (reply with feedback).
- Day 14: Direct ask with guidance. Final email in sequence.

## Segment variations

**Power users (high engagement):**
- Move Day 7 email to Day 5
- Add specific usage stats to subject line

**Low engagement users:**
- Delay entire sequence by 7 days
- Focus Day 7 email on re-engagement, not review ask

**Users who contacted support:**
- Only send if issue was resolved
- Add: "I know we had some bumps early on. If that's fixed now..."

---

## FTC compliance notes

- Never offer incentives for positive reviews
- Never ask specifically for 5 stars
- Always provide option to give negative feedback directly
- Don't suppress negative reviews
