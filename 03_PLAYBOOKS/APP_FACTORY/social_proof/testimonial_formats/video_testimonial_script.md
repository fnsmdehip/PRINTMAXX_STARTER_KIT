# Video testimonial script

Framework for collecting authentic video testimonials from users.

---

## Video testimonial value

Video testimonials convert 2-3x better than text because:
- Harder to fake (more credible)
- Emotional connection with viewer
- Shows real person behind the quote
- Can demonstrate product in use
- Shareable on social media

---

## Video formats

### Quick selfie testimonial (30-60 seconds)
- User records on phone
- Casual, authentic feel
- Lowest barrier to get
- Best for social proof volume

### Interview-style testimonial (2-5 minutes)
- You ask questions, they answer
- More structured and polished
- Good for website and ads
- Requires more coordination

### Screen share testimonial (1-3 minutes)
- User shows the product while talking
- Demonstrates actual use
- Great for feature highlights
- Combines social proof with demo

---

## Quick selfie script

Send this to users who agree to record a video:

```
Subject: Quick video instructions (takes 2 minutes)

Hey [Name],

Thanks for agreeing to record a quick testimonial! Here's all you need to do:

**Setup:**
- Good lighting (face a window if possible)
- Quiet space
- Hold phone horizontally
- Look at camera, not screen

**What to say (60 seconds or less):**

1. "Hi, I'm [your name], I'm a [your role]"

2. "I started using [App Name] because [problem you had]"

3. "Now I [result or benefit you've gotten]"

4. "If you're dealing with [same problem], I'd recommend trying it"

**That's it!** Don't worry about being perfect. Authentic > polished.

Send the video to [email/upload link] when you're done.

Thanks again,
[Your Name]
```

## Interview question script

Use for scheduled video calls or in-person interviews.

### Opening (set them at ease)

"Thanks for taking the time. This will be pretty casual. I'll ask a few questions and you just answer naturally. If you stumble over words, no worries, we can always edit. Ready?"

### The questions

**1. Introduction**
"Can you introduce yourself? Name, what you do, where you're based?"

*Prompt if needed:* "What does your day-to-day work look like?"

**2. The problem (before)**
"What were you struggling with before you found [App Name]?"

*Prompt if needed:* "What was that costing you? Time, money, frustration?"

**3. The discovery**
"How did you find out about [App Name]?"

*Prompt if needed:* "What made you decide to try it?"

**4. The experience**
"What was it like when you first started using it?"

*Prompt if needed:* "Was there a moment when it clicked?"

**5. The results (after)**
"What's different now? What results have you seen?"

*Prompt if needed:* "Can you put a number on it? Time saved, money saved?"

**6. Favorite feature**
"What's your favorite thing about [App Name]?"

*Prompt if needed:* "What would you miss most if you had to stop using it?"

**7. Recommendation**
"What would you say to someone considering [App Name]?"

*Prompt if needed:* "Who is it best for?"

### Closing

"That was great. Anything else you'd want to add that I didn't ask about?"

## Screen share script

For testimonials that show the product in action.

```
"I'll share my screen and show you how I actually use [App Name]..."

[Opens app]

"So this is my dashboard. The first thing I do every morning is [action]..."

[Demonstrates workflow]

"Before I had this, I used to [old way]. Now it takes me [time comparison]..."

[Shows specific feature]

"This part right here is what I love most. You just [action] and it [result]..."

[Wraps up]

"So yeah, that's basically how I use it. Saves me probably [time/effort] every week."
```

## Technical requirements

### For selfie videos

**Minimum specs:**
- 1080p resolution
- Horizontal orientation (landscape)
- Stable (tripod, lean against something)
- Clear audio (quiet room, close to phone)
- 30-90 seconds

**Common issues to avoid:**
- Backlit (face in shadow)
- Vertical video
- Background noise
- Too far from camera
- Looking at screen not lens

### For interview videos

**Equipment:**
- Good webcam or camera
- External microphone (lapel or USB)
- Lighting (ring light or window)
- Neutral background

**Technical setup:**
- Record locally (not just Zoom cloud)
- 1080p minimum, 4K if possible
- Separate audio recording as backup
- Test audio levels before starting

### For screen shares

**Setup:**
- Clean up desktop/browser before recording
- Close notifications
- Use dummy data if needed (privacy)
- Zoom to 100-125% for visibility
- Highlight cursor if possible

## Editing guidelines

### What to cut
- Long pauses
- Repeated "ums" and "likes"
- Off-topic tangents
- Confidential information
- Unflattering moments (if they'd want)

### What to keep
- Natural speech patterns
- Genuine reactions
- Specific details and numbers
- Authentic personality

### What to add
- Name/title lower third
- Company logo (with permission)
- Product screenshots (b-roll)
- Subtitles/captions (always)

## Permission and release

### Video release form (simplified)

```
VIDEO TESTIMONIAL RELEASE

I, [Name], grant [Your Company] permission to use my video testimonial for:
- Website and landing pages
- Social media (all platforms)
- Advertising (paid and organic)
- Email marketing
- Sales materials

I confirm that:
- My statements are my genuine opinions
- I was not paid for this testimonial (or: I received [compensation])
- I approve the final edited version

Signature: _______________
Date: _______________
Email: _______________
```

### Approval process

1. Record raw video
2. Edit draft
3. Send to user for review
4. Get written approval of final version
5. Then publish

Never publish without final approval.

## Distribution

### Website
- Hero section (short clip)
- Dedicated testimonials page (full videos)
- Relevant feature pages (specific clips)

### Social media
- Full video on YouTube
- 60-second cuts for Instagram/TikTok
- 30-second cuts for Twitter/X
- Clips with captions for LinkedIn

### Ads
- 15-30 second cuts work best
- Lead with strongest moment
- Add CTA at end
- A/B test different clips

### Email
- Thumbnail linking to video
- Embed where supported
- GIF preview as fallback

## Video testimonial request email

```
Subject: Quick video favor?

Hey [Name],

You mentioned [positive thing they said]. Would you be open to saying that on camera?

It would be:
- 60 seconds or less
- Selfie video on your phone
- I'll send you exactly what to say

Your video would help other [target audience] see that [App Name] actually works.

Interested?

[Your Name]

P.S. - I can send you a small thank you gift for your time. No pressure either way.
```

Note: Disclosing any compensation (including gifts) is required by FTC if you use the testimonial.
