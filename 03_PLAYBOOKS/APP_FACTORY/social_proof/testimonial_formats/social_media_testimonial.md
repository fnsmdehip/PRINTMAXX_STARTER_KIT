# Social media testimonial formats

Templates for sharing testimonials across social platforms.

---

## Platform-specific formats

### Twitter/X

**Quote tweet format:**
```
[Screenshot of user's positive tweet/post]

"[Pull key quote from their message]"

This is why we built [App Name].
```

**Thread format:**
```
Tweet 1:
[User Name] just shared their results after 30 days with [App Name]:

Tweet 2:
BEFORE:
[Their situation before]

Tweet 3:
AFTER:
[Their results now]

Tweet 4:
[Screenshot of their testimonial or DM]

Tweet 5:
If you want similar results: [link]
```

**Simple share:**
```
"[User quote about results]"

- [User Name], [Role]

[Link to product]
```

### LinkedIn

**Story format:**
```
I got this message from [User Name] yesterday:

"[Their testimonial in quotes]"

Here's the backstory:

[2-3 sentences about their situation]

[What they did with your product]

[The result they got]

This is exactly why I built [Product Name].

If you're dealing with [same problem], [CTA].

---
[Link in comments]
```

**Results post:**
```
[User Name] cut their [metric] by [amount] in [timeframe].

Here's how:

1. [Step/feature 1]
2. [Step/feature 2]
3. [Step/feature 3]

Their words:

"[Quote about the experience]"

The takeaway: [One lesson others can apply]

---
[DM me if you want to try it]
```

### Instagram

**Carousel format (for feed):**
```
Slide 1: "[Punchy quote from user]" - [User Name]
Slide 2: THE PROBLEM: [What they struggled with]
Slide 3: THE SOLUTION: [What they did]
Slide 4: THE RESULT: [Metrics/outcome]
Slide 5: [Screenshot of their actual testimonial]
Slide 6: [CTA - link in bio]
```

**Story format:**
```
Story 1: [Poll] Anyone else struggle with [problem]?
Story 2: [User Name] used to have the same issue...
Story 3: [Screenshot of their before state]
Story 4: Then they tried [Product Name]
Story 5: [Screenshot of their results/testimonial]
Story 6: Link to try it [swipe up/link sticker]
```

**Reel/video format:**
```
Hook (0-3s): "[Dramatic result quote]"
Problem (3-8s): [User describing their old struggle]
Solution (8-15s): [How they use the product]
Result (15-25s): [Their outcome with numbers]
CTA (25-30s): [How to get same result]
```

### TikTok

**Testimonial reaction format:**
```
[Stitch or duet with user's video]

"Wait, you did WHAT with [Product Name]?"

[Your reaction/commentary]

[Show the results they mentioned]

[CTA: link in bio]
```

**Results breakdown:**
```
POV: A customer just sent you this message

[Show screenshot]

"[Their testimonial text]"

Let me break down what happened:

[Quick walkthrough of their journey]

[The results they got]

Link in bio if you want the same
```

## Collecting social testimonials

### Monitor brand mentions

**Search queries to track:**
- "[Product Name]"
- "[Product Name] review"
- "[Product Name] saved me"
- "[Your handle] thanks"
- Related hashtags

**Tools:**
- Twitter/X advanced search
- Google Alerts
- Mention.com
- Brand24

### Encourage organic shares

**After positive support interaction:**
```
So glad that worked! If you ever feel like sharing your experience, we love seeing posts about [Product Name]. No pressure though.
```

**In product (after milestone):**
```
You just [achieved milestone]!

Want to share this win?

[Tweet this] [Post to LinkedIn]

(Pre-populated with achievement + product mention)
```

**Post-purchase email:**
```
If [Product Name] helps you [achieve goal], we'd love if you shared it.

Tag us @[handle] so we can celebrate with you.
```

## Permission and repurposing

### When someone posts organically

1. Like and reply/comment thanking them
2. Screenshot the post
3. DM asking permission to share:

```
Hey [Name]! Thanks so much for the kind words. Would you mind if we shared this on our [other platform/website]? Happy to credit you or keep you anonymous, whatever you prefer.
```

4. Wait for explicit "yes"
5. Then share

### When amplifying their content

**Always:**
- Credit the original poster
- Tag them (unless they prefer anonymous)
- Use their actual words, not paraphrased
- Keep context intact
- Link to original if possible

**Never:**
- Share without permission
- Edit their words to sound better
- Remove caveats they included
- Imply endorsement beyond what they said

## Posting schedule

**Testimonial content calendar:**

Monday: Customer quote card
Wednesday: Screenshot of organic mention
Friday: Results/metrics graphic
(Rotate with other content types)

**Frequency:**
- 1-2 testimonials per week on main feed
- More frequently in Stories/ephemeral content
- Don't overdo it (looks desperate)

---

## Engagement strategy

### When you share a testimonial

- Tag the customer (with permission)
- Respond to comments quickly
- Pin positive engagement
- Answer questions about results

### When customers post organically

- Like within 1 hour if possible
- Comment genuinely (not generic)
- Share to your Stories
- Add to your saves/collection for later use

## FTC compliance for social

### Required disclosures

If you gave them anything (free product, discount, payment):
```
[Their testimonial]

Disclosure: [User Name] received [what they got] in exchange for their honest feedback.
```

### Platform-specific rules

**Instagram:** Disclosure must be visible before "more" cutoff
**Twitter/X:** Include in main tweet, not just replies
**TikTok:** Verbal disclosure in video + text overlay
**LinkedIn:** Include in post text

### What counts as "material connection"
- Free product
- Discount
- Payment
- Referral bonus
- Contest entry
- Affiliate relationship
- Employment/investment
