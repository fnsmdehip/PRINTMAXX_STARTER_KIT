## Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that content available through [APP_NAME] infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice to our designated agent.

### Required Information

Your notice must include:

1. **Physical or electronic signature** of the copyright owner or authorized representative

2. **Identification of the copyrighted work** claimed to be infringed. If multiple works are covered, provide a representative list.

3. **Identification of the infringing material** and information reasonably sufficient to permit us to locate the material (e.g., URL, username, specific description)

4. **Contact information** including your address, telephone number, and email address

5. **Good faith statement** that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law

6. **Accuracy statement** under penalty of perjury that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner

### Submission Methods

Submit your DMCA notice via:

- **Email:** [DMCA_EMAIL] (preferred)
- **Mail:** [COMPANY_ADDRESS]

### Notice Format Template

```
DMCA Takedown Notice

1. I, [YOUR_NAME], am the copyright owner / authorized to act on behalf of
   the copyright owner of the following copyrighted work(s):
   [DESCRIBE THE COPYRIGHTED WORK]

2. The allegedly infringing material is located at:
   [PROVIDE SPECIFIC LOCATION / URL / DESCRIPTION]

3. My contact information:
   Name: [YOUR_NAME]
   Address: [YOUR_ADDRESS]
   Phone: [YOUR_PHONE]
   Email: [YOUR_EMAIL]

4. I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material
   described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent,
   or the law.

5. I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notice
   is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or authorized to act on
   behalf of the copyright owner.

Signature: ____________________
Date: ____________________
```

## Response to Valid Notices

Upon receiving a valid DMCA takedown notice, we will:

1. **Remove or disable access** to the allegedly infringing material promptly

2. **Notify the user** who posted the material that it has been removed

3. **Provide information** about the counter-notice process

4. **Document** the notice and our response

### Timeline

- Valid notices are typically processed within [1-3] business days
- Emergency situations involving ongoing harm may be expedited

## Counter-Notification Process

If you believe your content was removed in error or that you have authorization to use the material, you may submit a counter-notice.

### Required Information

Your counter-notice must include:

1. **Physical or electronic signature**

2. **Identification of the material** that was removed and its location before removal

3. **Statement under penalty of perjury** that you have a good faith belief the material was removed due to mistake or misidentification

4. **Your contact information** including name, address, telephone number, and email

5. **Consent to jurisdiction** - Statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in [JURISDICTION] and that you will accept service of process from the person who provided the original notice

### Submission

Submit your counter-notice to our designated agent via:

- **Email:** [DMCA_EMAIL]
- **Mail:** [COMPANY_ADDRESS]

### Counter-Notice Template

```
DMCA Counter-Notification

1. My name is [YOUR_NAME].

2. The material that was removed or disabled is:
   [DESCRIBE THE MATERIAL]

   It was located at:
   [ORIGINAL LOCATION]

3. I swear, under penalty of perjury, that I have a good faith belief that
   the material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or
   misidentification of the material.

4. My contact information:
   Name: [YOUR_NAME]
   Address: [YOUR_ADDRESS]
   Phone: [YOUR_PHONE]
   Email: [YOUR_EMAIL]

5. I consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the
   judicial district in which my address is located (or [JURISDICTION]
   if my address is outside the United States).

6. I will accept service of process from the person who provided the
   original DMCA notice or their agent.

Signature: ____________________
Date: ____________________
```

## Response to Counter-Notices

Upon receiving a valid counter-notice, we will:

1. **Forward** the counter-notice to the original complainant

2. **Inform the complainant** that we will restore the material in [10-14] business days unless we receive notice that they have filed a court action

3. **Restore the material** after [10-14] business days if no court action notice is received

Users may appeal a repeat infringer determination by contacting [DMCA_EMAIL] with:

- Their account information
- Explanation of circumstances

## Misrepresentation

Be advised that knowingly misrepresenting:

- That material is infringing, or
- That material was removed by mistake

may subject you to liability under 17 U.S.C. 512(f), including damages, costs, and attorney's fees.

**Do not submit a DMCA notice or counter-notice unless you are certain of your claims.**
