## App Store Purchases (Apple/Google)

### Apple App Store Purchases

**All purchases made through the Apple App Store are subject to Apple's refund policies.**

To request a refund for an Apple App Store purchase:

1. Go to [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Select "I'd like to" and choose "Request a refund"
4. Select the reason for your refund
5. Select the purchase and submit

**Important:**
- Apple processes all App Store refunds directly
- We cannot process refunds for App Store purchases
- Apple's decision on refunds is final
- Refund requests must be submitted within [90] days of purchase (Apple's standard)

For more information, see [Apple's refund request support page](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084).

### Google Play Store Purchases

**All purchases made through Google Play are subject to Google's refund policies.**

Google Play refund options:

1. **Within 48 hours of purchase:**
   - Open Google Play Store app
   - Go to your purchase history
   - Select the item and tap "Refund"

2. **After 48 hours:**
   - Go to [play.google.com/store/account](https://play.google.com/store/account)
   - Find the order and select "Report a problem"
   - Choose your refund reason

**Important:**
- Google processes all Play Store refunds directly
- We cannot process refunds for Play Store purchases
- Google's decision on refunds is final

For more information, see [Google Play's refund policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637).

## Subscription Cancellations

### How to Cancel

**Apple App Store:**
1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Select [APP_NAME]
5. Tap Cancel Subscription

**Google Play:**
1. Open Google Play Store app
2. Tap Menu > Subscriptions
3. Select [APP_NAME]
4. Tap Cancel subscription

### What Happens After Cancellation

- Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
- You retain access to premium features until the period ends
- Your subscription will not renew
- No partial refunds are given for unused time

### Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. To avoid charges for the next period, cancel before this deadline.
