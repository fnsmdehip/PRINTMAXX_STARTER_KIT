## 5. FTC Compliance Requirements

### 5.1 Disclosure Requirement

**CRITICAL: You MUST clearly disclose your affiliate relationship in ALL promotional content.**

This is required by the FTC (Federal Trade Commission) and similar regulations in other jurisdictions.

### 5.2 Disclosure Placement

Disclosures must be:

- Clear and conspicuous
- Near the affiliate link or promotional claim
- Visible before clicking the affiliate link
- In the same language as the content
- Not hidden in fine print, FAQs, or separate pages

### 5.3 Acceptable Disclosure Examples

**Social Media:**
- "#ad #affiliate"
- "Affiliate link"
- "I earn a commission if you buy through my link"
- "This post contains affiliate links"

**Blog Posts:**
- "Disclosure: This post contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you."
- Place disclosure at the TOP of posts, not just at the bottom

**Video Content:**
- Verbal disclosure within the video
- Written disclosure in description above the fold

**Email:**
- Clear disclosure near affiliate links

### 5.4 Non-Compliance Consequences

Failure to disclose properly may result in:

- Warning and request to add disclosure
- Commission withholding
- Account suspension
- Permanent termination
- Legal liability (you are responsible for your own compliance)
