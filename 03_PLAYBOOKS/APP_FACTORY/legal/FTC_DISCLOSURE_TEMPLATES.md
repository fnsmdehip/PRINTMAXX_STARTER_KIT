# FTC Disclosure Templates

**DISCLAIMER: This is a template requiring legal review before use. Consult with a qualified attorney to ensure compliance with applicable laws.**

---

## Overview

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) requires clear disclosure of material connections between endorsers and advertisers. This document provides disclosure templates for various content types.

**Key Principles:**
- Disclosures must be clear and conspicuous
- Disclosures must be near the relevant claim or link
- Disclosures must be in the same language as the content
- When in doubt, disclose

## 1. Affiliate Link Disclosures

### Short-Form (Social Media, Tweets)

```
#ad #affiliate

[YOUR CONTENT]
```

```
Affiliate link: [YOUR CONTENT]
```

```
(affiliate) [YOUR CONTENT]
```

### Medium-Form (Instagram, Facebook Posts)

```
[YOUR CONTENT]

Disclosure: This post contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you make a purchase at no extra cost to you.
```

```
[YOUR CONTENT]

#ad - I earn a commission from qualifying purchases through the links in this post.
```

### Long-Form (Blog Posts, Articles)

**Place at TOP of content, not buried at bottom:**

```
DISCLOSURE: This post contains affiliate links, meaning I may earn a
commission if you make a purchase through my links, at no extra cost to you.
I only recommend products I use and believe in. Read my full affiliate
disclosure here [LINK].
```

```
AFFILIATE DISCLOSURE

Some links in this article are affiliate links. This means if you click on
the link and purchase the item, I will receive an affiliate commission at
no extra cost to you. All opinions remain my own.
```

### Video Content (YouTube, TikTok)

**Verbal disclosure (say within first 30 seconds):**
```
"Quick disclosure: Some links in the description are affiliate links.
If you use them, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you."
```

**Written disclosure (in description, above the fold):**
```
AFFILIATE DISCLOSURE: This video contains affiliate links. Using these
links supports this channel at no additional cost to you.

Links mentioned:
(affiliate) Product Name - [LINK]
(affiliate) Product Name - [LINK]
(not affiliate) Product Name - [LINK]
```

## 2. Sponsored Content Disclosures

### Social Media Posts

```
#ad #sponsored

[YOUR CONTENT]
```

```
Paid partnership with [BRAND_NAME]

[YOUR CONTENT]
```

```
[BRAND_NAME] sponsored this post.

[YOUR CONTENT]
```

### Blog Posts / Articles

**Place at TOP:**

```
SPONSORED POST

This article is sponsored by [BRAND_NAME]. While I received compensation
for writing this post, all opinions expressed are my own.
```

```
DISCLOSURE: This is a sponsored post in partnership with [BRAND_NAME].
I have been compensated for creating this content. All views and opinions
expressed are my own.
```

### Video Content

**Verbal (say at beginning):**
```
"This video is sponsored by [BRAND_NAME]. Thank you to [BRAND_NAME] for
sponsoring this video."
```

**Written (in title/thumbnail if possible, and description):**
```
SPONSORED BY [BRAND_NAME]

This video is sponsored by [BRAND_NAME]. All opinions expressed in this
video are my own. Learn more at [BRAND_URL].
```

### Email Newsletters

```
SPONSORED CONTENT

Today's newsletter is brought to you by [BRAND_NAME].

[SPONSORED CONTENT HERE]

---

This section was sponsored by [BRAND_NAME]. All other content in this
newsletter represents my own views.
```

## 3. Testimonial Disclosures

### User Testimonials on Your Site

```
TESTIMONIALS DISCLOSURE

The testimonials on this page are from actual users of [APP_NAME].
Individual results may vary. These testimonials are not intended to
represent or guarantee that anyone will achieve the same or similar results.
```

### If Incentives Were Given

```
DISCLOSURE: [REVIEWER_NAME] received [FREE ACCESS/DISCOUNT/PAYMENT] in
exchange for their honest review. The opinions expressed are their own.
```

```
*This review was written by a user who received a complimentary
subscription. All opinions are their own.
```

### Results-Based Testimonials

```
[TESTIMONIAL QUOTE]
- [NAME], [LOCATION]

*Individual results may vary. The results described are not typical and
your experience will differ based on various factors including [RELEVANT_FACTORS].
```

### Before/After or Results Claims

```
RESULTS DISCLAIMER

The results shown are from actual users of [APP_NAME]. These results are
not typical, and individual experiences will vary based on:
- Starting point
- Effort and consistency
- Individual circumstances
- [OTHER_RELEVANT_FACTORS]

We do not guarantee any specific results from using [APP_NAME].
```

## 4. AI-Generated Content Disclosures

### Content Written by AI

```
AI DISCLOSURE: This content was generated with the assistance of
artificial intelligence and reviewed/edited by [YOUR_NAME/COMPANY_NAME].
```

```
This article was created using AI tools. A human editor has reviewed
and approved the final content.
```

### AI-Generated Images

```
IMAGE DISCLOSURE: This image was generated using AI ([TOOL_NAME]).
```

### AI Chatbot Disclosures

```
AI ASSISTANT DISCLOSURE

You are interacting with an AI assistant. This is not a human. For
complex issues, please contact our human support team at [SUPPORT_EMAIL].
```

### Mixed Content

```
CONTENT DISCLOSURE

Parts of this content were created with AI assistance. All AI-generated
content has been reviewed and edited by [YOUR_NAME/COMPANY_NAME] to
ensure accuracy. [Specific AI-generated sections are marked/Human-created
sections are authored by NAME].
```

## 5. Product Provided for Review

### Free Product Received

```
DISCLOSURE: [BRAND_NAME] provided this product free of charge for review.
However, all opinions expressed are my own and I was not required to
provide a positive review.
```

### Paid Review

```
DISCLOSURE: I was paid by [BRAND_NAME] to review this product. While I
received compensation, all opinions expressed are my own honest assessment.
```

### Gifted Product (No Review Obligation)

```
DISCLOSURE: This product was gifted to me by [BRAND_NAME]. I was not
required to create content about it, but chose to share my experience.
```

## 6. Investment/Financial Disclosures

### Equity Stake or Investment

```
DISCLOSURE: I am an investor in / have an equity stake in [COMPANY_NAME].
This may affect my objectivity. Please consider this when evaluating my
statements about this company.
```

### Financial Product Promotions

```
DISCLOSURE: This is not financial advice. I am not a licensed financial
advisor. The information provided is for educational purposes only.
[COMPANY_NAME] has compensated me for this promotion.

Please consult a qualified financial professional before making any
investment decisions.
```

## 7. Employee/Ownership Disclosures

### Company Employee

```
DISCLOSURE: I am an employee of [COMPANY_NAME]. My views expressed here
are my own but may be influenced by my employment relationship.
```

### Company Owner/Founder

```
DISCLOSURE: I am the founder/owner of [COMPANY_NAME]. Please consider
this relationship when evaluating any statements I make about the company
or its products.
```

## 8. Disclosure Placement Guidelines

### Social Media Character Limits

| Platform | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| Twitter/X | Start with #ad or #sponsored |
| Instagram | First line of caption or "Paid partnership" tag |
| TikTok | Text overlay in video + caption |
| YouTube | Verbal mention + description + "includes paid promotion" checkbox |
| Facebook | First paragraph + sponsored content tag |
| LinkedIn | First line or sponsored content tag |

### Blog/Website Placement

1. **Affiliate disclosure:** Top of article, not bottom
2. **Sponsored content:** Before the content begins
3. **Site-wide disclosure:** Footer link to full disclosure page
4. **Testimonial disclaimers:** Immediately following the testimonial

### Video Placement

1. **Verbal disclosure:** Within first 30 seconds
2. **Visual disclosure:** On-screen text if content is sponsored throughout
3. **Description:** Above the fold (visible without clicking "show more")
4. **Platform tools:** Use built-in sponsored content disclosure features

## 9. Full Disclosure Page Template

Create a dedicated disclosure page on your website:

```
# Disclosure Policy

Last Updated: [DATE]

## Affiliate Relationships

[YOUR_NAME/COMPANY_NAME] participates in affiliate programs. This means
we may earn a commission when you purchase products or services through
links on our website/app at no additional cost to you.

Our affiliate partners include but are not limited to:
- [PARTNER_1]
- [PARTNER_2]
- [PARTNER_3]

## Sponsored Content

From time to time, we may publish sponsored content. Sponsored posts are
always clearly marked as such. We only accept sponsorships from companies
we believe provide value to our audience.

## Product Reviews

When we receive products for free or are paid to review products, we
disclose this relationship. Our reviews represent our honest opinions
regardless of how we obtained the product.

## Testimonials

Testimonials on our site come from real users. Individual results may
vary. Some testimonial providers may have received free products or
compensation, which is disclosed alongside their testimonial.

## AI-Generated Content

Some content on this site may be created with AI assistance. All
AI-generated content is reviewed by humans before publication.

## Financial Disclaimer

Nothing on this site constitutes financial advice. We are not licensed
financial advisors. Please consult qualified professionals before making
financial decisions.

## Contact

Questions about our disclosure policy? Contact us at [EMAIL].
```

## 10. Common Mistakes to Avoid

### DO NOT:

- Bury disclosures at the bottom of posts
- Use unclear abbreviations (e.g., "sp" for sponsored)
- Hide disclosures among many hashtags
- Use tiny fonts or low-contrast text
- Rely on platform disclosure tools alone
- Assume viewers will click to see "more"
- Place disclosures only in bios or about pages
- Use vague language ("thanks to Brand" without clarifying relationship)

### DO:

- Place disclosures before or at the beginning of content
- Use clear language ("ad," "sponsored," "affiliate")
- Make disclosures visually prominent
- Disclose in the same format as the content (verbal in video, written in text)
- Repeat disclosures in longer content
- Update disclosures when relationships change

## 11. Enforcement and Penalties

### FTC Enforcement

The FTC can take action against:
- Advertisers/brands
- Endorsers/influencers
- Affiliate marketers

### Potential Consequences

- Cease and desist letters
- Required corrective advertising
- Civil penalties (up to $50,000+ per violation)
- Consent orders restricting future conduct
- Consumer redress requirements

### Protect Yourself

1. Always disclose material connections
2. Document your disclosures
3. Review FTC guidelines regularly
4. Train anyone creating content for you
5. When in doubt, disclose more, not less
