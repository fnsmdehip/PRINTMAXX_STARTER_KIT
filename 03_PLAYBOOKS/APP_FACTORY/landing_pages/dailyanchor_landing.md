## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: How long does DailyAnchor take each morning?**
A: 5 minutes average. Some users do it in 3, some take 10. You set your own pace. The app doesn't time you.

**Q: What Bible translation do you use?**
A: ESV by default. NIV and KJV available in settings. More coming based on requests.

**Q: Can I customize the prayer prompts?**
A: Yes. Default categories are Thanks, Confession, Requests, Others. You can edit, add, or remove any prompt.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Your streak resets but your history stays. Many users use "streak anxiety" as motivation, but if you miss a day, just start again tomorrow. Grace applies here too.

**Q: Can I use this with my spouse or small group?**
A: Individual use for now. We're building a "shared reflections" feature for couples and groups. Coming in 2025.

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Do you read my gratitudes and prayers?**
A: No. Your reflections are stored only on your device by default. Optional cloud backup (for device transfers) is encrypted and private.

**Q: Is my data sold?**
A: Never. We make money from subscriptions. Your spiritual life is not a product.

**Q: Can I export my journal entries?**
A: Yes. Export to PDF or plain text anytime. Your reflections belong to you.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How does the 14-day trial work?**
A: Full access to everything for 14 days. After that, choose a plan or the app becomes read-only (you can still see your history but can't add new entries).

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes. Monthly to annual prorated. Annual to lifetime also prorated. Email support for help.

**Q: Is there a family plan?**
A: Coming soon. For now, each family member needs their own subscription.

**Q: Do you offer church bulk pricing?**
A: Yes. Email churches@dailyanchor.app for group pricing. Many churches use DailyAnchor for congregation-wide challenges.
