## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: What denomination is this written from?**
A: Broadly evangelical, but written to welcome Christians from all backgrounds. We focus on scripture, not denominational distinctives. Users include Baptists, Presbyterians, Methodists, Catholics, and non-denominational believers.

**Q: Who writes the devotionals?**
A: A team of pastors, theologians, and Christian writers. All have seminary training or equivalent ministry experience. We don't use AI-generated content.

**Q: How is this different from a Bible app?**
A: Bible apps give you the text. We give you guided reflection. Think of it like the difference between reading a book alone and having a study group discuss it with you.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Yesterday's devotional stays available for 24 hours. Beyond that, you need Premium for the archive. But honestly, just read today's. Grace applies.

**Q: Is there a reading plan or can I go at my own pace?**
A: Just daily readings, no mandatory plan. Each devotional stands alone. You never feel "behind."

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Do you track what I read?**
A: We track which devotionals you've opened for your streak count and favorites. We don't analyze your reading patterns or sell data.

**Q: Are my notes private?**
A: Completely. Stored on your device. Never uploaded unless you choose to back them up (encrypted, private).

**Q: Do you share data with churches?**
A: Never without explicit permission. Some churches use DailyDevotion for congregation-wide campaigns, but individual reading data is never shared.

### Billing Questions

**Q: Can I try Premium before paying?**
A: Yes. 14-day free trial with full Premium access. No credit card required to start.

**Q: What happens when my subscription ends?**
A: You keep access to today's devotional (always free). Archive, audio, and premium features lock until you resubscribe. Your notes and favorites stay saved.

**Q: Is there church group pricing?**
A: Yes. Email churches@dailydevotion.app for bulk licensing. Many churches subscribe for their entire congregation.

**Q: Student discount?**
A: Yes. Email support@dailydevotion.app with your .edu email for 50% off annual.
