## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: What if I need to use a blocked app for an emergency?**
A: There's a hidden emergency unlock in settings. You type "I am breaking my commitment" to unlock immediately. It resets your streak and logs the bypass so you're accountable. We made it intentionally inconvenient.

**Q: Which apps can I block?**
A: Any app on your phone. Most users block social media (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook), games, YouTube, and news apps. You pick what distracts you most.

**Q: Does it actually work, or will I just disable it after a week?**
A: 73% of users who complete the 3-day trial are still active 90 days later. The streak psychology is powerful. Once you hit 7 days, you don't want to start over.

**Q: What Bible translation do you use?**
A: ESV by default, with NIV and KJV options. More translations coming based on user requests.

**Q: Can I customize when the lock resets?**
A: Yes. Default is 5am. You can set any time that matches when you wake up.

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Do you read my prayers or track what I type?**
A: No. We track only: completion timestamps, streak count, and app settings. No prayer content, no personal data, no selling to third parties.

**Q: Does PrayerLock have access to my other apps?**
A: We use accessibility services to detect when you try to open blocked apps and redirect you to PrayerLock. We cannot see your data inside those apps.

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
A: Yes. All data stored locally on your device with encryption. We don't have servers storing your information.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: 3 days of full access. After that, you'll see a paywall. If you don't subscribe, the app stops blocking but keeps your streak history.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel any time through the App Store or Google Play. Annual subscribers can cancel before renewal.

**Q: What happens to my streak if I cancel?**
A: Your streak data stays on your device. If you resubscribe later, it picks up where you left off.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. Email support@prayerlock.app and we'll process it within 48 hours.
