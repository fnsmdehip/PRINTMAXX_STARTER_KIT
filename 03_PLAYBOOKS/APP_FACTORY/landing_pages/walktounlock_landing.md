## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: What if I can't walk due to weather?**
A: Steps from treadmills count. So do indoor walks at malls or around your house. If you genuinely can't walk (sick, injured), use the emergency bypass and adjust your goal when you recover.

**Q: Can people cheat by shaking their phone?**
A: We use your health app data, which has motion sensors that distinguish walking from shaking. Could someone technically fake it? Maybe. But you're only cheating yourself.

**Q: What apps can I block?**
A: Any app on your phone. Most users block social media, games, YouTube, and streaming apps. Phone, Messages, and Maps stay accessible by default.

**Q: Does it drain my battery?**
A: Minimal impact. We check your steps every 5 minutes in the background. Users report less than 2% additional battery usage.

**Q: What if my step count doesn't sync?**
A: Pull down to refresh on the home screen. If issues persist, check that WalkToUnlock has permission to read your health data in settings.

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Do you track my location?**
A: No. We only read step count from your health app. No GPS, no location history, no tracking where you walk.

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: Step count syncs, streak data, and app settings. We don't store your walking routes, health records, or personal information.

**Q: Is my health data shared with anyone?**
A: Never. Your step data stays on your device and is used only to calculate whether you've hit your goal.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: 3 days of full access. Your apps get blocked, you walk to unlock them. After 3 days, subscribe to continue or the app stops blocking.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: You can cancel anytime. Your streak history stays saved. If you resubscribe later, you can pick up where you left off.

**Q: Is the annual plan refundable?**
A: Yes, within 30 days. After that, you can cancel but won't receive a partial refund for remaining months.

**Q: Do you have a student discount?**
A: Email support@walktounlock.app with your .edu email for 50% off annual.
