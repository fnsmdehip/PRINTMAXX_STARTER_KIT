## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: Do I have to track my cycle?**
A: No. Cycle-sync is completely optional. If you enable it, workouts adjust to your phase. If you don't, you still get great workouts based on your goals and schedule.

**Q: What if I'm post-menopausal?**
A: Cycle-sync isn't relevant, but the rest of the app works perfectly. We have programs specifically designed for women 50+ with different hormonal considerations.

**Q: What equipment do I need?**
A: You can filter for no-equipment, minimal (dumbbells + mat), or full gym. All programs have equipment alternatives so you're never stuck.

**Q: How long are the workouts?**
A: You choose: 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes. The app builds your program around your available time.

**Q: Are the trainers certified?**
A: Yes. All trainers are certified personal trainers with specializations in women's fitness, pre/postnatal, and cycle-synced training.

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Is my cycle data private?**
A: Completely. Stored only on your device. Never shared with anyone. We don't even have access to it.

**Q: What about my progress photos?**
A: Stored locally on your device only. They never upload to our servers. Your body is your business.

**Q: Do you sell data to advertisers?**
A: No. We make money from subscriptions. Your data is yours.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: 7 days of full access. After that, choose a plan or the app becomes view-only (you can see your history but not access new workouts).

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Annual and monthly can be cancelled anytime. We don't offer pauses, but you can cancel and resubscribe later without losing your history.

**Q: Is there a student discount?**
A: Yes. Email support@femfit.app with your .edu email for 40% off annual.

**Q: Do you have corporate wellness pricing?**
