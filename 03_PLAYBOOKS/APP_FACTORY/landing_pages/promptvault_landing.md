**Q: How often do you add new prompts?**

**Q: Can I submit my own prompts?**
A: Coming soon. We're building a community submission feature where you can submit prompts and earn credit if they're accepted.

**Q: What categories do you have?**
A: Writing (emails, content, social), Coding (debug, explain, generate), Marketing (ads, SEO, copy), Analysis (data, research, summary), Creative (stories, ideas, brainstorm), Business (proposals, planning, strategy), Productivity (tasks, notes, organization), Learning (explain, teach, quiz), Career (resume, interview, negotiation).

**Q: What does the Prompt Improver do?**
A: Paste your own prompt and our AI analyzes it, then rewrites it to be clearer, more specific, and more likely to get good outputs. Shows you what was changed and why.

**Q: Is my payment info secure?**
