## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: What if I get an important text during a study session?**
A: Phone, Messages, and essential apps stay accessible by default. You can customize which apps never get blocked.

**Q: Can I end a session early if I need to?**
A: You can end sessions, but it breaks your streak and logs as "incomplete." Most users say the fear of breaking their streak keeps them going.

**Q: What's Pomodoro mode?**
A: 25 minutes of focus, then a 5-minute break where apps unlock. After 4 cycles, you get a longer 15-minute break. Based on the Pomodoro Technique used by productivity experts.

**Q: Does it work on computers too?**
A: Currently mobile only. We're building a browser extension that syncs with your mobile sessions.

**Q: Can my parents control my settings?**
A: Yes. There's a parental mode where a parent can set the minimum session length and blocked apps. Student can add more blocks but can't remove parent-set ones.

### Privacy & Security Questions

**Q: Do you track what I'm studying?**
A: No. We track session times and streak data only. We don't know what subject you're studying or what's on your screen.

**Q: Is my data sold to anyone?**
A: Never. We make money from subscriptions, not data.

**Q: Can my school see my study data?**
A: No. Your data is private to you unless you choose to share screenshots.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How do I get the student discount?**
A: During checkout, enter your .edu email. We verify it and apply the discount automatically.

**Q: What happens after the free trial?**
A: After 7 days, you'll see the subscription options. If you don't subscribe, the app stops blocking but your history stays saved.

**Q: Can I share with roommates?**
A: Each account is individual. Your roommates should download their own. Family plan coming soon.

**Q: Do you offer school bulk pricing?**
A: Yes. Email schools@studylock.app for institutional pricing.
