# Affiliate Tier System

Complete breakdown of Starter, Pro, and Elite affiliate tiers. Commission rates, requirements, and benefits.

---

## Tier overview

| Tier | Requirement | First Payment | Recurring | Min Payout | Payment |
|------|-------------|---------------|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Starter** | Default (all affiliates) | 20% | 10% for 12 mo | $25 | Monthly |
| **Pro** | 10+ sales/month | 25% | 12% for 12 mo | $10 | Monthly |
| **Elite** | 50+ sales/month | 30% | 15% for 12 mo | None | Weekly |

---

## Starter tier

### Who qualifies

Everyone. All new affiliates start here.

### Commission structure

```
First payment:  20% of transaction
Recurring:      10% monthly for 12 months
```

### Example earnings (for $9.99/mo product)

| Event | Affiliate earns |
|-------|-----------------|
| Customer pays first month ($9.99) | $2.00 |
| Customer pays month 2 ($9.99) | $1.00 |
| Customer pays month 3 ($9.99) | $1.00 |
| ... | ... |
| Customer pays month 12 ($9.99) | $1.00 |
| **Total over 12 months** | **$13.00** |

### Payment terms

- Minimum payout: $25
- Payment frequency: Monthly (1st of each month)
- Payment method: PayPal
- Payout delay: 30 days after sale (refund window)

### Benefits

- Unique affiliate link
- Access to swipe files and assets
- Dashboard with real-time stats
- Email support
- Affiliate Discord access

### How to advance

Hit 10 or more paid conversions in a single calendar month to automatically upgrade to Pro tier.

## Pro tier

### Who qualifies

Affiliates who generate 10+ paid conversions in a calendar month.

### Commission structure

```
First payment:  25% of transaction
Recurring:      12% monthly for 12 months
```

### Example earnings (for $9.99/mo product)

| Event | Affiliate earns |
|-------|-----------------|
| Customer pays first month ($9.99) | $2.50 |
| Customer pays month 2 ($9.99) | $1.20 |
| Customer pays month 3 ($9.99) | $1.20 |
| ... | ... |
| Customer pays month 12 ($9.99) | $1.20 |
| **Total over 12 months** | **$15.70** |

### Upgrade math

At Pro tier with 15 sales/month:
- Month 1: 15 x $2.50 = $37.50
- Plus recurring from previous months
- Potential: $200-500+/month with consistency

### Payment terms

- Minimum payout: $10
- Payment frequency: Monthly (1st of each month)
- Payment method: PayPal or Wise
- Payout delay: 30 days after sale

### Benefits (everything from Starter plus)

- Lower payout threshold ($10 vs $25)
- Priority email support (24-hour response)
- Monthly strategy call access
- Early access to new features
- Featured in affiliate showcase (optional)
- Co-branded landing page option

### Maintaining Pro status

Pro tier evaluates monthly. If you drop below 10 sales:
- First month below: Warning, keep Pro status
- Second consecutive month below: Revert to Starter

### How to advance

Hit 50 or more paid conversions in a single calendar month to automatically upgrade to Elite tier.

## Elite tier

### Who qualifies

Affiliates who generate 50+ paid conversions in a calendar month.

### Commission structure

```
First payment:  30% of transaction
Recurring:      15% monthly for 12 months
```

### Example earnings (for $9.99/mo product)

| Event | Affiliate earns |
|-------|-----------------|
| Customer pays first month ($9.99) | $3.00 |
| Customer pays month 2 ($9.99) | $1.50 |
| Customer pays month 3 ($9.99) | $1.50 |
| ... | ... |
| Customer pays month 12 ($9.99) | $1.50 |
| **Total over 12 months** | **$19.50** |

### Upgrade math

At Elite tier with 75 sales/month:
- Month 1: 75 x $3.00 = $225
- Plus recurring from previous months
- Potential: $1,000-3,000+/month with consistency

### Payment terms

- Minimum payout: None
- Payment frequency: Weekly (every Friday)
- Payment method: PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer
- Payout delay: 7 days after sale

### Benefits (everything from Pro plus)

- No minimum payout
- Weekly payments
- Direct line to founder (Slack/Discord DM)
- Custom affiliate links and promo codes
- Higher tier promo codes (15% off for their audience)
- Custom co-branded assets
- Quarterly bonus pool (share of 5% of affiliate revenue)
- Beta testing access for new products
- Referral bonus (earn when you recruit affiliates who convert)
- Annual Elite retreat/meetup (if in-person)

### Maintaining Elite status

Elite tier evaluates monthly. If you drop below 50 sales:
- First month below: Keep Elite status
- Second month below: Revert to Pro
- Third consecutive month below Pro requirements: Revert to Starter

### Grandfather clause

Affiliates who maintain Elite for 6+ consecutive months get "Elite Permanent" status:
- Keep 30%/15% rates even if volume drops temporarily
- 90-day grace period instead of 30-day
- Automatic reinstatement if volume returns within 6 months

## How tiers are calculated

### Evaluation period

- Tiers evaluate on the last day of each month
- Sales counted: Paid conversions attributed to affiliate
- Refunds: Don't count toward tier qualification
- Chargebacks: Deducted from count

### Tier changes

- Upgrades: Effective immediately (new rates on next sale)
- Downgrades: After grace period (see maintaining status above)
- Notification: Email sent same day as tier change

### Rate application

- New tier rates apply to new sales only
- Existing recurring commissions stay at original rate
- Example: If you were Starter when customer signed up, you get 10% recurring even if you're now Pro

## FAQ

**Q: Can I start at a higher tier?**
No. Everyone starts at Starter. Tiers are earned through performance.

**Q: Do refunds affect my tier?**
Yes. Refunded sales don't count toward your monthly total.

**Q: What if I have a big month, then slow down?**
You get a grace period before tier downgrade. See "maintaining status" for each tier.

**Q: Do bonuses stack with tier rates?**
Yes. Elite + video bonus = 35% first payment for 30 days.

**Q: Can I negotiate custom rates?**
For high-volume affiliates (100+ sales/month consistently), contact us to discuss custom arrangements.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
Starter/Pro: Monthly on the 1st
Elite: Weekly on Fridays

**Q: How do I check my tier?**
Dashboard shows current tier, progress to next tier, and days remaining in evaluation period.
