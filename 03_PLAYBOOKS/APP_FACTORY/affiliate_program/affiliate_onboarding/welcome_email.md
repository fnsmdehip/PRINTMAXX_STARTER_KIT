## Email 2: Getting started (Day 1)

- 3 ways to get your first sale this week

**Body:**

```

Quick follow-up with 3 content ideas that convert:

3. "APPS I USE DAILY" (highest volume)
Create a list of your favorite apps. Include [App Name] with your affiliate link. This format gets shared a lot.

All three scripts are in your swipe file: [link]

Your stats so far:

The best affiliates post 2-3 times per week. Start with one this week and see what resonates.

Questions? Reply here.

[Your name]
```

## Email 3: Best practices (Day 3)

**Send:** 72 hours after welcome email

**Subject line options:**
- What top affiliates do differently
- Avoid these 3 mistakes (they kill conversions)
- Pro tips from our top earners

**Body:**

```
Hey [First Name],

Sharing what separates top-performing affiliates from the rest.

DO THIS

- Show the app in use (don't just talk about it)
- Be specific about one feature or benefit
- Post when your audience is most active
- Use your promo code in captions (people love discounts)
- Reply to comments and DMs about the app

AVOID THIS

- Generic "download this app" posts (low conversion)
- Forgetting FTC disclosure (add #ad or #affiliate)
- Only posting once and giving up (consistency wins)
- Hiding your link in bio without mentioning it

TOP PERFORMER INSIGHT

@[topaffiliate] earned $1,247 last month. Their strategy: one short video per day for a week during their launch, then 2x per week ongoing. The initial burst builds momentum.

Your current stats:
- Link clicks: [X]
- Signups: [X]
- Sales: [X]

Need custom assets or have content ideas? Reply and we'll help.

[Your name]
```

## Subject line testing notes

Test these variations:
- Direct (You're in!) vs. benefit (Start earning today)
- Personal (Hey [Name]) vs. brand ([App Name] Affiliate Program)
- Question (Ready to earn?) vs. statement (Your link is ready)
- Numbers ($200-2000/month) vs. no numbers

Track open rates to optimize over time.
