## How to promote

### Best platforms for [App Name]

**Highest conversion:**
1. TikTok (video tutorials)
2. Instagram Reels (same content, repurposed)
3. YouTube (reviews, comparisons)

**Good for volume:**
4. Twitter/X (thread breakdowns)
5. Email newsletters
6. Blog posts (SEO long-term)

**Lower but steady:**
7. Pinterest (visual guides)
8. Facebook groups (if allowed)
9. Discord/Slack communities

### Content that converts

**Video content (highest conversion)**

Format: 30-60 second screen recording
Hook: Problem statement or curiosity gap
Body: Show the app solving the problem
CTA: "Link in bio" or verbal mention of promo code

Example script:
```
[Hook - 3 seconds]
"This app changed how I [benefit]"

[Problem - 5 seconds]
"I used to struggle with [problem]"

[Solution - 20 seconds]
"Now I use [App Name] and here's what it does..."
[Show screen recording of key feature]

[Result - 5 seconds]
"Now I [outcome] every [timeframe]"

[CTA - 3 seconds]
"Link in bio if you want to try it. Code [YOUR_CODE]10 for 10% off."
```

**Written content (good for SEO/email)**

Format: Problem-solution-recommendation
Length: 300-500 words for social, 1000+ for blog

Structure:
1. Hook with relatable problem
2. Agitate (why existing solutions fail)
3. Introduce [App Name] as solution
4. Specific feature/benefit
5. Call to action with link

**List content (high engagement)**

Format: "5 apps I use for [goal]"
Include [App Name] in position 1-3
Link in comments or bio
Works well on all platforms

### Posting frequency

Minimum: 1x per week
Recommended: 2-3x per week
Aggressive: Daily for launch, then 3x/week ongoing

Consistency beats volume. Pick a schedule you can maintain.

### Best times to post

**TikTok:** 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-9 PM (user's local time)
**Instagram:** 11 AM - 1 PM, 7-9 PM
**Twitter:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
**YouTube:** Thursday-Sunday (upload), any time (post)

Test and check your analytics for your specific audience.

## FTC disclosure requirements

### The rule

You must disclose that you earn commission when promoting [App Name]. This is federal law (FTC), not optional.

### How to disclose

**TikTok/Instagram Reels:**
- Say it verbally: "This is an affiliate link"
- OR add text on screen: "#ad" or "#affiliate"
- OR include in caption (first 3 lines): "#ad"

**Instagram posts/stories:**
- Caption: "#ad" or "#affiliate" visible without clicking "more"
- Stories: Use "Paid partnership" tag or text overlay

**YouTube:**
- Verbal: "This video contains affiliate links" in first 30 seconds
- Check "Video contains paid promotion" in upload settings
- Description: "Affiliate links below"

**Twitter:**
- Include "#ad" in tweet
- Or: "affiliate link" before/after the URL

**Blog:**
- Disclosure at top of post: "This post contains affiliate links"
- Link to full affiliate disclosure page

### Disclosure examples

Good:
```
"I'm an affiliate for [App Name], which means I earn a small commission if you sign up through my link. Doesn't cost you extra."
```

Also acceptable:
```
"#ad"
"#affiliate"
"Affiliate link"
"Paid partnership with [App Name]"
```

Not acceptable:
```
- Hiding disclosure after 10+ hashtags
- Tiny text at end of video
- "Thanks to [App Name]" without mentioning affiliate relationship
- No disclosure at all
```
