# Blogger Pitch Templates

Email templates for recruiting bloggers as affiliates. Focus on SEO content creators.

---

## Why target bloggers

- Long-form content = higher intent clicks
- Evergreen articles drive traffic for years
- SEO rankings compound over time
- Often have established affiliate income (know the model)
- Can create comparison/review content
- Email lists for secondary distribution

---

## Finding blogger contacts

### Where to look

1. **Google search:**
   - "best [category] apps"
   - "[competitor] review"
   - "[category] comparison"
   - Check page 2-3 results (less competitive, more responsive)

2. **Affiliate disclosure check:**
   - Look for "affiliate disclosure" link in footer
   - These bloggers already do affiliate marketing
   - Familiar with the model, easier sell

3. **Contact page:**
   - Most bloggers have "Work with me" or "Contact" page
   - Use that email, not generic contact form

4. **About page:**
   - Sometimes includes personal email
   - Check social links for DM option

## Email templates

### Template 1: Review request

**Subject:** Review opportunity: [App Name] - [category] app

```
Hi [Name],

I found your blog while researching [topic] content - your article on [specific article title] was really helpful.

I'm reaching out because I run [App Name], a [category] app that [one-line description]. I think it could be a good fit for your readers who are looking for [relevant solution].

Would you be interested in:

1. Free premium access to review the app
2. Affiliate partnership (20-30% commission per sale)
3. Access to screenshots, specs, and anything else you need for a review

No pressure to write anything positive - an honest review is totally fine. If it's not a fit for your audience, just let me know.

Here's more info about [App Name]: [link]

Thanks for considering,
[Your name]
[App Name]
```

## What to offer bloggers

### Standard package

- Free lifetime premium access
- 25% commission per sale
- 30-day cookie
- Pre-made graphics (banner sizes for sidebar)
- Product screenshots and specs

### Premium package (for high-DA blogs)

- 30% commission
- 90-day cookie
- Custom landing page for their audience
- Exclusive discount code
- Content collaboration (co-authored piece)
- Feature in "as seen on" section

### Content support

Offer to provide:
- Product screenshots (all screens)
- Feature list with descriptions
- Pricing breakdown
- Comparison points vs competitors
- FAQ for common questions
- Quote from founder for article

## Article types that convert

### Highest converting

1. **"[App Name] review"**
   - High intent readers
   - Already considering purchase
   - Conversion rate: 3-5%

2. **"[App Name] vs [Competitor]"**
   - Comparison shoppers
   - Decision-stage content
   - Conversion rate: 2-4%

3. **"Best [category] apps"**
   - Discovery content
   - Higher volume, lower conversion
   - Conversion rate: 1-2%

### Good for volume

4. **"How to [goal] with [App Name]"**
   - Tutorial content
   - SEO long-tail
   - Conversion rate: 1-2%

5. **"[App Name] alternatives"**
   - Captures competitor traffic
   - Position as alternative
   - Conversion rate: 1-3%

### Suggest these angles to bloggers who ask for content ideas.

## Qualifying bloggers

### Green flags

- Existing affiliate disclosures (they know the model)
- Regular posting schedule
- Domain Authority 20+ (Moz, Ahrefs)
- Traffic visible in SimilarWeb
- Email list mentioned
- Social following for promotion
- Content quality is decent

### Red flags

- No posts in 6+ months (abandoned)
- Thin/spammy content
- No contact information
- Excessive affiliate links everywhere
- Very low DA (<10)
- No social proof

### Tier bloggers by priority

**High priority:**
- DA 40+
- Active posting
- Email list 1k+
- Already rank for relevant keywords

**Medium priority:**
- DA 20-40
- Some relevant content
- Active in last 3 months

**Low priority:**
- DA <20
- New blogs
- No relevant existing content

## Email timing tips

**Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**Best times:** 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (their timezone)
**Avoid:** Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend mode)

**Volume:**
- Personalize each email (2-3 minutes)
- Send 10-20 per day max
- Track responses to optimize
