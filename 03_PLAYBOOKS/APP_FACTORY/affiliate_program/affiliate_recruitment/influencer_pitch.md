# Influencer Pitch Templates

Outreach templates for social media influencers (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter). Organized by follower size.

---

## Micro-influencers (1k-10k followers)

### Why target them

- High engagement rates (5-10%)
- Responsive to DMs
- Hungry for monetization
- Authentic connection with audience
- Lower expectations (free product often enough)

### DM template

```
Hey [Name]!

Just watched your [recent video/post] about [specific topic] - loved the part where [specific detail].

I'm building [App Name], a [category] app for [audience]. Think your followers would dig it.

Would you want to try it free? If you like it, we can set you up with an affiliate link (20% per sale).

No pressure to post anything. Just thought you'd genuinely find it useful.

Lmk!
```

### What to offer

- Free premium access
- 20% affiliate commission
- Custom promo code (10% off for their audience)
- Swipe files if they want them

### Volume approach

- Target: 200 DMs
- Expected response rate: 10-15%
- Expected affiliate conversion: 40-50% of responders
- Time: 2-3 minutes per DM (personalization)

## Mid-influencers (10k-100k followers)

### Why target them

- Balance of reach and engagement
- Often looking for brand deals
- More professional, check DMs/email regularly
- Can move meaningful volume

### DM template

```
Hey [Name],

[Personalized opener - specific content reference]

I run [App Name] - [one sentence description]. We're building out our affiliate program and you're exactly who I had in mind.

Quick pitch:
- 25% commission per sale
- Custom code for your audience (they save 10%)
- Pre-made content (scripts, graphics) if helpful
- Free premium forever

Interested in chatting?

[Your name]
```

### Email template

**Subject:** Partnership: [App Name] x [Their Name]

```
Hi [Name],

[Personalized opener - reference specific content, be genuine]

I'm [Your name], founder of [App Name]. We make [description + key benefit].

Your content style matches perfectly with what we're doing, so I wanted to reach out about a potential partnership.

What I'm proposing:

- 25% commission on every sale you refer
- Custom promo code (your audience saves 10%)
- Free lifetime premium access
- Pre-made content assets (optional but available)
- Dedicated support for any questions

We've worked with [other creator if applicable] and it's been great for both sides.

Would you be open to trying the app and seeing if it's a fit?

[Your name]
[App Name]
[Link]
```

### What to offer

- Free lifetime premium
- 25% commission (Pro tier equivalent)
- Custom promo code
- Custom assets if requested
- Potential for featured spotlight

## Macro-influencers (100k-500k followers)

### Why target them

- Significant reach
- Established monetization expectations
- Often have managers/agents
- Can be expensive, but affiliate is performance-based

### Approach differences

- More likely to respond to email than DM
- May require rate card discussion
- Often want hybrid (flat fee + affiliate)
- Longer decision timeline

### Email template

**Subject:** Collab opportunity - [App Name]

```
Hi [Name/Team],

[Personalized opener - specific content reference with genuine detail]

I'm reaching out from [App Name], a [category] app with [X users/downloads/social proof].

We're exploring partnerships with creators in the [niche] space and your content stood out.

What we're offering:

Option A: Pure affiliate
- 30% commission per sale
- Custom landing page
- Dedicated promo code
- Pre-made content assets

Option B: Hybrid (if you typically work with flat fees)
- $[X] flat fee for [deliverable]
- Plus 20% commission ongoing
- Open to discussing what works for you

Happy to share more details or hop on a quick call if easier.

Best,
[Your name]
Founder, [App Name]
[Email] | [Phone if comfortable]
```

### What to offer

- Elite tier commission (30%)
- Hybrid option (flat fee + affiliate)
- Co-branded landing page
- Priority support
- Potential for equity/advisory (for very large creators)

## Platform-specific tips

### TikTok DMs

- Keep under 300 characters for first message
- Reference their most recent video (shows it's not mass blast)
- Don't include links in first DM (can be flagged)
- Follow up with link after they respond

### Instagram DMs

- Can be slightly longer
- Don't send on posts with huge engagement (gets buried)
- Story replies can work well (feels more personal)
- Check if they have linktree with email (might prefer email)

### Twitter/X DMs

- Must be mutual followers OR they have open DMs
- Quote tweet their content first to get on radar
- Keep very short (Twitter culture)
- Link to app in second message

### LinkedIn (for B2B apps)

- More formal tone
- Include mutual connections if any
- Focus on professional benefits
- InMail for non-connections (sparingly)

## Objection handling

### "I don't do affiliate deals"

```
Totally understand. Would a flat rate make more sense? Or I can just send you free access with no strings - if you end up mentioning it naturally, great. If not, no worries.
```

### "I need to see the product first"

```
100%. Here's free premium access: [CODE]. Take your time, and if it's a fit, let me know.
```

### "What's your budget?"

```
We're doing affiliate (20-30% per sale) rather than flat fee since we're early stage. Some creators prefer this since there's no cap on earnings. But open to discussing what works for you.
```

### "I need to check with my manager"

```
Of course. Happy to connect with them directly. What's the best email?
```

### "I'm too busy right now"

```
Totally get it. Mind if I follow up in [timeframe]? No pressure, just want to keep it on the radar.
```
