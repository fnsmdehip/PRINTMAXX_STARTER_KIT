# General Outreach Template

Universal template for affiliate recruitment. Adapt for any platform or creator type.

---

## Template structure

Every outreach message needs:
1. Personal opener (shows you did research)
2. Value proposition (what's in it for them)
3. Social proof (why they should trust you)
4. Low-friction ask (easy to say yes)

---

## DM template (short)

**Use for:** Instagram DMs, Twitter DMs, TikTok DMs

```
Hey [Name],

Loved your [specific recent post/video] about [topic]. [One specific detail that shows you watched it].

I'm reaching out because I run [App Name] - a [category] app that [one-line description].

We're building an affiliate program and I think you'd be a great fit. [X]% commission per sale + free premium access.

Would you be open to trying the app and potentially promoting it if you like it?

No pressure either way.

[Your name]
```

**Character count:** ~400 (fits most DM limits)

---

## Email template (standard)

**Use for:** Bloggers, YouTubers, newsletter creators with public emails

**Subject lines:**
```
A. Partnership opportunity - [App Name] x [Their Name]
B. Would you be open to a collab?
C. [Specific thing you liked] + a question
```

**Body:**

```
Hi [Name],

[Personalized opener - reference specific content they made, be specific]

I'm [Your name], founder of [App Name]. We make [one sentence description].

Reaching out because your content on [their niche] aligns perfectly with our audience.

Here's what I'm thinking:

- Free lifetime premium access to [App Name]
- [X]% commission on every sale you refer
- Custom promo code for your audience (they save, you earn)
- Pre-made content assets (swipes, scripts, graphics) if helpful

Not looking for a hard pitch. If you try the app and don't like it, no worries. But if you do, I'd love to have you as a partner.

Would you be open to giving it a try?

[Your name]
[App Name]
[Link to app]

P.S. [Optional: mention a specific piece of their content that relates to your app's use case]
```

## Variations by temperature

### Cold (never interacted before)

Keep it short. Respect their time. Make the ask low-friction.

```
Hey [Name],

Quick one - I run [App Name], a [category] app.

Your content on [topic] caught my attention. Thought you might like it.

Want me to send you free access to try it? If you like it, we have an affiliate program.

No pressure.

[Your name]
```

### Warm (engaged with their content)

Reference your engagement history.

```
Hey [Name],

We've been following each other for a bit - I always enjoy your [content type] posts.

Finally reaching out because I think there's a fit here.

I run [App Name]. We do [description].

Would you be interested in trying it? I can set you up with premium access and if you like it, we can talk affiliate partnership.

Let me know.

[Your name]
```

### Hot (they've mentioned you or shown interest)

Be direct. They already have context.

```
Hey [Name],

Saw you mention [app/category] in your [post/video].

I'm the founder of [App Name] - we do exactly that.

Want to give you free access and set you up as an affiliate. [X]% commission + exclusive promo code for your audience.

Let me know if you're interested.

[Your name]
```

## Follow-up sequence

If no response after initial outreach:

### Follow-up 1 (3 days later)

```
Hey [Name],

Following up on my message from a few days ago about [App Name].

Totally understand if you're busy or not interested - just wanted to make sure it didn't get buried.

Let me know either way?

[Your name]
```

### Follow-up 2 (7 days after first follow-up)

```
Hey [Name],

Last follow-up, I promise.

If [App Name] isn't a fit for you right now, no worries at all.

But if you're open to trying it, the offer stands: free premium access + [X]% affiliate commission.

Either way, keep up the great content.

[Your name]
```

### After 2 follow-ups

Stop. If they haven't responded, they're not interested. Don't burn the bridge.

---

## Response handling

### If they say yes

```
Awesome! Here's what I'll send over:

1. Premium access code: [CODE]
2. Your affiliate link: [LINK]
3. Swipe files (scripts, captions, graphics): [LINK]

Feel free to try the app first and promote only if you genuinely like it. No rush.

Any questions, just reply here.
```

### If they ask for more info

```
Happy to share more.

[App Name] is [2-3 sentence description including key feature and benefit].

We've got [X] users and [social proof point].

For affiliates:
- [X]% commission per sale
- [X] day cookie
- Monthly PayPal payments
- Free swipe files and assets

Want me to send you access to try it?
```

### If they say no

```
No problem at all. Thanks for letting me know.

If anything changes in the future, the offer's always open.

Keep making great content.
```

### If they ghost

Don't take it personally. Move on after 2 follow-ups.

## What NOT to do

**Don't:**
- Send copy-paste messages with no personalization
- Be pushy or follow up more than twice
- Promise things you can't deliver
- Lie about your app's features or traction
- Spam their DMs or email

**Don't say:**
- "I've been a huge fan forever" (unless true)
- "This will change your life"
- "You'd be perfect for this" (without specifics)
- "Quick question" (then send long pitch)
- "Don't mean to bother you but..."

## Volume approach

For recruiting 100+ affiliates:

**Target numbers:**
- Send 200 DMs to micro-influencers (1k-10k followers)
- Send 100 DMs to mid-influencers (10k-100k followers)
- Send 50 emails to bloggers
- Send 50 emails to YouTubers

**Expected results:**
- Response rate: 5-15%
- Conversion to affiliate: 30-50% of responders
- Target: 50-100 affiliates

**Time investment:**
- Personalization: 2-3 minutes per message
- 400 messages: ~15-20 hours
- Spread over 2-4 weeks
