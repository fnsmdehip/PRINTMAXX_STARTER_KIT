## Customization tips

### Personalize these sections

1. **Your specific results** - Don't use generic claims. Share your actual numbers.
2. **Your use case** - How you specifically use the app matters to your audience.
3. **Your voice** - Rewrite in your natural style. These are templates, not scripts.
4. **Your audience's problems** - Adjust pain points to match what your list cares about.

### What to keep

1. **FTC disclosure** - Required. Don't remove.
2. **Affiliate link** - Obviously.
3. **Promo code** - Increases conversion.
4. **P.S. line** - Second most-read part of emails.

### Avoid

1. **Hype words** - "Revolutionary," "game-changer," etc.
2. **False claims** - Only share results you actually got.
3. **Pressure tactics** - No fake urgency or scarcity.
4. **Over-promising** - Set realistic expectations.
