# Video Script Templates

Ready-to-film video scripts for TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, and YouTube. Customize and record.

---

## Format overview

| Format | Length | Platform |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Hook video | 15-30s | TikTok, Reels, Shorts |
| Demo video | 30-60s | TikTok, Reels, Shorts |
| Review video | 2-5 min | YouTube |
| Comparison video | 3-7 min | YouTube |

---

## Script 1: Quick hook (15 seconds)

**Best for:** TikTok, Reels, Shorts
**Style:** Face to camera, fast cuts

```
[HOOK - 3 seconds]
(Look at camera, slightly surprised/excited)
"This app just saved me [X hours/dollars/effort]"

[PROBLEM - 3 seconds]
"I used to [pain point]"

[SOLUTION - 5 seconds]
"Now I just [show app briefly on screen]"
(Quick screen recording overlay)

[CTA - 4 seconds]
"Link in bio if you want it"
"Code [PROMO] for 10% off"
(Point to bio or text on screen)
```

**Text overlays:**
- Hook: "[X] hours saved"
- Solution: "[App Name]"
- CTA: "Link in bio | Code: [PROMO]"

## Script 2: Before/after demo (30 seconds)

**Best for:** TikTok, Reels, Shorts
**Style:** Split screen or sequence

```
[BEFORE - 10 seconds]
(Show the old/frustrating way)
"This is how I used to [task]"
(Demonstrate pain: slow, messy, frustrating)
"Took me [X minutes/hours] every [time period]"

[TRANSITION - 3 seconds]
"Then I found [App Name]"
(Transition effect or quick cut)

[AFTER - 12 seconds]
(Show app in action)
"Now I just do this"
(Screen recording of app solving the problem)
"Done in [X seconds/minutes]"

[CTA - 5 seconds]
"Free trial link in bio"
"I'm an affiliate, so full disclosure there"
```

**Text overlays:**
- Before: "The old way" / "[X] minutes"
- After: "[App Name]" / "[X] seconds"
- CTA: "#ad | Link in bio"

## Script 3: Screen tutorial (45 seconds)

**Best for:** TikTok, Reels, Shorts
**Style:** Screen recording with voiceover

```
[HOOK - 5 seconds]
(Screen showing app icon or home screen)
VO: "Quick tutorial on [App Name] - the app I use for [goal]"

[STEP 1 - 10 seconds]
(Screen recording of first action)
VO: "First, you [action]. This does [benefit]."
(Highlight key tap or feature)

[STEP 2 - 10 seconds]
(Screen recording of second action)
VO: "Then [action]. Most people miss this, but it [benefit]."

[STEP 3 - 10 seconds]
(Screen recording of result)
VO: "And now you have [result]."
"Took [X seconds/minutes] instead of [old timeframe]."

[CTA - 10 seconds]
VO: "Link in bio to try it free."
"Code [PROMO] saves you 10%."
"I'm an affiliate, so yeah, I make money if you sign up. But I actually use this."
```

**Text overlays:**
- Step numbers on each segment
- Key feature names
- Time comparison
- CTA and promo code

## Script 7: YouTube full review (3-5 minutes)

**Best for:** YouTube
**Style:** Structured review format

```
[INTRO - 30 seconds]
"[App Name] review - is it worth it in [year]?"
"I've been using this app for [X months] and today I'm sharing everything."
"What it does well, what it doesn't, and whether you should get it."
"Let's get into it."

[WHAT IS IT - 45 seconds]
"So [App Name] is a [category] app that [main function]."
"It costs [pricing] and works on [platforms]."
"I started using it because [your reason]."

[KEY FEATURES - 90 seconds]
"Let me show you the main features."

"Feature one: [Name]"
(Screen recording demo)
"This lets you [function]. [Why it's useful]."

"Feature two: [Name]"
(Screen recording demo)
"[Explanation]"

"Feature three: [Name]"
(Screen recording demo)
"This is probably my favorite because [reason]."

[PROS - 45 seconds]
"What I like about [App Name]:"
"First, [pro]. [Elaboration]."
"Second, [pro]. [Elaboration]."
"Third, [pro]. [Elaboration]."

[CONS - 30 seconds]
"What could be better:"
"[Con]. [Why it matters, or why it doesn't]."
"[Con]. [Your workaround or hope for future update]."

[WHO IS IT FOR - 30 seconds]
"Who should get this app?"
"If you [description of ideal user], yes."
"If you [description of someone it's not for], maybe look elsewhere."

[VERDICT - 30 seconds]
"My overall take: [rating/recommendation]."
"After [X months], I [continue to use it / stopped using it] because [reason]."
"It's [worth/not worth] the [price]."

[CTA - 30 seconds]
"Links in the description if you want to try it."
"Use code [PROMO] for 10% off your first month."
"Full disclosure: I'm an affiliate, so I earn a commission if you sign up through my link."
"But I only recommend stuff I actually use."
"Thanks for watching. [Subscribe CTA if applicable]."
```

**B-roll suggestions:**
- App icon/logo
- Screen recordings of each feature
- Your face reacting (if personality-driven channel)
- Comparison graphics (if mentioning competitors)

## Recording tips

### Audio

- Film in quiet space
- Use lapel mic or phone close to face
- No echo (soft surfaces help)

### Lighting

- Face a window (natural light)
- Or ring light in front of you
- Avoid backlight (window behind you)

### Framing

- Head and shoulders for face-to-camera
- Leave space for text overlays
- Keep phone/screen visible for demos

### Energy

- More energy than feels natural
- Speak slightly faster (can slow in editing)
- Facial expressions read on camera
- First take usually worst, third take usually best

## Editing checklist

- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Text overlays on key points
- [ ] Captions (most watch without sound)
- [ ] No dead air (cut pauses)
- [ ] CTA clear and visible
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure present
- [ ] Promo code on screen

---

## Disclosure placement

**Short videos (under 60s):**
- Verbal: First 30 seconds OR end
- Text: Visible at some point
- Caption: #ad or "affiliate link"

**Long videos (YouTube):**
- Verbal: Intro and CTA sections
- Description: First line
- End card: Reiterate disclosure
