# PrayerLock - Go-to-Market Strategy

**Product:** PrayerLock
**Type:** Mobile App (iOS + Android)
**Price:** Free (with $4.99/mo or $29.99/yr Premium)
**Niche:** Faith
**Target Audience:** Christians who want to build consistent prayer habits; church communities seeking digital discipleship tools
**Core Promise:** Lock in daily prayer time with accountability, community, and streak tracking

---

## LAUNCH PHASE (Week 1-2)

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] Complete MVP app build (iOS + Android or PWA)
- [ ] App Store submission (allow 1-2 weeks for review)
- [ ] Set up App Store Optimization (ASO) - keywords, screenshots, description
- [ ] Create landing page with email waitlist
- [ ] Prepare 15 launch-day posts (Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook)
- [ ] Set up UTM tracking and analytics (Mixpanel/Amplitude free tier)
- [ ] Create 3 UGC demo videos ($15-45 total)
- [ ] Draft app store review request flow (in-app)
- [ ] Identify 10 churches for beta partnership
- [ ] Build church outreach list (100 churches)

### Beta User Acquisition Plan

**Target:** 100-200 beta users (enough for meaningful data)
**Method:** TestFlight (iOS) + direct APK (Android) or PWA

1. **Church Small Groups (50-100 users)**
   - Partner with 3-5 churches for closed beta
   - Offer: Free premium for all members during beta
   - Get pastor/leader endorsement

2. **Instagram/Facebook Faith Communities (30-50 users)**
   - Post in Christian groups with value-first approach
   - "Building a prayer accountability app. Looking for 50 believers to test free."

3. **Reddit Faith Communities (20-30 users)**
   - r/Christianity, r/TrueChristian, r/Reformed
   - Genuine engagement, not spammy promotion

**Beta Feedback Loop:**
- In-app feedback button
- Weekly Zoom calls with power users
- Track: DAU, prayer completion rate, streak retention
- Collect video testimonials from engaged users

### Launch Day Sequence

**Pre-Launch (Day -7 to -1):**
- Tease app in Stories and posts
- "Launching something for daily prayer next week"
- Build waitlist on landing page

**Day 0 (Launch):**
- Email blast to waitlist + beta users
- Instagram Reel (app walkthrough + testimony)
- TikTok short (hook: prayer accountability)
- Facebook posts to Christian groups (check rules)
- X thread about building the app
- Ask beta users to leave App Store reviews

**Day 1-3:**
- Share early user testimonials
- Respond to every App Store review
- Post user streaks/milestones in Stories
- Reddit posts in faith communities

**Day 7:**
- "First week" recap with download numbers
- User testimonial compilation video
- Soft push for premium upgrades

## CHURCH PARTNERSHIP PLAYBOOK (CRITICAL)

This is the highest-leverage channel for PrayerLock. Detailed strategy:

### The Value Proposition for Churches

**For the Pastor:**
- Free digital discipleship tool for congregation
- No cost, no IT overhead
- 20% revenue share on premium (ministry fund income)
- Dashboard to see congregation engagement (future feature)

**For Church Members:**
- Free prayer accountability
- Connect with church prayer partners
- Church-specific prayer requests and updates

### Phase 1: Research (Week 1-2)
- Build list of 200 target churches
- Criteria: 200-2000 members, modern website, active social, no existing app
- Use BuiltWith, Google, Yelp for research
- Find pastor email via church website or Apollo

### Phase 2: Outreach (Week 3-4)
- Send personalized email to pastor
- Reference something specific about their church
- Follow up via LinkedIn or phone if no response
- Offer: Free demo call, no commitment

### Phase 3: Pilot (Week 5-6)
- Start with one small group (10-20 people)
- Provide dedicated support
- Weekly check-ins with small group leader
- Collect testimonials and feedback

### Phase 4: Rollout (Week 7+)
- Church announcement (bulletin, service, email)
- Custom church code for tracking
- Ongoing support and engagement reports
- Quarterly check-ins with pastor
