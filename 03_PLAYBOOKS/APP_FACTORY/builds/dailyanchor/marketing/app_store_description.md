## Full Description (4000 chars max)

DailyAnchor helps you build consistent spiritual habits through simple daily tracking.

Check off your faith practices. Write what you're grateful for. Watch your streak grow.

**Why it works:** Small daily actions compound. DailyAnchor makes spiritual growth measurable without making it feel like a chore.

THE PROBLEM

You want to grow spiritually. But "grow spiritually" is vague. Hard to track. Easy to skip.

Without a system, faith practices happen inconsistently. Some weeks you pray daily. Other weeks, nothing.

THE SOLUTION

DailyAnchor gives you:
- A daily habit checklist for faith practices
- A gratitude journal to capture thankfulness
- Streak tracking to build consistency
- Daily Bible verses for inspiration

Simple structure. Real growth.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Set your daily faith habits (prayer, reading, etc.)
2. Check them off each day
3. Write 1-3 things you're grateful for
4. Build your streak and watch patterns emerge
