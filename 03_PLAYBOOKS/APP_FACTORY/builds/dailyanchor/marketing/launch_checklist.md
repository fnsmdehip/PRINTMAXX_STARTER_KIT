## Small Group Launch Strategy

### Pilot Structure

- Free premium for all participants
- 30-day habit challenge
- Weekly check-in prompts
- End-of-challenge testimonials

### Launch Sequence

1. Week -2: Identify 10 small groups
2. Week -1: Pitch pilots
3. Launch: Enroll 3-5 groups
4. Week 2: Expand to 10+ groups
5. Month 2: Scale based on results

## Emergency Procedures

### If app crashes

1. Check Sentry
2. Identify issue
3. Push hotfix
4. Notify users

### If reviews are negative

1. Respond professionally
2. Identify patterns
3. Prioritize fixes
4. Ask for positive reviews
