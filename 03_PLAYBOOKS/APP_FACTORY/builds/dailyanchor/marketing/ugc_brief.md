## The Product

DailyAnchor is a faith-focused habit tracker with gratitude journaling and daily scripture.

**Core features:**
1. Custom faith habit tracking (prayer, reading, etc.)
2. 3-item daily gratitude journal
3. Fresh scripture verse each morning
4. Streak tracking for consistency
5. 10 pre-built spiritual discipline templates

**Pricing:** 7-day free trial, then $6.99/mo or $34.99/yr
