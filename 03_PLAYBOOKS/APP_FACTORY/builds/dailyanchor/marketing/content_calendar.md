# DailyAnchor - 30-Day Content Calendar

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Primary)
- Post frequency: 2x daily
- Best times: 7am, 12pm, 6pm
- Content mix: 50% tutorials, 30% testimonials, 20% tips
- Focus: Habit tracking, gratitude, faith growth

### Instagram (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 1-2 Reels/day + Stories
- Best times: 6am, 12pm, 7pm
- Content mix: Aesthetic habits, carousels, community

### X/Twitter (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 2-4 tweets/day
- Best times: 6am, 12pm, 5pm
- Content mix: Tips, scripture, engagement
