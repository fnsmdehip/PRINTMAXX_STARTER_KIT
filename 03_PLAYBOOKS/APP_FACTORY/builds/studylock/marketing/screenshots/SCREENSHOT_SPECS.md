# StudyLock - App Store screenshot specs

**Device sizes:**
- iPhone 6.7" (1290 x 2796)
- iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778)
- iPad 12.9" (2048 x 2732)

**Design system:**
- Background gradient: #0D47A1 (deep blue) to #1565C0 (medium blue)
- Accent: #00E676 (neon green for completion states)
- Secondary accent: #FF6D00 (orange for streaks/urgency)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) primary, #00E676 for positive emphasis
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold (headlines), SF Pro Text Regular (body)
- Device frame: iPhone 15 Pro, midnight black
