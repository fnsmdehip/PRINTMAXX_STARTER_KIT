## Target influencer categories

**Study tip / study with me creators** - TikTok, YouTube (millions of views on "study with me" content)
**Student lifestyle creators** - College/HS audience, daily routine content
**Productivity YouTubers** - Thomas Frank, Ali Abdaal audience overlap
**Teachers/professors with social presence** - Direct recommendation to students
**Parents of students** - Facebook/Instagram, concerned about screen time
**MCAT/LSAT/boards prep creators** - Highest intent, willing to pay
**Study aesthetic / Studyblr community** - Tumblr/Instagram niche
