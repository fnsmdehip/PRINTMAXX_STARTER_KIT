## Founder story

I built StudyLock because I failed a test I studied for.

I "studied" for 4 hours the night before. In reality, I studied for about 90 minutes and spent 2.5 hours checking my phone. Quick glances that turned into 15-minute scrolling sessions. I didn't even realize it was happening until I looked at Screen Time data the next morning: 42 phone pickups during my "study session."

I downloaded Forest. Grew a tree for 3 days. Killed it on day 4 because a friend texted me. I downloaded a Pomodoro timer. Used it for one session. Skipped the next.

Every focus app trusted me to have willpower. I don't have willpower when my phone is within arm's reach. Nobody does.

So I removed the choice. StudyLock locks your phone. Not "limits." Not "reminds." Locks. The phone is inaccessible until the study timer ends.

The first week I used it, my actual study time went from 5 hours per week to 12. Not because I was more motivated. Because the phone was physically unavailable.

The exam countdown feature came from a med student who told me: "I have boards in 47 days and I need to see that number every time I open the app." That terror is better motivation than any gamification.

## FAQ for press

**Q: How is this different from Forest?**
A: Forest grows a virtual tree you can kill at any time. StudyLock locks your phone with no override option. The tree is digital guilt. The lock is physical restriction.

**Q: Can students call for emergencies?**
A: Emergency calls always work. There's an emergency unlock that requires typing a confirmation phrase and breaks the study streak.

**Q: Does this work for remote workers?**
A: Yes. Anyone who needs focused work time can use it. The Pomodoro timer and app blocking work for any focused task.

**Q: What about students who need their phone for studying (calculator, notes)?**
A: StudyLock blocks specific apps you choose. Calculator, Notes, and other utility apps can stay unblocked.

**Q: Is this just for Apple devices?**
A: iOS and Android. Full feature parity on both platforms.
