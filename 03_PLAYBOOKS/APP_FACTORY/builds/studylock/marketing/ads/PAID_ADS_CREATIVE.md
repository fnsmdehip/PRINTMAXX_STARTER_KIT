## A/B test plan

**Test 1: Hook**
- A: Forest comparison ("the one you can't cheat")
- B: GPA transformation ("2.8 to 3.5")
- C: Stat shock ("23 minutes per pickup")

**Test 2: Audience**
- A: College students (18-24, education interests)
- B: High school students (14-18, study app users)
- C: Graduate/med students (24-30, professional exam prep)
- D: Parents of students (35-55)

**Test 3: Timing**
- A: Always-on (baseline)
- B: Exam season push (2 weeks before midterms/finals)
- C: Back-to-school (August/September)

**Budget:** $40/day initial. Scale to $80/day for exam season.

**Targets:**
| Metric | Target | Kill |
|--------|--------|------|
| CPI | < $1.50 | > $3.00 |
| CTR | > 2.5% | < 0.8% |
| Trial start | > 50% | < 25% |
| Trial to paid | > 15% | < 8% |
| Day 7 retention | > 40% | < 20% |
