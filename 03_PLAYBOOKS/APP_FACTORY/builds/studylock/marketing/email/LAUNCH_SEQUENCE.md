## Feature deep-dive (Day 7)

**Subject:** 5 StudyLock features your GPA needs

**Body:**

**1. subject tagging**
tag every session. "Calculus" "English Lit" "Biology." at the end of the week, see a pie chart of where your hours went. the subjects with 0 hours will haunt you. good.

**2. exam countdown**
settings > exams > add exam. set the date. StudyLock shows "Finals in 12 days" on your home screen. with a suggested study sessions remaining count.

**3. focus sounds**
during a locked session, tap the sound icon. options: rain, library ambience, lo-fi beats, white noise, complete silence. helps some people. try it.

**4. Pomodoro mode**
default: 25 minutes study, 5 minute break, repeat. phone locks during study blocks. unlocks during breaks. after 4 rounds, 15 minute long break. the classic Pomodoro, but enforced.

**5. the break timer**
during breaks, a countdown shows when the next study block starts. no "I'll start in a minute" drift. the break ends, the phone locks again. automatic.
