HOW IT WORKS

1. set your daily step goal (200 to 15,000)
2. choose your lock time (morning, afternoon, or custom)
3. phone locks at your set time
4. walk until you hit the goal
5. phone unlocks automatically
6. streak builds every day you complete it

- step-gated app locking: choose which apps to block until you walk. Instagram, TikTok, games, anything.

- adjustable goals: start easy (200 steps) or go hard (10,000+). scale up as the habit builds.
- morning auto-mode: set your wake time once. phone locks automatically every morning.
- streak tracking: see your consecutive walk days. calendar view shows your commitment.
- rest days: schedule 3 rest days per week. for sick days or when you genuinely can't walk.
- daily stats: steps walked, time to unlock, calories burned estimate.
- walk history: track your progress over weeks and months.
- emergency unlock: for genuine emergencies. breaks your streak.
- privacy: all data stays on your device. no cloud. no tracking.
