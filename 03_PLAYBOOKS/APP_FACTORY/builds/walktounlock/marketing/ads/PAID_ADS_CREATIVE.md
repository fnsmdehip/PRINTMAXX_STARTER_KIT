## A/B test plan

**Test 1: Hook type**
- A: Weight loss testimonial ("lost 12 pounds")
- B: Stat-based ("80 pickups per day")
- C: Sweatcoin replacement angle

**Test 2: Step goal framing**
- A: Low barrier ("start with 200 steps")
- B: Results-focused ("3,200 extra steps per day")
- C: Time-based ("8 minutes to unlock")

**Test 3: Audience**
- A: Fitness interest (gym, yoga, running)
- B: Weight loss interest
- C: Sweatcoin/pedometer app users
- D: Digital wellness / screen time reduction

**Budget:** $50/day initial, scale winners to $100/day.

**Target metrics:**
| Metric | Target | Kill |
|--------|--------|------|
| CPI | < $1.50 | > $3.50 |
| CTR | > 2.0% | < 0.7% |
| Trial start | > 45% | < 20% |
| Trial to paid | > 12% | < 6% |
| Day 7 retention | > 35% | < 15% |
