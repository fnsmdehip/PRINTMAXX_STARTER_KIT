## Feature deep-dive (Day 7)

**Subject:** 4 WalkToUnlock features you probably haven't tried

**Body:**

**1. morning auto-mode**
settings > auto-mode. set your wake time. phone locks automatically every morning at that time. no manual activation needed. set it once, walks happen daily.

**2. rest days**
settings > rest days. pick up to 3 days per week. sick days, recovery days, genuinely bad weather days. your streak doesn't break on rest days.

**3. start stupidly low**
the default goal is 1,000 steps. if that feels like too much, go to settings > step goal > 200. 200 steps is 2 minutes. everyone can do 2 minutes. increase when it feels easy.

**4. walk history export**
settings > export. get a CSV of every walk: date, steps, time to complete, goal. useful for sharing with a doctor or tracking long-term progress.

no gamification. no leaderboards. no social feed. just a lock, a step counter, and a streak.
