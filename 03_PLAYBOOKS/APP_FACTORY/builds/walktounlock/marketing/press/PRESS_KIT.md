## One-pager

**What:** WalkToUnlock locks your phone until you walk a set number of steps. Set a step goal, phone locks, walk until you hit it, phone unlocks.

**Who it's for:** Anyone who wants to walk more but lacks motivation. Particularly: sedentary office workers, people recovering from Sweatcoin (170M users, token crashed 83%), students, anyone who picks up their phone 80+ times per day.

**Why it matters:** Walking is the most accessible fitness activity on earth. No gym, no equipment, any age, any location. Yet the average American walks 3,000-4,000 steps per day when 7,000-10,000 is recommended. The gap is 30-60 minutes of walking. WalkToUnlock closes that gap by making walking the price of phone access.

**Key numbers:**
- 3,200 extra steps per day (average user)
- 8 minutes average time to unlock
- 16-day average streak
- 170 million ex-Sweatcoin users as addressable market
- Users who start at 200 steps increase to 1,000+ within one week

**Business model:** Freemium. Free tier (1 blocked app, basic counting). Premium $3.99/month or $19.99/year.

**Available on:** iOS and Android

## Founder story

I built WalkToUnlock because I realized my phone was the only thing I consistently reached for every day.

Not my running shoes. Not my gym bag. My phone. 80+ times per day, every day, without fail.

So I asked: what if each of those 80 pickups required walking? Not running. Not a gym session. Just walking. The lowest-barrier physical activity that exists.

I built the first version, set a 500-step goal, and locked Instagram. Within 3 days I was walking 3,200 extra steps per day. Not because I wanted to exercise. Because I wanted to check Instagram.

The insight: you don't need motivation to walk. You need your phone to require it. The addiction does the motivating for you.

Then I found out Sweatcoin crashed. Token down 83%. Downloads down 60%. 170 million users who wanted an incentive to walk, now without one. Crypto tokens were the wrong incentive. Phone access is the right one.

WalkToUnlock is the fitness app that works because it doesn't feel like a fitness app. It feels like getting your phone back.

## FAQ for press

**Q: How is this different from Apple's Screen Time?**
A: Screen Time lets you override limits with one tap. WalkToUnlock ties app access to physical movement. You can't override it without actually walking.

**Q: What about people who can't walk?**
A: Emergency unlock is always available. Rest days can be scheduled (up to 3 per week). The app is designed for people who can walk but choose not to.

**Q: Does it work indoors?**
A: Yes. Steps are counted via the device pedometer. Walking indoors, on a treadmill, or outside all count.

**Q: Why not just use a step counter app?**
A: Step counters show you a number. WalkToUnlock changes behavior. The locked phone is the mechanism.

**Q: Is the free tier actually useful?**
A: It blocks 1 app and counts steps. Enough to test the concept. Premium unlocks unlimited blocking, custom goals, analytics, and auto-mode.
