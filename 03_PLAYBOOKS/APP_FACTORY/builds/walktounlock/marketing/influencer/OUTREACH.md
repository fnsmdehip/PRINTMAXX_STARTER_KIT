## Partnership tiers

**Tier 1: Organic mention ($150-400)**
- 1 post/story mentioning WalkToUnlock
- Must try app for 3+ days first
- Link/tag in post

**Tier 2: Dedicated review ($400-1,200)**
- Full video/post dedicated to WalkToUnlock
- Show actual step counter, lock state, unlock
- Before/after daily step count (their real data)
- Link in bio 7 days

**Tier 3: Ambassador ($800-2,500/month)**
- 2 posts per month
- Featured on website
- Unique promo code (WALK[NAME])
- 30% recurring commission
- Early access to features

---

## Affiliate program

**Commission:** 30% recurring
**Cookie:** 30 days
**Payment:** Monthly, PayPal or direct deposit
**Minimum:** $25

**Example:** 100 premium subs at $3.99/mo = $119.70/month recurring

---

## Creator code system

**Format:** WALK[NAME] (e.g., WALKCHRIS, WALKLISA)

**User benefit:** 25% off first month ($2.99 instead of $3.99)
**Creator benefit:** 30% recurring + attribution dashboard

## Target influencer categories

**Walking/step challenge creators** - Direct audience match
**Morning routine influencers** - Lock activates at wake time
**Weight loss journeys** - "Accidental exercise" angle
**Digital wellness/screen time** - Phone lock mechanism
**Former Sweatcoin promoters** - Replacement product
**Fitness beginner content** - Low barrier (200 steps)
**Dog walking accounts** - Walk the dog to unlock phone
**Outdoor/hiking** - Expansion of walking habit

## Outreach timeline

- Week -2: 50 DMs to micro-influencers, 10 emails to mid-tier
- Launch week: Follow up, share download links
- Week 2-4: 30 more DMs, Tier 2 pitches
- Monthly: 20 new outreach, 1-2 paid deals
