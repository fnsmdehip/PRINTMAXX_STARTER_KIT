# WalkToUnlock - App Store screenshot specs

**Device sizes required:**
- iPhone 6.7" (1290 x 2796) - iPhone 15 Pro Max
- iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778) - iPhone 14 Plus
- iPad 12.9" (2048 x 2732) - iPad Pro

**Design system:**
- Background gradient: #1B5E20 (deep forest green) to #00C853 (bright green)
- Accent color: #FFFFFF (white)
- Secondary accent: #FFD600 (gold/yellow for achievements)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) primary, #FFD600 for emphasis
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold (headlines), SF Pro Text Regular (body)
- Device frame: iPhone 15 Pro, midnight black
- Corner radius: Apple standard

- iPad: same content, wider layout, 3-column where applicable
