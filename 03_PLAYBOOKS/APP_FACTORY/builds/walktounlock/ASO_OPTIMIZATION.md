WalkToUnlock turns your phone addiction into a fitness habit. You want to check Instagram? Walk 500 steps first. You want to play games? Get moving.

1. Set your daily step goal (200, 500, 1000, or custom)
2. WalkToUnlock blocks the apps you choose
3. Walk until you hit your step count
4. Apps unlock automatically when you reach your goal

Your screen time reward requires physical movement. Every. Single. Day.

- Step-gated app locking: Choose which apps to block until you walk. Instagram, TikTok, games, anything.

- Streak tracking: See your consecutive walk days. Calendar view shows your commitment.
- Daily stats: Steps walked, time to unlock, calories burned estimate.
- Morning mode: Auto-activates at your wake-up time. No manual setup needed.
- Walk history: Track your progress over weeks and months.
- Emergency unlock: For genuine emergencies. Breaks your streak.
- Privacy: All data stays on your device. No cloud. No tracking.

WHY WALKTOUNLOCK WORKS

You already pick up your phone 80+ times per day. Each pickup now requires movement. That's 80 micro-walks you weren't taking before.

- Maintains a 16-day average streak
- Reports feeling more energized by 10 AM

PRICING

  - Unlimited app blocking

YOUR PHONE ADDICTION IS NOW YOUR FITNESS PLAN
