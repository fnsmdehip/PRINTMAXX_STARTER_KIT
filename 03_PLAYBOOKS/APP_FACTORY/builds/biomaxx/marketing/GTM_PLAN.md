# BioMaxx Go-to-Market Plan

**App:** BioMaxx - Your Daily Longevity Protocol
**Target Launch:** Q1 2026
**Budget:** $0-500 initial (bootstrapped), scale with revenue

---

## Executive Summary

BioMaxx enters the biohacking app market with differentiation through:
1. Multi-protocol tracking (vs single-protocol apps like Zero)
2. Science-based education (not just timers)
3. Protocol stacking recommendations (unique feature)
4. Affiliate revenue model (gear recommendations)
5. Earthy/premium aesthetic (vs cold/clinical competitors)

**Target:** 10,000 downloads month 1, 2,000 paid subscribers month 3, $20k MRR by month 6.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "The Optimizer"
- **Demographics:** 25-45, male (70%), high income ($80k+), tech-savvy
- **Psychographics:** Follows Andrew Huberman, Bryan Johnson, Peter Attia
- **Behavior:** Already doing 2-3 protocols, wants to track and optimize
- **Pain:** Uses multiple apps, no unified tracking, wants science-backed guidance
- **Channels:** X/Twitter, YouTube, Reddit, podcasts

### Secondary Persona: "The Curious Beginner"
- **Demographics:** 22-35, health-conscious, fitness-adjacent
- **Psychographics:** Interested in longevity, heard about cold plunges on TikTok
- **Behavior:** Looking to start biohacking, overwhelmed by options
- **Pain:** Doesn't know where to start, wants guidance
- **Channels:** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube shorts

### Tertiary Persona: "The Bryan Johnson Fan"
- **Demographics:** 30-50, follows Blueprint closely
- **Psychographics:** Wants to replicate Bryan's protocols affordably
- **Behavior:** Already tried some Blueprint protocols
- **Pain:** Blueprint app is limited, wants comprehensive tracking
- **Channels:** X/Twitter, Blueprint community, longevity podcasts

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| App Store rejection | Use "wellness" language, add disclaimers, avoid health claims |
| Low conversion | A/B test paywall timing, add more free value first |
| High churn | Focus on streaks, notifications, weekly engagement |
| Competition (Blueprint scales) | Differentiate on multi-protocol, education, affiliate |
| Ad costs too high | Double down on organic, influencer, SEO |
