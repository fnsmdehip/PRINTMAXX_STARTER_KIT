## Differentiation Opportunities

### Gap 1: Protocol Stacking Education

Current apps track protocols but don't teach optimal stacking:
- Red light + PEMF mat combo (2x efficiency)
- Sauna → cold plunge timing
- Supplement timing with fasting windows
- When to avoid combining (e.g., antioxidants post-workout)

**BioMaxx Angle:** "Protocol Stacks" feature with science-backed combinations

### Gap 2: Affiliate Revenue Integration

Biohacking involves expensive equipment:
- PEMF mats ($200-2000)
- Red light panels ($300-1500)
- Saunas ($3000-10000)
- Supplements ($50-200/mo)

No current app capitalizes on affiliate revenue properly.

**BioMaxx Angle:** Built-in affiliate recommendations for every protocol
- "Ready to upgrade your red light routine? These are our tested picks"
- iOS now allows external payment links globally for physical goods

### Gap 3: Bryan Johnson Content Gap

Blueprint app is limited. Millions follow Bryan Johnson with no comprehensive tracker.

**BioMaxx Angle:** "Blueprint-inspired" protocol templates
- Import Bryan Johnson's exact stacks
- Track his supplement protocol
- "Blueprint Mode" preset

### Gap 4: No Community/Accountability

Biohackr and Zero are solo experiences. Noom has coaches ($$$).

**BioMaxx Angle:** Lightweight accountability
- Weekly check-in reminders
- Share progress to social
- "Biohack of the day" push notification

### Gap 5: Premium Feel at Mid-Price

Biohackr: $24.99/yr (too cheap, signals low quality)
Zero: $70/yr (fasting only)
Noom: $200+/yr (too expensive)

**BioMaxx Angle:** $49.99/yr or $9.99/mo
- Premium UI (dark mode, warm accents)
- All protocols included
- Education content
- Affiliate recommendations

## Design Direction

### Color Palette (Differentiated)

**Problem:** Most health apps are sterile (white/blue) or dark/techy

**BioMaxx Direction:** Earthy/natural premium
- Primary: Deep emerald green (#10B981) - life, growth, longevity
- Secondary: Warm amber (#F59E0B) - energy, vitality
- Background: Soft charcoal (#1F2937) - premium dark mode
- Surface: Dark gray (#374151) - elevated cards
- Accent: Gold (#FFD93D) - achievements, premium feel
- Text: Off-white (#F9FAFB)

**Reasoning:** Green = longevity/life (vs blue = cold/tech). Amber = warmth/energy. Not the typical cold blue of health apps.

### Typography
- Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold (iOS native feel)
- Body: Inter Regular (clean, readable)
- Numbers/Stats: Tabular figures, large display

### UI Patterns
- Dark mode default (premium biohacker aesthetic)
- Card-based dashboard
- Circular progress rings for each protocol
- Streak flames
- Bottom tab navigation
- Haptic feedback on interactions
