## Founder story

I've been biohacking for 3 years. Cold plunges, fasting, supplement stacking, red light, blood work, the whole thing.

My tracking system was a disaster. Fasting in Zero app. Supplements in a Google Sheet. Sleep on my Apple Watch. Cold exposure in a Notes file. Blood work in PDFs from my doctor.

When my sleep improved after adding magnesium AND starting cold plunges in the same week, I had no way to figure out which one mattered.

The spreadsheet was supposed to help. It didn't. Correlation math by eyeball doesn't work. And updating 4 different tracking systems every day is friction that kills consistency.

I built biomaxx as the single tracking tool I wished existed. One app for every protocol. Correlation insights that find patterns I can't see manually. Dark mode because I use it at 5am and my eyes deserve mercy.

The hard paywall exists because a limited biohacking tracker is useless. You need to track everything or the correlations don't work. There's no meaningful "free tier" of a data product that requires comprehensive data.

No social feed. No community tab. No motivational quotes. This is a data tool for people who optimize, not people who perform.

## FAQ for press

**Q: Why no free tier?**
A: A biohacking tracker that only tracks 1-2 protocols can't generate correlation insights. The product requires comprehensive data to be useful. A free tier would be a worse product.

**Q: How does the correlation engine work?**
A: It compares protocol completion data with outcome ratings (sleep quality, energy, mood) over time. After 30+ data points per protocol, it calculates Pearson correlations and flags significant patterns. It's statistics, not AI.

**Q: Is this a medical device?**
A: No. biomaxx is a personal tracking tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Correlation insights are observational, not clinical. Users should consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

**Q: Who are your competitors?**
A: Individual protocol apps (Zero for fasting, Apple Health for sleep) and spreadsheets. No consumer app tracks the full biohacking protocol stack with correlation analysis.

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: None. All data is stored on-device. No cloud sync. No analytics on health data. No data sharing. Export via CSV is user-initiated only.
