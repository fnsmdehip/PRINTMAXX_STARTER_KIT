# biomaxx - App Store screenshot specs

**Device sizes:**
- iPhone 6.7" (1290 x 2796)
- iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778)
- iPad 12.9" (2048 x 2732)

**Design system:**
- Background: #0F172A (dark slate) to #1E293B (slate 800)
- Primary accent: #10B981 (emerald green)
- Secondary accent: #06B6D4 (cyan for data)
- Tertiary: #F59E0B (amber for streaks/fire)
- Text: #F8FAFC (slate 50) primary, #10B981 for emphasis
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold (headlines), SF Pro Text Regular (body)
- Device frame: iPhone 15 Pro, midnight black
- Aesthetic: dark mode, clean, premium, data-forward

## Production notes

- Dark mode ONLY. No light mode screenshots.
- Emerald green (#10B981) for positive states and primary accent
- Cyan (#06B6D4) for data/charts
- Amber (#F59E0B) for streaks and fire icons
- Clean, minimal, premium feel. More Bloomberg terminal than Instagram.
- Export at 2x retina
- File naming: biomaxx_screenshot_01.png through 06.png
