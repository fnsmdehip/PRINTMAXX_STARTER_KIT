- A: Spreadsheet replacement ("your spreadsheet is retired")
- B: Correlation insight ("which one worked?")
- C: Cost savings ("$47/month saved")

**Test 2: Audience**
- A: Huberman/Attia followers (longevity interest)
- B: Supplement buyers (supplement interest)
- C: Fasting community (intermittent fasting interest)
- D: Cold exposure / Wim Hof followers

**Test 3: Creative style**
- A: Dark mode UI showcase (product screenshots)
- B: Before/after (spreadsheet vs app)
- C: Data/correlation highlight

**Budget:** $60/day initial (higher CPI expected, niche audience).

**Targets:**

| CTR | > 1.5% | < 0.5% |
| Trial start | > 50% | < 25% |

| Day 7 retention | > 45% | < 20% |
| Day 30 retention | > 25% | < 10% |

Note: Higher CPI acceptable because LTV is higher (biohackers have higher purchasing power and retention).
