## Solution Section

### How StepUnlock works

**Step 1: Set your step goal**
Pick a number that's achievable. 2,000 steps. 5,000 steps. 10,000 if you're ambitious. You decide.

**Step 2: Lock your time-wasting apps**
Choose which apps stay locked until you hit your goal. TikTok, Instagram, games, whatever distracts you most.

**Step 3: Walk to unlock**
StepUnlock syncs with Apple Health. Hit your step goal and your apps unlock automatically. No walking, no scrolling.

### Why this beats fitness apps

Fitness apps track movement. StepUnlock forces it. You cannot open your locked apps until you walk. Period. That's the difference between "I should" and "I did."

## FAQ Section

### Frequently asked questions

**Q: What if I'm sick or can't walk?**
A: Rest days are built in. Tap "Skip Today" and your apps unlock. Use sparingly or it defeats the purpose.

**Q: Does it drain my battery?**
A: No. StepUnlock reads from Apple Health, which tracks steps passively. No GPS, no background drain.

**Q: Can I use it with my Apple Watch?**
A: Yes. Steps from your Watch sync to Apple Health, which StepUnlock reads automatically.

**Q: What if I forget my phone at home?**
A: Apple Watch steps still count. When you sync later, your apps unlock if you hit your goal.

**Q: Does it work for wheelchair users?**
A: Currently step-focused. We're exploring wheelchair push tracking for a future update.
