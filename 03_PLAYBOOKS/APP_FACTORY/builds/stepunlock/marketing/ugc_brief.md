# StepUnlock - UGC Creator Brief

## Overview

**App:** StepUnlock
**Niche:** Fitness / Digital Wellness / Productivity
**Target audience:** 18-35 year olds who struggle with phone addiction and want to walk more

---

## The Product

StepUnlock blocks social media apps until you hit your daily step goal.

**Core mechanism:**
1. User sets step goal (1,000-20,000 steps)
2. User selects apps to block (Instagram, TikTok, games, etc.)
3. Apps stay locked until step goal is reached
4. Progress syncs with Apple Health/Google Fit

**Pricing:** 3-day free trial, then $7.99/mo or $39.99/yr

## Video Concepts

### Concept 1: The Demo
Show yourself waking up, trying to open an app, seeing it's locked, checking step count, going for a walk, then showing the unlock.

### Concept 2: The Before/After
Show screen time report before StepUnlock. Show it after 7 days. Show step count change.

Dramatic/funny take on needing to scroll but having to walk first. Exaggerated reactions work well.

### Concept 4: The Testimonial
Straight to camera talking about how the app changed your daily routine. Be specific with numbers.

Show how StepUnlock fits into your morning routine. Wake up -> can't scroll -> go for walk -> start your day right.
