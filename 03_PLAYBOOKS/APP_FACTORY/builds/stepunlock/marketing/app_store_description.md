StepUnlock blocks your apps until you walk.

Set a step goal. Pick the apps you want blocked. When you hit your steps, they unlock automatically.

**Why it works:** You can't negotiate with willpower. StepUnlock removes the decision. Want to check Instagram? Walk first.

You know you should walk more. You also know you'll scroll through TikTok for an hour if you pick up your phone.

Screen time apps don't work because you can bypass them. Habit apps don't work because you ignore the reminders.

StepUnlock syncs with your phone's health data. Set 5,000 steps. Until you hit 5,000 steps, selected apps stay locked.

No negotiation. No bypass (unless you use emergency unlock, which resets your streak).

1. Set your daily step goal (1,000 to 20,000)
2. Select apps to block (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, games)
3. Start walking
4. Apps unlock when you hit your goal
