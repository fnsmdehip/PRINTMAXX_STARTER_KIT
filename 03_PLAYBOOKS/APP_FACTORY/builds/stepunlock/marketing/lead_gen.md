### How It Works Section

**Step 1: Set your goal**
Choose a step target that challenges you but is achievable. Start with 5,000 if you're new to walking.

**Step 2: Pick apps to block**
Select any app on your phone. Most users block Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and games.

**Step 3: Walk**
StepUnlock syncs with Apple Health or Google Fit. As you walk, your progress updates in real time.

**Step 4: Unlock and enjoy**
Hit your goal and your apps unlock for the rest of the day. Tomorrow, the challenge resets.

---

### FAQ Section

**Can I bypass the lock?**
Yes, there's an emergency unlock. But it resets your streak to zero. Most users never use it.

**Does it drain battery?**
No. StepUnlock uses your phone's built-in step counter, not GPS. Minimal battery impact.

**What if I'm sick or injured?**
You can adjust your goal anytime. Set it to 500 steps for rest days.

**Which apps can I block?**
Any app on your phone. You choose.
