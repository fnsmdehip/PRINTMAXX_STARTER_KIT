# StepUnlock - 30-Day Content Calendar

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Primary)
- Post frequency: 2-3x daily
- Best times: 7am, 12pm, 5pm, 9pm
- Content mix: 60% original, 40% trends
- Focus: Problem/solution, before/after, day-in-life

### Instagram (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 1-2 Reels/day + 5 Stories
- Best times: 11am, 2pm, 7pm
- Content mix: Repurpose TikTok + original carousels
- Focus: Aesthetic, community, testimonials

### X/Twitter (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 3-5 tweets/day
- Best times: 8am, 12pm, 5pm
- Content mix: Tips, threads, engagement
- Focus: Indie maker story, user wins, tips

### YouTube Shorts
- Post frequency: 1/day (repurpose TikTok)
- Best times: Any (algorithm-driven)
- Content mix: Same as TikTok
- Focus: Evergreen discovery
