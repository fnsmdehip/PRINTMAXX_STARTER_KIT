## Disclosure Requirements

### In-App Disclosure

Required on any page with affiliate links:

```
"We may earn commission from products linked on this page at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we've tested or believe benefit walkers."
```

### Email Disclosure

At bottom of any email with affiliate links:

```
"Disclosure: This email contains affiliate links. If you purchase through these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you."
```

### App Store Compliance

iOS allows external payment links in certain contexts. Ensure:
- Links open in external browser (Safari), not in-app WebView
- Clear disclosure before user taps
- No manipulation or dark patterns
