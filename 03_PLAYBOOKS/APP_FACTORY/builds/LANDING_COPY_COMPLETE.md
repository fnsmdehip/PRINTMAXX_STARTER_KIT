   - Headline (under 10 words)

   - CTA button text

2. **Problem Section**
   - 3 specific pain points with numbers
   - Relatable, direct language

3. **Solution Section**
   - 3-step "how it works"
   - Key differentiator explained

4. **Features Section**
   - 5 bullet points
   - Feature + outcome format

5. **Social Proof Section**
   - 3 testimonial templates
   - Target metrics (rating, downloads, retention)

6. **Pricing Section**
   - Free tier details
   - Premium tier with price

7. **FAQ Section**

8. **Final CTA**
