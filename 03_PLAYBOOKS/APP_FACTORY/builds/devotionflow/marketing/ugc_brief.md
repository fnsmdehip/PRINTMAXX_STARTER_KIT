# DevotionFlow - UGC Creator Brief

## Overview

**App:** DevotionFlow
**Niche:** Faith / Christian Lifestyle / Habit Tracking
**Target audience:** Christians 18-45 who want beautiful, faith-specific habit tracking

---

## The Product

DevotionFlow is a faith habit tracker with spiritual discipline templates, daily scripture, and beautiful design.

**Core features:**
1. 10 pre-built spiritual discipline templates
2. Daily Bible verse
3. Streak tracking with dove mascot
4. Purple/gold calming design
5. Custom habit creation

**Pricing:** Free tier (5 habits) + Premium ($4.99/mo or $29.99/yr)
