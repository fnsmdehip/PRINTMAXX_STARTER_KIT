# Privacy Policy for DailyDevotion

**Last Updated:** January 21, 2026

DailyDevotion ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Habit Data:** Names, descriptions, and categories of habits you create
- **Completion Records:** Dates and times when you mark habits complete
- **Preferences:** App settings, notification preferences, and display options

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Analytics:** Feature usage patterns, screen views, and interaction events
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs to help us fix bugs and improve stability

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone) unless you contact support
- Location data
- Contacts or address book
- Photos or media files
- Financial information (subscriptions are handled by Google Play)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Calculate streaks and statistics
- Send habit reminder notifications (if enabled)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Respond to support requests

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All habit data, completion records, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly export and share it.

### Cloud Services
- **Bible API:** We request daily verses from bible-api.com. No personal data is sent.
- **Analytics:** We use [Mixpanel/Amplitude] for anonymous usage analytics. You can opt out in settings.
- **Crash Reporting:** We use Firebase Crashlytics for crash reports. No personal data is included.

## Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy policy governs payment processing.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Play Services** - For app distribution and in-app purchases
- **Firebase Crashlytics** - For crash reporting
- **bible-api.com** - For daily Bible verses
- **RevenueCat** - For subscription management

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data (such as total app users or popular features) for business purposes.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 24 months, then deleted
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for 90 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all data stored on your device within the app
- **Export:** Export your habit data in CSV format (Premium feature)
- **Delete:** Delete all data by uninstalling the app or using "Clear Data"
- **Opt Out:** Disable analytics in app settings

## Children's Privacy

DailyDevotion is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

## European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@dailydevotion.app.
