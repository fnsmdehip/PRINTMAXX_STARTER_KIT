## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Build lasting spiritual habits with DailyDevotion, the habit tracker designed for your faith journey.

Track your prayer time, scripture reading, gratitude practice, and more with beautiful, faith-focused templates. Get daily Bible verses to inspire your walk. Celebrate your faithfulness streaks with our encouraging dove mascot.

WHY DAILYDEVOTION?

Most habit trackers feel secular and disconnected from your spiritual goals. DailyDevotion was built specifically for Christians who want to:

- Pray more consistently
- Read scripture daily
- Practice gratitude
- Build holy habits that stick

FEATURES

Faith Templates
Start immediately with 10 pre-built spiritual discipline templates:
- Morning Prayer
- Scripture Reading
- Gratitude Journal
- Church Attendance
- Evening Prayer
- Christian Meditation
- Fasting
- Tithe/Giving
- Sabbath Rest
- Scripture Memorization

Daily Scripture
Open the app to an encouraging Bible verse each day. Share it with friends and family to spread the blessing.

Faithfulness Tracking
Watch your streaks grow as you build consistency. Celebrate milestones at 7, 30, 100, and 365 days. Our dove mascot cheers you on every step of the way.

Beautiful Design
A calming purple and gold interface designed for peaceful reflection. No distracting ads in premium.
