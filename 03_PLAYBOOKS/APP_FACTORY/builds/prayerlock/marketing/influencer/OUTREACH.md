## Partnership proposal (for mid-tier and above)

**Tier 1: Organic mention ($200-500)**
- 1 post or story mentioning PrayerLock
- Authentic usage (must actually try the app for 3+ days)
- Talking points provided but not scripted
- Link/tag in post

**Tier 2: Dedicated review ($500-1,500)**
- Full video or post dedicated to PrayerLock
- 2+ minutes of screen time or 5+ slides

**Tier 3: Ongoing ambassador ($1,000-3,000/month)**

- Access to new features before public release
- Featured on PrayerLock website as ambassador
- Unique promo code for their audience (tracks attribution)
- 30% recurring commission on conversions through their code

## Affiliate program structure

**Commission:** 30% recurring on all subscription revenue from referred users
**Cookie/attribution window:** 30 days
**Payment:** Monthly, via PayPal or direct deposit
**Minimum payout:** $25

**How it works:**
1. Influencer gets unique referral link: printmaxx.com/prayerlock?ref=[code]
2. Anyone who downloads through that link is attributed to them
3. If the user subscribes ($4.99/mo or $29.99/yr), influencer gets 30% recurring
4. As long as the user stays subscribed, the influencer earns

**Example earnings:**
- 100 subscribers at $4.99/mo = $149.70/month recurring
- 50 annual subscribers at $29.99/yr = $449.85/year
- Combined: $2,246/year from one promotion

## Outreach timeline

**Week -2 to -1 (pre-launch):**
- Send 50 DMs to micro-influencers (10K-50K)
- Send 10 emails to mid-tier influencers (50K-200K)
- Gift lifetime premium to all who respond positively
- Ask them to try the app for 3+ days before posting

**Launch week:**
- Follow up with all who received early access
- "We're live! Here's the download link. Would love your honest take."
- Repost/quote-tweet any organic mentions

**Week 2-4:**
- Second wave: 30 more DMs to fresh targets
- Pitch Tier 2 sponsorships to creators who showed interest but didn't post organically
- Set up affiliate tracking for serious partners

**Monthly ongoing:**
- 20 new micro-influencer outreach per month
- 1-2 paid sponsorship deals per month
- Refresh creator code promotions
- Share influencer success stories as social proof
