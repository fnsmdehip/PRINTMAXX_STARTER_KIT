# PrayerLock - App Store screenshot specs

**Device sizes required:**
- iPhone 6.7" (1290 x 2796) - iPhone 15 Pro Max
- iPhone 6.5" (1284 x 2778) - iPhone 14 Plus
- iPad 12.9" (2048 x 2732) - iPad Pro

**Design system:**
- Background gradient: #1A237E (deep navy) to #4A148C (dark purple)
- Accent color: #FFD54F (warm gold)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) primary, #FFD54F gold for emphasis
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold (headlines), SF Pro Text Regular (body)
- Device frame: iPhone 15 Pro, midnight black
- Corner radius on device: Apple standard

## Production notes

- All text in sentence case (never Title Case)
- Zero em dashes in any caption
- Gold (#FFD54F) used only for emphasis, never full sentences
- Device bezels should be thin, modern (iPhone 15 Pro style)
- Background extends edge-to-edge, no padding on outer edges
- Each screenshot can be generated in Figma, Sketch, or via Remotion
- Export at 2x resolution for retina
- File naming: prayerlock_screenshot_01.png through prayerlock_screenshot_06.png
- iPad versions: same content, adjusted layout for wider screen, 3-column where applicable
