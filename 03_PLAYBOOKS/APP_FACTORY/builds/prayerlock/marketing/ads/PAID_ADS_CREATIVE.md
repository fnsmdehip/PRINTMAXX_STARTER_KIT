## A/B test plan

### Creative tests (run 2 weeks each):

**Test 1: Hook type**
- A: Problem statement ("every morning your phone wins")
- B: Statistic ("86% of Gen Z wants less screen time")
- C: Social proof ("I've prayed more in 2 weeks than all year")
- Metric: CTR + install rate

**Test 2: Visual style**
- A: Lock screen screenshot (app in action)
- B: Before/after split (scrolling vs praying)
- C: Stats overlay (23 min, 14-day streak)
- Metric: CTR + install rate

**Test 3: CTA framing**
- A: "Download free" (standard)
- B: "Lock your phone" (product action)
- C: "Start your streak" (gamification)
- Metric: Install rate + trial conversion

**Test 4: Audience targeting**
- A: Broad interest (prayer, Christianity, Islam)
- B: Competitor audiences (Hallow, Muslim Pro, Pray.com users)
- C: Digital wellness audiences (Screen Time, app blocker interests)
- D: Youth pastor / church leader audiences
- Metric: CPI + subscription conversion rate

### Budget allocation:

- Week 1-2: $50/day split across 3 creatives, broad targeting
- Week 3-4: $50/day, kill bottom performer, scale winner 2x
- Month 2: $100/day on proven creative + audience combinations
- Scale rule: only increase budget when CPI < $2 and trial-to-paid > 15%

### Target metrics:

| Metric | Target | Kill threshold |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| CPI (cost per install) | < $2.00 | > $4.00 |
| CTR | > 1.5% | < 0.5% |
| Trial start rate | > 40% | < 20% |
| Trial to paid conversion | > 15% | < 8% |
| Day 7 retention | > 30% | < 15% |
| ROAS (30 day) | > 1.5x | < 0.8x |
