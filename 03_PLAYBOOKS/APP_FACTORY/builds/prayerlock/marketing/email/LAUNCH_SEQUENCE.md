**2. Salah mode (for Muslims)**
settings > faith > Islam. enables 5 daily prayer locks synced to your exact location. supports MWL, ISNA, Egyptian, Makkah, and Karachi calculation methods. includes Qibla compass and Quranic verse rotation.

you're not stuck with 10 minutes. go to settings > prayer duration. options: 5, 10, 15, 20, 30 minutes. start with 5 if you're building the habit. increase when it feels natural.

15 minutes before your lock activates, you get a gentle notification. "prayer time in 15 minutes." this isn't a reminder to pray. it's a warning to finish what you're doing before your phone locks.

**5. streak export**
settings > export data. get a CSV of every prayer session: date, duration, emergency unlocks. useful for personal tracking or sharing with an accountability partner.

that's it. no hidden features. no gamification. no social feed. just a lock, a timer, and a streak.
