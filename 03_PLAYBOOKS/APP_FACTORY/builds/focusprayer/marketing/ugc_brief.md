# FocusPrayer - UGC Creator Brief

## Overview

**App:** FocusPrayer
**Niche:** Faith / Christian Lifestyle / Digital Wellness
**Target audience:** Christians 18-45 who struggle with phone-first mornings and want consistent devotion

---

## The Product

FocusPrayer blocks social media apps until you complete your morning devotion.

**Core mechanism:**
1. User sets devotion length (5, 10, or 15 minutes)
2. User selects apps to block
3. User completes guided prayer + scripture reading
4. Apps unlock for the day

**Pricing:** 7-day free trial, then $9.99/mo or $49.99/yr
