## Faith Community Launch Strategy

### Church Partnership Program

**Pilot structure:**
- Free premium for all church participants
- 30-day devotion challenge framework
- Weekly check-in with church leader
- End-of-challenge testimonial collection

**Launch sequence:**
1. Week -2: Identify 10 churches
2. Week -1: Pitch pilot program
3. Launch: Enroll 3-5 church partners
4. Week 2: Expand to 10+ churches
5. Month 2: Scale based on results

### Christian Influencer Seeding

**Strategy:**
- Send lifetime premium to 50 Christian creators
- No obligation, just ask them to try it
- Follow up after 14 days for feedback
- Request post if they love it

**Target profiles:**
- Christian lifestyle creators
- Devotional/Bible study accounts
- Worship leaders with social presence
- Pastor/ministry leader accounts
- Christian mom/family accounts

## Emergency Procedures

### If app crashes on launch day

1. Check Sentry for crash reports
2. Identify affected OS/device
3. Push hotfix if critical
4. Post status update on social
5. Email affected users

### If reviews are negative

1. Respond professionally with grace
2. Identify patterns in complaints
3. Prioritize fixes
4. Ask satisfied users to review
5. Update description if needed

### If church partner has issues

1. Direct phone support for church leaders
2. Resolve within 24 hours
3. Follow up personally
4. Document for future pilots
