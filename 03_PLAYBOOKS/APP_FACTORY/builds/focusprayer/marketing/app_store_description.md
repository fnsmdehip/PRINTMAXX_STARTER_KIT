## Full Description (4000 chars max)

FocusPrayer blocks your apps until you complete your morning devotion.

Set a prayer time. Read the daily scripture. When you're done, your apps unlock.

**Why it works:** Starting your day with God before your phone changes everything. FocusPrayer makes sure devotion comes first.

THE PROBLEM

You want to start your day with prayer. But the phone is right there. Notifications pull you in. Before you know it, you've scrolled for 30 minutes and prayer got skipped.

It happens to everyone. Even people with strong faith struggle with phone distraction.

THE SOLUTION

FocusPrayer locks distracting apps until you complete your devotion:
- 5-15 minute guided prayer time
- Daily scripture passage to read
- Apps unlock automatically when you're done

No willpower needed. The apps are simply unavailable until you pray.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Set your prayer duration (5, 10, or 15 minutes)
2. Select apps to block (social media, games, news)
3. Complete your morning devotion
4. Apps unlock for the day
