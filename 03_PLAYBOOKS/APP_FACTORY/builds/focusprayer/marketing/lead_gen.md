### Problem Section

**Headline:** The morning routine you don't want

You wake up. Before your feet hit the floor, you're checking notifications. Email. Instagram. News. By the time you think about prayer, you're already stressed, distracted, and running late.

You meant to pray. But the phone won.

This happens to believers with deep faith. It's not a character flaw. It's a design problem. Your phone is engineered to capture your attention. Your devotion isn't.

---

### Solution Section

**Headline:** Make devotion the gateway

FocusPrayer flips the equation:

1. Set your morning devotion time (5, 10, or 15 minutes)
2. Pick apps to block until devotion is complete
3. Complete guided prayer + scripture reading
4. Apps unlock for the rest of the day

Your phone doesn't open until you've spent time with God.

---

### How It Works Section

**Step 1: Set your devotion length**
Choose 5, 10, or 15 minutes. Start where you are. You can always increase later.

**Step 2: Block distracting apps**
Select social media, news, games, or any app that steals your morning attention.

**Step 3: Complete your devotion**
Follow the guided prayer timer. Read the daily scripture. Reflect. Pray.

**Step 4: Apps unlock**
When devotion is complete, your apps become available. You've already given God the first part of your day.

### FAQ Section

**Is this legalistic?**
No. FocusPrayer isn't about earning God's favor through works. It's about creating structure that supports the devotion you already want to have. Grace, not law.

**What if I have an emergency?**
Emergency unlock is always available. One tap and everything opens. Your streak resets, but you have access when you truly need it.

**Does it work with my church's devotional?**
Yes. Use FocusPrayer alongside any devotional plan. The app provides structure; you provide the content that resonates with your walk.

**What denominations is this for?**
FocusPrayer is for all Christians. Catholic, Protestant, Orthodox. If you believe in prayer, this is for you.
