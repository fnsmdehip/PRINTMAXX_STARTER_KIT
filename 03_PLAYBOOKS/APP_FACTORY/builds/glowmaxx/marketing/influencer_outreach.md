## Negotiation Guidelines

### Rates by Platform/Follower Count

**TikTok:**
- 1-10k: $50-100
- 10-50k: $100-250
- 50-100k: $250-500
- 100k+: $500+ (negotiate)

**Instagram:**
- 1-10k: $50-75
- 10-50k: $100-200
- 50-100k: $200-400
- 100k+: $400+ (negotiate)

**YouTube:**
- 1-10k: $100-200
- 10-50k: $200-500
- 50-100k: $500-1000
- 100k+: Negotiate

### What to Ask For

1. **Integration video** (15-60 seconds)
   - Natural mention in relevant content
   - Clear CTA (link in bio)
   - Usage footage in app

2. **Dedicated review** (1-3 minutes)
   - Honest review format
   - Show features in detail
   - Before/after if they have

3. **Story/post bundle**
   - 3 stories + 1 feed post
   - Swipe up/link sticker
   - Save highlight

### Red Flags

- Won't share analytics
- Fake engagement patterns
- Refuses to disclose sponsorship
- Wants payment before content
- Unclear content rights

## Content Guidelines for Influencers

### Required Elements

- App name mentioned clearly
- Link/CTA to download
- FTC disclosure (#ad or #sponsored)
- At least one screen recording of app

### Suggested Hooks

- "The app I use to track my mewing"
- "How I stay consistent with my glow up"
- "This app changed my routine"
- "Day [X] of using GlowMaxx"

### Don'ts

- Don't make health claims ("This will give you a jawline")
- Don't fake results
- Don't disparage competitors
- Don't promise specific outcomes

## Tracking & Attribution

### Unique Links

Generate unique links for each influencer:
- glowmaxx.app/[influencer_name]
- Or use deep links with UTM parameters

### Promo Codes

Create unique promo codes:
- [INFLUENCER]10 = 10% off annual
- Track redemptions in RevenueCat

### Reporting

Track for each influencer:
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Installs
- Trial starts
- Conversions
- Revenue

Calculate:
- CPA (cost per acquisition)
- ROAS (return on ad spend)
- Payback period
