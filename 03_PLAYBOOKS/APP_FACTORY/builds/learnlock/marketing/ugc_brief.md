# LearnLock - UGC Creator Brief

## Overview

**App:** LearnLock
**Niche:** Education / Productivity / Student Life
**Target audience:** Students 16-25, remote learners, anyone studying for exams or learning new skills

---

## The Product

LearnLock blocks social media and games while you study with a built-in focus timer.

**Core features:**
1. Focus timer (25, 45, 60 min or custom)
2. App blocking during study sessions
3. Break timer between sessions
4. Streak tracking for consistent study
5. Session statistics

**Pricing:** 3-day free trial, then $6.99/mo or $34.99/yr
