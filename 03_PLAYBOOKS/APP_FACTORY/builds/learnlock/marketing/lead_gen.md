### FAQ Section

**Can I bypass the lock?**
Yes, emergency unlock is available. But it ends your session immediately and resets your daily streak. Most students never use it.

**Does it work for all apps?**
Yes. Block any app on your phone. You choose.

**What about breaks?**
LearnLock has built-in break timers. Apps unlock during breaks, then block again when you start the next session.
