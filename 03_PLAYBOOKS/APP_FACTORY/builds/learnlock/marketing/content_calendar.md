# LearnLock - 30-Day Content Calendar

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Primary)
- Post frequency: 3x daily (students are high-frequency)
- Best times: 7am (before class), 12pm (lunch), 8pm (study time)
- Content mix: 40% relatable, 30% demo, 30% tips
- Focus: Study struggles, phone addiction, productivity

### Instagram (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 1-2 Reels/day + Stories
- Best times: 7am, 12pm, 8pm
- Content mix: Repurpose TikTok + carousels
- Focus: Aesthetic study content, tips

### X/Twitter (Secondary)
- Post frequency: 3-5 tweets/day
- Best times: 7am, 12pm, 5pm, 10pm
- Content mix: Tips, relatable content, engagement
- Focus: Student life, productivity, building in public

### YouTube Shorts
- Post frequency: 1-2/day (repurpose)
- Focus: Evergreen study tips
