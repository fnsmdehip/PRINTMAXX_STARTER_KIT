### Description (4000 chars max)

WalkToUnlock is the fitness app that locks your phone until you hit your step goal. No more excuses. Just walk.

**How It Works**

Set your step goal. Lock your phone. Walk until you hit your target. Phone unlocks automatically when you reach your steps.

**Features**

• Step-Activated Lock - Phone stays locked until you walk
• Customizable Goals - 100 to 10,000 steps per session
• Accurate Pedometer - Uses device motion sensors
• Streak Tracking - Build walking habit momentum
• Daily Stats - Steps, distance, time walking
• Health App Sync - Connects with Apple Health

**Why WalkToUnlock Works**

Most step counters just track. WalkToUnlock motivates. When your phone is locked until you walk, you actually walk. Simple psychology, powerful results.

**Use Cases**

• Morning walk before phone access
• Break from desk work
• Post-meal walks
• Building consistent exercise habit
• Reducing screen time with movement
