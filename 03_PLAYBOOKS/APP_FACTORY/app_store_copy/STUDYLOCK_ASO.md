### Description (4000 chars max)

StudyLock helps students focus by locking their phone during study sessions. Set a timer, lock your phone, and actually study.

**How It Works**

Set your study duration. Lock your phone. Focus on your work. Your phone stays locked until the timer ends.

**Features**

• Study Lock Timer - Phone locks for your set duration
• Flexible Durations - 15 min to 3 hour sessions
• Study Streak Tracking - Build consistent study habits
• Session History - See all your study sessions
• Break Timer - Optional short breaks between sessions
• Offline Mode - No internet needed

**Study Methods Supported**

• Pomodoro Technique - 25 min focus, 5 min break
• Deep Work - 90 min focused sessions
• Custom Sessions - Set any duration you need
• Exam Cramming - Extended 2-3 hour locks
