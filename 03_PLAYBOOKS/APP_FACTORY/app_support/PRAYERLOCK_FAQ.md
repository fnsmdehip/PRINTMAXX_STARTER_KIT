## Getting Started

### What is PrayerLock?

PrayerLock is a phone-locking app that blocks distracting apps until you complete your prayer time. Set a prayer goal, the app locks your selected apps, and you get them back when you finish. Built for Christians who want to prioritize God over their phone.

**Related:** How do I set up PrayerLock?

### How do I set up PrayerLock?

Download, open, pick your blocked apps, set your first prayer goal. Done in under a minute. The app walks you through each step on first launch.

**Related:** What permissions does PrayerLock need?

### What permissions does PrayerLock need?

Notification permission for timer alerts. Screen Time (iOS) or Usage Access (Android) to block apps. These are required for core functionality. We explain why before asking.

**Related:** Can I use PrayerLock without blocking apps?

### Can I use PrayerLock without blocking apps?

Yes. You can use it as a simple prayer timer without the blocking feature. But the app is designed around app blocking. That's the main value.

**Related:** Why isn't regular focus mode enough?

### Do I need to create an account?

No. The app works fully offline without an account. Creating one lets you sync data across devices and backup your prayer streaks.

**Related:** How do I create an account?

### Does PrayerLock work on iPad?

Yes. Universal app that works on iPhone and iPad. Same features, optimized for each screen size.

**Related:** Does PrayerLock work on Mac?

## Prayer Features

### How do I start a prayer session?

Open the app, set your prayer duration (or pick a preset), tap Start. That's it. Blocked apps lock immediately. Timer runs even if you close the app.

**Related:** What are the preset durations?

### What are the preset durations?

5 min, 10 min, 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour. You can also set custom durations. We recommend starting with 5-10 minutes and building up.

**Related:** Can I create my own presets?

### Can I create my own presets?

Yes with Premium. Create and name custom presets like "Morning devotion" (15 min) or "Deep prayer" (30 min). Free version uses default presets.

**Related:** What do I get with Premium?

### What apps can I block?

Any app on your phone. Common choices: social media, games, YouTube, Netflix, messaging apps. You pick what distracts you most from prayer.

**Related:** How do I add apps to block?

### How do I add apps to the block list?

Settings > Blocked Apps > Add. Browse the list and tap apps you want blocked during prayer. Takes effect on your next session.

**Related:** Can I block all apps?

### Can I end a prayer session early?

Not by design. That's the point. If you absolutely must end early, you can force quit the app, but your session won't count and you break your streak.

**Related:** What if there's an emergency?

### What if there's an emergency?

Triple tap the home button (iOS) or power button 3x (Android) to access emergency bypass. Use it only for real emergencies. Overuse defeats the purpose.

**Related:** Emergency bypass isn't working.

### Does the timer run in background?

Yes. Once started, the session continues even if you close the app or your phone locks. You'll get a notification when the session ends.

## Prayer Tracking

### What are prayer streaks?

Days in a row where you completed at least one prayer session. Miss a day, streak resets. Longest streak saved. Builds accountability.

**Related:** What happens if I miss a day?

### What happens if I miss a day?

Your current streak resets to 0. Your longest streak is saved. New streak starts with your next completed session.

**Related:** Can I restore my streak?

### Can I see my prayer history?

Yes. Stats page shows total sessions, total time prayed, average session length, and calendar view of completed days.

**Related:** Can I export my prayer data?

### What is prayer time goal?

Daily or weekly target you set for yourself. Example: "30 minutes daily" or "2 hours weekly." App tracks progress toward goal.

**Related:** How do I set a prayer time goal?

## Troubleshooting

### Blocked apps are still opening.

Check Screen Time (iOS) or Usage Access (Android) permissions. They must be fully enabled. Try revoking and re-granting permission, then restart the app.

**Related:** How do I fix permission issues?

### Timer stopped unexpectedly.

Check if your phone restarted or battery died. Low power mode on some phones can kill background apps. Add PrayerLock to battery optimization exceptions.

**Related:** How do I prevent the timer from stopping?

### I can't start a session.

Make sure you have at least one app blocked. The app requires blocked apps to start a session. Also check that you haven't hit any iOS Screen Time daily limits.

**Related:** Why is the Start button grayed out?

### My streak reset but I prayed yesterday.

Check your timezone. If you traveled, the day boundary might have shifted. Also ensure your session fully completed (didn't force quit early).

**Related:** Can you restore my streak?

### Can you restore my streak?

If it reset due to a bug or timezone issue, yes. Email support@prayerlock.app with your account info and we'll investigate and restore if warranted.

**Related:** How do I contact support?

### App is crashing.

Update to the latest version. If crashes continue, uninstall and reinstall. Your data syncs back when you sign in. Still crashing? Email us with phone model and OS version.

**Related:** How do I report a bug?
