# App Support Response Templates

Ready-to-use templates for common support scenarios. Copy, customize with specifics, send.

---

## General Response Structure

Every support response should:
1. Acknowledge the issue
2. Provide solution or next steps
3. Offer follow-up if needed
4. Keep it brief

**Tone:** Friendly, helpful, human. Not corporate, not robotic. Write like a person who actually cares.

## Common Support Responses

### Acknowledgment Templates

**Quick acknowledgment (when you need time to investigate):**
```
Hey [Name],

Got your message. Looking into this now and will get back to you within 24 hours with an answer.

Thanks for your patience.

[Your name]
```

**General issue acknowledgment:**
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out. Sorry you're running into this issue.

[Solution/next steps here]

Let me know if that doesn't fix it and I'll dig deeper.

[Your name]
```

### Bug Report Responses

**Bug confirmed, fix coming:**
```
Hi [Name],

You're right, this is a bug. Good catch.

We've logged it and are working on a fix. Should be in the next update, probably within 1-2 weeks.

In the meantime, here's a workaround: [workaround if one exists]

Really appreciate you reporting this. Helps us make the app better.

[Your name]
```

**Bug confirmed, already fixed in next version:**
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reporting this. We actually just fixed this bug. The fix will be in version [X.X], which should hit the app store in the next few days.

Update when it's available and you should be good.

Let me know if you still see the issue after updating.

[Your name]
```

**Can't reproduce the bug:**
```
Hi [Name],

Tried to reproduce this on our end but couldn't get it to happen. A few questions:

1. What phone/tablet are you using?
2. What OS version?
3. What version of the app? (Settings > About)
4. Can you walk me through exactly what you did before the issue?

A screenshot or screen recording would be super helpful if you can grab one.

Thanks for helping us track this down.

[Your name]
```

**User error (but don't say that):**
```
Hi [Name],

I see what's happening here. The way this works is [explanation].

To do what you're trying to do: [correct steps]

This trips up a lot of people, so we're looking at making it clearer in a future update.

Let me know if you run into any other issues.

[Your name]
```

### Refund Request Handling

**Directing to app store (standard response):**
```
Hi [Name],

Refunds are handled by [Apple/Google], not us directly. Here's how to request one:

**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find the purchase and tap "Report a Problem"
4. Select your reason and submit

**Android:**
1. Open Play Store
2. Tap profile > Payments & subscriptions > Budget & history
3. Find the purchase and tap "Report a problem"

They usually respond within a few days. Let me know if you run into issues with the process.

[Your name]
```

**Refund request with complaint:**
```
Hi [Name],

Sorry the app didn't work out for you. Here's how to get a refund:

[Include standard refund instructions above]

If you don't mind sharing what didn't work for you, I'd appreciate the feedback. Always looking to improve.

[Your name]
```

**User wants you to process refund:**
```
Hi [Name],

I wish I could process this directly, but Apple/Google handle all billing. I have no access to charge or refund your card.

The good news is their refund process is pretty straightforward:

[Include standard refund instructions]

If they give you trouble, email me back and I'll see if there's anything I can do from our end.

[Your name]
```

### Subscription Questions

**How to cancel:**
```
Hi [Name],

Here's how to cancel your subscription:

**iPhone/iPad:**
Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > [App Name] > Cancel Subscription

**Android:**
Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > [App Name] > Cancel Subscription

Note: Canceling stops future charges but you keep access until your current billing period ends.

Let me know if you run into any issues.

[Your name]
```

**Charged after canceling:**
```
Hi [Name],

That's frustrating. A few things could have happened:

1. Cancellation happened after the renewal date (you're charged at the start of each period)
2. Apple/Google billing delay (charges sometimes show up days after renewal)
3. Cancellation didn't go through (rare but happens)

Check your subscription status in [phone settings, as above]. If it shows as canceled but you were still charged, contact [Apple/Google] support directly - they can see your billing history and issue refunds.

If you need help with their support process, let me know.

[Your name]
```

**Subscription not activating:**
```
Hi [Name],

Sometimes there's a sync delay between the app store and our servers. Try these steps:

1. Force close the app completely
2. Make sure you're connected to wifi
3. Reopen the app and wait 30 seconds
4. Go to Settings > Restore Purchases

If Premium still isn't showing, email me your Apple/Google account email (the one you used to purchase) and I'll check our records.

[Your name]
```

### Account Issues

**Can't log in:**
```
Hi [Name],

Let's get you back in. A few things to try:

1. Double-check the email address (typos happen)
2. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check spam folder for password reset email
4. Try logging in with Apple/Google if you used that originally

If none of that works, tell me the email address you think you used and I'll check if we have an account for it.

[Your name]
```

**Account deletion request:**
```
Hi [Name],

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
3. Confirm deletion

This permanently removes all your data from our servers within 30 days. It can't be undone.

If you can't access the app, reply with the email address on your account and I can process the deletion manually.

[Your name]
```

**Data export request:**
```
Hi [Name],

Here's how to export your data:

1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings > Data > Export
3. Choose your format (CSV or JSON)
4. Share the file wherever you need it

The export includes all your [sessions/prayers/steps/etc.] and stats. Let me know if you need it in a different format.

[Your name]
```

```

```

```
Hi [Name],

I understand you're frustrated. I've explained [previous solution] and unfortunately that's the extent of what I can do from my end.

For billing/refund issues, [Apple/Google] support is the only one who can help: [relevant link]

[Your name]
```

**Threatening bad review:**
```
Hi [Name],

I'd prefer to solve this than have you leave unhappy. Let me try one more thing: [genuine attempt to help]

If that still doesn't work, I understand if you want to leave feedback. Either way, I appreciate you giving us a chance to help first.

[Your name]
```

## Response Time Guidelines

| Priority | Response Time | Examples |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| Critical | < 2 hours | App completely broken, billing error, security issue |
| High | < 24 hours | Bugs affecting core features, subscription issues |
| Normal | < 48 hours | General questions, feature requests, minor bugs |
| Low | < 1 week | General feedback, minor suggestions |

## Escalation Paths

**When to escalate:**
- Security vulnerabilities (immediately to engineering)
- Repeated billing issues (to billing/finance)
- Legal threats (to legal/management)
- Press inquiries (to communications)
- Large refund requests (to management)

**When NOT to escalate:**
- Angry users (handle with de-escalation templates)
- Feature requests (log them, respond normally)
- Standard bugs (log them, respond normally)
- "I want to speak to a manager" (try to resolve first)

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- Respond quickly (even if just acknowledging)
- Take ownership of issues
- Provide specific steps, not vague instructions
- Admit when you don't know something
- Follow up if you promised to

### Don't:
- Use corporate jargon ("we apologize for any inconvenience")
- Make promises you can't keep
- Blame the user (even when it's user error)
- Copy-paste without customizing
- Ignore the emotional component of complaints
