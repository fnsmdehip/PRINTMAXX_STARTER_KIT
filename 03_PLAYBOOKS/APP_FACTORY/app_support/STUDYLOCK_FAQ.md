## Getting Started

### What is StudyLock?

StudyLock is a phone-locking app that blocks distracting apps while you study. Set a study duration, the app blocks whatever you choose (Instagram, games, etc.) until the timer ends. Built for students who struggle with phone addiction during study sessions.

**Related:** How is this different from regular timers?

### How do I set up StudyLock?

Download, open, pick your blocked apps, set your first study session length. Done in under a minute. The app walks you through each step on first launch.

**Related:** What permissions does StudyLock need?

### What permissions does StudyLock need?

Notification permission for timer alerts. Screen Time (iOS) or Usage Access (Android) to block apps. These are required for core functionality. We explain why before asking.

**Related:** Can I use StudyLock without blocking apps?

### Can I use StudyLock without blocking apps?

Yes. You can use it as a simple focus timer without the blocking feature. But the app is designed around app blocking. That's the main value.

**Related:** Why isn't regular focus mode enough?

### Do I need to create an account?

No. The app works fully offline without an account. Creating one lets you sync data across devices and backup your study stats.

**Related:** How do I create an account?

### Does StudyLock work on iPad?

Yes. Universal app that works on iPhone and iPad. Same features, optimized for each screen size.

**Related:** Does StudyLock work on Mac?

## Timer & Focus

### How do I start a study session?

Open the app, set your duration (or pick a preset), tap Start. That's it. Blocked apps lock immediately. Timer runs even if you close the app.

**Related:** What are the preset durations?

### What are the preset durations?

25 min (Pomodoro), 45 min (class period), 60 min, 90 min, 2 hours. You can also set custom durations from 5 minutes to 4 hours.

**Related:** Can I create my own presets?

### Can I create my own presets?

Yes with Premium. Create and name custom presets like "Math homework" (45 min) or "Essay writing" (90 min). Free version uses default presets.

**Related:** What do I get with Premium?

### What is Pomodoro mode?

Classic productivity technique: 25 minutes focus, 5 minute break, repeat. After 4 cycles, longer break. StudyLock handles the timing automatically.

**Related:** How do I use Pomodoro mode?

### How do I use Pomodoro mode?

Settings > Timer Mode > Pomodoro. Customize work/break durations if you want. The app handles all the cycling automatically.

**Related:** Can I skip breaks in Pomodoro mode?

### Can I end a session early?

Not by design. That's the point. If you absolutely must end early, you can force quit the app, but your session won't count and you break your streak.

**Related:** What if there's an emergency?

### What if there's an emergency?

Triple tap the home button (iOS) or power button 3x (Android) to access emergency bypass. Use it only for real emergencies. Overuse defeats the purpose.

**Related:** Emergency bypass isn't working.

### Does the timer run in background?

Yes. Once started, the session continues even if you close the app or your phone locks. You'll get a notification when the session ends.

**Related:** Timer stopped when my phone went to sleep.

### Can I pause a session?

No. Sessions are meant to be completed. Pausing defeats the purpose of focused study. If you need flexibility, set shorter sessions.

**Related:** What if I get interrupted?

## Troubleshooting

### Blocked apps are still opening.

Check Screen Time (iOS) or Usage Access (Android) permissions. They must be fully enabled. Try revoking and re-granting permission, then restart the app.

**Related:** How do I fix permission issues?

### Timer stopped unexpectedly.

Check if your phone restarted or battery died. Low power mode on some phones can kill background apps. Add StudyLock to battery optimization exceptions.

**Related:** How do I prevent the timer from stopping?

### I can't start a session.

Make sure you have at least one app blocked. The app requires blocked apps to start a session. Also check that you haven't hit any iOS Screen Time daily limits.

**Related:** Why is the Start button grayed out?

### My streak reset but I studied yesterday.

Check your timezone. If you traveled, the day boundary might have shifted. Also ensure your session fully completed (didn't force quit early).

**Related:** Can you restore my streak?

### Can you restore my streak?

If it reset due to a bug or timezone issue, yes. Email support@studylock.app with your account info and we'll investigate and restore if warranted.

**Related:** How do I contact support?

### App is crashing.

Update to the latest version. If crashes continue, uninstall and reinstall. Your data syncs back when you sign in. Still crashing? Email us with phone model and OS version.

**Related:** How do I report a bug?

### Widget shows wrong data.

Open the main app to refresh, then check widget. Remove and re-add widget if still wrong. iOS caches widget data and sometimes needs a kick.

**Related:** Why isn't my widget updating?

### Sounds aren't playing.

Check your phone volume and mute switch. Try a different sound in settings. Some sounds require download first. Make sure your phone isn't on silent mode.

**Related:** Can I adjust sound volume in the app?

### Emergency bypass isn't working.

Make sure you're doing the correct gesture (triple tap home or 3x power). Some phone cases interfere with button detection. Practice the gesture before you need it.

**Related:** I used emergency bypass but it didn't work.
