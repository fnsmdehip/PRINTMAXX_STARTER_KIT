# Day 7 milestone campaign

Goal: Celebrate the first week and convert casual users into committed users.

D7 is the first major milestone. Users who reach it are 4x more likely to still be active at D30.

---

## Week 1 completion psychology

By day 7, users have:
- Formed initial habit loops
- Experienced your core value multiple times
- Decided if the app fits their identity

Your job now: Validate their progress and set up week 2.

## Targeting and segmentation

### Segment A: Power users

**Criteria:**
- 7/7 days active
- Multiple core actions per day
- High engagement score (>70)

**Approach:** Premium conversion focus. These users are ready.

### Segment B: Consistent users

**Criteria:**
- 5-6/7 days active
- Completed most core actions
- Medium engagement score (40-70)

**Approach:** Celebrate progress. Nudge toward premium with value focus.

### Segment C: Casual users

**Criteria:**
- 3-4/7 days active
- Some core actions completed
- Lower engagement score (20-40)

**Approach:** Re-engage with best content. Don't push premium yet.

### Segment D: At-risk users

**Criteria:**
- 1-2/7 days active
- Few or no core actions
- Low engagement score (<20)

**Approach:** Win-back messaging. Highlight what they're missing.
