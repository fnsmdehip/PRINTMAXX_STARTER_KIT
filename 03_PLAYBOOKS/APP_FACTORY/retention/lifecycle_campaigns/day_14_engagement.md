# Day 14 engagement campaign

Goal: Deepen engagement and identify users ready for premium conversion.

D14 users are committed. They've survived the first two weeks. Now optimize for LTV.

---

## The 2-week threshold

By day 14:
- Habit is established
- User knows app's value
- Initial excitement has faded
- Real utility determines continued use

Your job: Keep the experience fresh and reward loyalty.

## Targeting and segmentation

### Segment A: Premium ready

**Criteria:**
- 12+ days active in 14
- High feature usage
- Hit free tier limits

**Approach:** Direct premium offer with urgency

### Segment B: Engaged free

**Criteria:**
- 10-12 days active
- Consistent but not intensive use
- Hasn't explored all features

**Approach:** Feature discovery, soft premium nudge

### Segment C: Casual committed

**Criteria:**
- 7-10 days active
- Core action most days
- Light engagement

**Approach:** Deepen engagement before premium push

### Segment D: Fading

**Criteria:**
- <7 days active
- Declining engagement
- At risk for D30 churn

**Approach:** Re-engagement with best content

## D14 to D30 bridge

The next 2 weeks determine who becomes a long-term user.

Focus areas:
1. Maintain habit through variety (new content)
2. Deepen value through features
3. Build social connection (community/challenges)
4. Convert premium-ready users

D14 users who convert to premium retain at 80%+ at D30.
