# Churned user winback campaign

Goal: Re-activate users who have been inactive for 30+ days.

Win-back conversion rates are 2-5%. Low, but these users cost nothing to acquire again.

---

## Churned user definition

**Churned:** No app session in 30+ days

**Subcategories:**
- Recently churned: 30-60 days
- Long-term churned: 60-90 days
- Dormant: 90+ days

Win-back success rate drops with time. Focus energy on recently churned.

## Win-back by churn reason

### Reason: Lost interest

**Signals:** Gradual decline, fewer sessions over time

**Approach:**
- Highlight new content
- Offer fresh start option
- Show what's changed

**Message:**
```
Things have changed since you left
10 new [workouts/devotionals/features] added. Fresh start waiting.
```

### Reason: Technical issues

**Signals:** Crash reports, negative reviews, support tickets

**Approach:**
- Acknowledge the problem
- Confirm it's fixed
- Offer compensation

**Message:**
```
We fixed the bugs
Sorry about the issues you experienced. We've rebuilt [feature]. Give us another shot?
```

### Reason: Didn't fit routine

**Signals:** Never built consistent habit, sporadic usage

**Approach:**
- Offer flexibility
- Show quick options
- Reduce commitment ask

**Message:**
```
No routine? No problem.
New 2-minute option for busy days. Zero commitment.
```

### Reason: Price

**Signals:** Cancelled subscription, never converted

**Approach:**
- Lead with free value
- Offer discount
- Show ROI

**Message:**
```
Premium not for you? That's okay.
Free version got better. Or: here's 50% off if you change your mind.
```

## Win-back offer ladder

Escalate offers over time:

| Day | Offer | Value |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 30 | "What's new" content | $0 |
| 45 | Exclusive content unlock | ~$5 value |
| 60 | 7 days premium free | ~$3 value |
| 75 | 30 days premium free | ~$10 value |
| 90 | Lifetime 70% off | ~$50+ value |

Only show higher offers if lower ones failed.

## Suppression and fatigue rules

### Don't win-back:

- Users who explicitly unsubscribed from emails
- Users who left negative app store reviews
- Users who reported the app
- Users who requested data deletion

### Frequency limits:

- Maximum 1 push per week to churned users
- Maximum 1 email per 2 weeks
- Stop all outreach after 90 days
- Respect "do not contact" requests

### Fatigue prevention:

- Cap total win-back attempts at 6
- Mix channels (push, email, in-app)
- Space messages at least 10 days apart after D60
- Test reducing frequency vs. increasing

---

## When win-back isn't worth it

Some users aren't worth chasing:

- Never completed onboarding (likely wrong audience)
- Installed from incentivized campaigns (bot/farm)
- Multiple reinstalls and churns (pattern)
- Very low engagement even when active (<1 min sessions)

Focus win-back budget on users who showed real engagement before churning.
