# Day 30 loyal user campaign

Goal: Convert 30-day users into advocates and maximize LTV.

D30 users are your core base. They've proven commitment. Now optimize for retention, revenue, and referrals.

---

## The 30-day user profile

Users who reach D30 have:
- Integrated app into daily routine
- Experienced meaningful results
- Formed identity around the habit
- Developed preferences and patterns

These users are 6x more valuable than D1 users. Treat them accordingly.

## Premium conversion (if still free)

D30 free users are the hardest to convert. They've gotten value without paying.

### Approach 1: Feature lock

After D30, limit a previously free feature:

```
This feature is now Premium-only

You've had full access for 30 days. Time to decide.

Continue with Premium:
- Keep [feature]
- Plus [bonus 1]
- Plus [bonus 2]

Or continue free with basic features.

[Upgrade] [Stay free]
```

### Approach 2: Exclusive offer

Offer something they can't get otherwise:

```
30-Day Member Exclusive

This offer isn't on our website. It's only for users like you who've proven commitment.

Lifetime access: $79 (normally $199)
- Never pay again
- All future features included
- Priority support

Expires in 48 hours.

[Claim offer] [No thanks]
```

### Approach 3: Premium trial extension

Give a taste of premium:

```
Try Premium free for 7 days

You've been using free for 30 days.

See what you're missing:
- [Premium feature 1]
- [Premium feature 2]
- [Premium feature 3]

No credit card required. Cancels automatically.

[Start trial] [Stay free]
```

## Referral program details

### Mechanics

- User gets unique referral link
- Friend gets 1 month premium free
- User gets 1 month premium free when friend activates
- Both parties must complete D7 to qualify

### Tracking

```
Referral dashboard:
- Your link: [unique_link]
- Links shared: [X]
- Friends signed up: [Y]
- Months earned: [Z]
```

### Fraud prevention

- Max 10 referral rewards per user
- Friend must be new (not previously registered email/device)
- Both users must hit D7 retention
- IP validation to prevent self-referral

## Targeting and segmentation

### Segment A: Premium subscribers

**Approach:**
- Thank profusely
- Request review
- Invite to referral program
- Offer annual upgrade if monthly

### Segment B: High-engagement free

**Approach:**
- Premium conversion with exclusive offer
- If declined, aggressive referral program
- Feature gating begins

### Segment C: Moderate engagement free

**Approach:**
- Re-engage with new content
- Soft premium nudge
- Community invitation

### Segment D: Low engagement (D30 but barely)

**Approach:**
- Win-back best content
- No premium push
- Focus on reactivation

## D30 to D90 bridge

D30 users have formed a habit. The risk now is:
- Boredom (same content, no progression)
- Life changes (app doesn't fit anymore)
- Competition (better alternative appears)

Counter with:
1. Fresh content monthly
2. Progressive challenges
3. Community engagement
4. Feature releases

D30 users who hit D90 have 70%+ chance of becoming annual users.
