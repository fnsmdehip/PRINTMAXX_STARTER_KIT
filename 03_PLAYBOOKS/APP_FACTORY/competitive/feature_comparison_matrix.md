## Competitive Gap Summary

### Where We Win
1. **Price:** 50%+ cheaper than premium competitors
2. **Simplicity:** Single-purpose vs. feature bloat
3. **Community:** Accountability built in, not bolted on
4. **Cross-platform:** Both mobile OS from day one
5. **Positioning:** Beginners and casual users first

### Where We Lose
1. **Content depth:** Can't match years of content library
2. **Brand recognition:** Unknown vs. established players
3. **Features:** Fewer features than mature apps
4. **Integrations:** No device/ecosystem plays
5. **Resources:** Can't outspend on marketing

### Our Strategic Response
- Don't compete on features, compete on simplicity
- Don't compete on content, compete on community
- Don't compete on price with free, compete on value
- Don't compete on everything, own one thing
