**Sequential launch (Recommended):**

### Optimizing Existing Niche

**If lead cost is high (>$30):**
1. Check traffic source (wrong audience?)
2. Check ad copy (weak promise?)
3. Try second traffic source
4. Adjust targeting (more specific niche)

**If conversion is low (<2%):**
1. Check email sequence (weak copy?)
2. Use QUICK_START_TEMPLATES.md copy
3. Add social proof (testimonials)
4. Improve lead qualification

**If you're stuck:**
1. Read README.md → troubleshooting section
2. Review success stories → pattern matching
3. Research competitors → steal/improve positioning
4. Reach out to niche experts → validate assumptions

## Key Insights from All Documentation

### Insight 1: Tier Matters Most
- Tier 1 niches ($3K-8K MRR) have clear B2B buyers (businesses pay recurring fees)
- Tier 2 niches ($2K-6K MRR) have proven affiliate programs + some B2B
- Tier 3 niches ($1K-3K MRR) work best through course affiliate + partnerships
- Tier 4 niches ($500-3K MRR) are better as content affiliate, not local lead gen

**Action:** If you haven't picked a niche, start with Tier 1 for fastest revenue

### Insight 2: Multiple Revenue Streams Beat Single Stream
- Example (N024 Solo Travelers): Course 60% + Affiliate 20% + Retreat commission 20%
- Example (N031 Voice AI): Retainer 60% + Lead sales 25% + Affiliate 15%
- Single stream is fragile; 2-3 streams is resilient + higher total revenue

**Action:** Plan for 2-3 revenue sources from day 1

### Insight 3: Lead Cost < LTV ÷ 3 = Profitable
- If customer LTV = $1,000, you can spend up to $333 to acquire
- Most niches have $500-2K LTV, so lead cost should be < $150-650
- Start conservative (spend $15-30/lead), optimize upward

**Action:** Calculate your LTV before launching traffic

### Insight 4: Email Sequence > Ads
- Best performers: 7-email sequence gets 25-35% opens, 3-8% conversion
- Ad copy matters, but email sequence matters more
- Use QUICK_START_TEMPLATES.md proven structure (don't customize)

**Action:** Nail email copy before scaling ad spend

### Insight 5: Geography Creates Pricing Power
- Same lead in SF = 40% more expensive than Midwest
- Geographic arbitrage: operate from cheap area, sell to expensive area
- Rural/Midwest leads are cheaper to acquire but lower value

**Action:** Consider geography in niche selection

### Insight 6: B2B Partnerships = Scaling
- Lead sales (PPL) to coaches/therapists = immediate revenue
- Once proven with 10-20 leads, partners commit to retainers
- Retainer = recurring revenue = easier scaling

**Action:** Plan B2B cold outreach by Week 6

### Insight 7: Course Affiliate is Easiest Entry
- 60-70% commission on established courses (no product needed)
- Lower barrier than building course yourself
- Best for Tier 3-4 niches with existing affiliate programs

**Action:** Check if your niche has established course programs
