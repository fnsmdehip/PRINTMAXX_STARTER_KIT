## Metrics to Track from Day 1

**Essential metrics (non-negotiable):**
- Ad spend → Cost per lead
- Leads → Email opens → Click rate
- Leads → Customers → Conversion rate
- Lead cost → Customer LTV → Profit per customer

**Calculate daily:**
```
Lead cost = Total ad spend ÷ Total leads
Conversion rate = Sales ÷ Leads
Customer LTV = Revenue per customer × Average lifetime (months)
Profit = (LTV × Conversion rate) - Lead cost
ROAS = Revenue ÷ Ad spend (target: 2:1+)
```

**Target benchmarks by month:**

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Lead cost | $15-50 | $12-40 | $10-30 |
| Conversion | 1-3% | 2-5% | 3-8% |
| ROAS | 0.5:1 to 1.5:1 | 1:1 to 2:1 | 2:1 to 4:1 |
| MRR | $0 to $1K | $500 to $3K | $1.5K to $6K+ |

**If not hitting targets:**
- Lead cost too high? Lower cost per acquisition (cheaper traffic source or better targeting)
- Conversion too low? Improve email sequence or lead qualification
- ROAS below 1:1? Pause campaign, fix offer, restart with fresh ad copy

- YES → Pick Tier 3 niche matching expertise (N002-N028)
  - Easier to create credible content
  - Can leverage your network
  - Faster to position as authority
- NO → Continue to Question 2

**Question 2: Do I want maximum revenue potential?**
- YES → Pick Tier 1 niche (N031, N033, N029, N024, N025)
  - Highest pricing ($500-2K/month retainers or $100-200/lead)
  - Fastest to $5K+ MRR
  - Best for solopreneur scaling
- NO → Continue to Question 3

**Question 3: Do I want a validated market?**
- YES → Pick Tier 2 niche (N003, N009, N013, N014, N016, N030)
  - Proven business models
  - Established affiliate programs
  - Lower risk
- NO → Pick Tier 4 niche (N001, N004-N005, N007, N011, N015, N017-N020, N032)
  - Leverage existing audience
  - Content-driven (not lead gen focus)
  - Better as affiliate-first

### Which Traffic Source Should I Start With?

**By niche:**

| Niche Type | Traffic Source | Why |
|---|---|---|
| B2B (N031, N033, N029) | Google Ads or LinkedIn | High buyer intent |
| Professional (N016, N030) | Google Ads or LinkedIn | Decision-makers search |
| Fitness/Health (N003, N009, N022) | Facebook/Instagram or YouTube | Demographics + interest targeting |
| Crypto/Finance (N013, N014) | Reddit or YouTube | Community + engagement |

**General rule:**
- If targeting professionals/businesses → Google Ads
- If targeting consumers by interest → Facebook/Instagram
- If targeting passionate communities → Reddit
- If targeting inspiration seekers → Pinterest
- If product-focused → YouTube

### Which Business Model Should I Use?

**Quick flowchart:**

```
Do you want recurring revenue?
  YES → Retainer ($500-2K/month) or Membership ($10-40/month)
  NO → Pay-per-lead ($25-200/lead) or Affiliate (commission%)

Do you want immediate cash flow?
  YES → Pay-per-lead or Course affiliate
  NO → Retainer (takes 2-4 weeks to close)

Do you have existing product/course?
  YES → Course affiliate (60-70% commission)
  NO → Lead sales (40-50% commission on partner SaaS)

Do you want high-touch sales?
  YES → Retainer (takes sales calls) or B2B leads ($100-200/lead)
  NO → Course affiliate (automated) or Membership (passive)
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Picking Wrong Niche
**Result:** No leads, no revenue
**Avoid:** Start with Tier 1 or 2 niche. Have proof of business before starting.

### Mistake 2: No Lead Magnet
**Result:** 0% signup rate, zero leads
**Avoid:** Create free guide/assessment (15 pages max). Take 2-3 days. Launch.

### Mistake 3: Bad Email Sequence
**Result:** 50% opens but 0% conversions
**Avoid:** Use QUICK_START_TEMPLATES.md copy-paste (proven structure).

### Mistake 4: Wrong Traffic Source
**Result:** High lead cost ($100+), negative ROI
**Avoid:** Match traffic source to niche (see traffic source table above).

### Mistake 5: Underpricing
**Result:** High revenue but low profit (burnout)
**Avoid:** Research market rates in PRICING_RESEARCH_BY_NICHE.md first.

### Mistake 6: No B2B Follow-up
**Result:** 50% revenue left on table
**Avoid:** After course sales, cold email 30 coaches/specialists with lead offer.

### Mistake 7: Giving Up Too Early
**Result:** Launch stop after 2 weeks
**Avoid:** Plan to test for 4-6 weeks minimum. Optimize, don't quit.

## Support & Troubleshooting

**If your lead cost is too high ($50+):**
1. Are you targeting the right business type? (Check playbook)
2. Is your ad copy clear? (Are you leading with benefit, not features?)
3. Are you reaching the decision-maker? (LinkedIn targeting for B2B)
4. Should you switch traffic source? (Try second option in playbook)

**If conversion is too low (<1%):**
1. Is your email sequence compelling? (Use QUICK_START_TEMPLATES.md)
2. Does your offer match the lead magnet? (Consistency matters)
3. Are you following up? (48 hours max before first email)
4. Is your product good? (Are customers happy with leads?)

**If you're getting 0 leads:**
1. Is your landing page live? (Test it yourself)
2. Is your ad running? (Check Google Ads dashboard)
3. Is tracking pixel installed? (Verify conversion pixel working)
4. Is landing page optimized? (Check page load speed, mobile friendly)
