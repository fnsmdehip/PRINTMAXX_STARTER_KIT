## Lead Qualification Checklist (For B2B Sales)

Use this to decide which leads to follow up with first:

**Score 1-5 for each:**

- [ ] Budget available now? (1=no, 5=yes, unlimited)
- [ ] Pain point critical? (1=nice-to-have, 5=losing revenue)
- [ ] Decision-maker? (1=no, 5=yes, sole decision)
- [ ] Timeline? (1=6+ months, 5=this month)
- [ ] Competitor using similar service? (1=no, 5=yes)

**Score 20+:** Follow up immediately (hot lead)
**Score 15-19:** Follow up within 7 days (warm lead)
**Score <15:** Add to nurture sequence (cold lead)

## Tracking Spreadsheet Template

Track these metrics in a Google Sheet:

| Date | Traffic Source | Impressions | Clicks | Lead Cost | Signups | Email Opens | Course Sales | B2B Leads | Revenue | ROAS |
|------|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1/26 | Google Ads | 1,200 | 45 | $10 | 8 | 6 | 0 | 0 | $0 | - |
| 1/27 | Facebook | 850 | 32 | $15 | 5 | 4 | 1 | 0 | $58 | 0.39 |

**Key metrics to calculate:**
- Lead cost = Ad spend ÷ leads
- Conversion rate = Sales ÷ leads
- Email open rate = Opens ÷ emails sent
- ROAS = Revenue ÷ ad spend

**Target benchmarks:**
- Lead cost: < $30 (Tier 1), < $20 (Tier 2), < $15 (Tier 3)
- Conversion rate: 3-8%
- Email open rate: 25-35%
- ROAS: 2:1 minimum
