## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### Strategy 1: Start Low, Raise Over Time
```
Month 1-2: Launch at -20% below market
  Rationale: Prove concept, gather testimonials

Month 3-4: Raise to market rate
  Rationale: Have proof, testimonials, case studies

Month 5+: Raise above market if demand strong
  Rationale: High LTV customers, proven model
```

### Strategy 2: Tiered Pricing (Maximize Revenue)
```
Tier 1: Budget option (Lower price, higher volume)
Tier 2: Standard option (Market rate, most conversions)
Tier 3: Premium option (Higher price, concierge service)

Example (N031 Voice AI):
Tier 1: $300/month (1 location, basic support)
Tier 2: $500/month (1 location, priority support)
Tier 3: $1,000/month (multi-location, dedicated manager)

Reality: Most buy Tier 2. Some Tier 3 customers = high margin.
```

### Strategy 3: Pay-Per-Lead (Cash Flow First)
```
Launch with PPL model ($25-75/lead)
  Rationale: Immediate revenue, no long-term commitment

Then upsell to retainer customers
  Rationale: Once they see value, convert to recurring

Revenue mix: 50% PPL + 50% retainer = stable + growth
```

## Red Flags: Pricing That's Too Low

| Warning Sign | Implication | Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| Leads not converting | Price too high OR targeting wrong businesses | Increase targeting precision, lower price slightly |
| Can't cover ad costs | Lead cost exceeds profit | Raise prices 20-30% OR improve conversion |
| Constant competition on price | Market saturated | Add value (support, guarantees, results tracking) |
| Clients want discounts | You set price too high | Actually: raise prices, but justify with results |
| High churn (< 3 months) | Expectations mismatch | Better qualification or higher value delivery |

---

## Red Flags: Pricing That's Too High

| Warning Sign | Implication | Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| Long sales cycle (> 30 days) | Price creates hesitation | Lower to market rate + guarantee |
| Few/no sales despite leads | Buyers don't see value | Build case studies, testimonials, proof |
| Callbacks with objections | Price is barrier | Create payment plans or tiered options |
| Competition stealing customers | Price more expensive | Justify with superior results or service |
| Competitors undercutting | You're above market | Do competitive analysis, consider pivot |
