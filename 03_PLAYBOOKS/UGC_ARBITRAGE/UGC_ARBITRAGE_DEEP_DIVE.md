## The Opportunity

### The Arbitrage Model

```
Source: $3-50/video (Eastern EU, SEA, LATAM)
Sell: $100-500/video (US/UK brands)
Margin: 70-90%
```

**Why it works:**
- Geographic wage arbitrage (10-50x cost difference)
- US/UK brands pay premium for "authentic" content
- Direct sourcing eliminates platform middlemen
- AI alternatives provide infinite scalability
- Brands need constant creative refresh for ads

### Revenue Proof Points (From Alpha)

**@alexcooldev:** "Build the app. Post daily. Use UGC campaigns. Market more than you build. Hit $100K MRR."

**@hasan28d:** "$50K weeks on Meta by dropshipping with AI UGC. Generate 200+ AI UGC variants."

**@simonecanciello App Marketing Ladder:**
- $0-5k: Organic cheap iPhone + VPN + AI content
- $5k-20k: UGC creators
- $20k+: Meta + Spark ads

### Business Models

**Model 1: UGC Agency**
- Source cheap creators
- White-label to brands
- Manage production
- Margin: 60-80%

**Model 2: UGC Marketplace Middleman**
- Connect brands with creators
- Take 15-30% per transaction
- Lower touch, lower margin

**Model 3: Hybrid AI + Human**
- Use AI for initial drafts
- Human polish/voiceover
- Lowest cost, highest margin (80-90%)

**Model 4: AI UGC Factory**
- Full AI generation (no humans)
- Arcads, HeyGen, Kling stack
- Infinite scale potential
- Margin: 90%+ (tool costs only)

### Vetting Process

**Step 1: Initial Screen**
- [ ] Has portfolio (real videos, not stock)
- [ ] Responds within 24 hours
- [ ] Clear English communication
- [ ] Rates within your range
- [ ] Social proof (followers, testimonials)

**Step 2: Test Order**
- Single video only (not bulk)
- Clear brief with all specs
- Reasonable deadline (5-7 days)
- Pay agreed rate (don't lowball test)
- Evaluate: quality, communication, adherence to brief

**Step 3: Quality Assessment**
| Factor | Weight | Check |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Video quality | 30% | Lighting, audio, framing |
| Delivery | 25% | Natural, engaging, authentic |
| Brief adherence | 20% | Hit all points? |
| Turnaround | 15% | On time? Communicated delays? |
| Revision handling | 10% | Responsive? Defensive? |

**Step 4: Ongoing Tracking**
Track per creator in spreadsheet:
- Projects completed
- Average quality score (1-5)
- Revision rate (lower is better)
- Reliability score
- Rate changes

### Red Flags

**Immediate rejection:**
- Won't show portfolio
- Demands full payment upfront
- Prices too good ($1-2/video)
- Stock photos as "portfolio"
- No social presence

**Proceed with caution:**
- New to UGC (under 6 months)
- Slow communication
- Limited content types
- Only one niche experience

### Nano Banana Workflow (@maverickecom)

**The Stack:**
```
Pinterest reference image
       ↓
Nano Banana Pro (recreate style)
       ↓
Kling (add motion/animation)
       ↓
Veo 3.1 (video generation)
       ↓
Final UGC video
```

**Step-by-step:**
1. Find reference on Pinterest (pose, style, aesthetic)
2. Use Nano Banana to recreate with your character
3. Add motion in Kling (lip sync, gestures)
4. Generate final video in Veo
5. Add voiceover via ElevenLabs
6. Edit in CapCut (captions, music)

**Best for:**
- TikTok Shop product videos
- AI influencer content
- Faceless YouTube shorts
- Ads at scale

### HeyGen Workflow

**Creating AI UGC:**
1. Create or upload avatar image
2. Write script (30-60 seconds)
3. Select voice (or clone your own)
4. Generate video
5. Download and edit

**Tips:**
- Use consistent avatar across videos
- Match avatar to target audience demographic
- Add natural pauses in script
- Generate multiple takes
- A/B test different avatars

**Cost per video:**
- $24/mo tier: ~20 videos
- $180/mo tier: Unlimited
- Per-video cost: $1-5 depending on tier

**The play:**
1. Generate AI script + rough video
2. Send to cheap human creator
3. They film using your exact script
4. You get human authenticity at AI speed

**Or:**
1. Human films video
2. AI generates alternative hooks
3. A/B test which performs
4. Scale winner with AI variants

**Cost breakdown:**
- AI draft: $1-5
- Human filming: $10-30
- Total: $11-35/video
- Sell: $150-300/video
- Margin: 80%+

### Pricing Formula

**Quick calculation:**
```
Client Price = Creator Cost x 3 to 4

Examples:
$30 creator = $90-120 client price
$50 creator = $150-200 client price
$100 creator = $300-400 client price
```

**Full calculation:**
```
Creator cost: $40
+ Revisions estimate: $5
+ Management time: $10
+ Tool costs: $5
= Total cost: $60

Target margin: 65%
Price = $60 / (1 - 0.65) = $171
Round to: $175 or $200
```

## Client Acquisition

### Ideal Client Profile

**Best clients:**
- DTC (direct-to-consumer) brands
- E-commerce with paid ads running
- Funded startups (check Crunchbase)
- Brands hiring for marketing
- Agencies needing overflow

**Buying signals:**
- Running Facebook/TikTok ads (check Ad Library)
- Active social presence
- Recently funded
- Product launches coming
- Polished ads (need UGC style)

**Red flags:**
- No website yet
- "Exposure" offers
- Very small budget
- Unclear on needs
- No marketing presence

### Finding Prospects

**Method 1: Facebook Ads Library**
1. Go to facebook.com/ads/library
2. Search product categories
3. Filter: Active ads, United States
4. Find brands running video ads
5. Note: If running ads = they have budget

**Method 2: TikTok Creative Center**
1. Go to ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter
2. Browse top ads by category
3. Find brands in your target niches
4. Note what's working (offer similar)

**Method 3: Product Hunt**
1. Browse recent launches
2. Find funded/growing products
3. Especially: Consumer products, apps
4. Just launched = need content NOW

**Method 4: LinkedIn**
1. Search: "DTC" + "Marketing Manager"
2. Search: "E-commerce" + "Brand Manager"
3. Filter by company size (11-200)
4. Connect and nurture

1. "What's driving your interest in UGC right now?"
2. "What have you tried before? What worked/didn't?"
3. "What does success look like for this project?"
4. "What's your timeline?"
5. "What's your budget range for content?"
6. "Who else is involved in this decision?"

| Objection | Response |

| "Need to think" | "Of course. What specifically do you need to consider?" |

### Conversion Math

```
Want 5 clients/month:
5 clients / 0.3 close rate = 17 proposals
17 proposals / 0.5 call rate = 34 calls
34 calls / 0.3 reply rate = 113 replies
113 replies / 0.1 email rate = 1,130 emails

~300 emails/week = ~5 clients/month
```

       ↓

       ↓

       ↓
4. Contract signed + 50% deposit
       ↓
5. Creative brief created
       ↓

       ↓

       ↓

       ↓

       ↓

       ↓

       ↓

### Creative Brief Template

```markdown
# UGC Creative Brief

## Project Info
- Client: [Company Name]
- Product: [Product Name]
- Deliverables: [X] videos, [length], [aspect ratio]
- Due date: [Date]

## Brand Context
- Brand voice: [Casual/professional/playful/etc.]
- Target audience: [Demographics]
- Key competitors: [List]

## Video Specs
- Length: [15s/30s/60s]
- Aspect ratio: [9:16/1:1/16:9]
- Platform: [TikTok/Instagram/Facebook]

## Script

### Hook (0-3s)
[Exact words to say]

### Problem (3-10s)
[What pain point to address]

### Solution (10-20s)
[How product solves it]

### CTA (20-30s)
[What to tell viewer to do]

## Visual Requirements
- Lighting: Natural/ring light
- Location: [Home/office/outdoor]
- Wardrobe: [Casual/professional]
- Product shots: [Yes/No - how many]

## Don'ts
- Don't mention [competitor names]
- Don't make [specific claims]
- Don't use [music/sounds]

## Delivery
- File format: MP4
- Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum
- Raw footage: [Include/exclude]
- Deadline: [Date]
```

- [ ] Correct aspect ratio
- [ ] Good lighting (face visible)
- [ ] Clear audio (no background noise)
- [ ] Stable footage (no shaky cam)
- [ ] Correct length

**Content:**
- [ ] Hit all script points
- [ ] Natural delivery (not robotic)
- [ ] Product shown correctly
- [ ] CTA included
- [ ] No competitor mentions

**Compliance:**
- [ ] FTC disclosure if testimonial
- [ ] No unsubstantiated claims
- [ ] Usage rights confirmed

- Manual processes
- Focus: Client acquisition

**Stage 2: With Help ($5-20k/mo)**
