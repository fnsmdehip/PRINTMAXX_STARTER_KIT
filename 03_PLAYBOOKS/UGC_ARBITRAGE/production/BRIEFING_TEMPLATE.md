# UGC creator briefing template

Use this template to brief creators on every project. Clear briefs = fewer revisions.

---

## Brief checklist (before sending)

Before sending any brief, verify:

### Creative
- [ ] Script/talking points included
- [ ] Multiple hooks provided
- [ ] Reference videos linked
- [ ] Tone/energy specified
- [ ] CTA clearly stated

### Technical
- [ ] Format and resolution specified
- [ ] Framing requirements clear
- [ ] Background guidance provided
- [ ] Lighting requirements noted
- [ ] File delivery method set up

### Product
- [ ] Product shipped (if physical)
- [ ] Product pronunciation clear
- [ ] Features to highlight listed
- [ ] Claims approved and compliant
- [ ] Things to avoid noted

### Business
- [ ] Payment terms clear
- [ ] Delivery deadline realistic
- [ ] Revision policy stated
- [ ] Usage rights confirmed
- [ ] Contact info provided

---

## Common briefing mistakes

### Too vague
BAD: "Make a video about the product"
GOOD: Full script with talking points, hooks, and references

### Too restrictive
BAD: Word-for-word script that sounds robotic
GOOD: Key points to hit with freedom for natural delivery

### Missing context
BAD: Just the script, no background
GOOD: Who the audience is, what problem it solves, why it matters

### Unclear visuals
BAD: "Film a video"
GOOD: Specific framing, background, lighting requirements

### No references
BAD: "Make it trendy"
GOOD: Links to 2-3 specific videos showing desired style

## Sample filled brief

Example for skincare product:

```
UGC CREATIVE BRIEF

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Client: GlowSerum Co
Product: Vitamin C Brightening Serum
Content type: Video
Duration: 30 seconds
Quantity: 2 videos (2 different hooks)

DELIVERY: Feb 15, 2024
PAY: $60 total ($30/video) via PayPal

---

SCRIPT (Hook-Problem-Solution):

HOOK 1: "The one product that finally fixed my dull skin."
HOOK 2: "Why I stopped using [expensive brand] for this."

PROBLEM:
"I've tried so many vitamin C serums. They're either too sticky, oxidize in a week, or do nothing."

SOLUTION:
"GlowSerum is different. Lightweight, stable formula, and I actually saw results in 2 weeks."

BENEFITS:
"My dark spots are fading, skin looks brighter, and it layers perfectly under makeup."

CTA:
"Link in bio if you want to try it."

---

TONE: Casual, like telling a friend. Not salesy.
REFERENCE: [TikTok link]

VISUALS:
- Vertical 9:16
- Natural lighting, bathroom or bedroom
- Show applying the product
- Close-up of label at some point

PRODUCT NOTES:
- Pronounced: "Glow Serum"
- Highlight: Stable formula, lightweight, visible results
- Don't say: "cure" "anti-aging miracle" or compare to specific brands

DELIVER VIA: Google Drive link (I'll send folder)

Questions? DM me. Otherwise, confirm and get started!
```
