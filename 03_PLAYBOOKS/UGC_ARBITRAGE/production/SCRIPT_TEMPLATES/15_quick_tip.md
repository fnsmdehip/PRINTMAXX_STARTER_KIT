# Script template: Quick tip

Short, punchy value content. High save/share rate.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 2-3 seconds]
Promise the tip

[TIP - 10-15 seconds]
Deliver the value

[PRODUCT TIE-IN - 5-8 seconds]
Connect to product naturally

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Quick close
```

---

## Example script: Productivity (20 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"Quick tip that saves me an hour every week."

TIP:
"Batch your email. Check it 3 times a day instead of constantly. 9 AM, 1 PM, 5 PM. You'll be amazed how much less distracted you are."

PRODUCT TIE-IN:
"I use [Product] to block email notifications outside those times. Game changer."

CTA:
"Link if you want to try it."
```

## Hook formulas (short)

### Direct
- "Quick tip:"
- "Here's a hack:"
- "Try this:"

### Benefit-focused
- "Tip that saves me [time/money]"
- "How to [benefit] instantly"
- "[Category] tip that works"

### Curiosity
- "Tip nobody told me"
- "The [category] secret"
- "What I wish I knew sooner"

### Challenge
- "Stop doing [common mistake]"
- "You're doing [thing] wrong"
- "Most people miss this"

---

## Tip delivery guidelines

### Keep it simple
- One core idea
- Actionable immediately
- No prerequisites

### Be specific
- Exact steps
- Specific numbers
- Clear example

### Make it memorable
- Simple phrase
- Easy to recall
- Shareable format

## Creator brief notes

For quick tip content:
- Provide exact tip to share
- Keep product tie-in natural (not forced)
- Tip should work even without product
- Under 25 seconds total
- High-value, immediately actionable
