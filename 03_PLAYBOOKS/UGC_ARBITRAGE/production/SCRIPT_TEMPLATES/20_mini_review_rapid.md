# Script template: Mini review / rapid fire

Super short format. Quick hits, high scroll-stop.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 1-2 seconds]
Instant attention grab

[CORE MESSAGE - 8-12 seconds]
Key points rapid-fire

[VERDICT - 3-5 seconds]
Quick conclusion + CTA
```

---

## Example script: 15-second review

```
HOOK:
"[Product] - honest review."

CORE MESSAGE:
"Pros: [Pro 1], [Pro 2], [Pro 3].
Con: [One honest con].
Worth it? For [target audience], yes."

VERDICT:
"Link below."
```

---

## Example script: 15-second rapid fire

```
HOOK:
"3 things in under 15 seconds."

CORE MESSAGE:
"One - [point].
Two - [point].
Three - [point]."

VERDICT:
"That's it. [Product] link in bio."
```

---

## Example script: Quick take (15 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"Is [Product] worth it?"

CORE MESSAGE:
"[One-sentence verdict].
[Key reason why].
[Who it's for]."

VERDICT:
"Try it. Link below."
```

---

## Rapid-fire formats

### The checklist
"Does it [thing]? Yes.
Does it [thing]? Yes.
Worth $[price]? [Answer]."

### The verdict
"[Product]. [Verdict]. [Key reason]. Link below."

### The comparison
"[Competitor]: [Con]. [Product]: [Pro]. Easy choice."

### The numbers
"$[Price]. [Time to result]. [Key stat]. Worth it."

### The test
"Testing [Product]... [Shows result]... Verdict: [Answer]."

---

## Hook styles (under 2 seconds)

### Direct
- "[Product] - honest review"
- "Quick take on [Product]"
- "Is [Product] worth it?"

### Bold claim
- "Best [category] I've tried"
- "This actually works"
- "Finally, something good"

### Question
- "Does [Product] live up to hype?"
- "Worth $[Price]?"
- "Should you try this?"

---

## Core message techniques

### Bullet style
- Short phrases
- Clear points
- No filler words

### Before/after
- Quick transformation
- Visual proof
- Minimal words

### Single focus
- One key benefit
- Hammer it home
- Memorable takeaway

---

## Verdict phrases

### Positive
- "Worth it."
- "Try it."
- "Game changer."
- "Buy it."
- "Approved."
- "10/10."

### Qualified
- "Worth it for [audience]."
- "If [condition], yes."
- "At this price, buy."

### Honest
- "Pretty good."
- "Does the job."
- "Better than expected."

---

## Visual execution

### Pacing
- No dead air
- Quick cuts
- Constant movement
- Energy throughout

### Text overlays
- Key words appear
- Reinforce spoken word
- Make it skimmable

### Product visibility
- Always visible
- Clear shots
- Brand readable

## Best formats for rapid content

### Platform-specific
- TikTok: 15-30s sweet spot
- Reels: 15-30s performs well
- Shorts: Under 60s required

### Content types that work
- Product reviews
- Quick tips
- Yes/no verdicts
- Simple tutorials
- Reaction clips

---

## Creator brief notes

For rapid-fire content:
- Keep script under 50 words
- Provide exact talking points
- Specify must-say phrases
- Allow quick, natural delivery
- Energy level: high
- Cuts: frequent okay
