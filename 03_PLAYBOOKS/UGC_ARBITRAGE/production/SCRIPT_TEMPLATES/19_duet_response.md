# Script template: Duet/response

React to existing content or questions. Riding trends and engagement.

---

## Structure

```
[SETUP - 2-3 seconds]
Reference the original content/question

[REACTION/RESPONSE - 15-25 seconds]
Your take, answer, or commentary

[PRODUCT TIE-IN - 8-12 seconds]
How product relates

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Close and direct
```

---

## Example script: Responding to question (30 seconds)

```
SETUP:
[Shows question: "What's one product you'd tell your younger self about?"]

REACTION/RESPONSE:
"Honestly? [Product]. I wasted so much time and money trying to [solve problem] the hard way."

"Like, I tried [alternative 1], [alternative 2], nothing worked. If I had found [Product] earlier, I would have saved [specific time/money/frustration]."

PRODUCT TIE-IN:
"It just works. [Key benefit] without [common drawback]."

CTA:
"Link in bio. Do your future self a favor."
```

---

## Example script: Duet with claim (30 seconds)

```
SETUP:
[Shows viral video claiming "You don't need [product type]"]

REACTION/RESPONSE:
"Okay, I have thoughts on this."

"They're not wrong that you don't need [expensive/complex option]. But saying you don't need [product type] at all? That's where I disagree."

"The right [product type] at the right price makes a real difference."

PRODUCT TIE-IN:
"[Product] is what I use. Not expensive, not complicated, just works."

CTA:
"Link below if you want something that actually helps."
```

---

## Example script: Trend participation (30 seconds)

```
SETUP:
[Uses trending sound: "Tell me you [X] without telling me you [X]"]

REACTION/RESPONSE:
[Shows product]
"Tell me you've got your [routine/life area] figured out without telling me."

[Shows organized system/glowing skin/productive setup]

"I used to struggle with [problem]. Now I just [simple solution with product]."

PRODUCT TIE-IN:
[Product prominently featured throughout]

CTA:
"This is [Product]. Link in bio."
```

## Duet/response types

### Answer format
- Responding to questions
- Reacting to polls
- Addressing common myths
- Answering challenges

### Commentary format
- Adding to existing content
- Agreeing/disagreeing
- Providing expertise
- Sharing experience

### Trend participation
- Using trending sounds
- Following video formats
- Joining challenges
- Adapting memes

### Stitch/build
- Building on someone's point
- Showing the "after"
- Completing the story
- Adding the solution

---

## Content to respond to

### Questions to answer
- "What product actually works for [problem]?"
- "What's your one recommendation for [area]?"
- "What do you wish you knew about [topic]?"
- "Best investment under $[amount]?"

### Claims to address
- Controversial takes in your niche
- Misconceptions
- Overgeneralizations
- Missing nuance

### Trends to join
- Sound trends that fit product
- Format trends (POV, GRWM, etc.)
- Challenge trends
- Meme formats

### Content to build on
- Videos showing the problem (you show solution)
- Before content (you show after)
- Struggles (you show what helped)

---

## Response tones

### Agreeing response
"YES. This is exactly right. And here's what helped me..."

### Disagreeing response
"Okay, I have a different experience. For me..."

### Adding nuance
"This is true, but there's more to it..."

### Personal story
"This was literally me until..."

### Expert input
"As someone who [credential/experience]..."

---

## How to find content to respond to

### Search strategies
- Hashtag: #[niche]question
- Comments: "What product do you use?"
- Duet-worthy videos in niche
- Trending sounds in category

### Content that works well
- Questions with high engagement
- Controversial takes getting discussion
- Relatable struggles
- Trending formats with legs

### What to avoid
- Drama/negativity
- Offensive content
- Copyright issues
- Already oversaturated trends

---

## Technical execution

### Duet format
- Original video on left/top
- Your response on right/bottom
- React at key moments
- Add clear value

### Stitch format
- Show clip of original
- Cut to your content
- Build on or respond to
- Complete thought

### Response format
- Screenshot/text of original
- Your video response
- No duet needed
- More flexibility

## Timing breakdown

| Section | Duration | Focus |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Setup/original | 2-5s | Context |
| Response | 15-20s | Your take |
| Product | 8-10s | Tie-in |
| CTA | 3-4s | Close |
| **Total** | ~35s | |

---

## Best practices

### Do
- Add genuine value
- Be authentic in response
- Credit original creator
- Stay on topic
- Tie product in naturally

### Don't
- Force product into unrelated content
- Be negative about original creator
- Miss the point of original
- Over-explain the connection
- Be late to trends

---

## Creator brief notes

For duet/response content:
- Provide original video link OR question/prompt
- Note whether duet, stitch, or response format
- Specify tone (agreeing, adding, countering)
- Clarify product connection
- Allow authentic reaction
- Note any trending sounds to consider
