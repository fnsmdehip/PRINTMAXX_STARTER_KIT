# Script template: Tutorial walkthrough

Step-by-step demonstration. Shows product in action.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 3-5 seconds]
What they'll learn or achieve

[CONTEXT - 5-8 seconds]
Why this matters, who it's for

[STEP 1 - 8-12 seconds]
First action with explanation

[STEP 2 - 8-12 seconds]
Second action with explanation

[STEP 3 - 8-12 seconds]
Third action (if needed)

[RESULT - 8-10 seconds]
Show the outcome

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Where to get it
```

---

## Example script: Software tool (45 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"Let me show you how I organize my entire week in under 5 minutes."

CONTEXT:
"If you're constantly forgetting tasks or double-booking yourself, this is going to help."

STEP 1:
"First, open [Product] and go to the weekly view. Drag your biggest tasks to specific time blocks like this."
[Shows screen]

STEP 2:
"Next, set your priorities. I color-code mine - red is urgent, yellow is important, green is nice-to-have."
[Demonstrates]

STEP 3:
"Last thing - turn on smart reminders. It'll ping you 15 minutes before anything important."
[Shows setting]

RESULT:
"Now I can see my whole week at a glance and I actually stick to my schedule."

CTA:
"Try it free - link in bio."
```

## Example script: Beauty tutorial (45 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"This 3-step routine got rid of my dark circles in 2 weeks."

CONTEXT:
"I know everyone says drink water and sleep more. I tried that. This actually worked."

STEP 1:
"Morning - apply [Product 1] to clean skin. Just a thin layer under your eyes like this."
[Demonstrates application]

STEP 2:
"Before bed - use [Product 2]. Tap it in gently, don't rub. Let it absorb for a minute."
[Shows technique]

STEP 3:
"Once a week - [Product 3] mask for 10 minutes. This is what made the biggest difference honestly."
[Shows product]

RESULT:
"Here's what my under-eyes look like now versus 2 weeks ago."
[Shows comparison if possible]

CTA:
"All three products linked below."
```

## Tutorial frameworks

### The "Here's how" structure
- How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]
- How to use [product] for [purpose]
- How I [accomplishment] with [product]

### The "Step-by-step" structure
- Step 1: [Action]
- Step 2: [Action]
- Step 3: [Action]
- Result: [Outcome]

### The "Before you do X, do Y" structure
- Before [common approach], try [better method]
- Shows what not to do, then correct way

---

## Step explanation formula

For each step:
1. State what you're doing
2. Show the action
3. Explain why it matters
4. Mention any tips/tricks

Example:
"First, [action]. This is important because [reason]. Pro tip: [insider knowledge]."

---

## Transition phrases

Between steps:
- "Next..."
- "Then..."
- "After that..."
- "Now here's the key..."
- "This next part is important..."
- "Once that's done..."

---

## Visual demonstration tips

### Screen recordings (software)
- Zoom in on relevant areas
- Slow down cursor movements
- Highlight clicks/actions
- Keep movements smooth

### Physical products
- Show hand movements clearly
- Get proper angle for visibility
- Use good lighting
- Slow down for key moments

### Before/after
- Same lighting both shots
- Same angle
- Clear difference visible
- Timestamp if relevant

## Common mistakes to avoid

- Too many steps (max 3-4 for short form)
- Steps too complex to follow
- Talking without showing
- Moving too fast
- Not explaining WHY each step matters
- Forgetting the result/payoff

---
