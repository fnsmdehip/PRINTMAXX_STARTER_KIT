# Script template: Social proof / "everyone's using this"

Leverage popularity and trends. Creates FOMO.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 2-3 seconds]
Highlight popularity

[EVIDENCE - 10-12 seconds]
Proof of adoption

[WHY IT'S POPULAR - 12-15 seconds]
Explain the appeal

[PERSONAL EXPERIENCE - 8-10 seconds]
Add your validation

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Join the trend
```

---

## Example script: App/software (30 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"This app is everywhere right now and I finally tried it."

EVIDENCE:
"Half my feed is talking about [Product]. [Creator] uses it. [Other creator] uses it. Even my coworker who hates new apps switched to it."

WHY IT'S POPULAR:
"I get why now. It just works. No learning curve, no weird bugs, no features I'll never use. It does [core function] better than anything else I've tried."

PERSONAL EXPERIENCE:
"I've been using it for 2 weeks and honestly? I'm not going back."

CTA:
"Link if you want to see what the hype is about."
```

## Social proof types

### Quantity proof
- "X million sold"
- "Sold out [X] times"
- "[Number] reviews"
- "Trending for [X] weeks"

### Authority proof
- "[Expert] recommends"
- "Featured in [publication]"
- "[Professional] approved"
- "Award-winning"

### Celebrity/influencer proof
- "[Name] uses this"
- "Seen on [platform]"
- "[Creator]'s favorite"
- "Viral on [platform]"

### Peer proof
- "Everyone in my [group] uses this"
- "My [friend/coworker] told me about it"
- "All my friends switched to this"
- "[Community] loves this"

### Scarcity proof
- "Constantly sells out"
- "Waitlist has [number] people"
- "Hard to get"
- "Limited stock"

## Hook formulas

### FOMO hooks
- "Everyone's using this and I finally tried it"
- "Why is this everywhere right now"
- "The product that broke the internet"
- "Finally tried the [Product] everyone talks about"

### Curiosity + popularity
- "I had to see what the hype was about"
- "Is this actually worth the cult following?"
- "Why did this go viral?"

### Trend hooks
- "2024's most hyped [category]"
- "The [Product] taking over my feed"
- "If you've been on [platform], you've seen this"

## "Why it's popular" explanations

### Quality-based
"It just works. Better than anything else."
"The quality is actually insane for the price."

### Simplicity-based
"It's so simple. That's literally why."
"Does one thing and does it perfectly."

### Results-based
"Because it actually delivers on the promise."
"People are seeing real results."

### Value-based
"Great quality for what you pay."
"Outperforms products 3x the price."

- "I was skeptical but now I get it"

- "Didn't expect to like it this much"

### Enthusiastic adopter

- "Adding this to my permanent [routine/stack]"

- "It lives up to the hype"

## Visual enhancements

### Show the evidence
- Screenshots of reviews
- Sales numbers
- Trending tags
- Influencer posts

### Create urgency
- Stock levels
- Waitlist status
- Sale ending

### Community feel
- Other people using it
- Comments/reactions
- User-generated content

---

## Authenticity balance

### Make it genuine
- Actually try the product
- Give honest assessment
- Acknowledge if overhyped in some areas
- Share real experience

### Avoid
- Making up numbers
- Claiming false endorsements
- Creating fake urgency
- Sounding like an infomercial

---

## Creator brief notes

For social proof content:
- Provide actual numbers/stats
- List any celebrity/influencer endorsements
- Note any awards/features
- Clarify what claims can be made
- Product should be trending or have genuine proof
