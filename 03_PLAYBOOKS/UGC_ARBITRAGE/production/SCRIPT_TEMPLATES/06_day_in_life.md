# Script template: Day in the life

Lifestyle integration content. Shows product in natural context.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 2-3 seconds]
Draw viewer into the day

[MORNING SCENE - 10-15 seconds]
Start of day with product integration

[MIDDAY SCENE - 10-15 seconds]
Activity showing product use

[EVENING/CLOSE - 8-10 seconds]
End of day or result of using product

[SOFT CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Natural mention of where to get it
```

---

## Example script: Coffee product (45 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"Spend a morning with me."

MORNING SCENE:
[Waking up, stretching]
"6 AM wake up. First thing I do - make my [Product] coffee."
[Shows making coffee]
"I switched to this a few months ago because it has [benefit]. Tastes like regular coffee but [differentiator]."

MIDDAY SCENE:
[Working at desk]
"Usually by 10 AM I'd be crashing. Now I have steady energy through my whole morning work block."
[Shows productive work]

EVENING/CLOSE:
"Best part? No afternoon crash. I actually have energy for the gym after work now."
[Quick gym clip or evening activity]

SOFT CTA:
"If you want to try it, I'll link it below."
```

## Product integration styles

### Direct integration
Product is the focus of the scene
"I use [Product] every morning because..."

### Ambient integration
Product visible but not explicitly featured
[Product on desk in background while working]

### Problem-solution integration
Day would be harder without product
"Before [Product], this part of my day was always stressful..."

### Transition integration
Product connects two parts of day
"After my workout, I always use [Product] before starting work."

## Audio options

### Voiceover style
- Narrate over B-roll
- More polished, controlled
- Easier for editing

### Talking head moments
- Direct to camera sections
- More personal, authentic
- Mix with B-roll

### Music + text
- Trending audio
- Text overlays for key points
- Most "TikTok native"
