# Script template: Hook-problem-solution

Classic direct response structure. Works for most products.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 2-3 seconds]
Attention-grabbing opening that stops the scroll

[PROBLEM - 5-10 seconds]
Relatable pain point the viewer experiences

[AGITATION - 5-10 seconds]
Make the problem feel worse, show consequences

[SOLUTION - 10-15 seconds]
Introduce product as the answer

[BENEFITS - 10-15 seconds]
Specific results/features

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Clear call to action
```

---

## Example script: Skincare product (30 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"I finally found what actually works for [problem]."

PROBLEM:
"For years I dealt with [specific problem]. I tried everything - [competitor type 1], [competitor type 2], nothing worked."

AGITATION:
"It was so frustrating waking up every morning and still seeing [problem]."

SOLUTION:
"Then I discovered [Product Name]. It's different because [key differentiator]."

BENEFITS:
"Within [timeframe], I noticed [specific result]. Now [transformation statement]."

CTA:
"Link is in bio if you want to try it."
```

## Example script: Productivity app (30 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"This one app replaced 5 others I was paying for."

PROBLEM:
"I was juggling [app 1], [app 2], [app 3] just to stay organized. Nothing talked to each other."

BENEFITS:
"I save [time] every week and my projects actually stay on track now."

```

## Hook variations (A/B test these)

### Question hooks
- "Ever wonder why [common struggle]?"
- "Why does nobody talk about [problem]?"
- "What if I told you [counterintuitive claim]?"

### Statement hooks
- "This changed everything for me."
- "I wish I knew this sooner."
- "Stop doing [common mistake]."

### Challenge hooks
- "I didn't believe it until I tried it."
- "I was skeptical too, but..."
- "Okay, I was wrong about [topic]."

### Result hooks
- "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]."
- "[Specific result] and it only took [effort]."
- "The reason my [area] improved overnight."

---

## Problem statement formulas

### The "I used to" formula
"I used to [struggle with problem]. Every [frequency], I would [painful action]."

### The "You know that feeling" formula
"You know that feeling when [relatable frustration]? That was me every [timeframe]."

### The "Like most people" formula
"Like most people with [characteristic], I was [common struggle]."

---

## Solution transition phrases

- "Then I found [Product]."
- "That's when I discovered [Product]."
- "[Product] completely changed that."
- "Enter [Product]."
- "Here's what finally worked: [Product]."

---

## CTA options

### Soft CTAs
- "Link in bio if you want to check it out."
- "I'll leave the link below."
- "Just sharing what worked for me."

### Direct CTAs
- "Click the link and try it."
- "Get yours at [URL]."
- "Use my code [CODE] for [discount]."

### Urgency CTAs
- "Sale ends [timeframe]."
- "They're almost sold out."
- "Limited time discount in the link."

## Timing breakdown (30 second version)

| Section | Duration | Word count |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Hook | 2-3s | 5-10 words |
| Problem | 5-7s | 15-25 words |
| Agitation | 5-7s | 15-25 words |
| Solution | 8-10s | 25-35 words |
| CTA | 3-5s | 10-15 words |
| **Total** | ~30s | ~80-100 words |
