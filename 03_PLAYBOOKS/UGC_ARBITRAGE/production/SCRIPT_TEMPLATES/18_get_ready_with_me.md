# Script template: Get ready with me (GRWM)

Popular format for lifestyle/beauty. High watch time.

---

## Structure

```
[INTRO - 3-5 seconds]
What you're getting ready for

[ROUTINE STEPS - 30-45 seconds]
Walk through process with product integration

[FINAL LOOK - 5-10 seconds]
Show completed result

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
Products used and link
```

---

## Example script: Skincare GRWM (45 seconds)

```
INTRO:
"Get ready with me for a date night."

ROUTINE STEPS:
"Starting with clean skin. First up - [Product 1] as my base."
[Applies product]
"Love this because it hydrates without being heavy."

"Next, [Product 2] just on the areas that need it."
[Shows application]

"And my secret weapon - [Main Product]. This is what actually makes my skin glow. A little goes a long way."
[Demonstrates]

"Then I do my makeup - nothing crazy, just want to look natural."
[Quick makeup montage]

FINAL LOOK:
[Shows final result]
"And we're ready. Skin looks hydrated, not greasy."

CTA:
"Products linked below. [Main Product] is the game changer."
```

## Example script: Morning routine GRWM (60 seconds)

```
INTRO:
"Get ready with me - morning work routine."

ROUTINE STEPS:
"6:30 AM. First thing - I check [Product/App] to see what's actually important today."
[Shows app/planner]
"I time-block my priorities before anything else."

"Then coffee. Essential."
[Making coffee]

"Quick workout - 20 minutes. I use [Product] for the routine."
[Quick workout clips]

"Shower, get dressed."
[Quick montage]

"Before I start work, I review my to-do list one more time."
[Shows final check]

FINAL LOOK:
"Ready to actually be productive instead of just busy."

CTA:
"[Main Product] keeps me organized. Link in bio."
```

## Product integration methods

### Primary product focus
One product is the star, mentioned multiple times
"This is the key step..."
"You can skip everything else but not this..."

### Multiple product showcase
Several products mentioned in routine
"Step 1 is [Product 1], then [Product 2]..."
Works for affiliate/multi-product

### Subtle integration
Product appears naturally, minimal selling
"I always use this at this step..."
More organic feel

---

## Routine pacing tips

### Show transitions
- Each step clearly visible
- Smooth flow between actions
- Skip mundane parts
- Highlight interesting moments

### Time indicators
- "First..."
- "Next..."
- "Then..."
- "Finally..."

### Skip/montage when needed
- Long processes (hair drying) = quick montage
- Boring steps = time-lapse
- Key steps = real-time

## Visual elements

### Camera angles
- Face-forward for application
- Side angles for profile
- Close-ups for technique
- Full shots for context

### Lighting
- Natural light ideal
- Consistent throughout
- Face clearly visible
- Products readable

### Setting
- Bathroom for skincare
- Desk for productivity
- Gym for fitness
- Varies by routine type

---

## Audio options

### Voiceover
- Pre-recorded narration
- Edited over B-roll
- More polished feel

### Live talking
- Talk while doing
- More authentic
- Higher engagement

### Music + text
- Trending audio
- Text overlays explain
- TikTok native feel

### Mix
- Talk for key moments
- Music for transitions
- Best of both worlds

## Timing breakdown (45 second version)

| Section | Duration | Focus |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Intro | 3-5s | Context |
| Step 1-2 | 10-12s | Building routine |
| Main product | 12-15s | Key focus |
| Remaining steps | 8-10s | Complete routine |
| Final look | 5-7s | Result |
| CTA | 3-4s | Products/link |
| **Total** | ~45s | |

## Engagement boosters

### Questions/polls
- "What should I wear?"
- "Should I do bold or natural?"
- Creates interaction

### Commentary
- Thoughts about the day
- Casual conversation
- Behind-the-scenes feel

### Tips sprinkled in
- "Pro tip: do this first"
- "Game changer: [technique]"
- Adds value beyond the routine

---

## Creator brief notes

For GRWM content:
- Specify routine type and context
- Note which products to feature
- Indicate primary vs. secondary products
- Allow natural conversation
- Provide talking points but allow personality
- Specify audio preference (voiceover vs. live)
