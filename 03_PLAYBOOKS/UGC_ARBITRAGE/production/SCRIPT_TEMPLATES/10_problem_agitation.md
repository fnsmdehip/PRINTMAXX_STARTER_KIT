# Script template: Problem agitation

Heavy focus on the pain. Makes viewers desperate for solution.

---

## Structure

```
[HOOK - 2-3 seconds]
Identify with the problem

[PROBLEM STATEMENT - 8-10 seconds]
Describe the issue clearly

[AGITATION - 15-20 seconds]
Make the problem feel worse

[FAILED SOLUTIONS - 8-10 seconds]
What doesn't work

[THE ANSWER - 10-12 seconds]
Product as relief

[CTA - 3-5 seconds]
How to get it
```

---

## Example script: Sleep product (45 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"If you're lying awake at 3 AM reading this, I get it."

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
"You're exhausted but your brain won't shut off. You've got work tomorrow and you're doing math on how little sleep you can survive on."

AGITATION:
"And the worst part? It's not just one night. It's every night. You wake up tired. You drag through the day. Coffee stops working. You snap at people you care about. And then night comes and you dread getting in bed because you know it's going to happen again."

FAILED SOLUTIONS:
"You've tried the apps, the podcasts, the breathing exercises. Maybe even melatonin. Nothing sticks."

THE ANSWER:
"[Product] actually helped me break the cycle. [Key mechanism]. Now I fall asleep in under 20 minutes and stay asleep."

CTA:
"Link in bio. You deserve actual rest."
```

## Example script: Productivity (30 seconds)

```
HOOK:
"Does your to-do list make you want to cry?"

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
"There's too much to do. You don't even know where to start. So you stare at the list, feel overwhelmed, and end up doing nothing."

AGITATION:
"Then the deadlines pile up. You're working late. Missing things. Feeling like you're constantly behind."

FAILED SOLUTIONS:
"Sticky notes don't help. Neither does that complicated app you set up and never use."

THE ANSWER:
"[Product] is dead simple. One place for everything. Clear priorities. I actually finish my list now."

CTA:
"Try it free - link below."
```

## Agitation techniques

### Emotional amplification
- "And the worst part..."
- "But it gets worse..."
- "And then you realize..."
- "Meanwhile..."

### Future pacing (negative)
- "If this continues..."
- "Think about where you'll be in 6 months..."
- "Every day it gets a little worse..."

### Social consequences
- "People are starting to notice..."
- "Your [relationship/work] is suffering..."
- "You're missing out on..."

### Physical/emotional impact
- "The stress shows up in your [body part]..."
- "You feel [negative emotion] every..."
- "It's affecting your [health/energy/mood]..."

## Problem categories and agitation points

### Sleep issues
- Exhaustion affecting work
- Mood/relationship impact
- Health concerns
- Cycle of dread
- Missing life moments

### Productivity issues
- Falling behind
- Missed deadlines
- Constant stress
- Never "off"
- Feeling like a failure

### Skin issues
- Avoiding mirrors
- Self-consciousness
- Photos/social situations
- Money wasted on products
- Feeling out of control

### Financial issues
- Anxiety about money
- Missing opportunities
- Future uncertainty
- Comparison to others
- Feeling stuck

### Fitness issues
- Clothes not fitting
- Low energy
- Avoiding activities
- Health concerns
- Cycle of starting and stopping

## Failed solutions by category

### Sleep
- Apps and podcasts
- Melatonin
- Cutting caffeine
- New mattress/pillows
- "Sleep hygiene" tips

### Productivity
- Multiple apps
- Planners/journals
- Getting up earlier
- Working harder
- Time blocking (that fails)

### Skincare
- Drugstore products
- Expensive brands
- DIY remedies
- Different routines
- Dermatologist visits (maybe)

### Fitness
- Gym memberships
- Diets
- Workout apps
- Supplements
- Personal trainers

## Timing breakdown

| Section | Duration | Emotional arc |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Hook | 2-3s | Identification |
| Problem | 8-10s | Recognition |
| Agitation | 15-20s | Pain amplification |
| Failed solutions | 8-10s | Frustration |
| The answer | 10-12s | Relief |
| CTA | 3-5s | Hope |
| **Total** | ~50s | |

---

## Tone guidance

### Energy curve
- Hook: Knowing, empathetic
- Problem: Understanding
- Agitation: Increasingly frustrated/emotional
- Failed solutions: Exhausted
- Answer: Relieved, hopeful
- CTA: Encouraging

### Voice qualities
- Relatable, not preachy
- "I've been there"
- Genuine frustration
- Authentic relief

---

## Caution

### Don't overdo it
- Agitation should feel relatable, not depressing
- Balance pain with hope
- Don't make viewers feel bad without offering solution
- Keep it under 20-25 seconds of agitation

### Sensitive topics
- Mental health: Be careful, don't diagnose
- Health claims: Stay within legal bounds
- Financial: Don't prey on desperation
- Body image: Don't shame

---

## Creator brief notes

For problem-agitation content:
- Specify exact pain points to hit
- Provide emotional guidance
- Note any sensitive areas to avoid
- Include failed solutions relevant to audience
- Ensure product can genuinely help
