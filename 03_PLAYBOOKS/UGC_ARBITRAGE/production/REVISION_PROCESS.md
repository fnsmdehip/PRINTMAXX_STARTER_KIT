# UGC revision process

How to handle revisions efficiently. Minimize back-and-forth, maximize quality.

---

## Revision workflow

### Step 1: Review delivery

When content arrives:

1. **Watch fully before noting anything**
   - First impression matters
   - Don't pause to nitpick
   - Get overall feel

2. **Check against brief**
   - All talking points covered?
   - Hooks recorded as specified?
   - Visual requirements met?

3. **Technical check**
   - Video quality (resolution, focus)
   - Audio quality (clear, no noise)
   - Lighting acceptable?
   - Framing correct?

4. **Performance check**
   - Natural delivery?
   - Energy level right?
   - Believable?
   - Would you keep watching?

---

### Step 2: Categorize issues

**Critical issues (must fix):**
- Wrong talking points
- Technical problems (audio/video quality)
- Missing required elements
- Brand safety issues
- Legal/compliance problems

**Nice-to-have (optional):**
- Slightly different pacing
- Minor wording preferences
- Small framing adjustments
- Personal style differences

**Not worth fixing:**
- Tiny imperfections
- Personal preference only
- Things only you'd notice
- "Perfect" vs "good enough"

---

### Step 3: Provide feedback

**Format for clear feedback:**

```
Hi [Creator],

Thanks for the delivery! Here's my feedback:

WHAT'S GREAT:
- [Specific positive]
- [Specific positive]

REVISIONS NEEDED:
1. [Specific issue] - [Specific fix]
   Timestamp: [X:XX]

2. [Specific issue] - [Specific fix]
   Timestamp: [X:XX]

OPTIONAL (if you have time):
- [Nice-to-have improvement]

Please send revised version by [Date].

Thanks!
```

---

## Feedback best practices

### Be specific

**Bad feedback:**
"The energy is off."
"Make it more natural."
"It doesn't feel right."

**Good feedback:**
"At 0:15, the delivery sounds like reading. Can you make it more conversational, like you're telling a friend?"

"The hook needs more enthusiasm. Watch this reference [link] - match that energy level."

"The product hold at 0:22 blocks the label. Can you angle it so the brand name is visible?"

---

### Provide timestamps

Always reference exact moments:

```
0:00-0:03 - Hook delivery is perfect
0:15 - Missed the key benefit about [feature]
0:22 - Need to show product label more clearly
0:28 - Great closing, keep as is
```

---

### Explain the why

Creators learn faster when they understand reasoning:

**Without why:**
"Re-record the hook."

**With why:**
"The hook needs to grab attention faster. This will be used in ads where we have 2 seconds before someone scrolls. Start with the most compelling word."

---

### Include references

Show don't just tell:

"For the energy level, reference this video: [link]"
"The product hold should look like this: [screenshot]"
"Here's an example of the pacing we're looking for: [link]"

## Common revision requests

### Audio issues

| Problem | How to communicate |
|---------|-------------------|
| Background noise | "There's fan/traffic noise throughout. Can you re-record in a quieter space?" |
| Echo | "The audio has bathroom echo. Try recording in a room with more soft surfaces." |
| Too quiet | "Voice is quiet - are you able to boost the audio or record closer to mic?" |
| Inconsistent volume | "Volume drops at 0:15. Can you match the levels throughout?" |

### Video issues

| Problem | How to communicate |
|---------|-------------------|
| Out of focus | "The camera focused on the background. Re-record with focus on your face." |
| Bad lighting | "The lighting creates shadows on your face. Can you face the light source directly?" |
| Wrong framing | "Head is cut off at top. Pull camera back slightly or adjust angle." |
| Shaky footage | "Footage is shaky - can you stabilize camera on a tripod or surface?" |

### Performance issues

| Problem | How to communicate |
|---------|-------------------|
| Reading script | "Delivery sounds scripted. Try memorizing key points and speaking naturally." |
| Low energy | "Energy needs to be higher - like you're excited to share this with a friend." |
| Too fast | "Pacing is rushed. Try speaking at 80% speed and let points breathe." |
| Not believable | "The testimonial doesn't feel authentic. Can you talk about your genuine experience?" |

### Content issues

| Problem | How to communicate |
|---------|-------------------|
| Missed talking point | "We need to include [specific point] - can you add it after the hook?" |
| Wrong pronunciation | "Product name is pronounced [phonetic]. Re-record just that section." |
| Missing CTA | "Need the CTA at the end: 'Link in bio to try it'" |
| Compliance issue | "Can't say 'guaranteed results' - change to 'I saw results'" |

## Revision scope management

### What's included in standard revision

- Re-recording specific sections
- Adjusting delivery/pacing
- Fixing audio issues (if recording error)
- Adding missing talking points
- Correcting mistakes

### What's a reshoot (additional cost)

- Complete creative direction change
- Different location/setting
- Different outfit/look
- Fundamentally different approach
- Client changed their mind

### How to communicate scope

```
This revision is within scope because:
- We're fixing delivery on specific sections
- Original requirements weren't met

This would be a reshoot because:
- We're changing the creative direction
- Original brief was followed correctly

For reshoot, additional fee of $[X] applies.
```

---

## Revision limits

### Standard policy

- Round 1: Detailed feedback, all issues
- Round 2: Minor tweaks only
- Round 3+: Additional fee

### Communicate upfront

In your brief:
"This project includes 1 round of revisions. Additional rounds at $[X] per round."

### When to cut losses

If after 2 rounds it's still not working:
- Pay for work completed
- Don't continue the relationship
- Find a different creator
- Don't throw good money after bad

---

## Revision turnaround times

### Your feedback
- Standard: Within 24-48 hours
- Rush: Same day

### Creator revisions
- Standard: 2-3 business days
- Rush: 24-48 hours (communicate clearly)

### Set expectations early
"I'll provide feedback within 24 hours. Please deliver revisions within 48 hours."

- Identify slow responders
- Calculate actual turnaround times
- Spot patterns in revision needs

## Approval and final delivery

### Final approval message

```
Hi [Creator],

The video is approved.

Final checklist:
- [ ] Please send via [delivery method]
- [ ] Include raw footage
- [ ] Use naming convention: [format]

Payment will process within [X] days of receiving final files.

Great work!
```

### Quality creators to keep

Note creators who:
- Deliver with minimal revisions
- Respond quickly
- Take feedback well
- Consistently meet brief

These become your A-list for priority work.
