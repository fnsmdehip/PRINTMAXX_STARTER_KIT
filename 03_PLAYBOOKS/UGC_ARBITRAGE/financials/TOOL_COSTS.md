## Cost optimization tips

### What to avoid paying for

- Expensive CRM before you need it
- Multiple overlapping tools
- "Pro" tiers you don't use fully
- Automation before manual mastery
- Tools that don't directly impact revenue

### When to upgrade tools

- Free tier limits hit regularly
- Tool inefficiency costs you time
- Client experience requires it
- Team needs collaboration features
- Time saved > cost of tool

### Free alternatives worth knowing

| Paid tool | Free alternative |
|-----------|------------------|
| Calendly Pro | Cal.com |
| Zapier | Make.com (free tier) |
| Slack Pro | Discord |
| Dropbox Pro | Google Drive |
| Adobe Suite | Canva + DaVinci |
| Pipedrive | HubSpot free |

---

## Tool ROI calculation

Before adding a tool, ask:

1. **Time saved per month?**
   - Value = Hours saved x Your hourly rate

2. **Revenue enabled?**
   - Can you take on more clients?
   - Can you deliver faster?

3. **Cost of NOT having it?**
   - Lost deals
   - Inefficiency
   - Client experience

**Example:**
- Calendly Pro: $10/month
- Time saved: 2 hrs/month scheduling
- Your rate: $50/hr
- Value: $100/month
- ROI: 10x
