# Scaling to $10K/month

Roadmap from zero to $10K monthly revenue.

---

## Revenue milestones

### Phase 1: $0 to $1K/month (Months 1-2)

**Goal:** Get first paying clients, prove concept.

**Revenue breakdown:**
- 2-3 small projects
- Average: $300-500 each
- Total: $800-1,500

**Focus areas:**
- Build initial portfolio (spec work)
- Get 3-5 reliable creators
- Land first 2-3 clients
- Deliver successfully
- Get testimonials

**Key metrics:**
- Clients acquired: 2-3
- Videos delivered: 10-15
- Creator roster: 5
- Margin target: 60%+

**Time investment:** 20-30 hrs/week

---

### Phase 2: $1K to $3K/month (Months 3-4)

**Goal:** Consistent client flow, refine operations.

**Revenue breakdown:**
- 4-6 projects/month
- Average: $500-700 each
- Possible 1 retainer at $1,500+
- Total: $2,500-4,000

**Focus areas:**
- Systematize briefs and delivery
- Expand creator roster to 10+
- Build referral pipeline
- Raise prices 10-15%
- Convert project clients to retainers

**Key metrics:**
- Clients acquired: 4-6 new
- Active clients: 6-8
- Videos/month: 30-40
- Margin target: 65%+

**Time investment:** 25-35 hrs/week

---

### Phase 3: $3K to $6K/month (Months 5-7)

**Goal:** Predictable revenue, efficient systems.

**Revenue breakdown:**
- 2-3 retainers at $1,500-3,000 each
- 3-5 projects at $500-1,000 each
- Total: $5,000-7,500

**Focus areas:**
- Retainer conversion priority
- Process documentation
- Creator tier system
- Quality consistency
- Time efficiency improvements

**Key metrics:**
- Retainer revenue: 50%+ of total
- Videos/month: 50-80
- Profit margin: 70%+
- Time per video: <1 hr

**Time investment:** 30-40 hrs/week

---

### Phase 4: $6K to $10K/month (Months 8-12)

**Goal:** Scale without proportional time increase.

**Revenue breakdown:**
- 3-5 retainers at $2,000-4,000 each
- 2-4 projects at $800-1,500 each
- Total: $8,000-12,000

**Focus areas:**
- Premium positioning
- Expand into agency clients
- Consider first hire/contractor
- Automated systems
- Passive income streams

**Key metrics:**
- Monthly revenue: $10K+
- Profit margin: 70-75%
- Revenue per hour: $100+
- Client retention: 80%+

**Time investment:** 35-45 hrs/week (or less with team)

## Scaling strategies

### Strategy 1: Increase average deal size

**Current:** $500 average project
**Target:** $1,500 average project

**How:**
- Package deals instead of single videos
- Upsell additional hooks/variations
- Add strategy/consulting component
- Position as premium service

**Math:**
- 10 projects at $500 = $5,000
- 10 projects at $1,500 = $15,000
- Same client count, 3x revenue

---

### Strategy 2: Increase client frequency

**Current:** Client buys 1x every 2 months
**Target:** Client buys 1x per month (or retainer)

**How:**
- Convert to retainers
- Proactive outreach for repeat work
- Show content performance data
- Bundle with monthly strategy calls

**Math:**
- 10 clients x $1,000 x 6 times/year = $60,000
- 10 clients x $1,000 x 12 times/year = $120,000
- Same clients, 2x revenue

---

### Strategy 3: Increase client count

**Current:** 5 active clients
**Target:** 15 active clients

**How:**
- Increase outreach volume
- Build referral system
- Content marketing (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Agency partnerships

**Math:**
- 5 clients x $2,000/month = $10,000
- 15 clients x $2,000/month = $30,000
- Same revenue per client, 3x total

---

### Strategy 4: Improve margins

**Current:** 65% margin
**Target:** 80% margin

**How:**
- Better creator rates (volume)
- Fewer revisions (better briefs)
- Faster processes (systems)
- Reduce tool costs

**Math:**
- $10,000 at 65% = $6,500 profit
- $10,000 at 80% = $8,000 profit
- Same revenue, +$1,500 profit

## Scaling bottlenecks

### Bottleneck 1: Your time

**Symptoms:**
- Working 60+ hours/week
- Declining quality
- Missed deadlines
- No time for sales

**Solutions:**
- Systematize everything
- Hire VA for admin tasks
- Outsource QC/delivery prep
- Raise prices (fewer clients, same revenue)

---

### Bottleneck 2: Creator capacity

**Symptoms:**
- Creators overbooked
- Quality declining
- Missed deadlines
- Turning down work

**Solutions:**
- Expand roster
- Develop tier B creators
- Build relationships for priority
- Diversify across regions/timezones

---

### Bottleneck 3: Client acquisition

**Symptoms:**
- Feast or famine revenue
- Dependent on few clients
- Too much sales time

**Solutions:**
- Build referral system
- Content marketing
- Agency partnerships
- Retainer focus (less sales needed)

---

### Bottleneck 4: Cash flow

**Symptoms:**
- Can't take on more work
- Late payments hurt
- No buffer for growth

**Solutions:**
- Require deposits
- Shorter payment terms
- Build cash reserve
- Credit line for emergencies

## Team scaling

### First hire: Virtual assistant ($500-1,000/month)

**Responsibilities:**
- Client communication (routine)
- File organization
- Delivery preparation
- Follow-up emails
- Calendar management

**Impact:**
- Saves 10-15 hrs/week
- Focus on high-value work
- Better client experience

---

### Second hire: QC/Production coordinator ($1,000-2,000/month)

**Responsibilities:**
- Creator communication
- Brief preparation
- Quality review
- Revision management
- Delivery execution

**Impact:**
- Saves 20+ hrs/week
- Consistent quality
- Faster turnaround

---

### Third hire: Sales/Account manager ($2,000-4,000/month)

**Responsibilities:**
- New client acquisition
- Proposal writing
- Client relationships
- Upselling/retention

**Impact:**
- Remove yourself from sales
- Scale client base
- Focus on strategy
