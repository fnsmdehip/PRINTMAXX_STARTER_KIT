# UGC arbitrage workflow

End-to-end process from client to delivery.

---

## High-level workflow

```
CLIENT ACQUISITION
      ↓
   PROJECT BRIEF
      ↓
  CREATOR MATCHING
      ↓
   PRODUCTION
      ↓
  QUALITY REVIEW
      ↓
    DELIVERY
      ↓
   FEEDBACK
      ↓
    REPEAT
```

---

## Phase 1: Client acquisition

### Inputs
- Lead from outreach/referral
- Discovery call completed
- Client needs understood

### Process
1. Send proposal (see PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md)
2. Follow up sequence (Day 3, 7, 14)
3. Handle objections
4. Close deal
5. Send invoice (50% upfront)
6. Receive payment

### Outputs
- Signed agreement or email confirmation
- 50% deposit received
- Client ready for kickoff

### Timeline
- Proposal to close: 3-14 days typical

---

## Phase 2: Project kickoff

### Inputs
- Confirmed project
- Deposit received

### Process
1. Send kickoff questionnaire
   - Brand guidelines
   - Product details
   - Target audience
   - Existing assets
   - Goals and metrics

2. Collect brand assets
   - Product photos
   - Logo files
   - Brand voice guidelines
   - Competitor examples
   - Reference videos

3. Product shipping (if physical)
   - Get client shipping address
   - Coordinate product samples to creators
   - Track shipments

4. Develop creative brief
   - Select script templates
   - Customize talking points
   - Define hooks
   - Specify visuals

### Outputs
- Complete creative brief
- Products shipped/received
- Project ready for production

### Timeline
- Kickoff: 1-2 business days

---

## Phase 3: Creator matching

### Inputs
- Complete creative brief
- Product received (if physical)
- Delivery timeline

### Process
1. Review brief requirements
   - Content type
   - Duration
   - Number of videos
   - Creator demographic needed

2. Match creators from roster
   - Check availability
   - Verify look/style match
   - Confirm rate and timeline
   - Consider backup creators

3. Send brief to creators
   - Use BRIEFING_TEMPLATE.md
   - Include all assets
   - Set clear deadline
   - Get confirmation

4. Handle product logistics
   - Ship to creator if needed
   - Digital product access
   - Any special requirements

### Outputs
- Creators assigned and confirmed
- Brief sent and acknowledged
- Production timeline set

### Timeline
- Matching: 1 business day

---

## Phase 4: Production

### Inputs
- Assigned creators
- Confirmed briefs
- Products with creators

### Process
1. Monitor progress
   - Check in at midpoint
   - Answer creator questions
   - Resolve issues quickly

2. First draft review
   - Check against brief
   - Technical quality check
   - Performance quality check
   - Use QUALITY_CHECKLIST.md

3. Internal revisions (if needed)
   - Provide specific feedback
   - Set revision deadline
   - Review revised content

### Outputs
- Content ready for client review
- All technical specs met
- Multiple hooks recorded

### Timeline
- Production: 3-5 business days

---

## Phase 5: Quality review

### Inputs
- Content from creators
- Original brief

### Process
1. Technical QC
   - Resolution check
   - Audio check
   - Format check
   - File quality

2. Content QC
   - All talking points covered
   - Hooks as requested
   - CTA included
   - Brand compliance

3. Performance QC
   - Natural delivery
   - Energy appropriate
   - Believable
   - Would you keep watching?

4. Organize deliverables
   - Proper file naming
   - Folder structure
   - Final files + raw footage

### Outputs
- QC-approved content
- Organized delivery folder
- Ready for client

### Timeline
- QC: 0.5-1 business day

---

## Phase 6: Client delivery

### Inputs
- QC-approved content
- Delivery folder organized

### Process
1. Upload to delivery folder
   - Google Drive/Dropbox
   - Proper organization
   - All files included

2. Send delivery email
   ```
   Subject: Your UGC Content is Ready!

   Hi [Name],

   Great news - your content is ready for review!

   [LINK TO FOLDER]

   Included:
   - [X] final videos
   - [X] hook variations
   - Raw footage

   Please review and let me know if you need any revisions.
   Revision round 1 is included.

   Looking forward to your feedback!
   [Your name]
   ```

3. Send final invoice (50% balance)
   - Include upon-approval terms
   - Payment due on approval

### Outputs
- Content delivered to client
- Final invoice sent
- Awaiting feedback

### Timeline
- Delivery: Same day as QC complete

---

## Phase 7: Revisions

### Inputs
- Client feedback
- Original content

### Process
1. Review feedback
   - Categorize issues
   - Determine if revision or reshoot
   - Check scope (included vs. additional)

2. Communicate with creators
   - Share specific feedback
   - Set revision deadline
   - Provide reference if needed

3. Review revised content
   - Verify issues fixed
   - Run QC again
   - Prepare for re-delivery

4. Re-deliver to client
   - Updated files
   - Note what changed
   - Request approval

### Outputs
- Revised content delivered
- Client approval received
- Project complete

### Timeline
- Revision round: 2-3 business days

---

## Phase 8: Project close

### Inputs
- Approved content
- Final payment received

### Process
1. Confirm final approval
   - Email confirmation from client
   - Content officially delivered

2. Collect payment
   - Final 50%
   - Follow up if needed
   - Update accounting

3. Request feedback
   - How was the experience?
   - Would they recommend?
   - Testimonial request

4. Archive project
   - Save all files
   - Document learnings
   - Update creator notes

5. Set follow-up
   - Next project check-in
   - Retainer discussion
   - Stay in touch

### Outputs
- Payment received
- Testimonial (ideally)
- Relationship maintained

### Timeline
- Close: 1-3 business days

---

## Timeline summary

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Proposal to close | 3-14 days |
| Kickoff | 1-2 days |
| Creator matching | 1 day |
| Production | 3-5 days |
| QC | 0.5-1 day |
| Client review | 1-3 days |
| Revisions | 2-3 days |
| **Total** | **10-14 business days** |

Can compress to 5-7 days with rush fee.
