# UGC creator brief template

App-specific briefs for our app portfolio. Copy, customize, and send to creators.

---

## Brief template structure

Every brief should include:
1. App name and one-line description
2. Target audience
3. Key talking points (what TO say)
4. Compliance notes (what NOT to say)
5. Video format and structure
6. Technical requirements
7. Deliverables

---

## PelvicPro / FemFit Brief

### About the app
**App:** FemFit (workout tracker for women)
**What it does:** Track workouts, log sets and reps, build streaks. Features Luna the cat mascot for encouragement.
**Target audience:** Women 18-35 who gym 2-5x per week

### Video requirements
**Length:** 30-45 seconds
**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (TikTok/Reels)
**Style:** Casual, authentic, at the gym or at home

### Key talking points (INCLUDE these)
- "Finally a workout app that gets it"
- Mention Luna the cat mascot
- Show the app interface briefly (we'll provide mockups)
- Mention streak tracking and progress features
- Soft, encouraging vibe (not drill sergeant energy)

### Phrases to use
- "I've been using this for [X] weeks"
- "The cat mascot is actually kind of motivating"
- "It tracks my exercises without feeling intimidating"
- "Finally an app that doesn't feel like it was made for bodybuilders"

### DO NOT say (compliance)
- No weight loss claims or promises
- No medical claims about health benefits
- No comparison to specific competitor apps by name
- No "guaranteed results"
- No before/after body transformation claims
- Avoid: "Best app ever" or superlative claims

### FTC disclosure requirement
Must include one of these:
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" in caption
- Say "partnered with" or "working with" in video
- Clear disclosure visible in video

### Video structure
```
Hook (0-3s): [Relatable gym moment or struggle]
Problem (3-8s): [What's annoying about other workout apps]
Solution (8-20s): [Show FemFit, mention key features]
Social proof (20-30s): [Your experience/results]
CTA (30-45s): [Check it out, link in bio]
```

### Hook options (record all 3)
1. "POV: You finally found a workout app that doesn't make you feel like you need to be a bodybuilder"
2. "Why I switched from [generic] to this app with a cat mascot"
3. "The gym app I wish I had when I started lifting"

### Deliverables
- Raw footage (unedited MP4)
- Final edited version with captions
- 3 hook variations
- Vertical 9:16 format
- 1080p minimum resolution

## General brief checklist (use for all apps)

### Before sending brief
- [ ] App name and description clear
- [ ] Target audience specified
- [ ] 3 hook variations included
- [ ] DO NOT say section complete
- [ ] FTC disclosure requirement stated
- [ ] Video length specified
- [ ] Deliverables listed
- [ ] Payment terms mentioned (in separate communication)

### Technical requirements (all apps)
- Resolution: 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical)
- Audio: Clear voice, no background music (we add in post)
- Lighting: Natural or ring light, face clearly visible
- Delivery: Google Drive or WeTransfer

### Revision policy
- 1 revision round included
- Re-shoots: 50% of original rate
- Turnaround: 3-5 business days standard
