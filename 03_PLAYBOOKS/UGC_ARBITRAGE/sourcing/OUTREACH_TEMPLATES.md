# UGC creator outreach templates

10 tested DM templates for recruiting creators. Customize for your niche.

---

## Template 1: Direct opportunity (Instagram)

**Subject context:** Cold DM to creator who posts UGC samples

```
Hey [Name]! Love your UGC style, especially the [specific video] you posted.

I run a small UGC agency and looking for reliable creators to add to our roster. We work with [niche] brands and need [content type].

Looking for ongoing work, not just one-offs. Typically 5-10 videos per month.

Would you be open to a quick chat about rates and availability?
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific compliment shows you watched their content
- Clear opportunity (ongoing vs one-time)
- Volume indication helps them gauge opportunity

---

## Template 2: Rate inquiry (Fiverr/marketplace)

**Subject context:** Message before ordering on marketplace

```
Hi [Name]!

Your UGC samples look great. I'm sourcing creators for multiple brands in [niche].

Before ordering, quick questions:
1. What's your rate for bulk orders (5+ videos)?
2. Can you deliver raw files + final cuts?
3. What's your typical turnaround?

Looking for ongoing partnership, not just one video.
```

**Why it works:**
- Signals volume buyer (they'll prioritize you)
- Asks practical questions upfront
- "Ongoing partnership" shows long-term value

## Outreach best practices

### Do:
- Personalize first line (reference specific content)
- Be clear about what you're offering
- Include rate range upfront (saves time)
- Respond quickly when they reply

### Don't:
- Send identical copy-paste messages
- Be vague about opportunity
- Lowball obviously
- Follow up more than once

### Response rate benchmarks:
- Cold DM: 10-20% response rate
- Marketplace message: 40-60% response rate
- Referral intro: 60-80% response rate

### Volume needed:
To get 5 good creators, expect to reach out to ~50 people.
