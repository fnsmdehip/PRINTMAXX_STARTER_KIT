# UGC pricing tiers

What to pay creators based on quality, reach, and deliverables. These are your costs. Target 50-70% margin when reselling to brands.

---

## Tier overview

| Tier | Price range | Who it's for | Quality expectation |
|------|-------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Entry | $25-50 | New creators, students, overseas | Basic quality, may need direction |
| Standard | $50-100 | Established micro-creators | Good quality, reliable |
| Premium | $100-200 | Proven performers, niche experts | High quality, minimal revision |
| Influencer | $200-500 | Creators with audience, proven converters | Exceptional, potential organic reach |

---

## Tier 1: Entry level ($25-50)

### Creator profile
- New to UGC (under 6 months)
- Students or side gig creators
- International creators (Eastern Europe, SEA, LATAM)
- Fewer than 1,000 followers
- Limited portfolio

### What you get
- Basic 30-second video
- 1-2 hook variations
- May need detailed scripts
- Standard turnaround (5-7 days)
- 1 revision round included

### Pricing breakdown

| Deliverable | Entry rate |
|-------------|------------|
| 15s video | $25-30 |
| 30s video | $30-40 |
| 45s video | $35-50 |
| 60s video | $40-60 |
| Extra hook | +$5-10 |
| Raw footage | +$5-10 |

### When to use
- Testing new app angles
- Volume content needs
- Budget constraints
- Simple, straightforward briefs

### Margin potential
- Your cost: $35 average
- Resell price: $100-150
- Margin: 65-77%

## Package pricing

Bulk discounts for ongoing work:

### Starter package
- 3 videos at 30s each
- 3 hooks per video
- Entry tier: $75-120
- Standard tier: $150-225
- **Discount:** 15-20% vs individual

### Growth package
- 5 videos at 30s each
- 3 hooks per video
- Entry tier: $100-175
- Standard tier: $225-375
- **Discount:** 20-25% vs individual

### Scale package
- 10 videos at 30s each
- 3 hooks per video
- Entry tier: $175-300
- Standard tier: $400-650
- **Discount:** 25-30% vs individual

### Monthly retainer
- Guaranteed X videos per month
- Priority turnaround
- Reduced per-video rate
- First dibs on rush projects
- Negotiate based on volume

## Price negotiation tactics

### Getting lower rates

1. **Volume commitment:** "I can guarantee 10 videos/month"
2. **Quick payment:** "I pay within 48 hours of approval"
3. **Easy process:** "I send detailed briefs, rarely need revisions"
4. **Long-term framing:** "Looking for ongoing partnership, not one-off"
5. **Bulk pricing:** "What's your rate for 5 videos?"

### What to say

"I'm building a roster for ongoing work. My typical budget for this content type is $X. If you can work at that rate, I can guarantee consistent monthly volume and quick payment."

### When to pay more

- Specific demographic match needed
- Rush turnaround required
- High-complexity content
- Proven high-converter
- Client specifically requested
