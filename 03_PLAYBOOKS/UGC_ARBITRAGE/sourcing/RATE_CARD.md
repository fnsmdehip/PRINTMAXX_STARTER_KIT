# UGC rate card

What to pay creators for different content types. These are your cost inputs.

---

## Video content rates

### Standard talking head videos

| Duration | Budget rate | Standard rate | Premium rate |
|----------|-------------|---------------|--------------|
| 15 seconds | $10-15 | $20-35 | $50-75 |
| 30 seconds | $15-25 | $35-50 | $75-100 |
| 45 seconds | $20-35 | $45-65 | $90-125 |
| 60 seconds | $25-40 | $50-75 | $100-150 |
| 90 seconds | $35-55 | $70-100 | $140-200 |

**Budget rate:** New creators, Eastern Europe, SEA
**Standard rate:** Established creators, US beginners
**Premium rate:** Experienced US creators, specific look needed

## Package pricing

Bulk pricing for ongoing relationships:

| Package | Videos included | Budget rate | Standard rate |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|---------------|
| Starter | 3 videos (30s) | $40-60 | $90-120 |
| Growth | 5 videos (30s) | $60-100 | $150-200 |
| Scale | 10 videos (30s) | $100-175 | $275-375 |
| Monthly retainer | 10+ videos | Negotiate | Negotiate |

Typical bulk discount: 15-25% off per-video rate

---

## Revision policies

What to include in your rate:

| Revisions | Standard practice |
|-----------|-------------------|
| 1 revision round | Included in base rate |
| 2 revision rounds | Add +$5-10 |
| Unlimited revisions | Add +20-30% (avoid this) |
| Re-shoots | 50% of original rate |

Clear revision limits prevent scope creep.

---

## Usage rights pricing

Standard rates assume you're getting:
- Full commercial usage rights
- Perpetual license
- Rights to edit/modify

If creator pushes back on rights:

| Rights level | Modifier |
|--------------|----------|
| Social media only | Base rate |
| Paid ads included | +10-25% |
| TV/broadcast | +50-100% |
| Exclusive (they can't reuse) | +25-50% |

Most creators at budget rates don't negotiate this.

## Rate negotiation tactics

### How to get better rates:

1. **Volume commitment** - "I can guarantee 10 videos/month"
2. **Long-term relationship** - "Looking for ongoing partnership"
3. **Quick payment** - "I pay within 48 hours of delivery"
4. **Easy briefs** - "I provide detailed scripts and examples"
5. **Low revision** - "I rarely ask for more than 1 revision"

### What to say:

"I'm building a roster for ongoing work. My typical rate for this content type is $X. If you can work at that rate, I can guarantee consistent monthly volume."

### When to pay more:

- Specific look/demographic needed
- Rush turnaround required
- High complexity content
- Proven high-converter
- Exclusive rights needed
