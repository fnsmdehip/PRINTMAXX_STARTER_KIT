# UGC creator agreement template

Simple contract for usage rights and deliverables. Modify as needed.

---

## Basic agreement (email version)

For quick agreements via DM/email:

```
Hi [Creator Name],

Confirming our agreement for UGC content:

PROJECT DETAILS:
- Content type: [X videos/photos]
- Duration: [X seconds per video]
- Product: [Product name]
- Delivery date: [Date]

PAYMENT:
- Total: $[Amount]
- Payment method: [PayPal/Wise/etc]
- Payment timing: [50% upfront, 50% on delivery] OR [Full payment within X days of delivery]

DELIVERABLES:
- Final edited videos
- Raw footage files
- [X] alternate hooks

USAGE RIGHTS:
You grant [Your Name/Company] full commercial usage rights including:
- Use in paid advertising on all platforms
- Editing and modification rights
- Perpetual license (no expiration)
- Right to use your likeness in connection with the product

REVISIONS:
- 1 round of revisions included
- Additional revisions at $[X] per round

Please reply confirming you agree to these terms before we proceed.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

## Standard creator agreement (formal version)

```
UGC CREATOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between:

Creator: [Creator Full Name]
Email: [Creator Email]
("Creator")

And:

Client: [Your Name/Company Name]
Email: [Your Email]
("Client")

Effective Date: [Date]

---

1. SERVICES

Creator agrees to produce the following content:

Description: [Detailed description of content]
Quantity: [Number of videos/photos]
Duration: [Length of each video]
Format: [Vertical 9:16, 1080p minimum]
Delivery deadline: [Date]

---

2. COMPENSATION

Total Payment: $[Amount]

Payment Schedule:
[ ] Full payment upon delivery
[ ] 50% upfront ($[Amount]) / 50% upon delivery ($[Amount])
[ ] Other: [Specify]

Payment Method: [PayPal/Wise/Bank Transfer]
Payment Email: [Creator's payment email]

Payment will be made within [X] business days of approved delivery.

---

3. DELIVERABLES

Creator will provide:
- Final edited video(s) in MP4 format
- Raw/unedited footage files
- [X] additional hooks/variations
- [Any other specific deliverables]

All files delivered via [Google Drive/Dropbox/WeTransfer].

---

4. GRANT OF RIGHTS

Creator grants Client the following rights to all delivered content:

a) COMMERCIAL USE: Full rights to use content for commercial purposes including paid advertising, social media, websites, and marketing materials.

b) PLATFORMS: All current and future platforms including but not limited to: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Google Ads, display networks, email, and websites.

c) EDITING RIGHTS: Right to edit, modify, crop, add text/graphics, combine with other content, and create derivative works.

d) DURATION: Perpetual license with no expiration date.

e) TERRITORY: Worldwide usage rights.

f) TRANSFERABILITY: Client may transfer these rights to their clients or partners.

g) LIKENESS: Right to use Creator's name, image, and likeness in connection with the content.

---

5. CREATOR REPRESENTATIONS

Creator represents and warrants that:

a) Creator has full authority to enter this agreement
b) Content is original and does not infringe any third-party rights
c) Creator is at least 18 years of age
d) Any testimonials or opinions expressed are genuine
e) Creator will not use copyrighted music or materials without rights

---

6. REVISIONS

Revisions included: [1] round
Additional revisions: $[X] per round
Major reshoots: 50% of original fee

Client will provide specific, actionable feedback for revisions.

---

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Creator agrees to keep confidential:
- Product details before public launch
- Client's business information
- Terms of this agreement

Creator may use content in their portfolio unless marked confidential.

---

8. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement:
- For material breach with 5 days notice
- For convenience before production begins (refund any upfront payment)

If Client terminates after production begins, Creator is owed for completed work.

---

9. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Creator is an independent contractor, not an employee. Creator is responsible for their own taxes and insurance.

---

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Neither party's liability shall exceed the total payment amount under this agreement.

---

11. GOVERNING LAW

This agreement is governed by the laws of [Your State/Country].

---

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This document constitutes the entire agreement between parties. Modifications must be in writing.

---

SIGNATURES

Creator:
Name: _____________________
Signature: _____________________
Date: _____________________

Client:
Name: _____________________
Signature: _____________________
Date: _____________________
```

## Important notes

### What must be included:
1. Clear scope of work
2. Payment terms
3. Delivery timeline
4. Usage rights (be explicit)
5. Revision policy

### Red flags from creators:
- Won't sign any agreement
- Wants to limit usage rights significantly
- Demands full payment upfront (new relationship)
- Unclear about what they'll deliver

### When to use formal vs. email version:
- **Email version**: Small projects, established creators, under $200
- **Formal version**: New creators, larger projects, over $200, clients who need documentation

### Keep records:
Save all agreements and correspondence. You may need to prove rights ownership to your clients.

### International creators:
Same agreement works internationally. Specify payment method clearly. Note that enforcement is harder across borders, so work with trusted creators.
