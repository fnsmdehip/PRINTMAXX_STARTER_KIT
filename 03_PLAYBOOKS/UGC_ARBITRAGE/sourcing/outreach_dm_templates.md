## Platform-specific notes

### TikTok
- Keep DMs short (character limit)
- Reference specific videos to show you watched
- Many creators have Instagram linked - DM there if TikTok fails
- Follow before messaging (higher response rate)

### Instagram
- Can be longer and more detailed
- Stories replies often get better responses than DMs
- Comment on their posts first to warm up
- Check if they have a business email in bio

### LinkedIn
- More professional tone
- Good for "content creator" professionals
- Reference their work history if relevant

### Twitter/X
- Short and direct
- Quote tweet their content with compliment before DMing
- Good for creators with smaller followings

## Response templates

### When they ask about rates
```
Great question! Rates depend on deliverables:

Basic (1 video, 1 hook): $50-75
Standard (1 video, 3 hooks): $75-125
Package (3 videos, multiple hooks): $150-250

Happy to discuss what works for your time and quality level.
```

### When they ask about the app
```
[App] is a [brief description]. It helps [target audience] with [key benefit].

The brand is looking for authentic content that shows real use, not scripted ads. They want creators who actually fit the target demographic.

I can send you the full brief with talking points, compliance notes, and examples if you're interested.
```

### When they're interested
```
Awesome! Let me send you the brief.

Quick questions first:
1. What's your rate for a 30-45 second video?
2. Can you deliver both raw footage and final edit?
3. What's your typical turnaround time?

I'll get you the brief as soon as I hear back.
```

### When they ghost after interest
```
Hey [Name]! Following up on the [App] brief.

Let me know if you have questions or if the timing doesn't work. Happy to reconnect later if that's better.
```

## Best practices

### Do
- Reference specific content (shows you watched)
- Be clear about what you're offering
- Include rate range upfront
- Respond within 24 hours when they reply
- Keep it casual and human

### Don't
- Copy-paste identical messages (they can tell)
- Be vague about opportunity or rates
- Follow up more than once if no response
- Pressure or guilt-trip
- Promise things you can't deliver

### Expected response rates
- Cold DM (TikTok): 10-15%
- Cold DM (Instagram): 15-25%
- Warm outreach (commented first): 25-35%
- Referral intro: 50-70%

### Volume needed
To get 5 good creators for each niche, expect to reach out to 30-50 people per niche.
