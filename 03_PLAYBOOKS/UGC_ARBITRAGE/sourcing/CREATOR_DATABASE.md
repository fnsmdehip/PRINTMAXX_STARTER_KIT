## Tier 1: Direct creator recruitment ($5-25/video)

### Eastern European creators

Best value. English fluency varies but improving. Professional quality at fraction of US rates.

**Instagram accounts to recruit from:**
- Search hashtags: #ugccreator #ugcportfolio #ugceurope #ugccroatia #ugcpoland
- Location tags: Warsaw, Krakow, Zagreb, Belgrade, Bucharest
- Bio keywords: "UGC creator" "available for collabs" "content creator"

**Where to find them:**
- Instagram explore (search UGC + European cities)
- TikTok creator marketplace (filter by country)
- Facebook groups: "UGC Creators Europe" "Eastern European Content Creators"
- LinkedIn (search "UGC creator" + filter location)

**Typical rates:**
| Content type | Rate range |
|--------------|------------|
| Static photo | $5-15 |
| 15s video | $10-25 |
| 30s video | $15-35 |
| 60s video | $25-50 |
| Package (5 videos) | $75-150 |

**Pros:**
- 3-5x cheaper than US creators
- Often more professional (hungry for work)
- Willing to do revisions
- Good English skills improving rapidly

**Cons:**
- Timezone differences (6-9 hours ahead of EST)
- Payment logistics (PayPal, Wise)
- Some shipping delays for physical products

## Tier 2: Marketplace platforms ($15-75/video)

### Billo

**URL:** billo.app

**How it works:**
- Post a task with your requirements
- Creators apply
- You select and pay through platform
- Platform takes cut

**Pricing:**
- Videos start around $59 per video
- Bulk pricing available
- Platform takes ~30%

**Best for:**
- Quick turnaround
- Vetted creators
- Escrow protection

**Downsides:**
- More expensive than direct
- Platform fees eat margin
- Less relationship building

### Fiverr ($15-75/video)

**Search terms:**
- "UGC video"
- "product review video"
- "testimonial video"
- "TikTok style video"

**Best gigs (under $50):**
- Filter by: new sellers with good samples
- Look for portfolio quality, not ratings
- Many charge $15-30 for 30s videos

**Tips:**
- Message before ordering
- Ask for raw files
- Request multiple hooks
- Specify usage rights explicitly

**Example gigs to look for:**
- New sellers from Eastern Europe with strong samples
- Sellers offering "UGC packages"
- Product review specialists

## Tier 3: Direct recruitment tactics

### Method 1: Instagram DM outreach

1. Search hashtags: #ugccreator #ugcportfolio
2. Filter by recent posts
3. Check follower count (500-5000 ideal)
4. Review their content quality
5. DM with offer

**Target profile:**
- 500-5000 followers
- Posting UGC samples
- Bio says "available for work"
- Recent activity

### Method 2: TikTok recruitment

1. Search "UGC creator" on TikTok
2. Filter by accounts with <10k followers
3. Check for portfolio videos
4. Message on Instagram (TikTok DMs unreliable)

### Method 3: Facebook groups

**Groups to join:**
- UGC Creators and Brands
- UGC Creator Community
- TikTok Content Creators
- Content Creator Jobs

**Approach:**
- Post looking for creators
- Specify your rates upfront
- Request samples before committing

### Method 4: University recruitment

1. Post on local university job boards
2. Target communications/marketing students
3. Offer $25-50 per video
4. They're hungry for portfolio work

### Method 5: Former employees

People who left retail/service jobs make natural UGC creators. They know products and can speak authentically.

## Building your creator roster

### Ideal roster size: 5-10 reliable creators

**Roster structure:**
- 2-3 primary creators (your best, most reliable)
- 3-4 secondary creators (backup, specialized looks)
- 2-3 in development (testing, training)

### Creator profiles to recruit

**Diverse demographics:**
- Young woman (20-30) - most common ask
- Young man (20-30) - growing demand
- Mom/parent type - parenting products
- Professional look - B2B/finance
- Fitness type - health/wellness
- Older demographic (40+) - specific niches

### Creator evaluation criteria

Before adding to roster:

1. **Video quality** - Lighting, audio, composition
2. **Natural delivery** - Not reading, authentic feel
3. **Reliability** - Response time, deadline adherence
4. **Revision handling** - Professional attitude
5. **Rate flexibility** - Willing to negotiate packages

### Red flags

- No portfolio or samples
- Rates significantly above market
- Slow response times (48h+)
- Resistance to signing agreements
- Watermarked samples only
- Requesting full payment upfront

## Payment methods by region

### Eastern Europe
- Wise (formerly TransferWise) - best rates
- PayPal - universal but higher fees
- Direct bank transfer (for established creators)

### Latin America
- PayPal
- Wise
- Payoneer

### Southeast Asia
- PayPal
- Wise
- Payoneer
- GCash (Philippines)

### Payment timing
- 50% upfront, 50% on delivery (new creators)
- Net 7 after delivery (established creators)
- Monthly invoicing (retainer creators)
