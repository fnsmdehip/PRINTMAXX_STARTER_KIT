## Overview

Sell products through TikTok Shop's affiliate program. No inventory needed. Create short-form videos featuring products, add TikTok Shop affiliate links, earn 10-30% commission on sales. TikTok Shop hit $66.2B GMV in 2024 with 100% YoY growth. Small creators (<50K followers) get 30% click-through rate, 4.3x higher than bigger accounts.

Key insight: TikTok Shop affiliate is the lowest-effort ecom model. You never touch inventory. Products are shipped by the seller. You just make videos.

Proof: TikTok Shop did $66.2B GMV. Average affiliate commission is 10-30%. Creators with 5K-50K followers are the sweet spot because small accounts get algorithmic preference for Shop content.

## Step-by-step implementation

### Day 1: Account setup (1-2 hours)

**Task 1: Set up TikTok Shop affiliate access**

Two paths:
1. **Already have 1K+ followers:** Go to TikTok Shop Seller Center > Apply as Affiliate
2. **Under 1K followers:** Build to 1K first using content farm methods (MM006), or buy warmed account

Apply at: seller-us.tiktok.com > Creator > Affiliate

Once approved:
1. Browse product catalog in TikTok Shop
2. Add products to your "showcase" (storefront)
3. Get unique affiliate links per product
4. Link appears as shopping tag on your videos

**Task 2: Select first 10-20 products**

Pick products that:
- Have 4.5+ star ratings and 100+ reviews
- Commission rate 15%+ (some go to 30%)
- Price $10-50 range (sweet spot for impulse buys)
- Visual/demo-friendly (easy to show in video)
- Trending or seasonal

Best categories for affiliates:
```
Beauty/skincare:    15-25% commission, highly visual
Kitchen gadgets:    10-20% commission, demo-friendly
Tech accessories:   10-15% commission, review format
Home organization:  15-25% commission, before/after
Fitness gear:       10-20% commission, demo format
```

Add 10-20 products to your showcase immediately.

### Day 2-3: Create first 10 videos (4-6 hours)

**Task 3: Film TikTok Shop videos**

Video formats that convert:

1. **Product demo** (highest conversion): Show product in use
   ```
   Hook (1-2 sec): "This $12 gadget changed my morning routine"
   Demo (10-20 sec): Show it working
   Result (5 sec): Before/after or satisfied reaction
   CTA (2 sec): "Link in bio" or shopping tag tap prompt
   ```

2. **Unboxing**: Open package on camera, show surprise/delight
3. **Comparison**: This vs that product (compare 2, recommend 1)
4. **Problem/Solution**: Show common problem, reveal product as fix
5. **Day in my life featuring product**: Natural integration

Production tips:
- Natural lighting (near window) or ring light
- Vertical video, 1080x1920
- Captions on (80% watch without sound)
- First 1-2 seconds = hook (most important part)
- 15-30 seconds total (short performs better for Shop)

**Task 4: Add shopping tags**

When uploading video:
1. Tap "Add link" or shopping bag icon
2. Select product from your showcase
3. Product appears as clickable tag on video
4. Viewer taps tag -> Product page -> Buy -> You earn commission

### Day 3-7: Post daily and optimize

**Task 5: Post 2-3 videos per day**

Posting schedule:
```
Morning (7-9 AM):  Educational/review video
Afternoon (12-2 PM): Trending format + product
Evening (6-9 PM):  Demo/unboxing video
```

First 7 days: Post all 10+ videos, one per slot.

TikTok Shop algorithm tips:
- Videos with shopping tags get boosted in For You feed
- Engagement in first 30 minutes determines reach
- Reply to every comment (especially product questions)
- Pin comment with product info

**Task 6: Request free product samples**

Once you have 5+ videos with shopping tags:
1. Message sellers directly on TikTok Shop
2. Show them your views/engagement
3. Request free samples for review
4. Most sellers with 100+ products will send free samples

This gives you infinite product supply for content.

### Week 2-4: Scale and optimize

**Task 7: Analyze what sells**

After 20+ videos, check TikTok Shop analytics:
- Which products got most clicks?
- Which video format converted best?
- What time of day drives most sales?
- What commission rate are you averaging?

Double down on winning:
- Products with high click rate -> Make more videos for those
- Formats that convert -> Use that format as template
- Kill products with zero clicks after 5+ videos

**Task 8: Go live for real-time sales**

TikTok LIVE shopping is the highest-converting format:
- Show products live, answer questions real-time
- Add shopping links during LIVE
- Average LIVE conversion is 3-5x higher than video
- Start with 30-min LIVEs, 2-3x/week

LIVE structure:
```
0-5 min:  Greeting, today's products preview
5-20 min: Product demos one by one
20-25 min: Q&A, answer comments
25-30 min: Flash deals, last-chance offers
```

**Task 9: Apply for TikTok Shop campaigns**

TikTok runs promotional campaigns:
- Flash Sales (higher commission during events)
- Super Brand Day (brand-specific promotions)
- Holiday campaigns (Black Friday, Christmas, etc.)

Apply in TikTok Shop Seller Center > Campaigns.

## Revenue mechanics

### How money flows
Create video with shopping tag -> Viewer watches -> Taps product -> Buys on TikTok Shop -> Seller ships product -> TikTok pays commission after delivery confirmed

### Commission economics

| Product Price | Commission Rate | Your Earnings | Videos to Make $1K/mo |
|--------------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| $15 | 20% | $3/sale | 334 sales (~11/day) |
| $30 | 20% | $6/sale | 167 sales (~6/day) |
| $50 | 15% | $7.50/sale | 134 sales (~4/day) |

At 2-3% conversion rate on 10K views/video, 3 videos/day:
- 30K views/day x 2% click x 3% buy = 18 sales/day
- At $5 avg commission = $90/day = $2,700/mo

### First dollar timeline
Day 0-2: Setup. Day 2-7: First videos posted. Day 7-14: First sales. Day 14-28: Consistent daily sales.

## Risk management

- **TikTok ban risk (US):** Diversify to Instagram Shop, YouTube Shopping. Cross-post content. Save all videos locally.
- **Product quality issues:** Only promote 4.5+ star products. Check reviews before showcasing. Your reputation matters.
- **Algorithm suppression:** Diversify content (not 100% shopping). Mix entertainment/value content with product posts. 70/30 rule.
- **Commission rate changes:** Diversify products and sellers. Don't depend on one product. Build direct relationships with sellers for better rates.
- **Account suspension:** Follow community guidelines strictly. No misleading claims. Proper disclosures.
