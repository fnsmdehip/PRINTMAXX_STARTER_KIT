## Getting Started

### Affiliate Requirements

**Minimum requirements:**
- 1,000+ followers on TikTok
- 18+ years old
- US-based account (for US Shop)
- Business or creator account
- No major policy violations in past 180 days

**Application process:**
1. Go to TikTok Shop Seller Center or Creator Center
2. Navigate to "Affiliate" section
3. Submit application
4. Wait 1-3 business days for approval
5. Access product catalog and generate affiliate links

### Seller Requirements

**For US sellers:**
- Valid US business registration (LLC, Corp, Sole Prop)
- EIN or SSN
- US bank account
- Valid government ID
- Proof of address
- Product liability insurance (recommended for physical goods)

**For international sellers (selling to US):**
- Valid passport
- Business registration in home country
- Can use Payoneer or similar for payouts
- Must comply with US import/customs

### Account Types

| Account Type | Best For | Features |
|--------------|----------|----------|
| Creator | Affiliates, influencers | Affiliate access, creator tools |
| Business | Sellers, brands | Full shop features, ads access |
| Business + Creator | Hybrid | All features |

## Affiliate Program Deep Dive

### How It Works

1. Browse TikTok Shop marketplace for products
2. Add products to your "Showcase" (storefront)
3. Create content featuring products
4. Viewers buy through your link/showcase
5. Earn commission (typically 5-30%)

### Commission Rates

**Commission structure varies by:**
- Product category
- Seller's commission offering
- Your negotiation (for targeted campaigns)

**Typical ranges:**

| Category | Commission Range |
|----------|------------------|
| Beauty/Skincare | 10-25% |
| Fashion | 10-20% |
| Electronics | 3-8% |
| Home/Kitchen | 8-15% |
| Health/Wellness | 10-30% |
| Pet Products | 10-20% |
| Jewelry | 15-25% |

### Affiliate Strategies

**Strategy 1: Showcase Curation**
- Build themed showcases (e.g., "Best Fitness Products Under $30")
- Optimize showcase SEO (keywords in bio, showcase name)
- Rotate products based on performance

**Strategy 2: Review Content**
- Honest product reviews perform best
- Show real usage, not just unboxing
- "X days later" follow-up videos get high engagement

**Strategy 3: Trend Riding**
- Find trending products early (use TikTok Shop trending)
- Create content FAST when product goes viral
- Ride the algorithm wave

**Strategy 4: Targeted Campaigns**
- Reach out to sellers for higher commission
- Negotiate exclusive rates for your audience
- Offer package deals (X posts for Y commission)

### Finding High-Commission Products

**In TikTok Shop:**
1. Seller Center > Affiliate > Open Marketplace
2. Filter by commission rate (highest first)
3. Check product reviews and sales velocity
4. Look for new products with high commission (sellers buying visibility)

**External research:**
- Kalodata.com - TikTok Shop analytics
- FastMoss - Product research
- Pipiads - Ad spy (what's being promoted)

## Seller Program Deep Dive

### Fee Structure

| Fee Type | Amount | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Referral Fee | 2-8% | Category dependent |
| Transaction Fee | 2.8% + $0.30 | Payment processing |
| Affiliate Commission | You set | Typically 10-25% |
| FBT Fees | Varies | If using Fulfilled by TikTok |

**Example calculation:**
- $50 product sale
- Referral fee (5%): $2.50
- Transaction fee: $1.70
- Affiliate commission (15%): $7.50
- Net: $38.30 (76.6% of sale price)

### Seller Success Factors

**What sells on TikTok Shop:**
1. Products that demonstrate well in video
2. Under $50 impulse buys
3. Problem-solution products
4. Trendy/viral potential items
5. Products with visual before/after

**What doesn't work:**
- Commodities (buy on Amazon)
- High-consideration purchases
- Products that need detailed specs
- Anything requiring size fitting (without virtual try-on)

### Fulfillment Options

**Self-fulfillment:**
- Ship from your location
- Full control over packaging/branding
- Best for low volume, custom products

**Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT):**
- Send inventory to TikTok warehouses
- They handle picking, packing, shipping
- Faster shipping, better customer experience
- Required for some "fast shipping" badges

**Third-party logistics (3PL):**
- ShipBob, ShipMonk, Deliverr
- Good for scaling without FBT
- Maintain some brand control

## Product Selection

### Winning Product Criteria

**The TikTok Shop Formula:**

```
Viral Product = Visual Demo + Problem Solve + Under $50 + Emotional Hook
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Can demonstrate in 15 seconds?
- [ ] Solves clear problem?
- [ ] Price under $50 (ideally under $30)?
- [ ] Has "wow factor" or transformation?
- [ ] High perceived value?
- [ ] Easy to ship (not fragile, not oversized)?
- [ ] Good profit margin (3x+ cost)?

### Hot Categories (2025-2026)

| Category | Why Hot | Example Products |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| Beauty tools | Visual transformation | Ice rollers, LED masks, hair tools |
| Kitchen gadgets | Problem-solving demos | Vegetable choppers, smart bottles |
| Fitness accessories | Before/after potential | Massage guns, resistance bands |
| Phone accessories | Universal appeal | Projectors, mounts, chargers |
| Pet products | Emotional content | Feeders, toys, grooming |
| Cleaning products | Satisfying content | Steam cleaners, organizers |

### Product Research Process

**Step 1: Identify trending products**
- TikTok Shop > Trending
- Hashtag research (#tiktokmademebuyit)
- Competitor analysis (what are big sellers pushing?)

**Step 2: Validate demand**
- Search product on TikTok - how many videos?
- Check Amazon BSR for category
- Google Trends for search interest

**Step 3: Find supplier**
- Alibaba/1688 for manufacturing
- US-based wholesalers for faster shipping
- Existing brands for affiliate/wholesale

**Step 4: Calculate margins**
- Product cost
- Shipping to you or 3PL
- TikTok fees (8-15% total)
- Affiliate commission if using
- Target: 40%+ net margin

## Content Strategy

### Video Formats That Convert

**1. The Hook-Demo-CTA (15-30 sec)**
```
0-3s: Hook ("POV: You finally found...")
3-15s: Product demo
15-20s: Results/transformation
20-30s: CTA ("Link in bio" or yellow basket)
```

**2. The Problem-Solution (30-60 sec)**
```
0-5s: Show the problem
5-10s: "Until I found this..."
10-40s: Demo the solution
40-50s: Before/after comparison
50-60s: Price + CTA
```

**3. The Review (60-90 sec)**
```
0-10s: "I've been using this for X weeks..."
10-40s: Honest pros
40-50s: Any cons (builds trust)
50-80s: Final verdict
80-90s: CTA + price
```

**4. Trend Hijack**
- Use trending sounds
- Apply trending format to your product
- Speed is everything - first 24-48 hours

**5. UGC/Testimonial**
- Customer videos (ask for permission)
- Duet customer content
- Stitch viral videos about the problem

### Content Best Practices

**Do:**
- Show the product in use immediately
- Use natural lighting
- Include captions (most watch muted)
- Face camera (builds trust)
- Price transparency
- Authentic reactions

**Don't:**
- Long intros
- Stock footage feel
- Over-produced content
- Hiding negatives (kills trust)
- Misleading claims

### Hashtag Strategy

**Primary hashtags:**
- #TikTokShop
- #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt
- #[ProductCategory]TikTok
- #ShopWithMe

**Secondary hashtags:**
- Product-specific (#skincare #fitnessproducts)
- Niche-specific (#fitnessmom #faithjourney)
- Trending sounds/challenges

**Volume:**
- 3-5 hashtags per post
- Don't overload
- Mix high and low competition

### Posting Schedule

**Optimal times (US):**
- Morning: 7-9 AM EST
- Lunch: 12-1 PM EST
- Evening: 7-9 PM EST
- Weekend mornings perform well

**Frequency:**
- Minimum: 1x/day
- Optimal: 3-5x/day
- Test different times and track

## Live Selling

### Why Live Matters

- 30%+ of TikTok Shop GMV from lives
- Higher conversion than static content
- Real-time engagement builds trust
- Algorithm boosts live sellers
- Can drive massive volume in hours

### Live Selling Formats

**1. Product showcase live**
- Cycle through products (10-15 min each)
- Demo, answer questions, offer deals
- Pin current product

**2. Q&A + selling**
- Answer audience questions
- Naturally introduce products
- More engagement-focused

**3. Flash sale live**
- Limited time deals
- Urgency and scarcity
- Higher conversion rates

**4. Tutorial/educational**
- Teach something valuable
- Products are tools/solutions
- Builds authority

### Live Best Practices

**Pre-live:**
- Announce 24 hours before
- Tease products
- Build anticipation

**During live:**
- Pin products being discussed
- Acknowledge buyers by name
- Run giveaways (engagement boost)
- Address comments directly
- Keep energy high

**Post-live:**
- Clip best moments
- Repurpose as static content
- Thank buyers

### Live Requirements

- Minimum follower count varies (typically 1000+)
- Must have TikTok Shop access
- Mobile or streaming setup
- Good lighting and audio
- Products ready to demo

## Dropshipping

### TikTok Shop Dropshipping Models

**Model 1: Direct from supplier (risky)**
- Order from AliExpress/supplier when sale comes in
- Long shipping times = bad reviews
- NOT recommended for TikTok Shop

**Model 2: US warehouse dropship**
- Partner with suppliers who stock in US
- CJ Dropshipping, ZenDrop, Spocket
- 3-7 day shipping possible
- Better but margins tighter

**Model 3: Hybrid (recommended)**
- Test products with dropship
- Winner products: buy inventory
- Use FBT or 3PL for proven winners
- Best of both worlds

### Recommended Dropship Suppliers

| Supplier | Shipping Time | Integration | Best For |
|----------|---------------|-------------|----------|
| CJ Dropshipping | 7-15 days (US warehouse: 3-7) | TikTok Shop direct | General products |
| ZenDrop | 3-7 days | Shopify→TikTok | Quality products |
| Spocket | 2-5 days | Shopify→TikTok | US/EU products |
| Printful | 3-7 days | Direct | Print-on-demand |

### Dropship Workflow

```
1. Find winning product (research)
2. Source from US warehouse supplier
3. List on TikTok Shop
4. Create content / recruit affiliates
5. Order auto-fulfills when sold
6. Track margins and reviews
7. Winner products → buy inventory
8. Scale with FBT
```

### Dropship Risks

- Shipping time complaints
- Quality control issues
- Supplier stock outs
- Thin margins after fees
- Policy changes

**Mitigation:**
- Only US warehouse suppliers
- Order samples first
- Have backup suppliers
- Move winners to inventory ASAP

## Risks and Compliance

### Platform Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Policy changes | High | Diversify platforms |
| Account suspension | High | Follow ToS strictly |
| Commission cuts | Medium | Build direct relationships |
| Algorithm changes | Medium | Diversify content types |
| Increased competition | Medium | Niche down, build brand |

### Compliance Requirements

**Product claims:**
- No unsubstantiated health claims
- FTC disclosure for paid/affiliate
- Honest representations

**Disclosure requirements:**
- "#ad" or "Paid Partnership" when required
- Clear affiliate disclosure
- Honest reviews (can't only say positives)

**Restricted products:**
- Weapons, drugs, tobacco
- Counterfeit goods
- Adult content
- Cryptocurrency/financial products (varies)

### Account Safety

**Best practices:**
- One account per person/business
- Real information on registration
- Respond to customer issues quickly
- Maintain good seller ratings
- Don't use bots or fake engagement
