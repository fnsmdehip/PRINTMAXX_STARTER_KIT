## Whop Affiliate Program (Distribution Hack)

Whop's built-in affiliate system pays 30% recurring on referred purchases. This means:

**As a seller:** Enable affiliates. Other Whop users promote your products for 30% cut. Free marketing, pay only on results.

**As an affiliate:** Promote other Whop products to your audience. 30% recurring on every referral. Stack with content farm accounts.

**Revenue example as affiliate:**
- Refer 100 users to $49/mo products
- 30% of $49 = $14.70/user/month
- 100 users = $1,470/mo recurring
- Growing as referred users stay subscribed

**Distribution stack:**
1. Faith content farm accounts promote faith products on Whop
2. Fitness content farm accounts promote fitness products
3. Tech content farm accounts promote dev tools
4. Each account earns 30% affiliate on referrals
5. Content farm monetization without needing own products immediately
