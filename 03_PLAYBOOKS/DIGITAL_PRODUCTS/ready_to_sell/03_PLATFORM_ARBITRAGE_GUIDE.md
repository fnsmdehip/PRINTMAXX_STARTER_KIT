## Chapter 1: What Is Platform Arbitrage

Platform arbitrage means posting the same content where it pays the most. One TikTok video earns $0.01 per 1,000 views. The exact same video on Facebook Reels earns $4.40 per 1,000 views. That is a 440x difference for the same work.

Most creators pick a platform and stick with it. They leave money on the table every single day. This guide shows you exactly where the money is in February 2026, how much each platform pays, and how to cross-post efficiently.

**The core principle:** Create once, distribute everywhere, collect revenue from the platform that pays the most.

**Why this works now:** Platforms are in an arms race for short-form video content. They offer wildly different creator payments to attract talent. The creators who post everywhere win. The creators who are loyal to one platform get exploited.

## Chapter 3: Facebook Reels - $4.40/1K

### Why Facebook Reels Pays 4-440x More

Facebook has 3.07 billion monthly active users. Their ad infrastructure is the most sophisticated in the world. They charge advertisers $10-$50+ CPM. They can afford to pay creators $1-$4.40 per 1,000 views because they are making much more from the ads shown alongside your content.

TikTok has 1.6 billion users but significantly lower ad rates and less mature ad targeting. Their creator fund was always underfunded.

### Verified Numbers

Multiple creators have confirmed Facebook Reels payouts in 2026:

- Fitness niche: $4.40 per 1,000 views (confirmed January 2026)
- Finance niche: $3.20 per 1,000 views
- Entertainment: $1.00-$2.00 per 1,000 views
- Average across niches: $2.50 per 1,000 views

Compare to TikTok:
- Base creator fund: $0.01-$0.02 per 1,000 views
- TikTok Creativity Program: $0.40-$6.00 per 1,000 views (requires 1M+ views)

At 1 million monthly views (achievable with consistent posting), the difference between TikTok base ($15) and Facebook Reels ($4,400) is $4,385 per month. For the exact same content.

### How to Set Up Facebook Reels Monetization

**Requirements:**
1. Facebook Page (not personal profile) with 10,000+ followers OR 600,000 total minutes viewed in last 60 days
2. At least 5 active videos
3. Must be in an eligible country
4. Content must comply with Facebook monetization policies

**Step-by-step:**

1. Create a Facebook Page for your niche (if you do not have one)
2. Go to Facebook Creator Studio or Meta Business Suite
3. Navigate to Monetization > Stars and Reels
4. Apply for In-Stream Ads on Reels
5. Wait for approval (typically 3-7 days)
6. Start posting Reels

**Growth strategy to hit 10K followers fast:**
- Cross-post your best-performing TikTok/YouTube Shorts content
- Post 3-5 Reels per day for the first 30 days
- Use trending audio from Facebook's audio library
- Engage with comments (algorithm boost)
- Join Facebook Reels groups for cross-promotion

**Pro tip:** Your content does NOT need to be original to Facebook. You can post the exact same videos from TikTok and YouTube Shorts. Remove watermarks before cross-posting (use a watermark remover tool).

### Content That Performs Best on FB Reels

### The Math on Cross-Posting

If you are already creating TikTok content, cross-posting to Facebook Reels takes approximately 5-10 minutes per video (download, remove watermark, upload, add caption).

At 3 videos per day:
- Time added: 15-30 minutes per day

### How TikTok Shop Works

1. You create content featuring a product
2. Product link appears on the video
3. Users tap the link and buy without leaving TikTok
4. You earn a commission (10-50% depending on the program)

**Two models:**

Model 1: Affiliate
- Promote other people's products
- Earn 10-50% commission
- No inventory, no shipping, no customer service
- Start with $0

Model 2: Seller
- Sell your own products through TikTok Shop
- Keep all margin
- Handle or outsource fulfillment
- Requires product sourcing

### Getting Started as TikTok Shop Affiliate

1. Create TikTok Shop Seller Center account (shop.tiktok.com)
2. Select "Creator" path (not "Seller" unless you have products)
3. Browse the affiliate marketplace for products
4. Request sample products (many sellers send free samples)
5. Create review/demo videos
6. Add product links to videos
7. Earn commission on every sale

**Best product categories for affiliates:**

- Kitchen gadgets ($15-$40, viral potential)

## Chapter 13: The Cross-Post Workflow

### Create Once, Post Everywhere

**Daily workflow (45 minutes for 6 platforms):**

1. **Create content** (20 minutes): Write one post, record one video, or both
2. **Format for each platform** (15 minutes):
   - Twitter/X: Text post, 280 chars or thread
   - Threads: Same text, slightly modified
   - Facebook: Same video, add FB-specific caption
   - TikTok: Original video
   - YouTube Shorts: Same video, YouTube-specific title
   - Instagram Reels: Same video, hashtags for IG
3. **Schedule** (10 minutes): Use Buffer (free for 3 channels) or Publer ($12/month for all)

### Video Cross-Post Checklist

- [ ] Remove TikTok watermark before posting elsewhere
- [ ] Add platform-specific captions
- [ ] Use platform-native audio when possible (better reach)
- [ ] Adjust aspect ratio if needed (all short-form is 9:16)
- [ ] Add product links where applicable (TikTok Shop, Gumroad in bio)
- [ ] Respond to first 10 comments on each platform (algorithm boost)

### Text Cross-Post Checklist

- [ ] Adjust length for each platform (X: 280, Threads: 500, LinkedIn: 3000)
- [ ] Remove platform-specific references ("as I said in my last tweet")
- [ ] Add platform-appropriate hashtags
- [ ] Include link in bio reference for monetized platforms
- [ ] Time posts for peak engagement per platform
