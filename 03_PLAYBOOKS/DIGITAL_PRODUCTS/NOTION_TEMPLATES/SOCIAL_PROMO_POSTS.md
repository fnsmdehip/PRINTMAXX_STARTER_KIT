## POST FORMAT RULES

1. Start with a lowercase hook. No "Hey everyone!" No "Excited to announce!"
2. Use line breaks between ideas. Dense paragraphs die on X.
3. Product mentions go at the end, not the beginning. Value first.
4. Never use: game-changer, revolutionary, comprehensive, robust, unlock, empower.
5. Prices always stated directly. No "DM for pricing."
6. "link in bio" is the only CTA. No "click here" or "check this out."
7. Under 280 characters for pure text posts. 350 max with image.
8. Every post should work without the visual. Image adds, never carries.
