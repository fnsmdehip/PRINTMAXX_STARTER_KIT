## Launch Sequence

### Phase 1: Free Template (Day 0-3) - Build the email list

**Free Product:** "5 AI Prompts That Save 2 Hours Per Week" (1-page Notion template)
- Simple, high-value, fast to create
- Email gate on Gumroad (free + email = lead captured)
- Thank-you page upsells to AI Tool Stack Dashboard ($12)
- Builds the email list before paid products launch

**Why free first:**
- Gumroad algorithm favors products with downloads. Free product gets volume.
- Email list enables direct marketing to buyers who already trust you
- Converts "I'll think about it" into "I already use their stuff"
- Free-to-paid conversion rate on Gumroad averages 3-8% for digital products

### Phase 2: Cheapest Paid Product (Day 3-7)

**Launch:** Morning Grace 7-Day Challenge ($7)
- Lowest price = lowest friction
- 7-day format creates urgency (start this week)
- Accountability partner feature = built-in viral loop (they invite a friend who also needs the template)
- First-50-copies-at-$3 launch promotion drives initial reviews

### Phase 3: Mid-Tier Products (Day 7-14)

**Launch:** Prayer Warrior ($9) + AI Tool Stack ($12)
- Cross-sell Morning Grace buyers into Prayer Warrior (same niche, natural progression)
- AI Tool Stack launches to productivity audience separately
- Two niches now generating revenue independently

### Phase 4: Premium Products (Day 14-21)

**Launch:** 12-Week Body Recomp ($17) + Content Creator Command Center ($27)
- Higher price points require more trust, which the earlier launches build
- By now you have reviews, social proof, and an email list
- Command Center at $27 is your highest-margin individual product

### Phase 5: Bundles (Day 21-30)

**Launch:** All 3 bundles
- Niche bundles for people who only care about one area
- Complete bundle as the "best deal" anchor
- Email blast to all previous buyers: "Now available as bundles - save up to $25"
- Any previous single-purchase buyers get a discount code for upgrading to bundle (pay the difference only)

### Upgrade Logic

|----------------|-----------|-------|

| Morning Grace ($7) | Faith Bundle ($14) | "Upgrade to both for $7 more (you already own Morning Grace)" |

| AI Tool Stack ($12) | Command Center ($27) | "Now manage your content with the same level of control" |
| AI Tool Stack ($12) | Productivity Bundle ($35) | "Add the Command Center for $23 more (bundle upgrade)" |
| Command Center ($27) | AI Tool Stack ($12) | "Track the AI tools that power your content machine" |
| Any single ($7-27) | Complete Bundle ($47) | "Get everything for $47. Credit what you already paid." |
| 12-Week Recomp ($17) | Complete Bundle ($47) | "Add faith + productivity systems. $30 more for 4 more templates." |

### Credit-for-Upgrade Strategy

When a customer already bought an individual template and wants the bundle:

**Option A (Simple):** Discount code for (bundle price - what they paid). Example: Bought Morning Grace ($7), wants Complete Bundle ($47). Give code for $40.

**Option B (Simpler):** Don't track credits. Just offer bundles at bundle price. Most customers won't buy everything individually first. The few who do won't complain about a $7 overlap on a $47 bundle.

**Recommendation:** Option B. Don't overcomplicate. Focus on new sales.

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring

Every product page should show the "value" price next to the actual price:

| Product | "Value" | Price | Framing |
|---------|---------|-------|---------|
| Morning Grace | "Worth $15" | $7 | "7 guided devotionals for less than a coffee" |
| Prayer Warrior | "Worth $25" | $9 | "Replaces a $20/year prayer app" |
| AI Tool Stack | "Worth $30" | $12 | "Saves $87/mo in wasted AI subscriptions" |
| 12-Week Recomp | "Worth $50" | $17 | "Replaces a $150/mo personal trainer" |
| Command Center | "Worth $70" | $27 | "Replaces 3-4 separate creator tools" |
| Complete Bundle | "$72 individually" | $47 | "Save $25 - all 5 templates" |

### Pay What You Want

Gumroad supports PWYW with a minimum. Strategy:

- Set minimum at ~50-60% of listed price
- Most people pay the listed price or slightly above
- The minimum captures price-sensitive buyers who would otherwise bounce
- 5-10% of buyers pay above listed price (the "generous supporter" effect)

### Decoy Pricing

The Productivity Bundle ($35) serves as a decoy that makes the Complete Bundle ($47) look like a steal.

"I can get 2 templates for $35 or ALL 5 for just $12 more?"

This is intentional. The Complete Bundle is the highest-revenue SKU. The niche bundles exist to make the complete bundle look irresistible.

## Affiliate / Referral Strategy

### Gumroad Affiliate Program

Gumroad has a built-in affiliate feature. Enable it for all products.

**Commission structure:**
- Individual templates: 30% commission
- Bundles: 25% commission
- Complete Bundle: 20% commission

**Why different rates:** Higher-priced bundles already give affiliates more dollars per sale. $47 x 20% = $9.40 per referral vs $7 x 30% = $2.10.

### Who to Recruit as Affiliates

| Affiliate Type | Where to Find Them | Why They'd Promote |
|----------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Faith bloggers | Christian blog directories, Instagram faith accounts | Prayer Warrior + Morning Grace align with their audience |
| Fitness YouTubers | YouTube channels with 1K-50K subs (reachable) | 12-Week Recomp is an easy recommend for their viewers |
| Notion creators | Twitter #NotionTemplate community, Reddit r/Notion | They review templates, your 30% commission incentivizes them |
| Productivity newsletters | Beehiiv/Substack newsletters about tools and productivity | AI Tool Stack + Command Center fit their reader base |
| Content creator coaches | Twitter/YouTube creator education accounts | Command Center is relevant to their audience |

### Outreach Template (Cold DM / Email)

"hey [name] - I built a [template name] Notion template that your audience would actually use. it's $[price] and I'll give you 30% on every sale. no minimum, just a unique link. want me to send the details?"

Short. No pitch deck. No "partnership opportunity" language. Just: here's the product, here's your cut, want in?

### Built-In Virality (Morning Grace)

Morning Grace has an accountability partner feature. Each buyer gets pre-written text/email templates to invite a friend to do the challenge together.

The friend needs to buy their own copy ($7). This is built-in word-of-mouth at the product level.

**Estimated viral coefficient:** If 20% of buyers invite one friend, and 30% of invited friends buy: 0.20 x 0.30 = 0.06 additional sales per original sale. Not explosive, but free and compounding.

## Gumroad Optimization Tactics

### Listing Optimization

1. **Product thumbnails:** Mock up the template in action. Show data filled in, not empty templates. People buy the result, not the blank canvas.

2. **Social proof:** After first 10 sales, add "X people bought this" badge. After first positive review, pin it at the top of the description.

3. **FAQ section in listing:** Add to every product:
   - "Do I need a paid Notion account?" No, free works.
   - "Can I customize it?" Yes, duplicate and edit anything.
   - "Is there a refund policy?" 30 days, no questions.
   - "Does this work on mobile?" Yes, Notion has iOS and Android apps.

4. **Suggested products:** Gumroad lets you suggest other products on the thank-you page. Configure cross-sells for every product (use the upgrade table above).

### Email Sequences (Post-Purchase)

**Day 0 (Purchase):** Delivery email with template link + "How to get started in 2 minutes" quick guide.

**Day 3:** "How's it going with [template name]? Here's a quick tip: [one non-obvious feature]."

**Day 7:** "People who use [template] also love [cross-sell template]. Here's $2 off: [code]."

**Day 14:** "Would you leave a review? It takes 30 seconds and helps other [niche] people find this. [review link]."

**Day 30:** "I just released [new template]. As a previous customer, here's 15% off: [code]."

### SEO on Gumroad

Gumroad products rank on Google. Optimize:
- Product title includes primary keyword: "Notion Template" + niche term
- Description includes long-tail keywords naturally
- Tags use all relevant search terms (15 max per product)
- Profile page is complete with bio and links

**Target keywords:**
- "prayer journal notion template"
- "workout tracker notion"
- "ai tools notion dashboard"
- "content creator notion template"
- "morning devotional template"
- "fitness planner notion"
- "ai prompt library notion"
