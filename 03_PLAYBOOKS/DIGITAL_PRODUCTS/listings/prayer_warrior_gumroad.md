## FAQ

**Q: How is this different from Morning Grace?**
A: Morning Grace is a 7-day guided challenge to build the habit. Prayer Warrior is the full daily system you use after those 7 days. Morning Grace = starter. Prayer Warrior = the long-term tool.

**Q: Do I need a paid Notion account?**
A: no. the free Notion account works perfectly. no features are locked behind paid.

**Q: Can I share this with my small group?**
A: each person needs their own copy. have them purchase individually, or buy the template and share the link with your group. the template duplicates into each person's workspace separately.

**Q: How long does the daily devotional take?**
A: 10-15 minutes for the basic daily log. 20-30 minutes if you do scripture memory and gratitude journaling too. you decide how deep to go.

**Q: Can I customize the prompts?**
A: yes. everything is editable. change the prompts, add your own scripture, modify the weekly reflection questions. it's your workspace.

**Q: Is the scripture from a specific Bible translation?**
A: pre-loaded verses use NIV and ESV. you can change them to any translation you prefer.
