## FAQ

**Q: Does this work for both iOS and Android?**
A: yes. the checklist covers App Store Connect (iOS) and Google Play Console (Android) separately. asset requirements, listing optimization, and review processes are specific to each platform.

**Q: How much should I budget for ads?**
A: the playbook starts with $10-30/day per platform for testing. you can launch with zero ad spend using the organic content pipeline and cold outreach templates. ads amplify what's already working.

**Q: I've already launched my app. Is this still useful?**
A: yes. most of the playbook is about post-launch optimization. the analytics framework, content calendar, and iteration checklists apply whether you launched yesterday or 6 months ago.

**Q: How long does the full launch process take?**
A: 2-4 weeks from "app is done" to "launch day." the pre-launch content pipeline can run in parallel with final testing. if you skip content and go straight to launch, expect worse results.

**Q: Do I need all 4 paid ad platforms?**
A: no. start with one. Apple Search Ads has the highest intent (people are literally searching for apps). the playbook recommends starting there and expanding based on results.

**Q: What analytics tools do I need?**
A: RevenueCat (free until $2.5K MRR) for revenue. Mixpanel or Amplitude (both have free tiers) for behavior. App Store Connect and Google Play Console (free) for acquisition. total cost: $0 to start.
