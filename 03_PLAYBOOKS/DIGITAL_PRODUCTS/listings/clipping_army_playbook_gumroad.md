## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to know video editing?**
A: no. if you're starting a clipping business, you hire clippers who do the editing. this playbook teaches you the business operations, client acquisition, and management. if you want to BE a clipper, the tool stack section covers everything you need to learn.

**Q: What kind of creators need clipping services?**
A: anyone creating long-form content (podcasts, streams, YouTube videos, courses) who isn't repurposing into short-form. that's most of them. the prospecting guide tells you exactly how to identify and approach them.

**Q: How much can I realistically make?**
A: one client at $1,000/month minus two clippers at $3/hr (40 hrs/month each = $240) = $760 profit per client. five clients = $3,800/month profit. ten clients with a VA manager = $7,000+/month. the revenue math section walks through every scenario.

**Q: What if I want to build a clipping army for my OWN content?**
A: the first section covers exactly that. how to recruit voluntary clippers, incentivize sharing, build community around redistribution, and structure the whole operation. the 750-person case study is broken down step by step.

**Q: How long before I get my first client?**
A: if you send 20 cold DMs per day using the templates, most people land their first client in 1-2 weeks. the "free audit" approach converts about 40% of interested prospects.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: yes. you're providing a content distribution service. creators hire you to repurpose and distribute their content. all above board.
