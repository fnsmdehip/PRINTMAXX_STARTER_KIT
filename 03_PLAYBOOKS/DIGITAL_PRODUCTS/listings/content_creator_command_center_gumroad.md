## FAQ

**Q: Which platforms does this track?**
A: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and custom. add any platform in 30 seconds.

**Q: Does this pull analytics automatically?**
A: no. Notion doesn't connect to platform APIs. you log analytics manually or copy-paste from platform dashboards. the value is having everything in one place with insights you can't get from individual platform analytics.

**Q: Can I use this for a team?**
A: yes. share the Notion workspace with team members. each person can see the calendar, add ideas, and track their assigned content. the collab pipeline works as a lightweight CRM.

**Q: How is this different from just using a spreadsheet?**
A: spreadsheets don't have calendar views, automatic priority scoring, linked databases, or visual status tracking. this template connects your ideas to your calendar to your analytics to your revenue in ways a spreadsheet never will.

**Q: Do I need paid Notion?**
A: no. the free account handles everything for individual creators. teams of 3+ might want Notion paid for better collaboration features, but the template itself works fine on free.

**Q: Can I customize the priority scoring?**
A: yes. the scoring formula is visible and editable. change the weights for trending potential, ease, and monetization to match what matters most for your content strategy.
