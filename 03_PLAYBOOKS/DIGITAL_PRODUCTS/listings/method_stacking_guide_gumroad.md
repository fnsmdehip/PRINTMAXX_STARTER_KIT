## FAQ

**Q: Should I start with one stack or multiple?**
A: one. pick the stack closest to your existing skills. run it for 2-4 weeks. then add the second method in that stack. the playbook has exact implementation priority for someone starting from zero.

**Q: How do I know which stack fits me?**
A: the playbook maps each stack to skill sets. can you code? Stack 2 (App Empire) or Stack 6 (X Launch). can you write? Stack 1 (Authority) or Stack 8 (Content Multiply). can you sell? Stack 7 (Outbound Agency) or Stack 9 (Community). the decision tree is on page 8.

**Q: Are the revenue projections realistic?**
A: every number is sourced from published benchmarks. cold email ROI ($42 per $1 spent) from Smartlead's 2026 research. newsletter CPM ($20-200) from Beehiiv's data. app cross-promotion (20%+ of revenue) from Adikteev's research. sources listed at the end of the playbook.

**Q: How much does the tool stack cost?**
A: shared foundation is $200/month (Claude Max + free tools). stack-specific additions range from $8/month (App Empire) to $178/month (Community Revenue). most stacks run on $200-350/month total. every tool has a free alternative listed.

**Q: Can I run all 10 stacks at once?**
A: no. solo operators should run 2-4 stacks max. the time allocation matrix shows exactly how many hours each stack takes. the playbook recommends starting with 2, expanding to 3 at month 2-3, and maxing at 4-5 with automation and contractors by month 12-18.

**Q: What's the fastest stack to revenue?**
A: Stack 7 (Outbound Agency) has the fastest path to $5K+/month because cold outbound closes deals directly. Stack 2 (App Empire) scales highest long-term. the implementation priority section ranks all 10 stacks by time-to-first-dollar and time-to-$5K.
