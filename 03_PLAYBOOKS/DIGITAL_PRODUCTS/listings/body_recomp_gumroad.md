## FAQ

**Q: Is this for bulking or cutting?**
A: body recomp. the meal database has options for both surplus and deficit. the weekly check-in tells you when to adjust. the 3-phase structure handles periodization so you don't need to guess.

**Q: Do I need a gym membership?**
A: the exercise library includes bodyweight variations, but the full program is designed for a standard gym with barbells, dumbbells, cables, and machines. filter the library by equipment if your setup is limited.

**Q: Can women use this?**
A: the exercise library and nutrition frameworks work for anyone. the pre-programmed phases skew toward male-typical training goals (Push/Pull/Legs for muscle growth). women may want to adjust exercise selection for different emphasis areas.

**Q: How long does each workout take?**
A: 45-75 minutes depending on the phase and rest times. Phase 1 (foundation) skews shorter. Phase 3 (peak) skews longer.

**Q: Do I need a paid Notion account?**
A: no. free Notion works perfectly.

**Q: Can I swap exercises?**
A: yes. the exercise library has 80+ options tagged by muscle group. swap anything while keeping the overall program structure.
