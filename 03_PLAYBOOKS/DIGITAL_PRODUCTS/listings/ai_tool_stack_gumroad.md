## FAQ

**Q: Which AI tools are pre-loaded?**
A: ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Leonardo.ai, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, D-ID, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Perplexity, Jasper, Copy.ai, Runway, Kling, Suno, Descript, and 12 more. you can add any tool in 30 seconds.

**Q: Do I need a paid Notion account?**
A: no. the free account handles everything.

**Q: Can I track team usage?**
A: the template is designed for individual use. you can share it with a team in Notion, but it doesn't have multi-user tracking built in.

**Q: How do I calculate per-use costs?**
A: the cost tracker divides your monthly subscription by the number of times you used the tool that month. it shows you that your $20/month tool you used 3 times cost $6.67 per use.

**Q: Is the prompt library searchable?**
A: yes. every prompt is tagged by category, model, quality rating, and date. search, filter, and sort however you want.
