**Body:**

most cold email "templates" online are garbage. they sound like a robot having a stroke. "i hope this email finds you well" finds your email in the trash.

this is different. these are real sequences that generated real revenue across 7 different industries. not theory. not "best practices." the actual emails, subject lines, follow-up timing, and reply scripts.

the foundation is the 6-question framework. answer these 6 questions in under 100 words and your cold email writes itself:

1. what you do
2. who you do it for
3. how you do it
4. what problem you solve
5. proof it works
6. what the ROI looks like

every sequence in this playbook follows that framework. you swap in your details and send.

what's inside:

- **the 6-question framework:** the complete system for writing cold emails that don't sound like cold emails. fill in the blanks, get a working email in 3 minutes. works for any industry

- **7 industry-specific sequences:** agency owners, coaches, content creators, ecommerce, healthcare/dental, legal services, SaaS. each with custom pain points, proof angles, and CTAs specific to that vertical
- **15 LinkedIn connection requests:** not "i'd love to connect." actual messages that get accepted and lead to conversations. includes voice note scripts for 3x response rate
- **deliverability setup guide:** SPF, DKIM, DMARC explained in plain English. the exact DNS records to add. how to warm your domain in 14 days. common mistakes that land you in spam
- **A/B testing framework:** what to test first (subject lines), what to test second (opening line), what to test last (CTA). minimum sample sizes. how to read results. what "good" looks like by industry
- **reply handling playbook:** scripts for positive replies, negative replies, "not now" replies, referrals, and objections. 12 templates covering every scenario. stop winging it when someone actually responds
- **send timing guide:** best days and hours by industry. why Tuesday 10am is not universal. timezone optimization for B2B vs B2C

**Closing:**

every day you send bad cold emails, you burn leads you can't get back. that prospect who ignored your "just checking in" follow-up is gone.

these sequences work because they follow the 6-question framework. specific. brief. human-sounding. the kind of email you'd actually read if it showed up in your inbox.

**Q: How many emails should I send per day?**
A: the guide covers this in detail. short answer: start with 20/day on a new domain, scale to 50/day after 2 weeks of warmup. the warmup protocol is included.

**Q: What's the expected reply rate?**
A: across 14,000 sends, average 12% reply rate. top-performing sequence hit 19%. your results depend on your targeting, offer, and follow-through. the A/B testing framework helps you optimize.

**Q: Can I customize these for my business?**

**Q: Is LinkedIn outreach included?**
A: yes. 15 connection request templates plus voice note scripts. LinkedIn has 3x the response rate of cold email for certain industries. the guide tells you when to use which channel.
