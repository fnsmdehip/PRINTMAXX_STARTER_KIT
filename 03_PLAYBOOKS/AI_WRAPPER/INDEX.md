## AI Wrapper Method Overview

### What is an AI Wrapper?
A SaaS product that wraps Claude (or other AI APIs) with niche-specific UI + context + integrations.

**Example:** PromptMaster Pro wraps Claude API with smart routing logic + prompt history + team features

### Why AI Wrappers?
- **Speed:** Ship in 2-4 weeks
- **Cost:** $200-800/month infrastructure for $10K+/month revenue (95%+ margins)
- **PMF:** Niche markets are underserved (low competition)
- **Recurring:** Subscription model = predictable revenue
- **Scalability:** No hiring needed until $100K+ MRR

### Revenue Model
- **Free tier:** 3-5 uses/month (funnel to paid)
- **Pro:** $4.99-$19.99/mo (individual users)
- **Premium:** $29.99-$99/mo (power users)
- **Enterprise:** $499-$2,499/mo (teams/businesses)

## Failure Modes to Avoid

❌ **Building without users:** Interview before coding
❌ **Too many features:** Ship MVP (2-3 features only)
❌ **Bad positioning:** "AI wrapper" doesn't sell. "Prayer scheduling for Christians" does
❌ **Wrong pricing:** Too cheap = wrong customers. Charge $9.99+ minimum
❌ **No differentiation:** "Better AI" fails. "AI for X" wins
❌ **Slow iteration:** Ship weekly. Iterate daily based on user feedback
