HOW IT ACTUALLY WORKS

Your body can't fully hide stress. When you lie, your brain works harder, your sympathetic nervous system activates, and your physiology changes in measurable ways. These are the same signals a professional polygraph measures. TruthScope reads them through your iPhone.

FINGER PULSE DETECTION (Contact PPG)
Place your fingertip over the rear camera with the flash on. TruthScope captures blood volume changes frame-by-frame, extracts the pulsatile signal, applies bandpass filtering (0.7-4.0 Hz), and calculates your heart rate and Heart Rate Variability (HRV) using RMSSD. Stress causes HR to rise and HRV to drop. Validated research shows smartphone PPG correlates >0.996 with ECG for heart rate measurement.

FACE SCAN (Remote Photoplethysmography)
Your face contains subtle color fluctuations invisible to the naked eye but detectable by your camera. TruthScope extracts the green channel signal from your facial region, applies the CHROM separation algorithm, and tracks your cardiac rhythm without you touching anything. Contactless. Passive. Real.

VOICE STRESS ANALYSIS
Your voice changes under cognitive load. Pitch rises from vocal cord tension. Response latency increases because constructing a lie takes more mental effort than telling the truth. Speech rate shifts. TruthScope measures fundamental frequency, jitter, shimmer, response time, and speech patterns in real-time.

FACIAL BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS
Blink rate increases under cognitive load (baseline 15-20/min, stressed 25-30+). Genuine smiles engage the eyes (Duchenne markers). Micro-expressions flash across your face in under 200 milliseconds. TruthScope tracks blink rate, gaze stability, facial asymmetry, lip compression, eye contact percentage, and rapid expression changes.

MULTI-MODAL FUSION
Combine all four channels into a single weighted deception score. The analyzer uses published meta-analysis weights: physiological signals (40%), vocal analysis (30%), and facial cues (30%). Temporal context smoothing accounts for question-response patterns and baseline drift. Real-time confidence scoring based on signal quality, consistency, and measurement duration.
