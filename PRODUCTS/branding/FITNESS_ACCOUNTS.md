## Cross-promotion strategy between accounts

The two accounts should feel like separate brands that occasionally reference each other, not clones.

| Direction | Method | Frequency |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Account 2 promotes WalkToUnlock | "I use this app to force my 10K steps" organic mentions | 2-3x per week |
| Account 1 shares fitness tips | Retweet/share Account 2's walking-related content | 1-2x per week |
| Joint content | Same walking challenge, both accounts post their version | 1x per month |

**Rules:**
- Never make Account 2 feel like an ad for WalkToUnlock. It's a fitness account that happens to use the app.
- Account 1 stays focused on the app. Fitness tips only when they relate to walking/steps.
- Cross-promotion should feel like two friends shouting each other out, not corporate synergy.
