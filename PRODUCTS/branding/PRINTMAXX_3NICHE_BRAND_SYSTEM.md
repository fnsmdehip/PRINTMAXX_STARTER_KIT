## Voice Consistency Rules

**All 3 niches follow same voice principles:**

✅ DO:
- Lead with consequences/results (not explanation)
- Use exact numbers (not "many" or "lots")
- Name specific tools/methods
- Short, punchy sentences
- Lowercase for casual energy
- Admit failures and tradeoffs

❌ NEVER:
- Em dashes (—)
- AI vocabulary (leverage, unlock, empower, etc.)
- "It's not just X, it's Y" constructions
- Vague claims without proof
- Promotional adjectives
- Hedging to death
