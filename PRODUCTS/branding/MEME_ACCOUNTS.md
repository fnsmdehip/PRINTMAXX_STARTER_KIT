## Operational Notes

### Tools
- Meme generation: Canva (free tier), Photopea (free Photoshop), meme generators
- Scheduling: Buffer or Publer (multi-platform)
- Analytics: native platform analytics + spreadsheet tracking
- Content sourcing: Reddit (r/ProgrammerHumor, r/startups, r/me_irl), Twitter trending, TikTok sounds

### Account Safety
- Use separate email per account
- Don't cross-link accounts publicly (keep brand separation)
- VPN/anti-detect browser if running from same device
- Don't mass-follow/unfollow (organic growth only, paid engagement for initial boost if needed)
- Read platform TOS quarterly. meme accounts get nuked for copyright sometimes.

### Content Workflow
1. Source 20-30 meme ideas daily (10 min scroll session across Reddit, Twitter, TikTok)
2. Batch create 1 week of content in 2-3 hours on Sunday
3. Schedule via Buffer/Publer
4. Real-time engagement: respond to comments for 15 min after each post
5. Track what hits, double down on formats that get 2x+ avg engagement
