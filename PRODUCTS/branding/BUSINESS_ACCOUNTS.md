```
I audit about 10 local business websites per week. The same 5 problems show up in 80% of them.

1. No phone number in the header. Visitors on mobile want to tap and call. If they have to scroll or hunt for your number, they'll call your competitor instead.

2. Homepage says what you do but not where. Google can't rank you for "[city] + [service]" if your city name isn't on the page.

3. Stock photos instead of real photos. People can tell. A phone photo of your actual team beats a $500 stock image every time.

4. No clear next step. Every page should answer: "What do I want the visitor to do right now?" If the answer isn't obvious, add a button.

5. Slow load time. If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load on mobile, you're losing roughly half your visitors before they see anything.

All 5 of these are fixable in a weekend. You don't need a redesign. You need a tune-up.

If you want me to run a quick audit on your site, drop your URL in the comments. I'll check the basics for free.
```
