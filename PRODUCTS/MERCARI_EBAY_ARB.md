## HOW DROPSHIP ARBITRAGE WORKS

1. Find a product on AliExpress/Alibaba at a low price
2. List it on eBay/Mercari/FB Marketplace at a higher price
3. When someone buys, order from AliExpress and ship to your customer
4. OR: buy inventory in bulk (higher margins, faster shipping, better reviews)

**Two models:**

| Model | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|-------|------|------|----------|
| **Drop ship (no inventory)** | $0 upfront, no risk | Slow shipping (7-20 days), can't control quality | Testing products |
| **Bulk buy (inventory)** | Fast shipping (2-3 days), higher margins, better reviews | Upfront cost, storage needed | Proven products |

**Recommended approach:** List as dropship first. If a product sells 5+ units in 2 weeks, buy 20-50 units in bulk for faster shipping and higher margins.

## PRODUCT SELECTION CRITERIA

Every product below was selected based on:
- **Margin:** Buy price < 30% of sell price (target 3x+ markup)
- **Weight:** Under 2 lbs (keeps shipping cheap)
- **Size:** Fits in a USPS flat rate envelope or small box
- **Demand:** Category with consistent sales volume
- **Competition:** Not dominated by Amazon/Walmart (niche enough)
- **Fragility:** Low breakage risk (no glass, no electronics with screens)
- **Returns:** Low return rate category (avoid clothing fit issues, electronics defects)

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC LISTING TIPS

### eBay
- **Best for:** Electronics accessories, tech products, branded searches
- **Listing type:** "Buy It Now" with Best Offer enabled (catch negotiators)
- **Photos:** 12 images per listing (white background + lifestyle + size comparison)
- **Promoted Listings:** 2-5% ad rate on competitive products (pays for itself)
- **Shipping:** Offer free shipping (bake cost into price). Buyers filter for free shipping.
- **Returns:** Accept 30-day returns (eBay ranks return-friendly sellers higher)
- **Seller fees:** 13.25% final value fee + $0.30 per order

### Mercari
- **Best for:** Trending/viral products, lifestyle items, younger demographic
- **Listing type:** Fixed price (no auction, no best offer)
- **Photos:** 12 images (lifestyle photos perform best, not white background)
- **Shipping:** Use Mercari labels (prepaid, simple) or SmartPost
- **Pricing:** Mercari buyers expect lower prices than eBay. Price 10-15% lower.
- **Seller fees:** 10% selling fee + payment processing

### Facebook Marketplace
- **Best for:** Home decor, bulky items, local pickup items
- **Listing type:** Fixed price
- **Photos:** Lifestyle shots (in a real home setting)
- **Shipping:** Offer both local pickup (free) and shipping
- **Pricing:** Lowest prices expected. Keep margins tight or use as loss-leader.
- **Seller fees:** 5% selling fee (lowest of the three)
- **Advantage:** Local pickup = no shipping cost = highest margin

## SUPPLIER TIPS

- **AliExpress for testing:** Small quantities (1-10 units), no MOQ, free shipping
- **Alibaba for scaling:** Bulk pricing (50-500 units), lower per-unit cost, 15-30 day production
- **1688.com for max savings:** Chinese domestic marketplace, lowest prices, needs agent (Superbuy, CSSBuy) for ordering
- **Quality check:** Always order 1 sample first. Photograph everything. Test the product yourself before listing.
- **Shipping speed:** ePacket (7-15 days), AliExpress Standard (10-20 days), DHL/FedEx (5-7 days, expensive)
- **Bulk ship to you:** Order 50+ units shipped to your address via AliExpress/Alibaba. You then ship individually to customers. Faster delivery = better reviews = more sales.
