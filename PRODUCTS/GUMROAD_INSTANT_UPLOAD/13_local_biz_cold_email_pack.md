## How to use this pack

1. Pick your target industry below
2. Customize the {{VARIABLES}} in each email
3. Send email 1 on day 1
4. Send follow-up 1 on day 3 (if no reply)
5. Send follow-up 2 on day 7 (if still no reply)
6. After 3 emails with no reply, move to next prospect

**Sending limits:** 10-15 emails/day per inbox for the first 2 weeks. After warmup: 50-80/day per inbox. Want 500/day? Use 7 inboxes.

**The golden rule:** 58% of replies come from email 1. If your first email doesn't work, more follow-ups won't fix it. Spend your optimization time on email 1.

## DNS SETUP QUICKSTART (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Buy 3 domains ($12-15 each)

Go to Porkbun or Namecheap. Buy 3 domains:
- yourname-consulting.com
- yourname-dev.com
- yourname-agency.com

Never cold email from your primary domain.

### Step 2: Add DNS records (per domain)

**SPF:**
```
Type: TXT
Host: @
Value: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
```

**DKIM:** generate in your email tool (Instantly, DeliverOn, or Google Workspace). Add the provided key as a TXT record.

**DMARC:**
```
Type: TXT
Host: _dmarc
Value: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com
```

### Step 3: Create inboxes

2-3 inboxes per domain = 6-9 total inboxes.

**Cheapest:** Google Workspace at $7/inbox/month
**Fastest:** DeliverOn at $49/month (pre-warmed, skip warmup)

### Step 4: Warmup (if not using pre-warmed)

- Days 1-7: send 10-15 emails/day to friends. Have them reply.
- Days 8-14: increase to 20-30/day. Mix in cold sends.
- Days 15-21: increase to 30-50/day. Begin cold outbound.
- Day 22+: full volume at 50-80/inbox/day.

Total setup time: 15 minutes for domains + DNS. Warmup runs in background for 2-3 weeks (or skip with pre-warmed inboxes).
