## DESCRIPTION (paste into Gumroad body)

You're not getting replies because your emails say too much.

The 6-question framework: What you do. Who for. How. Problem solved. Proof. ROI. Under 100 words. That's it. When you answer all 6, the email writes itself and reads in 45 seconds.

Tested across 10,000+ sends. 42% open rate. 12% reply rate.

**What's inside:**

The 6-question framework with 3 filled examples. Not fill-in-the-blank templates. Worked examples you can study and mirror.

20 subject lines tested across 10,000+ sends. Open rates between 45-68%. No clickbait, no tricks — patterns that hold up over time.

5 complete email sequences. First touch through follow-up 5. Each sequence covers a different scenario: cold prospect, warm intro, competitor customer, inbound lead, referral.

Reply handling for 7 responses. "Not interested." "Send more info." "Too expensive." "Maybe later." "Who are you?" "Forwarding this." And the most common one: silence.

Safe send limits. Start at 15/day. Scale to 80/day by week 3. Want 500/day? 7 warmed inboxes at $37/month total.

DNS setup in 15 minutes. SPF, DKIM, DMARC. Without this, your emails land in spam regardless of how good they are.
