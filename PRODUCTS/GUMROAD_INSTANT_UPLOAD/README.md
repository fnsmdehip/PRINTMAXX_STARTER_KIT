# GUMROAD INSTANT UPLOAD

**Purpose:** Everything needed to list all 13 products + 3 bundles on Gumroad in under 60 minutes.

**Process:**
1. Go to gumroad.com, create account
2. Connect Stripe for payouts
3. For each product below, copy the listing text into Gumroad's product creation form
4. Upload the product file (the .md content file — convert to PDF via any Markdown-to-PDF tool, or upload as-is)
5. Set price, tags, category as specified
6. Publish

**Upload order (strategic):**
1. Product 10 (FREE lead magnet) — gets marketplace visibility, captures emails
2. Product 9 ($7 impulse buy) — lowest friction sale
3. Products 7-8 ($17 each) — easy upsells
4. Products 5-6 ($27 each) — mid-tier
5. Products 2-4 ($47 each) — premium tier
6. Product 1 ($97) — flagship
7. Micro Products 11-13 ($19-$39) — targeted niche products
8. Bundles — create after all individual products are live
