## DESCRIPTION (paste into Gumroad body)

You need clients. The cold email tools want $200–$400/month before you've made a single dollar.

Instantly: $97/month. Apollo: $49/month. Lemlist: $59/month. Smartlead: $39/month. Add a lead database and you're at $300–$500/month in overhead before you send your first email.

This system eliminates all of those costs. You'll build the same infrastructure, find the same quality leads, and send the same personalized sequences — using tools that cost $0.

This is not Gmail blasts from your personal email. This is a professional cold email system with custom domains, proper DNS authentication, warmed sending accounts, and AI-personalized outreach. The kind of setup that agencies charge $2K to configure.

**What's inside:**

Infrastructure setup. Custom domain ($10/year). DNS records: SPF, DKIM, DMARC. Google Workspace or ImprovMX for routing. Brevo for sending (free to 300 emails/day). Configured for deliverability from day one.

Account warming. 2-week warm-up protocol using Mailwarm-free-tier and manual seeding. How to get from 0 to 100 sends/day without hitting spam. The metrics to watch. The red flags that mean slow down.

Lead sourcing without Apollo. Google Maps scraping for local businesses. LinkedIn Sales Navigator workarounds. Reddit, HN, and Twitter for inbound signals. Hunter.io free tier for email finding. The 5 sources that replace a $49/month lead database.

7 cold email sequences. By niche: local business, SaaS, agency, consultant, freelance design, content, and dev. Each sequence: 4 emails over 14 days. Subject lines, body copy, and follow-up cadence.

AI personalization at scale. Claude or GPT-4o prompt templates that generate personalized first lines from a LinkedIn URL or website. Batch processing 50 leads in one sitting.

Reply handling. The decision tree for every reply type: interested, not now, wrong person, unsubscribe, referral. Templates for each. How to move interested replies to booked calls.

Compliance. CAN-SPAM checklist. GDPR notes for EU leads. What to include in every email. How to honor unsubscribes automatically.

**The math:**
$0 tool cost. 100 sends/day. 15-day outreach window. 3% reply rate. 25% close rate on replies. Conservative: 1 close per month at $1,500 = $18,000/year from a $0 system.

$37. One client covers it 40x over.
