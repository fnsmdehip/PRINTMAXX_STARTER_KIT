## 2. Requirement Breakdown

The 2nd Brigade (2BDE) at Fort Hood needs a commercial, subscription-based mass text messaging platform to replace traditional communication methods (printed flyers, command channels).

**Core Requirements:**
- Minimum 600,000 pre-paid message credits at contract start
- SMS and MMS capability
- Dedicated, high-volume A2P 10DLC registered phone number
- Dedicated onboarding and training for government users
- Assigned account manager for ongoing support
- Platform security aligned with industry data-protection practices

**Contract Type:** Anticipated Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP)

**Deliverables:**
1. Hosted messaging platform access
2. 600K+ pre-paid message credits
3. A2P 10DLC registered phone number
4. Onboarding/training sessions
5. Ongoing account management

## 7. Bid/No-Bid Recommendation

### RECOMMENDATION: BID (Strong)

**Rationale:**
1. **Low barrier to entry.** This is a commercial product procurement, not custom development. If you resell or operate a text messaging platform, you already have the core capability.
2. **Small business set-aside.** Eliminates competition from Twilio and other large providers. The small business size standard under NAICS 517121 is $47M revenue, which is generous.
3. **Sources Sought = positioning opportunity.** Responding now positions you as a capable provider when the actual RFP drops. The government is literally asking who can do this.
4. **Firm-Fixed-Price = predictable revenue.** No cost-plus risk. Price your 600K credits with margin and deliver.
5. **Repeat business potential.** Military installations rarely switch messaging platforms once adopted. This could become a multi-year contract.

**No-Bid if:**
- You have zero experience with A2P 10DLC registration
- You cannot demonstrate message delivery to military installations
- You have no capability statement for text messaging services

### Estimated Bid Preparation Time
- Capability statement: 4-8 hours
- Past performance write-up: 2-4 hours
- Total: 1-2 business days

### Estimated Contract Value
Based on commercial pricing for 600K message credits:
- Low estimate: $25,000/year (bulk SMS at ~$0.04/message)
- Mid estimate: $60,000/year (with MMS, account management, training)
- High estimate: $150,000/year (multi-year with options, premium support)
