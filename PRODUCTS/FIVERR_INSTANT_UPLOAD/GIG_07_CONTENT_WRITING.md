## Description (copy-paste ready)

```
You open Instagram to post. Stare at the screen for 15 minutes. Type something generic. Delete it. Close the app. Post nothing.

Meanwhile your competitor posts daily, gets engagement, and books clients from their DMs.

I write 30 days of content in one batch. You get a spreadsheet with every post ready to copy, paste, and publish. No more "what should I post today?"

WHAT EACH POST INCLUDES:

- Caption written for the specific platform (Twitter is not Instagram is not LinkedIn)
- Relevant hashtags researched for your niche (not generic #love #inspo garbage)
- Content type label (value, engagement, story, promotional)
- Posting time recommendation based on your audience

THE CONTENT MIX:

I don't write 30 promotional posts. That kills your account. The mix:

- 40% value posts (tips, how-tos, data, frameworks your audience needs)
- 25% engagement posts (questions, polls, hot takes that drive comments)
- 20% story posts (behind-the-scenes, journey updates, lessons learned)
- 15% promotional posts (your offers, testimonials, CTAs)

This ratio is tested across 200+ accounts. Pure value gets saved but not shared. Pure promo gets ignored. This mix grows followers AND converts them.

PLATFORM DIFFERENCES:

Twitter: short, punchy, opinion-driven. 280 characters max.
Instagram: longer captions (150-300 words), carousel-friendly, hashtag strategy.
LinkedIn: professional tone, story-driven, long-form (500-1,300 characters).
TikTok: trend-aware captions, hashtag research, hook-first.
Facebook: community-focused, question-driven, link-friendly.

I don't write one generic post and paste it everywhere. Each platform gets content written for how people use that platform.

NICHES I WRITE FOR:

Fitness coaches (32 clients), SaaS companies (18), local businesses (45), personal brands (27), ecommerce (14), real estate agents (9), consultants (21), food/restaurants (11).

Send me your niche and Instagram handle. I'll write 3 free sample posts in 2 hours so you can see the quality before ordering.
```

## FAQs

**Q: Do you create the images/videos too?**
```
This gig covers the written content: captions, hashtags, content type labels, and posting schedule. Premium includes visual direction notes (what the image/graphic should show, color suggestions, layout ideas). If you need actual graphic design, I offer that as a separate service or can recommend Canva templates that match your brand.
```

**Q: Can you match my brand voice?**
```
Yes. Send me 5-10 of your existing posts that represent your voice, plus any brand guidelines. I'll match the tone, vocabulary, and energy. If you're starting fresh, I'll create a consistent voice based on your niche, audience, and goals. First 3 posts delivered as a sample for your approval before I write the rest.
```

**Q: What format is the content delivered in?**
```
Google Sheets or Excel with columns: Date, Platform, Content Type, Caption, Hashtags, Visual Direction (Premium), Notes. Standard and Premium also include a Buffer-ready CSV you can import directly for scheduled posting. One click to schedule the whole month.
```

**Q: Can you handle multiple brands?**
```
Yes. Each brand is a separate order. If you manage multiple clients (agencies, VAs), message me about bulk pricing. 3+ brands at once gets a 15% discount.
```

**Q: How do you research what works in my niche?**
```
I analyze top-performing accounts in your space: what formats get the most engagement, which hooks drive comments, what hashtags are active vs dead, what posting times work. This isn't guesswork. It's pattern matching from accounts that are already winning in your niche.
```
