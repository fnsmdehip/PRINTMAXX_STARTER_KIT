## Description (copy-paste ready)

```
You want to rank for 100 keywords. Writing 100 individual pages by hand would take 200+ hours. I generate them programmatically in 3-7 days.

HOW THIS WORKS:

Programmatic SEO. Instead of manually writing each page, I build a template and generate hundreds of unique pages targeting specific keyword patterns.

The most common pattern: "[your service] in [city]"

Example for a plumber: "emergency plumber in Austin TX", "drain cleaning in Dallas TX", "water heater repair in Houston TX". One template generates 200+ pages targeting 200+ cities.

Each page includes:
- Unique title tag and meta description targeting the keyword
- 300-500 words of relevant content (not duplicated, not spun)
- Local schema markup (helps Google understand your service area)
- Internal links to your main service pages
- Contact information and CTA
- Mobile-responsive layout

WHY THIS WORKS:

Long-tail keywords like "[service] in [city]" have low competition but high intent. Someone searching "emergency plumber in Cedar Park TX" is ready to call. These pages rank faster than competitive head terms and convert at 3-5x the rate.

A single page might get 10-50 visits per month. 100 pages = 1,000-5,000 visits per month. All high-intent, local, ready to buy.

WHO THIS IS FOR:

- Local service businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, cleaning)
- Law firms (targeting "[practice area] lawyer in [city]")
- Dental clinics (targeting "[procedure] dentist in [city]")
- Real estate (targeting "homes for sale in [neighborhood]")
- Any business that serves multiple locations

I've generated 10,000+ pages across 40+ industries. Average time to first ranking: 2-6 weeks for low-competition local terms.

Send me your business type and service area. I'll estimate how many pages you need and what traffic to expect.
```

## FAQs

**Q: Isn't this duplicate content?**
```
No. Each page has unique content tailored to the specific city/keyword. I don't copy-paste the same text across 100 pages. The template varies content by location, includes city-specific details, and generates unique meta tags. Google treats these as separate pages targeting separate queries.
```

**Q: How do I add these to my website?**
```
I deliver the pages as HTML files with a sitemap.xml. If your site is WordPress, I include upload instructions. If you use a different platform, I'll format the output for your CMS. Premium includes deployment assistance. You can also host these as a standalone microsite on a subdomain.
```

**Q: How long until the pages rank?**
```
Low-competition local terms (under 30 keyword difficulty): 2-6 weeks. Medium competition: 1-3 months. This depends on your domain authority, existing site age, and the specific keywords. I target keywords you can actually win, not terms dominated by Yelp and HomeAdvisor.
```

**Q: Can you target keywords other than "[service] in [city]"?**
```
Yes. Other patterns I've built: "[product] vs [product]", "best [product] for [use case]", "[topic] statistics [year]", "how to [task] in [city]", "[service] cost in [city]". Any repeatable keyword pattern works. Tell me your keywords and I'll confirm.
```

**Q: Do I provide the keyword list?**
```
Basic: you provide the keywords (e.g., "here are 25 cities I serve"). Standard and Premium: I do keyword research to find the highest-traffic, lowest-competition targets. Premium also includes a competitor gap analysis to find keywords your competitors rank for that you don't.
```
