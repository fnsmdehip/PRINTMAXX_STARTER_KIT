## Description (copy-paste ready)

```
Cold email works. The people saying it's dead are the ones sending "Hi, I noticed your company and wanted to reach out to discuss synergies."

That's not cold email. That's spam with a college vocabulary.

Real cold email gets 15-40% open rates and 3-8% reply rates when the copy is right. I've written sequences that booked 12 meetings from 200 sends. That's a 6% reply-to-meeting rate. No ads budget. No warm intros. Cold outreach.

THE FRAMEWORK:

Every email I write answers 6 questions in under 100 words:
1. What do you do?
2. Who do you do it for?
3. How do you do it?
4. What problem do you solve?
5. What proof do you have?
6. What's the next step?

If your cold email doesn't answer all 6 in the first scroll, it gets deleted.

WHAT YOU GET:

A complete sequence, not one email. Because 80% of replies come from follow-ups 2-5. Most people send one email, get no response, and quit. The sequence does the work.

Email 1: The opener. Short, specific, one clear ask.
Email 2: The value add. New angle, social proof, quick win offer.
Email 3: The breakup. Creates urgency without being desperate.
Emails 4-5 (Standard/Premium): Objection handlers and re-engagement.

INDUSTRIES I'VE WRITTEN FOR:

SaaS (14 campaigns), marketing agencies (22), web design (18), consulting (11), local services (31), recruiting (8), real estate (6). If you sell B2B and need meetings, I've probably written for your space.

THE RULES I FOLLOW:

- Under 100 words per email (nobody reads long cold emails)
- No "I hope this finds you well"
- No "just following up"
- Personalization variables built in (not generic mass sends)
- CAN-SPAM and GDPR compliant
- Written to sound like a human, not a sales bot

Send me your offer, target audience, and biggest objection. I'll send back a free subject line in 30 minutes.
```

## FAQs

**Q: Will these work for my industry?**
```
If you sell B2B (business to business) and your deal size is $500+, cold email works. I've written for SaaS, agencies, consultants, local services, real estate, recruiting, and more. The framework is the same. The personalization changes. Tell me your industry and I'll confirm.
```

**Q: Do you help with sending/deliverability?**
```
Premium includes a sending tool setup guide (Instantly, Smartlead, or Lemlist) with warmup protocol and deliverability best practices. I don't manage the sending, but I give you the exact steps so your emails land in the inbox, not spam. Basic and Standard are copy only.
```

**Q: How many emails should I send per day?**
```
Start with 20-30/day from a warmed domain. Scale to 50-100/day after 2 weeks. Never send 500 emails on day 1 from a new domain. That's how you get blacklisted. Premium includes the full warmup timeline.
```

**Q: Can you build my lead list?**
```
Premium includes 100 verified leads (name, email, company, title) matching your ICP. I use Apollo, Hunter, and manual verification. For larger lists, I offer lead list building as a separate gig or add-on.
```

**Q: What reply rate should I expect?**
```
With good copy and a targeted list: 15-40% open rate, 3-8% reply rate. If you're under 2% reply rate, either the list targeting is off or the offer doesn't match the audience. I optimize the copy. You need to nail the targeting.
```
