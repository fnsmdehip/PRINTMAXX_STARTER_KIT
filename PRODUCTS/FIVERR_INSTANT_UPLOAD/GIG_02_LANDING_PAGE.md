## FAQs

**Q: What platform do you build landing pages on?**
```
I build custom pages deployed on Vercel or Netlify (free hosting, fast load times). No Wix, no Squarespace, no drag-and-drop builders. If you specifically need Webflow, WordPress, or Unbounce, I can do that too. Mention it in your order.
```

**Q: Can you write the copy or do I need to provide it?**
```
Basic: you provide the copy (headline, body text, CTA). Standard and Premium: I write the copy based on your offer, audience, and competitors. I study what's converting in your space and write accordingly. You approve before I build.
```

**Q: How do you track conversions?**
```
Standard includes Google Analytics 4 setup with conversion events. Premium adds Meta pixel and Google Ads pixel for retargeting. You'll see exactly how many visitors convert, where they come from, and what they click.
```

**Q: Can you integrate with my email tool?**
```
Yes. I integrate with Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Beehiiv, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, Brevo, and most platforms with an API or Zapier connection. Standard package includes the integration. Basic is a standalone form that forwards to your email.
```

**Q: What if my conversion rate is low after launch?**
```
Standard and Premium include an A/B test variant. I build two versions with different headlines or layouts so you can test which converts better. If you're still under 3% after 500 visitors, message me and I'll review your traffic source. Low conversion is often a traffic quality problem, not a page problem.
```
