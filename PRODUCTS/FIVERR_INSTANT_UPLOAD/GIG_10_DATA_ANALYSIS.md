## Description (copy-paste ready)

```
You have data. Spreadsheets, CSVs, databases, analytics exports. You know there are insights in there. You just can't see them because the data is messy, scattered across 6 files, and nobody has time to make sense of it.

I turn your raw data into clear answers and a dashboard you'll actually look at every morning.

THE PROCESS:

1. You send me your data (CSV, Excel, Google Sheets, database export, whatever format)
2. I clean it (remove duplicates, fix formatting, handle missing values, standardize fields)
3. I analyze it (find patterns, trends, outliers, and the 5-15 things you need to know)
4. I build a dashboard that shows your key numbers at a glance
5. I write up what the data means and what to do about it

WHAT I'VE ANALYZED:

- Sales data: "Which products actually make money and which are losing margin?" (ecommerce client found 30% of SKUs were break-even or negative)
- Marketing data: "Which channels bring paying customers vs just traffic?" (agency client cut $2K/month in wasted ad spend)
- Customer data: "Who are our best customers and what do they have in common?" (SaaS client found their ideal customer profile was wrong)
- Operations data: "Where are the bottlenecks?" (logistics client reduced delivery time 22% by fixing one step)
- Financial data: "Where is money actually going?" (startup found $800/month in duplicate subscriptions)

DASHBOARD TOOLS:

Google Sheets (free, shareable, works for most use cases). Looker Studio (free, auto-updates from Google Analytics, Ads, Sheets). Excel (if you prefer offline). Notion (if you use it for project management). The tool fits your workflow, not the other way around.

WHAT YOU GET IS NOT A PRETTY CHART:

Charts are decoration without context. Every visualization I build has: what the data shows, why it matters for your business, and what action to take. A graph going up means nothing if you don't know why it's going up or what to do about it.

Send me a sample of your data (even a screenshot of the spreadsheet) and I'll tell you in 15 minutes what insights are hiding in there.
```

## FAQs

**Q: What format should I send my data in?**
```
Anything works. CSV, Excel (.xlsx), Google Sheets link, database export, even PDFs or screenshots of tables. I'll handle the conversion and cleanup. The messier the data, the more value you get from the cleaning step.
```

**Q: I don't know what insights I'm looking for. Is that okay?**
```
That's the whole point. Most clients know they have data but don't know what questions to ask. I start with exploratory analysis: what patterns exist, what's unusual, what's trending. Then I surface the findings and let you decide which ones matter most for your business.
```

**Q: Can the dashboard update automatically?**
```
Premium includes auto-updating dashboards. If your data lives in Google Sheets, a database, or an API, the dashboard refreshes automatically (hourly, daily, or real-time depending on source). Standard dashboards are static snapshots that you update by pasting new data.
```

**Q: How much data can you handle?**
```
Up to 100,000 rows for Basic and Standard. Up to 1 million rows for Premium. If your dataset is larger, message me and I'll quote accordingly. Most business datasets are well under 100K rows.
```

**Q: Can you connect multiple data sources?**
```
Premium includes up to 5 data sources merged into one dashboard. Example: Google Analytics + Shopify sales + email marketing stats + ad spend, all in one view. I handle the merging and matching of records across sources.
```

## Requirements (what buyer provides)

```
To get started, I need:

1. Your data file(s):
   - CSV, Excel, Google Sheets link, database export
   - If multiple files, describe how they relate

2. What's in the data:
   - Brief description of what each column/field means
   - OR just tell me the general topic and I'll figure out the schema

3. What questions you want answered (if you have them):
   - "Which products are most profitable?"
   - "What's our customer retention rate?"
   - "Where is ad spend being wasted?"
   - Or: "I don't know what to look for. Just find what's interesting."

4. How you'll use the dashboard:
   - Daily check-in (need it simple, 5-second glance)
   - Weekly review (more detail, trends over time)
   - Client reporting (needs to look polished)
   - Team meeting (presentation-friendly)

5. Dashboard tool preference:
   - Google Sheets (default)
   - Looker Studio
   - Excel
   - Notion
   - No preference (I'll recommend)

6. [Premium] All data sources to connect (list tools/platforms)

7. [Premium] KPIs you track (revenue, churn, CAC, LTV, conversion rate, etc.)

8. [Premium] Who else needs dashboard access? (team members, clients)
```
