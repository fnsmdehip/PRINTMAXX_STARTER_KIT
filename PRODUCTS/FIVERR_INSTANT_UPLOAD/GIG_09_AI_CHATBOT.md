## FAQs

**Q: What AI model does the chatbot use?**
```
I use OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini for most bots (fast, cheap, good quality) with the option to upgrade to GPT-4o for more nuanced conversations. The AI only answers based on your training data. It won't make things up or go off-topic. If it doesn't know the answer, it says "I don't have that info, let me connect you with the team."
```

**Q: How much does it cost to run after setup?**
```
Most chatbots cost $5-30/month in API calls depending on conversation volume. A local business getting 100 chats/month typically spends $5-10/month. High-traffic sites (1,000+ chats/month) might spend $20-50. I'll estimate your monthly cost based on your traffic before building.
```

**Q: Can the chatbot handle bookings?**
```
Premium includes direct booking integration with Cal.com (free) or Calendly. The bot checks available times, suggests slots, and confirms the booking. No human needed. Standard and Basic can direct users to your booking page with a link.
```

**Q: What if the chatbot gives a wrong answer?**
```
The bot only answers from the data you provide. If your FAQ says a cleaning costs $150, it says $150. I include a feedback mechanism where you can flag wrong answers, and I'll update the training data. Premium includes 30 days of optimization where I monitor conversations and improve accuracy.
```

**Q: Can it connect to my CRM?**
```
Premium includes CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce, Airtable, Google Sheets, or any tool with an API). Every lead the chatbot captures gets automatically added to your CRM with conversation context. Standard sends leads via email notification.
```
