## Gig Description

Are you a small business looking at federal government contracts but overwhelmed by 200-page RFPs?

I help contractors make faster, smarter bid/no-bid decisions on SAM.gov opportunities. I read the full solicitation, break down exactly what the government wants, map your capabilities against requirements, and tell you whether to bid or walk away.

**What you get:**

- Full contract summary (agency, NAICS, set-aside, value estimate, key dates)
- Requirement breakdown in plain English (no government jargon)
- Compliance matrix (every requirement mapped to what you need to demonstrate)
- Competitive landscape analysis (who else is likely bidding)
- Risk assessment with severity ratings
- Clear BID or NO-BID recommendation with specific reasoning
- Key dates and action item timeline

**Why work with me:**

I use AI-powered analysis to process solicitation documents in minutes instead of days. I cross-reference against federal procurement databases, NAICS size standards, set-aside eligibility, and competitive intelligence. You get institutional-quality analysis at a fraction of the cost of a GovCon consultant.

**My analysis covers:**
- SAM.gov solicitations, presolicitations, sources sought, and RFIs
- All NAICS codes and PSC codes
- Small business set-asides (8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, WOSB, SBA)
- DoD, civilian agencies, and VA opportunities
- Construction, IT, services, supplies, and professional services

**Turnaround:** Basic analysis in 24 hours. Full analysis with proposal outline in 48 hours.

Send me the SAM.gov link or notice ID and I will get started.

## FAQ

**Q: What information do I need to provide?**
A: Just the SAM.gov link, notice ID, or solicitation number. I will pull all publicly available data. If you have the full RFP/solicitation document, share that too for a deeper analysis.

**Q: Do you write full proposals?**
A: This gig focuses on bid/no-bid analysis and strategy. I can outline your proposal structure and win themes. If you need a full proposal written, message me for a custom quote.

**Q: Can you help with capability statements?**
A: Yes. The Standard and Premium packages include guidance on positioning your capabilities. For a full capability statement write-up, ask about my other services.

**Q: What types of contracts do you analyze?**
A: All types posted on SAM.gov: solicitations, combined synopsis/solicitations, presolicitations, sources sought notices, and RFIs. All agencies (DoD, VA, civilian, state/local via GSA).

**Q: Do you guarantee contract wins?**
A: No one can guarantee a government contract award. What I guarantee is a thorough, data-driven analysis that saves you time and helps you make better bid decisions. My analysis identifies the specific factors that will determine the winner.

**Q: How is this different from reading the RFP myself?**
A: Three things: speed, pattern recognition, and competitive intelligence. I analyze solicitations against a database of federal procurement patterns, set-aside trends, and competitive data that would take you days to compile manually. Most contractors waste 40+ hours bidding on contracts they were never going to win. My analysis tells you in 24 hours whether your time is worth it.

**Q: I am new to government contracting. Can you help?**
A: Absolutely. My analysis includes plain-English explanations of all government procurement terms, set-aside categories, and compliance requirements. The Standard and Premium tiers include specific guidance for your situation.
