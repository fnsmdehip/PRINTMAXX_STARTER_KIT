- Rush delivery is a genuine differentiator. Most Fiverr sellers take 3-5 days.
- Unlimited revisions removes buyer risk. They pay more because they know they'll get what they want.
- You can outsource fulfillment to the same Fiverr sellers and pocket the margin.
