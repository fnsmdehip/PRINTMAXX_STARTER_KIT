## Description

One account is a bet. Five accounts is a portfolio. When one gets algorithm-nerfed, the other four keep printing.

This blueprint is the complete system for running 5+ content accounts across different niches and platforms, powered by AI content generation. Not theoretical. Not "post consistently." Specific workflows, templates, and schedules for producing content at scale while keeping your sanity.

**What's inside:**

**Niche selection matrix.** 15+ niches ranked by RPM, competition, and AI-friendliness across 3 tiers. Recommended portfolio combinations for maximum revenue, growth speed, or lifestyle fit.

**Account architecture.** Identity framework for each account. Cross-promotion rules (which accounts link to each other). Portfolio map system.

**AI content generation system.** The 3-tier approach (70% pure AI, 20% AI-assisted, 10% original). Batch generation workflow that produces a week of content for all 5 accounts in 60 minutes. Platform-specific adapters for Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Pinterest.

**Growth tactics by platform.** Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest -- each with specific posting frequency, hashtag strategy, engagement tactics, and algorithm hacks.

**Monetization stacking.** 8 revenue sources per account. Revenue source matrix showing startup time, monthly potential, and difficulty. Per-account monetization examples. Revenue timeline from month 1 ($0-100) to month 12 ($5,000-15,000).

**Daily operations.** The 2.5-hour daily schedule across all accounts. Weekly batch system. Automation layer for everything that doesn't need creative judgment.

**Security and risk management.** Anti-ban practices. The rule of thirds (no single platform or account >40% of revenue). The email list safety net.

**Scaling guide.** When to add accounts 6-10. Hiring a VA at $300-500/month. The content repurposing matrix that turns 5 pieces into 30+.
