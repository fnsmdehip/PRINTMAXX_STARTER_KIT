## Profile Title

## Hourly Rate
$75/hour (or fixed-price per analysis)

## Profile Overview

I help small businesses win federal government contracts by turning 200-page RFPs into clear bid/no-bid decisions.

Most small contractors waste 40+ hours bidding on contracts they were never positioned to win. I analyze SAM.gov opportunities in hours instead of weeks, using AI-powered document processing, federal procurement pattern matching, and competitive intelligence.

**What I deliver:**
- Bid/no-bid analysis reports (contract summary, compliance matrix, competitive landscape, risk assessment, recommendation)
- Proposal strategy and win themes
- Capability statement development
- Past performance narrative writing
- Set-aside eligibility analysis (8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, WOSB)
- Teaming partner identification

**Industries I cover:**
- IT and cybersecurity (NAICS 541511, 541512, 541519)
- Professional services (NAICS 541611, 541618, 541690)
- Construction (NAICS 236220, 237990, 238990)
- Facilities maintenance (NAICS 561210, 561720)
- Healthcare services (NAICS 621498, 621420)
- Telecommunications (NAICS 517121, 517311)
- Manufacturing and supplies

**My process:**
1. You send me the SAM.gov link or notice ID
2. I pull the full solicitation, analyze every requirement, and cross-reference against procurement patterns
3. You get a structured report with a clear recommendation and next steps
4. If you decide to bid, I provide proposal strategy and compliance guidance

**Results:** I have analyzed 50+ federal opportunities across DoD, VA, civilian agencies, and state/local contracts. My analyses help contractors focus their limited proposal resources on contracts they can actually win.
