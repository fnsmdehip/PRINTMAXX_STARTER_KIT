# Gumroad Cover Image Specs - Canva Instructions

**Dimensions:** 1280 x 720 px (16:9 ratio)
**Tool:** Canva (free tier works for all of these)
**Time per cover:** 3-5 minutes each
**Total time:** 30-45 minutes for all 13 covers

---

## Universal Design Rules

1. **Background:** Always dark (#0D0D0D or #1A1A2E)
2. **Primary text:** White (#FFFFFF), bold, large
3. **Accent text:** Teal (#00D4AA) or green (#00FF88)
4. **Font:** Use "Inter" or "DM Sans" in Canva (clean, modern, readable)
5. **No stock photos.** Icons and text only. Clean and data-forward.
6. **Text hierarchy:** One big number or statement at top, subtitle below, badge or detail in corner
7. **Leave margins:** At least 40px from all edges

---

## How to Create Each Cover in Canva

### Setup (do this once)

1. Go to **canva.com** (sign in or create free account)
2. Click **Create a design** (top right)
3. Click **Custom size**
4. Enter **1280** x **720** px
5. Click **Create new design**
6. Set background to **#0D0D0D** (click background, then the color square, type the hex code)

### For each cover below, duplicate the first design and modify it:
- Click the design name > **Make a copy**
- Change text and colors per the specs below
- Download as **PNG** (File > Download > PNG)

## Quick Canva Tips

- **Duplicate designs:** Right-click the design > Make a copy. Faster than starting from scratch.
- **Align text:** Select text > Position > Center horizontally
- **Color picker:** Click any colored element > click the color square > paste hex code
- **Transparent rectangles:** Select shape > click the checkered square icon (transparency) > slide to desired opacity
- **Download all at once:** Select all designs from home > Download > PNG > Download all
