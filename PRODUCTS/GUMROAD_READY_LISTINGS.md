## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY NOTES

- All prices end in $7 ($7, $17, $27, $37, $47, $97, $197). This is intentional. $7 outperforms $5 and $10 for digital products on Gumroad.

- PWYW minimums are set at roughly 50-60% of listed price. People who pay more feel ownership. People who pay less still convert.
