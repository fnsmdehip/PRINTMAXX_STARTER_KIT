## 1. Algorithm Mechanics (Current)

### How FYP Distribution Works

TikTok uses a multi-stage distribution system:

**Stage 1: Initial Pool (0-500 views)**
- New video shown to small test group (typically 200-500 users)
- Algorithm measures engagement signals within first 30-60 minutes
- Geographic proximity and follower overlap considered

**Stage 2: Expanded Pool (500-10K views)**
- Videos passing initial metrics pushed to larger audience
- Similar interest clusters targeted
- Engagement signals continue to be measured

**Stage 3: Viral Pool (10K+ views)**
- Top performers pushed to broader FYP
- Can reach millions if metrics sustain
- International expansion for highest performers

**Stage 4: Evergreen/Discovery**
- Strong videos resurface in Search and suggested
- Can go viral weeks or months later
- SEO/hashtag relevance becomes important

### Watch Time vs Completion Rate

**Completion Rate (Most Important)**

| Video Length | Target Completion |
|--------------|-------------------|
| < 15 sec | 90%+ |
| 15-30 sec | 80%+ |
| 30-60 sec | 60%+ |
| 60 sec+ | 40%+ |

**Key Insight:** A 15-second video watched 3 times beats a 60-second video watched 50%. Loop views signal strong engagement.

### Engagement Signals Ranked by Importance

**Tier 1: Highest Weight**
1. Completion rate / loops
2. Shares (especially to DMs)
3. Saves to collections
4. Profile visits after watching

**Tier 2: High Weight**
5. Comments (especially replies)
6. Follows from video
7. Likes
8. Time spent reading comments

**Tier 3: Moderate Weight**
9. Sound page visits
10. Hashtag exploration
11. Duet/Stitch usage

### How Content Gets Pushed to Wider Audiences

**The "Graduation" System:**
- Initial test (300 views) at 10% engagement threshold
- Expanded test (3K views) at 8% engagement threshold
- Broad push (30K views) at 6% engagement threshold
- Viral territory (100K+) at 4% engagement threshold

**Velocity Matters:** First hour performance is critical. Rapid engagement = faster escalation.

## 2. Optimal Content Specs

### Video Length Sweet Spots

| Content Type | Optimal Length | Why |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| Hook-only | 7-10 seconds | Maximum completion rate |
| Tutorial | 21-34 seconds | Value without drop-off |
| Story | 45-60 seconds | Emotional arc |
| Educational | 60-90 seconds | Depth for niche |
| Long-form | 1-3 minutes | Monetization eligible |

### Aspect Ratios
- **9:16** - Always use this for TikTok
- 1080 x 1920 minimum resolution

### Caption Strategy
- 3-5 hashtags total (1 broad, 2-3 niche)
- Short captions (< 150 chars) = highest engagement
- Hashtags matter less than 2023; content signals matter more

### Sound Strategy
- Trending sounds within 48-72 hours = highest boost
- Rising sounds (10K-100K uses) = best risk/reward
- Original audio for tutorials/voiceover

## 3. Posting Strategy

### Best Times to Post (US EST)
- Tuesday-Thursday: 10am-12pm, 7pm-9pm
- Friday: 11am-1pm
- Saturday: 9am-11am
- Sunday: 7pm-9pm

### Posting Frequency

| Account Stage | Posts/Day |
|---------------|-----------|
| New (0-1K) | 1-3 |
| Growing (1K-10K) | 2-4 |
| Established (10K-100K) | 1-3 |
| Large (100K+) | 1-2 |

### Account Warmup Protocol
- Days 1-3: Browse only, engage normally
- Days 4-7: 1 post/day, heavy engagement
- Days 8-14: Normal posting (2-3/day)
- Days 15+: Full frequency

## 4. Hook Patterns That Work

### First 3 Second Formulas
- **Direct Statement:** "I made $X doing Y."
- **Curiosity Gap:** "Nobody talks about this..."
- **Pattern Interrupt:** "Wait, this actually works."
- **Direct Address:** "If you're struggling with [problem]..."
- **Numbers/Lists:** "3 tools that replaced..."

### Loop Content
- Keep under 15 seconds
- End frame = Start frame
- 3+ loops = strong viral signal

### Thumbnail Best Practices
- Face with expression performs best
- Text overlay: 3-5 words max
- High contrast, readable at small size

## 5. Growth Tactics

### Duet/Stitch
- Gets pushed to original creator's audience
- Lower barrier than original content
- Add unique value, don't just react

### Comment Engagement
- Reply within first hour
- Pin best comment
- "Reply with video" to top questions

### Series Content
- "Day X of [journey]" format
- Post same time each day
- Creates binge behavior

### Going Live
- Direct notification to followers
- 30-60 minutes optimal
- Go live after posting (drives traffic)

## 6. Account Health

### Shadowban Signs
- Views drop 90%+ overnight
- Videos not appearing in hashtag searches
- Stuck at 200-300 views

### Recovery Protocol
- Days 1-3: No posting, normal browsing
- Days 4-7: 1 safe post/day, heavy engagement
- Days 8-14: Gradually increase posting

### What Triggers Restrictions
- Mass following/unfollowing
- VPN usage (shared IPs)
- Same content across multiple accounts
- Automation tools detected
- Fake engagement (pods, bots)

### Safe Daily Limits

| Action | Safe Limit |
|--------|------------|
| Posts | 3-5 |
| Comments | 50-100 |
| Follows | 30-50 |
| Likes | 100-200 |

## 7. Monetization

### Creativity Program Requirements
- 10,000+ followers
- 100,000+ views in last 30 days
- 18+ years old
- Original content 1+ minute
- US, UK, France, Germany, or Brazil

### Estimated RPM (2025)
- Low: $0.20-0.40 per 1K views
- Average: $0.50-1.00 per 1K views
- High: $1.00-2.00 per 1K views (US audience)

### TikTok Shop
- 1,000+ followers for Shop Tab
- 5,000+ followers for Live Shopping
- Native content performs best
- Live shopping = 10x conversion vs static

### Sponsored Content Rates

| Followers | Rate/Post |
|-----------|-----------|
| 1K-10K | $50-200 |
| 10K-50K | $200-1,000 |
| 50K-100K | $1,000-3,000 |
| 100K-500K | $3,000-10,000 |
| 500K-1M | $10,000-25,000 |
| 1M+ | $25,000+ |

## Quick Reference

### Do This
- Hook in first 0.5 seconds
- End with loop potential
- Post consistently
- Engage with comments in first hour
- Use trending sounds early
- Complete your profile

### Avoid This
- VPNs (especially shared)
- Mass follow/unfollow
- Deleting underperforming videos
- Automation for engagement
- Banned hashtags
- Copyrighted music

### Metrics That Matter
- Completion rate > 60% (30s video)
- Watch time > 1.5x video length
- Shares > 1% of views
- Saves > 2% of views
