## Instagram Growth - January 2026 State

### What's Working
1. **Reels-first strategy**: 3-5 Reels per week with trending audio and clear 3-second hooks
2. **Carousel posts**: 12% more interactions than Reels, 2.1x more than single photos
3. **Mixed-format carousels** (images + videos): Highest engagement at 2.33%
4. **Trial Reels**: Test content with non-followers before sharing to audience (built-in A/B testing)
5. **Stories with interactive features**: Polls, quizzes, Q&A stickers drive engagement
6. **1-2 hashtags** outperform posts with 20 hashtags (hashtag following being deprecated)
7. **Post 5+ times weekly**: 7.3% more engagement than less frequent posting
8. **Cross-platform repurposing**: TikTok to Reel with minor adjustments
9. **First 24-48 hours** of engagement matter most for distribution

### What's Dead/Dying
- Bot farms, follow/unfollow automation (Meta AI detection)
- Large engagement pods (detected and deprioritized)
- Hashtag stuffing (hashtag following being removed)
- Clickbait and engagement bait phrases ("What do you think?")
- Purely AI-generated content without human insight
- Overly promotional posts

### Engagement Pods in 2026
- Early forms completely nullified by Meta AI
- If used at all: must be small (5-7 members), private, near-identical niches
- Focus on "Save" and "Share" actions (more algorithmic weight than likes)
- Engagement pods damage brand partnerships (brands audit for authentic patterns)
- Follower quality > follower quantity

### Platform Detection
- Instagram "Authentic Connections" update (2025) measures quality of connection pathway
- How a follower is acquired impacts content distribution to them
- AI-driven security layers systematically detect artificial engagement
- LinkedIn also detects pod patterns and deprioritizes

## TikTok Growth - January 2026 State

### What's Working
1. **1 high-quality video per day** beats 5 low-quality posts
2. **Longer content (60-180 seconds)** with first-hour engagement optimization
3. **Problem-solution format**: Hook in first 3 seconds with clear pain point
4. **Community interaction**: Respond to comments, turn them into content
5. **Live shopping** growing in beauty, home, tech
6. **Consequence-first hooks**: What happened, not explanation

### Growth Services (Paid)
- Several services available but scrutinize for real followers vs bots
- Key evaluation: cost per follower, engagement quality, delivery safety
- TikTok engagement rate: 2.50% average (5x higher than Instagram)
- But: TikTok engagement down ~34% YoY (Rival IQ data)

### What's Dead/Dying
- Posting frequency alone driving reach
- Trending audio alone sufficient
- Generic "dance" content without value
- Algorithm hacking via hashtag tricks

## Cold Email Warmup - January 2026 State

### What's Working
1. **Dedicated warmup tools**: MailReach (best standalone), Warmup Inbox ($15/inbox budget)
2. **Built-in warmup**: Instantly, Smartlead, Saleshandy all include
3. **2-4 weeks minimum** before sending campaigns
4. **Slow, consistent activity** with real engagement beats brute-force
5. **Multi-inbox sending** for volume without burning domains
6. **ESP matching**: Sending from same provider as recipient improves deliverability

### What's Dead/Dying
- Brute-force high-volume sending
- Treating warmup as one-time checkbox
- Generic personalization (name insertion alone)
- Skipping infrastructure setup
