# TREND028: Tiger 21 (Exclusive Peer Network)

**Handle:** tiger21.com
**Niche:** Ultra-High-Net-Worth Peer Network
**Platform:** Private membership organization
**Est. MRR:** ~$3.3M ($40M annual)
**Replication Score:** 7/10
**Date Analyzed:** 2026-02-01

## What They're Doing
Tiger 21 is an exclusive peer membership group for ultra-high-net-worth individuals (min $10M liquid assets). 1,200 members pay $30,000/year for monthly peer meetings, portfolio defense sessions, and access to deal flow. No advisors/vendors allowed - pure peer learning.

## Funnel Map
Referral/word-of-mouth → Application (strict vetting) → Interview process → $30K/year membership → Monthly local chapter meetings → Portfolio defense (each member presents annually) → Deal flow sharing

## Monetization Stack
1. Annual membership fees ($30K/year) - $40M annual (primary revenue)
2. Events/retreats - Additional revenue (not disclosed)
3. No product sales/upsells - Membership model only

## Unique Mechanism
**Portfolio Defense:** Each member presents their full portfolio to the group annually. Peers challenge assumptions, offer perspectives, create accountability. This creates immense value - members report saving millions from bad decisions avoided.

**Strict exclusivity:** $10M+ liquid assets minimum. Rejection rate high. Scarcity = belonging + quality conversations.

## Replicable Frameworks
1. **Ultra-selective membership** - Quality > quantity, rejection creates desire
2. **Peer accountability model** - Portfolio defense format replicable for any niche
3. **Local chapters** - Geographic pods maintain intimacy at scale
4. **No vendor pitching** - Purity of peer learning (key differentiator)

## PRINTMAXX Application
- **Methods:** MM006 (paid communities), MM062 (fractional exec services)
- **Niche adaptation:**
  - **Faith-based founders network** - Christian entrepreneurs, $50K+ revenue minimum, $2K-5K/year membership
  - **Fitness entrepreneurs** - Gym owners, supplement brands, fitness coaches, $3K/year
  - **Tech solopreneurs** - SaaS/app builders making $10K+/mo, $2K/year

- **Timeline:** 12-18 months to build credibility for premium positioning
- **Missing infrastructure:**

  - Vetting process and criteria
  - Local chapter infrastructure (start virtual, expand to cities)

## Gap/Weakness
Requires existing high-net-worth network to seed initial members. Can't launch day 1 without credibility. Lower price point versions ($2K-5K/year vs $30K) more accessible for PRINTMAXX initial launch.

**Better PRINTMAXX path:** Launch Skool community first ($49-94/mo, 500-2K members) → identify top 50 performers → invite to exclusive mastermind tier ($2K-5K/year) → build track record → raise exclusivity/pricing over time.
