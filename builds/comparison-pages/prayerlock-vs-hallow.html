when to pick PrayerLock

you want a simple streak tracker that keeps you accountable to daily prayer. you don't need guided content -- you have your own prayer practice. you want something tiny (55KB vs 200MB+), private (no account, data stays on your phone), and free. you're not Catholic specifically and want denomination-specific tracking (Orthodox, Lutheran, Methodist, Baptist, Sikh, Buddhist, and more).

when to pick Hallow

you want a full prayer experience with guided sessions, audio content, and celebrity narrators. you're Catholic and want content specifically designed for Catholic prayer traditions (rosary, novenas, liturgy of the hours). you want community features to pray with others. $70/yr feels reasonable for what you get.

the verdict

these aren't really competitors. Hallow is a premium content platform for Catholic prayer. PrayerLock is a free accountability tool for anyone who prays. if you need guided meditation content, Hallow is worth the money. if you just want to track your prayer streak and stay consistent, PrayerLock does that for $0. many people use both -- Hallow for content, PrayerLock for the streak.
