when to pick FocusLock

you want a simple, free focus tool that blocks distracting apps during work sessions. you don't need the "nuclear lockdown" mode. you want something that works on any device without downloading a native app. you're a student or solopreneur and $99/yr is real money.

when to pick Opal

you need the Deep Focus nuclear option where you literally cannot bypass the block even by deleting the app. you want website blocking in addition to apps. you need cross-device sync so your phone and laptop stay locked simultaneously. $99/yr is pocket change for you.
