Marketing Disclosure

Last updated: 2026-02-15

Summary: We disclose material connections (sponsorships, affiliates, paid partnerships) and we label content that could reasonably be misunderstood as coming from a real person when it is generated by or features AI/virtual characters.

1) Official vs UGC vs Satire/Fiction

  • Official content: content published by SiteScore Pro or its owned accounts.
  • UGC (user-generated content): content created by third parties. If there is a material connection (paid, free product, affiliate), it should be disclosed clearly in the content itself.
  • Satire/fiction: if we publish content intended as satire or fictionalized storytelling, we label it as such where it appears.

2) Affiliate Links and Sponsored Content

Some pages/posts/emails may include affiliate links or sponsored placements. When that happens:

  • We include an in-content disclosure such as #ad, Paid partnership, or I may earn a commission (format varies by platform).
  • Disclosures should be clear and conspicuous, not hidden in a bio, footer, or after long blocks of hashtags.

3) AI / Virtual Personas and Synthetic Media

SiteScore Pro may use AI tools to create or assist with text, images, audio, video, or to operate virtual characters. If content features a virtual/AI character that could be mistaken for a real person:

  • We label the account and/or post as AI / virtual.
  • We do not impersonate real individuals or use someone’s likeness without permission.
  • We avoid “personal experience” claims that imply a real human lived the story when it is not true.

4) Testimonials and Results

  • Testimonials are presented as real customer opinions unless explicitly labeled as simulated/illustrative.
  • Results depend on many factors; results may vary.

5) Questions or Corrections

If you believe a disclosure is missing or unclear, contact us: fnsmdehip@proton.me.
