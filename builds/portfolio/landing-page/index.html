Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Six core features, zero bloat. Built for teams that ship, not teams that plan to plan.

Smart boards

Kanban, list, or timeline. Switch views instantly without losing context. Boards auto-sort by priority and deadline.

Real-time analytics

Cycle time, throughput, bottlenecks. See where work gets stuck and fix it before your sprint review, not after.

Team sync

Async standups that write themselves. Flowstack pulls status from your work. No more "what did you do yesterday?" meetings.

Inline comments

Comment directly on tasks, files, and decisions. Threaded discussions stay attached to context, not buried in Slack.

Smart notifications

AI-filtered alerts that only ping you when it matters. Batch updates for the rest. Your focus time stays protected.

API-first

Full REST and GraphQL APIs. 200+ integrations out of the box. Connect Flowstack to GitHub, Figma, Slack, and everything else.
