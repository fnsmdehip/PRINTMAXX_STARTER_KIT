Questions

How accurate is the score?

We use Google's own PageSpeed Insights API and Lighthouse engine. The same tools Google uses to evaluate your site for search rankings. The data is real, not estimated.

Do I need to install anything?

No. We scan your site externally, the same way Google does. No code changes, no plugins, no scripts to add.

What does monitoring include?

We run the full scan automatically every week and track your scores over time. If anything drops significantly, you get an email alert. Pro users also get a competitor comparison.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your dashboard or just email us.

Who is this for?

Anyone with a website. Small business owners, freelancers, agencies managing client sites, developers who want automated monitoring. If your site matters to your business, you should know when it breaks.
