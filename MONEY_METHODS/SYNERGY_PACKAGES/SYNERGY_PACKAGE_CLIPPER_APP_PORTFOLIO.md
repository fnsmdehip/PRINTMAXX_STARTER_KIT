## Why This Stack Works

Build 2-3 apps per month. Each app gets instant distribution through the clipper network testing 20 hooks simultaneously. Winners get swarm amplification across 10-20 owned accounts. Portfolio apps share a single RevenueCat backend so paywall learnings transfer across all apps.

The clipper network is the permanent distribution moat. Once built, every new app launches with 500 simultaneous content creators. Each successful app funds more clipper payments, creating a self-funding growth loop. The 30-app portfolio means even a 10% hit rate produces 3 winners.

## Implementation Sequence

### Month 1: Foundation
1. Build 3 MVP apps (PrayerLock, WalkToUnlock, FocusLock ADHD)
2. Recruit 100 clippers via Twitter DMs + Whop community
3. Offer $1/1K views base rate + bonuses for installs
4. Give each app to 30+ clippers with 5 different hook scripts
5. Set up shared RevenueCat backend across all apps

### Month 2: Validate + Scale
6. Measure which hooks and apps get traction (views, installs, conversion)
7. Double down on winner app with 200+ clippers
8. Launch 2 new apps targeting different niches
9. Start building swarm accounts (1 per niche: faith, fitness, productivity, ADHD, sleep)
10. X viral launch for winning app: "Comment FOCUS for early access"

### Month 3: Portfolio Mode
11. Scale clipper network to 300-500
12. Launch 2-3 new apps per month
13. Kill apps that don't hit 1K downloads in 30 days
14. Cross-promote from swarm accounts (faith account promotes faith app, etc.)
15. Paywall A/B test learnings from top app deployed to all others

### Month 4+: Compounding
16. Network effects kick in: clipper quality improves with experience
17. Each new app launch gets better ROI than the last
18. Revenue from winners funds expansion
19. Top clippers become brand ambassadors with revenue share

## Risk Factors and Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Clipper quality inconsistent | High | Tiered system: base rate + performance bonuses. Cut bottom 20% monthly |
| Platform bans on coordinated accounts | Medium | GoLogin + SOAX for fingerprint/IP separation. Stay under 100 actions/day/account |
| App Store rejection cycle | Medium | Use template architecture so rejections get fixed once across all apps |
| Revenue doesn't cover clipper costs early on | High | Start with 100 clippers at $500/mo budget. Scale only after first winner validates |
