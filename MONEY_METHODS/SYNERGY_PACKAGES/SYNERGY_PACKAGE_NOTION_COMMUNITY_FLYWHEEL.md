## Why This Stack Works

Free Notion templates capture emails through a quality-first lead magnet that demonstrates competence AND creates lock-in (users build their business on your templates). The newsletter sends 15-30 days of pure value, then executes flash sales for premium template bundles and community access.

Templates go viral on X/Reddit ("here's my $0 business operating system"), driving more email captures. The community retention is powered by new monthly template drops. The flywheel: template virality drives signups, signups drive newsletter authority, authority drives flash sale revenue, revenue funds better templates.

Zero code required. Zero fulfillment cost. Highest implementation score (25/25) in the entire synergy database.

## Implementation Sequence

### Week 1: Create Templates (Zero Cost)
1. Build 5 Notion templates: Business Dashboard, Content Calendar, Revenue Tracker, Project Manager, Simple CRM
2. List for free on Whop with email capture gate
3. Post template screenshots on X ("here's my $0 solopreneur operating system")
4. Post on Reddit: r/Notion (2M+ members), r/SideProject, r/EntrepreneurRideAlong

### Week 2: Newsletter Setup
5. Beehiiv account (free tier to 2.5K subscribers)
6. Build 15-email welcome sequence about running a micro-business
7. Each email includes one actionable framework + template recommendation
8. No selling in first 15 emails. Pure value.

### Week 3: First Flash Sale
9. At 200+ subscribers, create premium template bundle ($27-$97)
10. Premium bundle: everything in free tier + Revenue Projector, Client Pipeline, Tax Tracker
11. Execute flash sale: 2 emails in 48 hours, countdown timer, limited bonus
12. Track conversion rate, revenue, unsubscribe rate

### Week 4: Community Launch
13. Offer Skool community ($29/mo) to flash sale buyers as immediate upsell
14. Community value: monthly template drops, live Q&A, peer networking
15. Add monthly template drop schedule (new template every 2 weeks)
16. Create career pivot template sub-pack for N029 audience
