## Risk Mitigation

### Platform Risk:
- **TikTok Rewards changes:** Diversify to Instagram Reels (FB Bonus), YouTube Shorts (Partner Program)
- **TikTok Shop changes:** Have backup affiliate programs (Amazon Associates, LTK)
- **Account bans:** Use clipper's personal accounts, not centralized. 500 accounts = distributed risk.

### Clipper Risk:
- **Low quality:** QA review before publish, reject <50% of submissions initially
- **Copyright violations:** Brief clippers on music/footage rules, use royalty-free only
- **Ghosting:** Overhire 2x needed clippers, expect 50% to drop off

### Product Risk:
- **Low conversion:** Test 20 products minimum, kill bottom 50% monthly
- **Commission cuts:** Negotiate direct deals with sellers after proving volume
- **Saturation:** Rotate products monthly, find new trending items
