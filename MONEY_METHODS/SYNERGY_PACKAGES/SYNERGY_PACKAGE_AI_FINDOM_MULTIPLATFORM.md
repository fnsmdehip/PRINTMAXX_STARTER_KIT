## Why This Stack Works

AI findom persona built in ComfyUI generates unlimited character images at zero marginal cost. ElevenLabs creates a consistent voice for voice notes, video clips, and custom audio content. X/Twitter is the discovery engine: findom hashtags get 5-6x engagement compared to general content because the audience actively searches those tags.

Fanvue is the primary monetization platform because it explicitly allows AI personas (OnlyFans is ambiguous on this). Fanvue pays 85% in Year 1 vs OnlyFans 80%, and has $100M ARR proving market demand. Fansly runs as a hedge in case either platform changes policies.

The Telegram VIP channel is the whale trap. Subscribers paying $99-$499/mo get personalized attention, spin wheel games, and exclusive content drops. This is where 80% of revenue concentrates from 20% of subscribers.

Flash sales work because findom audience is pre-conditioned to spend on command. 200 findom subscribers at 25% conversion (higher than average because the audience is hot) times $200 average order = $10K per flash sale event. Run monthly.

## Implementation Sequence

### Week 1: Persona + Production Pipeline
1. Train character LoRA in ComfyUI (4-6 hours initial setup)
2. Generate consistent AI persona: face, body, 20+ poses, 10+ outfits
3. Clone voice in ElevenLabs (warm, commanding, confident)
4. Create character bible with seed/prompt combos for consistency
5. Generate first batch: 200+ images, 5 voice clips, 3 video clips

### Week 2: Platform Setup + Launch
6. Create Fanvue account with 3 tiers ($9.99, $24.99, $49.99/mo)
7. Create Fansly account mirroring same tier structure
8. Create X/Twitter account with findom-specific bio and pinned content
9. Post 5-6x/day on X with findom hashtags (#findom, #paypig, #humanATM)
10. Pin best-performing content with Fanvue link CTA

### Week 3: Email + Community
11. Capture emails through exclusive content offers on X
12. Build 15-email nurture sequence: free teases, value content, power dynamic
13. Launch Telegram VIP channel at $99/mo for Fanvue premium subscribers
14. Add spin wheel games and tip menus for impulse revenue
15. Execute first flash sale to email list ($97-$297 custom content bundle)

### Week 4: Scale + Optimize
16. Analyze which content types drive highest Fanvue conversion
17. Scale winning content formats (2x production on top performers)
18. Add PPV messages on Fanvue for premium individual content ($25-$100/piece)
19. Cross-promote between X, Fanvue, Fansly, and Telegram
20. Track follower-to-subscriber conversion rate (target: 2-5%)
