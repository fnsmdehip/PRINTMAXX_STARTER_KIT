## Why This Stack Works

PrayerLock is a screen time blocker that requires completing a prayer to unlock your phone. The $600K/mo Opal model (ALPHA011) proves the screen blocker category. Duolingo-style gamification (ALPHA038) applied to prayer streaks creates compounding engagement that faith audiences already respond to (devotional plans, accountability groups).

The newsletter captures churned users and brings them back. Prayer data feeds personalized push notifications timed to prayer schedules. Every component feeds the next: app generates content for newsletter, newsletter recovers churned users, retained users create word-of-mouth, word-of-mouth drives installs.

Remove any one component and the stack loses 30-50% of its value.

## Implementation Sequence

### Week 1: MVP + Paywall
1. Build PrayerLock MVP with hard paywall
2. Implement animated paywall (ALPHA032: 2.9x conversion)
3. Default to annual plan pricing: $6.99/mo billed $49.99/yr
4. Trial only available on annual plan (ALPHA034)
5. Set up RevenueCat for subscription management

### Week 2: Gamification Layer
6. Add prayer streak counter (consecutive days)
7. Add XP system for prayers completed
8. Daily rewards for opening the app
9. Milestone celebrations at 7, 30, 90 days
10. Weekly leaderboards within prayer groups (optional social feature)

### Week 3: Retention Infrastructure
11. Push notification system timed to prayer schedule (ALPHA042)
12. Personalized reminders: "You've prayed 14 days straight. Don't break the streak."
13. Launch Beehiiv newsletter capturing email during onboarding
14. First 5 newsletter issues: faith encouragement + app tips

### Week 4: Content + Growth
15. TikTok content: streak screenshots + faith motivation
16. Create 20 carousel posts for Instagram (ADHD content format works for faith too)
17. Reddit posts in r/Christianity, r/prayer
18. First flash sale attempt on newsletter list at Day 30
