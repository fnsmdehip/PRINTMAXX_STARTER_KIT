## Why This Stack Works

Sleep content on YouTube is a compounding asset. One "10 Hours Rain Sounds for Deep Sleep" video generates views for years. There are 2.4 billion sleep-related searches on YouTube annually. The content costs near-zero to produce with AI audio tools.

The ambient YouTube channels (rain, ocean, forest, binaural beats) serve as the top-of-funnel. Every video description links to a sleep tracker app with affiliate code. The app uses hard paywall with annual plan default (ALPHA034: 44% annual retention, which is 2.6x better than monthly subscribers).

ElevenLabs adds a narrated sleep story layer. "10 Hours Rain Sounds" is one format. "Guided Sleep Meditation with Rain" is another format that commands higher CPM ($5-12 in the meditation niche vs $3-5 for pure ambient). The narrator voice is consistent across all content, building brand recognition.

Long-form videos (8+ hours) are particularly valuable because YouTube auto-places mid-roll ads. An 8-hour video gets 4-8 ad impressions per view. At $5-12 CPM, a video with 100K monthly views generates $500-$1,200/mo from ads alone, indefinitely.

The sleep app captures users who want active tracking beyond just listening. Bedtime lock feature (screen blocker for sleep schedule), sleep quality tracking, and ambient sound library. Mattress and supplement affiliates add secondary revenue at 5-15% commission on $200-$1,000 products.

## Implementation Sequence

### Week 1: Channel + Content
1. Create YouTube channel for sleep/ambient content (optimize name, art, description)
2. Generate 10 AI ambient audio tracks (rain + music, ocean waves, forest crickets, lo-fi sleep)
3. Create 10 ambient videos using AI audio + stock footage/generated visuals
4. Upload 5 videos with SEO-optimized titles ("10 Hours Rain Sounds for Deep Sleep 2026")
5. Set up ElevenLabs narrator voice for guided meditation content

### Week 2: Narrated Content + App
6. Produce 5 narrated sleep stories using ElevenLabs (8+ hours each)
7. Build or affiliate with sleep tracker app (annual plan $49.99/yr)
8. Add affiliate links in every video description + pinned comment
9. Upload remaining 5 ambient videos + 3 narrated videos
10. Apply for YouTube Partner Program (need 1K subs + 4K watch hours)

### Week 3: Scale + Monetize
11. Scale to 3 new videos per week (mix ambient and narrated)
12. Add mattress affiliate links (Casper, Purple pay 5-10% on $1K+ AOV)
13. Add supplement affiliate links (melatonin, magnesium brands)
14. Create playlist structures: "Rain Sounds," "Sleep Stories," "Deep Sleep Music"
15. Cross-post 30-second clips to YouTube Shorts for discovery

### Week 4: App Integration
16. Build sleep app MVP with bedtime lock + sleep tracking + ambient library
17. Hard paywall: $4.99/mo or $49.99/yr (annual default per ALPHA034)
18. Integrate app's sound library with YouTube channel content
19. Push notification at bedtime: "Time to wind down" (ALPHA042: 3x retention)
20. Track which YouTube content drives most app installs
