## 2. SCOPE OF WORK

This SOW is governed by the terms of the Master Services Agreement referenced above. All capitalized terms not defined herein have the meanings set forth in the MSA.

### Package: Signal Map — $1,500

*A diagnostic assessment of the client's current operations to identify automation opportunities, quick wins, and a prioritized implementation roadmap.*

**Deliverables:**

1. **Operations Audit Report** — Documented review of current workflows, tools, and communication patterns. Includes identification of manual bottlenecks and data entry redundancies.
2. **Automation Opportunity Map** — Prioritized list of automation candidates ranked by estimated ROI, implementation complexity, and risk.
3. **Tool & Integration Recommendations** — Recommended technology stack with cost estimates, integration requirements, and vendor comparisons.
4. **90-Day Implementation Roadmap** — Phased action plan with timelines, dependencies, resource requirements, and projected savings.

---

### Package: Phone Pilot — $3,500

*Implementation of an AI-powered phone system handling inbound calls, appointment scheduling, and basic customer inquiries.*

**Deliverables:**

1. **AI Phone Agent Configuration** — Fully configured AI phone agent with custom greeting, call routing logic, and FAQ responses tailored to Client's business.
2. **Appointment Scheduling Integration** — Two-way calendar sync with Client's scheduling system (e.g., Calendly, Google Calendar, practice management software).
3. **Call Flow Documentation** — Documented call scripts, decision trees, and escalation procedures for all anticipated call scenarios.
4. **Testing & QA Report** — Results of test calls across scenarios including edge cases, with pass/fail documentation and resolution notes.
5. **Runbook & Training Guide** — Operational guide for Client's staff covering: system monitoring, common issues, escalation procedures, and how to update call scripts.

---

### Package: Ops Pilot — $4,500

*End-to-end automation of a core operational workflow, from trigger to completion, with monitoring and human-in-loop checkpoints.*

**Deliverables:**

1. **Workflow Automation Build** — Fully functional automation of the agreed-upon workflow using [n8n / Make / Zapier / custom], including error handling, retry logic, and logging.
2. **Integration Setup** — Connected integrations across Client's tool stack (CRM, email, calendar, billing, etc.) with authenticated API connections and data mapping.
3. **Monitoring Dashboard** — Real-time dashboard showing automation status, success/failure rates, processing volumes, and alert notifications.
4. **Testing & Validation Report** — End-to-end testing results with sample data, edge case analysis, and performance benchmarks.
5. **Runbook & Training Guide** — Comprehensive operations guide covering: daily monitoring checklist, troubleshooting procedures, escalation contacts, and modification instructions.

---

### Package: Managed Ops — $[1,500-3,000]/month

*Ongoing management, monitoring, and optimization of deployed automations with monthly reporting and continuous improvement.*

**Monthly Deliverables:**

1. **System Monitoring & Maintenance** — Daily monitoring of all deployed automations. Error detection, alerting, and resolution within [SLA_HOURS] business hours.
2. **Monthly Performance Report** — Metrics dashboard covering: uptime, automation success rates, volume processed, errors resolved, and time/cost savings.
3. **Optimization Recommendations** — Proactive identification of improvement opportunities based on performance data and emerging tools.
4. **Support & Troubleshooting** — Up to [SUPPORT_HOURS] hours of reactive support per month for automation issues, user questions, and minor adjustments.
5. **Quarterly Business Review** — Scheduled review of automation performance, ROI analysis, and roadmap planning for next quarter.

## 5. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

### 5.1 Deliverable Review

Client shall have five (5) business days from the date of delivery to review each deliverable and provide written acceptance or a detailed list of deficiencies ("Review Period").

### 5.2 Deemed Acceptance

If Client does not provide written notice of deficiencies within the Review Period, the deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

### 5.3 Deficiency Resolution

If Client provides a timely list of deficiencies, EAS shall use commercially reasonable efforts to address the identified deficiencies within five (5) business days. A second Review Period of three (3) business days shall apply to revised deliverables.

### 5.4 Acceptance Standard

Acceptance shall not be unreasonably withheld. Deliverables that substantially conform to the specifications in this SOW shall be deemed acceptable. Minor cosmetic issues or feature requests beyond the original scope do not constitute valid grounds for rejection.
