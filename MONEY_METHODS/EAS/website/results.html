HVAC and plumbing Autonomous Dispatch Agent Tier 3
Job cycle time cut 40%. Invoicing went from 5-day lag to same-day. System paid for itself in under 5 months.
Before the agent

Regional HVAC company running 40+ service calls daily. Dispatchers manually entering job details into 3 different systems. Invoicing lagged 4-5 days behind job completion. Monthly reconciliation errors costing $4,200/month in missed billings.

What we installed (5 weeks, contract to live)

Multi-agent system: dispatch coordinator agent routes jobs based on tech location, skill, and load. Job completion agent auto-generates invoices, updates inventory, and syncs CRM. Intelligence agent monitors for scheduling conflicts, parts shortages, and revenue anomalies.

B2B marketing agency AI Meeting Brain Tier 1
98% of meeting decisions captured and tracked. Weekly status meetings cut from 5 hours to 1.5.
Before the agent

22-person agency with 3 partners spending 5+ hours per week across 8 recurring status meetings. Decisions made in meetings but never tracked. Action items buried in Slack threads. Nobody knew what was decided last week, so they'd meet again to re-decide.

What we installed (10 days, contract to live)

AI Meeting Brain: auto-joins all Zoom calls, transcribes with speaker identification, extracts every decision, action item, owner, and deadline. Syncs to Notion decision log. Daily Slack digest with yesterday's decisions and due items. Weekly leadership summary with unresolved items.

After 30 days

98% of action items now tracked with assigned owners and deadlines. Weekly status meetings dropped from 5 hours to 1.5 hours. Partners report actually knowing what's happening across all 14 active projects for the first time. Zero decisions lost between meetings.
