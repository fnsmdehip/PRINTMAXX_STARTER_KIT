AI Receptionist

Install an AI receptionist in 10 days

Step-by-step guide to deploying a conversational voice AI that answers every call, qualifies leads, and books appointments directly into your calendar. This is the exact process we use for dental offices, law firms, and home service companies. Covers platform selection, conversation design, integration architecture, testing, and go-live.

10-day install timeline

Days 1-2
Call audit. Record 50+ real calls. Map conversation patterns, common questions, booking rules.
Days 3-4
Build conversation flows in Vapi/Bland AI. Script 12-15 conversation branches. Configure voice persona.
Days 5-6
Integrate with calendar, CRM, and practice management. Build call logging and daily digest pipeline.
Days 7-8
QA testing. 47 test scenarios. Edge cases. Hostile callers. Silence handling. Transfer logic.
Days 9-10
Parallel run (AI + human). Measure accuracy. Tune confidence thresholds. Go live.

What the playbook covers

  • Vapi vs Bland AI vs Retell comparison (scored by latency, voice quality, cost)
  • Conversation design templates for 4 industries
  • Integration architecture diagrams (CRM, calendar, PMS)
  • QA testing checklist (47 scenarios including edge cases)
  • Confidence threshold tuning guide
  • Go-live runbook with rollback plan
  • Cost model: per-minute pricing at different call volumes
  • Staff training deck for the handoff

Intelligence System

Build a competitive intelligence pipeline

How to set up an autonomous system that monitors your competitors, scores every signal, and delivers actionable digests daily. This is the same architecture we use internally to monitor 200+ sources across multiple industries. Covers source selection, scraping infrastructure, AI scoring, alert triggers, and RAG knowledge base setup.

3-week build timeline

Week 1
Source mapping. Identify 50-200 monitoring targets: competitor sites, forums, social accounts, job boards, SEC filings.
Week 2
Build scraping infrastructure (Playwright + n8n). AI scoring pipeline. Alert threshold configuration. Daily digest template.
Week 3
RAG knowledge base setup (Pinecone). Historical data backfill. Testing and tuning. Go-live with team training.

What the playbook covers

  • Source taxonomy: 8 categories of competitive signals
  • Scraping architecture: Playwright + n8n + proxy rotation
  • AI scoring framework: opportunity vs threat vs neutral
  • Alert trigger rules (price changes, new hires, product launches)
  • RAG setup with Pinecone: making your intel searchable by AI
  • Daily digest format and delivery (Slack, email, or both)
  • Self-healing patterns: what to do when a source changes structure
  • Cost model at different monitoring scales
