Our approach

How we build and why it matters.

Agents, not workflows

A workflow fires when triggered and follows a script. An agent wakes up, assesses its environment, makes decisions, takes action, and logs what it did. Your AI receptionist doesn't follow a phone tree. It has a real conversation, qualifies the caller, checks your calendar in real time, and books the appointment. It runs continuously without supervision.

Self-healing infrastructure

Every agent has three-level circuit breakers: per-action, per-agent, and system-wide. If an external dependency fails, the system reroutes, retries with exponential backoff, and switches to a fallback. If the fallback fails, it pauses that function and continues operating on everything else. When the dependency recovers, the agent resumes automatically. You never restart anything manually.

Your data, your infrastructure

We deploy wherever your security requirements dictate. Cloud-hosted, private cloud on your AWS account, or fully air-gapped on your own hardware. No vendor lock-in. No monthly platform fees to us. You own the code, the data, and the agents. If you fire us tomorrow, everything keeps running.

We run this internally

We operate 30+ autonomous AI agents in our own business, every day. Content generation, lead qualification, competitive monitoring, system health checks, decision logging. Every agent we build for clients is a version of something we already run at production scale. The architecture is tested on our own revenue before it touches yours.

Data security and deployment

Four deployment models. You choose based on your compliance requirements.

For financial firms, legal practices, healthcare organizations, and any business with strict data residency requirements, we deploy on your terms.

Standard

Cloud-hosted

Encrypted infrastructure on our managed servers. SOC 2 compliant. Data isolated per client. Lowest cost option for businesses without strict data residency rules.

Private

Your cloud account

Agents run on dedicated instances in your AWS, GCP, or Azure account. You control access, logging, and data retention. We manage the agents, you own the infrastructure.

Air-gapped

On-premise

Open-source models running on your hardware. Data never leaves your network. No external API calls. Built for HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II, FINRA/SEC, and FedRAMP compliance requirements.

Hybrid

Split architecture

Reasoning queries go to cloud models for maximum capability. Your data stays on-prem. The two never mix. The AI processes the question without accessing the underlying data directly.

Common questions

Things people ask before installing their first agent.

How is this different from a traditional automation firm? +

Traditional automation connects apps. When the trigger fires, the workflow runs. Our agents don't wait for triggers. They wake up on a schedule, assess their environment, make decisions, take action, and log results. Your AI receptionist has a real conversation, qualifies the caller, checks your calendar, and books the appointment. A workflow follows a script. An agent exercises judgment.

What does it cost, and what are the ongoing fees? +

Agent installs range from $1,500 to $4,500 depending on scope and complexity. These are one-time fixed fees. You pay when the agent is live and working. Ongoing costs are your own infrastructure (cloud compute, API keys) and optional managed support if you want us to monitor and evolve the agents over time. No monthly platform fees to EAS. Full pricing is on our packages page.

How fast until we see results? +

Signal map takes 3-5 days. Agent install takes 10-14 days. Your agent is live and working within 3 weeks of signing. Most clients see measurable impact in the first week of the agent running. AI receptionist clients typically see 40-60% fewer missed calls in week one. Business DNA Analysis takes 30 days of observation before we build, but the agents that come out of it are significantly more accurate because they're built on real behavioral data.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake? +

Every agent has guardrails and escalation paths. We build confidence thresholds into every decision point. If the agent is 90%+ confident, it acts and logs. Below that, it routes to a human with full context. The AI receptionist transfers calls it cannot handle. The support agent escalates tickets beyond its knowledge base. The lead machine flags edge cases for review. Every action is logged and auditable.

What about data security? Our industry has strict compliance requirements. +

We offer four deployment models: cloud-hosted (encrypted, SOC 2 compliant), private cloud (your AWS/GCP/Azure account), fully air-gapped on-premise (data never leaves your network), and hybrid (cloud reasoning, on-prem data). For finance, healthcare, legal, and defense clients, we deploy open-source models locally. Your proprietary data never touches an external API. We sign mutual NDAs before any deep-dive engagement.

Can you build agents for use cases not listed on your site? +

Yes. The agents on this site are common starting points, not the full menu. If a human does it on a schedule, follows a process, and makes decisions based on data, we can build an agent for it. We've built agents for financial reconciliation, regulatory compliance monitoring, supply chain coordination, and recruiting pipelines. If you can describe the workflow, we can scope an agent.

Do we need to change our existing tools? +

No. Our agents connect to your existing stack via standard protocols. If you use HubSpot, the agent logs to HubSpot. Google Calendar, it books there. Slack, it sends digests to Slack. We adapt to your tools. The only new infrastructure is the AI agent itself and the orchestration layer that powers it.
