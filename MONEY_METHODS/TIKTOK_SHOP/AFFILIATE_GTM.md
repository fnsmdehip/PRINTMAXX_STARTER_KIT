### Day 3-7: Content Creation

**Content Formats That Convert:**

1. **Honest Review**
   - "I tried [product] for 30 days..."
   - Show real results, not fake hype
   - 60-90 seconds

2. **Get Ready With Me (GRWM)**
   - Natural product integration
   - "My morning routine includes..."
   - 30-60 seconds

3. **Comparison**
   - "Dupe vs original"
   - "Best [category] under $20"
   - 45-90 seconds

4. **Problem/Solution**
   - "If you struggle with [X], try this"
   - Direct pain point → product
   - 15-30 seconds

5. **Aesthetic/Lifestyle**
   - Product in beautiful setting
   - Minimal talking, vibes
   - 15-30 seconds
