## Sending tips

- **Volume:** 20-30 personalized emails per day. Not 200 generic ones.
- **Personalization:** Run `personalize_template.py` for each business so the demo URL shows THEIR name, phone, and address.
- **Hosting demos:** Use Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages (free tier). Each demo gets a unique URL like `demo-{business-slug}.vercel.app`.
- **Follow-up cadence:** Day 0 (initial), Day 3 (follow-up), Day 7 (final). Stop after 3 emails.
- **Best send times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am local time. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons.
- **Subject line A/B test:** "I built this for [Business Name]" vs "Your website is costing you customers" vs "I searched for [Business Name] online."
- **Response rate benchmark:** 5-10% reply rate on cold. 15-25% with a personalized demo link.
- **Close rate:** 20-30% of replies convert to paid clients when you have a demo ready.

## Objection handling

**"Too expensive"** - Your competitors pay $200-500/mo for worse. I'm giving you a $5,000 site for $500.

**"I already have a website"** - Open it on your phone. Does it load in 2 seconds? Is there a click-to-call button? If no, you're losing customers.

**"I need to think about it"** - Totally fair. The demo will be live for 7 days. Take a look and let me know.

**"Can you make changes?"** - Absolutely. The $50/month includes one design change per month. Bigger changes are $100-200 depending on scope.

**"How do I know it works?"** - Look at it right now on your phone. That's how your customers will see it. Does it look good? That's how you know.
