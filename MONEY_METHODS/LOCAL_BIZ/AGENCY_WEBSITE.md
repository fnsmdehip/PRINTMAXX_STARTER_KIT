## Trust signals (critical for conversion)

### Must-have trust signals

1. **LLC registration.** File in your state. Costs $50-200. Takes 1-3 days online. Shows on secretary of state website. "WebCraft Digital LLC" sounds real.

2. **Phone number.** Get a dedicated business number via Google Voice (free) or OpenPhone ($15/mo). Display prominently on every page. Local area code preferred.

3. **Physical address.** Use a virtual office service ($20-50/mo) or just your city/state without street address. "Austin, TX" is enough. Don't use a PO Box.

4. **Professional email.** hello@webcraftdigital.com (not gmail). Already handled by Google Workspace setup in cold email checklist.

5. **SSL certificate.** Obviously. Free via Let's Encrypt or Cloudflare.

6. **Privacy policy and terms.** Use a generator (Termly, PrivacyPolicies.com). Free. Takes 5 minutes.

7. **Google Business Profile.** Create one. Even for a service-area business. Gets you in local search and adds legitimacy.

### Nice-to-have trust signals

8. **BBB listing.** $300-500/year. Worth it for closing larger deals. Adds "BBB Accredited" badge.

9. **Industry certifications.** Google Partner badge (free after cert). HubSpot certified (free). Shows competence.

10. **Case study PDFs.** Downloadable before/after case studies. Build after first 3 clients.

11. **Blog.** 2-4 articles about local business websites, SEO tips, website checklist. Adds SEO value and shows expertise.

12. **Social proof widgets.** Embed Google reviews, Clutch reviews, or Trustpilot. Even 5-10 reviews helps.

## Technical spec

### Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js or plain HTML + Tailwind (fastest to deploy)
- **Hosting:** Vercel (free tier, auto-SSL, global CDN)
- **Domain:** webcraftdigital.com (or chosen name)
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Plausible (privacy-friendly backup)
- **Forms:** Formspree (free tier, 50 submissions/month) or Google Forms embedded
- **Chat:** Tawk.to (free live chat widget) or Crisp (free tier)

### Performance targets

- Lighthouse score: 95+
- LCP: <1.5s
- Mobile-friendly: 100%
- Page weight: <500KB

### Pages

1. **Homepage** (wireframe above)
2. **About** (team, story, approach)
3. **Portfolio** (6 template demos, linked)
4. **Pricing** (3 tiers, clear comparison)
5. **Contact** (form, phone, email, Calendly embed)
6. **Blog** (placeholder, add articles later)
7. **Privacy Policy** (generated)
8. **Terms of Service** (generated)

### Build time estimate

- Design + develop: 4-6 hours (using Tailwind templates)
- Content writing: 1-2 hours
- Deploy to Vercel: 15 minutes
- DNS setup: 30 minutes
- **Total: 6-9 hours**
