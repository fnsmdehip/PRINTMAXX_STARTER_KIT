Our Story

Cooked Over Fire. Sourced From Here.

Chef Marcus Torres spent 12 years in Michelin-starred kitchens before returning home with a different vision: extraordinary food, zero pretension.

Ember & Oak opened in 2019 in a converted 1920s firehouse. We cook over wood and charcoal, source from 14 local farms, and change our menu every week.

No food miles. No freezers. No shortcuts.
