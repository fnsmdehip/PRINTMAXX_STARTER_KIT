### RANK 2: SEMrush (SEO/Marketing Suite)

**Commission:** $200 per sale (Pro plan), up to $350-$450 per sale (Guru/Business, depends on partner tier), plus $10 per free trial activation
**Cookie Duration:** 120 days (best in class, 4x industry standard of 30 days)
**Signup URL:** https://www.semrush.com/lp/affiliate-program/en/ (via Impact.com)
**Payout:** Monthly via Impact.com
**Plan Prices:** $139.95/mo (Pro), $249.95/mo (Guru), $499.95/mo (Business)
**Signup Friction:** Medium. Requires Impact.com account. Application review takes 1-3 business days.

**Why #2 for our audience:**
The 120-day cookie is the single most underexploited feature in SaaS affiliate marketing. Someone clicks your link in January, buys in April, you get paid. Most affiliates chase recurring commissions and overlook SEMrush because it is one-time per sale. But at $200-$450 per conversion, one sale per month beats most recurring programs for the first 6-12 months. Solopreneurs researching SEO tools are high-intent buyers comparing before purchasing.

**Economics (12-month projection):**
- Base: 1 sale/mo + 3 trials = $230/mo = $2,760/yr
- Bull: 4 sales/mo = $800+/mo = $10,560/yr
- Bear: 1 sale every 3 months + trials = $800+/yr

**Existing PRINTMAXX assets:**
- semrush-vs-ahrefs.surge.sh (LIVE, needs real affiliate ID)
- pagescorer-vs-gtmetrix comparison page exists

**Arbitrage angle:** Most affiliate marketers sleep on one-time bounties in favor of recurring. But $200+ per sale with a 120-day window means every single mention of SEMrush in content, emails, or tool stack pages has an abnormally long conversion tail. This is mispriced.

## WHAT IS MISPRICED (Arbitrage Opportunities)

1. **SEMrush's 120-day cookie is radically underexploited.** Most affiliates ignore one-time bounties. But $200-$450 per sale with a 4-month attribution window means any SEO content you publish today can convert traffic from August. The expected value per click is higher than most 30% recurring programs when you model it over 12 months with typical churn rates.

2. **Smartlead's lifetime recurring + 90-day cookie is the best pure economics in cold email.** Instantly gets more search volume but pays less per referral long-term. Saleshandy pays 25% lifetime with 90-day cookie. Per-click, Saleshandy and Smartlead both generate more expected value than Instantly. Promote all three in comparison content -- the reader picks their preference, you get paid regardless.

3. **Beehiiv's 50% rate is temporarily generous.** They are buying market share. This rate will decrease. Lock in referrals now while the commission is high. Every referral you get in 2026 pays 50% for a full year.

4. **Kit's lifetime tail is invisible alpha.** Most comparison articles rank Beehiiv above Kit because 50% > 30% (or they cite the old 30% figure). But Kit's 10-20% lifetime tail after year 1 means a Kit referral who stays 3+ years generates more total commission than a Beehiiv referral. The market is mispricing Kit.

5. **Comparison pages are the highest-intent affiliate content.** Someone searching "instantly vs smartlead" or "semrush vs ahrefs" has already decided to buy and is choosing between options. PRINTMAXX already has 8 comparison pages LIVE on surge.sh. These are the most valuable pages in the portfolio and they all have placeholder affiliate IDs. Swapping in real IDs is the single highest-ROI action available right now.
