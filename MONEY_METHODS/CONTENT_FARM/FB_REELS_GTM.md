## Content Optimization for FB Reels

### What Performs on FB (Different from TikTok!)

| Element | TikTok | FB Reels |
|---------|--------|----------|
| **Length** | 15-60s | 30-90s (longer OK) |
| **Audience** | Gen Z | Millennials, Gen X, Boomers |
| **Tone** | Trendy, fast | Relatable, shareable |
| **Topics** | Trends, dance | Family, nostalgia, life hacks |
| **Hooks** | Pattern interrupt | Emotional, curiosity |

### FB-Specific Optimizations

1. **Add captions** - FB is often watched muted
2. **Slower pace** - Older audience, don't cut as fast
3. **Relatable > trendy** - "Things only [X] understand"
4. **Family-friendly** - More shareable to family members
5. **Nostalgia** - "Remember when..." performs well

### Hashtag Strategy

```
#reels #fbreels #viral #foryou #trending
#[niche] #[topic] #relatable #fyp
```

Use 5-10 hashtags max. FB algorithm prefers fewer than TikTok.
