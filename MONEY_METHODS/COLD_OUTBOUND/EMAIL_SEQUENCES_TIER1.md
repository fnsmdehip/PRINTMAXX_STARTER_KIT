## Critical 2026 Cold Email Meta (Read First)

**From ALPHA751-754 research:**

1. **Reply Rate Benchmarks (Instantly.ai 2026):**
   - Elite teams: >10% reply rate
   - Average: 3.43% reply
   - Top quartile: 5.5%
   - **58% of replies come from Email 1** (optimize first email aggressively)

2. **Precision Over Volume (2026 shift):**
   - ESPs now track: time reading, reply depth, conversation length
   - Relevance is the real deliverability hack
   - 30-80 emails/day per warmed mailbox (scale horizontally not vertically)

3. **Anti-AI Detection:**
   - Too polished = AI flag
   - Casual beats perfect
   - Under 125 words performs best
   - NO tracking pixels (Gmail warns users 2026)

4. **Warmup Requirements:**
   - 14-21 days minimum (was 7-14 in 2025)
   - Subdomain for cold (protect main domain)
   - Engagement signals > open rates

5. **Intent-Based Timing:**
   - 2-4x reply rate vs spray-and-pray
   - Business context personalization > first name tokens

## Implementation Notes

### Sending Infrastructure (ALPHA752)

**Per Mailbox Limits:**
- Days 1-7: 10-15 emails/day
- Days 8-14: 20-30 emails/day
- Days 15-21: 30-50 emails/day
- Days 22+: 50-80 emails/day MAX

**Scale Horizontally:**
- 1 mailbox = 80 emails/day max
- Want 500/day? = 7 warmed mailboxes
- NEVER push one inbox to 200/day (instant spam folder)

### Personalization Tokens

**Minimum Required:**
- {{FIRST_NAME}}
- {{COMPANY_NAME}} or {{PRACTICE_NAME}} or {{BROKERAGE_NAME}}

**High-Impact Optional:**
- {{CITY}}
- {{PAIN_POINT}} (identified from website/linkedin)
- {{CURRENT_TOOL}} (what they use now)

### A/B Testing Priority (ALPHA751)

**Test in this order:**
1. **Subject line** (biggest impact on open rate)
2. **Email 1 body** (58% of replies from first email)
3. **CTA type** (calendly vs reply vs loom)
4. **Send time** (Tuesday-Thursday 9-11am performs best)

### Tracking Setup (ALPHA754)

**DO NOT USE:**
- Open tracking pixels (Gmail warns users)
- Link tracking (flags as marketing)
- Read receipts

**DO USE:**
- Reply rate (primary metric)
- Conversation depth (how many back-and-forths)
- Booked calls (ultimate conversion)

### Deliverability Checklist

- [ ] Subdomain for cold sending (protects main domain)
- [ ] SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured
- [ ] 14-21 day warmup minimum
- [ ] 30-80 emails per inbox maximum
- [ ] NO tracking pixels
- [ ] Under 125 words per email
- [ ] Casual tone (anti-AI detection)
- [ ] One clear CTA per email
