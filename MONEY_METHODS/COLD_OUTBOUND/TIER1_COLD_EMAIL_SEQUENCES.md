## DELIVERABILITY SETUP (2026 Requirements)

### Domain Setup
1. **Buy 3 domains** (Porkbun, Namecheap - $12-15 each)
   - yourname-consulting.com
   - yourname-dev.com
   - yourname-agency.com
2. **DNS records** (set in Cloudflare or domain registrar)
   - SPF: `v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all`
   - DKIM: Generate in Google Workspace or email tool
   - DMARC: `v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com`
3. **Subdomain for cold** (protects main domain)
   - cold.yourname-consulting.com
   - mail.yourname-dev.com

### 2026 Gmail Changes (CRITICAL)

**Tracking Pixel Warning:**
- Gmail now warns users: "this email is tracking you"
- **SOLUTION:** Disable all tracking (opens, clicks)
- Use reply rate as success metric, not opens

**Warmup Timeline Increase:**
- Was: 7-14 days
- Now: 14-21 days
- Start slower, ramp slower

**Safe Sending Limits:**
- New inbox: 30 emails/day max
- 2-week warmed: 50 emails/day
- 3-week warmed: 80 emails/day
- NEVER exceed 80/day per inbox

### Personalization Tokens

**Use These:**
- {{FIRST_NAME}}
- {{COMPANY_NAME}} or {{PRACTICE_NAME}} or {{LAW_FIRM}} or {{BROKERAGE}}
- {{CITY}}
- {{PAIN_POINT}} (manually research 1 per prospect)
- {{COMPETITOR_FIRM}} (if applicable)

**Do NOT Use:**
- {{LAST_NAME}} (too formal, feels automated)
- {{TITLE}} (often wrong in databases)
- Generic {{INDUSTRY}} tokens (obvious mail merge)

### A/B Testing Framework

**Test These:**
1. **Subject lines** (5 variations per sequence)
   - Short vs long
   - Question vs statement
   - Numbers vs no numbers
   - Personalized vs generic

2. **Email length** (test 50 vs 100 vs 150 words)

3. **CTA** (test 3 variations)
   - "15-min call: [CALENDLY]"
   - "reply 'interested' and i'll send details"
   - "quick call? [CALENDLY]"

4. **Timing** (send time testing)
   - 8am vs 11am vs 2pm vs 5pm
   - Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday perform best

**Track:**
- Open rate (benchmark: 40-60%)
- Reply rate (benchmark: 5-12%, elite 10%+)
- Booked calls (benchmark: 1-3%)
