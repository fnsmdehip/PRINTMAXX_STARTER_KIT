## Platform Breakdown (Verified Feb 2026)

### Facebook Reels
**RPM:** $0.20-$4/1K views (average $0.40-0.80 for most creators)

**Requirements:**
- 10K followers OR
- 600,000 views in last 60 days
- Content Monetization Program (CMP) eligibility

**Verified Sources:**
- Meta CMP documentation
- Creator earnings reports
- ALPHA517/524 validation

**Reality Check:** The $4.40/1K claim was from ONE high-performing US-only creator. Most earn $0.20-0.80. Still 2-10x YouTube Shorts.

**Action:** Cross-post ALL content. Even at $0.20/1K it's free money.

---

### TikTok Rewards
**RPM:** $0.40-$6/1K views (niche dependent)

**Requirements:**
- 10K followers
- 100K views in last 30 days
- Videos MUST be 1+ minute (critical change)

**Format Optimization:**
- 1-3 minutes = sweet spot
- Longer ≠ better (3+ min sees drop-off)
- Completion rate matters more than length

**Verified Sources:**
- TikTok Creator Rewards documentation
- Multiple creator case studies
- 10-20x old Creator Fund confirmed

**Action:** Shift ALL TikTok content to 1+ minute format. Zero downside.

---

### YouTube Shorts
**RPM:** $0.01-$0.06/1K views

**Why it's worth it:**
- Algorithm reach (Shorts often go viral)
- Builds subscriber base
- Long-form upsell opportunity
- Cross-platform SEO

**Don't optimize for Shorts revenue. Optimize for reach.**

### TikTok Shop

**Categories (highest performing):**
- Beauty/skincare: 20-30% commission

**Small Creator Advantage:**

- **4.3x better engagement on small accounts**

**Verified:** TikTok Shop $66.2B GMV, small creator performance data confirmed.

**Action:** Add affiliate links to ALL TikTok content. Even 5% conversion = significant revenue.

## Content Creation Strategy

### Format: Short-Form (15-90 seconds)

**Structure:**
```
0-3 seconds: Hook (stop the scroll)
3-15 seconds: Problem setup
15-45 seconds: Solution/value
45-60 seconds: Call to action
60-90 seconds: Extended value (TikTok Rewards requirement)
```

### Niche Selection

**High-RPM Niches:**
- Finance/money: $2-6/1K (highest)
- Tech reviews: $1-4/1K
- Fitness/health: $0.80-2/1K
- Motivation: $0.50-1.50/1K

**High-Volume Niches:**
- Sleep/relax: 10M+ views possible
- Memes/entertainment: Viral potential
- Sports highlights: Consistent engagement
- News/breaking: Trending traffic

**Recommendation:** Test 3 niches in parallel. Double down on highest RPM × volume.


---

### Week 2-4: TikTok Shop Integration

**Product Selection:**
- [ ] Browse TikTok Shop Creator Marketplace
- [ ] Filter by commission % and reviews
- [ ] Choose 10-20 products in your niche
- [ ] Create product showcase videos

**Content Strategy:**
```
Video 1-5: Pure value (no sales)
Video 6: Product mention (soft sell)
Video 7-10: Pure value
Video 11: Product focus (hard sell)
```

**Hook Formulas:**
```
"this [product] changed my [outcome]"
"why I stopped using [competitor] and switched to [product]"
"[problem]? this fixes it in [timeframe]"
"I tested 10 [products]. this one won"
```
