## AI UGC Quality Control

### Nano Banana Best Practices

**What works well:**
- Product unboxing (hands are convincing)
- Close-up product shots
- Lifestyle scenes (product in environment)
- Before/after comparisons

**What to avoid:**
- Full-body human shots (uncanny valley)
- Complex hand movements
- Multiple products in one video
- Face-focused content (stick to hands/products)

## FTC Compliance (CRITICAL)

### Disclosure Requirements

**AI-generated content MUST be disclosed:**
```
Caption: "AI-generated content for entertainment"
OR
On-screen text: "Created with AI"
```

**Affiliate links MUST be disclosed:**
```
"This video contains affiliate links.
I may earn commission at no cost to you."
```

**Violation penalties:**
- FTC: $51,744 per violation (fake reviews)
- Platform bans
- Brand partnership loss

**DO NOT skip disclosures. Legal risk is real.**
