## Anti-Saturation Tactics

the window will close. here's how to stay ahead:

1. **first-mover in micro-niches**: don't do "fitness" do "postpartum fitness for new moms." smaller audience, less competition, higher affiliate payouts.

2. **build email list from traffic**: every video CTA should funnel to a lead magnet. "free 7-day workout plan" -> email capture -> email affiliate sequence. email list outlasts algorithm changes.

3. **platform diversification**: don't depend on one platform. if TikTok bans you, you need Reels, Shorts, Pinterest running.

4. **quality over pure volume**: when everyone is doing 100 AI videos/day, the ones that look hand-crafted win. use Seedance 2.0 reference mode to match specific aesthetic. add real voiceover ($5 on Fiverr).

5. **own the niche**: build a brand account, not a spam account. @SleepMaxx that posts sleep content daily becomes an authority. spam accounts that post random affiliate offers get flagged.

6. **stack with other revenue**: affiliate is the quick hit. but also sell your own digital product ($9-$19 guide), grow a newsletter (ad revenue), and build an app (recurring). the video pipeline feeds all of these.
