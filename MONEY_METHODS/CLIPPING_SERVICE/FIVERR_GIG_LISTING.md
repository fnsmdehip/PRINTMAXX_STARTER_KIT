## FAQs

**Q: What format do you need the source video in?**
A: Just send a YouTube/Vimeo/Google Drive link. I handle the download and processing. If it's a private video, share via Google Drive or WeTransfer.

**Q: How do you pick which moments to clip?**
A: AI analysis scans the full transcript for high-engagement moments: unexpected statements, emotional reactions, funny bits, key insights, and "wait what?" reveals. I then manually review each pick to make sure it works as a standalone clip (no extra context needed).

**Q: What about music/sounds?**
A: Standard and Premium packages include music matching. I use royalty-free tracks that fit the content tone. If you have a specific sound or trending audio in mind, send it and I'll match.

**Q: Can you add my logo/watermark?**
A: Yes, included at no extra cost. Send your logo as a PNG with transparent background.

**Q: What if I have a lot of content?**
A: Message me about monthly retainers. $500/mo for 20 clips, $1K/mo for 50 clips, $2K/mo for unlimited. Retainer clients get priority delivery and a dedicated Notion board.

**Q: Can you post the clips for me?**
A: I deliver the files + posting schedule + captions. I don't manage posting accounts. If you need a social media manager, I can recommend one.

**Q: What's your turnaround?**
A: Basic/Standard: 48 hours (most in 24). Premium: 72 hours. Rush delivery available for +$30.

**Q: Do you do Twitch VOD clipping?**
A: Yes. Send the VOD link or Twitch channel URL. Same pricing applies.

## getting first 5 reviews (critical for fiverr ranking)

fiverr algorithm heavily weights reviews in first 30 days. plan:

1. **day 1-3:** message 10 podcasters/youtubers offering 1 free clip as a "sample." condition: if they like it, order the Basic package and leave a review.
2. **day 4-7:** deliver free samples. follow up with "if this was useful, here's my Fiverr link for more."
3. **day 7-14:** first 3 paying clients should come from free sample conversions.
4. **day 14-21:** offer 30% discount to next 5 clients who order within 48 hours of messaging.
5. **day 21-30:** should have 5+ reviews. fiverr starts showing gig in search.

**target: 5 reviews with 5.0 rating in first 30 days.** this puts you on page 1 for "podcast clips" and "short form video editing" in your category.

after 10 reviews: raise Basic from $25 to $35.
after 25 reviews: raise Basic to $45, Standard to $75.
after 50 reviews: $65/$100/$250. your pipeline cost doesn't change.
