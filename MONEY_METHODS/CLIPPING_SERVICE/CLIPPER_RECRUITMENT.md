## QA review checklist (for reviewing clipper submissions)

check each clip against this list before approving:

### must pass (reject if any fail)

- [ ] **length:** 15-90 seconds
- [ ] **format:** 9:16 vertical, fills screen
- [ ] **captions:** readable, no typos, properly timed
- [ ] **hook:** first 3 seconds have visual or text hook
- [ ] **audio:** clear, no distortion, music doesn't overpower speech
- [ ] **standalone:** makes sense without watching full source video
- [ ] **no TOS violations:** no copyrighted music (unless trending sound), no prohibited content

### nice to have (feedback, don't reject)

- [ ] **engaging thumbnail/first frame:** would you tap this in a feed?
- [ ] **pacing:** no dead air, cuts are tight
- [ ] **CTA:** mentions link in bio, follow, or next clip at end
- [ ] **trend awareness:** uses current trending format/sound if applicable
- [ ] **platform optimization:** correct hashtags, description, tags

### rejection reasons (common)

| reason | how to fix | frequency |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| too short (<15s) | extend clip or pick longer moment | 15% |
| captions unreadable | increase font size, add shadow/outline | 20% |
| no hook | add text overlay or visual hook in first 3s | 25% |
| bad audio | normalize levels, reduce music volume | 10% |
| needs context | pick a more standalone moment | 15% |
| copyright music | switch to royalty-free or trending sound | 10% |
| wrong format | export as 9:16, not 16:9 | 5% |

## clipper sourcing priority (ranked)

1. **small TikTok creators (1K-10K followers)** - they already edit. they want income. DM 10/day.
2. **r/forhire** - freelancers actively looking for work. post weekly.
3. **fiverr "video editing" sellers** - flip them from one-off to ongoing. message 5/day.
4. **upwork "short form editor" profiles** - post job, filter by hourly rate <$15.
5. **discord clip communities** - join 3-5 servers, recruit from active members.
6. **twitter #clippingjobs** - post monthly, DM people who engage.
7. **onlinejobs.ph** - Philippines-based editors, $3-5/hr, excellent for basic clipping.
8. **university film/media students** - post on campus job boards, offer portfolio building + pay.

**target: recruit 2-3 new clippers per week until you hit 25. then maintain with 1/week to replace churn.**

expected churn: 30-40% drop off in first month. overhire by 2x to compensate.

## the math (why this works for clippers)

a clipper posting 4 clips/week on TikTok:

- 4 clips x 4 weeks = 16 clips/month
- average 20K views/clip (conservative for decent content)
- total: 320K views/month
- TikTok Rewards (if qualified): 320K x $0.002 = $640
- our payment to them: (320 x $0.50) + (25% x $640) = $160 + $160 = $320

**clipper earns: $640 TikTok Rewards + $320 from us = $960/month for ~8 hours/week of work.**

that's $30/hr effective rate for a part-time gig with no fixed schedule.

this is why they'll stay. the pay is real and scales with their performance.
