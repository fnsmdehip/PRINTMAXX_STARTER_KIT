# Monetization Strategy

> Revenue model for AI character content business. All income is from disclosed AI-generated content.

---

| Stream | % of Revenue (target) | Effort | Description |
|--------|----------------------|--------|-------------|

## Stream 1: Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)

### Tier Structure

**Regular - $9.99/mo**
- What subscribers get:
  - Daily photo posts (1 per day minimum)
  - Access to character's feed and stories
  - Basic DM access (template responses)
  - Polls and Q&A participation
- Target: 60% of total subscribers
- Churn expectation: 15-20% monthly
- LTV at 15% churn: ~$67 per subscriber

**Premium - $24.99/mo**
- Everything in Regular plus:
  - Extended photo sets (8-15 images vs 3-5)
  - AI voice messages (2-3 per week per character)
  - Behind-the-scenes content generation process
  - Priority DM responses
  - Early access to new content
- Target: 30% of total subscribers
- Churn expectation: 10-12% monthly
- LTV at 10% churn: ~$250 per subscriber

**VIP - $49.99/mo**
- Everything in Premium plus:
  - 2 custom content requests per month (specific pose, outfit, setting)
  - Extended voice messages (personal thank-yous, custom audio)
  - Direct access to character chat (higher priority, longer responses)
  - Exclusive VIP-only photo sets (monthly)
  - Input on future content direction (polls, requests)
- Target: 10% of total subscribers
- Churn expectation: 8-10% monthly
- LTV at 8% churn: ~$625 per subscriber

### Pricing Strategy Rationale

**Why $9.99 as the entry point:**
- Low enough to impulse-purchase
- Standard for the creator economy ($5-15 is the sweet spot)
- High enough to filter out non-serious followers
- Multiplied by 200+ subs = real revenue

**Why the 2.5x jump to Premium ($24.99):**
- Voice messages and extended sets justify the premium
- Subscribers who pay 2.5x are 3x less likely to churn
- The jump feels like "getting a lot more" not "getting gouged"

**Why $49.99 VIP:**
- Custom content is high-value and labor-intensive
- Limits VIP to truly engaged fans (quality over quantity)
- 10 VIP subscribers at $50 = $500/mo with high LTV

### Promotional Pricing

**First-month discount:**
- Offer $4.99 first month (50% off Regular) to reduce friction
- Convert trial subscribers to full price at month 2
- Expected trial-to-paid conversion: 40-60%

**Bundle pricing (cross-character):**
- "Follow all 3 characters" bundle: $24.99/mo (vs $29.97 separately)
- Increases total revenue per user while offering perceived value
- Only offer this once characters are established (Month 2+)

## Stream 2: PPV (Pay-Per-View) Content

### PPV Types and Pricing

| PPV Type | Price | Content | Frequency |
|----------|-------|---------|-----------|
| Standard photo set | $5-8 | 5-8 themed images | 2x/week |
| Premium photo set | $12-20 | 10-20 high-quality themed images | 1x/week |
| Voice message pack | $8-15 | 3-5 personalized voice clips | 1-2x/month |
| Behind-the-scenes | $5-10 | Generation process, prompt reveals | 1x/month |
| Collaboration set | $10-15 | Two characters in one set | 2x/month |

### PPV Strategy

**Rule: PPV supplements subscriptions. It never replaces them.**
- Subscribers should feel the subscription is worth it on its own
- PPV is "extra special" content, not content withheld from subs
- Free teaser image from each PPV set posted to the feed
- PPV purchase rate target: 5-15% of subscriber base per drop

**PPV Messaging Template:**
```
New [Theme] set just dropped! [X] exclusive images you won't see anywhere else.
Here's a preview... [teaser image]
Unlock the full set for just $[price].
```

## Stream 3: Custom Content Requests

### Pricing

| Custom Type | Price | Delivery Time | Effort |
|-------------|-------|--------------|--------|
| Specific outfit/setting photo | $25 | 24-48 hours | 15-30 min generation |
| Custom themed set (5 images) | $50 | 2-3 days | 1-2 hours |
| Custom themed set (10 images) | $75 | 3-5 days | 2-3 hours |
| Custom voice message (60 sec) | $15 | 24 hours | 15 min |
| Custom voice message (3 min) | $30 | 24 hours | 30 min |
| Custom written story/diary | $20 | 24-48 hours | 30 min |

### Custom Content Rules

1. **Requests must be within platform TOS.** Reject anything that violates rules.
2. **Characters stay in character.** Custom requests must align with the character's persona.
3. **Disclosure still applies.** Custom content is still AI-generated and labeled as such.
4. **Queue management.** Cap custom requests at 10 per character per week to maintain quality.
5. **VIP priority.** VIP subscribers get 2 free custom requests/month. Non-VIP pay full price.

### Custom Content Workflow

1. Subscriber sends request via DM
2. VA reviews request against rules
3. If approved: confirm price and delivery timeline
4. Generate content using Midjourney/SD with specific parameters
5. Apply watermark
6. Deliver via DM as PPV (subscriber pays before seeing)
7. Follow up: "Hope you loved it! Let me know what you'd like next."

## Stream 4: Tips & Gifts

### Optimization Tactics

Tips are passive but can be optimized:

1. **Thank every tipper publicly** (first name only): "Thank you [name] for the tip! You made my day."
2. **Tip goals:** "Help me hit $100 in tips this week and I'll drop an extra set."
3. **Tip-triggered content:** "Tip $10 and I'll send you a personal voice message."
4. **Birthday/milestone tips:** Post on character "birthdays" asking for celebration tips.
5. **Reaction to tips in DMs:** Personalized thank-you message for any tip over $5.

### Expected Tip Revenue
- Per subscriber: $0.50-2.00/mo average in tips
- 200 subscribers: $100-400/mo
- Low effort, fully passive once engagement habits are established

**Kai (Lifestyle/Music):**
- Streetwear brands
- Sneaker releases
- Music production tools
- Hair care products
- Skincare brands

### Affiliate Implementation

1. Sign up for Amazon Associates (general affiliate)
2. Sign up for niche-specific affiliate programs matching each character
3. Integrate naturally into content: "Training in my favorite [brand] leggings today -- link in bio"
4. Track which character and which products convert best
5. Disclosure: "Affiliate link" clearly stated per FTC requirements

### Sponsorship (Phase 3+, at 1,000+ total subscribers)

Once audience is proven:
- Approach brands for sponsored content deals
- Rate: $50-200 per sponsored post at small scale
- Always disclose: "Sponsored post" AND "AI-generated character"
- Only work with brands that align with character personas

## Pricing Optimization Framework

### Monthly Review (What to Check)

1. **Conversion rate by tier:** Are most subs on Regular? Consider adding a mid-tier.
2. **PPV purchase rate:** Below 5%? Prices too high or content not compelling. Above 20%? Could increase prices.
3. **Churn rate by tier:** High churn at Regular? Content quality issue. High churn at Premium? Value proposition issue.
4. **Custom request volume:** Maxed out? Raise prices. Low volume? Lower prices or promote more.
5. **Subscriber lifetime value:** Track how long subs stay and total spend. Focus on increasing LTV not just new subs.

### Kill and Double-Down Rules

**Kill (at 60 days):**
- Character with fewer than 30 subscribers after 60 days of consistent posting
- Platform generating less than 10% of revenue with same effort
- PPV content type with less than 2% purchase rate over 30 days

**Double down:**
- Character growing 20%+ month over month: increase posting frequency
- PPV type with 15%+ purchase rate: create more of that type
- Platform with lowest churn: make it the primary focus

## Payment Processing Fees Summary

| Platform | Creator Fee | Payment Processing | Net to Creator |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|---------------|
| Fanvue | 15% | Included | 85% |
| Fansly | 20% | Included | 80% |
| Patreon | 8-12% | +2.9% + $0.30 per transaction | ~85% |
| Stripe (direct) | 2.9% + $0.30 | That's it | ~97% |

### Why Not Go Direct (Stripe-Only)?

- Platforms provide built-in audience and discovery
- Platform trust signals reduce payment friction
- Handling subscriptions, DMs, and content delivery yourself is a full product build
- Use platforms until $30K+/mo, then consider a custom site with Stripe
