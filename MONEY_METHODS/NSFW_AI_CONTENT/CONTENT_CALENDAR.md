## Content Generation Batching

**Batch schedule (VA workflow):**
1. **Day 1 of each month:** Generate all standard images for the month (90-120 images across 3 characters)
2. **Day 2:** Write all captions using templates + character voice guides
3. **Day 3:** Schedule all posts using scheduling tool or platform native scheduling
4. **Day 4-5:** Generate PPV content sets (3-4 premium sets per character)
5. **Ongoing:** Daily DM responses and engagement (1-2 hours/day)
6. **Weekly:** Generate 2-3 "reactive" posts based on trending topics or subscriber feedback

This front-loads the creative work and makes the remaining 25 days mostly scheduling and engagement.
