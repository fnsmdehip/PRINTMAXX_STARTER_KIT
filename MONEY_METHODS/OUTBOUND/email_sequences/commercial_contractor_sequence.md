## FOLLOW-UP AFTER SEQUENCE

Commercial contractors are slow to respond but high-value when they do.
Wait 60 days. Re-contact before summer and fall peak seasons.
Never re-pitch cold — always reference the original email: "sent you this back in March, wanted to check if timing is better now."
