**Tier 1 (Volume plays):** $18.99-21.99. Trending phrases, meme shirts, high volume niches. Compete on virality not margin.

**Tier 3 (Premium):** $34.99-49.99. Hoodies, premium blanks (Bella Canvas), limited seasonal designs. Higher margin per unit, lower volume.

### Reposting strategy (same content, 4x revenue)

**The workflow:**

1. Create content once (phone camera, 15-60 seconds)
2. Export without watermark (remove TikTok watermark before reposting using SnapTik or similar)
3. Post on TikTok first (fastest algo feedback, 2-hour virality window)
4. 24 hours later: post on Instagram Reels (different caption, same video)
5. 48 hours later: post on YouTube Shorts (different title, same video)
6. 72 hours later: post on Facebook Reels (different description, same video)

**Why stagger:** Each platform penalizes duplicate detection if posted simultaneously. 24-72 hour gaps avoid this.

**Watermark rules:**
- TikTok penalizes Instagram watermarks (and vice versa)
- YouTube Shorts penalizes TikTok watermarks
- Facebook Reels penalizes all other watermarks
- Always export clean (no watermark) from your editing app
