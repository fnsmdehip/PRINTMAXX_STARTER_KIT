# The Print Run - Newsletter Launch Plan

**Newsletter:** The Print Run
**Frequency:** Weekly, Sundays at 9 AM EST
**Platform:** Beehiiv (free tier to start)
**Brand:** @PRINTMAXXER
**Voice:** PRINTMAXX copy style (lowercase energy, specific numbers, consequence-first, no AI slop)
**From Name:** The Print Run

---

## Sections (Every Issue)

### 1. This Week I Shipped
what actually got built, deployed, or launched this week. specific numbers. screenshots when possible. no "exciting update" language. just: here's what exists now that didn't exist 7 days ago.

format: 2-3 bullet points with links. 100-150 words max.

### 2. Tool of the Week
one tool. real review. actual cost. what it replaced. how long setup took. would I pay for it again in 6 months?

format: tool name, cost, one-line verdict, 3-4 sentence review. 80-120 words.

### 3. Alpha Drop
the best tactical insight from the week. could be a framework, a metric that matters, a pricing trick, a distribution hack. something the reader can use today.

format: the insight in bold, then 150-200 words of context and how to apply it.

### 4. Community Wins
reader submissions, replies from previous issues, wins people shared on social. builds community loop and gives readers a reason to engage.

format: 2-3 curated reader highlights. 50-100 words.

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: 0 to 500 subscribers (weeks 1-8)

**primary channels:**
- Twitter bio link (direct to Beehiiv signup page)
- lead magnets (see LEAD_MAGNETS.md) gated behind email signup
- Reddit posts with genuine value that mention the newsletter in bio/profile only (never in post body)
- cross-post newsletter content to Substack for discovery

**tactics:**
- every Twitter thread ends with "I write about this stuff weekly" + link
- pin a tweet with the newsletter CTA
- reply to solopreneur/indie hacker tweets with genuine insight, profile links to newsletter
- cold DM 10 relevant accounts per week offering to feature their wins in Community Wins section

**target:** 50-75 new subscribers per week

### Phase 2: 500 to 1,000 subscribers (weeks 9-16)

**primary channels:**
- referral program (Beehiiv built-in: share with 3 friends, unlock bonus content)
- guest features (invite 2-3 indie hackers per month to co-write Alpha Drop section)
- LinkedIn cross-posting (reformatted newsletter excerpts as posts)
- Product Hunt launch of the newsletter itself

**tactics:**
- launch referral rewards: 3 referrals = access to "PRINTMAXX Vault" (archive of all lead magnets)
- swap newsletter mentions with 3-5 similar-sized newsletters
- submit to newsletter directories (Letterlist, Newsletter Stack, The Sample)

**target:** 75-100 new subscribers per week

### Phase 3: 1,000 to 5,000 subscribers (weeks 17-40)

**primary channels:**
- paid recommendations on Beehiiv ($1-2 per subscriber from partner newsletters)
- sponsor swaps (feature a partner, they feature you)
- content upgrades embedded in Medium/Substack articles
- SEO-optimized newsletter archive pages

**tactics:**
- A/B test subject lines (Beehiiv supports this natively)
- segment audience by engagement (open rate, click rate) for targeted content
- launch "best of" compilations quarterly
- use reader survey data to refine topic selection

**target:** 100-150 new subscribers per week

## Monetization

### At 1,000 subscribers: Sponsor slots

- 1 sponsor slot per issue
- pricing: $50-75 per issue (CPM of $50-75 is standard for niche newsletters)
- format: 2-3 sentence native ad in the "Tool of the Week" section or standalone sponsor block
- requirement: sponsor must be relevant to solopreneur audience
- outreach: cold email tool companies, SaaS products, course creators

estimated monthly revenue: $200-300/month (4 issues)

### At 2,500 subscribers: Affiliate integration

- include affiliate links in Tool of the Week recommendations
- only recommend tools you actually use (FTC compliant, clearly disclosed)
- estimated: $100-400/month depending on tool pricing tiers

### At 5,000 subscribers: Paid tier launch

**"The Print Run Pro" - $9/month or $79/year**

paid tier includes:
- weekly "Alpha Drop Extended" (deeper tactical breakdown with templates)
- monthly "Stack Audit" (full teardown of a working solopreneur tech stack)
- access to private Discord/community channel
- downloadable templates, scripts, and swipe files
- early access to PRINTMAXX digital products

estimated conversion rate: 3-5% = 150-250 paid subscribers
estimated monthly revenue: $1,350-2,250/month

### At 10,000 subscribers: Premium sponsorships

- sponsor slots priced at $200-400 per issue
- dedicated sponsor issues (1 per month, clearly labeled)
- annual sponsor packages at discount
- estimated: $800-1,600/month
