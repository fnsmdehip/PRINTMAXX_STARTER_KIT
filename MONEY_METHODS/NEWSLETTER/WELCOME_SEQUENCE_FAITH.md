### Email 3 - Day 3
**Subject Line:** 5 prayer frameworks that changed my mornings | Save this email (you'll use it all year)
**Preview Text:** Framework #3 is the one I use 4 days out of 5
**From Name:** Daily Grace

This email is a keeper. Save it or screenshot it.

I've tested dozens of prayer methods over the past 3 years. Most felt forced. These 5 stuck because they actually fit into a normal morning.

**1. The ACTS method (classic for a reason)**
Adoration, Confession, Thanksgiving, Supplication. 10 minutes. Start with who God is, not what you need. It reframes everything.

**2. The 3-name prayer**
Pick 3 people. Pray one specific thing for each. Done in 5 minutes. I do this when I'm running late, which is most days.

**3. The written conversation**
Open a notebook. Write to God like you're texting a friend. Write what you think he'd say back. This sounds weird until you try it. Then it becomes the most honest prayer you've ever prayed. I use this 4 mornings out of 5.

**4. The gratitude walk**
Pray out loud while walking. Name things you see that you're grateful for. Works great with little kids because they point stuff out too.

**5. The psalm rewrite**
Pick a psalm. Rewrite it in your own words with your own situations. Psalm 23 becomes "The Lord is my shepherd even when my boss is impossible and my bank account is empty."

Pick one. Try it for 3 days. That's it.
