### ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

**reply patterns (daily, 30-60 min):**

- reply to EVERY comment on your posts within 4 hours
- like 10-15 posts from other findommes daily
- quote tweet 2-3 paypig confession posts with domme commentary
- reply to 5 potential subs in findom hashtag threads
- retweet 3-5 posts from findom RT networks
- NEVER like or reply to competitors' sub's DMs (don't poach visibly)
- respond to DMs within 2 hours during active hours (creates urgency, not availability)

**engagement rules:**

- alternate between responsive (2-3 days) and distant (1-2 days) to create withdrawal
- high-paying subs get faster responses than low-paying
- never respond instantly to tributes. wait 30 min minimum. indifference = power.
- acknowledge tributes publicly on timeline (social proof + humiliation)
- use "good boy" sparingly. variable reinforcement. not every tribute gets praise.
