# Trending Meme/Slang POD Automation Playbook

**Method:** Find trending phrases/memes → Slap on shirts → Sell via socials → Automate
**Margin:** 40-60%
**Time to first sale:** 3-7 days
**Risk:** LOW (no inventory, print on demand)

---

## The Model

```
Trending phrase detected (Twitter/TikTok/Reddit)
    ↓
AI generates 5 design variations (Canva/Kittl/Leonardo)
    ↓
Upload to Printful/Printify (auto-mockups)
    ↓
List on Etsy + TikTok Shop
    ↓
Post meme content tagging the shirt
    ↓
Profit while it's still trending
```

## Step 1: Find Trending Phrases (Daily)

### Sources to Monitor

| Source | What to Look For | Tool |
|--------|------------------|------|
| **Twitter/X Trending** | Phrases with 10K+ tweets | Natively or TweetDeck |
| **TikTok Sounds** | Audio with 100K+ uses | TikTok Creative Center |
| **Reddit** | r/OutOfTheLoop, r/memes rising | Sort by Top - Today |
| **Google Trends** | Breakout searches | trends.google.com |
| **Know Your Meme** | Trending section | knowyourmeme.com |
| **Urban Dictionary** | New slang | urbandictionary.com |

### Current Hot Phrases (Feb 2026 Examples)

| Phrase | Origin | Shirt Potential |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| "type shit" | TikTok/Twitter slang | HIGH |
| "no cap fr fr" | Gen Z speak | MEDIUM |
| "delulu is the solulu" | TikTok mental health | HIGH |
| "it's giving..." | Fashion/commentary | MEDIUM |
| "understood the assignment" | Performance praise | HIGH |
| "main character energy" | Self-improvement | HIGH |
| "touch grass" | Internet culture | HIGH |
| "chronically online" | Self-deprecating | HIGH |
| "roman empire" | Meme trend | MEDIUM (fading) |
| "very demure, very mindful" | TikTok trend | HIGH |

### Phrases to AVOID (Legal Risk)

- **Trademarked terms** (check USPTO.gov)
- **Celebrity names/likenesses** (defamation risk)
- **Song lyrics** (copyright)
- **Sports team names/logos** (trademark)
- **Direct brand parodies** (trademark)
- **Anything involving minors**
- **Explicit slurs** (platform bans)

### Gray Area (Proceed with Caution)

- Pop culture references without names
- Generic meme formats
- Slang that's entered common usage
- Parody clearly labeled as such

## Step 2: Design Generation (15 min per phrase)

### AI Tools for Design

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| **Canva** | Quick text designs | Free/$13mo |
| **Kittl** | Premium typography | Free/$15mo |
| **Leonardo.ai** | AI imagery | $12/mo |
| **Midjourney** | Complex art | $10/mo |
| **Adobe Firefly** | Commercial-safe | Free tier |

### Design Templates That Work

**1. Simple Bold Text**
```
[TRENDING PHRASE]
- Font: Impact, Bebas Neue, or Oswald
- Color: White on black or neon on dark
- Placement: Center chest
```

**2. Distressed/Vintage**
```
[PHRASE] with worn texture overlay
- Fonts: Retro serif, grunge
- Colors: Muted earth tones
- Vibe: Thrifted look
```

**3. Minimalist**
```
small [phrase] on pocket area
- Font: Sans-serif, thin weight
- Color: Single tone
- Placement: Upper left chest
```

**4. Meme Format**
```
[Image above]
[Caption below]
- Standard meme layout
- Bold white Impact font with black outline
```

**5. Typography Art**
```
Phrase arranged in creative shape
- Multiple fonts/sizes
- Forms pattern or image
- More premium aesthetic
```

## Step 7: Scaling Winners

### When to Scale

- **3+ sales in first week** → Create color variations
- **10+ sales** → Add to more products (hoodie, tank, mug)
- **50+ sales** → Run paid ads ($5-10/day test)
- **100+ sales** → Full product line

### Paid Ads Strategy (Only for Winners)

**TikTok Spark Ads:**
1. Post organic content with product
2. If it gets 1K+ views organically, boost it
3. $5-10/day budget
4. Target: 18-34, interests related to phrase

**Meta Ads:**
1. Use best-performing mockup
2. Carousel format (3-5 product shots)
3. Target: Interest in meme pages, internet culture
4. $5/day, 3-day test

## Legal Checklist (BEFORE LISTING)

- [ ] Phrase is NOT trademarked (check USPTO.gov)
- [ ] No celebrity names or likenesses
- [ ] No copyrighted characters
- [ ] No explicit hate speech
- [ ] No defamatory content
- [ ] Parody is clearly parody
- [ ] Design is original (not copied)

### Safe Categories

✅ Internet slang entered common usage
✅ Generic meme formats
✅ Self-deprecating humor
✅ Relatable life situations
✅ Profession-specific humor
✅ Abstract concepts

### Risky Categories

⚠️ Political statements (platform restrictions)
⚠️ Religious content (niche but controversial)
⚠️ Relationship drama (can be seen as offensive)
⚠️ Health claims (FTC issues)
