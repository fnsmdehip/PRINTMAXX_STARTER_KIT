### 1. Sleep Cycle (4.6 stars, 19,857 ratings, ~$950K/mo revenue)

- Step 4-6: Alarm setup with tactile scroll wheel interface

- Step 9: Privacy-focused educational messaging
- Step 10: Paywall (01:18 timestamp) - 7-day free trial, Hard Paywall

**Paywall Design:**
- Type: Free Trial - Hard Paywall (cannot access core features without trial)
- Visual timeline explaining trial duration and reminder notifications
- Monthly and Yearly options presented
- Appears at END of onboarding after value demonstration

**Navigation (verified):**
- Bottom tab bar with 4 tabs: Sleep, Journal, Statistics, Profile
- Sleep tab: primary interface with alarm + tracking
- Journal tab: daily entries
- Statistics tab: data visualization
- Profile tab: settings + subscription

**Key Visual Elements:**
- Dark theme interface (primary)
- Color-coded sleep stage line graphs (easy to interpret)
- Circular sliders for goal-setting with visual feedback
- Animations used for education, not just delight
- Sleep coaching content (Dr. Mike coaching, parental sleep)
- Unique "Cough Radar" feature mapping anonymized local health trends

**Color Scheme (verified):**
- Primary: Dark navy/black background
- Accent: Orange/peach (from icon identity)
- Purple secondary tones
- High contrast text on dark backgrounds

**What Makes It Feel Premium:**
- AI sleep coach "Luma" branding
- "Patented sound analysis" technology claims
- Editors' Choice badge
- 40M+ downloads social proof
- Cough Radar as differentiated viral feature
- Educational animations that teach, not just decorate

### Onboarding Length Distribution

| Steps | Apps | Pattern |

**Key insight:** There is NO correlation between fewer steps and higher ratings. ShutEye (31 steps) has 4.8 stars. Finch (18 steps) has 4.9 stars. What matters is value demonstration per step, not step count.

### Paywall Timing Patterns

| Pattern | Apps | Revenue Impact |

**Key insight:** Hard paywalls generate the highest per-app revenue when combined with thorough onboarding (Cal AI: 25 steps + hard paywall = $2.0M/mo). But soft paywalls with emotional engagement also work (Finch: 18 steps + soft paywall = $1.75M/mo).

## TOP 5 DESIGN PATTERNS THAT DRIVE $100K+/MO REVENUE

1. **Deep personalization quiz (15-31 steps) + paywall at end** - ShutEye, Cal AI, Opal all use this. The quiz builds investment; paywall converts that investment. Average revenue: $767K/mo.

2. **Emotional companion/pet mechanic** - Finch's virtual pet drives $1.75M/mo. Users care about a digital creature. This is the highest-monetizing single mechanic across all 15 apps.

3. **Dual paywall strategy** - ShutEye shows paywall, then if dismissed, shows urgency discount. This "second chance" approach works: $950K/mo.

4. **Hard paywall after thorough onboarding** - Cal AI (25 steps then hard paywall = $2.0M/mo) proves you can block features IF you've demonstrated enough value first.

5. **7-day free trial with yearly plan emphasis** - Used by 7/15 apps. The standard that works. Always highlight annual as "savings" over monthly.
