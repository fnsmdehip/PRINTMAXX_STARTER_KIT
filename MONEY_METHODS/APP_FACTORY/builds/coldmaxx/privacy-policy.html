Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

ColdMaxx ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data.

1. No Data Collection

ColdMaxx does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • No account required
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No analytics tracking or behavioral profiling
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers

2. Local Storage

Your prospect information, generated emails, and saved templates are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Email Generation

Email templates are generated entirely within your browser using JavaScript. No prospect data, company names, or email content is transmitted to any server. The email generation logic runs 100% locally.

4. Permissions

The app may request:

  • Clipboard access: To let you copy generated emails with one tap (triggered only by your action)
  • Storage: To save templates and history locally

5. Third-Party Services

ColdMaxx loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log font file requests. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Children's Privacy

ColdMaxx is intended for business use and is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted within the app.
