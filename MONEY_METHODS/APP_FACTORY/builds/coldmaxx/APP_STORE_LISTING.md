## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

**Write cold emails that actually get replies. 3 proven frameworks. 30 seconds.**

Most cold emails get deleted in 2 seconds. Not because the product is bad — because the email sounds like a template. ColdMaxx gives you battle-tested copywriting frameworks that convert, without sounding like every other pitch in the inbox.

---

**3 FRAMEWORKS. ONE APP.**

**AIDA** (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
The classic. Grab attention with a hook, build interest, create desire, close with one clear ask. Works for SaaS, agencies, and service businesses.

**PAS** (Problem → Agitate → Solution)
Start with their pain. Make it worse. Then position yourself as the relief. Highest-converting framework for pain-point-driven outreach.

**BAB** (Before → After → Bridge)
Paint their current situation, show the dream outcome, then position your offer as the path between. Great for results-oriented pitches.

---

**WHAT YOU GET**

• Generate 3 email variants simultaneously (AIDA, PAS, BAB)
• Subject line generator — 5 options per campaign
• Follow-up sequence builder (3-email follow-up chain)
• Spam word checker — flags terms that trigger spam filters
• Estimated read time + word count
• One-tap copy to clipboard
• Save your best emails as templates
• Swipe library of high-converting subject lines by industry
• Works offline — generate emails without internet

---

**BUILT FOR**

• Founders doing their own outreach
• Agency owners landing new clients
• Sales reps hitting quota
• Freelancers pitching cold
• Anyone who sends emails and wants more replies

**PRIVATE**

No account required. Your prospect lists and email content stay on your device. Nothing sent to any server.
