## Competitor Gap
- **Quittr** — had a privacy scandal (exposed user data). Trust destroyed.
- **Streaks** — generic, not sobriety-specific, no milestone content
- **Sober Grid** — social/community focused, NOT private
- **I Am Sober** — requires account creation, stores data on servers

## Our Positioning
"The only NoFap and sobriety tracker that keeps your data 100% on your device. No account. No server. No one knows your streak but you."

## App Store Description (copy-ready)

**Tagline:** Track your streak. Keep your privacy.

**Short Description (30 chars):** Private sobriety streak tracker

**Full Description:**
SoberStreak is the most private habit-breaking app ever built. Zero servers. Zero accounts. Zero data collection. Everything lives on your phone.

WHAT YOU CAN TRACK:
• NoFap / porn-free
• Alcohol-free days
• Quit smoking
• Gambling-free
• Any custom habit

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Pick your habit
2. Check in each day you stay clean
3. Build your streak. Beat your record.

THE EMERGENCY BUTTON:
When an urge hits, one tap opens your urge-surfing toolkit. Box breathing. Personalized prompts. Quick interrupts that actually work.

REAL MILESTONES:
Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 90. Each milestone comes with a message written specifically for your habit — not generic motivational fluff.

YOUR PRIVACY:
Quittr exposed user data. Other apps require sign-up. SoberStreak has no server, no backend, no analytics. Your streak is yours alone.

FREE FOREVER:
The core streak tracker is 100% free. Premium unlocks milestone celebrations, multi-habit tracking, and detailed insights.
