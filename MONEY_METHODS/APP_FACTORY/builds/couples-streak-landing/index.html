Today's Prompts

Real examples from the daily prompt system
Active Listening
Today's technique: Before responding, summarize what your partner just said in your own words. "What I'm hearing is..." Wait for them to confirm before sharing your view.
Appreciation
Complete this sentence: "Today I noticed you _______ and it made me feel _______." Be specific. "You made coffee without being asked and it made me feel cared for."
Question
Ask your partner: "What's something you've wanted to tell me but haven't found the right moment?" Listen without defending. This one's for them.
Connection
15-minute ritual: No phones, no TV. Ask about one thing that happened today that you haven't mentioned yet. Share one thing from yours. That's it.
