## ASO Description (App Store / Play Store copy)

**Short description (80 chars):**
Private sobriety tracker. No account. No data. Just your streak.

**Long description:**

SoberStreak is the sobriety tracker that respects your privacy.

NO account required. NO data sent to servers. NO tracking. NO ads.

100% of your data stays on YOUR device.

Whether you're tracking NoFap, quitting alcohol, smoking, weed, or any other habit — SoberStreak gives you the tools to stay clean without compromising your privacy. We built this after the Quittr privacy scandal exposed millions of users' sensitive recovery data. Never again.

FEATURES:
✓ Day counter — see exactly how many days you have been clean
✓ Milestone badges — earn unlockable achievements at 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 days
✓ Craving emergency panel — 4-7-8 breathing, cold shower protocol, 5-minute delay tactic
✓ Daily journal — write private notes that NEVER leave your device
✓ Activity heatmap — visualize your clean days over time
✓ Multi-habit tracking — NoFap, alcohol, smoking, weed, junk food, social media, or custom
✓ Offline-capable — works without internet after first load
✓ No account — no email, no password, nothing

PRIVACY FIRST:
Other apps sell your recovery data. SoberStreak stores everything in browser localStorage. If you uninstall, it's gone. If you clear browser data, it's gone. We literally cannot access your data because we don't have a server.

Download. Start your streak. Own your recovery.
