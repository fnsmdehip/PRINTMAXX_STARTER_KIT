Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

SoberStreak collects zero personal data. No servers. No accounts. No analytics. Everything you enter stays on your device in your browser's localStorage.

What We Collect

Nothing. SoberStreak has no backend, no database, and no network requests that send your data anywhere.

Local Storage

SoberStreak stores your streak data, journal entries, and settings in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

SoberStreak loads fonts from Google Fonts and the Tailwind CDN for styling. These services may log standard request metadata (IP address, timestamp) per their own privacy policies. No personal app data is shared.

Advertising

None. No ads. Ever.
