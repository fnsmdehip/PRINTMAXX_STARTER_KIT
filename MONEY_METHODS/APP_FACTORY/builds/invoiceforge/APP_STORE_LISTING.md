## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

**Make professional invoices in 60 seconds. Built for plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, contractors, and freelancers.**

Stop losing money to unpaid invoices or unprofessional paperwork. InvoiceForge lets you build a complete invoice on your phone, export it as a PDF, and send it before you even leave the job site.

---

**BUILT FOR TRADESPEOPLE**

Most invoice apps were designed for accountants. InvoiceForge was designed for the person on the roof, under the sink, or wiring a panel. Fast, no-nonsense, and built for how you actually work.

**WHAT YOU CAN DO**

• Create invoices in under 60 seconds
• Pick from trade-specific templates: Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, General Contractor, Landscaping, Cleaning, Freelance
• Auto-calculate line item totals, subtotals, and tax
• Export invoices as PDFs instantly using your phone's built-in PDF export
• Add your logo, business name, and payment terms
• Save client info for repeat business
• Set up a direct payment link (Stripe, PayPal, Venmo Business — your choice)
• Track which invoices are paid, pending, or overdue
• Works completely offline — no internet needed on the job site

**PRIVATE & SECURE**

All your invoice data stays on your phone. Nothing is sent to any server. No account required. No cloud storage. Your client information is yours alone.

**NO SUBSCRIPTIONS TO START**

InvoiceForge is free. Create unlimited invoices. Export unlimited PDFs. No limits, no paywalls, no "you've hit your free tier" nonsense.

---

**WHO IT'S FOR**

• Plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians
• General contractors and handymen
• Landscapers and lawn care professionals
• House cleaners and maids
• Freelancers and consultants
• Anyone who invoices clients and wants to look professional

---

Works on iPhone and iPad. Installs as a home screen app for instant access. No account needed. Open it and go.
