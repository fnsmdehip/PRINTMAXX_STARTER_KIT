Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

InvoiceForge ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

1. No Data Collection

InvoiceForge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app is entirely client-side.

  • No account registration required
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies beyond what your browser creates locally

2. Local Storage

All invoice data (client names, line items, totals, saved templates) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is cleared when you uninstall the app or clear browser data

3. PDF Export

When you export an invoice as a PDF, the file is generated locally in your browser using the browser's built-in print/PDF functionality. No data is uploaded to any server during this process.

4. Payment Links

InvoiceForge optionally allows you to add a payment link (e.g., your Stripe link) to invoices. We do not process, store, or have access to any payment information. You are solely responsible for your payment provider's terms.

5. Permissions

The app may request the following browser permissions:

  • Storage: To save invoices and templates locally on your device

No location, camera, microphone, or contact permissions are requested.

6. Third-Party Services

InvoiceForge loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This means Google may log that your device requested these font files. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Children's Privacy

InvoiceForge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app.
