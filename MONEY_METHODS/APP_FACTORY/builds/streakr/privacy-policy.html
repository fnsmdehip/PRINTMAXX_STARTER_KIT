Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Streakr is designed with privacy as a core feature, not an afterthought.

What data we collect

None. Streakr stores all your habit data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. No data is transmitted to any server. No account is required or created. We have no servers that could receive your data.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, cookies for tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect user data. There is no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar service integrated.

Payments

If you upgrade to Streakr Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. Your payment information is handled entirely by Stripe and is never stored by us. We receive only a confirmation of successful payment. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Advertising

We do not show ads or sell advertising space.

Children

Streakr is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, the updated date above will change. We will not begin collecting data as a result of any update.
