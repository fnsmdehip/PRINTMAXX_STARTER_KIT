the deep dive

Instantly.ai

what it is: cold email platform built specifically for high-volume outbound. their warmup network of 200K+ real email accounts is the largest in the market, and it shows in deliverability numbers.

deliverability: we hit 62% average open rates across 5,000 emails on properly warmed accounts. that's inbox placement, not just "delivered to spam." the warmup pool size matters because your emails interact with real inboxes, training Google/Microsoft to trust your domain.

what makes it different: unlimited email accounts on the $30/mo plan. most competitors charge per seat or per sending account. if you're running 20+ domains for outbound (which you should be), this saves hundreds per month.

the catch: their lead finder is separate ($47/mo for 1,000 verified leads). if you need leads AND sending, you're looking at $77/mo. still cheaper than Lemlist's $69/mo plan that includes leads but caps you at 3 sending accounts.

Smartlead

what it is: built for agencies managing multiple client outbound campaigns. the white-label feature at $94/mo lets you reskin the entire platform with your agency branding. if you run outbound for clients, this is the move.

deliverability: solid at 55% open rates in our tests. slightly behind Instantly but the difference narrows with proper warmup (2-3 weeks). their warmup network is smaller but growing fast.

what makes it different: the master inbox. all replies from all campaigns across all clients land in one place. for agencies juggling 10+ client accounts, this is a massive time saver. also supports 6,000 emails/day vs Instantly's 5,000.

the catch: no built-in lead finder. you need a separate tool like Apollo ($49/mo) or Clay. UI is functional but not as polished as Instantly. onboarding takes longer. support is responsive but documentation is thin.

deliverability8.5/10
ease of use7.0/10
value for money8.0/10

Lemlist

what it is: multichannel outreach platform that combines cold email with LinkedIn and phone. if your strategy isn't just email but includes LinkedIn connection requests, profile visits, and voice drops, Lemlist is the only platform that handles all three in one sequence.

deliverability: 51% open rates in our tests. lemwarm (their warmup tool) is solid but the smaller warmup network compared to Instantly means slightly lower inbox placement rates. still good, just not the best.

what makes it different: multichannel sequences. you can build a flow that sends email on Day 1, visits their LinkedIn on Day 3, sends a connection request on Day 5, and follows up by email on Day 7. no other tool does this natively. also includes a lead database on the $69/mo plan (450M+ contacts).

the catch: starter plan only includes 1 sending email for $39/mo. to get unlimited sending emails, you need the $69/mo plan. LinkedIn automation requires their Chrome extension running on your machine. per-seat pricing means team costs scale fast.
