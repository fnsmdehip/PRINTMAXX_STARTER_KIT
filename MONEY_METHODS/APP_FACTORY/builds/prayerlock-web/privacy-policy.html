Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

PrayerLock ("we", "our", or "the app") takes your privacy seriously, especially given the personal and spiritual nature of religious practice data.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

PrayerLock does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your prayer times or streak data
  • We do not collect your location beyond what your device calculates locally for Qibla direction
  • We do not collect your tasbih counts or spiritual practice data
  • No account registration is required or possible
  • No analytics, behavioral tracking, or profiling

2. Local Storage

Your prayer streak, timer settings, tasbih history, and preferences are stored exclusively in localStorage on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is inaccessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear browser storage

3. Location / Qibla Compass

The Qibla compass feature uses your device's geolocation API to determine the direction toward Mecca. This works as follows:

  • Location is accessed only when you open the Qibla tab and grant permission
  • Your coordinates are used only for the bearing calculation, which happens entirely on your device
  • Your location is never transmitted to any server
  • You can deny location permission — the compass will not function but all other features remain available

4. Device Compass / Orientation

The Qibla compass uses your device's compass sensor (DeviceOrientationEvent). This data is processed locally and never stored or transmitted.

5. Notification Permissions

If you enable prayer time reminders, PrayerLock requests notification permissions. These are handled entirely by your device's OS and are never transmitted externally.

6. Third-Party Services

PrayerLock loads fonts from Google Fonts and the Tailwind CSS CDN. These services may log basic request metadata (IP address, browser type). See their respective privacy policies for details.

7. Religious Data Sensitivity

We recognize that religious practice data is sensitive. Our architecture is specifically designed so that no prayer data, streak data, or religious preferences ever leave your device. This is a core design principle, not an afterthought.

8. Children's Privacy

PrayerLock does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any data entry of personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted within the app and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
