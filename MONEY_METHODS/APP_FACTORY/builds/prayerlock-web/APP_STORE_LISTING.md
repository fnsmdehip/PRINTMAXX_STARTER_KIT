## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

**Build a prayer habit that actually sticks. Timer, streak tracker, Qibla compass, tasbih counter — all in one beautiful app.**

Prayer is the most important daily habit for millions of people. But staying consistent is hard. PrayerLock gives you everything you need to build your prayer habit one day at a time.

---

**PRAYER TIMER**

Set a focused prayer session from 5 to 60 minutes. Beautiful circular countdown. Gentle completion sound. Optional ambient sounds (rain, nature, silence) to help you focus. No notifications, no interruptions — just you and your prayer.

---

**STREAK TRACKER**

See your prayer consistency the same way developers see their GitHub contribution graph — a full year of prayer history, one square per day. Watch your streak grow. Milestones at 7, 14, 30, 60, and 90 days. Once you see the streak, you'll do anything to keep it.

---

**QIBLA COMPASS**

Find the direction of Mecca from anywhere in the world. Uses your device's compass and GPS to calculate the exact Qibla bearing. Works offline after first use. Accurate to within a few degrees.

---

**TASBIH COUNTER**

Digital prayer bead counter with haptic feedback. Preset targets at 33 and 99. Custom targets for any dhikr. Count with one tap. Session history saved automatically.

---

**DAILY VERSE**

One inspirational verse per day from the Quran and Bible. Share with one tap to any app.

---

**WHY PRAYERLOCK IS DIFFERENT**

• No account required — open it and use it
• Works completely offline — add to home screen and never worry about connectivity
• Private — all your prayer data stays on your device, never sent anywhere
• Interfaith — designed for Muslims and Christians, because prayer is universal
• No ads, no premium paywall blocking core features, no guilt

---

**FOR MUSLIMS**

Built with authentic Islamic features: accurate Qibla compass, tasbih counter with standard dhikr targets (33/99), Islamic geometric design language, Arabic-script verse display.

**FOR CHRISTIANS**

Daily Bible verse, prayer journal, streak motivation to maintain a consistent quiet time.
