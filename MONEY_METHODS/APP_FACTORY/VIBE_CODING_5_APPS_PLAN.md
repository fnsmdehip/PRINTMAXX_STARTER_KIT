## 5 Lock Apps to Build

### Day 1: PrayerLock (Faith Niche)
**Concept:** Lock phone for prayer duration. Faith-based accountability.

**Vibe coding prompt:**
```
Build an iOS app called PrayerLock:
- User sets prayer duration (5, 10, 15, 30 min)
- When locked, can only access Quran/Bible apps
- Shows inspirational quotes during lock
- Tracks streak (days in a row)
- Prayer time reminders
- Dark mode with Islamic/Christian themes
```

**Unique features:**
- Qibla direction indicator
- Prayer bead counter
- Verse of the day

**Target:** 2M+ Muslims + Christians seeking phone discipline
