# App Store Audit - February 2026

**Date:** 2026-02-10
**Source:** Apple RSS API, iTunes Search API, live App Store data
**Methodology:** Pulled real-time top charts + searched 40+ categories + analyzed 30+ apps across 10 international markets

---

## Part 1: Current App Store Landscape (Feb 2026)

### Top Free Apps (Overall - Feb 10 2026)

The top 50 free apps are dominated by:
1. **Streaming/Entertainment:** Peacock, NBC, YouTube TV, Tubi, TikTok
2. **AI Chatbots:** ChatGPT (#2), Google Gemini (#5), Grok (#10), Claude (#16) - AI is eating the App Store
3. **Shopping/Commerce:** Temu, SHEIN, Walmart, Amazon, Shop by Shopify
4. **Social:** Threads, X, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Discord, Telegram
5. **Finance:** Kalshi (prediction markets at #4), TurboTax, PayPal, Cash App
6. **Food Delivery:** DoorDash, Dunkin', Carl's Jr.

### Key Trends Observed

**1. AI chatbot apps are now mainstream chart toppers.** ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and Claude all sit in the top 20 free. This was not the case a year ago. AI wrapper apps still have a window.

**2. Prediction markets broke into top 5.** Kalshi at #4 during Super Bowl week shows event-driven finance apps can spike hard.

**3. Health & Fitness apps dominate the "quiet money" category.** Not in overall top 50, but every subcategory search returns apps with 100K-1.8M reviews and 4.7-4.9 ratings. This is where subscription money lives.

**4. Faith apps are massively underserved outside Christianity.** Hallow (Catholic) has 327K reviews. Muslim Pro has 584K. Hindu, Buddhist, Jewish, and Sikh apps have almost zero competition with modern UX.

**5. Simple "tracker" apps print money.** Step counters, water reminders, habit trackers, mood journals, fasting trackers - all freemium with 3-7 day trials leading to $4.99-9.99/mo subscriptions.

- **Streaks** (#18, $5.99) - habit tracker, Apple Design Award winner
- **Things 3** (#21) - premium to-do app

**Pattern:** Paid apps that succeed are either (a) niche professional tools, (b) premium versions of free categories with no ads, or (c) dead-simple utilities that do ONE thing perfectly.

## Part 6: Pricing Intelligence

### Subscription Pricing Tiers Observed

| Price Point | Category | Examples |
|-------------|----------|----------|
| $2.99-3.99/mo | Simple utilities | Timer, counter, basic tracker |
| $4.99-6.99/mo | Standard trackers | Habit, water, mood |
| $7.99-9.99/mo | Premium health | Sleep, meditation, self-care |
| $12.99-14.99/mo | Premium longevity | Fasting + nutrition, fitness coaching |
| $19.99+/mo | Ultra premium | Noom ($59/mo), medical-grade |

### Annual Pricing Sweet Spot
- Most apps use 40-60% discount for annual vs monthly
- $29.99-49.99/year is the sweet spot for health/wellness apps
- Conversion rate highest at $29.99/year with 7-day free trial

### Free Trial Norms
- 3-day trial: aggressive, lower conversion but filters tire-kickers
- 7-day trial: industry standard, best balance
- 14-day trial: education/complex apps
- 30-day trial: rare, usually for fitness coaching apps
