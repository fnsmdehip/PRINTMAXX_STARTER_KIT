Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

ADHD-Streak stores all habit data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not have a server. We do not collect or store your habit data.

  • Habit names, check-in dates, and streaks: stored locally on your device only
  • Pro waitlist email: if you submit your email, it may be stored in a secure third-party service (Mailchimp or similar) for the purpose of notifying you about Pro launch
  • Analytics: we use Plausible Analytics (privacy-first, no cookies, no personal data) to count page visits and feature usage in aggregate

Affiliate links

This app contains links to third-party services (Focusmate, Sunsama). These are affiliate links — we may earn a commission if you sign up. This does not affect your experience or cost.

Your data

All your habit data lives on your device. To delete it, use the "Reset all data" option in Settings, or clear your browser's local storage. To export it, use the "Export data" option in Settings.
