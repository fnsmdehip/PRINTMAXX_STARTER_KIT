## Long Description
Scripture Streak helps Christians build a daily Bible reading habit — with real accountability and a 365-verse plan that keeps you coming back.

**How it works:**
- Open the app each day to receive your daily verse (365 curated KJV verses)
- Log your reading to maintain your streak
- Track your habit with a 52-week heatmap calendar
- Set reminders at your preferred devotional time

**Why KJV-only:**
King James Version is the most widely trusted and memorized translation in Christian tradition. We use the real text from bible-api.com, not paraphrased or summarized versions. Every verse is accurate.

**Features:**
- 365 daily verses with full passage context
- Streak counter with milestone badges
- 52-week visual heatmap
- Morning/evening reminder scheduling
- Prayer journal (free tier: 3 entries)
- Full journal + verse archive with Pro
- Zero ads, zero data collection

**Free vs Pro:**
Free: daily verse, streak tracking, 3 journal entries, basic milestones
Pro ($2.99/mo or $29.99/yr): unlimited journal, all 365 verses accessible anytime, streak recovery, advanced milestones
