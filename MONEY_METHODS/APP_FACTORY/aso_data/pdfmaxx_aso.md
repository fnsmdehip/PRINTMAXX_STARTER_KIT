## Long Description
PDFMaxx is the PDF editor that actually respects your files.

Every other PDF tool uploads your documents to their servers. PDFMaxx doesn't. Your files are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript — they never leave your device.

**What you can do:**
- Merge multiple PDFs into one (drag, drop, done)
- Split a PDF by page range (1-3, 5, 7-9 or split all)
- Compress PDFs to reduce file size
- Page reorder with Pro

**No accounts. No uploads. No waiting.**
Works offline once loaded. Install as a PWA for desktop-app experience.

**Who needs this:**
- Freelancers combining contracts and invoices
- Students merging assignments
- Lawyers and accountants handling sensitive documents
- Anyone who doesn't want their files on someone else's server
