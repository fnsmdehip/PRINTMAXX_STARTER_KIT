## Long Description
cnsnt is the private consent documentation app for couples, therapists, coaches, and anyone who values clarity before connection.

**Why cnsnt:**
- 11 legally-inspired templates — tailored to real situations
- AES-256-GCM encryption with PBKDF2 (100K iterations) — military-grade privacy
- Zero-knowledge architecture — your documents never touch a server
- Full audit log with timestamps and hash-verified integrity
- Cloud backup to your own storage (iCloud / Google Drive)

**Features:**
- Template library: relationship consent, therapy informed consent, coaching agreements, activity waivers, NDA templates
- PIN lockout with configurable attempts
- Audit trail — every view, edit, and signature timestamped
- Encrypted export (share safely with partners, attorneys, or therapists)
- Works 100% offline after install
- No account required, no telemetry

**Who uses cnsnt:**
- Couples who value explicit communication
- Therapists and counselors documenting informed consent
- Life coaches and wellness professionals
- Anyone building a safer, more accountable relationship practice

**Privacy matters:**
cnsnt stores nothing on servers. Your documents exist only on your device (and in your personal encrypted cloud backup if you choose it). Not even we can read them.
