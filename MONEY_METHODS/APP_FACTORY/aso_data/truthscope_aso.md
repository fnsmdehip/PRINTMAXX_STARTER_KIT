## Long Description
TruthScope is the only lie detection app that reads REAL biometric signals from your phone.

**3 real detection engines:**
- **Finger PPG** — measures heart rate variability from your camera's pulse detection (real photoplethysmography, not random numbers)
- **Voice Stress Analysis** — measures F0 (fundamental frequency) jitter and shimmer using real audio processing
- **Facial Micro-Expressions** — detects involuntary facial muscle movements using computer vision

**What makes TruthScope different:**
Every other "lie detector" app in the App Store generates random percentages. We measure actual physiological signals. The readings are real. The accuracy is what published research shows (not 99% we invented).

**Party Mode** — designed for social fun:
- Multiplayer rounds with a host
- Dramatic verdict animation
- Works with 2-8 people
- Questions pre-loaded for entertainment mode

**14-step science onboarding:**
Understand the real science before using the app. We explain what polygraphs actually measure, what phone sensors can and can't detect, and where TruthScope fits on the accuracy spectrum. Transparency builds trust.

**Note on accuracy:**
Phone sensors are less precise than dedicated polygraph equipment. TruthScope shows you REAL readings with scientifically accurate confidence ranges. We don't claim medical-grade accuracy. We claim real data — which is more than every competitor offers.
