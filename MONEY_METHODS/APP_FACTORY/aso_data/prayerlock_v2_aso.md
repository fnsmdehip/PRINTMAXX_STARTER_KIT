## Long Description
PrayerLock helps Muslims build a consistent prayer habit — for free, offline, with zero ads.

**Features:**
- Prayer timer with visual countdown
- Streak tracker with 52-week calendar heatmap
- Qibla compass (GPS-based direction)
- Tasbih counter with haptic feedback
- Salah times for your location (5 methods)
- AI Dua Assistant — 30+ authentic duas by situation

**The Dua Assistant** (new in v2):
Search by feeling or situation. "Anxious?" Get the Yunus dua. "Breaking fast?" Get the iftar dua instantly. Arabic text, transliteration, English translation, and hadith source — every time. 5 free searches/day, unlimited with Pro.

**Why PrayerLock:**
- Works 100% offline (install it as a PWA)
- No account required
- No tracking or data collection
- Built by someone who actually prays
