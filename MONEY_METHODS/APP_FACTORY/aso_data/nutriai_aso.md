## Long Description
NutriAI turns your phone camera into a nutrition expert — powered by Google Gemini Flash AI.

**How it works:**
1. Take a photo of any food (plate, package, restaurant dish)
2. Gemini Flash AI identifies the food and estimates portions
3. Get instant macros: calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber
4. Log it in one tap — no manual entry, no barcode scanning

**Accurate TDEE calculation:**
- Mifflin-St Jeor formula (the gold standard, not outdated Harris-Benedict)
- Accounts for: gender, age, height, weight, activity level
- Weekly adjustment based on your logged progress

**16-step personalized onboarding:**
We ask the right questions before you log your first meal: goal (cut/bulk/maintain), dietary restrictions, meal timing preferences, activity level. Your targets are set correctly from day one.

**Features:**
- AI meal scanning with Gemini Flash (real network calls, not pre-programmed food database)
- 1,200+ food database fallback for offline use
- Daily macro rings with progress tracking
- Weekly and monthly trends
- Water tracking
- Body measurements log
- Redux-persisted history (your data stays local)

**Free vs Pro:**
Free: 3 AI scans/day, full macro tracking, manual logging
Pro ($4.99/mo or $29.99/yr): unlimited AI scans, meal history export, advanced insights, recipe builder
