## App Store Description (Long — 4000 chars)

**Stop tracking 10 habits. Track 3 non-negotiables.**

Most habit trackers fail because they make you track too much. You add 8 habits, miss one, feel like a failure, and quit by week two.

CoreDay is different.

Pick the 3 things that, if you do them, make today a success. That's your "Win Day." Do all 3 = green day. Build a streak of green days. That's the entire app.

**How it works:**
→ Set your 3 cores (your non-negotiables)
→ Tap each one as you complete it
→ All 3 done = Win Day (with a satisfying celebration)
→ Watch your streak grow
→ Never break it

**Features:**
• Define your 3 core habits with emoji + custom name
• One-tap check-in — no friction, just complete
• Win Day animation when all 3 are done
• Streak counter for consecutive Win Days
• 5-week heatmap showing your Win Days at a glance
• Monthly win rate percentage
• Works completely offline — no internet needed
• All data stays on your device (privacy-first)
• No account required — zero signup friction

**CoreDay Pro:**
• 4th and 5th core habits
• Day notes (1-sentence reflection)
• 90-day history view
• Data export (CSV)
• Custom color themes

**Who this is for:**
People who've tried and abandoned habit trackers. People who want simplicity over complexity. Anyone who's ever said "I track too many things and complete none of them."

The concept: if you define 3 non-negotiables and do them, you've had a successful day. Not 10. Not 7. 3.

Download CoreDay. Define your 3. Win the day.
