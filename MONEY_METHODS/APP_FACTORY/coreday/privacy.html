Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

CoreDay stores all your data locally on your device. We don't have a server. We can't see your habits. We don't sell your data. Your cores, streaks, and history never leave your phone.

What data we collect

CoreDay does not collect personal data. All app data (your cores, daily logs, streak history) is stored in your browser's localStorage — on your device only.

We use Plausible Analytics (privacy-first, cookieless) to understand basic usage patterns (page views, feature usage). Plausible does not track individuals and is GDPR compliant by design. No cookies. No fingerprinting.

Data storage

  • Your cores, daily completions, and history live in localStorage on your device
  • Clearing your browser data or uninstalling will delete all CoreDay data
  • We have no way to restore your data — it's yours, stored locally

Pro email waitlist

If you join the Pro waitlist, we store your email address to notify you when Pro launches. We will only use your email for CoreDay-related updates. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Third-party services

  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-first, cookieless analytics. See Plausible Privacy Policy.
  • Surge.sh — web hosting. Standard CDN hosting, no personal data collected.

Your rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request deletion of. Your data is already yours — it's on your device. If you submitted your email for the Pro waitlist and want it removed, contact us.

Contact

Questions? Email: privacy@coreday.app

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll note the new date above. We won't make changes that reduce your privacy without notice.
