## Sponsorship Rate Benchmarks (2025-2026)

| Metric | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Pre-roll CPM (15-30s) | $18-$25 |
| Mid-roll CPM (host-read, 60s) | $15-$30 |
| Post-roll CPM | $10-$20 |
| Health/biohacking niche premium | 1.5-2x standard CPM |
| Small podcast flat rate (5K-10K downloads/ep) | $75-$300/episode |
| Mid podcast flat rate (10K-50K downloads/ep) | $300-$1,500/episode |
