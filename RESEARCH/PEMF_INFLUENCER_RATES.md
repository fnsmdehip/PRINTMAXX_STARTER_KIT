## 2. Negotiation Playbook

### Getting Rates Down Without Insulting Creators

**Rule 1: Never open with "what's your rate?"**
Instead say: "We're launching a PEMF wellness device and looking for founding partners to grow with us. Interested in exploring what a collaboration could look like?"

This frames it as partnership, not transaction. Creators charge less for partnerships than one-off sponsored posts.

**Rule 2: Lead with value, not budget constraints**
- "We're giving founding partners lifetime access to our product line"
- "Top partners get featured on our site and marketing"
- "We're building a creator advisory board with equity-like upside"
- Never say: "We have a small budget" or "We're a startup so..."

**Rule 3: Anchor with a hybrid offer**
Present base fee + performance bonus FIRST:
- "We're offering $200 base + 15% commission on all sales through your link"
- This feels more generous than a flat $500 because the upside is uncapped

**Rule 4: Use the "test campaign" frame**
- "We'd love to start with a test campaign of 2 posts. If it performs well, we'll scale to a 6-month partnership at premium rates."
- Creators accept lower initial rates when there's a clear path to more money

**Rule 5: Bundle for discounts**
- Request 3-5 posts/videos instead of 1. Creators give 15-30% volume discounts
- Multi-platform bundles (TikTok + Instagram + Story) save 20-40%
- Long-term deals (3-6 months) save 20-40% vs. one-offs

### Profit-Sharing Models That Work

**Model 1: Pure Affiliate (Zero Upfront)**
- Commission: 15-25% per sale
- Cookie window: 30-90 days
- Best for: Nano/micro creators, product-market fit testing
- PEMF math: Device at $499, 20% commission = $99.80/sale to creator
- Pro: Zero risk. Con: Fewer creators will accept

**Model 2: Hybrid (Small Base + Commission)**
- Base: $100-$500 per post
- Commission: 10-15% per sale
- Best for: Micro/mid-tier creators who need guaranteed income
- PEMF math: $200 base + 12% on $499 device = $200 + $59.88/sale
- Pro: Motivates creators AND gives them security

**Model 3: Tiered Commission**
- 0-10 sales/month: 15% commission
- 11-25 sales/month: 20% commission
- 26+ sales/month: 25% commission
- Best for: Incentivizing top performers without overpaying low performers
- Pro: Self-selects for motivated creators

**Model 4: Revenue Share (Long-Term Partner)**
- 5-10% of revenue from all customers they bring, including repeat purchases
- Lifetime attribution (or 12-month window)
- Best for: Podcast hosts and YouTubers with high-trust audiences
- PEMF math: If a customer buys device ($499) + accessories ($200) over a year, creator earns $35-$70 total
- Pro: Aligns incentives for long-term promotion

**Model 5: Product-for-Post (Zero Cash)**
- Send free device ($499-$2,000 value) in exchange for content
- No cash payment required
- Best for: Nano creators, seeding strategy, building social proof
- Pro: Scales cheaply. Con: Less control over content quality/timing

**Model 6: Founding Partner Program**
Structure a tiered "founding partner" program:

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Seed Partner | Accept free device, post 1x | Free device, 10% affiliate commission |
| Bronze Partner | 3 posts/month, 5+ sales | Free device + accessories, 15% commission, brand page feature |
| Silver Partner | 5 posts/month, 15+ sales | Everything above + $200/month retainer, early product access |
| Gold Partner | 8+ posts/month, 30+ sales | Everything above + $500/month retainer, advisory board seat |
| Founding Partner | Top 5 creators by sales | Everything above + 2% revenue share on their cohort, co-branded content, event appearances |

**Key:** Tiers unlock better rewards (not just higher commissions). Gamification drives creator engagement. "Founding Partner" language creates urgency and exclusivity.

### Barter Deals: Product-for-Post

PEMF devices are perfect for barter because they're high-value physical products ($500-$5,000). Creators perceive $500+ of product value differently than $500 cash.

**How to pitch:**
- "We'd love to send you our [Device Name] ($X value) for your honest review. No script, no requirements, just try it and share if you love it."
- Remove obligation pressure. Creators who genuinely love the product will create better content.

**Conversion stats:** 40-60% of seeded creators post organically without any formal agreement. The content is more authentic because it's not "sponsored."

**Scale:** Send 50 devices at $200 COGS each = $10,000 investment. If 25 post (50% rate) and each drives 3 sales at $499 = $37,425 revenue. 3.7x ROI before considering long-tail effects.

### Performance-Based Pricing

| Model | Structure | Risk to Brand | Creator Motivation |
|-------|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| CPA (cost per acquisition) | $50-$100 per sale | Low | High |
| CPL (cost per lead) | $5-$20 per email/quiz signup | Low | Medium |
| Revenue share | 10-20% of attributed revenue | Low | High |
| Hybrid | Base + CPA bonus | Medium | Highest |
| Pay per view | $0.01-$0.05 per view | Medium | Low (views != sales) |

**For PEMF:** CPA at $75-$100/sale is the sweet spot. On a $499 device with ~$200 COGS, $75 CPA leaves $224 gross margin per sale. Sustainable and motivating for creators.

### Product Seeding Strategy

"Send free products and hope for organic posts" is actually a proven, scalable strategy when done right.

**Seeding funnel:**
1. **Identify 100+ targets** across podcast, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X
2. **Personalize outreach** (reference their content, explain why PEMF fits their brand)
3. **Ship device with handwritten note** (NOT automated. Handwritten converts 3-5x better)
4. **Follow up at Day 3** ("Did it arrive safely?")
5. **Follow up at Day 14** ("How's your experience been?")
6. **Never ask them to post.** If they love it, they will. If they don't love it, forced content hurts your brand.
7. **When they post organically**, reach out with formal partnership offer

**Expected conversion:** 40-60% of seeded creators post something. 15-25% become repeat promoters. 5-10% become long-term ambassadors.

**Biohacking conference seeding:** Attend Biohackers World Conference (LA, March 28-29, 2026) or similar events. Set up booth, let influencers try device on-site, gift devices to everyone with 5K+ followers. Conference attendees are already in the biohacking mindset.

### Community Ambassador Programs

**Structure:**
1. Create private Slack/Discord for "PEMF Partners"
2. Share exclusive content, early access, creator tips
3. Monthly leaderboard (top sellers get bonus rewards)
4. Quarterly virtual meetups with product team
5. Annual in-person event for top 20 ambassadors

**Why it works:** Community creates switching costs. Creators feel invested and are less likely to promote competitors. They also recruit other creators into the program.

### Referral/MLM-Adjacent Structures (Legal Ones)

**Two-tier affiliate (legal, NOT MLM):**
- Creator A refers Creator B to the ambassador program
- Creator A gets 5% override on Creator B's sales for 12 months
- This incentivizes top creators to recruit more creators
- Legal because: compensation is based on PRODUCT SALES, not recruitment fees

**Important:** Must be product-sale-based compensation only. No payment for recruiting. No required inventory purchases. Consult FTC guidelines on multi-level structures.

## 4. Red Flags for Overcharging Influencers

### Rate Card Inflation Tactics

| Red Flag | What It Means | Counter |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| "Standard rate card" with no flexibility | Inflated starting point | Everything is negotiable. Ask for bundle/partnership rate |
| Adding "management fee" or "agency fee" | 15-30% markup from their agent | Offer to work directly or ask for net rate |
| Quoting per-platform separately when you want multi-platform | Charges $500 for IG + $500 for TikTok + $500 for Story | Negotiate bundle: all 3 for $800-$1,000 |
| "Usage rights" fee exceeding the post fee | Common upsell, sometimes justified | Time-box usage rights: 30-60 days, not perpetual. Saves 40-60% |
| "Exclusivity" surcharge without you asking for it | Pre-baked into rate | Decline exclusivity or negotiate narrow scope (direct PEMF competitors only) |
| Rush fees for reasonable timelines | Charging extra for 7-14 day turnaround | Plan ahead. 3-4 week lead time avoids rush fees |

### Audience Verification (Is It Real?)

**Free tools:**
- HypeAuditor Free Checker (Instagram, TikTok)
- Modash Free Fake Follower Check
- Social Blade (growth trajectory)
- Collabstr Fake Follower Checker

**Paid tools (for scale):**
- HypeAuditor (Audience Quality Score across 53 fraud patterns)
- GRIN (full suite)
- Social Auditor

**Manual checks (do these EVERY time):**

| Check | Healthy Signal | Red Flag |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| Engagement rate | 2-6% (micro), 1-3% (macro) | <1% or >10% (likely botted) |
| Comments quality | Specific questions, pushback, stories | Generic: "Amazing!", emojis only, single words |
| Follower growth | Steady, organic curve | Sudden spikes followed by flat periods |
| Likes-to-comments ratio | 10:1 to 50:1 | 1000:1 or higher (likes bought, comments not) |
| Audience geography | Matches their content language | 50%+ followers from unexpected countries |
| Account age vs followers | Years of consistent growth | 3 months old, 200K followers |
| Story views vs followers | 5-15% of followers view stories | <1% (dead/fake followers) |

**PEMF-specific check:** Does their audience actually buy health/wellness products? Check if they've promoted other health devices and whether comments indicate purchase intent ("Where can I buy this?", "What's the link?") vs. passive engagement ("Cool!", heart emojis).

### Engagement Quality vs Vanity Metrics

**What matters for PEMF sales:**
- Save rate (people saving posts to buy later) > likes
- Link clicks / swipe-ups > impressions
- Comment questions about the product > comment count
- DM inquiries > share count
- Actual sales through promo code > all of the above

**Ask creators for:**
- Screenshot of past campaign performance (clicks, conversions)
- Their audience demographics (age, location, interests)
- Past brand collaboration results
- Case studies from similar product categories

### When to Walk Away

Walk away immediately if:
- They refuse to share any audience analytics
- Engagement rate is below 1% with no explanation
- They demand full payment upfront with no deliverable guarantees
- Their rate exceeds 3x the market average for their tier with no justification
- They've promoted 10+ competing products in the last 3 months (audience fatigue)
- They require 12+ month exclusivity
- They won't include FTC disclosures
- Comments on their health posts are clearly botted

## 5. Contract Essentials

### Key Terms to Include

**Every influencer agreement must cover:**

1. **Deliverables** (exact number, type, platform, timeline)
   - "3 TikTok videos, minimum 30 seconds each, posted between March 1-15, 2026"
   - Not: "Some social media posts"

2. **Content approval process**
   - Brand reviews draft before posting
   - 2 rounds of revisions included
   - 48-hour approval window (brand must respond or auto-approves)

3. **Compensation and payment terms**
   - Flat fee amount and/or commission structure
   - Payment schedule (50% upfront / 50% on delivery is standard)
   - Net-30 payment terms after content goes live
   - Commission tracking method (unique URL, promo code, UTM)

4. **Performance metrics and reporting**
   - Creator provides analytics screenshot within 7 days of posting
   - Minimum performance guarantees (optional, aggressive)

5. **Content ownership and usage rights**
   - Creator retains ownership of original content
   - Brand gets non-exclusive license to repurpose
   - Specify channels: social ads, website, email, retail
   - Specify duration: 90 days, 6 months, 12 months, or perpetual

6. **FTC compliance clause**
   - Creator must include clear disclosure (#ad, #sponsored, or "Paid partnership")
   - Disclosure must appear in the content itself (not just caption for video)
   - Brand can terminate for non-compliance

7. **Termination clause**
   - Either party can terminate with 14-30 days notice
   - Completed deliverables are still compensated
   - Unreleased content reverts to creator

8. **Non-disparagement**
   - Creator won't publicly disparage brand during and 90 days after campaign
   - Brand won't publicly disparage creator

### Usage Rights: How to Structure

| License Type | Cost Premium | Duration | Best For |
|-------------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Organic only | 0% (included) | Content stays on creator's page | Testing, low budget |
| Organic + brand repost | +10-20% | 90 days | Website testimonials |
| Paid ads (whitelisting) | +50-100% | 30-90 days | Meta/TikTok ad campaigns |
| Full buyout | +200-500% | Perpetual | Hero content, cornerstone assets |

**PEMF recommendation:** Start with organic only. If a creator's content performs, negotiate paid ad rights for 90 days at +50% of original fee. Only do full buyouts for top 1-2 performing pieces.

**Time-boxing trick:** Negotiate 90-day usage rights instead of perpetual. Saves 40-60%. If the content is still performing at 90 days, renew at a fraction of the original cost.

### Exclusivity: Avoid or Negotiate Down

**Default position:** No exclusivity. It costs 2-5x more and limits your creator pool.

**If creator demands exclusivity:**
- Narrow the scope: "Only direct PEMF competitors" (not all wellness products)
- Shorten duration: 30 days during campaign, not 6 months after
- Add compensation: Exclusivity = guaranteed monthly retainer, not just a one-time bump
- Category-only: They can still promote supplements, fitness gear, etc. just not competing PEMF devices

### Payment Terms That Protect the Brand

| Term | Standard | Protective |
|------|----------|------------|
| Payment timing | Net-30 after content posts | 50% upfront, 50% after analytics verified |
| Cancellation | Pay in full if content created | Pay pro-rated for completed deliverables only |
| Clawback | None | Refund if content removed within 90 days |
| Performance floor | None | Minimum views/engagement or partial refund |
| Commission tracking | Creator's honor system | Third-party tracking (Impact, PartnerStack, Refersion) |

**Affiliate tracking platforms for PEMF:**
- Impact.com (enterprise, robust)
- PartnerStack (SaaS-focused, good for subscriptions)
- Refersion (Shopify-native, easy setup)
- GoAffPro (budget-friendly)
- Tapfiliate (good for health/wellness)

### FTC Disclosure Requirements (2026 Update)

**What's required:**
- Clear and conspicuous disclosure of material connection
- Must use: #ad, #sponsored, "Paid partnership with [Brand]", or built-in platform tools
- Must appear NEAR the sponsored content (not buried at end of caption)
- For video: must appear IN the video itself, not just caption
- For podcasts: verbal disclosure at beginning of sponsored segment

**2025-2026 enforcement update:**
- FTC issued 150+ warning letters in 2025 alone
- Fines: $5,000-$43,792 per violation
- FTC fined brands $112 million in 2025 for disclosure violations
- Brands are liable when they direct, finance, or benefit from non-compliant endorsements

**Health claims extra caution:**
- PEMF devices cannot make medical claims without FDA clearance
- Creators must not claim PEMF "cures" or "treats" any condition
- Acceptable: "I noticed better sleep," "My recovery feels faster"
- Not acceptable: "PEMF cures chronic pain," "This device treats inflammation"
- Include in contract: list of approved and prohibited claims

**Contract language (include verbatim):**
> "Creator agrees to clearly and conspicuously disclose the sponsored nature of all content created under this agreement, in compliance with FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255). Disclosures must appear within the content itself (not solely in captions for video content). Creator shall not make any health claims not pre-approved by Brand. Brand reserves the right to terminate this agreement immediately for non-compliance with FTC guidelines."
