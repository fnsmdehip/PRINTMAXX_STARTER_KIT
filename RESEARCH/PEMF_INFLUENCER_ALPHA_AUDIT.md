### The 5-Touch Outreach Approach (From Engagement Farming Tactics)

Do NOT cold DM influencers asking for partnerships. Use the relationship-building approach:

1. **Touch 1:** Like + genuine value-add reply on their post (Day 1)
2. **Touch 2:** Different post, add data or personal experience (Day 3)
3. **Touch 3:** They start recognizing your name (Day 7)
4. **Touch 4:** Light engagement, reference past replies (Day 10)

## SECTION 8: COMPLIANCE AND FTC REQUIREMENTS

**Sources:** `OPS/GREY_HAT_LEGAL_PLAYBOOK_2026.md`, `MONEY_METHODS/AI_INFLUENCER/AI_NSFW_FINDOM_EXECUTION_PLAN.md` (compliance framework)

### FTC Requirements for PEMF Influencer Marketing

**Mandatory disclosures:**
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" at BEGINNING of post (not buried)
- "Affiliate link" next to any purchase links
- "Paid partnership with [Brand]" using platform-native tools
- Clear and conspicuous (visible without scrolling/clicking)

**Health-Specific FTC Requirements:**
- Cannot claim PEMF cures/treats specific diseases (FDA violation)
- Must include "Results not typical" disclaimers for testimonials
- Testimonials must be genuine and representative of typical results
- Cannot make unsubstantiated health claims
- Before/after transformations carry high legal risk
- "Individual results may vary" is minimum required language
- Income/savings claims about the product must be substantiated

**Penalty:** Up to $53,088 per violation. Each post can be a separate violation. Both brands AND influencers are liable.

### Compliant Influencer Content Templates

**Safe PEMF Claim Language:**
- YES: "I personally experienced relief using this device"
- YES: "Research suggests PEMF may support recovery" (with citation)
- YES: "Many users report improved sleep quality"
- NO: "PEMF cures chronic pain"
- NO: "This device treats arthritis"
- NO: "Guaranteed to reduce inflammation"

**Required Disclosure Template for Influencers:**
```
#ad | I received this PEMF device free from [Brand].
Individual results vary. This is not medical advice.
Consult your healthcare provider before starting any new therapy.
```
