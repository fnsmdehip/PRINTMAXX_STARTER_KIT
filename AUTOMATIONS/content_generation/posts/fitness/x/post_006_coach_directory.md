# Post 006 — Fitness Coach Marketplace

**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (4 tweets)
**Niche:** Fitness

---

launched a fitness coach marketplace. indie trainers list themselves ($49/mo). clients find + book sessions. i take 15% commission. 24 coaches listed. 8 active bookings/week. $340/mo commission revenue.

the play:
- coaches need a way to find clients (they're invisible)
- clients need a way to find coaches (they trust reviews)
- i provide the platform

commission model:
- 24 coaches × $49 = $1.1k/mo subscription revenue
- 8 bookings/week × 4 weeks × $15 commission (avg) = $480/mo commission revenue
- total: $1.5k/mo

at scale (100 coaches, 30 bookings/week):
- $4.9k coaching subscriptions
- $1.8k commissions
- $6.7k/mo revenue

i do zero fulfillment (coaches deliver sessions, handle payments, build relationships). i just manage the platform (1 hour per day).

margin: 80%+ (platform costs $200/mo to host).

the lesson: marketplaces work if supply (coaches) is invisible and demand (clients) is frustrated. fix both, take the spread.
