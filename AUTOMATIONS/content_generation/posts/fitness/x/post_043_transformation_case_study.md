week 0-4: figuring out calories
- ate the same thing daily (chicken, rice, broccoli)
- tracked in cronometer
- landed at 2,000 cal/day to lose 1.5 lbs/week

week 4-12: consistent deficits
- kept 2,000 calories
- added resistance training 4x/week

week 12-16: final push
- dropped to 1,800 calories

the system:
1. pick one diet (doesn't matter: keto, paleo, carnivore - calories matter more)
2. eat it daily (removes decision fatigue)
3. track 2 weeks to calibrate (know your deficit)
4. adjust 1 variable at a time (calories, or training, not both)
5. check scale 1x/week (daily fluctuates)

what didn't work:
- 6 different diet attempts (diet-hopping doesn't work)
- "clean eating" without tracking (you'll overeat)
- training without diet (can't out-exercise poor nutrition)
