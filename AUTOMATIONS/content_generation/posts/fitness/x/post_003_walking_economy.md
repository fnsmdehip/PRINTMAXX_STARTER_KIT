walk-to-unlock app is the easiest fitness monetization play. person needs to walk 10k steps to "unlock" their streak. they already have their phone with them. you just gamify it.

the walktounlock.surge.sh app is live. no marketing yet. 200 users in 2 weeks because it showed up in a reddit thread organically.

why it works:
- everyone walks
- pedometer data is free (phone sensors)
- 10k steps is a real goal (not arbitrary)
- sharing a streak is social (friends try to beat your streak)
- replay loop is built in (do it every day or lose it)

monetization is stupid simple:
- free tier: daily 10k step check, lose streak on miss
- premium ($2.99/mo): allow 1 skip per month
- family plan ($5.99/mo): track family members, turn off streak loss for them

expected ltv per user: $6-12/year. if 1000 users hit premium (5% conversion), that's $72k/year. cost to acquire: $0 so far.

build one of these. deploy. wait for payment processors. revenue on autopilot.
