**CAPTION:**

the streak economy is real.

i deployed 7 fitness apps with the same template. different names. different habits. all following the same psychological pattern.

coldlock (cold plunge streaks)
fitneslock (daily workouts)
walktounlock (10k steps)
stretcharx (daily stretching)
+ 3 more

none of them are complex. they're all beautifully simple:
- track your daily habit
- show your streak number
- reset to zero if you miss
- charge $5-10/mo for premium features

the magic: by day 30, the user has lost more from giving up than they can make back elsewhere.

i have users on day 140+ of their cold plunge streak. they'll defend that number. they'll pay to protect it.

this works because you're not selling fitness. you're selling identity.

"i'm the kind of person who doesn't break their streak"
