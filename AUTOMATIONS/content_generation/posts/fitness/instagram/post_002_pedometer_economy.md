- everyone walks
- pedometer data is free (phone sensors)
- people will literally optimize their entire day to hit 10k
- streaks make it social (friend competition)
- no equipment needed

the numbers:
200 users today
5% conversion rate = 10 premium users
$3/mo = $30 monthly
scale to 1000 users = $150/mo
scale to 10k users = $1,500/mo
