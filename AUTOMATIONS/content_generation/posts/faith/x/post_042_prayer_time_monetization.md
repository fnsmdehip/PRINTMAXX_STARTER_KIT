stream 1: prayer time app + ads
- free app (adhan reminders, prayer times)
- google adsense (prayer time apps = tech-adjacent)
- 10k users = $500-1k/month from ads
- cost: $20/month api, free hosting

stream 2: white-label prayer widget for mosques
- mosque websites embed your widget
- widget shows prayer times + donation button
- you take 5% of donations
- mosque gets passive revenue
- you scale to 500 mosques = 5-10 donations/day

stream 3: sms reminders
- users pay $0.99/month for text reminders 15 min before each prayer
- api cost: $0.01 per sms (twilio)
- your margin: $0.98
- 1k subscribers = $10k/month

the moat: speed + accuracy. prayer times require precise location + timezone. once people trust your times, they stick.

combined projection: 50k users across products = $20-40k/month from ads + donations + subscriptions.

the play: start with the free app (build audience), layer in white-label + sms premium tiers as you go.
