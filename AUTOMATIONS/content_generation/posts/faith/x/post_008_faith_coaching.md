# Post 008 — Faith Coaching Productization

**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (5 tweets)
**Niche:** Faith

---

packaged my personal faith journey into a 12-week "faith transformation" program. $297 enrollment. 47 students enrolled. $13.9k revenue in month one.

the program:
- week 1-4: foundation (prayer, scripture, community)
- week 5-8: deepening (obstacles, relationships, purpose)
- week 9-12: integration (daily practice, maintenance, evolution)

each week:
- 1 guided video (me teaching)
- 1 workbook (reflections + exercises)
- 1 group call (q&a + community)
- unlimited email access

my time: 2 hours per week (1 video + 1 call). course runs on kajabi (auto-delivers). money comes in passively.

the edge: people will pay for guidance on things they care deeply about. faith is the deepest care. they'll pay even if they could learn it free.

scaling: 100 students = $29.7k revenue. 1% of faith community size = retirement income.

the play: take your expertise (whatever it is). package it as a 12-week program. charge $200-500. assume 50+ enrollments in your first year. that's $10k-25k for 2 hours per week.
