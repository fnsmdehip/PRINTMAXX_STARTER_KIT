**CAPTION:**

the formula that makes cold email work.

most cold emails fail because they're generic. "i can help you grow your business" — everyone says that.

the ones that work are specific.

"chrome shows your customers 'not secure' before they see your prices"
"your .net and .com are fighting for google authority"
"wedding photographers in your city all have websites. you're instagram-only"

specificity creates trust. trust creates replies. replies create deals.

i found 10 of these leads yesterday. emailed 5. two replied asking for a call.

the play: find a specific problem. prove you found it. offer to fix it. charge accordingly.

this works because you're not selling. you're helping someone stop losing money.

drop a reply if you've done this successfully
