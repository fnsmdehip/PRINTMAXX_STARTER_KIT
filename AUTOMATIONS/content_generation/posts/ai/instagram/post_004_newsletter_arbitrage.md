# Post 004 — Newsletter Curation Business

**Platform:** Instagram
**Format:** Carousel (5-7 slides)
**Niche:** AI / Automation

---

**Slide 1 (Hook):**
"Scraping 50 AI newsletters. Curating daily digest. $500/mo passive income."

**Slide 2 (The Problem):**
"Newsletter burnout is real. Average person reads 3-5 newsletters.
50+ exist on AI alone.
Who has time to read everything?
Smart people pay someone else to curate."

**Slide 3 (The Solution):**
"I built an automated pipeline:
- Scrape 50 newsletters daily (API)
- Extract key insights with Claude
- Score by novelty + actionability
- Compile daily digest
- Email to subscribers"

**Slide 4 (The Numbers):**
"Cost: $50/mo Claude API
Revenue target: $15k/year
Break-even: 300 subscribers at $50/year
Current path: 500 subscribers in 3 months
Revenue at 500 subs: $25k/year (recurring)"

**Slide 5 (Why People Pay):**
"Curated content is more valuable than raw content
Time-poor people will pay for filtering
Substack readers already have payment method
Warm audience = warm leads"

**Slide 6 (The Scaling Path):**
"Month 1: Launch with 100 insights (free)
Month 2: 100 paid subscribers ($5k/year)
Month 3: 300 paid ($15k/year)
Month 6: 500 paid ($25k/year)"

**Slide 7 (CTA):**
"Which niche could you curate a daily digest for?"
