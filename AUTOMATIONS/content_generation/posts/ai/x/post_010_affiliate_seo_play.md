the formula:
- pick high-intent comparison keywords (chatgpt vs claude, figma vs webflow)
- write 2000 word comparison (claude generates 80%, you edit 20%)
- embed affiliate links naturally (not spammy)
- optimize for page speed + mobile
- deploy to surge / vercel (free)
- wait 30 days for google crawl

google ranking this works because:
1. "X vs Y" keywords have high search volume (10k-50k searches per month)
2. low competition for micro-sites (most affiliates suck)
3. reader intent is high (they're comparing, ready to buy)
4. you own the narrative (you choose the winner)

cost per page: $0-20 (claude api). revenue per page: depends on affiliate commission. chatgpt affiliate link alone = $15-30 per conversion.

at 8% click through rate on 100 visitors per month = 8 clicks. at 5% conversion = 0.4 sales. at $25/sale = $10/mo per page. 50 pages = $500/mo passive.
