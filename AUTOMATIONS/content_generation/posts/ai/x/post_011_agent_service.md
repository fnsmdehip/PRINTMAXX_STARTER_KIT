launched "autonomous competitor intelligence" service. i sell a 30-day agent subscription for $500/month. it scrapes competitors daily, scores products, sends weekly reports.

the actual product: one claude agent running via cron every 6 hours. it:
1. scrapes 50 competitor sites
2. scores product changes on 5 rubrics
3. flags new features
4. emails report every friday
5. costs me $20/mo in claude api

customer says: "my sales team saves 10 hours per week reading this". they pay $500/mo. i spend 30 min per week on support.

the edge: most people don't have time to monitor competitors. they know they should. they're willing to pay for automation.

scaling play: 5 customers = $2.5k/mo. 10 customers = $5k/mo. 20 customers = $10k/mo. at $20 api cost per customer, you're at 80%+ margin.

time to first customer: 10 days (build agent, post on twitter, close first customer).

the game: find any recurring manual task that costs businesses time. automate it with an agent. sell subscriptions. margin is insane because your marginal cost approaches zero.
