selling landing pages to solo founders is free money. they don't have designers. paying $500-2k for a custom design when you can build one in 2 hours with claude + next.js.

the play: find 20 founders with active products but trash landing pages. 80% are working out of notion or wix. they know it's bad. they just haven't prioritized it.

send cold emails:
"your product is solid but your landing page is costing you users. i rebuild pages in 48 hours for [price]. here's my portfolio: [3 examples]"

close rate: 2-5% because founders see the immediate problem. it's not speculative. it's "your page sucks and you know it."

economics: 2 hours build + 1 hour revisions = 3 hours work. 20 prospects per week = 4 closes per month. 4 x $1.5k = $6k/mo, part time.

the edge: use claude to match page styling to competitor sites. founders see "you matched our competitor's aesthetic but with our unique messaging." instant trust.
