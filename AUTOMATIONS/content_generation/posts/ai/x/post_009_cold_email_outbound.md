**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (6 tweets)
**Niche:** AI / Automation

running a cold email campaign to 2000 b2b prospects. 87.4% margin using claude api for personalization. here's the math:

outbound sequence: 3 emails over 14 days, personalized by prospect company and role.

the play:
1. buy lead list (2000 prospects, $200-500)
2. scrape prospect company info (crunchbase, google search)
3. use claude api to generate 3-email sequence per prospect (1000x personalization depth)
4. send via instantly.ai or lemlist ($50-100/mo)
5. track replies (instantly auto-tags)
6. follow up hot replies manually

margin: $100-500 per deal signed. let's say 2% close rate = 40 deals. at $200 average deal = $8k revenue. subtract $1k (lead list + tools) = $7k margin.

time investment: 2 weeks setup, 1 week followup. that's 120 hours of work. $8k / 120 hours = $66/hr.

most teams don't do this because: it feels spammy (it's not, it's personalized). they underestimate the power of 1-to-1 emails. they'd rather spend $5k on ads.

the edge: your reply rate will be 3-8% on first touch. the industry average is 1-2%. that's because claude personalization is insanely good at finding the actual pain point.
