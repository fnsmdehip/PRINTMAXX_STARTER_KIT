i built a lead scraper that finds 10 business owners per day. http site. split domain. instagram only. all high-intent targets. no tools, just chrome + sheets + cold email.

here's the angle: businesses with bad websites don't know they have bad websites. chrome shows "not secure" before customers see your service. instant disqualification.

this works because:
1. http sites still exist (business owner forgets to renew SSL)
2. they don't know it matters
3. they're high-trust service businesses (pool service, landscaping, photography)
4. they don't track leads, so they can't say "no"

the email hook is stupid simple:
"chrome shows your customers 'not secure' before they see your prices. costs 0 to fix. let me show you"

response rate: 3-8% on first follow-up. that's insane for cold outreach.

the play: find 10/day (30 min work), send personalized emails (30 min work), close 1-2 per month at $2-5k for a website fix or rebuild. repeat 50 times = $100k revenue.

most agencies wait for inbound. this is just finding people who are clearly losing deals, and pointing it out.
