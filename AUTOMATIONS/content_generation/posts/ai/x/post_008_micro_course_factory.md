# Post 008 — Micro-Course Factory

**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (5 tweets)
**Niche:** AI / Automation

---

you don't need 10,000 hours to build a course. you need claude and 2 hours to build a $500-2k micro-course.

micro-course definition:
- single skill (not "learn web dev", but "debug react performance")
- 20-30 min of video
- 5-7 worksheets
- 1 live q&a
- price: $500-1k

the build:
- define learning outcome (3 minutes)
- claude writes script (15 minutes)
- screen record yourself teaching from script (30 minutes)
- claude generates worksheets + quizzes (10 minutes)
- upload to gumroad or kajabi (5 minutes)

total time: 60 minutes. price: $600-1000. margin: 95% (if you host on gumroad, they take 10%).

the edge: micro-courses sell because they're specific. "debug react performance" converts at 10x the rate of "learn react" because buyers know exactly what they're getting.

revenue potential: 10 courses × 5 buyers each = $50k/year. that's hiring one person part-time to sell them, recording them in your spare time.

the game is: find 10 specific problems your audience has. build a 60-minute course for each. forget about "building a full curriculum". that's how courses die incomplete.
