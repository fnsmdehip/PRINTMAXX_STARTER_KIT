# Post 043 — Data Labeling Arbitrage

**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (6 tweets)
**Type:** Scaling Play
**Niche:** AI / Automation

---

hired 5 people on upwork to label data. charging clients 10x their hourly cost. profitable from month 1.

the play:
1. client needs X thousand images labeled (bounding boxes, classifications, etc)
2. they quote $15k (thinking it's hard)
3. i quote $8k and 1 week delivery
4. i pay upwork workers $15/hour, 40 hours each = $3k total cost
5. i pocket $5k, they get faster turnaround

why it works:
- clients think ai training data is specialized (it isn't)
- they don't know upwork rates
- they value speed over cost
- quality bar is actually low (you just need consistent labels)

scale:
- month 1: 1 project, $5k profit
- month 3: 3 projects concurrent, $15k profit
- month 6: 8-10 projects, hire a coordinator, $30k+ profit

the ceiling: you're limited by coordinator bandwidth, not your own time. eventually hire another coordinator.

risk: quality control. solve this by spot-checking 20% of labeling work.
