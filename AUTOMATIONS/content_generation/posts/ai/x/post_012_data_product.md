# Post 012 — Data Product Bundling

**Platform:** X (Twitter)
**Length:** Thread (4 tweets)
**Niche:** AI / Automation

---

bundled 6 months of competitive intelligence into a $47 ebook. sold 240 copies in 2 weeks. $11.3k revenue (gumroad takes cut).

the product: 147 pages of:
- competitor pricing analysis
- feature comparison matrices
- go-to-market playbooks
- customer win/loss reasons
- product roadmap predictions

i scraped nothing new. i compiled data i already collected (twitter, product hunt, g2 reviews, my own analysis).

the edge: done-for-you research sells because buyers don't want to do the work themselves. they'd rather pay $47 than spend 40 hours on research.

scaling: sell same ebook to 5 niches (saas, ecommerce, fintech, healthcare, education). that's $250k potential revenue if you hit 1k sales per niche.

revenue potential: high. time per book: low (mostly compilation). this is the actual micro-product meta.

only works if you already have an audience. but if you do, data products are nearly free money.
