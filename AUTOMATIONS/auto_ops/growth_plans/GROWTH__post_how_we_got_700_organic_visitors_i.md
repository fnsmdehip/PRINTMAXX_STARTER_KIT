## Tactics

1. Programmatic internal linking across all 47+ surge sites to boost domain authority cluster
2. Auto-submit to 50+ free web directories weekly (no-cost backlinks)
3. Cross-link all app landing pages to each other with contextual anchors
4. Repurpose each link-building win as a Twitter thread on SEO tactics (Rule 9 content)

## Budget Tier Strategies

### MID
$50-150/mo for guest post placements on DA30+ sites or niche-specific paid directories

- [ ] Run SEO audit on all 47 deployed sites using playwright crawler to find missing meta, broken links, thin content

- [ ] Compile list of 100 free directories and auto-submit top 20 sites weekly via cron

- [ ] Generate 3 tweets + 1 thread per week from SEO wins (Rule 9)
