# Growth Plan: I spent > $60K/month on PR agencies at a startup that raised

**Created:** 2026-03-20 18:35
**Venture:** OUTBOUND
**Budget Tier:** FREE
**Revenue Est:** $0-300/mo indirect (press drives traffic to existing monetized apps/products, compounds over time as backlinks boost SEO)

---

## Tactics

1. Piggyback trending news angles to boost pitch open rates
2. Cross-post press mentions across all social accounts for compounding credibility
3. Use press coverage screenshots as social proof on landing pages
4. Reply to journalists asking for sources on Twitter with our products as examples

## Budget Tier Strategies

### FREE
HARO responses (free tier), Twitter journalist engagement, direct email pitches from scraped contacts, press page on existing sites

### LOW
$0-50/mo: Connectively (HARO replacement) paid tier for more queries, custom domain for press@ email

### MID
$50-200/mo: Featured.com or similar press distribution for 1-2 key launches per month

- [ ] 1. Scrape journalist contacts covering indie apps, solopreneur tools, faith-tech, fitness-tech beats
