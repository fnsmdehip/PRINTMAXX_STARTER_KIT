**Created:** 2026-03-20 13:50

## Tactics

1. Warm up Facebook accounts with genuine engagement before posting affiliate content
2. Use value-first posts (80% helpful content, 20% soft affiliate mentions)
3. Target groups with 1K-50K members where moderation is lighter

## Budget Tier Strategies

### FREE
Join 10-20 niche groups, post value content with soft affiliate CTAs, use existing content from posting_queue repurposed for Facebook format

### LOW
$10-20/mo for GoLogin multi-account if scaling past 1 Facebook profile

### MID
$50-100/mo for Facebook ad boosting on top-performing organic group posts

## Daily Actions

- [ ] Scrape top Facebook groups in our niches (productivity, faith apps, dev tools) using Playwright
- [ ] Queue 2 value posts per week per group through content_repurposer with soft affiliate links
- [ ] Track which groups and post styles get engagement vs suppression
- [ ] Kill groups with zero traction after 30 days, double down on performers
