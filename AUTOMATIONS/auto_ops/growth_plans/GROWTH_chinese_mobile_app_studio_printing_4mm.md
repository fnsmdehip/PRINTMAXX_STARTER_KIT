# Growth Plan: Chinese mobile app studio printing $4M/month with simple AI 

**Created:** 2026-03-20 13:50
**Venture:** APP
**Budget Tier:** FREE
**Revenue Est:** $200-2000/mo per app, $1000-8000/mo across portfolio of 5-8 identifier apps

---

## Tactics

1. ASO keyword stacking: target '[thing] identifier', '[thing] scanner', 'identify [thing]', 'what [thing] is this' — high intent, low sophistication competitors
2. Cross-promote between own identifier apps (CoinSnap users might want AntiqSnap)
3. TikTok/Reels viral loops: film scanning random objects with the app, satisfying reveal format
4. Reddit seeding in niche communities (r/coins, r/Antiques, r/PlantIdentification) with genuine helpful posts using the app
5. Build free web version as SEO funnel that upsells to app for camera features
6. Subscription pricing psychology: $4.99/week with 3-day free trial (industry standard for this category)

## Budget Tier Strategies

### FREE
ASO optimization, cross-app promotion, TikTok/Reels organic content showing app in action, Reddit community seeding, web landing pages for SEO, app review solicitation via in-app prompts

### LOW
$0-50/mo: Apple Search Ads basic campaigns on exact-match identifier keywords (typically $0.30-0.80 CPI in these niches), boost 2-3 TikTok posts showing satisfying scan reveals

### MID
$50-200/mo: Apple Search Ads scaled to competitor keywords, micro-influencer gifting in niche communities (coin collectors, plant lovers, TCG players), Facebook/IG ads targeting hobby demographics
