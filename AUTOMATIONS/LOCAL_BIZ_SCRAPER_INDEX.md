## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need coding skills?**
A: No. Just copy/paste the command. The script works out of the box.

**Q: How do I find business URLs?**
A: Google Maps, Yelp, Yellow Pages. Manually compile into CSV. 50 URLs takes 20 minutes.

**Q: What if I don't know web development?**
A: Use WordPress + Elementor (drag-and-drop). YouTube has thousands of tutorials. Or hire a VA to build for $8-$12/hour.

**Q: Can I guarantee results?**
A: Guarantee deliverables (mobile site, SSL, SEO), NOT revenue (you don't control their business).

**Q: What if they already have a website?**
A: That's the point. You're fixing their OLD, BROKEN website with a MODERN one.

**Q: How do I get testimonials?**
A: Offer first 3 projects at cost ($200-$300) in exchange for testimonial + data access.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: Yes. Scraping public business websites for prospect identification is legal. Don't spam, respect opt-outs, follow CAN-SPAM.

**Q: What about competition?**
A: Thousands of local businesses, minimal competition. Most web agencies focus on large clients ($10K+ projects). You're in the underserved $500-$2K market.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**"Missing dependencies" error:**
```bash
pip install requests beautifulsoup4 tqdm
```

**No emails found:**
Common for sites using contact forms. Use LinkedIn to find decision maker or call directly.

**Connection timeout:**
Increase `--rate-limit` to 3.0 or higher. Check internet connection.

**Output CSV is empty:**
Check input CSV format. Must have `url` column at minimum.
