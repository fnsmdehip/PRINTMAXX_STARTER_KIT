## 3. Account Setup Requirements

**Age verification:** Required on ALL monetization platforms. Government-issued ID + selfie match. OnlyFans, LoyalFans, iWantClips all mandate 2257 compliance (US record-keeping for adult content).

**Compliance essentials:**
- Valid government ID (18+)
- 2257 records if producing visual content
- Tax documentation (W-9 for US, W-8BEN international)
- Separate business bank account recommended (processors flag personal accounts)
- Platform TOS compliance: OnlyFans prohibits certain findom language in DMs, LoyalFans is more permissive

**Payment processor risk:** High-risk merchant category. Chargebacks are the #1 revenue killer. CashApp/Venmo have zero seller protection. Platform-based payments (OF, LoyalFans) handle disputes but take 20%+ cut as the tradeoff.

## Business Model Summary

**Revenue structure:** Tributes (40-60% of income), clip sales (20-30%), subscriptions (10-20%), wishlists (5-10%). Top accounts in the 30K-50K range report $5K-$20K/mo, but earnings claims in this niche are notoriously inflated. Median is likely $1K-$3K/mo at 10K-20K followers.

**Unit economics:** Near-zero COGS (phone camera + Twitter account). Platform fees are the primary cost (20-40%). Time investment is the real cost: 2-4 hours/day for DM management at scale.

**Key risk:** Platform deplatforming. Twitter/X periodically purges adult accounts. Diversification across 2-3 monetization platforms is standard practice.
