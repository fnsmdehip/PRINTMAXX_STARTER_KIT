**The gap:** Instantly ($37-$97/month) sells email infrastructure. Apollo ($49-$119/user/month) sells leads. Durable ($15-$95/month) sells DIY website building. Nobody combines all three into a service that outputs $500-$2,000 websites for local businesses.

**Action plan:**

3. Build websites using AI builders (cost: $3-$25/month)
4. Sell at $500-$2,000 per website + optional $29/month hosting

**Revenue estimate:** 10 websites/month at $750 average = $7,500/month. Plus $29/month hosting x growing base. Infrastructure cost: ~$80/month (Smartlead + AI builder).

**Effort:** Low-Medium. Scraper exists. Cold email templates exist. AI builder does the production work.
