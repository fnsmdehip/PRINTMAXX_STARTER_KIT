**COMPETITIVE GAPS WE CAN EXPLOIT:**

1. **Denomination-specific apps don't exist.** Hallow = Catholic only. Glorify = generic Christian. Nobody serves Orthodox, Lutheran, Methodist, Baptist, Sikh, Buddhist with dedicated daily apps. We have 13+ built. This is a real moat.

3. **Bible Chat (AI Bible Q&A) is growing fast** at 312K reviews and 4.93 rating. We have zero AI-powered faith features. Consider adding AI chat to PrayerLock.
4. **Hevy proves bootstrapped fitness apps work.** $600K/mo with no funding, no paid marketing, pure organic. Our fitness streak app is far simpler but could capture the "I just want a streak" segment.
5. **Opal's $99/yr pricing creates room.** Our FocusLock is free. Position as "the free alternative that actually works."

**THREAT LEVEL:** LOW-MEDIUM. We're not competing head-to-head with $157M-funded apps. We're going after the long tail they ignore (denomination-specific, free tier, streak mechanics, PWA accessibility).

### 4. LOCAL BIZ (OpenClaw Nationwide)

**Our assets:** OpenClaw model, dental-demo on surge.sh, local biz website scraper, 2.87M business leads.

| Competitor Type | Pricing | Approach |
|----------------|---------|----------|
| Fiverr web design gigs | $30-$419 (avg $261) | Portfolio-based, wait for orders |
| Local agencies | $2,000-$5,000+ | Relationship-based, high-touch |
| Wix/Squarespace DIY | $17-$36/mo | Self-service, templates |
| Durable.co (AI) | $12-mo | AI generates site in 30 seconds |
| B12.io | $249/mo | AI + human design team |
| 10Web | $10-$60/mo | WordPress AI builder |

**COMPETITIVE GAPS:**

1. **The $149-$299 gap.** Agencies charge $2K+. Fiverr avg is $261 but inconsistent quality. DIY is cheap but business owners don't want to DIY. We can offer AI-generated, human-reviewed sites at $149-$299 -- faster than agencies, higher quality than cheap Fiverr, and turnkey (not DIY).
2. **Durable.co is the real AI competitor** -- generates business websites in 30 seconds for $12/mo subscription. But their sites look generic. Our OpenClaw model can beat them on customization + local SEO.
3. **Preview-first outreach is our killer advantage.** Build the site BEFORE the cold email. "I built your new website, check it out at dental-demo.surge.sh" converts 5-10x better than "would you like a website?"
4. **Fiverr charges sellers 20% of earnings.** Direct outreach keeps 100% of revenue.

**THREAT LEVEL:** MEDIUM. AI site builders are the long-term threat but currently produce generic output. Our preview-first + cold outreach model is differentiated.
