## Nuclear Blocker: robots.txt

**This is the single most damaging issue in the entire SEO portfolio.**

surge.sh injects `Disallow: /` into every deployed site's robots.txt by default. This means:

- All 262 sites are completely invisible to Google and Bing
- All 262 sites are invisible to AI search engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT browsing, Claude web search)
- Every piece of structured data, every keyword-optimized title tag, every comparison page is wasted until this is fixed
- The programmatic SEO build at `builds/programmatic_seo/` (600+ pages) has zero indexing

**Fix in progress this cycle:** Custom robots.txt files are being deployed to each site directory, overriding surge.sh's default before the CDN serves them.

**Long-term fix:** Migrate the top 20 highest-value sites to Vercel or Cloudflare Pages. Both platforms serve user-supplied robots.txt without overriding it. This eliminates the blocker permanently for priority assets.
