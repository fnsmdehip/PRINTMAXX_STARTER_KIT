## NEW THREATS DETECTED

### THREAT 1: Microsoft Auto-Publishes PWAs

Microsoft Store auto-publishes qualifying PWAs discovered via Bing indexing. We have 7+ PWAs deployed on surge.sh that are NOT being indexed by Bing. This is free app store distribution we're leaving on the table.

**Action required:** Add proper PWA manifests, service workers, and meta tags to all deployed PWAs. Submit URLs to Bing Webmaster Tools. Microsoft may auto-list them.

### THREAT 2: Sanctum -- New Faith App Entrant

Sanctum (Bible Chat & Prayer) appeared on the App Store (ID: 6746232042). New competitor combining Bible chat with prayer tracking. Directly overlaps PrayerLock's positioning.

**Action required:** Monitor Sanctum's growth. If it gains traction, our denomination-specific apps become even more important as differentiation.

### THREAT 3: Durable.co Price Increase Creates Both Risk and Opportunity

Durable raised from $12/mo to $22/mo. Their free plan still exists but is limited. This creates a gap:
- Durable free plan -> limited
- Durable paid -> $22/mo ($264/yr)
- Our one-time -> $149-$299

**Opportunity:** "Pay once, own forever" positioning against Durable's $264/yr subscription. After 7 months, Durable costs MORE than our one-time price.

### THREAT 4: Whop Marketplace Explosion

Whop now has 18.4M users and $3B annual GMV with 25% monthly growth. Gumroad is stagnant by comparison. Whop's fees are also lower (2.7% vs Gumroad's 10%).

**Action required:** Prioritize Whop listings over Gumroad. The marketplace discovery alone could drive sales without our own traffic.

### THREAT 5: Cold Email Market Consolidation

The big 4 (Instantly, Smartlead, Saleshandy, Lemlist) are all adding AI features. Instantly now has 450M+ lead database built in. Smartlead added webhooks/API for technical users.

**Impact on us:** We don't compete with these tools. We USE them. Monitor pricing changes to pick cheapest option when we start sending. Saleshandy at $25/mo is currently cheapest for unlimited emails.
