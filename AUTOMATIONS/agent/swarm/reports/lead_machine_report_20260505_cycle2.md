### New Patterns Identified This Cycle

**1. Medical practices on GoDaddy/Wix = maximum credibility gap**
Breathe Easy Allergy & ENT (Dallas) showed the sharpest disconnect: a doctor using Gmail + GoDaddy subdomain signals "I cut corners" before a patient speaks to them. The medical segment hasn't been targeted in prior cycles. Additional niches: chiropractors, physical therapists, optometrists, veterinarians, dermatology practices. All are high-trust, high-ticket, and patient-acquisition-dependent.

**2. Netlify.app = harder trust kill than GoDaddy**
PowerWash Pros (Nashville) on netlify.app shows a unique angle: consumers don't know Netlify, so the domain looks like a tech company or software project — not a contractor. This creates MORE confusion than godaddysites.com. Search for more local service businesses (contractors, trades, small retail) on netlify.app.

**3. HVAC summer timing is the highest-dollar urgency in the contractor space**
A missed week of HVAC calls in Phoenix in June = $2,000-5,000 in lost service revenue. No other niche has this per-week loss multiplier. Add Tucson AZ, Las Vegas NV, Dallas TX, Bakersfield CA — same seasonal urgency, same GoDaddy/Wix subdomain vulnerability. These are all HOT leads in May.

**4. Pool service + pool retail are separate verticals but same urgency**
Blue Wave Pool Service (Tampa) and Lakeside Pool & Spa (Atlanta) both operate in the pool ecosystem with the same May urgency but different pitch angles. Pool service = route stops lost. Pool retail = foot traffic lost to franchises. Run both searches together next cycle.

**5. Event rental graduation timing is hyper-specific**
OSU graduation dates are public. Every major university has published spring commencement dates. Future cycles can target event rental companies in: Columbus OH (OSU done, high school next), Ann Arbor MI (UMich), Gainesville FL (UF), Baton Rouge LA (LSU), College Station TX (TAMU). Graduation party bookings = 30-40% of annual event rental revenue for local companies.
