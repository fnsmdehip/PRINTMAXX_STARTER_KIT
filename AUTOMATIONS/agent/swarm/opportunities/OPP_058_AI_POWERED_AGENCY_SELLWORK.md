## What

Sell finished deliverables (landing pages, email sequences, ad copy, mini-apps) using AI to produce them at 10-20x speed. Price as agency work ($500–$5,000 per project), not as AI access ($50/mo SaaS). The margin is in the gap between AI production cost (~$2) and perceived value of the finished work (~$2,000).

YC explicitly stated in their Spring 2026 RFS: "Don't sell access to an AI tool for $50/mo. Use the AI yourself and sell the finished work for $5,000."
