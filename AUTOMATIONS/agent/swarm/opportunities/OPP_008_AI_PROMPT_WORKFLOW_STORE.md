## What

Create and sell AI prompt packs, Claude Code workflow templates, and automation bundles on Gumroad/Lemon Squeezy. Package our actual working systems (scraper prompts, content generation prompts, cold email templates, ralph loop configs) into sellable digital products.

## Why

- AI-enhanced digital products = fastest-growing category globally in 2026
- 80-95% profit margins on digital products (vs 30-50% physical)
- Notion templates alone generate millions on Gumroad — AI prompt packs are the 2026 equivalent
- We ALREADY HAVE the assets: 90+ scripts, 1,278 posts, 7 apps, 32 autonomous agents
- Claude Code is exploding — 50+ best MCP servers lists, plugin marketplaces emerging
- "The winning products in 2026 are 30-80 page actionable guides with checklists and frameworks"
- Zero marginal cost per sale

## How

1. **Product 1: "The Solopreneur Claude Code Starter Kit"** — 50 battle-tested prompts for building apps, scraping data, cold outreach, content generation. $19 on Gumroad.
2. **Product 2: "Ralph Loop Templates Pack"** — 10 pre-configured autonomous loop configs (content machine, alpha scanner, lead gen, etc). $29 on Gumroad.
3. **Product 3: "AI Cold Email Mastery Bundle"** — 25 cold email templates + Claude prompts for personalization + lead scoring framework. $39 on Gumroad.
4. **Product 4: "PWA Builder's Playbook"** — Step-by-step guide to shipping a PWA in 48 hours with Claude Code. 40 pages + templates. $24 on Gumroad.
5. **Content flywheel:** Each product generates 5+ tweets ("here's prompt #7 from my pack, it does X"), driving sales organically.

## Expected ROI

- Month 1: 4 products x 30 avg sales x $25 avg price = $3,000
- Month 3: Organic + SEO + word of mouth → 4 products x 80 sales x $25 = $8,000/mo
- Month 6: Bundle deals + new products → $12,000-15,000/mo
- Cost: $0 (we already have all the content)
