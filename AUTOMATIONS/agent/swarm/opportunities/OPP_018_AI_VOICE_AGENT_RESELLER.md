# OPP-018: AI Voice Agent Reseller for Small Businesses

**Score:** 8.0/10 (Fit: 7, Effort: 6, ROI: 9)
**Startup Cost:** $99-299/month (white-label platform fee)
**Time to First Revenue:** 2-3 weeks
**Monthly Potential:** $5,000-20,000
**Competition:** Low-Medium (voice AI is newer than chatbots, fewer agencies offer it)

## What

Resell AI phone bots to small businesses that handle inbound calls, appointment booking, FAQ answering, and after-hours coverage. Platforms like Trillet, Bland.ai, or Vapi handle the voice AI. You white-label, brand, and sell to local businesses at $497-997/month.

## Why Now

- Voice AI agents market climbing from $8B (2025) to $11.78B (2026)
- Cost per minute: $0.09 avg. Even at $197/mo pricing, margins are healthy
- Small businesses lose 62% of calls that go to voicemail (industry stat)
- After-hours coverage alone justifies $497/mo for most businesses
- Adjacent to OPP_015 (chatbots) but higher value, less competition
- Our cold outbound pipeline can target the same local biz leads
- Dental offices, contractors, restaurants have the highest pain point

## How

1. Sign up for Trillet or Bland.ai white-label voice agent platform
2. Build 3 industry templates: dental (appointment booking), restaurant (orders/reservations), contractor (quote requests)
3. Create demo recordings showing the AI handling real call scenarios
4. Price: $497/mo (basic, business hours) or $997/mo (24/7 + appointment booking)
5. Cold call or email local businesses from existing lead lists
6. Offer free 7-day trial to reduce friction

## Expected ROI

- Platform cost: ~$0.09/min + $99-299/mo base
- Average small biz gets 50-200 calls/month = $4.50-18/mo in AI costs per client
- 10 clients at $497/mo = $4,970/mo revenue, ~$4,500 profit
- 30 clients at $697/mo avg = $20,910/mo revenue, ~$19,500 profit
- HIGHER retention than chatbots (phone is mission-critical for small biz)

## First 3 Steps

1. Sign up for Bland.ai or Vapi developer account, build 1 demo voice agent for dental office scenario
2. Record a demo call video showing the AI booking an appointment naturally
3. Cold email 30 dental offices from existing leads with: "your patients call after hours. we answer. free trial."

## Stack Fit

Cold outbound infrastructure ready. Local biz leads already scraped. Python can handle integrations. Voice AI platforms are no-code/low-code. Cross-sell with OPP_015 chatbots and OPP_014 automation packages.

## Risk

- Voice quality must be convincing (test multiple providers)
- HIPAA concerns for healthcare (mitigate: use HIPAA-compliant platforms)
- Call volume variability affects per-minute costs (mitigate: set monthly caps)
