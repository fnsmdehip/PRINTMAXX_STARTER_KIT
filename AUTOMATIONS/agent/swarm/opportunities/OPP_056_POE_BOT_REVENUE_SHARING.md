## What

Poe (by Quora) is currently the ONLY major AI platform that pays creators directly through per-message revenue sharing. Creators build bots using Claude, GPT-4, Llama, or custom models — Poe pays per message the bot receives, plus creators can set their own pricing for premium bot access.

Revenue model:
- **Free bots:** Poe pays a small per-message rate from subscription revenue pool
- **Paid bots:** Creator sets price per message ($0.01-0.50+) — Poe handles billing
- **Subscription bots:** Monthly fee gated access to your bot

The opportunity: build 5-10 niche-specific AI bots and list them on Poe. No audience required — Poe brings the users.
