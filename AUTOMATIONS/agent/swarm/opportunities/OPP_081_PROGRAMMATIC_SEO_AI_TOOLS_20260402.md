2. Build a Python scraper to extract all product names, categories, and buyer questions from their marketplaces (ArtStation, Booth.pm, Gumroad)

4. Deploy on Vercel/Cloudflare Pages (free tier)
5. Monetize via affiliate links to Gumroad/ArtStation products (30-50% commission), AdSense, and our own matching digital products
