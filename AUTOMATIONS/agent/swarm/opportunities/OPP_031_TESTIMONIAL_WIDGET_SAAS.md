## What

Build a lightweight testimonial collection + embeddable widget tool. Customers get a unique link, submit text/video testimonials, and the business owner embeds a widget on their site showing social proof. Target: solopreneurs, indie hackers, course creators.

## Why

- Famewall reached $1K MRR in 12 months with this exact model
- Senja, WiserReview, Boast.io all in this space = validated demand
- Starting price $9.99/mo = low friction for solopreneurs
- Our audience (solopreneurs, indie hackers) IS the target market
- Cross-sell: bundle with our landing page audit tool (PageScorer) and cold email services
- Zero startup cost to build on our stack

## How

1. Next.js app: Dashboard for managing testimonials + public collection page per user
2. Embeddable widget: JS snippet that renders testimonial carousel/grid on any site
3. Stripe subscriptions: Free (3 testimonials) + Pro ($9.99/mo, unlimited) + Agency ($29.99/mo, white-label)
4. Deploy on Vercel
5. Differentiator: AI-generated "social proof snippets" from testimonials (auto-creates tweets, email quotes, landing page copy from raw testimonials)

## Expected ROI

- Build time: 3-4 days
- Startup cost: $0
- Revenue month 1: $50-150 (launch + organic)
- Revenue month 6: $500-1,000
- Revenue month 12: $1,000-3,000
- Market validated by Famewall, Senja, Boast.io

## First 3 Steps

1. Build MVP: Collection page (text input + star rating) + simple dashboard to approve/reject. Embed widget as `<script>` tag. 2 days.
2. Create landing page with clear pricing. Add to printmaxx.surge.sh as a product. Set up Stripe. 1 day.
3. Launch on IH, r/SideProject, X. Offer free lifetime plan to first 50 users for testimonials (meta!). 0.5 days.
