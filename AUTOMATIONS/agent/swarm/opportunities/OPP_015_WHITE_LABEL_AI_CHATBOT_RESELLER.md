# OPP-015: White-Label AI Chatbot Reseller

**Score:** 8.5/10 (Fit: 8, Effort: 7, ROI: 9)
**Startup Cost:** $99-399/month (white-label platform fee)
**Time to First Revenue:** 1-2 weeks
**Monthly Potential:** $4,000-14,000 (at 20 clients)
**Competition:** Medium (growing but most agencies still don't offer this)

## What

Resell white-label AI chatbots to small businesses (restaurants, dental offices, law firms, salons, contractors). Platforms like Stammer.ai, Trillet, or SiteSpeakAI handle all the tech. You brand it, sell it, collect recurring monthly fees.

## Why Now

- One person can manage 20-40 chatbot clients since AI handles most conversations
- Agencies report $200-700/month profit per client after platform costs
- Top agencies with 50+ clients report $20K-40K/month in profit
- Whitelabel platforms cost $99/mo (3 clients) to $399/mo (unlimited)
- 60% of small businesses plan to increase freelance/service hiring in 2026 (Fiverr report)
- We already have cold outbound infrastructure (MM007) to find clients
- Value-based pricing justifiable: if AI captures 10 extra leads/mo at $500 avg value, $997/mo is easy sell

## How

1. Sign up for Stammer.ai or Trillet white-label plan ($99-299/mo)
2. Brand it under a PRINTMAXX sub-brand (e.g., "ChatMaxx" or similar)
3. Create 3 vertical-specific templates: restaurants, dental, contractors
4. Price: $497/mo (basic) or $997/mo (premium with voice)
5. Use existing cold outbound pipeline to prospect local businesses
6. Upsell existing LOCAL_BIZ leads who already showed interest

## Expected ROI

- Platform cost: $99-399/month
- 5 clients at $497/mo = $2,485/mo revenue, ~$2,100 profit
- 20 clients at $497/mo = $9,940/mo revenue, ~$9,500 profit
- Client churn is low (sticky service, hard to switch once trained on their data)
- Cross-sell: website redesign, SEO, automation packages from OPP_014

## First 3 Steps

1. Sign up for Stammer.ai free trial and build 1 demo chatbot for a restaurant vertical
2. Create a landing page showcasing the chatbot with live demo
3. Cold email 50 local restaurants from existing AUTOMATIONS/leads/ pipeline with offer: "free 14-day chatbot trial"

## Stack Fit

Cold outbound pipeline already built. Local biz lead lists already scraped. Python automation handles onboarding. Zero custom code needed - pure resale arbitrage on top of existing infrastructure.

## Risk

- Platform dependency (mitigate: build on 2+ platforms)
- Client support overhead (mitigate: create FAQ docs, video tutorials)
- Price pressure as more agencies enter (mitigate: lock in annual contracts early)
