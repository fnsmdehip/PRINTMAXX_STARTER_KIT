# OPP-017: Whop Marketplace Digital Product Portfolio

**Score:** 8.3/10 (Fit: 8, Effort: 5, ROI: 8)
**Startup Cost:** $0
**Time to First Revenue:** 2-3 days
**Monthly Potential:** $1,000-5,000
**Competition:** Medium (growing marketplace but specific niches still open)

## What

List our existing digital products on Whop instead of (or alongside) Gumroad. Whop is a marketplace-first platform with organic discovery. Buyers browse and find products without needing to visit our website. $2B+ already sold through the platform. Lower fees (6% vs Gumroad's 10%).

## Why Now

- Whop has organic marketplace discovery (Gumroad does NOT)
- $2B+ sold through Whop = proven buyer traffic
- 6% total fees vs Gumroad's 10% = better margins at every level
- We already have 5+ digital products built (cold email playbook, twitter growth playbook, AI automation toolkit, vibe coding playbook, local biz client system)
- ALPHA_MICRO_INFO_001 already validated $29-39 micro products strategy
- Brazilian creators on Whop have 4.79% breakout rate vs 1.65% USD (emerging market angle)
- Whop supports communities, courses, AND digital downloads (consolidate platforms)

## How

1. Create Whop seller account
2. List all existing digital products from DIGITAL_PRODUCTS/ folder
3. Optimize listings with Whop-specific SEO (categories, tags, descriptions)
4. Price at $29-49 impulse zone per ALPHA_MICRO_INFO_001 strategy
5. Create 2-3 new micro products per week targeting Whop trending categories
6. Use Whop's built-in affiliate system to get others promoting our products

## Expected ROI

- Setup time: 1-2 days (listing existing products)
- Whop organic traffic = free customer acquisition
- 100 sales/mo at $34 avg = $3,400/mo (Whop takes $204, we keep $3,196)
- Same products on Gumroad = $3,400 - $340 fees = $3,060 (but no organic discovery)
- Compound: each new product adds to portfolio, marketplace ranking improves

## First 3 Steps

1. Create Whop seller account and brand page
2. List top 3 existing products (Cold Email Playbook, AI Automation Toolkit, Vibe Coding Playbook)
3. Create 1 new micro product specifically for Whop trending categories (check what's hot on Whop homepage)

## Stack Fit

Products already exist. Zero dev work. Pure distribution arbitrage. Whop handles payments, delivery, and provides organic traffic we're not getting on Gumroad.
