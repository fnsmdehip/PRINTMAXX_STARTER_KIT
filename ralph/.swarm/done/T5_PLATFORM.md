## CRITICAL DEBUNKING: Facebook Reels $4.40/1K Claim

**VERDICT: DEBUNKED (Confidence: 95%)**

The claim that Facebook Reels pays $4.40/1K views is MISLEADING:

- **What it was:** An invite-only legacy bonus program that paid top performers in premium geographies
- **What happened:** Facebook sunset Performance Bonuses, Ads on Reels, and Reels Play Bonus on August 31, 2025
- **Current reality:** Most creators earn $0.02-$0.20/1K views through the new Content Monetization Beta
- **Premium tier:** Only creators with US/UK/CA/AU audiences see $2-$5 RPM, and this requires high engagement
- **Source validation:** zeely.ai, Medium Monetization Playbook 2026, post-bridge.com all confirm the lower ranges

**RECOMMENDATION:** Remove $4.40/1K claim from alpha staging. Replace with $0.02-$0.20 (typical) or $2-$5 (premium geo only).

**Multiplier: 6-14x vs YouTube Shorts**

**Multiplier: 1.9x per subscriber**
- Kick: 95/5 split = $4.75 per $5 sub
- Twitch: 50/50 split = $2.50 per $5 sub
- **Confidence: 85%** - Consistent across all sources
- **Caveat:** Twitch has 140M MAU vs Kick's smaller audience

### 3. Whop vs Gumroad Fees
**Savings: ~55% lower fees**
- Whop: 3% + 2.7% Stripe = ~5.7-6.2% total
- Gumroad: 10% + $0.50 + 2.9% Stripe = ~13-14% total
- Gumroad Discover: Additional 30% = 40%+ total
- **Confidence: 90%** - Fee structures publicly documented
- **At $10K revenue:** Whop saves $740-$830/month vs Gumroad direct, $3,400+/mo vs Gumroad Discover

### 4. Beehiiv Scale vs Substack
**Savings: ~3x at scale**
- Substack: 10% + ~3% Stripe = ~13% always
- Beehiiv Scale: 0% + ~3% Stripe = ~3% only
- **Confidence: 85%** - At $5K/mo saves $100/mo ($1,200/yr)
- **Break-even:** Beehiiv becomes better around $2,500+/mo revenue

### 5. X/Twitter Revenue Share (2026 Changes)
**Status: IMPROVING**
- Early 2026 payouts doubled/tripled for many creators
- ~$8.50/1M verified impressions (Premium user views only)
- Subscriptions: 97% up to $50K, then 80%
