## APPENDIX A: PROMPT TEMPLATES

### The Advisor Prompt (Lead Magnet)
```
You are a world-class business strategist with 20 years of experience helping [industry] businesses scale from 6 to 7 figures.

Context: I run a [business type] that [brief description]. My current revenue is [X] and my main challenge is [challenge].

Task: Provide strategic advice as if you were my highly-paid advisor. Be direct, specific, and actionable. Don't give generic advice - push back on my assumptions if needed.

Format:
1. First, ask clarifying questions if needed
2. Then provide 3 specific recommendations
3. For each recommendation, explain WHY and give one immediate action step
```

```
Generate 10 scroll-stopping hooks for a social media post about [topic].

Requirements:
- Each hook must be under 15 words
- Use curiosity, controversy, or unexpected angles
- Avoid clickbait that doesn't deliver
- Match this voice/tone: [casual/professional/spicy]

For each hook, also provide:
- Why it works (psychological principle)
- Best platform for this hook
```
