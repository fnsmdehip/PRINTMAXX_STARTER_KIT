# Instagram Reel - AI002

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** AI | **Structure:** CS002 - Results Reveal | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

I used to work 50 hours/week. Now 20. Same revenue.

## Before Screenshot (2-10 seconds)

Calendar fully booked. Inbox at 200 unread emails. To-do list: 47 items.

## What Changed (10-20 seconds)

Spent one weekend automating everything:
- Email responses: Claude API
- Meeting scheduling: Calendly + Zapier
- Report generation: n8n workflow

## After Screenshot (20-25 seconds)

Calendar: 3 hours of focused work
Inbox: Empty every day
To-do list: 5 items (only revenue stuff)

## The Point (25-30 seconds)

Automation freed up 30 hours/week for actually building.

That's where the money comes from.

Setup guide in bio.

## Production Notes
- Strong visual contrast (before/after)
- Time impact is quantified
- Outcome is revenue (not just "less busy")
- Aspirational but believable
