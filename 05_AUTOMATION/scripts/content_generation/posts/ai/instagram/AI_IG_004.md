# Instagram Reel - AI004

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** AI | **Structure:** CS004 - Myth Bust | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Most people get AI wrong.

They think it makes them smarter.

It doesn't.

## The Myth (2-10 seconds)

Slide 1: "AI will do all my work"
Slide 2: "I just need the right prompt"
Slide 3: "AI is magic"

All false.

## Reality (10-20 seconds)

AI is a tool. Like a hammer.

A hammer doesn't build a house. You do.

AI doesn't build your business. You do.

AI just removes friction. That's it.

## The Fix (20-28 seconds)

Stop asking: "What can AI do?"
Start asking: "What bores me?"

Automate the boring stuff. Spend your time on things only you can do.

## Payoff (28-30 seconds)

That's how AI actually makes money.

Strategy guide in bio.

## Production Notes
- Myth busting format is engaging
- Challenges hype and false expectations
- Reframes AI as a tool, not magic
- Practical reframing (focus on boring tasks)
