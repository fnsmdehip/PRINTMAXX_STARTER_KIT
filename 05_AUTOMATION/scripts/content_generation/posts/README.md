## Platform Optimization

### X/Twitter (18 posts)
**Best for:** Specific insights, frameworks, short stories
**Format:** 280 character max + link in bio
**CTA:** Reply (drives conversation)
**What works:** Numbers, contrarian takes, quick wins

Example: "I spent $200 on AI tools. Should've spent $0. Reply 'STACK' for my 3-tool stack."

### TikTok (16 posts)
**Best for:** Visual transformations, tutorials, emotional hooks
**Format:** 30-45 seconds, Hook→Problem→Solution→Result→CTA
**CTA:** Comment or Bio (platform algorithm favors both)
**What works:** Relatability, speed, humor, drama + resolution

Example: "I automated my job. Now I work 15 hours/week. Here's how."

### Instagram (16 posts)
**Best for:** Comprehensive listicles, comparisons, detailed guides
**Format:** Carousel (5-7 slides) or 30-45 second reel
**CTA:** Bio link (drives to landing page)
**What works:** Visual polish, before/afters, comprehensive value

Example: "5 AI tools that replaced my $5k/month agency" (5-slide carousel)
