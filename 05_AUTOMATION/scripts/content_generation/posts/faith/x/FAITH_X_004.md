Most people get faith wrong.

They think it's a feeling.

It's not. Feelings come and go.

Faith is a decision. Every single day.

On good days: "I trust God."
On terrible days: "I trust God."
On days when it feels like nothing: "I trust God."

Proverbs 3:5-6 - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart."

All your heart includes the days you don't feel it.
