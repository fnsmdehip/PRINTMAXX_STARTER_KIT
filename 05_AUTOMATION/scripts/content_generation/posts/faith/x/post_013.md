The ANCHOR method for daily faith:

A - Acknowledge (gratitude)
N - Navigate (scripture)
C - Commit (one action)
H - Honor (evening review)
O - Overcome (what tried to stop you)
R - Repeat

12 minutes/day. Unbreakable faith.
