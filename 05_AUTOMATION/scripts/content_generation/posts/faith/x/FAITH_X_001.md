Discipline is built in micro reps.

Not "I'll pray for 30 minutes every morning."

Instead: Pray for 2 minutes right now. Again in 2 hours. Again tonight.

Small wins compound. Big commitments break.

Proverbs 22:29 - "Do you see a man skilled in his work?"

Skill comes from reps. Not intensity.

Comment 'STREAK' if you're starting small today.
