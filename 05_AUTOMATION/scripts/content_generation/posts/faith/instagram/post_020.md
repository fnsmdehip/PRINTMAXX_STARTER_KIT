The 12-minute morning routine that changed my faith:

1. Acknowledge (2 min) - 3 things I'm grateful for
2. Navigate (5 min) - One scripture, one reflection
3. Commit (5 min) - One faith action for today

Evening check-in:
4. Honor - Did I keep my commitment?
5. Overcome - What tried to stop me?

Repeat. Don't break the streak.
