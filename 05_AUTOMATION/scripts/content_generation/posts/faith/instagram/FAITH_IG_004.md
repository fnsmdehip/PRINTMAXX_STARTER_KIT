# Instagram Reel - FAITH004

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS004 - Myth Bust | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Slide: "Most people get discipleship wrong"

## Myth Slide (2-10 seconds)

Slide 1: "Discipleship = Bible study"
Slide 2: "More knowledge = stronger faith"
Slide 3: "Learning all the theology = being like Jesus"

All false.

## Reality (10-20 seconds)

Slide: Jesus' method

"Follow me" (Matthew 4:19)

Jesus didn't teach theology. He lived it.

His disciples weren't scholars. They were fishermen, tax collectors, ordinary people.

They learned by watching. By doing. By failing. By trying again.

## The Fix (20-28 seconds)

Slide: "Real discipleship looks like:"

Finding someone ahead of you. Learning from them.
Becoming someone behind them. Teaching them.

Repeat.

It's not about information. It's about imitation.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Slide: "Who are you following? Who's following you?"

Discipleship guide in bio.

---

## Production Notes
- Challenges academic faith
- Returns to Jesus' actual method
- Simple, relatable language
- Empowers average people (not scholars)
- Creates accountability (both follower and leader)
