# Instagram Reel - FAITH005

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS005 - Tutorial Speed | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Start this today. Takes 15 minutes.

## Step 1 (2-8 seconds)

Slide: "Pick one book of the Bible"

Show: Bible app interface

Pick something short (Philippians, 1 Peter, Proverbs)

Not the whole Bible. Just one.

## Step 2 (8-14 seconds)

Slide: "Read it all today"

(If it's short, do it all at once)

Or: Chapter per day for 1 week

Don't over-think. Just read.

## Step 3 (14-22 seconds)

Slide: "Journal one thing you noticed"

What jumped out?
One question?
One thing you'll do differently?

That's the study. One thing. Depth over quantity.

## Step 4 (22-28 seconds)

Slide: "Share it with one person"

Send to a friend. Invite them to do it too.

Accountability makes it stick.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Slide: "Start today. One book. One week."

Full plans in bio.

---

## Production Notes
- 15-minute barrier to entry
- Specific timeframe (one week)
- Depth over breadth
- Community component
- Immediately actionable
