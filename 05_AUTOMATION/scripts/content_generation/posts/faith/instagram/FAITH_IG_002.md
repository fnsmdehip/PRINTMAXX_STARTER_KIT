# Instagram Reel - FAITH002

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS002 - Results Reveal | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

I prayed consistently for 6 months. Here's what changed.

## Slide 1: Before (2-8 seconds)

Visual: Overwhelmed person
"Anxious. Struggling. Angry at God."

Praying: Never
Bible reading: Never
Church: Sometimes (guilt-driven)

## Slide 2: The Shift (8-14 seconds)

"Decided: I'm going all in for 6 months"

Daily: 10-minute prayer
Weekly: Full chapter reading
Monthly: Joined small group

## Slide 3: After (14-22 seconds)

Visual: Peaceful person
"Less anxious. More purpose. Actually hopeful."

Situations didn't change. My response did.

## Slide 4: The Data (22-26 seconds)

Anxiety attacks: 3/week → 1/month
Sleep quality: Terrible → 7+ hours
Relationships: Strained → Strong

All from showing up consistently.

## CTA Slide (26-30 seconds)

"Consistency compounds"

Full 6-month plan in bio.

---

## Production Notes
- Real metrics (anxiety, sleep, relationships)
- Timeline is specific (6 months)
- Honest: situations didn't change, response did
- Visual before/after persuades
- Practical disciplines listed
