# Instagram Reel - FAITH001

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS016 - Daily Devotional | **CTA:** Comment | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook Slide (0-2 seconds)

Slide 1: "3 habits that changed my faith"

## Habit 1 (2-10 seconds)

Slide: "Micro prayers (not macro praying)"

Before: Tried 30-min prayer sessions. Lasted 2 weeks.
After: 2 minutes, 3 times a day.
Changed: Everything

Proverbs 22:29 shows that mastery comes from reps.

## Habit 2 (10-18 seconds)

Slide: "Read scripture IN context"

Before: Random verses from social media
After: Full chapters. Whole books.
Changed: Actually understanding God's heart

2 Timothy 2:15 - "Present yourself to God... rightly handling the word"

## Habit 3 (18-26 seconds)

Slide: "Accountability > Perfection"

Before: Hid struggles from church
After: Real friends. Real conversations.
Changed: Real growth

1 Thessalonians 5:11 - "Encourage one another"

## CTA Slide (26-30 seconds)

Slide: "Start with one. Add others later."

"Comment which one you're trying this week"
