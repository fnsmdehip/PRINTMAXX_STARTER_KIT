# TikTok Post - FAITH001

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS016 - Daily Devotional | **CTA:** Comment | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Your faith isn't weak because you pray 2 minutes.

It's strong because you pray every day.

## Scripture (2-10 seconds)

1 Thessalonians 5:17 - "Pray without ceasing."

This doesn't mean 8 hours on your knees.

It means constant connection. Constant conversation.

## Application (10-20 seconds)

Try this today:

Morning: Pray on your commute (2 min)
Afternoon: Pray before lunch (2 min)
Night: Pray before bed (2 min)

That's 6 minutes total. That's the whole day filled with Him.

## Challenge (20-28 seconds)

Quality isn't determined by time. It's determined by heart.

A 2-minute desperate prayer is worth more than 30 minutes of habit.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Start today. Comment 'STREAK' if you're in.
