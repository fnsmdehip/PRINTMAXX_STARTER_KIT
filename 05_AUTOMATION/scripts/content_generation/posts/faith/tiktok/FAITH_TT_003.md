# TikTok Post - FAITH003

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS001 - Problem-Solution | **CTA:** Comment | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Stop waiting to feel spiritual.

## Problem (2-10 seconds)

You're waiting for the big feeling.

The moment in church. The song that hits. The sermon that changes everything.

But faith isn't a feeling. It's a discipline.

## Solution (10-22 seconds)

Wake up and decide: Today I trust God.

Not because you feel it.

Because you choose it.

Pray anyway. Read anyway. Believe anyway.

Especially when you don't feel like it.

## Why This Works (22-28 seconds)

Feelings follow actions.

Do the work. The feeling catches up.

Matthew 21:22 - "Therefore I tell you, whatever you ask for in prayer, believe that you have received it"

Believe. Then feel. Not the other way.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Comment 'CHOOSE' if you're choosing faith today.
