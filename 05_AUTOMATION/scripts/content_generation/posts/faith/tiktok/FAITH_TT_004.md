# TikTok Post - FAITH004

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS004 - Myth Bust | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-3 seconds)

Most people think doubt means weak faith.

Wrong.

## Myth (3-10 seconds)

Show text overlay: "Real faith never questions"
Show text overlay: "Doubt means you're not faithful enough"

Both are lies.

## Truth (10-20 seconds)

Doubt is where faith lives.

If you never doubt, you never really believe. You just memorize.

Doubt is you wrestling with God. That's biblical.

David doubted. Job doubted. Even Jesus asked why He was abandoned.

## Fix (20-28 seconds)

Stop hiding your doubt.

Bring it to Him. Wrestle with it. Your faith gets stronger, not weaker.

Psalm 34:1 - "I sought the Lord and he answered me"

He wants your real questions. Not your fake certainty.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

You're not alone. Full guide in bio.
