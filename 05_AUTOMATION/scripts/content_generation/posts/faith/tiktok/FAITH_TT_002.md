# TikTok Post - FAITH002

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS017 - Accountability | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-3 seconds)

I tried reading the Bible alone for 3 years.

Never finished even once.

## Problem (3-10 seconds)

No accountability = no finish line.

I'd start strong. By week 2: stopped.

By month 2: forgotten.

## Solution (10-20 seconds)

Found 2 friends. Same Bible plan.

Every Sunday: Text proof (screenshot of where we are)
Every Wednesday: Quick call (5 minutes. What we learned)

## Result (20-28 seconds)

First time ever: Finished the Bible in a year.

Then again the next year.

Now it's a habit.

Proverbs 27:12 - "As iron sharpens iron"

You can't sharpen yourself alone.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Find your people. Full guide in bio.
