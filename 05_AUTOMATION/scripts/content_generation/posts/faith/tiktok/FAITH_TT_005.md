# TikTok Post - FAITH005

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Faith | **Structure:** CS021 - Relationship Bait | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-3 seconds)

My brother just came back to church after 10 years and told me this.

## Setup (3-12 seconds)

Text message conversation:
Brother: "Haven't been in 10 years. Felt broken."
Me: "What changed?"
Brother: "Realized I was waiting to be perfect before coming back."

## Reveal (12-22 seconds)

"You don't have to be fixed to go to church. You go to get fixed."

He went last Sunday. Week before his birthday.

Everyone knew his story. Nobody judged.

They just welcomed him.

## Wholesome Payoff (22-28 seconds)

Now he goes every week.

The gate wasn't perfection.

It was community.

Hebrews 10:25 - "Not giving up meeting together"

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

That's what church is supposed to be.

Full welcome guide in bio.
