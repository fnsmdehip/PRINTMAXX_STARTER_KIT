Most people think soreness means it worked.

Wrong.

Soreness means you did something your body hasn't adapted to yet.

It's data. Not validation.

Proof: Pro athletes aren't sore. Beginners are sore.

The goal isn't soreness. It's progress.

Stop chasing the burn. Chase the numbers (weight lifted, reps added, strength gained).
