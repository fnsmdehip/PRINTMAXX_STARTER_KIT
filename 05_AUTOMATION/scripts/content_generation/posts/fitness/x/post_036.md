If you haven't lifted in 6+ months, start here:

Week 1-4: Empty bar only. Learn the movements.
Week 5-8: Add weight slowly. Focus on form.
Week 9-12: Progressive overload. Add 5lbs/week.

No ego. No rush. Build the foundation first.

The people who skip this step get hurt.
