The people in the best shape at my gym:

- Train 3-4 days/week
- Focus on compound lifts
- Have been consistent for years

The people spinning their wheels:

- Train 6 days/week
- Do a different program every month
- Wonder why nothing works

Consistency beats intensity. Every time.
