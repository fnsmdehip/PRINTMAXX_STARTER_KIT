## Reality (10-20 seconds)

Slide: "What actually matters"

Protein goal: 0.7-1g per pound of body weight per day.

That's it. Spread across meals.

Doesn't matter if it's:
- Chicken or powder
- Post-workout or breakfast
- Timing is irrelevant. Total matters.

Slide: "Real rules"

Hit daily total. Any source. Any timing.
Consistency > optimization
Train hard. Eat enough. Rest.

Those 3 things > any supplement or timing hack.
