# Instagram Reel - FITNESS005

**Platform:** Instagram Reels | **Niche:** Fitness | **Structure:** CS005 - Tutorial Speed | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

Build this routine in 20 minutes. Use it for a year.

## Step 1 (2-8 seconds)

Slide: "Pick 3 main lifts"

Squat (legs + core)
Bench press (chest + arms)
Deadlift (back + legs)

That's your foundation.

## Step 2 (8-14 seconds)

Slide: "Schedule"

Monday: Squats + accessory
Wednesday: Bench + accessory
Friday: Deadlift + accessory

Accessories = isolation (bicep curls, leg press, etc)

## Step 3 (14-22 seconds)

Slide: "Rep range"

Main lift: 5-8 reps (heavy)
Accessory: 8-12 reps (moderate)
3 sets each. Done in 45 minutes.

Simple. Repeatable. Proven.

## Step 4 (22-28 seconds)

Slide: "Track it"

Log every weight. Every rep.

Week 1: Bench 185 x 8
Week 4: Bench 185 x 10

Progressive overload. That's progress.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Slide: "Full workout log in bio"
