10 hours/week in the gym = no time for life

So I did nothing for 2 years.

Then I found the minimum effective dose:
- 3 hours/week
- Just squats, bench, deadlifts
- Add weight every session

12 weeks later: Strongest I've ever been.

Time matters. Use less of it better.
