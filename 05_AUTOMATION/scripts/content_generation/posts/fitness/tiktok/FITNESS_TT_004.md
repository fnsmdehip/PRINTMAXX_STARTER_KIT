## Hook (0-3 seconds)

Most people get abs wrong.

Abs aren't built in the gym.

## Myth (3-10 seconds)

Show text: "Do more abs exercises = more abs"
Show text: "Crunches = six pack"
Show text: "Abs exercises matter most"

All false.

## Truth (10-22 seconds)

Abs are 80% diet. 20% training.

You can do a thousand crunches. If you eat pizza daily, nobody sees them.

But eat clean for 12 weeks + train hard 3x/week?

Abs appear without dedicated ab work.

Progressive overload on compound lifts > 100 crunches.

## The Real Path (22-28 seconds)

Strong core development = compound lifts (squats, deadlifts, rows)
Visible abs = consistent nutrition

That's it. That's the whole formula.
