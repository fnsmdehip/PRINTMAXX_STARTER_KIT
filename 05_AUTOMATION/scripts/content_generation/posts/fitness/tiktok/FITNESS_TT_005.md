# TikTok Post - FITNESS005

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Fitness | **Structure:** CS021 - Relationship Bait | **CTA:** Bio | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-3 seconds)

I haven't heard from my gym buddy in 3 weeks and he just sent me this.

## Setup (3-12 seconds)

Text: "Haven't been to the gym. Just want you to see something"

Photo: His physique progress (3-week progress pic)

Actually bigger. More defined.

Me: "How did you do this without training?"

Him: "I finally fixed my diet. That's literally it."

## Reveal (12-22 seconds)

He ate the same way I do (thinking he was training hard).

I was training hard with bad diet.

We both looked the same.

He removed the barrier (bad diet).

Now: One transformation happening without gym time.

Mine? Stuck.

## Wholesome Payoff (22-28 seconds)

That's when I realized: Diet isn't secondary.

It's THE primary. Training is the second priority.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Full nutrition guide in bio.
