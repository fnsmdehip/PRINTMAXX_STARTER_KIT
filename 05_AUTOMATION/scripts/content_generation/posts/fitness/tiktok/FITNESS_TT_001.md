# TikTok Post - FITNESS001

**Platform:** TikTok/Reels | **Niche:** Fitness | **Structure:** CS018 - Results Focus | **CTA:** Comment | **Length:** 30-45 seconds

---

## Hook (0-2 seconds)

I look more jacked at 3 workouts/week than I did at 6.

Here's why.

## Problem (2-10 seconds)

Show: Busy person stressed, going to gym every day

Most people do 6 days/week thinking more = faster results.

Reality: You burn out. You quit. You get nowhere.

## Solution (10-22 seconds)

Switch to 3 days:

Monday: Upper body (45 min)
Wednesday: Lower body (45 min)
Friday: Full body (45 min)

4 days rest. Recovery is when you grow.

## Result (22-28 seconds)

Visible muscle in 6 months.
Visible strength in 12 months.
People asking about your routine in 18.

## CTA (28-30 seconds)

Comment 'SPLIT' for the exact program.
