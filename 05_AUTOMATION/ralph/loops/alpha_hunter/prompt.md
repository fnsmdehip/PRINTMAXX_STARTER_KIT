# Alpha Hunter Research Loop

**Mission:** Find NOVEL money-making methods, edge tactics, and opportunities we haven't documented yet. Think like an Ivy League hedge fund quant hunting for alpha.

---

## The Alpha Hunter Mindset

1. **Arbitrage everywhere** - Price differences = opportunity
2. **Platform asymmetries** - New features, algorithm changes, policy gaps
3. **Information asymmetry** - What do insiders know that normies don't?
4. **Timing edge** - Early to trends = max profit
5. **Automation edge** - What can be 10x'd with AI/bots?

---

## Your Task Each Iteration

1. Read `.ralph/progress.md` for completed categories
2. Pick ONE category to research
3. Find 3-5 novel opportunities with PROOF
4. Write to `LEDGER/ALPHA_STAGING.csv` with category `ALPHA_HUNTER`
5. Update progress.md
6. Output `<promise>COMPLETE</promise>` when all done

## Research Categories

### 1. PLATFORM_ARBITRAGE
**Goal:** Find price/feature differences between platforms

**Search:**
- "sell on X buy on Y arbitrage 2026"
- Platform fee comparisons
- New platform launches with incentives
- Geographic arbitrage (US vs international pricing)

**Examples to find:**
- Kindle Unlimited vs other ebook platforms
- Fiverr vs Upwork pricing gaps
- US vs international software pricing
- New marketplace launch incentives

### 2. API_WRAPPERS_UNTAPPED
**Goal:** APIs that could become products

**Search:**
- "API products ideas"
- New APIs launched (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc)
- Developer tools trending on GitHub
- "Built with [API name]" searches

**Find:**
- APIs with no good wrappers yet
- APIs with free tiers to exploit
- Combining multiple APIs into something new
- Verticalized API products (API for lawyers, doctors, etc)

### 3. COMMUNITY_GOLDMINES
**Goal:** Paid communities and their gaps

**Search:**
- Skool.com trending communities
- Discord paid servers
- Patreon creator niches
- "Paid community $X/month" posts

**Find:**
- Niches without good paid communities
- Communities with bad execution to outcompete
- Community + product stacks
- Community flywheel opportunities

### 4. AUTOMATION_OPPORTUNITIES
**Goal:** Manual processes that can be automated and sold

**Search:**
- "I spend X hours on [task]" complaints
- Virtual assistant task lists
- Repetitive business processes
- "How to automate [X]" searches with no good answers

**Find:**
- High-value manual tasks (billing, reporting, etc)
- Processes with clear input/output
- Things people pay VAs $20+/hr to do
- Industry-specific automation gaps

### 5. EMERGING_PLATFORMS
**Goal:** New platforms with early-mover advantage

**Search:**
- "New social media app 2026"
- Product Hunt for new platforms
- VC funding announcements for consumer apps
- "Alternative to [big platform]" growing

**Find:**
- Platforms pre-monetization (build audience before ads)
- Platforms with creator funds launching
- Platforms with algorithm favoring new creators
- Platform migration opportunities

### 6. REGULATORY_ARBITRAGE
**Goal:** Legal opportunities from regulation differences

**Search:**
- "Legal in [state] illegal in [state]"
- New legislation creating opportunities
- Compliance-as-a-service gaps
- Industry deregulation news

**Find:**
- Cross-state service arbitrage
- New compliance requirements = new service opportunities
- Sunset clauses creating urgency
- Gray areas being clarified (legitimizing edge tactics)

### 7. DEMOGRAPHIC_SHIFTS
**Goal:** Underserved demographics with money

**Search:**
- "Gen Alpha spending habits"
- "Boomer technology adoption"
- "Hispanic market growth"
- Demographic spending reports

**Find:**
- Demographics with money but poor product options
- Cultural niches underserved by mainstream
- Age groups adopting new tech late
- Immigrant/diaspora market opportunities

### 8. CONTENT_FORMAT_EDGE
**Goal:** New content formats before saturation

**Search:**
- "New content format going viral"
- Platform feature launches
- Creator tool launches
- "This format is blowing up"

**Find:**
- Platform features with low competition
- AI content formats (AI voice, AI video)
- Interactive content opportunities
- Cross-platform content arbitrage

### 9. PRICING_PSYCHOLOGY
**Goal:** Pricing tactics and monetization innovations

**Search:**
- "Pricing strategy that worked"
- "Changed pricing model revenue increased"
- Freemium vs premium debates
- Usage-based pricing examples

**Find:**
- Underpriced markets (should charge more)
- Overpriced markets (undercut opportunity)
- Novel pricing models (reverse trials, etc)
- Bundling/unbundling opportunities

### 10. TALENT_ARBITRAGE
**Goal:** Skill gaps and talent market opportunities

**Search:**
- "Can't hire [skill]"
- "Learning [skill] in demand"
- Upwork/Fiverr trending skills
- Job posting trends

**Find:**
- Skills in demand, few suppliers
- Skills about to be in demand (AI-adjacent)
- Geographic talent arbitrage
- Training/certification gaps

### 11. DATA_PRODUCTS
**Goal:** Data that could be packaged and sold

**Search:**
- "I wish I had data on [X]"
- "Where to find [X] data"
- Data broker alternatives
- Industry-specific data needs

**Find:**
- Publicly scrapeable valuable data
- Data aggregation opportunities
- Real-time data feeds gaps
- Niche industry data needs

### 12. PHYSICAL_DIGITAL_BRIDGES
**Goal:** Physical world opportunities with digital leverage

**Search:**
- "Local business [X] going digital"
- "Offline to online [industry]"
- QR code business models
- Phygital trends

**Find:**
- Local services that should be apps
- Physical products needing digital companions
- Event/experience digitization
- Hybrid model opportunities
