# Content Farm Research Loop

## Context
You are scanning for new content farm opportunities across YouTube, TikTok, and other platforms. Focus on formats that generate passive revenue through ads, creator fund, or sponsorships.

## Task
1. Check state.md for the next uncompleted category
2. Research that category using web search and available sources
3. Write findings to disk at the specified output location
4. Mark the category as complete in state.md
5. Output `<promise>COMPLETE</promise>` when all categories done

## Sources to Scan
- YouTube trending: Timer channels, lofi beats, ASMR, nature sounds
- TikTok Creator Fund: High RPM content types
- algrow.online: Terminated channels (arbitrage opportunities)
- r/juststart: Content site strategies
- @Jonnyvandel: Mass content automation patterns
- @FearedBuck, @kirawontmiss: Clip channel strategies

## Research Categories
1. **Timer/Countdown formats** - Revenue data, best performing lengths
2. **Lofi/ambient formats** - Channel analysis, revenue estimates
3. **ASMR/sleep formats** - What's trending, monetization methods
4. **News/compilation formats** - Faceless news channels, sports highlights
5. **Quote/motivation formats** - Text overlay content, engagement rates
6. **Clip/reaction formats** - Streamer clips, commentary patterns

## Output Format
For each opportunity found, create a file at:
`LEDGER/CONTENT_OPPORTUNITIES/[date]_[format_type]_[name].md`

Include:
- Source URL or channel examples
- Revenue data (if available): RPM, views, estimated monthly
- Content creation workflow
- Tools needed
- Estimated time to produce
- Cross-pollination with other methods (apps, AI influencer, etc.)
- Risk level (copyright, demonetization)

## Quality Filter
APPROVE if:
- Has specific revenue numbers or social proof
- Replicable within our current stack
- Clear production workflow

DIG DEEPER if:
- High engagement but vague method
- Successful channel but unclear monetization
