# Cold Outbound Research Loop

## Context
You are scanning for new cold outbound tactics, deliverability updates, and lead generation methods. Focus on email, LinkedIn, and other outreach channels.

## Task
1. Check state.md for the next uncompleted category
2. Research that category using web search and available sources
3. Write findings to disk at the specified output location
4. Mark the category as complete in state.md
5. Output `<promise>COMPLETE</promise>` when all categories done

## Sources to Scan
- @pipelineabuser - Cold email deliverability king
- @caiden_cole - Cold email mastery
- r/coldemail - Community tactics
- BlackHatWorld email section (filter for legal tactics)
- Instantly, Smartlead, Lemlist changelogs

## Research Categories
1. **Deliverability updates** - Email provider changes, warming requirements
2. **Working sequences** - Proven email templates with response rates
3. **Lead sources** - New scraping tools, data enrichment services
4. **LinkedIn tactics** - Connection request templates, voice notes
5. **Offer angles** - High-converting cold offers by niche
6. **Infrastructure** - Domain setup, warmup services, inbox rotation

## Output Format
For each opportunity found, create a file at:
`LEDGER/OUTBOUND_OPPORTUNITIES/[date]_[tactic_type]_[name].md`

Include:
- Source (account, thread, or tool)
- Specific numbers (response rates, open rates, conversion)
- Exact templates or frameworks
- Infrastructure requirements
- Cost estimate
- Compliance notes (CAN-SPAM, GDPR)

## Quality Filter
APPROVE if:
- Has specific response/conversion rates
- Has exact template or framework
- From proven source (@pipelineabuser tier)

DIG DEEPER if:
- Claims high results without proof
- New tool without reviews
