# DISCOVERY ENGINE - Meta Vision Intelligence System

**Mindset:** Not "optimize what we have" but "discover what we're missing"

**Rule:** If it exists and makes money, we should know about it. If we don't, the system failed.

**Goal:** Never ask "what should we build?" Always know the answer from continuous discovery.

## META VISION APPROACH (NOT PRESCRIPTIVE CATEGORIES)

**The user's critical clarification:**

> "Those examples (demographic arbitrage, geographic arbitrage, etc.) were just examples. I want you to have META VISION and intelligently scrape out ALL the opportunity from ALL the research. Don't prescriptively say 'do arbitrage' - the PROCESS of research should find arbitrage opportunities AND other kinds of opportunities."

**What this means:**

❌ **WRONG:** "Check for geographic arbitrage. Check for demographic arbitrage. Check for..."
✅ **RIGHT:** "Research this space deeply. What opportunities exist? The process will surface arbitrage, gaps, transient windows, method stacks, platform shifts, regulation changes, and opportunities we haven't even categorized yet."

**The intelligence is in the PROCESS, not the categories.**

## THE RESEARCH PROCESS (Meta Vision Framework)

### Phase 1: DEEP IMMERSION (Absorb Everything)

**Scan the entire landscape of a space:**

```
For ANY research target (app category, niche, platform, method):

1. TREND SOURCES (what's happening NOW):
   - GitHub trending (if technical)
   - X/Twitter trending + high-signal accounts
   - Product Hunt launches
   - HackerNews frontpage
   - Reddit top posts (last week)
   - YouTube trending (if applicable)

2. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE:
   - Top 20 apps/products in space
   - Revenue estimates (data.ai, SensorTower, public metrics)
   - User reviews (1-star, 5-star, recent)
   - Founder Twitter accounts
   - Launch stories (IndieHackers, blogs)

3. COMMUNITY SIGNALS:
   - Reddit communities (r/[topic], what are people complaining about?)
   - Discord servers (what tools/tactics being shared?)
   - Facebook groups (what questions repeat?)
   - Twitter threads (what's going viral?)

4. MARKET DATA:
   - Search volumes (if SEO-relevant)
   - Ad costs (if paid acquisition space)
   - Job postings (growing industries hire)
   - News articles (macro trends)
   - Regulatory changes (windows opening/closing)
```

**Don't search FOR specific opportunities. Just ABSORB the space.**

---

### Phase 2: PATTERN RECOGNITION (See What Others Miss)

**While immersing, your brain should be asking:**

#### OPPORTUNITY DETECTION QUESTIONS:

**Gap Analysis:**
- What do users want that doesn't exist?
- What exists but is overpriced/complicated/ugly?
- What works in Space A but doesn't exist in Space B yet?
- What demographic is underserved by existing solutions?

**Arbitrage Detection:**
- Does this work in Country A but not Country B? (geographic)
- Does this work for Men but not Women? (demographic)
- Does this work as B2C but not B2B? (market)
- Does this work as app but not as service? (business model)
- Does this work on Platform A but not Platform B? (distribution)

**Timing Windows:**
- Is this trend early (transient alpha opportunity)?
- Is regulation about to change (compliance = moat)?
- Is a platform algo changing (distribution shift)?
- Is a big player about to exit (market void)?
- Is technology enabling this for first time (tech unlock)?

**Stack Synergies:**
- Does this combine with our existing methods?
- Does this unlock a new method we haven't considered?
- Does this solve a problem we have in another op?
- Does this 10x one of our current lanes?

**Revenue Signals:**
- Are people paying for this (proof of demand)?
- What's the monetization model (ads, subs, transactions)?
- What's the ARPU/LTV (unit economics work)?
- What's the CAC (can we acquire cheaper)?
- Is there a high-ticket upsell (value ladder)?

**Competitive Moats:**
- Can we build this faster/cheaper/better?
- Do we have an unfair advantage (existing audience, tech, network)?
- Is the barrier to entry low enough to move fast?
- Is the market winner-take-all or multi-player?

**DO NOT check these as a TODO list. Let them run as BACKGROUND PROCESSES while you research.**

### Phase 3: INTELLIGENT EXTRACTION (Capture Opportunities)

**As patterns emerge, extract them:**

#### EXTRACTION FORMAT:

For EVERY opportunity your meta vision sees, ask:

1. **WHAT is the opportunity?** (1 sentence)
2. **WHY does it exist?** (gap, arbitrage, timing, tech unlock)
3. **WHO is it for?** (demographic, geography, psychographic)
4. **HOW big is it?** (market size, revenue potential)
5. **WHEN should we act?** (transient window, evergreen)
6. **PROOF?** (revenue screenshots, user demand, trending metrics)
7. **MOAT?** (can we compete, what's our advantage)
8. **STACK?** (cross-pollinates with what we have)
9. **EXECUTION?** (time to build, resources needed, first steps)

**Save to appropriate LEDGER file:**
- New niche → `LEDGER/NICHE_OPPORTUNITIES.csv`
- New method → `LEDGER/METHOD_OPPORTUNITIES.csv`
- App clone → `LEDGER/APP_CLONE_OPPORTUNITIES.csv`
- Platform meta → `LEDGER/PLATFORM_META.csv`
- Tool alpha → `LEDGER/TOOL_OPPORTUNITIES.csv`
- Cross-pollination → `LEDGER/CROSS_POLLINATION_MATRIX.csv`
- Arbitrage (any type) → `LEDGER/ARBITRAGE_OPPORTUNITIES.csv`
- Transient windows → `LEDGER/ALPHA_STAGING.csv` (mark `window_closes: [date]`)

**If it doesn't fit existing files, CREATE a new tracker. Don't force-fit.**

### Phase 4: STRESS TESTING (Jane Street Discipline)

**Every opportunity gets challenged:**

#### HEDGE FUND STRESS TEST:

**Base Rate Check:**
- How many attempts in this space? How many succeed?
- What's the failure mode? (regulatory, platform ban, market timing)
- Survivorship bias? (seeing winners, missing failures)

**Math Check:**
- Revenue claim: $50K/mo → 1,000 users × $50 = realistic?
- Growth rate: 20% MoM → from what base? Sustainable?
- Unit economics: LTV $100, CAC $30 → does this hold at scale?
- Time to breakeven: 6 months → assumes what conversion rate?

**Moat Analysis:**
- Can we compete? (yes/no + reasoning)
- Unfair advantage needed? (audience, tech, capital, network)
- Defensibility? (brand, network effects, switching costs, IP)
- What happens if 10 competitors launch tomorrow?

**Regulatory Risk:**
- FTC compliance? (synthetic media, testimonials, income claims)
- Platform TOS? (automation limits, API changes, bans)
- International law? (GDPR, data localization, export controls)
- Licensing required? (financial, health, legal services)

**Platform Risk:**
- Dependent on one platform? (Apple, Meta, Google)
- Platform has history of killing this? (Twitter API, Instagram API)
- Algorithm change kills this? (TikTok, YouTube)
- Can we own distribution? (email list, app installs, owned channels)

**Window Analysis:**
- First-mover advantage real or hype?
- When does window close? (competition, regulation, tech commoditization)
- Is this a 6-month play or 10-year play?
- Can we pivot if wrong?

**IF an opportunity fails stress test but still has signal:**
- Mark `risk_level: HIGH` with `risk_notes: [specific concerns]`
- Still track it (conditions may change)
- Maybe small bet test (<$500, <40 hours)

**IF it passes stress test:**
- Mark `validated: TRUE`
- Add to GTM priority queue
- Calculate ROI ranking vs other ops

### Phase 5: CROSS-POLLINATION (Method Stacks)

**Every validated opportunity should be asked:**

#### SYNERGY ANALYSIS:

**Can this opportunity:**
- **Feed another method?** (app drives newsletter, newsletter drives course)
- **Be fed by another method?** (content farm promotes app)
- **Share infrastructure?** (same payment, same hosting, same VA)
- **Share audience?** (fitness app + fitness persona + fitness affiliate)
- **Reduce CAC?** (own distribution vs paid ads)
- **Increase LTV?** (value ladder, upsell path)

**Example:**
```
Opportunity: "FocusIndia" app (Opal clone for India market)

Synergy scan:
- Feeds: Newsletter (productivity tips for Indian students)
- Fed by: Content farm (study motivation posts on Instagram India)
- Shares: Payment infrastructure (Paytm already integrated for other apps)
- Shares audience: N010_Students niche (StudyLock app + FocusIndia = stack)
- Reduces CAC: Instagram content farm drives free installs
- Increases LTV: Free app → $1.20/mo sub → $15 productivity course → $150 coaching
- Cross-pollination score: 92/100 (HIGH)
```

**Update `LEDGER/CROSS_POLLINATION_MATRIX.csv` with new synergies.**

---

### Phase 6: PRIORITIZATION (ROI Ranking)

**Portfolio approach:** We have 88+ methods, 33+ niches, finite time/capital.

#### RANKING CRITERIA (Weighted):

**Revenue Potential (35%):**
- Year 1 revenue estimate (conservative)
- Profit margin (after CAC, COGS, platform fees)
- Scalability (linear, network effects, winner-take-all)

**Time to Revenue (25%):**
- How fast can we launch? (2 weeks vs 6 months)
- How fast to first dollar? (day 1 vs month 3)
- Compounding or one-shot? (recurring vs one-time)

**Effort Required (20%):**
- Dev hours (if applicable)
- Manual labor hours (VA, human-in-loop)
- Ongoing maintenance (set-and-forget vs constant babysitting)

**Risk Adjusted (15%):**
- Regulatory risk (FTC, platform TOS, legal)
- Platform dependency (own distribution vs rented)
- Competitive moat (can defend vs race to bottom)
- Failure mode severity (lost time vs reputational damage)

**Strategic Fit (5%):**
- Cross-pollination score (0-100)
- Synergy with existing ops
- Learn valuable skills (transferable to other ops)

**CALCULATE: Weighted ROI Score (0-100)**

**Add to `LEDGER/GTM_OPTIMIZATION_PRIORITIES.csv` with ROI rank.**

**TOP 10 highest ROI → Active execution queue**
**11-30 → Validated backlog**
**31+ → Monitor for condition changes**

## INTEGRATION INTO MEGA LOOP

**The mega loop should NOT have prescriptive categories like:**
- ❌ "INT-07 Geographic Arbitrage"
- ❌ "INT-08 Demographic Arbitrage"
- ❌ "INT-09 New Niche Discovery"

**Instead, research categories should be:**
✅ **DR-00: REAL-TIME META SCAN** (catch trends same-day)
✅ **DR-01: APP_FACTORY DEEP DIVE** (immerse in app space, meta vision extracts ALL opportunities)
✅ **DR-02: CONTENT_FARM INTELLIGENCE** (immerse in viral content, meta vision finds format/platform/niche opportunities)
✅ **DR-03: COLD_OUTBOUND RESEARCH** (immerse in deliverability/sequences, meta vision finds tool/service/arbitrage)
... etc.

**Each research category:**
1. Deep immersion (absorb landscape)
2. Meta vision (pattern recognition runs in background)
3. Intelligent extraction (capture ALL opportunities seen, not just prescribed ones)
4. Stress testing (Jane Street discipline)
5. Cross-pollination (synergy analysis)
6. ROI prioritization (weighted scoring)

**The PROCESS is meta vision. The OUTPUT is opportunities of all types.**

**NOT:** "I checked for arbitrage" ✅ FOUND arbitrage
**BUT:** "I researched the space deeply" ✅ META VISION surfaced arbitrage + 5 other opportunity types

**What's working?**

**What's not working?**

**How can we improve?**
- New tools discovered → integrate (Virlo example)
- New sources discovered → add to rotation

## LAUNCH PROTOCOL

**When mega loop runs in INTELLIGENCE phase, it should:**

1. **Pick a research target** (from rotation or priority queue)
2. **Deep immersion** (scan landscape, absorb)
3. **Let meta vision run** (pattern recognition in background)
4. **Extract opportunities** (ALL types, not prescribed)
5. **Stress test** (hedge fund discipline)
6. **Cross-pollinate** (synergy analysis)
7. **Prioritize** (ROI ranking)
8. **Save to LEDGER** (appropriate files)
9. **Update progress.md** (what was found)
10. **Flag for human review** (checkpoints/ if needed)

**DO NOT:**
- Run through checklist of opportunity types
- Force-fit findings into categories
- Stop at first opportunity found
- Skip stress testing
- Ignore cross-pollination

**Autonomous mode:** System runs overnight, discovers opportunities, saves to LEDGER, flags items for morning review. Human wakes up to 10-20 validated opportunities, ROI-ranked, ready to execute.

**This is Jane Street level. This is the game.**
