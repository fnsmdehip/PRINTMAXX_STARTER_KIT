### 1. Buying Warmed X Accounts
- **Risk:** Platform TOS violation. Account could be reclaimed or banned.
- **Mitigation:** Use AccsMarket/Fameswap (established marketplaces). Change email/password immediately. Warm with organic activity 3-5 days before posting. Use anti-detect browser.
- **Reward:** Skip 2-4 week new account penalty. Immediate reach.
- **Recommendation:** APPROVE with caution. Also create manual organic accounts for A/B comparison.

### 2. Buying Initial Engagement
- **Risk:** Platform detection of inauthentic engagement. Reduced organic reach if caught.
- **Mitigation:** Use engagement pods (legal, just coordination). Buy services that use real accounts. Only for first 48 hours to trigger algorithm.
- **Reward:** Algorithm boost. Content that gets initial engagement gets 10x organic reach.
- **Recommendation:** APPROVE for first 2-3 posts only, then organic.

### 3. Hard Paywall (No Free Tier)
- **Risk:** Lower initial download numbers. Some negative reviews ("too expensive").
- **Mitigation:** 7-day free trial softens the wall. 3-dismiss warm-to-hard escalation. A/B test against soft paywall.
- **Reward:** 8x revenue per user (ALPHA465). Higher quality users. Less support burden.
- **Recommendation:** STRONGLY APPROVE. Data is overwhelming. A/B test to validate.

### 4. AI UGC Video Content
- **Risk:** FTC enforcement ($51,744/violation if undisclosed). NY S.8420-A (June 2026) adds state requirements.
- **Mitigation:** ALWAYS disclose AI generation in bio, video overlay, and caption. Never present as real testimonials. Product demos only, not fake reviews.
- **Reward:** 10x content production speed. Professional quality at $6-24/mo vs $100+ per human creator.
- **Recommendation:** APPROVE with mandatory disclosure. Speed is the moat, not deception.

### 5. Cold Email to Legal Services
- **Risk:** CAN-SPAM compliance. Gmail 0.1% complaint threshold.
- **Mitigation:** AI personalization (unique per email, not templates). 45-word emails. Proper opt-out. Warmed inboxes. Multi-domain strategy. Target 8+ ICP score only.
- **Reward:** Legal services = 10% reply rate (highest vertical). $2,500-5,000 per closed deal.
- **Recommendation:** APPROVE with proper infrastructure (warmed inboxes, multi-domain, AI personalization).
