## CAN-SPAM Compliance (for any email promoting these products)

all promotional emails for these products MUST include:

1. **Sender identification:** your real name or business name and physical mailing address
2. **Unsubscribe mechanism:** clear unsubscribe link that works within 10 business days
3. **Accurate subject lines:** subject line must reflect actual email content
4. **"Ad" identification:** if the email is primarily promotional, it must be identifiable as an advertisement

example footer for promotional emails:

```
---
To unsubscribe from future emails, reply STOP or click here: {{UNSUBSCRIBE_LINK}}

{{YOUR_NAME}} | {{BUSINESS_ADDRESS}} | {{CITY}}, {{STATE}} {{ZIP}}

This is a commercial message sent in compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act.
```
