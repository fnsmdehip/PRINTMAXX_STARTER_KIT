## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

**Typography:**
- Headers: Bold serif (Playfair Display or similar)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- Scripture: Italic serif
- Size: 11-12pt body, 18-24pt headers

**Layout:**
- Page size: 8.5" x 11" (US Letter)
- Margins: 1" all sides
- Generous spacing (not cramped)
- Lines for writing (dotted or light grey)

**Visual Elements:**
- Anchor icon as page footer
- Simple line dividers between sections
- Checkboxes for commitments/tracking
- Streak tracker visual (Days 1-14 with circles to fill)
- Minimal color (navy + gold accents only)

**Printing:**
- Black & white friendly
- Single-sided or duplex compatible
- Coil binding recommended (lays flat)
