# 1. FAITH/PRAYER

## Top Viral Tweet Formats

1. **"God is not done with you" affirmation thread** - Simple declarative statements of faith that invite shares. Hook: "Read this if you woke up feeling like giving up." Accounts like @PreachersNFaith use this daily.

2. **Scripture + modern situation parallel** - Hook: "David had anxiety too. Here's what he did about it." Pairing biblical stories with relatable modern struggles (rent, job loss, heartbreak).

3. **"This prayer changed my life" personal testimony** - Hook: "I prayed this one prayer for 30 days straight. What happened next..." Storytelling format with cliffhanger hooks.

4. **Controversial opinion / hot take on church culture** - Hook: "Church hurt is real. But here's what nobody talks about..." High-engagement format because disagreement drives replies.

5. **Daily prayer thread (morning routine)** - Hook: "Your morning prayer for [Day of Week]:" These get massive bookmarks and are shared in group chats.

## Top TikTok Sound Trends

- "Holy Forever" by Chris Tomlin - used in transformation and testimony videos
- "God Did" (worship remix) - creative edits showing blessings/breakthroughs
- "God Loves You" (country-gospel crossover) - blending country roots with gospel passion
- "Praise God" (Dear God Remix) by ScorpionKiller - faith-through-struggle narratives
- Stephen McWhirter acoustic worship clips - direct-to-camera praise moments
- Ambient prayer/worship instrumentals for guided prayer content

## Top Ad Creative Formats

- **Raw UGC testimonials** outperform polished church productions 3-5x
- iPhone-shot "prayer walk" videos with native TikTok text overlays
- Before/after transformation stories (spiritual journey, not physical)
- Green screen format with Bible verse on screen, creator reacting

## Best App Store Copy Patterns

- First screenshot: main promise ("Your daily prayer, delivered")
- Reference specific use cases: morning prayer, bedtime devotional, anxiety relief

# APPENDIX: CROSS-NICHE VIRAL HOOK FORMULAS

## The 7 Universal Hooks That Work Across All Niches

1. **"I [did X] for [timeframe]. Here's what happened:"** - Experiment/transformation
2. **"Stop [doing X]. Do [Y] instead."** - Direct, contrarian advice
3. **"[Number] [things] nobody tells you about [topic]:"** - Curiosity gap + list
4. **"[Bold claim]. Here's proof:"** - Attention + evidence
5. **"I spent [time/money] testing [X] so you don't have to:"** - Research labor savings
6. **"Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"** - Drives replies through disagreement
7. **"The [topic] nobody is talking about:"** - Exclusivity/insider knowledge

## The E.H.A. Framework (Most Effective Viral Formula)

**E**motional trigger + **H**ook + **A**ction

- High-arousal emotions (awe, excitement, anger, humor, surprise) significantly outperform low-arousal emotions (sadness, contentment, neutrality)
- First 5-7 words determine read-through rate
- First 3 seconds determine video watch-through rate
- 90% of ad recall impact occurs in the first 6 seconds (TikTok data)

## X/Twitter Algorithm Key (2026)

- **Primary metric:** Time-on-post (longer engagement = more reach)
- **Strongest ranking factor:** Engagement velocity (speed of likes, replies, reposts in first 30-60 minutes)
- **15 replies in 10 minutes** will massively outperform **50 likes over 6 hours**
- Threads get 3x more engagement than single tweets
