## Placement Guidelines

### Where to Place Disclosures

**FTC Requirements:**
- Disclosures must be **clear and conspicuous**
- Must appear **before** the affiliate link when possible
- Must be visible without clicking "read more" or scrolling
- Cannot be hidden in fine print

**Best Practices:**

| Content Type | Disclosure Location |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Blog post | Top of post + near first affiliate link |
| Product review | Top of review |
| Email | Top of email body |
| Social post | In post text, not just hashtags |
| Video | Verbally + in description |
| Podcast | Verbally at start and near mention |

### What NOT to Do

**Do NOT:**
- Hide disclosures at the bottom of long posts
- Use vague language ("we may be compensated")
- Make disclosures the same color as background
- Use tiny font sizes
- Bury disclosures in menus or footers only
- Rely on platform-specific tools alone (use your own disclosure too)
- Assume everyone understands "affiliate" means commission

### Mobile Considerations

Disclosures must be visible on mobile devices:

- Use full-width disclosure boxes
- Test on multiple screen sizes
- Ensure text is readable without zooming
- Disclosures should not be cut off
