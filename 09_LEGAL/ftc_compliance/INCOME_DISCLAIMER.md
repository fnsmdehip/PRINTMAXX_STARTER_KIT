## What NOT to Say

### Prohibited Claims

**Never say (unless you have solid evidence):**

- "Guaranteed income"
- "Easy money"
- "Get rich quick"
- "Anyone can do it"
- "No experience needed" (if experience helps)
- "Works while you sleep" (without context)
- "Unlimited income potential" (without disclaimer)
- Specific income figures without disclaimer

### Problematic Phrasing

**Avoid:**
- "I made $X, and you can too" (implies guarantee)
- "Replace your income in 30 days" (unrealistic timeline)
- "Financial freedom is just one click away"
- "This system never fails"
- "Proven formula for success" (without evidence)

### Red Flag Phrases

If you use these, add strong disclaimers:

| Phrase | Required Disclaimer |
|--------|---------------------|
| "6-figure income" | Typical results + disclaimer |
| "Quit your job" | Risk disclosure |
| "Financial freedom" | Timeline and effort required |
| "Life-changing money" | Individual results vary |
| "Done for you" | Effort still required |

## Placement Guidelines

### Where Disclaimers Must Appear

| Location | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Sales pages | Before purchase button |
| Landing pages | Visible without scrolling |
| Testimonials | Adjacent to income claims |
| Webinars | At beginning and near offers |
| Ads | In the ad itself |
| Emails | In email body |
| Videos | Verbally + description |

### Formatting Requirements

Disclaimers must be:
- **Readable:** Not tiny text
- **Visible:** Not hidden or hard to find
- **Clear:** Plain language
- **Proximate:** Near the claims they relate to
