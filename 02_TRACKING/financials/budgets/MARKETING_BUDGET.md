# Marketing Budget Allocation

## Philosophy

- Organic first, paid second
- Prove ROI at small scale before scaling
- Content is compounding; ads are renting
- Budget for experiments, not hopes

---

## Bootstrap Phase ($0-1K/mo revenue)

**Marketing Budget: $0-50/month**

### Allocation

| Channel | Budget | Time Investment | Expected Return |
|--------|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| SEO/Content | $0 | 15 hrs/week | Long-term traffic |
| Social organic | $0 | 8 hrs/week | Audience building |
| Community engagement | $0 | 3 hrs/week | Trust + leads |
| Content promotion | $25-50 | 1 hr/week | Amplification |
| **Total** | **$0-50** | **27 hrs/week** | |

### Where to Spend $50

| Option | Cost | Use Case |
|--------|------|----------|
| Twitter boost | $25-50 | Boost top-performing tweet |
| Giveaway budget | $25 | Newsletter growth prize |
| Guest post tools | $15 | Outreach (Hunter.io) |
| Design assets | $10-20 | Canva Pro trial |

### What NOT to Buy Yet

- Paid ads on major platforms
- Influencer sponsorships
- PR services
- Conference sponsorships
- Fancy video equipment

### Campaign Structure

**Facebook/Instagram:**
```
Campaign Budget: $250/month (~$8/day)

Campaign 1: Cold traffic (60%)
- Interest targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Video + carousel creative

Campaign 2: Retargeting (40%)
- Website visitors (7, 30, 90 days)
- Email list
- Video viewers
```

**Twitter:**
```
Campaign Budget: $150/month (~$5/day)

Campaign 1: Promoted tweets (70%)
- Boost top-performing content
- Target competitor followers
- Interest + keyword targeting

Campaign 2: Follower campaign (30%)
- Grow targeted following
- Engagement focus
```

### Influencer Marketing

| Tier | Cost | Audience Size | Expected Results |
|------|------|---------------|------------------|
| Nano (1-5K) | $50-150 | 1,000-5,000 | 50-200 clicks |
| Micro (5-25K) | $150-500 | 5,000-25,000 | 200-1,000 clicks |
| Mid (25-100K) | $500-2,000 | 25,000-100,000 | 1,000-5,000 clicks |

**Selection criteria:**
- Engagement rate >3%
- Audience overlap with your ICP
- Previous sponsored content quality
- Authentic use of similar products

### When to Cut a Channel

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| CPA > 2x product price | Cut immediately |
| ROAS <1x after 2 weeks | Pause and analyze |
| No conversions after $200 | Test different creative |
| High clicks, no conversions | Landing page problem |

## Common Mistakes

1. **Spending before tracking** - Set up attribution first
2. **Scaling too fast** - 20% weekly increase max
3. **Ignoring organic** - Paid should amplify, not replace
4. **Wrong audience** - Broad targeting wastes money
5. **Weak creative** - Test 3-5 versions minimum
6. **Landing page mismatch** - Ad promise must match page
7. **Impatience** - Give campaigns 7-14 days of data
8. **Not retargeting** - Lowest CPA channel ignored
