## Traffic to Conversion Rates

### Conversion Funnel Benchmarks

| Stage | Conversion Rate | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| Visit → Click affiliate link | 2-8% | Depends on content relevance |
| Click → Landing page view | 80-95% | Link quality matters |
| Landing page → Sign up/trial | 5-20% | Vendor's conversion |
| Trial → Purchase | 20-60% | Vendor's onboarding |

### End-to-End Conversion

```
Overall Conversion = Click Rate x Landing Conv x Trial Conv

Example (SaaS affiliate):
- 5% click your affiliate link
- 15% start trial
- 40% convert to paid

Overall: 0.05 x 0.15 x 0.40 = 0.3%

Per 1,000 visitors: 3 paid customers
```

### Conversion by Content Type

| Content Type | Click Rate | Overall Conv | Why |
|--------------|------------|--------------|-----|
| Review post | 5-10% | 0.5-2% | High intent |
| Comparison post | 4-8% | 0.4-1.5% | Decision stage |
| Tutorial | 2-5% | 0.2-0.5% | Tool in context |
| Listicle | 1-3% | 0.1-0.3% | Scattered intent |
| General blog | 0.5-2% | 0.05-0.2% | Low relevance |

## Revenue Per 1,000 Visitors (RPM)

### Formula

```
Affiliate RPM = (Visitors x Click Rate x Conversion x Commission) / 1000 x 1000

Example:
- 1,000 visitors
- 5% click rate = 50 clicks
- 15% trial conversion = 7.5 trials
- 40% paid conversion = 3 customers
- $30 commission each = $90

RPM = $90 per 1,000 visitors
```

## Content ROI Analysis

### Time Investment vs Revenue

| Content Type | Hours to Create | Monthly Traffic | Monthly Revenue | ROI/Hour |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|----------|
| Review post | 6-10 hrs | 500-2,000 | $15-100 | $1.50-10/hr |
| Comparison | 8-15 hrs | 1,000-5,000 | $40-250 | $2.50-17/hr |
| Tutorial | 4-8 hrs | 300-1,500 | $5-30 | $0.60-4/hr |
| Ultimate guide | 15-30 hrs | 2,000-10,000 | $60-500 | $2-17/hr |

### Lifetime Content Value

```
Content LTV = Monthly Revenue x Content Lifespan (months)

Example review post:
- Monthly revenue: $50
- Lifespan: 24 months (with updates)
- LTV: $50 x 24 = $1,200

Creation time: 8 hours
ROI: $1,200 / 8 = $150/hour effective
```

### High-ROI Content Strategy

**Focus on:**
1. High-commission products ($50+ per sale)
2. Buyer-intent keywords ("best X for Y", "X vs Y")
3. Evergreen topics (refresh annually)
4. Products you actually use (authentic reviews)

**Avoid:**
1. Low-commission (<$10) unless massive volume
2. Informational-only content (no purchase intent)
3. Trending products (short lifespan)
4. Products you can't honestly recommend

## Tracking and Attribution

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Click-through rate | Clicks / Visitors | 2-8% |
| EPC (earnings per click) | Revenue / Clicks | $0.50-5.00 |
| Conversion rate | Sales / Clicks | 1-10% |
| Average order value | Revenue / Sales | Varies |
| Revenue per post | Revenue / Posts | $20-200/mo |

### Attribution Tracking Setup

1. **UTM parameters** - Track traffic sources
2. **Unique links per page** - Know which content converts
3. **Dashboard** - ConvertKit, Affiliatly, or spreadsheet
4. **Monthly review** - Kill underperformers, double down on winners

## Scaling Affiliate Revenue

### Phase 1: Foundation ($0-500/mo)

- 10-20 pieces of affiliate content
- 2-3 core programs
- Focus on high-intent keywords
- Build email list for repeat traffic

### Phase 2: Growth ($500-2,000/mo)

- 30-50 pieces of content
- 5-7 programs
- Add comparison content
- Email sequences for recommendations
- Guest posting for backlinks

### Phase 3: Scale ($2,000-10,000/mo)

- 100+ pieces of content
- Diversified portfolio
- Video content (YouTube)
- Exclusive deals/bonuses
- Consider paid traffic to proven content
