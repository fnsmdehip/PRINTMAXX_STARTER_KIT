## Risk Management

### Growth Phase Risks

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Content burnout | Medium | Delegate early, batch create |
| Revenue plateau | Medium | Add revenue streams |
| Platform dependency | Medium | Diversify channels |
| Quality drop with scale | Low | Quality gates, SOPs |
| Cash flow gaps | Low | Build 2-month reserve |

### Early Warning Signs

- Newsletter growth slowing (<5%/week)
- Conversion rates dropping
- Unsubscribe rate increasing (>2%)
- Negative reviews or feedback
- Competitor copying your content
