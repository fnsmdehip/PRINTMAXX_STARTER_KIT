## Recurring Revenue Development

### Option 1: Membership Community

**Structure:**
- Monthly subscription: $29-49/month
- Annual option: 2 months free
- Community access + monthly content
- Live calls 1-2x/month

**Economics:**
```
Target: 500 members @ $39/month = $19,500 MRR
Churn: 5%/month
Monthly acquisition needed: 25 new members
LTV: $39 / 0.05 = $780
CAC target: <$260 (3:1 LTV:CAC)
```

**Timeline:**
- Q1: Beta launch with 50 founding members
- Q2: Scale to 150-200 members
- Q3: Reach 300-350 members
- Q4: Hit 500 member target

### Option 2: SaaS Product

**Structure:**
- Solves specific workflow problem
- $29-99/month pricing
- Self-serve onboarding
- Low support burden

**Economics:**
```
Target: 300 users @ $49/month = $14,700 MRR
Churn: 5%/month
Development cost: $10-30K
Payback: 6-12 months
LTV: $49 / 0.05 = $980
CAC target: <$327
```

**Timeline:**
- Q1: MVP development + beta
- Q2: Public launch + iterate
- Q3: Scale acquisition
- Q4: Feature expansion

### Option 3: Hybrid Model

Combine membership with tool access:
- Community + templates: $29/mo
- Community + tool access: $49/mo
- Community + tool + coaching: $99/mo
