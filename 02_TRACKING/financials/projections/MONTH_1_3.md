## Key Activities

### Month 1: Foundation

**Week 1-2:**
- Set up landing site (Next.js on Vercel)
- Create 3 truth pages (core content pillars)
- Set up newsletter with lead magnet
- Start daily Twitter posting

**Week 3-4:**
- Write 5 affiliate review/comparison posts
- Create first free lead magnet (PDF/template)
- Engage daily on Twitter (30-60 min)
- Guest post or podcast pitch (1-2)

**Month 1 Milestones:**
- [ ] 100 newsletter subscribers
- [ ] 500 Twitter followers
- [ ] 5 blog posts published
- [ ] 1,000 site visitors

### Month 2: First Revenue

**Week 1-2:**
- Create $27 mini product (template, guide)
- Set up Gumroad/Lemonsqueezy
- Write sales page
- Soft launch to email list

**Week 3-4:**
- Create 5 more SEO-focused posts
- Build 3 comparison pages (high affiliate intent)
- Public launch of first product
- Start building second product

**Month 2 Milestones:**
- [ ] 300 newsletter subscribers
- [ ] 1,000 Twitter followers
- [ ] First 5 product sales
- [ ] 3,000 site visitors

### Month 3: Validate and Scale

**Week 1-2:**
- Launch second product ($47 tier)
- Create product ladder (free → $27 → $47)
- Pitch 3-5 newsletter sponsors
- Double down on what's working

**Week 3-4:**
- Analyze and optimize conversion funnel
- Create email nurture sequence (5-7 emails)
- Plan month 4-6 content calendar
- Set up basic automation

**Month 3 Milestones:**
- [ ] 500 newsletter subscribers
- [ ] 2,000 Twitter followers
- [ ] $500+ revenue
- [ ] 5,000 site visitors
- [ ] 2% conversion rate on sales page

## Risk Mitigation

### Common Failure Modes

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| No product-market fit | Medium | High | Validate with audience before building |
| Content not ranking | Medium | Medium | Focus on long-tail, less competitive terms |
| Low email conversion | Medium | Medium | Test different lead magnets |
| Burnout | Medium | High | Sustainable 25-30 hr/week max |

### Pivot Triggers

**Consider pivoting if by Month 3:**
- Newsletter growth <10 subs/week
- Product sales <5 total
- Traffic <2,000/month
- No engagement on content

---

## Success Indicators

### Green Lights (Keep Going)

- Newsletter growing 20%+ week over week
- Product conversion rate >2%
- Email open rate >30%
- Positive feedback on content
- Organic mentions/shares

### Yellow Lights (Adjust)

- Growth slowing after initial spike
- Conversion rate 1-2%
- High unsubscribe rate (>1%/email)
- No repeat customers

### Red Lights (Major Pivot Needed)

- Newsletter shrinking
- Zero sales after 50+ visitors to sales page
- No engagement despite consistent posting
- Negative feedback on product
