## How to Use These Benchmarks

### Interpretation

1. **Below average:** Prioritize improvement
2. **Average:** Maintain, look for quick wins
3. **Good:** Optimize for excellent
4. **Excellent:** Maintain and scale

### Context Matters

- New businesses: Compare to early-stage benchmarks
- Different niches have different standards
- Your audience size affects metrics
- Seasonality impacts everything

### Action Framework

| Performance vs Benchmark | Action |
|--------------------------|--------|
| 50%+ below | Major intervention needed |
| 25-50% below | Significant focus required |
| Within 25% | Normal optimization |
| Above benchmark | Document what's working |
| Top 10% | Scale and maintain |
