# Warning Signs Dashboard

## Critical Alerts (Act Immediately)

These signals require immediate action. Drop everything else.

### Financial Red Flags

| Signal | Threshold | What It Means | Action |
|--------|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Cash runway <30 days | <1 month expenses | Survival threat | Emergency cost cutting |
| Revenue drop >50% | Month over month | Critical problem | Diagnose and fix immediately |
| Refund rate >15% | Single product | Product or marketing issue | Pause sales, investigate |
| Churn spike >2x normal | Single month | Major product problem | All-hands on retention |
| Payment processor freeze | Any | Revenue blocked | Contact support, find backup |

### Audience Red Flags

| Signal | Threshold | What It Means | Action |
|--------|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Email list shrinking | Net negative growth | Value problem | Survey audience, improve content |
| Open rate <10% | Sustained | Deliverability or relevance | List cleaning, content audit |
| Unsubscribe spike >5% | Single email | Content miss | Analyze and adjust |
| Platform ban/suspension | Any | Policy violation | Appeal, diversify platforms |
| Traffic drop >50% | Week over week | Algorithm or technical | Check for site issues, penalties |

## Serious Warnings (Address This Week)

| Trial to paid <20% | SaaS | Onboarding or product problem |

| Signal | Threshold | Concern |

## Early Warning Signs (Monitor Closely)

### Growth Indicators

| Signal | Watch When | Why It Matters |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Growth rate slowing | Quarter over quarter | Losing momentum |
| CAC increasing | 20%+ month over month | Acquisition getting harder |
| Content performance declining | Avg views down | Algorithm or relevance shift |
| Referral rate dropping | Below 30% | Word of mouth weakening |
| NPS declining | Below 30 | Customer satisfaction issue |

### Market Signals

| Signal | What to Watch | Implication |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Competitor launches | Similar product | Market pressure |
| Price competition | Undercutting you | May need to adjust |
| Platform policy changes | Terms of service | Business model risk |
| Algorithm updates | Traffic patterns | Distribution changes |
| Economic indicators | Customer spending | Demand changes |

### Operational Signals

| Signal | Threshold | Risk |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Support tickets increasing | 2x normal | Product or scaling issue |
| Contractor reliability issues | Missed deadlines | Team problem |
| Tool costs creeping up | >10% of revenue | Efficiency problem |
| Your hours increasing | >50/week sustained | Burnout risk |
| Project delays | 2+ weeks behind | Planning or capacity issue |

## Diagnostic Frameworks

### Revenue Drop Diagnosis

When revenue drops, check in this order:

```
1. Technical issues?
   └─ Payment processor working?
   └─ Website functional?
   └─ Links working?
   └─ Prices correct?

2. Traffic changes?
   └─ Overall traffic down?
   └─ Traffic source changes?
   └─ Algorithm update?
   └─ Seasonal pattern?

3. Conversion changes?
   └─ Same traffic, fewer sales?
   └─ Which step is broken?
   └─ Mobile vs desktop?

4. External factors?
   └─ Competitor action?
   └─ Market event?
   └─ Economic conditions?

5. Product issues?
   └─ Increased refunds?
   └─ Negative feedback?
   └─ Product quality change?
```

### Churn Spike Diagnosis

When churn increases suddenly:

```
1. Recent changes?
   └─ Price changes?
   └─ Feature changes?
   └─ Billing issues?

2. Customer feedback?
   └─ Support tickets?
   └─ Cancellation reasons?
   └─ Social mentions?

3. Product issues?
   └─ Bugs or downtime?
   └─ Performance problems?
   └─ Missing features?

4. Competitive pressure?
   └─ Better alternative?
   └─ Cheaper option?
   └─ Feature gap?

5. Customer segment?
   └─ Which cohort churning?
   └─ Usage patterns before churn?
   └─ Price sensitivity?
```

### Engagement Drop Diagnosis

When engagement metrics decline:

```
1. Content quality?
   └─ Topic relevance?
   └─ Production quality?
   └─ Posting frequency?

2. Audience changes?
   └─ Subscriber quality?
   └─ Audience expectations?
   └─ List hygiene?

3. Platform changes?
   └─ Algorithm shift?
   └─ Feature changes?
   └─ Competition for attention?

4. Timing issues?
   └─ Posting schedule?
   └─ Seasonal factors?
   └─ Audience availability?

5. Technical issues?
   └─ Deliverability?
   └─ Rendering problems?
   └─ Mobile experience?
```

## Response Playbooks

### Revenue Emergency (>30% drop)

**Day 1:**
- Verify all technical systems working
- Check payment processor status
- Review traffic for anomalies
- Check for site errors

**Day 2-3:**
- Analyze by revenue source
- Compare conversion rates to baseline
- Survey recent customers
- Check competitor activity

**Day 4-7:**
- Implement quick fixes
- Launch recovery campaign
- Activate backup revenue (services, flash sale)
- Daily monitoring

**Week 2:**
- Deeper analysis
- Strategic adjustments
- New acquisition tests
- Recovery plan execution

### Churn Emergency (>2x normal)

**Immediate:**
- Reach out to churned customers
- Analyze cancellation reasons
- Check for product issues
- Review recent changes

**This week:**
- Implement fixes for identified issues
- Launch retention campaign
- Improve onboarding
- Consider win-back offers

**This month:**
- Restructure onboarding
- Add customer success touchpoints
- Review pricing/value proposition
- Build early warning system

### Audience Decline Emergency

**Immediate:**
- Clean email list (remove bounces)
- Check deliverability scores
- Review recent content performance
- Analyze unsubscribe reasons

**This week:**
- Survey engaged subscribers
- Test new content angles
- Improve welcome sequence
- Reactivation campaign

**This month:**
- Content strategy review
- Lead magnet refresh
- Acquisition channel diversification
- Community building focus

## Prevention Strategies

### Build Resilience

| Risk | Prevention |
|------|------------|
| Revenue concentration | Diversify income streams |
| Platform dependence | Build owned audience (email) |
| Single point of failure | Document processes, backup systems |
| Customer concentration | No customer >20% of revenue |
| Tool dependence | Know alternatives for critical tools |

### Early Warning Investment

1. **Customer feedback loops** - Regular surveys, NPS
2. **Usage analytics** - Know how customers use product
3. **Competitor monitoring** - Stay aware of market
4. **Financial forecasting** - See problems coming
5. **Relationship building** - Customers tell you before leaving

### Recovery Fund

Maintain reserves specifically for emergencies:
- 3-6 months operating expenses
- Separate from growth capital
- Only use for true emergencies
- Replenish immediately after use

## Key Principles

1. **Detect early** - Small problems are easier to fix
2. **Act fast** - Speed matters in crisis
3. **Diagnose first** - Don't assume the cause
4. **Communicate** - Keep customers informed
5. **Learn** - Prevent recurrence
6. **Document** - Build institutional knowledge
7. **Stay calm** - Panic makes things worse
8. **Ask for help** - Mentors, communities, experts
