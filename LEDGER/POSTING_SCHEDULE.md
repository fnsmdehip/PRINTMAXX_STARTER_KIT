## Frequency recommendations

### Per platform per niche

| Platform | Minimum | Recommended | Maximum | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------------|---------|-------|
| X | 1/day | 2-3/day | 5/day | Space 4+ hours apart |
| Instagram | 3/week | 5/week | 1/day | Stories daily, posts 5x |
| TikTok | 1/day | 2-3/day | 5/day | More = faster growth |
| YouTube Shorts | 3/week | 1/day | 2/day | Consistency matters most |

### Content recycling rules

- Same content on different platforms: 24-hour gap minimum
- Repost same content to same platform: 30-day gap minimum
- Cross-niche content: Never (keep accounts separate)

## Weekend vs weekday strategy

### Weekday strategy

**Monday**
- High-energy content
- Week kickoff themes
- Goal-setting posts
- Best engagement day for fitness

**Tuesday-Thursday**
- Educational content
- How-to posts
- Tips and tricks
- AI niche peak

**Friday**
- Lighter content
- Wins and celebrations
- Weekend prep
- Engagement drops after 3 PM

### Weekend strategy

**Saturday**
- Lifestyle content
- Longer-form engagement
- Community building
- Lower posting volume (50% of weekday)

**Sunday**
- Faith niche peak day
- Reflection content
- Week prep posts
- Fitness rest/recovery content

---

## Posting queue rules

1. Never post same content_type back-to-back on same platform
2. Rotate through all 3 content types before repeating
3. Instagram posts lag X by 24-48 hours (repurpose)
4. TikTok/Shorts can be same-day cross-posts
5. Promotional posts: Max 1 per 5 regular posts

---

## Account warmup phase (first 30 days)

**Week 1-2**
- 1 post per day per platform
- Focus on engagement (comments, likes)
- No promotional content
- Build follower base

**Week 3-4**
- Scale to 2 posts per day
- Introduce light CTAs
- Start tracking metrics
- Test optimal times

**Week 5+**
- Full posting schedule
- A/B test content types
- Optimize based on data
- Scale promotional mix
