## Niche Wrapper Angles

### 1. Faith Niche: "PrayerBuddy" or "FaithPet"
- Pet grows when you pray, read Bible, attend church
- Morning devotional check-ins
- Evening gratitude prayer
- Bible verse of the day
- Prayer journal with pet reactions
- **Audience:** Christian women 18-45

### 2. Fitness Niche: "GymBuddy Pet"
- Pet grows when you work out, eat healthy, sleep well
- Workout logging with pet encouragement
- Nutrition tracking simplified
- Rest day reminders
- Streak system for gym consistency
- **Audience:** Fitness beginners, gym-curious

### 3. Student Niche: "StudyBuddy"
- Pet grows when you study, attend class, complete homework
- Pomodoro timer with pet
- Study session logging
- Exam prep encouragement
- Sleep schedule tracking
- **Audience:** College students, high schoolers

### 4. Women's Wellness: "SelfLove Pet"
- Focused on women's self-care
- Period tracking integration
- Skin care routine tracking
- Mental health check-ins
- Affirmations and self-love prompts
- **Audience:** Women 18-35
