## Detailed Opportunities

### 1. GetThis - Voice/Screenshot to Tasks
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_getthis.md`

- **What:** AI brain dump organizer - speak or screenshot to organized tasks
- **Why Clone:** Simple tech stack, clear monetization, multiple niches
- **Best Niches:** ADHD, Moms, Students
- **Build Time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Monetization:** $4.99/mo subscription

### 2. PayPing - Recurring Payments Tracker
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_payping.md`

- **What:** Track all subscriptions in one place
- **Why Clone:** Universal problem, very simple to build
- **Best Niches:** Gen Z/Students, Couples, Gamers
- **Build Time:** 1-2 weeks
- **Monetization:** $2.99/mo subscription

### 3. BrainLoom - PDF to Flashcards
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_brainloom.md`

- **What:** Turn PDF highlights into spaced repetition flashcards
- **Why Clone:** Students pay for study tools, high retention
- **Best Niches:** Med Students, Faith (Bible study), Nursing
- **Build Time:** 4-6 weeks
- **Monetization:** $6.99/mo or $49 lifetime

### 4. Kids Cash Register - Play POS System
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_kids_cash_register.md`

- **What:** Educational game for kids to play store/restaurant
- **Why Clone:** Parents actively seek quality educational apps
- **Best Niches:** Restaurant, Lemonade Stand, Ice Cream Shop themes
- **Build Time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Monetization:** $4.99 one-time purchase

### 5. Callum - AI Calendar Assistant
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_callum.md`

- **What:** Natural language calendar management
- **Why Clone:** Mobile-first opportunity in desktop-dominated space
- **Best Niches:** Parents, Students, Church/Ministry
- **Build Time:** 4-6 weeks
- **Monetization:** $5.99/mo subscription

### 6. Krisp Mobile Call Recorder
**File:** `2026-01-24_ph_krisp_call_recorder.md`

- **What:** Record calls with AI transcription and summaries
- **Why Clone:** High ARPU professional tool
- **Best Niches:** Sales, Real Estate, Legal
- **Build Time:** 8-12 weeks (native required)
- **Monetization:** $14.99/mo subscription
- **Note:** Legal complexity, consider video meeting recording instead
