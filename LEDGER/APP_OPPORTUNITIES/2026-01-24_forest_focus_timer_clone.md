## Niche Wrapper Angles

### Faith-Based (PrayerFocus)
- **Positioning:** Focus timer for prayer, Bible study, devotional time
- **Features:**
  - Grow "prayer garden" instead of forest
  - Track prayer streaks and devotional consistency
  - Biblical meditation prompts during breaks
  - Accountability groups with church friends
- **Monetization:** Christian book affiliates, devotional subscription content, Bible study courses
- **Market:** Christians who struggle with distracted prayer time - massive TAM
- **Unique Hook:** "Grow your spiritual garden while you pray"

### Fitness (WorkoutFocus / GymTimer)
- **Positioning:** Focus timer for workouts and gym sessions
- **Features:**
  - Grow "fitness garden" or muscle icons during workout
  - Rest period timers between sets
  - Workout streak tracking
  - Integration with Apple Health/Google Fit
- **Monetization:** Supplement affiliates (high commission), workout program subs, equipment links
- **Market:** Gym-goers who get distracted by phone during workouts
- **Unique Hook:** "Stay off your phone, stay in the zone"

### Student Productivity (StudyForest)
- **Positioning:** Focus timer specifically for students
- **Features:**
  - Grow "knowledge tree" for each subject
  - Track study hours per class
  - Integration with assignment deadlines
  - Leaderboards with classmates
- **Monetization:** Study tool affiliates (Notion, Quizlet, Anki), productivity courses, tutoring referrals
- **Market:** 20M+ college students, millions more in high school
- **Unique Hook:** "Level up your GPA while growing your study forest"

### ADHD/Neurodivergent (FocusTree ADHD)
- **Positioning:** Focus timer designed for ADHD brains
- **Features:**
  - Shorter default intervals (15 min instead of 25)
  - Dopamine-friendly gamification
  - Body doubling mode (virtual co-working)
  - Reminder sounds customizable for sensory needs
- **Monetization:** ADHD coaching referrals, productivity courses, therapy app affiliates
- **Market:** 10M+ adults with ADHD in US alone, massively underserved
- **Unique Hook:** "Built for brains that work differently"
