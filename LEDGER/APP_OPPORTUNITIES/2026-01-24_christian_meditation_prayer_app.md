### Audio Production
**Option A:** Hire voice talent on Fiverr
- $5-15 per meditation (700 words = 5 min)
- Total for 10 sessions: $50-150
- Pros: Professional quality, fast turnaround
- Cons: Ongoing cost for new content

**Option B:** Record yourself
- Free, full control
- Cons: Lower quality unless you have good mic + skills
- Recommendation: Buy Blue Yeti mic ($100), learn editing

**Option C:** Partner with Christian meditation leader
- Offer revenue share (30-50%)
- Pros: Credibility, built-in audience
- Cons: Split revenue, less control

**Recommendation:** Start with Option A (Fiverr) for launch, build audience, then hire dedicated voice talent or partner with credible Christian leader.
