## Mascot Design Principles

From Waterllama's success:
1. **Cute but not childish** - Appeal to adults
2. **Reactive personality** - Happy when you drink, sad when dehydrated
3. **Collectibility** - Multiple characters/outfits
4. **Simple animation** - Smooth but not complex
5. **Consistent brand** - Mascot everywhere
