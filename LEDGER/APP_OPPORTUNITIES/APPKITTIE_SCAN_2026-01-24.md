## Common Success Patterns

### Mascot/Character Strategy
- Finch: Bird companion
- Waterllama: Llama character
- Pattern: Cute but not childish, reactive, collectible

### Widget-First Design
- All top apps have iOS widgets
- Home screen presence = daily engagement
- Lock screen widgets (iOS 16+) are premium

### Gamification Elements
- Streaks (don't break the chain)
- Challenges (themed goals)
- Collectibles (characters, themes)
- Progress visualization

### Monetization Patterns
- Free tier with core features
- Premium unlocks: themes, categories, ad removal
- Subscription: Monthly + Annual + Lifetime options
- Price range: $3.99-$9.99/month

## Technical Stack Recommendations

For all these clones:
- **Framework:** React Native (Expo SDK 52+)
- **Backend:** Firebase or Supabase
- **Payments:** RevenueCat
- **Widgets:** iOS WidgetKit (Swift bridging)
- **Animations:** Lottie or Rive
- **Storage:** WatermelonDB for offline-first
- **Health:** Apple HealthKit integration
