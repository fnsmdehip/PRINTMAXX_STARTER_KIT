## What It Does

A POS (point of sale) system designed for kids to play:
- Pretend restaurant/shopping games
- Practice math with real checkout scenarios
- Learn money handling concepts
- Likely: scanning items, calculating change, receipts

**Tags:** Kids, Education

---

## Clone Difficulty

**Rating:** EASY

**Why:**
- Simple UI with big buttons
- Basic math calculations
- No real payment processing
- Fun sound effects and animations
- No backend required

**Tech Stack Estimate:**
- React Native (Expo)
- Local storage for "inventory"
- Fun animations (Lottie)
- Sound effects for engagement
- Optional: parent dashboard

## Monetization Model

**Primary:** Freemium
- Free: Basic register, 10 items
- Full Version ($4.99 one-time): All themes, unlimited items, no ads
- Bundle ($9.99): Includes printable play money and menu templates

**Secondary:**
- Ad-supported free tier (kid-safe ads only - very limited)
- In-app purchases for new "store themes"
- Print-at-home activity packs

**Parent Subscription Option:**
- $2.99/mo: Progress reports, curriculum alignment, printables library

## Competitive Landscape

- **Toca Boca apps** (premium kids games, $4.99 each)
- **Educational games** on App Store
- **Generic cash register apps** (often low quality)

**Differentiation opportunity:** Educational focus (actual math curriculum alignment), parent dashboard showing progress, multiple store themes
