## Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: AI food logging accuracy**
- Mitigation: Allow manual correction, show confidence %
- Partner with established food database (Nutritionix, MyFitnessPal)

**Risk 2: Period tracker medical claims**
- Mitigation: Disclaimers, no medical advice, just tracking
- Check FDA guidance on wellness apps

**Risk 3: MIT repos abandoned/buggy**
- Mitigation: Fork early, fix issues, don't depend on upstream updates
- Budget extra 1 week for repo cleanup

**Risk 4: App Store rejection**
- Mitigation: Follow GREY_HAT_COMPLIANCE_PLAYBOOK.md
- No fake reviews, real screenshots, proper age ratings
