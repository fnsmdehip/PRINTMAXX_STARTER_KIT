# Info Product Pricing: Sweet Spot Price Points by Type

## Source
- Online course pricing research 2026
- Ebook pricing benchmarks
- Digital product pricing strategy

## Tactic
Price info products at proven conversion-optimized price points based on product type and depth.

## Specific Numbers by Product Type

### Ebooks
- **Fiction sweet spot: $2.99-$5.99**
- **Non-fiction/business: $4.99-$9.99**
- **Standalone professional ebooks: $10-$20**

### Online Courses
- **Entry-level/intro: <$100**
- **Comprehensive/in-depth: $200-$500**
- **Premium/certification: $500-$3,000**
- **Sweet spot for actionable workflows: $97-$197**

### Conversion Rates by Price
- **$100 course: 2-5% conversion** (healthy)
- **$1,000 course: 0.5-1% conversion** (normal)

### Course Length Correlation
- **Short courses (<5 hours): $50-$500**
- **Comprehensive video courses: $500-$3,000**

## Psychological Price Points
The magic numbers (proven buyer familiarity):
- $27 (entry impulse buy)
- $49 (low commitment)
- $97 (sweet spot #1)
- $197 (sweet spot #2)
- $297 (premium entry)
- $497 (premium comprehensive)
- $997 (high-ticket)
- $1,997 (expert/certification)

**Why these work:** Consumers are conditioned to these numbers from years of digital product pricing.

## How It Works
1. **Determine product depth**:
   - Entry (under 5 hours, single outcome): $27-$97
   - Comprehensive (5-20 hours, multiple outcomes): $197-$497
   - Premium (20+ hours, transformation): $497-$1,997

2. **Set initial price** at proven psychological point

3. **Test price increases**: Raise 50-100% on next launch if first goes well

4. **A/B test**: 10-25% increases have little impact on volume but boost margins

## Implementation Requirements
- Value-based pricing (not cost-based)
- Clear outcome/transformation promise
- Social proof (testimonials, case studies)
- Sales page with clear value proposition

## Tools Needed
**Platforms:**
- Gumroad (10% fee, good for testing)
- ThriveCart (one-time fee, better at scale)
- Kajabi (premium course hosting)
- Teachable/Thinkific (course-specific)

**Payment:**
- Stripe (integrate with any platform)
- PayPal (additional option)

## Implementation Priority
HIGHEST - Info products = high margin, no marginal cost, scalable

## Pricing Strategy by Audience

### Solopreneurs/Creators
- Entry: $27-$49 (email list building)
- Core: $97-$197 (main course offering)
- Premium: $297-$497 (coaching included)

### B2B/Professional
- Entry: $97-$197 (team member level)
- Core: $497-$997 (manager level)
- Premium: $1,997-$4,997 (executive/certification)

### Consumer/Hobbyist
- Entry: $19-$49 (impulse buy)
- Core: $97-$197 (committed learner)
- Premium: $297 max (casual hobby ceiling)

## Tiered Pricing Structure
**Good-Better-Best model:**

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Basic | $97 | Video course only |
| Standard | $197 | Course + workbook + templates |
| Premium | $497 | Everything + group coaching calls |

**Key insight:** Middle tier converts best (price anchoring).

## Progressive Price Increase Strategy
**Launch 1:** $97
- Test market, get testimonials

**Launch 2:** $147-$197 (50-100% increase)
- Add testimonials, case studies

**Launch 3:** $297 (50% increase)
- Add bonuses, group coaching

**Ongoing:** $297-$497 (final price)
- Evergreen pricing with occasional launches

## Conversion Optimization
1. **Remove spouse/boss approval friction**: Price under $200 for impulse buy
2. **Use psychological anchors**: Show "$997 value" then "Today only $197"
3. **Payment plans**: $497 → 3 payments of $197 (higher total, better conversion)
4. **Early bird pricing**: $97 for first 50, $197 after (urgency)

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Pricing too low ($7-$27 for comprehensive course = undervalued)
- Too many tiers (3 max, don't offer 6 options)
- Random pricing ($83 instead of $97 = looks unprofessional)
- No payment plans for high-ticket (kills conversion)
- Cost-based pricing (ignoring market value)

## Platform Fee Considerations
**Gumroad (good for testing):**
- Fee: 10% + $0.50 per sale
- $97 course = $9.70 + $0.50 = $10.20 fee
- Net: $86.80
- **Good for:** First launch, testing, under $5K/month revenue

**ThriveCart (better at scale):**
- Fee: One-time $495 purchase (no recurring fees)
- $97 course = $0 fee (you pay Stripe ~3%)
- Net: ~$94
- **Good for:** Consistent revenue, over $5K/month

## Gumroad Scale Analysis
At what revenue should you leave Gumroad?

| Monthly Revenue | Gumroad Fees | Alternative Platform |
|----------------|--------------|----------------------|
| $1,000 | $100 | Stay on Gumroad |
| $5,000 | $500 | Consider switching |
| $10,000 | $1,000 | Definitely switch to ThriveCart/Kajabi |

**Breakeven:** ThriveCart pays for itself after $5K in sales ($495 ÷ 10% savings).
