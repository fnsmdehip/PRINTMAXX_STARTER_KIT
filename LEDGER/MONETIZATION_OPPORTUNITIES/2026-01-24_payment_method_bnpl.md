# Payment Method: Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) Integration

## Source
- Multiple payment industry reports
- 2026 alternative payment method trends

## Tactic
Add BNPL options at checkout to reduce cart abandonment and increase conversions.

## Specific Numbers
- **10%** of shoppers abandon carts due to insufficient payment methods
- **60%** of consumers prefer BNPL over credit cards
- **59%** of Gen Z/Millennials would shop elsewhere without BNPL
- Global BNPL market: **$565B by 2026**
- BNPL share of B2C e-commerce projected to **more than double by 2026**

## How It Works
1. Integrate BNPL provider (Klarna, Affirm, Afterpay)
2. Display BNPL option at checkout
3. Customer splits payment over time (typically 4 installments)
4. Merchant receives full payment upfront
5. BNPL provider handles collections

## Implementation Requirements
- Payment processor integration (most support BNPL)
- Display BNPL messaging on product/checkout pages
- Minimum purchase amounts may apply

## Tools Needed
- Klarna, Affirm, Afterpay, or similar
- Stripe/PayPal (both support BNPL)

## Conversion Impact
- Reduces cart abandonment (at least 10% recovery potential)
- Increases average order value
- Improves conversion rates (familiar payment methods = trust)

## Applicable Money Methods
- APP_FACTORY (MM001) - Annual subscription BNPL for higher-priced apps
- INFO_PRODUCTS (MM002) - Course purchases $100+ split into payments
- SAAS (MM004) - Annual plans with BNPL option
- AGENCY_SERVICES (MM005) - High-ticket services split into payments

## Implementation Priority
HIGH - Proven conversion lift, especially for younger demographics and higher-priced products

## Best Practices
- Display BNPL prominently on product pages (not just checkout)
- Use for products $50+ (most BNPL minimums)
- Target Gen Z/Millennial audiences specifically
- Test messaging: "Pay in 4 interest-free installments"
