# Subscription Model: Annual Discount (30-40% Sweet Spot)

## Source
- RevenueCat State of Subscription Apps 2025
- Mobile subscription benchmarks

## Tactic
Offer annual subscriptions at 30-40% discount vs monthly to maximize conversion and LTV.

## Specific Numbers
- **Industry standard annual discount: 17%** (baseline)
- **Mobile app optimal discount: 30-40%** (higher conversion)
- **59% of mobile subscribers prefer annual plans** at 30-40% discount
- **Annual subscribers are 2.4x more profitable** than monthly
- **Annual retention rate: 44.1%**
- **Monthly retention rate: 17.0%**
- **Weekly retention rate: 3.4%**

## How It Works
1. Price monthly subscription (e.g., $10/month)
2. Offer annual at 30-40% discount (e.g., $72-84/year instead of $120)
3. Display savings prominently ("Save $36-48/year")
4. Position annual as "Best Value" in center
5. Show monthly equivalent price for comparison

## Value Calculation Examples
| Monthly | Annual (17%) | Annual (30%) | Annual (40%) |
|---------|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| $10/mo  | $99/year     | $84/year     | $72/year     |
| $20/mo  | $199/year    | $168/year    | $144/year    |
| $50/mo  | $499/year    | $420/year    | $360/year    |

## Why Annual Converts Better at 30-40%
- Perceived value increases significantly over 17% baseline
- 59% preference rate shows strong market demand
- Discount feels "worth it" for commitment
- Crosses psychological threshold for long-term planning

## Profitability Math
**Monthly subscriber:**
- $10/month × 17% retention = $1.70 monthly expected value
- LTV ≈ $20.40/year

**Annual subscriber (40% discount):**
- $72/year × 44.1% retention = $31.75 annual expected value
- 2.4x more profitable despite discount
- Lower churn risk (locked in for year)

## Implementation Requirements
- Dual pricing structure (monthly + annual)
- Visual emphasis on annual savings
- RevenueCat or subscription platform

## Applicable Money Methods
- APP_FACTORY (MM001) - All subscription apps
- CONTENT_FARM (MM006) - Subscription content
- SAAS (MM004) - SaaS annual plans
- INFO_PRODUCTS (MM002) - Annual course access

## Implementation Priority
HIGHEST - 2.4x profitability, 44% retention vs 17%, locks in revenue

## Messaging Best Practices
- Lead with savings amount: "Save $48/year"
- Show monthly equivalent: "$6/month when billed annually"
- Add "Best Value" badge
- Use .99 pricing: $71.99/year not $72
- Highlight annual in center of 3-tier pricing

## Testing Strategy
1. Start with 17% discount (industry standard)
2. A/B test 30% discount variant
3. A/B test 40% discount variant
4. Measure conversion rate AND annual revenue impact
5. Find optimal discount for your category
