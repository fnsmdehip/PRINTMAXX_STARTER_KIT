# Payment Method: Stripe Crypto Payments via Crypto.com

## Source
- Stripe + Crypto.com partnership (Jan 2026)
- Multiple payment industry sources

## Tactic
Enable cryptocurrency payments through Stripe with automatic fiat conversion.

## Specific Numbers
- Launched Jan 2026 in US (international expansion planned)
- Customers pay with crypto/stablecoins
- Merchants receive local fiat currency automatically

## How It Works
1. Customer checks out on your site/app
2. Pays with preferred cryptocurrency via Crypto.com Pay
3. Stripe converts to your local currency
4. Money deposited in your bank account

## Implementation Requirements
- Existing Stripe account
- Enable Crypto.com integration in Stripe dashboard
- No additional KYC/compliance on merchant side (handled by Stripe)

## Tools Needed
- Stripe account
- Crypto.com Pay integration

## Applicable Money Methods
- APP_FACTORY (MM001) - Enable crypto payments for app subscriptions
- INFO_PRODUCTS (MM002) - Accept crypto for course/ebook purchases
- SAAS (MM004) - Add crypto payment option for SaaS subscriptions
- AFFILIATE_SITES (MM003) - Accept crypto for referred products

## Implementation Priority
MEDIUM - Early adopter advantage, expanding payment options for crypto-native audiences

## Notes
First cryptocurrency platform integrated directly with Stripe for balance payments. Removes friction of crypto-to-fiat conversion for merchants.
