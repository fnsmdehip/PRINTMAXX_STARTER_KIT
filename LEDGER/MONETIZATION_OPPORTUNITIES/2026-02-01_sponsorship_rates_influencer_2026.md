## The Tactic

**Platform-specific influencer rates** vary dramatically by follower count and format. TikTok has emerged as the **highest-engagement platform**, YouTube commands **highest per-post rates** due to production effort, and **email/newsletter placements** convert 4.24x better than social (per previous research).

### CPM/CPV Pricing Models

**Alternative pricing structure:**
- **CPM (Cost Per Mille):** $50-$100 per 1,000 views
- **CPV (Cost Per View):** Direct payment per actual view
- **YouTube CPM:** $20-$50 per 1,000 followers as base multiplier

## Why It Works

1. **Audience access:** Brands pay for targeted reach
2. **Trust transfer:** Influencer's authority transfers to product
3. **Performance:** Better ROI than traditional ads for niche products
4. **Engagement:** Higher engagement than brand-owned channels
5. **Authenticity:** Feels like recommendation vs ad

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Don't** underprice (devalues your audience)
2. **Don't** accept every brand (damages authenticity)
3. **Don't** hide sponsorships (FTC violation, kills trust)
4. **Don't** forget usage rights fees (brands repurposing content pay extra)
5. **Don't** ignore performance data (track affiliate conversions to justify rates)

## Rate Negotiation Tips

1. **Start high:** Easier to discount than raise
2. **Bundle posts:** Offer 3-post package with 15% discount
3. **Add value:** Throw in Stories/Reels for free with main post
4. **Usage rights:** Charge 2-5x for content repurposing rights
5. **Exclusivity:** Charge 20-50% premium for category exclusivity
6. **Performance data:** Show past affiliate conversions to justify rates

## FTC Compliance (CRITICAL)

- **Always disclose:** #ad, #sponsored, or "Paid partnership with [Brand]"
- **Clear and conspicuous:** Disclosure must be visible without clicking "more"
- **Above the fold:** Place disclosure at start of post, not buried
- **Every platform:** Same rules apply to IG, TikTok, YouTube
- **Penalties:** $51,744 per violation (FTC fake reviews rule, Aug 2024)
