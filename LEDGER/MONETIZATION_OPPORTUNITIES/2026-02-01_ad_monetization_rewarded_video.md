# Ad Monetization: Rewarded Video Dominance (2026)

**Category:** AD_MONETIZATION
**Date Added:** 2026-02-01
**ROI Potential:** HIGHEST
**Applicable Methods:** MM001 (APP_FACTORY), MM006 (CONTENT_FARM), MM029 (FACELESS_YOUTUBE)

---

## The Tactic

**Rewarded video ads** generate the highest CPM/eCPM rates of all ad formats, commanding **$10-$20 CPM** with completion rates above **95%**. They deliver engagement rates **3.5x higher** than traditional video formats while being user-initiated (better UX).

## Specific Numbers

### CPM/eCPM Rates by Format (2026)

**Rewarded Video (HIGHEST):**
- **US gaming apps:** $11-$20 eCPM (iOS $19.63, Android $16.49)
- **Premium regions:** $10-$50 eCPM
- **2-3x higher** than standard video ads

**Interstitial Ads (Mid-Range):**
- **2024 forecast:** $4.80 per 1,000 views
- **Tier 1 countries:** $5.00-$8.00
- **Q4 2024 leaders:** US $14.32 iOS / $14.08 Android

**Banner Ads (Lowest):**
- **2024 forecast:** $2.80 per 1,000 views
- **Tier 1 countries:** $0.50-$1.50
- **Below $1 on both platforms**

### Engagement Metrics

- **Rewarded video completion rate:** 95%+
- **Engagement rate:** 3.5x higher than traditional video
- **User-initiated:** Better UX, less intrusive

## Why It Works

1. **Value exchange:** User chooses to watch ad for in-app reward
2. **High completion:** 95%+ watch full ad (vs 60-70% for interstitials)
3. **Premium CPM:** Advertisers pay more for guaranteed completion
4. **Better UX:** User-initiated, not forced interruption
5. **Stackable:** Can combine with other monetization (IAP, subscriptions)

## Implementation Requirements

**Tools Needed:**
- AdMob (Google), ironSource, AppLovin, Unity Ads, or Chartboost
- Mediation platform (MAX by AppLovin, ironSource LevelPlay)
- Ad placement strategy (where/when to show rewarded videos)

**Technical Setup:**
1. Integrate ad SDK (AdMob, ironSource, etc.)
2. Set up mediation for optimal fill rate and eCPM
3. Design reward structure (coins, lives, power-ups, unlocks)
4. Place opt-in buttons strategically in app flow
5. Implement frequency capping (max 1-2 per session)
6. Track eCPM and completion rates

**Time to Implement:** 4-8 hours for basic integration, 2-3 days for optimized mediation

## Expected Results

### Revenue Projections (Per 1,000 DAU)

**Casual gaming app (US, high engagement):**
- 1,000 DAU × 60% opt-in rate × 2 videos/user × $15 eCPM = **$18/day** = **$540/month**

**Utility app (mixed regions, moderate engagement):**
- 1,000 DAU × 30% opt-in rate × 1 video/user × $8 eCPM = **$2.40/day** = **$72/month**

**Content app (global, low engagement):**
- 1,000 DAU × 20% opt-in rate × 1 video/user × $5 eCPM = **$1/day** = **$30/month**

### Format Comparison

| Format | CPM | Intrusiveness | User Experience | Best For |
|--------|-----|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| Rewarded Video | $10-$20 | Low (opt-in) | Excellent | Gaming, utility apps |
| Interstitial | $4.80 | High (forced) | Poor | High-frequency apps |
| Banner | $2.80 | Medium (persistent) | Fair | Content/news apps |

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Don't** show rewarded videos without user consent (kills UX)
2. **Don't** make rewards too small (users won't opt in)
3. **Don't** show ads too frequently (cap at 1-2 per session)
4. **Don't** use banner ads as primary monetization (10x lower eCPM)
5. **Don't** rely solely on ads if you have strong IAP potential

## Proof/Case Studies

- **Industry data:** Rewarded video CPM runs 2-3x higher than standard video
- **Completion rates:** 95%+ vs 60-70% for forced video ads
- **Engagement:** 3.5x higher than traditional video formats
- **Regional leaders:** US dominates at $14-$20 eCPM

## Variations

1. **Optional rewards:** Watch ad for extra coins/lives (gaming)
2. **Unlock rewards:** Watch ad to unlock premium feature for 24 hours
3. **Boost rewards:** Watch ad for 2x XP/coins for next hour
4. **Skip timers:** Watch ad to skip 4-hour wait timer
5. **Daily bonus:** Watch 3 ads for bonus reward package

- [ ] Design reward structure that feels valuable

- [ ] Implement frequency capping (1-2 per session max)
- [ ] A/B test reward amounts and placement
- [ ] Track eCPM by region and optimize geo-targeting

## Best Placement Points

**Gaming apps:**
- After level completion (win or lose)
- On "continue" screen after game over
- In shop when user is out of currency
- Daily reward screen

**Utility apps:**
- After completing core task
- When hitting free tier limit
- In settings for "remove ads permanently" comparison

**Content apps:**
- Between articles/videos
- Unlock premium content for 24 hours
- Ad-free reading for X hours

## Revenue Optimization Tips

1. **Mediation:** Use 3-5 networks to maximize fill + eCPM
2. **Geo-targeting:** Focus on Tier 1 countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia)
3. **Reward balance:** Make reward valuable but not required
4. **Placement:** Natural break points in user flow
5. **Frequency:** Less is more - quality over quantity
6. **Hybrid:** Combine with IAP "remove ads" option ($2.99-$4.99)
