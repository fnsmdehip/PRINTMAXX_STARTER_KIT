## Tactic
Promote SaaS products with 30%+ recurring lifetime commissions instead of one-time payouts.

## Specific Numbers
- **SaaS average commission: 30%** recurring
- **SaaS range: 20-70%** (highest paying niche)
- **Thrive Themes: 35% recurring + 25% second-tier**
- **Kartra: 40% recurring** (high retention)
- **AWeber: 30-50% recurring** (tiered structure)
- **ConvertKit: 30% lifetime recurring**
- **DTC brands: 10-15%** first purchase + 2-5% tier bump

## How It Works
1. Sign up for SaaS affiliate programs with recurring commissions
2. Promote via content, email, social
3. Customer subscribes to SaaS
4. **Receive commission every month** customer remains subscribed
5. Lifetime value compounds over time

## Why Recurring > One-Time
**One-time commission example:**
- Promote $99/year product at 30% = $29.70 once

**Recurring commission example:**
- Promote $99/year product at 30% recurring
- Year 1: $29.70
- Year 2: $29.70
- Year 3: $29.70
- **3-year LTV: $89.10** (3x more)

## Best Performing Niches for SaaS Affiliates
1. **Marketing tools** (email, SEO, social) - 30-40% recurring
2. **Creator tools** (ConvertKit, Kajabi) - 30% recurring
3. **E-commerce platforms** (Shopify, Kartra) - 20-40% recurring
4. **Productivity** (Notion, productivity apps) - 20-30%
5. **Developer tools** (hosting, APIs) - 20-50%

## Strategic Focus
**Don't chase highest commission rate. Prioritize:**
1. **Conversion rate** - Easy to sell product > high commission hard sale
2. **Retention** - Sticky products = longer lifetime value
3. **Relevance** - Match your audience needs
4. **Reputation** - Only promote quality products

## Tiered Structure Optimization
**AWeber model (best for volume):**
- 0-9 customers: 30% recurring
- 10-49 customers: 40% recurring
- 50+ customers: 50% recurring

**Strategy**: Focus on one program, build to 50+ refs for maximum rate.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Promoting low-quality products for high commission (kills trust)
- Only promoting products you've never used (obvious)
- Chasing one-time payouts over recurring (short-term thinking)
- Spreading thin across 50 programs (focus on 3-5 max)
