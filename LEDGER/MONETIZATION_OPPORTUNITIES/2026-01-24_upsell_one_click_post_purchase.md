# Upsell Flow: One-Click Post-Purchase Upsells

## Source
- Shopify upsell app data 2026
- E-commerce conversion benchmarks
- SaaS upsell statistics

## Tactic
Add one-click upsell offers immediately after purchase (before thank you page) to increase AOV.

## Specific Numbers
- **Average post-purchase upsell conversion: 4%** (baseline)
- **Well-targeted offers: 8-15% acceptance rate** (optimized)
- **One-click upsells convert: 4-10%** of buyers
- **AOV increase: 10-30%** when executed well
- **SaaS upsells convert at 27.6%** (higher than physical products)
- **Lift LTV by up to 20%** by converting one-time to multi-product buyers
- **60-70% likely to sell to existing customer** vs 5-20% to new prospect

## How It Works
1. Customer completes initial purchase
2. Immediately show relevant upsell offer (before thank you page)
3. One-click acceptance (payment info already captured)
4. No re-entering payment details
5. Add to order seamlessly

## Upsell Offer Types
**E-commerce:**
- Related products
- Bundles with discount
- Extended warranty/protection
- Subscription for recurring product

**SaaS/Apps:**
- Annual upgrade (vs monthly)
- Higher tier with more features
- Add-on features
- Team/multi-user plan

## Conversion Optimization
- **Timing is critical**: Show immediately after purchase (commitment bias)
- **Relevance matters**: Contextual offers based on initial purchase
- **Remove friction**: One-click = no re-entering payment
- **Value framing**: "Complete your order with..."

## Upsell Sequence Best Practices
1. **First upsell**: Related/complementary product (highest conversion)
2. **If declined, downsell**: Smaller/cheaper version of upsell
3. **If accepted, second upsell**: Premium version or bundle
4. **Limit to 2 offers max**: Don't annoy with too many

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Upselling unrelated products (tanks conversion)
- Requiring re-entering payment info (kills conversion)
- Too many upsell screens (3+ offers = annoying)
- Upsell more expensive than original purchase (low conversion)
