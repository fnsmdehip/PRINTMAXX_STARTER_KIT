# Pricing Strategy: Annual Plan Anchoring (Center Position)

## Source
- App pricing model research 2026
- Subscription app case studies

## Tactic
Place annual plan in the center position marked as "Best Value" to use price anchoring psychology.

## Specific Numbers
- **Price anchoring works almost universally** for influencing perception
- **Annual plans in center** marked "Best value" drive highest conversion
- **One paywall redesign** using better anchoring lifted conversion to **3.24% (20% improvement)**
- **Anchoring with higher reference price** increased perceived value by **32%** and boosted purchase intent
- **Visual cues** can lift bundle conversion rates by **up to 30%**
- **Even .99 ending** can lift conversion by **3-7%**

## How It Works
1. Design pricing page with 3 tiers
2. Place annual plan in center (not right)
3. Add "Best Value" badge/ribbon
4. Show monthly equivalent price for comparison
5. Display savings prominently ("Save 40%")

## Implementation Requirements
- 3-tier pricing structure
- Visual design supporting center emphasis
- Clear savings calculation

## Visual Best Practices
- Highlighted/elevated center option
- Color contrast for "Best Value" badge
- Larger font/box for middle tier
- Monthly equivalent shown below annual price
- Savings in dollars AND percentage

## Pricing Psychology Principles
- **Anchoring**: High price on left makes middle feel reasonable
- **Framing**: "Save $100" > "Only $300" messaging
- **Center bias**: Users naturally gravitate to middle option
- **.99 pricing**: $9.99 converts better than $10.00 (3-7% lift)

## Applicable Money Methods
- APP_FACTORY (MM001) - Annual subscription positioning
- INFO_PRODUCTS (MM002) - Course tier positioning
- SAAS (MM004) - SaaS plan positioning
- CONTENT_FARM (MM006) - Subscription content tiers

## Implementation Priority
HIGH - Proven 20-32% improvement in conversion/perceived value, zero cost to implement

## Example Structure
```
[Basic - Monthly]     [BEST VALUE - Annual]     [Premium - Annual]
     $9.99/mo              $99.99/year              $199.99/year
                        (Save $20 vs monthly)
```

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Annual plan on the right (not center)
- No "Best Value" visual indicator
- Hiding the savings calculation
- More than 3 options
- Whole dollar pricing ($10 vs $9.99)
