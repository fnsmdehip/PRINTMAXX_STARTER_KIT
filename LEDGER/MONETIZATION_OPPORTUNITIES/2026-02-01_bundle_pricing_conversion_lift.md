## The Tactic

**Product bundling** simplifies decision-making and increases perceived value, resulting in a **55% lift in AOV** and **86% increase in revenue per user** when executed properly. Bundles can increase Average Order Value by **30-150%** for bundle orders.

## Specific Numbers

### Conversion Impact

- **AOV lift:** 55% increase in Average Order Value
- **Revenue per user:** 86% increase
- **Bundle order AOV:** 30-150% higher than single-item purchases
- **Visual cue impact:** Up to 30% lift in bundle conversion rates
- **Cross-sell revenue:** 10-30% of total eCommerce revenue (Forrester)

### Real Brand Results

**Coconu (intimacy lubricant brand):**
- **20% increase in AOV** since implementing bundles

**Maev (DTC dog food):**
- **15% increase in AOV**
- **20% jump in units per transaction**

### Discount Strategy

- **Lower-priced bundles ($60):** Use percentage ("Save 25%")
- **Higher-priced bundles ($150):** Use dollar amount ("Save $30")
- **Sweet spot:** 15-25% discount vs individual item pricing

## Why It Works

1. **Simplifies choice:** Pre-curated bundles reduce decision paralysis
2. **Perceived value:** Discount creates urgency and value perception
3. **Discovery:** Exposes customers to products they wouldn't buy separately
4. **Higher margins:** Bundle low-margin with high-margin items
5. **Inventory management:** Move slow-moving products via bundles

## Implementation Requirements

**Tools Needed:**
- Shopify bundle app (if eCommerce) or custom bundle logic
- Pricing calculator to ensure profitability
- Bundle design assets (product photos, graphics)
- A/B testing platform (Google Optimize, VWO)

**Technical Setup:**
1. Identify complementary products that make sense together
2. Calculate bundle pricing (15-25% discount vs individual)
3. Create bundle SKUs in inventory system
4. Design bundle presentation (visual cues critical)
5. Set up cross-sell recommendations
6. Test bundle placement on product pages

**Time to Implement:** 4-8 hours for basic bundles, 1-2 weeks for optimized cross-sell system

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Don't** bundle unrelated products (damages perceived value)
2. **Don't** offer tiny discounts (<10% - not motivating enough)
3. **Don't** over-discount (>30% - trains customers to wait for sales)
4. **Don't** hide individual pricing (creates distrust)
5. **Don't** create too many bundle options (decision paralysis)

## Variations

### 1. Complementary Bundles
**Example:** "Complete Writing Stack"
- Notion templates + content calendar + swipe file
- **Best for:** Logical product pairings

### 2. Good-Better-Best Tiers
**Example:** "Twitter Growth Bundle"
- **Good:** Templates only ($27)
- **Better:** Templates + guide ($47)
- **Best:** Templates + guide + 1-on-1 call ($197)
- **Result:** 60% choose Better, 25% choose Best

### 3. Buy More, Save More
**Example:** App subscriptions
- 1 app: $9.99/month
- 2 apps: $16.99/month (15% off)
- 3 apps: $21.99/month (27% off)

### 4. Seasonal/Limited Bundles
**Example:** "New Year Productivity Bundle"
- Time-limited offer creates urgency
- Combine products across categories
- 30-day availability only

### 5. Cross-Category Bundles
**Example:** "Solopreneur Starter Pack"
- App Factory guide + cold email templates + landing page builder
- **Benefit:** Exposes customers to products they wouldn't discover

## Bundle Pricing Formula

**Step 1: Calculate individual total**
Product A + Product B + Product C = $X

**Step 2: Apply discount**
15-25% discount = $Y savings

**Step 3: Set bundle price**
Bundle price = $X - $Y

**Step 4: Verify margin**
Ensure bundle maintains 50%+ profit margin

**Example:**
- Individual total: $120
- 20% discount: $24 savings
- Bundle price: $96
- Cost of goods: $40
- Bundle margin: 58% ✓

## Personalization Strategy

**96% of marketers agree:** Segmentation is key to boosting bundling offers.

**Personalization tactics:**
1. **Behavioral bundles:** Based on browsing history
2. **Purchase history bundles:** "Complete your collection"
3. **Cart abandonment bundles:** Add complementary item with discount
4. **Email segmentation:** Different bundles for different customer segments
5. **Quiz-based bundles:** Recommend bundle based on quiz answers

## Visual Presentation Best Practices

**High-converting bundle design includes:**
- All products clearly visible (not just bundle box)
- Savings amount prominently displayed ("Save $45")
- Price comparison (crossed-out individual total vs bundle price)
- "Most popular" or "Best value" badge
- What's included checklist
- Individual product images (not generic bundle graphic)

**A/B test elements:**
- Badge placement ("Best value" vs "Most popular")
- Savings format ("Save 25%" vs "Save $30")
- Layout (grid vs vertical stack vs carousel)
- CTA copy ("Get bundle" vs "Start saving" vs "Buy now")

## Advanced: Dynamic Bundling

**Automatically suggest bundles based on:**
1. Cart contents (add these items for $X more)
2. Frequently bought together data
3. Customer segment (new vs returning)
4. Time of year (seasonal bundles)
5. Inventory levels (push slow-moving products)

**Tools:**
- Shopify bundles app
- Custom recommendation engine
- AI-powered product matching
