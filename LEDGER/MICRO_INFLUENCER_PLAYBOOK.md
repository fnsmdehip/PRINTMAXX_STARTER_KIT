## Outreach Template (Cold DM/Email)

### Subject: Quick collab idea for {handle}

```
Hey {name},

Saw your content on {specific post/topic}. Really liked how you {specific thing they did}.

Building {product} - {one sentence description}. Think your audience would actually use it.

Open to:
- Gifted access (full free account)
- Paid collab ($X for {deliverable})
- Affiliate rev share (20-30%)

No pressure either way. Figured I'd reach out since it actually fits what you post about.

- {Your name}
{Your handle}
```

### Key Principles:
- Reference specific content (shows you actually looked)
- Offer multiple comp structures
- Keep under 100 words
- No fluff/superlatives
- Make declining easy

## Engagement Signals to Prioritize

When evaluating micro-influencers:

### GREEN FLAGS (High-Trust Audience)
- Comments are actual conversations (not just emojis)
- Reply rate from creator is high
- Dedicated community terms (inside jokes, rituals)
- Low follower-to-engagement ratio (<5% is great)
- Repeat commenters across posts
- Discord/community attached to account

### RED FLAGS (Fake/Low-Value)
- Comments all generic ("nice!" "love this!")
- Sudden follower spikes (bought)
- No replies to comments from creator
- High followers, no engagement
- Giveaway-heavy account
- Engagement pods visible

## Campaign Types (Bootstrap Budget)

### 1. Product Seeding ($0)
- Send free product/access
- No guarantee of post
- Works for nano creators who like product
- 10-20% conversion to organic post

### 2. Gifted + Requested ($0-50)
- Free product + small bonus for posting
- More reliable than pure seeding
- Good for micro creators

### 3. Flat Fee ($150-500)
- Set price for deliverable
- Clear expectations
- Negotiate usage rights separately

### 4. Affiliate/Rev Share (0% upfront)
- 15-30% commission
- Lower risk
- Works with engaged audiences
- Combine with gifted product

### 5. Hybrid (Recommended)
- Small upfront ($50-100) + rev share (15-20%)
- Aligns incentives
- Best ROI typically
