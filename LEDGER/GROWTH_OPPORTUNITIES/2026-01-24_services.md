### Email Warmup Timeline & Strategy

**New Email Address:**
- **Duration:** 14 days minimum, up to 4 weeks
- **Starting Volume:** 5-10 emails/day
- **Target Volume:** 60-100+ emails/day
- **Increase:** Gradual, monitoring engagement

**New Domain:**
- **Duration:** 2-4 weeks
- **Starting Volume:** 5-10 emails/day
- **Critical:** Domain reputation starts from zero

**Older Domain:**
- **Duration:** 1-2 weeks
- **Faster ramp-up:** Existing reputation
