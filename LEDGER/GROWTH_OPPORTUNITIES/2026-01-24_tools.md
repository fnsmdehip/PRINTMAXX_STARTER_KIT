## Social Media Management Tool Comparison

### Buffer vs Hootsuite

**Buffer:**
- **Pricing:** Free (3 channels) → $5/month per channel
- **Best For:** Simple scheduling, small teams
- **UX:** Best interface and user experience (modern)
- **Cost Example:** 2 users, 2 channels = $20/month

**Hootsuite:**
- **Pricing:** Starting at $99/month (5 accounts), no free tier
- **Best For:** Enterprise, feature-rich needs
- **UX:** Feels outdated and clunky
- **Cost Example:** 2 users, 2 channels = $198/month

**Recommendation:** Buffer for bootstrap/solopreneurs
