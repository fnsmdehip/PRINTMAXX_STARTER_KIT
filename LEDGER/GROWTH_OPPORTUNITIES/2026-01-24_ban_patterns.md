## Instagram Ban Patterns

### What is a Shadowban?

**Official Term:** "Reduced visibility" or "recommendation limits"

**What Happens:**
- Posts don't appear in hashtag feeds
- Reduced reach to non-followers
- Stories hidden from Explore

**Duration:** 1-3 weeks (most cases resolve within 7 days)

**Evidence:** [Instagram Shadowban 2026](https://hidemyacc.com/instagram-shadowban)

---

### Shadowban Triggers (Instagram)

**1. Banned/Problematic Hashtags**
- Hashtags flooded with spam
- Hashtags removed from search
- Using them hurts visibility
- **Check:** Search hashtag first, if no "Top" posts = banned

**Evidence:** [Instagram Shadow Ban](https://litcommerce.com/instagram-shadow-ban/)

---

**2. Excessive or Spammy Activity**
- Sudden spike in activity
- Repetitive content/behavior
- Following too many accounts in short span
- Inconsistent engagement patterns

**Risk:** Triggers spam detection algorithms

**Evidence:** [Instagram Bans Guide 2026](https://socialrails.com/blog/instagram-bans-and-restrictions-guide)

---

**3. Community Guidelines Violations**
- Violence content
- Sexually suggestive content
- Misinformation
- Hate speech

**Evidence:** [Spikerz Instagram Shadowban](https://www.spikerz.com/blog/5-common-causes-of-being-shadowbanned-on-instagram)

---

**4. Fake Engagement and Bots**
- Paying for likes/comments
- Using bots to increase followers
- Engagement pods
- Automated actions

**Risk:** Automatic shadowban trigger

**Evidence:** [Instagram Shadowban Fix](https://www.adspower.com/blog/solutions-how-to-fix-instagram-shadowban-in-2024)

---

**5. Inconsistent Posting Patterns**
- Sporadic posting (long gaps then bursts)
- Repetitive content
- Identical captions
- Same type of content repeatedly

**Evidence:** [Instagram Shadowban Complete Guide](https://contentstudio.io/blog/instagram-shadowban)

---

**6. Multiple Accounts on Same IP**
- Running 10+ profiles on shared Wi-Fi
- Instagram links them via IP
- Flagged as "farm"
- Agencies with 100 accounts without IP rotation: 80% ban rate

**Evidence:** [Instagram IP Ban 2026](https://okkproxy.com/blog/how-to-solve-instagram-ip-ban-with-okkproxy/)

---

### Instagram Recovery Steps

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Pause for 48-72 hours (reset account activity)
2. Remove banned hashtags from all posts
3. Stop all automation tools
4. Review content for Community Guidelines compliance
5. Delete any violating posts

**After Pause:**
1. Resume organic activity only
2. Post high-quality, original content
3. Engage authentically (no automation)
4. Use 3-5 safe hashtags maximum
5. Avoid sudden activity spikes

**Evidence:** [Later Instagram Shadowban](https://later.com/blog/instagram-shadowban/)

## TikTok Ban Patterns

### Common Ban Reasons

**1. Community Guidelines Violations (Most Common)**
- Hate speech, harassment
- Nudity, sexually explicit content
- Real-world violence/torture
- Misinformation
- Copyrighted material without permission

**Evidence:** [TikTok Content Violations](https://support.tiktok.com/en/safety-hc/account-and-user-safety/content-violations-and-bans)

---

**2. Fake Engagement and Spam**
- Using bots to inflate followers/likes/comments
- Third-party growth services detected
- Repetitive or misleading content
- Bot detection software triggers

**Evidence:** [TikTok Account Suspended Guide](https://socialrails.com/blog/tiktok-account-suspended-complete-guide)

---

**3. Age Restrictions**
- Under 13 (under 14 in Indonesia, Quebec, South Korea)
- Immediate ban if detected
- No warning given

**Evidence:** [TikTok Account Safety](https://support.tiktok.com/en/safety-hc/account-and-user-safety/account-safety)

---

**4. Prohibited Links and DMs**
- Posting prohibited links (external sites)
- Spammy DMs
- "Not Allowed" content in messages
- Repeat violations = account ban

**Evidence:** [TikTok Transaction Policy](https://support.tiktok.com/en/safety-hc/account-and-user-safety/transaction-policy-violations-and-bans)

---

**5. Impersonation/Fraud**
- Impersonating another person/entity
- Deceptive behavior
- Fraud attempts

**Evidence:** [TikTok Accounts Features](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/accounts-features)

---

### TikTok Strike System

**Warning Strike:**
- First violation (non-severe) = warning
- No penalty yet
- Educational notice

**Strike:**
- Severe first violation = immediate strike
- Repeat violations = strike
- Strikes expire after 90 days

**Permanent Ban:**
- Multiple strikes
- Serious violations
- Attempts to bypass restrictions

**Evidence:** [TikTok Account Suspended](https://multilogin.com/blog/tiktok-account-suspended/)

---

### TikTok Recovery

**Appeal Process:**
1. Submit one well-written appeal with all evidence
2. Don't submit multiple appeals (delays review, hurts case)
3. If denied, wait 30 days before new appeal
4. Provide additional evidence in second appeal

**Response Time:** 3-14 business days

**Evidence:** [TikTok Suspended Complete Guide](https://socialrails.com/blog/tiktok-account-suspended-complete-guide)

## Appeal Best Practices (All Platforms)

### General Guidelines

**DO:**
1. Submit ONE well-written appeal
2. Include all evidence upfront
3. Verify identity if requested
4. Be professional and concise
5. Wait for response (3-14 days)

**DON'T:**
1. Submit multiple appeals immediately
2. Be aggressive or demanding
3. Create new accounts to bypass ban
4. Rush the process
5. Lie or provide fake documentation

**Evidence:** [How To Appeal Guide](https://onlinecensorship.org/how-to-appeal)

---

### Platform-Specific Appeal Tips

**Instagram:**
- Visit Instagram Help Center
- "My account was disabled" option
- Verify identity (email, phone, government ID)
- Persistence works (multiple attempts over time)
- Direct support channels if available

**Evidence:** [Instagram Bans Recovery](https://socialrails.com/blog/instagram-bans-and-restrictions-guide)

---

**TikTok:**
- One comprehensive appeal
- All evidence included
- If denied, wait 30 days
- New evidence required for second appeal
- Response: 3-14 business days

**Evidence:** [TikTok Appeal Process](https://socialrails.com/blog/tiktok-account-suspended-complete-guide)

---

**Twitter/X:**
- File appeal immediately
- Remove violating content first
- Verify identity if requested
- Review varies (days to weeks)
- Follow up if no response after 2 weeks

## Prevention Checklist

### Account Setup
- [ ] Strong unique password
- [ ] Two-factor authentication enabled
- [ ] Verified email and phone
- [ ] Government ID on file (if needed)
- [ ] Profile complete and legitimate

### Daily Operations
- [ ] Stay within platform rate limits (see Automation Limits doc)
- [ ] Use only approved automation tools (API-based)
- [ ] Avoid banned hashtags (check before using)
- [ ] Consistent posting schedule (no sudden bursts)
- [ ] Original content (no copyright violations)

### Content Guidelines
- [ ] No hate speech, violence, nudity
- [ ] No misinformation
- [ ] No impersonation
- [ ] Proper age verification
- [ ] Cite sources for claims

### Technical
- [ ] Stable IP address (avoid frequent VPN changes)
- [ ] Same device/browser for logins
- [ ] Mobile proxies if running multiple accounts (see SOAX guide)
- [ ] Avoid linking too many accounts to one IP
- [ ] Clear cookies/cache periodically

## Emergency Response Plan

**If Account Restricted/Banned:**

**Hour 1:**
1. Stop all activity immediately
2. Document what happened (screenshots)
3. Review recent posts for violations
4. Check email for platform notices

**Day 1:**
1. File appeal (if eligible)
2. Remove violating content
3. Verify identity if requested
4. Pause other accounts on same IP

**Week 1:**
1. Monitor appeal status
2. Prepare backup accounts (different IPs)
3. Export data if possible
4. Notify stakeholders (if business account)

**After Resolution:**
1. Resume slowly (50% normal activity)
2. Monitor closely for 2-4 weeks
3. Document what triggered ban (update guardrails)
4. Implement prevention measures

## Key Metrics to Monitor (Early Warning Signs)

**Engagement Drop:**
- 50%+ drop in reach = possible shadowban
- Comments/likes suddenly decrease
- Story views plummet

**Visibility Issues:**
- Posts not appearing in hashtag feeds
- Profile not showing in search
- Content hidden from Explore

**Platform Notices:**
- "Action blocked" messages
- "We removed your post" notifications
- "Your account has been restricted" warnings

**Take Action Immediately if You See These**
