## Instagram Algorithm Overhaul

**Recommendation Engine Shift**
- Instagram is now a "recommendation engine" not content-sharing app
- Multiple AI-driven ranking systems (Feed, Stories, Reels, Explore)
- Watch time now most important metric (not just views)

**Critical Timing:**
- First 24-48 hours of engagement determine distribution
- Early window crucial for reach

**What Algorithm Prioritizes (in order):**
1. **Watch time & retention** - How long viewers stay
2. **Saves & shares** - Deeper relevance signals than likes
3. **Caption dwell time** - Time spent reading captions
4. **Engagement quality** - Relevance over follower count

**New Features:**
- Trial Reels (test with non-followers before posting to audience)
- Carousel expansion (20 photos/videos per post, up from 10)
- Original content rewards (stop recycling TikToks)

**Hidden Metrics Tracked:**
- Dwell time on posts
- Save/share frequency
- Scroll speed past content
- Caption expansion rate

**Format Priority:**
- Reels get most distribution (discovery engine)
- Carousels for engagement
- Stories for retention

**Implementation:**
1. Focus on watch time optimization (hook in first 3 seconds)
2. Use Trial Reels to test content before main feed
3. Write long captions to increase dwell time
4. Create original content (no TikTok reposts)
5. Post Reels consistently for discovery

**Evidence:**
- [Instagram Algorithm 2026](https://blog.hootsuite.com/instagram-algorithm/)
- [Buffer IG Guide](https://buffer.com/resources/instagram-algorithms/)
- [Shopify IG Algorithm](https://www.shopify.com/blog/instagram-algorithm)

**Risk Assessment:** LOW - Clear signals, actionable

## TikTok Algorithm & Ownership Changes

**Major Ownership Shift:**
- Oracle audit of recommendation system (US operations)
- Algorithm retrained on US user data only
- Transfer completed January 22, 2026

**Algorithm Changes:**
1. **Watch time priority** - Videos watched in full/rewatched favored
2. **Follower-first distribution** - New videos shown to followers first (first few days), then algorithm decides wider distribution based on follower engagement
3. **Niche content focus** - Deep engagement beats broad reach
4. **Consistency boost** - 3 videos/week over 6 months beats 10 videos in 3 days then silence
5. **Search value metric** - Content must align with user searches

**Monetization Updates:**
- Creator Rewards Program: $0.40-$1.00+ per 1,000 views (20-30x increase from old Creator Fund)
- Video must be 60+ seconds to monetize
- Search value now affects earnings
- TikTok Shops expansion

**Implementation:**
1. Create 60+ second videos (monetization requirement)
2. Post 3x per week minimum (consistency boost)
3. Show to followers first, optimize for their engagement
4. Focus on niche topics with search value
5. Full watch time = priority (hook + retention)

**Evidence:**
- [TikTok Updates 2026](https://www.heyorca.com/blog/tiktok-social-news)
- [TikTok Algorithm Shift](https://www.traackr.com/influencer-marketing-blog/tiktok-algorithm-evolution-influencer-marketing-strategy)
- [Creator Monetization](https://medium.com/@marketolog4limes/tiktok-creator-hub-2026-new-monetization-opportunities-for-micro-creators-bb90d9635573)

**Risk Assessment:** MEDIUM - Ownership changes may cause unpredictable shifts
