## Pattern Recognition System

### Pattern 1: AI Agent Product (MOLT-Type)

**Characteristics:**
- Real platform/product launches FIRST
- Token launches AFTER platform gains traction
- Autonomous agents/users create organic content
- Network effects sustain growth beyond initial hype

**Entry Signals:**
- ✅ Platform has 10K+ real users/agents before token
- ✅ Viral X/Reddit coverage with >100K views
- ✅ Major tech announcement (AI, automation, social)
- ✅ Team is doxxed or has track record

**Entry Timing:** Within 2h of token launch announcement

**Exit Strategy:**
- Take 50% profit at 10x
- Let 50% ride with trailing stop (Peak -20%)
- Maximum hold: 48 hours

**Expected ROI:** 5,000-18,000%
**Win Rate:** 100% (based on MOLT)
**Time to 2x:** 2-6 hours
**Risk Level:** HIGH (fast exit required)

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ Token launches BEFORE platform has users
- ❌ Anonymous team
- ❌ No working product demo
- ❌ Low liquidity (<$50K)

---

### Pattern 2: Brand Virality (SARATOGA-Type)

**Characteristics:**
- Viral video/event features specific brand
- Video has >50M views in <72 hours
- Brand is recognizable (not obscure)
- Content is meme-able (eccentric, funny, shocking)

**Entry Signals:**
- ✅ Viral video >50M views in <48h
- ✅ Specific brand name (not generic concept)
- ✅ Meme-able content
- ✅ Coin launches DURING viral peak (not after)
- ✅ No major competing tokens

**Entry Timing:** Within 2h of coin launch detection

**Exit Strategy:**
- Fixed 48h hold (DO NOT hold past 72h)
- Trailing stop: Peak -20%
- If 10x in 24h, take 50% profit immediately

**Expected ROI:** 5,000-30,000%
**Win Rate:** 100% (if timing is perfect)
**Time to 2x:** 3-6 hours
**Risk Level:** VERY HIGH (trend exhaustion after 3 days)

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ Viral video is >7 days old
- ❌ Generic trend (not specific brand)
- ❌ Competing token with 10x liquidity
- ❌ Brand sends cease & desist

---

### Pattern 3: Viral Cultural Trend (GHIBLI-Type)

**Characteristics:**
- Major tech/culture announcement (OpenAI, Apple, etc.)
- Generic trend (AI art, style transfer, etc.)
- Multiple competing tokens launch simultaneously
- No product/utility backing

**Entry Signals:**
- ✅ Major platform announcement (OpenAI, Apple, Netflix)
- ✅ Global brand recognition (Studio Ghibli, Pokemon, Star Wars)
- ✅ Visual/meme-able content
- ✅ You are FIRST to launch (within 2h of announcement)

**Entry Timing:** Within 2h of FIRST coin launch (not 10th)

**Exit Strategy:**
- Fixed 48h hold MAXIMUM
- Take profit at ANY 2x
- Do not chase higher multipliers

**Expected ROI:** 30-50% (realistic)
**Win Rate:** 50-62.5%
**Time to Peak:** 60-72 hours
**Risk Level:** VERY HIGH (low ROI, slow growth, high competition)

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ 10+ similar coins already launched
- ❌ Announcement is >12h old
- ❌ No visual/meme-able component

**Verdict:** AVOID unless you are first to market AND accept 30-50% gains (not 1000%+)
