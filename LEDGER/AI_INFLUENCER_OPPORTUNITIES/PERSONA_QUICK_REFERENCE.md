## Persona Specs

### ASMR
**Visual:** Calming, peaceful (optional - many ASMR creators don't show face)
**Voice:** Soft, gentle, whispered (ElevenLabs critical)
**Content:** Trigger sounds, roleplay, ambient sounds (30min-2hr)
**Platforms:** YouTube primary, TikTok for discovery
**Monetization:** Ads ($2-10 CPM), Patreon ($5-20/mo), sponsorships (Calm, Headspace, Casper, Purple)
**Cross-Pollination:** Sleep timer apps, relaxation content farm

---

### FINDOM
**Visual:** Luxury aesthetic, confident, dominant poses
**Voice:** Commanding, superior
**Content:** Tribute demands, drain sessions, lifestyle flex, task assignments
**Platforms:** Twitter/X (discovery), LoyalFans (monetization), Telegram (private)
**Monetization:** Tributes ($20-100+), custom content ($50-500), drain sessions ($100-1000+), subs ($20-50/mo)
**Cross-Pollination:** None (standalone niche)
**RISK:** Adult content, platform bans, chargebacks

---

### FITNESS COACH
**Visual:** Athletic, energetic, motivational
**Voice:** Encouraging, energetic
**Content:** Workout demos, form tips, nutrition basics, fitness challenges
**Platforms:** TikTok primary, Instagram secondary, YouTube tertiary
**Monetization:** Affiliate (MyProtein, Bodybuilding.com 5-8%), workout plan subs ($20-50/mo), supplement sponsors, YouTube ads
**Cross-Pollination:** WalkToUnlock app, fitness timer apps, fitness content farm

---

### FAITH DEVOTIONAL
**Visual:** Peaceful, warm, approachable
**Voice:** Gentle, encouraging, compassionate
**Content:** Daily devotionals, scripture reflections, prayer tips, faith encouragement
**Platforms:** TikTok primary, Instagram secondary, YouTube tertiary
**Monetization:** Affiliate (Christian books, apps, devotionals), subs ($5-15/mo), digital products (prayer guides $10-50), sponsorships (faith brands)
**Cross-Pollination:** PrayerLock app, faith content farm

---

### NICHE EXPERT (AI/Productivity)
**Visual:** Professional, tech-savvy, accessible
**Voice:** Clear, informative, helpful
**Content:** Tool reviews, productivity tips, AI explainers, software comparisons
**Platforms:** X/Twitter primary, LinkedIn secondary, YouTube tertiary
**Monetization:** B2B affiliate (SaaS 20-50%), sponsored posts (software companies), info products (courses, templates), consulting (human-delivered)
**Cross-Pollination:** Productivity apps, automation tools

---

### GAMING
**Visual:** Gamer aesthetic, energetic, relatable
**Voice:** Energetic, enthusiastic, relatable
**Content:** Game tips, reactions, commentary, gameplay highlights
**Platforms:** Twitch/YouTube primary, TikTok for clips
**Monetization:** Ads, sponsorships (peripherals, energy drinks), affiliate (gaming gear), subscriptions (Discord, Patreon)
**Cross-Pollination:** Gaming content farm

---

### LIFESTYLE
**Visual:** Polished, aspirational, aesthetic
**Voice:** Friendly, relatable, warm
**Content:** Product recommendations, lifestyle tips, organization, home decor
**Platforms:** Instagram primary, TikTok secondary, Pinterest tertiary
**Monetization:** Affiliate (Amazon, fashion, beauty, home goods), sponsored posts (lifestyle brands), digital products (planners, templates)
**Cross-Pollination:** Women's content farm

---

### RELATIONSHIP ADVICE
**Visual:** Warm, trustworthy, empathetic
**Voice:** Compassionate, supportive, understanding
**Content:** Relationship tips, communication strategies, Q&A, couple challenges
**Platforms:** TikTok primary, Instagram secondary, YouTube tertiary
**Monetization:** YouTube ads, affiliate (relationship courses, books), sponsored posts (dating apps), digital products (guides, workshops)

## Launch Checklist (Per Persona)

### Pre-Launch
- [ ] Character design documented (visual, voice, personality)
- [ ] Avatar assets created (Leonardo.ai character model)
- [ ] Voice established (ElevenLabs settings locked)
- [ ] 10 pieces of content ready
- [ ] Disclosure language finalized ("I'm an AI [role]")
- [ ] Platform accounts created (TikTok first)
- [ ] Bio includes AI disclosure + affiliate disclosure
- [ ] Affiliate programs joined

### Launch Week
- [ ] Post first 3-5 pieces
- [ ] Engage with early comments
- [ ] Monitor for issues
- [ ] Adjust based on response
- [ ] Track metrics (views, engagement rate)

### Post-Launch (First 30 Days)
- [ ] Post daily consistently
- [ ] Build engagement routine
- [ ] Track metrics weekly
- [ ] Iterate on content format
- [ ] Identify high-engagement topics
- [ ] Double down on winners

### Monetization (Month 3+)
- [ ] 1k+ followers achieved
- [ ] Affiliate links driving clicks
- [ ] First sponsorship pitch sent (at 10k followers)
- [ ] Digital product created (if applicable)
- [ ] Subscription platform set up (Patreon/Ko-fi)

## Key Success Metrics

### Growth Targets
- Month 1: 0-500 followers
- Month 2: 500-1k followers
- Month 3: 1k-3k followers
- Month 6: 10k followers (brand deal threshold)
- Year 1: 50k+ followers (scale)

### Engagement Targets
- Engagement rate: 3-5% minimum
- Save rate: 1-2% (quality signal)
- Link CTR: 0.5-2%

### Revenue Targets
- Month 1-3: $0-50/mo (learning phase)
- Month 3-6: $100-500/mo (first affiliate commissions)
- Month 6-12: $500-2k/mo (sponsorships + products)
- Year 1+: $2k-10k/mo (brand deals + multiple streams)
