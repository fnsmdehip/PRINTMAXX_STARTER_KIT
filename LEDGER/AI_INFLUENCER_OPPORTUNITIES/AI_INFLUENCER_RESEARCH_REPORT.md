## Compliance (Critical)

### Double Disclosure Requirement
AI influencers need TWO disclosures:
1. **AI nature disclosure:** "I'm an AI [role]"
2. **Affiliate/sponsored disclosure:** "This post contains affiliate links. I earn commission on purchases."

### NY Law (June 2026)
- AI disclosure required for ads
- Must be conspicuous
- Video disclosure must be spoken (not just caption)

### FTC Requirements
- Disclosure before affiliate link
- Same medium (video mention in video)
- Understandable to audience
- No hidden relationships

### Visual Disclosure Options
1. Bio: "I'm an AI"
2. Watermark: "AI-Generated Content"
3. Intro slate: "I'm [Name], your AI [role]"
4. Caption: "[AI avatar]" visible throughout

## Content Strategy

### 90/10 Value-Promotion Ratio
- **90% value content:** Educational, entertaining, engaging, helpful
- **10% promotional content:** Natural product mentions, dedicated reviews, comparison content, deal alerts

### Value Content Types
- Tips and tutorials
- Q&A sessions
- Myth-busting
- Listicles (Top 5, Best 10)
- Case studies
- Behind-the-scenes (AI creation process)

### Promotional Content Types
- Honest reviews (positives + negatives + verdict)
- Tool stacks
- Problem-solution posts
- Deal alerts

### Posting Cadence
- Post daily until something hits
- Analyze engagement by content type
- Double down on high-engagement formats
- Track: views, likes, comments, saves, shares, link clicks

## Voice Framing for AI Personas

### Instead of Human Framing:
❌ "I love this product"
❌ "In my experience..."
❌ "I recommend this"

### Use Data-Backed Framing:
✅ "This product consistently gets positive feedback for [specific benefit]"
✅ "Users report..." or "Research shows..."
✅ "Top-rated option for [use case]"

### Why This Works:
- Builds credibility (AI can cite data)
- Avoids deceptive "I tried it" claims
- FTC compliant
- Plays to AI strengths (research, synthesis)

## Affiliate Integration

### Honest Review Framework
**Every affiliate review includes:**
1. **Positives (3-5 points):**
   - Specific benefits
   - Who it's good for
   - What impressed (data-backed)

2. **Negatives (1-3 points):**
   - Honest limitations
   - Who it's NOT for
   - What could be better

3. **Verdict:**
   - Clear recommendation or not
   - Context for decision
   - Alternative options

### Why Honesty Works:
- Builds trust
- Reduces returns (better product-audience match)
- Creates loyal audience
- FTC compliance
- Better long-term conversion
