### 2. Deliverability Requirements (CRITICAL)

**File:** `2026-02-01_deliverability_gmail_yahoo_2026_enforcement.md`

**BREAKING: November 2025 Enforcement Active**
- Gmail and Yahoo NOW strictly enforcing requirements
- Non-compliant emails face temporary or permanent rejection
- This is not future tense - it's happening NOW

**Mandatory Setup:**
- SPF record (authorize sending servers)
- DKIM signature (cryptographic validation)
- DMARC policy (alignment requirement)
- PTR records (reverse DNS)
- One-click unsubscribe

**Spam Rate Thresholds:**
- Hard ceiling: **0.3%** (exceeding = rejection)
- Recommended target: **<0.1%**

**Who Must Comply:**
- Anyone sending >5,000 emails/day to Gmail/Yahoo
- Realistically: Everyone doing cold email should comply

**Cost to Comply:**
- Domains: $30-75/year (3-5 domains)
- Email tool with warmup: $40-300/mo
- Monitoring: $0 (Google Postmaster Tools free)

**What Happens If You Skip:**
- Best case: Spam folder
- Likely case: High bounce rate, sender score tanks
- Worst case: Domain/IP blocklisted
- Recovery time: 30-90 days minimum

**Implementation Priority:** CRITICAL (do this FIRST or don't do cold email)

### 3. LinkedIn Voice Messages

**File:** `2026-02-01_linkedin_voice_messages_30pct_boost.md`

**Key Finding:**
- Voice messages: **+30% engagement** vs text-only
- Estimated response rate: **8-9%** (vs 6-7% baseline)

**Why It Works:**
- Novelty factor (still relatively new)
- More personal/human
- Harder to ignore
- Stands out in crowded inbox

**Best Practices:**
- Keep it **30 seconds max**
- Ask permission first (text DM → voice message)
- Focus on value, not pitch
- Clear CTA at end

**30-Second Script Template:**
1. Intro (5 sec): "Hey [Name], this is [You] from [Company]"
2. Context (10 sec): Reference specific observation, mention similar case
3. Value (10 sec): Share result achieved
4. CTA (5 sec): "Would you be open to 15-min call?"

**Omnichannel Sequence:**
- Day 0: Connection request (personalized)
- Day 1: Welcome message (text)
- Day 2: Cold email
- Day 5: Voice message (30 sec)
- Day 8: Email follow-up
- Day 12: LinkedIn text (resource)
- Day 15: Breakup email

**Implementation Priority:** MEDIUM
- Test on 30 highest-value targets
- Compare to text-only baseline
- Scale if 30%+ boost confirmed

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: Domain/IP Burnout**
- Mitigation: Use 3-5 domains, rotate sending, monitor spam rate daily
- Cost of failure: Lost domains ($30-75), 30-90 day recovery

**Risk 2: Low Response Rates**
- Mitigation: A/B test constantly, use CIR framework, target quality lists
- Cost of failure: Wasted time + tool cost

**Risk 3: Compliance Issues**
- Mitigation: Follow SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup, include unsubscribe, <0.1% spam rate
- Cost of failure: Blocklisting, legal issues (CAN-SPAM fines)

**Risk 4: LinkedIn Automation Detection**
- Mitigation: Manual voice messages (no automation), stay under 10-15/day
- Cost of failure: Account restriction/ban
