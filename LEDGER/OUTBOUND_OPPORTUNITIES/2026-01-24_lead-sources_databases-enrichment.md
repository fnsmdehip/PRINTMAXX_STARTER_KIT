## LinkedIn Lead Scraping (Legal Considerations)

### Legal Status 2026
- **Scraping public data:** Legal in many jurisdictions (hiQ Labs v. LinkedIn precedent)
- **Scraping private/login-protected:** NOT legal
- **LinkedIn ToS:** Explicitly prohibits all scraping
- **Risk:** Account restrictions or bans

### Compliance Best Practices
- Scrape ONLY public data
- Respect rate limits (throttle, rotate, low-frequency)
- Mimic organic user behavior
- Avoid PII (emails, phone numbers from profiles)
- Use LinkedIn official APIs when possible (Sales Navigator API)
- Start small, increase slowly (avoid new account + mass scraping)

### Legal-Compliant Tools

**Evaboot** (Sales Navigator focused)
- Chrome extension for Sales Navigator
- GDPR-compliant lead generation
- Email finding from SalesNav data
- Best for: Sales Navigator users

**ScrapIn**
- GDPR, CCPA compliant
- Real-time B2B data extraction
- Legal framework focus
- Best for: Compliance-first approach

**PhantomBuster**
- Transparent automation
- Publicly available data only
- Platform guideline adherence
- Best for: Automated scraping at scale

**Skrapp**
- Business email discovery
- LinkedIn profile → contact data
- Outbound sales focused
- Best for: Email enrichment from LinkedIn

### Anti-Ban Strategies
- Spread out actions (profile visits, connection requests)
- Start with low volume, increase gradually
- Use residential/mobile proxies
- Cloud scrapers with safe IP rotation
- Avoid sudden activity spikes from new accounts
