## Winning Framework: CIR (Context-Insight-Relevance)

**Structure:**
1. **Context:** Reference something specific (company, role, recent news)
2. **Insight:** Share observation or pattern you've noticed
3. **Relevance:** Connect to how you can help

**Why It Works:**
- Demonstrates research (not spray-and-pray)
- Shows expertise without pitching
- Natural transition to offer

**Source:** [Cold Email Benchmark Report 2026](https://instantly.ai/cold-email-benchmark-report-2026)

## 3×3 Cold Email Sequence

**Pattern:** 3 emails over 3 weeks

**Email 1 (Day 0):**
- <80 words
- Single CTA
- CIR framework
- Personalized opening

**Email 2 (Day 7):**
- New angle or insight
- Reference first email subtly
- Different value prop

**Email 3 (Day 14):**
- Breakup or final attempt
- "Should I close your file?" angle
- Permission-based close

**Results:** Gentle persistence without spam vibes. Spaced touches increase odds.

**Source:** [Email Sequence Strategy 2026](https://www.cleverly.co/blog/email-sequence)

## 2026 Shift: Volume → Precision

**Key Trend:**
> "In 2026, the winners shift from volume to precision, with elite cold email teams running intelligence-led outbound and optimizing for engagement-first metrics."

**Elite Team Characteristics:**
- AI agents handle ~80% of research & sequencing
- Hyper-targeted lists (quality > quantity)
- Deep personalization at scale
- Engagement metrics prioritized over send volume

**Implication:** Don't just scrape massive lists. Use AI to research each prospect and craft specific opens.

## Best Practices (2026 Data-Backed)

**Email Length:**
- First email: <80 words
- Keep it scannable
- One idea per email

**CTAs:**
- Single CTA only
- Question format works best
- Low friction (15-min call, not "demo")

**A/B Testing:**
- Frequent message testing required
- Test subject lines continuously
- Small tweaks = big lifts

**Follow-Up Timing:**
- First follow-up: 3-7 days
- Second follow-up: 7-14 days
- Don't exceed 3 total touches in one sequence

## Infrastructure Requirements

- SPF, DKIM, DMARC (mandatory)
- Warmup period: 2-4 weeks
- Send limits: Start 20-30/day per inbox, scale to 50-70
- Multiple domains for rotation
- Monitor spam complaint rate: <0.1% target, <0.3% hard ceiling

**Cost Estimate:**
- Email tool: $30-100/mo (Instantly, Smartlead)
- Domains: $10-15/year each (need 3-5)
- Warmup service: Often included in tool
- Lead data: $50-200/mo (Apollo, Hunter)

**Total:** ~$100-300/mo for full stack

## Compliance Notes

**CAN-SPAM Requirements:**
- Physical address in footer
- Clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days
- No deceptive subject lines

**GDPR (if EU targets):**
- Legitimate interest basis
- Easy opt-out
- Data processing documentation

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Set up 3-5 domains with proper DNS (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Choose tool: Instantly (recommended for flat-fee unlimited) or Smartlead (best deliverability infra)
- [ ] Warmup inboxes for 2-4 weeks
- [ ] Build targeted list (<500 to start)
- [ ] Write CIR-framework first email (<80 words)
- [ ] Create 2-email sequence (first + 1 follow-up at 7 days)
- [ ] A/B test 2 subject line variants
- [ ] Monitor reply rate and spam complaints daily
- [ ] Scale sending gradually (20/day → 50/day over 2 weeks)
