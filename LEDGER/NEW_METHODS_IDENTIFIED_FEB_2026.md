### MM090: AI_SPEED_ARBITRAGE_SERVICE
**Source:** ALPHA528 (@gregisenberg)

**Core Model:**
- Deliver services 10-30x faster using AI tools
- Charge market rates (priced for human time)
- Market pricing lags AI capability 6-12 months
- Speed = arbitrage opportunity

**Examples:**
- Content writing: AI delivers in 1 day, charge for 15 days
- Design work: AI mockups in 2 hours, charge for 2 weeks
- Research reports: AI compiles in 4 hours, charge for 40 hours
- Code: AI generates in 1 week, charge for 1 month

**Revenue Model:**
- Service pricing at market rates
- AI tools cost $200-500/mo
- Margins: 80-95%

**Why New Method:**
- Not just AI wrapper (MM026)
- Not agency (MM005)
- Unique arbitrage window (temporary until market reprices)
- Service delivery not product

**Cross-Pollination:**

### MM095: OUTCOME_BASED_SAAS (Pricing Model Evolution)
**Source:** ALPHA525 (@gregisenberg outcome-based pricing)

**Core Model:**
- Charge per result not per seat/usage
- Examples:
  - Email tool: charge per booked meeting (not per inbox)
  - PrayerLock: charge per streak milestone (not monthly sub)
  - Content tool: charge per viral post (not per publish)
- Performance risk = customer acquisition advantage

**Why New:**
- Not traditional SaaS subscription (MM004)
- Not service (MM005)
- Hybrid: software + outcome guarantee

**Revenue Model:**
- Pay-per-outcome pricing
- Higher per-transaction revenue
- Lower conversion friction (no upfront commitment)
- Align incentives with customer success

**Challenges:**
- Must deliver outcomes reliably
- Higher operational complexity
- Performance risk

**Cross-Pollination:**
- Stacks with MM004_SAAS (delivery mechanism)
- Stacks with MM007_COLD_OUTBOUND (outcome = booked meeting)
- Stacks with MM001_APP_FACTORY (outcome = behavior change)
