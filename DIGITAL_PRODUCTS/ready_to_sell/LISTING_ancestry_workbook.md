Most family histories die with the last person who remembers them.

This workbook changes that.

**The Ancestry Workbook** is a 20-page guided workbook for anyone who wants to document their family's story before it's gone — whether you've done DNA testing, you're just starting out, or you have a grandfather who's been meaning to share his stories for 30 years.

### What's Inside

**Phase 1 — The Living Map**
A 5-generation family tree you can fill in by hand. It goes back far enough to show you where your family really came from — not just who your grandparents were, but who theirs were.

**Phase 2 — Ancestor Profiles**
Two full profile pages for key ancestors. Name, dates, birthplace, occupation, immigration story, siblings, children — and space for the stories that don't fit in any database.

**Phase 3 — The Interview Guide**
40 questions to ask elderly relatives while you still can. Three categories: early life, heritage and migration, and values and legacy. Most families never have this conversation. These questions start it.

**Phase 4 — Heritage Discovery**
Map your DNA ethnicity breakdown across 6 regions. Document DNA test results, haplogroups, and match surprises. Build a family migration timeline from pre-1800 to present.

**Phase 5 — Story and Legacy**
7 guided writing prompts. A 26-row research log. A genealogy source checklist with 30 databases and document types. Heritage recipe pages. Heirloom documentation.
