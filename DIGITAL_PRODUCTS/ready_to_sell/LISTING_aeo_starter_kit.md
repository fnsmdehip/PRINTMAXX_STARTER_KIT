**You're writing content that Google crawls. But your customers are asking ChatGPT.**

In 2026, AI assistants — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini — handle more searches than Google for information-based queries. They read your content. They decide whether to cite it. Most content gets skipped entirely.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the discipline of making your content the source AI engines quote. This toolkit is the only interactive tool built specifically for it.

### What's inside (one HTML file, runs locally, no subscription):

**1. Content AEO Auditor**
Paste any page or blog post. Get an instant score across 10 AEO signals — question-format headers, structured lists, specific stats, defined terms, content depth. See exactly what's blocking AI citation and get a prioritized fix list.

**2. Schema Markup Builder (4 schema types)**
Generate production-ready JSON-LD in seconds:
- FAQPage schema — the single highest-impact AEO change you can make
- Article schema — author, date, headline structured data
- HowTo schema — step-by-step content for process queries
- Product schema — for product pages targeting "best X" queries

Copy-paste the output directly into your page head. No code knowledge needed.

**3. AI Query Mapper**
Enter any topic. Get 20+ question queries mapped by intent (how-to, what-is, best-of, comparison, pricing). Each query tagged with citation potential and content gap status. Know exactly what to write before you write it.

**4. 87-Check AEO Master Checklist**
Six categories: Content Structure (15 checks), Schema & Technical (15), Query Targeting (15), Authority & Trust (12), Measurement (10), Distribution (11). Checkboxes persist in browser storage. Progress bar tracks readiness score. Built for auditing existing content and planning new content.

**5. Prompt Template Library (4 prompts)**
Copy-paste prompts for Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI:
- AEO Rewrite: transforms existing content into citation-ready format
- FAQ Generator: creates 15 Q&A pairs in the exact phrasing AI engines receive
- Featured Snippet Optimizer: targets both Google and AI citations simultaneously
- Citation Bait Creator: generates stat blocks, definition cards, comparison tables

**6. AI Citation Tracker**
Track whether ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini cite your content for specific queries. Log results weekly. See your citation rate. Export to CSV for client reporting.
