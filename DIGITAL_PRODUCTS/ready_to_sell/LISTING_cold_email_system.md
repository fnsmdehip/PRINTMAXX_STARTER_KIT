**Chapter 1: Infrastructure (Your Foundation)**

Set up 3 sending domains with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication. Configure free email accounts through Zoho Mail or ImprovMX + Gmail. Warm your domains using free tools so your emails land in Primary, not Spam. Includes a mail-tester.com scoring walkthrough — you'll know your deliverability score before sending a single outreach email.

Total cost: $10/year per domain. $0 for everything else.

**Chapter 2: Prospecting (Your Hit List)**

Build a list of 500+ qualified prospects using Google Maps scraping, LinkedIn Boolean search, competitor customer poaching, job board mining, and community research. Each method includes exact search queries, free tools with setup instructions, and a lead qualification framework so you don't waste emails on bad prospects.

Every prospect gets a personalization data point — something specific enough that they'll think you spent 10 minutes researching them.

**Chapter 3: AI Personalization (Your Edge)**

Use Claude to generate personalized emails in batches of 10. Each email references specific details about the prospect's business — their recent LinkedIn post, a specific page on their website, a metric from their Google listing. The master prompt template eliminates AI slop: no "leverage," no "innovative," no "I hope this finds you well."

50 personalized emails in 30 minutes. Each one sounds like you wrote it by hand.

**Chapter 4: Seven Proven Sequences**

Not one template. Seven complete sequences, each with 3-4 follow-up emails:

1. **The Problem Spotter** — for when you've found a specific issue on their website
2. **The Social Proof Lead** — for when you have a killer case study in their industry
3. **The Value-First Audit** — for when you can provide free value upfront
4. **The Referral Ask** — for finding the right decision-maker
5. **The Trigger Event** — for capitalizing on funding, launches, and expansions
6. **The Mutual Connection** — for leveraging shared networks
7. **The Content-First Approach** — for prospects active on social media

Each sequence includes the initial email, follow-up timing, and the breakup email. Pick the one that matches your situation and deploy it today.

Reply handling for every response type: positive interest, soft interest, wrong person, "not now," objections, and hard no. Discovery call script (20 minutes, structured). Proposal template with a two-option pricing strategy. Follow-up cadence after sending the proposal. Pipeline tracking spreadsheet.
