## Sample Data Guidelines

- Use realistic but fictional company names (Acme Corp, NovaTech, Peak Digital, etc.)
- Lead names should be diverse (mix of first names, no stereotypes)
- Deal values: $500-$10,000 range (typical freelance/agency deals)
- Reply rates: 10-20% range (realistic for cold outreach)
- Close rates: 3-8% range
- Show enough volume (200+ leads) to demonstrate the system at scale
- Include some "Dead" leads to show the template handles rejection realistically
- Dark mode Notion theme for all screenshots
