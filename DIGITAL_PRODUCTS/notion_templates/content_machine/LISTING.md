- 10 LinkedIn post frameworks

- 5 YouTube title/thumbnail concept templates
- each template includes: structure, example, why it works, when to use it

- track views, engagement, clicks, and conversions per post
- weekly content performance summary
- best performing content type identification
- platform-by-platform comparison
- "double down" indicators: which content formats to make more of

**content bank**
- dump every idea, screenshot, bookmark, and inspiration into one database
- tag by topic, platform, content type, and urgency
- "evergreen" flag for ideas that work any time
- "trending" flag for time-sensitive ideas
- search and filter to find the right idea for the right moment

- most content calendars are just calendars. this is a pipeline. ideation feeds creation feeds scheduling feeds tracking feeds back to ideation.
- the repurposing tracker alone saves 5+ hours per week. one piece of content becomes 6.
- 120 pre-loaded hooks means you never stare at a blank screen.
- everything auto-connects. publish a post, it shows up in your analytics. tag it as "repurposed from X", it links back to the original.
