## Screenshot 1: 90-Day Calendar View
**Purpose:** Hero image. Shows the content calendar with color-coded platforms across multiple weeks.
**What to capture:**
- Calendar view showing 3 weeks of March 2026
- 3-5 content items per day, color-coded by platform
- Platform pills clearly visible and distinguishable
- Mix of statuses visible (Published in green, Scheduled in blue, Draft in gray)
- Week numbers visible in left margin

**Crop:** Full calendar view, tight margins
**Used on:** Gumroad hero, Notion Marketplace hero, all marketplaces primary
**Filename:** `01_calendar_overview.png`

## Marketplace-Specific Requirements

### Gumroad
- Cover: 1280x720 (use Screenshot 1 or custom cover from LISTING.md spec)
- Gallery: Up to 7 images. Use all 7 screenshots.

### Notion Marketplace
- Cover: 1600x900
- Gallery: Up to 5 images
- Priority: Screenshot 1, 2, 3, 5, 6

### Whop
- Cover: 1200x630
- Gallery: Up to 6 images
- Priority: Screenshot 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6

### Etsy
- Primary: 2700x2025 (reframed Screenshot 1)
- Gallery: Up to 10 images
- Use all 7 + 3 text-overlay slides (features list, platform comparison, what's included)

### Creative Market
- Cover: 1820x1214
- Preview: Up to 6 images
- Priority: Screenshot 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7

## Sample Data Guidelines

- Use realistic content titles that a solopreneur would actually post
- Views range: 200-15,000 (realistic for growing creator)
- Engagement rates: 0.5%-8% range
- Show 1-2 "Viral" posts with 10K+ views alongside mostly 500-3,000 view posts
- Dates centered around March 2026
- Dark mode Notion theme for all screenshots
- Show enough data to demonstrate the system works but not so much it looks cluttered
