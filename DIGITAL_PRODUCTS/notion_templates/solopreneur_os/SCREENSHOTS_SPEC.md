## Marketplace-Specific Image Requirements

### Gumroad
- Cover image: 1280x720 minimum (use Screenshot 1 or custom cover image per spec in LISTING.md)
- Gallery: Up to 7 images. Use all 8 screenshots, drop Screenshot 8 if limited.
- Format: PNG or JPG

### Notion Marketplace
- Cover: 1600x900
- Gallery: Up to 5 images
- Priority order: Screenshot 1, 3, 7, 2, 5
- Format: PNG

### Whop
- Cover: 1200x630
- Gallery: Up to 6 images
- Priority: Screenshot 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 4
- Format: PNG or JPG

### Etsy
- Primary: 2700x2025 (4:3 ratio) - create a cropped/reframed version of Screenshot 1
- Gallery: Up to 10 images
- Use all 8 screenshots, add 2 text-overlay slides (features list, "what's included")
- Format: JPG preferred

### Creative Market
- Cover: 1820x1214
- Preview: Up to 6 images
- Priority: Screenshot 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 6
- Format: PNG

---

## Sample Data Guidelines

All sample data in screenshots must:
- Use realistic but fictional names (no real companies or people)
- Show revenue numbers in the $1K-$10K range (target audience level)
- Use current dates (March 2026)
- Include a mix of "messy real" data (not everything perfect, some items overdue, some habits missed)
- Show enough entries to demonstrate scale but not so many the view is overwhelming
- Use the dark mode Notion theme for all screenshots
