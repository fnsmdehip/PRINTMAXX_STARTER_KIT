- Kanban board with 6 columns: Lead (3 cards), Proposal Sent (4 cards), Negotiating (2 cards), Active (5 cards), Delivered (3 cards), Paid (8 cards)

- Realistic gig names: "Website redesign - Peak Digital", "Email sequence - FitPro", "Brand strategy - NovaTech"
**Crop:** Full board view
**Used on:** Gumroad hero, Notion Marketplace hero, all marketplaces primary
**Filename:** `01_gig_pipeline.png`

## Sample Data Guidelines

- Use realistic freelance gig names and prices ($500-$5,000 range)
- Client names: fictional but realistic (Peak Digital, NovaTech, FitPro Academy, etc.)
- Show a mix of project types (web dev, content, consulting, marketing)
- Revenue numbers in the $3K-$6K/month range (realistic solo freelancer)
- Include one overdue invoice to show the template handles real situations
- Show some "Below Target" rate gigs to demonstrate why tracking matters
- Include at least one repeat client with high LTV
- Dark mode Notion theme for all screenshots
- Dates centered around March 2026
