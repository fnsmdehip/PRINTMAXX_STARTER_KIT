## Full Description

Cold email is how most B2B SaaS companies get their first 10-100 customers. Not content marketing. Not product-led growth. Cold email, done correctly.

The problem is that most cold email advice is written for agencies and freelancers. SaaS is different: your product domain is also your login, billing, and support infrastructure. Your buyers are corporate, using Microsoft Exchange or hardened Google Workspace. Your sale is a demo, a trial, and a conversion — not a single transaction.

This playbook covers everything specific to SaaS outbound.

**What is inside:**

Part 1 — Infrastructure
Domain architecture that keeps your product domain clean. Google Workspace setup for outreach inboxes. SPF, DKIM, DMARC configuration step-by-step. Warmup protocol for B2B deliverability. CRM setup with the exact deal stages that matter. Three free tools for finding verified B2B emails.

Part 2 — Prospecting
ICP definition framework across four variables: company profile, tech stack, budget signal, and pain signal. Six specific prospect sources: G2/Capterra review mining, Product Hunt, LinkedIn signal search, GitHub for developer SaaS, community mining (Reddit, Slack, Discord), and funding announcement targeting. Five prospect research templates tied to specific triggers. The full workflow for building lists from competitor review sites — the highest-ROI method for early-stage SaaS.

Part 3 — Sequences
Seven complete 3-email sequences (initial + 2 follow-ups) with subject lines and personalization instructions:
1. The Better Alternative — targeting users of competitor products
2. The Integration Play — your tool works with their stack
3. The ROI Calculator — specific dollar savings framing
4. The Case Study Drop — lead with a relevant success story
5. The Free Audit — genuine process assessment
6. The Mutual Connection — shared context outreach
7. The Problem-Aware — they publicly posted about the problem you solve

Each sequence includes a reply rate benchmark and instructions for when to use it based on prospect signal type.

Part 4 — Deliverability
What corporate spam filters actually evaluate and how to stay out of them. The plain-text advantage in B2B outreach. Optimal send times by company size. The scaling ladder: 10 emails/day to 200 emails/day without burning domains. The 50-emails-per-inbox ceiling and how to scale past it.

Part 5 — Closing
Reply categorization with the right response for each type. Demo booking and structure. Objection handling for five common SaaS objections (competitor, no budget, we'll build it, not a priority, price). Free trial versus paid trial decision framework. The 30/60/90-day pipeline nurture for "not now" prospects.

Includes benchmark data: open rates, reply rates, demo conversion, trial-to-paid by method.

**Format:** Markdown (readable in any text editor, browser, or Notion). Structured for sequential execution, not random reference.

**Length:** 1,000+ lines. Every section is operational, not theoretical.
