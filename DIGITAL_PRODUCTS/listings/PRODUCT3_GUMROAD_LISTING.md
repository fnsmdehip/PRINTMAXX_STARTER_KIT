698 lines of outreach copy. tested across 7 verticals. copy-paste ready.

**what's inside:**

**the 6-question framework**
every cold email must answer these 6 questions in under 100 words:
1. what do you do?
2. who do you do it for?
3. how do you do it?
4. what problem does it solve?
5. what's the proof?
6. what's the ROI?

most cold emails answer 1-2 of these. the ones that convert answer all 6. framework + examples for each question.

**4 complete email sequences**
- sequence 1: problem-first (3 emails, 7-day cadence)
- sequence 2: case study lead (3 emails, 5-day cadence)
- sequence 3: question-hook (4 emails, 10-day cadence)
- sequence 4: breakup sequence (2 emails, after no reply)
- every email: subject line, body, CTA, timing, A/B variant

**7 vertical-specific templates**
- agency owners: "we built [result] for [similar agency]"
- coaches: "your content is doing the hard work. this fills the calendar"
- content creators: "you're leaving $X/mo on the table from your existing audience"
- ecommerce: "your abandoned cart rate is probably 70%. here's the fix"
- healthcare: "HIPAA-compliant patient acquisition that doesn't feel like marketing"
- legal: "your competitors are getting leads from [channel]. you're not."
- SaaS: "your trial-to-paid is probably 8-12%. here's how to hit 20%+"

each template includes the full 3-email sequence plus follow-up timing.

**15 LinkedIn templates**
- 5 connection request templates (industry-specific hooks)
- 5 first-message templates (after connection accepted)
- 5 voice note scripts (30-second scripts that get replies)
- timing: when to send, when to follow up, when to stop

**deliverability setup**
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC configuration (step-by-step)
- domain warmup schedule (14-day plan)
- sending limits by provider (Google Workspace, Outlook, custom SMTP)
- spam trigger words to avoid
- reply rate benchmarks by industry

**A/B testing framework**
- what to test first (subject line > opening line > CTA)
- sample sizes for statistical significance
- tracking spreadsheet template
- 12 subject line formulas with open rate data

**reply handling playbook**
- positive reply: how to move to call (exact script)
- neutral reply: how to re-engage without being pushy
- objection handling: price, timing, "we have someone", "not interested"
- when to stop: the 3-touch rule
