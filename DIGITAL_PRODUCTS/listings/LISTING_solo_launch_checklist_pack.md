## Full Description

most solo launches fail because of execution gaps, not product gaps.

the app works. the pricing is fine. the positioning is reasonable. but the sitemap was never submitted. SPF wasn't configured. the Product Hunt listing went live at 3 PM instead of 12:01 AM. the refund policy linked to a 404.

these are $1,000 mistakes that cost $9 to prevent.

**what's inside:**

**Checklist 1: App Launch (35 items)**
pre-launch infrastructure through post-launch triage. domain setup, SSL, analytics, error tracking, payment integration, legal baseline, launch day social sequence, monitoring protocol, and post-launch iteration priorities. covers every phase from code-complete to first paying customer.

**Checklist 2: SEO Audit (28 items)**
technical SEO, on-page, and off-page. robots.txt, sitemap submission, Core Web Vitals, canonical URLs, title tags, meta descriptions, H1 structure, internal linking, alt text, directory submissions. run it once at launch, quarterly after. most founders skip half of this.

**Checklist 3: Cold Email QA (22 items)**
SPF, DKIM, DMARC setup. warmup status. spam score testing. personalization token QA. copy review: subject line, word count, CTA, spam trigger words. post-send monitoring: bounce rate, reply management, follow-up triggers. run this before every campaign, not once.

**Checklist 4: Product Hunt Launch (23 items)**
the exact 2-week preparation sequence, 1-week warm-up, launch day timing (it starts at 12:01 AM PST — not whenever you wake up), first comment strategy, all-day response protocol, and post-launch follow-through. product hunt is a one-shot window. most people waste it on logistics they should have handled 10 days earlier.

**Checklist 5: Revenue Infrastructure (27 items)**
Stripe configuration, payment link setup, refund policy, fraud protection, funnel conversion tracking, cohort retention metrics, email capture, lead magnets, upsell sequences, annual plan upgrade timing, affiliate program setup, directory listings, and legal minimum viable compliance (terms, privacy policy, cookie consent, GDPR deletion requests).
