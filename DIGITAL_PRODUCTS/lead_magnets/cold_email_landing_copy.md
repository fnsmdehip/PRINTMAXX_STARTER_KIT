## Notes for Implementation

- Gate is email only (no name field). Fewer fields = higher conversion.
- Deliver the file immediately on submit via email or redirect to a download page.
- Follow-up sequence: single email at day 3 pointing to ColdMaxx or the ROI calculator. Nothing more.
- Track open rate on the confirmation email, not just the opt-in rate. If they don't open the confirmation, the list is cold from day one.
