🔒 DNS and Authentication (35 points)
SPF record configured8 pts
TXT record: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all (or your ESP). check at mxtoolbox.com/spf.aspx
DKIM signing enabled8 pts
DKIM adds a cryptographic signature to every email. your ESP should provide the DNS record. verify at dkimvalidator.com
DMARC policy set7 pts
TXT record at _dmarc.yourdomain.com: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com. start with p=none, move to p=quarantine after 2 weeks.
Custom tracking domain5 pts
don't use shared tracking domains from your ESP. set up track.yourdomain.com or links.yourdomain.com as a CNAME.
MX records valid4 pts
your sending domain needs valid MX records even if you only send from it. receiving mail proves domain legitimacy.
Reverse DNS (PTR) set3 pts
IP address resolves back to your domain. usually handled by your ESP. check at mxtoolbox.com/ReverseLookup.aspx
🔥 Warmup Protocol (25 points)
Warmup tool active (14+ days)6 pts
use Instantly or Smartlead's built-in warmup. minimum 14 days before any cold sends. 21 days is safer for new domains.
Warmup volume: 30-50/day5 pts
start at 5-10 warmup emails/day, ramp to 30-50 over 2 weeks. don't rush. inbox providers track send velocity.
Inbox placement > 85%5 pts
check your warmup dashboard. if inbox rate is below 85%, pause cold sends and keep warming. 90%+ is the goal.
Keep warmup ON during campaigns5 pts
never turn off warmup when you start cold sending. it maintains your sender reputation while real campaigns run.
Domain age > 30 days4 pts
new domains get extra scrutiny. buy domains at least 30 days before you need them. 60+ days is better.
📨 Sending Setup (25 points)
Daily limit: 30-50 per account5 pts
max 30-50 cold emails per mailbox per day. more = spam folder. scale with more mailboxes, not more volume per box.
Multiple sending accounts (3+)4 pts
rotate across 3+ mailboxes minimum. 10+ is ideal for serious outbound. each on a different domain or subdomain.
Send window: business hours only4 pts
send between 8am-6pm in the recipient's timezone. no weekends. no holidays. humans send during business hours.
Random delays between sends4 pts
60-300 second random delay between emails. consistent timing = bot detection. your tool should handle this.
List verified (bounce rate < 3%)4 pts
run every list through a verification tool (ZeroBounce, NeverBounce) before sending. bounces over 3% = reputation killer.
Unsubscribe link included4 pts
required by CAN-SPAM. add a plain text unsubscribe line at the bottom. looks more human than a fancy button.
✍️ Email Content (15 points)
No spam trigger words4 pts
avoid: "free," "guaranteed," "act now," "limited time," "click here," "make money," excessive caps, multiple exclamation marks.
Plain text format (no HTML)3 pts
plain text emails have higher deliverability than HTML. no images, no fancy formatting. looks like a real person wrote it.
Max 1 link per email3 pts
multiple links = marketing email = spam filter. first email: zero links. follow-ups: max one link. calendly link is fine.
Personalization variables3 pts
use {{firstName}}, {{company}}, {{something specific}}. merge tags aren't optional. personalized emails get 2x reply rates.
Under 120 words2 pts
shorter emails get more replies. aim for 50-120 words. if you can't explain the value in 3 sentences, you don't have a clear offer.
