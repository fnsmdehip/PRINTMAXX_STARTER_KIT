## Offer Structure

### Option A: Flat Fee Per Post
| Content Type | Payment Range | Notes |
|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Instagram Story (3-5 frames) | $50-75 | Lowest barrier, good for testing |
| Instagram Reel (30-60s) | $100-150 | Best ROI for reach |
| TikTok video (30-60s) | $75-125 | Highest organic reach potential |
| YouTube mention (60-90s integrated) | $150-200 | Highest trust, longest shelf life |
| YouTube dedicated review (5-10 min) | $200+ (negotiate) | Only for top performers |

### Option B: Affiliate CPA (Performance-Based)
| Vertical | CPA (per verified signup) | Notes |
|----------|--------------------------|-------|
| GLP-1 programs | $100-175 | Highest payout, highest average order value |
| TRT programs | $100-150 | Strong repeat rate (ongoing subscription) |
| Peptide therapy | $75-150 | Varies by program and peptide type |

### Option C: Hybrid (Recommended Starting Structure)
- $50 upfront per post (covers their time)
- Plus $100-150 CPA for every conversion through their link
- Minimum 2 posts per month
- 30-day trial period, then review performance

### Why Hybrid Works Best:
- $50 upfront gets them to say yes (skin in the game on both sides)
- CPA incentivizes them to create content that converts, not just content that looks good
- Low risk for us: $50/post is nothing if it drives even 1 conversion at $100+ CPA
- Easy to scale: keep paying performers, cut non-performers after 30 days

## Content Brief Template

Send this to every influencer after they agree. Adapt per vertical.

---

**CONTENT BRIEF: [VERTICAL] Promotion**

**What we're promoting:** Online [GLP-1 / TRT / peptide therapy] comparison and reviews

**Your unique link:** [INSERT TRACKING LINK]

**What to say (pick one angle or combine):**
1. "I found this comparison that breaks down [X] programs by price, quality, and what's included. Link in bio."
2. Share your own experience with [the condition/treatment] and mention you found a solid resource for people researching it.
3. "One thing I wish I knew before starting [treatment] is [X]. Here's a guide that covers everything."

**What NOT to say:**
- No medical claims ("this cures X" or "this is clinically proven to do X")
- No income claims ("I made $X from this")
- No fake urgency ("only 3 spots left!")
- No health guarantees ("you WILL lose X pounds")

**FTC requirements (non-negotiable):**
- You MUST include #ad or #sponsored in the caption
- You MUST disclose the paid partnership. Instagram's "Paid Partnership" tag is ideal.
- Verbal disclosure in video: "This video is sponsored by [brand]" or "I'm working with [brand] on this"

**Dos:**
- Be authentic. Share your real opinion.
- Mention specific things you found useful (pricing breakdown, side effect info, clinic comparison)
- Direct people to the link in bio or comment
- Film in your normal style. Don't change your voice for this.

**Don'ts:**
- Don't script it to sound like a commercial
- Don't make claims we haven't approved
- Don't use before/after photos that aren't yours
- Don't remove the disclosure or bury it

**Deliverables:**
- [1-2 posts or videos] within [X days]
- Send us the draft for approval before posting (optional but preferred)
- Share screenshot of analytics 7 days after posting

## Red Flags to Avoid

Before partnering with any influencer, check for these:

### Fake Followers
- **Follower-to-engagement ratio below 1%** on Instagram (5K followers should have 50+ likes per post minimum)
- **Sudden follower spikes** visible on Social Blade (bought followers show as vertical jumps)
- **Generic comments** ("nice!" "great post!" "love this") from accounts with no profile photos
- **Follower location mismatch** (US health influencer with 60% followers from Brazil or Indonesia)

### Engagement Pods
- **Same 20-30 accounts commenting on every post** within minutes of posting
- **Comment quality doesn't match content** (lengthy praise on a simple photo)
- **Engagement drops off after the first 30 minutes** (pod engagement is front-loaded)

### FTC Non-Compliance
- **No #ad or #sponsored tags on clearly paid content** (indicates they don't take compliance seriously)
- **History of promoting sketchy products** (MLMs, crypto scams, fake supplements)
- **No disclosure policy on their website or link in bio** (lack of professionalism)

### Quality Red Flags
- **Content quality is inconsistent** (some posts are professional, others are blurry phone screenshots)
- **They promote a new product every other post** (audience is ad-fatigued)
- **They won't share analytics** (if they won't show you their numbers, the numbers are bad)
- **DMs feel copy-pasted** (they're mass-pitching brands, not genuinely interested)
- **They ask for payment upfront with no deliverable guarantee** (no contract = no accountability)

### Health Content Red Flags
- **Making medical claims without credentials** ("I guarantee this will lower your A1C")
- **Promoting multiple competing health products simultaneously** (today it's GLP-1, tomorrow it's a juice cleanse)
- **No disclaimers on health content** (FTC requires clear disclaimers on health-related sponsored content)
- **Before/after photos that look edited** (audience will notice, kills credibility)

## Compliance Requirements (Non-Negotiable)

### FTC Guidelines for Health Affiliate Promotions

1. **Clear and conspicuous disclosure.** Every paid post must include #ad or #sponsored, or use the platform's built-in partnership disclosure tool. Disclosure must be visible before the "more" fold on captions.

2. **No health claims without substantiation.** Influencers cannot claim a product will cure, treat, or prevent any disease or condition. "This helped me" (personal experience) is acceptable. "This will help you" (health claim) is not.

3. **No fake testimonials.** Influencers can only share their genuine experience. They cannot fabricate results or use someone else's before/after photos.

4. **Material connection disclosure.** If they're being paid (cash, free product, affiliate commission), they must disclose that.

5. **Platform-specific rules:**
   - Instagram: Use "Paid Partnership" tag + #ad in caption
   - TikTok: Use "Branded Content" toggle + verbal disclosure in video
   - YouTube: Check "This video contains paid promotion" in upload settings + verbal disclosure in first 30 seconds

6. **No targeting minors.** GLP-1, TRT, and peptide content must not target anyone under 18. Influencer audience demographics should be 90%+ adult.

7. **Include in contract:** "Influencer agrees to comply with all FTC guidelines for endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). Failure to include proper disclosures may result in termination of this agreement."

## Outreach Process (Step by Step)

### Week 1: Research and List Building
1. Search each platform using queries above
2. Build a spreadsheet: Handle, Platform, Follower Count, Engagement Rate, Niche, Contact Info
3. Target 50 influencers per vertical (150 total)
4. Verify each against red flag checklist
5. Prioritize by: engagement rate > follower count > content quality > niche relevance

### Week 2: Initial Outreach
1. Send cold DM or email to all 150 (use templates from outreach_templates.md)
2. Personalize the first line of every message (reference a specific post of theirs)
3. Follow up non-responders at Day 3

### Week 3: Negotiation and Onboarding
1. Negotiate terms with interested influencers (use hybrid offer structure)
2. Send content brief
3. Set up unique tracking links
4. Get signed agreement (use template from outreach_templates.md)

### Week 4: First Posts Go Live
1. Review draft content before posting
2. Confirm FTC disclosures are included
3. Track performance from day 1
4. Send screenshot request at day 7

### Monthly Ongoing:
1. Review performance of all active influencers
2. Cut non-performers (0 conversions after 3 posts)
3. Increase investment in top performers
4. Add 10-20 new influencers per month to replace churn and scale winners

## Budget Planning

### Starter Budget ($500/month)
- 5-10 influencers at $50/post (flat fee or hybrid)
- 10-20 posts per month
- Expected conversions at 1-2% click-to-signup: 5-15 signups
- At $100-175 CPA revenue per signup: $500-2,625 potential revenue
- Breakeven: 3-5 conversions

### Growth Budget ($2,000/month)
- 20-30 influencers
- 40-60 posts per month
- Mix of flat fee ($50-100) and hybrid deals
- Expected: 20-50 signups
- At $100-175 CPA: $2,000-8,750 potential revenue

### Scale Budget ($5,000+/month)
- 50+ influencers
- 100+ posts per month
- Top performers on retainer ($500-1,000/month exclusive)
- Dedicated YouTube reviewers
- Expected: 50-150 signups
- At $100-175 CPA: $5,000-26,250 potential revenue
