## Launch Sequence Notes

### Timing
- Day 0: Launch day (Tuesday or Wednesday recommended)
- Day 2: Thursday or Friday
- Day 4: Weekend
- Day 6: Sunday (last call before Monday price increase)

### Scarcity Mechanics
- Real price increase (not fake)
- Date specific (not countdown timer)
- Optional: Can also use "Early bird" cohort limit (first 100 buyers)

### A/B Test Ideas
- Subject line: Product name vs. benefit
- CTA: "Get PRINTMAXX OS" vs. "Get it now"
- Price framing: "$97" vs. "$97 (one-time)"

### Tracking Metrics
- Email 1 open rate (target: 30-50%)
- Email 1 click rate (target: 5-10%)
- Email 2-3 open rate (target: 25-40%)
- Email 4 open rate (target: 20-35%)
- Overall conversion rate (target: 3-8%)

### Technical Setup
- Send time: 9am-11am recipient's timezone
- Plain text format
- Clear CTA links (use pretty links, not long URLs)
- Mobile-optimized (test on phone)

### Compliance
- FTC: Digital product disclosure
- CAN-SPAM: Unsubscribe link
- Refund policy clearly stated
- No fake urgency (price increase must be real)
- No income guarantees

### Post-Launch
- After Email 4, move non-buyers to regular content sequence
- Tag buyers for "customer only" emails
- Send product delivery + onboarding sequence

### Refund Handling
- 30-day guarantee (requires proof of use)
- Process within 48 hours
- Ask for feedback (improve product)
- No argument refunds (builds trust)
