## EPISODE 001
**Title: "The 14-Account Twitter Portfolio Strategy ($2,300/mo from Small Niches)"**
**Format: Solo deep-dive | Estimated length: 35-45 min**
**Guest pitch angle: "Why small niche accounts beat large general ones for affiliate income"**

**Hook (2 min):**
- Most people build one Twitter account and try to go viral
- What if the opposite works better?
- I made $2,300 last month from 14 accounts. Combined follower count: 62,200. Average followers per account: 4,442.
- That's more money than most people make from 100K+ followers on one general account.

**Segment 1: Why portfolio beats single account (8 min)**
- Algorithmic risk: one shadowban = revenue cliff if it's your only account
- Niche intent beats size: notary tips account (800 followers) vs general business (6K followers) — notary wins on affiliate income
- Compounding: 14 small accounts compound separately, one bad month doesn't hurt all 14
- Real numbers: breakdown of what each tier earns

**Segment 2: How to pick the niches (10 min)**
- The criteria: people spend money adjacent to the niche, affiliate programs exist, community is underserved on Twitter
- Examples: notary, trucking finance, paralegal study, foster care parents, ketamine therapy info
- How to validate: do affiliate programs exist? do people in this niche buy specific products? is the niche active on Twitter?
- The mistake: picking niches where the audience is there but the spend is low (general "motivation," fitness without specific affiliate angle)

**Segment 3: The content system (10 min)**
- 5 templates that rotate across all accounts
- The Monday batch: 2.5 hours, 42 posts, done for the week
- Buffer + Typefully for scheduling
- The reply strategy: where most of the early growth comes from
- Time math: 45 minutes per day actual work

**Segment 4: Monetization layer (8 min)**
- Affiliate links go in bio on day 1, not after some follower threshold
- The 10/90 rule: 10% promotional, 90% value — what that looks like in practice
- Average commission per niche: $30-100 per conversion
- When to add a paid product layer vs staying affiliate-only

**Close (5 min):**
- Where to start: pick two niches, post for 90 days, then scale
- The portfolio mindset vs the viral mindset
- Resources linked

**Key quotes for show notes/clips:**
- "Small audience, high intent beats large audience, low intent every time."
- "The notary account with 800 followers makes more money than the general business account with 6K. niche is the unlock."
- "I batch all 42 posts on Monday. The rest of the week I'm not thinking about content."

- Day 4: one-sentence bump ("wanted to make sure this didn't get buried")

## EPISODE 005
**Title: "The SEO Play Nobody Runs: 47 Articles, 12K Monthly Visitors, Zero Link Building"**
**Format: Solo technical deep-dive | Estimated length: 40-50 min**
**Guest pitch angle: "How I rank in the top 5 for competitive keywords without any link building"**

**Hook (2 min):**
- I don't build links. Haven't done link outreach in 8 months.
- 47 articles ranking top 5. 12K monthly organic visitors. $1,800/month in affiliate revenue.
- The play: find keywords where current top results are bad, then be better.

**Segment 1: The core insight (8 min)**
- Google ranks the best answer to a query, not the most-linked page
- Forums (Reddit, Quora), old PDFs, and mismatched articles leave keywords open
- The specific pattern: keywords where top results are 5+ years old forum threads
- Why this works now more than it did 3 years ago (Google's helpful content focus)

**Segment 2: Finding the opportunities (12 min)**
- Ahrefs keyword explorer walkthrough
- Filter settings: KD under 20, volume 100-2,000/mo
- SERP preview check: what does the current top result look like?
- The "gap" signals: forum in top 3, PDF in top 3, news article in top 3
- Live examples across 3 niches

**Segment 3: The content format that ranks (10 min)**
- Short beats long in the "better answer" play: 400-800 words, not 4,000
- The structure: answer first, then context, then detail
- The specific numbers formula: "6 steps, $300 filing fee, 3-week timeline"
- Why you don't need a backlink when you're replacing a bad result with a good one

**Segment 4: The tool stack and math (8 min)**
- Ahrefs ($99), Surfer SEO ($89), Perplexity ($20) = $208/month
- Revenue from 12K visitors: $1,800/month affiliate
- ROI: 8.6x on tools
- The scaling math: 8 articles/month, outsourced at $15/hr

**Segment 5: What doesn't work in this strategy (5 min)**
- High-competition keywords (KD 30+) with established sites in top 3
- Topics where intent doesn't match affiliate products
- Brand-name queries

**Close (5 min):**
- Starting point: find 3 keywords this week using free Ahrefs trial
- Write 3 articles before evaluating
- The 6-month patience requirement: SEO is not fast
