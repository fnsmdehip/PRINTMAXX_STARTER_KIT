## PRINTIFY — Primary Supplier

### Why Printify Wins on Margin

Printify is a marketplace of print providers. Best providers:
- **Monster Digital** (US): Best quality US fulfillment, $6.80 tee, 3-5 day production
- **Printify Express** (US): $7.10 tee, 2-3 day rush option
- **Printify China** (via SPOKE): $4.20 tee, 12-18 day shipping — use only for budget-conscious niches
- **DTF Transfers** partners: $5.50, good for all-over print

### Printify Plans
| Plan | Cost | Discount | Break-even |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0/mo | 0% | Always available |
| Premium | $29/mo | 20% off all products | 15+ orders/mo |
| Enterprise | Custom | 20%+ | High volume |

**Premium math:** $29/mo → saves $1.36 per Gildan tee. Break-even = 22 tees/month. If you sell 50+/mo, Premium pays for itself 3x over.

### Printify Setup SOP
1. Create account at printify.com
2. Connect Etsy store (Settings → Stores → Connect)
3. Select product catalog: Start with Gildan 18500 Hoodie + Bella Canvas 3001 Tee
4. Choose Monster Digital as print provider (consistently highest quality in reviews)
5. Upload designs via mockup generator
6. Publish directly to Etsy with auto-populated listing
7. Set pricing: Use Printify's suggested retail → verify $15+ margin after Etsy fees
8. Order sample ($20-30) before scaling any design

### Printify Quality Notes
- **Best quality tees:** Bella Canvas 3001 (soft, retail feel, $9.50 base), Gildan 64000 ($7.20 base, slightly stiffer)
- **Best quality hoodie:** Gildan 18500 ($14.90 base) vs Bella Canvas 3719 ($19.80 base, premium)
- **Mugs:** Printify 11oz ceramic ($5.80) is solid; upgrade to Orca Coatings for dishwasher safety
- **Print quality test:** Order sample of each SKU before listing. Monster Digital DTG consistently the best.
- **Avoid:** Random Chinese providers with no reviews. Check provider rating before selecting.

## PRINTFUL — Secondary / Premium Brand Use

### When to Use Printful
- Building a standalone brand (not just Etsy flipping)
- Shopify storefront where brand quality matters
- Products Printify doesn't carry well (embroidery, cut-and-sew)
- International fulfillment (Printful has EU/JP warehouses = fast EU shipping)

### Printful Pricing Reality
| Product | Printful Base | Printify Best | Difference |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bella Canvas 3001 Tee | $13.50 | $9.50 | $4.00 |
| Gildan Hoodie | $24.90 | $14.90 | $10.00 |
| 11oz Mug | $7.95 | $5.80 | $2.15 |
| Phone Case | $12.95 | $8.50 | $4.45 |
| Tote Bag | $11.50 | $7.80 | $3.70 |

**Conclusion:** Printful is 30-40% more expensive. Only justified if brand positioning supports higher prices OR you need specific products.

### Printful Advantages
- Warehousing available (store your own inventory at Printful = no POD fees)
- Embroidery quality best-in-class ($3-8/placement)
- Returns handled by Printful directly
- Integrates with WooCommerce, Squarespace, BigCommerce, Wix in addition to Shopify/Etsy

## GOOTEN — Backup Supplier

### When to Use Gooten
- Printify stock outs (rare but happens during peak season)
- Products not available on Printify (some niche items)
- Second pricing source for margin optimization

### Gooten Pricing
Similar to Printify at $7-8 range for tees. Quality is slightly more variable — require samples before scaling.

### Gooten Integration
- Connects to Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy
- API available for custom integrations
- Less polished UI than Printify but functional

## AMAZON MERCH ON DEMAND — Passive Layer

### The Amazon Merch Opportunity

Zero cost. Upload design. Amazon handles printing, shipping, customer service, returns. You collect royalty.

### Royalty Table
| Sale Price | Your Royalty |
|---|---|
| $15.99 | $2.08 |
| $19.99 | $3.85 |
| $24.99 | $6.49 |
| $29.99 | $8.55 |
| $39.99 | $13.45 |

### The Catch: Tier System
- New accounts start at **Tier 10** — can only have 10 active designs
- Gets bumped up as you sell: 10 → 25 → 100 → 500 → 1000 → 2000 → 4000 → 8000
- Tier advancement requires sales, not just time
- Getting to Tier 500 takes 6-18 months of consistent selling

### Amazon Merch Strategy
1. Apply at merch.amazon.com (invite system, may wait weeks)
2. Tier 10: Upload 10 highest-confidence designs first. Maximize early sales to advance quickly.
3. Keyword research: Use Merch Informer ($19.99/mo) or free Chrome extension "Merch Titan"
4. Pricing: Start at $19.99. Adjust based on BSR performance.
5. Once Tier 100+: Volume play. Upload 10 new designs per week minimum.

### Amazon Merch vs Etsy Comparison
| Factor | Amazon Merch | Etsy POD |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic | Built-in (massive) | Build your own |
| Margin | ~35% royalty | 60-75% with Printify |
| Control | None | Full |
| Reviews | Amazon ecosystem | Etsy reviews |
| Scale potential | Very high (passive) | High (active) |
| Recommended use | Passive layer | Primary revenue |

## REDBUBBLE — Long-tail Asset

### Why Redbubble Exists in Your Stack

Redbubble is free to join. Upload once. Gets indexed by Google Images. Long-tail SEO traffic finds you for years. Some designs still generating royalties 3 years after upload.

### Redbubble Economics
- Redbubble sets base price. You set markup (20-30% recommended — higher = fewer sales).
- Example: Sticker base $2.59. At 20% markup: you earn $0.52/sticker. At 100 stickers/mo = $52/mo pure passive.
- Not going to make you rich. Will add $50-300/mo with zero ongoing work after initial uploads.

### What Performs on Redbubble
- Stickers (highest volume, lowest margin, Google Images traffic)
- Art prints (higher margin, niche aesthetics)
- Phone cases (consistent demand)
- NOT recommended: Apparel (Amazon Merch and Etsy better for clothing)

### Redbubble Upload Strategy
1. Upload all designs created for Etsy/Amazon
2. Tag aggressively (Redbubble allows 15 tags)
3. Enable ALL product types when uploading
4. Focus tags on aesthetic descriptors people search: "minimalist faith shirt", "vintage mountain art"
5. Check monthly: Disable products with zero sales in 90 days, re-enable with new tags

## WHERE TO SELL: CHANNEL STRATEGY

### Recommended Stack by Stage

**Stage 1 (0-$500/mo):** Etsy + Printify only
- Reason: Etsy has built-in traffic. Low setup cost. Focus on design volume.
- Goal: 100+ listings, first 50 sales, first 10 reviews

**Stage 2 ($500-$2K/mo):** Add Amazon Merch + Redbubble
- Reason: Diversify traffic sources. Amazon Merch builds passively while Etsy is your active income.
- Goal: Tier 25-100 on Merch, 50+ Redbubble uploads

**Stage 3 ($2K-$10K/mo):** Add Shopify store + own brand
- Reason: Own your customer. Email list. Repeat buyers. Influencer/UGC marketing.
- Shopify Printify plan: $29/mo Shopify + free Printify = brand-ready
- Goal: 1K email subscribers, social following, brand identity

**Stage 4 ($10K+/mo):** Consider Printful warehousing for top 10% SKUs
- Reason: Volume discounts on physical inventory. Faster shipping. Better margins at scale.
- Threshold: Only makes sense when a single SKU sells 50+/mo consistently

## FEES FULL BREAKDOWN (Etsy + Printify)

| Fee Type | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Etsy listing fee | $0.20/listing | Renews every 4 months if no sale |
| Etsy transaction fee | 6.5% | On sale price + shipping |
| Etsy payment processing | 3% + $0.25 | US buyers |
| Printify product cost | $6.80-9.50 | Varies by product/supplier |
| Printify shipping | $4.00-6.50 | Domestic US |
| Etsy Ads (optional) | Variable | $1-5/day recommended while scaling |

### Net Margin Calculation Example (Tee at $27.99)
| Line item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Sale price | $27.99 |
| Etsy listing (amortized) | -$0.05 |
| Etsy transaction 6.5% | -$1.82 |
| Etsy payment processing | -$1.09 |
| Printify Bella Canvas tee | -$9.50 |
| Printify shipping | -$4.50 |
| **Net margin** | **$11.03 (39%)** |

**With Printify Premium ($29/mo):**
Tee drops to $7.60 → Net margin $12.93 (46%). Premium breaks even at ~23 sales/month.

**Key insight:** Charge for shipping separately OR embed $5 shipping into product price. Free shipping listing in Etsy search = higher ranking but lower margin. Test both.
