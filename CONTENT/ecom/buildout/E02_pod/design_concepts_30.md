# E02 Print-on-Demand — 30 Design Concepts

## Niche Selection Framework

Before designs: niche drives everything. Best POD niches share 4 traits:
- Passionate community (will buy 3+ items, not just 1)
- Underserved on Etsy/Redbubble (search volume > listing density)
- Specific enough to feel personal ("Jesus loves you" = generic; "3AM prayer warrior" = sells)
- Repeat buyer potential (seasonal + new designs = reorder)

Top 10 niches by margin + competition score (lower = better entry):

## Quick Scale System

### Template Multiplier Strategy
These design templates each generate 10+ listings:
1. **"[Breed] Mom/Dad"** — 50+ breeds = 50+ listings. 1 day of work.
2. **"[State] Native"** — 50 states = 50 listings. Same template.
3. **"[Profession] Mode On"** — nurse, teacher, coach, etc. 20+ professions.
4. **"[City] Is Home"** — 300+ cities = 300+ listings. Geo-targeting goldmine.
5. **"Proud [Nationality] American"** — 50+ nationalities. Gift-ready.

### Revenue Target by Design Count
| Active Listings | Avg Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|
| 10 | $180-400 |
| 50 | $900-2,000 |
| 100 | $1,800-4,500 |
| 200 | $3,500-9,000 |
| 500 | $8,000-22,000 |

Key: 500 listings takes ~60 days at 8/day. Most sellers give up at 50.
