# E06: Etsy SEO Strategy — Algorithm, Tags, Titles

---

## HOW ETSY SEARCH ACTUALLY WORKS

Etsy's search algorithm (called "Ranking") scores listings on 6 factors:

| Factor | Weight (est.) | What it means |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| Relevance | 30% | Title + tag match to search query |
| Listing quality | 25% | Click-through rate + conversion rate |
| Customer experience | 20% | Shop score, reviews, response time |
| Recency | 15% | Newer listings get temporary ranking boost |
| Shipping | 5% | For physical — NA for digital |
| Context-specific ranking | 5% | Buyer location, history |

**Key insight:** Etsy's algorithm is conversion-first. A listing that gets 100 visits and 5 sales outranks a listing that gets 1,000 visits and 5 sales. Optimize for conversion rate, not just traffic.

---

## THE 3-PHASE ETSY ALGORITHM EXPLOIT

### Phase 1: Launch Boost (Days 1-7)
Every new listing gets a temporary recency boost. Use it.

- Launch at 20-30% below your target price to spike conversions
- Run Etsy Ads at $1-2/day during launch week (even tiny budget triggers more impressions)
- Share listing in relevant subreddits, Pinterest, email list to get external traffic
- Goal: 3+ sales in first 48 hours = algorithm tags listing as "converting"

### Phase 2: Lock In Quality Score (Days 7-30)
Quality score = conversion rate × review rate. Once set, it's sticky.

- Target 3-5% conversion rate minimum (industry average: 1-2%, so 3%+ is "above average" = ranking boost)
- Send automated follow-up message at day 3 post-delivery asking for review
- Respond to any messages within 2 hours (affects customer experience score)
- Star Seller badge threshold: 95% 5-star reviews, 95% on-time delivery, $300+ revenue

### Phase 3: Long-tail Compound (Month 2+)
Old listings compound if quality score stays high. New listings with same tags get cross-shop boost.

- Add 1-2 new listings per week minimum to maintain "active shop" signal
- Run sales (20% off) quarterly — triggers "sale" badge in search results, boosts CTR by ~15%
- Refresh listing descriptions every 60 days (minor edits signal fresh content)

## ETSY SEARCH ANATOMY

### How buyers actually search on Etsy:

**Long phrases over single keywords.** Etsy's buyers search like they talk:
- NOT: "planner"
- YES: "habit tracker printable instant download"

**The 4-word sweet spot.** Most converting Etsy searches are 3-5 words.
- "prayer journal printable pdf" = 8,400/mo
- "prayer journal" = 44,000/mo but 10x more competition and lower conversion

**Qualifiers that signal buyer intent:**
- "printable" — they want instant download
- "instant download" — urgency signal
- "editable" — they want to customize
- "bundle" — they expect more value
- "template" — commercial/professional use
- "A4", "A5", "8x10", "letter size" — they know exactly what they need (late-stage buyer)

## TITLE FORMULA

**Etsy title = first 70 characters are shown in search results. Front-load keywords.**

Formula:
```
[PRIMARY KEYWORD] | [SECONDARY KEYWORD] | [FEATURE/FORMAT] | [USE CASE] | [BRAND SIGNAL]
```

Rules:
- Character limit: 140 total, 70 visible in search
- Separate phrases with "|" or commas — both work, "|" reads cleaner
- Capitalize the first letter of each phrase
- Include the word "Printable" or "Template" or "Digital Download" — these trigger Etsy's digital category filter
- No: "beautiful", "amazing", "perfect" — these waste characters
- Yes: format specs ("PDF", "Google Sheets", "Notion"), size specs ("A5", "Letter"), audience signals ("ADHD", "Homeschool", "Christian")

**Examples by product type:**

Digital planner (Notion):
```
Solopreneur Notion Dashboard | Business Planner Template | Freelancer OS | Productivity System | Digital Download
```

Printable PDF:
```
30 Day Prayer Journal Printable | Daily Prayer Pages | Scripture Journal | Instant Download | Christian Planner
```

Google Sheets template:
```
Cold Email Tracker Google Sheets | Sales Pipeline Template | Freelance CRM | Instant Download | Small Business
```

Wall art:
```
Minimalist Quote Wall Art Printable Set of 6 | Entrepreneur Prints | Home Office Decor | Instant Download PDF
```

## TAG STRATEGY

Etsy gives you 13 tags. Each tag can be up to 20 characters. Multi-word tags count as one tag.

**Tag hierarchy:**
- Tags 1-3: Primary keywords (exact match to how buyers search)
- Tags 4-6: Secondary keywords (related searches, synonyms)
- Tags 7-9: Use case / audience (who it's for)
- Tags 10-11: Format / feature descriptors
- Tags 12-13: Occasion / gift angle (broadens reach)

**Rules:**
- Don't repeat words already in your title (Etsy already indexes those)
- Use all 13 — wasted tags = wasted ranking
- Multi-word tags are more specific = higher conversion when they match
- No plurals of what's already in title (algorithm handles it)
- Update tags quarterly based on what's trending in Etsy's search suggestions

## CATEGORY SELECTION

**Always select the deepest available sub-category.** Broad categories = too much competition.

| Product Type | Category Path |
|-------------|--------------|
| Printable planner/journal | Paper & Party Supplies > Paper > Stationery > Planners & Organizers |
| Notion template | Craft Supplies & Tools > Patterns & How To > Other |
| Google Sheets template | Craft Supplies & Tools > Patterns & How To > Other |
| Printable wall art | Art & Collectibles > Prints > Digital Prints |
| Educational printable | Paper & Party Supplies > Paper > Educational Materials |
| Kids activity | Paper & Party Supplies > Paper > Kids' Activities |

**Note on digital templates (Notion/Sheets):** Etsy's categories don't have a perfect fit. Most sellers list under "Craft Supplies > Patterns & How To > Other". This works because it's low competition within that path.

## DESCRIPTION FORMULA

**First 160 characters** = shown as meta description in Google. Write for Google AND Etsy.

Structure:
```
[Consequence-first hook — what problem this solves]

WHAT YOU GET:
- Item 1 (be specific: "30 daily prayer pages, A5 + Letter PDF")
- Item 2
- Item 3

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Step 1 (simple)
2. Step 2
3. Step 3

PERFECT FOR:
- Audience 1
- Audience 2
- Use case 1

FORMATS INCLUDED:
- PDF (A5, A4, US Letter sizes)
- Printable at home or at a print shop

QUESTIONS? Message me and I'll respond within 24 hours.

Note: This is a DIGITAL DOWNLOAD — nothing will be shipped. Files available immediately after purchase.
```

**Key:** Include the phrase "digital download" and "instant download" in the description body. Etsy's search algorithm also indexes the full description.

## ETSY ADS STRATEGY

**Budget: $1-3/day per listing during launch phase. Kill after 30 days if ROAS < 3x.**

Setup:
1. Enable Etsy Ads in Shop Manager
2. Set manual bidding (not automatic — automatic overbids)
3. Start at $0.20 CPC
4. Monitor for 7 days: if CTR > 2%, keep. If CTR < 1%, pause and rewrite title/thumbnail
5. Scale winning ads to $3-5/day

Target ROAS by product price:
- $5-8 products: need 8+ ROAS to be profitable (conversion must be high)
- $10-18 products: 4+ ROAS = profitable
- Bundles $20+: 3+ ROAS = profitable

**The $5 product math:**
- $5 price → $4.10 after Etsy fees (18.3%)
- $0.25 average CPC on ads → need 1 sale per 16 clicks for break-even
- 6.25% conversion rate needed → high bar
- **Better to run no ads on $5 products. Use organic SEO only.**
- **Run ads only on $12+ products.**

## THUMBNAIL (LISTING PHOTO) STRATEGY

Etsy's own data: thumbnail is responsible for 70% of the click decision.

**What converts:**
- Mockup showing the product "in use" (planner open on a desk, journal with coffee)
- High contrast — white/cream background + dark text thumbnail = highest CTR
- Show the page layout clearly — buyers want to see what they're getting
- Include a "word badge" in corner: "Printable PDF", "Instant Download", "Notion Template"
- Mobile-first: 60% of Etsy browsing is on mobile. Text must be readable on 3cm wide thumbnail.

**Image specs:**
- Minimum 2000x2000px
- Ideal: 3000x2250px (4:3 ratio)
- JPG or PNG
- First image = thumbnail (what shows in search)
- Upload 5-10 photos: mockups, all pages shown, size comparison, how-to-use

**Free thumbnail tools:**
- Canva (mockup templates)
- Smartmockups.com (device mockups)
- Creative Market (Etsy-specific mockup bundles, $20-60 one-time)

**Mockup angles to show:**
1. Product in-use on desk (lifestyle)
2. All pages spread out (shows value)
3. Zoomed-in detail on one key section
4. Size comparison (next to common objects)
5. Before/after of problem it solves (optional, high conversion)

## COMPETITOR RESEARCH SOP

Run this before listing any new product:

1. Search your primary keyword on Etsy
2. Filter by "Most Recent" — see what's newly listed
3. Filter by "Top Customer Reviews" — see what's winning
4. Note: price range, thumbnail style, tag use (click "Details" on any listing to see their tags if they're visible in URL)
5. Find the gap: is there a design style no one's doing? A size no one's offering? A niche-within-niche underserved?
6. Use EtsyHunt (free tier) or Alura ($19/mo) for volume data and competitor tag analysis

**Competitor signals to steal:**
- Their 5-star review language = exact words buyers use = use in your tags/description
- Their "frequently bought with" items = bundle opportunity
- Their price point + sales count = demand validation
