# E06: Etsy Digital Downloads Pricing Guide

**The rule:** $5-18 is the sweet spot. Under $5 = buyers assume low quality. Over $25 = buyers expect physical.

Every sale on Etsy costs:

| Fee | Amount | Notes |

| Listing fee | $0.20 | Per listing, per 4 months. Auto-renews. |
| Transaction fee | 6.5% | On sale price + shipping |

| **Total effective fee** | **~18-20%** | On a $10 sale, you net ~$8.05 |

## PRICE SENSITIVITY BY PRODUCT TYPE

### $3-5: Volume Play
**Use for:**
- Single-page printables (one habit tracker, one journal page)
- Seasonal/holiday templates with limited use window
- Loss leaders to get reviews and shop visibility

**Who buys at $5:** Budget-conscious buyers, first-time digital buyers testing the waters, impulse purchasers.

**Conversion rate at $5:** 4-8% (high — low friction)
**Monthly revenue ceiling at $5:** 80 sales × $4.10 = $328/mo

**Risk:** Race to the bottom. Competitors undercut. Hard to run ads profitably.

---

### $7-10: Optimal Entry Price
**Use for:**
- Multi-page printable packs (5-10 pages)
- Single Notion or Google Sheets template
- Seasonal bundles (4-6 templates for one occasion)

**Who buys at $9:** Practical buyers who did 5 minutes of research. Know they're getting a digital product. Care about value over price.

**Conversion rate at $9:** 2.5-4% (good — still impulse territory)
**Monthly revenue ceiling at $9:** 50 sales × $7.35 = $367/mo

**Sweet spot math:** $9.99 psychologically = "less than $10" while netting you 98% more than a $5 product.

---

### $12-18: Value Bundle Zone
**Use for:**
- Multi-template bundles (3+ templates in one listing)
- Notion OS / full workspace templates (multiple databases)
- Google Sheets with multiple tabs/dashboards
- PDFs with 20+ pages + multiple formats (A4 + Letter + A5)

**Who buys at $15:** Research buyers. They compare 3-5 listings before buying. They'll spend 2 minutes reading your description. Quality matters more than price.

**Conversion rate at $15:** 1.5-2.5% (lower but much higher per-sale revenue)
**Monthly revenue ceiling at $18:** 25 sales × $14.65 = $366/mo

**At $12+ — ads become profitable:**
- $12 sale × 82% net = $9.84
- At $0.25 CPC: need 1 sale per 39 clicks = 2.6% CVR → achievable
- ROAS of 4x on $12 = $48 revenue per $12 in ads = very healthy

---

### $20-35: Premium / Bundle Positioning
**Use for:**
- Large bundles (10+ templates in one listing)
- Complete systems (planner + tracker + spreadsheet + Notion all bundled)
- Editable Canva templates (higher perceived value than PDFs)
- "Ultimate" packs with 50+ pages

**Who buys at $25:** Committed buyers. They searched specifically, read carefully, checked your reviews. They're investing, not impulse-buying.

**Conversion rate at $25:** 0.8-1.5% (lower — but high value customers)
**Monthly revenue ceiling at $25:** 15 sales × $20.17 = $302/mo

**When to use $25+:**
- Your listing has 20+ positive reviews
- You have a recognizable brand/shop
- The product is genuinely differentiated (no one else has it)
- Bundle of already-proven individual sellers

---

### $50+: Course/Coaching Territory
Etsy buyers generally don't expect digital products above $50. Above this threshold, move buyers to Gumroad, Whop, or Gumroad.

Exception: Editable InDesign/Illustrator source files, full brand kits, large Canva template collections (100+ templates). These can sell for $39-89 on Etsy.

## THE $5→$15 PRICING ESCALATION STRATEGY

Launch all products at $4.99-7.99. Once you have 10+ sales:
1. Raise price by $2
2. Watch conversion rate for 2 weeks
3. If conversion rate holds (within 20% of original), raise again
4. Repeat until conversion rate drops >30% from launch rate

This works because:
- Reviews increase buyer confidence = higher willingness to pay
- Star Seller badge = trust signal = supports higher prices
- Shop with 50+ sales converts at higher rates at higher prices

**Real escalation example:**
- Launch: $4.99 → 40 sales in first month (good demand signal)
- Month 2: $6.99 → 32 sales (20% drop, acceptable, net rev increased)
- Month 3: $9.99 → 22 sales (31% drop, hold here)
- **Settled at $9.99 → $10x higher revenue than launching at $9.99 from day 1 with no reviews**

## BUNDLE PRICING MATH

A bundle of 3 products that individually sell for $7, $7, $9 = $23 value.

**Bundle price target:** 60-70% of combined individual prices.
- $23 × 0.65 = $14.95 bundle price
- Still profitable (higher perceived discount), drives larger AOV
- Buyers love "save X%" framing even on digital products

**How to do it on Etsy:** Create a new listing that includes all 3 products. List separately AND as a bundle. Etsy allows the same files in multiple listings.

**Bundle listing title formula:**
```
[Niche] Planner Bundle | [Product 1] + [Product 2] + [Product 3] | Save 35% | Instant Download
```

## SEASONAL PRICING CALENDAR

Some products spike in seasonal demand. Raise prices during peak windows — buyers are less price-sensitive when time-limited.

| Season | Products | Price Bump | Timing |
|--------|----------|------------|--------|
| Jan 1-15 | Goal planners, habit trackers | +$3-5 | Jan 1-20 |
| Feb (Valentine's) | Couple journals, love letters | +$2 | Feb 1-14 |
| Ramadan (Mar 2026) | Ramadan trackers, prayer logs | +$2 | Feb 28 - Mar 28 |

**Return to base price** after peak period to maintain conversion rate.

## COMPETITIVE PRICE ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

Before setting price for any new product:

1. Search exact primary keyword on Etsy
2. Note the price of the top 10 results (by "Top customer reviews")
3. Note average price
4. **Your price = average - 15% for launch, then raise to average after 10 reviews**

Sample competitive analysis (run before listing):

## ETSY SALES + COUPON STRATEGY

**Run a 20% off sale every 90 days.** Reasons:
- Etsy sends email notifications to people who "favorited" your listings — free traffic
- Sale badge shows in search results — improves CTR by ~15%
- Psychological urgency even at 20% discount

**Abandoned cart coupon:** Etsy lets you auto-send a coupon (15-20% off) to buyers who add to cart but don't purchase within 24 hours. Enable this.

**Thank-you coupon:** After every purchase, send a 10% off coupon for their next order. Repeat buyers = highest lifetime value.
