# E05 KDP — Keyword Research (Publisher Rocket Methodology)

## How KDP Keywords Work

Amazon gives you 7 keyword fields (max 50 characters each) in the book setup.
These keywords determine search placement — NOT your title/subtitle keywords.
Think: title = branding, keyword fields = discoverability.

**Two-track keyword strategy:**
1. Title/subtitle: branding + one primary keyword (e.g., "Solopreneur Daily Planner")
2. 7 keyword fields: long-tail discovery phrases

---

## Publisher Rocket Workflow (Step-by-Step)

**Tool cost:** $97 one-time (Publisher Rocket) or use AMZScout free tier for initial research

**Step 1 — Competition Analysis**
- Search your main keyword in Publisher Rocket
- Filter: Books category only, Paperback
- Look for keywords where BSR #1 competitor < 200,000 (means real sales)
- Ideal: Top 3 results have BSR between 10,000-100,000 = hot niche
- Avoid: BSR > 500,000 across all top results (dead niche)

**Step 2 — Keyword Estimation**
- Publisher Rocket shows estimated monthly searches per keyword
- Target keywords: 1,000-10,000 monthly searches
- Avoid: <500 (too niche) and >50,000 (too competitive without AMS budget)

**Step 3 — AMS Keyword Mining**
- Create an AMS campaign with $5/day budget
- Use auto-targeting first 14 days
- Download search term report after 14 days
- Pull converting search terms → add to book keywords
- This is the best free keyword research method on Amazon

## Category Selection Strategy

**Two-category rule:** Each book gets 2 categories. Pick one broad (high volume) + one narrow (easier ranking).

### Category Paths (Full Amazon Path)

**Planners/Journals broad:** Books > Self-Help > Motivational
**Finance niche:** Books > Business & Money > Personal Finance > Budgeting
**Health niche:** Books > Health, Fitness & Dieting > Diets & Weight Loss
**Christian niche:** Books > Religion & Spirituality > Christianity > Christian Living
**Tech/AI niche:** Books > Computers & Technology > AI & Machine Learning

**Pro tip:** After uploading, email KDP support to get added to additional categories:
- "Blank Books & Journals > Planners & Organizers"
- "Calendars" subcategories
These are restricted — you must request them manually via support email.

## Kindle vs. Paperback Strategy

**General rule:**
- Planners/trackers/journals: Paperback ONLY (you can't fill in a Kindle)
- Prompt books/guides/workbooks: Both formats (Kindle at $4.99-7.99)
- AI Prompts book: Strong Kindle candidate (readers want to reference it digitally)

**Kindle pricing sweet spots:**
- $0.99: Kindle Unlimited pool (pays per page read, ~$0.0045/page)
- $2.99: 35% royalty tier minimum
- $4.99-9.99: 70% royalty zone (prime zone)
- $9.99: Psychological ceiling for most Kindle readers
- Never price above $9.99 for Kindle without major brand/following
