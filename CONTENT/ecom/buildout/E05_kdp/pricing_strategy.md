# E05 KDP — Pricing Strategy

## Royalty Calculator

Amazon pays royalties on paperbacks at: 60% × list price − printing cost

**Printing cost formula (black & white interior):**
- Fixed: $0.85 per book
- Per-page cost: $0.012/page (US standard, 6x9 inch)
- 120 pages: $0.85 + (120 × $0.012) = $0.85 + $1.44 = **$2.29**
- 200 pages: $0.85 + (200 × $0.012) = $0.85 + $2.40 = **$3.25**

**Color interior adds ~$0.07/page — avoid for all low-content books.**

## Pricing by Book Type

### Low-Content (Blank/Lined Journals, Trackers, Planners)

| Sub-type | Recommended Price | Rationale |
|----------|------------------|-----------|
| Basic lined notebook | $5.99-7.99 | Commodity, compete on niche |
| Niche tracker (fasting, habit) | $8.99-10.99 | Specialized enough to command premium |
| Business planner | $11.99-13.99 | Professional context = higher willingness to pay |
| Gift-positioned journal | $13.99-16.99 | Gift buyers don't price-hunt |
| Devotional/faith journal | $13.99-16.99 | Gift + personal use both |

### Medium-Content (Some Text + Worksheets)

| Sub-type | Recommended Price | Rationale |
|----------|------------------|-----------|
| Guided workbook | $14.99-18.99 | Value prop justifies price |
| Prompt collection | $14.99-19.99 | Knowledge product framing |
| Action plan journal | $14.99-17.99 | Outcome-oriented = premium |

### Kindle Pricing

| Paperback Price | Kindle Recommended | Royalty Rate |
|----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| $5.99-8.99 | Not recommended | N/A |
| $9.99-12.99 | $4.99 | 70% |
| $13.99-16.99 | $5.99-6.99 | 70% |
| $17.99+ | $7.99-9.99 | 70% |

**Kindle royalty calculation:**
- $4.99 × 70% = $3.49/sale (minus delivery cost ~$0.05) = **$3.44**
- $7.99 × 70% = $5.59/sale = **$5.54**
- No printing cost on Kindle = pure margin above $2.99

## Launch Pricing Playbook

### Phase 1: Launch Week (Days 1-7)
**Discounted price to get initial reviews and rank.**

| Normal Price | Launch Price | Discount | Goal |
|-------------|-------------|---------|------|
| $12.99 | $9.99 | 23% off | First 10 reviews + BSR momentum |
| $14.99 | $11.99 | 20% off | First 10 reviews + BSR momentum |
| $16.99 | $13.99 | 18% off | First 10 reviews + BSR momentum |

**Who to send launch copies to:**
- Personal network: Ask 5-10 people to actually buy (not gift — buy and review)
- Reddit communities: r/selfpublishing, r/kdp, niche-specific subs
- Facebook groups: KDP self-publishing groups
- Twitter/X: Your own following
- ARC (Advance Review Copy) via BookSirens or NetGalley Shelf ($150/mo — skip until 5+ books)

### Phase 2: AMS Launch Campaign (Days 1-14)
**Auto-targeting only. Let Amazon find your audience.**

Budget: $5/day per book
Bid: $0.50 CPC starting point (adjust after 7 days)
Duration: Run indefinitely, optimize weekly

After 14 days: Pull search term report → identify converting keywords → create manual campaign targeting those exact keywords at $0.30-0.40 CPC (lower bid, same terms, better ROI)

### Phase 3: Normal Pricing (Day 8+)
Restore to normal price. AMS keeps driving discovery. Organic rank builds.

## Competitive Price Analysis Framework

**Step 1:** Search your primary keyword on Amazon
**Step 2:** Sort by Bestsellers
**Step 3:** Record prices of top 10 results

| BSR Position | Price Range Observed | Your Move |
|-------------|---------------------|-----------|
| #1-3 (market leaders) | Note their price | Price at or $1-2 above if yours has better design |
| #4-10 (established) | Note their price | Match or go $1 below to undercut |
| #11-20 (climbing) | Note their price | Price at their level, compete on reviews/design |

**General rule:** Price within 20% of the #1 bestseller in your category.

## Expanded Distribution Pricing

KDP lets you opt into Expanded Distribution (libraries, bookstores, schools).
This requires setting a higher list price because Amazon takes a bigger cut.

**Minimum prices for Expanded Distribution:**
- 100 pages: $8.99 minimum
- 200 pages: $10.99 minimum
- 300 pages: $12.99 minimum

**Recommendation:** Enable for all books. The incremental royalty is small ($0.20-0.40/sale) but adds up. Also builds credibility (your book listed at Barnes & Noble website).

## Kindle Unlimited Strategy

**KDP Select:** Enroll Kindle edition in KU for 90 days.
- Makes book available to KU subscribers (50M+ users)
- Pays per page read (~$0.0045/page in 2025)
- A 120-page book fully read = ~$0.54 per borrow

**KU break-even vs. direct sale:**
- Direct Kindle at $4.99 = $3.44 royalty
- KU full read = $0.54 royalty
- KU needs 6.4× more borrows than sales to match revenue

**When to use KU:**
- First 90 days of a new book (gets discovery boost)
- Books where your audience is heavily KU subscribers (fiction, romance, self-help)
- NOT for business planners/trackers (those buyers want to own the Kindle too)

**After 90 days:** Evaluate. If KU borrows >> Kindle sales → renew KU. If Kindle sales dominant → exit KU and go wide (Apple Books, Kobo, Google Play).

## International Pricing

KDP auto-calculates international prices based on your USD price.
Adjust manually for:

| Market | Price Adjustment | Reason |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| UK (GBP) | 90% of USD price | Strong GBP, active KDP market |
| Germany/DE | 100% of USD price | Premium market |
| Japan | 110% of USD price | Books valued higher |
| India | 40% of USD price | Very price-sensitive market |
| Canada | 110% of USD price | Weaker CAD vs USD |
| Australia | 115% of USD price | Weaker AUD |

**International royalty note:** Royalty rate drops to 40% in some international markets (instead of 60%). Factor into pricing decisions.

## Revenue Model at Scale

**Portfolio of 10 books, 6 months post-launch:**

Conservative (each book at BSR 50,000-100,000 = 3-10 sales/month):
- 10 books × 6 sales avg × $4.50 royalty avg = **$270/month**

Moderate (each book at BSR 20,000-50,000 = 10-30 sales/month):
- 10 books × 18 sales avg × $4.50 royalty avg = **$810/month**

Aggressive (3 books crack sub-10,000 BSR = 100+ sales/month):
- 3 books × 100 sales × $5.50 royalty = $1,650
- 7 books × 15 sales × $4.50 royalty = $472
- **Total: $2,122/month**

**Path to $2K+/month:** 3 breakout books. Get one book into BSR <10,000 via focused AMS spend + strong keyword match + decent cover. Then use that revenue to fund AMS for the next 2 books. Compound.

**AMS RCOS (Return on Cost of Sale) target:** <40%
- If you spend $1 on AMS and make $2.50 in royalties → RCOS = 40% → profitable
- If RCOS > 70% → pause campaign, fix keywords or cover
