# E01 TikTok Shop — Supplier List and Vetting Guide

## Primary Suppliers (Tested and Integrated with TikTok Shop)

### 1. CJ Dropshipping
**URL:** app.cjdropshipping.com
**TikTok Shop integration:** Native plugin — orders sync automatically
**Shipping times to US:** 7-14 days standard, 5-8 days CJ Packet
**Minimums:** None — 1 unit order supported
**Payment:** PayPal, credit card, CJ Wallet

**Strengths:**
- Largest product catalog in dropshipping (~400K SKUs)
- Product sourcing service — send them any product, they find it and store it
- US/EU warehouse options for faster delivery (stock in advance)
- Free product photography for whitelabel
- Supports custom packaging with logo for 100+ unit orders

**Weaknesses:**
- Product quality varies by supplier
- Customer service can be slow during peak season
- Some products have inaccurate stock levels

**Pricing approach:** Use CJ Quote Request for any product not listed. They source it within 48-72 hours with price and MOQ.

**Best categories on CJ:** Home gadgets, LED products, kitchen tools, phone accessories, pet supplies

### 2. Zendrop
**URL:** zendrop.com
**TikTok Shop integration:** Via Shopify + TikTok Shop connection
**Shipping times to US:** 5-8 days (US warehouse), 10-14 days (China warehouse)
**Minimums:** None on standard products
**Payment:** Credit card, subscription plans

**Plans:**
| Plan | Cost | Features |
|------|------|---------|
| Free | $0 | 10,000+ products, basic features |
| Pro | $49/mo | Unlimited products, US warehouse access, auto-fulfillment |
| Plus | $79/mo | Custom branding, premium products, dedicated agent |

**Strengths:**
- US warehouse products ship in 5-8 days (huge for TikTok reviews)
- Better quality control than CJ on their curated catalog
- Auto-fulfillment works reliably
- US-based company — easier support

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller catalog than CJ
- Pro plan needed for best features
- Fewer niche/obscure products

**Best for:** Buyers who want reliable, quality-controlled supply for top 20% of products

### 3. AutoDS
**URL:** autods.com
**TikTok Shop integration:** Full automation — monitors price changes, auto-orders, tracks inventory
**Plans start:** $26.90/mo

**Key differentiator:** AutoDS isn't a supplier — it's an automation layer that connects to 25+ suppliers:
- AliExpress, Amazon, Walmart, eBay
- CJ Dropshipping, Zendrop
- Banggood, Alibaba

**Strengths:**
- Price monitoring — alerts you when supplier price changes (protects margins)
- Auto-ordering — when a TikTok Shop order comes in, AutoDS orders from supplier automatically
- Inventory sync — no overselling
- Best price finder across multiple suppliers

**Weaknesses:**
- Learning curve
- Subscription cost stacks with supplier costs
- Occasional sync delays

**Recommended setup:** AutoDS + CJ Dropshipping for main catalog, Zendrop US warehouse for hero products

### 4. Alibaba / 1688
**URL:** alibaba.com / 1688.com (Chinese marketplace)
**When to use:** After validating a product — buy in bulk for better unit economics
**MOQ:** Typically 50-500 units
**Lead time:** 20-45 days (sea freight) / 7-12 days (air)

**Margin improvement example:**
- CJ Dropshipping per unit: $8
- Alibaba bulk (200 units): $3.50/unit
- Savings: $4.50/unit × 200 orders = $900 margin improvement per batch

**How to find reliable suppliers:**
1. Look for Gold Supplier badge (paid verification)
2. Minimum 2+ year trading history
3. >90% positive response rate
4. Request sample before committing (pay for sample + shipping)
5. Use Trade Assurance for payment protection

**1688 (China-only marketplace):**
- Prices are 30-50% lower than Alibaba equivalent
- Requires agent to handle: Superbuy, Wegobuy, or CSSBuy
- Best for bulk orders after proof of concept

## Supplier Vetting Checklist

Before adding any supplier to your active roster:

**Quality Check:**
- [ ] Order sample — test packaging, product quality, accuracy to photos
- [ ] Check AliExpress reviews for same product (look for verified purchases)
- [ ] Look at 1-star reviews specifically — is the issue systemic?
- [ ] Test the unboxing experience (is it passable for video reviews?)

**Reliability Check:**
- [ ] Order during peak period (Nov-Jan) — note delivery times
- [ ] Check stock levels: is inventory consistently available?
- [ ] Verify they have 100+ units available before marketing the product
- [ ] Confirm they have backup stock or reorder cycle

**Communication Check:**
- [ ] Response time under 24 hours?
- [ ] English-speaking rep available?
- [ ] Can they handle urgent restock requests?
- [ ] Do they proactively communicate shipping delays?

**Integration Check:**
- [ ] Does the supplier integrate with your automation stack?
- [ ] Can they receive automated orders?
- [ ] Do they support custom packaging or inserts?
- [ ] Are tracking numbers provided within 24 hours of shipment?

## Red Flags — Suppliers to Avoid

- No sample available (they don't believe in their own product)
- Product images inconsistent with actual item
- Reviews mentioning product arrives damaged regularly
- Shipping times misrepresented by >5 days
- No tracking number within 48 hours of order
- Supplier changes pricing without notice
- Cannot provide product safety certificates for applicable categories (toys, electronics, supplements)

---

## Backup Supplier Strategy

For every hero product, have a backup supplier identified. When primary supplier:
- Goes out of stock
- Increases price >15%
- Shows >3 quality complaints in 30 days

Switch to backup immediately. Customers don't care about your supplier problems.

Maintain backup supplier list in a shared Google Sheet with these columns:
Product Name | Primary Supplier | Primary COGS | Backup Supplier | Backup COGS | Last Verified Date | Notes
