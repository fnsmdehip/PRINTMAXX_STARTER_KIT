# E01 TikTok Shop — Pricing Strategy

## The Core Math

TikTok Shop has more fee layers than most platforms. You need to account for all of them before you set a price.

### Fee Breakdown (per order)

| Fee | Amount | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| TikTok platform fee | 2-8% of sale price | Varies by category; most categories 5-6% |
| Affiliate commission | 10-25% of sale price | What you offer to creators |
| Payment processing | ~2.9% | Included in TikTok's fee structure |
| Return reserve | 3-7% of revenue | Budget for returns and refunds |
| Shipping cost | $3-6/unit | CJ standard to US |
| COGS | varies | Your product cost |

**Total non-COGS deductions:** 25-42% of sale price depending on affiliate rate and category

## Margin Targets by Volume Tier

| Monthly GMV | Minimum Net Margin | Target Net Margin |
|-------------|-------------------|------------------|
| $0-5K | 45% | 55%+ |
| $5K-20K | 40% | 50%+ |
| $20K-100K | 35% | 45%+ |
| $100K+ | 30% | 40%+ |

At scale, negotiate lower COGS with Alibaba bulk. Margins compress slightly as you run more affiliates but volume compensates.

---

## Pricing Formula

```
Minimum Sale Price = COGS / (1 - Total Fee %)

Where Total Fee % = Platform Fee + Affiliate Commission + Return Reserve + Shipping as % of sale

Example:
COGS: $7.50 (including shipping)
Platform fee: 5%
Affiliate commission: 15%
Return reserve: 5%
Total fee %: 25%

Minimum price = $7.50 / (1 - 0.25) = $10.00 (break-even)
Target price at 45% net margin = $7.50 / (1 - 0.25 - 0.45) = $25.00

Sell at $25 → Net profit per unit: $11.25 → Net margin: 45%
```

## Competitive Pricing Research

### Step 1 — Find Your Competitors on TikTok Shop
1. Search your product keyword in TikTok Shop
2. Sort by Sales (highest first)
3. Note the top 5 sellers' prices
4. Check Kalodata for estimated monthly units sold at those prices

### Step 2 — Price Positioning Matrix

| Position | When to Use | Price vs Competitor |
|----------|------------|-------------------|
| Penetration | Launch phase, building reviews | 10-15% lower |
| Parity | Established category, strong reviews | Same range |
| Premium | Unique angle, better branding, extras | 10-20% higher |
| Value bundle | Add accessories, justify higher price | Bundle 20-40% above hero product |

### Step 3 — Price Testing Cadence
- Week 1-2: Test at penetration price to get first 50 reviews
- Week 3-4: Move to parity once you have social proof
- Month 2+: Test premium positioning with upgraded packaging/bundle

## Affiliate Commission Strategy

TikTok Shop's affiliate marketplace is where volume comes from. You set the commission rate — creators see it when browsing products to promote.

### Commission Rate by Product Price

| Product Price | Minimum to Attract Creators | Sweet Spot | Top Creator Rate |
|--------------|---------------------------|------------|-----------------|
| $10-20 | 10% ($1-2/sale) | 15% | 20% |
| $20-35 | 12% ($2.40-4.20/sale) | 18% | 25% |
| $35-50 | 10% ($3.50-5/sale) | 15% | 20% |
| $50-80 | 8% ($4-6.40/sale) | 12% | 18% |

**Reality check:** Creators making real money on TikTok Shop want $3-8 per sale minimum. Price your product and commission to hit that number or you won't attract quality affiliates.

### Sample Creator Payout at Different Commission Rates

Product: $35 | Commission: 15%
Creator gets $5.25 per sale
Creator with 50 sales/day = $262.50/day ($7,875/mo)
This is a meaningful income for mid-tier creators → attracts serious affiliates

## Bundling Strategy

Bundles increase AOV without requiring a new product. TikTok Shop allows product bundles.

### Bundle Examples

**Kitchen gadget store:**
- Hero product: Vegetable chopper — $24.99 solo
- Bundle: Chopper + silicone mat + storage container — $39.99
- Bundle margin: Better per unit because COGS only goes up $4-6

**Skincare device store:**
- Hero product: LED facial wand — $39.99 solo
- Bundle: Wand + hydrating serum + storage case — $59.99
- Bundle AOV up $20, COGS up $8-10

**Bundle rules:**
1. Hero product must stand alone
2. Add-ons should solve the next logical problem
3. Bundle should save buyer 15-25% vs buying items separately
4. Bundle listing gets its own SEO and separate review count

## Launch Pricing Playbook

### Week 1: Price Penetration + Coupon Stack
- Set product at penetration price (10-15% below competitors)
- Create seller coupon: $3-5 off first order
- Request TikTok Shop free sample from platform (available to new sellers)
- Goal: First 20-30 reviews, 4.5+ star average

### Week 2-3: Normalize Price
- Remove coupon
- Move to market price
- If reviews are positive, test 5% price increase
- Watch conversion rate — if it drops >20%, hold at market price

### Month 2: Bundle Launch
- Launch bundle at 20-30% over hero product price
- Create separate affiliate campaign for bundle
- Bundle gets own video content showcasing the complete solution

### Month 3+: Price Premium Test
- If you have 200+ reviews at 4.5+ stars
- Test 10-15% price increase
- Monitor weekly: if units sold stays within 15%, keep new price
- Calculate impact: fewer units but higher margin per unit may win

## Price Monitoring

Check weekly:
1. Top 3 competitors' prices for each hero product
2. Your conversion rate — if dropping without traffic decline, price too high
3. Affiliate dashboard — if creators stopping promotion, check if competitors offer better commission
4. COGS changes from suppliers — adjust price immediately if COGS goes up >15%
