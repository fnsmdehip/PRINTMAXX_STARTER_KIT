# E04 Dropshipping Automation — Supplier Vetting Guide

## Why Supplier Vetting Matters

Bad supplier = chargebacks, negative reviews, account suspension. One viral "scam" TikTok about your store erases months of brand building. Vet hard upfront, automate monitoring ongoing.

Common failure modes:
- Supplier ships wrong item → chargeback + 1-star review
- Supplier runs out of stock mid-ad campaign → $500 in ads, 0 in sales
- Supplier ships directly to customer with AliExpress packaging → brand destruction
- Supplier inflates shipping times → customer support overload
- Supplier sends poor quality product → return wave + Stripe account at risk

## Supplier Vetting Checklist (Run Before Listing)

### Quality Check (Pre-launch)
- [ ] Order 1 sample unit at retail price
- [ ] Inspect packaging on arrival (branded or generic?)
- [ ] Test the product (does it work as advertised?)
- [ ] Photograph the product (use for real listing photos if better than supplier stock)
- [ ] Note actual dimensions vs listed (common discrepancy)
- [ ] Check for AliExpress branding on packaging or inserts (deal-killer for premium stores)

### Supplier Score Check (On Platform)
- [ ] Rating: 4.5+ out of 5
- [ ] Review count: 100+ reviews minimum
- [ ] Dispute rate: Under 2% (visible on AliExpress)
- [ ] Processing time: 3 days or under
- [ ] Response time: Under 24 hours
- [ ] Country of warehouse: Check US warehouse availability

### Logistics Check
- [ ] Shipping method: ePacket (cheapest tracked) or AliExpress Standard Shipping
- [ ] Tracking available: Yes (non-trackable = chargeback magnet)
- [ ] Estimated delivery: Confirmed via recent buyer reviews (not just stated time)
- [ ] US warehouse option: Always preferred

### Financial Check
- [ ] Margin at retail price: 30%+ minimum (40%+ target)
- [ ] Buffer for returns: Add 5% to COGS estimate
- [ ] Price stability: Run 3-month Keepa chart or CJ price history (does price spike at peak season?)

## Ongoing Supplier Monitoring SOP

**Daily (automated via AutoDS or CJ Shopify app):**
- Stock level sync
- Price sync
- Auto-unpublish when OOS

**Weekly (manual 15 min):**
- Check CJ or Zendrop supplier rating change
- Review any new 1-star reviews on supplier listing
- Check shipping time forum posts or buyer reviews for delays

**Monthly:**
- Re-run quality check on top 5 products (order fresh sample if returns spiking)
- Renegotiate pricing if ordering 100+ units/mo (CJ accepts bulk discount requests)
- Add 1 backup supplier per hero product (never single-source your top seller)

---

## Red Flags: Cut the Supplier Immediately If

- Return rate on their products spikes over 8%
- Chargeback rate triggers Stripe warning (over 0.9%)
- Supplier ships wrong item 3+ times in a month
- Supplier goes unresponsive for 48+ hours on a dispute
- AliExpress listing disappears (product was knockoff/IP violation)
- Delivery time extends past stated maximum 3 months in a row
- Multiple 1-star reviews citing "doesn't match description"

---

## Backup Supplier Protocol

For every product doing over $1,000/mo revenue:
1. Identify backup supplier (same product, different source)
2. Order sample from backup
3. Store backup product URL in store notes
4. Maintain 2-week buffer inventory if budget allows (send to CJ/Zendrop 3PL)

Rule: Never be 1 supplier away from losing your top product.
