# E10 PEMF Market Research — Full Analysis

## WHAT IS PEMF?
Pulsed Electromagnetic Field therapy — devices that emit electromagnetic pulses to stimulate cellular repair, improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and improve sleep.

**The claim stack buyers respond to:**
- Better sleep (8 hours → feel like 10 hours)
- Faster muscle recovery (athletes + gym crowd)
- Reduced chronic pain (arthritis, back pain)
- Mental clarity and stress reduction
- Improved bone density (FDA-cleared for this specific use)

**FDA status:** Class II medical device. FDA-cleared devices exist (like Orthofix for bone healing). Consumer wellness devices exist in gray zone — can't make disease claims but wellness claims are fine.

## COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST (CRITICAL)

Before any content or product:
- [ ] All health claims: wellness positioning ONLY (not disease treatment)
- [ ] FTC disclosure on all affiliate content: "#ad #sponsored" or explicit written disclosure
- [ ] No claim it "cures" or "treats" any condition
- [ ] No testimonials making medical claims
- [ ] If selling product: device liability insurance active
- [ ] If selling product: include FDA general wellness disclaimer on all packaging + site
- [ ] If white-labeling: supplier provides safety test certificate (CE mark or equivalent)

**Safe claim examples:**
- "Many users report better sleep after 30 days" (outcome-based, not medical)
- "Used by athletes for recovery" (lifestyle claim)
- "Supports general wellness and relaxation" (generic wellness)

**Unsafe claims to never use:**
- "Treats chronic pain" (disease claim)
- "FDA approved for sleep disorders" (false — FDA clearance ≠ approved)
- "Reverses aging" (unsubstantiated)
- "Eliminates cancer cells" (immediately illegal)
