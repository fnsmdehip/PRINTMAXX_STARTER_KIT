# Influencer Network Playbook — S15

## The Model

Build a network of 50-200 micro-influencers (5K-100K followers) who promote your products/services
on a pay-per-performance or rev-share basis. No upfront ad spend. Pure arbitrage.

**Revenue model options:**
1. Affiliate rev-share: influencer earns 20-40% of sales they drive
2. Flat fee per post: $50-500 depending on reach (after proving they convert)
3. Your own product sold through their audience: keep 60-80% margin
4. Managing this network as a service for clients: charge $1.5K-3K/mo

## Part 1: Building the Network Yourself

### Finding Micro-Influencers

**Niche targets (high engagement, low cost, our audiences):**
- Solopreneur/indie hacker: 10K-100K followers on X, LinkedIn, YouTube
- Faith/Christian lifestyle: 5K-50K followers on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Personal finance/frugal: 10K-100K on YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest
- Productivity/self-improvement: all platforms
- AI/tech tools: X/Twitter primarily, YouTube secondary

**Research method — Instagram:**
1. Search niche hashtag (#solopreneurlife, #sidehuslte, #faithbasedentrepreneur)
2. Filter to posts with 500-5,000 likes (signals real engagement at micro scale)
3. Click profile — check follower count (5K-100K sweet spot) and post frequency (2+/week)
4. Check last 5 posts engagement rate: total likes+comments / followers × 100
   - Good: 3-8% engagement rate
   - Great: 8%+ engagement rate
   - Skip: Under 2% (dead audience or bought followers)

**Research method — TikTok:**
1. Search niche keywords in TikTok search
2. Filter: 10K-500K followers, posted in last 30 days
3. Check for: consistent view counts (not one viral + 500 views afterward)
4. Target creators with 20K-100K who get 50K-300K views per video (view/follower ratio > 1)

**Research method — YouTube:**
1. TubeBuddy or VidIQ for channel research
2. Target: 5K-50K subs, 10%+ video view rate (views ÷ subscribers)
3. Check: monetization enabled? (signals commitment to channel)
4. Engagement: comments should be specific, not just emojis

**Research method — Twitter/X:**
1. Use Followerwonk or SparkToro for audience research
2. Search bio keywords: "solopreneur", "side hustle", "making money online", "faith"
3. Target: 10K-100K followers, tweets 3-7x/week, replies get 5-50 replies each

### Qualification Criteria Before Signing

Before onboarding an influencer, verify:
- [ ] Engagement rate 3%+ (Instagram/TikTok) or 10%+ view rate (YouTube)
- [ ] Audience matches your buyer ICP (check follower demographics if possible)
- [ ] No brand safety issues (no controversial political content if you need mass appeal)
- [ ] Previous paid partnerships disclosed properly (FTC compliance habit)
- [ ] Responsive (took under 48 hours to reply to your DM)
- [ ] Has a real email (not just DM — you need a paper trail for agreements)

## Part 2: Running the Network

### Onboarding New Influencer (30 minutes)

1. Send partnership agreement (basic 1-pager via DocuSign/HelloSign)
   - Includes: commission rate, payment terms, FTC disclosure requirement, 90-day exclusivity clause for competing products
2. Send product access or affiliate link (set up via Gumroad/LemonSqueezy/Rewardful)
3. Send brand brief (1 page: what product does, who it's for, what NOT to say, 3 sample posts for reference/inspiration — NOT scripts)
4. Agree on posting window (next 2 weeks, any day they choose)
5. Add to influencer tracking spreadsheet

### Communication Cadence

**Week 1 after signing:**
- Check in: "did you get the link/product okay? any questions before you post?"

**After first post:**
- Send performance update within 48 hours: "your post drove X clicks and Y sales so far. really appreciate it."
- Compliment something specific about how they presented it

**Monthly:**
- Payout + performance report email
- Ask: "anything you'd change about the product messaging?" (VoC goldmine)
- Soft pitch: "we're running a bonus next month — [X]% extra commission on the 15th and 16th. want to plan a post around that?"

**Quarterly:**
- Offer rate increase for top performers (25% → 30% → 35%)
- Propose deeper collab: YouTube integration, email blast, story series

### Incentive Structure

**Base commission:** 25-30%
**Volume bonus:** 35% if >$1,000 driven in a month
**Loyalty bonus:** $100 bonus at 6-month mark if consistently active
**Exclusive partner:** 40% commission + co-created product (for top 5 performers)

## FTC Compliance (Non-Negotiable)

Every post must include disclosure. Required disclosures:
- Instagram/TikTok: "#ad" or "#sponsored" in caption (NOT buried in hashtags, must be prominent)
- YouTube: verbal disclosure AND description disclosure ("This video contains paid partnerships")
- X/Twitter: "#ad" in tweet

Include in partnership agreement:
"Partner agrees to disclose all paid partnerships in accordance with FTC guidelines and applicable platform policies. Failure to disclose is grounds for immediate termination of agreement and forfeiture of earned commissions."

**Template disclosure for partner use:**
- Instagram: "this is a paid partnership with [brand]. all opinions are my own."
- YouTube: "this video is sponsored by [brand]. i tried it for [X days] and here's my honest take..."
- TikTok: "#ad i've been using [product] for [X] weeks — here's what happened"

## Revenue Math

**Scenario: Managing 75 active influencers for a client product that converts at 3%**

- 75 influencers × 1 post/month average = 75 posts
- 75 posts × 5,000 avg reach = 375,000 total reach
- 375,000 × 2% click rate = 7,500 clicks
- 7,500 × 3% conversion = 225 sales
- 225 × $47 product price = $10,575 gross revenue
- 225 × $14 (30% commission) = $3,150 influencer payouts
- Net to client: $7,425
- Your fee: $1,997/mo
- Client ROI: 3.7x on management fee

**Your economics:**
- 5 clients × $1,997 = $9,985/month
- VA cost to run outreach + tracking: ~$600/mo
- Tools (Rewardful, Notion, email): ~$100/mo
- Net: ~$9,285/month
