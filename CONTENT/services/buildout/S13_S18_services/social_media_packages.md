# Social Media Management Packages — S13

## The Offer

Most agencies charge $2K-5K/month for basic posting. You charge $497-1,497/mo and deliver MORE
because your stack is automated. Margin: 70-80% once systems are built.

---

## Package Tiers

### Starter — $497/month

**Who it's for:** Local businesses and solopreneurs who need a presence but have no time.

**Deliverables:**
- 3 platforms (pick from: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, TikTok)
- 15 posts/month (5 per platform)
- Canva-designed graphics (templates, not custom design work)
- Captions written in client voice
- Hashtag research (30 per niche, refreshed quarterly)
- Monthly performance report (reach, engagement, follower growth)
- 1 strategy call/month (30 min via Loom async, not live)

**What you don't do:**
- Community management / replying to comments
- Paid ads management
- Stories/Reels production (static posts only)
- Custom video content

**Your time investment:** 8-10 hours/month per client after templates built
**Tool cost:** ~$50/month (Buffer Pro $15 + Canva Pro $13 + ChatGPT Plus $20)
**Gross margin:** ~$440/month ($497 - $57 tools)
**Target client count:** 8-12 clients = $4K-6K/month

---

### Pro — $997/month

**Who it's for:** Growing brands with $500K+ revenue needing serious online presence.

**Deliverables:**
- 4 platforms (same options as Starter)
- 30 posts/month (mix of static + Reels/Shorts)
- 4 custom Reels/Shorts per month (scripted, edited, branded)
- Captions + hashtags + SEO optimization
- Basic community management (replies to first 10 comments per post)
- Competitor analysis (quarterly report)
- Monthly strategy call (45 min live via Zoom)
- A/B testing: 2 headline variants tested monthly
- Story highlights setup and maintenance
- Email for content approvals with 48-hour SLA

**What you don't do:**
- Paid ads management
- Influencer outreach
- Crisis management

**Your time investment:** 18-22 hours/month per client
**Tool cost:** ~$80/month (Buffer Pro + Canva Pro + Claude API + CapCut)
**Gross margin:** ~$900/month per client
**Target client count:** 5-7 clients = $5K-7K/month
**Sweet spot:** This tier. Highest revenue/effort ratio.

---

### Agency — $1,497/month

**Who it's for:** Mid-market brands, e-commerce stores doing $2M+/year, funded startups.

**Deliverables:**
- 5 platforms (all major + 1 niche platform like Pinterest or YouTube)
- 50 posts/month
- 8 short-form videos/month (Reels, Shorts, TikToks)
- Full community management (all comments + DMs, 9AM-6PM weekdays)
- Monthly content calendar with approval workflow (Notion-based)
- Paid ads consultation (not management, but strategy input)
- Influencer collab identification (list of 10 potential partners/month)
- Crisis communication plan template
- Quarterly brand audit (full 20-page report)
- 2 live strategy calls/month (60 min each)
- Dedicated Slack channel for real-time comms

**Your time investment:** 35-45 hours/month per client
**Tool cost:** ~$120/month
**Gross margin:** ~$1,350/month per client
**Target client count:** 3-4 clients = $4.5K-6K/month
**Warning:** This tier requires part-time VA at 2+ clients. Hire VA at ~$300/mo per client.

## Pricing Anchoring Strategy

Always present all 3 tiers together. Pro converts at 55-65% when shown next to Agency.

**Sales page order:** Agency → Pro → Starter (top to bottom, high to low)

**Discount structure:**
- 3-month prepay: 5% off
- 6-month prepay: 10% off
- Annual: 15% off (powerful for cash flow)
- Referral: $100 credit per referral that converts

## Client Onboarding SOP

**Day 1 (contract signed):**
1. Send welcome email with Notion onboarding doc link
2. Client fills Brand Questionnaire (voice, competitors, audience, goals, no-go topics)
3. Request: logins or admin access to all platforms
4. Request: brand assets (logo, fonts, color codes, photo library)
5. Book month 1 strategy call

**Day 2-5:**
6. Audit existing content performance (last 90 days)
7. Draft month 1 content calendar (15/30/50 posts depending on tier)
8. Submit to client for approval in Notion

**Day 7:**
9. Start posting (Buffer scheduled queue loaded for weeks 2-4)
10. Set up Buffer analytics reporting

**Day 30:**
11. Performance report sent (Loom walkthrough for Starter, live call for Pro/Agency)
12. Month 2 content calendar draft sent for approval

## Sales Process

**Lead source 1:** Cold email to local businesses (restaurants, gyms, med spas, law firms, real estate)
**Lead source 2:** LinkedIn DMs to founders in funded startup list
**Lead source 3:** Referrals (offer 1 free month after 3 months)

**Cold email subject line options:**
- "your last 3 Instagram posts got [X] likes — here's why that's fixable"
- "I noticed [Company] hasn't posted on LinkedIn in 3 weeks"
- "quick question about [Company]'s social strategy"

**Discovery call structure (20 min):**
1. Current state: where are you posting, what results are you getting
2. Future state: what does winning look like for you
3. Gap: here's what we'd do differently
4. Present tiers (share screen with pricing page)
5. Close: "which of these feels right for where you are now"

**Objection: 'We had an agency before and it didn't work'**
Response: "what specifically didn't work — the content or the results?" (if content: show samples; if results: show case study or offer 30-day pilot at 50% off first month)

**Objection: 'We could just do it ourselves'**
Response: "absolutely — most of our clients tried that first. the issue isn't capability, it's consistency. what does your posting schedule look like right now?"

## Content Production Workflow

**Monday batch day (4 hours covers 5 clients):**
1. Pull content briefs from all client Notion portals
2. Write captions in bulk with Claude (prompt: "write 10 Instagram captions for [niche] targeting [audience], voice: [samples provided], no em dashes, no buzzwords")
3. Design graphics in Canva (use templates — build master templates per client on day 1)
4. Export + load into Buffer
5. Flag any posts needing client approval before going live

**Wednesday:**
- Reply to comments and DMs (Agency tier clients)
- Check analytics, note any posts performing above baseline

**Friday:**
- Performance snapshot sent to Pro/Agency clients
- Load next week's posts if any gaps
