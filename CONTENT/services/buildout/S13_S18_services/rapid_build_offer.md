## Intake Process

### Step 1: Async discovery (20 minutes total, no call needed for Starter tiers)

Send client a Typeform or Notion intake form. Required before work begins.

**Intake form fields:**
1. What are you building? (describe in plain language)
2. What existing assets do you have (logo, copy, brand guide, content)?
3. Hard launch date or flexibility?
4. Top 2-3 reference sites that you want to emulate (visually or functionally)
5. Who is the user and what problem does this solve for them?
6. One sentence: what is the MOST important thing this [site/app/product] needs to accomplish?
7. Any technical requirements or constraints I should know?
8. Upload brand assets (logos, photos, fonts) here: [file upload field]

### Step 2: Scope confirmation (15 minutes)

Before accepting payment:
- Send back 1-paragraph confirmation: "I'm building [X] with [features]. Delivery by [date]. Here's what's NOT included: [scope limits]. Does this match your expectations?"
- Get written "yes" before invoicing

### Step 3: Payment

- Stripe invoice: 100% upfront for <$1,500 projects, 50% upfront / 50% on delivery for $1,500+
- No work starts until payment clears
- Payment link auto-sends access to project Notion portal

### Step 4: Build (72 hours max)

- Day 0 (intake cleared): Build scaffolding, set up repo/design file, deploy skeleton
- Day 1: Core functionality / structure / layout
- Day 2: Polish, content integration, testing, mobile check
- Day 3: Final QA, performance check, deliver + walkthrough Loom
