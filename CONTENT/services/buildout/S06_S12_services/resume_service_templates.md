## Cover Letter Templates

### Cover Letter 1: General (Works for all roles)

[Date]

Dear [Hiring Manager Name or "Hiring Team"],

[Company] is building [what they're building — 1 sentence from their website]. that's exactly the space I've been working in for the last [X] years.

at [previous company], I [one specific achievement with a number]. same discipline, same type of problem you're solving here.

I'm not going to restate my resume. the reason I'm reaching out specifically to [Company]:

[1-2 sentences about why this company specifically. Not generic. Reference something specific — a product launch, a blog post, a recent hire they announced.]

I'd like 20 minutes to learn more about the role and what success looks like in the first 90 days. I'll come prepared with 2-3 specific ideas based on what I know so far.

[Your name]
[Email] | [Phone]

---

### Cover Letter 2: Career Change

Dear [Hiring Manager],

I'm not the typical applicant for this role. I'm coming from [current field] — not [target field]. here's why that's not a problem.

in [current field], I spent [X] years doing [transferable skill]. the mechanics are different. the underlying problem is the same: [shared problem both fields solve].

what I bring that most [target field] candidates don't:
- [Advantage 1 from previous field — specific]
- [Advantage 2]
- [Advantage 3]

I'm applying because [specific reason about this company/role, not just the field change].

10 minutes? I'll show you exactly how my background maps to what you need.

[Your name]

---

### Cover Letter 3: High-Ticket Executive

Dear [First Name],

[Company]'s expansion into [market/product] is exactly the kind of problem I've solved before.

at [Company], I led [relevant initiative] — [result in numbers]. the situation wasn't identical, but the dynamics were: [1 sentence connecting the situations].

I won't take more of your time in a cover letter. the work speaks in the resume. what I'd want you to know before we talk:

[Company] is a company I've followed for [X] years. I turned down a [competing offer] to have this conversation first.

happy to share reference contacts from [relevant previous companies] before the first call if that's helpful.

[Your name]
