## FAQ

**Q: Is web scraping legal?**
A: Depends on the site and use. Scraping publicly available data is generally legal in most jurisdictions (see hiQ vs. LinkedIn). I don't scrape private data, bypass paywalls, or violate CFAA. I'll let you know if your target site raises concerns.

**Q: What if the site blocks scrapers?**
A: I handle most anti-bot measures with Playwright stealth mode + rate limiting. For heavy-block sites (Cloudflare enterprise, Akamai), I use ScraperAPI or Bright Data (extra cost for proxy service).

**Q: Can you run the scraper for me on a schedule?**
A: Yes. Premium includes cloud deployment with cron scheduling. Results delivered to Google Sheets, Airtable, or email.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: Simple HTML site: 1-2 days. Complex JS/SPA: 3-5 days. Multi-site projects: 10-14 days.
