## GIG DESCRIPTION

### Most SEO audits give you a list of 200 problems. Mine tells you the 10 that actually matter.

I run a full technical SEO audit on your site and deliver a prioritized action plan sorted by revenue impact. You get the 20% of fixes that produce 80% of the ranking improvement.

**What I audit:**

Technical health:
- Crawlability (Google can't rank what it can't crawl)
- Core Web Vitals: LCP, FID/INP, CLS — with specific fix instructions
- Mobile responsiveness issues
- HTTPS and mixed content issues
- XML sitemap and robots.txt problems
- Broken links and redirect chains (404s, 301 loops)
- Canonical tag issues (duplicate content)
- Schema markup (structured data)
- Page speed (what's actually slowing you down, not a generic "compress images")

On-page signals:
- Title tag optimization (what to change and what to change it to)
- Meta description coverage and click-through optimization
- Heading structure (H1-H6 hierarchy)
- Image alt text gaps
- Internal linking analysis (link equity flow)
- Content thin pages (Google penalizes these)

Off-page overview:
- Backlink profile health check (toxic links)
- Domain Authority trajectory
- Competitor gap analysis (keywords they rank for that you don't)
