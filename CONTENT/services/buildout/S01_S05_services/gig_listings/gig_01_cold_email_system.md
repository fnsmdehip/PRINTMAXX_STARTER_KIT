## GIG DESCRIPTION (1,200 words)

### Your cold emails are going to spam. Here's how to fix it.

Most people set up cold email wrong. They buy a domain, hook up Gmail, start sending, and wonder why nobody opens anything. The problem isn't your copy — it's your infrastructure.

**I build cold email systems from the ground up.**

What you get:

**Domain Setup (Deliverability-First)**
- 3 sending domains per 1,000 emails/day target (never burn your main domain)
- Proper DNS: SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured correctly (most people get this wrong)
- Mailbox warming via Smartlead or Instantly warmup pools
- 2-week warmup schedule before first send

**Inbox and Sending Setup**
- Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 mailboxes (I recommend which)
- Connected to Smartlead or Instantly (your choice)
- Sending limits configured by day/hour to protect deliverability
- Reply detection and auto-remove from sequences on positive signals

**Sequence Build**
- Up to 3 sequences (niche targeting variants)
- 4-step sequences: initial + 2 follow-ups + breakup email
- Spintax variables to avoid duplicate content flags
- Personalization tokens setup

**Lead List Prep**
- Validation workflow (NeverBounce or ZeroBounce API)
- Deduplication
- Bounce rate targeting under 2%

**Reporting Dashboard**
- Google Sheets tracking: opens, clicks, replies, meetings booked
- Weekly health checks: reputation scores, bounce rates, unsubscribe rates

**Deliverables:**
- Fully configured system
- 100 validated test leads to verify setup
- 30-min Loom walkthrough of everything
- 14-day support for questions
