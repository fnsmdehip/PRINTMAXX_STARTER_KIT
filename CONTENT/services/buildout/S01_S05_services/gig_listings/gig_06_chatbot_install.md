## GIG DESCRIPTION

### Your website answers the same 10 questions 1,000 times a day. Let AI answer them instead.

I install and configure AI chatbots trained on your content — product docs, FAQs, pricing pages, anything you have. The bot handles customer questions 24/7 without a human.

**What I use:**
- Chatbase (best for document-trained bots, no coding)
- Tidio (best for e-commerce + live chat hybrid)
- Voiceflow (best for multi-step conversational flows)
- Custom RAG setup (for advanced use cases with private data)

---

**What's included:**

Content ingestion (trained on):
- Your website URLs (I crawl them)
- PDF documents, SOPs, product guides
- FAQ documents
- Pricing pages

Configuration:
- Bot persona (name, tone, guardrails — it won't answer off-topic questions)
- Escalation flow (sends to human when it can't answer)
- Lead capture integration (bot collects email before answering certain questions)
- Connected to your email or CRM when lead is captured

Installation:
- JavaScript snippet added to your site (Webflow, WordPress, Squarespace, custom Next.js)
- Widget positioning and styling to match your brand
- Mobile responsiveness tested

Testing:
- 30+ test questions before delivery
- Edge case handling (abusive users, out-of-scope questions)

## FAQ

**Q: Will the bot make things up?**
A: I configure it to only answer from your provided content. If it doesn't know, it says so and escalates. Hallucinations are a configuration problem — I fix them before delivery.

**Q: Which platform should I choose?**
A: Chatbase for simple FAQ bots (cheapest). Tidio for e-commerce (live chat + bot hybrid). Custom RAG for large knowledge bases or private data. I'll recommend during intake.

**Q: What does it cost me monthly?**
A: Chatbase: $19-99/mo. Tidio: $19-59/mo. Voiceflow: $50-99/mo. Custom RAG: $20-50/mo (VPS + OpenAI API).

**Q: Can it handle orders or refunds?**
A: With Shopify integration — yes, basic order status lookup. Full transactional actions require custom development (Premium tier + add-on).
