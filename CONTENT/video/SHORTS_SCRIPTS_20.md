## Production Notes

**General pacing:** 2-3 words per second narration
**Visual style:** Fast cuts every 3-5 seconds, text overlays for key numbers
**Audio:** Clear voiceover, subtle background music (lo-fi for tech, phonk for hustle content)
**Captions:** Always on (80% watch without sound)
**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)

**A/B test variables:**
- Hook variations (test 2-3 opens per concept)
- CTA style (comment vs link in bio vs follow)
- Video length (30s vs 45s vs 60s)
- Text overlay density (minimal vs heavy)

**Distribution:**
- Upload to YouTube Shorts
- Cross-post to TikTok (adjust hashtags)
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels (adjust caption length)
- Repurpose audio for Twitter/X video posts

**Performance tracking:**
- Views, watch time %, engagement rate
- Comment volume (engagement bait indicator)
- Link clicks (if CTA included)
- Follower conversion rate
