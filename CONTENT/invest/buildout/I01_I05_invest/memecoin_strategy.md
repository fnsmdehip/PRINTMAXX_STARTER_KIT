## Entry Criteria (ALL must pass)

**Green flags:**
- Market cap under $500K at entry (micro-cap moonshots)
- Liquidity pool locked (verify on Unicrypt or Team.Finance)
- No mint function enabled (check on Etherscan/Solscan)
- Dev wallet under 5% of supply
- Telegram active, not bot-farmed (check join rate vs activity)
- At least 2 reputable CT accounts organically posting
- 24h volume > 10% of market cap (real trading activity)

**Auto-reject:**
- Honeypot detected (Honeypot.is shows fail)
- Dev holds >15% supply
- Copy of existing coin with no differentiation
- No locked liquidity
- Launched by known ruggers (check creator wallet history)

## Position Sizing — Kelly Criterion Applied

Standard Kelly for speculative assets: **fractional Kelly, 25% of full Kelly**

Full Kelly formula: `f = (bp - q) / b`
- b = odds (potential return multiple)
- p = probability of success
- q = 1 - p

**Practical memecoin position sizing:**

| Scenario | Max Position | Rationale |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Strong narrative + locked LP + CT push | 2-3% of crypto portfolio | Best-case setup |
| Moderate signals, 2/5 green flags | 0.5-1% | Speculative flier |
| FOMO entry, missed the play | 0% | Never chase |
| Already up 10x, still holding | Scale to 50% original | Let runners run |

**Portfolio allocation to memecoins total: max 10% of crypto, max 5% of total liquid net worth**

## Entry / Exit System

### Entry Ladder (never all-in at once)

1. **Tranche 1 (50%)** — enter on confirmation of 2+ green flags, low MC
2. **Tranche 2 (30%)** — add on first dip after initial pump if narrative holds
3. **Tranche 3 (20%)** — add if new CT accounts pick up organically (not yet at peak)

### Exit Rules (pre-define before entering)

**Mandatory sell triggers:**

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| 10x from entry | sell 50%, let rest ride free |
| 25x from entry | sell another 25% |
| 50x from entry | sell 20%, hold 5% lottery ticket |
| Dev wallet moves | sell 75% immediately |
| Telegram goes quiet | sell 50% |
| New narrative emerges making this one obsolete | exit 100% |
| Liquidity removed warning | exit 100% immediately |

**Time-based exits:**
- If no 3x in 7 days: reassess, likely exit
- If no 5x in 30 days: free capital for next play
