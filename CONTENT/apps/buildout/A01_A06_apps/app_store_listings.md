## Cross-App ASO Notes

**Shared tactics across all 6 apps:**

**Review velocity (first 30 days):**
- Target: 10 reviews in week 1, 50 in month 1
- Method: in-app prompt after 3rd session (not first) using SKStoreReviewRequest
- Timing: prompt after user completes a milestone (first streak, first sleep score, etc.)
- Never ask after negative event (missed prayer, broken streak)

**Localization priority:**
| App | Top Secondary Market | Localization ROI |
|-----|---------------------|-----------------|
| PrayerLock | Indonesia, Malaysia | High — 240M+ Muslims |
| FocusLock | India, Brazil | Medium |
| SleepMaxx | Japan, Germany | High — sleep obsessed |
| WalkToUnlock | UK, Australia | Medium |
| Streakr | Global English | Low — English fine |
| Vault | Global | Low — privacy universal |

**App preview video specs:**
- iOS: 886x1920 (6.5" for iPhone Pro Max), H.264, 15-30 seconds
- Android: 1080x1920, MP4, max 2 minutes
- Show core loop in first 5 seconds (no intro animation)
- No voiceover needed — captions in-app

**Screenshot spec:**
- iOS 6.5": 1284x2778 px
- iOS 5.5": 1242x2208 px
- Android: 1080x1920 px
- Tool: use ScreenshotCreator.com or Previewed.app
- Frame: iPhone 15 Pro on dark background, always

**Category strategy:**
- Primary category: most competitive for downloads but drives keyword association
- Secondary: use for less competitive category to rank higher on related searches
- Both categories count toward keyword associations Apple uses for ranking

**Pricing psychology:**
- $1.99/month: impulse tier, coffee comparison converts
- $9.99/year: shows commitment, 3x monthly = reasonable
- $19.99/year: premium positioning, worth it for daily-use apps
- Lifetime: offer only via PaywallKit "special event" triggers, not standard

**Launch sequence:**
1. Day 1: Submit to App Store (7-10 day review time)
2. Day 7-8: Submit to Google Play (1-3 day review)
3. Day 10: Both live simultaneously
4. Day 10: ProductHunt launch (both listed same day)
5. Day 10: Reddit posts to relevant subreddits
6. Day 10-14: Reach out to 20 relevant micro-influencers for reviews
7. Day 30: First ASO update based on keyword ranking data

**Tools for ASO tracking:**
- AppFollow: keyword ranking tracking ($29/mo)
- Sensor Tower: market intelligence (free tier)
- AppAnnie/data.ai: competitor analysis
- AppTweak: keyword difficulty scores ($69/mo)
- MobileAction: alternative to AppTweak, similar pricing
