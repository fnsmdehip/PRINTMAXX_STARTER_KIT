# Digital Product Affiliates — Gumroad, Whop, Courses, Communities

## Overview

Digital product affiliates are different from SaaS affiliates. The math:
- Lower price points ($15-97 vs $49-299/mo)
- One-time commissions (no recurring)
- Higher commission % (30-50% vs 20-30%)
- No cookie expiry issues (instant purchase decisions)

Best play: Build a library of review/comparison content, promote across Twitter, newsletters, YouTube, and let the traffic compound.

---

## Gumroad Affiliate Program

### How It Works
- Gumroad's built-in affiliate system is creator-managed — each creator sets their own commission rate
- Discovery comes from Gumroad marketplace, your own content, or direct DMs to creators
- You get a unique affiliate link per product
- Commissions paid via Gumroad's payout system (PayPal, bank transfer)

### Commission Rates
Creator-set. Most ranges:
- Digital downloads: 25-40%
- Templates/toolkits: 30-50%
- Courses: 30-40%
- Software licenses: 20-30%

| Product Type | Typical Price | Commission % | Per Sale | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Notion templates | $9-29 | 30% | $2.70-8.70 | High volume, easy content |
| Email swipe files | $17-47 | 40% | $6.80-18.80 | MMO audience converts |
| Code templates | $29-97 | 30% | $8.70-29.10 | Dev audience, niche content |
| Social media kits | $19-49 | 35% | $6.65-17.15 | Creator audience |

### How to Find Gumroad Affiliates
1. Go to gumroad.com/discover → filter by category → find bestsellers
2. Check if product has "Become an affiliate" button on product page
3. DM creator: "I run [site/newsletter/channel] in [niche]. Interested in promoting your [product]. What's your affiliate commission?"
4. Search Twitter for "[product name] gumroad" to find real buyers and affiliates

### Best Content Angles for Gumroad Products
- "The Notion templates actually worth buying in 2026" — round-up with affiliate links
- "I spent $200 on digital products so you don't have to" — honest reviews
- "Best AI prompt packs for [specific use case]" — niche-specific

## Whop Affiliate Program

### How It Works
- Whop has a built-in affiliate system AND a marketplace with thousands of products
- Two levels: promote individual products (creator sets rate) OR promote Whop platform itself ($10/referral)
- whop.com/affiliates — apply for platform affiliate
- Individual product affiliates: find products on whop.com, click "Become an affiliate" on product page

### Commission Structure
| Level | Commission | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Platform referral | $10 per seller signup | When someone creates a Whop store via your link |
| Individual product | 20-50% (creator-set) | One-time or recurring depending on product |
| Community subscription | 30% recurring typical | For paid Discord/Telegram communities |

### Top Whop Categories to Promote

| Category | Typical Price | Conversion Rate | Best Audience |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trading/investing signals | $29-297/mo | High (FOMO-driven) | Finance/crypto Twitter |
| Sports betting picks | $49-197/mo | High | Sports audience |
| Discord communities (MMO) | $29-99/mo | Medium | Solopreneur/creator audience |
| AI tools/courses | $47-197 | Medium | AI/tech audience |
| Fitness programs | $19-97/mo | Medium | Fitness audience |
| Reselling courses | $97-497 | Low-Medium | Ecom/side hustle audience |

### Content Strategy for Whop
- Build a "best Whop communities" round-up article (this gets organic search traffic)
- Review individual communities (join free trials, document the experience)
- Twitter thread: "I spent $500 on Whop communities. Here's what was actually worth it."

## Course Platform Affiliates

### Udemy
- **Commission:** 15% per sale
- **Cookie:** 7 days
- **Avg course price:** $13-20 (after discount) → $1.95-3/sale
- **Verdict:** Not worth building content around. Too low. Use only if you already mention courses.

### Coursera
- **Commission:** 15-45% per purchase
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Avg sale:** $49-399
- **Content:** "best coursera courses for [skill]," "coursera plus review"
- **Note:** Coursera Plus ($59/mo) is recurring — 15% = $8.85/mo per referral

### Skillshare
- **Commission:** $7 per trial sign-up
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Note:** Flat rate, not % based. 100 referrals/mo = $700. Easy to promote with "free 1-month trial" angle.

### Thinkific
- **Commission:** 30% recurring
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Avg plan:** $49-99/mo
- **Commission/mo:** $14.70-29.70/mo per referral
- **Content:** "best course platform for small creators," "thinkific review," "thinkific vs teachable"

### Teachable
- **Commission:** 30% recurring
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Avg plan:** $59/mo
- **Commission/mo:** $17.70/mo per referral
- **Content:** "how to create an online course," "teachable review," "teachable vs thinkific"

### Podia
- **Commission:** 30% recurring
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Avg plan:** $39/mo
- **Commission/mo:** $11.70/mo per referral

### MasterClass
- **Commission:** 25% per sale
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Avg sale:** $120/yr
- **Commission:** $30 per sale
- **Note:** Easier to promote to general audience — well-known brand

## Community & Membership Platform Affiliates

### Skool
- **Commission:** 40% recurring for 12 months
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Platform cost:** $99/mo
- **Commission:** $39.60/mo for 12 months = up to $475 per referral
- **Who to target:** Course creators, coaches, anyone building a community
- **Content:** "skool review," "skool vs discord," "skool vs mighty networks," "how to build a paid community"

### Circle.so
- **Commission:** 20% recurring
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Avg plan:** $89-399/mo
- **Commission:** $17.80-79.80/mo per referral
- **Content:** "best community platform," "circle review," "circle vs skool"

### Kajabi (Communities)
- Covered in SaaS list. 30% recurring. Strong for course + community bundling.

### Mighty Networks
- **Commission:** 30% recurring
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Avg plan:** $33-360/mo
- **Content:** "mighty networks review," "best community platform for course creators"

### Patreon
- **No affiliate program.** Cannot earn referral commissions. Build on Patreon but don't prioritize affiliate content about it.

## Operations Checklist

### Before promoting any digital product
- [ ] Purchase or get free review access to the product
- [ ] Actually use it for at least 2-3 days before writing review
- [ ] Check commission % is worth your traffic (min $5/sale or $8/mo recurring)
- [ ] Confirm affiliate tracking is working (click your own link, check dashboard)
- [ ] Add FTC disclosure to every piece of content

### Monthly audit
- [ ] Check all Gumroad products still for sale (creators delete products)
- [ ] Log commission by program in tracker
- [ ] Kill any program earning under $20/mo at month 3+
- [ ] DM 5 new creators about affiliate partnerships

### Priority affiliate stack to build first
1. ConvertKit (newsletter) — high volume content opportunity

3. Skool (community) — $39.60/mo/referral
