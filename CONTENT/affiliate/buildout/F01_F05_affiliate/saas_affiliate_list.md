## Tier 1: Best Programs (Build First)

### 1. ConvertKit (Kit)
- **URL:** kit.com/affiliate
- **Commission:** 30% recurring for 24 months
- **Cookie:** 60 days
- **Avg plan size:** $49-99/mo
- **Why it's top tier:** Email marketing is high intent. Creators convert well. $14.70-29.70/mo per referral for 24 months = up to $712 per customer over lifetime.
- **Content that converts:** "best email marketing tool for creators," "ConvertKit vs Mailchimp," "how to start a newsletter"
- **Gotcha:** Commission stops after 24 months. Upsell to Creator Pro ($99/mo) for higher monthly commish.

### 2. Beehiiv
- **URL:** beehiiv.com/partner
- **Commission:** 50% first month, 20% ongoing
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Why it's top tier:** Newsletter space is exploding. $42/mo avg plan. 20% recurring = $8.40/mo/referral indefinitely.
- **Content that converts:** "beehiiv review," "best newsletter platform," "beehiiv vs substack vs convertkit"
- **Note:** Apply via their partner page. Need active newsletter or review site.

### 3. HubSpot
- **URL:** hubspot.com/partners/affiliates
- **Commission:** 30% recurring for 1 year
- **Cookie:** 90 days (longest cookie in CRM space)
- **Avg plan:** $115/mo (Starter CRM)
- **Payout:** $34.50/mo per referral for 12 months = up to $414 per customer
- **Content that converts:** "best CRM for small business," "free CRM comparison," "hubspot review"
- **Note:** Managed via Impact platform. Requires approval. High-quality content required.

### 4. Semrush
- **URL:** semrush.com/kb/870-semrush-affiliate-program
- **Commission:** $200 per new subscription + $10 per free trial sign-up
- **Cookie:** 120 days
- **Why it's top tier:** $200 flat per referral. One sale/month covers a meaningful income stream.
- **Content that converts:** "best seo tools," "semrush review," "semrush vs ahrefs," "keyword research tools"
- **Note:** Through BeRush (their in-house program) or Impact.

### 5. ClickFunnels
- **URL:** clickfunnels.com/affiliate
- **Commission:** 40% recurring
- **Cookie:** 45 days (sticky — follows customer)
- **Avg plan:** $97-297/mo
- **Payout:** $38.80-118.80/mo per referral
- **Why it's top tier:** Highest recurring % in funnel builder space. The "dream car" bonus program pays extra at volume.
- **Content that converts:** "clickfunnels review," "best funnel builder," "clickfunnels vs kajabi"
- **Gotcha:** Products are polarizing. Some audiences distrust ClickFunnels brand. Review honestly.

## Tier 2: High Commission One-Timers

### 6. Shopify
- **Commission:** $150 per merchant referral (standard affiliate), up to $500 for Shopify Plus
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Content:** "how to start a shopify store," "best ecommerce platform," "shopify review 2026"
- **Volume needed:** 10 referrals/mo = $1,500/mo. Very achievable with SEO content.

### 7. SiteGround
- **Commission:** $50-100+ per hosting sale (tiered: 1-5 sales = $50, 6-10 = $75, 11-20 = $100)
- **Cookie:** 60 days
- **Content:** "best web hosting," "cheap wordpress hosting," "siteground review"
- **Gotcha:** Hosting market is saturated. Need specific long-tail angles.

### 8. ThriveCart
- **Commission:** 35% of $495-690 one-time license = $173-242 per sale
- **Cookie:** 60 days
- **Content:** "thrivecart review," "best checkout software," "thrivecart vs samcart"
- **Note:** One-time license model is rare. High AOV = high commission. Fewer sales needed.

## Tracking Setup

### Tools
- **Lasso** ($29/mo) — link management, auto-disclosure, broken link alerts, analytics per link
- **Google Analytics 4** — UTM-tagged affiliate links, conversion events
- **Spreadsheet tracker** — one row per affiliate program, monthly referrals, monthly commission, status

### UTM Structure
`?utm_source=site&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=[program]&utm_content=[article-slug]`

### Monthly Audit
- Check all affiliate links still active (programs close or change)
- Review top 5 content pieces for conversion rate
- Kill or update any program paying under $20/mo at 3+ months
- Add 2 new programs each quarter as site grows
