# White-Label SaaS Resell Guide — 5 Products

**The play:** pay wholesale for white-label SaaS access. resell under your own brand at 3-10x markup. no code, no support infrastructure, no server costs. pure arbitrage.

---

## How White-Label SaaS Works

You pay the white-label provider $X/mo for platform access. They give you:
- Your own domain (yourapp.com)
- Your logo, colors, name
- Your Stripe account for payments (you keep the revenue)
- Access to their support and infrastructure

You charge clients $Y/mo (Y >> X). the spread is your profit.

**Example math:**
- GoHighLevel reseller: pay $497/mo for unlimited sub-accounts
- Charge each agency client $299/mo for a "local marketing platform"
- 5 clients = $1,495/mo revenue - $497 cost = $998/mo net at 66% margin
- 20 clients = $5,980/mo revenue - $497 cost = $5,483/mo net at 91% margin

The model gets better at scale. costs are fixed, revenue is variable.

## White-Label Positioning Script

When talking to prospects, never say "GoHighLevel" or any provider name.

**What you say:**
"we built a proprietary marketing platform for local businesses. it handles your CRM, email follow-ups, SMS reminders, appointment scheduling, and reputation management — all in one place. most clients see a 30% reduction in missed appointments and 40% more reviews within 90 days."

**What they hear:** custom software built just for them.
**What it actually is:** GHL with your logo.
**Why it works:** they don't know GHL, they don't care. they care about results.

## Client Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Collect payment via Stripe
- [ ] Create sub-account in platform
- [ ] Install relevant snapshot for their niche
- [ ] Configure: phone number, domain, email sending domain
- [ ] Import their contacts (get CSV from client)
- [ ] Set up their first automation (missed call text-back is always first)
- [ ] Record 5-min Loom walkthrough of their dashboard
- [ ] Send credentials + Loom
- [ ] Schedule 30-min onboarding call for day 3
- [ ] Add to Slack/WhatsApp group for support questions
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for month 1 check-in

**Time to onboard one client:** 45-90 minutes for a competent operator
**Target after 10 clients:** < 30 minutes using pre-built templates
