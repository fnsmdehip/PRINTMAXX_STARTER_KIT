## Build + Sell Workflow

**Time to build each product:**
- Simple packs (under 30 templates): 3-5 hours in Canva
- Complex packs (50+ templates): 6-10 hours
- Total portfolio (all 15): ~80-90 hours to build from scratch

**Delivery method:**
- Canva: Share as template link (free tier works for most)
- Canva Pro templates: create from Pro account, buyer needs Pro to edit (mention in listing)
- Non-Canva buyers: export as editable .pptx or static JPG/PNG for reference

**Etsy SEO for top performers:**
- Primary keyword in title: exact match ("Instagram Carousel Templates Canva")
- 13 tags: use all, mix broad + specific
- 5-7 images: show templates in use, not blank
- Price $9-$19 sweet spot for Etsy discovery

**Revenue math:**
- 15 products × avg $15 × 25 sales/mo each = $5,625/mo gross
- Etsy fee (6.5% + $0.20): ~$411
- Gumroad fee (10%): ~$281 on Gumroad-only sales
- Net estimated: ~$4,700/mo at steady state (6+ months)

**Template multiplier play:**
From these 15 base products, can create 30+ niche variants at 1-2 hours each.
Every niche variant is another Etsy listing with its own search traffic.
Example: Social Media Pack → Finance Pack → Fitness Pack → Faith Pack = 4 listings from 1 design system.
