# Printable Product Ideas — 20 Concepts for Etsy + Gumroad

Target: Etsy (highest search demand for printables), Gumroad secondary.
Price range: $3-$15 per product. High volume, low effort per sale.
Build time: 2-4 hours per product in Canva or Google Slides.

---

## Etsy Launch Strategy

**Week 1-2:**
- List all 20 products with optimized titles, 13 tags each, 5-7 images showing templates in use
- Price competitively: $3-9 to start, raise after first 5 reviews
- Enable Etsy Ads: $1-3/day per listing for first 30 days

**Social proof:**
- First 5 buyers: email asking for review, offer a second printable free
- Screenshots of customer setups shared on Instagram/Pinterest
- Pinterest: create boards for each product category (evergreen traffic)

**Revenue projection:**
| Month | Products Listed | Avg Sales/Product | Avg Price | Monthly Gross |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | 10 | 5 | $6 | $300 |
| 3 | 20 | 12 | $6.50 | $1,560 |
| 6 | 30+ | 20 | $7 | $4,200 |
| 12 | 50+ | 25 | $7.50 | $9,375 |

**Key growth lever:** Template multiplier. Each base design → 4-6 variants (color palettes, styles, niche tweaks) = 4-6x the Etsy listings from the same work.

**Etsy fees:**
- Listing fee: $0.20/listing (one-time, renews on sale)
- Transaction fee: 6.5%
- Offsite ads fee: 15% if buyer comes through Etsy ads
- Net take-home after fees: ~77-83% of sale price
