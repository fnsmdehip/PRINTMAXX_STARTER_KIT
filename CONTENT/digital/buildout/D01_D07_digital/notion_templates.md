# Notion Template Concepts — 10 Products Ready to Build

All templates target solopreneurs, creators, and solo business owners. Each one is a standalone sellable product at $9-$29.

---

## 1. Solopreneur OS (flagship — already has landing page)
**Price:** $19 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Whop
**What it is:** Full business operating system. CRM, revenue tracker, content calendar, project tracker, daily dashboard, idea vault, SOP library, goals/OKRs.
**Template multiplier:** Add industry variants (freelancer OS, creator OS, consultant OS) — 3 products from one base.
**Build time estimate:** 8 hours for base, 2 hours per variant.

---

## 2. Content Creator OS
**Price:** $17 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** Content-specific version of the OS. Platforms: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, podcast, newsletter. Idea capture, batch production planner, analytics log, brand deal tracker, repurpose tracker.
**Best for:** Multi-platform creators who need to plan across channels.
**Differentiation:** Pre-filled with 50 content ideas across 5 niches.

---

## 3. Freelance Business Dashboard
**Price:** $15 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** Client CRM + project tracker + invoice log + time tracker + revenue targets. Simple, not bloated.
**Key features:** Client status pipeline (lead → proposal → active → invoiced → paid), project profitability tracker, monthly billable hours target vs actual.
**Template multiplier:** Designer variant, developer variant, copywriter variant.

---

## 4. Launch Tracker
**Price:** $12 | **Platform:** Gumroad
**What it is:** Single-purpose launch planning template. For digital products, apps, SaaS, newsletters. Pre-launch tasks, launch week calendar, metrics dashboard, post-launch retro.
**Timeline views:** 30-day countdown calendar + Kanban task board.
**Best for:** Anyone launching a product and needing to track 50+ moving pieces.

---

## 5. Personal Finance + Budget Tracker
**Price:** $15 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** Income tracking, expense categorization, savings goals, debt paydown tracker, net worth log. Monthly and annual views.
**Differentiation:** Designed for people with multiple income streams (salary + freelance + passive). Not a single-income-source template.
**Template multiplier:** Freelancer finance variant, creator finance variant.

---

## 6. Job Search & Networking CRM
**Price:** $12 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** Track job applications, company research, interview stages, networking contacts, follow-up reminders.
**Differentiation:** Includes a "company culture research" database with custom fields for Glassdoor notes, tech stack, remote policy.
**Audience:** Job seekers, career switchers, people using networking to land roles.

---

## 7. Book Notes + Learning Vault
**Price:** $9 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** Capture book notes, key ideas, quotes, action items. Link ideas across books. Tag by topic. Weekly review of top insights.
**Features:** Books database + idea database with bidirectional linking. Monthly reading log.
**Template multiplier:** Course notes variant, podcast notes variant.

---

## 8. Social Media Growth Tracker
**Price:** $12 | **Platform:** Gumroad
**What it is:** Track follower count, engagement rate, post performance, and growth milestones across 5 platforms. Weekly screenshot log.
**Key feature:** Platform comparison dashboard. See which platform is growing fastest.
**Best for:** Creators managing multiple platforms who need to know where to double down.

---

## 9. SaaS Startup OS
**Price:** $29 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Whop
**What it is:** For early-stage SaaS founders. Feature roadmap, user feedback database, competitor tracker, MRR log, marketing experiments tracker, hiring pipeline.
**Target buyer:** Solo founders pre-product or in beta.
**Differentiation:** Includes a "feature vs bug vs idea" triage system and user interview notes template.

---

## 10. Habit + Goals 90-Day Tracker
**Price:** $9 | **Platform:** Gumroad + Etsy
**What it is:** 90-day habit tracker with daily checkboxes, weekly review, and goal progress log. Calendar view + database view.
**Features:** 5-habit tracker, streak counter formula, weekly review prompts, monthly retro page.
**Best for:** People who bought an expensive planner and need a digital equivalent.

## Template Production Notes

**Build order by revenue potential:**
1. Solopreneur OS (already have landing page)
2. Content Creator OS (large audience)
3. Freelance Business Dashboard (large audience, Etsy friendly)
4. Personal Finance Tracker (massive Etsy demand)
5. Habit + Goals (impulse buy, $9 no-brainer)

**Distribution strategy:**
- Gumroad: main store for all 10
- Etsy digital: templates 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 10 (Etsy search traffic)
- Whop: templates 1, 9 (higher price point, community buyers)
- Ko-fi: lower-priced templates as introductory products

**SEO titles for Etsy:**
- "Notion Freelance Dashboard Template | Client CRM | Invoice Tracker | Business Planner"
- "Notion Budget Tracker | Personal Finance Template | Income Expense Log | Money Dashboard"
- "Notion Habit Tracker | 90 Day Goals Planner | Daily Routine Template | Productivity System"

**Pricing strategy:**
- Under $10: impulse buy, no research needed
- $10-20: considered buy, needs trust (reviews help)
- $20+: needs strong social proof and landing page

**Revenue math at steady state (6 months):**
- 10 templates × avg $13 × 20 sales/mo each = $2,600/mo gross
- After Gumroad/Etsy fees (~18%): $2,132/mo net
- Scaling lever: bundle all 10 for $49 ($49 × 15/mo = $735 bonus)
