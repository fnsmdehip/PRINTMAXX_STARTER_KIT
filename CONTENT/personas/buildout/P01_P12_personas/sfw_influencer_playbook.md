## Phase 1: Account Setup (Week 1)

### Name + Handle Selection
- Name: memorable, 1-2 words, says exactly what it is
- Handle: @[name] or @[name]daily or @[name]tips
- Consistent across all platforms you use
- No numbers in handle (looks new/fake)

### Profile Infrastructure
1. **Profile photo**: AI-generated face (Midjourney/DALL-E 3) OR illustrated avatar
   - Midjourney prompt structure: `[demographic] woman/man, [aesthetic], professional headshot, natural lighting, bokeh background, photorealistic --ar 1:1 --v 6`
   - Illustrated: Canva avatar builder, HeyGen avatar creation
2. **Bio**: hook + value prop + CTA (see bio_templates.md)
3. **Link in bio**: Beacons.ai or Linktree — link to affiliate products + newsletter
4. **Pinned content**: best-performing post on each platform

## Phase 2: Content Strategy

### The 80/20 Rule for Persona Accounts
- **80% value content**: tips, tools, insights, tutorials, inspiration
- **20% promotional**: product mentions, affiliate links, digital products

Breaking this ratio = follower trust erosion. Stay disciplined.

### Content Pillars (use 3-5 per persona)
Pick 3-5 from this list based on niche:
- **How-to**: step-by-step actionable posts
- **Myth-busting**: contrarian takes on common beliefs
- **Tools/resources**: curated finds with affiliate angles
- **Inspiration**: quotes, mindset, relatable struggle
- **Data/numbers**: specific stats, case studies, comparisons
- **Behind the scenes**: how the system/process works
- **Trending commentary**: your take on niche news

### Posting Frequency by Platform
| Platform | Frequency | Format Priority |
|----------|-----------|----------------|
| TikTok | 1-2x/day | Short video |
| Instagram | 1x/day + 3-5 Stories | Reels + Carousels |
| YouTube | 2-3x/week | Long-form + Shorts |
| X/Twitter | 3-5 tweets/day | Text + Threads |
| Pinterest | 5-10 pins/day | Static + Idea pins |
| LinkedIn | 1x/day weekdays | Text posts + Docs |

### Batch Production Schedule (Monthly)
- **Week 1**: Script 30 posts, write 4 threads, outline 4 newsletters
- **Week 2**: Record/produce 30 videos or create 30 carousels
- **Week 3**: Edit + schedule all content via Buffer
- **Week 4**: Engage + analyze + plan next month

## Phase 3: Growth Tactics

### Early Growth (0-1K followers)
1. **Reply guy method**: find top 20 accounts in niche, reply with genuine value to every post. Do this 30 min/day.
2. **Hashtag research**: use 5-10 mid-size hashtags (100K-2M posts), avoid mega tags
3. **Cross-platform traffic**: put Instagram link in TikTok bio and vice versa
4. **Collab content**: reach out to similar-size accounts for collab posts or duets
5. **Engagement pod**: find 5-10 similar accounts, commit to liking/commenting immediately on each other's posts

### Mid Growth (1K-10K followers)
1. **SEO-optimized content**: use keywords in captions, titles, Alt text (Instagram has search now)
2. **Trending audio/formats**: remix trending sounds in your niche's style
3. **Content series**: "30 days of X" drives follows + bookmarks
4. **Story engagement**: polls, Q&As, "swipe to see" drive algorithm signals
5. **Sub-niche targeting**: go deeper, not broader (more specific = more loyal audience)

### Scale Growth (10K+ followers)
1. **Newsletter cross-promotion**: mention newsletter in every post, build email list
2. **Podcast appearances**: reach out as AI expert / niche expert
3. **Brand collaborations**: start pitching brands for sponsored posts
4. **Product creation**: create and sell digital products to your audience
5. **Paid promotion**: $5-20/day Spark Ads on TikTok to amplify top content

## Disclosure and Compliance

**MANDATORY — FTC compliance:**
- Any affiliate link: "#ad" or "#affiliate" or "Affiliate link" in caption
- Any sponsored content: "#sponsored" or "Paid partnership" clearly visible
- AI-generated persona disclosure: Not legally required in US (as of 2026) but best practice for trust
- Income claims: Never claim specific earnings unless you can substantiate them
- Platform-specific rules: Read each platform's creator monetization policies

**Safe phrases:**
- "this is an affiliate link — I earn a small commission at no cost to you"
- "partnered with [brand] to share this"
- "I only recommend what I actually use or have researched"

## Persona Longevity

**Avoid:**
- Posting about topics outside your niche (kills algorithm signals)
- Buying followers (engagement ratio drops, brand deals fall apart)
- Going dark for 2+ weeks (algorithm punishes inactivity)
- Posting low-quality visuals (first impression = everything)

**Protect:**
- Store all AI-generated images locally (cloud backup)
- Back up audience lists via email (don't depend only on platform followers)
- Maintain posting schedule even during low-engagement periods
- Document all affiliate relationships and brand deal terms in a spreadsheet
