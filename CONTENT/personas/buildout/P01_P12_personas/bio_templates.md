# Bio Templates — 10 Persona Bios (All Platforms)

## Format System

Each bio includes:
- **Instagram bio** (150 chars): hook + value + CTA
- **TikTok bio** (80 chars): ultra-compressed
- **X/Twitter bio** (160 chars): personality + keywords
- **YouTube description** (first 100 chars shown): search-optimized
- **LinkedIn tagline** (220 chars): professional tone
- **Linktree/Beacons headline** (what they see when they click bio link)

## Bio Writing Rules (for future personas)

1. **Lead with what you do** — not who you are (nobody cares about your identity until they care about your content)
2. **Include a specific number** if possible ("3 revenue streams", "$4K/mo", "3 countries in 90 days")
3. **State who it's for** — "for solopreneurs", "for busy people", "for students"
4. **One CTA only** — bio link. Don't give multiple options.
5. **Avoid:** "I help people [vague thing]", "passionate about [generic topic]", inspirational quotes, emoji overload
6. **Platform-specific compression:** Instagram allows the most text. TikTok is brutal. Twitter allows personality.

## CTA Direction (Linktree/Beacons)

For each persona, the bio link should direct to:
1. **Freebie first** (lead magnet email capture — convert browser to subscriber)
2. **Best content** (curated highlights for new visitors)
3. **Primary affiliate** (top-converting product in their niche)
4. **Digital product** (if they have one)
5. **Newsletter** (for ongoing relationship building)

Never link directly to an affiliate product as the first link. Build trust → capture email → then sell.
