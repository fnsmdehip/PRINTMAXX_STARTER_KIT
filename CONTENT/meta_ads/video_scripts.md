## PRODUCTION NOTES
- All scripts designed for vertical 9:16 format
- Hook MUST land in first 2 seconds (before skip)
- Captions mandatory (85% of feed video watched on mute)
- End card with CTA + URL for 3 seconds minimum
- Voice-over pacing: 150-160 words per minute (conversational, not rushed)
- B-roll can be AI-generated via Runway or sourced from Pexels/Artgrid

## POST-PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

For every video before upload:
- [ ] Captions burned in (CapCut auto-caption or manual SRT)
- [ ] "Ad" label visible in first 3 seconds
- [ ] "Results vary" disclaimer appears on screen when claims are made
- [ ] "Physician-supervised" appears at least once on screen
- [ ] End card holds for minimum 3 seconds with URL
- [ ] Audio levels: VO at -6dB, music at -18dB, SFX at -12dB

## ELEVENLABS VOICE SETTINGS

**For authority/educational scripts:**
- Voice: "Adam" or "Antoni" (male), "Rachel" (female)
- Stability: 0.65
- Similarity boost: 0.80
- Style: 0.15 (slightly expressive, not flat)

**For UGC/testimonial scripts:**
- Voice: "Josh" or "Bella" (younger, conversational)
- Stability: 0.50 (more natural variation)
- Similarity boost: 0.75
- Style: 0.30 (more emotional range)

**For urgency/data scripts:**
- Voice: "Clyde" or "Elli" (crisp, direct)
- Stability: 0.75 (consistent, confident)
- Similarity boost: 0.85
- Style: 0.10 (minimal emotion, all business)
