## COMPLIANCE NOTES (all ads)
- No "guaranteed" hormone level increases
- No before/after physique transformations implying TRT alone caused them
- No claims of sexual performance improvement without "results vary"
- All treatments require bloodwork and physician evaluation
- Must state "Prescription required" or "Physician-supervised"
- No imagery suggesting illegal steroid use

## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE NOTES

**Budget Allocation:**
- PAS ads: 35% (cold traffic, men are harder to convert)
- Social Proof: 20%
- Price Comparison: 20%
- Educational: 15% (TRT needs more education than GLP-1)
- UGC: 10%

**Key Insight:** Men convert slower on health ads. Educational content builds trust over 3-5 touchpoints before booking. Run ED ads as first touch, retarget with PAS and SP.

**Custom Audiences:**
- Lookalike 1%: men who completed bloodwork order
- Retarget: visited symptoms page, didn't book
- Interest stack: fitness + over 35 + NOT interested in steroids/bodybuilding competitions

**Placement:**
- Feed: All variants
- Stories: PAS-01 and UGC-01 (personal, hook-driven)
- Reels: Avoid for TRT (medical content gets flagged faster in Reels)
