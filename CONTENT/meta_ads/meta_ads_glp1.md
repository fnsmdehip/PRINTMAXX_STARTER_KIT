## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE NOTES

**Budget Allocation:**
- PAS ads: 30% (cold traffic)
- Social Proof: 20% (warm traffic retarget)
- Price Comparison: 20% (comparison shoppers)
- Educational: 10% (top of funnel awareness)
- Urgency: 10% (bottom of funnel push)
- UGC: 10% (social proof retarget)

**A/B Testing Priority:**
1. Test PAS-01 vs PAS-03 vs PAS-05 first (highest volume hooks)
2. Winner gets 60% of PAS budget
3. Test PC-01 vs PC-03 for price-sensitive segment
4. Rotate UGC weekly to prevent fatigue

**Custom Audiences:**
- Lookalike 1%: based on landing page visitors who spent >45 sec
- Retarget: visited pricing page but didn't book
- Exclusion: existing patients (upload email list)

**Placement:**
- Feed: All PAS and SP variants
- Stories/Reels: UGC variants (crop to 9:16)
- Right column: PC variants (smaller format works for data-heavy)
