# POST 1, Catholic Facebook groups
(Target: "Catholic Faith Community", "Catholic Women of Grace", "Traditional Latin Mass Catholics", "Daily Catholic Devotionals", any Catholic parish group)

I built a free daily devotional tracker for Catholics and wanted to share it here in case it's helpful for anyone.

It's a free web app (no download, no account required, works offline) that tracks:

- Daily rosary with which mysteries you prayed
- Liturgy of the Hours (Morning Prayer, Daytime, Evening, Night Prayer)
- Saints feast days so you know whose feast it is each day
- Friday abstinence and all fasting days flagged automatically
- Daily Lectionary readings so you're in sync with the Mass readings
- Your current liturgical season displayed front and center

The streak mechanic is the point. every day you complete your devotional practice, the streak goes up by 1. miss a day and it resets. it sounds simple but the mild sting of breaking a streak is genuinely effective for building consistency. same principle that makes Duolingo work for language learning.

I built this because my own prayer life was inconsistent and I was embarrassed by it. tracking it changed that. I've had 19 consecutive days now since I started using it.

Free, no ads, no paywall ever.

catholic-streak.surge.sh
