## Usage Instructions

**How to use:**
1. Read the original post
2. Pick template category based on intent (agree, disagree, add value, be funny, ask question)
3. Fill in {{tokens}} with specific details
4. Read it out loud - does it sound like a real person?
5. If yes, post. If no, rewrite until it does.

**Algorithm hacking notes:**
- Questions drive the most OP replies (good for your visibility)
- Value-adds get retweeted/screenshot (expands reach)
- Funny replies get likes but lower conversion to profile clicks
- Disagreement gets engagement but can backfire if done wrong
- Agreement is safe but needs specifics to stand out

**Profile click triggers:**
- Specific numbers (shows you actually do the thing)
- Mentioning tools/tests/results (shows expertise)
- Counter-intuitive takes (makes people curious who you are)
- Humor that lands (personality = follow)

**What NOT to do:**
- "Great post!" (invisible)
- "Thanks for sharing!" (invisible)
- "This is so true!" (invisible)
- Essay-length replies (save it for your own thread)
- Overly promotional (instant unfollow)
- Arguing in bad faith (tanks your reputation)

**Success metrics:**
- Did the OP reply? (best signal)
- Did other people reply to your reply? (second best)
- Did you get profile clicks? (check analytics)
- Did you gain followers from this thread? (ultimate goal)

The goal is not to be helpful. The goal is to be interesting enough that people click your profile.
