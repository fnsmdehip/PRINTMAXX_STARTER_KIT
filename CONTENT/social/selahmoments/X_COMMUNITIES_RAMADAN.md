8. that 2am tahajjud prayer hits different when you're already awake for suhoor. the house is quiet. just you and your creator. if you're waking up for suhoor anyway, add 10 minutes of tahajjud. don't skip it.

9. ramadan strips away the noise whether you planned for it or not. 5 days in and my screen time is already dropping. the fast does something to your priorities that no productivity system can replicate.

10. the best dua is the one you make at iftar when you're exhausted and your heart is completely open. don't waste that 2-minute window scrolling. put the phone down. talk to allah. then eat.
