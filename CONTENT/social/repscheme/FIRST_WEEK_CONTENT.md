- Schoenfeld et al. (2021): Low-load training did NOT improve 1RM as well as heavy training.
- Mattocks et al. (2017): Strength gains are specific to the load used in training.
- Campos et al. (2002): Only the heavy group improved maximal strength, despite all groups gaining similar muscle.

5:30 - Key insight 2: Hypertrophy is load-agnostic. Strength is not. Train heavy if you want to lift heavy.

6:00 - Study 7-9: The practical advantages of different rep ranges.
- Higher reps (15-30): more metabolic stress, better for isolations, less joint stress, higher fatigue per set.
