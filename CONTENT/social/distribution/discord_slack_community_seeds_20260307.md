## Community Engagement Rules

1. GIVE VALUE FIRST. spend 2 weeks being helpful before sharing anything
2. no drive-by promotions. engage with existing threads
3. respond to questions with detailed, helpful answers
4. only share tools when they directly solve someone's stated problem
5. never spam multiple channels with the same message at once
6. adapt tone to the community (casual in Discord, professional in Slack)
7. always disclose you're the maker when sharing your own tools
