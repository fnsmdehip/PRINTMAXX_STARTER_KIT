### Post 1: Comparison SEO Strategy
```
comparison pages might be the highest ROI content play for indie hackers.

here's why:

someone searches "focuslock vs opal" — they're already looking for alternatives. they're at the decision point. you don't need to convince them they have a problem. you just need to show them your solution is better (or free).

i just deployed comparison pages for two of my free tools:
- focuslock vs opal (screen time app, $100/yr vs free)
- prayerlock vs hallow (prayer app, $70/yr vs free)

the math: these pages rank for "[tool] vs [competitor]" and "[competitor] alternative" queries. every visitor has purchase intent. conversion rates on comparison pages are 3-5x higher than landing pages.

if you're building free alternatives to paid tools, make comparison pages. it's the easiest SEO win you're not doing.
```
