### POST 1: 180 websites in 35 days

**Hook:** I deployed 180 websites in 35 days as a solo developer. Here's the unsexy truth.

---

No VC. No team. No budget beyond $200/month for Claude.

Here's what actually happened:

Day 1: I thought the bottleneck was building. Wrong.

Day 15: I had 80 sites live. Zero traffic. Zero revenue. The problem wasn't building speed. It was distribution.

Day 35: 180 sites. Still the same problem.

The architecture that made 180 possible:
- PWA stack (no app store, no review, no 30% cut)
- surge.sh free tier for static hosting
- Python scripts that templated and deployed in batches
- One codebase, 40+ variations

I could ship a new site in 14 minutes once the template was locked.

But here's what nobody tells you: shipping is not the hard part.

The hard part is getting one person to care.

180 deployments taught me that build speed is the easiest problem to solve. Distribution is not.

If you're building right now: stop at site 5. Figure out who's going to see it. Then build sites 6 through 180.
