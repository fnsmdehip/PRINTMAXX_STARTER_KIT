**Tweet 2:**
the 5 products:

- 73 Cold Email Subject Lines (PDF)
- Funnel Teardown Pack (PDF)
- AI Automation Blueprint (PDF)
- Solopreneur OPS System (PDF)
- Cold Email Playbook (PDF)

total distribution time so far: ~3 hours

that's the problem.

---

**Tweet 3:**
mistake 1: building in the wrong order.

I spent 80% of my time on the product and 20% on distribution. it should be the other way.

nobody finds a Gumroad page by accident. you have to push it.

the product quality is table stakes. getting it in front of people is the actual job.

---

**Tweet 4:**
mistake 2: waiting until everything was perfect.

the cold email playbook went through 4 drafts before I published it.

draft 1 would have been good enough. the extra 3 drafts bought me approximately zero extra sales.

ship at 80%. collect feedback. update if needed.

---

**Tweet 5:**
what actually moved the needle: specificity.

"cold email playbook" is boring.
"73 subject lines with 47-61% open rates" is a thing you want.

every product that converted had a specific number in the title or description.

vague promises lose. specific claims win.

---

**Tweet 6:**
the funnel teardown pack was the easiest to sell.

not because it was the best product. because the concept is easy to explain in one sentence: "I analyzed 14 funnels that are making money right now."

simple concept + specific number = people get it immediately.

lesson: if you can't explain your product in 10 words, rewrite the positioning, not the product.
