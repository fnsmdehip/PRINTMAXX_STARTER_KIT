Assalamu alaikum everyone.

We're 19 days into Ramadan and I wanted to share something I built that a few people in my local community found useful, in case it helps anyone here too.

I've struggled for years to track my own Ramadan consistently. I'd start strong, lose track of my Quran streak around day 10, tell myself I'd catch up, and end up finishing Ramadan without really knowing where I landed. No accountability, no data, no way to look back at what I actually did.

This year I built three simple free tools to solve that for myself:

1. Ramadan Tracker (ramadan-tracker.surge.sh) - tracks your prayers, fasting, and Quran reading day by day. Shows you your streak and where you stand.

2. Quran Streak (quran-streak.surge.sh) - specifically for people doing a khatm. Tracks your daily pages and tells you if you're on pace to finish by Eid.

3. PrayerLock - a lightweight prayer times app. 55 kilobytes. Loads in under a second. Works without wifi. Comment below if you want the link.

All three are completely free. No accounts, no ads, no data collection. Add them to your home screen on iPhone or Android and they work like apps even offline.

We have about 19 days left. Some of us are ahead of where we hoped to be, some are behind. Either way, having a clear picture of the last stretch helps.

May Allah accept from all of us. Ramadan Mubarak.
