## HN POST 2 — Show HN: AI Slop Detector

**Title:**
```
Show HN: AI writing detector that shows which signals triggered the flag
```

**Body:**
```
Most AI detection tools give you a percentage and nothing actionable. I built one that breaks down exactly which signals are firing so you can understand (and fix) the specific patterns.

Signals it detects:
- Perplexity variance (how predictable word choices are at each position)
- Burstiness (variance in sentence length; LLMs cluster more tightly)
- Lexical diversity across sliding 500-word windows
- Filler phrase density (transition phrases LLMs overuse)
- Paragraph entropy (information density variation)

All runs client-side in the browser. No data sent anywhere.

[ai-slop-detector.surge.sh](https://ai-slop-detector.surge.sh)

Feedback on the scoring weights welcome — I used published NLP research but the calibration is my best effort.
```
