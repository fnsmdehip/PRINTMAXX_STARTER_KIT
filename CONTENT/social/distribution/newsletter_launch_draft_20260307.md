## Issue #1: "77 sites, $0 revenue. here's the plan."

**Subject line:** I built 77 websites. Revenue: $0. Here's my 30-day fix.

**Preview text:** building is a comfort zone. distribution is the real work.

---

**Body:**

### the situation

77 websites deployed. 13 apps live. 131 digital products ready to list. 10 SaaS tools built. 6 local business demo sites ready for cold outreach.

total revenue: $0.

not because the products are broken. because I never distributed them. I treated "deployed" as "done." it's not.

I spent 3 months building. I got really good at shipping fast. the problem: shipping without distribution is just organized file storage with a public URL.

### the 30-day distribution plan

starting this week, I'm doing nothing but distribution. no new features. no new products. just getting what exists in front of people who'd pay for it.

**week 1: listings**
- list all digital products on Gumroad (13 products)
- list Fiverr gigs for web dev services (10 gigs)
- submit PrayerLock and PageScorer to Product Hunt
- post streak apps to r/SideProject

**week 2: outreach**
- cold email 100 local businesses using demo sites
- post on LinkedIn about the $0 tech stack
- submit tools to AlternativeTo, SaaSHub, BetaList
- post on IndieHackers about the path

**week 3: content**
- launch this newsletter
- start tweeting daily from @printmaxxer
- write 3 Reddit value posts (not promotional, just useful)
- create comparison content (PrayerLock vs Hallow, FocusLock vs Opal)

**week 4: optimize**
- double down on whatever channel shows traction
- kill channels that showed zero results
- optimize listings based on initial data
- close any warm leads from outreach

### the stack (for the curious)

everything runs on:
- surge.sh for hosting (free, 30-second deploys)
- vanilla HTML/CSS/JS for apps (no framework overhead)
- python for build scripts and automation
- github for version control
- no database, no backend, no server costs

total monthly infrastructure cost: $0.

### the thesis

if you can build fast and host free, the only variable is distribution. the product is the easy part. finding people who'll pay is the entire game.

I'll share real numbers every week. open rates, conversion rates, revenue. no inflated screenshots. just the actual dashboard.

that's it. no fluff. no "5 tips for entrepreneurs." just a builder sharing real numbers.
