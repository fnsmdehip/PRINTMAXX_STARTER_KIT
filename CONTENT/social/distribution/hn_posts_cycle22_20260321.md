## POST 1 — Show HN: Fitness streak trackers with zero friction (no login, no ads)

**URL:** pushup-streak.surge.sh (lead with pushup, mention plank/yoga/cycling/hiit in comments)

**Comment to post:**
I built 5 single-purpose fitness streak trackers (pushup, plank, yoga, cycling, HIIT). No login, no ads, works offline.

The design principle: the biggest competitor to any habit app is the friction of opening it. So I removed everything between "open URL" and "log rep."

Each app is one page. Hit a button. Streak updates. Leave.

Built with vanilla JS, deployed on surge. Total build time per app: ~90 minutes.

I'm testing whether focused single-purpose apps retain better than comprehensive fitness trackers. Would love to know if anyone else has tried this approach.
