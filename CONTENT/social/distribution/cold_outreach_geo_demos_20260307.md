## TEMPLATE 1: "I already built your website"

**Best for:** Geo-specific demos. Use when you have a demo that matches their city AND industry exactly.

---

### Subject line options

1. `I built a site for {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} businesses`
2. `your new website is at {{DEMO_URL}}`
3. `{{BUSINESS_NAME}} - I made you something`

---

### Body

```
{{FIRST_NAME}},

I build demo websites for local businesses before reaching out.

yours is live right now: {{DEMO_URL}}

I built it for {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} businesses specifically. mobile-optimized, loads in under a second, shows up in Google for local searches.

takes me about 2 hours to swap in your logo, phone number, photos, and services.

want me to do that? reply and I'll send you a live version with your actual business info.

[YOUR NAME]
[PHONE]

PS - no obligation. if you like it, we talk price. if not, it stays a demo and costs you nothing.
```

**Word count:** 89 words
**CTA:** reply to trigger swap-in
**Why it works:** consequence-first (the site is already live). lowest-friction response mechanism. PS removes the risk objection before they raise it.

## TEMPLATE 2: "Your competitor has a better website"

**Best for:** Prospects with an obviously outdated or missing site. Use sitescore-app.surge.sh to pull their score first.

---

### Subject line options

1. `{{BUSINESS_NAME}} vs your competitors online`
2. `your {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} competitors are winning on Google`
3. `quick question about {{BUSINESS_NAME}}'s site`

---

### Body

```
{{FIRST_NAME}},

I ran {{CURRENT_SITE_URL}} through a site audit.

mobile score: [SCORE]/100. page speed: [LOAD TIME]s. Google ranking for "{{INDUSTRY}} {{CITY}}": page [X].

I built a demo showing what your site could look like: {{DEMO_URL}}

mobile-first. loads in under 1 second. structured for local Google search.

I built this in 2 hours. yours could look like this by tomorrow.

worth a quick call? reply and we can set one up.

[YOUR NAME]
[PHONE]
```

**Word count:** 78 words
**CTA:** reply for call
**Note:** Fill [SCORE], [LOAD TIME], [X] manually from sitescore-app.surge.sh before sending. specific audit data makes this 3x more effective than generic copy.

## TEMPLATE 3: Follow-up (Day 3)

**Send:** 3 days after Template 1 or 2 with no reply.

---

### Subject line options

1. `re: {{DEMO_URL}}`
2. `re: {{BUSINESS_NAME}} website`
3. `one more thing about your site`

---

### Body

```
{{FIRST_NAME}},

circling back on the demo I built for you: {{DEMO_URL}}

two things worth knowing:

1. it loads in under 1 second on mobile. 53% of visitors leave if a site takes over 3 seconds.
2. it has schema markup built in, so Google can read your business info for local searches.

most {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} businesses don't have either.

if you want me to put your actual info in, just reply. takes me a day.

[YOUR NAME]
```

**Word count:** 83 words
**CTA:** reply
**New value added:** mobile speed stat (53% abandonment) + schema markup specifics
**Why it works:** short, adds one new data point, re-links the demo, no pressure

## TEMPLATE 4: "Free audit + demo" offer

**Best for:** Cold contacts where you don't yet have their audit data. Positions audit as the lead-gen hook, demo as the payoff.

---

### Subject line options

1. `free site audit for {{BUSINESS_NAME}}`
2. `what's {{CURRENT_SITE_URL}} scoring on Google?`
3. `I'll audit {{BUSINESS_NAME}}'s site for free`

---

### Body

```
{{FIRST_NAME}},

I audit local business websites for free and send back a score report.

tells you: page speed, mobile score, Google ranking for your main keywords, and what's costing you leads.

I already built a demo for {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} businesses so you can see what a fixed version looks like: {{DEMO_URL}}

reply with your website URL and I'll run the audit today and send you the report.

no pitch. just the numbers.

[YOUR NAME]
[PHONE]
```

**Word count:** 79 words
**CTA:** reply with URL
**Why it works:** free audit removes all friction. "no pitch. just the numbers." line disarms the sales-guard reflex. demo URL gives them something to look at before they reply.

## TEMPLATE 5: Breakup email (Day 7, final touch)

**Send:** 7 days after initial email with no reply. This is the last email in the sequence.

---

### Subject line options

1. `last email - {{BUSINESS_NAME}}`
2. `figured you're busy`
3. `closing the file on {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} demo`

---

### Body

```
{{FIRST_NAME}},

figured you're probably busy.

just wanted to make sure you saw what I built for {{CITY}} {{INDUSTRY}} businesses: {{DEMO_URL}}

if you ever want your info swapped in, it's here.

[YOUR NAME]
```

**Word count:** 35 words
**CTA:** passive - the link does the work
**Why it works:** dead-short. non-needy. leaves the door open without any pressure. the demo URL is the entire ask.
