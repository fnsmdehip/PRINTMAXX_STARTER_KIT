## POST 1 — AI Content Arbitrage

Solo operators sell AI-generated videos for $50-100 each.

Agencies resell those same videos to clients for $500-2,000.

The markup isn't skill. It's packaging.

The agency adds: a brand brief, one revision round, and a Zoom call. That's it.

If you're already making AI video content for cheap, here's the move:

1. Stop selling on Fiverr at $75/video
2. Package 4 videos as a "monthly content retainer"
3. Price at $2,000-3,000/mo
4. Outsource production to other operators at $75-100 each
5. Your cost: $400. Your revenue: $2,500. Margin: 84%

The moat is never the production. It's the relationship with the check-writer.
