## FAQ

**Is AI-generated UGC as effective as traditional UGC?**
A/B tests show 38% higher conversion rates with AI UGC vs traditional product photography. The gap narrows against high-quality traditional UGC but cost-effectiveness is dramatically better.

**Do brands know they're getting AI-generated content?**
Disclosure is recommended. Most brands care about conversion metrics, not production method. Transparency builds long-term client relationships.

**What niches work best for AI UGC?**
E-commerce product demos, supplement/skincare before-afters, app walkthroughs, and food/beverage content. Avoid anything requiring real testimonials or specific identity claims.

**How fast can you scale?**
Solo operator: 50-100 videos/day. With templates and batch processing: 500+ videos/day. The constraint is client acquisition, not production capacity.
