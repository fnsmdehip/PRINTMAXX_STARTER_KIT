# Thread: The Teach vs Track Gap

## 1/6
my competitor stalker agent found something interesting.

i told it to scan health and wellness apps for streak opportunities.

it came back with 15 niches. combined community: 80 million users.

existing streak apps in all 15 niches: zero.

here's what it found and why nobody's building these.

## 2/6
the pattern:

every health app is a TRACKER.

MyFitnessPal tracks calories (50M users). Strava tracks runs (100M users). Whoop tracks recovery ($30/mo).

none of them teach you the actual science.

what leucine threshold means. why cold exposure timing matters. how the Bohr effect explains why overbreathing reduces oxygen delivery.

the "daily science lesson as a streak" category doesn't exist.

## 3/6
top 5 from the scan:

1. recovery science - 14.2M community. nobody teaches what HRV means.
2. sports nutrition - 14.2M community. nobody teaches protein synthesis.
3. nutrition science - 8.5M community. nobody teaches gut microbiome.
4. flow state - 6.5M community. nobody teaches the challenge-skill ratio.
5. intermittent fasting - 5.2M community. nobody teaches what happens at 16h vs 72h.

combined: 48.6M users. competitors: 0.

## 4/6
why nobody builds these:

developers build tools, not textbooks.

a calorie tracker is a technical problem. it's fun to build.
a daily nutrition science lesson is a content problem. it's boring to build.

so 50M people use MyFitnessPal and still think "clean eating" is a real thing.

the hard part isn't coding the app. it's writing 365 accurate science lessons. that's an AI problem now.

## 5/6
what we're building:

each niche = one PWA with:
- one science concept per day
- streak counter + XP system
- mastery badges (e.g., "Circadian Anchor," "Autophagy Activator")
- progression from beginner to advanced

total build time per app: ~4 hours (Claude Code + Capacitor)

monetization: $20-30/year premium tier with deeper explanations + quizzes

## 6/6
the meta-lesson:

when every competitor in a space solves the SAME problem (tracking), the blue ocean is solving a DIFFERENT problem (teaching).

this works everywhere, not just health apps.

every niche has tools. very few niches have daily education streaks.

the scanner runs every 4 hours. 289 blue oceans found so far.
