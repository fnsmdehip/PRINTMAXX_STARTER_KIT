48,000 MCP servers across 5 directories. the pendulum is swinging back to simplicity. if you built an MCP server, consider wrapping a CLI instead. less infrastructure, same functionality.

747 HN points. the biggest academic preprint server on earth cut ties with its host institution. this follows the same pattern as every platform that outgrows its original infrastructure: twitter leaving google cloud, discord leaving google cloud, now arxiv leaving cornell.

the builder lesson: own your infrastructure before it owns you. the dependency you ignore today is the migration you panic about tomorrow.
