## POST 1 — Cold Email Shifts

Cold email fundamentals shifted in 2026. Three changes worth knowing:

Gmail now warns users about tracking pixels. Disable open tracking entirely.

First name personalization triggers spam filters. Reference the prospect's actual product or a recent company event instead.

58% of all replies come from the first email in a sequence. Not the follow-ups.

The takeaway: one great first email beats a 7-email sequence of mediocre ones. Brevity and business context win.
