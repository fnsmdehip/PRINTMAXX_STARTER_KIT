3 revenue channels just broke. not slowly. overnight.

if you're still running the 2024 playbook, here's why it stopped printing.

## 1. Cold email personalization is backwards now

gmail flags tracking pixels. "{FirstName}" triggers spam filters. polished copy gets detected as AI-generated.

what works: casual tone, business context (reference their actual product, not their name), 4 lines max, no open tracking.

58% of all replies come from the first email. if email #1 doesn't hit, the other 6 in your sequence are just noise.

## 2. SEO lost its monopoly to GEO

43% more brands got cited by ChatGPT this year vs last year. McKinsey projects $750B in consumer spending will flow through AI answers by 2027.

the move: optimize for AI citation, not just Google ranking. add structured tables to your pages. write content that matches how people ask ChatGPT questions. post on Reddit (Perplexity cites Reddit more than any other source).

Princeton research shows 30-40% visibility boost from GEO tactics. the effort is low. the upside is massive.

## 3. Pricing is 2-4x more valuable than features

25-60% revenue increase from pricing optimization alone. per-seat models are collapsing (usage-based grew 126% YoY). Gartner says 40% of SaaS will be outcome-based by 2028.

if you're charging a flat monthly fee for unlimited access, you're subsidizing power users with revenue from casual users. credit-based or usage-based pricing captures the actual value you create.
