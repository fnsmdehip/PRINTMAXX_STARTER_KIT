## THREAD-002-MAIN: Cold email tactics with actual numbers

cold email gets 3%+ reply rates when you follow this exact structure. here's what actually works right now.

(thread)

1/ the subject line kills most campaigns before they start. "quick question" gets 34% open rate. "following up" gets 18%. personalized subject with company name gets 41%. use the company name.

2/ line 1 has one job: prove you're not a bot. reference something specific. their last funding round, a product they just launched, a post they wrote. generic line 1 = delete.

3/ the offer lives in line 2-3. not what you do. what they get. "i can get you 3 qualified calls booked in the next 14 days or you pay nothing" beats "we provide lead gen services" by 400%.

4/ the ask has to be small. "15-minute call?" outperforms "schedule a discovery meeting?" by 2-3x. friction kills conversions. ask for the smallest thing that moves the deal forward.

5/ follow-up sequence matters more than the first email. most replies come on email 3 or 4. day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14. 4 emails total. if nothing after 14 days, move on.

6/ the numbers from my last 300-email test: 41% open rate, 4.1% reply rate, 6 calls booked, 2 closed. $3,400 in revenue from 300 emails. that's $11.33 per email sent. not bad for a google doc and apollo.io.
