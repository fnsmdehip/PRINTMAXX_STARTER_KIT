## Tweet 1/7 (Hook)

i make one piece of content per week. it turns into 20.

here's the exact system. it takes 2 hours on monday and runs itself the rest of the week.

no team. no VA. just a workflow.

## Tweet 2/7 (The Hub)

start with one long-form piece. blog post, newsletter, or youtube video. this is your hub.

everything else branches off of it.

the key: write the long-form first. never start with tweets and try to expand up. always start big and cut down.

expanding short content into long content = padding. cutting long content into short content = density.

## Tweet 3/7 (The Spoke: Twitter/X)

from 1 blog post, extract:
- 3-5 standalone tweets (one insight per tweet)
- 1 thread (the compressed version of the full post)
- 2-3 reply bait posts (hot takes from the post without context)

that's 7-9 pieces from one source. takes 20 minutes with claude.

## Tweet 4/7 (The Spoke: Short-Form Video)

from that same blog post:
- 1 youtube short (50-60 seconds, that's the sweet spot in 2026)
- 1 tiktok (same video, remove any watermarks, ig penalizes cross-posts)
- 1 instagram reel (vertical, first 2 seconds = hook, DM shares are the new priority metric)

18-22 shorts per month = optimal posting frequency on youtube. one blog post per week handles most of that.

## Tweet 5/7 (The Spoke: LinkedIn + Newsletter)

linkedin: rewrite the blog post as a first-person story. add a lesson at the end. remove all links from the post body (algorithm buries external links). put the link in the first comment.

newsletter: the blog post IS the newsletter. add a personal intro paragraph and a "one thing to do this week" section at the bottom.

two platforms. 15 minutes of adaptation. zero original writing.

## Tweet 6/7 (The Spoke: Reddit + SEO)

reddit: find 2-3 subreddits where your topic is relevant. post a value-first comment or thread. no self-promotion in the post. link in profile or reply only if asked.

reddit posts = perplexity gold. a well-written reddit answer gets cited more than a polished blog post.

seo: add tables, stats, and Q&A sections to the original blog. 30-40% visibility boost when AI engines cite you. this is geo (generative engine optimization) and it matters more than traditional seo now.

## Tweet 7/7 (The Math)

1 blog post = 5 tweets + 1 thread + 3 short videos + 1 linkedin post + 1 newsletter + 2 reddit posts + geo optimization

that's 14-20 content pieces. per week. from one writing session.

the people posting 3x/day aren't creating 3x/day. they're repurposing 1x/week.

stop creating more. start distributing better.
