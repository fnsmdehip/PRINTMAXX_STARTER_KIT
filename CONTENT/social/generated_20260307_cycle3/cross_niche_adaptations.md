## FAITH NICHE (@selahmoments / ramadan content)

### Post, Ramadan Content Distribution

ramadan content creators: the algorithm just changed and it's in your favor.

tiktok is pushing tutorials over entertainment. youtube shorts are now in search results.

a 50-second "how to set up your iftar schedule" video is now discoverable when someone searches "ramadan schedule 2026."

that didn't work 3 months ago. it works now.

the play for the last 10 days: post one short tutorial per day. make it searchable. text overlays with specific keywords. "ramadan dua day 21" not "beautiful reminder."

search intent > viral intent during ramadan. people are looking for specific things. be the answer.

### Post, Content Repurposing for Faith Creators

you don't need to create 20 pieces of content per week for your faith community.

you need to create 1.

write one reflection or lesson. then:
- 3 tweets from the key lines
- 1 short video reading the strongest paragraph
- 1 linkedin post retelling it as a personal story
- share in 2 relevant reddit communities

one piece of writing. 7+ touchpoints. same message, different formats.

the people posting daily aren't writing daily. they're distributing weekly.

### Post, Platform Shift for Fitness Creators

instagram just made DM shares the top priority metric. not likes. shares.

for fitness content, this changes everything.

the content people share via DM: workout plans, meal prep guides, form check videos. specific utility content.

the content people like publicly: transformation photos, motivation quotes. that's not what the algorithm rewards anymore.

pivot from "inspiration" to "send this to your gym buddy." that's the new fitness content formula.

## ESOTERIC / DRIFTHOUR NICHE

### Post, The Discovery Shift

the way people find niche content is changing.

reddit answers get cited by perplexity AI. structured blog posts get cited by chatgpt. youtube shorts now appear in google search.

if you create content about niche topics (astrology, tarot, lucid dreaming, consciousness), the discovery channels just tripled.

the old game: hope the algorithm shows your post to your followers.
the new game: be the answer when someone asks an AI "how do tarot readings work" or searches youtube for "lucid dreaming technique."

add tables, stats, and Q&A sections to your content. AI engines love structured answers. your beautifully written essay gets skipped. your well-structured FAQ gets cited.
