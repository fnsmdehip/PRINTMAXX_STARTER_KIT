## 1. NEW ACCOUNT WARMUP STRATEGY

The 2026 algorithm punishes cold-start accounts. If you post immediately after creating an account, TikTok flags you as potential bot/spam and throttles your reach. The warmup is non-negotiable.

### Pre-Setup Checklist
- Use a **unique email or phone number** not linked to any other TikTok account
- Use a **phone that hasn't managed multiple TikTok accounts**
- Connect via **stable local Wi-Fi**, never a VPN (TikTok detects and flags VPN usage)
- Complete your profile fully:
  - Clear, original profile picture (not a stock photo)
  - Brandable username relevant to your niche (no random numbers)
  - Bio with **niche keywords** (e.g., "Building automated businesses in public | AI + Automation")
  - Link your Instagram/YouTube if you have them

### The 7-Day Warmup Protocol

**Days 1-3: Pure Consumption (DO NOT POST)**
- Spend 20-30 minutes/day scrolling the FYP
- Watch videos in your niche **all the way through** (completion signals your interests)
- Like 10-15 videos per session in your niche
- Follow 5-8 creators in your space
- Leave 5-10 **genuine, thoughtful comments** (not "nice!", write real reactions)
- Search for niche keywords: "automation," "solopreneur," "AI business," "building in public"

**Days 4-7: Active Community Member (STILL NO POSTING)**
- Continue daily engagement (15-30 min/day)
- Reply to other people's comments
- Save videos you genuinely find valuable
- Share 1-2 videos via DM (signals real usage)
- Follow a few more niche creators
- Start creating a swipe file of hooks, formats, and sounds you see performing

**Day 8+: First Posts**
- Prepare 4-5 short videos (15-30 seconds) before posting any
- Film in 9:16 vertical on your phone
- Post through the **mobile app** for your first 10+ posts (not web, not API, not a scheduler)
- Start with 1 post/day
- Post at peak times (see Posting Cadence section)

### Why This Works in 2026
TikTok's 2026 algorithm now uses a **"follower-first" distribution model**. Your video first shows to a small test audience of your followers. If they engage, it goes wider. If they don't, it dies. The warmup period:
1. Establishes your account as a real human (not a bot)
2. Trains TikTok's interest graph to understand your niche
3. Builds a baseline trust score so your first posts get fair algorithmic evaluation
4. Avoids the "newborn shadowban" that kills cold-start accounts

## 3. HOOK FORMULAS

TikTok's own research: **63% of videos with the highest click-through rates hook viewers within the first 3 seconds.** The average mobile content viewing decision is **1.7 seconds**. Your hook is everything.

### The 2026 Completion Rate Bar
- You need **70%+ completion rate** to trigger viral distribution (up from 50% in 2024)
- Aim for **65%+ retention at the 3-second mark**
- The hook's job: stop the scroll AND create a reason to watch until the end

### Top-Performing Hook Categories

#### A. Results-First Hooks (Highest performers for business content)
```
"We got 10,000 signups from a single TikTok, here's how"
"This one change doubled our watch time overnight"
"I automated my entire business in 30 days, here's what happened"
"$0 to $X/month with just AI and automation"
"I replaced my team of 5 with this AI stack"
```

#### B. Curiosity Gap Hooks
```
"I found a $0 automation that cut my workload by 3 hours/day"
"Why doesn't anyone talk about this?"
"Nobody is doing this yet and it's free"
"I found an API gap that lets me post to 6 platforms for free"
"The thing nobody tells you about [topic]..."
```

#### C. Controversy / Pattern Interrupt Hooks
```
"TikTok ads are a waste of money for most startups"
"Posting more content is actually hurting your growth"
"Stop building in public, here's why"
"AI won't replace you. But someone USING AI will."
"Most solopreneurs are doing automation completely wrong"
```

#### D. Direct Value Promise Hooks
```
"Save this, you'll need it later"
"Here's exactly how I [specific result]"
"The free tool that replaced [expensive tool]"
"3 automations running my business right now"
"If you're a solopreneur, watch this"
```

#### E. Story / POV Hooks
```
"POV: You just automated your first client's business"
"Day 47 of building an AI business from scratch"
"What my screen looks like at 3am building automations"
"I showed my boss my automation, he wasn't ready"
```

#### F. Visual Hooks (First Frame)
- Screen recording of an impressive automation running
- Revenue dashboard / analytics screenshot
- Before/after split screen
- Rapid text appearing on screen
- You looking shocked/excited at your screen

### Hook Construction Formula
```
[PATTERN INTERRUPT] + [SPECIFIC OUTCOME] + [IMPLIED METHOD]
```
Example: "Stop scrolling" + "I made $5K this week" + "using a tool nobody talks about"

### Pro Tips
- Put the hook in **on-screen text** AND say it out loud (TikTok reads both via OCR + speech recognition)
- Never start with "Hey guys" or "So today I wanted to..."
- The hook should create an **open loop** that only closes at the END of the video
- Test the same content with 3 different hooks, the hook matters more than the content itself

## 4. POSTING CADENCE

### New Account Ramp-Up Schedule

| Phase | Timeline | Frequency | Notes |
|-------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Warmup** | Days 1-7 | 0 posts | Engage only. See Section 1. |
| **Launch** | Weeks 2-4 | 3-5 posts/week | 1 post/day max. Test formats. Post via mobile app. |
| **Growth** | Weeks 5-8 | 1 post/day | Build consistency. Analyze what works. |
| **Scale** | Weeks 9-12 | 1-2 posts/day | Add second daily post if quality stays high. |
| **Mature** | Week 13+ | 1-3 posts/day | Based on your capacity. Quality > quantity always. |

### Optimal Posting Times (2026 Data)

**Highest engagement windows:**
| Time Slot | Why It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **7-9 AM local** | Morning commute scrollers |
| **12-1 PM local** | Lunch break peak |
| **3-4 PM local** | After school/work wind-down |
| **6-9 PM local** | Evening prime time (highest overall) |
| **9-11 PM local** | Late night browsing (high engagement quality) |

**Best days**: Tuesday through Thursday tend to outperform, with weekends showing higher total usage but more competition.

**Ideal windows**: Weekdays 2-5 PM and evenings 7-11 PM consistently perform best.

### Critical Spacing Rule
**Space uploads at least 3-4 hours apart** to avoid competing against yourself in the algorithm. If you post 2 videos within an hour, they cannibalize each other's distribution.

### The Quality Threshold
Going from 1 post/week to 2-5 posts/week provides the **most meaningful lift in views**. Beyond that, returns diminish unless every post maintains high quality. One viral video > ten mediocre ones.

## 5. CONTENT FORMATS THAT WORK NOW

### Format Performance Ranking (2026)

#### Tier 1: Highest Engagement
1. **Talking Head + Screen Recording Combo**
   - You in a small circle (bottom corner) narrating while showing your screen
   - 31% higher engagement than heavily produced content
   - Best for: tutorials, tool demos, automation walkthroughs
   - Key: raw, authentic energy, not polished corporate

2. **"Day X of Building..." Series Format**
   - Serialized content that builds an audience over time
   - Creates follow incentive (people follow to see the next episode)
   - Best for: building in public, serialized progress content
   - Split 60-second ideas into Part 1/Part 2 for double the posts

3. **Text Overlay + B-Roll / Screen Recording**
   - Bold text statements with supporting visuals
   - Works great for faceless accounts
   - TikTok's OCR reads on-screen text for SEO
   - Most users watch without sound, captions are mandatory

#### Tier 2: Strong Performers
4. **Green Screen with Screenshots/Data**
   - You standing in front of a screenshot, dashboard, or article
   - Great for reacting to news, showing results, breaking down concepts

5. **Photo Carousel (Slide Format)**
   - 5-10 slides optimal (up to 35 allowed)
   - High save rate = algorithm boost
   - Best for: listicles, step-by-step guides, before/after comparisons
   - 1080x1920 px per slide

6. **POV / Reaction Format**
   - "POV: Your automation just landed its first client"
   - Relatable scenarios that your target audience lives

#### Tier 3: Solid Options
7. **Tutorial / How-To (Educational)**
   - 30-60 seconds for quick tips
   - Up to 3 minutes for deep dives
   - Clear structure: Problem → Solution → Result

8. **Screen Recording Only (Faceless)**
   - Works for tool demos and automation showcases
   - Add voiceover + captions
   - Lower trust-building than face-on-camera but scales easier

### Video Length Sweet Spots
| Goal | Optimal Length |
|------|---------------|
| Virality / maximum reach | 11-18 seconds |
| Storytelling / engagement | 21-34 seconds |
| Educational / how-to | 30-60 seconds |
| Deep dive / authority | 60-180 seconds |

**2026 Shift**: TikTok is pushing longer content (2-10 minutes), and 60-180 second videos are outperforming 15-second clips for established accounts. But for new accounts, start with 15-34 seconds to build completion rates.

### AI Content Guidelines (2026)
- **Allowed without labeling**: AI-generated captions, AI-written descriptions/scripts, AI hashtags, AI text overlays, ChatGPT-written hooks
- **Requires AIGC label**: Realistic AI-generated humans, deepfakes, AI avatars that look like real people
- **Algorithm preference**: Authentic human creators are favored over fully AI-generated content
- **Best practice**: Use AI for scripting and editing, but keep your face/voice as the human element

### Production Quality
- Clear audio is non-negotiable (use a lav mic or AirPods mic at minimum)
- Good lighting (natural light or a ring light)
- 1080x1920 vertical (9:16), horizontal videos get 40% less engagement
- "Good quality" videos see 50-70% more views, but "quality" means clear/watchable, NOT cinematic

## 7. ALGORITHM SIGNALS

### 2026 Signal Hierarchy (Ranked by Weight)

| Rank | Signal | Weight | What It Means |
|------|--------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | **Watch Time / Completion Rate** | Highest | 70%+ completion = viral potential. THE most important metric. |
| 2 | **Rewatches** | Very High | Someone watching again = strongest individual signal |
| 3 | **Shares (to DMs/other apps)** | Very High | Weighted **3x higher than likes**. Shares = "this is worth sending to someone" |
| 4 | **Saves** | High | "Worth coming back to", strongest indicator of content utility |
| 5 | **Comments** | High | Especially quick comments in first 60 min |
| 6 | **Engagement Speed** | High | Strong responses within **first 60 minutes** = flagged for expansion |
| 7 | **Follows from Video** | Medium-High | Someone following after watching = high-quality content signal |
| 8 | **Likes** | Medium | Still matters but weighted less than shares/saves in 2026 |
| 9 | **Profile Visits** | Medium | Curiosity signal, your content made them want to see more |
| 10 | **Hashtag/Sound Relevance** | Medium | Helps initial distribution targeting |

### The 2026 Distribution Funnel

```
POST VIDEO
    |
    v

    |
    v
Do followers engage? (likes, comments, shares, completion)
    |
    YES → [SMALL NON-FOLLOWER POOL], 200-500 similar-interest users
    |
    v
Does this pool engage? (same metrics)
    |
    YES → [LARGER POOL], 1K-10K users
    |
    v
Still performing? → [FYP DISTRIBUTION], 10K-100K+
    |
    v
Viral threshold hit? → [MASS DISTRIBUTION], 100K-Millions
```

### Actionable Implications

1. **Your followers matter more than ever**: Build a follower base that actually cares about your content. Quality > quantity.
2. **First 60 minutes are critical**: Be online to reply to comments during the first hour after posting.
3. **Optimize for saves + shares, not just likes**: Create content people want to reference later (tutorials, lists, tools) or send to a friend.
4. **Create "share triggers"**: End with "Send this to someone who needs to hear this" or "Save this for later."
5. **Completion rate is king**: Keep videos short enough to finish. A 15-second video watched fully beats a 60-second video abandoned at 30 seconds.
6. **Rewatch bait**: Include a detail that rewards a second viewing ("Did you catch the easter egg?") or pack enough value that people need to watch again to absorb it.
7. **Comment bait**: Ask a question, make a slightly controversial statement, or use a CTA like "Comment [emoji] if you want Part 2."

### Negative Signals (What Kills Reach)
- "Not interested" taps
- Skipping within first 1-2 seconds
- Low completion rate (< 30%)
- Competitor watermarks
- Recycled/duplicate content
- Mass-produced or spammy posting patterns

## 8. CROSS-POSTING STRATEGY

### The Cardinal Rule
**Every platform's algorithm actively penalizes content with competitor watermarks.** Instagram literally suppresses videos with the TikTok watermark. Never upload a watermarked video to another platform.

### Workflow: One Video, Three Platforms

```
1. PRODUCE → Create video in editing app (CapCut, Premiere, DaVinci)
2. EXPORT  → Save the clean, watermark-free master file
3. POST    → Upload the clean file to each platform separately
4. ADAPT   → Customize caption, hashtags, and description per platform
5. STAGGER → Space posts 24-48 hours apart
```

### Platform-Specific Optimization

| Element | TikTok | Instagram Reels | YouTube Shorts |
|---------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Max Length** | 10 min | 3 min | 3 min |
| **Sweet Spot** | 15-60 sec | 15-30 sec | 30-60 sec |
| **Captions** | 500 chars | 2,200 chars | 100 chars (title) |
| **Hashtags** | 3-5 niche | 5-10 mixed | 3-5 in description |
| **Sounds** | Trending TikTok audio | Trending Reels audio | Original/royalty-free |
| **Caption Culture** | Casual, emoji-light | More polished, hashtag-heavy | SEO keyword-focused |
| **CTA Style** | "Follow for Part 2" | "Save this" | "Subscribe" |

### Posting Order Strategy

**Option A: TikTok First (Recommended for new accounts)**
1. Post to TikTok first (your primary growth platform)
2. Wait 24-48 hours to see performance
3. If it performs well, post to Reels and Shorts
4. Adapt caption/hashtags for each platform

**Option B: Simultaneous (For established accounts)**
- Post to all three on the same day but staggered by hours
- Use different trending sounds per platform when possible

### Watermark Handling
- **Best practice**: Always export from your editing app BEFORE posting anywhere
- **If you only have a watermarked version**: Use SnapTik, SaveTik, or similar tools to download without watermark, OR re-export from your project file
- **YouTube watermarks Shorts too**, download from your original, not from YouTube

### Cross-Platform Audience Building
Don't just cross-post, use each platform to drive traffic to the others:
- TikTok bio: "Full tutorials on YouTube"
- Reels caption: "More daily tips on TikTok @handle"
- Shorts description: "Follow the build on TikTok @handle"
