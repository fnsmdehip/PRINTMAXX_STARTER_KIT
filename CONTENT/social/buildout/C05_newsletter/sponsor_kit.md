## WHAT WE OFFER

### Sponsorship Placements

**PLACEMENT 1: Primary Sponsor Mention (Top of Issue)**
- Position: above the fold, first thing they read
- Format: 75-100 word written mention in newsletter voice (not an ad block)
- Includes: one link, one CTA
- Looks like: editorial recommendation, not advertisement

Example:
> This issue is brought to you by Clay.
> I've been using Clay for 90 days to enrich leads before cold email. The difference: I know company size, tech stack, LinkedIn activity, and hiring signals before I write a single word. My reply rate went from 4% to 11%.
> If you're doing any volume of cold outreach: clay.com/yourlink

**PLACEMENT 2: Mid-Issue Mention**
- Position: between sections 1 and 2
- Format: 40-60 word integration
- Includes: one link
- Best for: product announcements, time-sensitive offers

**PLACEMENT 3: Footer Mention**
- Position: bottom of issue before sign-off
- Format: 25-30 word callout
- Lower cost, lower visibility, good for ongoing campaigns

**PLACEMENT 4: Dedicated Issue (Sponsored Deep-Dive)**
- Full issue: one sponsor, one topic, fully sponsored
- Our voice. Our editorial standard. Their product as the tool.
- 800-1,200 words of genuine use-case breakdown.
- Our audience knows it's sponsored, we label it. But we only accept products we'd actually use.

## WHAT WE DELIVER

After each sponsored issue:

- **Beehiiv analytics report** (opens, clicks, click-through rate on sponsor link)
- **Estimated reach** (based on open rate × subscriber count)
- **Reply data** (if any readers responded about the product)
- Delivered within 48 hours of issue send

Average sponsored link CTR in this niche: 1.5-3.5%
At 3,000 subscribers, 42% open rate = 1,260 opens.
At 2% CTR = 25 clicks per sponsored link minimum.
At 5% conversion (industry average for warm B2B traffic) = 1-2 new customers per mention.

For SaaS products with $50+/mo price points: one mention can return 5-20× the sponsorship cost.
