|-------------|-----------|-----|

| Prediction markets / finance | $18-28 | Financial services advertisers pay premium |
| Sports analysis (general) | $10-16 | Seasonal spikes (NFL Sept-Feb) |
| Gaming investigative | $8-15 | Tech/gaming advertisers mid-range |
| Gaming entertainment | $4-8 | Mass audience, lower CPM |
| Chess content | $6-12 | Educated audience, moderate CPM |

### RPM vs. CPM
- CPM = what advertisers pay per 1,000 impressions
- RPM = what you earn per 1,000 views (after YouTube's 45% cut + unskipped ad rate)
- RPM is typically 40-60% of CPM
- At $20 CPM, expect $9-12 RPM
- At $10 CPM, expect $4-6 RPM

### Revenue Math (Month 6 Projection)
```
30K monthly views × $7 RPM average = $210/mo AdSense
Spike during NFL season (Sept-Jan): 2-3x multiplier
Conservative month 6: $300-500/mo AdSense alone
```

### Path to Partner Program (YPP)
- Requirement: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours in 12 months
- Or: 1,000 subscribers + 10 million Shorts views in 90 days
- **Fastest path:** Mix of long-form (watch hours) + Shorts (discovery)
- Realistic timeline: 60-90 days if uploading 5x/week

## Legal Compliance Checklist

Before monetizing sports betting content:
- [ ] FTC disclosure in video (verbal): "This video contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you sign up."
- [ ] FTC disclosure in description: "Affiliate disclosure: [platforms listed]"
- [ ] Age gate disclaimer: "Must be 21+ and in a legal jurisdiction to use sports betting services"
- [ ] Responsible gambling notice: Include hotline number (1-800-522-4700) in description
- [ ] YouTube paid promotion toggle: Enable in video settings for any sponsored content
- [ ] State compliance: Don't actively target states where online betting is illegal (geo-restriction happens automatically via affiliate platforms)

**Note:** YouTube does allow sports betting affiliate links and sponsored content as long as all disclosures are in place. Ad revenue on betting-adjacent content can be restricted on individual videos, this is normal, appeal if incorrectly flagged.
