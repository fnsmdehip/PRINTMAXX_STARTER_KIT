## SEO Technical Requirements Per Article

- Target keyword in H1 (exact match preferred)
- Secondary keyword in at least 1 H2
- Target keyword in first paragraph (naturally)
- Meta description: 155 characters max, includes target keyword
- Image alt text includes keyword at least once
- Internal links: 2-3 per article, to related cluster content
- External links: 2-3 per article, to authoritative sources (cited data, tools)
- Word count: 1,800-2,500 (sweet spot for comprehensiveness without bloat)
- URL slug: target keyword, hyphens, no stop words (e.g., /cold-email-guide-get-clients)
