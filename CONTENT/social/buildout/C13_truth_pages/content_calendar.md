## The Publishing Logic

**Why 2 articles/week:**
- Google rewards publishing velocity on new domains (signals active site)
- Each article is a search entry point
- 104 articles by month 12 = significant long-tail traffic base
- Internal linking strengthens every article's rank as the cluster grows

**Phase approach:**
- Months 1-3: Quick-win, low-KD articles (build DA while ranking fast)
- Months 4-6: Mid-tier targets (DA now supports harder keywords)
- Months 7-12: High-volume pillars + expansion (full content hub)

## Content Production SOP

**Per article workflow:**
1. Pull keyword from priority list
2. Research: Perplexity for current data + competitor articles (30 min)
3. Outline: H1, H2s, key points per section (15 min)
4. Draft: Claude with detailed prompt + paste outline (20 min)
5. Edit: add specific numbers, update pricing, insert internal links (30 min)
6. SEO check: keyword in H1, meta description, URL slug, alt text (10 min)
7. Publish: CMS upload, schedule, ping Google Search Console

**Total per article: ~105 minutes**

At 2 articles/week: 3.5 hours/week on content production.

## Internal Linking Strategy

**Rule:** Every new article links to 3 existing articles.

**Anchor text guidelines:**
- Use keyword variation, not exact match always (Google Penguin)
- "cold email guide" not always "cold email guide to get clients"
- Mix: branded ("as I covered in my Instantly.ai review") + keyword-rich + natural ("this tool")

**Priority internal links:**
- All articles link to their cluster's pillar page
- All pillar pages link to every article in their cluster
- Cross-cluster links where relevant (AI tools article can link to faceless YouTube article)

## Quality Standards

Every article must have:
- [ ] Real numbers (not "many" or "some" — exact figures with source)
- [ ] Current tool pricing (verify before publish)
- [ ] Comparison table (for any "best X" or "X vs Y" content)
- [ ] Actionable steps (not just "here's what you could do")
- [ ] PRINTMAXXER voice (no AI slop — check copy-style.md checklist)
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 related articles
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure where relevant (FTC requirement)
- [ ] Meta description under 155 characters with keyword
