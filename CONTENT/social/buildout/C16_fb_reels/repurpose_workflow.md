# Repurpose Workflow: TikTok → IG Reels → FB Reels → YouTube Shorts

## The Core Logic

One video. Four platforms. Zero extra shoot time.

You record or generate one 30-60s vertical video. You export four platform-optimized versions in under 20 minutes. Total additional reach per piece: 3-10x, depending on platform size.

This works because all four platforms now support:
- 9:16 vertical format
- 15-60 second content
- Captions burned-in OR auto-generated
- Music/audio layering

The differences are in the metadata, caption length, hashtag culture, and algorithm timing, not the raw video.

---

## Full Workflow: Source-to-All-Platforms

### Step 1: Create the Master File

Shoot or generate one video in 1080x1920 (9:16). Export as MP4, H.264, 30fps.

Name it: `YYYY-MM-DD_[topic]-MASTER.mp4`

Always keep the master. Never upload the master directly, always use platform exports.

### Step 2: Edit in CapCut (free, handles all 4 exports)

Open master in CapCut Desktop.

Add:
- Captions (auto-caption then clean up)
- Text overlays (hook on frame 1, key points throughout)
- Background music at 15% volume (trending audio from TikTok)
- Ending frame (3 seconds: call to action text)

Do NOT add platform-specific elements at this stage. Keep it universal.

### Step 3: Export Queue

Export 4 times with these specs:

| Platform | Resolution | Bitrate | Frame Rate | File |
|----------|-----------|---------|------------|------|
| TikTok | 1080x1920 | 8Mbps | 30fps | MASTER_tiktok.mp4 |
| IG Reels | 1080x1920 | 5Mbps | 30fps | MASTER_ig.mp4 |
| FB Reels | 1080x1920 | 5Mbps | 30fps | MASTER_fb.mp4 |
| YT Shorts | 1080x1920 | 8Mbps | 30fps | MASTER_yt.mp4 |

For TikTok and YouTube: higher bitrate → better quality retention after compression.
For IG and FB: Meta recompresses aggressively, 5Mbps is enough.

### Step 4: Platform Upload Sequence

**Order matters for algorithm seeding:**

1. TikTok first (largest discoverability surface)
2. IG Reels second (Meta learns from TikTok signals via crawling)
3. YouTube Shorts third
4. FB Reels last (lowest priority, but free distribution)

Wait 30-60 min between TikTok and IG uploads. Some accounts report IG penalizing same-day reposts that are fingerprinted by TikTok watermarks, use CapCut export to remove watermarks.

### Step 5: Metadata Assembly Per Platform

Each platform gets a unique caption, hashtag set, and audio tag. See `optimization_per_platform.md` for full specs.

## Watermark Removal (Critical)

TikTok watermarks on reposts are a known reach killer on IG and YouTube.

Options:
1. CapCut export without watermark (free, works)
2. Remove.bg's video tool ($9/mo), cleaner
3. Export from original project in each platform's native app

Never post TikTok-watermarked content to IG, YouTube, or FB. Their algorithms actively suppress it.

## Content Types That Repurpose Best

Not all TikToks repurpose equally. These formats travel cleanest:

**High repurposability (use these):**
- Talking head + text overlay (universal)
- Screen recording tutorials
- Voiceover + B-roll
- List videos (numbered tips)
- Before/after reveals

**Low repurposability (adapt or skip):**
- TikTok-specific trends (audio tied to trend culture)
- Duets / Stitches (format doesn't translate)
- TikTok Live recordings (wrong aspect ratio/quality)

## Volume Math

Producing 1 original video/day:
- 7 originals/week
- Posted across 4 platforms = 28 total posts/week
- Monthly output: 28 originals → 112 posts
- Annual output: 365 originals → ~1,460 posts across platforms

At 500 avg views per post across all platforms: 730,000 views/month from 28 originals.

That's the floor. When one piece pops on TikTok, the same video on YouTube Shorts can independently go viral 2-3 weeks later.
