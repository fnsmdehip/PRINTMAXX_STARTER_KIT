## Stream 1: YouTube AdSense

### CPM by content type
ASMR is among the highest-CPM YouTube niches due to long session times and advertiser-friendly content.

| Video Category | CPM Range | Why |
|----------------|-----------|-----|
| Sleep/relaxation general | $8-12 | Broad audience, health advertisers |
| Finance ASMR | $14-22 | Finance advertisers pay premium |
| Productivity ASMR | $12-18 | SaaS advertisers, B2B adjacent |
| Pure ambient (no narration) | $6-10 | Lower engagement signals |
| Tech/AI content | $10-16 | High-value audience |

### Watch time math
- Average 10K-sub ASMR channel: 60-90K monthly views
- Average view duration: 22-35 min (ASMR audience watches long)
- 70K views × 25 min avg × $10 CPM = $291/month at 10K subs (conservative)
- 8-hour videos count as one view but generate massive watch time, algorithm loves them

### Optimization rules
- Upload 3x/week minimum for algorithm traction
- Titles must contain: "ASMR" + sleep keyword + secondary hook word
- First 30 seconds: no loud sounds, no jump scares, retention cliff
- Cards/end screens: link to Patreon and next video only
- Enable "Made for kids" = NO (kills monetization, locks out Patreon CTAs)

## Stream 2: Patreon

### Tier structure

**Tier 1: Deep Sleep ($3/month)**
- Ad-free versions of all public videos
- Early access (48 hours before YouTube)
- Monthly "quiet hours" exclusive 2-hour ambient
- Target: 2-5% of subscribers convert

**Tier 2: Full Sleep Library ($9/month)**
- Everything in Tier 1
- Access to full archive (300+ hours goal)
- Monthly exclusive 8-hour ambients (never goes to YouTube)
- Patron-only Discord server
- Vote on next month's soundscapes
- Target: 0.5-1.5% of subscribers

**Tier 3: Sleep Sanctuary ($19/month)**
- Everything in Tier 2
- Personalized 30-min custom ambient per quarter (AI-produced on request)
- Name in video credits
- Direct Discord DM access to creator
- Target: 0.1-0.3% of subscribers

### Revenue math by subscriber count
| Subs | Tier 1 (3% of 30% Patreon aware) | Tier 2 (1%) | Tier 3 (0.15%) | Monthly |
|------|---------------------------------|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 10K | 90 × $3 = $270 | 30 × $9 = $270 | 15 × $19 = $285 | $825 |
| 50K | 450 × $3 = $1,350 | 150 × $9 = $1,350 | 75 × $19 = $1,425 | $4,125 |
| 100K | 900 × $3 = $2,700 | 300 × $9 = $2,700 | 150 × $19 = $2,850 | $8,250 |

### Patreon optimization
- Pin Patreon link in every video at 3-min, 15-min, and 45-min marks via cards
- Description first line: "No ads version → [Patreon link]"
- Community posts: give Patrons early access previews
- Post Patreon-only polls: "Next month's sound: ocean / forest / city / train?"
