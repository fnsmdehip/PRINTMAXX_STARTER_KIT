**Niche:** AI-produced ASMR for sleep, focus, and relaxation
**Voice:** ElevenLabs "Charlotte" (soft British) or "Alice" (calm American) — no human required

### Concept 1: "Rainy Night at a Cozy Library"
**Runtime:** 60 min
**Format:** Ambient soundscape + soft whispered narration (AI host reads book summaries)
**Audio layers:**
- Rain on windows (looping, 60 min)
- Gentle page-turning every 40-90 seconds
- Distant thunder (every 8-12 min, very soft)
- Fireplace crackle underneath (low mix)
**Narration style:** AI voice reads 3-minute condensed book summaries (stoicism, fiction, philosophy) in a 35-second-on / 4-minute-off pattern
**Visual:** Single static image — candlelit library shelf, warm tones
**Hook line (thumbnail text):** "fall asleep in a library"
**Monetization angles:** Patreon exclusive 8-hour version, Audible affiliate links in description
**SEO keywords:** ASMR library rain, ASMR bookshelf sounds, study ASMR rain window
