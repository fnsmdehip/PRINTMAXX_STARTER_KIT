## Revenue Stream 1: Display Advertising

### Networks by Traffic Tier

**Under 10,000 monthly sessions — use Google AdSense**
- No traffic minimum
- RPM: $3-8 (mediocre, but it's something)
- Sign-up: google.com/adsense
- Approval: 2-4 weeks, needs decent content volume

**10,000-50,000 monthly sessions — try Ezoic**
- No hard minimum (accepts sites with 10K+ sessions)
- RPM: $8-18 (2x AdSense)
- AI-optimized ad placement
- Free to use, they take 10% of increased revenue
- Sign-up: ezoic.com

**50,000+ monthly sessions — Mediavine (the goal)**
- Minimum: 50,000 sessions/month
- RPM: $20-45 (5-10x AdSense)
- At 50K sessions: $1,000-2,250/mo from ads alone
- Best for: content sites in personal finance, food, lifestyle, DIY
- Application: mediavine.com/apply

**100,000+ sessions — AdThrive (now Raptive)**
- Minimum: 100,000 monthly pageviews
- RPM: $25-55
- Premium brands, higher CPM
- Application: raptive.com

### RPM by Niche (Revenue per 1,000 visitors)
| Niche | AdSense RPM | Mediavine RPM |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Personal finance | $10-20 | $30-55 |
| Business/solopreneur | $8-15 | $25-40 |
| AI tools/software | $8-14 | $22-38 |
| Make money online | $6-12 | $18-30 |
| Ecommerce | $5-10 | $15-25 |

### Ad Placement Optimization
- Above fold: 1 banner (300x250 or 728x90)
- In content: every 400-600 words, 1 ad unit
- After content: 1 sticky footer (highest RPM unit)
- Never: more than 3 ads above fold (Google policy violation)

### Affiliate Link Placement Strategy

**In-article placements (highest conversion):**
- First mention of the tool in body text → text hyperlink
- Comparison tables → button "Get Started" per row
- Dedicated "tools needed" section → list with links
- CTA at end of article → big button

**Avoid:**
- Banner images (banner blindness, low CTR)
- Sidebar widgets (low conversion, slows page)
- Popups triggering immediately (bounce rate killer)

**Disclosure (FTC required):**
Place at TOP of article, above fold:
> "This article contains affiliate links. We earn a commission if you click and purchase, at no extra cost to you."

## Revenue Stream 3: Email List / Lead Gen

### Lead Magnet Strategy by Niche

**Money Methods:**
- "The solopreneur income calculator" (Google Sheets template)
- "50 cold email templates that get 8%+ reply rates" (PDF swipe file)
- "The passive income starter kit" (checklist + tool list)
- Conversion target: 3-5% of article readers opt in

**AI Tools:**
- "AI tools comparison spreadsheet" (updated monthly)
- "100 Claude/ChatGPT prompts for solopreneurs" (prompt library)
- "The $50/month AI stack that replaces $500 in subscriptions"
- Conversion target: 4-6% (AI audience = early adopters who love free tools)

**Ecommerce:**
- "Etsy product research template" (searchable spreadsheet)
- "Print-on-demand margin calculator" (interactive spreadsheet)
- "Dropshipping product criteria checklist"
- Conversion target: 3-5%

### Email Monetization Paths

**Path 1: Affiliate promos (lowest effort)**
- 2 emails/week: 1 educational, 1 promotional
- At 1,000 subscribers: $100-300/month in affiliate commissions
- At 5,000 subscribers: $500-1,500/month
- Tool: ConvertKit or Beehiiv for automation

**Path 2: Sponsored newsletter content**
- Rate at 2,500+ subscribers: $50-150 per issue
- Rate at 10,000+ subscribers: $300-800 per issue
- Find sponsors: Beehiiv Ad Network, Paved.com, direct outreach

**Path 3: Sell own digital products**
- Price: $15-97 per product
- Convert: 1-3% of list buys each product launch
- At 2,000 subscribers: $600-1,800 per product launch
- Products: templates, courses, prompt packs, tool bundles

**Path 4: High-ticket consulting upsell**
- After 90 days of newsletters: offer "consulting call"
- Price: $200-500/hour
- At 2,000 subscribers: 1-2 bookings/month = $400-1,000/month extra

### Email Platform Selection

| Platform | Free Tier | Paid Starts | Best For |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------|
| Beehiiv | 2,500 subs | $42/mo | Newsletter-first, built-in referral |
| ConvertKit | 1,000 subs | $15/mo | Automation-heavy, product sellers |
| Mailchimp | 500 subs | $13/mo | Beginners, brand recognition |
| ActiveCampaign | None | $29/mo | E-commerce integration, CRM |

**Recommendation:** Start on Beehiiv (free to 2,500 subs). Move to ConvertKit when automation matters more than growth features.

## Content Monetization Decision Tree

```
New Article Published
        │
        ▼
Does it have a clear affiliate angle?
YES → Add affiliate links + comparison table
NO → Add generic tool recommendations
        │
        ▼
Does it solve a painful enough problem?
YES → Add lead magnet CTA (relevant to article topic)
NO → Add generic email signup ("get more guides like this")
        │
        ▼
Is this a high-traffic keyword (1,000+ searches/mo)?
YES → Optimize ad placements for RPM
NO → Focus on affiliate conversion (traffic lower, intent higher)
```

## The 90-Day Revenue Activation Plan

**Month 1 — Foundation (target: first dollar)**
- Publish 8-12 articles targeting KD under 25
- Join all affiliate programs (no approval wait)
- Set up lead magnet + email list
- Add AdSense (approval takes 2-4 weeks, apply now)
- Revenue target: $5-50

**Month 2 — Compound (target: $100)**
- Publish 8-12 more articles
- Update Month 1 articles with more affiliate links
- First email sequence live (5 emails, 14 days)
- Revenue target: $50-150

**Month 3 — Optimize (target: $300)**
- Analyze which articles get traffic, double down on that topic
- A/B test affiliate link placement in top 5 articles
- Apply for Ezoic (if over 10K sessions)
- Revenue target: $150-400

## Tracking Setup

### Essential (free)
- Google Analytics 4: traffic + behavior
- Google Search Console: keyword rankings, impressions, CTR
- Affiliate program dashboards: revenue per program

### Intermediate ($)
- Pretty Links ($79/yr): link cloaking + click tracking per affiliate
- Ahrefs ($99/mo): keyword ranking tracker, competitor analysis

### Spreadsheet tracking (manual, free)
Track per article:
- Monthly organic visitors
- Affiliate clicks + conversions per article
- Revenue per article (ad + affiliate combined)
- Email optins from article

Sort by "revenue per visitor" monthly to find top performers. Double down on those topics.
