## General Account Optimization

### TikTok SEO (Yes, it exists now)
- Profile name includes keywords: TikTok searches profile names
- Bio keywords matter: TikTok's search now indexes bios
- Caption keywords: First 150 characters of caption matter for search
- Use "keyword research" tool in TikTok Creator Search Insights

### Creator Tools to Enable Immediately
- Creator Mode: On (unlocks analytics, sound search, trend discovery)
- Auto-captions: On (accessibility + algorithm signal)
- Profile link: Enable as soon as eligible (1,000 followers)
- Creator Marketplace: Apply at 10,000 followers

### Monetization Unlocks by Follower Count
| Milestone | Unlocks |
|-----------|---------|
| 1,000 followers | Link in bio |
| 5,000 followers | Q&A feature, LIVE access |
| 10,000 followers | Creator Marketplace, Series (paid content) |
| 100,000 followers | Brand partnership invites, higher CPM |
| 1,000,000 followers | Creator Fund significant payout |

### Account Safety Rules
- Never use third-party automation for posting or follows
- Never buy followers or engagement
- Post original content, do not repost from other platforms with watermarks
- Keep devices logged into TikTok (device trust matters for reach)
- Avoid posting 3x in first 2 hours (triggers spam filter), space posts 2+ hours apart
