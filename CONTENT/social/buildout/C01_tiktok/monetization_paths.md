# C01 TikTok — Monetization Paths

## Revenue Streams by Account Type + Realistic Numbers

---

## 1. TikTok Creator Fund / Creativity Program

**What it is:** TikTok pays per 1,000 views on videos over 1 minute
**Rate:** $0.40-$0.80 per 1,000 views (Creativity Program, launched 2024)
**Eligibility:** 10,000 followers, 100,000 views in last 30 days, 18+, US account
**Content requirement:** Videos must be 1-minute minimum for payout

**Revenue math:**
- 100,000 views/month: $40-$80
- 500,000 views/month: $200-$400
- 1,000,000 views/month: $400-$800

**Assessment:** Mediocre standalone. Good supplemental. DO NOT build strategy around this.

**How to qualify faster:**
- Post 1-minute+ videos 3x per day from day 1
- Focus on series content (accountability posts = return viewers = faster view accumulation)
- Use "save this" CTAs (saves extend video life in algorithm)

## 2. TikTok Shop (Highest Priority)

**What it is:** Affiliate links directly in TikTok videos. Commission when viewers buy.
**Commission rates:** 5-20% depending on product category and seller
**Average order value:** $15-$60 for most TikTok Shop products
**Top categories:** Beauty, household items, fitness gear, tech accessories

**How it works:**
1. Sign up for TikTok Shop Affiliate (no follower minimum for affiliate, just a US account)
2. Browse the TikTok Shop marketplace for products to promote
3. Add product links to videos or LIVE streams
4. Earn commission on every sale

**Revenue math (conservative):**
- 50,000 views/month on product content, 0.5% click-through = 250 clicks
- 3% conversion rate on product pages = 7-8 sales
- Average $30 AOV × 10% commission = $22-$24/month from 50K views

**Revenue math (optimized):**
- 500,000 views/month, 1% CTR = 5,000 clicks
- 3% conversion = 150 sales
- Average $35 AOV × 12% commission = $630/month from product pins

**Best products to promote (high commission + high conversion):**
- Fitness equipment: 8-12% commission, strong demo content
- Tech accessories (phone cases, cables): fast delivery, high trust
- Beauty tools: highest conversion category on TikTok
- Supplements: 10-15% commission, high LTV

**Content that converts to Shop:**
- Unboxing + review in 60 seconds
- "I bought 5 of these and this one won" comparison
- Before/after demonstration with product link

## 4. Brand Deals / Sponsored Content

**What it is:** Brands pay creators to feature their products
**Rates by follower count:**
| Followers | Rate per video |
|-----------|---------------|
| 5K-10K | $50-$150 |
| 10K-50K | $150-$500 |
| 50K-100K | $500-$1,500 |
| 100K-500K | $1,500-$5,000 |
| 500K+ | $5,000-$20,000 |

**How to land deals before 10K followers:**
- Reach out to brands directly (NOT via influencer marketplaces until 10K+)
- Email their marketing department: "I have [X] followers in [specific niche]. my last 3 posts got [X] views. I want to do an organic integration for [product] — here's my rate card."
- Target: brands that are already advertising on TikTok (look for TikTok-style ads)
- Niche micro-influencer advantage: a 3K-follower faith/fitness account can land deals a 50K general account can't

**Tools for finding brand deals:**
- Creator Marketplace (10K+ required, TikTok's own platform)
- Bonsai ($25/month) — contract management + invoice for creators
- Collabstr (free search for brand campaigns)
- Grin (enterprise, but worth checking as your account grows)

**@printmaxxer brand targets:** Tool companies (Instantly.ai, Apollo.io, Cursor, Perplexity)
**@selahmoments brand targets:** Halal brands, Islamic fashion, prayer accessories, faith-based apps
**@aiformakers brand targets:** AI tools, SaaS products, productivity software

## 5. TikTok LIVE Gifting

**What it is:** Viewers send "gifts" during LIVE sessions, which convert to coins, then to real money
**Rate:** TikTok takes 50% of LIVE gift value. Creator gets 50%.
**Minimum payout:** $100 balance before withdrawal

**Revenue math:**
- 100-person average LIVE audience (achievable at 5K-10K followers)
- 3-5% of audience sends gifts average
- Average gift value: $5-$15
- 5 gifters × $10 average × 50% = $25/LIVE session
- 5 LIVE sessions/week × $25 = $500/month

**LIVE content that drives gifting:**
- Q&A sessions (people feel connection, give gifts to "thank" you)
- Build-in-public sessions (coding LIVE, writing LIVE, building LIVE)
- Ramadan LIVE sessions (faith audience gifts heavily during Ramadan)
- Revenue milestone announcements (people celebrate with you)

**How to increase gifting:**
- Acknowledge every gift by name (people gift more when recognized)
- Set milestone goals: "at 1,000 coins we unlock the full template"
- Be consistent with LIVE schedule so audience knows when to show up

## 6. UGC (User Generated Content) for Brands

**What it is:** Brands pay you to create content they use in THEIR ads (not posted to your account)
**Rate:** $50-$500 per video depending on niche and format
**No follower minimum.** Brands pay for content quality, not your audience size.

**How to pitch:**
1. Build a portfolio: 5-10 sample UGC videos in your niche
2. Find brands advertising on TikTok (look at TikTok Ads Library)
3. Email: "I create UGC for brands in [niche]. here are 3 sample videos. my rate is $150/video with 2 revision rounds included."

**Best niches for UGC rates:**
- Beauty/skincare: $150-$400/video
- Tech/apps: $100-$300/video
- Fitness/supplements: $150-$500/video
- Food/beverage: $100-$250/video

**Platforms to list UGC services:**
- Billo ($50-$150/video, volume discount)
- Fiverr (list as "TikTok UGC creator")
- Contra (no fees for freelancers)
- Direct outreach (highest rates, no platform cut)
