## PHASE 1: 0 → 1,000 SUBSCRIBERS (Month 1-4)

**Target:** 1K subscribers, 4,000 watch hours (YouTube Partner Program eligibility)
**Revenue this phase:** $200-$800/month (affiliates only)

### What's Unlocked at 500 Subscribers
- Channel Memberships (limited features)
- Super Thanks donations
- Shopping integration (limited)

### Affiliate Revenue Projection (Month 1-3)
Based on average 2 affiliate sales per video, 2 videos/week:
- Month 1: 4 videos × 2 sales × $8 avg commission = $64
- Month 2: 8 videos cumulative × traffic compounding = $180
- Month 3: 12 videos × compounding + replay traffic = $340

These are conservative. Cold email video (Script 09) alone could generate 50+ Instantly.ai signups.

### Watch Hour Math
- Target: 4,000 hours
- Average video length: 10 minutes = 1/6 hour
- If 50% watch rate: each complete view = 5 minutes = 1/12 hour
- To hit 4,000 hours: 4,000 × 12 = 48,000 half-complete views
- 2 videos/week × 16 weeks × 1,500 average views = 48,000
- Realistic if you hit 1,500 views/video average by week 8

### Key Action This Phase
- Post 2 videos per week without fail
- Reply to every comment (algorithm boosts engagement)
- Add email capture card at end of every video
- Build to 200-400 email subscribers alongside YouTube

## PHASE 2: 1,000 → 10,000 SUBSCRIBERS (Month 4-10)

**Revenue target:** $500-$2,500/month
**New monetization unlocked:** YouTube Partner Program (ads), full Channel Memberships

### YouTube Partner Program Revenue
At 1K subscribers + 4K watch hours: apply immediately.
Approval: 1-4 weeks.

**CPM by niche (your target niches):**
| Content Type | CPM Range | RPM (what you actually earn) |
|-------------|-----------|------------------------------|
| AI Tools | $14-$35 | $7-$18 |
| Money Methods | $10-$20 | $5-$10 |
| Productivity | $8-$18 | $4-$9 |
| Tech Tutorials | $12-$28 | $6-$14 |

**At 10K subscribers, estimated monthly views:** 50,000-80,000
**At $10 RPM:** $500-$800/month from ads alone

### Affiliate Revenue Scaling
20 videos live × avg 5 affiliate sales/video/month = 100 sales
100 × $12 avg commission = $1,200/month affiliate

Specific example at 10K subscribers:
- ElevenLabs: 40 referrals × $22/month × 22% = $193/month recurring
- Descript: 25 referrals × $24 × 15% = $90 one-time, some recurring
- Ahrefs: 10 referrals × $29 × 20% = $58/month recurring
- Total affiliates: ~$600-$900/month

### Digital Product Launch (Month 6)
Build and launch first digital product at 5K subscribers.
Target: YouTube Workflow Templates pack at $47.
5K subscribers → 1% conversion to email → 50 email subs from YouTube.
Launch to email list: 50 subscribers × 10% buy rate × $47 = $235 launch.
Gumroad long-tail: 3-5 organic sales/week from video traffic.

## PHASE 3: 10,000 → 50,000 SUBSCRIBERS (Month 10-18)

**Revenue target:** $2,500-$8,000/month
**Key lever:** Sponsorships begin at 10K-15K subscribers in this niche

### Sponsorships
AI/productivity niche brands actively look for channels with:
- 10K+ subscribers
- 30-40% audience in US/UK/AU/CA
- 4%+ engagement rate
- Relevant niche (they want to reach tool buyers)

**Rate benchmarks:**
- 10K subs: $200-$400 per integration
- 25K subs: $500-$1,000
- 50K subs: $1,500-$3,000

**Who sponsors this type of channel:**
- ElevenLabs (already paying affiliates, may upgrade to sponsors)
- Descript, CapCut, Pictory
- Notion, Airtable, Monday.com
- Hostinger, Kinsta, Cloudflare
- Software review platforms (Capterra, G2)

**Outreach approach at 10K:**
Email: `sponsorships@[companyname].com` or their marketing team via LinkedIn.
Include: subscriber count, average views per video, audience demographics, rate card.

### Newsletter Paid Tier (Month 10-12)
By month 10: target 2,000-4,000 email subscribers.
Launch paid tier at $9/month.
10% conversion from free → paid: 200-400 paid subscribers.
200 × $9 = $1,800/month recurring.

## PHASE 4: 50,000+ SUBSCRIBERS (Month 18+)

**Revenue target:** $8,000-$20,000+/month
**New opportunities:** Course launch, agency/brand deals, speaking

### Course Launch
At 50K subscribers: launch a $197-$497 course.
Topic: Faceless YouTube Blueprint (meta course about building a channel like yours).
Or: The Full AI Tool Stack (deep-dive into every tool with implementation guides).

Pre-sell to email list first. 5,000 email subs × 1% = 50 sales × $297 = $14,850 launch.
Ongoing: 10 sales/month × $297 = $2,970/month from Evergreen course.

### Brand Deals
At 50K+ in AI/productivity niche: inbound brand deals start arriving.
Typical deal: $2,500-$7,500 per video integration.
1 deal/month = $2,500-$7,500.
2 deals/month = significant portion of total revenue.

### Revenue Per View Optimization
Increase RPM by focusing on high-CPM content categories:
- Finance/investing: $15-$45 RPM
- Software tutorials (with purchase intent): $12-$35 RPM
- Business education: $10-$25 RPM
Avoid: gaming, entertainment, vlogs (low CPM, wrong audience)

## REALISTIC EXPECTATIONS (Unfiltered)

Month 1-3: Making almost nothing. This is normal. You're training the algorithm.
Month 4-6: First real money. Usually $200-$600/month. Motivation dips here — push through.
Month 7-12: Compounding starts. Old videos keep driving views. Revenue grows without new effort.
Month 12-18: First $1K month. This is when most people who started at the same time have quit.
Month 18-24: $3K-$6K/month realistic if content quality and consistency held.

The #1 predictor of success: posting 2 videos per week for 18 months regardless of early performance.
That's the only variable you fully control.
