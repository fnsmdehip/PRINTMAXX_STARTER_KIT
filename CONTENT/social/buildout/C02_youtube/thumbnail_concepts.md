## PRODUCTION NOTES
- All thumbnails: 1280x720px
- Font: Montserrat ExtraBold (free, Google Fonts)
- Never use more than 2 colors beyond black/white
- Title text: max 6 words on thumbnail (viewers scan at 0.3 seconds)
- Always A/B test: one with face mockup (stock photo), one without
- Canva template link: [Create from template: YouTube Thumbnail Dark Tech]
- CTR benchmark: aim for 6%+ (industry average 4%)
