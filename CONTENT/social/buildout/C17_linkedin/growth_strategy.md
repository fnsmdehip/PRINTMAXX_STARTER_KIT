## The Three Growth Levers

### Lever 1: Organic Content (70% of growth)

**The algorithm in simple terms:**
LinkedIn shows your post to ~5-10% of your followers in the first hour. If they engage (click, comment, like, share), it expands to a wider audience. If not, it dies.

**How to win in the first hour:**
1. Post at 7-8am when your audience is on (breakfast scroll time)
2. Immediately comment on 5 other posts in your niche (gets their creators on your content)
3. Reply to every comment within 15 minutes (each reply = new notification to commenter → they often re-engage)
4. Share post to a relevant LinkedIn Group in your niche

**Post frequency math:**
5 posts/week × 4 weeks = 20 posts/month
At 500 followers: 20 posts × average 800 impressions = 16,000 monthly impressions
At 2,000 followers: 20 posts × average 3,200 impressions = 64,000 monthly impressions

**Content quality threshold:**
Every post needs at least one of:
- Real number
- Specific tool name
- Counter-intuitive insight
- Personal experience

Generic posts don't grow audiences. They maintain them at best.

---

### Lever 2: Strategic Commenting (20% of growth)

**The "give to get" commenting strategy:**

Target 20-30 accounts in your niche who post regularly and have 2,000-20,000 followers (not huge, but established).

Comment on their posts within the first 30-60 minutes of their posting.

Quality comments:
- 40-80 words
- Adds specific value (not "great post!")
- References your own experience with numbers
- Ends with a question (drives more comments on their post, they'll notice)

**Why this works:**
- Creators of popular posts get notified when you comment
- Other readers of that post see your comment (audience borrowing)
- If your comment gets likes, LinkedIn surfaces it to more people
- The post creator often visits your profile and follows you

**Target volume:** 10-15 strategic comments/day = 20-30 minutes
**Expected result:** 10-20 new followers/day from this alone

---

### Lever 3: DM Networking (10% of growth, highest quality followers)

**The "value first" networking DM:**

After commenting on someone's posts 2-3 times, send a brief DM:
```
Hey [Name], been following your posts on [topic] for a few weeks.

Your [specific post] was genuinely useful, I used [specific insight] to [specific result].

Wanted to say thanks. If you ever want to compare notes on [topic], happy to jump on a quick call.
```

This approach:
- Converts at ~40% for a reply
- Builds relationships with other creators in your niche
- Often results in mutual post engagement (they start commenting on yours)
- Sometimes leads to cross-promotion or collaboration

**Target volume:** 5 DMs/week = 20/month
**Expected result:** 5-10 genuine relationships built per month, each of whom amplifies your content

## Content Strategy by Follower Stage

### Stage 1: 0-500 Followers (Foundation)
**Priority:** Establish your positioning. Prove you have something worth following for.

Post types to prioritize:
1. Tutorial/Framework posts (highest saves = reach extension)
2. Income transparency (builds trust fast)
3. Story posts (emotional connection)

What NOT to do at this stage:
- Don't post about follower growth (nobody cares yet)
- Don't post generic inspirational content (no differentiation)
- Don't run polls (low-effort, low-trust signal)

Goal: 3 posts go "small viral" (500+ impressions each) by the end of 90 days.

---

### Stage 2: 500-2,000 Followers (Momentum)
**Priority:** Find your 2-3 highest-performing post formats and double down.

New tactics to add:
- Start a consistent "series" (e.g., "Weekly Revenue Update" every Monday)
- Collaborate with one peer-level creator (cross-post or mention each other)
- Launch a lead magnet (free resource), capture emails from your growing audience

Content calendar adjustment:
- Keep 5 posts/week
- Test one new format per week
- Double whatever format gets the most saves

---

### Stage 3: 2,000-5,000 Followers (Compounding)
**Priority:** Build inbound pipeline. Start converting audience to revenue.

New tactics:
- Add a strong CTA in every 6th post ("DM me [word] to get [resource]")
- Publish one long-form article per month (LinkedIn Articles rank in Google)
- Start tagging relevant people in posts (sparingly, when genuinely relevant)
- Explore LinkedIn Live (Creator Mode required), even 200 viewers builds trust fast

Revenue integration:
At this stage, content should be generating 5-15 inbound leads/month.
Convert to diagnostic calls. Price at $200-500.

---

### Stage 4: 5,000-10,000 Followers (Authority)
**Priority:** Position as the definitive voice in your niche.

New tactics:
- Publish original research posts ("I analyzed 200 cold emails. Here's what I found.")
- Launch paid product to LinkedIn audience (newsletter upgrade, template, course)
- Start appearing on podcasts (content repurposing + credibility signal)
- Consider LinkedIn Newsletter (subscribers get notified directly, high open rates)

Revenue at this stage: $10,000-25,000/month if content → service pipeline is working.

## LinkedIn Algorithm Deep Dive

**How posts are ranked (confirmed signals):**

| Signal | Weight (estimated) | How to optimize |
|---|---|---|
| Comment quality + length | High | Get longer comments. Reply to drive second comments. |
| Dwell time | High | Write longer posts. Use line breaks to encourage scrolling. |
| Early engagement velocity | Very High | Post at peak time. Seed with 5-10 comments in first 30 min. |
| Saves (bookmarks) | Medium-High | Include step-by-step systems people want to reference later |
| Shares (external) | Medium | Share-worthy = specific, surprising, useful |
| Reactions | Medium | Likes are still counted but weighted less than comments/saves |
| Profile visits after seeing post | Medium | Optimized profile closes the loop |

**What hurts reach:**
- External links in post body (LinkedIn limits distribution): Always put links in first comment
- Posting inconsistently (algorithm favors consistent creators)
- Low engagement posts (they signal your content isn't valuable → future posts shown to fewer people)
- Too many hashtags (3 max, LinkedIn's own recommendation)

## LinkedIn Groups Strategy

Most LinkedIn Groups are dead. But 3 types still work:

1. **Industry-specific professional groups** (10K+ members, active moderation)
2. **Your ideal client's community** (e.g., "E-Commerce Founders" group)
3. **Niche skill groups** (e.g., "Cold Email Practitioners")

**How to use groups:**
1. Join 5-10 relevant groups
2. When you publish a post, share it to 2-3 relevant groups
3. This can add 200-1,000 extra impressions per post
4. Comment in groups 2-3x/week (same quality comment formula as main feed)

**Don't:** Spam groups with promotional content. They'll remove you.
**Do:** Share value posts only. Save the pitch for DMs.

## Cross-Platform Amplification

Each LinkedIn post can feed growth on other platforms:

**LinkedIn post → Twitter/X thread:**
- Expand the best insight into a 5-7 tweet thread
- Link to the LinkedIn post in tweet 7: "Full breakdown on LinkedIn (link in bio)"
- Twitter audience visits LinkedIn, follows you there

**LinkedIn post → Newsletter:**
- Include 1-2 LinkedIn posts per issue as "expanded" content
- Newsletter readers often don't follow on LinkedIn, tell them to
- "If you want the full breakdown + 29 more posts like this, follow me on LinkedIn"

**LinkedIn post → Reddit:**
- Adapt tutorial posts into Reddit value posts in relevant subreddits
- No self-promotion, just value
- Include LinkedIn profile link in your Reddit bio (not in posts)

## The 90-Day Quick Start

**Days 1-7:**
- Complete profile optimization
- Write and schedule 10 posts (2 weeks of content)
- Connect with 100 people in your niche (personalize each request)
- Identify 30 target prospects and turn on notifications

**Days 8-30:**
- Post 5x/week consistently
- Comment 10-15x/day on target prospect posts
- Reply to every comment on your posts within 2 hours
- Track: impressions, comments, profile views in weekly Notion log

**Days 31-60:**
- Review top 3 posts. Identify the pattern.
- Double down on that format for posts 31-60
- Begin Phase 3 DM strategy for first 10 prospects
- Add featured lead magnet to profile

**Days 61-90:**
- First 2-3 discovery calls should be booked
- First client should be closed (or close)
- Launch CTA post ("DM me X to get Y")
- Review: what content pillar drove the most DMs? Double it.

**90-day target:** 300-600 followers, 1-2 clients closed, $1,500-3,600 revenue attributed to LinkedIn.
