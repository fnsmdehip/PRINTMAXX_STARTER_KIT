# C06 Podcast Factory — Monetization

## Revenue Model Overview

The podcast is not a standalone revenue source. It's a trust-building machine that feeds every other revenue stream. The money comes from:

1. Affiliate mentions (immediate, no minimum downloads)
2. Sponsors (at 500+ downloads/episode)
3. Premium content upsell (Patreon, Apple Subscriptions)
4. Consulting/service CTA (any audience size)
5. Product sales (courses, templates, tools)
6. Transcript blog posts → SEO traffic → product sales

---

## Stream 1: Affiliate Mentions

### How It Works
Every episode naturally mentions tools. Every tool mention is an affiliate link in show notes.

Structure the mention naturally:
- BAD: "Check out our sponsor Instantly.ai"
- GOOD: "[Guest] mentioned they use Instantly.ai for email sending. I've used it for 6 months — link in show notes. $47/month."

## Stream 2: Sponsor Deals

### When to Pursue Sponsors
Start pitching sponsors at 500 downloads/episode. Most sponsors use CPM pricing (cost per 1,000 downloads).

### CPM Rate Guide by Niche

| Niche | CPM Rate | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Business / Entrepreneurship | $20-40 | Standard |
| SaaS / Tech tools | $25-50 | Higher intent |
| Finance / Investing | $30-60 | Highest CPM category |
| General business | $15-25 | Broader audience |

**Formula:** Downloads × CPM / 1,000 = Revenue per episode per sponsor

At 1,000 downloads/episode and $30 CPM:
- 1 sponsor × $30 CPM = $30/episode
- 2 episodes/week × 4 weeks = $240/month
- 2 sponsor slots = $480/month

At 5,000 downloads/episode and $40 CPM:
- 2 sponsor slots × $40 × 5 = $400/episode
- $400 × 8 episodes/month = $3,200/month

### Sponsor Slot Types

| Slot | Position | Length | Premium |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| Pre-roll | First 60 seconds | 30-60 sec | +20% premium |
| Mid-roll | Minute 10-12 | 60-90 sec | Standard rate |
| Post-roll | Final 2 minutes | 30-60 sec | -20% discount |

**Best performing: mid-roll.** Listeners who reach minute 10 are highly engaged.

### How to Find Sponsors

**Step 1 — Inbound first:**
Add this to show notes on every episode:
"Sponsor this show: if you sell to solopreneurs, indie hackers, or online business builders, email [contact]. 500+ downloads/episode."

**Step 2 — Outbound targeting:**
Target SaaS companies with affiliate programs (they already understand podcast ROI). Find email via:
- LinkedIn: search "[company] head of marketing" or "podcast partnerships"
- Company website contact page
- Twitter/X DM

**Pitch email template:**
```
Subject: podcast sponsorship — [Show Name], [X] downloads/episode

Hi [Name],

I run The Printmaxx Podcast — [X] downloads/episode, audience is solopreneurs and online builders. I've used [their tool] for [X months] and mentioned it in [number] episodes.

Would you be open to a paid sponsorship? I'm offering mid-roll at $[rate]/episode. Happy to share listener stats and past episode examples.

Worth a quick call?

[Your name]
```

### Sponsor Tier Pricing (Template)

| Package | Inclusions | Price |

| Silver | 1 mid-roll + pre-roll, show notes + social post | $150/episode |

## Stream 3: Premium Content (Patreon / Apple Subscriptions)

### Patreon Setup

**Tiers:**
- $5/month "Supporter" — Ad-free episodes, 1-week early access
- $15/month "Builder" — Ad-free + bonus Q&A episodes (1 extra/month) + full transcripts
- $49/month "Operator" — Everything + monthly group call with host

**Revenue target at each milestone:**
| Supporters | Monthly Revenue |
|-----------|----------------|
| 20 at $5 | $100 |
| 50 at average $10 | $500 |
| 100 at average $12 | $1,200 |
| 500 at average $12 | $6,000 |

**Conversion rate from listeners to Patreon:** 0.5-2% (podcast industry average)
At 2,000 downloads/episode, expect 10-40 Patreon backers.

### Apple Podcasts Subscriptions
- Set up at podcastsconnect.apple.com
- Apple takes 30% first year, 15% ongoing
- Gate bonus episodes (Q&As, extended interviews) behind $2.99-$9.99/month paywall
- Activates automatically for Apple users without additional friction

## Stream 4: Consulting / Service CTA

Every episode is a 25-minute advertisement for your expertise. At the end of every episode:

**CTA script:**
"If you want help implementing any of this — whether it's the cold email setup, the content system, or figuring out which revenue stream makes sense for your situation — you can book a 30-minute strategy call. Link in show notes."

**Pricing for calls:**
- $97 — first call (discovery, low-friction entry)
- $297 — follow-up implementation call
- $1,500/month — ongoing advisory (1 call/week)

**Conversion rate:** 1-3% of listeners who hear CTA will click. 10-20% of those will book.

At 1,000 downloads/episode × 2% CTA click × 15% booking rate = 3 calls/month = $291-$891/month from calls alone.

## Stream 5: Transcript Blog Posts (SEO Revenue)

Every episode transcript becomes a blog post. Blog posts rank in Google. Google traffic buys products.

**Blog post monetization:**
- Display ads (Mediavine at 50K pageviews/month, AdThrive at 100K): $15-40 RPM
- Affiliate links embedded in text: same programs as above
- Email list capture with lead magnet: builds list for product launches

**SEO timeline:**
- Month 3-6: posts start indexing, 100-500 monthly organic visits
- Month 6-12: 1,000-5,000 monthly organic visits
- Month 12-18: 10,000+ monthly organic visits if consistent

At 10,000 visits/month:
- Display ads: $150-400/month
- Affiliate: $200-500/month
- List signups: 50-100/month → product sales
