# C08 Pinterest, Board Strategy (10 Boards, SEO-Optimized)

## Pinterest SEO Fundamentals

Pinterest is a visual search engine, not social media. Boards rank on:
1. Board title (exact keyword match)
2. Board description (keyword density)
3. Pin quality and engagement rate
4. Domain authority of linked site
5. Pin freshness (active boards rank higher)

Target: 10 active boards, 50+ pins per board, 5-10 new pins/day total.
