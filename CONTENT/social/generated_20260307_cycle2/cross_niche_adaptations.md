## FITNESS NICHE (@fitness related)

### Post, Fitness App Pricing Intel
fitness apps that charge per month are losing to apps that charge per result.

credit-based pricing grew 126% yoy across all SaaS. fitness is no exception.

instead of "$14.99/month for workout plans," the winners are doing "$2 per custom program generated" or "free until you hit your first goal, then $X to keep going."

animated paywalls convert 2.9x better than static ones. if your paywall is a wall of text with a subscribe button, you're leaving money on the table.
