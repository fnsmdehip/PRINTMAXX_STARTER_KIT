## Production Notes

**Filming setup:**
- Screen recordings: QuickTime or OBS
- Talking head: iPhone front camera, natural light, clean background
- Text overlays: CapCut (add 2-3% film grain for authenticity per ALPHA15408)
- Captions: Auto-generate in CapCut, edit for accuracy

**Cross-posting schedule (stagger 24-48h):**
1. TikTok first (original upload)
2. Instagram Reels 24h later (remove TikTok watermark with SnapTik or SaveTok)
3. YouTube Shorts 48h later
4. Twitter video clip (shorter version, 15-30s)

**Hashtags (3-5 per post):**
- #buildinpublic #indiehacker #aiautomation #solopreneur #sidehustle
- Rotate: #techtok #learnontiktok #entrepreneurlife #passiveincome #automation

**Posting times (new account warmup):**
- Week 1: NO posts (consume only, 30-50 videos/day)
- Week 2-3: 1 video every other day, 7-9 PM local
- Week 4+: 1 video/day, 7-9 PM
- Week 6+: 1-2 videos/day

**Engagement strategy:**
- Comment on 10-15 similar creators' videos daily
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Duet/stitch trending videos in niche
- Never ask for follows in first 10 videos
