THREAD: deployed 30 free websites for local businesses yesterday. zero ad spend. 3-5x close rate vs traditional proposals.

1/ found businesses without websites (or with terrible ones) using free data. yelp listings, google maps, secretary of state databases.

2/ built them free preview sites. each one takes ~10 minutes with templates. mobile-friendly, fast, has their real phone and address.

3/ deployed each site to a free host so they can actually click and see it.

4/ cold email or call: "i built your business a free website. take a look: [link]. if you like it, $500 one-time to put it on your domain."

5/ the psychology: you're not pitching. you're showing. most cold emails say "i can build you a website." mine says "i already built you one."

6/ close rates on "already built it" outreach are 3-5x higher than traditional proposals. because seeing > imagining.

7/ today i deployed 30 sites in Louisville KY. tree services, landscaping, junk removal, pressure washing, window cleaning.

8/ total cost: $0. free hosting. free templates. free business data. only cost is time.

9/ even at 3% close rate that's 1 client at $500-1500. from one afternoon of work.

10/ now i repeat this in every city. the templates scale. the outreach scales. the sites take 10 min each.

this is what agency work looks like when you stop overthinking and start shipping.
