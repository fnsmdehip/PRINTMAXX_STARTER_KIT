THREAD:
Cold Email Reality Check 2026

Everything changed. Here's what actually works:

58% of replies come from your FIRST email
Not your sequence. Not your follow-up. Your first message.
Implication: Spend 2 hours on email #1. Save 30 minutes for email #3.

Engagement depth > open rates
Gmail now warns users when they're tracked. Pixel tracking is dead.
New metric: Did they click? Did they engage? Inbox placement shifted from opens to interaction depth.

Intent-based timing = 2-4x multiplier
Send when your prospect is actively researching.
Use: SEMrush, ZoomInfo intent signals, keyword alerts.
Timing beats personalization. Personalization beats volume.

Step 1 email = entire strategy
Make it:
- Specific problem statement (not generic pain)
- Relevant insight or data point
- Clear next step (not "let's chat")

The AI/human split: 80/20
AI: Research, data enrichment, personalization at scale
Human: Strategy, relationship, decision-making
Letting AI write 100% = generic. Letting humans research 100% = slow.

The cold email game in 2026 isn't about outreach. It's about precision.
