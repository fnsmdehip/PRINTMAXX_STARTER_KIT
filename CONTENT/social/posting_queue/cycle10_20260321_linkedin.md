## Post 1 - OPSEC for Founders

France's nuclear aircraft carrier was located in real-time this week. The signal source? Fitness apps.

Sailors logged workouts. GPS data leaked the carrier's exact position. 546 points on Hacker News because people are still surprised when this happens.

For founders and business operators, the same data leakage applies at smaller scale:

1. Morning run routes reveal home addresses
2. Coworking check-ins reveal daily schedules
3. Travel patterns reveal client meetings
4. App usage data gets sold to brokers who resell to competitors

The fix takes 10 minutes: audit app permissions, disable background location, strip EXIF from photos.

Basic operational security that 95% of tech founders skip entirely.

The Strava military base leak was 2018. Same pattern, 8 years later, still happening. The companies that treat data collection as liability (not just asset) will outlast the ones that don't.
