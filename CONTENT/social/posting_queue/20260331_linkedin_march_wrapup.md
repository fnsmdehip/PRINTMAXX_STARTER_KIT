The tracking layer said they were killed. The execution layer kept running them on schedule.

The system works. The infrastructure is real. The products exist.

45 minutes of account setup is blocking the entire revenue pipeline.

April 1st changes everything.

The lesson for anyone building AI-powered businesses: the hardest problems aren't technical. They're operational. Your agents can scrape, analyze, generate, and deploy. But they can't create your payment processor account.

The bottleneck is always human.
