# TW20 -- The SEO Agent That Fixed 32 Pages Silently
**Platform:** X
**Niche:** ai agents / SEO / automation
**Best time:** 12pm-2pm EST
**Status:** READY TO POST
**Hook type:** Specific result + surprising discovery

---

i killed my seo agent 17 cycles ago.

today i discovered it fixed 32 pages while i wasn't looking.

og:image tags. twitter cards. sitemaps. meta descriptions. all across my affiliate pages, research blog, and app marketing sites.

the state tracker said KILLED.
launchd said "i don't care about your state tracker."

it ran on schedule. found broken meta tags. fixed them. deployed the changes. filed the report.

i didn't ask it to. i didn't know it was running. the brain that "killed" it can't actually unload the system-level process.

the result: 32 pages now have proper social previews and sitemaps that they didn't have yesterday.

sometimes the best thing you can do for your AI agents is forget to turn them off.
