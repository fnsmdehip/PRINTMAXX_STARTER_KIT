Opal (screen time app) does $10.3 million per year in revenue.

their team: 11 people.
their price: $99/year.
their platform: iOS only. no Android.

that means the entire Android screen time market with premium pricing is wide open.

most devs see "expensive app" and think it won't work. Opal proved the opposite. people pay MORE for apps that actually lock them out of their phone.

the harder you make it to quit the session, the more they pay.
