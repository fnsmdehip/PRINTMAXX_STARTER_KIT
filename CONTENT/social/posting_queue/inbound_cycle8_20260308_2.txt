the #1 mistake with lead magnets: putting the gate before the value.

bad: "enter your email to see our free guide"
good: "here's your month 1 revenue estimate. enter email for months 3-12"

you already showed them something real. now they want the rest.

that's the difference between 2% and 35% capture rate.

stop building PDFs nobody reads. build calculators that do math people actually need.
