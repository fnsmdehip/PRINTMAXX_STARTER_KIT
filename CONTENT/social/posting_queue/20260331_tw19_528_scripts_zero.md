i've written 528 automation scripts in 56 days.

lead scrapers. content generators. SEO fixers. alpha scanners. competitive intel. email formatters. app builders. site deployers.

$0 in revenue.

here's what i learned the hard way:

building systems that build systems is addictive. you feel productive. agents running. crons firing. logs growing.

but there's a difference between building and selling.

my scripts scrape 1.4M leads. my agents generate 1,300+ posts. my pipeline qualifies 17,000 hot prospects.

none of that matters until someone pays for something.

april: i stop building and start selling. the machine works. the business doesn't. time to fix the only part that actually matters.
