THREAD: 1 year. 6 products. $12k total. honest breakdown of what worked.

most people quit after product 1 flops. the game is portfolio theory.

---

product 1: total flop. $0. built what i wanted, not what people searched for.
product 2: $800 total. found a real pain point but execution was mid.
product 3: first monthly revenue. $200/mo. nothing sexy, just solved one specific problem.

---

product 4: $400/mo. took what worked from product 3 and applied it to adjacent niche.
product 5: $1.2k/mo. this is where compounding kicked in. reused 60% of product 3's code.
product 6: $2k/mo and growing. everything i learned from 1-5 baked in from day 1.

---

the pattern nobody talks about:

products 1-2 = tuition. you're paying to learn what works.
products 3-4 = signal finding. you found the vein.
products 5-6 = compounding. each new product takes 50% less time and converts 2x better.

---

a 17 year old built a greeting card maker that hit 100k monthly visitors in 3 weeks.

no connections. no audience. no budget.

just solved a specific problem people actually googled.

the bar is on the floor. stop overthinking. ship product 1 and start learning.

---

my stack for all 6: next.js + vercel + stripe + one landing page per product.

total monthly cost: $20.
total monthly revenue: $3.8k.

the math works if you survive the first 2 flops.
