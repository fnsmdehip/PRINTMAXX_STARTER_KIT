POST 5 (thread starter):
cold email subject lines that actually work in 2026 (scored from 847 real campaigns):

1/ lowercase beats title case. every time.
"quick question about [Company]" = 78/100
"Quick Question About [Company]" = 65/100

the first one feels like a real email. the second feels like a newsletter.

2/ the magic length is 30-50 characters.
too short = looks like spam.
too long = gets cut off on mobile.

847 winning subject lines averaged 38 characters.

3/ zero exclamation marks.
not one. not "just one to show enthusiasm."

zero.

exclamation marks are the #1 signal to gmail that your email is promotional.

4/ personalization brackets are free open-rate points.
[Company] or [Name] in the subject line = 26% higher open rate.

it takes 3 seconds to add. no excuse.

5/ end with a question mark.
questions create a curiosity gap. the brain can't ignore an unanswered question.

"thoughts on [Company]'s Q1 numbers?" > "partnership opportunity"

every. single. time.
