I tested 7 cold email tools side by side.

here's which ones actually land in the inbox (and which ones are overpriced garbage):

Instantly: $30/mo. cleanest UI. 160M+ lead database built in. if you're starting out, this is the one.

Smartlead: $39/mo. unlimited mailboxes. best deliverability toolkit. agencies and high-volume senders use this.

Lemlist: $39/mo. image and video personalization. if you're doing high-ticket ($5k+ deals), this doubles reply rates.

Saleshandy: $25/mo. cheapest option that's actually usable. unlimited accounts.

Apollo: $49/mo but has a free tier. 275M+ contacts. prospecting + outreach in one tool.

the rest (Woodpecker, Reply.io) are niche. good for specific use cases but not the move for most people.
