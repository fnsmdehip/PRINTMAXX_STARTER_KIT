cold email not booking calls? it's one of these 4 things:

1. wrong list. you're emailing people who don't have the problem you solve. fix targeting before touching copy.
2. bad subject line. under 5 words. no caps. looks like a reply, not a pitch.
3. too long. 50-75 words max. nobody reads paragraph 3 of a cold email. nobody.
4. weak CTA. "would love to chat" is weak. "can I send you a 2-min video showing exactly how this works for [company]?" is specific.

diagnose before you rewrite. most people rewrite when they should re-target.
