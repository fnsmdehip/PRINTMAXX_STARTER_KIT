TITLE: I compared every major cold email tool on the market. Here's my honest take after actually using them.

---

Most "comparison" posts are written by people who read the tool's own marketing copy and called it research. I spent 3 weeks actually testing cold email infrastructure.

Here's what I found:

**The tools I tested:**
- Instantly
- Lemlist
- Smartlead
- Apollo
- Woodpecker
- Hunter.io
- Mailshake

**The methodology:**

I ran each through 6 criteria:
1. Deliverability out of the box (not after 4 hours of warmup setup)
2. Reply rate on the same sequence, same offer, same target list
3. UI friction, how long does it take a new user to send their first campaign
4. Support response time (I submitted tickets to all 7)
5. Pricing at scale (50k contacts, 500k emails/mo)
6. Exit cost, how painful is it to leave

**What I actually found:**

Instantly is the best pure-volume tool right now. Clean UI, solid deliverability, $37/mo entry price. If you're an agency blasting cold email at scale, this is probably your answer.

Lemlist wins on personalization. The dynamic image inserts and liquid syntax variables get real replies in founder-to-founder outreach. Costs more ($59/mo). Worth it if your offer justifies one-to-one energy.

Smartlead is Instantly's closest competitor. Better inbox rotation controls. The pricing structure gets complicated fast at scale. Good option if you've already maxed out Instantly's deliverability.

Apollo is a database tool first, outreach tool second. The contact data is genuinely good. The sequencing is mediocre. Most power users export from Apollo and send through Instantly.

Woodpecker is expensive for what it is. The interface hasn't changed in 3 years. Hard to recommend at their current pricing.

**The honest answer on deliverability:**

No tool fixes a bad domain setup. If you haven't warmed your domains, haven't set up DMARC/DKIM/SPF correctly, and haven't segmented your inboxes properly, the tool doesn't matter.

Most people blaming Instantly for poor deliverability are actually blaming their own infrastructure.

**If you're starting from zero:**

Instantly for volume. Lemlist for personalization. Apollo for data, then export.

Full breakdown with pricing tables and side-by-side feature comparisons here:
https://best-cold-email-tools.surge.sh
