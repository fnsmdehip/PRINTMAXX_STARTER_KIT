cold email in 2026 is completely different from 2025.

warmup went from 7-14 days to 14-21 days.
first name tokens are now a spam flag.
gmail warns users about tracking pixels.
over-polished copy triggers AI detection.
58% of replies come from email 1 (not email 5).

the move: write like a real person. short. no tracking pixel. no "Hi {first_name}". reference something specific about their business.

one dentist's site took 9.6 seconds to load. that's the opener. not "i help businesses grow."
