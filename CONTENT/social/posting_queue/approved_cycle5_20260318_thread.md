### 1/7 (Hook)

cold email in 2026 is a completely different game than 2024. I analyzed every major deliverability report from Instantly, Saleshandy, Mailshake, and Smartlead. here's everything that changed and exactly what to do about it.

### 2/7, The Tracking Pixel Death

gmail now actively warns users about emails with tracking pixels. the little "this email contains a tracking image" badge tanks trust instantly.

fix: disable open tracking entirely. measure replies, not opens. opens were always a vanity metric anyway.

### 3/7, Personalization That Actually Works

"{FirstName}" tokens now trigger spam filters. every spam email in history used first name personalization. the filters caught on.

what works: business context personalization. reference their specific product, a recent blog post, a funding round, a job listing. show you actually looked at their company. 4 sentences max.

### 4/7, The First Email Rule

Saleshandy data: 58% of all cold email replies come from the first email. not the second. not the fifth follow-up.

meaning: if your first email doesn't land, your 7-email sequence is just annoying 7 times instead of once.

spend 80% of your time on email #1. the rest is just gentle reminders.

### 5/7, Casual Beats Perfect

overly polished emails get flagged as AI-generated. perfect grammar, structured bullet points, clear CTAs. that's what spam looks like now.

what works: casual, slightly messy, sounds like a real person texting a colleague. lowercase. short. one question. no formatting.

the irony: AI detection works by looking for "too good" writing. be worse on purpose.

### 6/7, Infrastructure Changes

warmup timeline extended from 7-14 days to 14-21 days. plan accordingly.

subdomain is mandatory. never cold email from your main domain. protect it.

volume consistency matters more than volume size. 50 emails/day every day beats 200 on Monday then 10 on Thursday.

intent-based timing (sending when prospects are actively researching) gets 2-4x reply rates vs random sends.

### 7/7, The Stack

$200/month total stack:
- instantly.ai or smartlead for sending ($97/mo)
- mailforge for bulk warmup ($3/inbox)
- clay for intent data enrichment ($50/mo)
- subdomain on your main domain ($0)

58% of replies from email #1. casual tone. no tracking pixels. business context not first names. consistent daily volume.

cold email isn't dead. 2024 cold email is dead. adapt or get filtered.
