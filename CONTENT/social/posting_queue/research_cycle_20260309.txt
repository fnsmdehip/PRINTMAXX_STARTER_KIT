THREAD (5 tweets) - cold email playbook
=============================================================

[THREAD 1/5]

cold email did 80% of the heavy lifting for a solo consultant who crossed 6 figures.

not ads. not content. not a personal brand.

2000 emails over 3 months. woodpecker. done.

here's the actual breakdown of how to run this without guessing:

[THREAD 2/5]

step 1: pick a list you can actually verify.

not a scraped data dump. not a bought list from some marketplace with 40% bounce rate.

apollo.io, hunter.io, or manual LinkedIn pulls filtered by title and company size.

400-500 contacts per month is enough to get real signal. quality beats volume at this stage.

[THREAD 3/5]

step 2: one email. one ask.

the people getting 2-4% reply rates are not writing better copy.

they're asking for less.

"15 minutes to see if this fits" beats "hop on a 30-minute strategy call to discuss synergies" every time.

woodpecker handles sequencing. 3-touch max. if they don't reply by touch 3, move on.

[THREAD 4/5]

step 3: track what's killing you before you optimize.

open rate below 30% = subject line problem.
reply rate below 2% = offer or ICP problem.
reply rate above 2% but no closes = call problem.

most ppl optimize subject lines when the real issue is they're emailing the wrong people.

2000 emails over 3 months is about 22 emails per day. that's 45 minutes of work.

[THREAD 5/5]

the math:

2000 emails. 2% reply rate = 40 conversations. 15% close rate = 6 clients.

if your average deal is $5k, that's $30k from one 90-day cold email run.

the consultant in this thread hit 6 figures because they ran this cycle more than once.

just send the emails.
