THREAD: the $0/mo solopreneur stack (that actually works)

1/ I audited every tool I use as a solopreneur.

33 paid tools. mapped each one to a free or cheaper alternative.

total savings: $400-800/mo for most people.

here's what I actually use at $0/mo:

2/ EMAIL MARKETING
paid: Mailchimp ($20), ConvertKit ($29), Beehiiv Scale ($49)
free: Beehiiv free tier. 2,500 subs. monetization built in.

most solopreneurs don't need a paid email tool until 2,500+ subscribers.

3/ SEO
paid: Ahrefs ($99), Semrush ($130)
free: Google Search Console + Ubersuggest free tier

GSC shows exactly what queries you rank for. Ubersuggest shows keyword difficulty. that's 80% of what you need starting out.

4/ DESIGN
paid: Canva Pro ($13), Figma ($15)
free: Canva free tier + Figma free (3 projects)

Figma free handles UI design. Canva free handles social graphics. you don't need pro features until you're doing client work at scale.

5/ WEBSITE + HOSTING
paid: Webflow ($18), Squarespace ($16), Vercel Pro ($20)
free: Surge.sh (static HTML), Vercel Hobby, Carrd free

static HTML on free hosting loads faster than any page builder. I host 20+ sites for $0/mo total.

6/ AUTOMATION
paid: Zapier ($20), Make ($9)
free: n8n self-hosted, or just write a Python script

most "automations" are just if-this-then-that logic. a 15-line Python script replaces a $20/mo Zapier plan.

7/ AI TOOLS
paid: ChatGPT Plus ($20), Jasper ($49), Cursor Pro ($20)
free: Claude Max covers coding + writing + analysis in one subscription.

one $20/mo AI tool > three separate $20-49/mo AI tools.

8/ the full audit tool is free: saas-stack-audit.surge.sh

add your actual tools. see your real burn. get free alternatives for each one.

took 60 seconds to build. saves $200-800/mo.

that's the whole point: spend less on tools, keep more of what you earn.
