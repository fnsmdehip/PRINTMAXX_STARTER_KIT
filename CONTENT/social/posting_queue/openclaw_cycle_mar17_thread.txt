THREAD: how I automated cold outreach with free website demos

1/ most cold emails for web design say "I noticed your website could use some work. let me know if you'd like to chat."

delete rate: 100%.

here's what actually works.

2/ I built a script that:
- searches DuckDuckGo for "{service} in {city}"
- visits every result
- grades the website A through F
- auto-generates a modern preview for F-grade sites
- deploys to a live URL
- queues a cold email with the link

the whole pipeline runs every 4 hours.

3/ the email is simple:

"I was looking for plumbers in Tampa and noticed your site could use a refresh. I went ahead and built a free preview: [link]. no strings attached. if you like it, one-time fee to put it on your domain."

that's it. no pitch deck. no discovery call CTA.

4/ grading criteria:
- no SSL = instant F
- no viewport meta tag = F
- "under construction" = F
- table-based layout = D
- parked GoDaddy domain = F

these businesses are PAYING for Google Ads and sending traffic to broken sites.

5/ current stats:
- 556 leads across 42 US cities
- 189 outreach emails queued
- 16 new preview sites deployed today
- ~20 seconds to generate and deploy each site

the sites are real. mobile-friendly. live URLs. not mockups.

6/ pricing: $500-2000 one-time to put it on their domain. no monthly fees, no contracts.

at even a 2% close rate on 189 emails, that's 3-4 clients at $500-2000 each.

the script cost me nothing to run. surge.sh is free. DuckDuckGo is free.

7/ biggest lesson: delivered value > promised value.

"I already built this for you" converts 3-5x better than "I could build something for you."

stop asking for permission. just build the thing and show them.
