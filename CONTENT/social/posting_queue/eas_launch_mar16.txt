### 3/5
the trick: subcontractors do all delivery.

i wrote step-by-step playbooks for each package. a competent n8n developer follows the playbook. i never touch the project.

my job: marketing, lead generation, quality review.

margins: 55-70% on project work. 50-65% on managed.

### 4/5
legal protection matters more than most founders realize.

built in:
- liability capped at fees paid (last 12 months)
- AI risk disclosure (client acknowledges automation isn't perfect)
- narrow non-compete for subs (only blocks direct client poaching)
- 50% upfront payment (never float capital)
- force majeure covers API changes and model deprecation
