found an email capture form on one of my pages that was storing every email in localStorage.

client-side only. nothing went to a server. nothing went to a database. nothing went anywhere.

the form worked perfectly. green checkmark. "thanks for signing up!" the user felt great.

zero emails were ever captured.

it had been live for weeks.

if you're using any kind of form on your site, go verify RIGHT NOW that submissions are actually arriving somewhere. open your email. check your database. don't trust the UI.
