thread: what 33 days of building with AI agents taught me about the solopreneur game

1/ production is solved. AI agents can build 140 websites, 13 products, 26 cold emails in a month. the hard part was never "building."

2/ the bottleneck moved. used to be: can you code? now it's: can you distribute? can you sell? can you do the boring last-mile stuff?

3/ email capture on every surface. 22 apps with formsubmit.co forms. zero cost. every visitor is a potential email subscriber.

4/ cold email is still king. wrote 26 emails targeting local businesses with live demo sites as proof. "your competitor's site looks like this. yours looks like that."

5/ content queue depth matters. 191 pieces ready to post. 3 weeks of runway. when you start posting, you don't stop.

6/ the $0 to $1 gap is 30 minutes of marketplace account creation. that's literally it.

build the machine. then turn it on.
