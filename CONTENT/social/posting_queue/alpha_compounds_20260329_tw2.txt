TWEET 2 - Method Replication (Engagement Bait)
============================================================

The "I pivoted 6 times before it worked" post gets 15K-40K engagement.

Pattern:
- Lead with failure
- Specific pivot count
- Imply there's a method underneath

Repurpose angle: Share your 3 worst attempts + 1 win. People upvote struggle narratives.

Works for: apps, content, outbound, products.
