2/
Option 1: MailerLite

- Free up to 1K subscribers, 12K emails/mo
- Drag-and-drop builder that does not suck
- Automations: basic but solid for most solopreneurs
- What it lacks: advanced segmentation, SMS, e-commerce integrations at scale

Best for: newsletters, course creators, early-stage SaaS

3/
Option 2: ConvertKit (now Kit)

- $25/mo for 1K subscribers
- Visual automation builder is genuinely good
- Creator-first: landing pages, tip jars, paid newsletters all built in
- 30-day free trial, 30% affiliate commission (lifelong)

Best for: creators, coaches, info product sellers

4/
Option 3: Beehiiv

- $42/mo for up to 2.5K subscribers (Scale plan)
- Built-in paid newsletter + ad network (you get paid to run ads in your newsletter)
- Growth tools: referral programs, boosts, subscriber analytics
- 20% affiliate commission, 30-day cookie

Best for: newsletter operators who want a paid tier

5/
The migration from Klaviyo takes about 2 hours:

- Export your list from Klaviyo (CSV)
- Import into new platform
- Warm up: send to engaged subscribers first (opens in last 90 days)
- Don't send to the whole list day 1 — spam filters will kill you
