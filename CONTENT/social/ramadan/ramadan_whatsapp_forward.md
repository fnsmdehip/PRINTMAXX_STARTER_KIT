## Sharing Strategy

### Who to send to:

1. **Family group chats** - Use Version 3 (bilingual) for mixed-language families
2. **Masjid/community group chats** - Use Version 1 (English) or Version 2 (Arabic) based on group language
3. **Friends individually** - Use Version 5 (personal recommendation)
4. **Large broadcast lists** - Use Version 4 (short forward)
5. **Islamic study groups** - Use Version 1 with a personal note about the dua collection

### Timing:

- **First wave (Feb 14-16):** Send to close family and friends. Get genuine feedback.
- **Second wave (Feb 17-21):** Send to wider community group chats. Include any updates based on feedback.
- **Third wave (Feb 22-27):** Send to all remaining contacts. This is peak Ramadan prep time.
- **Ramadan Day 1 (Feb 28/Mar 1):** Final push. "Ramadan Mubarak! Here's the fasting tracker I mentioned."

### WhatsApp Status Updates:

Post to WhatsApp Status (Story) at these key moments:
- Feb 15: Screenshot of the app with "Building something for Ramadan"
- Feb 20: "10 days to Ramadan - Hilal fasting tracker is ready"
- Feb 27: "Ramadan starts tomorrow insha'Allah - link in bio"
- Ramadan Day 1: "Ramadan Mubarak! Tracking my fast with Hilal"
- Ramadan Day 15: "Halfway through! Quran progress on track"
- Last 10 nights: "Maximizing the last 10 nights with Hilal's tracker"
