## If a Creator Says Yes

1. Send them a clean feature summary (3-5 bullet points, not a wall of text)
2. Offer custom screenshots sized for their platform
3. Ask if they want a specific link to track shares (UTM parameter)
4. Let them write their own copy. Authenticity beats scripted endorsement.
5. Thank them publicly after they post (comment, reshare, story mention)
6. Send a sincere thank-you DM after Ramadan ends

## If a Creator Says No

1. Thank them for their time
2. Wish them Ramadan Mubarak
3. Do not follow up again
4. Do not take it personally
5. Move on to the next creator
