## Rules to Avoid Getting Flagged

1. Never copy-paste the exact same post across groups
2. Vary the opening line each time
3. Engage in the group for 2-3 days before posting your content
4. Comment on at least 3 other posts in the group before sharing yours
5. If a group has "no self-promotion" rules, share as a question: "Has anyone tried any good Ramadan trackers this year? I built one called Hilal and would love feedback."
6. Reply to every comment on your posts within 4 hours
7. If a post gets deleted by admins, do not repost. Move on.
8. Thank admins if they approve your post
9. Never argue in comments. If someone criticizes, respond with grace.
10. If a group requires admin approval, DM the admin with a short personal note explaining the app
