# Product Hunt Launch Playbook — N53

**Goal:** Top 5 Product of the Day on launch day. This drives: 500-2,000 signups in 24 hours, domain authority backlinks, social proof ("featured on Product Hunt"), press pickup potential.
**Timeline:** 3 weeks pre-launch + launch day + 1 week post
**Cost:** $0 (organic) to $50 (optional PR wire for press pickup)

---

## Pre-Launch (3 Weeks Before)

### Week 1: Foundation

**Day 1-3: Build your PH account**
- If you don't have a PH account: create one, complete profile (photo, bio, social links)
- Engage genuinely: upvote 5-10 products per day, leave thoughtful comments
- PH algorithm weights: account age + engagement history. A 6-month-old account that's engaged outperforms a fresh account that just upvotes on launch day.

**Day 4-7: Find a hunter**
- A "hunter" is someone who submits your product for you. Top hunters have massive follower counts on PH and their submissions get extra visibility.
- Finding hunters: check producthunt.com/leaderboard/hunters for top hunters
- DM top hunters with your product description. Offer early access or a personal demo.
- Most hunters are happy to do it if the product is genuine. They benefit from discovering quality products.
- If you can't find a hunter: self-submit is fine. The hunter advantage is smaller than it used to be.

**Day 7: Build your launch email list**
- Collect emails from beta users, newsletter subscribers, social followers
- Everyone who agrees to be notified will get a personal "it's live, please upvote" message on launch day
- Target: 200+ people who have agreed to upvote in advance

---

### Week 2: Asset Preparation

**Launch listing requirements:**
- Product name (max 60 chars)
- Tagline (max 60 chars — the most important copy you'll write)
- Description (max 260 chars — appears in feed)
- Thumbnail (240x240px)
- Gallery images (1270x952px, up to 8 images)
- Optional: 30-60 second video (autoplay in gallery)
- Topics: select 5 relevant topics

**Tagline formula:**
"[Verb] [specific outcome] without [friction]"

Examples:
- "Track habits without opening another app"
- "Send cold emails that actually get replies"
- "Find profitable niches before they trend"

**Gallery image structure:**
1. Hero: product name + tagline + clean screenshot
2. Problem: the specific pain point (with copy-style.md voice)
3. Solution: how your product solves it
4. Key feature 1 (with screenshot)
5. Key feature 2 (with screenshot)
6. Social proof: testimonials, numbers, logos
7. Pricing: show the plan clearly
8. CTA: "Try free" or "Get started"

**Video (optional but 2x conversion):**
- 30-60 seconds
- First 5 seconds: show the result, not the sign-up flow
- Screen recording + ElevenLabs voiceover
- End with pricing and CTA

## Launch Day Playbook

**Best time to launch:** 12:00 AM PST (midnight) — PH day resets at midnight PST. Launching at midnight gives you a full 24 hours to accumulate votes.

**Hour 1 (12:00-1:00 AM PST): Alert your inner circle**
- Text/WhatsApp your most engaged supporters (not mass messaging)
- Post on X: "we're live. [link]. first day is everything."
- Post in Discord if you have a community
- Post LinkedIn update

**Hour 2-6: Let early votes compound**
- Monitor PH page for comments — respond to EVERY comment within 5 minutes during first 6 hours
- The maker who responds fastest gets featured in PH's notifications
- Don't ask for upvotes in PH comments (against their rules)
- Ask people to leave thoughtful comments + questions instead

**8:00 AM PST: Mass outreach**
- Send prepared emails to your full list
- Post to Reddit communities (r/SideProject, r/Entrepreneur)
- Post to Indie Hackers
- Twitter/X post #2 (launch update with first hours traction if positive)

**12:00 PM PST: Midday check**
- Where are you in the rankings?
- Top 10 by noon = on track for Top 5 by end of day
- Not top 10 yet? Send another wave of outreach to communities

**6:00 PM PST: Final push**
- X post #3: "6 hours left. [product] is at [position]. trying to crack top 5."
- Personal DMs to your 20-30 closest contacts if you haven't already
- LinkedIn post if you haven't

**11:59 PM PST: Done**
- Screenshot your final rank + metrics
- The badge ("Featured on Product Hunt", "#2 Product of the Day") is yours to use forever

## Post-Launch (Days 2-7)

**Day 2:**
- Post your results: "finished #3 on Product Hunt — 847 upvotes, 234 new signups in 24 hours"
- This result tweet often gets more engagement than the launch day tweet
- Write a "launch lessons" post for Indie Hackers (huge SEO value, drives ongoing signups)

**Day 3-7:**
- Respond to all outstanding PH comments
- Follow up with signups (automated welcome sequence)
- Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to your landing page
- Submit to: BetaList, SaaSHub, AlternativeTo, Crunchbase (free listings, free SEO)

---

## What Kills a PH Launch

- Asking for upvotes in PH comments (violates rules, gets votes removed)
- Launching on Tuesday-Thursday (best days: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday in that order — avoid weekends)
- Not responding to comments (PH surfaces engaged makers)
- Gallery images with too much text (tiny text doesn't read in feed thumbnails)
- Misleading tagline (community will call it out in comments)
- Bad landing page UX (PH traffic converts poorly if they hit a bad page)

---

## Product Positioning for Different Products

**App/SaaS:** Lead with the outcome ("Track your habits. Miss zero days.")
**Digital product:** Lead with transformation ("Cold email playbook that got me to $3K/mo")
**Service:** Less suited for PH. Better for: Indie Hackers, Reddit, X

**Best PH categories for PRINTMAXX products:**
- Productivity
- Developer Tools
- Marketing
- Artificial Intelligence
- SaaS
