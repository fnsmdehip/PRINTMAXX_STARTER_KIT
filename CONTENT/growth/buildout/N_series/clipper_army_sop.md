# Clipper Army SOP — N04

**Concept:** A network of smaller accounts that clip, repurpose, and amplify content from your main account. Extends reach without spending on ads. Each "clipper" account handles a niche or format.
**Goal:** 10x your content distribution by having 5-10 secondary accounts that push your content to new audiences.
**Setup time:** 4 hours initial + 30 min/week maintenance
**Cost:** $0 (manual) to $47/mo (semi-automated)

---

## How the Clipper Army Works

**Main account (@printmaxxer):** Posts original content — threads, insights, data-backed takes.

**Clipper accounts (5-10 accounts):** Each picks the best content from your main account and repurposes it for a specific audience. They link back, quote tweet, or adapt the content.

**Example clipper structure:**
| Clipper Account | Platform | Niche | Content Format |
|----------------|----------|-------|----------------|
| @printmaxxclips | X/Twitter | Solopreneur highlights | Quote tweets + single insights |
| @buildwithclaude | X/Twitter | AI/dev tools | Technical clips from threads |
| @selahmoments | Instagram | Faith audience | Adapted wisdom from MMO content |
| @hustlehijabi | TikTok | Muslim women entrepreneurs | Short clips + faith money angle |
| @aimoneytools | YouTube Shorts | AI tools | Screen recordings of tools mentioned |
| @printmaxxclips | Pinterest | General | Quote card pins from best tweets |

## Account Setup (Per Clipper)

**Step 1: Create the account**
- Separate email, separate device/IP (follow multi_account_warmup.md)
- Bio: clearly states it's "highlights from @[main account]" or has its own angle
- Profile photo: AI-generated or niche-appropriate stock image

**Step 2: Connect to main account publicly (optional)**
- Main account bio can say "clips: @printmaxxclips"
- Builds cross-platform audience movement

**Step 3: Content pipeline**
- Clipper accounts never create original content
- They pull from a shared content bank (Notion page or Google Doc)
- Main account posts → within 2 hours → clipper formats and reposts

## Content Repurposing Rules per Format

### X Quote Tweets (clipper on X)

1. Find main account's highest-performing tweet within 24 hours (use X Analytics)
2. Quote tweet with added context: "this is the part most people miss:" or "saved this. key insight:"
3. Add 1-2 lines of your own commentary to qualify for algorithm (not just pure RT)
4. Post within 6-12 hours of original (not so fast it looks coordinated)

**Do NOT:** Like or RT your own posts from the clipper. That's coordinated behavior. Only quote tweet with added commentary.

---

### Instagram Clips (repurposed from X threads)

1. Pick a thread from main account
2. Convert to carousel: one slide per tweet
3. Slide 1: Hook from thread (same text)
4. Slide 2-8: Rest of thread, one point per slide
5. Last slide: "Follow @printmaxxer on X for more"
6. Caption: 50-100 words, first 2 lines visible without "more" click
7. Post 6-24 hours after main account thread goes live

---

### TikTok/YouTube Shorts (repurposed from long-form content)

1. Take a specific insight from a newsletter issue or long post
2. Script a 30-45 second video covering just that one point
3. Record: AI voiceover (ElevenLabs) + screenshare or B-roll + text overlay
4. Caption references the main account or newsletter
5. Post as standalone value — not just "watch my video" promo

---

### Pinterest (quote cards from best tweets)

1. Every week: take top 5 tweets by engagement from main account
2. Design quote cards: Canva template, clean background, tweet text, "via @printmaxxer" credit
3. Post to relevant Pinterest board
4. Description: expand on the quote with 2-3 sentences + SEO keywords

## Weekly Clipper Army Workflow

**Monday (10 minutes):**
- Pull last week's top 5 posts from main account
- Queue them for clipper accounts this week

**Daily (5 minutes each clipper):**
- Post 1-2 clips per day per clipper account
- Engage with comments on clipper accounts (builds them up as standalone accounts)

**Friday (15 minutes):**
- Review clipper account analytics
- Note which clip formats drove the most back-traffic to main account
- Double down on what's working

## Scaling: Outsourcing to Real People

Once you have the format and content pipeline working, hire part-time VAs to operate clipper accounts:

**Where to hire:** Onlinejobs.ph ($3-6/hr), Fiverr (for content clipping), Contra (remote workers)

**What to train them on:**
- Content repurposing SOP (this document)
- Brand voice guidelines (copy-style.md)
- Which formats work per platform
- Daily posting schedule

**Cost at scale:** 2 hours/day × $4/hr = $8/day = $240/month for fully outsourced clipper army

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Never like/RT your own main account from clipper accounts (coordinated behavior)
- Never copy-paste identical content across platforms (algorithm penalizes duplicates)
- Never use clipper accounts to artificially inflate main account's like counts
- Keep clipper accounts to their angle — don't try to monetize clippers before they're established
