**Goal:** Establish authority content on two massive distribution platforms. Medium drives SEO traffic and new-to-you readers. Substack drives subscriber growth and monetization through its native discovery features.
**Monthly time investment:** 4-6 hours (if repurposing from existing content)
**Revenue potential:** $200-2,000/mo paid subscriptions at 3-6 months; ongoing passive SEO traffic from Medium

## Medium Strategy

### How to Get Curated (Critical)

Medium's curated articles appear in the "For You" feed and topic feeds. Non-curated articles have almost zero reach.

**Curation criteria (from Medium's stated guidelines):**
- Well-written, original content
- Not a republication of content first published elsewhere (syndication allowed with canonical tag, but original performs better)
- Not self-promotional
- Reading time 4-8 minutes (1,000-2,000 words)
- Strong headline and first paragraph

**How to signal curation-readiness:**
- Write for a human, not for SEO (Medium's curators are humans)
- First paragraph must hook — Medium shows first 3 lines in feeds
- Use Medium's native formatting (not pasted HTML)
- Add 3-5 relevant tags after publishing

**Best publications to submit to:**
- Better Programming (developers, coding — 170K followers)
- The Startup (entrepreneurs — 700K followers)
- Towards Data Science (AI/data — 400K followers)
- Mind Cafe (productivity/mindset — 200K followers)
- Level Up Coding (technical — 140K followers)

Getting into a publication = their followers see your article. A Startup article can get 10,000+ reads vs a self-published article getting 50 reads.

**How to get into publications:**
- Read their submission guidelines (each has a form or email)
- Pitch 1-2 sentence article idea before writing
- Acceptance rate: 20-40% if article is good quality

### Medium Content Calendar

**Frequency:** 4-6 articles per month

**Article types that perform on Medium:**

| Type | Example title | Expected reads |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| "How I did X" | "How I built a $1K/mo side project in 90 days" | 1,000-20,000 |
| Listicle | "7 AI tools I use every day as a solopreneur" | 500-5,000 |
| Contrarian | "Cold email is NOT dead. Here's the data." | 2,000-30,000 |
| Tutorial | "Build a cold email automation system in 3 hours" | 500-3,000 |
| Analysis | "I analyzed 200 successful indie hackers. Here's what they all did." | 5,000-50,000 |

**Medium SEO (yes, Medium ranks in Google):**
- Medium articles rank for long-tail keywords within 2-4 weeks
- Title = H1 = primary keyword
- Subtitle = H2 for secondary keyword
- First 150 words should include primary keyword naturally
- Include internal Medium links (Medium rewards keeping readers on platform)

---

### Medium Repurposing Workflow

Don't write Medium articles from scratch. Repurpose from existing content:

1. Take any newsletter issue from C05 that's 800+ words
2. Expand to 1,200-2,000 words (add more examples, data, context)
3. Remove any promotional mentions (Medium is strict on self-promotion in article body)
4. Publish as original on Medium
5. Add canonical tag pointing to your Substack version if published there first

**Note:** If you've published on your newsletter first, add a canonical tag in Medium's advanced settings pointing to your Substack URL. This tells Google the original source is your newsletter, not Medium.

### Medium Partner Program

Apply after publishing 3+ articles. Requirements:
- Account in good standing
- 100+ followers (easy to reach)

**Revenue math:**
- 10,000 reads/month × $0.02-0.04 = $200-400/month
- Curated articles can hit 50,000-200,000 reads
- 100K reads month = $2,000-4,000 one-time

**Realistic expectation at 6 months:** $50-200/month if posting consistently. Not a primary income source. Value is in distribution, not Medium Partner earnings.

## Substack Strategy

### Why Substack Over Beehiiv

You may already have a newsletter on Beehiiv. Substack is DIFFERENT in these ways:
- Substack has a social network layer (Notes, Recommendations)
- Substack readers discover new newsletters organically
- Substack Notes is like Twitter — posts go into a feed for your subscribers AND can be featured

**Strategy:** Run your MAIN newsletter on Beehiiv (better email analytics, better monetization tools). Run a Substack as a separate publication for:
- Long-form essays (the ones too personal for the MMO newsletter)
- A niche publication that feeds into your main newsletter

---

### Substack Notes (Underused Growth Hack)

Substack Notes launched in 2023. It's a Twitter-like feed, but only Substack subscribers see it.

**How to use Notes for growth:**
1. Post 3-5 Notes per day (short — 1-3 sentences + link or image)
2. Notes from large publications get "Recommended" to other subscribers
3. If your Note gets 10+ likes, it gets pushed to Notes feed broadly
4. Other writers "Restacking" your note = their subscribers see it

**Note formats that work:**
- Single insight with a link to your full article
- Opinion on a trending topic in your niche
- Behind-the-scenes of your work
- Question to your audience

**Growth from Notes:** 10-50 new subscribers per week if you post consistently and other writers engage.

---

### Substack Recommendations System

When you recommend another Substack, and they recommend you, each other's subscribers get shown the recommendation.

**How to get recommended:**
1. Recommend 5-10 newsletters in your niche (genuine ones you read)
2. Email those writers: "I just recommended your newsletter to my readers. Would love to exchange recommendations if you see fit."
3. 20-40% of writers you recommend will recommend you back
4. Each mutual recommendation can drive 5-50 new subscribers

**Tracking:** Substack shows you how many subscribers came from each recommendation source.

---

### Substack Paid Subscription Setup

**Only enable paid if you have 100+ free subscribers.** Under that, you're not ready.

**Pricing:**
- $5/month or $50/year (simplest)
- $10/month or $100/year (for higher-value content)

**What makes people pay:**
- Exclusive content they can't get elsewhere
- Early access to your work
- Private community (Discord, Telegram, Substack Chat)
- Personal access to you (ask me anything, DMs)

**Conversion rate benchmark:** 3-8% of free subscribers convert to paid. 1,000 free subscribers = 30-80 paid × $5/mo = $150-400/mo.

**Paid content to offer:**
- Full deep dives (free gets the summary, paid gets the full research)
- Exclusive case studies with revenue numbers
- Monthly strategy calls or AMAs (async voice)
- All your past issues (free subscribers only see last 3 months)
