## Server Rules (7 Rules)

1. **no promotion without permission** — don't drop your link unless someone asks or it's relevant to a thread. first offense: warning. second: kick.
2. **be specific** — "what tools do you use for X?" not "what's a good tool?" vague posts get deleted.
3. **no engagement farming** — "check my content and follow me" type posts = instant remove
4. **real numbers only** — when posting results, post real numbers. we are allergic to "i made a lot of money" posts with zero proof.
5. **add value before asking** — new members: post 3 helpful things before asking for anything
6. **no gatekeeping** — if someone asks how you did something, tell them. the alpha compounds when shared.
7. **inner circle content stays inner circle** — don't screenshot or share paid-tier content externally. violation = permanent ban.
