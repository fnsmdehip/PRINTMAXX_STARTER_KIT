# Paid Newsletter Upsell — Free to Paid Conversion Funnel

## Overview

Platform: Beehiiv (has native paid subscription built in)
Free tier: everyone who subscribes
Paid tier: $9/mo or $29/mo (two-tier model works better than one)

Goal: convert 2-5% of free subscribers to paid. at 5,000 free subs, that's 100-250 paying = $900-$7,250/mo.

---

## Tier Structure

### Free Tier (The Hook)

**What free gets:**
- Weekly newsletter every Tuesday (1,000-1,500 words)
- 1 main tactic + 3 quick finds
- Links to public content (YouTube, Twitter threads)
- Open job board / tool discounts

**What free does NOT get:**
- Deep breakdowns with real numbers
- Templates and worksheets
- Case studies with full SOPs
- Sponsor-free reading experience
- Community access

**Free tier purpose:** build the list, build trust, demonstrate value, create desire for more.

---

### Paid Tier 1: Builder — $9/mo

**Who it's for:** people who want more depth but aren't ready to commit $29

**What Builder gets (on top of free):**
- Extra Thursday issue: "The Build" — shorter, tactical, behind-the-scenes format
- Monthly worksheet or template (downloadable)
- No ads in any issue (free tier has 2 sponsor placements per issue)
- 10% discount on any PRINTMAXX product

**Revenue math:**
- At 1,000 free subs: 2% convert = 20 × $9 = $180/mo
- At 5,000 free subs: 2% convert = 100 × $9 = $900/mo
- At 10,000 free subs: 3% convert = 300 × $9 = $2,700/mo

---

### Paid Tier 2: Inner Circle — $29/mo

**Who it's for:** serious builders who want the full stack

**What Inner Circle gets:**
- Everything in Builder
- Saturday "Alpha Drop" issue: one deep tactic, real numbers, SOP-level detail
- Monthly "Revenue Report" issue: my actual numbers broken down (what made money, what didn't)
- Direct email access: reply to any issue, i respond within 48 hours
- Discord Inner Circle role
- 50% off all PRINTMAXX courses + products
- Founding member bonus: forever-locked price for life (if joining in month 1)

**Revenue math:**
- At 1,000 free subs: 1% convert = 10 × $29 = $290/mo
- At 5,000 free subs: 2% convert = 100 × $29 = $2,900/mo
- At 10,000 free subs: 2.5% convert = 250 × $29 = $7,250/mo

### Stage 2: Ongoing Upgrade Triggers (Recurring Emails)

**Tease-and-withhold pattern (use in free issues):**
- In every free issue, include ONE section that's truncated:
  "the full template (with 47 pre-written subject lines) is in the Builder tier. [upgrade]"
- Never withhold the MAIN value of the issue. Only withhold the bonus asset.

**Monthly upgrade email (separate from regular issue):**
- First of every month: "here's what paid subscribers got last month that you missed"
- List 3 specific pieces: template names, revenue report highlights (teaser numbers), the alpha drops
- CTA: "get full access for $9/mo or $29/mo"

**Trigger-based upgrade prompts:**
- When subscriber opens 8+ issues without upgrading → send "you're a regular reader" email with upgrade offer
- When subscriber clicks 3+ paid-tier CTAs → send "you keep clicking this" email with 7-day free trial offer

## Beehiiv Setup (Paid Subscription)

1. Dashboard → Monetize → Paid Subscriptions → Enable
2. Set tiers:
   - Builder: $9/mo or $89/yr
   - Inner Circle: $29/mo or $279/yr
3. Set up gating:
   - In each paid post: toggle "Paid subscribers only" (Builder or Inner Circle)
   - Free preview: first 200 words visible to free tier
4. Welcome email for paid: auto-sends on upgrade. customize with specific onboarding steps.
5. Stripe connected: payouts on the 1st of every month.

**Annual pricing math:**
- Builder annual: $89/yr vs $108/yr monthly = save $19 (17% discount)
- Inner Circle annual: $279/yr vs $348/yr monthly = save $69 (20% discount)
- Annual subscribers: 2-3x lower churn than monthly

## Churn Prevention

**On cancellation trigger in Beehiiv:**
Auto-email: "before you go — is there anything i could have done better?"

Give 3 options:
1. Pause subscription for 1 month (button)
2. Downgrade to free (keeps them on list)
3. Tell me why (reply)

**At-risk subscriber detection (monthly audit):**
- Filter: paid subscriber, hasn't opened last 3 issues
- Send personal email: "noticed you haven't been reading. is everything okay?"
- Offer: free 1-month extension in exchange for feedback

**Quarterly pricing review:**
- Year 1: $9/$29
- Year 2: consider moving to $12/$39 (grandfather existing)
- Never raise prices without emailing the list first
