## Whop Affiliate Program Setup

Whop has a built-in affiliate system. Enable it.

**Settings:**
- Commission rate: 30% recurring (for subscription products)
- Commission rate: 20% one-time (for digital products)
- Cookie duration: 90 days
- Minimum payout: $50

**How it works:**
Every community member gets a unique affiliate link. They share it. Someone buys. They earn 30% of what that person pays, every month.

**Example:**
- Member refers 5 Builder subscribers ($29/mo each)
- Member earns 30% × $29 × 5 = $43.50/mo recurring
- They love this. they promote for free.
- You lose 30% but you'd have paid 0% without them.

**Promote the affiliate program to members:**
- Welcome message mentions it
- Monthly "affiliate leaderboard" post in community
- Top affiliates get featured + bonus perks
