**1. Twitter/X (biggest driver)**

- 1-2 posts/week mentioning the community

**2. Newsletter CTA**
- Every newsletter issue bottom CTA: "go deeper inside the Academy"
- First email of welcome sequence = pitch the free trial

**3. Referral Program**
- Built into Skool: members get referral link
- Offer: refer 3 members = 1 month free
- Top referrer each month = featured + bonus session

**4. YouTube**
- Video descriptions: "template/worksheet inside PRINTMAXX Academy [link]"
- Pinned comment on every video

**5. Guest Experts**
- Invite 1 guest per month to do a call
- Guest promotes to their audience → free members → some convert
- Offer guests: free membership for life in exchange

## Churn Reduction Playbook

- Day 3 after join: auto DM "what brought you here? what's your biggest blocker?"
- Day 14: check if they've completed any courses. if not, message them.
- Cancellation survey: always ask why. offer 1 month pause instead of cancel.
- Re-engage at-risk members (no posts/logins in 14 days): personal message
- Monthly email: "here's what you missed this month" for inactive members
