| Profile visits | 200+ |
| Link clicks | 50+ |
| Conversions | 5+ sales |

### Optimization (Week 2)

**If views low (<500):**
- Test different hooks (first 3 seconds)
- Use trending audio
- Post at optimal times (6-9pm)

**If engagement low (<2%):**
- Stronger CTA
- Add controversy/hot take
- Use captions (80% watch without sound)

**If clicks low but views high:**
- Make CTA more urgent
- Add scarcity ("limited time")
- Show more product proof
